Vikram Cement v Commissioner of Central Excise, Indore

Supreme Court of India

16 February 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Singh, Altamas Kabir

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 251; 2006 (197) E.L.T. 145

Case Digest

Subject: Excise

Summary: Excise - Modvat/Cenvat credit on inputs - Matter covered by the decision in the case of Vikram Cement [2006 Indlaw SC 81] - Appeals where credit on inputs is concerned, are allowed - If the mines are captive mines so that they constitute one integrated unit together with the concerned cement factory, Modvat/Cenvat credit on capital goods will be avail able to the assessee - If the mines are not captive mines Modvat/Cenvat credit on capital goods used in such mines will not be available to the concerned assessee - Matters remanded to the respective original authorities for decision only - Appeal disposed of.
