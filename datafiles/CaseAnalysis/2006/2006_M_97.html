Union of India and Another v Malti Sharma

Supreme Court of India

24 February 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, P.P. Naolekar

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 64; (2006) 9 SCC 262; 2006 (5) AWC 5124; JT 2006 (3) SC 154; 2006(2) SCALE 578; [2006] 2 S.C.R. 547; 2006 (3) SLR 226; 2006 (2) UPLBEC 1423

Case Digest

Subject: Health & Drug; Practice & Procedure; Service

Keywords: Indian Nursing Council Act, 1947

Summary: [A.] Service - Indian Nursing Council Act, 1947, ss. 13, 8(2)(d) - Respondent's appointment as inspector - Termination - WP against - Post abolished during pendency of writ - Single Judge held that service of Respondent was terminated mala fide - Appeal against - Held, appellant had not disclosed that a WP was pending - Whether post had been revived before such reinstatement again has not been disclosed - Its action was plainly mala fide both on facts as well as in law - No case has been made out for interference - Appeal dismissed.
