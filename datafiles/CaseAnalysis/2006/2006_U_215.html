Uttar Pradesh State Road Transport Corporation v Babu Ram

Supreme Court of India

04 July 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, R.V. Raveendran

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 284; (2006) 5 SCC 433; 2006 (7) AWC 7185; JT 2006 (6) SC 103; 2006(6) SCALE 514; 2006 (5) SLR 68; [2006] Supp3 S.C.R. 212; 2006 (3) UPLBEC 2256

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Practice & Procedure - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - Uttar Pradesh Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 10 - Temporary Appointment - Termination - Reinstatement - Sustainability - Respondent was appointed on temporary basis in 1980 to meet urgent needs during Khumbh Festival - As there was no further need for engaging respondent and others, their services were terminated by order dt. 19-9-1983 - Labour Court held that termination/retrenchment of respondent was illegal and invalid - Consequently Labour Court directed that respondent should be reinstated with continuity of service and should be paid back wages and other benefits - Appellant filed appeal contending that respondent had not offered any explanation for belated claim raised after 15 years in view of fact that grievance of respondent was raised in conciliation proceedings initiated in 1998 - On appeal by appellant, HC upheld order of Labour Court - Hence, instant appeal - Whether reinstatement of respondent in service, even after belated claim raised after 15 years, was sustainable - Held, workman had not placed any material to show that respondent had raised dispute within reasonable time, and/or that respondent was not responsible for delayed decision if any in conciliation proceedings - It was for respondent, to show that dispute was raised within reasonable time and that respondent was not responsible for any delay - Neither Labour Court nor HC had even noted factual position - Conclusion of HC was based on surmises and conjectures - Hence, order of HC was set aside and matter was remitted to HC to consider question of delay in seeking reference and should decide matter afresh in accordance with law - Appeal disposed of.
