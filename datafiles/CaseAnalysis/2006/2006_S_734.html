Tanaji Ramchandra Nimhan and Others v Swati Vinayak Nimhan and Others

Supreme Court of India

31 January 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

P.K. Balasubramanyan, S.B. Sinha

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 30; (2006) 2 SCC 300; AIR 2006 SC 1218; 2006 (3) Bom.C.R. 166; JT 2006 (2) SC 129; 2006(2) SCALE 81; 2006 (2) SCJ 170; [2006] 1 S.C.R. 948

Case Digest

Subject: Election; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Election Agents

Summary: [A.] Election - Bombay Provincial Municipal Corporations Act, 1949, ss. 16, 403 - Election petition challenging election of appellant - Alleged irregularities in counting - Ordered recounting by election tribunal and HC confirmed it - Appeal against - Held, election petitioner has not supplied adequate material in support of her prayer for an order of recount and there is no sufficient reason for ordering a recount - Appeals allowed.
