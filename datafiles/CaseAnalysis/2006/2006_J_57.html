Joint Commissioner of Income Tax v Mandideep Engineering and Pkg. India P. Limited

Supreme Court of India

12 April 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Ashok Bhan, D.K. Jain

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 1316; [2007] 292 ITR 1

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 80HH and 80-1 - Whether sections 80HH and 80-1 of the Income Tax Act, 1961 are independent of each other? - Whether, therefore, a new industrial unit can claim deductions under both the sections on the gross total income independently? - View of Madhya Pradesh High Court in P. Tobacco Products P. Ltd. v. CIT that both the sections are independent - Department has not appealed against the judgments of different High Courts following the view taken by the Madhya Pradesh HC - Held, The Department having accepted the view taken in those judgments cannot be permitted to take a contrary view in the present case involving the same point - Civil appeal is dismissed.
