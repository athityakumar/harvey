Adishwar Jain v Union of India and Another

Supreme Court of India

19 October 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Dalveer Bhandari

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 718; (2006) 1 SCC 339; JT 2006 (9) SC 432; 2006 (4) RCR(Criminal) 931; 2006(10) SCALE 553; [2006] Supp7 S.C.R. 801

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: [A.] Criminal - Conservation Of Foreign Exchange And Prevention Of Smuggling Activities Act, 1974, s. 3 - Customs Act, 1962, s. 108 - Smugglers and Foreign Exchange Manipulators (Forfeiture of Property) Act, 1976 - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 136, 22(5) - Challenge to detention order - Plea of unexplained delay, non placement of vital/ material documents before detaining authority, non- Supply of documents relied on or referred to in order of detention as also non-application of mind on part of detaining authority had been raised - Held, statements of Appellant and Prabhjot Singh, were material - They could not have been withheld - If original of said documents were not available, xeroxed copies thereof could have been made available to him - Appeal allowed.
