State Of U.P. & Ors. v Harihar Bhole Nath

Supreme Court of India

01 November 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Markandey Katju

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 738; (2006) 13 SCC 460; 2007 (1) AWC 566; 2007 (3) JhrCR(SC) 80; JT 2006 (9) SC 567; 2006(11) SCALE 322; 2007 (1) SLR 474; [2006] Supp8 S.C.R. 241; 2007 (1) UPLBEC 56

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Service

Keywords: Commutation Of Pension

Summary: [A.] Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 166(3), 309, 311(2) - Civil Service Regulations, regns. 351-A, 470 - Civil Service (Classification, Control and Appeal) Rules, rr. 351-A, 55 - Conduct of Business Rules - Orissa State Financial Corporation Employees' Provident Fund Regulations, 1959 - Interpretation and application of reg 351-A - Guilty of commission of misconduct or negligence causing pecuniary loss to Govt. - Held, order was authenticated in terms of cl (3) of art.166 of Constitution of India, as proceeding was initiated under orders of Governor and order of punishment was also passed under order of Governor, no sanction of Governor was required - Appeal allowed.
