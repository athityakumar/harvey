Yashwith Construction P. Limited v Simplex Concrete Piles India Limited and Another

Supreme Court of India

03 July 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

P.K. Balasubramanyan, R.V. Raveendran

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 378; (2006) 6 SCC 204; AIR 2006 SC 2798; 2006 (4) AWC 3353; 2006 (4) MLJ(SC) 945; 2006 (3) RCR(Civil) 592; 2007 (1) RLW 65; 2006(7) SCALE 48; [2006] Supp3 S.C.R. 96

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, ss. 11, 15(2) - Arbitrator resigned - Managing Director of respondent company appointed second arbitrator - Chief Justice of HC found that appointment of second arbitrator by Managing Director, after resignation of first arbitrator, was valid in law since it was permissible under contract and right to make such an appointment was saved by s.15(2) - Held, Managing Director of respondent is saved by s.15(2) and he has exercised that power on terms of arbitration agreement - No reason to grant leave to appeal - Petition dismissed.
