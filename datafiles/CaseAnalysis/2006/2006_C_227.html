Commnr. Of Central Excise, Indore v M/S Virdi Brothers And Ors

Supreme Court of India

12 December 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 951; (2007) 15 SCC 24; 2007 (207) E.L.T. 321; 2006(14) SCALE 115; 2006 (8) SCJ 945; [2006] Supp10 S.C.R. 830

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Excisability of goods

Summary: Central Excise Act, 1944 - CEGAT held that no excise duty is leviable and thus these plants are not subject to excisability - Whether refrigeration plant/cold storage plant/Central air-conditioning plant/caustic soda plant can be subjected to duty? - Held, in view of Circular No.58/1/2002-CX dated 15th January, 2002, which has been issued in exercise of power conferred under Section 37B of the Act, the view of the CEGAT cannot be faulted - Appeal dismissed.
