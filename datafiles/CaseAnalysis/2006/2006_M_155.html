Commissioner of Central Excise, Delhi v Messrs Allied Air-Conditioning Corporation (Regd.)

Supreme Court of India

13 September 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 482; (2006) 7 SCC 735; 2006 (202) E.L.T. 209; JT 2006 (9) SC 331; 2006(9) SCALE 170; [2006] Supp6 S.C.R. 41

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax; Trade

Keywords: Central Excise Officer

Summary: [A.] Excise - Central Excise Tariff Act, 1985 - Central Excise Act, 1944, s. 11(A) - Central Excise Rules, 1944, r. 173Q, r. 9(2) - Tribunal allowed respondent's appeal relying on a decision of this Court in PSI Data System Ltd v CCE (1996 Indlaw SC 2055) - Valuation of 'packaged type Air Conditioner' - Whether extended period of limitation is invokable in demanding duty - Held, tribunal has not analysed each item individually - It has also not indicated how ratio in PSI's case (supra) has any relevance - Remitted matter to CEGAT to consider stand of revenue as regards disputed items and deal with items individually and also examine rival stand on question of limitation - Appeals disposed of.
