Kamla Devi v Khushal Kanwar & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

15 December 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Markandey Katju

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 969; (2006) 13 SCC 295; AIR 2007 SC 663; 2007 (66) ALR 620; 2007 (2) AWC 1148; 2007 (1) CTC 186; 2007 (2) JhrCR(SC) 202; 2007 (2) MPLJ 25; 2007 (1) RCR(Civil) 468; 2007 (102) RD 425; 2006(13) SCALE 645; [2006] Supp10 S.C.R. 1147; 2007 (1) UC 397

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Family & Personal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 - Application of s. 100-A - Whether Special Appeal filed by Respondent No.1 before a Division Bench of the Rajasthan High Court was maintainable? - Held, letters patent appeal, which was filed prior to coming into force of the 2002 Act would be maintainable - Unable to accept the contentions of counsel for the appellant that s. 100A will have retrospective effect so as to bring within its fold even the appeals preferred prior to coming into force of the said Act - Appeal dismissed.
