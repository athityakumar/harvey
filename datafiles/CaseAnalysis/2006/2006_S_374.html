Devinder Singh and Others v State of Haryana and Another

Supreme Court of India

07 April 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Tarun Chatterjee

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 1263; (2006) 5 SCC 720; AIR 2006 SC 2850; 2007 (1) AWC 181; JT 2006 (6) SC 312

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Haryana Ceiling on Land Holding Act, 1972

Summary: Civil Procedure - Land & Property - Practice & Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 100 - Haryana Ceiling on Land Holding Act, 1972, ss. 18 and 26 - Whether suit filed by appellant/plaintiff is maintainable being barred by s. 26 of Act? - Dhulabhai v. State of M P, 1968 Indlaw SC 137, followed - Held, when statute has given finality to orders of special tribunal, Civil Court's jurisdiction can be regarded as having been excluded if there is adequate remedy to do what Civil Court would normally do in a suit - Appeal dismissed.
