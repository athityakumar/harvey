G. Amalorpavam and Others v R.C. Diocese of Madurai and Others

Supreme Court of India

06 March 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Tarun Chatterjee

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 79; (2006) 3 SCC 224; 2006 (63) ALR 301; 2006 (2) AWC 1463; 2006 (5) Bom.C.R. 896; JT 2006 (3) SC 183; 2006 (2) MLJ(SC) 63; 2006 (2) RCR(Civil) 426; 2006(3) SCALE 90; 2006 (3) SCJ 632; [2006] 2 S.C.R. 899

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure; Rent Control

Summary: [A.] Civil Procedure - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, o. 41, r. 31, s. 100 - Suit for possession of suit property as well as recovery of arrears of rent and damages - Decreed - Sub Judge and HC confirmed findings - Appeal against - Alleged non-compliance of o. 41, r. 31 - Held, where appellate court has considered entire evidence on record and discussed same in detail, come to any conclusion and its findings are supported by reasons even though point has not been framed by appellate Court there is substantial compliance with provisions of o. 41, r. 31 and judgment is not in any manner vitiated by absence of a point of determination - Appeals dismissed.
