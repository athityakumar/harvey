Sandvik Asia Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax-I, Pune and others

Supreme Court of India

27 January 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

AR. Lakshmanan, H.K. Sema

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 18; (2006) 2 SCC 508; AIR 2006 SC 1223; 2006 (4) Bom.C.R. 886; 2006 (1) CTC 741; 2006 (200) CTR 505; 2006 (196) E.L.T. 257; [2006] 280 ITR 643; JT 2006 (2) SC 7; 2006 (2) MLJ(SC) 25; 2006(1) SCALE 569; 2006 (2) SCJ 422; [2006] 1 S.C.R. 811; 2007 (8) S.T.R. 193

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Interest On Refund

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 11B, 139(8), 214, 214(1A), 215, 237, 240, 244, 244(1), 244(1A), 245, 264 - Direct Tax Laws (Amendment) Act, 1987 - Claim of interest - Whether an assessee is entitled to be compensated by Income-tax Department for delay in paying to assessee amounts admittedly due to it? - Held, Act recognizes principle that a person should only be taxed in accordance with law and hence where excess amounts of tax are collected from an assessee or any amounts are wrongfully withheld from an assessee without authority of law, revenue must compensate assessee - Directed payment of simple interest @ 9% p a from date it became payable till date it is actually paid - Appeals allowed.
