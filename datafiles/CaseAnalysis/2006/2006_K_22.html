K.S. Krishnaswamy Etc. v Union Of India & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

23 November 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

H.K. Sema, P.K. Balasubramanyan

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 839; (2006) 13 SCC 215; AIR 2007 SC (Supp) 1756; 2006 (7) AWC 6580; JT 2006 (10) SC 479; 2006(12) SCALE 307; 2007 (2) SLR 22; [2006] Supp9 S.C.R. 330; 2007 (1) UPLBEC 487

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Service

Keywords: Computation Of Pension

Summary: [A.] Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 136, 14 - Executive Business Rules - Controversy relates to scale of pay recommended by 5 th Pay Commission and corresponding acceptance of Govt. by a Policy decision and Executive Instructions clarified by Executive Instructions - Whether O M dated 11/5/2001 over-rides earlier O M dated 17/12/1998 clarifying Policy Resolution of Govt. dated 30/9/1997? - Whereas O M dated 17/12/1998 speaks of minimum pay in revised scale of pay w e f 1/1/1996 of post last held by pensioner, O M dated 11/5/2001 clarifies it as minimum of corresponding scale as on 1/1/1996 of scale of pay held by pensioner at time of superannuation/ retirement - Held, an increase in pay scale in any recommendation of a pay commission is a corresponding increase in pay scale - Executive Instructions dated 11/5/2001 were in form of further clarifying Executive Instructions dated 17/12/1998 and do not over-ride same - Appeals disposed of.
