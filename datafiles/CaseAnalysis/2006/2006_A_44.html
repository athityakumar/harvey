Reliance Airport Developers Pvt. Ltd. v Airports Authority Of India And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

07 November 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 913; (2006) 10 SCC 1; JT 2006 (10) SC 424; 2006(11) SCALE 208; [2006] Supp8 S.C.R. 398

Case Digest

Subject: Air Law; Carriers & Transportation; Constitution

Summary: Airport Privatisation- Challenge to the judgment of Delhi High Court confirming dismissing a challenge made to the exercise of discretion and against the decision taken by a group of Ministers in a matter of joint venture partnership as a part of the privatization policy. Held, Exercise of Discretion by group of Ministers proper- Appeal Dismissed
