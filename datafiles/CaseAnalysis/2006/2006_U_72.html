Union of India and Another v Tarsen Lal and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 September 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Lokeshwar Singh Panta

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 512; AIR 2007 SC 259; 2007 (1) AWC 131; 2006 (4) Law Herald (P&H) 3052; 2006(9) SCALE 485; 2006 (3) ShimLC 334; 2006 (6) SLR 5

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: [A.] Service - Indian Railway Establishment Manual - Respondent filed Original Application claiming that he was entitled to pay and allowance from date on which proforma promotion was given and not from date of actual promotion - Held, in view of what has been stated in Virender Kumar, General Manager, Northern Railways, New Delhi vs. Avinash Chandra Chadha and Ors (1990 Indlaw SC 814) and P O Abraham and Ors in C.A. 8904/1994 decided on 13/8/1997, Tribunal and HC were not justified in granting relief to respondent - No arrears on this account shall be payable as he did not actually shoulder duties and responsibilities of higher posts - Appeal allowed.
