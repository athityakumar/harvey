Municipal Corporation, Jabalpur v Om Prakash Dubey

Supreme Court of India

05 December 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Markandey Katju

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 905; (2007) 1 SCC 373; AIR 2007 SC 893; 2007 (1) AWC 422; 2007 (3) RCR(Criminal) 945; 2006(13) SCALE 266; 2007 (6) SLR 531; [2006] Supp10 S.C.R. 85

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Madhya Pradesh Municipal Corporation Act, 1956 - Service - Is there any distinction between 'irregular appointment' and 'illegal appointment'? - Held, Division Bench, by reason of impugned judgment had issued directions in exercise of its jurisdiction under s. 12 of the Contempt of Courts Act, 1971, without arriving at a finding as to how the Corporation has violated its order - It issued directions which are contrary to or inconsistent with the directions issued by a Single Judge - Appeal allowed.
