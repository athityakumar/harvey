Vikramjit Singh @ Vicky v State Of Punjab

Supreme Court of India

24 November 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Markandey Katju

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 856; (2006) 12 SCC 306; (2007) 1 SCC (Cr) 732; 2007 CRLJ 1000; 2007 (1) JCC 64; 2006(12) SCALE 321B; [2006] Supp9 S.C.R. 375

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Criminal - Code Of Criminal Procedure, 1973, s. 313 - Indian Evidence Act, 1872, s. 106, s. 8 - Murder - Death sentence - Appeal against - Held, circumstances which according to prosecution lead to proof of guilt against accused must be put to him in his examination u/s. 313 of CrPC - It was not done - Recovery of a knife alone is not sufficient to arrive at a finding of guilt - Appeal allowed.
