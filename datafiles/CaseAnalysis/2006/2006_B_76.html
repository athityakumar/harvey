Commissioner of Central Excise, Mumbai v Bigen Industries Limited

Supreme Court of India

12 April 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Ashok Bhan, D.K. Jain

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 546; 2006 (197) E.L.T. 305

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax; Trade

Summary: Excise - Central Excise Act, 1944, s. 35 L(b) - Central Excise Tariff Act, 1985 - Notification No. 140/83-C E - Whether tribunal erred in holding appellant entitled to exemption under notification no. 140/83-C E? - Held, once trade mark has been registered in name of assessee by statutory authority authorized to do so recognizing assessee to be sole proprietor of trade mark for India, adjudicating authority as well as Commr. (Appeals) erred in denying benefit of notification under consideration - No infirmity in impugned order - Appeals dismissed.
