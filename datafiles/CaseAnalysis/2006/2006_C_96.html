Goetze (India) Limited v Commisioner of Income Tax

Supreme Court of India

24 March 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Ruma Pal, Dalveer Bhandari

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 663; 2006 (204) CTR 182; [2006] 284 ITR 323; 195 TAXATION 228; [2006] 157 TAXMAN 1

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 254 - Whether appellant assessee could make a claim for deduction other than by filing a revised return? - Deduction was disallowed by the Assessing Officer on the ground that there was no provision under the Income Tax Act to make amendment in the return of income by modifying an application at the assessment stage without revising the return - Held, tribunal was right in upholding the Assessing Officer's order - Appeal dismissed.
