Ramchandra Murarilal Bhattad & Ors. v State Of Maharashtra & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

05 December 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Dalveer Bhandari

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 906; (2007) 2 SCC 588; AIR 2007 SC 401; 2007 (2) Bom.C.R. 63; [2006] Supp10 S.C.R. 106

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority Act, 1974, Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (Disposal of Land) Regulations, 1977

Summary: Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority Act, 1974 - Tender for establishing a Convention and Exhibition Centre - Writ petition questioning validity of clause 2.4 of Request for Proposal in which Authority reserved right to reject at any stage all or any of the bids without assigning any reason; power of the Authority to cancel or reject the bids; cancellation of the bid by the decision purported to have been made on 1.9.2003 - Whether the Authority had any jurisdiction to cancel the bid? - Held, in the absence of any plea that the policy decision adopted by the Authority would be violative of the provisions of the Act or any master plan, the same cannot be entertained - Appeals dismissed.
