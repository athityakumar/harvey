Uttaranchal Road Transport Corpn.And Ors v Mansaram Nainwal

Supreme Court of India

28 July 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Lokeshwar Singh Panta

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 349; (2006) 6 SCC 366; AIR 2006 SC 2840; 2007(2) ALL MR 457; 2006 (6) AWC 6182; 2006 (4) JhrCR(SC) 163; JT 2006 (7) SC 25; 2006(7) SCALE 430; 2006 (5) SLR 261; [2006] Supp4 S.C.R. 168; 2006 (3) UC 1691

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Service - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 4K - Central Civil Services (Classification, Control and Appeal) Rules, r. 5(1) - Respondent-driver found guilty of rash and negligent driving and removed from service - HC set aside termination and ordered reinstatement without backwages on ground that in criminal trial respondent was acquitted placing reliance on a decision of this Court in Capt M Paul Anthony (1999 Indlaw SC 280) - Appeal against - Held, HC has not discussed factual scenario as to how Anthony's case had any application - Remitted matter back to HC for fresh consideration - Appeal partly allowed.
