Kerala State Science and Technology Museum v Rambal Company and Others

Supreme Court of India

02 August 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Lokeshwar Singh Panta

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 355; (2006) 6 SCC 258; 2006 (5) ALD(SC) 74; 2006 (4) AWC 4008; JT 2006 (7) SC 132; 2006 (4) MLJ(SC) 1824; 2006(7) SCALE 479; 2006 (6) SCJ 289; [2006] Supp4 S.C.R. 243

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Banking & Finance; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Contract & Commercial - Revenue Recovery Act, 1968, s. 34 - Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, s. 11 - Travancore Cochin Literary and Scientific Societies Registration Act, 1995 - Limitation Act, 1963, art. 112 - Kerala Revenue Recovery Act, 1968, s. 71 - Demand notice - Plea of limitation - Single Judge referred matter to DB to decide question of limitation - DB held that quantification of damages done and demanded from respondent No 1 cannot be legally sustained and accordingly they were set aside - Appeal against - Held, when reference is made on a specific issue either by a Single Judge or DB to a larger Bench, Larger Bench cannot adjudicate upon an issue which is not question referred - Set aside order of DB and remitted matter back for fresh consideration limiting examination to question whether demand by barred by limitation - Appeals disposed of.
