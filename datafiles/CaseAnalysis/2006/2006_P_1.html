Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 11, 11A, 12, 12(2), 18, 30, 4, 6, 9(2), 9(4)(3) - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 299 - Award by collector - Plea that after award was approved by Govt., Collector should have signed award and thereafter communicated his award to parties and in eye of law no award was made by Collector - Held, award which has already been signed by Collector becomes an award as soon as it is approved by Govt. without any alteration - There was no necessity for Collector to sign award again, nor does s.11 require that for purpose of pronouncing award notice should be given by Collector to persons interested - Appeals dismissed.
