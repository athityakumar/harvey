Bhimapa Chandappa Hosamani and Others v State of Karnataka

Supreme Court of India

20 September 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Singh, Altamas Kabir

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 504; (2006) 1 SCC 323; 2006 (3) JCC 1670; 2006(9) SCALE 406; [2006] Supp6 S.C.R. 393

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 302 and 34 - Trial Court acquitted appellants - Appellants were sentenced to undergo imprisonment for life by High Court - Appeal against - Held, evidence leads to suspect assertion of PW-2 that he is an eye witness - Several circumstances which give rise to a serious doubt about truthfulness of PW-1 - No reliable corroboration of evidence - Gave benefit of doubt to appellants - Appeal allowed.
