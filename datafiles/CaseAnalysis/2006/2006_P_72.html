Prakash Singh and Others v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

22 September 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.K. Sabharwal, C.K. Thakker, P.K. Balasubramanyan

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 514; (2006) 8 SCC 1; 2006 (4) RCR(Criminal) 439; 2006(9) SCALE 444; [2006] Supp6 S.C.R. 473

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Defence & Security Forces; Parliament & Legislature; Service

Keywords: Attorney-General

Summary: [A.] Defence & Security Forces - Indian Police Act, 1861 - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 142, 144, 32, 355 - Police Act, 1861 - Prayer for issue of directions to Govt. of India to frame a new Police Act on lines of model Act drafted by Commission in order to ensure that police is made accountable essentially and primarily to law of land and people - Issued directions to Central Govt, State Govts and Union Territories for compliance till framing of appropriate legislations - Order accordingly.
