Mohinder Kaur and others v Hira Nand Sindhi (Ghoriwala) and another

Supreme Court of India

05 May 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

K.G. Balakrishnan, D.K. Jain

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 1696; (2015) 4 SCC 434

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation

Keywords: Loss of Consortium

Summary: Carriers & Transportation - Award - Loss of Consortium - Aggrieved petitioner filed petition challenging order of the tribunal.

Held, HC granted Rs.50,000 for loss of consortium with interest @ 9% p.a. from the date of the passing of the order till the date of realization. HC granted interest @ 9% per annum on the amount of Rs.50,000 from the date of filing of the petition. Petitions disposed of.
