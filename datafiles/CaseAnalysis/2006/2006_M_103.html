Management of National Seeds Corporation Limited v K.V. Rama Reddy

Supreme Court of India

29 September 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Lokeshwar Singh Panta

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 566; (2006) 11 SCC 645; 2006 (6) AWC 6049; 2007 (2) LLJ 30; 2006(9) SCALE 701; 2006 (3) ShimLC 279; [2006] Supp6 S.C.R. 725

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: [A.] Administrative - Service - National Seeds Corporation (Conduct, Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1992, r. 31(7) - Central Civil Services (Classification, Control & Appeal) Rules 1965, r. 14(8) - Departmental proceedings against respondent - Permission to take assistance of one Shri V Vishwanathan who was a retired Assistant Manager of Corporation rejected - Engagement of legal practitioner in a disciplinary proceeding - Held, an employee has no right to representation in departmental proceedings by another person or a lawyer unless Service Rules specifically provide for same - Right to representation is available only to extent specifically provided for in Rules - It has not been shown as to how a legal practitioner would be in a better position to assist respondent so far as documents in question are concerned - Appeal allowed.
