T.V. Saravanan @ S. A.R. Prasana Venkatachaariar Chaturvedi v State Through Secretary and Another

Supreme Court of India

16 February 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Singh, Altamas Kabir

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 57; (2006) 2 SCC 664; AIR 2006 SC 1462; 2006 CRLJ 1619; JT 2006 (2) SC 433; 2006 (2) RCR(Criminal) 1; 2006(2) SCALE 358; 2006 (3) SCJ 587

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Human Rights; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Criminal - Tamil Nadu Prevention of Dangerous Activities of Bootleggers, Drug Offenders, Forest Offenders, Goondas Immoral Traffic Offenders and Slum Grabbers and Video Pirates Act, 1982, s. 3(1) - Detention of a goonda - HC upheld detention order - Appeal against - Whether an order of detention was justified, even though appellant was in custody on date of issuance of order of detention? - Held, bail applications moved by appellant had been rejected by Courts and there was no material whatsoever to apprehend that he was likely to move a bail application or that there was imminent possibility of prayer for bail being granted - In absence of such material on record mere ipse dixit of detaining authority is not sufficient to sustain order of detention - Directed release of appellant - Appeal allowed.
