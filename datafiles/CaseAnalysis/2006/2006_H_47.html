Himmat Singh v State of Haryana and Others

Supreme Court of India

24 February 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Dalveer Bhandari

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 66; (2006) 9 SCC 256; 2006 (2) AWC 1759; 2006 (2) Law Herald (P&H) 1048; 2006 (2) MLJ(SC) 416; 2006(2) SCALE 560; 2006 (3) SCJ 220; [2006] 2 S.C.R. 557

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Defence & Security Forces; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: [A.] Service - CrPC, 1973, ss. 133, 140, 143, 144, 145, 87, 88, 95, 99 - Punjab Police Rules, 1934 - Voluntary retirement - Appellant had withdrawn offer of voluntary retirement - Not allowed - Writ dismissed by HC - Appeal against - Held, HC arrived at a finding of fact on basis of affidavit evidence - Alleged non compliance rule relating to maintenance of records furthermore has not been raised by Appellant either in Writ or in special leave petition - Appellant was not entitled to any relief in view of his conduct as he suppressed material facts - Appeal dismissed.
