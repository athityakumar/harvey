Union Of India & Ors v S.K. Saigal & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

15 November 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

H.K. Sema, P.K. Balasubramanyan

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 762; (2007) 14 SCC 556; AIR 2007 SC 1211; 2007 (5) AWC 5262; 2007 (3) JhrCR(SC) 77; JT 2006 (10) SC 284; 2006(12) SCALE 367; 2007 (1) SLR 207; [2006] Supp8 S.C.R. 1165

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Service

Keywords: Central Ground Water Board (Scientific Group 'A' Posts) Recruitment Rules, 1995

Summary: [A.] Service - Central Ground Water Board (Scientific Group 'A' Posts) Recruitment Rules, 1995, r. 7 (2)(b) - Promotion - Whether particular Rules governing service conditions could be brushed aside without challenging Rules and mandate of Rules could be ignored in absence of a challenge and without striking them down? - Held, Rule shows that deputationists for promotion to post of Grade 'C' required 8 years experience as Jr Hydrologists on a regular basis whereas departmental candidates must have completed 5 years regular service in Grade 'B' in scale of Rs. 2200- 4000 - There is not even a whisper of challenging Rules as discriminatory or ultra- Vires, much less r.7(2)(b) - No mandamus can be issued which would be contrary to Act and Rules - Appeals allowed.
