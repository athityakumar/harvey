Bapu v State Of Maharashtra

Supreme Court of India

16 November 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Markandey Katju, S.B. Sinha

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 767; (2006) 12 SCC 73; 2007 CRLJ 310; 2006 (3) JCC 2144; JT 2006 (10) SC 197; 2007 (1) RCR(Criminal) 29; 2006(11) SCALE 561; [2006] Supp9 S.C.R. 52

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 302 - Appellant was convicted and sentenced to undergo life imprisonment and fine of Rs. 1, 000/- - Appeal against - Held, in all dying declarations, deceased stated that it was appellant who poured kerosene on her and set her on fire by a matchstick and all these dying declarations are consistent with each other - No reason to disbelieve dying declaration of deceased - Appeal dismissed.
