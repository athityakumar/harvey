Minor Sunil Oraon Tr. Guardian & Ors v C.B.S.E. & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

13 November 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Lokeshwar Singh Panta

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 764; (2006) 13 SCC 673; AIR 2007 SC 458; 2006 (7) AWC 7641; JT 2006 (10) SC 375; 2006(12) SCALE 203; [2006] Supp8 S.C.R. 1125

Case Digest

Subject: Education; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Education - Prayer for a direction to respondent-Central Board of Secondary Education (in short 'CBSE') to allow students to appear in examination conducted by CBSE and to publish their results - Single Judge permitted candidates to appear pursuant to interim order, subsequently WP was dismissed on ground that school was not affiliated to CBSE - Appeal against - Held, interim orders of nature passed in present case are detrimental to education and its efficient management - Though ultimate victims are innocent students that cannot be a ground for granting relief to appellant - Appeal dismissed.
