Ashok Kumar Sahu v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

08 August 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Dalveer Bhandari

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 393; (2006) 6 SCC 704; AIR 2006 SC 2879; 2006 (6) AWC 6389; JT 2006 (7) SC 274; 2006(7) SCALE 593; 2006 (5) SLR 417; [2006] Supp4 S.C.R. 394; 2006 (3) UPLBEC 2427

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: [A.] Service - All India Services Act, 1951 - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 142 - All India Services (Death-cum- Retirement Benefits) Rules, 1958, r. 16 - All India Services (DCRB) Rules, 1953, r. 16(2A) - Central Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1972, r. 48-A - Withdrawal of offer seeking voluntary retirement - Division Bench opined that request of appellant for voluntary retirement being accepted by Joint Cadre Authority and a notification having been given effect thereto, no irregularity or illegality can be said to have been committed by respondents - Appeal against - Directions issued - Appellant shall be paid all his pensionary benefits with interest at rate of 9% per annum with effect from 01-08-1997 - Appellant shall be paid his salary for period 01-08-1997 to 08-09-1997 - Appeal partly allowed.
