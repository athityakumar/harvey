Accountant General Of Orissa And Anr v R. Ramamurty And Anr

Supreme Court of India

29 November 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 896; (2006) 12 SCC 557; (2007) 2 SCC (L&S) 330; AIR 2007 SC 622; 2006 (7) AWC 7553; 2007 (1) KLT 170; 2006(13) SCALE 20; 2007 (2) SLR 33; [2006] Supp9 S.C.R. 776

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Service

Keywords: Commutation Of Pension

Summary: CCS (Pension) Rules, 1972 - What is the mode of calculating of restoration of pension in respect of employees covered by r. 37A? - Held, applicants are to be treated on the same footing as other Central Government employees insofar as the question of restoration of one-third of commuted pension is concerned - Anything which is not a part of pension has to be paid in full insofar as those who have commuted one-third pension - Appeals dismissed.
