Mohinder Prasad Jain v Manohar Lal Jain

Supreme Court of India

24 February 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, P.K. Balasubramanyan

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 152; (2006) 2 SCC 724; AIR 2006 SC 1471; 2006 (63) ALR 506; 2006 (4) Bom.C.R. 851; 2006 (3) JhrCR(SC) 77; JT 2006 (2) SC 620; 2006 (2) Law Herald (P&H) 886; 2006 (143) PLR 667; 2006 (2) RCR(Civil) 36; 2006 (1) RCR(Rent) 250; 2006(2) SCALE 531; 2006 (2) SCJ 649; [2006] 2 S.C.R. 513

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure; Rent Control

Summary: [A.] Rent Control - Haryana Urban (Control of Rent and Eviction) Act, 1973, s. 13 - Eviction petition by a co-owner on bona fide requirement of a non-residential premises - Maintainability - Held, it is not necessary for co-owner to show before initiating eviction proceeding before Rent Controller that he had taken option or consent of other co-owners - Nothing has been brought on record to show that co-owners of respondent had objected to eviction proceedings initiated by respondent - Appeal dismissed.
