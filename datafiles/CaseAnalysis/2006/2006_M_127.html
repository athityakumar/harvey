State of Uttar Pradesh and Others v Maqbool Ahmad

Supreme Court of India

25 August 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

C.K. Thakker, Markandey Katju

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 436; (2006) 7 SCC 521; 2006 (7) ADJ 189; 2006 (6) AWC 5979; JT 2006 (8) SC 166; 2006(8) SCALE 556; 2006 (5) SLR 455; [2006] Supp5 S.C.R. 375; 2006 (3) UPLBEC 2910

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: [A.] Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 14, 16, 23, 39(d) - Respondent herein joined Irrigation Department on September 30, 1970 - Up to November 4, 1977, he continued with Irrigation Department and on approval of UPPSC, he was shifted to Public Works Department in November, 1977 - Held, there was no break of service and it remained continuous all throughout - There was no reason to deprive him of selection grade or suppertime scale as per Govt. Order - HC was right and justified in allowing claim of respondent and in granting benefits in his favour - Appeal dismissed.
