Regional Provident Fund Commissioner v Sanatan Dharam Girls Secondary School and Others

Supreme Court of India

30 October 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

AR. Lakshmanan, Tarun Chatterjee

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 728; (2007) 1 SCC (L&S) 167; (2007) 1 SCC 268; AIR 2007 SC 276; 2006 (7) AWC 7515; 2006 (3) CLR 1046; 2007 (112) FLR 314; 2007 (2) JhrCR(SC) 101; JT 2006 (10) SC 159; 2007 (1) LLJ 458; 2007 (1) RLW 788; 2006(11) SCALE 500; 2007 (1) SLR 834; [2006] Supp7 S.C.R. 849

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Employees' Provident Funds and Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1952, Rajasthan Industrial Tribunal (Constitution & Proceedings) Validating Act, 1959, Rajasthan Non-Government Educational Institutions Act, 1989

Summary: [A.] Service - Employees' Provident Funds and Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1952, ss. 16(1), 16(1)(b), 17(1) - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 246(2), 254(2) - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 92 - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 7 - Rajasthan Industrial Tribunal (Constitution & Proceedings) Validating Act, 1959 - Rajasthan Non-Government Educational Institutions Act, 1989 - Whether provisions of EPF Act, 1952 are applicable to Non- Govt Educational Institutions or not in view of provisions contained in s 16? - Whether respondents Institutions will fall under exceptions stated in s 16(1) (b)? - Held, State Govt. has power of Superintendent or authority to direct, restrict or regulate working of educational institutions - Admittedly State Act has been enacted and has received assent of president subsequent to applicability of EPF Act, 1952 on educational institutions - Appeals dismissed.
