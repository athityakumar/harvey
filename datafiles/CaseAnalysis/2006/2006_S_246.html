State Of Karnataka & Ors v Ameerbi & Ors

Supreme Court of India

07 December 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Markandey Katju

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 999; (2007) 11 SCC 681; 2006 (7) AWC 7424; JT 2007 (1) SC 279; 2006(13) SCALE 319; 2007 (7) SCJ 707; 2007 (1) SLR 722

Case Digest

Subject: Administrative; Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Representation of People Act, State Administrative Tribunal

Summary: Administrative Tribunals Act, 1985, s. 15 - Integrated Child Development Service (ICDS) Programme - Tribunal held that although Anganwadi workers and helpers are paid honorarium, they hold civil post - Held, appointments made under a scheme and recruitment process being carried out through a committee, in our opinion, would not render the incumbents thereof holders of civil post - Tribunal has no jurisdiction to entertain the application - Appeals allowed.
