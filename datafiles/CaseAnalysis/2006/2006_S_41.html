Sadhu Singh v Darshan Singh and Another

Supreme Court of India

26 July 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Dalveer Bhandari

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 388; (2006) 6 SCC 255; 2006 (5) ALD(SC) 12; 2006 (64) ALR 905; 2006 (4) AWC 3253; JT 2006 (7) SC 95; 2006 (4) MLJ(SC) 949; 2006 (4) RCR(Civil) 55; 2006(7) SCALE 368; 2006 (Supp) RD 637; [2006] Supp4 S.C.R. 76

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Election; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Election - Election petition - Election Tribunal directed recounting of ballot papers - Revision application dismissed - Appeal against - Held, conditions precedent necessary for a direction of recounting of votes stand satisfied - No case has been made out for interference with impugned judgment - Appeal allowed.
