National Fertilizers Limited and Others v Somvir Singh

Supreme Court of India

12 May 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, P.P. Naolekar

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 632; (2006) 5 SCC 493; (2006) SCC (L&S) 1152; AIR 2006 SC 2319; 2006 (7) AWC 7038; 2006 (4) JhrCR(SC) 25; JT 2006 (11) SC 279; 2006 (3) MLJ(SC) 90; 2006(6) SCALE 101; 2006 (5) SLR 50; [2006] Supp2 S.C.R. 397

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law; Service; Contract & Commercial

Keywords: Re Appointment, Public Sector, Temporary Or Ad Hoc

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 12, 142 and 136 - Indian Contract Act, 1872, s. 70 - State - Recruitment - Whether appointment of respondent against a policy decision on contractual basis is valid and legal? - Held, respondent do not have any legal right though working for long time and since respondent is not holding any post so not entitled to paid salary on regular scale of pay and HC judgment set aside in exercise of jurisdiction u/art. 142 in keeping principle embodied u/s. 70 of Contract Act - Appeal allowed without any costs.
