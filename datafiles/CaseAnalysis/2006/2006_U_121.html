Rajinder Arora v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 March 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, P.K. Balasubramanyan

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 87; (2006) 4 SCC 796; AIR 2006 SC 1719; 2006 CRLJ 2102; 2006 (1) JCC 482; JT 2006 (3) SC 518; 2006 (2) RCR(Criminal) 752; 2006 (4) SCJ 140; [2006] 3 S.C.R. 9

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Customs; Fema

Keywords: Foreign Trade Development and Regulation Act, 1994

Summary: [A.] Criminal - Customs Act, 1962, ss. 108, 2(39) - Foreign Trade Development and Regulation Act, 1994, ss. 10, 11, 9(4) - Detention under COFEPOSA - WP filed by Appellant praying for quashing of order of detention dismissed - Appeal against - Held, delay caused in issuing order of detention has not been explained - Status report called for from Customs Department has not been taken into consideration by competent authorities - Impugned order of detention cannot be sustained - Appeal allowed.
