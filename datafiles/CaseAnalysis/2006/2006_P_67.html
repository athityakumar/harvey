Shakuntala Chandrakant Shreshti v Prabhakar Maruti Garvali & Anr

Supreme Court of India

10 November 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Markandey Katju

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 843; (2007) 11 SCC 668; 2006 (4) ACC 769; AIR 2007 SC 248; JT 2007 (1) SC 15; 2006(11) SCALE 609; 2007 (2) SLR 348; [2006] Supp8 S.C.R. 939

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: [A.] Labour & Industrial Law - Workmen's Compensation Act, 1923 - Minimum Wages Act, 1948 - Held, unless evidence is brought on record to elaborate that death by way of cardiac arrest has occurred because of stress or strain, Commissioner would not have jurisdiction to grant damages - Commissioner did not go into jurisdictional facts not arrived at any finding based on any legal evidence in regard to causal connection between employment and death - Appeal dismissed.
