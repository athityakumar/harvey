A.P.S.R.T.C. and Another v B.S. David Paul

Supreme Court of India

01 February 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, R.V. Raveendran

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 23; (2006) 2 SCC 282; AIR 2006 SC 961; 2006 (5) AWC 4803; 2006 (1) CLR 775; 2006 (2) JhrCR(SC) 119; JT 2006 (2) SC 149; 2006(2) SCALE 110; 2006 (2) SCJ 167; [2006] 1 S.C.R. 1001; 2006 (2) SLR 383; 2006 (1) UPLBEC 708

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: [A.] Service - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 10, 33C(2) - Back wages denied to reinstated workmen in terms of judgment in APSRTC and Anr Vs S Narsagoud (2003 Indlaw SC 25) - Held, relief of back-wages must be deemed to have been denied, for what is claimed but not granted necessarily gets denied in judicial or quasi- Judicial proceeding - Appeals allowed.
