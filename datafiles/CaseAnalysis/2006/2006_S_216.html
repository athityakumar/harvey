Star Paper Mills Limited v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

19 September 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 508; (2006) 10 SCC 201; 2006 (6) AWC 6061; 2006 (101) RD 568; 2006(9) SCALE 390; [2006] Supp6 S.C.R. 380

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: [A.] Trade - Uttar Pradesh Krishi Utpadan Mandi Niyamavali, 1965, rr. 2(xiii), 70 - Uttar Pradesh Krishi Utpadan Mandi Adhiniyam, 1964, s. 40 - Prayer to restrain respondents Rajya Krishi Utpadan Mandi Parishad and several Krishi Utpadan Mandi Samitis from levying and/or collecting any market fee on purchases made by appellant from Uttar Pradesh Forest Corporation, Lucknow - Held, not a case where no factual adjudication is necessary - HC was justified in view that statutory remedy is to be availed -Let appellant file necessary details as required under Niyamavali within three weeks from today and place its stand before concerned authority for consideration - Appeals disposed of.
