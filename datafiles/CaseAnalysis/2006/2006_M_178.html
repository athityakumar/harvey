Shin Satellite Public Company Limited v Messrs Jain Studios Limited

Supreme Court of India

31 January 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 24; (2006) 2 SCC 628; AIR 2006 SC 963; 2006 (1) ARBLR 286; 2006 (2) AWC 1259; 2006 (2) CompLJ 417; JT 2006 (2) SC 89; 2006 (1) RCR(Civil) 612; 2006(2) SCALE 53; 2006 (2) SCJ 252; [2006] 1 S.C.R. 933

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Contract & Commercial

Summary: [A.] Contract & Commercial - Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, s. 11(6) - Companies Act, 1956 - Indian Contract Act, 1872, s. 28 - Application to appoint an arbitrator - Arbitration Agreement - Validity - Contention of respondent that cl. 23 of Arbitration Agreement that is arbitrator's determination shall be final and binding between parties and parties waive all rights of appeal or objection in any jurisdiction, could not be termed as 'Arbitration clause' - Whether arbitration agreement is legal, valid and enforceable? - Held, if court holds agreement severable, it should implement and enforce that part which is legal, valid and in consonance of law - Cl. 20 (severability) expressly states that if any provision of agreement is held invalid, illegal or unenforceable, it would not prejudice remainder - Arbitrator appointed - Petition allowed.
