Balasaheb K. Thackeray and Another v Venkat @ Babru (S/o Wamanrao Deshpande Charthankar and Another )

Supreme Court of India

05 July 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 381; (2006) 5 SCC 530; 2006 (2) JCC 1183; JT 2006 (7) SC 44; 2006 (3) RCR(Criminal) 631; 2006(6) SCALE 557; [2006] Supp3 S.C.R. 301

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Criminal - IPC, 1860, ss. 34, 500 - CrPC, 1973, ss. 256, 302, 482, 495 - Private complaint - What is effect of death of complainant? - Held, permission to conduct prosecution has to be obtained from Magistrate inquiring into or trying a case - If any permission is sought for by legal heirs of deceased complainant to continue prosecution, same shall be considered in its perspective by Court dealing with matter - Order accordingly.
