Malik Mazhar Sultan and Another v Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission and Others

Supreme Court of India

03 April 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.K. Sabharwal, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 108; (2006) 9 SCC 507; 2006 (3) ADJ 621; 2006 (2) AWC 1993; 2006 (2) ESC 171; JT 2006 (4) SC 531; 2006(4) SCALE 1; 2006 (5) SCJ 277; [2006] 3 S.C.R. 689; 2006 (3) SLR 71; 2006 (3) Supreme 493; 2006 (2) UPLBEC 1603; 2006 (3) UPLBEC 2571

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: [A.] Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 234, 309 - Uttar Pradesh Judicial Service Rules, 2001, r. 4(m) - Recruitment process - Recruitment to posts of Civil Judge (Junior Division) under U.P. Judicial Service Rules 2001 as to eligibility of some candidates from point of view of age - Is HC right in its conclusion or 01-07-2001 or 01-07-2002 is relevant date for determining age as a condition of eligibility as contended on behalf of those candidates who stand excluded as a result of impugned judgment? - Held, r.10 makes those eligible who are eligible in recruitment year in which process of recruitment is initiated by appointing authority - Those who were eligible as on 01-07-2002 and on 01-07-2004 - Appeals allowed.
