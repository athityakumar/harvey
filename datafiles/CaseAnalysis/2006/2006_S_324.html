Vemareddy Kumaraswamy Reddy and Another v State of Andhra Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

13 February 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, R.V. Raveendran

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 45; (2006) 2 SCC 670; AIR 2006 SC 3517; 2006 (2) ALD(SC) 92; JT 2006 (2) SC 361; 2006(2) SCALE 314; [2006] 2 S.C.R. 190

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Andhra Pradesh Land Reforms (Ceiling on Agricultural Holdings) Rules, 1974, Limitation Act, 1939, Andra Pradesh Forest Act, 1882

Summary: [A.] Land & Property - Andhra Pradesh Land Reforms (Ceiling on Agricultural Holdings) Act, 1973, s. 15 - Andra Pradesh Forest Act, 1882, s. 26 - Andhra Pradesh Land Reforms (Ceiling on Agricultural Holdings) Rules, 1974, r. 11 - Scope and ambit of r.11 - Surrender of surplus land by appellant - Seignorage rates - Dispute relates to amount payable in respect of fruit bearing trees standing on land - HC held that appellants are not entitled to get amount for 30 years but it was payable only for one year - Appeal against - Held, amounts are to be calculated from 5 th to 30 th years - Stand of State Govt. as accepted by HC that seignorage rate is for one year and accordingly fixing it for 12 year is clearly unsustainable - Trees were 12 years old at time land were surrendered and, therefore, for balance 18 years only appellants will be entitled to at relevant seignorage rates - Appeal partly allowed.
