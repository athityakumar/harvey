M/S. Pandey & Co. Builders Pvt. Ltd v State Of Bihar & Anr

Supreme Court of India

10 November 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Dalveer Bhandari

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 749; (2007) 1 SCC 467; AIR 2007 SC 465; 2007 (1) AWC 837; 2007 (2) JhrCR(SC) 360B; JT 2006 (10) SC 414; 2007 (2) RCR(Civil) 457; 2006(11) SCALE 665; [2006] Supp8 S.C.R. 997

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Foreign Award, Domestic Award

Summary: [A.] Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, ss. 14, 14(2), 15, 2(1)(e), 31(4), 34(2), 37, 37(2), 11 - Bengal, Agra and Assam Civil Courts Act, 1857 - Arbitration Act, 1940 - Foreign Awards (Recognition and Enforcement) Act, 1961 - Arbitration (Protocol and Convention) Act, 1937 - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 15 - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 136, 225 - Appellant undertook a contract for execution of canal repair work for Rs. 11, 33, 421/- - Superintending Engineer already passed an award - Claim before arbitrator - Arbitrator held that he had no jurisdiction to proceed with matter - Appeal against dismissed by HC holding that it had no jurisdiction to hear appeal as in terms of s 37(2) of 1996 Act, appeal lay before District Court - Appeal against - Held, no reason to differ with HC Appeal dismissed.
