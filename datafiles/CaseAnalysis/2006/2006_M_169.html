Messrs BSES Limited @ Reliance Energy Limited v Messrs Fenner India Limited and Another

Supreme Court of India

03 February 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Srikrishna, H.K. Sema

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 35; (2006) 2 SCC 728; AIR 2006 SC 1148; 2006 (2) ALD(SC) 1; 2006 (62) ALR 388; 2006 (2) AWC 1155; 2006 (2) BC 20; [2006] 130 Comp Cas 8; 2006 (2) CompLJ 430; 2006 (2) CTC 137; 2006 (2) DRTC 18; JT 2006 (2) SC 192; 2006 (2) MLJ(SC) 304; 2006 (143) PLR 227; 2006 (1) RCR(Civil) 638; 2006(2) SCALE 186; 2006 (3) SCJ 9; [2006] 2 S.C.R. 47; 2006 (1) UPLBEC 711

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Banking & Finance; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, ss. 9, 17 - Bank Guarantee - HC granted injunction against Appellant restraining them from encashing or receiving any amount under bank guarantees - Appeal against - Held, no case of fraud or irretrievable injury made out by respondent - Appellant had right to encash any or all of guarantees for any breach in any of terms of four contracts - Appeal allowed.
