Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan and Others v Sajal Kumar Roy and Others

Supreme Court of India

19 October 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Dalveer Bhandari

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 720; (2006) 8 SCC 671; 2006 (7) AWC 6577; JT 2006 (9) SC 292; 2006(10) SCALE 422; 2007 (1) SLR 50; [2006] Supp7 S.C.R. 607

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Service; Trusts & Associations

Summary: [A.] Service - Societies Registration Act, 1860 - Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (Appointment, Promotion, Seniority) Rules, 1971 - Whether, in terms of extant Rules, age of general candidates was relaxable? - Held, as Respondents do not come within purview of exception contained in art.45 of Education Code, Tribunal and HC committed a manifest error in issuing directions - Appeal allowed.
