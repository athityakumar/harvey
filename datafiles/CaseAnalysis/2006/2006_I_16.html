Tejinder Singh Gujral v Inderjit Singh and Another

Supreme Court of India

19 October 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Dalveer Bhandari

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 697; (2007) 1 SCC (Cr) 389; (2007) 1 SCC 508; 2006 (4) ACC 453; 2006 (65) ALR 784; 2007 (1) AWC 401; 2006 (3) JCC 1944; 10000 (2007(1)) JhrCR(SC) 58; 2006 (4) Law Herald (P&H) 3274; 2007 (2) MLJ 287; 2007 (145) PLR 20; 2007 (1) RLW 650; 2006(10) SCALE 448; 2006 (3) ShimLC 314; [2006] Supp7 S.C.R. 599

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Insurance; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Carriers & Transportation - Motor Vehicles Act, 1988, s. 166 - Prayer for enhanced compensation - Held, appellant may have suffered some injuries but same would not mean that he would not be in a position to rise in his profession only by reason thereof - Both Single Judge as also DB was more than generous in awarding compensation to Appellant under different heads - Appeals dismissed.
