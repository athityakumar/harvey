Branch Manager, Madhya Pradesh State Agro Industries Development Corporation Limited and Another v S.C. Pandey

Supreme Court of India

24 February 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, P.K. Balasubramanyan

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 263; (2006) SCC (L&S) 434; (2006) 2 SCC 716; 2006 (5) AWC 5158; JT 2006 (3) SC 348; 2006(2) SCALE 619; 2006 (4) SCJ 189; [2006] 2 S.C.R. 648; 2006 (2) SLR 684

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Madhya Pradesh Industrial Employment (Standing Order) Act, 1961, Service Recruitment Selections Regulations, 1976

Summary: [A.] Service - Madhya Pradesh Industrial Employment (Standing Order) Act, 1961 - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 25B - Service Recruitment Selections Regulations, 1976 - Madhya Pradesh Industrial Employment (Standing Orders) Rules, 1963 - Order of termination on ground that services were no longer required - HC held that termination of services of respondent was illegal - Appeal against - If an appointment made by Branch Manager was wholly without jurisdiction, order of appointment itself was void - Appointment of respondent made only to meet exigencies of services and not in terms of service regulations - Appellant is directed to pay a sum of Rs. 10, 000/- By way of compensation - Appeal allowed.
