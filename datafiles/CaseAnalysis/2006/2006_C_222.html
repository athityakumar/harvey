Messrs Sonebhadra Fuels v Commissioner, Trade Tax, Uttar Pradesh, Lucknow

Supreme Court of India

01 August 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Markandey Katju, Ashok Bhan

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 362; (2006) 7 SCC 322; JT 2006 (7) SC 73; 2006(7) SCALE 458; 2006 (147) STC 594; [2006] Supp4 S.C.R. 213

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Keywords: Madhya Pradesh General Sales Tax Act, 1958, Processing, Uttar Pradesh Trade Tax Act, 1948, Kerala General Sales Tax Act, 1984

Summary: [A.] Indirect Tax - Central Sales Tax Act, 1956, ss. 14, 15 - Uttar Pradesh Trade Tax Act, 1948, ss. 2(e-1), 3-A - Assessee applied for exemption/rebate of sales tax claiming that coal briquettes are same commodity as coal which had already been subjected to tax - Claim rejected - Appeal against - Held, a finding of fact has been given by Tribunal that 'coal dust' and 'coal briquettes' are entirely different commercial commodities and cannot interfere with this finding of fact - Appeal dismissed.
