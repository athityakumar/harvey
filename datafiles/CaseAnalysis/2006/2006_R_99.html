Ramreddy Rajeshkhanna Reddy and another v State of Andhra Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

24 March 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, P.P. Naolekar

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 144; (2006) 10 SCC 172; (2006) 3 SCC (Cr) 512; AIR 2006 SC 1656; 2006 (1) JCC 541; JT 2006 (4) SC 16; 2006 (2) RCR(Criminal) 462; 2006(3) SCALE 452; 2006 (5) SCJ 33; [2006] 3 S.C.R. 348

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Criminal - IPC, 1860, ss. 34, 302 - Appeal against conviction - Held, no eye witnesses - Prosecution cannot be said to have established guilt of Appellants herein beyond all shadow of doubt - Prosecution story has a large number of loopholes - Appellants are entitled to benefit of doubt - Appeals allowed.
