Chief Engineer Of B.P.D.P./R.E.O., Ranchi v Messrs Scoot Wilson Kirpatrick India Private

Supreme Court of India

10 November 2006

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Lokeshwar Singh Panta

Where Reported

2006 Indlaw SC 754; (2006) 13 SCC 622; 2007 (66) ALR 668; 2007 (1) AWC 555; 2007 (2) JhrCR(SC) 271; 2007 (1) RCR(Civil) 283; 2006(11) SCALE 644; [2006] Supp8 S.C.R. 836

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996 - Arbitration Act, 1940, s. 39(1)(vi) - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 20 r. 4(2), O. 9 r. 13 - Limitation Act, 1963, s. 5 - HC held that appeal filed was not maintainable as same does not fall within ambit of s.37 - Appeal against - Held, question in case is not about applicability of s 5 of Limitation Act, and question really is whether appeal was maintainable - HC did not consider this aspect - Appeal is clearly maintainable - Appeal disposed of.
