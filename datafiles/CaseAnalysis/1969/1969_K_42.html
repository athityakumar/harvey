Jitendra Bahadur Singh v Krishna Behari & Ors

Supreme Court of India

13 August 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, A.N. Ray

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 103; (1969) 2 SCC 433; AIR 1970 SC 276; [1970] 1 S.C.R. 852

Case Digest

Subject: Election; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Recount

Summary: Representation of People Act, 1951, s. 92 - Conduct of Election Rules,1961, r. 93 - Election - Inspection of ballot boxes - Requirements to be satisfied before Tribunal can permit inspection - Scrutiny sought on basis of assertions, which were neither accompanied by statement of material facts nor supported by any evidence - Held, scrutiny should not be ordered - Appeal Allowed.
