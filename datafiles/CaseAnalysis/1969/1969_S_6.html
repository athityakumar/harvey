Hari Nandan Sharan Bhatnagar v S. N. Dixit and Another

Supreme Court of India

25 April 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

G.K. Mitter, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 179; (1969) 2 SCC 245; AIR 1970 SC 40; 1970 LabIC 1; [1970] 1 S.C.R. 421; 1969 SLR 468

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - United Provinces Legislative Department Rules, r. 7 - Whether recruitment of respondent to post of Superintendent in preference to appellant in violation of mandatory provisions of r. 7? - Appellant was an Upper Division Assistant - Respondent was working as a Treasurer in same office - Pay scales were same - Held, 'grade' in r. 7 was suggestive of status and it did not refer to a class or a particular class - All officials working in same scale of pay in a department, although holding posts with different designations, shall be deemed to be holding posts in same grade, because their rank in same department will be same and equal to one another - Post of Superintendent referred to in r. 7 is a selection post and seniority by itself is not a sufficient qualification for promotion, he could consider claims of others who were in same grade and pick out person considered by him to be qualified in all respects to perform duties of a Superintendent - Appeal dismissed.
