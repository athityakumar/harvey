Sales Tax Officer & Anr. v Sudarsanam Iyengar & Sons

Supreme Court of India

13 August 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, J.C. Shah, V. Ramaswami

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 102; (1969) 2 SCC 396; AIR 1970 SC 311; 1969 KLT 783; [1970] 1 S.C.R. 859; 1970 (25) STC 252

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure; Sales Tax

Keywords: Sales Tax Authorities

Summary: Travancore Cochin General Sales Tax Rules, 1950, r. 33 - Limitation Act, 1908 - Punjab General Sales Tax Act, 1948, s. 11(4) - Meaning of word 'Determine' u/r. 33 - Notice for reopening of original assessment - Ground, certain turnover escaping assessment, issued within time limit of 3 years - Assessment could not be completed , due to writ petition and stay orders, before expiry of time limit - Assessment proceedings held were within time - Appeal Allowed.
