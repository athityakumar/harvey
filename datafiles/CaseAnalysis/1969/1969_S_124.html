 Rt. Rev. Bishop S. K. Patro and Others v State of Bihar and Others

Supreme Court of India

02 April 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, Mohammad Hidayatullah, V. Ramaswami, G.K. Mitter, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 161; (1969) 1 SCC 863; AIR 1970 SC 259; 1969 KLT 312; [1970] 1 S.C.R. 172

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Education; Indirect Tax; Service

Summary: Education - Bihar High Schools (Control and Regulation of Administration) Act, 1960, s. 8, -Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 26, 29 and 30 - Kerala Education Bill, 1957 - Status of Minority Educational Institution - Entitlement - Whether school is entitled to claim protection of art. 30 of Constitution of India, 1950 on ground that Church Missionary Society Higher Secondary School at Bhagalpur is an educational institution of their choice established by a minority? - Held, Yes - Management of institution was carried on by some persons who may not have been born in India is, not a ground for denying protection of art. 30(1) - Appeal allowed.
