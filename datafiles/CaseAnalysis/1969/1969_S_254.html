Union of India v Surjeet Singh Atwal

Supreme Court of India

22 April 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

Vaidynathier Ramaswami, R.S. Bachawat, S.M. Sikri

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 367; (1969) 2 SCC 211; AIR 1970 SC 189; 1970 (6) DLT 101; 1970 (72) PLR 257; [1970] 1 S.C.R. 351

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Summary: Arbitration Act, 1940, ss. 8, 20, 31(4) and 34 - (A) Application for stay u/s. 34 - Object of - (B) Application u/ss. 8 and 20 - Through clearly application anterior to the reference, lead to a reference - Held, not an application in a reference u/s. 31(4) - Are application in a reference within s. 31(4) - Appeal Allowed.
