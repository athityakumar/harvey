Tufail (Alias) Simmi v State of Uttar Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

14 January 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

C.A. Vaidyialingam, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 573; (1969) 3 SCC 198; (1970) SCC (Cr) 55; 1969 UJ 25

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, ss. 302, 201 r/w ss. 34 and 394 - Circumstantial evidence - Whether High Court rightly convicted appellant u/ss. 302, 201 r/w ss. 34 and 394 based solely on circumstantial evidence? - Held, circumstances from which conclusion of guilt of appellant is to be drawn have been fully established and did not leave any ground for conclusion consistent with innocence of appellant - Appellant has been rightly convicted - Appeal dismissed.
