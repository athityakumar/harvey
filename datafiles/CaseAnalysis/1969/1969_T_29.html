 Venkatarao Esajirao Limbekar & Ors v The State Of Bombay & Ors

Supreme Court of India

15 April 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, Vaidynathier Ramaswami, G.K. Mitter, Mohammad Hidayatullah, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 173; (1969) 2 SCC 81; AIR 1970 SC 126; 1969 MahLJ 643; [1970] 1 S.C.R. 317

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Parliament & Legislature; Service

Summary: Hyderabad Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Re-enactment, Validation and Further Amendment) Act, 1961, s. 2 - Hyderabad Tenancy and Agricultural Lands Act, 1950, s. 38E - Constitution, sch. 9, arts. 19, 31, 31B, Entries 35 and 36 - States Reorganisation Act, 1956 - (A) Tenancy - Repeal and re-enactment of Hyderabad Act , 1950 and its Amending Act - Effect - Govt. can enquire u/s. 38E of 1950 Act - (B) Constitution - Assent of President to Amending Act - Held, the 1961 Act and Hyderabad Act, 1950 together with Hyderabad Act, 1954 do not contravene Arts.19 and 31 and are protected by art.31B of the Constitution - Assent to parent Act, even if not accorded earlier, must be taken to have been granted when Amending Act was assented to - Appeal Dismissed.
