Shri Chandrakant Kalyandas Kakodkar v State of Maharashtra and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 August 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

P. Jaganmohan Reddy, S.M. Sikri, G.K. Mitter

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 500; (1969) 2 SCC 687; AIR 1970 SC 1390; 1970 CRLJ 1273; [1970] 2 S.C.R. 80

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Morality, Obscene

Summary: IPC, 1860, s. 292 - Obscene books etc. - (A) Question of obscenity - If depend on oral evidence - (B) Obscenity - Determination of - Aspects to be considered by Court - Does not altogether depend on oral evidence but must be judged by the Court - Aspects indicated by the court - Appeal Allowed.
