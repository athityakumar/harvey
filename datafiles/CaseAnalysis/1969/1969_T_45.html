Commissioner Of Wealth-Tax, Calcutta, Nowwest Bengal Ii v Tungabhadra Industries Ltd., Calcutta

Supreme Court of India

08 August 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

V. Ramaswami, A.N. Grover, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 156; (1969) 2 SCC 528; AIR 1970 SC 352; [1970] 75 ITR 196; [1970] 1 S.C.R. 789

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Land & Property

Keywords: Land Acquisition, Capital Reserve, Question Of Fact And Law

Summary: Wealth Tax Act, 1957, ss. 7(2)(a) and 27(6) - Evidence Act, 1872, ss. 101, 102, 103 and 104 - (A) Business with regular accounts - Determination of net value of fixed assets -Onus to prove that written down value is true value liess on assessee - Failure to prove - (B) Reference by Appellate Tribunal on question of law - Nature of duty of Tribunal - Held, adoption of value shown in balance-sheet is proper - Nature of duty of Tribunal to dispose of case after judgment given in reference, pointed out - Order Accordingly.
