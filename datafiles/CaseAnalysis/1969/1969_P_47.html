Hindusthan Commercial Bank Limited v Punnu Sahu (Dead) Through Legal Representatives

Supreme Court of India

01 December 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 436; (1971) 3 SCC 124; AIR 1970 SC 1384; 1970 UJ 261

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: CPC, 1908 O. 21, r. 90, Proviso cl. (b) - (A) Meaning of expression 'entertain' in proviso - (B) Application to set aside sale - Application made prior to amendment - No compliance with cl. (b) of proviso to r. 90 of O.21 even after its introduction - Effect - Power conferred upon Court by clause (b) - Nature - Held, it means 'adjudicate upon' or 'proceed to consider on merits' and does not refer to initiation of proceedings - Appeal Allowed.
