Ratan Lal Shah v Firm Lalmandas Chhadammalal & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

15 April 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, G.K. Mitter

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 171; (1969) 2 SCC 70; AIR 1970 SC 108; 1970 ALJ 53; [1970] 1 S.C.R. 296

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: CPC, 1908, O. 41, r. 4 - Object - Suit against firm through two of its partners for decree for value of goods supplied - One of them denying liability for claim in its entirety - While other admitting supply of goods and accepting his liability for 1/5 of amount claimed - Claim decreed in its entirety against both of them and firm - Appeal by former partner impleading the latter parrtner as second respondent - Failure to serve notice of appeal on to latter partner - Dismissal of appeal - Sustainability - Dismissal of appeal, held, not sustainable - Appeal Allowed.
