The Cannanore Spg. & Wvg. Mills Ltd v Collector Of Customs & Central Excise

Supreme Court of India

15 October 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 578; (1969) 3 SCC 112; AIR 1970 SC 1950; [1970] 2 S.C.R. 830

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944 - Finance Act, 1961, s. 13(i) - Central Excise Rules, 1944, r. 8 - Whether coils of cotton yarn cleared out of appellant's factory during period from 17-08-1962 to 14-12-1962 are exempt from excise duty in view of Exts P-2 and P-3 which exempts from excise duty cotton yarn of 17 counts or more but less than 35 counts, if cleared out of factory in 'hanks'? - It may be taken that word 'hank' has acquired a technical meaning in commercial circles - Definite and positive evidence on record to show authorities who issued those notifications did not use word 'hank' as understood in commercial circles - Appeal allowed.
