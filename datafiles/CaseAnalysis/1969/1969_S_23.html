D.L.F. Housing & Construction Company Private Ltd., New v Sarup Singh And Others

Supreme Court of India

12 September 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

I.D. Dua, C.A. Vaidyialingam

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 391; (1969) 3 SCC 807; AIR 1971 SC 2324; 1971 (7) DLT 115; [1970] 2 S.C.R. 368

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: CPC, 1908, s. 115, 141 and 115 - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 30 - (A) Revisional jurisdiction - Exercise of Limitations - Order staying proceedings u/s.30 of Land Acquisition Act - Revision - Maintainability - (B) Order passed u/s.115 - Application to High Court u/s.151 r/w s. 141 for clarification of revisional order - HC passing order ex parte which could more appropriately be passed in apeal or revision - Held, order of the lower Court could not be interfered with in exercise of revisional jurisdiction u/s.115, CPC, 1908 - Order is invalid - Appeal allowed.
