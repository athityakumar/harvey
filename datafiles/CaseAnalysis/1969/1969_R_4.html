State of Gujarat v R. G. Teredesai and Another

Supreme Court of India

10 April 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, J.C. Shah, V. Ramaswami

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 356; (1969) 2 SCC 128; AIR 1969 SC 1294; 1969 (10) G.L.R. 982; 1969 LabIC 1547; [1970] 1 S.C.R. 251; 1969 SLR 519

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 311(2) - Whether omission to supply a copy of recommendations of Enquiry Officer in matter of punishment amounted to a failure to provide reasonable opportunity? - Along with show cause notice, report of Enquiry Officer containing his findings was sent - No copy of recommendations furnished - Held, although Enquiry Officer is under no obligation or duty to make any recommendations but if he has also made recommendations in matter of punishment that is likely to affect mind of punishing authority with regard to penalty or punishment to be imposed on such officer as recommendations were a part of appropriate material for consideration of Govt. in matter of imposition of punishment, he was entitled to a copy of those recommendations at time when he was called upon to show cause - Requirement of reasonable opportunity, therefore, would not be satisfied unless entire report of Enquiry Officer including his views in the matter of punishment are disclosed to the delinquent servant - Requirement of a reasonable opportunity not satisfied - Proceedings vitiated from stage of show cause notice relating to punishment and order of removal set aside - Appeal dismissed.
