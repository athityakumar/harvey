Sub-Divisional Officer, Sadar, Faizabad v Shambhoo Narain Singh

Supreme Court of India

31 March 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, S.M. Sikri, R.S. Bachawat

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 394; (1969) 1 SCC 825; AIR 1970 SC 140; [1970] 1 S.C.R. 151

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: U.P. Panchayat Raj Act, 1947- Panchayats - Power to punish u/s. 95(1)(g) - Enquiry into charges levelled against Officer of Gaon Sabha - While enquiry pending officer suspened in exercise of power - Held, suspension is without jurisdiction - Appeal dismissed.
