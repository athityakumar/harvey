Shyamal Chakraborty v Commissioner Of Police, Calcutta & Anr

Supreme Court of India

04 August 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

S.M. Sikri, G.K. Mitter, K.S. Hegde

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 132; (1969) 2 SCC 426; AIR 1970 SC 269; [1970] 1 S.C.R. 762

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal

Keywords: Preventive Detention Act, 1950

Summary: Preventive Detention Act, 1950, ss. 3, 7, 10 and 11- Public Safety - Writ of habeas corpus - (A) Detention - Advisory Board dealing with matter - Governor confirming detention - Representation by detenu and prayer to produce him before Board - (B) Grounds for detention must have relevance to maintenance of public order - Fact that acts alleged were also an offence under IPC, 1860 - Prayers held could not be acceded to however government asked to deal with representations - Grounds held had such relevance and fact is irrelevant - Petition Dismissed.
