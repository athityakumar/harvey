Income-Tax Officer, Special Investigationcircle-B, Meerut v M/S. Seth Brothers And Ors

Supreme Court of India

15 July 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, G.K. Mitter

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 143; (1969) 2 SCC 324; AIR 1970 SC 292; [1969] 74 ITR 836; [1970] 1 S.C.R. 601

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 132, 295(1), Rules framed under , r. 112 - Constitution, arts. 31 and 226 - CrPC, 1898, ss. 103, 165 - Limitation Act, 1908 - (A) Rule 112 framed u/s. 295(1) of the Act - Exercise of power u/s. 132 - (B) Writ petition u/art. 226 challenging order u/s.132 - Serious allegations made - But High Court concluding on the basis of affidavits - Held, should be bona fide - HC must take evidence viva voice and must not base its conclusion on affidavits - Appeal Allowed; Matter Remitted To High Court .
