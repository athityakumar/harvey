Baldevdas Shivlal & Anr. v Filmistan Distributors (India) Private Limited

Supreme Court of India

29 April 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, G.K. Mitter

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 184; (1969) 2 SCC 201; AIR 1970 SC 406; [1970] 1 S.C.R. 435

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: CPC, 1908, ss. 11 and 115 - Practice and Procedure - (A) Order of trial Court overruling objection - Objection to question which was put to witness regarding legal relationship arising out of agreement - Question held not barred, since previous consent decree had not decided the same - Revision against order - High Court giving finding on question of res judicata - Trial court had not decided the issue - (B) Case decided - Trial Court overruling objection to a certain question put to witness - Held, HC could not give finding on question of res judicata when trial court had not decided the issue - Not a case decided - Appeal Allowed.
