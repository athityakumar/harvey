 A. V. S. Narasimha Rao And Others v The State Of Andhra Pradesh And Another

Supreme Court of India

28 March 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

Mohammad Hidayatullah, G.K. Mitter, A.N. Grover, J.C. Shah, V. Ramaswami

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 168; (1969) 1 SCC 839; AIR 1970 SC 422; 1970 (1) MLJ(SC) 81; [1970] 1 S.C.R. 115; 1970 SLR 388

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Keywords: Public Employment (Requirement As To Residence) Act, 1957, Andhra Pradesh Employment (Requirements to residence) Act 1957, AP Public Employment (Requirement as to Residence) Rules 1959

Summary: Public Employment (Requirement as to Residence) Act, 1957, s. 3 - Constitution, arts.16(3), s. 35 - A.P. Public Employment (Requirement as to Residence) Rules, 1959, r. 3 - Provision of s. 3 of 1957 Act in so far as it relates to Telangana and r. 3 of 1959 Rules framed under it - Vires of , as regards to art.16 (3) of Constitution - Held, are ultra vires art.16(3) - Held, cl.(3) of art.16 is an exception to the rule contained in cls.(1) and (2) must be strictly construed - Petition Allowed.
