 State Of Bihar v K. K. Misra & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

29 October 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, K.S. Hegde, A.N. Ray, J.M. Shelat, C.A. Vaidyialingam

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 222; (1969) 3 SCC 337; AIR 1971 SC 1667; [1970] 3 S.C.R. 181

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Keywords: Code of Criminal Procedure 1898

Summary: Constitution, arts.19(1), 19(3), 19(4), 19(5), 166 - CrPC, 1898, s. 144(6) Part 2 - (A) Reasonableness of restriction - Test - Constitutionality of second part of s. 144(6) of the Code - (B) Direction given by State Govt. u/s. 144 (6) of the Code - Constitutional validity -Held, violates art.19(1) (b), (c) and (d) - Direction is an executive act within art. 166 - Appeal Dismissed.
