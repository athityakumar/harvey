Goppulal v Thakurji Shriji Shriji Dwarkadheeshji and Another

Supreme Court of India

12 March 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Bachawat, S.M. Sikri, K.S. Hegde

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 195; (1969) 1 SCC 792; AIR 1969 SC 1291; 1969 RCJ 442; 1969 RCR(Rent) 300; [1969] 3 S.C.R. 989

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Rajasthan Premises (Control of Rent and Eviction) Act, 1950, s. 13(1) - (A) Whether a changed rent implies surrender of an existing lease and grant of new tenancy? - Held, a mere increase or reduction of rent does not necessarily import surrender of existing lease and grant of a new tenancy - (B) Whether a subletting before coming into force of Act is within purview of cl. (e) of s. 13(1)? - Held, relevant words are 'has sub-let' - Present perfect tense contemplates a completed event connected in some way with present time - Words take within their sweep any sub-letting which was made in past and has continued up to present time - It does not matter that sub-letting was either before or after Act came into force - All such sub-lettings are within purview of cl. (e) - Plaintiffs have established ground of eviction u/s. 13(1 )(e) with regard to two shops on northern side of staircase of temple - Appeal partly allowed.
