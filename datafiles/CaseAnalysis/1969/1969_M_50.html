State of Punjab and Others v Messrs Chandu Lal Kishori Lal and Others

Supreme Court of India

27 February 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

V. Ramaswami, Mohammad Hidayatullah, G.K. Mitter

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 249; (1969) 1 SCC 695; AIR 1969 SC 1073; [1969] 3 S.C.R. 849; 1970 (25) STC 52; 1969 UJ 262

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Keywords: Declared Goods, Punjab Sales Tax Act, 1948

Summary: Sales Tax - Central Sales Tax Act, 1956, ss. 2(c), 14 and 15 - Punjab Sales Tax Act, 1948, s. 5(2)(a)(vi) - Whether respondent is entitled to exemption u/s. 5(2)(a)(vi) of Punjab Sales Tax Act in context and setting of language of ss. 14 and 15 of Central Sales Tax Act? - Ratio of judgement in Civil Appeal No. 2518 of 1966 relied upon - Held, respondent are not entitled to exemption - Appeal allowed.
