 Pankaj Kumar Chakrabarty And Ors v State Of West Bengal

Supreme Court of India

01 May 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

J.M. Shelat, Vishishtha Bhargava, K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover, C.A. Vaidyialingam

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 128; (1969) 3 SCC 400; AIR 1970 SC 97; [1970] 1 S.C.R. 543

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal; Socio-Economic

Keywords: Preventive Detention Act, 1950

Summary: Constitution, arts. 22(4), 22(5) and 22(7) - Preventive Detention Act, 1950, ss. 7, 8, 9 and 13 - Detention order - Right of detenu to make representation - Dual right to have representation considered by Govt. and also by Advisory Board - Representation to Govt. made after case is referred to Advisory Board - Effect - Govt. held, not relieved of obligation to consider representation - Petition Allowed.
