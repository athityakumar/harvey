Ananda Bazar Patrika Private, Limited v Its Workmen

Supreme Court of India

07 February 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

Vishishtha Bhargava

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 8; (1970) 3 SCC 248; 1969 (18) FLR 186; 1969 (2) LLJ 670; 1969 UJ 129

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Service - Suit for reinstatement of employee - To determine the nature of work done by a employee depends upon whether the main and principal duties carried out by him are those of a supervisory character, or of a nature carried out by a clerk - Main work is of clerical nature, some incidental supervisory will not convert a clerk into one in supervisory capacity - Appeal dismissed.
