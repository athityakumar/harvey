Commissioner Of Income-Tax (Central) Calcutta v India Discount Co. Ltd

Supreme Court of India

07 August 1969

Case Analysis

Bench

V. Ramaswami, A.N. Grover, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1969 Indlaw SC 141; (1969) 2 SCC 514; AIR 1970 SC 410; [1970] 75 ITR 191; [1970] 1 S.C.R. 767

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1922, ss. 10 and 12 - Private limited Company - Dealing in shares and securities - Purchasing shares from share broker - Shares sold with arrear dividends declared long ago but not yet claimed by previous owners - Assesse company received arrear dividends - Taxability - Held, amount cannot be treated as income liable to tax either u/s. 10 or u/s. 12 of the Act - Appeal Dismissed.
