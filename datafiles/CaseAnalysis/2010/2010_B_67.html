Vinaykishore Punamchand Mundhada and another v Bhumi Kalpataru and others

Supreme Court of India

05 August 2010

Case Analysis

Bench

B. SUDARSHAN REDDY, Surinder Singh Nijjar

Where Reported

2010 Indlaw SC 656; (2010) 9 SCC 129; 2010(6) ALL MR 474; 2010 (82) ALR 907; 2010 (5) Bom.C.R. 584; 2010 (6) CTC 319; JT 2010 (8) SC 108; 2010 (8) MLJ(SC) 202; 2010 (2) RCR(Rent) 286; 2010(7) SCALE 761; [2010] 9 S.C.R. 963

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Keywords: C.P. & Berar Letting of Houses and Rent Control Order, 1949

Summary: Rent Control - C.P. & Berar Letting of Houses and Rent Control Order, 1949, clause 13(3)(iii) - Landlords (Appellants) filed application submitting that original tenant firm had sub-let the suit premises without the sanction of the landlords - Rent Controller upheld the plea of sub-tenancy and granted permission under clause 13(3)(iii) of Order, 1949 - Reviewing authority restored the order of the Rent Controller - However, on appeal HC set aside the order of the reviewing authority - Hence, present appeal - Whether order of the HC could be set aside? - Held, original tenant parted away with legal possession by inducting a new firm into possession of which the original tenant is not even a partner and such parting away with the possession was without the knowledge and consent and much less the written permission from the landlords - Impugned order of the HC set aside - Appeal allowed.
