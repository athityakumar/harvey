Pravinbhai Kashirambhai Patel v State Of Gujarat & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

08 July 2010

Case Analysis

Bench

Altamas Kabir, Cyriac Joseph

Where Reported

2010 Indlaw SC 522; (2010) 7 SCC 598; AIR 2010 SC 3511; 2010 ALL MR (Cri) 2631; 2010 CRLJ 3867; 2011 (1) G.L.R. 69; 2010 (3) JCC 2261; JT 2010 (6) SC 656; 2010 (3) RCR(Criminal) 596; 2010(6) SCALE 537; [2010] 8 S.C.R. 211

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Criminal - Practice & Procedure - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, ss. 439, 482 - Order granting anticipatory bail - Legality - 2nd and 3rd respondents allegedly attacked the petitioner - Petitioner lodged a criminal complaint against the respondents - Police registered a criminal case against the respondents - Respondents sought anticipatory bail - Sessions Court granted anticipatory bail - Petitioner challenged the Sessions Court order before the HC u/s. 439(2) r/w. 482 of CrPC - HC dismissed petitioner's application - Held, petitioner made different versions in three different complaints made in respect of the incident to various police authorities - Petitioner sought to add various allegations against respondents with regard to the offences u/ss. 395, 397, 467, 468 and 471 IPC at a later stage of investigation - Petitioner has not made out any case for allowing the petitioner's application u/ss. 439(2) r/w s. 482 CrPC - Petition dismissed.
