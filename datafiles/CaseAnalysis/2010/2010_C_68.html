Tata Chemicals Limited v Collector of Central Excise

Supreme Court of India

04 August 2010

Case Analysis

Bench

B. Sudarshan Reddy, K.S. Radhakrishnan, Surinder Singh Nijjar, Swatanter Kumar

Where Reported

2010 Indlaw SC 1537; (2016) 1 SCC 293

Case Digest

Subject: Excise

Summary: Excise - Practice & Procedure - Precedents - Conflict with - Order of Reference stating that three-Judge Bench judgment of SC in Triveni Glass Limited vs. Union of India and Ors, 2005 Indlaw SC 115, is in conflict with SC judgment in Mahalakshmi Glass Works (P) Limited vs. Collector of Central Excise, Bombay, 1988 Indlaw SC 794 - Held, assumption made in referral Order to the effect that SC decision in Triveni Glass case is in conflict with judgment of another Bench of SC in Mahalakshmi Glass Works (P) Limited case, is erroneous - Further, judgment of SC in Triveni Glass case in turn follows judgment in Mahalakshmi Glass Works (P) Limited case - Hence, Order of reference is set aside - Civil appeals would be heard by appropriate Bench in accordance with law - Reference answered.
