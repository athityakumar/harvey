Indure Limited and another v Commercial Tax Officer and others

Supreme Court of India

20 September 2010

Case Analysis

Bench

Deepak Verma, Dalveer Bhandari

Where Reported

2010 Indlaw SC 761; (2010) 9 SCC 461; JT 2010 (10) SC 109; 2010(10) SCALE 4; [2010] 11 S.C.R. 556; [2010] 34 VST 509

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax; Contract & Commercial

Keywords: Bengal Finance (Sales Tax) Act, 1941, Sale In The Course Of Export, West Bengal Taxation Tribunal Act, 1987, West Bengal Sales Tax Rules

Summary: Sales Tax - Contract & Commercial - Central Sales Tax Act, 1956, s. 5(2) - Bengal Finance (Sales Tax) Act, 1941, ss. 5(2)(a)(v) and 11(A) - Exemption u/s. 5(2) - Import of MS Pipes - Petitioner filed its return claiming benefit u/s. 5(2) of the Act in respect of MS Pipes submitting that it was pursuant to a term of contracts between petitioner and NTPC for erection and commissioning of the Ash handling plant - Commercial Tax Officer disallowed said claim of the petitioner and raised a demand of Rs. 12,60,795.00/- as sales tax and held that it was neither obligatory nor mandatorily required for the petitioner to have imported the impugned goods - Petitioner preferred an appeal u/s. 11(1) of the BFST Act before Asst. Commissioner but the same came to dismissed - Tribunal also dismissed appeal filed against said order - DB confirmed the order of the Tribunal - Hence, present appeal - Whether petitioner entitled for exemption u/s. 5(2) of Act in respect of imported goods? - Held, import of MS Pipes was occasioned only on account of the covenant entered into between the petitioner and NTPC and the imported pipes were used exclusively for erection and commissioning of the plant - Respondents have failed to establish that impugned MS pipes were not used in the plant of NTPC - Impugned MS pipes were used as components in the Ash Handling Plant in the same condition as they were imported without altering its originality - Hence, petitioner entitled to claim benefit of s. 5(2) of the Act - Order of the HC and Tribunal set aside and quashed - Appeal allowed.
