 Brij Lal and others v Commissioner of Income Tax, Jalandhar

Supreme Court of India

21 October 2010

Case Analysis

Bench

S.H. Kapadia, B. SUDARSHAN REDDY, K.S. Panicker Radhakrishnan, Surinder Singh Nijjar, Swatanter Kumar

Where Reported

2010 Indlaw SC 907; (2011) 1 SCC 1; JT 2010 (11) SC 332; 2010(11) SCALE 171; [2010] 11 S.C.R. 1167; [2010] 194 TAXMAN 566

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Application For Revision, Collection And Recovery

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 234, 245D(1) and 154 - (A) Whether s. 234B applies to proceedings of the Settlement Commission under Chapter XIX-A of the said Act? - Held, Chapter XIX- A is a self-contained Code; the special procedure u/ss. 245C and 245D in Chapter XIX-A shows that a special type of computation of total income is engrafted in the said provisions which is nothing but assessment which takes place at s. 245D(1) stage - However, in that computation, one finds that provisions dealing with a regular assessment, self-assessment and levy and computation of interest for default in payment of advance tax, etc. are engrafted - (B) Whether interest is payable under Chapter XIX-A up to the date of the order u/s. 245D(1) of IT Act or up to the date of the order u/s. 245D(4) of IT Act? - Held, interest u/ss. 234A to 234C of IT Act is payable only up to the date of the s. 245D(1) order and not up to the date of the s. 245D(4) of IT Act order - (C) What happens in cases where 90% of the assessed tax is paid but on the basis of the Commission's order u/s. 245D(4) the advance tax paid turns out to be less than 90% of the assessed tax as defined in the Explanation to s. 234B(1) of IT Act? - Held, in such a situation no interest is payable for shortfall as parliament has not extended the provisions and the liability to pay interest beyond the date of application for settlement - Interest u/s 234B of IT Act is chargeable only till the order of the Settlement Commission u/s 245D(1) of IT Act - (D) Whether the Settlement Commission can re-open its concluded proceedings by having recourse to Section 154 of the Act so as to levy interest under section 234B, if it was not done in the original proceedings? - Held, no - Settlement Commission cannot re-open its concluded proceedings by invoking s. 154 of the IT Act so as to levy interest u/s. 234B of IT Act, particularly, in view of s. 245I of IT Act - Reference answered.
