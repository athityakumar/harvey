Kalinga Tubes Limited v Their Workmen

Supreme Court of India

03 May 1968

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, K.S. Hegde, J.M. Shelat

Where Reported

1968 Indlaw SC 449; AIR 1969 SC 90; 1968 (35) CutLT 223; 1968 (17) FLR 311; 1969 LabIC 90; 1969 (1) LLJ 557; [1969] 1 S.C.R. 287

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Gherao, Closure

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Dispute Act, 1947, s. 25-F(1) - Whether appellant shall be liable to pay compensation under principal part of sub-s (1) of s. 25-F of Act? - Management decided to close down - Notice to compensate as payable under provision - Not satisfied that closure of undertaking was due to unavoidable circumstances - Held, compensation would be payable as if undertaking was closed down 'for any reason whatsoever' within s. 25-F(1) of Act - Appeal allowed.
