Mohd. Usman Military Contractor, Jhansi v Union of India, Ministry of Defence

Supreme Court of India

26 September 1968

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Bachawat, S.M. Sikri

Where Reported

1968 Indlaw SC 189; AIR 1969 SC 474; 1969 ALJ 387; [1969] 2 S.C.R. 232

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Repeal

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Civil Procedure - Practice & Procedure - Arbitration Act, 1899 - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 - Arbitration Act, 1940,s. 20 - Indian Limitation Act, 1908, art. 181 - Whether an application u/s. 20 of Arbitration Act, 1940 is governed by art. 181 of Indian Limitation Act? - Applications referred to in art. 178 are applications ejusdem generis, i.e., applications under CPC - Held, District Judge held that there was no period of limitation for making an application u/ss. 8 and 20 - It is well settled that operation of art. 181 is limited to applications under CPC - Decision of High Court set aside and order of District Judge, is restored - Appeal allowed.
