Commissioner of Income Tax, Patiala v Patiala Flour Mills Company Private Limited

Supreme Court of India

06 October 1968

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, R.S. Pathak, V.D. Tulzapurkar

Where Reported

1968 Indlaw SC 1432; (1979) SCC (Tax) 190; (1979) 2 SCC 621; AIR 1979 SC 216; 1978 (7) CTR 144; [1978] 115 ITR 640; [1979] 1 S.C.R. 1128; 1978 TAXLR 1352

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961 - Scope, applicability of 80J - Mode of computation of profits or gains of new industrial undertaking - Held, modes of computation of profits or gains of new industrial undertaking pointed - Appeal dismissed.
