 State of Punjab v Sat Pal Dang and Others

Supreme Court of India

30 July 1968

Case Analysis

Bench

Mohammad Hidayatullah, Vaidynathier Ramaswami, G.K. Mitter, A.N. Grover, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1968 Indlaw SC 429; AIR 1969 SC 903; [1969] 1 S.C.R. 478

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 132, 133(1)(c) and 208 - Punjab Appropriation Acts - Punjab Ordinance - Constitutionality of Act and ordinance challenged - Held, not unconstitutional and valid under power derive from arts. 209 and 213 of constitution - High Court order set aside - Appeals allowed.
