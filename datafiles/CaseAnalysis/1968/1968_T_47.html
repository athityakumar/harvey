Management Of Fertilizer Corporation Ofindia v The Workmen

Supreme Court of India

15 November 1968

Case Analysis

Bench

C.A. Vaidyialingam, Vishishtha Bhargava

Where Reported

1968 Indlaw SC 170; AIR 1970 SC 867; 1970 (21) FLR 73; 1970 LabIC 743; 1970 (2) LLJ 25; [1969] 2 S.C.R. 706

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Public Sector, Minimum Bonus

Summary: Companies Act, 1956, s. 26 - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 24 - Bombay Industrial Disputes Act, 1938 - Company - Strike - Ex-gratia bonus - Circular by Central Govt. under directive of President of India - Effect -Exercise of powers of the Board of Directors - Arts. 67 and 110 of the Co. - (A) Whether Co. is bound to implement circular and grant ex-gratia payment of bonus to workers in the light of s. 26 of the Companies Act - (B) Whether strike in exercise of right to strike u/s. 24 of the 1947 Act be justified - Strike held unjustified - Co. held bound to pay ex gratia payment of bonus to workers - Appeal partly allowed.
