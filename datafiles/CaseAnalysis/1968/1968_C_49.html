Commissioner of Income Tax, Uttar Pradesh v Jagannath Mahadeo Prasad

Supreme Court of India

02 August 1968

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, J.C. Shah, Vaidynathier Ramaswami

Where Reported

1968 Indlaw SC 263; AIR 1969 SC 209; [1969] 71 ITR 296; [1969] 1 S.C.R. 537

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Vocation

Summary: CPC, 1908 - Income Tax Act, 1922, ss. 6, 10 - Scope of First Proviso, expln. (1) of s. 24(1) - Whether speculative losses can be set off against profits from any other business activity u/s. 10 in spite of first proviso to s. 24 (1) - Held,- speculative losses cannot be set off against profits from any other business activity u/s. 10 - Appeal Allowed.
