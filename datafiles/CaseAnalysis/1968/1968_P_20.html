Perspective Publications Private Limited and Another v State of Maharashtra

Supreme Court of India

19 November 1968

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, J.C. Shah, Vaidynathier Ramaswami

Where Reported

1968 Indlaw SC 467; AIR 1971 SC 221; 1971 CRLJ 268; [1969] 2 S.C.R. 779

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Practice & Procedure - Contempt of court - Defamation of a Judge - Appellants were found guilty of having committed contempt of Justice Tarkunde in his judicial capacity and of court - Appellant No. 2 is Editor, Printer and Publisher of Perspective Publications (P) Ltd - Impugned article was 'Story of a Loan and Blitz Thackersey Libel Case' - Alleged that article contained scandalous allegations and was calculated to obstruct administration of justice and constituted gross contempt of court - Appeal - It is open to anyone to express fair, reasonable and legitimate criticism of any act or conduct of a Judge in his judicial capacity or even to make a proper and fair comment on any decision given by him because, a distinction must be made between a mere libel or defamation of a Judge and what amounts to a contempt of court - Test in each case would be whether impugned publication is a mere defamatory attack on Judge or whether it is calculated to interfere with due course of justice or proper administration of law by court - It is only in latter case that it will be punishable as contempt - Alternatively test will be whether wrong is done to judge personally or it is done to public - Held, in impugned article there was a clear imputation of impropriety, lack of integrity and oblique motives to Justice Tarkunde in matter of deciding Thackersey-Blitz suit which, on principles undoubtedly constituted contempt of court - Further held, appellant No. 2 showed no contrition in matter of publication of impugned article - He never even tendered an unqualified apology - High Court, in these circumstances, was fully justified in punishing him for contempt of court and in awarding sentence which was imposed - Appeal dismissed.
