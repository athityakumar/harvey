State of Mysore v Workers In Gold Mines (Of Kolar Gold Fields)

Supreme Court of India

22 May 1958

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, A.K. Sarkar, K. Subba Rao, Vivian Bose

Where Reported

1958 Indlaw SC 8; AIR 1958 SC 923; 1958 (2) LLJ 479; 1958 SCJ 1243; [1959] 1 S.C.R. 895

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Deferred Wages

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Whether in addition to statutory depreciation allowed, appellant is entitled to claim any deduction under head of rehabilitation? - (B) Whether award in substance add back initial contribution to pension fund twice over in making calculations for ascertaining available surplus? - (C) Whether in allowing statutory depreciation of appellant for relevant years, has award virtually allowed said depreciation twice over having regard to fact that large amount has been written off by appellant towards depreciation for said period? - Tribunal is directed to submit its findings on above three issues along with evidence to be recorded hereafter to this Court within three months from date of order, proceedings will accordingly be remitted to said tribunal - Appeal disposed of.
