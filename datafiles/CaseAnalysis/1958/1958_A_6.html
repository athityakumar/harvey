 Gullapalli Nageswara Rao and Others v Andhra Pradesh State Road Transport Corporation and Another

Supreme Court of India

05 November 1958

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Subba Rao, Sudhi Ranjan Das, Natwarlal Harilal Bhagwati, Kailas Nath Wanchoo, Bhuvaneshwar Prasad Sinha

Where Reported

1958 Indlaw SC 156; AIR 1959 SC 308; 1959 (2) MLJ(SC) 156; 1959 SCJ 967; [1959] 1 S.C.R. 319; [1959] Supp1 S.C.R. 319

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation

Keywords: Transport Department, Nationalisation, Working Journalists (Conditions of Service) and Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1955, Constitution (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1955, Madras Government Business Rules, Eastern Bengal And Assam Excise Act, 1910

Summary: Carriers & Transportation - Motor Vehicles Act 1939 , ss. 68(A)(b) and (C) - Road Transport Corporation Act, 1950 - Constitution, 1950, ss. 31,(2),(2A),191, 311, 19(6) and 32 - General Clauses Act, 1897 - Bihar Land Reforms Act, 1950 - Eastern Bengal and Assam Excise Act, 1910 - Madras Government Business Rules - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 32, 191, 311, 19 and 31 - An application u/art. 32 of the Constitution for the enforcement of the petitioners fundamental right to carry on the business of motor transport in Krishna District in AP, and for prohibiting the respondents from taking over the routes on which the petitioners have been plying their stage carriages
