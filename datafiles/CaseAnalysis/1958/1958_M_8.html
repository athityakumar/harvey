Talab Haji Hussain v Madhukar Purshottam Mondkar and Another

Supreme Court of India

07 February 1958

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, Natwarlal Harilal Bhagwati, Syed Jaffer Imam

Where Reported

1958 Indlaw SC 61; AIR 1958 SC 376; 1958 CRLJ 701; 1958 (2) MLJ(SC) 37; 1958 SCJ 672; [1958] 1 S.C.R. 1226

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Code of Criminal Procedure 1898

Summary: Criminal - Practice & Procedure - Criminal Procedure Code, 1898, ss. 496 and 561 A - (A) Whether High Court can cancel bail granted to a person accused of a bailable offence using its inherent power ? - Held, yes, such power can be exercised in interest of justice - Subsequent conduct of accused who is granted bail must not interfere with continuance of fair trial - Appeal dismissed.
