Keshav H. Gholve and another v Thermax Limited and Etc.

Supreme Court of India

23 August 2016

Case Analysis

Bench

Kurian Joseph, R.F. Nariman

Where Reported

2016 Indlaw SC 682; 2016 (4) WLN(SC) 240

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Representative in domestic enquiry - Instant appeal filed by appellants being aggrieved by orders of HC whereby appellants were not permitted to be represented by lawyer in domestic enquiry - Held, in view of apprehension of appellants, it is clarified that dehors any restrictions in standing orders, appellants are permitted to be represented by employee of their choice from respondent-company or a representative of trade union operating in respondent-management. Appeals disposed of.
