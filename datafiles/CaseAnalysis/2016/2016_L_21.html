Balwan Singh and others v Land Acquisition Collector and another

Supreme Court of India

09 March 2016

Case Analysis

Bench

Kurian Joseph, R.F. Nariman

Where Reported

2016 Indlaw SC 214; AIR 2016 SC 1565; 2016 (116) ALR 474; 2016 (2) CLT(SC) 194; JT 2016 (3) SC 334; 2016 (132) RD 114; 2016(8) SCALE 747; [2016] 2 S.C.R. 163; 2016 (2) WLN(SC) 19

Case Digest

Subject: Administrative; Land & Property

Keywords: Practice & Procedure, Land Acquisition Act, 1894

Summary: Administrative - Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 4(1) - Whether the appellants are entitled to interest for the period from the date of dispossession to the date of Notification u/s. 4(1) of the Act.

Held, in view of decisions of SC, respondents are directed to award additional interest by way of damages, at the rate of 15% p.a. for period between 1-7-1984, the date when appellants were dispossessed till 2-9-1993, the date of Notification u/s. 4(1) of the Act. Appeals disposed of.

Ratio - As per decision of SC, land owner is not entitled to interest under the Act. However, land owner is entitled to get rent or damages for use and occupation for the period the Govt. retained possession of the property.
