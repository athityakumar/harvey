Central Agricultural Research Inst. and another v Presiding Officer, Labour Court and others

Supreme Court of India

24 October 2016

Case Analysis

Bench

Kurian Joseph, Rohinton Fali Nariman

Where Reported

2016 Indlaw SC 959

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Industrial Disputes Act, 1947

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s 17 - Award - Implementation of Scheme - Labour Court passed award in favour of respondent o.2/workers against appellant/ industry in referral of dispute with regard to wages - HC in appeal confirmed award passed by Labour Court - Hence, instant appeals.

Held, the appellants have framed a Scheme with regard to the settlement of grievance of the respondents. Hence, appeals are disposed of directing the appellants to implement the Scheme, in substitution of the award passed by the Labour Court. Further, in case the respondents have any surviving grievance, it will be open to them to make a representation before appellant No.1 or pursue the same in appropriate proceedings. Appeals disposed of.

Legislation Cited

Industrial Disputes Act, 1947

Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 s. 17
