Arun Baban Sawant v Surekha Keru Pimparkar @ Surekha A. Sawant

Supreme Court of India

02 May 2016

Case Analysis

Bench

Kurian Joseph, R.F. Nariman

Where Reported

2016 Indlaw SC 424; JT 2016 (5) SC 198; 2016 (3) RCR(Civil) 605

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Family & Personal - Practice & procedure - Amicable settlement between parties - Parties have been duly divorced - Custody of their daughter was with the respondent-mother - The question needs to be answered is regarding visitation rights of the appellant-father and the permanent alimony for the respondent and future maintenance for the child.

Held, as per settlement between parties, it was agreed that appellant would pay Rs.30 Lakh to respondent-mother and daughter towards alimony and maintenance within two half years and in case of default in payment, appellant would be liable for contempt of SC apart from other consequences. Furthermore, appellant is granted visitation rights to visit daughter in presence of student- councilor on two non-working Saturdays in a month. In view of settlement, all pending cases between parties stand dismissed. Appeals disposed of.
