Central Coalfields Limited and another v S. L. L.-S. M. L. (Joint Venture Consortium) and others

Supreme Court of India

17 August 2016

Case Analysis

Bench

Madan B. Lokur, R.K. Agrawal

Where Reported

2016 Indlaw SC 970; 2016 (5) AWC 5215; JT 2016 (9) SC 242; 2016 (4) RCR(Civil) 919; 2016(8) SCALE 99; [2016] 4 S.C.R. 890

Case Digest

Subject: Contract & Commercial

Keywords: Contract, Cheque

Summary: Contract-Bid-Rejection of-For submitting bank guarantee not in format given in Notice Inviting Tender read with General Terms and Conditions-Central Coalfield Limited had not relaxed or deviated from requirement of furnishing bank guarantee in prescribed format-Hence, every bidder was obliged to adhere to prescribed format of bank guarantee-Failure of joint venture consortium to furnish bank guarantee in prescribed format found to be sufficient reason for C.C.L. to reject its bid-Process taking decision by C.C.L. that bank guarantee furnished by J.V.C. ought to be rejected could not be said to be flawed in any manner-Decision taken by C.C.L.-appellant to reject bank guarantee furnished by J.V.C. and to adhere to requirements of N.I.T. and G.T.C. could not be held to be arbitrary, unreasonable or perverse-Impugned judgment and order of Division Bench of High Court setting aside decision to reject bid set aside-Appeals allowed.
