Mamta Goyal v Ramgopal

Supreme Court of India

04 May 2016

Case Analysis

Bench

Kurian Joseph, R.F. Nariman

Where Reported

2016 Indlaw SC 325; JT 2016 (5) SC 36; 2016(6) SCALE 727

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal

Keywords: Indian Penal Code, 1860

Summary: Family & Personal - Dissolution of marriage - Legality - Order of dissolution of marriage between appellant wife and respondent husband was confirmed by HC - Hence, instant appeal - Held, parties agreed that by way of full and final settlement of all claims due to appellant, except dispute with regard to Stridhan, which is the subject matter of Civil First Appeal No. 221 of 2014 pending before HC, all other claims can be settled - On the basis of compromise, respondent should pay an amount of Rs. 5.50 Lakhs by way of a Demand Draft drawn in name of appellant - Order accordingly.
