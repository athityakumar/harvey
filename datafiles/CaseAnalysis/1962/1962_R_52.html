Workmen of Rohtak General Transport Company v Rohtak General Transport Company

Supreme Court of India

27 February 1962

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar

Where Reported

1962 Indlaw SC 33; 1966 (13) FLR 353; 1962 (1) LLJ 634

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - Question relating to nature of dispute? - Competency of reference - If a dispute is sponsored by workmen and reference follows, dispute which was an industrial dispute at date of reference, does not cease to be an industrial dispute, merely because it has lost sympathy of workmen thereafter - Labour court was in error in coming to conclusion that reference was bad and dispute was not an industrial dispute because only a minority of workmen had espoused cause in question - Matter is sent back to labour court.
