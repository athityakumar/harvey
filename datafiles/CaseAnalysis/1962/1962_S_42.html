Messrs Amarchand Lalitkumar v Shree Ambica Jute Mills Limited

Supreme Court of India

03 May 1962

Case Analysis

Bench

S.K. Das, Mohammad Hidayatullah, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1962 Indlaw SC 140; AIR 1966 SC 1036; [1963] 2 S.C.R. 953

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Keywords: Forward Contracts (Regulation ) Act, 1952

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration Act, 1940, ss.5 and 34 - Prevention of arbitration either by revocation or by stay - Application to revoke arbitrator's authority u/s. 5 of Arbitration Act - Difference between application u/s. 5 and one u/s. 34 - Held, Court should be satisfied that substantial miscarriage of justice will take place - Difference as to point of time when application is made, if proceedings are commenced in Court, application is made u/s. 34; otherwise u/s. 5 - Appeal dismissed.
