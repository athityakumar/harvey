 Board of High School and Intermediate Education v Ghanshyam Das Gupta and Others

Supreme Court of India

06 February 1962

Case Analysis

Bench

Kailas Nath Wanchoo, S.K. Das, A.K. Sarkar, K. Subba Rao, N. Rajagopala Ayyangar

Where Reported

1962 Indlaw SC 555; AIR 1962 SC 1110; 1962 ALJ 776; 1962 (2) ILR(All) 661; [1962] 3 S.C.R. 36; [1962] Supp3 S.C.R. 36

Case Digest

Subject: Education

Summary: Education - Administrative law - Natural justice - UP Intermediate Education Act, No II of 1921, ss. 7, 13, 14, 15 and 20 - Use of unfair means-Examination result cancelled - (A) Whether respondents were entitled to a hearing before appellant decided to cancel results? - Held, principles of natural justice which require that other party must be heard, will apply to proceedings before Committee before cancelling examination - (B) Whether Committee under Act and Regulations is to act as a quasi-judicial body? - Further held, Committee when it exercises its powers u/r. 1(1) is not acting administratively - Appeal dismissed.
