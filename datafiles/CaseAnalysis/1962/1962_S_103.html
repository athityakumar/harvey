State of Kerala v Narayani Amma Kamala Devi

Supreme Court of India

19 March 1962

Case Analysis

Bench

K.C. Das Gupta, J.L. Kapur, Raghubar Dayal

Where Reported

1962 Indlaw SC 538; AIR 1962 SC 1530; 1962 CRLJ 506; [1962] 3 S.C.R. 943; [1962] Supp3 S.C.R. 943

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Procedure Code,1973, s. 439 - Whether revision of criminal trial to High Court after death of accused, could be maintained - Held, yes - HC could exercise its power of revision after death of accused - In case of appellate jurisdiction, appeal can be filed only after same is filed by convicted person but there is no such limitation on Courts revisional jurisdiction - There is no provision that revisional application cannot be made in respect of an order of conviction when convicted person is dead - HC was right in holding that application for revision could be entertained u/s. 439 of CrPC - Appeal dismissed.
