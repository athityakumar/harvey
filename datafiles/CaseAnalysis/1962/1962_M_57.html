Mrs. V. G. Paterson v Mr. O. V. Forbes and Others

Supreme Court of India

13 September 1962

Case Analysis

Bench

K.C. Das Gupta, J.L. Kapur, Raghubar Dayal

Where Reported

1962 Indlaw SC 493; AIR 1963 SC 692; 1963 CRLJ 633; 1963 (2) MLJ(SC) 69; [1963] Supp1 S.C.R. 40

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Contempt of Courts Act, 1952, s. 3 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, ss. 87 and 88 -(A) Are ss. 87 and 88 of CrPC, 1973 applicable to contempt proceedings? - Held, ss. 87 and 88 not available to secure presence of a person who is alleged to commit contempt - (B) Should heir of contemner be restored back property from possession of state Govt.? - Further held, attachment of property subsists as long as contemner is alive - On his death - Attachment cannot continue - Rightful owner entitled to restoration of property on contemner's death - Appeal allowed.
