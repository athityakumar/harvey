Sitaram v State of Madhya Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

05 February 1962

Case Analysis

Bench

J.L. Kapur, K.C. Das Gupta, Raghubar Dayal

Where Reported

1962 Indlaw SC 238; AIR 1962 SC 1146; 1962 CRLJ 258; [1962] 3 S.C.R. 21; 1962 (13) STC 285; [1962] Supp3 S.C.R. 21

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - Central Provinces & Berar Sales Tax Act, 1947, ss. 10, 15, 24, 24(1)(b), 24(1)(g), 26(1), 26(2), 34(1)(b) and 34(1)(g) - Whether returns filed by appellants were false and accounts produced were incorrect and therefore an offence u/s. 24(1)(b) and (g) of Act was committed? - When appellants submitted their returns they did so u/s. 10 of Act and when they produced their accounts, they did so u/s. 15 of Act - Held, both making of returns and production of accounts were done under Act and cannot be said to be outside provisions of Act - Appeal allowed.
