 Messrs Mangalore Ganesh Bedi Works v State of Mysore and Another

Supreme Court of India

25 September 1962

Case Analysis

Bench

J.L. Kapur, S.K. Das, A.K. Sarkar, Mohammad Hidayatullah, Raghubar Dayal

Where Reported

1962 Indlaw SC 267; AIR 1963 SC 589; 1963 (14) STC 198; [1963] Supp1 S.C.R. 275

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - Mysore Sales Tax Act, 1948 - Indian Coinage Act, 1906, s. 14 - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 197, 199, 202 and 265 - Whether enhancement of tax by reasons of amendment in s. 14 of Coinage Act is ultra vires of art. 265 of Constitution? - Held, by substitution of new coinage i.e., naya paisas in place of annas, pice and pies no enhancement of tax was enacted but it was merely a substitution of one coinage by another of equivalent value - U/art. 255, a tax cannot be challenged on ground that it is contrary to provisions of Constitution - Enhancement of tax is valid - Appeal dismissed.
