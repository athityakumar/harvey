Messrs New India Sugar Mills Limited v Commissioner of Sales Tax, Bihar

Supreme Court of India

26 November 1962

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, Mohammad Hidayatullah, J.L. Kapur

Where Reported

1962 Indlaw SC 302; AIR 1963 SC 1207; 1964 (1) SCJ 644; [1963] 2 S.C.R. 459; 1963 (14) STC 316; [1963] Supp2 S.C.R. 459

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Keywords: Value Of Goods, Building Contract, Collected, Practice, Turnover Tax, Bihar Sales Tax Act, 1947, Defence of India Rules, 1939, Government of India Act 1935, Bombay Prohibition Act 1949, Sugar and Sugar Products Control Order 1946

Summary: Sales Tax - Bihar Sales Tax Act, 1947, s. 2(g) - Contract Act, 1872, ss. 2(a), 2(b) and 2(c) - Sugar & Sugar products Control Order, 1946, cl. 17 - Sale of Goods Act, 1930, ss. 2(10), 4 and 2(15) - Government of India Act, 1935, sch. 7, entry 48 - Official directions issued by sugar controller of India to Sugar factory in Bihar Province for Dispatching sugar to Madras Province - Whether 'amount to sale' to Madras Province - By applying harmonious construction held there was no sale by assessee - Appeal allowed.
