Om Prakash Gupta v Rattan Singh and Another

Supreme Court of India

17 December 1962

Case Analysis

Bench

Bhuvaneshwar Prasad Sinha

Where Reported

1962 Indlaw SC 315; [1964] 1 S.C.R. 259

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958 - Jurisdiction of Rent Control Authority - Whether Rent Control authorities jurisdiction to entertain proceedings, in as much as landlord tenant relationship is denied? - Held, proceedings u/s. 15 are primarily meant for benefit of tenant - S. 15 authorises Controller to make an order directing tenant to pay amount found to be due to landlord or to deposit it with Controller - Such an order can be passed by Controller only if Controller decides that person against whom proceedings for eviction had been initiated is in position of a tenant - Any order passed by Controller assumes that Controller has jurisdiction to determine issue of landlord-tenant relationship - Act proceeds on assumption that there is such a relationship - If relationship is denied, authorities under Act have to determine that question also, because a simple denial of relationship cannot oust jurisdiction of tribunals under Act - Appeal dismissed.
