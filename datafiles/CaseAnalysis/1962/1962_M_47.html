Ishwari Prasad Mishra v Mohammad Isa.

Supreme Court of India

27 August 1962

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, K.C. Das Gupta, Raghubar Dayal

Where Reported

1962 Indlaw SC 256; AIR 1963 SC 1728; 1963 BLJR 226; [1963] 3 S.C.R. 722

Case Digest

Subject: Contract & Commercial

Summary: Contract & Commercial - Specific performance of an agreement of sale of a house - agreement was executed, the consideration was settled at Rs. 14, 000/- out of which Rs. 10, 900/- were paid as earnest money - Balance unpaid - Whether the agreement on which the appellants suit is based is genuine, valid and for consideration? - Held, we feel no difficulty in holding that the Trial Court was right that the suit agreement was genuine and valid and is supported by consideration, in our opinion, the High Court was not justified in interfering with this conclusion, HC's order set aside - Appeal allowed.
