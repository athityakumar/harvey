Strawboard Manufacturing Company v Gobind

Supreme Court of India

06 March 1962

Case Analysis

Bench

Kailas Nath Wanchoo, Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, A.K. Sarkar

Where Reported

1962 Indlaw SC 51; AIR 1962 SC 1500; 1962 ALJ 975; 1962 (4) FLR 403; 1962 (1) LLJ 420; [1962] 3 S.C.R. 618; [1962] Supp3 S.C.R. 618

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 33 - United Provinces Industrial Disputes Act, s. 6 E(2)(b) - Whether applications should be made, where more than one dispute is pending before more than one tribunal, to all tribunals or whether an application to one of them would suffice? - Held, appellant made application to tribunal simultaneously, without delay on passing order of dismissal, in accordance with proviso to s. 6 E(2)(b) - Labour Court's opinion that application must be made before dismissal is incorrect - Appellant complied with proviso when he dismissed workman, paid wages and also posted application to tribunal for approval - Appeal allowed.
