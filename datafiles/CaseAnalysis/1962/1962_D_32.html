 Dr. A. Lakshmanaswami Mudaliarand Others v Life Insurance Corporation

Supreme Court of India

11 December 1962

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, Bhuvaneshwar Prasad Sinha, Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, Kailas Nath Wanchoo, K.C. Das Gupta

Where Reported

1962 Indlaw SC 579; AIR 1963 SC 1185; [1963] 33 Comp Cas 420; [1963] 2 S.C.R. 887; [1963] Supp2 S.C.R. 887

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Insurance

Summary: Insurance - Life Insurance Corporation Act, 1956, ss. 7 and 15 - Contract Act, 1872, s. 10 - Transfer of business to LIC - Donation of Rs. 2 lakhs by insurer - To trust (within five years before 19-01-1956) - From share holders dividend amount - When dividend not declared and Articles of Association not authorising donation - Construction of Articles to determine - Whether LIC can demand amount from trustees? - Held, LIC can demand amount from trustees - Appeal dismissed.
