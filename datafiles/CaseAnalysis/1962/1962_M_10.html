M.S.Anirudhan v Thomco's Bank Limited

Supreme Court of India

14 February 1962

Case Analysis

Bench

J.L. Kapur, A.K. Sarkar, Mohammad Hidayatullah

Where Reported

1962 Indlaw SC 359; AIR 1963 SC 746; [1963] 33 Comp Cas 185; 1964 (1) SCJ 495; [1963] Supp1 S.C.R. 63

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Summary: Contract & Commercial - Banking & Finance - Indian Contract Act, 1872, ss. 133 and 188 - Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881, s. 87 - Indian Evidence Act, 1872, s. 115 - (A)Whether guarantor can avoid instrument of Guarantee if any alteration to his benefit is made while in custody of principal debtor? - Held, while making alteration to Benefit of guarantor,though not to disadvantage of guarantor, principal debtor is acting as agent of Guarantor - Avoidance of contract by material alteration is inapplicable - Appeal dismissed.
