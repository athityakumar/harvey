Dr. Vimla v Delhi Amdinistration

Supreme Court of India

29 November 1962

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Subba Rao, J.R. Madholkar, N. Rajagopala Ayyangar, Syed Jaffer Imam

Where Reported

1962 Indlaw SC 362; AIR 1963 SC 1572; 1963 CRLJ 434; [1963] 2 S.C.R. 585; [1963] Supp2 S.C.R. 585

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 24, 25, 467 and 468 - Meaning of word 'Defraud' - Two elements - Deceit and injury to person deceived - No injury to person deceived - Whether accused liable for 'defraud'? - Held, accused not guilty u/ss. 467 and 468 - Appeal allowed.
