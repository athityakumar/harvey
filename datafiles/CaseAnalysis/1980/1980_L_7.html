Jain Ink Manufacturing Company v Life Insurance Corporation Of India & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

22 August 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, Y.V. Chandrachud, A.D. Koshal

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 177; (1980) 4 SCC 435; AIR 1981 SC 670; 1980 (2) RCJ 459; 1981 (1) RCR(Rent) 590; 1980 (2) RentLR 650; [1981] 1 S.C.R. 498

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Municipalities & Local Governments; Rent Control; Service

Keywords: Public Premises Act 1971

Summary: Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) Act, 1971, ss. 2 and 2(2)(g) - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958, ss. 3(a), 14 and 25B - Slum Areas (Improvement and Clearance), Act, 1956, s. 19 - (A) Whether 1971 Act overrides - (1) Delhi Rent Control Act - (2) Slum Areas (Improvement and Clearance), Act - (B) Relavent factor for interpretation of definition of 'unauthorised occupation' u/s 2(2)(g) of 1971 Act - (A) Held, premises Act overrides provisions of Rent Act - Premises Act overrides Slums Act - (B) What is germane is whether or not the person concerned was in occupation of the public premises when the Premises Act was passed - Appeal Dismissed.
