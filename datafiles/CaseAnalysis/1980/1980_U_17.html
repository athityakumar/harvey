Amrik Singh And Ors. v Union Of India And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

11 April 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 209; (1980) 3 SCC 393; (1980) SCC (L&S) 415; AIR 1980 SC 1447; 1980 LabIC 735; 1980 (2) LLN 301; [1980] 3 S.C.R. 485; 1980 SLJ 550; 1980 (2) SLR 110

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Service - Constitution, arts. 14, 16 and 226 - All India Services Act, 1951, s. 3(4) - Indian Police Service (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1954, rr. 3(iii)(b), 7, 8 and 9 - All India Services (Conditions of Service-Residuary Matters) Rules, 1960, r. 3 - Administrative Law - Right to hearing - Service Rules - IPS - (A) Administrative lapses in fixation of year of allotmement of Promotee - No injustice senior officers, rather relaxation granted - Promotion, if can be challenged - (B) Validity of r, 3 of the 1960 , Rules - Promotion, held, could not be challenged on ground of lapses - Compliance with rules of natural justice is implicit - Appeal dismissed.
