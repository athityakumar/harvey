State Of Madhya Pradesh v Shanker Lal & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

16 January 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

N.L. Untwalia, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 105; (1980) SCC (L&S) 176; (1980) 1 SCC 702; AIR 1980 SC 643; 1980 (1) LLN 295; [1980] 2 S.C.R. 786; 1980 SLJ 212; 1980 (1) SLR 461; 1980 UJ 323

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Madhya Pradesh Municipalities Act, 1961

Summary: Municipalities and Local Governments - MP Municipalities Act, 1961, ss. 94(1), 94(2) and 94(7) - Interpretation of Statutes - Power of State Govt. to transfer any Officer or servant of Municipal Council - Scope - Whether power extends to transfer of Lecturers and teachers appointed in Schools - Held, s. 94(7) to be interpreted liberally and not in a restricted sense - Appeal dismissed
