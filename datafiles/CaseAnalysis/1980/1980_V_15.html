Managing Director, Uttar Pradesh Warehousing Corporation v Vinay Narayan Vajpayee

Supreme Court of India

16 January 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Sarkaria, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 217; (1980) SCC (L&S) 453; (1980) 3 SCC 459; AIR 1980 SC 840; 1980 ALJ 358; 1990 CRLJ 1420; 1980 (1) LLJ 222; [1980] 2 S.C.R. 773; 1980 SLJ 204; 1980 (1) SLR 497

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Public Sector, Personal Service, Oil and Natural Gas Commission, Statutory Bodies

Summary: Constitution, arts. 12, 226 and 311 - U.P. State Warehousing Corporation - Departmental Enquiry - Dismissal of Employee - Breach of rules of natural justice - Found to be illegal - Direction for payment of full back-wages - Issues, main and ancillay - If it is an 'authority' - Legality of dismissal - Enquiry when commence - Direction if proper - Held, it is an 'authority' - Illegal - After the charge-sheet is drawn up and served upon the delinquent and the latter's explanation is obtained - Not proper -

Appeal Dismissed.
