Regional Transport Officer Chittoor Etc. v Associated Transport, Madras Private Limited and

Supreme Court of India

05 September 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, A.D. Koshal

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 289; (1980) 4 SCC 597; AIR 1980 SC 1872; [1981] 1 S.C.R. 627; 1980 UJ 916

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation

Keywords: Andhra Pradesh Motor Vehicles (Taxation of Passengers and Goods) Act, 1952

Summary: Andhra Pradesh Motor Vehicles (Taxation of Passengers and Goods) Act, 1952, s. 4(1) -

Retrospective deletion of sub-rr. (3), (4) and (5) of r.1 of 1957 Rules framed under the Act - Legality - Held, s.4 (1) did not confer on Government power to make retrospective rules - Retrospective deletion is illegal - Appeal Dismissed.
