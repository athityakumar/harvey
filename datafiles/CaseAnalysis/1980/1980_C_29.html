Mahalaxmi Sugar Mills Co. Ltd. v Commissioner Of Income-Tax, Delhi, New Delhi

Supreme Court of India

09 April 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, N.L. Untwalia, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 184; (1980) SCC (Tax) 324; (1980) 3 SCC 475; AIR 1980 SC 754; 1980 (16) CTR 198; [1980] 123 ITR 429; [1980] 3 S.C.R. 421; 1980 TAXLR 452

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Sugarcane Cess (Validation) Act, 1961, Uttar Pradesh Sugarcane Purchase Tax Act, 1961, Uttar Pradesh Sugarcane Cess Act, 1956

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 10(2)(xv) - UP Sugarcane Cess Act, 1956, s. 3(3) - Nature of interest paid on cess u/s. 3 (3) of 1922 Act - Whether it is deductible while computing income tax - Held, not a penalty - Assessee can claim it as deduction u/s. 10(2)(xv) of Income Tax Act - Appeal allowed.
