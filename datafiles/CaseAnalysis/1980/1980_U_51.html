 Kamal Kanti Dutta and Others v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

23 April 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, N.L. Untwalia, P.S. Kailasam, D.A. Desai, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 148; (1980) 4 SCC 38; (1980) SCC (L&S) 485; AIR 1980 SC 2056; 1980 LabIC 1090; 1980 (2) LLJ 182; [1980] 3 S.C.R. 811; 1980 (2) SLR 62

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 32 and 309 - ITO (Class I) Service (Regulation of Seniority) Rules, 1973 - Disputes between promotees and direct recruits in various departments - Validity of 1973, Rules - Whether decisions of Previous cases (1967 INDLAW SC 280, 1972 INDLAW SC 305, 1974 INDLAW SC 80, 1977 INDLAW SC 219) should be reviewed - Held, not safe to test constitutionality of a service rule on touchstone of fortunes of individuals, matter referred to larger bench - Supreme Court refused to reconsider its previous decisions, as on merits Court saw no justification for reconsidering - Petition dismissed.
