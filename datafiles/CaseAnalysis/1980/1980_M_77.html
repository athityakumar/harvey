State of Maharashtra v Mohd. Yakub and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 March 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Sarkaria, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 2; (1980) 3 SCC 57; (1980) SCC (Cr) 513; AIR 1980 SC 1111; 1980 CRLJ 793; 1980 CRLR 251; 1980 CrLR(SC) 251; 1983 (13) E.L.T. 1637; [1980] 2 S.C.R. 1158; 1980 TAXLR 793

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Keywords: Actus Reus

Summary: Penal Code, 1860, s. 511 - Customs Act, 1962, s. 135(2) - FERA, 1947, ss. 12(1) and 23(1) - Attempt to smuggle silver out of India - Determination - Truck driven in night to a lonely creek - From where the silver could be transferred into a sea-faring vessel - Meaning of word 'Attempt' - Held, contravention of provisions of Customs Act and FERA - What constitutes an 'attempt' is a mixed question of law and fact - Appeal Allowed.
