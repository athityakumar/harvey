Vishnu Awatar Etc. v Shiv Autar And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

02 May 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 116; (1980) 4 SCC 81; AIR 1980 SC 1575; 1980 ALJ 751; 1980 (6) ALR 354; [1980] 3 S.C.R. 973; 1980 UJ 619

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Summary: CPC, 1908, s. 115 - Revision - Scope of powers of High Court and District - Held, decision of District Courts rendered in appeal or revision are beyond revision by the High Court, if the suit is of less than Rs. 20,000 - But Subject to an exception engrafted by the first proviso to s. 3 of the Act - Petition Dismissed.
