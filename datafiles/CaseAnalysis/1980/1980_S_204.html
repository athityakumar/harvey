Mangalbhai Motiram Patel v State of Maharashtra and Others

Supreme Court of India

26 September 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, E.S. Venkataramiah, P.N. Bhagwati

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 31; (1980) 4 SCC 470; (1981) SCC (Cr) 49; AIR 1981 SC 510; 1981 CRLJ 331; 1981 CrLR(SC) 592; [1981] 1 S.C.R. 852

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Fema

Summary: Constitution Of India, 1950, art.22(5)- Conservation of Foreign Exchange And Prevention Of Smuggling Activities Act, 1974, ss. 3(3) - Detenu was acting as a 'courier' between M/S. S.K. Malhotra Imports and Exports, Brussels having a widespread network abroad and M/S. Apex Distributors, Bombay who were engaged in a criminal conspiracy to smuggle contraband goods on a wide scale- (A)Whether detenu has right to be furnished with grounds of detention along with documents relied upon? - Right to make a representation implies what it means right of making an effective representation - Where certain documents are relied upon in grounds of detention? - Grounds would be incomplete without such documents - Held, detenu has right to be furnished with grounds of detention along with documents relied upon - Order accordingly.
