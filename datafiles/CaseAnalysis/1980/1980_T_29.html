The Tulsipur Sugar Co. Ltd. v The Notified Area Committee, Tulsipur

Supreme Court of India

27 February 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, D.A. Desai

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 127; (1980) 2 SCC 295; AIR 1980 SC 882; 1980 ALJ 401; [1980] 2 S.C.R. 1111

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax

Keywords: City of Bangalore Municipal Corporation Act, 1949, Uttar Pradesh Town Areas Act, 1914

Summary: Indirect Taxes - Municipal and Local Governments - UP Town Areas Act, 1914, ss. 3 and 3(2) - Constitution, art. 226 - Town Area - Extension of notified area by publication of notification in Gazette - (A) Principle of 'Audi alteram partem' if attracted - (B) Plea that the area is a part of agricultural village - (C) Octroi imposed - Omission in notification to specify octroi limits but mentioning applicability to town area - Rectifing notification - Whether earlier notification if ineffective due to omisssion - Held, not attracted - Plea not available - Held mere non-mention of octroi limit would not mislead the people; notification was neither incomplete nor ineffective - Appeal dismissed.
