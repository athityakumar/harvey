Maimoona Khatun And Anr. v State Of U.P. And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

16 April 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, P.S. Kailasam, A.D. Koshal

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 315; (1980) SCC (L&S) 464; (1980) 3 SCC 578; AIR 1980 SC 1773; 1980 (6) ALR 390; 1980 (41) FLR 307; 1980 LabIC 1109; 1980 (2) LLJ 164; 1980 (2) LLN 441; [1980] 3 S.C.R. 676; 1980 (3) SLR 455; 1980 UJ 557

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Limitation Act, 1963, art. 7 - Limitation Act, 1908, art. 102 - Expression 'When the wages accrue due' - When limitation period commences for claim to arrears of salary by reinstated employee - Held, u/art. 102 of 1908 Act it commence from the date when the right actually accrues the date of the reinstatement by the appointing authority where no suit is filed or the date of the decree where a suit is filed and decreed - Appeal Allowed.
