Frances Coralie Mullin v W. C. Khambra and Others

Supreme Court of India

27 February 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, R.S. Sarkaria

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 590; (1980) 2 SCC 275; (1980) SCC (Cr) 419; AIR 1980 SC 849; [1981] 51 Comp Cas 343; 1980 CRLJ 548; [1980] 2 S.C.R. 1095

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 22, 22(5) and 226 - Conservation Of Foreign Exchange And Prevention of Smuggling Activities Act, 1974, s. 3 - Writ petion against detention - (A) Practice and Procedure - Writ of Habeas Corpus - Affidavits filed in Court - Points to considered - (B) Preventive detention - Right to make representation - Court's duty to guard personal freedom against casual indifference and red-tape - Held, should be precise and clear - Expeditious disposal at every stage is essential - Petition dismissed.
