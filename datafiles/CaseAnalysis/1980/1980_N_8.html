Nainmal Partap Mal Shah v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

23 June 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 457; (1980) 4 SCC 427; (1980) SCC (Cr) 987; AIR 1980 SC 2129; 1980 CRLJ 1479; 1980 CRLR 723; 1980 UJ 682

Case Digest

Subject: Customs

Summary: Constitution, art. 22(5) - COFEPOSA, 1974, s. 3 - Detention - Grounds of - Supplied to detenu in English ( a language which he does not understand) - Whether art. 22 (5) of Constitution is violated - Held, clear violation of art. 22 (5) - Petition Allowed.
