Col. Avtar Singh Sekhon v Union Of India

Supreme Court of India

31 July 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 168; (1980) Supp SCC 562; (1981) SCC (L&S) 381; AIR 1980 SC 2041; 1981 (7) ALR 124; 1981 (29) BLJR 110; 1981 (2) LLJ 405; [1981] 1 S.C.R. 168; 1981 UJ 180

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution, art. 137 - CPC, 1908, s. 116 - Service matter - Promotion - Review is not a routine procedure - For it miscarriage of justice must be proved - From the material error on the face of the earlier order - Held, in instant case that the earlier judgment of the Supreme Court upholding promotion of respondent was correct and needed no review - Petition dismissed.
