Srinivasa Enterprises and Others v Union of India Etc

Supreme Court of India

24 September 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, R.S. Pathak, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 197; (1980) 4 SCC 507; AIR 1981 SC 504; [1981] 51 Comp Cas 464; [1981] 1 S.C.R. 801; 1980 UJ 962

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance

Keywords: Reserve Bank of India Act, Regional Rural Banks Act, 1976

Summary: Constitution - Prize Chits and Money Circulation Schemes (Banning) Act, 1978, - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 14, 19(1)(f) and (g ), 246, sch. 7, list 2, entry 34, list 3, entry 7 - Constitutionality of 1978 Act - Held, act is not ultra vires art.19 (1)(f) and (g) and 14, on ground that in pith and substance it falls in State List - And s. 11 not violative of art. 14 as categories exempted do not spell out discriminatory treatment - Petition dismissed.
