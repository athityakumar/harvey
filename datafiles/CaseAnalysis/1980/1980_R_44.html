Ramchandra A. Kamat v Union Of India And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

20 February 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

P.S. Kailasam, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, A.D. Koshal

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 122; (1980) 2 SCC 270; (1980) SCC (Cr) 414; AIR 1980 SC 765; [1981] 51 Comp Cas 337; [1980] 2 S.C.R. 1072; 1980 UJ 394

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - CrPC, 1973, ss. 3(1) and 11 - Constitution, arts. 22(5) - Detention - Delay in serving grounds, copies of the statements and documents - Unable to make effective representation - Held, detention was not according to procedure prescribed by law - Petition allowed.
