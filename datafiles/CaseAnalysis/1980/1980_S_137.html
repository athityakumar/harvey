Shah Guman Mal v State of Andhra Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

06 February 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, A.D. Koshal

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 592; (1980) 2 SCC 262; (1980) SCC (Cr) 432; AIR 1980 SC 793; 1980 CRLJ 557; 1980 CrLR(SC) 179; 1983 (13) E.L.T. 1631; [1980] 2 S.C.R. 1005

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Customs Act, 1962, ss. 135(b) and 123 - Evidence Act, 1872, ss. 106 and 114 - Burden of proof - Seizure of gold biscuits in violation of law - Non availability of presumption u/s 123 of Customs Act - Availability of presumption u/ss. 106 and 114 of Evidence Act is to be determined - Presumption held could be availed by prosecution in special facts of case - Appeal dismissed.
