Champak Lal H. Thakkar And Others v State Of Gujarat And Another

Supreme Court of India

18 August 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

A.D. Koshal, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 172; (1980) 4 SCC 329; (1981) SCC (L&S) 9; AIR 1980 SC 1889; 1980 CRLJ 1325; 1980 (41) FLR 362; 1980 LabIC 978; 1981 (1) LLJ 134; 1980 (2) LLN 466; [1981] 1 S.C.R. 440

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Questionnaire

Summary: Minimum Wages Act, 1948, sch., Part 1, item 5 - 'Employment in any oil mill' - Employment in a Vanaspati manufacturing concern whether an employment in an oil mill - Held, where the Company sells oil in its unhydrogenated form would make the Company an oil mill even if the bulk of the oil produced by it is converted into vanaspati and sold in that form - Appeal Dismissed.
