Santosh Mehta v Om Prakash And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

02 April 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 115; (1980) 3 SCC 610; AIR 1980 SC 1664; 1980 (1) RCJ 697; 1980 (2) RCR(Rent) 516; 1980 (2) RentLR 434; [1980] 3 S.C.R. 325; 1980 UJ 722

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958, s. 15(7), 25B and 38 - Striking out defence of tenant - Discretionary power - Failure to pay rent and defence or gross negligence, essential - (A) Payment of rent by tenant to his advocate who in turn failed in depositing same in court - (B) Order if barred by s. 25B of the Act - Held, defence could not be struck out - Order falls u/s. 15, appealable u/s 38 and s. 25B does not operate as bar - Appeal allowed.
