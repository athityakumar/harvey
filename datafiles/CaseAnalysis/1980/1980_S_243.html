Delhi Cloth and General Mills Company Limited v State of Rajasthan and Others

Supreme Court of India

08 May 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, N.L. Untwalia

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 262; (1980) 4 SCC 71; (1980) SCC (Tax) 348; AIR 1980 SC 1552; 1981 ECR 51; 1980 (6) E.L.T. 383; [1980] 3 S.C.R. 1109; 1980 (46) STC 256; 1980 TAXLR 1756

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Keywords: Rajasthan Saels Tax Act, 1954, Rajasthan Taxation Laws (Amendment) Act, 1964

Summary: Sales Tax - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944 - Rajasthan Sales Tax Act, 1954, ss. 4 and 15 - Rajasthan Taxation Laws (Amendment) Act, 1964 - Additional Duties of Excise (Goods of Special Importance) Act, 1957, s. 2 - Whether 'Rayon Tyre Cord Fabric' manufactured by appellant is a rayon fabric covered by item 18 of schedule to Rajasthan Sales Tax Act, 1954, and therefore exempt from sales tax under Central Sales Tax Act? - Held, rayon tyre cord fabric manufactured by appellant is covered by that item of 18 - Therefore, it is exempt from sales tax and there is no jurisdiction in sales tax authorities to assess appellant on its turnover - Appeal allowed.
