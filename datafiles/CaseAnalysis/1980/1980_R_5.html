 R. R. Engineering Company v Zila Parishad, Bareilly and Another

Supreme Court of India

10 March 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, V.R. Krishna Iyer, N.L. Untwalia, P.N. Shingal, A.D. Koshal

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 323; (1980) 3 SCC 330; AIR 1980 SC 1088; [1980] 3 S.C.R. 1

Case Digest

Subject: Defence & Security Forces; Indirect Tax; Municipalities & Local Governments; Service

Summary: U.P. Kshetra Samitis and Zilla Parishads Adhiniyam, 1961, s. 119 - Constitution , arts. 276, 277 and sch. 7, list 2, entries 49, 60 and 58, list 1, 7, 97 - Uttar Pradesh Town Areas Act, 1914, s. 14(1)(f) - Taxation - Levy of circumstances and Property tax - Constitutionality - (A) Tax levied u/s. 119 of 1961 Act - (B) Impugned tax was levied by the Town Area Committee in pursuance of the power conferred by s. 14(f) which was introduced by a post-Constitution amendment - (A) Held, tax is not on income - Valid and falls under items 49, 60 and 58 - arts. 276 and 277 do not apply - (B) Held, it is not tax on income - It is valid and covered by Items 49, 60 and art. 277 not applicable - Appeals Dismissed.
