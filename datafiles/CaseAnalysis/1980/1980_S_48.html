Panna Lal v Shri Chand Mal and Others

Supreme Court of India

08 February 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, P.S. Kailasam, A.D. Koshal

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 543; (1980) 2 SCC 314; 1980 ACJ 233; AIR 1980 SC 871

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation

Summary: Motor Vehicles Act 1939, s. 31 - Transfer of moveable property - Vehicle transferred by transferring registration certificate - Claim for refund of sale price - Maintainablity - Ground, transferor's failure to get registration certificate transferred in transfree's name - Heid, not maintainable - Appeal Dismissed.
