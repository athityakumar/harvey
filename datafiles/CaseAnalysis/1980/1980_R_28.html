State of Uttar Pradesh v Ram Babu Misra

Supreme Court of India

19 February 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, R.S. Sarkaria

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 326; (1980) 2 SCC 343; (1980) SCC (Cr) 444; 1980 (17) ACC 174; AIR 1980 SC 791; 1980 ALJ 350; 1980 MLJ 477; [1980] 2 S.C.R. 1067; 1980 UJ 386

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Practice & Procedure - Evidence Act, s. 73 - Identification of Prisoners Act, 1920 - Whether court can take specimen writings at stage of investigation? - Held, no - s. 73 of Evidence Act does not permit a Court to give a direction to accused to give specimen writings for anticipated necessity for comparison in a proceeding which may later be instituted in Court - Appeal dismissed.
