Grindlays Bank Limited v Income Tax Officer, Calcutta and Others

Supreme Court of India

15 January 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, N.L. Untwalia

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 331; (1980) 2 SCC 191; (1980) SCC (Tax) 230; AIR 1980 SC 656; 1980 (15) CTR 157; [1980] 122 ITR 55; [1980] 2 S.C.R. 765; 1980 TAXLR 377; [1980] 3 TAXMAN 38; 1980 UJ 332

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 153(a)(iii) - Constitution, art. 226 - Income Tax - Effect of suspension of proceedings u/s. 142 by High Court in conssequence of writ petition - Applicability of bar of limitation on Order directing fresh assessment - Held, Order was necessary in order to do complete justice between the parties - It could not be faulted on the ground of limitation - Appeal dismissed.
