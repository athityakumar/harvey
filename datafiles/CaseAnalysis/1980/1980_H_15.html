Harjit Singh Mann v S. Umrao Singh and Others

Supreme Court of India

14 December 1980

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Shingal, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1980 Indlaw SC 236; (1980) 1 SCC 713; AIR 1980 SC 701; [1980] 2 S.C.R. 501

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Election; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Election - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 173 - Indian Limitation Act, 1908 - Representation of the People Act, 1951, ss. 33, 34, 36, 79 and 123 - Bribery - Proof of - Whether it is corrupt practice unless obtains promises from voters in return? - Held, when element of bargain was completely absent from allegation against respondent, Trial Court was justified in holding that alleged corrupt practice had not been established - Appeal dismissed.
