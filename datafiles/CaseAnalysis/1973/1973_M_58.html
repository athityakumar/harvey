Union of India and another v Mohan Lal Capoor and Others

Supreme Court of India

26 September 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, M. Hameedullah Beg

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 55; (1973) 2 SCC 836; (1974) SCC (L&S) 5; AIR 1974 SC 87; 1974 LabIC 338; 1973 (2) LLJ 504; [1974] 1 S.C.R. 797; 1973 (2) SLR 824

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Indian Police Service (Appointment by Promotion) Regulations 1955, Regn. 5(5) - Indian Administrative Service - Select list - (A) Validity of selection - (B) Supression - Reaons - (C) Need of notice - To senior who is proposed to be passed over -Selection is made on basis of merit and suitability with due regard to seniority - Selection committee must record its reasons for the proposed supersession - Notice need not be given to senior who is proposed to be passed over and audi alteram partem rule does not apply - Appeal Dismissed.
