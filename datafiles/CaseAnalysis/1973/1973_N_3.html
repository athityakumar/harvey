Commissioner of Income Tax and Controller of v N. R. Ramarathanam and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 March 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

Hans Raj Khanna, P. Jaganmohan Reddy, K.S. Hegde

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 229; (1973) SCC (Tax) 574; (1974) 3 SCC 268; AIR 1973 SC 2598; 1973 (2) CTR 421; [1973] 91 ITR 1; 1973 TAXLR 1450

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Indirect Tax

Summary: Land and Property - Estate Duty Act, 1953, s. 10 - Transfers of funds - Deceased transfered from his partnership account to accounts of his children, other partners - Whether passed on death of deceased - Held, sums so transferred could not be deemed to have passed to the donees on the death - Even though the sums so transferred remained in the partnership business till the death - Appeal dismissed.
