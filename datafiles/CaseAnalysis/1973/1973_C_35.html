G. Murugesan and Brothers v Commissioner of Income Tax, Madras

Supreme Court of India

08 February 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, P. Jaganmohan Reddy, Hans Raj Khanna

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 251; (1973) 4 SCC 211; AIR 1973 SC 2369; 1973 (2) CTR 279; [1973] 88 ITR 432; [1973] 3 S.C.R. 515; 1973 TAXLR 1294

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 3 - What constitute 'Association of persons' - Assessability - Members of the association, must join together for the purpose of producing income - Held, where the assessees themselves had submitted their returns, for certain assessment years, in the status of 'Association of Persons' they can be assessed as an 'association of persons - Appeal dismissed.
