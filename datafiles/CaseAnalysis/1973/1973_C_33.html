Sri Ramamohan Motor Service v Commissioner of Income Tax, Hyderabad

Supreme Court of India

11 April 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, Hans Raj Khanna

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 140; (1974) 3 SCC 116; AIR 1973 SC 1445; 1973 (2) CTR 247; [1973] 89 ITR 274; [1973] 3 S.C.R. 959; 1973 TAXLR 1048

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922 - Income Tax Rules, 1922, r. 2 - Provisions of s. 26 A of 1922 Act - Determination of effect of failure to strictly comply with requirements of sub-section (1) or sub-section (2) - Held, disentitles applicant to benefit of section - Appeal dismissed.
