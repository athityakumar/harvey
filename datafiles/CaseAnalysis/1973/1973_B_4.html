Gajadhar Prasad and Others v Babu Bhakta Ratan and Others

Supreme Court of India

14 August 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Hameedullah Beg, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 176; (1973) 2 SCC 629; AIR 1973 SC 2593; [1974] 1 S.C.R. 372; 1973 UJ 775

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 - Proviso of O. 21, r. 66 - Sale proclamation - Mere mention of valuation of properties as stated by decree-holder if material irregularity - Held, was material irregularity as judgment-debtor suffered substantial injury by sale - Object of r. 66 and duty of court thereunder pointed out - Appeal partly allowed.
