State of Uttar Pradesh v Zahoor Ahmad and Another

Supreme Court of India

08 August 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, D.G. Palekar, S.M. Sikri

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 318; (1973) 2 SCC 547; AIR 1973 SC 2520; [1974] 1 S.C.R. 344; 1973 UJ 805

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land and Property - Government Grants Act, 1895, ss. 2 and 3 - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 116 - (A) Lease of land for erecting a temporary rice mill - Whether Govt. grant - (B) Construction of grant - Applicability of s. 116 of 1882 Act - Held was not a Govt. Grant - Held, applicable - Appeal dismissed.
