State Of Uttar Pradesh & Anr. v Janki Saran Kailash Chandra & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

23 April 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

I.D. Dua, A. Alagiriswami, C.A. Vaidyialingam

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 325; (1973) 2 SCC 96; AIR 1973 SC 2071; [1974] 1 S.C.R. 31

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Arbitration and Adr - Arbitration Act, 1940 - CPC, 1908 - Whether court has discretion to grant stay of suit u/s. 34 of the 1940 Act - Held, discretion is available where application for adjournment for filing a written statement is moved - Appeal dismissed.
