Management of Tournamulla Estate v Workmen

Supreme Court of India

26 March 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, S.N. Dwivedi, C.A. Vaidyialingam

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 90; (1973) 2 SCC 502; (1973) SCC (L&S) 510; AIR 1973 SC 2344; 1973 LabIC 1465; 1973 (2) LLJ 241; [1973] 3 S.C.R. 762

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Payment of Gratuity Act, 1972, ss.4(5) and 4(6)(b) - Service matter - Agreement between employer and employee in force - Act not applicable - Terms of agreement cl.(4) - Dismissal of worker on ground of misconduct in or near factory - When misconduct involving violent and disorderly behaviour - Entire amount can be forfeited - Validity - Held, rule is conducive to industrial harmony and in consonance with public policy - Appeal allowed.
