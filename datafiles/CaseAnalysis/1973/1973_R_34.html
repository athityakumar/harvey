Kalu Ahir And Others v Ramdeo Ram

Supreme Court of India

01 May 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

I.D. Dua, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 268; (1973) 2 SCC 583; (1973) SCC (Cr) 903; AIR 1973 SC 2145; 1973 CRLJ 1404; 1973 CrLR(SC) 493; [1974] 1 S.C.R. 130

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Private Party

Summary: Criminal - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1898, s.439 - Power to order retrial in revision against acquittal - Held, in revision against acquittal by a private complainant High Court cannot reappraise evidence for itself as if it is acting as a Court of appeal and then order a retrial - Appeal allowed.
