Tika and Others v State of Uttar Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

12 November 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, S.N. Dwivedi

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 393; (1974) SCC (Cr) 67; (1974) 3 SCC 589; AIR 1974 SC 155; 1974 CRLJ 303; 1974 CRLR 1

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Evidence Act, 1872, s.101 - Criminal trial - Evidence - Onus of proof - Falsity of defence - Duty of prosecution - Held, case of the prosecution has to be tested independently of defence version - Where case depends for its proof on direct testimony, falsity of the defence will not help the prosecution to establish its own case - Appeal Dismissed.
