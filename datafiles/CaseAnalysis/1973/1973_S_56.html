Sri Bhimeshwara Swami Varu Temple v Pedapudi Krishna Murthi and Others

Supreme Court of India

27 March 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, S.N. Dwivedi, J.M. Shelat

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 463; (1973) 2 SCC 261; AIR 1973 SC 1299

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Evidence Act, 1872, ss. 32 and 114 - Presumption of ownership and possession of land - Rebuttal - Proof - Held, presumption arising from several entries in revenue records of large number of years in respect of ownership and possession of land with certain person does not stand rebutted by mere stray entries in favour of others when evidence is of uncertain character and is inadequate - Appeal dismissed.
