 State Of Jammu & Kashmir v Triloki Nath Khosa & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

26 September 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, V.R. Krishna Iyer, A.N. Ray, D.G. Palekar, P.N. Bhagwati

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 304; (1974) 1 SCC 19; (1974) SCC (L&S) 49; AIR 1974 SC 1; 1974 LabIC 1084; 1974 (1) LLJ 121; [1974] 1 S.C.R. 771; 1974 (1) SLR 536

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Equal Justice, Equal protection of law, Equality before law

Summary: Constitution, 1950 - Service Recruitment Rules (1970), sch - Service Rules - Constitutionality - Promotion to the post of executive engineers - Classification of Asstt. Engineers between diploma-holders and degree-holders - Rule held, does not violate arts. 14 and 16 of Constitution - Appeal allowed.
