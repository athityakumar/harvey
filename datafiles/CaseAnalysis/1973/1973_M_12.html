The Deputy Commissioner Of Agricultural Income Tax And Sales v M/S. Kotak & Co., Bombay, Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

03 April 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, Hans Raj Khanna

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 87; (1973) SCC (Tax) 512; (1974) 3 SCC 148; AIR 1973 SC 2491; 1973 (2) CTR 355; [1973] 3 S.C.R. 883; 1973 (32) STC 6; 1973 TAXLR 2466

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - Central Sales Tax Act, 1956, s.5(2) - Constitution Of India, 1950 art.286(1)(b) - Sale in course of import - Contract by assessee to supply foreign cotton to textile mills - Cotton imported by assessee on basis of import licence issued to mills - Sale effected by assessee - Taxability - Sale effected by assessee held occasioned import within s.5 (2) and was exempt from taxation - Appeal dismissed.
