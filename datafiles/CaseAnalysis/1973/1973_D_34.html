 Kanu Sanyal v District Magistrate, Darjeeijng & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

11 September 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, A.N. Ray, D.G. Palekar, Y.V. Chandrachud, V.R. Krishna Iyer

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 270; (1973) 2 SCC 674; (1973) SCC (Cr) 980; AIR 1973 SC 2684; 1973 CRLJ 1818; 1973 CrLR(SC) 660; [1974] 1 S.C.R. 621

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Constitution; Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice & procedure - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 32 - Supreme Court Rules, 1966, O. 35, rr. 4 and 5, O. 47, rr. 1 and 6 - Writ of Habeas corpus - Production of body of detenu - If must, before writ application is finally heard and decided - Held, not essential before application for writ is finally heard and decided - Order accordingly.
