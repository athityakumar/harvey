T. Govindaraja Mudaliar Etc. Etc. v The State Of Tamil Nadu & Others

Supreme Court of India

09 January 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, A.K. Mukherjea

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 342; (1973) 1 SCC 336; AIR 1973 SC 974; [1973] 3 S.C.R. 222

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Service

Summary: Carriers and Transportation - Constitution, art. 226 - Motor Vehicles Act, 1939, s. 68C and 68D - Schemes of road transport - Nationalization of Routes - Res judicata - Bias in hearing of objections - Co-ordination of services on all routes if a condition precedent - Objection to scheme - Held, validity of chap. IV-A on ground of infringement of art.19(1)(f), an open question which was not raised in earlier Writs cannot be raised - Not so biassed, as to disqualify him from hearing objections u/s. 68D of the Act - No - Objection not sustainablr - Appeals dismissed.
