 State of Punjab v K. R. Erry and Another

Supreme Court of India

21 September 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

D.G. Palekar, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, S.N. Dwivedi, Y.V. Chandrachud, J.M. Shelat

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 343; (1973) SCC (L&S) 63; (1973) 1 SCC 120; AIR 1973 SC 834; 1973 LabIC 440; 1973 (1) LLJ 33; [1973] 2 S.C.R. 405; 1973 SLJ 5; 1973 UJ 370

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Service - Punjab Civil Services Rules, 1959, r. 64 - Service Rules - Civil Service - State Govt. - Proposed reduction in pension and gratuity - Applicability of show cause - Held, where a body or authority is judicial or where it has to determine a matter involving rights judicially because of express or implied provision, principle of natural justice audi alteram partem applies - Appeals dismissed.
