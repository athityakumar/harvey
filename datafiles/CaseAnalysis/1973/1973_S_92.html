S. Parthasarathi v State of Andhra Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

20 September 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, A.K. Mukherjea

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 52; (1973) SCC (L&S) 580; (1974) 3 SCC 459; AIR 1973 SC 2701; 1973 LabIC 1607; [1974] 1 S.C.R. 697; 1974 SLJ 286; 1974 (1) SLR 427; 1973 UJ 882

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Hyderabad Civil Services (Classification, Control and Appeal) Rules, 1955, r.22 - Service matter - Domestic enquiry - Removal of employee - Enquiry conducted by biased officer vitiates order passed - Enquiry officer appointed as such by virtue of office held in officiating capacity - But reverted to his substantive post, continuing inquiry - Validity - Held, continuance of inquiry was bad in law - Subsequent authorisation did not validate it - Appeal allowed.
