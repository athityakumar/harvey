Oyami Ayatu v State of Madhya Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

20 August 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

Hans Raj Khanna, A. Alagiriswami

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 419; (1973) SCC (Cr) 925; (1974) 3 SCC 299; AIR 1974 SC 216; 1974 CRLJ 305

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Penal Code, 1860, s. 84 - Evidence Act, 1872, s.105 - Penal Code, 1860 - Applicability of s. 84 - Plea of insanity - Burden of proof - On whom lies - Held, burden, is upon the accused to prove that he was of unsound mind at the time of the commission of the offence and as such. incapable of knowing the nature of his act or that he was doing what was either wrong or contrary to law - Appeal Dismissed.
