Jose Alias Kolli Jose v State of Kerala

Supreme Court of India

10 January 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

A. Alagiriswami

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 475; (1973) SCC (Cr) 372; (1973) 3 SCC 472; AIR 1973 SC 944; 1973 CRLJ 687; 1973 CRLR 167

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Brothel, Poisoning

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 300 and 302 - Murder - (A) Validity of conviction for murder based on testimony of a single witness - (B) Sentence - Co-accused awarded life imprisonment - Part played by accused not greater than that of co-accused - Held, can be based on honest and trustworthy testimony even of a single witness - Sentence of death reduced to life imprisonment - Appeal dismissed.
