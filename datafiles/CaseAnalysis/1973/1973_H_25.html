Controller of Estate Duty, Gujarat v Hussainbhai Mohmedbhai Badri

Supreme Court of India

30 April 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, Hans Raj Khanna

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 163; (1974) 3 SCC 142; AIR 1973 SC 2150; 1973 (2) CTR 365; [1973] 90 ITR 148; [1974] 1 S.C.R. 122; 1973 TAXLR 1134

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Estate Duty Act, 1953 - Property which 'passes' u/s 5 on death - Determination - Income from trust property to be shared equally by wife and two sons of settler - And trust estate to be divided equally among two sons on wife's death - Held, on wife's death only one third of estate 'passes' and not whole of it - Appeal dismissed.
