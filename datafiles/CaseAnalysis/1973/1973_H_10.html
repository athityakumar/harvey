Gorakh Nath Dube v Hari Narain Singh and Others

Supreme Court of India

07 August 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Hameedullah Beg, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 166; (1973) 2 SCC 535; AIR 1973 SC 2451; 1975 ALJ 175; [1974] 1 S.C.R. 339

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Uttar Pradesh Consolidation of Holding Act, 1954

Summary: Land & Property - Uttar Pradesh Consolidation of Holdings Act, 1954, s. 5(2) - Suit for cancellation of a sale-deed, pending on date of notification u/s 4 of Act - Whether abates u/s 5(2) - Held, where plaintiff's claim is that sale of his half share by his uncle is invalid, inoperative and void, such a claim can be adjudicated upon by consolidation courts - Suit for cancellation would abate u/s 5(2) - Petition allowed.
