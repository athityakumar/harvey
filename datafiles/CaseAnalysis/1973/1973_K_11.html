State Of U. P. v Kailash Nath Agarwal & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

16 March 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

C.A. Vaidyialingam, A. Alagiriswami, I.D. Dua

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 349; (1973) SCC (Cr) 698; (1973) 1 SCC 751; AIR 1973 SC 2210; 1973 CRLJ 1196; 1973 CrLR(SC) 369; [1973] 3 S.C.R. 728

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - CrPC, 1898, ss. 337, 337(1) and 338 - Criminal Law (Amendment) Act, 1952 - (A) What is the nature of powers of District Magistrate regarding tender of pardon - (B) Order granting pardon u/s.337 or 338 - Whether revisable - Held, District Magistrate can grant a pardon even after the enquiring Magistrate has declined to grant pardon - Order granting pardon u/s. 337 or 338 is revisable - Appeal partly allowed.
