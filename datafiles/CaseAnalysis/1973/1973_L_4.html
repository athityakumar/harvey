Hukumdev Narain Yadav v Lalit Narain Mishra

Supreme Court of India

21 December 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

P. Jaganmohan Reddy, S.N. Dwivedi, P.K. Goswami

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 359; (1974) 2 SCC 133; AIR 1974 SC 480; [1974] 3 S.C.R. 31

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Civil Procedure; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Representation of the People Act, 1951, s. 81 - Limitation Act, 1963, ss. 5 and 29(2) - Prohibition Act - General Clauses Act, 1897, s. 10 - (A) Election petition - Limitation - Applicability of s.10 of 1897 Act - (B) What amounts to exclusion of provisions of ss. 4 to 24 of 1963 Act - Express reference to such provisions in special law not necessary -

Held, even if s.4 of 1963 Act does not apply s.10 of 1897 Act will certainly apply to election petitions - Held, s. 5 does not apply to election petition under 1951 Act - Appeal Dismissed.
