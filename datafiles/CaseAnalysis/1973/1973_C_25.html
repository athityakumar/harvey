 Official Trustee of West Bengal For The Trust of Chitra v Commissioner of Income Tax West Bengal, Calcutta

Supreme Court of India

04 December 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

A. Alagiriswami, A.N. Ray, Hans Raj Khanna, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, P.N. Bhagwati

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 285; (1974) 3 SCC 616; AIR 1974 SC 1355; [1974] 93 ITR 348; [1974] 2 S.C.R. 583; 1974 TAXLR 474

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 3 - Income tax Act, 1961, s. 2(31) - Hindu deity - Falls within the meaning of the word 'individual' - As such if it taxable to income tax - Held, it would naturally be taxed through its shebaits who are in possession and management of its property - Appeals dismissed.
