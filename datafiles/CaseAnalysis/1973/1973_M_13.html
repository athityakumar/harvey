Board Of Revenue, Madras v M/S. Raj Brothers Agencies Etc.

Supreme Court of India

06 February 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, P. Jaganmohan Reddy, Hans Raj Khanna

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 122; (1973) 4 SCC 216; AIR 1973 SC 2307; 1973 (2) CTR 235; 1975 (1) MLJ(SC) 7; [1973] 3 S.C.R. 492; 1973 (31) STC 434; 1973 TAXLR 2423

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure; Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - TN General Sales Tax Act, 1959, s. 34(l) - Indian Limitation Act, 1908 - Revision u/s.34(l) of 1959 Act - Scope - Dismissal of appeal against order - On ground of limitation - Revision if lies against such order - Board of Revenue held, has jurisdiction to decide the validity of an order appeal against which has been dismissed on ground of limitation (as it is not an effective appeal ). Appeal dismissed.
