State of Uttar Pradesh v Bansi Dhar and Others

Supreme Court of India

11 December 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, D.G. Palekar

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 368; (1974) 1 SCC 446; AIR 1974 SC 1084; [1974] 2 S.C.R. 679

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Attorney-General

Summary: Trusts Act, 1882, s. 83 - CPC, 1908 - Religious and Charitable Endowments -Applicability of doctrine of Cypres - Conditional donation to Govt. to construct a women's hospital - Failure of condition - Claim for refund - Applicability of provisions of s.83 of 1882 Act - Held, provisions of s. 83 proprio vigore do not apply - Cypres doctrine applies if the nature of the charitable object is general and not specific - Held that the transaction was not a gift simpliciter but was subject to the matching grant from Govt., building having to be made with such augmented amount by the donor etc. - Appeal Dismissed.
