Rajender Singh and Others v Santa Singh and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 August 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Hameedullah Beg, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 316; (1973) 2 SCC 705; AIR 1973 SC 2537; [1974] 1 S.C.R. 381

Case Digest

Subject: Defence & Security Forces; Land & Property

Keywords: Evacuee Property & Displaced Persons

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Limitation Act, 1908, s. 3 and 28, art.44 and 142 - Administration of Evacuee Property Act, 1950 - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 52 - Adverse possession of defendants for over 12 years - Pendency of earlier suit by defendants which was finally dismissed by High Court - Doctrine of lis pendens, contained in s. 52 of 1882 Act - Applicability - Held, doctrine would not arrest running of period of limitation during pendency of that suit - Appeal dismissed.
