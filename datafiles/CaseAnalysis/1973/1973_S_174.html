Pukhraj v State of Rajasthan and Another

Supreme Court of India

29 August 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

A. Alagiriswami, Hans Raj Khanna

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 184; (1973) 2 SCC 701; (1973) SCC (Cr) 944; AIR 1973 SC 2591; 1973 CRLJ 1795; 1973 CrLR(SC) 623; [1974] 1 S.C.R. 559; 1973 UJ 832

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Code of Criminal Procedure 1898

Summary: Criminal - Practice & Procedure - Code of Criminal Procedure 1898, s. 197 - When can an act of a public servant be said not to be done in purported exercise of his duty? - Held that mere fact that accused proposed to raise appeal that act was done by him in furtherance of his duty is not sufficient to justify case for being thrown out for want of sanction - Order accordingly.
