Johri Lal (H. U. F.) v Commissioner of Income Tax, Uttar Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

09 February 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, Hans Raj Khanna, P. Jaganmohan Reddy

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 253; (1973) 4 SCC 175; AIR 1973 SC 2352; 1973 (2) CTR 283; 1974 (1) ITJ 1; [1973] 88 ITR 439; 1973 TAXLR 1288

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922 - Conversion of reassessment proceedings u/s 34(1)(b) into proceedings u/s 34(1)(a) of Income Tax Act - By Appellate Tribunal - Permissibility - Appellate Tribunal held, convert them as conditions prescribed in s.34(1)(a) are not satisfied - Appeal allowed.
