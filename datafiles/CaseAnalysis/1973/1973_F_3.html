Faqir Chand v Shri Ram Rattan Bhanot

Supreme Court of India

30 January 1973

Case Analysis

Bench

A. Alagiriswami, I.D. Dua, C.A. Vaidyialingam

Where Reported

1973 Indlaw SC 335; (1973) 1 SCC 572; AIR 1973 SC 921; 1973 RCJ 704; 1973 RCR(Rent) 221; [1973] 3 S.C.R. 454

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments; Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958, s. 14(1), Proviso (k) - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 115 - Tenancy - Landlord letting out premises for use against condition of his lease - Effect - Held, neither lease can be said to be void ab initio on ground that it was for unauthorised user nor landlord is estopped from claiming possession on account of he himself being a party to breach of condition - Appeals allowed.
