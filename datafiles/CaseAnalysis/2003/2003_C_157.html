Punjab Processors Private Limited v Collector of Customs

Supreme Court of India

04 September 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

Ashok Bhan, Ruma Pal

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 1390; (2007) 15 SCC 704; 2003 (157) E.L.T. 625

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Customs - Review - Whether value of the imported goods was lower than the invoice? - Held, tribunal order was correct - Customs authorities while assessing the value of the import are not bound by the figure mentioned in the invoice and the customs authorities can rely on contemporaneous evidence to show that the invoice is not the correct value - Appeals dismissed.
