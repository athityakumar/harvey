Goaplast Pvt. Ltd. v Shrichico Ursula D'Souza and Another

Supreme Court of India

07 March 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

Arun Kumar, M.B. Shah

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 218; (2003) 3 SCC 232; (2003) SCC (Cr) 603; AIR 2003 SC 2035; 2003 (51) ALR 52; 2003 (2) AWC 1429; 2003 (2) BC 71; 2003 (1) CCR 410; [2003] 114 Comp Cas 644; 2003 (2) Crimes 55; 2003 CRLJ 1723; 2003 (103) DLT 407; 2003 (2) G.L.R. 1629; 2003 (1) JCC 99; 2003 (1) JIC 895; JT 2003 (3) SC 42; 2003 (2) MahLJ 366; 2003 (2) MPLJ 102; 2003 (2) RCR(Criminal) 131; 2003(3) SCALE 117; [2003] 2 S.C.R. 712; 2003 (2) SLT 410; 2003 (2) Supreme 536; 2003 (2) UC 920

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Corporate; Criminal

Summary: Banking & Finance - Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881 - Banking, Public Financial Institutions and Negotiable Instruments Laws (Amendment) Act, 1988 - Post-dated cheque issued - Issuer wrote to drawee Bank to stop payment of cheques - Notice u/s. 138 of Negotiable Instrument Act, 1881 - Whether an issuer of cheque can be tried u/s. 138, when the person issuing it stops its payment by issuing instructions to the drawee bank before the due date of payment? - Drawer of cheque should not be allowed to abuse the accommodation given to him by a creditor by way of acceptance of post-dated cheque, where stoppage of payment of a post-dated cheque is permitted to take the case out of the purview of s. 138 - Issuer of cheque can be tried u/s. 138 - Appeal allowed.
