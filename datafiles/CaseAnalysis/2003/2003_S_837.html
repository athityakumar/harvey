M.D., Army Welfare Housing Organisation v Sumangal Services Private Limited

Supreme Court of India

08 October 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Brijesh Kumar

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 869; (2004) 9 SCC 619; AIR 2004 SC 1344; 2003 (52) ARBLR 361; JT 2003 (Supp2) SC 300; 2003 (3) RAJ 447; 2003 (4) RCR(Civil) 767; 2003(8) SCALE 424(B); 2003 (6) SLT 221; [2003] Supp4 S.C.R. 385; 2003 (8) Supreme 520

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Keywords: West Bengal Municipal Act, 1993, West Bengal Municipal Act, 1932, West Bengal Land Reform Act

Summary: Arbitration Act, 1940 - ss. 30, 33 - Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996 - s. 17 - Award - Whether the award of the learned arbitrators suffered from any legal infirmity - Development of land and construction of a composite housing project on a turn-key basis - Contract job to third party - Arbitrator did not determine the question as to whether he had jurisdiction to pass an interim order or not - Held, the award of the arbitrations in relation to claim No. 2 must be set aside. Consequently, no interest thereupon shall be payable.
