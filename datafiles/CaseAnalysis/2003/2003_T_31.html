Collector Of Central Excise v Technoweld Industries

Supreme Court of India

27 March 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Variava, H.K. Sema

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 1099; (2003) 11 SCC 798; 2003 (155) E.L.T. 209; 2003 (57) RLT 383

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax; Trade

Summary: Excise - Trade - Central Excise Tariff Act, 1985 - Whether by drawing wire into a thinner gauge, manufacture has taken place? - Initial product was a wire rod and ultimately product is also a wire - All that is done is that gauge of rod is made thinner and product is finished a little better - Held, there is no manufacture of a new product - Merely because there are two separate entries does not mean that product becomes excisable - Product becomes excisable only if there is manufacture - Appeals dismissed.
