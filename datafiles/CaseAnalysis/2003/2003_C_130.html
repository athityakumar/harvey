Union Carbide India Limited v Collector of Central Excise, Calcutta

Supreme Court of India

25 September 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

Ruma Pal, G.P. Mathur

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 1195; 2003 (158) E.L.T. 15; 2003 (58) RLT 715

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Valuation - Captive consumption - Cost of production means actual cost of production together with notional profit which would have been earned if goods had been sold and not merely the notional sale price of those goods without considering actual cost - appeal allowed
