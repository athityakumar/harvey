Messrs Continental Construction Limited v State of Uttar Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

22 September 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 774; (2003) 8 SCC 4; 2003 (53) ALR 432; 2003 (52) ARBLR 320; 2004 (1) AWC 23; JT 2003 (Supp2) SC 410; 2003 (3) RAJ 318; 2003 (4) RCR(Civil) 430; 2003(8) SCALE 14; 2003 (6) SLT 23; [2003] Supp3 S.C.R. 1002; 2003 (6) Supreme 957

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Summary: Arbitration Act, 1940 - s. 30 - Construction of dam, powerhouse, structures and appurtenant works - Arbitration agreement - Non-speaking award - Held, in the instant case, the Umpire has merely set out the claims, given the history of the claims and awarded certain amount. He has not disclosed his mind indicating as to why he had done so or what was done. The Courts, therefore, could not interfere with the award merely on ipse dixit.
