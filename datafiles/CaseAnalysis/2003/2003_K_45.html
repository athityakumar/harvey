Ibrahimpatnam Taluk Vyavasaya Collie Sangham v K.Suresh Reddy & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

19 August 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

Shivaraj V. Patil, D.M. Dharmadhikari

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 649; (2003) 7 SCC 667; AIR 2003 SC 3592; JT 2004 (6) SC 70; 2003(6) SCALE 615; 2003 (1) SCW 4084; [2003] Supp2 S.C.R. 698

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Andhra Pradesh (Telangana Area) Tenancy and Agricultural Land Act, 1950 - s. 50-B(4) - Whether Collector can exercise suo-motu power under s. 50-B(4), Andhra Pradesh (Telangana Area) Tenancy and Agricultural Land Act, 1950 at any time or such power is to be exercised within a reasonable time - Held, the action of the Joint Collector in exercising suo-motu power after several years and not within reasonable period and passing orders canceling validation certificates given by Tehsildar could not be sustained.
