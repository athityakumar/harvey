North Zone Cultural Centre & Anr. v Vedpathi Dinesh Kumar

Supreme Court of India

17 April 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, B.P. Singh

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 382; (2003) 5 SCC 455; (2003) SCC (L&S) 697; AIR 2003 SC 2719; 2003 (3) ALD(SC) 121; 2003 (3) AWC 2300; 2003 (2) CLR 376; 2003 (3) ESC 329; 2003 (97) FLR 808; JT 2003 (4) SC 155; 2003 (2) LLJ 839; 2003 (3) LLN 30; 2003(4) SCALE 181; 2003 (3) SLR 293; 2003 (4) SLT 103; 2003 (3) Supreme 532; 2003 (2) UPLBEC 1825

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Service - resignation - whether the resignation of the respondent was accepted by the appellant on 18.11.1988 or not - held, non-communication of the acceptance does not make the resignation inoperative provided there is in fact an acceptance before the withdrawal. The guidelines indicate that the resignation takes effect the moment the same is accepted. - In the present case the resignation is dated 18.11.1988 and the same is accepted on 18.11.1988 itself. - The communication was on 1.12.1988 about 13 days thereafter which delay is not an undue delay so as to draw an inference that there has been no acceptance of the resignation.
