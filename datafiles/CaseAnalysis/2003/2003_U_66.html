B.P. Sharma v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

18 August 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

Brijesh Kumar, Arun Kumar

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 629; (2003) 7 SCC 309; AIR 2003 SC 3863; 2003 (5) AWC 3646; 2004 (4) ESC 616; JT 2003 (7) SC 159; 2003 (4) LLJ 694; 2003(6) SCALE 498; 2003 (6) SLT 343; [2003] Supp2 S.C.R. 684; 2003 (5) Supreme 597; 2003 (4) UPLBEC 3028

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Environment, Wildlife & Animal

Summary: Constitution of India-Article 19 (1) (g)-Ancient Monuments and Archaeological Sites and Remains Act, 1958-Sections 18 and 38-Ancient Monuments and Archaeological Sites and Remains Rules, 1959 - Rule 8 (d)-Fundamental right of practising any profession guaranteed under Article 19 (1) (g)-Profession of guides-Identity card of Department of Tourism, G.O.I.-Condition that card not to be renewed after guide attains age of 60 years-Whether within scope of Sections 18 and 38 and Rule 8 (d)?-Held, “no”-Whether restriction reasonable so as to not offend Article 19 (1) (g)?-Held, “no”-It amounts to total prohibition-Condition ultra vires and quashed.
