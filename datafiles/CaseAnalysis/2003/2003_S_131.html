Ameer Trading Corporation Limited v Shapoorji Data Processing Limited

Supreme Court of India

18 November 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, AR. Lakshmanan

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 1036; (2004) 1 SCC 702; AIR 2004 SC 355; 2004 (1) ALD(SC) 34; 2004 (54) ALR 198; 2004 (1) AWC 673; 2004 (1) CHN 103; 2004 (97) CutLT 417; JT 2003 (9) SC 109; 2004 (1) RCR(Civil) 259; 2004 (1) RD 386; 2003(9) SCALE 713; 2003 (1) SCW 6340; 2003 (7) SLT 791; [2003] Supp5 S.C.R. 634; 2003 (8) Supreme 634

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure; Rent Control

Summary: Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 - O. 18 rr. 4, 5 - Maharashtra Rent Control Act, 1999 - Interpretation - Held, O. 18 rr. 4 and 5 are required to be harmoniously construed. Both the provisions are required to be given effect to and as O. 18 r. 5 cannot be read as an exception to O. 18 r. 4.
