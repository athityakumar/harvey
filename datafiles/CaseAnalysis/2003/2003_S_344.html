Rai Vimal Krishna and Others v State of Bihar and Others

Supreme Court of India

07 July 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

Ruma Pal, B.N. Srikrishna

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 507; (2003) 6 SCC 401; AIR 2003 SC 2676; AIR 2003 SCW 3360; 2003 (4) ALD(SC) 70; JT 2003 (5) SC 333; 2003(5) SCALE 184; 2003 (4) SLT 457; [2003] Supp1 S.C.R. 358; 2003 (4) Supreme 417

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Bihar and Orissa Municipalities Act, 1922, Patna Municipal Corporation Act, 1951, Annual Rental Value of Holding Rules, 1993

Summary: [A.] Municipalities & Local Governments - Patna Municipal Corporation Act, 1951 - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 226 - Bihar and Orissa Municipalities Act, 1922, ss.106, 106(2), 107, 116 - Annual Rental Value of Holding Rules, 1993, rr. 3(2), 5(1) - Assessment of appellants holdings in Patna - (1) Whether provisions of ss. 133, 134 and 137 of Act have been followed by Corporation in matter of preparation of valuation and assessment list - (2) publication of notice of assessment had not been done in manner prescribed by s 149(1) of Act - (3) assessment list could not be prepared piecemeal at different times for different properties in a discriminatory manner - (4) new rate of tax could take effect only after objections u/s. 150 had been decided by Chief Executive Officer - Held, (1) submission of appellants that Corporation was bound to comply with provisions of Act for valuation and assessment before publishing assessment list is unacceptable in view of promulgation of 1993 Rules, and notifications issued thereunder, validity of all of which has been upheld by this Court - (2) mode of publication prescribed in s 149(1) as opposed to publication itself, was mandatory and hold that publication in newspapers was in substantial compliance with requirements of sub-section - (3) 1993 Rules and notifications issued thereunder clearly provide for assessment based on localities as well as different kinds of properties, classified according to its user and type of construction - Additionally, proviso to s.138 (1) expressly indicates that assessment lists may be prepared in respect of a specified area within Corporation - Further held, s 149(1) itself shows that assessment lists may be made in respect of 'any part of Patna' - (4) it is open to Corporation to recover tax as determined on basis of impugned assessment lists pending disposal of appellants' applications u/s. 151, until and unless, by virtue of an order under ss 151 or 152 passed thereon, assessment list is amended or altered.
