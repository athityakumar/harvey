Madhya Pradesh State of Electricity Board v Smt. Jarina Bee

Supreme Court of India

15 July 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Doraiswamy Raju

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 514; (2003) 6 SCC 141; (2003) SCC (L&S) 833; AIR 2003 SC 2657; 2003 (4) AWC 2595; 2003 (3) ESC 380; 2003 (98) FLR 595; 2003 (3) JhrCR(SC) 175; JT 2003 (5) SC 542; 2003 (3) KHCACJ 329; 2003 (3) LLJ 244; 2003 (3) MPLJ 534; 2003(5) SCALE 259; 2004 (1) SLJ 96; 2003 (4) SLR 749; [2003] Supp1 S.C.R. 535; 2003 (4) Supreme 510; 2003 (2) UPLBEC 1881

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Employment - Workman-Dismissal-Order of dismissal set aside by labour court on account of violation of principles of natural justice with direction for reinstatement-Whether award of full back wages is natural consequence of setting aside of dismissal order?-Held, “no”-Payment of back wages has discretionary element-It has to be dealt with in facts and circumstances of each case-No straight-jacket formula can be evolved-However, there is statutory sanction to direct payment of full back wages-Workman dying in meantime-Opportunity was also granted to employer to proceed in accordance with law-Hence, direction to pay Rs. 85,000 towards back wages to meet ends of justice.
