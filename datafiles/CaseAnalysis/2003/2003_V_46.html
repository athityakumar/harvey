Collector Of Central Excise, Ahmedabad v Vikshara Trading and Invest. P. Ltd.

Supreme Court of India

27 August 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, G.P. Mathur

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 1121; (2004) 13 SCC 49; 2003 (157) E.L.T. 4; 2003 (27) PTC 603; 2003 (58) RLT 604

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax; Intellectual Property

Summary: Excise - Intellectual Property - Trade and Merchandise Marks Act, 1958 - Non registration of Trademark -Whether benefit of Notification No. 223/87-C E D/- 22-9-1987 was applicable? - Held, when as a matter of fact it is held that there was an assignment in favour of first respondent and that fact was not in serious dispute mere fact that assignment was not registered could not alter position - Appeal dismissed.
