M.D., U.P. Land Dev. Corpn. and another v Amar Singh and others

Supreme Court of India

01 April 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

Shivaraj V. Patil, Arijit Pasayat

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 331; (2003) 5 SCC 388; (2003) SCC (L&S) 690; AIR 2003 SC 2357; 2003 (3) ESC 305; 2003 (97) FLR 642; JT 2003 (3) SC 615; 2003 LabIC 1757; 2003 (3) LLJ 220; 2003 (2) LLN 1103; 2003(3) SCALE 675; [2003] 3 S.C.R. 266; 2003 (2) SLJ 310; 2003 (3) SLR 360; 2003 (4) SLT 201; 2003 (3) Supreme 174; 2003 (2) UPLBEC 1791

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Service - regularisation of - Held, reading of the documents and the contentions raised on either side go to show that the appointments of the respondents were temporary under the 'Million Well Scheme'. Therefore, when the work of the scheme had come to an end, the respondents were not entitled to claim regularisation of their services.
