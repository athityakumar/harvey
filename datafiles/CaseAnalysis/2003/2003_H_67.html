Regional Manager, U.P.S.R.T.C. Etawah and others v Hoti Lal and another

Supreme Court of India

11 February 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Shivaraj V. Patil

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 127; (2003) SCC (L&S) 363; (2003) 3 SCC 605; AIR 2003 SC 1462; 2003 (2) AWC 1070; 2003 (2) ESC 97; 2003 (96) FLR 1076; JT 2003 (2) SC 27; 2003 LabIC 757; 2003 (2) LLJ 267; 2003 (1) LLN 843; 2003(2) SCALE 54; [2003] 1 S.C.R. 1019; 2003 (2) SLJ 56; 2003 (2) SLR 419; 2003 (2) SLT 112; 2003 (2) UPLBEC 1047

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Service

Summary: Service - U.P. State Road Transport Corporation - Misconduct by employee bus conductor - Termination - Petitioner was found to be carrying on ticketless passengers and certain old and used tickets were recovered from his possession - Service terminated - On appeal HC set aside order of termination - Whether the HC was justified in interfering with the quantum of punishment awarded to the respondent no 1? - Held, the Court or Tribunal while dealing with the quantum of punishment has to record reasons as to why it is felt that the punishment does not commensurate with the proved charges - Unfortunately, in the present case, no reasons whatsoever have been indicated as to why the punishment was considered disproportionate Where the person deals with public money or is engaged in financial transactions or acts in a fiduciary capacity, highest degree of integrity and trust-worthiness is must and unexceptionable Judged in that background, conclusions of the Division Bench of the HC do not appear to be proper - Division Bench order set aside and order of learned Single Judge upholding order of dismissal restored - Appeal allowed.
