The Land Acquisition Officer, Kammarapally Village, Nizamabad District, Andhra Pradesh v Nookala Rajamallu and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 November 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Doraiswamy Raju

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 1073; (2003) 12 SCC 334; AIR 2004 SC 1031; 2004 (54) ALR 182; 2004 (1) AWC 373; JT 2003 (9) SC 433; 2004 (1) RCR(Civil) 293B; 2004 (1) RD 392; 2003(10) SCALE 307; 2003 (1) SCW 6674; 2003 (7) SLT 367; [2003] Supp6 S.C.R. 67; 2003 (8) Supreme 507

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Agriculture & Agri. Products

Summary: Land Acquisition Act, 1894 - ss. 4(1), 18, 23 - Compensation - Enhancement - Agricultural lands - Held, the claimants shall be entitled to compensation at the rate of Rs. 40/- per sq. yard along with statutory entitlements including interest on solatium.
