National Thermal Power Corporation Ltd. v Karri Pothuraju & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

13 August 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, Doraiswamy Raju

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 637; (2003) 7 SCC 384; (2003) SCC (L&S) 1053; AIR 2003 SC 3647; 2003 (3) CLR 285; 2003 (5) ESC 71; 2003 (99) FLR 8; 2003 (4) JhrCR(SC) 191; JT 2003 (7) SC 149; 2003 LabIC 3120; 2003 (3) LLJ 567; 2003(6) SCALE 304; 2003 (5) SLR 97; 2003 (5) SLT 85; [2003] Supp2 S.C.R. 426; 2003 (6) Supreme 183

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Bombay Industrial Relations act

Summary: Factories Act, 1948 - ss. 46, 48 - Contract Labour (Regulation and Abolition) Act, 1970 - Regularisation of services of canteen workers - Statutory liability to maintain a canteen by the principal employer in the factory/establishment - Held, the Division Bench has chosen to leave liberty to the appellant to consider the claims of the workers as to whether they satisfy the requirements and whether they are otherwise unfit for confirmations.
