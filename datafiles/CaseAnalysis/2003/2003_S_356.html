Shankar K. Mandal and others v State of Bihar and others

Supreme Court of India

17 April 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Shivaraj V. Patil

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 352; (2003) 9 SCC 519; (2003) SCC (L&S) 1145; AIR 2003 SC 4043; 2003 (2) ESC 217; 2003 (97) FLR 907; 2003 (3) JhrCR(SC) 38; 2004 (1) JhrCR(SC) 55; 2003(4) SCALE 203; [2003] 3 S.C.R. 796; 2003 (1) SCW 2980; 2003 (4) SLT 114; 2003 (3) Supreme 442; 2003 (4) UPLBEC 3374

Case Digest

Subject: Administrative; Service

Keywords: Eligibility Conditions

Summary: Service - Recruitment - Eligibility criteria - Whether the appellants herein were legally recruited as teachers during the period from 1981 to 1983? - Whether appellants were over age on the date of their initial appointment? - Facts of the case indicating that between 1981 to 1983 primary teachers were appointed and legality of such appointments were challenged and enquiries were conducted, and eventually appontments were terminated - Writ petition filed by appointees - HC issued direction for re-appointment of terminated candidates on merit with direction as to exemption of age if petitioners were in the meantime go over age - Fresh appointment process began - Present appellant felt aggrieved as they were not appointed, filed again petition before HC - HC's judgement whether justified? -

Appellants have taken the stand that in terms of this Court's judgment, a person who was not over age on the date of initial appointment was to be considered - Though it was conceded before the HC that they were over age at the time of initial appointment, such would turn as to what is the date of initial appointment - HC had not considered as to what was the applicable rule so far as the eligibility regarding age is concerned - Precedents quoted - Principles culled out from such precedents as follows; (i) The cut off date by reference to which the eligibility requirement must be satisfied by the candidate seeking a public employment is the date appointed by the relevant service rules; (ii) If there is not cut off date appointed by the rules then such date shall be as appointed for the purpose in the advertisement calling for applications; and (iii) If there is no such date appointed then the eligibility criteria shall be applied by reference to the last date appointed by which the applications were to be received by the competent authority - Directions issued to authority for decision as to which of the three conditions were applicable to the facts of case - Further direction as to time period within which the decision has to be taken, issued - Appeals disposed of.
