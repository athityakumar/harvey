Pure Helium India Private Limited v Oil and Natural Gas Commission

Supreme Court of India

09 October 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 862; (2003) 8 SCC 593; AIR 2003 SC 4519; 2003 (3) ARBLR 409; JT 2003 (Supp2) SC 596; 2003 (3) RAJ 484; 2003 (4) RCR(Civil) 791; 2003(8) SCALE 553; 2003 (1) SCW 5274; 2003 (6) SLT 164; [2003] Supp4 S.C.R. 561; 2003 (8) Supreme 264

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Constitution; Corporate

Summary: Arbitration Act, 1940 - s. 30 - Whether jurisdiction of an arbitrator to interpret a contract can be subject-matter of an objection under s. 30, Arbitration Act, 1940 - Held, the arbitrators were called upon to determine a legal issue which included interpretation of the contract. The arbitrators, therefore, cannot be said to have been travelled beyond jurisdiction in making the award.
