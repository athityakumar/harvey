Mishra Dhatu Nigam Ltd. Etc. Etc v M. Venkataiah And Ors. Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

13 August 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, Doraiswamy Raju

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 636; (2003) 7 SCC 488; (2003) SCC (L&S) 1066; AIR 2003 SC 3124; 2003 (5) AWC 3640; 2003 (3) CLR 516; 2003 (5) ESC 92; 2003 (99) FLR 5; 2004 (1) JhrCR(SC) 67; JT 2003 (7) SC 95; 2003 (3) LLJ 847; 2003(6) SCALE 294; 2004 (1) SLJ 298; 2003 (5) SLR 85; 2003 (7) SLT 602; [2003] Supp2 S.C.R. 411; 2003 (6) Supreme 187; 2003 (3) UPLBEC 2412

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Factories Act, 1948-Section 46-Employment - Regularisation-Appellant required by law to maintain canteen for benefit of workmen-Canteen of appellant run by workers engaged through contractors-Canteen workers held to be employees of appellant, i.e., principal employer-Hence, entitled to regularisation of their services-No merit in appeals.
