Oriental Insurance Company Limited v T.S. Sastry

Supreme Court of India

30 October 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 961; (2004) SCC (L&S) 20; (2004) 1 SCC 136; AIR 2004 SC 795; 2004 (1) CLR 108; 2003 (99) FLR 1070; 2004 (2) JhrCR(SC) 231; JT 2003 (9) SC 196; 2004 (1) LLN 447; 2004 (1) MLJ(SC) 69; 2003(9) SCALE 485; 2003 (1) SCW 6988; 2004 (1) SLJ 253; 2004 (1) SLR 23; 2003 (7) SLT 39; [2003] Supp5 S.C.R. 34; 2003 (8) Supreme 33; 2004 (1) UPLBEC 991

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Service

Keywords: Eligibility - Seniority - Cadre

Summary: Service - Promotion - Respondent's name featured in the waiting list prepared for promotion - High Court issued direction to give notional promotion and seniority to the respondent - Monetary benefit given only from the date of filing of the writ petition - Held, the insurance company acted mala fide in depriving the respondent herein his legitimate claim for promotion.
