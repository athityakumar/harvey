Smt. V. Rajeshwari v T.C. Saravanabava

Supreme Court of India

16 December 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, Ashok Bhan

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 1187; (2004) 1 SCC 551; 2004 (56) ALR 173; 2004 (98) CutLT 38; 2004 (2) MPLJ 392; 2004 (1) RCR(Civil) 498; 2003(10) SCALE 768; 2004 (1) SLT 686; 2003 (Supp) RD 270; [2003] Supp6 S.C.R. 927; 2003 (8) Supreme 781

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Code of Civil Procedure, 1973 - O. XLI r. 27 - Res judicata - Adverse possession - Property - Issue of title - Held, the plea of res judicata has neither been raised nor proved - Further held, the plea of adverse possession raised by the defendant is devoid of any merit and cannot be countenanced - Directions issued.
