Nalin Thakor and Others v State of Gujarat and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 November 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, AR. Lakshmanan

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 950; (2003) 12 SCC 461; 2004(2) SCALE 68

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Environment, Wildlife & Animal; Practice & Procedure; Water

Summary: Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1974 - ss. 43, 44, 47 - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 - s. 482 - Issuance of process by magistrate - Held, the issue of summons to the appellants was without an application of mind as nothing was said in the complaint or in the statement recorded by the 5th Joint Judicial Magistrate before issue of summons that the offence was committed with the consent or connivance of or was attributable to any negligence on the part of the appellants - Further held, there being no allegation as regards the requirement of s. 47(2), the Judicial Magistrate committed an error in issuing summons to the appellants herein.
