Ramakrishna Kamat and others v State of Karnataka and others

Supreme Court of India

05 February 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

Shivaraj V. Patil, Arijit Pasayat

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 464; (2003) SCC (L&S) 284; (2003) 3 SCC 374; AIR 2003 SC 1530; 2003 (2) AWC 1098; 2003 (1) CLR 803; 2003 (96) FLR 1159; JT 2003 (2) SC 88; 2003 LabIC 1196; 2003 (1) LLN 822; 2003(1) SCALE 638; 2003 (2) SLR 470; 2003 (2) SLT 189; 2003 (1) UPLBEC 831

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Master And Servant Relationship

Summary: Service - Regularisation -Teachers selected by Betterment Committees on honorarium paid by school management committees for schools run by Zila Parishads - Regular appointment and arrears of salary claimed - No evidence showing that their appointments were made either by Zilla Parishad or officers of the State Government against any sanctioned post - Held, petitioners not entitled to regularisation - Services rendered by them is entitled to be considered at the time of regular appointment.
