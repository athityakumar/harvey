State of West Bengal and another v E.I.T.A. India Limited and others

Supreme Court of India

05 March 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

S.S.M. Quadri, Ashok Bhan

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 295; (2003) 5 SCC 239; AIR 2003 SC 4126; JT 2003 (3) SC 365; 2003(3) SCALE 361; [2003] 2 S.C.R. 668; 2003 (3) SLT 654; 2003 (131) STC 111; 2003 (2) Supreme 870

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Keywords: Bengal Finance (Sales Tax) Act, 1941, Trader, Transporter, West Bengal Sales Tax Act, 1994, Tripura Sales Tax Act, 1976, West Bengal Sales Tax Rules, 1995, Bengal Finance (Sales Tax) Delhi Amendment Act, 1956, Rajasthan Sales Tax Act, 1944, Assam Taxation (On Goods Carried By Road Or Inland Waterways) Act, 1961, West Bengal Sales Tax Rules, 1994

Summary: West Bengal Sales Tax Act, 1994 - Ss 2(6), 11 and 14(4) - West Bengal Sales Tax Rules, 1994 - Rules 172, 173, 174, 188 and 189 - constitutional validity of - held, the validity of the impugned provisions and rules is upheld - orders of the Tribunal set aside.
