Indian Handicrafts Emporium and Others v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

27 August 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Y.K. Sabharwal

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 721; (2003) 7 SCC 589; AIR 2003 SC 3240; 2003 (6) ALD(SC) 39; 2003 (5) AWC 4251; 2003 (106) DLT 350; JT 2003 (7) SC 446; 2003(6) SCALE 831; 2003 (5) SLT 289; [2003] Supp3 S.C.R. 43; 2003 (6) Supreme 439

Case Digest

Subject: Environment, Wildlife & Animal

Keywords: Endangered Species, Wild Life (Protection) Amendment Act, 1991, Wild Birds and Animals Protection Act, 1912, Bombay Prohibition Act 1949, Wild Life (Protection) Amendment Act, 2002, Wild Birds Protection Act, 1887, United Nations Covenant on Civil and Political Rights, 1966 (ENG)

Summary: (1) Wild Life (Protection) Act, 1972-Section 49C (as inserted by Act No. 44 of 1991)-Constitution of India-Articles 14 and 19 (1) (g)-Section 49C as inserted by 1991 Amending Act imposing total prohibition in trade of imported ivory-Whether offending Articles 19 (1) (g) and 14?-Held, “no”-Amendments brought for purpose of saving endangered species from extinction.
