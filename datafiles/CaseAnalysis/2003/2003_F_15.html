Commissioner of Income Tax v Foramer France

Supreme Court of India

16 January 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Srikrishna, Ruma Pal

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 1243; 2003 (185) CTR 512; [2003] 264 ITR 566; [2003] 129 TAXMAN 72

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Assessment

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income-tax Act, 1961 - Assessment - Impugned order - Instant appeals were filed against order passed by HC - Whether impugned order passed by HC was justified.

Held, SC heard counsel for parties and considered facts of case. SC saw no reason to interfere with decision of HC. Appeals dismissed.
