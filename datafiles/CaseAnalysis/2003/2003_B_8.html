Commandant, 11th Battalion A.P. Special Police (IR), Cuddapath, Cuddapah District v B. Shankar Naik

Supreme Court of India

25 April 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Shivaraj V. Patil

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 359; (2003) 5 SCC 580; (2003) SCC (L&S) 731; AIR 2003 SC 2249; 2003 (3) ALD(SC) 117; 2003 (3) ESC 287; 2003 (97) FLR 912; JT 2003 (4) SC 268; 2003 LabIC 2158; 2003 (3) LLN 1; 2003(4) SCALE 308; [2003] 3 S.C.R. 905; 2003 (2) SLJ 391; 2003 (3) SLR 447; 2003 (4) SLT 219; 2003 (3) Supreme 515; 2003 (2) UPLBEC 1693

Case Digest

Subject: Defence & Security Forces; Service

Keywords: Eligibility Conditions

Summary: Service - A.P. State and Subordinate Services Rules, 1962 - R. 13-A(a)(i) - A.P. Police Subordinate Service Rules - R. 11(a) - recruitment - training - dismissal - challenged - Held, when the recruit was to undertake ten months basic training, it obviously means successful completion of the training. Undisputedly the same has not been done. The authorities were justified in passing the order of discharge.
