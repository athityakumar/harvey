Commissioner Of Central Excise, Guntur v Asian Peroxide Ltd.

Supreme Court of India

06 May 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

Ruma Pal, B.N. Srikrishna

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 1097; 2003 (155) E.L.T. 431

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excise Act, 1944, s. 35C - Scope of Appeal - Rate of duty - Calculation of the duty - Subject matter of the show cause notice as well as the proceeding before the departmental authorities was limited strictly to the valuation of the goods clear to the DTA - Relied on Fabworth (India) case [2002 Indlaw CEGAT 474] - Parties to raise all issues as regards the valuation as well as the rate and calculation of duty - Appeal disposed off.
