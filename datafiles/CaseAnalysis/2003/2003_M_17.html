M. Anasuya Devi and Another v M. Manik Reddy and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 October 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 874; (2003) 8 SCC 565; 2004 (54) ALR 431; 2003 (52) ARBLR 404; 2003 (6) AWC 5296; JT 2003 (8) SC 276; 2004 (1) MLJ(SC) 59; 2003 (3) RAJ 500; 2003 (4) RCR(Civil) 763; 2003(9) SCALE 12; 2003 (6) SLT 537; [2003] Supp4 S.C.R. 853; 2003 (7) Supreme 466

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Summary: Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996 - ss. 10, 34(1), 36, 37(1)(b) - Partition - Joint Hindu properties - Dispute referred to Arbitral Tribunal - Whether the Award is required to be stamped and registered - Held, the question whether an Award requires stamping and registration is within the ambit of s. 47, CPC, 1908, and not covered by s. 34 of the Act - Matter remitted to the High Court.
