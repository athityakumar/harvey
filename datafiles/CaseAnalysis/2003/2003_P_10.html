Collector and others v P. Mangamma and others

Supreme Court of India

28 February 2003

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat

Where Reported

2003 Indlaw SC 212; (2003) 4 SCC 488; AIR 2003 SC 1706; 2003 (4) ALD(SC) 86; JT 2003 (2) SC 286; 2003(2) SCALE 499; [2003] 2 S.C.R. 430; 2003 (4) SLT 216; 2003 (2) Supreme 393

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Andhra Pradesh Assigned Lands (Prohibition of Transfers) Act, 1977, Andhra Pradesh (Telangana Area) Land Revenue Act, 1950, Special Loani Rules

Summary: Andhra Pradesh Assigned Lands (Prohibition of Transfers) Act, 1977 - Andhra Pradesh (Telangana Area) Land Revenue Act, 1950 - S. 166(B) - respondents questioned correctness of the orders passed by the Collector - held, even jurisdictional questions can be considered by an authority deciding the question whether proceeding was validly initiated or not - non-applicability of the Prohibition Act not considered by the Division Bench - matter remitted back to the High Court for a fresh consideration by the Division Bench.
