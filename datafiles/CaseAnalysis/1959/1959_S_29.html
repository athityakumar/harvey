Shri B. P. Hira, Works Manager, Central Railway v Shri C. M. Pradhan Etc

Supreme Court of India

08 May 1959

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, Bhuvaneshwar Prasad Sinha, Kailas Nath Wanchoo

Where Reported

1959 Indlaw SC 101; AIR 1959 SC 1226; 1959 (2) LLJ 397; [1960] 1 S.C.R. 137

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Mines & Minerals; Practice & Procedure; Contract & Commercial

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Practice and Procedure - Factories Act, 1948, ss. 2(1), 2, 2(m), 59 and 70 - Payment of Wages Act, 1936 - Bombay Shops and Establishments Act, 1948, ss. 70 and 63 - (A)Whether 'factory' is included in expression 'establishment' u/s. 2(3) of Bombay Shops and Establishments Act, 1948? - Held, no, though definition of 'establishment' is wide enough, it does not include factory for purposes of Act - (B) Whether s. 4 governs application of s. 70 of Bombay Shops and Establishments Act, 1948? - Further held, s. 4 would not affect application of s. 70 of Act - Object underlying s. 70 is to emphatically point out that it will operate independently of other provisions of Act and in that sense it stands apart from them - Appeal dismissed.
