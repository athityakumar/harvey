Chaganlal Textile Mills Private, Limited v Chalisgaon Girni Kamgar Union

Supreme Court of India

31 March 1959

Case Analysis

Bench

A.K. Sarkar

Where Reported

1959 Indlaw SC 7; AIR 1959 SC 722; 1959 (2) LLJ 1

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Bombay Industrial Relations Act, 1947, s. 42, Chap. II, item. 1 - Whether validity of terminating services of employees can be tested by reference to s. 42, Bombay Industrial Relations Act? - Held, item. 1 of Sch. II and s. 42 of Act is concerned only with posts - When, therefore, an employer does not desire to reduce posts but only to retrench certain employees holding certain posts, no question of giving a notice of change as mentioned in s. 42 of Act arises - Notice served on employees terminating their services is not a notice of change and its validity cannot be tested by reference to s. 42 of Act - Appeal allowed.
