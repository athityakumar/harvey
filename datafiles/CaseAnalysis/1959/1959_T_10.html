Trustees of The Charity Fund, Esplanade Road, Fort, Bomba v Commissioner of Income Tax, Bombay

Supreme Court of India

05 May 1959

Case Analysis

Bench

Sudhi Ranjan Das, Natwarlal Harilal Bhagwati, Mohammad Hidayatullah

Where Reported

1959 Indlaw SC 80; AIR 1959 SC 1060; [1959] 36 ITR 513; 1961 (1) SCJ 477; [1959] 2 S.C.R. 923

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Indian Income Tax Act, 1922, s.4(3)(i) - Income of charitable Trust - Exemption - Appellants were trustees of a charity fund - X and 4 other persons were holding securities for purpose of charity and had been applying same for and towards charitable purposes - Respondent charged income tax and on appeal to Tribunal order was confirmed which was further affirmed by HC - Hence, instant appeal - Whether Trust property exempted from Income tax within s. 4(3)(i) of the Act - Held, provision for giving preference to poor and indigent relations or members of the family of X cannot affect public charitable trust constituted - Income from trust properties comes within scope of s. 4(3)(i) of the Act and hence entitled to exemption - Appeal allowed.
