Divisional Superintendent, Eastern Railway, Dinapur and Others v Sri L. N. Keshri and Others

Supreme Court of India

06 August 1974

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew

Where Reported

1974 Indlaw SC 15; (1974) SCC (L&S) 435; (1975) 3 SCC 1; AIR 1974 SC 1889; 1974 LabIC 1275; 1974 (2) LLJ 372

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution, art. 311 - Service matter - Pay scale fixed and employee confirmed - Subsequent reduction of pay scale without hearing him - Legality - Reduction held, is illegal - On confirmation employee becomes entitled to rights to the post and to the scale of pay fixed - Appeal Dismissed.
