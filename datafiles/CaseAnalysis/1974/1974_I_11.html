Isha Valimohamad & Anr. v Haji Gulam Mohamad & Haji Dada Trust

Supreme Court of India

14 August 1974

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, A.N. Ray

Where Reported

1974 Indlaw SC 214; (1974) 2 SCC 484; AIR 1974 SC 2061; [1975] 1 S.C.R. 720; 1974 UJ 521

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Bombay Rents, Hotel and Lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947, s. 51, Proviso (1)(ii) and (2) - Saurashtra Rent Control Act, 1951, ss. 13(1)(e) and 15 - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 111 - Sub-letting before 01-01-1964 - Notice to terminate not given - Suit for eviction on the ground of sub-letting after 01-01-1964 - Maintainability - Held, maintainable - Appeal Dismissed.
