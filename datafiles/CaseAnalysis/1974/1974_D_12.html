Maruti Bala Raut v Dashrath Babu Wathare & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

27 August 1974

Case Analysis

Bench

A. Alagiriswami, P. Jaganmohan Reddy, M. Hameedullah Beg

Where Reported

1974 Indlaw SC 130; (1974) 2 SCC 615; AIR 1974 SC 2051; [1975] 1 S.C.R. 899

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure; Agriculture & Agri. Products

Summary: Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands Act, 1948, s. 76 - Constitution , art. 227 - (A) Revision - Findings of fact - Whether Revenue Tribunal can appreciate evidence - (B) Petition u/art. 227 of Constitution - Jurisdiction of High Court - Appreciation of evidence - Interference with finding of fact - Tribunal held, has no power to appreciate evidence and come to its own conclusion - Held, order of the Court could not be sustained - Appeal Allowed.
