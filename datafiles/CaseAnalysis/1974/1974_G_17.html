Golam Alias Golam Mallick v State of West Bengal

Supreme Court of India

12 September 1974

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Sarkaria, P. Jaganmohan Reddy, P.K. Goswami, P.N. Bhagwati

Where Reported

1974 Indlaw SC 495; (1975) 2 SCC 4; (1975) SCC (Cr) 370; AIR 1976 SC 754; 1976 CRLJ 630

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Maintenance Of Internal Security Act, 1971, ss. 3 and 8 - Detention under MISA, 1971 - Detenu not furnished with all grounds - Was not able to make effective representation - Whether detention of detenu is legal? - Solitary ground communicated to detenu was so truncated and shorn of material particulars on which District Magistrate had based order of detention, that detenu was disabled to make an effective representation - Held, detention was illegal as it is violative of art. 22(5) of Constitution - Petition allowed.
