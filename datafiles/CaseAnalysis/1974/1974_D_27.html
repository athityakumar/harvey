Dhan Singh Ramkrishna Chaudhri & Ors. v Laxminarayan Ramkishan & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

16 April 1974

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Sarkaria, M. Hameedullah Beg

Where Reported

1974 Indlaw SC 228; (1974) 2 SCC 293; AIR 1974 SC 1613; [1975] 1 S.C.R. 94; 1974 UJ 382

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands Act, 1948, s. 25(2) - Tenancy - Interpretation of expression 'If he has failed for any 3 years to pay rent within the period specified in s. 14' - Application for eviction - When rent due did not exceed 2 years' rent - Applicability of s. 25(2) - Held, partial default will also be a failure - Appeal dismissed .
