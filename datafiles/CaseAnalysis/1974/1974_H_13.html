Harihar Prasad Singh and Others v Balmiki Prasad Singh and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 December 1974

Case Analysis

Bench

A. Alagiriswami, M. Hameedullah Beg, V.R. Krishna Iyer

Where Reported

1974 Indlaw SC 234; (1975) 1 SCC 212; AIR 1975 SC 733; [1975] 2 S.C.R. 932

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Practice & Procedure - Civil Procedure Code, 1908, O. 22, r. 3, O. 41, rr. 4 and 33 - Whether abatement which ex concessis took place, as regards one of respondents should be confined to share of deceased respondent as against whom appeal has abated, or it would result in abatement of entire appeal? - Held, abatement of entire appeal would depend on nature of decree and nature of interest of deceased in property, if decree is joint and indivisible, abatement would be total - Appeals dismissed.
