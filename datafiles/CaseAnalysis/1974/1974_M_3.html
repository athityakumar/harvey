M. P. Peria Karuppan Chettiar and another v Commissioner of Income Tax, Wealth Tax, Gift Tax, Madras

Supreme Court of India

19 November 1974

Case Analysis

Bench

A.C. Gupta, Hans Raj Khanna

Where Reported

1974 Indlaw SC 498; (1975) 3 SCC 439; AIR 1975 SC 431; 1975 (4) CTR 15; [1975] 99 ITR 1; 1975 (2) MLJ(SC) 3; 1975 UJ 100

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 2(31) - Income Tax Act, 1961 - Gift - Donor donating property out of love and affection to donees (sons), their respective heirs, executors and assigns - (A) Deed - Construction of - Matters to be considered - (B) Status of assessee - Held, gilt was to sons absolutely and not as heads of their respective HUFs - Interest transferred was not limited in any way but was absolute to sons - Appeals dismissed.
