Union Of India & Anr. v M/S. Parameswaran Match Works Etc.

Supreme Court of India

04 November 1974

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, A.N. Ray, N.L. Untwalia

Where Reported

1974 Indlaw SC 240; (1975) 1 SCC 305; AIR 1974 SC 2349; 1978 (2) E.L.T. 436; [1975] 2 S.C.R. 573; 1975 TAXLR 1223

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Excise - Central Excise Rules, 1944, r. 8(1) - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 14 - Notification No. 205 of 04-09-1967 issue u/ r. 8(1) - Constitutionality of cl. (b) providing for concessional rate of duty on match boxes only to those manufacturers who have filed declaration, as required by proviso, before 04-09-1967 - Choice of a date for granting concessional rate of duty - If discriminatory - Held, is not violative of art. 14 choice of a date as a basis for classification cannot always be dubbed as arbitrary - Appeals allowed.
