Sri K. Ramadas Shenoy v Chief Officers, Town Municipal Council Udipi and Others

Supreme Court of India

09 August 1974

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew

Where Reported

1974 Indlaw SC 371; (1974) 2 SCC 506; AIR 1974 SC 2177; [1975] 1 S.C.R. 680; 1974 UJ 553

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments; Tort

Summary: Municipalities & Local Govts - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 226 - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 115 - Karnataka Municipalities Act, 1964, s. 256 - Tort - Breach of statutory duty - Suit for damages - Proof - Constitution - Municipality giving sanction to construct cinema building in contravention of Town Planning Scheme - Impeached resolution of Municipality - Exercise of statutory power - Validation - Held, plaintiff must establish a breach of a statutory obligation which, on proper construction of statute was intended to be a ground of civil liability to a class of persons of whom he is one and he must establish an injury or damage of a kind against which statute was designed to give protection - Impeached resolution of Municipality quashed - Not permissible - Appeal allowed.
