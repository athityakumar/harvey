Amarjit Kaur v Pritam Singh & Others

Supreme Court of India

06 August 1974

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, A.N. Ray

Where Reported

1974 Indlaw SC 262; (1974) 2 SCC 363; AIR 1974 SC 2068; [1975] 1 S.C.R. 605

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: CPC, 1908. O. 41, r. 27 - Punjab Pre-emption (Repeal) Act, 1973, s. 3 - Bar to pass decree - Appeal against pre-emption decree - Act coming into force during pendency of appeal - Held, Appellate Court cannot dismiss appeal as it would amount to passing of decree - Appeal dismissed.
