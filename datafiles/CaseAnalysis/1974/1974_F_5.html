Fida Ali And Others v State Of Jammu And Kashmir

Supreme Court of India

30 April 1974

Case Analysis

Bench

P.K. Goswami, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, A. Alagiriswami, R.S. Sarkaria

Where Reported

1974 Indlaw SC 213; (1974) 2 SCC 253; AIR 1974 SC 1522; [1975] 1 S.C.R. 340; 1974 UJ 411

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Municipalities & Local Governments; Socio-Economic

Keywords: Jammu and Kashmir Agrarian Reforms Act 1972

Summary: J. and K. Agrarian Reforms Act, 1972, Pre. - Constitution, art. 31A - Constitutional validity of 1972 Act - Held, Act relates to agrarian reforms and is protected from challenge u/arts. 14, 19 and 31 by virtue of art. 31-A of Constitution - Application Dismissed.
