Sinclaire Murray and Company Private Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax, Calcutta

Supreme Court of India

06 November 1974

Case Analysis

Bench

Hans Raj Khanna, A.C. Gupta

Where Reported

1974 Indlaw SC 108; (1975) 3 SCC 521; AIR 1975 SC 198; 1974 CTR 283; [1974] 97 ITR 615; [1975] 2 S.C.R. 629; 1975 (35) STC 142; 1975 TAXLR 103

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure; Sales Tax

Keywords: Trader, Collected, Orissa Sales Tax Act 1947

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 4 - Income -Trading receipt - Assessee receiving sales tax from purchaser of goods but neither crediting same to Govt. nor returning it to purchaser - Held, sales tax so retained is his trading receipt liable to be included in his total income - Appeal dismissed.
