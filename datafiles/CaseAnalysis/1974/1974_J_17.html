Jehan Singh v Delhi Administration

Supreme Court of India

27 March 1974

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Sarkaria, M. Hameedullah Beg

Where Reported

1974 Indlaw SC 403; (1974) 4 SCC 522; (1974) SCC (Cr) 558; AIR 1974 SC 1146; 1974 CRLJ 802; 1974 CRLR 426; [1974] 3 S.C.R. 794; 1974 UJ 401

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: CrPC, 1898, s. 561A - Inherent jurisdiction to quash proceedings - If can be exercised at the stage of investigation by Police - Held, Court cannot, in exercise of its inherent jurisdiction u/s. 561-A, interfere with the statutory powers of Police - Appeal Dismissed.
