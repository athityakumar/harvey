Municipal Corporation Of Greater Bombay v M/S Polychem Ltd.

Supreme Court of India

20 March 1974

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Hameedullah Beg, Y.V. Chandrachud

Where Reported

1974 Indlaw SC 230; (1974) 2 SCC 198; AIR 1974 SC 1779; [1974] 3 S.C.R. 687

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Annual Value, Rental, Annual Rental, Letting Value, Hypothetical, Calcutta Municipal Act, 1923

Summary: Bombay Municipal Corporation Act, 1888, ss. 3(r) and 154(1) - Land and Property - Method of valuation of land on which building is being constructed - Retcable - Applicability of doctrine of sterility - Doctrine of sterility is not applicable - Appeal allowed.
