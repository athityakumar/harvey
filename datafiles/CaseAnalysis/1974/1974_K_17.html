Saligram Ruplal Khanna & Anr v Kanwar Rajnath

Supreme Court of India

01 May 1974

Case Analysis

Bench

Hans Raj Khanna, M. Hameedullah Beg, Y.V. Chandrachud

Where Reported

1974 Indlaw SC 33; (1974) 2 SCC 642; AIR 1974 SC 1094; [1975] 1 S.C.R. 358

Case Digest

Subject: Trusts & Associations

Keywords: Administration of Evacuee Property Act, 1950, Displaced Persons (Claims) Act, 1950

Summary: Limitation Act, 1908, art. 106 - Partnership Act, 1932, ss. 42 and 47 - (A) Suit for rendition of accounts - Limitation - (B) Interpretation of Statutes - Meaning of transaction - In the light s. 47 of the 1932 Act - Held, was barred by limitation, the period of limitation being three years from the date of dissolution - Includes matters relating to affairs of partnership - Appeal Dismissed.
