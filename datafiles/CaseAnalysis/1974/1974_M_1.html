M. A. Rasheed and Others v State of Kerala

Supreme Court of India

18 September 1974

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, V.R. Krishna Iyer

Where Reported

1974 Indlaw SC 215; (1974) 2 SCC 687; AIR 1974 SC 2249; [1975] 2 S.C.R. 93; 1974 UJ 611

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Municipalities & Local Governments; Socio-Economic

Keywords: Quantity

Summary: Criminal - Constitution - Defence Of India Act, 1971, ss. 3(2)(21) and 38 - Defence of India Rules, 1971, rr. 114(2) and 114 - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 14, 30, 166, 226 and 302 - Kerala Govt. issued notification SRO No 474-173 D/- 26-07-1973 - Whether r. 114 of Rules is in consonance with powers u/s. 3(2)(21) of 1971 Act - Notification prohibiting extraction of fiber from Coconut husks by machinery only in three out of eleven districts - Whether violative of s. 38 of Act - Constitutionality of notification - Extent of judicial review of administrative decisions - Held, r. 114 is in complete consonance with powers conferred u/s. 3(2)(21) and Rules under Act have legislative sanction - Not violative of s. 38 of Act - Does not offend arts. 14 and 301 of Constitution - When 'they are satisfied' or when 'it appears to them' or when 'in their opinion' a certain state of affairs exists, or when powers enable public authorities to take 'such action as they think fit' in relation to a subject matter, courts will not readily defer to conclusiveness of an executive authority's opinion - Appeal dismissed.
