Jacky v Tiny @ Antony and others

Supreme Court of India

09 April 2014

Case Analysis

Bench

Sudhansu Jyoti Mukhopadhaya, Sharad Arvind Bobde

Where Reported

2014 Indlaw SC 237; (2014) 6 SCC 508; AIR 2014 SC 1615; 2014 (4) ALD(SC) 121; 2014 (3) AWC 2963; 2014 (2) KLT 303; 2014 (3) MLJ 764; 2014 (2) RCR(Rent) 3; 2014 (3) RLW 2364; 2014(4) SCALE 597; [2014] 5 S.C.R. 85; 2014 (2) WLN(SC) 59

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: (1) Constitution of India-Articles 226 and 227-Writ petition to quash plaint-Maintainability-Whether High Court while exercising its power under Articles 226 and 227 is competent to set aside plaint?-Held, "no"-Power under Articles 226 and 227 cannot be exercised to question plaint-Impugned judgment and order of High Court-Set aside.

(2) Constitution of India-Articles 226 and 227-Writ petition in matter of landlord-tenant dispute-Maintainability-Petition under Article 226 or 227 can neither be entertained to decide landlord-tenant dispute nor it is maintainable against private individual to determine intense dispute including question whether one party harassing other party-Impugned judgment and order of High Court-Set aside.
