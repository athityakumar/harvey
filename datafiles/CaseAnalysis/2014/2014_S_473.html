Sobran Singh v State of Uttar Pradesh and others

Supreme Court of India

23 September 2014

Case Analysis

Bench

T.S. Thakur, C. Nagappan, Adarsh Kumar Goel

Where Reported

2014 Indlaw SC 911; (2014) 10 SCC 799; 2015 (108) ALR 651; 2014 (6) AWC 6458; 2015 (3) BC 64; 2015 (2) DRTC 305; JT 2014 (13) SC 199; 2014 (4) RCR(Civil) 933; 2014 (125) RD 608; 2014(11) SCALE 520; [2014] 10 S.C.R. 657

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance

Keywords: Pledge, Uttar Pradesh Public Moneys (Recovery of Dues) Act, 1972

Summary: U. P. Public Moneys (Recovery of Dues) Act, 1972-Sections 3 and 4-Recovery of outstanding loan amount-Recovery from guarantor-Sale of immovable property of borrower mortgaged, charged or encumbered alleged to be condition precedent-Held, protection under Section 4 (2) (b) is confined only to the principal debtor who has charged his properties-Guarantors do not enjoy any protection against recovery proceedings under Section 4 (2) of the Act.
