Demerara Distilleries Private Limited and others v Demerara Distilleries Limited

Supreme Court of India

24 November 2014

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranjan Gogoi

Where Reported

2014 Indlaw SC 925; 2015 (1) AWC 207; 2015 (2) CompLJ 39; 2015 (1) RCR(Civil) 425

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Summary: Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996-Section 11 (6)-Appointment of Arbitrator-Objection of respondent-company that prayer for appointment of Arbitrator under Section 11 (6) not maintainable at the instance of petitioners-Held-Though petition under Section 11 (6) may not be maintainable at the instance of first petitioner, there is no reason to doubt maintainability thereof at the instance of petitioner Nos. 2, 3 and 4-No merit in contention that if two or more parties join in an action, dismissal of action qua one petitioner entails dismissal qua others-Objection raised by respondent-company-Fail-Former Judge of Supreme Court appointed as sole Arbitrator.
