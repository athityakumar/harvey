Bhule Ram and another v Union of India and another

Supreme Court of India

28 March 2014

Case Analysis

Bench

Balbir Singh Chauhan, J. Chelameswar

Where Reported

2014 Indlaw SC 203; (2014) 11 SCC 307; AIR 2014 SC 1957; 2014 (3) AWC 2366; 2014 (2) CLT(SC) 210; JT 2014 (5) SC 110; 2014 (3) Law Herald (P&H) 1941; 2014 (124) RD 713; 2014 (4) RLW 3255; 2014(4) SCALE 349; 2014(4) SCALE 357; [2014] 4 S.C.R. 247; [2014] 4 S.C.R. 262

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: 1) Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (since repealed)-Section 23 (Sections 28, 30 and 69 of New Act, 2013)-Compensation for land ac-quisition-Determination of market value of land-Law summarised-Market value of land to be assessed keeping in mind limitation prescribed in certain exceptional circumstances under Section 23-Guess work, though allowed, is permissible only to limited extent.

(2) Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (since repealed)-Sections 23 and 9 (Sections 21, 28, 30 and 69 of New Act, 2013)-Compensation for land acquisition-Determination of market value-Duty of claimant that he must produce relevant evidence for deter-mining market value while filing his claim under Section 9 at least before trial court or before reference court for reason that appellate court may not permit party to adduce additional evidence in appeal.
