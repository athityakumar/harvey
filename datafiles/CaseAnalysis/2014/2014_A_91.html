Sanjay Kumar v Ashok Kumar and another

Supreme Court of India

24 January 2014

Case Analysis

Bench

V. Gopala Gowda, Sudhansu Jyoti Mukhopadhaya

Where Reported

2014 Indlaw SC 47; (2014) 5 SCC 330; 2014 (1) ACC 372; 2014 ACJ 653; 2014 (104) ALR 236; 2014 (2) AWC 1893; JT 2014 (3) SC 237; 2014 (2) Law Herald (P&H) 1120; 2014 (174) PLR 335; 2014 (1) RCR(Civil) 875; 2014(1) SCALE 584

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation

Keywords: Enhancement of compensation, Compensation

Summary: Motor Vehicles Act, 1988-Sections 166 and 168-Compensation-Enhancement-Injury case-Appellant suffered permanent disability due to amputation of right leg above knee-Appellant employed as embroidery worker-Loss of earning capacity assessed at 70% due to permanent disability suffered by appellant-Tribunal awarded compensation of R 4,83,472 alongwith interest @ 7% p.a.-High Court enhanced compensation awarded by Tribunal by R 1,52,336, i.e. total sum of R 6,35,808-Appellant entitled to R14,59,100 alongwith interest @ % p.a. as total compensation instead of R 6,35,808 as awarded by High Court-Compensation amount thus further enhanced by sum of R 8,23,292-Just and proper to increase interest awarded from 7% to 9% p.a.-Impugned judgment and order of High Court-Modified.
