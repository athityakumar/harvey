State of Madhya Pradesh and another v Suresh Narayan Vijayvargiya and others

Supreme Court of India

27 February 2014

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Panicker Radhakrishnan, Sharad Arvind Bobde, Balbir Singh Chauhan

Where Reported

2014 Indlaw SC 127; (2014) 11 SCC 694; 2014 (2) ALD(SC) 142; 2014 (104) ALR 265; 2014 (4) AWC 3808; 2014 (2) JabLJ 90; JT 2014 (3) SC 468; 2014 (3) RCR(Civil) 387; 2014 (3) RCR(Criminal) 251; 2014 (123) RD 804; 2014(3) SCALE 124; [2014] 3 S.C.R. 374; 2014 (6) SLR 335

Case Digest

Subject: Education; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Contempt, Interim Order, Madhya Pradesh Niji Vyavsayik Shikshan Sanstha (Pravesh Ka Viniyaman Avam Shulk Ka Nirdharan) Adhiniyam, 2007, Madhya Pradesh Niji Vishwavidyalaya (Sthapana Avam Sanchalan) Adhiniyam, 2007, Madhya Pradesh (Admission and Fee Regulatory Committee) Act, 2007, Private University Act, 2007, Sharing and fixation of quota of seats for MBBS/BDS, Admitting students over and above the sanctioned strength

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Education - Madhya Pradesh (Admission and Fee Regulatory Committee) Act, 2007 - Madhya Pradesh Niji Vishwavidyala (Sthapana Avam Sanchalan) Adhiniyam, 2007 - Sharing and fixation of quota of seats for MBBS/BDS - Admitting students over and above the sanctioned strength - Interim order - Contempt - Respondents (Contemnors) challenged the validity of 2007 Act, empowering the State Govt. (petitioner) to fill all the seats (including the NRI seats) in all the education institutions in the State, including private medical and dental collages - Since serious disputes were raised with regard to seat sharing and fixation of quota of seats for MBBS/BDS, SC felt that some interim arrangement should be made taking note of the interest of both the parties and also that of students - SC, thus, as an interim measure, passed an order on 27-5-2009 in said civil appeal - However, respondents have filled up the entire 150 seats available for the year 2011-2012, without sharing it with State Govt., violating orders of SC dt.27-5-2009 and 27-1-2011 - Hence, instant contempt petition - Petitioners contended that the contemnors had sent a letter stating that they would fill up the entire seats during the academic year 2011-2012 since their colleges would be functioning under the 2007 Adhiniyam - Whether contemnors have violated the interim orders dt.27-5-2009 and 27-1-2011 passed by SC in the matter of sharing of MBBS seats between the respondent private medical college and State Govt. -

Held, in facts, it was found that there was a willful disobedience by contemnors of the orders passed by SC, which was nothing but interference with the administration of justice - Disobedience of an order of a Court, which was willful, shakes the very foundation of the judicial system and could erode the faith and confidence reposed by people in the Judiciary and undermines rule of law - Contemnors have shown scant respect to the orders passed by the highest Court of the land and depicted undue haste to fill up the entire seats evidently not to attract better students or recognize merit, but possibly to make unlawful gain, adopting unhealthy practices, as noticed by SC in TMA Pai Foundation & Ors. v. State of Karnataka & Ors, 2002 Indlaw SC 1375 and various other cases - Contemnors tendered unconditional and unqualified apology and volunteered to set right the illegality committed by them, but the purpose for flouting the orders has been achieved, that was the contemnors wanted to fill up the entire seats by themselves - Thus, to maintain the sanctity of the orders of SC and to give a message that parties could not get away by merely tendering an unconditional and unqualified apology after enjoying the fruits of their illegality, Court was inclined to impose a fine at Rs.50 lakhs - Admission of students under the State quota for academic year 2011-12 in Medical College was valid and legal and appropriate steps should be taken by State Govt. and Medical Council of India to regularize the admission - Excess 107 admissions made by Medical College for the MBBS during the year 2011-12 and the previous year, be adjusted in the session 2014-15 in full taking note of the full sanctioned strength and the balance seats be adjusted in year 2015-16 - Petition disposed of.
