Sita Ram v State of Haryana and another

Supreme Court of India

25 November 2014

Case Analysis

Bench

V. Gopala Gowda, Adarsh Kumar Goel

Where Reported

2014 Indlaw SC 796; (2015) 3 SCC 597; 2014 (6) ALD(SC) 218; 2015 (110) ALR 534; 2015 (1) AWC 74; 2014 (4) CLT(SC) 333; 2015 (2) CTC 568; JT 2014 (13) SC 234; 2015 (1) Law Herald (P&H) 658; 2015 (6) MahLJ 62; 2015 (4) MPLJ 263; 2015 (1) RCR(Civil) 255; 2015 (128) RD 422

Case Digest

Subject: Administrative; Land & Property

Keywords: Explosives Rules, 1983

Summary: Land & Property - Administrative - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 5A - Right to Fair Compensation and Transparency in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2013, s. 24(2) - Land acquisition proceedings - Influx of time - Appellant was running a fireworks manufacturing unit - Respondent authority issued notification acquiring appellant's land - Appellant sought to release their acquired land by raising objection u/s. 5A of the Act - Respondent rejected objection - Appellant filed writ petition - HC dismissed writ petition - Hence, instant appeal.

Held, award was passed on 8-10-2003 and five years have elapsed long back and the compensation was not paid within 5 years to the appellant. The conditions mentioned in s. 24(2) of the Act are satisfied by the appellant for allowing the plea as stated by him that the land acquisition proceedings in respect of his acquired land and building must be deemed to have lapsed in terms of s. 24(2) of the Act. Appeal disposed of.
