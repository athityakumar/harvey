D. D. Tewari (D) Through Lrs. v Uttar Haryana Bijli Vitran Nigam Limited and others

Supreme Court of India

01 August 2014

Case Analysis

Bench

V. Gopala Gowda, Dipak Misra

Where Reported

2014 Indlaw SC 496; (2014) 8 SCC 894; AIR 2014 SC 2861; 2014 (5) AWC 4746; 2014 (143) FLR 157; JT 2014 (10) SC 104; 2015 (1) OLR 81; 2014 (4) RLW 2950; 2014(9) SCALE 78; 2014 (5) SLR 721; 2014 (3) WLN(SC) 136

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Service - Payment of Gratuity Act, 1972 - Awarding interest - Delayed payment of pension - Appellant retired from service on attaining the age of superannuation - Retiral benefits of the appellant were withheld by the respondents on the alleged ground that some amount was due to the employer - Disciplinary proceedings were not pending against the appellant on the date of his retirement - Writ petition was filed seeking for issuance of a direction to the respondents regarding payment of pension and release of the gratuity amount which were retiral benefits with an interest at the rate of 18 percent on the delayed payments - Single Judge directed the respondents to release the withheld amount of gratuity without awarding interest as claimed by the appellant - Appeal filed thereagainst was dismissed - Hence, instant appeal - Whether respondents erroneously withheld payment of gratuity amount -

Held, respondents have erroneously withheld payment of gratuity amount for which the appellant was entitled in law for payment of penal amount on the delayed payment of gratuity under the provisions of the Act - Hence, there was a miscarriage of justice in denying the interest to be paid or payable by the employer from the date of the entitlement of the deceased employee till the date of payment - Interest awarded at the rate of 9 percent p.a. both on the amount of pension due and the gratuity amount which were to be paid by the respondent - Appeal allowed.
