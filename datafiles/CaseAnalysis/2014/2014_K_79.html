Praful Manohar Rele v Krishnabai Narayan Ghosalkar and others

Supreme Court of India

03 January 2014

Case Analysis

Bench

T.S. Thakur, Vikramajit Sen

Where Reported

2014 Indlaw SC 2; (2014) 11 SCC 316; 2014 (2) ALD(SC) 149; 2014 (102) ALR 660; 2014 (1) AWC 585; 2014 (2) Bom.C.R. 100; 2014 (1) CTC 402; JT 2014 (1) SC 405; 2014 (2) Law Herald (P&H) 1185; 2014 (1) RCR(Civil) 851; 2014 (1) RCR(Rent) 48; 2014 (122) RD 528; 2014 (2) RLW 1438; 2014(1) SCALE 67; [2014] 1 S.C.R. 44; 2014 (2) WLN(SC) 95

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Occupation as licensee or tenant, Alternative pleas

Summary: Suit-For possession of suit property-Primary case pleaded by plaintiff that defendants occupying suit premises as gratuitous licensees upon termination whereof plaintiff entitled to decree for possession-Alternative case set up by plaintiff on plea of tenancy-Whether plea of licence and tenancy could be together urged by plaintiff for grant of relief in suit for possession?-Held, "yes"-Whether alternative case set up by plaintiff in plaint contradictory to primary case pleaded by him?-Held, "no"-Alternative plea of plaintiff and defence set up by defendants was no different from each other-Case of defendants was that they were in occupation of suit premises not as licensees but as tenants-Plaintiff, therefore, entitled on that basis alone to ask for alternative relief of decree for eviction on grounds permissible under Rent Control Act-Such alternative plea did not fall foul-Impugned judgment of High Court-Set aside-Judgment of first appellate court-Restored.
