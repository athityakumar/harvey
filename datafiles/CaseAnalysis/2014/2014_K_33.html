Kalpanaraj and others v Tamil Nadu State Transport Corporation

Supreme Court of India

22 April 2014

Case Analysis

Bench

V. Gopala Gowda, Gyan Sudha Misra

Where Reported

2014 Indlaw SC 282; (2015) 2 SCC 764; 2014 (2) ACC 448; 2014 ACJ 1388; 2014(4) ALL MR 896; 2014 (3) AWC 2753; JT 2014 (7) SC 226; 2014 (3) Law Herald (P&H) 2577; 2014 (175) PLR 540; 2014 (2) RCR(Civil) 876; 2014(5) SCALE 479; [2014] 6 S.C.R. 577

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation

Keywords: Entitlement of

Summary: Motor Vehicles Act, 1988-Sections 166 and 168-Compensation-Death case-Deceased aged 46 years-Tribunal determined sum of Rs. 32,40,000 as compensation-However, Tribunal restricted sum of compensation to Rs. 20,90,000 since that was amount claimed by appellants-claimants-Tribunal awarded interest @ 12% p.a. on compensation amount-High Court awarded total compensation of Rs. 5,76,000-Interest rate reduced to 9% p.a. by High Court from 12% awarded by Tribunal-Whether compensation amount reduced by Tribunal calls for any interference?-Held, "yes"-Appellants-claimants awarded total sum of Rs. 14,51,000 as compensation alongwith interest @ 9% p.a. as awarded by High Court-Impugned judgment and order of High Court-Modified.
