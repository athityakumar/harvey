State Of Punjab v Devinder Kumar And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

07 April 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, Amarendra Nath Sen

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 374; (1983) 2 SCC 384; (1983) SCC (Cr) 501; AIR 1983 SC 545; 1983 (1) Crimes 1054; 1983 CRLJ 980; 1983 CRLR 222; 1983(1) SCALE 365; [1983] 2 S.C.R. 714

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection; Criminal; Health & Drug; Socio-Economic

Summary: Health and Drug - Prevention of Food Adulteration Act, 1954, ss. 7(i) and 11 - CrPC, 1973, s. 482 - Prevention of Food Adulteration Rules, 1955, rr. 22, 22A and 22B - (A) High Court quashing proceedings of prosecution for contravention of s. 7 (i) of Act - Validity of - Held, High Court committed a serious error in these cases in quashing the criminal proceeding in different Magistrates' courts at a premature stage in exercise of its extraordinary jurisdiction u/s.482, CrPC, 19783 - (B) Legality of taking sample by opening one of the sealed containers of vanaspati in possession for sale - Not illegal and sending of sealed container not necessary - Appeal allowed.
