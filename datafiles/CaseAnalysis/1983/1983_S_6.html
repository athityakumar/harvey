Mansaram v S. P. Pathak and Others

Supreme Court of India

29 September 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 148; (1984) 1 SCC 125; AIR 1983 SC 1239; 1984 MahLJ 47; 1984 (1) RCJ 130; 1984 (1) RCR(Rent) 15; 1983(2) SCALE 1027; [1984] 1 S.C.R. 139; 1983 UJ 1030

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: C.P. and Berar Letting of Houses and Rent Control Order, 1949, cls. 25, 22(2), 23 and 28 - Indian Limitation Act, 1908 - Tenancy - (A) Eviction - Ground, contravention of cl. 22(2) of 1949 Order - Tenant alleged that landlord assured him that premises were let out in accordance with cl. 23 (2) - No finding recorded that allegation was false - (B) Eviction of tenant holding an office of profit - (C) Initial unauthorised entry - Eviction after 22 years -Held, Order of eviction is vitiated - Existence of allotment order u/cl. 23 or 24A is necessary - Eviction cannot be ordered after 22 years, power u/cl. 28 must be exercised within reasonable time - Appeal Allowed.
