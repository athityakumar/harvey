Hindustan Aeronautics Limited v State Of Karnataka

Supreme Court of India

16 December 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, V.D. Tulzapurkar, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 296; (1984) 1 SCC 706; AIR 1984 SC 744; 1984 (1) CompLJ 157; 1983(2) SCALE 1090; [1984] 2 S.C.R. 248; 1984 (55) STC 314; 1984 TAXLR 2822; 1984 UJ 789

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Sales Tax; Contract & Commercial

Summary: Sales Tax - Central Sales Tax Act, 1956 - Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957- Appeals by special leave from the judgment of High Court involving the questions of assessability of the appellant to sales tax, Central as well as State - Appellant is a manufacturer of spare parts and accessories of various aircrafts and has also established facilities for assembling, serving, repairing, overhauling of aircrafts, their instruments and accessories - Issue regarding taxability of the turnover of the spare parts and materials supplied in execution of job works - Held, not taxable - Appeal allowed.
