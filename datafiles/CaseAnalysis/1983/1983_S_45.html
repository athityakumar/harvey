Sher Singh and Others v State of Punjab

Supreme Court of India

24 March 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, V.D. Tulzapurkar, A. Varadarajan

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 382; (1983) 2 SCC 344; (1983) SCC (Cr) 461; AIR 1983 SC 465; 1983 (1) Crimes 1017; 1983 CRLJ 803; 1983 CRLR 247; 1983 (2) MLJ 46; 1983 (2) MLJ(SC) 46; 1983(1) SCALE 283; [1983] 2 S.C.R. 582

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 21 - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, ss. 432 and 433 - Petition for commuting death sentence to life imprisonment - Execution was delayed for two years - Effect - Held, no hard and fast rule that death sentence will be quashed if delayed by two or more years and in meanwhile notice will go to Govt. of Punjab - Order accordingly.
