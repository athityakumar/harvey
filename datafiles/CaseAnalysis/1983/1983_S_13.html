Kanpur University And Others v Samir Gupta And Others

Supreme Court of India

27 September 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, R.S. Pathak, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 143; (1983) 4 SCC 309; AIR 1983 SC 1230; 1983(2) SCALE 89; [1984] 1 S.C.R. 73; 1983 UJ 991

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Education; Indirect Tax; Service

Keywords: Uttar Pradesh State Universities Act 1973

Summary: Constitution, art. 226 - Admission to Medical Colleges - CPMT - Error in key answer commotted by Paper setter - Although students answering correctly but not in accord with key answer - Held, they cannot be failed - Moreover, are entitled to admission on revaluation of their papers - Appeal Dismissed.
