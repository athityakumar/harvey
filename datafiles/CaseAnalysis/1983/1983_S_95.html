Kamlakar Prasad Chaturvedi v State Of M. P. And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

07 October 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, A. Varadarajan, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 432; (1983) 4 SCC 443; (1983) SCC (Cr) 848; AIR 1984 SC 211; 1984 (1) Crimes 326; 1983 CRLJ 1982; 1983 CRLR 626; 1983 CrLR(SC) 626; 1983(2) SCALE 729; [1984] 1 S.C.R. 317

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - National Security Act, 1980, s.3 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 32 - Writ petition u/art. 32 of the Constitution for quashing the Order of detention as being arbitrary and unreasonable and for the issue of a writ of habeas corpus directing the immediate release - Sustainability of detention u/s. 3(2) - Some grounds found too remote and not proximate - Detention held, unsustainable in law - Order accordingly.
