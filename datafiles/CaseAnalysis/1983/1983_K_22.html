Krothapalli Satyanarayana v Koganti Ramaiah and Others

Supreme Court of India

11 March 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, Amarendra Nath Sen

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 385; (1984) 2 SCC 439; AIR 1983 SC 452; 1983(1) SCALE 862; 1983 UJ 411

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Earth

Summary: Land and Property - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 115 - Specific Relief Act, 1963, ss. 34, 39 - Specific Relief - Encroachment - Suit for a declaration and for removal of an encroachment by first constructing a wall in a passage used as a lane for passing and repassing at points and also by dumping some earth - Plaintiff having failed to file suit for 9 years - Even prayer seeking removal of encroachment made after 4 years - Held, mandatory injunction granted and Court directed that the defendants shall remove the encroachment - Appeal allowed.
