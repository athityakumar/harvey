Satish Chand Makhan and Others v Govardhan Das Byas and Others

Supreme Court of India

27 October 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 314; (1984) 1 SCC 369; AIR 1984 SC 143

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Rent Control - CPC, 1908, O. 7 r. 7 - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, ss. 106, 111 and 116 - Registration Act, s.49 - Tenancy - Lease expired - Leasee still in occupation - Is tenant holding over - (A) Maintainability of suit for ejectment without notice u/s.106 of 1982 Act - (B) Applicability of provision to s. 49 of Registration Act or O. 7. r. 7 of CPC,1908 - Held, not maintainable and he does not become tenant at sufferance on expiry of term under unregistered lease executed before filing of suit - Provisions also not attracted - Appeal partly allowed.
