Gamon India Limited v Niranjan Dass

Supreme Court of India

05 December 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, R.B. Misra, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 100; (1984) SCC (L&S) 144; (1984) 1 SCC 509; AIR 1984 SC 500; 1984 (48) FLR 310; 1983 LabIC 1865; 1984 (1) LLJ 233; 1984 (1) LLN 90; 1983(2) SCALE 863; [1984] 1 S.C.R. 959; 1984 (1) SLJ 150; 1984 UJ 195

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Closure, Unjustified

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, 1947, ss. 2(oo), 25F - Retrenchment on account of recession and reduction in work - Notice of termination - Non compliance of pre-requisites for valid retrenchment - Held, retrenchment ab initio void - Order accordingly.
