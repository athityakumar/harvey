Ajit Singh and Others v State of Punjab and Another

Supreme Court of India

10 March 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 282; (1983) 2 SCC 217; (1983) SCC (L&S) 303; AIR 1983 SC 494; 1983 (46) FLR 407; 1983 LabIC 614; 1983 (1) LLJ 410; 1983 (2) LLN 1; 1983(1) SCALE 262; [1983] 2 S.C.R. 517; 1983 (1) SLJ 370; 1983 (2) SLR 1; 1983 UJ 351

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments; Service

Summary: Service - Punjab Town Improvement Act, 1922, s. 17(2) - Punjab Trust Services (Recruitment and Conditions of Service) Rules, 1978, r. 9(1) - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14 and 16 - Service Rules - Executive officers - Dispensing of services, after dissolving Board of Trustees - Other staff retained and functions continued - Reconstitution of Board - (A) Period probation prescribed, 2 years, if mandatory - (B) Appointing new officers without recalling dismissed officers - Validity of action - Held, not mandatory, shorter period can be prescribed by employee - Action arbitrary and violative of arts. 14 and 16 of Constitution - Appeals dismissed.
