Munshi Ram v Narsi Ram & Another

Supreme Court of India

14 January 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, P.N. Bhagwati

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 61; (1983) 2 SCC 8; AIR 1983 SC 271; 1983 (9) ALR 197; 1983(1) SCALE 11; [1983] 2 S.C.R. 233; 1983 UJ 157

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice and Procedure - Limitation Act, 1963, s. 21, Proviso, s.2(h) and art. 97 - CPC, 1908, O. 1, r. 10 - Whether the application made by the appellant for impleading an additional defendant to the suit out of which this appeal arises was in time - Finding that mistake of not impleading one of the vendees in suit for possession based upon right of pre-emption was committed in good faith - Held, Proviso to s. 21 of Limitation Act applied - Appeal allowed.
