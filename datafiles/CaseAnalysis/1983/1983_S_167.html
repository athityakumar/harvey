State of Karnataka and Another v H. Ganesh Kamath and Others

Supreme Court of India

31 March 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Madon, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 281; (1983) 2 SCC 402; (1983) SCC (Cr) 514; AIR 1983 SC 550; 1983 CRLR 501; 1983(1) SCALE 321; [1983] 2 S.C.R. 665; 1983 UJ 345

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Constitution; Criminal

Summary: Carriers and Transportation - Constitution - Karnataka Motor Vehicles Rules, 1963 - Motor Vehicles Act, 1939, ss. 7, 15, 16, 17 and 21 - Rule 5(2) of the 1963 Rules inconsistent with s. 7(7) and (8) of 1939 Act - Legality - As it does not merely prescribe a qualification not provided for in the Act, but prescribes a qualification which is contrary to that provided in the Act - Held, ultravires - Appeals dismissed.
