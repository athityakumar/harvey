Devaki Nandan Prasad v State Of Bihar & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

22 April 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 233; (1983) 4 SCC 20; (1983) SCC (L&S) 495; AIR 1983 SC 1134; 1983 (31) BLJR 343; 1983 (47) FLR 39; 1983 LabIC 847; 1984 (1) LLJ 237; 1983 (2) LLN 329; 1983(1) SCALE 463; [1983] 2 S.C.R. 921; 1983 (2) SLJ 118; 1983 (2) SLR 97; 1983 UJ 442

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Inter-State Migrant Workmen (Regulation Of Employment And Conditions Of Service)

Summary: Constitution, arts. 32, 311 - Writ of mandamus - Government service - Pension - Failure to carry out Sureme Court direction in the writ - For about 12 years - By State Government and its officers - Regarding payment of pension - State Government directed to pay arrears of pension with interest - Exemplary costs to the petitioner for harassment by the State Government Officers - Order Accordingly.
