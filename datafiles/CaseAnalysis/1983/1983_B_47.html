Union Of India & Ors. Etc. Etc v Bombay Tyre International Ltd. Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

07 October 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, P.N. Bhagwati, Amarendra Nath Sen

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 452; (2005) 3 SCC 787; 1983 ECR 2233; 1984 (17) E.L.T. 329

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944 - (A) Trade Discounts - Discounts allowed in Trade - Held, trade discounts shall not be disallowed only because they are not payable at time of each invoice or deducted from invoice price - (B)Taxes - Further held, taxes if proved to have been paid, should be allowed - (C) Insurance - Further held, transit transport insurance covering transportation of goods from factory gate to place or places of delivery to be included as a deduction - Interim orders.
