Bhagwan Dass Arora v First Addl. Distt. Judge, Rampur

Supreme Court of India

25 July 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 118; (1983) 4 SCC 1; AIR 1983 SC 954; 1983 ALJ 995; 1983 (9) ALR 540; 1983(2) SCALE 40; [1983] 3 S.C.R. 493; 1983 UJ 881

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Provincial Small Cause Courts Act, 1887, s. 17(1), Proviso - CPC, 1908, O. 9, r. 13 - Indian Limitation Act, 1908 - Security Bond - Furnished in time - Stamped with court fee stamp of Rs. 2/- - Irreconcilable difference between Madras and Calcutta High Court on requirement of valid security bond - Application for setting aside ex party decree filed - Held, preferences of Judges should not be allowed to work hardship on litigant - Application must be allowed - Appeal Allowed.
