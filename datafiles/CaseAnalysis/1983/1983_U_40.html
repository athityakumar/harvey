Brahma Chandra Gupta v Union of India

Supreme Court of India

29 November 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, R.B. Misra, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 303; (1984) SCC (L&S) 268; (1984) 2 SCC 433; AIR 1984 SC 380; 1984 (48) FLR 387; 1984 LabIC 177; 1984 (1) LLN 253; 1983(2) SCALE 910; 1984 (1) SLJ 566; 1984 (2) SLR 165

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 311 - Suspension of service of employee due to criminal prosecution - Dismissed (on conviction) - Reinstatement (on acquittal in appeal) - 1/4th salary withheld for suspension period - Suit against - Held, he was entitled to full amount of salary on reinstatement - Appeal allowed.
