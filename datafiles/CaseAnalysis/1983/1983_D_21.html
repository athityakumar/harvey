Smt. Lachi Tewari and Others v Director of Land Records and Others

Supreme Court of India

03 October 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, Amarendra Nath Sen

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 318; (1984) Supp SCC 431; AIR 1984 SC 41; 1983(2) SCALE 1016

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges

Summary: Advocates & Judges - Practice & Procedure - Rule nisi obtained - Discharged for non appearance - When rule nisi came up for hearing High Court rejected the same and rule was discharged - Application for recalling order was rejected - Hence, present appeal - Party having done everything in his power to effectively participate in proceedings can rest assured that he has neither to go to HC to inquire as to what is happening in HC with regard to his appeal nor is he to act as a watchdog of advocate that latter appears in matter when it is listed - Held, petitioner obtained rule nisi in 1976 and waited for 7 years for its being heard - Petitioner had taken extra caution to engage three learned counsels - Petitioner did everything at his disposal including engaging three lawyers - Relied on Rafiq v. Munshilal, 1981 Indlaw SC 297 and Goswami Krishna Murarilal Sharma v. Dhan Prakash, 1981 Indlaw SC 474 - Civil Rule restored to be heard on merits by HC - Appeal allowed.
