Sengara Singh and Others v State of Punjab and Others

Supreme Court of India

02 August 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 13; (1983) 4 SCC 225; (1983) SCC (L&S) 519; AIR 1984 SC 1499; 1983 (47) FLR 321; 1983 LabIC 1670; 1984 (1) LLJ 161; 1983 (2) LLN 691; 1983 (3) SLR 685

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 - Service matter - Dismissal from service on account of participation in the Police agitation - Some of them had been taken back by the Govt. - Writ petition praying that the' Government should also consider petitioners' cases for reinstatement - Validity - Held, discrimination ; appellants must receive the same benefit which those reinstated received in the absence of any distinguishing feature in their cases - Appeal allowed.
