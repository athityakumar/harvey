Shambu Nath Goyal v Bank of Baroda and Others

Supreme Court of India

27 September 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, A. Varadarajan, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 144; (1983) 4 SCC 491; (1984) SCC (L&S) 1; AIR 1984 SC 289; 1983 (47) FLR 438; 1983 LabIC 1697; 1983 (2) LLJ 415; 1984 (1) LLN 8; 1983(2) SCALE 931; [1984] 1 S.C.R. 85; 1983 (2) SLJ 607; 1984 (1) SLR 212; 1983 UJ 962

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Majority Union, Sastri Award

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 33 and sch. 3, item 1, s. 10,11, 11A - Industrial Dispute - Desai Award - Sastry Award - Notice of enquiry to bank employee specifying Enquiry Officer and also appellate authority - Application by Management seeking permission u/s. 33 - Defect in domestic enquiry pleaded - (A) Enquiry Officer not specifically clothed with powers of disciplinary authority - Effect - (B) Management's right to adduce additional evidence to substantiate charge - Held, enquiry Officer was also the disciplinary authority - Application, to be filed at the earliest stage, without delay - Appeal allowed.
