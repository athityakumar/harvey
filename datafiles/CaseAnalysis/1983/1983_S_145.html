Manubhai Jehtalal Patel and Another v State of Gujarat and Others

Supreme Court of India

27 September 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, A. Varadarajan, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 324; (1983) 4 SCC 553; AIR 1984 SC 120; [1984] 56 Comp Cas 85; 1983(2) SCALE 979; 1983 UJ 905

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Summary: Land and Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 4, 5A, 6 and 38 - Land Acquisition for Gujarat State Road Transport Corporation - As per s. 38, neither Part VII nor Company Acquisition Rules attracted - Substantial question of law of general public importance about the vires of ss. 4, 5-A and 6 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894 - Provisions held, intra vires the Constitution - Court refused to determine validity of acquisition - Appeal dismissed.
