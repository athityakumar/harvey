Jagdamba Paper Industries (P) Limited etc. v Haryana State Electricity Board and Others

Supreme Court of India

29 September 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, Amarendra Nath Sen

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 263; (1983) 4 SCC 508; AIR 1983 SC 1296; 1983(2) SCALE 1008; [1984] 1 S.C.R. 165; 1984 UJ 126

Case Digest

Subject: Electricity & Energy; Land & Property

Summary: Electricity (Supply) Act, 1948, s. 49 - Indian Electricity Act, 1910 - Electricity Board can unilaterally revise conditions of supply of electricity - Condition as to security for meters and for payment of energy Bill - unilateral enhancement of service - Jurisdiction - Held, unilateral enhancement of such security is unassailable - Order Accordingly.
