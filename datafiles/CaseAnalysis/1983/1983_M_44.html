Hindustan Machine Tools Limited, Bangalore v Mohd. Usman and Another

Supreme Court of India

24 August 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, A. Varadarajan, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 36; (1984) SCC (L&S) 92; (1984) 1 SCC 152; AIR 1984 SC 321; 1983 (47) FLR 536; 1983 (1) LabIC 1739; 1983 (2) LLJ 386; 1983 (2) LLN 655; 1983(2) SCALE 402; 1983 UJ 804

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 11 A - Misconduct - Labour Court holding that punishment of termination is disproportionately heavy and reducing it to stoppage of increments for two years - Interference sought - Held, no interference by Supreme Court is called for in absence of any important legal principle - Appeal dismissed.
