Cotton Corporation Of India v United Industrial Bank

Supreme Court of India

19 September 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 223; (1983) 4 SCC 625; AIR 1983 SC 1272; [1984] 55 Comp Cas 423; 1983(2) SCALE 324; [1983] 3 S.C.R. 962; 1983 UJ 978

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure; Trusts & Associations

Summary: Specific Relief Act, 1963, s. 41(b) - CPC, 1908, s. 151 and O.39 - (A) Specific Relief Act - Injunction - Restraining a person from initiating proceedings in court of co-ordinate or superior jurisdiction - If can be granted - (B) CPC,1908 - Whether inherent power to grant interim injunction can be invoked to nullify provision of s. 41 (b) the Act - Held, court cannot grant an injunction - Power cannot be invoked to nullify provision of s. 41(b) the Act - Appeal Allowed.
