M. Karunanidhi and Others v Dr. H. V. Hande and Others

Supreme Court of India

31 March 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 379; (1983) 2 SCC 473; AIR 1983 SC 558; 1984 (1) MLJ(SC) 1; 1983(1) SCALE 344; [1983] 2 S.C.R. 629; 1983 UJ 377

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Civil Procedure; Exchange Control & FDI; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Election - Representation of the People Act, 1951 ss. 81, 83 and 100 - Corrupt practice - Appellant challenged for failure to supply copy of photographs of fancy banner along with the election petition as violation of s. 81(3) - Words 'copies thereof' in s. 81(3) read in context of s. 83(2) must necessarily refer not only to election petition proper but also to schedules or annexure thereto containing particulars of any corrupt practice alleged therein - High Court dismissed election petition - Whether HC right in dismissing election petition on ground that mandatory provisions have not been fulfilled? - Held, judgment of HC reserved insofar as it holds that photograph of fancy banner could not treated as integral part of election petition by merely piece of evidence as to nature and type of fancy banner erected by appellant - Failure to supply copy of photograph to appellant did not amount to violation of s. 81(3) - Appeal dismissed.
