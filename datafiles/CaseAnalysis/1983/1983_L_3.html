State Of U.P. And Others Etc v L.J. Johnson And Another, Etc

Supreme Court of India

08 September 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, M.P. Thakkar

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 121; (1983) 4 SCC 110; AIR 1983 SC 1303; 1983 ALJ 1261; 1983 (9) ALR 610; 1983 (2) Bom.C.R. 774; 1983 (2) RCR(Rent) 602; 1983(2) SCALE 292; [1983] 3 S.C.R. 897; [1983] 3 S.C.R. 900; 1983 UJ 921

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Urban Land (Ceiling and Regulation) Act, 1976, ss. 4(9), 2(g) and 2(q)(ii) and 2(q)(iii) - Land and Property - Meaning of expression 'land appurtenant thereto' u/s. 4 (9) of 1976 Act - Held, not a land contiguous to some other land but very land which is part of same plot or area containing building or dwelling house - Appeal Dismissed.
