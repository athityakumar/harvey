Jai Bhagwan v The Management Of The Ambala Central Cooperative Banklimited

Supreme Court of India

29 September 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, D.A. Desai, A. Varadarajan

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 149; (1983) 4 SCC 611; (1984) SCC (L&S) 21; AIR 1984 SC 286; 1983 (47) FLR 532; 1983 LabIC 1694; 1984 (1) LLJ 52; 1983 (2) LLN 951; 1983(2) SCALE 528; [1984] 1 S.C.R. 158; 1984 (1) SLJ 245; 1983 (3) SLR 544; 1983 UJ 1012

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Constitution, art. 136 - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 10(1), sch. 2, item 3 - (A) Industrial Disputes - Workman - Termination - Other remedy - Effect - (B) Tribunal - Finding that termination was justified - Non application of mind - And non pursuance of records - (C) Termination - Breach of principles of natural justice - Delay in raising industrial dispute - Validity of awarding full back wages - Held, does not bar him from raising industrial dispute - Finding rejected as entirely worthless - Held was not justified - Appeal allowed.
