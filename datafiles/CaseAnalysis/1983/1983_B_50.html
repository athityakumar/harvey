Mange Ram v Brij Mohan and Others

Supreme Court of India

03 August 1983

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1983 Indlaw SC 78; (1983) 4 SCC 36; AIR 1983 SC 925; 1983(2) SCALE 63; [1983] 3 S.C.R. 525; 1983 UJ 749

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Keywords: Code of Civil Procedure (Amendment) Act, 1956

Summary: Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 - Election Petition - Scope of O. 16, rr. 1 and 1A - Whether court can decline to examine, when on evidence date, petitioner able to keep his witnesses present? - When there names exixts in the list - Court held, cannot decline - Nomination not material - Appeal Allowed.
