Bajaj Tempo Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax

Supreme Court of India

24 April 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

R.M. Sahai, A.S. Anand

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 1142; (1992) 3 SCC 78; AIR 1992 SC 1622; 1992 (104) CTR 116; [1992] 196 ITR 188; JT 1992 (3) SC 185; [1992] 2 S.C.R. 765; [1992] 62 TAXMAN 480

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Promoter

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income-tax Act, 1922, s. 15 - New Industrial Undertaking - Benefit of exemption - In instant case, part played by taking of premises of lease could not be said to be dominant in formation of company - Held, company could not therefore be denied benefit of exemption - Appeals allowed.
