T.P. George And Ors. Etc. Etc. v State Of Kerala And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

24 March 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

M.H. Kania, S. Mohan

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 928; (1992) Supp3 SCC 191; 1993 (23) ATC 353; 1992 (1) CLR 885; 1992 (65) FLR 47; JT 1992 (3) SC 88; 1992 (1) KLT 793; 1992 (2) LLN 2; 1992(1) SCALE 889; [1992] 2 S.C.R. 311; 1992 (4) SLR 9; 1992 (2) UJ 31

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Education; Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Education Norms

Summary: Education - Constitution - University Grants Commission -Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 - Superannuation for teachers - (A) Whether there is voilation of art. 14 in applying different schemes to teachers in Universities and teachers in private colleges? - Held, teachers in Universities are governed in respect of their conditions of service and age of retirement by separate statutes made by Universities concerned and teachers in private colleges or affiliated colleges are governed in respect of their conditions of service by regulations or rules framed by Govt. - Two classes of Universities teachers and teachers in private colleges cannot be regarded as similar for purposes of conditions of service as to bring case u/art. 14 of Constitution - (B) Whether court is competent to prescribe age of retirement of teachers at affiliated colleges? - Further held, it is not for court to prescribe correct age of retirement of teachers at affiliated colleges but that is a policy function requiring considerable expertise which can properly be done by State Govt. or State Legislature or Universities concerned - Appeals dismissed.
