Jammi Raja Rao v Anjaneyaswami Temple Valu Etc.

Supreme Court of India

06 March 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, M. Fathima Beevi

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 1015; (1992) 3 SCC 14; AIR 1992 SC 1110; JT 1992 (2) SC 470; 1993 (1) MLJ(SC) 9; 1992(1) SCALE 571; [1992] 2 S.C.R. 47

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Andhra Pradesh Charitable and Hindu Religious Institutions and Endowments Act, 1966

Summary: Land & Property - Socio-Economic - Andhra Pradesh Charitable and Hindu Religious Institutions and Endowments Act, 1966, ss. 77 and 103 - (A) Religious Endowment - Temple - Whether public or private - Determination - Considering entries in inam Register, oral evidence and concurrent finding - (B) Title over property of temple - Prescription - Adverse possession claimed by claimant by acquisition from 1946 - Suit temple falling in Andhra area of State of A P - Relevant date for acquisition of right of prescription would be 30-09-1951 - Held, (A) suit temple was public temple - (B) Claimant cannot be held to have acquired title over suit properties by adverse possession - Appeal dismissed.
