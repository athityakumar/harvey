N. T. C. (South Maharashtra) Limited and Others v Rashtriya Mill Mazdoor Sangh and Others

Supreme Court of India

24 November 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

P.B. Sawant, Kuldip Singh, N.M. Kasliwal

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 761; (1993) 1 SCC 217; 1993 (1) CLR 602; 1993 (49) DLT 283; JT 1992 (Supp) SC 202; 1993 (1) LLJ 954; 1992(3) SCALE 276; 1993 (1) SLR 32; [1992] Supp3 S.C.R. 229; 1993 (1) UJ 247

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Closure, Textile Undertakings (Taking Over of Management) Act, 1983

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 2(ss.), 25-F and 25-FF - Textile Undertakings (Taking Over of Management) Act, 1983, ss. 3(3) to 3(7), 6(1), 6(2) and 13 - Whether, on taking over of management of mill by NTC, workmen ceased to be workmen of textile mills and, therefore, of NTC w.e.f 10-02-1984 by virtue of provisions of ss. 3(3) and 3(4) of Textile Undertaking Act and s. 25-FF of Act, 1947? - That ss. 3(3) and 3(4) do not refer to contract of employment of workmen but have in their view contract other than contracts of employment of workmen, is made clear by provisions of s. 13 of Act, 1983 which specifically provides for contract of employment with workmen; it states that if contract of employment of a workman is to be terminated, custodian can do so if he is of opinion that it is unduly onerous, however while terminating contract, he to gives 1 month's notice in writing or salary or wages, as case may be, for 1 month in lieu of notice, therefore, ss. 3(3) and 3(4) do not have effect of terminating contract of employment of workmen - Further s. 25-FF of Act, 1947 by itself does not put an end to contract of employment on transfer of ownership or management of undertaking to new employer and only provides for compensation to workmen if such transfer results in termination of contract of employment; three conditions under which workman becomes ineligible to notice and retrenchment compensation u/s. 25-F make it abundantly clear that by itself neither s. 25-FF nor transfer of undertaking as such, puts an end to contract of employment and in fact, section envisages continuation of employment - Held, provisions of ss. 3(3) and 3(4) r/w s. 13 make it clear that in fact transfer of management in present case has not resulted in termination of contract of employment of workmen - Appeals dismissed.
