Director, Institute Of Management Development U.P v Smt. Pushpa Srivastava

Supreme Court of India

04 August 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mohan, L.M. Sharma, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 1190; (1992) 4 SCC 33; (1992) SCC (L&S) 767; AIR 1992 SC 2070; 1992 (2) AnWR 43; 1992 (21) ATC 377; 1992 (3) AWC 1827; 1992 (65) FLR 571; JT 1992 (4) SC 489; 1992 LabIC 2055; 1993 (1) LLJ 190; 1992 (2) LLN 863; 1992(2) SCALE 155; [1992] 3 S.C.R. 712; 1993 (1) SLJ 124; 1992 (5) SLR 86; 1993 (1) UPLBEC 7

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Research

Summary: Service - Kerala State and Subordinate Service Rules, 1958, r. 9(1) - Regularisation of ad hoc appointee - Entitlement - Whether respondent being continued service from time to time on 'ad hoc' basis for more than a year, entitled to regularisation ? - Appointment is contractual and by efflux of time, appointment comes to an end, respondent could have no right to continue in post - Held, on sympathetic consideration, respondent services may be continued till end of this calendar year on same terms as spelt out in order D/- 1-09-1990 - Appeal disposed of.
