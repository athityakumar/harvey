Secretary, Finance Department And Ors. v West Bengal Registration Service Association Andors.

Supreme Court of India

20 February 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, M.M. Punchhi

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 342; (1993) Supp1 SCC 153; (1993) SCC (L&S) 157; AIR 1992 SC 1203; 1993 (24) ATC 403; 1992 (2) CalLT(SC) 1; 1993 (1) CLR 523; JT 1992 (2) SC 27; 1992 LabIC 1047; 1992 (2) LLN 386; 1992(1) SCALE 437; [1992] 1 S.C.R. 897; 1992 (2) SLR 82; 1992 (2) UJ 359

Case Digest

Subject: Defence & Security Forces; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Service - Constitution - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14, 16 and 311- Fixation of - Equation of posts and equation of salaries - Whose function, executive or judiciary - Placing Sub-Registrars (Registration Service) and Judicial Magistrates (Munsiffs) in same pay scale - If arbitrary - Held, is primary function of executive and not judiciary - Is arbitrary - Appeal allowed.
