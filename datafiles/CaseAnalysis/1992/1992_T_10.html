Tahil Ram Issardas Sadarangani and Others v Ramchand Issardas Sadarangani and Another

Supreme Court of India

16 October 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, P.B. Sawant

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 682; (1993) Supp3 SCC 256; AIR 1993 SC 1182

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Constitution of India, 1950 art. 226 - Writ Petition - Client not present on date of hearing as he was not aware of date - Dismissal of petition in default - Held, litigant, for no fault of his should not suffer - Order set aside - Appeal allowed.
