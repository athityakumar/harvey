Bhagwan Sharma v Bani Ghosh Smt

Supreme Court of India

22 July 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

L.M. Sharma, S. Mohan, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 1287; (1993) Supp3 SCC 497; AIR 1993 SC 398

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 103(b) - Powers of High Court as to finding of fact in second appeal - Predetermination - But for that HC must hear parties fully with reference to entire evidence on records relevant to issue in question - Held, on a reappraisal of entire evidence ultimate conclusion may go in favour of either party and it cannot be prejudged - Appeal allowed.
