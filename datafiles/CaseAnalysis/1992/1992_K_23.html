Food Corporation Of India v Kamdhenu Cattle Feed Industries

Supreme Court of India

03 November 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

J.S. Verma, N. Venkatachala, Yogeshwar Dayal

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 426; (1993) 1 SCC 71; AIR 1993 SC 1601; AIR 1993 SCW 1509; JT 1992 (6) SC 259; 1992(3) SCALE 85B; [1992] Supp2 S.C.R. 322; 1993 (1) UJ 258

Case Digest

Subject: Contract & Commercial

Summary: Contract & Commercial - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 and 299 - Contract Act, 1872, s. 38 - State action in contractual sphere - Requirements of doctrine of legitimate expectation - Amount quoted in highest tender found inadequate so negotiations with all tenderers and got offer of higher amount - Constitutionality - Held, not arbitrary - Appeal allowed.
