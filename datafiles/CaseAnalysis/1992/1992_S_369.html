Dr. Snehlata Patnaik and Others v State of Orissa and Others

Supreme Court of India

22 January 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

M.H. Kania, P.B. Sawant, T.K. Thommen

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 850; (1992) 2 SCC 26; (1992) SCC (L&S) 401; 1992 (19) ALR 256; 1992 (20) ATC 187; JT 1992 (1) SC 305; 1992(1) SCALE 126A; [1992] 1 S.C.R. 335; 1992 (1) SLR 592; 1992 (2) UPLBEC 1067

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Tamil Nadu Entertainments Tax Act, 1939

Summary: Miscellaneous - Health & Drug - Service - Constitution of India, 1950 - Whether some preference might be given to in-service candidates who have done five years of rural service? - Yes, court held, authorities might well consider giving weightage up to a maximum of 5 per cant of marks in favour of in-service candidates who have done rural service for five years or more and actual percentage would certainly have to be left to the authorities - Petition disposed of.
