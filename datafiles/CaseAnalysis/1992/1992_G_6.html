State of Uttar Pradesh and Others v G.P. Swami

Supreme Court of India

16 October 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

M.N. Venkatachaliah, M.M. Punchhi

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 1329; (1993) SCC (L&S) 1058; AIR 1993 SC 1740; 1993 (25) ATC 754

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Service - Enquiry - Validity of - High Court quashed order of dismissal as well as subsequent order and held that enquiry, stands vitiated and order of dismissal could not be passed on basis of such an enquiry - Whether judgment/ order passed by HC needs to be modified? - Held, in modification of order of HC it is directed that respondent be treated as in service till 21-02-1984 (when Chief Conservator sought to give effect certain orders of service tribunal)and in rank of a Ranger from 19-08-1977 to 21-02-1984 and shall be deemed to have been compulsorily retired on expiry of 21-02-1984 - Further held, he shall be entitled to salary and emoluments of a Ranger during this period, less of course, whatever has been paid to him by way of subsistence allowance or otherwise - For purposes of pension payable from 21-02-1984 respondent shall be deemed to have held post of a Ranger during that period in a substantive capacity - Appeal disposed of.
