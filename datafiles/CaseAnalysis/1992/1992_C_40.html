Chhittar v State of Rajasthan

Supreme Court of India

08 September 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

G.N. Ray, K. Jayachandra Reddy

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 538; (1995) SCC (Cr) 248; (1995) Supp4 SCC 519; AIR 1994 SC 214; 1994 CRLJ 245

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 24 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 300 - Murder - Extra judicial confession - Version of accused as per alleged confession inconsistent with medical evidence - Conviction of accused solely on basis of retracted extra judicial confession - Whether proper - Held, not proper - Appeal allowed.
