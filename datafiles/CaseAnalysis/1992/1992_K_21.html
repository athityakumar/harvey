Chand Rani v Kamal Rani

Supreme Court of India

18 December 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mohan, B.P. Jeevan Reddy, L.M. Sharma, S.P. Bharucha, S. Ratnavel Pandian

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 1223; (1993) 1 SCC 519; AIR 1993 SC 1742; AIR 1993 SCW 1371; 1992 (3) CCC 918; 1993 (49) DLT 257; JT 1993 (1) SC 74; 1992(3) SCALE 544; [1992] Supp3 S.C.R. 798

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Indirect Tax; Service

Summary: Contract & Commercial - Specific Relief Act, 1963, s. 16 - Contract Act, 1872, s. 55 - Agreement to sell immovable property - Intention of parties to make time essence of contract - Purchaser not willing to make party payment of amount within specified time without fulfilment of some conditions which was contrary to agreement - Whether purchaser was entitled to specific performance of contract? - Held, purchaser was not entitled to specific performance of contract - Appeal dismissed.
