Continental Construction Limited v Commissioner Of Income-Tax, Central-1

Supreme Court of India

15 January 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ranganathan, N.D. Ojha, Veeraswami Ramaswami

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 315; (1992) Supp2 SCC 567; AIR 1992 SC 803; 1992 (2) CompLJ 50; 1992 (101) CTR 386; [1992] 195 ITR 81; JT 1992 (1) SC 140; 1992(1) SCALE 65; [1992] 1 S.C.R. 57; [1992] 60 TAXMAN 429; 1992 (2) TLR 421

Case Digest

Subject: Exchange Control & FDI; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Foreign Project, Finance Act, 1982, Royalties, Finance (No2) Act, 1991

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 9, 80 HHB and 80 O - Foreign Projects - Payment under - Royalty Commission etc. - Exemption - Contracts approved by Board of taxes - Assessee under contracts making available technical information to foreign Govt. and also rendering services to foreign Govt. outside India - Held, assessee entitled to relief u/s. 80 O for assessment years earlier to 1983-84 - For assessment year 1983-84 assessee would be eligible for deduction u/s. 80 HHB and not u/s. 80 O - Appeal allowed.
