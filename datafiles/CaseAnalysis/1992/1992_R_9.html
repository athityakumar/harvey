R.L. Bansal And Ors. v Union Of India And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

08 May 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 1112; (1992) Supp2 SCC 318; (1992) SCC (L&S) 773; AIR 1993 SC 978; 1992 (21) ATC 503; JT 1992 (3) SC 243; 1993 (2) LLJ 750; 1992 (2) LLN 39; 1992(1) SCALE 1179; [1992] 3 S.C.R. 133; 1992 (4) SLR 445; 1992 (2) UJ 645

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India, Arts.311, 309, 14 and 16; Central Public Works Department Assistant Engineers (Central Engineering Service and Central Electrical Engineering Service) Group 'B' (Confirmation and Seniority) Rules, 1979, rr.4 and 5 (Amendment 1982) - Seniority - Determination - Assistant Engg. - Date of confirmation - Basis of seniority, by Rule - Held, predicates seniority of Assistant Engg. on the date of confirmation - Wholly unequal and discriminatory basis - Unconstitutional - Direction to determine seniority from date of appointment.
