Madhyamik Siksha Parishad, U.P v Anil Kumar Mishra and Others

Supreme Court of India

19 August 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

M.N. Venkatachaliah, N.P. Singh, P.B. Sawant

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 1292; (2005) 5 SCC 122; (2005) SCC (L&S) 628; AIR 1994 SC 1638; 1994 ALJ 389; 1994 LabIC 1197; 1994 (2) LLJ 977; 1994 (1) LLN 851

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, 25 B - Regularisation of service - Of persons working on posts not sanctioned under Education Board on ad hoc assignment - Challenged that grant of status of workman on completing 240 days of work is not proper and does not create right to regularisation - Direction of High Court for regularisation of services of all such employees - Direction set aside - Appeal disposed of.
