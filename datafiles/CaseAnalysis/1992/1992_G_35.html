Gujarat State Road Transport Corporation and Another v Mulu Amra

Supreme Court of India

10 March 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, S. Mohan

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 1055; (1995) SCC (L&S) 141; (1995) Supp4 SCC 548; AIR 1994 SC 112; 1994 (2) LLJ 552

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, sch. II, item 3 - Dismissal from service - Reinstatement - Long lapse of time - Lump-sum compensation in lieu of reinstatement - Whether proper ? - Held, Is proper - Employer to assist delinquent, belonging to under privileged class in depositing compensation amount in long term fixed deposit with Bank - Appeal allowed.
