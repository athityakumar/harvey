Shamkant Narayan Despande v Maharashtra Industrial Developmentcorporation And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

21 October 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

P.B. Sawant, G.N. Ray

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 690; (1993) Supp2 SCC 194; (1993) SCC (L&S) 419; AIR 1993 SC 1173; 1993 (24) ATC 416; 1993 (2) CLR 11; 1994 (68) FLR 1013; JT 1992 (Supp) SC 166; 1992 LabIC 2401; 1993 (2) LLJ 686; 1992 (2) LLN 1023; 1992(2) SCALE 857; 1993 (3) SLJ 70; 1992 (5) SLR 622; [1992] Supp2 S.C.R. 95; 1993 (1) UJ 135

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Service - Maharashtra Industrial Development Act, 1961, ss. 63 and 64 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 and 16 - Promotion to post of Superintending Engineer - Whether classification of degree-holder and diploma-holder Executive Engineers violative of art. 14 and 16? - Classification made by Corporation by passing mere resolution - Held, classification among same class on basis of qualifications is not violative of art. 14 and 16 - Change in service condition by passing mere resolution not illegal - Appeal dismissed.
