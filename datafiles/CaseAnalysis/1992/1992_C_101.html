P. V. Srinivasa Sastry and Others v Comptroller and Auditor General, and Others

Supreme Court of India

11 December 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

N.M. Kasliwal, N.P. Singh

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 722; (1993) SCC (L&S) 206; (1993) 1 SCC 419; AIR 1993 SC 1321; 1993 (23) ATC 645; 1993 (1) CLR 657; JT 1992 (Supp) SC 442; 1993 LabIC 421; 1993 (1) LLJ 824; 1993 (1) LLN 642; 1992(3) SCALE 469; 1993 (1) SLR 25; [1992] Supp3 S.C.R. 503; 1993 (1) UJ 278

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 311(2) - Departmental proceedings - Initiation of, by subordinate authority in absence of Rules - Validity of - Order of reduction in rank to such post - Which employee never held or never belonged to - Held, order must have nexus with post held by delinquent officer - Imposing punishment of reduction in rank - Liable to be quashed - Initiating proceedings - Need not be only by appointing authority.
