Ramanand Chaudhary v State of Bihar and Others

Supreme Court of India

07 August 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, N.M. Kasliwal

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 193; (2002) 1 SCC 153; AIR 1994 SC 948; 1994 (2) BLJ 324; 1994 CRLJ 1221

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Prevention of Corruption Act, 1947, s. 5 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 134 - Delayed proceedings in bribery case were in question as no action was taken by prosecution for six years after raid was conducted - Consistent opinion of Public Prosecutor that no criminal case made out against accused - Sanctioning authority on independent consideration refusing to grant sanction earlier but later ready to ask Deputy Inspector General of Police - Held, prosecution against accused was pending for more than thirteen years and it was enough to quash prosecution.
