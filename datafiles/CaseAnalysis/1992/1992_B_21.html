Commissioner Of Sales Tax, U.P. And Ors. Etc. Etc. v Bakhtawar Lal Kailash Chand Areti And Ors. Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

05 August 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, S. Ranganathan, Veeraswami Ramaswami

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 460; (1992) 3 SCC 750; AIR 1992 SC 1952; JT 1992 (4) SC 388; 1992(2) SCALE 138; [1992] 3 S.C.R. 734; 1992 (87) STC 196

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - Indirect Tax - Uttar Pradesh Sales Tax Act, 1948, ss. 3 B, 3 D - Central Sales Tax Act, 1956, s. 3 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 286(1) - 'Inter-State purchase' - Purchase of goods by Commission agent in U P on behalf of principal outside State and its despatch to principal forth with - Whether exigible to tax under U P Act - (B) Applicability of s. 3 D of the 1948 Act - Dealer issuing Forms III-C-I and paying tax on purchases made by him under U P Act - High Court's decision holding such purchase to be inter-state purchase not taxable under U P Act but taxable only under Central Act - Held, (A) Was inter-state purchase not exigible - (B) Dealer cannot be proceeded against u/s. 3 B for preventing authorities from taxing transaction under Central Act - Appeal dismissed.
