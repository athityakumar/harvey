State of Rajasthan v Bhawani Singh and Others

Supreme Court of India

06 March 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, M.H. Kania

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 1017; (1993) Supp1 SCC 306; AIR 1992 SC 1018; AIR 1992 SCW 930; JT 1992 (3) SC 531; 1992(1) SCALE 687; 1992 (1) UJ 491

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Scope of writ jurisdiction - Disputed questions relating to title - Cannot be raised or adjudicated in writ petition - Writ petition seeking declaration of title of petitioner to disputed property - Held, petition was misconceived - Appeal allowed.
