Toguru Sudhakar Reddy and Another v Government of A. P. and Others

Supreme Court of India

09 December 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, N.M. Kasliwal

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 596; (1993) Supp4 SCC 439; AIR 1994 SC 544

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Service

Summary: Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies Act, 1964, s. 31(1)(a), Proviso - Andhra Pradesh Co-operative Societies Rules, 1964, rr. 22(C) and 22 A(3)(a) - Constitution of India, 1950 art. 14 - Nomination by Registrar of two women members of certain class of societies - Arbitrary - Total reservation would go beyond 50 per cent - Held, not violative of art. 14.
