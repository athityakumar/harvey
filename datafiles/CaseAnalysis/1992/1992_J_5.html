Y. Srinivasa Rao v J. Veeraiah And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

27 April 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

L.M. Sharma, A.S. Anand

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 994; (1992) 3 SCC 63; AIR 1993 SC 929; JT 1992 (3) SC 84; 1992(1) SCALE 935; [1992] 2 S.C.R. 780; 1992 (1) UJ 752

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Service - Aandhra Pradesh Scheduled Commodities (Regulation of Distribution by Card System) Order, 1973, cl. 3 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 - Constitutionality of - (A) Settlement of fair price shops - Govt. policy - Appointment of dealer - Eligibility - Qualification - Preference to less educated persons - (B) Interview to be sole basis of selection - Absence of guidelines for taking interview - Held, arbitrary - And violative of art. 14 - Appeal allowed.
