State Bank of India and Others v D. C. Aggarwal and Another

Supreme Court of India

13 October 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

R.M. Sahai, S. Ratnavel Pandian

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 1211; (1993) 1 SCC 13; (1993) SCC (L&S) 109; AIR 1993 SC 1197; 1993 (23) ATC 403; 1992 (2) BankCLR 596; 1993 (1) BC 517; 1992 (3) CCC 373; 1993 (1) CLR 2; 1993 (66) FLR 164; JT 1992 (6) SC 673B; 1992 LabIC 2585; 1993 (1) LLJ 244; 1993 (1) LLN 1; 1992(2) SCALE 836; 1993 (2) SLJ 88; 1992 (5) SLR 598; [1992] Supp1 S.C.R. 956; 1993 (1) UPLBEC 25

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Civil Procedure; Labour & Industrial Law; Trade

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, sch. II, item 6 - Service matter - Procedural fairness in departmental enquiry - Punishment based on material which neither supplied nor disclosed to him - Plea that report of Central Vigilance Commission (basis for punishment) is a privileged document - Whether proceedings proper and plea allowable ? - Held, not proper - Not sustainable - Appeal dismissed.
