State Of Gujarat And Anr v P.J. Kampavat And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

28 April 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, M.M. Punchhi

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 386; (1992) 3 SCC 226; (1992) SCC (L&S) 654; (1992) SCC (L&S) 6545; AIR 1992 SC 1685; 1992 (21) ATC 112; JT 1992 (Supp) SC 102; 1992 LabIC 1687; 1993 (2) LLJ 771; [1992] 2 S.C.R. 845

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Defence Service

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 310(2) - Bombay Civil Services Rules, 1959, rr. 2, 9(56) and 33 - Temporary employee - Contractual appointment - Persons appointed by minister of their choice on his establishment - Legitimacy - Held, appointees not temporary Govt. servants - Co-terminous with minister's office.
