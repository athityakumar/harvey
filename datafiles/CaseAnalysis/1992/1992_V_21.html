State of Uttar Pradesh and Others v Vii Additional District Judge and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 September 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

G.N. Ray, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 607; (1992) 4 SCC 429; AIR 1993 SC 1232; 1992 (20) ALR 1046; JT 1992 (5) SC 471; 1992 (2) RCJ 407; 1992 (2) RCR(Rent) 517; 1992 (2) RentLR 664; 1992(2) SCALE 489; [1992] Supp1 S.C.R. 600; 1992 (2) UJ 794

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property

Summary: Uttar Pradesh Urban Buildings (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972, s. 3(i), 21(8), Proviso. - Rent - Determination of - 'Building' - Held, includes land underneath structure - Values of both superstructure and land underneath have to be considered.
