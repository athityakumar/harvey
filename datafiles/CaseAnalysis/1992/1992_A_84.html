Deputy Commissioner Of Sales Tax Etc. Etc. v Aysha Hosiery Factory (P) Ltd. Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

16 January 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

Veeraswami Ramaswami, S. Ranganathan, N.D. Ojha

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 317; (1992) Supp2 SCC 178; AIR 1992 SC 874; JT 1992 (1) SC 379; 1992 (1) KLT 423; 1992(1) SCALE 207; [1992] 1 S.C.R. 140; 1992 (85) STC 106

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Keywords: Additional Sales Tax, CP and Berar Letting of House and Rent Control Order 1949

Summary: Indirect Tax - Sales Tax - Central Sales Tax Act, 1956, s. 8(2A) - Kerala General Sales Tax Act, 1963, s. 5 - Kerala Additional Sales Tax Act, 1978, s. 2 - Levy of additional sales tax under Additional Sales Tax Act, 1978 - (A) Whether sales tax of same category as in original Act ? - (B) Whether assessee is liable to pay sales tax at rate including additional sales tax in respect of their inter-state sale under Central sales tax assessment orders ? - Held, (A) Is also sales tax of same category - (B) Assessee is so liable - Appeal allowed.
