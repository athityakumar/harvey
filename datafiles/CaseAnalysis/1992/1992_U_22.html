Escorts Limited and Another v Union Of India And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

22 October 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ranganathan, B.P. Jeevan Reddy, Veeraswami Ramaswami

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 698; (1993) 1 SCC 249; AIR 1993 SC 1325; 1992 (108) CTR 275; [1993] 199 ITR 43; JT 1992 (Supp) SC 619; 1992(2) SCALE 867; [1992] Supp2 S.C.R. 153; [1992] 65 TAXMAN 420; 1993 (2) TLR 162

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Competence Of The Legislature

Summary: Income-tax Act, 1922, ss. 10(2)(vi), 10(2)(xiv), 32 and 35 - Business income - Computation - Double deduction in regard to same business expenditure - Held, not permissible - Retrospective amendment effected by Finance (No. 2) Act of 1980 - Clarifies position and is not unconstitutional.
