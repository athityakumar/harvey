Smt. Victoria Fernandes v Lalmal Sawma and Others

Supreme Court of India

20 January 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, A.M. Ahmadi

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 109; (1992) 2 SCC 97; (1992) SCC (Cr) 271; (1995) SCC (Cr) 454; 1992 (29) ACC 143; AIR 1992 SC 687; 1992 AllCriC 143; 1992 (1) Crimes 497; 1992 CRLJ 702; 1992 CRLR 151; 1992(1) SCALE 99

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - National Security Act, 1980, s. 3(2) - (A) Preventive detention -Distinction between 'Law and Order and 'Public Order'- Solitary incident of assault on journalist coupled with two acts of threats to journalists - If constitute acts relating to public order - Order of detention based thereon - Whether Justified - (B) Challenge as to - Counter affidavit by authority other than one passing order of detention - Same not tainted by mala-fides or extraneous consideration - Held, (A) do not constitute acts relating to public order - (B) Order, not justified - Can be taken into consideration - Appeal allowed.
