State Of Himachal Pradesh And Anr. v Kailash Chand Mahajan And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

20 February 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mohan, R.M. Sahai

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 897; (1992) Supp2 SCC 351; (1992) SCC (L&S) 874; AIR 1992 SC 1277; AIR 1992 SCW 1247; 1992 (21) ATC 528; JT 1992 (2) SC 144; 1992 LabIC 1371; 1992(1) SCALE 454; [1992] 1 S.C.R. 917; 1992 (2) SLR 30

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Electricity & Energy; Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Financial Matters

Summary: Electricity & Energy - Electricity (Supply) Act, 1948, ss. 3(1), 5(6) and 10 - Member of Electricity Board - (A) Disqualification on ground of age - Scope - If legal - Failure to incorporate amendment as to disqualification in s. 10 - Consequence thereof - (C) Scope - S. 3 prescribes sixty five years as age of superannuation - Incumbent by reason of appointment enabled to continue after attaining age of sixty five, whether continuing is void - Held, (A) Is legal - S. 5(6) applies to initial appointment as well as to those continuing in appointment - (B) Is of no consequence - (B) Such continuing is void - Order accordingly.
