Joti Parshad v State of Haryana

Supreme Court of India

20 October 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jayachandra Reddy, G.N. Ray

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 92; (1993) Supp2 SCC 497; (1993) SCC (Cr) 691; AIR 1993 SC 1167; 1992 (3) Crimes 756; 1993 CRLJ 413; 1992 CRLR 747; 1992(3) SCALE 42; 1993 (1) UJ 236

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Indian Penal Code 1860, ss. 258, 259 and 26 - Counterfeit court-fee stamps were recovered from possession of accused, a licensed stamp vendor - Allegation by accused that he purchased all stamps from treasury without producing register of such purchase - No effort made to summon treasury records - Held, conviction proper as it could be inferred that accused had 'knowledge' or 'reason to believe' that stamps were counterfeit.
