Regional Director, E. S. I. Corporation and Another v Francis De'COSTA and Another

Supreme Court of India

05 May 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, B.P. Jeevan Reddy

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 458; (1993) Supp4 SCC 100; (1994) SCC (L&S) 195; 1992 (2) ACJ 636; 1992 (2) CCC 167; 1992 (65) FLR 317; JT 1992 (3) SC 332; 1993 (1) LLN 6; 1992(1) SCALE 1083; [1992] 3 S.C.R. 23; 1992 (4) SLR 349

Case Digest

Subject: Insurance; Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Social Security, Peril, Instructions

Summary: Insurance - Workmen's Compensation Act, 1923 - Indian Limitation Act, 1908 - Employees' State Insurance Act, 1948 - National Insurance (Industrial Injuries) Act, 1946 - Social Security Act, 1975 - Workmen's Compensation Act, 1906 - Whether the injury caused by an accident on a public road, while on his way to join the duty just 15 minutes before reporting to duty at a distance of 1 Kilometre from the factory premises, arises out of and in the course of his employment?
