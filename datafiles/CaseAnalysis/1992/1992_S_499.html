Pradeep Kumar and Others v State of Uttar Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

14 February 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, R.C. Patnaik, R.M. Sahai

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 127; (1995) SCC (Cr) 395; (1995) Supp4 SCC 419; AIR 1994 SC 104; 1993 ALJ 1362; 1994 CRLJ 148

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Uttar Pradesh Children Act, 1952, s. 2(4) - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 302 - Child accused - Jail span - Attained age of 30 years in jail - Commital of offence at age of 16 - Sent to jail contrary to provisions of Act - Held, releasing accused forthwith while sustaining conviction under all charges framed against them and quashing sentence.
