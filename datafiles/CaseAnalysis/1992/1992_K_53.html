Premier Tyres Limited v Kerala State Road Transport Corporation

Supreme Court of India

04 September 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

R.M. Sahai, B.P. Jeevan Reddy, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 433; (1993) Supp2 SCC 146; AIR 1993 SC 1202; 1993 (2) KLT 130

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 11 - Res judicata - Applicability in connected suits - Suits decreed by common judgment, partly decreeing plaintiff's suit as well as ordering suit filed by defendant - Whether failure to file appeal against one suit precludes Court from proceeding with appeal filed against another suit? - Held, finality of finding recorded in connected suit, due to non-filing of appeal, precluded court from proceeding with appeal in other suit - Appeal dismissed.
