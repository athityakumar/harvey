Smt. Chand Dhawan v Jawahar Lal And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

28 April 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Fathima Beevi, S. Ratnavel Pandian

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 34; (1992) 3 SCC 317; (1992) SCC (Cr) 636; AIR 1992 SC 1379; 1992 (2) AWC 938; 1992 (2) CCR 69; 1992 (2) Crimes 342; 1992 CRLJ 1956; 1992 CrLR(SC) 460; JT 1992 (3) SC 618; 1992(1) SCALE 996; [1992] 2 S.C.R. 837; 1992 (2) UJ 46

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, s. 482 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 494 and 109 - Bigamy - Husband of appellant married another woman - High Court quashed proceedings against husband - Wife moved Special Leave Petition - Held, facts of case clearly indicate that offence u/s. 494 commited by respondent - Original complaint restored - Appeal allowed.
