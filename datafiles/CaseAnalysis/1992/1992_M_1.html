(1) Municipal Corporation of Delhi and Another v Children Book Trust Etc.

Supreme Court of India

21 April 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mohan, S. Ranganathan, N.D. Ojha

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 973; (1992) 3 SCC 390; AIR 1992 SC 1456; JT 1992 (3) SC 19; 1992 (2) RCR(Rent) 84; 1992(1) SCALE 851; [1992] 2 S.C.R. 535; 1992 (2) UJ 345

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Municipalities & Local Governments - Delhi Municipal Corporation Act, 1957, ss. 115 and 115(4)(a), Proviso, Explanation - Constitution of India, 1950, sch. 7, List 2, Entry 5 - (A) Local Self Govt. - Power of taxation - Nature of - (B) Property tax - Lands and buildings used for charitable purpose Exemption - (C) Charitable purpose - Institution imparting education - Public benefit or philanthropy should be present - Educational Institution run on commercial lines with profit motive - (D) 'Society supported wholly or in part by voluntary contributions' - Test to determine - (E) Property tax - Society supported wholly or in part by voluntary contribution - School run by society - (1) Its funds by way of fees, donations transferred to society - Not 'voluntary contribution' - Such transfer prohibited under Delhi School Education Rules - Premises occupied by School and not Society - (2) Income utilized for running school - Profit or dividend not distributed to members - Charitable purpose - Ingredient that society is supported by voluntary contribution lacking - Held, (A) Municipality does not act as Legislature, power of taxation is a necessary adjunct to its other powers - (B) Conditions for claiming exemption stated - (C) Not charitable - (D) Word 'part' means substantial part and words 'voluntary contributions' cannot amount to compulsory donations - (E) Exemption cannot be claimed by society - No exemption - Appeal dismissed.
