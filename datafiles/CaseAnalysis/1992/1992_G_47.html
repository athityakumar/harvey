C.I.T., Bombay v Messrs Gwalior Rayon Silk Manufacturing Company

Supreme Court of India

29 April 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, N.M. Kasliwal

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 449; (1992) 3 SCC 326; AIR 1992 SC 1782; 1993 (1) Bom.C.R. 425; 1992 (104) CTR 243; 1992 (104) CTR 248; [1992] 196 ITR 149; JT 1992 (3) SC 158; 1992(1) SCALE 1000; [1992] 2 S.C.R. 1017; [1992] 62 TAXMAN 471; 1992 (2) TLR 865

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 32 - Income Tax Rules, 1962, rr. 5, Appendix I - Meaning of building - Whether 'building' includes roads and drains laid within factory premises? - Capital expenditure incurred thereon - Whether admissible to depreciation? - Appendix I as amended in 1983 is clarificatory - Capital expenditure incurred prior to 1983 - Held, building - Includes roads and drains in factory - Admissible to depreciation - Prior to 1983, admissible - Appeal dismissed.
