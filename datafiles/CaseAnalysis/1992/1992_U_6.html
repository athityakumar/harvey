Ramjas Foundation And Ors. v U.O.I. And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

13 November 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

N.M. Kasliwal, M.H. Kania, N.P. Singh

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 1037; (1993) Supp2 SCC 20; AIR 1993 SC 852; AIR 1992 SCW 3460; JT 1992 (Supp) SC 370; 1992(3) SCALE 121; [1992] Supp2 S.C.R. 426

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Limitation - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 4 and 6 - Delay in challenging issues u/ss. 4 and 6 of 1894 Act on the ground that neither award is passed nor 'owner' is disposed - Held, appellants were sitting on fence and did not take any steps of challenging notification u/s. 4 and 6 of Act till 1973 though grounds now sought to be urged were available to appellants as soon as such notifications were issued - Hence petition dismissed on ground of laches and delay - Appeal dismissed.
