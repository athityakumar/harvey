State of Orissa v B. N. Agarwala

Supreme Court of India

24 November 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, G.N. Ray

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 1220; (1993) 1 SCC 140; AIR 1993 SC 2521; 1993 (2) ARBLR 452; 1992 (3) CCC 702; 1993 (49) DLT 122; JT 1992 (Supp) SC 552; 1992(3) SCALE 229; [1992] Supp3 S.C.R. 221

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure

Summary: Arbitration Act, 1940 - Claim not mentioned in contract out of purview of arbitration - Arbitrator has power to grant interest for pre-reference period - Held, claim can be made in in arbitration aithough arbitrator has no power to grant interest for pre-reference period.
