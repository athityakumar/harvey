Kirtikumar Maheshankar Joshi v Pradipkumar Karunashanker Joshi

Supreme Court of India

04 May 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 431; (1992) 3 SCC 573; (1992) SCC (Cr) 778; AIR 1992 SC 1447; 1992 (20) ALR 743; 1992 (2) CCC 55; JT 1992 (3) SC 195; 1992(1) SCALE 1107; 1992 (1) UJ 741

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Women & Children

Summary: Women & Children - Guardians And Wards Act, 1890, s. 12 - Custody of minor children - Death of mother - Father facing charge u/s. 498A, Indian Penal Code - Not willing to live with father - Narrated episodes of ill-treatment of their mother - Happy with maternal uncle - Held, Supreme Court in interest and welfare of children declined to hand over their custody to their father though he had preferential right to custody - Order accordingly.
