Sarojini Tea Co. (P) Ltd v Collector Of Dibrugarh, Assam And Anr

Supreme Court of India

24 January 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, M.N. Venkatachaliah

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 867; (1992) 2 SCC 156; AIR 1992 SC 1264; AIR 1992 SCW 1217; JT 1992 (1) SC 327; 1992(1) SCALE 159; [1992] 1 S.C.R. 371; 1992 (1) UJ 219

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Finance Act, 1964, Assam Land (Requisition and Acquisition) Act 1948, Assam Fixation of Ceiling on Land Holdings Act 1956, Assam Land Revenue & Rent (Surcharge) Act 1970, Assam Land and Revenue Regulation , 1886, Assam Local Rates Regulation 1879

Summary: Land & Property - Constitution - Limitation Act, 1963, s. 5 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 133 - Assam Fixation of Ceiling on Land Holdings Act, 1957, ss. 1 and 12 - Assam Land Revenue and Rent (Surcharge) Act,1970, s. 3 - Assam Local Rates Regulation (1879), Pre and ss. 3, 4 and 5 - (A) Proceedings under Ceiling Act - Ss. 4 to 24 of Limitation Act apply - Appeal against compensation - Delay - Condonation of, by appellate Court in interest of justice - If discretion exercised by appellate Court to be interfered with - (B) Compensation for acquisition of excess land - Determination - Surcharge on land revenue levied under Surcharge Act, 1970 - If part of land revenue - Whether to be considered ? - (C) Compensation for acquisition of excess land - Computation - Local rate payable under 1879 regulation - If Land Revenue - Whether to be considered while determining compensation - Held, (A)Supreme Court refused to interfere - Has to be considered - (B)Surcharge is part of land revenue and not a distinct levy - Not Land Revenue - (C)Not to be considered - Appeal partly allowed.
