Parameswaran Govindan v Krishnan Bhaskaran And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

06 February 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, K. Jayachandra Reddy

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 455; (1993) Supp1 SCC 572; AIR 1992 SC 1135; JT 1992 (2) SC 130; 1992(1) SCALE 316; [1992] 1 S.C.R. 582

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Municipalities & Local Governments; Agriculture & Agri. Products

Keywords: Debt Laws

Summary: Land & Property - Kerala Land Reforms Act, 1963, ss. 4 A, 132(3) - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, ss. 60,76(4)and 83 - Kerala Compensation for Tenants Improvements Act, 1958, ss. 2(d), 2(a), 4 and 5 - Certain mortgagees to be deemed tenant - Redemption decree providing for possession of mortgagor - Mortgagee remained in possession for payment of compensation under Improvements Act, even after decree - His possession as mortgagee, however, not continuous for 50 years immediately proceeding commencement of Amending Act - Effect - Held, cannot acquire status of 'deemed tenant' - Appeal allowed.
