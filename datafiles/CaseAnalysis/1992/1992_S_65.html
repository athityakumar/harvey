Ahmedabad Urban Development Authority v Sharad Kumar Jayantikumar Pasawalla And Ors

Supreme Court of India

15 May 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

G.N. Ray, M.M. Punchhi, S. Mohan

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 479; (1992) 3 SCC 285; AIR 1992 SC 2038; JT 1992 (3) SC 417; 1992(1) SCALE 1212; [1992] 3 S.C.R. 328; 1992 (2) UJ 67

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Environment, Wildlife & Animal; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Town Planning

Summary: Municipalities & Local Governments - Gujarat Town Planning and Urban Development Act, 1976, s. 119 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 245 - Development fee - Express provision for imposition of fee absent - Regulations framed under Act by Development Authority for imposition of development fee - Legality - Held, unauthorised and illegal - Appeal dismissed.
