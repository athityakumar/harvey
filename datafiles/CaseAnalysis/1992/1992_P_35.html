Supreme Court of India

12 August 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, A.M. Ahmadi, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Temporary Or Ad Hoc, Kerala Water Supply And Sewerage Act, 1986

Summary: Kerala Water Supply And Sewerage Act, 1986 - Constitution of India, arts. 311, 226 and 32 - Service matter - Regularisation of - Creating or abolition of Post - If prerogative of executive - Direction by High Court to regularise unconditionally all persons who have put in one year's service - Role of Court - Held, there is presumption of necessity of regular post subject to constitutional consistency if temporary or ad hoc appointment continues for long period - Direction of High Court not sustainable - Is prerogative of executive on creation or abolition of Post.
