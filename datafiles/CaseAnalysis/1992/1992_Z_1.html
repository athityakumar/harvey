 Kihoto Hollohan v Zachillhu And Others

Supreme Court of India

18 February 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

M.N. Venkatachaliah, K. Jayachandra Reddy, L.M. Sharma, S.C. Agrawal, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 889; (1992) Supp2 SCC 651; AIR 1993 SC 412; AIR 1992 SCW 3497; JT 1992 (3) SC 600; 1992(1) SCALE 338; [1992] 1 S.C.R. 686

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Election; Practice & Procedure; Tort

Keywords: Freedom Of Speech And Expression

Summary: Constitution of India, arts. 105, 194 and 19, sch. 10, para 2 - Constitutionality - Political defections - Disqualification - Members of Parliament and Legislatures of State - Provisions under sch. 10 - Held, not unconstitutional - Political defection, valid.
