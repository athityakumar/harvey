 Krishna Swami and another v Union Of India & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

27 August 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

J.S. Verma, K. Jayachandra Reddy, K. Ramaswamy, N.M. Kasliwal, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 1258; (1992) 4 SCC 605; AIR 1993 SC 1407; JT 1992 (5) SC 92; [1992] Supp1 S.C.R. 53

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Constitution & Amendments, Judges (Inquiry) Act, 1968

Summary: Constitution of India,1950, arts. 32, 129 and 131 - Public interest litigation - Maintainability - Concerned Judge not impleaded as party - Binding nature of reference order - Held, petitions not maintainable - Reference order does not bind Constitution Bench to give decision on merits.
