Atma Ram Aggarwal and Others v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

03 November 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, L.M. Sharma

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 427; (1993) Supp1 SCC 1; 1993 (1) UJ 4

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Urban Land (Ceiling And Regulation) Act, 1976, ss.2(q), 4(9) and 8 - Whether lands in question were held by appellants as 'kartas' of their respective Hindu undivided families ? - Held, upon authority of Meera Gupta case [1991 Indlaw SC 319] which held that land built upon prior to appointed day was outside purview of 'vacant land' within meaning of Act and was not to be reckoned in calculating extent of 'vacant land' that was held by its owner, each of appellants are entitled to a deduction of maximum permissible area of 2000 sq. metres in this behalf - Appeals allowed.
