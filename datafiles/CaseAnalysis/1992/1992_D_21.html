Union of India and Others v Deep Chand Pandey and Another

Supreme Court of India

27 August 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

L.M. Sharma, N. Venkatachala, S. Mohan

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 581; (1992) 4 SCC 432; (1993) SCC (L&S) 21; AIR 1993 SC 382; 1992 (20) ALR 1132; 1993 (23) ATC 356; 1992 (3) AWC 1795; 1992 (65) FLR 798; JT 1992 (5) SC 276; 1993 LabIC 98; 1992 (2) LLN 745; 1992(2) SCALE 440; 1992 (3) SLJ 71; 1992 (5) SLR 341; [1992] Supp1 S.C.R. 49; 1992 (2) UJ 714; 1992 (2) UPLBEC 1169

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Administrative Tribunals Act, 1985, s. 14 - Scope - Casual typists in Govt. Department - Termination of service - Claiming right of continuance - With additional claim of temporary status - Held, High Court has no jurisdiction to entertain their claim - As claim covered by Act.
