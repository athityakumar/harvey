Mafabhai Nagarbhai Raval v State of Gujarat

Supreme Court of India

14 August 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jayachandra Reddy, N.P. Singh

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 55; (1992) 4 SCC 69; (1992) SCC (Cr) 810; AIR 1992 SC 2186; 1992 CRLJ 3710; JT 1992 (4) SC 555

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Evidence Act, 1872, ss. 3 and 45 - Murder - Appreciation of evidence - Doctor's opinion - Examining and conducting post mortem on deceased - Whether it can be substituted with court's opinion? - Held, cannot be as doctor is most competent witness - Unless something inherently defective.
