 Sarojini Ramaswami v Union Of India And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

27 August 1992

Case Analysis

Bench

J.S. Verma, K. Jayachandra Reddy, K. Ramaswamy, N.M. Kasliwal, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1992 Indlaw SC 1259; (1992) 4 SCC 506; AIR 1992 SC 2219; AIR 1992 SCW 2683; JT 1992 (5) SC 1; 1992(2) SCALE 257; 1992 (7) SLR 481; [1992] Supp1 S.C.R. 108; 1993 (1) UJ 174

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Judges (Inquiry) Act, 1968, Judges (Inquiry) Rules, 1969, Judges (Inquiry) Rules 1969, Parliamentary (Judges) Commission of Inquiry Act, 1988, Punjab Welfare Officer Recruitment and Conditions of Service Rules, 1952

Summary: Constitution of India,1950, art. 124(4), 124(5) and 136 - Judges (Inquiry) Act, 1968, ss. 3 and 4 - Judges (Inquiry) Rules; 1969, r. 9 - Supreme Court Judge - Removal of - Report of Enquiry Committee - Judicial review - When permissible ? - Resort to Judicial review between time of conclusion of inquiry by Committee and making of order of removal by President - If proper - Held, permissible only on passing of order of removal by President - Premature and unwarranted.
