Government of Andhra Pradesh v Guntur Tobaccos Limited

Supreme Court of India

18 November 1964

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Subba Rao, S.M. Sikri, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1964 Indlaw SC 220; AIR 1965 SC 1396; [1965] 2 S.C.R. 167; 1965 (16) STC 240

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Keywords: Packing Material, Madras General Sales Tax Act, 1939, Practice, Packing Materials

Summary: Sales Tax - TN General Sales Tax Act, 1939, ss. 2(h) and 2(i) - Evidence Act, 1872, ss. 101, 102, 103 and 104 - Sale of Goods Act, 1930, s. 4 - Company carrying on business of re-drying raw tobacco - And delivering it after packing - For which it charging consolidated charge - Sales tax is not exigible on turnover of packing material - Taxable - Contract for work involving transfer of contractor's goods - (A) On whom does burden of showing that transfer constituted taxable sale lies - (B) New plea raised for first time before Supreme Court - Burden lies on Revenue - Plea not entertained - Appeal dismissed.
