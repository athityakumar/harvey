Regional Provident Fund Commissioner v Shibu Metal Works

Supreme Court of India

09 November 1964

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, Kailas Nath Wanchoo, Mohammad Hidayatullah

Where Reported

1964 Indlaw SC 178; AIR 1965 SC 1076; 1965 (2) AnWR 133; 1965 (11) FLR 46; 1965 (1) LLJ 473; 1965 (2) MLJ 133; 1965 (2) SCJ 656; [1965] 2 S.C.R. 72

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Manufacturing Process

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Employees Provident Fund Act, 1952 - Industry - Character - Determination - Whether respondents factory of Electrical, Mechanical or general engineering products fell within purview of Act? - Proper way to determine content of this entry is that all products which are generally known as electrical engineering products, or mechanical engineering products, or general engineering products, are intended to be covered by entry - Sch.1 - Object - Held, every industry which is engaged in manufacture of electrical engineering products, mechanical engineering products, or general engineering products, falls within purview of Act - It is character of products that helps to determine content of entry - Not process - Appeal allowed.
