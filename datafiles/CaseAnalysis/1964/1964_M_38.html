Mrs. M. N. Clubwala and Another v Fida Hussain Saheb and Others

Supreme Court of India

03 February 1964

Case Analysis

Bench

J.R. Madholkar, K. Subba Rao

Where Reported

1964 Indlaw SC 424; AIR 1965 SC 610; 1964 (2) MLJ(SC) 83; 1964 (2) SCJ 448; [1964] 6 S.C.R. 642

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Municipal Act - Whether plaintiffs-respondents are lessees of appellants or only their licensees? - Right which stall-holders had was to exclusive use of stalls during stated hours and nothing more - Landlords did not part with possession of stalls - Held, intention of parties was to bring into existence merely a license and not a lease and word 'rent' was used loosely for 'fee' - Appeal partly allowed.
