Commissioner of Agricultural Income Tax, Hyderabad v Rajan Ratan Gopal

Supreme Court of India

21 September 1964

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Subba Rao, S.M. Sikri, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1964 Indlaw SC 268; [1966] 59 ITR 728

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Hyderabad Agricultural Income-tax Act, 1950 - Whether co-heirs to an estate are an 'association of individuals' within the meaning of the Act? - To constitute an association of individuals two or more individuals should have joined in the promotion of a joint enterprise with the object of producing income, profits or gains - Held, in the present case, the said test is not satisfied - Collection of the entire income from the estate by one of the sharers or even by a common employee will not make that income an income from a joint venture - Each of the sharers gets his income as an individual and not as an association of individuals - Appeal dismissed.
