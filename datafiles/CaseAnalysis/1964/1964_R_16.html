 Rajkumar Narsingh Pratap Singh Deo v State of Orissa and Another

Supreme Court of India

09 March 1964

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, N. Rajagopala Ayyangar, S.M. Sikri, Kailas Nath Wanchoo, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1964 Indlaw SC 414; AIR 1964 SC 1793; 1965 (31) CutLT 721; [1964] 7 S.C.R. 112

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Keywords: Foreign Jurisdiction Act, 1947

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950 art. 372 - Whether sanad issued is existing law within meaning of art. 372 of constitution r/w cl. 4(b) of O. 31? - Relied on Shree Umaid Mills Ltd. v. Union of India 1962 Indlaw SC 444 - Held, sanad is executive act and cannot be regarded as law - Appeal dismissed.
