 Tata Engineering and Locomotive Company Limited v State of Bihar and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 February 1964

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, N. Rajagopala Ayyangar, S.M. Sikri, Kailas Nath Wanchoo, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1964 Indlaw SC 109; AIR 1965 SC 40; 1964 BLJR 834; [1964] 34 Comp Cas 458; 1964 (1) CompLJ 280; 1964 (1) SCJ 666; [1964] 6 S.C.R. 885

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Keywords: Companies Act, 1913

Summary: Corporate - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 19 and 32 - Company - Doctrine of lifting of corporate veil - Whether Corporations or Company can file petition u/art. 32 like citizens of Company - Whether to allow lifting or piercing of corporate veil? - Corporations and Companies, not being citizens cannot petition u/art. 32 - Lifting veil of corporation cannot be allowed - Petition dismissed.
