Bombay Steam Navigation Company (1953) Private v Commissioner of Income Tax, Bombay

Supreme Court of India

21 October 1964

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Subba Rao, J.C. Shah, S.M. Sikri

Where Reported

1964 Indlaw SC 203; AIR 1965 SC 1201; [1965] 56 ITR 52; [1965] 1 S.C.R. 770

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Indian Income Tax Act, 1922, s.10 (1), 10(2)(iii) and 10(2)(xv) - (A) When is deduction u/ss. 10(2)(iii), 10(2)(xv) or 10(1) of Income Tax Act permissible on an amount already paid as an interest by assesee? - Held, in order to be approved as a deduction, interest must be paid in context of capital borrowed and amount paid must be for purpose of business - Therefore, deduction is permissible as interest was paid while business was being carried on - Appeal allowed.
