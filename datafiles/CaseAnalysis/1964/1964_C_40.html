Commissioner of Income Tax, Gujarat v Ashokbhai Chimanbhai

Supreme Court of India

20 October 1964

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, S.M. Sikri, K. Subba Rao

Where Reported

1964 Indlaw SC 276; AIR 1965 SC 1343; [1965] 56 ITR 42; [1965] 1 S.C.R. 758

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922, ss.3 and 4 - Accrual of income takes place when right thereto vests in assessee - Time of accrual of profits of trading firm - Profits accrue to partners not from day to day or from transaction to transaction but on day fixed for settlement of accounts - Held, right to receive share of profits of firm arose on date of settlement - Held, that no part of profits accrued to family for calendar year, 1955 - Appeal dismissed.
