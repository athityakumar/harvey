Baldev Krishan v The State Of Haryana

Supreme Court of India

28 January 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Kurdukar, Faizan Uddin

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 809; (1997) 4 SCC 486; (1997) SCC (Cr) 575; AIR 1997 SC 1666; 1997 (1) CCR 203; 1997 (2) Crimes 1; 1997 CRLJ 1162; 1997 (1) HLR 282; JT 1997 (2) SC 39; 1997(1) SCALE 509; 1997 (1) SCJ 265; 1997 (3) Supreme 304

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Penal Code, 1860, s.300 - Dowry death - Deceased burnt in her matrimonial - Motive and ill- treatment to deceased proved from statement of her mother and letters written to her aunt - Fact that piece of cloth was found in her mouth militates against accused and completely rules out possibility of accidental burn injuries - Held, Accused in-laws liable to be convicted for murder - Their conviction not bad even though one of the accused, brother in law of deceased was aquitted by giving benefit of doubt.
