Central Arecaunt and Cocoa Marketing and v State Of Karnataka & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

16 September 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Jagannadha Rao, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2917; (1997) 8 SCC 31; AIR 1998 SC 2399; JT 1997 (8) SC 119; 1997(6) SCALE 137; 1997 (107) STC 553; 1997 (8) Supreme 283

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Constitution - Constitution, art. 226 - Writ jurisdiction - Determination of Academic issues - Writ petition challenging Sales tax exemption given only to certain traders - Subsequent notification extending benefit of exemption to all traders - Issue in question thereby becoming acadamic - Whether HC still going into merits and allowing writ petition proper - Exempted traders thereby exposed to tax liability for intervening period - Held, academic issues - Not to be determined - judgment of the HC is set aside - Appeal allowed.
