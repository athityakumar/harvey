Indira v Arumugam and Another

Supreme Court of India

09 September 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Majmudar, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1497; (1998) 1 SCC 614; AIR 1999 SC 1549; JT 1998 (4) SC 279; 1998 (2) MLJ(SC) 49; 1998 (89) RD 506

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Limitation Act, 1963, art. 65 - Adverse possession - Suit based on title for possession - Title established on basis of relevant documents - Held, plaintiffs cannot be non-suited unless defendant proves adverse possession for prescriptive period - Provision u/art. 142 of 1908 Act has undergone metamorphic change in view of art. 65 of 1963 Act - Decision reversed.
