Commissioner of Income Tax v P. V. S. Beedies Private Limited

Supreme Court of India

01 October 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Sen, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2789; (1998) 9 SCC 272; 1999 (155) CTR 538; [1999] 237 ITR 13; JT 1998 (9) SC 118; [1999] 103 TAXMAN 294

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 80(g) and 147(b) - (A) Whether reopening u/s. 147(b) is permissible on basis of a report given by audit department? - Held, yes - Audit party has merely pointed out a fact which has been overlooked by the ITO - Reopening of the case u/s. 147(b) in the facts of this case was on the basis of factual information given by the internal audit party and was valid in law - Appeals allowed.
