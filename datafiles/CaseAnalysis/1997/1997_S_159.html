Chief General Manager, State Bank of India and v Shri Bijoy Kumar Mishra

Supreme Court of India

24 September 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

J.S. Verma, B.N. Kirpal

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 624; (1997) 7 SCC 550; (1997) SCC (L&S) 1778; AIR 1997 SC 3981; 1998 (79) FLR 30; JT 1997 (8) SC 221; 1998 LabIC 129; 1998 (1) LLJ 580; 1997(6) SCALE 222; 1999 (1) SLJ 94; 1997 (5) SLR 609; 1997 (Supp) AWC 713; [1997] Supp4 S.C.R. 214; 1997 (8) Supreme 388; 1997 (3) UPLBEC 1917

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: State Bank of India Act, 1955, s.49; State Bank of India Officers (Determination of Terms and Conditions of Service) Order 1979, para 15 and para 16 - Deemed confirmation - Permissibility - Remaining in probation after expiry of allowed maximum period - No objection by employer - Held, not deemed to have been confirmed.
