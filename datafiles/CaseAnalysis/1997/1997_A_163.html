Bhana Khalpa Bhai Patel v Assistant Collector of Customs, Bulsar, Gujarat and Another

Supreme Court of India

18 November 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Srinivasan, M.M. Punchhi

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1665; (1998) 1 SCC 222; (1998) SCC (Cr) 304; AIR 1998 SC 1487; 1998 (1) CCR 23; 1997 (73) ECR 721; 1997 (96) E.L.T. 211; 1998 (2) G.L.R. 1319; 1998 (1) JCC 7; JT 1997 (9) SC 114; 1997 (23) RLT 455; 1997(7) SCALE 24; 1998 (1) SLT 17; [1997] Supp5 S.C.R. 163; 1997 (9) Supreme 461

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Criminal - Customs Act, 1962, ss. 135(1A) and 135(1B) - Gold Control Act, 1968 - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 30 - Evidence of evasion of custom Duty - Conviction on basis of duly proved statements of other accused persons making reference to accused - And also statement of one of accused who pleaded guilty, if justified - Held, justified - Appeal dismissed.
