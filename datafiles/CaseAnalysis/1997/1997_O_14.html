Oriental Insurance Co. Ltd. v Sunita Rathi & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

04 December 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

J.S. Verma, S.P. Bharucha, A.P. Misra

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1146; (1998) 1 SCC 365; (1999) SCC (Cr) 146; 1998 ACJ 121; AIR 1998 SC 257; 1998 ALJ 89; 1998 (1) CLT 49; [1998] 91 Comp Cas 496; JT 1997 (9) SC 767; 1998 (1) MLJ(SC) 80; 1997(7) SCALE 469; [1997] Supp6 S.C.R. 200; 1998 (1) Supreme 52

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Summary: Motor Vehicles Act, 1988, s.149 - Liability of insurer - Policy of insurance - Date and time of - Issued on accident day but effective some time after accident - Policy clearly mentioning date and also time to be effective - Held, in view of clear mention time in policy occasion to anti time it to previous mid-night does not arise - Insurer not liable.
