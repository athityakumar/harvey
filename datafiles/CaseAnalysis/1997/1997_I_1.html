Commissioner of Income Tax v I. A. E. C. (Pumps) Limited

Supreme Court of India

03 April 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Paripoornan, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2812; (1998) 8 SCC 460; 1998 (150) CTR 126; [1998] 232 ITR 316

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961 - Whether amount paid by respondent-assessee to foreign collaborator for technical know how is a capital expenditure or a revenue expenditure? - Held, as per terms of Agreement assessee only acquired license to use other party's patent and knowledge - Expenditure is revenue in nature - Appeal dismissed.
