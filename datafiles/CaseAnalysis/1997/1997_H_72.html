Hindustan Education Society and Another v Sk. Kaleem Sk. Gulam Nabi and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 March 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, G.T. Nanavati

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2690; (1997) 5 SCC 152; (1997) SCC (L&S) 1210; AIR 1997 SC 2126; 1998 (2) Bom.C.R. 146; 1997 (76) FLR 262; JT 1997 (4) SC 92; 1997 LabIC 2094; 1997 (1) LLJ 1071; 1997 (1) LLN 1028; 1997(3) SCALE 107; 1997 (2) SCJ 220; [1997] 2 S.C.R. 910; 1997 (2) SLJ 204; 1997 (2) SLR 43; 1997 (3) Supreme 292; 1999 (2) UPLBEC 83

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Maharashtra Employees of Private Schools (Conditions of Service) Regulation Act, 1978, s. 5 - Candidate appointed in clear vacancy for 11 months - Whether his appointment is temporary or permanent? - Held, where appointment letter states that appointment of candidate in clear vacancy is purely temporary for a period of 11 months, his appointment has to be taken as purely temporary for limited period and appointment cannot be considered permanent appointment - Therefore, candidate cannot be treated as regularly appointed employee - Appeal allowed.
