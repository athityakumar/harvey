Rajasthan State Road Transport Corporation, Jaipur v Smt. Poonam Pahwa And Others

Supreme Court of India

09 July 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

G.N. Ray, G.T. Nanavati

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2455; (1997) 6 SCC 100; AIR 1997 SC 2951; [1998] 92 Comp Cas 444; JT 1997 (6) SC 142; 1997(4) SCALE 505; [1997] Supp1 S.C.R. 698; 1997 (6) Supreme 314; 1997 (2) UJ 427

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Civil Procedure

Keywords: Maharashtra State Agricultural Lands (Ceiling on Holdings) Act, 1961, Bihar and Orissa Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1930

Summary: Civil Procedure - Insurance - CPC, 1908, O.21, r. 1 - Motor Vehicles Act, 1939, ss.110C, 110CC - Punjab Motor Accidents Claims Tribunal Rules, 1969 , r. 20 -Question relating to interest on decretal amount - Judgment-debtor deposited the compensation amount in Court - No information regarding the same given to the claimants - Order passed allowing interest from date of deposit till decree-holder got information of deposit - Legality of - Held, said order is legal - As per O. 21, r. 1 the judgment-debtor has to give notice to the decree-holder about deposit of decretal amount - Appeal dismissed.
