Balkrishna Ramchandra Kadam v Sangeeta Balkrishna Kadam

Supreme Court of India

04 September 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Venkataswami, A.S. Anand

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2764; (1997) 7 SCC 500; AIR 1997 SC 3562; 1998 (1) Bom.C.R. 462; JT 1997 (7) SC 742; 1997(6) SCALE 25; 1997 (3) SCJ 133; [1997] Supp4 S.C.R. 1; 1997 (8) Supreme 31

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Women & Children

Summary: Family & Personal - Women & Children - Hindu Marriage Act, 1955, s. 27 - (A) Property presented at or about time of marriage - Connotation - Whether considered to be property given to spouse at time of marriage? - (B) Disposal of property given to spouses at or about time of marriage - Matrimonial Court trying any matrimonial cause under Act - Whether has jurisdiction to pass order as to disposal of property? - Held, (A) Does not only mean property given at time of marriage - Before or after marriage also covered by s. 27, so long as it is relatable to marriage - (B) Has jurisdiction to pass order as to disposal of property - Order accordingly.
