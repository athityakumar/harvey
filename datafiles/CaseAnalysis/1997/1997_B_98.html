Rai Bareli Kshetriya Gramin Bank v Bhola Nath Singh & Ors

Supreme Court of India

28 February 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1050; (1997) 3 SCC 657; (1997) SCC (L&S) 865; AIR 1997 SC 1908; 1997 (1) CLR 838; 1997 (2) CLT 185; 1997 (76) FLR 313; JT 1997 (3) SC 717; 1999 (1) LLJ 947; 1997 (3) LLN 59; 1997 LLR 407; 1997(3) SCALE 86; 1997 (1) SCJ 595; [1997] 2 S.C.R. 588; 1997 (2) SLJ 126; 1997 (2) SLR 433; 1997 (3) Supreme 480

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Constitution, art. 226 - Bombay Industrial Disputes Act, 1938 - Scope of Judicial review in writ jurisdiction - Dismissal from service - Permissibility of HC examining evidence and reversing finding of fact recorded by enquiry officer - Held, not permissible - Appeal allowed.
