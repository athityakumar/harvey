Fabril Gasosa and another v Labour Commissioner and Others

Supreme Court of India

31 January 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, S.B. Majmudar

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2352; (1997) 3 SCC 150; (1997) SCC (L&S) 667; AIR 1997 SC 954; 1997 (2) AWC 1146; 1997 (2) CLT 224; 1997 (75) FLR 715; JT 1997 (2) SC 171; 1997 LabIC 912; 1997 (1) LLJ 872; 1997 (2) LLN 55; 1997(1) SCALE 544; 1997 (1) SCJ 369; [1997] 1 S.C.R. 725; 1997 (2) SLR 347; 1997 (2) Supreme 515

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 2(p), 33 C(1), 33 C(2)and 10(1) - Industrial Disputes (Central) Rules, 1957, r. 58 - Bombay Industrial Disputes Act, 1938 - Evidence Act, 1872, ss. 91 and 92 - Settlement of industrial dispute - (A) If ' settlement' in definition u/s. 2(p) of 1947 Act r/w r. 58 of rules contemplates both written as well as oral agrement - (B) Rate of variation dearness allowance (VDA) pre-determined in settlement terms - Period undisputed - Maintainability of claim petition for recovery of VDA arrears u/s. 33 C(1) - Held, only written settlement, no oral agreement can be pleaded to vary, modify or supersede a written settlement - Maintainable as recourse to either s. 10(1) or s. 33 (2) is permissible only when amount due is required to be determined - Appeal disposed of.
