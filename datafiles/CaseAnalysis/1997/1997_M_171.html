State of Maharashtra v Messrs Embee Corporation, Bombay

Supreme Court of India

21 August 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

V.N. Khare, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 584; (1997) 7 SCC 190; AIR 1998 SC 1549; JT 1997 (7) SC 472; 1997(5) SCALE 537; 1997 (107) STC 196; [1997] Supp3 S.C.R. 497; 1997 (7) Supreme 570

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - Central Sales Tax Act, 1956, s. 5(2) - Constitution, art. 286 - Interpretation of Statutes - Expression 'sale or purchase occasions such import' u/s.5(2) of the Act - If require that completed sale should precede import - Held, does not require - Petition dismissed.
