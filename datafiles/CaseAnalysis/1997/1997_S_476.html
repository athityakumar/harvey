Durga Devi and Another v State of Himachal Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

11 April 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, A.S. Anand

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 3038; (1997) 4 SCC 575; (1997) SCC (L&S) 982; AIR 1997 SC 2618; JT 1997 (4) SC 642; 1997 LabIC 2647; 1997 (2) LLN 980; 1997(3) SCALE 546; [1997] 3 S.C.R. 855; 1997 (2) SLR 797; 1997 (4) Supreme 342

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Service - Administrative Tribunals Act, 1985, s. 14 - Jurisdiction of SAT as to interference with selection - Power to judge to compare merits of candidates and fitness for post - Actually a function of duly constituted Selection Committee - Tribunal quashed selection by itself, scrutinising comparative merits of candidates - Tribunal cannot sit as an Appellate Court and quash selection by itself - Appeal disposed of.
