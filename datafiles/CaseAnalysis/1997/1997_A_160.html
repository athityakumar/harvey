Coastal Gases and Chemicals (P) Ltd. v Assistant Collector of Central Excise, Visakhapatnam and Others

Supreme Court of India

01 April 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1750; (1997) 7 SCC 223; 1997 (92) E.L.T. 460; JT 1997 (10) SC 679; 1997 (20) RLT 824

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Excise - Practice & Procedure - Central Excise Act, 1944, s. 11 - B - Central Excise Rules, 1944, r. 11 - Indian Limitation Act, 1908 - Appellant claiming exemption from payment of duty as per notification issued by Govt. - While classification list pending before Asst. Collector of customs for approval duty paid by appellant - Whether appellant can make a refund claim after six months from date of payment of duty or refund claim is barred by limitation u/r. 11 of 1944 Act? - All payments made pending approval of classification list can only be provisional payments - Held, claim for refund which was made even prior to approval of classification list cannot be considered as time - barred u/r. 11 and period provided by r. 11 would run only from date of approval of classification list - Appeal disposed.
