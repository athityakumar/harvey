Associated Stone Industries (Kotah) Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax, Rajasthan

Supreme Court of India

05 February 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Paripoornan, B.P. Jeevan Reddy

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2531; (1997) 3 SCC 323; 1997 (138) CTR 260; [1997] 224 ITR 560; JT 1997 (2) SC 401; 1997(1) SCALE 763; [1997] 1 S.C.R. 957; 1997 (4) Supreme 197; [1997] 90 TAXMAN 553; 1997 (2) TLR 212

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, s. 34(1)(a) - Income Taxes - (A) Whether proceeding initiated under s. 34(1)(a) of the Act, can be sustained for the three years 1954-55, 1955-56 and 1956-57? Held, no - They could be sustained or justified under s. 34(1)(b) of the Act, since the materials on record disclose that the conditions required to be fulfilled under s. 34(1)(b) are satisfied - Therefore, liable to be cancelled - (B) Whether an appeal can lie against an order levying penal interest under s. 18A of the Act for the assessment years 1957-58 to 1961-62? - Held, there was no specific right of appeal against an order levying interest - But, if an appeal is preferred against an order of assessment and interest is levied by the assessment order itself, the assessee can raise the question regarding the exigibility of interest - (C) Whether the appellant-company is entitled to credit of the amount of excess royalty paid by it to the State Govt. in lieu of income tax and super tax liability? - (D) Whether the payment of royalty in excess of Rs. 1,50,000 paid under cl. 18 of the lease deed dated May 2, 1945, is a permissible deduction for the assessment years 1957-58 to 1960-61? - Held, this is a matter for verification from the records and it is for the High Court to apply its mind to the above aspects and render a proper decision - Declined to answer questions Nos. 6 and 7, on the basis of the available records - However remit the matter to the HC to consider questions Nos. 6 and 7 afresh - The matter remitted to the HC - Appeal disposed of.
