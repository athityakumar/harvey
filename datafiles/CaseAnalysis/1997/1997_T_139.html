Smt. Jasbir Kaur Sehgal v The District Judge Dehradun & Ors

Supreme Court of India

27 August 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 589; (1997) 7 SCC 7; AIR 1997 SC 3397; 1997 ALJ 2091; 1997 (31) ALR 340; 1997 (5) ALT(SC) 25; 1997 (3) CLT 398; JT 1997 (7) SC 531; 1998 (1) MLJ(SC) 28; 1997(5) SCALE 579; 1998 (5) SLT 551; 1997 (Supp) AWC 245; [1997] Supp3 S.C.R. 529; 1997 (7) Supreme 642; 1997 (2) UJ 637

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal

Keywords: Oil and Natural Gas Commission, Hindu Adoptions And Maintenance Act, 1956.

Summary: Constitution of India, art.133; Hindu Marriage Act, 1955, s.24 - Maintenance - Hindu wife with unmarried daughter - Held, s.24 cannot have restricted meaning - Wife's right to claim maintenance would include her own maintenance and that of her unmarried daughter - Impleading lower Courts as respondents - Not proper.
