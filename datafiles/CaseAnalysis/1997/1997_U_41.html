Sri Sanjoy Bhattacharjee v Union Of India & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

10 March 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, G.T. Nanavati

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2679; (1997) 4 SCC 283; (1997) SCC (L&S) 1069; AIR 1997 SC 2179; 1997 (1) CLR 842; JT 1997 (3) SC 736; 1997 (2) LLN 973; 1997(3) SCALE 103; 1997 (1) SCJ 644; [1997] 2 S.C.R. 915; 1997 (2) SLJ 207; 1997 (2) SLR 44; 1997 (3) Supreme 319

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution, art.14 and 16(1) - Appointment - Name of candidate appearing in waiting list - Does not confer any right of appointment - Held, direction sought for not to fill up the vacancies having arisen subsequently until the candidates in the waiting list are exhausted, cannot be granted - Petition dismissed.
