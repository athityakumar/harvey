Satnam Singh and Others v Punjab and Haryana High Court and Others

Supreme Court of India

07 February 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

J.S. Verma, S.P. Kurdukar

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 922; (1997) 3 SCC 353; (1997) SCC (L&S) 803; AIR 1997 SC 983; 1997 (1) CLR 811; JT 1997 (2) SC 319; 1997 LabIC 924; 1997(1) SCALE 781; [1997] 1 S.C.R. 1038; 1997 (1) SLR 517; 1997 (2) Supreme 38; 1997 (1) UJ 615

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Financial Matters

Summary: Service - Constitution, arts. 229 and 231 - Punjab and Haryana High Court Establishment (Appointment and conditions of Service) Rules, 1973, rr. 16 and 30 - When rr. 16 and 30 relating to recruitment of Assistants and method of determination of seniority respectively come into force - Do not involve Financial implications - Held, came into force on 01-03-1974 and not on 23-01-1975 on which date approval to rules involving financial implications was given by the President - Appeal dismissed.
