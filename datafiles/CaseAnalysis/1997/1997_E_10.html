Commissioner Of Wealth Tax,Gujarat-Iii, Ahmedabad v Ellis Bridge Gymkhana Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

21 October 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Sen, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1610; (1998) 1 SCC 384; AIR 1998 SC 120; 1997 (143) CTR 138; [1998] 229 ITR 1; JT 1997 (8) SC 585; [1997] Supp4 S.C.R. 626; [1997] 95 TAXMAN 143; 1997 (2) TLR 1072

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Excess Profits Tax Act, 1940, Expenditure Tax Act, 1957, Finance Act, 1981, Charge Of Wealth Tax, Business Profits-tax Act, 1947

Summary: Income Tax & Direct taxes - Wealth Tax Act, 1957, s. 3 - Societies Registration Act, 1860 - Fiscal statute - Club - Interpretation of charging s. 3 of Act - And if extends to 'club' for purpose of wealth Tax assessment as an 'individual' - Held, charging section to be construed strictly - Cannot be stretched to include association of persons like Club - Appeal allowed.
