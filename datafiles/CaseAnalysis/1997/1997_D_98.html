Donat Louis Machado and Others v L. Ravindra and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 December 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Majmudar, S.P. Kurdukar

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1378; (1998) 8 SCC 633; (1998) SCC (Cr) 1729; 2000 (2) ACC 602; 2000 (6) SLT 571

Case Digest

Subject: Insurance

Keywords: Additional Amount

Summary: Insurance - Whether what is the appropriate compensation payable to the claimants on account of the death of their breadwinner on whom they were depending? - Court held, Rs. 6 lakhs can easily be visualised that claimants who are the parents and unmarried sister and who are dependent on him would have got at least 1/3 amount as he would have spent the rest of 2/3 amount of his earnings on his own family which he would have raised and on himself - Appeal partly allowed.
