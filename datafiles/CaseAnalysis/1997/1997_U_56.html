D. Stephen Joseph. v Union Of Lndia & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

25 April 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

G.N. Ray, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1157; (1997) 4 SCC 753; (1997) SCC (L&S) 990; AIR 1997 SC 2602; AIR 1997 SCW 2558; 1997 (76) FLR 509; JT 1997 (5) SC 126; 1997 (2) LLN 998; 1997(3) SCALE 748; [1997] 3 S.C.R. 1040; 1998 (1) SLJ 20; 1997 (4) SLR 465; 1997 (4) Supreme 605

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution, art. 16 - Service - Promotion to post of Asstt. Engineer - 50% reservation for degreeholders of Electrical Engineering or an equivalent - With 3 years' regular service in grade of junior engineer - Determination of experience - Held, experience of 3 years has to be reckoned from date of acquisition of degree - Appeal dismissed.
