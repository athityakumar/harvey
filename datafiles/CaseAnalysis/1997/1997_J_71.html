The State Of Punjab v Justice .S. Dewan(Retired Chief Justice) & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

25 April 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, K. Ramaswamy, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1162; (1997) 4 SCC 569; (1997) SCC (L&S) 1153; AIR 1997 SC 2388; JT 1997 (5) SC 26; 1997 LabIC 2333; 1997 (2) LLJ 357; 1997(3) SCALE 708; [1997] 3 S.C.R. 1027; 1997 (2) SLR 647; 1997 (4) Supreme 329; 1997 (1) UJ 710

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges

Keywords: High Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Act, 1954

Summary: Advocates and Judges - High Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Act, 1954, Part III - Punjab Superior Judicial Service Rules, 1963, r. 16 - Constitution, art. 309 - Retirement of a judge before amended r. 16 came into force - Judge opted for fixation under Part III of Act - Whether Judge entitled to benefit of addition of ten years of practice at bar while computing qualifying service for pension provided u/r. 16 (amended) - Held, Judge not entitled to benefit provided under amended rule - Appeal dismissed.
