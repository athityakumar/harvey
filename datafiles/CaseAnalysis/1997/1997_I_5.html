I.K. Mishra v Union Of India & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

11 July 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2702; (1997) 6 SCC 228; (1997) SCC (L&S) 1654; AIR 1997 SC 3740; 1997 (77) FLR 190; JT 1997 (6) SC 390; 1997 LabIC 2866; 1997 (3) LLN 284; 1997(4) SCALE 646; 1997 (4) SLR 627; 1997 (Supp) AWC 402B; [1997] Supp2 S.C.R. 260; 1997 (2) UJ 396

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution, arts. 226, 309 and 311 - Central Civil Services Pension Rules, 1972, r. 48(b) - Service Rules - Compulsory retirement - Adverse remarks in annual confidential report - (A) Order compulsorily retiring passed after considering entire service record by Review Committee - Challenged as arbitrary - (B) Employee not promoted to higher post despite of passing departmental examination - Effect on adverse entries - Held, cannot be interfered with as not arbitrary - Not wiped out,
