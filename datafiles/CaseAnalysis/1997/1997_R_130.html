Raymond Woollen Mills Limited v Income Tax Officer and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 December 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Sen, S.S.M. Quadri

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2867; (2008) 14 SCC 218; 1999 (152) CTR 418; [1999] 236 ITR 34

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961 - Reopening of the assessment - Assessee was charging to its profit and loss account, fiscal duties paid during the year as well as labour charges, power, fuel, wages, chemicals leading to undervaluation of inventories and understatement of profits - Suppression of material facts - Held, court cannot strike down the reopening of the case in the facts of this case - Open to the assessee to prove that the assumption of facts made in the notice was erroneous - Appeals dismissed.
