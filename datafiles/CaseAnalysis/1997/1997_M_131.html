Ram Kali Devi Smt v Manager, Punjab National Bank, Shamshabad and

Supreme Court of India

01 August 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Kurdukar, S.C. Sen

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1260; (1998) 9 SCC 558; 1997 (31) ALR 222A; 1998 (4) AWC 138B; JT 1998 (8) SC 529B

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Indian Limitation Act, 1908 - Condonation of delay - High Court gave no reasons for condoning delay in filing appeal except a reference to merits of case - Held, merits of case cannot be looked at without condoning delay - Case remanded - Appeal allowed.
