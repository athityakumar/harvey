State of H. P. v Jafli Devi

Supreme Court of India

14 March 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1817; (1997) 5 SCC 301; 1998 (1) CLR 277; JT 1997 (10) SC 677; 1998 (2) LLN 57

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Whether the High Court has rightly directed the appellants to reconsider the case of the son of the deceased, for appointment on compassionate grounds? - HC has over looked the policy laid down by the Govt. in the Office Memorandum D/- 18-01-1990 which directed that the respondent could not be given appointment on compassionate grounds since another son of the deceased employee was already in government service - Application of the respondent seeking appointment for his son had been rightly rejected by the Director of Fisheries - Held, the HC erred by setting aside the said order passed by the Director of Fisheries and by directing the appellants to reconsider the case of the son of respondent for appointment on compassionate grounds - Appeal allowed.
