Collector of Central Excise v Malleable Iron and Steel Castings Company Private Limited

Supreme Court of India

04 December 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Srinivasan, S.C. Sen

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1358; (1998) 9 SCC 410; AIR 1999 SC 1553; 1999 (80) ECR 444; 1998 (100) E.L.T. 8; JT 1998 (8) SC 433

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, s. 5 A, Chap. 1, item 68 - Benefit of exemption - Goods valued up to Rs. 30 lakhs and falling under Tariff Item 68 exempted from taxation - Held, Value of goods taxed under Item 25 cannot be added to value of goods cleared under Item 68 - No benefit of exemption.
