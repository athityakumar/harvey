Commissioner of Income Tax v Stepwell Industries Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

27 August 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Saghir Ahmad, S.C. Sen

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2847; (1997) 7 SCC 655; AIR 1997 SC 3521; 1997 (142) CTR 345; [1997] 228 ITR 171; JT 1997 (7) SC 763; 1997(6) SCALE 66; [1997] Supp3 S.C.R. 580; 1997 (8) Supreme 476; [1997] 94 TAXMAN 280; 1997 (2) TLR 922

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 35 B - Various appeals - Whether assessee was entitled to weighted deduction in respect of commission payments u/s. 35 B? - Held, onus lies on assessee to prove that he is entitled to such deduction - Expenditure should have been incurred outside India by or on behalf of assessee and for purposes of its business - Appeals partly allowed.
