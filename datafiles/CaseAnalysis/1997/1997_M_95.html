Union of India v Madras Telephones Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes Social Welfare Association

Supreme Court of India

13 February 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, S.C. Sen

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 3052; (1997) 10 SCC 226; (1997) SCC (L&S) 1279

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Service

Summary: Service - Telegraph Engineering Service (Class II) Recruitment Rules, 1966, Appendix I (6) - Promotion - Validity - How should eligibility list of candidates, who have passed Departmental Qualifying Examination for consideration of Departmental Promotion Committee be prepared? - Held, clause r/w instructions of Govt., it is clear that eligibility lists have to be prepared according to year of recruitment/appointment - Not on basis of year of confirmation - Preparing elgibility list on basis of year of passing of Departmental Qualifying Examination is invalid - Appeal allowed.
