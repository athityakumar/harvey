I. T. C. Limited v Debts Recovery Appellate Tribunal and Others

Supreme Court of India

19 December 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Jagannadha Rao, S.C. Sen

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1253; (1998) 2 SCC 70; AIR 1998 SC 634; [1998] 92 Comp Cas 1; JT 1997 (10) SC 334; 1998 (2) MLJ(SC) 79B; 1997(7) SCALE 767; [1997] Supp6 S.C.R. 683; 1998 (1) Supreme 90

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Corporate; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Framing Of Issues

Summary: Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 7, r. 11 - Rejection of plaint - Recovery suit - Claim by bank - Ground, fraud or misrepresentation - On basis of letters of credit - Allegations, non-supply of goods by seller to buyers - No specific allegation of presentation of forged or fraudulent documents - Held, is not merely a ground for cause of action - Claim not tenable - Non framing of issue is not a ground to for not considering application under O. 7, r.11.
