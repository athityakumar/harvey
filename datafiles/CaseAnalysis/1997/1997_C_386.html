Amenabai Tayebaly & Ors. v Competent Authorityunder Safema & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

19 November 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Majmudar, M. Jagannadha Rao

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2931; (1998) SCC (Cr) 482; (1998) 1 SCC 703; AIR 1998 SC 484; 1998 (2) Bom.C.R. 289; 1998 CRLJ 685; [1998] 230 ITR 892; JT 1997 (9) SC 200; 1998 (1) RCR(Criminal) 89; 1997(7) SCALE 64; 1998 (1) SLT 51; [1997] Supp5 S.C.R. 246

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Smugglers and Foreign Exchange Manipulators (Forfeiture of Property) Act, 1976, s.11 and 6 - Applicability - Property in name of wife of COFEPOSA detenu - Forfeiture of - Sale of said property by wife - Injunction and undertaking by vendor for not transferring or alienating said property in pending writ proceedings - Held, Not a bona fide purchaser, hit by s.11 - Transaction void - Such a still-born transaction cannot be resurrected by passing an order of fine in lieu of forfeiture, confirming under this writ petition.
