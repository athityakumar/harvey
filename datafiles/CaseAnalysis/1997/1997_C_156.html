MRF Ltd. v Collector of Central Excise, Madras

Supreme Court of India

12 March 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, B.P. Jeevan Reddy, S.P. Kurdukar

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2237; (1997) 5 SCC 104; 1999 (84) ECR 66; 1997 (92) E.L.T. 309; 1997 (20) RLT 656

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excise Rules, 1944, rr. 9-A and 173-C(2)(vi) - Whether CEGAT erred in rejecting assessee's claim for refund of excise duty on differential between price on date of removal and reduced price at which tyres were sold on direction of Govt.? - Subsequent fluctuation in prices of commodity can have no relevance whatsoever so far as liability to pay excise duty is concerned - No merit in appeal brought by assessee - Not entitled to refund - Appeal dismissed.
