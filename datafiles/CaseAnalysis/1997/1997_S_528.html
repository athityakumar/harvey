Kaptan Singh and Others v State of Madhya Pradesh and Another

Supreme Court of India

24 April 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

M.K. Mukherjee, S.P. Kurdukar

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 3236; (1997) 6 SCC 185; (1997) SCC (Cr) 870; AIR 1997 SC 2485; 1997 (2) CCR 109; 1997 (2) Crimes 52; 1997 CRLJ 2987; JT 1997 (5) SC 35; 1997(3) SCALE 607; 1997 (2) SCJ 201; 1997 (4) Supreme 211; 1997 (1) UJ 791

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, s. 401 - Revision against acquittal - Murder case - Trial Court acquittal solely on basis of investigation result - Held, judgment of Trial Court patently wrong causing miscarriage of justice - Setting aside acquittal order by High Court in exercise of its revisional power - Is proper.
