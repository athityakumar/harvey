State of Tamil Nadu and Another v M.R. Alagappan and Others

Supreme Court of India

08 April 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Majmudar, M. Jagannadha Rao

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 943; (1997) 4 SCC 401; (1997) SCC (L&S) 1080; AIR 1997 SC 2006; JT 1997 (4) SC 515; 1997 (2) LLJ 711; 1999 (3) LLN 276; 1997(3) SCALE 464; [1997] 3 S.C.R. 717; 1997 (2) SLR 554; 1997 (4) Supreme 67

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Education; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution Of India, 1950, Arts.16 and 39(d) - Equal pay for equal work - Deputy Agricultural Officer and Agricultural Officer - Duties substantially same - Certain special duties, however, entrusted exclusively to A.O. only - DAO holding non-gazetted post while AO holding gazetted post - Educational qualification different - DAO is promotion while that for AO has direct recruitment - Held, in view of these distinguishing features DAO not entitled to parity of pay scale with AO.
