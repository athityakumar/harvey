Union of India and Another v Delhi Cloth and General Mills Company Limited and Another

Supreme Court of India

06 May 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, S.C. Sen, M. Jagannadha Rao

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1400; (1997) 5 SCC 767; AIR 1997 SC 2429; 1997 (70) ECR 737; 1997 (92) E.L.T. 315; JT 1997 (5) SC 474; 1997 (20) RLT 363; 1997(4) SCALE 251; [1997] Supp1 S.C.R. 150; 1997 (5) Supreme 414

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Central Excises and Salt Act 1944, s.3 - Levy of excise duty was in question - Calcium carbide manufactured for further utilization in production of acetylene gas is neither of a purity that renders it marketable nor packed in airtight containers so as to make it marketable - Held, it is not excisable.
