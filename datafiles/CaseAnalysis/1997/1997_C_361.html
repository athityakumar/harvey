U. P. Co-operative Cane Union Federation Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax

Supreme Court of India

30 January 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2791; (1997) 11 SCC 287; AIR 1999 SC 1597; 1999 (157) CTR 569; [1999] 237 ITR 574; JT 1998 (9) SC 376; [1999] 103 TAXMAN 376; 1999 (2) TLR 165

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Trusts & Associations

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 80 P(2)(a)(i) - Co-Operative Societies Act, 1912 [2] - 'Members' - Co-operative Society - Held, not includes members of a primary co-Operative society which is a member of federated co-operative society seeking exemption - Principle of lifting corporate veil - Not applies.
