Mahesh Kumar Saharia v State Of Nagaland & Ors

Supreme Court of India

14 October 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Venkataswami, A.S. Anand

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 649; (1997) 8 SCC 176; AIR 1998 SC 1561; 1997 (4) CLT 85; [1998] 92 Comp Cas 96; JT 1997 (8) SC 401; 1997(6) SCALE 367; [1997] Supp4 S.C.R. 458; 1997 (9) Supreme 184; 1997 (2) UJ 792

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Corporate; Environment, Wildlife & Animal; Foreigners

Summary: Nagaland Forest Products Ltd. (Acquisition of Shares) Act,1982 s.1- Wtether the Act is ultra vires the powers of Nagaland State Legislature in view of s. 20 of Industries (Development and Regulation) Act - Whether the case can be reffered to the larger bench on the fact that certain aspects were not considered and some others require reconsideration by larger constitutional Bench - Held, the Act is not ultra vires the powers of Nagaland State Legislature in view of s. 20 of Industries (Development and Regulation) Act - Matters cannot be referred to larger bench on this ground.
