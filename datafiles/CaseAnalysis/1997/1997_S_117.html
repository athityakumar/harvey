Commissioner of Income Tax, Gujarat v Sercon Private Limited, Ahmedabad

Supreme Court of India

02 April 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Paripoornan, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1766; (1997) 6 SCC 512; [1998] 229 ITR 120

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961 - Whether view of High Court that tribunal erred in holding that land sold by assessee, subject-matter of this proceeding, is not agricultural land, correct? - Held, in all income tax and allied tax matters which come up before Supreme Court, it is essential for appellant or petitioner, as case may be, to file order rendered by Appellate Tribunal in appeal and also statement of case in cases where such a statement of case is drawn up - If decision is rendered by Supreme Court without looking into such documents, it will be unsatisfactory - Time granted to Revenue to produce relevant documents - Order accordingly.
