State of Haryana and Others v Om Prakash Bhasin (D), By Lrs. and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 September 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, M. Srinivasan

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 3321; AIR 1998 SC 2898; 1998 (33) ALR 83; JT 1997 (8) SC 309; [1997] Supp4 S.C.R. 297

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 4 and 18 - Land Acquisition Amendment Act, 1984, s. 23(1-A) - Land acquisition proceedings - Dispute regarding determination of compensation - Whether solatium at 30% u/s. 23(1-A) of Amending Act and interest at 9% and 15% admissible to respondent? - Award was made prior to 1982 and even District Judge disposed of reference u/s. 18 prior to 1982, interest at 9% and 15% was not admissible to respondent - Held, in view of judgment of Supreme Court in K.S. Paripoornan v. State of Kerala, 1994 Indlaw SC 1227 and K.S. Paripoornan (II) v. State of Kerala, 1994 Indlaw SC 511, respondents are not entitled to grant of any solatium and rate of interest shall be confined to 6% pa only - Appeal disposed of.
