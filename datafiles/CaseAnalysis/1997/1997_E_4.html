Icici Limited v East Coast Boat Builders and Engineers Limited

Supreme Court of India

21 October 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Jagannadha Rao, S.B. Majmudar

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1613; (1998) 9 SCC 728; 1998 (3) RAJ 317

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Practice & Procedure - Municipalities & Local Governments - Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, s. 11 - Whether in an application u/s. 11, authority concerned had jurisdiction to consider dispute sought to be referred for arbitration was arbitrable or not? - Whether on merits disputes raised in present case were covered by arbitration agreement or not? - Held, both issues are interrelated and decision of first issue against respondent may require consideration of second question on all its aspects including dispute regarding escalation of price - Consequently, in order to avoid such truncated decision on main questions in controversy between parties, it is appropriate that entire matter may be referred for decision to a Bench of three Judges of Supreme Court - Order accordingly.
