Collector of Central Excise, Bhubaneswar v Re-Rolling Mills

Supreme Court of India

15 July 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2082; (1998) 9 SCC 185; 1998 (79) ECR 517; 1997 (94) E.L.T. 8; JT 1998 (7) SC 505A; 1997 (22) RLT 8

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Indirect Tax - Customs Act, 1962, s. 28 - Excise Act, 1944, s. 11-A - Issuance of Notice - Challenged.

Held, instant appeal is covered by the decision of SC in Union of India v. Jain Shudh Vanaspati Ltd. 1996 Indlaw SC 188. In that case the Court was dealing with s. 28 of the 1962 Act which is in pari materia with s. 11-A of the 1944 Act. The said decision is thus applicable to the present case also. Appeal dismissed.
