Radha Kishun v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 February 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1051; (1997) 9 SCC 239; (1997) SCC (L&S) 1185; JT 1997 (4) SC 116; 1997 (1) LLJ 1165; 1997 (1) LLN 1030; 1997(2) SCALE 616; 1997 (1) SCJ 613; [1997] 2 S.C.R. 582; 1997 (2) SLJ 130; 1997 (2) SLR 418; 1997 (3) Supreme 278

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Whether, the petitioner has any right to claim salary and other benefits as he continued in service after his superannuation? - Held, petitioner worked during period of superannuation, when he is not to continue to be in service as per law, he has no right to claim the salary - No illegality in the action taken by the authorities in refusing to grant the benefits to the petitioner - Govt. of India to take disciplinary action against all concerned persons - Petition dismissed.
