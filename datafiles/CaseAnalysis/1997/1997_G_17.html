Assam State Electricity Board & Ors v Gajendra Nath Pathak & Ors

Supreme Court of India

03 September 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 3083; (1997) 11 SCC 3; AIR 1997 SC 3385; 1997 (77) FLR 459; JT 1997 (7) SC 666; 1997 LabIC 3339; 1997(5) SCALE 664; 1999 (1) SLJ 73; 1997 (6) SLR 119; 1997 (8) Supreme 157; 1997 (3) UPLBEC 1729

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Constitution of India, Arts.14 and 16; Electricity (Supply) Act, 1948, s.79(c); Assam State Electricity Board Engineering Service Regulations, 1973, Regn.1 - Promotion - Post of Executive Engineer - Classification on basis of educational qualification - Diploma holders and non-diploma holders or between diploma holders and graduate engineers - Reasonable, whether - Held, is reasonable - Valid.
