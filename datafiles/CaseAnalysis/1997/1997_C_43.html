Kasambahi F. Ghanchi v Chandubhai D.Rajput and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 November 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

J.S. Verma, B.N. Kirpal, M. Jagannadha Rao

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2717; (1998) 1 SCC 285; AIR 1998 SC 815; 1998 (1) Bom.C.R. 679; 1998 (2) G.L.R. 1304; JT 1997 (9) SC 419; 1998 (2) KLT 24; 1998 (1) MahLJ 1; 1998 (1) MPLJ 1; 1997(7) SCALE 248; 1998 (1) SLT 174; [1997] Supp5 S.C.R. 401; 1997 (10) Supreme 234

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Election - Gujarat Municipalities Act, 1964, s. 33 - Gujarat Municipalities (Reservation of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Backward Class and Women for Office of President) Rules, 1994, rr. 2 and 3 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 243 T - Reservation in election of president Municipality - Seat reserved for backward class candidate but councillor belonging to backward class elected from unreserved seat - If he can stand for election for post of President - Held, candidate for that post need not be person elected from seat reserved for backward class only - Petition dismissed.
