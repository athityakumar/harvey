Ess Ess Kay Engineering Company P. Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax

Supreme Court of India

23 July 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Sen, S.P. Kurdukar

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 3275; (2001) 10 SCC 189; 2001 (166) CTR 396; [2001] 247 ITR 818

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income-tax Act, 1961, s.147(a) - Assessment - Reopen assessment - Whether Income-tax Officer could proceed to reopen the case - Held, there was material on basis of which Income-tax Officer could proceed to reopen the case - Assessee was accepted as correct in original assessment for A/Y - It does not preclude Income-tax Officer to reopen assessment of an earlier year on basis of his findings of fact made on the basis of fresh materials in the course of assessment of next A/Y - Appeal dismissed.
