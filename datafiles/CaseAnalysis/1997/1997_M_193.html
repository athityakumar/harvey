State of Uttar Pradesh and Others v Ministerial Karamchari Sangh

Supreme Court of India

15 October 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Venkataswami, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1585; (1998) SCC (L&S) 287; (1998) 1 SCC 422; AIR 1998 SC 303; 1998 ALJ 94; 1998 (78) FLR 204; JT 1997 (8) SC 415; 1998 LabIC 407; 1998 (1) LLN 321; 1997(6) SCALE 388; 1997 (5) SLR 769; [1997] Supp4 S.C.R. 484; 1997 (9) Supreme 1; 1997 (3) UPLBEC 2125

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution - Service - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 39(d) and 16 Whether 'Equal pay for equal work' is applicable in matter of Lower Division and Upper Division Assistants in Secretariat vis-a-vis Lower Division and Upper Division Assistants (Clerical Cadre) in Directorate of Information? - Held, no - Though they are persons holding same posts performing similar work, their mode of recruitment, qualification and promotion is different - Reasonable ground for fixing different scales - Appeal allowed.
