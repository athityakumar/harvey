Sharad Vasant Kotak and Others v Ramniklal Mohanlal Chawda and Another

Supreme Court of India

17 December 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Venkataswami, S.C. Sen

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2977; (1998) 2 SCC 171; AIR 1998 SC 877; 1998 (3) Bom.C.R. 369; 1998 (1) CLT 29; JT 1997 (10) SC 174; 1997(7) SCALE 640; 1998 (1) SLT 500; [1997] Supp6 S.C.R. 543; 1998 (1) Supreme 35

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance

Keywords: Debt Laws

Summary: Partnership Act, 1932, ss. 69(2 A), 58, 59, 60, 61, 62 and 63 - Scope - Registered firm - Death of one partner - New partner introduced in place of him - Held, amounts to reconstitution of existing firm - Fresh registration not necessary - Failing to inform Registrar of Firms about change - Attracts only penalties - Registration of firm does not get ceased - Object of Maharashtra Amendment not militated.
