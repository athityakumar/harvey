Mrs. Manju Bhatia & Anr. v New Delhi Municipal Council & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

06 May 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, S. Saghir Ahmad, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1420; (1997) 6 SCC 370; AIR 1998 SC 223; 1998 (1) AWC 32; 1998 (3) Bom.C.R. 364; JT 1997 (5) SC 574; 1998 (1) MLJ(SC) 55; 1997(4) SCALE 350; [1997] Supp1 S.C.R. 156; 1997 (5) Supreme 579

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Tort

Keywords: Breach Of Duty, Tortious Liability

Summary: Constitution of India, art.226 - Rule of equity - Applicability - Unauthorised construction - Building constructed and flats therein sold - Some unauthorised constructed floors of building demolished later - Flat owners had no information about illegal construction - Held, flat owners entitled to be re-compensated for loss suffered by them - Direction to Builder to pay back paid amount of flat owners plus certain amount on account of escalation of price.
