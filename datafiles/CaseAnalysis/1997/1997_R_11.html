R. Kandasmy v Chief Engineer, Madras Port Trust

Supreme Court of India

22 August 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 585; (1997) 7 SCC 505; (1997) SCC (L&S) 1825; JT 1997 (7) SC 660; 1999 (1) SLJ 108; [1997] Supp3 S.C.R. 516; 1997 (3) UPLBEC 1727

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Appellant called for interview for appointment to post of mazdoor by respondent - Respondent did not accept community certificates issued by Tehsildar and asked him to produce certificate issued by Revenue Divisional Officers - Challenged - Held, community certificate issued to a schedule Tribe candidate by Tehsildar prior to 11-11-1989 is a good and valid community certificates not cancelled - Authorities cannot decline to take that into consideration and insists upon a fresh community certificate from Revenue Divisional Officers - Appeal allowed.
