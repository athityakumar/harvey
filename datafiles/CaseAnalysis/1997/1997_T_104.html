T.N. Godavarman Thirumulkpad Etc v Union Of India & Ors. Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

04 March 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1140; (1997) 3 SCC 312; AIR 1997 SC 1233; 1997 (2) CLT 283; JT 1997 (3) SC 338; 1997(2) SCALE 435; [1997] 2 S.C.R. 642; 1997 (2) Supreme 634

Case Digest

Subject: Environment, Wildlife & Animal

Summary: Environment, Wildlife and Animal - Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980, s. 2 - Forest conservation - Orders passed by Supreme Court - Analysis of Aftermath - (A) Strict implementation of order, in North-Eastern region of India - (B) Applicability of directions given by SC, D/-12-12-96 to minor forest produce including bamboos - (C) Unlicensed Saw, Veneer and Plywood industries in Maharashtra and UP - (D) Scope of SC orders D/- 12-12-1996 and D/- 04-03-1997 - Held, high power committee directed to do constituted - Not to apply - Directed to be closed and no fresh licence to be granted - Shall apply to all Autonomous Hills councils in North-Eastern States as well as Union Territories - Application allowed.
