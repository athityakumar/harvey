Noor Saba Khatoon v Mohd. Quasim

Supreme Court of India

29 July 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1969; (1997) 6 SCC 233; (1997) SCC (Cr) 924; AIR 1997 SC 3280; 1998 (1) Bom.C.R. 340; 1998 (3) CCR 84; 1997 (3) Crimes 106; 1997 CRLJ 3972; 1998 (2) DMC 322; JT 1997 (7) SC 104; 1997 (2) KLT 363; 1997(5) SCALE 248; 1998 (5) SLT 516; [1997] Supp3 S.C.R. 129; 1997 (6) Supreme 523

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Family & Personal; Women & Children

Keywords: Marriage & Divorce, Minority, Adoption & Guardianship

Summary: Family and Personal - CrPC, 1973, s. 125 - Muslim Women (Protection of Rights on Divorce) Act, 1986, s. 3 - Majority Act, 1875 - Right to maintenance - Scope and extent of right of muslim children - When childern living with divorced wife - Held, entitled till they attain majority or are able to maintain themselves or female get married - Immateial - Appeal allowed.
