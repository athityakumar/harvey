State of Rajasthan v Hitendra Kumar Bhatt

Supreme Court of India

01 August 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar, M. Jagannadha Rao

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 347; (1997) 6 SCC 574; (1997) SCC (L&S) 1744; AIR 1998 SC 91; AIR 1997 SCW 4043; 1997 (77) FLR 240; JT 1997 (7) SC 287; 1998 LabIC 378; 1998 (2) LLJ 59; 1997 (3) LLN 70; 1997(5) SCALE 393; 1998 (1) SLJ 56; 1997 (Supp) AWC 249; [1997] Supp3 S.C.R. 224; 1997 (7) Supreme 128; 1997 (2) UJ 400

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16 - Recruitment - Selection - Determination of eligibility - From date of application as per advertisement - Respondent not eligible on said date - Appointed in view of interim order of Court - Made subject to outcome of petition - Held, service liable to be discontinued - Sympathetic view - Is injustice - To others who had not applied because of cut-off date - Petition dismissed.
