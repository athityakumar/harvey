Choksi Tube Company Limited v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 January 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, Faizan Uddin

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2340; (1997) 11 SCC 179; 1998 (77) ECR 38; 1998 (97) E.L.T. 404

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Customs Act, 1962, s. 25(1) - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 - Notification No. 256-Cus/76 - Whether action of Board in declining to give to petitioner relief that had been given in identical circumstances to another mill was arbitrary and against provisions of art. 14 of Constitution? - Held, there was neither any public interest nor any circumstances of exceptional nature involved and that others placed in circumstances similar to that in which said mills were placed must have same benefit as was advanced to other mills - Respondents directed to grant to petitioners same benefit that was granted to said mills - Petition allowed.
