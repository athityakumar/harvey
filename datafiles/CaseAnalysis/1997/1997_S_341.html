Dr. Sadhna Devi & Ors. v State Of U.P. & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

19 February 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Sen, B.P. Jeevan Reddy

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 843; (1997) 3 SCC 90; AIR 1997 SC 1120; AIR 1997 SCW 1146; 1997 ALJ 677; 1997 (3) AWC 1736; JT 1997 (3) SC 255; 1997(2) SCALE 189; [1997] 2 S.C.R. 186; 1997 (2) SLR 72; 1997 (2) Supreme 351; 1997 (1) UJ 458; 1997 (2) UPLBEC 981

Case Digest

Subject: Education

Keywords: Indian Medical Council (Amendment) Act, 1993

Summary: Education - Constitution, arts. 32 and 226 - Reservation for SC, ST and OBCin admission to PG Medical Courses - Dispensing with requirement of obtaining minimum qualifying marks in written examination held for admission, by Govt. - Held, not permissible - Petition disposed of.
