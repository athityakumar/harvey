D.N. Venkatarayappa And Another v State Of Karnataka And Others

Supreme Court of India

09 July 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, D.P. Wadhwa

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 3062; (1997) 7 SCC 567; AIR 1997 SC 2930; 1997 (3) CLT 174; JT 1997 (6) SC 155; 1997(4) SCALE 715; [1997] Supp2 S.C.R. 187; 1997 (6) Supreme 494; 1997 (2) UJ 416

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Land and Property - Limitation Act, 1963, arts. 64 and 64 - Karnataka Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes (Prohibition of Transfer of Certain Lands) Act, 1979 - Karnataka SCs and STs Act, s. 5 - Land allotted with prohibition on alienation - Land purchased from original allottee - Subsequently Karnataka Act passed that prohibited alienation of land upto particular period - Ejectment proceedings initiated against purchaser under Act - Whether purchaser proved adverse possession - Held, purchaser failed to disclaim title under which he came in possession and set up title open and hostile to knowledge of true owner - Plea of adverse possession not proved - Appeal dismissed.
