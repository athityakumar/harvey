State Through C.B.I v Dawood Ibrahim Kaskar and Others

Supreme Court of India

07 May 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

M.K. Mukherjee, B.N. Kirpal, G.T. Nanavati

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 3282; (1997) SCC (Cr) 636; (2000) 10 SCC 438; AIR 1997 SC 2494; 1997 (2) CCR 46; 1997 (2) Crimes 92; 1997 CRLJ 2989; JT 1997 (5) SC 651; 1997 (3) RCR(Rent) 567; 1997(4) SCALE 156; 1997 (2) SCJ 387; [1997] Supp1 S.C.R. 212; 1997 (4) Supreme 490

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Socio-Economic

Keywords: Non-bailable Warrant

Summary: Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, ss. 309(2) and 167 - Words 'accused if in custody' - Meaning of - Court taking cognizance of offence - Accused arrested subsequently by police during further investigation - Held, Court can detain in police custody in exercise of its power u/s. 167 - Warrant of arrest could not be issued by Court solely for production of accused before police in aid of investigation.
