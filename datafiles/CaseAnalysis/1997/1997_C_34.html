Chairman, Railway Board and another v C. R. Rangadhamaiah and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 July 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, S.P. Bharucha, J.S. Verma, M.M. Punchhi

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2126; (1997) 6 SCC 623; (1997) SCC (L&S) 1527; AIR 1997 SC 3828; JT 1997 (7) SC 180; 1998 (3) SLJ 76; 1997 (4) SLR 759; [1997] Supp3 S.C.R. 63; 1997 (7) Supreme 408; 1997 (3) UPLBEC 2217

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Computation Of Pension, Running Allowance, Railway Services (Revised Pay) Rules, 1973

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 309, 14, 16, 31(1) and 19(1)(f) (since omitted) - Railway Establishment Code, Vol II, r. 2544 - Pension and retrial benefits - Computation in case of 'running staff' of Railways - Consideration of 'running allowance' granted to them - Notifications retrospective - Reducing maximum limit from 75% to 45% for period from January 1, 1973 to March 31, 1979 and to 55% from April 1, 1979 onwards - Held, violating arts. 31(1), 19(1)(f), 14 and 16 - Unreasonable and arbitrary.
