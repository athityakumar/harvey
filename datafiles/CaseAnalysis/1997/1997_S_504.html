K.Jayamohan v State of Kerala and Another

Supreme Court of India

25 April 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, D.P. Wadhwa

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 894; (1997) 5 SCC 170; (1997) SCC (L&S) 1140; AIR 1997 SC 2619; 1997 (76) FLR 606; JT 1997 (5) SC 368; 1997 LabIC 2648; 1997(4) SCALE 155; 1997 (2) SCJ 36; [1997] 3 S.C.R. 1046; 1998 (1) SLJ 19; 1997 (4) SLR 313; 1997 (4) Supreme 608

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution, 1950 - Entitlement to right to appointment - When appointments made to a no. of posts advertised but the candidate in question still kept in waiting list - Whether he can claim right of appointment on this ground - Held, cannot claim - Appeal dismissed.
