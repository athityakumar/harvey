Baleshwar Tewari/& Ors. v Sheo Jatan & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

20 March 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1090; (1997) 5 SCC 112; AIR 1997 SC 2089; JT 1997 (5) SC 685; 1997(3) SCALE 381; [1997] 3 S.C.R. 140; 1997 (4) Supreme 235

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Evidence Act, 1872, s.35; Bihar Land Reforms Act, 1950, s.6(1) - Possession of khas by intermediary - Contemplates actual possession without any dilution - Sale of land by intermediary - Land sold was in possession of lessee for years - Purchaser getting possession only in execution of decree - Held, no assumption for khas possession of intermediary - Entry in revenue record - Evidentiary value - Attitude of tiller towards revenue entry - Value that would be attached to such entry.
