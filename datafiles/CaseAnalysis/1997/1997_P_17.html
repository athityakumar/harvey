E.S.I. Corporation & Anr. v P.K. Srinivasmurthy & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

23 July 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1819; (1997) 11 SCC 533; AIR 1997 SC 2983; 1997 (77) FLR 219; JT 1997 (7) SC 11; 1997 LabIC 2878; 1997 (2) LLJ 760; 1997(5) SCALE 64; 1998 (1) SLJ 88; 1997 (4) SLR 749; 1997 (7) Supreme 585

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Service - Constitution, art.16 - Pay scale - Stepping up - Removal of anomaly of senior drawing less pay than junior - Upper Division Clerk working as Cashier senior to UDC in-charge - UDC in-charge on promotion as Head Clerk getting his pay fixed on basis of revised scale - UDC Cashier not entitled to revision of his pay, since pay of UDC Cashier and UDC in-charge not same - Appeal allowed.
