M/S. Serai Kella Glass Work Pvt. Ltd. v Collector Of Central Excise, Patna

Supreme Court of India

08 April 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Sen, K.T. Thomas

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2140; (1997) 4 SCC 641; AIR 1997 SC 1948; 1997 (70) ECR 230; 1997 (91) E.L.T. 497; JT 1997 (4) SC 535; 1997 (19) RLT 821; 1997(3) SCALE 454; [1997] 3 S.C.R. 700; 1997 (4) Supreme 1

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Indian Limitation Act, 1908 - Central Excise Rules 1944, r. 173-I - Central Excises and Salt Act 1944, ss. 11 A and 37 - Provisional assessment was quashed by High Court while fresh assessments was ordered - Held, there was no question of issuing show cause notice as it is necessary only in case of short levy or non - levy of duty after final assessment and payment of duty is made.
