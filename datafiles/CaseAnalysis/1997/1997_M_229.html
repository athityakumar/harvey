Mr. P. Sriramamurthy v Mrs. Vasantha Raman

Supreme Court of India

07 February 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 925; (1997) 9 SCC 654; AIR 1997 SC 1388; 1997 (2) MLJ(SC) 46; 1997 (1) RCJ 417; 1997 (1) RentLR 209; 1997(2) SCALE 171; 1997 (1) SCJ 611; [1997] 1 S.C.R. 1045; 1997 (2) Supreme 495; 1997 (1) UJ 491

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - T.N. Buildings (Lease and Rent Control) Act, 1960, s. 10(2)(i) - Constitution, art. 133 - Eviction sought on ground of willful default in payment of rent - Lease granted for specific period - Landlord staying abroad - During pendency of proceedings landlord meet with accident and require premises to come back and stay in India - Whether eviction to be granted considering said subsequent events - SC granted eviction on ground of personal necessity - Appeal disposed of.
