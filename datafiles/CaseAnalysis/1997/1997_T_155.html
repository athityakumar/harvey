Smt. Bhagti (Deceased) Through Her L.Rs. Jagdish v The State Of Haryana

Supreme Court of India

27 January 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, G.T. Nanavati

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 801; (1997) 4 SCC 473; AIR 1997 SC 1793; 1997 (2) CLT 67; JT 1997 (2) SC 291; 1997 (2) MLJ(SC) 108; 1997(1) SCALE 788; 1997 (1) SCJ 299; [1997] 1 S.C.R. 576; 1997 (2) Supreme 734; 1997 (1) UJ 632

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Land and Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s.18, 23, 28 A - Indian Limitation Act, 1908 - Time-limit for filing application for re- Determination of compensation - It must be made within three months' limitation period prescribed - Application can be made only on basis of judgment of the Reference Court u/s 18 enhancing compensation in an award, and not that of HC - - Petition dismissed.
