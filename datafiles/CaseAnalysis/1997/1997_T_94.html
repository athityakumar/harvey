T. M. Jacob v C. Poulose and Others

Supreme Court of India

18 December 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

V.N. Khare, B.N. Kirpal, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1241; (1998) 2 SCC 31; AIR 1998 SC 2939; JT 1998 (4) SC 361

Case Digest

Subject: Election

Summary: Election - Representation of People Act, 1951, ss. 81(3) and 86(1) - Practice & Procedure - Copy of affidavit supplied with notice of election petition - Name and designation of notary not indicated - Seal and stamp of Notary also absent - Whether there is non-compliance of s. 81(3) of 1951 Act and principle indicated in certain precedent (1996 INDLAW SC 630)? - Held, papers be laid before Chief Justice for constitution of a larger bench.
