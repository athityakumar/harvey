Collector of Central Excise, Hyderabad v Messrs Bakelite Hylam Limited Etc

Supreme Court of India

10 March 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2650; (1997) 10 SCC 350; AIR 1997 SC 1988; 1997 (69) ECR 193; 1997 (91) E.L.T. 13; JT 1997 (3) SC 639; 1997 (19) RLT 203; 1997(2) SCALE 732; [1997] 2 S.C.R. 825; 1997 (4) Supreme 49

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Central Excises and Salt Act 1944, Sch.1, Item 15A(2), Item 68 ; Central Excise Tariff Act 1986, Sch. Heading No. 3920.31, Heading No.3920.37 ; Central Excise Tariff Act 1986, Sch. Heading No. 7014/8546 - Can laminated sheets made up of composite material consisting of either paper or glass fabric and also resins be classified under Entry 15-A(2) - Where can decorative laminated sheets be classified - Where are industrial laminates and glass epoxy laminates classified - Held, laminated sheets made up of composite material can be classified under residuary Entry 68 and not under Entry 15-A(2) - Decorative laminated sheets can be classified under Tariff Entry 3920.31/3920.37 and not under Entry 4818.90/,4823.90 - Classification of industrial laminates and glass epoxy laminates under Tariff Item 8546 of New Customs Tariff and under Tariff Entry 7014 under New Customs Tariff was proper.
