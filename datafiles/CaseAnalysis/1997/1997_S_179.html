Shri Kartikeya V.Sarabhai v Commissioner of Income Tax

Supreme Court of India

04 September 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, K.T. Thomas

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2763; (1997) 7 SCC 524; AIR 1997 SC 3794; 1997 (142) CTR 150; [1997] 228 ITR 163; JT 1997 (7) SC 709; 1997(5) SCALE 705; 1997 (3) SCJ 165; 1998 (5) SLT 569; [1997] Supp3 S.C.R. 746; 1997 (8) Supreme 195; [1997] 94 TAXMAN 164

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Cumulative Preference Shares

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 2(47) and 45 - Companies Act, 1956, ss. 87(2)(a) and 100(1)(c) - Determination of taxability of capital gains - Arising out of amount paid on reduction of share capital to share-holder of company - When reduction in face value results in extinguishment of right in shares - Held, exigible to capital gain tax - Appeal dismissed.
