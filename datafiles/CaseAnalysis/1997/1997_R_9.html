R. K. Sethi and Another v Oil and Natural Gas Commission and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 January 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, Faizan Uddin

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2338; (1997) 10 SCC 616; (1997) SCC (L&S) 1720; AIR 1997 SC 899; 1997 (75) FLR 737; JT 1997 (2) SC 201; 1997(1) SCALE 587; [1997] 1 S.C.R. 616; 1997 (2) SLR 120; 1997 (3) Supreme 557

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Oil and Natural Gas Commission, Executive Instructions

Summary: Service - Oil and Natural Gas Commission Act, 1959, s. 32 - ONGC Terms and Conditions of Appointment and Service Regulations, 1975, regn. 19, cls. H(iii), B(iii) and (b) - Fixation of inter se seniority for promotion on merger of two cadres - Category of Telex Operators redesignated as Asst. Grade-II (AG-II) in view of fitment policy - Meaning and effect of redesignation - Held, means merger of cadres of Telex Operator into cadre of AG-II and not promotion - Their seniority in cadre of AG-II will be governed by cl. H(iii) and not by cl. B(iii)(b) - Appeal dismissed.
