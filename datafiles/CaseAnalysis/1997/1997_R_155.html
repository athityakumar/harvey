Ashis Kumar Hazra v Rubi Park Co-Operative Housing Society Limited

Supreme Court of India

05 May 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, D.P. Wadhwa

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2978; (1997) 6 SCC 26; AIR 1997 SC 2724; 1997 (2) CLT 495; JT 1997 (5) SC 589; 1997(4) SCALE 301; [1997] Supp1 S.C.R. 85; 1997 (6) Supreme 9

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice and Procedure - Limitation Act, 1963, ss. 5 and 14 - Notice for arbitration Dispute among Co-operatice Society and its members - Condonation of delay in filing application after prescribed limitation of two months - Bona fide proceedings before Civil Court - Maintainability - Held not maintainable - Time spent till disposal of matters has to be excluded - However, explanation for period of delay from date of notice till date of filing Civil suit not given - If this delay can be condoned - Delay cannot be condoned - Petition dismissed.
