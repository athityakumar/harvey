Garhwal Jal Sansthan Karmachari Union & Another v State Of U.P. & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

27 March 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Paripoornan, S.P. Kurdukar

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1119; (1997) 4 SCC 24; (1997) SCC (L&S) 929; AIR 1997 SC 2143; 1997 ALJ 1175; 1997 (1) CLR 850; 1997 (76) FLR 114; JT 1997 (4) SC 206; 1997 LabIC 2101; 1997 (2) LLJ 147; 1997(3) SCALE 325; [1997] 3 S.C.R. 376; 1998 (1) SLJ 126; 1997 (2) SLR 600; 1997 (2) UPLBEC 1209

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Uttar Pradesh Water Supply and Sewerage Act, 1975

Summary: Uttar Pradesh Water Supply And Sewerage Act, 1975, s.18; Constitution of India, Arts.16 and 39(d) - Discrimination - Claim by employees for same pay scale - Held, Nigam and sansthan are two independent body corporate though some of their functions overlap - Different Jurisdictions - Duties assigned to employees of Nigam and Sansthan are qualitatively different - Claim for equal pay based on principle of equal pay for equal work - Not sustainable.
