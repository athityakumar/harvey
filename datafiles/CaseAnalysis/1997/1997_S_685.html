State of Uttar Pradesh and Another v Jogendra Singh and Another

Supreme Court of India

28 November 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

Ms. Justice Sujata V. Manohar, M. Jagannadha Rao

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1725; (1998) SCC (L&S) 300; (1998) 1 SCC 449; AIR 1998 SC 658; 1998 ALJ 293; 1998 (1) AWC 660; 1998 (1) CLR 393; 1998 (78) FLR 287; JT 1997 (9) SC 395; 1998 LabIC 889; 1998 (1) LLN 42; 1997(7) SCALE 299; 1998 (1) SLR 95; 1998 (1) SLT 127; [1997] Supp5 S.C.R. 581; 1997 (10) Supreme 272; 1998 (1) UPLBEC 8

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Voluntary Retirement, Retirement

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 309 and 16 - Uttar Pradesh Fundamental Rules, r. 56(e), Proviso (as added by Amendment of 1976) - Voluntary retirement - Employee receiving retrial benefits under rules then in force - Subsequent amendment by adding proviso - Claim, retirement benefits for additional service in persuasion of proviso - Held, amendment does not has retrospective effect - No entitlement to benefit of proviso having retired prior to its insertion.
