Chemical and Fibres of India Limited v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

07 January 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar, A.M. Ahmadi

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 286; (1997) 2 SCC 664; AIR 1997 SC 558; 1997 (68) ECR 491; 1997 (89) E.L.T. 633; 1997(1) SCALE 109; [1997] 1 S.C.R. 71

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Central Excises and Salt Act 1944, Sch. I, Item 15 A - Plastics - Assessee manufacturing nylon yarn - Processing caprolactum monomer and obtaining at intermediate State a product called 'Nylon chips' which are consumed in manufacture of finished product nylon yarn - Does Item 15 A cover is question? - Held, they are not known as synthetic resins or as plastics so answer is in negative.
