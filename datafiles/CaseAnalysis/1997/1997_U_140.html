Bihar Distillery and Another v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

Case Analysis

Bench

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Excise

Keywords: Industrial Purposes

Summary: Constitution - sch. 7, list 1, entries 7 and 52, list 2, entries 8 and 24, list 3, entry 33 - Industries (Development And Regulation) Act, 1951 - Excise Duty - Demarcation of control over rectified spirit - Among UOI and States - Determination - Demarcation of control stated - Petition disposed Of.
