Pawan Alloys & Casting (P) Ltd. v U.P. State Electricity Board and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 August 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Majmudar, K.T. Thomas

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 1364; (1997) 7 SCC 251; AIR 1997 SC 3910; 1997 (4) CLT 49; JT 1997 (7) SC 224; 1997(5) SCALE 342; [1997] Supp3 S.C.R. 266; 1997 (8) Supreme 81

Case Digest

Subject: Electricity & Energy

Summary: Electricity (Supply) Act, 1948, s. 49 - Notification D/- 31-07-1986 - Incentive development rebate - Grant by board for three years - To new industries - Establishment of appellant industries on basis of said promise - Signing standard agreement for supply of electricity - One clause therein providing revision of general rates - Held, does not confer any further power on board to withdraw development rebate provision within guaranteed period of three years - Fixation of tariff including incentive rebate by board - Is of quasi legislative nature exercising delegated legislative power - Notification has no retrospective effect - Not liable to be struck down.
