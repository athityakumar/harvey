Commissioner of Income Tax v Alcock Ashdown and Company Limited and Another

Supreme Court of India

05 February 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Paripoornan, B.P. Jeevan Reddy

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2533; (1997) 3 SCC 358; AIR 1997 SC 1997; 1997 (139) CTR 1; [1997] 224 ITR 353; JT 1997 (2) SC 278; 1997(1) SCALE 753; [1997] 1 S.C.R. 939; 1997 (2) Supreme 5; [1997] 90 TAXMAN 521; 1997 (2) TLR 577

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Debt Laws

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 84(1) - Capital asset - Capital employed in undertaking - Held, major aspect is acquisition of assets - That itself guarantees assesse to get relief - 'Employment' of capital has nothing to do with actual user - Appeal dismissed.
