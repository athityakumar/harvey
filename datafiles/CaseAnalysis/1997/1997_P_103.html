Dwarikesh Sugar Industries Limited v Prem Heavy Engineering Works Private Limited and Another

Supreme Court of India

07 May 1997

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, K.S. Paripoornan, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1997 Indlaw SC 2066; (1997) 6 SCC 450; AIR 1997 SC 2477; 1997 ALJ 1425; 1997 (2) ARBLR 350; 1997 (3) AWC 1963; 1997 (2) CLT 468; [1997] 89 Comp Cas 619; JT 1997 (5) SC 417; 1998 (1) MLJ(SC) 62; 1997(4) SCALE 103; [1997] Supp1 S.C.R. 184; 1997 (5) Supreme 291

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Corporate; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Contract Act, 1872, s. 126 - Contract - Bank guarantee - Restraining bank from making payment - Alleging fraud in application for injunction - Plaint of suit not alleges so against beneficiary - Held, no grant of injunction on bald assertion - Allegation made only to obtain order of injunction.
