State Of Punjab v Renuka Singla

Supreme Court of India

26 November 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

N.P. Singh, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 802; (1994) 1 SCC 175; AIR 1994 SC 595; AIR 1993 SCW 330; JT 1993 (6) SC 524; 1993(4) SCALE 564; 1994 (1) SLR 257; 1994 (1) UJ 119

Case Digest

Subject: Health & Drug; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Education - Service - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 32 and 226 - Dentists Act, 1948, ss. 10 A and 10 B - Appeal against High Court direction to admit students in BDS Course on compassionate ground - Held, direction set aside as HC cannot disturb balance between capacity of institution and no of admissions on that ground as it amounts to a direction to violate ss. 10 A and 10 B(3) of Dentists Act - Appeal allowed.
