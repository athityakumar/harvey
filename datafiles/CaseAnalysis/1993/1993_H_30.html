Hindustan Lever v Hindustan Lever Mazdoor Sabha

Supreme Court of India

28 September 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jayachandra Reddy, G.N. Ray

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1504; (1994) Supp1 SCC 1; (1994) SCC (L&S) 380; AIR 1994 SC 834; 1994 (1) Bom.C.R. 584; 1993 (67) FLR 1218; JT 1993 (5) SC 459; 1994 LabIC 70; 1994 (1) LLJ 668; 1994 (1) LLN 884; 1993(3) SCALE 894; 1993 (5) SLR 611; [1993] Supp2 S.C.R. 540; 1993 (2) UJ 760

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Bombay Industrial Relation Act, 1946, House Rent Allowance Act, 1983, Maharashtra Private Security Guards (Regulations of Employment and Welfare) Act 1981

Summary: Maharashtra Workmen's Minimum House Rent Allowance Act, 1988, s.13; Notification No. BRA 3191 / 992 / LAB, dated 9-10-92 - Notification - Exemption from payment of house rent allowance - No specified period of exemption - Provision contemplates exemption for specific period - Held, ultra vires s.13.
