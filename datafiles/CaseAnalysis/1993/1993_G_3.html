Union of India and Others v G. K. Sangameshwar and Others

Supreme Court of India

13 July 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, B.P. Jeevan Reddy

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1210; (1993) Supp3 SCC 697; (1994) SCC (L&S) 116; AIR 1994 SC 612; 1994 (26) ATC 92; 1993 (2) CLR 836; 1993 (67) FLR 705; JT 1993 (4) SC 264; 1993 LabIC 2268; 1994 (1) LLJ 281; 1993 (2) LLN 662; 1993(3) SCALE 112; 1993 (4) SLR 577; [1993] Supp1 S.C.R. 1

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Indian Administrative Service (Regulations of Seniority) Rules, 1954, r. 3(3)(c), Proviso - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14 and 16 - Indian Administrative Service (Appointment by Selection) Regulations, 1956, Regn. 3, Paragraph 2 - Circular Central Government D/- 06-06-1978 - Seniority - Inter se Seniority - Dependent on length of service - Held, not applicable only to cases where State and non-state Officers are considered for appointment in same year.
