Svenska Handelsbanken v Indian Charge Chrome (Dayal, J.)

Supreme Court of India

15 October 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

Yogeshwar Dayal, B.P. Jeevan Reddy, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 728; (1994) 1 SCC 502; AIR 1994 SC 626; 1994 (1) ALT(SC) 37; 1994 (1) BC 215; [1994] 79 Comp Cas 589; JT 1993 (6) SC 189; 1993(4) SCALE 124

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Corporate; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Civil Procedure Code, 1908, O. 39, r. 1 - Contract Act, 1872, s. 126 - Sale of Goods Act, 1930, ss. 12(3) and 59 - Limitation Act, 1908 - Whether injunction can be granted against Bank guarantee when fraud or irretrievable injury not proved - Held, injunction cannot be granted - Appeal allowed.
