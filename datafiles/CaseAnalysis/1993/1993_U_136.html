Prabhu Dayal and Others v Union of India

Supreme Court of India

20 April 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, R.M. Sahai

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1676; (1995) Supp4 SCC 221; AIR 1994 SC 1451; AIR 1994 SCW 891

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Requisitioning and Acquisition of Immovable Property Act, 1952, s. 23(2) - Enhancement of market value of lands situated on main roads and acquired for widening road - Upward rise in prices - Market value enhanced from Rs. 6 to Rs. 8 per sq yard after deducting 1/3 as development charges - Held, solatium granted @ 15% on market value though not provided for by Act - Appeal allowed.
