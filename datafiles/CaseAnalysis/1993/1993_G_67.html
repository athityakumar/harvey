Gurdeep Singh v State of Jammu and Kashmir and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 March 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

M.N. Venkatachaliah, Yogeshwar Dayal

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1134; (1995) Supp1 SCC 188; (1995) SCC (L&S) 378; AIR 1993 SC 2638; AIR 1993 SCW 3420; 1995 (29) ATC 149B; JT 1993 (Supp) SC 497

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Education - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Admission of MBBS Course - Selection - Reservation of seats for candidates excelling in certain categories of sports specifically notified as approved sports - Sport not inclued in list of approved sports at cut-off date when applications were invited - (A) Whether could later be introduced to provide eligibility retrospectively to a single candidate? - Whether selection of candidate liable to be quashed? - Held, (A) Could not be introduced later - (B) Selection, by adopting such illegal means, quashed - Appeal allowed.
