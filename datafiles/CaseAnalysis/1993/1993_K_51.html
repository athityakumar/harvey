Dashrath Baburao Sangale And Others v Kashimath Bhaskar Data

Supreme Court of India

10 February 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, B.P. Jeevan Reddy

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1283; (1994) Supp1 SCC 504; AIR 1993 SC 2646; 1994 (3) Bom.C.R. 447; JT 1993 (Supp) SC 506; 1996 (1) RCJ 173

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Transfer Of Property Act, 1882, s. 108 - Bombay Rents, Hotel And Lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947, s. 13(1)(a) - Eviction for change of user - Uses of premises by lessee contemplated by agreement for a specific business - Use of land for purpose (business) in addition to one contemplated under lease agreement - Plea of lessee that lease being for carrying on business any business could be carried on by him - Negatived by High Court - Whether interference by Supreme Court required - Held, Supreme Court declined to interfere - Appeal dismissed.
