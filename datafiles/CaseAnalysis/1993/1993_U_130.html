D. V. Bakshi v Union of India

Supreme Court of India

14 July 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 20; (1993) 3 SCC 663; (1993) SCC (L&S) 991; AIR 1993 SC 2374; AIR 1993 SCW 2760; 1993 (25) ATC 206; 1993 (48) ECR 209; 1994 (74) E.L.T. 200; JT 1993 (4) SC 180; 1993(3) SCALE 69; [1993] Supp1 S.C.R. 200; 1993 (2) UJ 606

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Clearing

Summary: Contract & Commercial - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 - Customs Act, 1962, s. 146(2) - Grant of Regular Licence to Customs House Agent - Legality of regns. 8 and 9 of Customs House Agents Licensing Regulations, 1984 - Held, not illegal - Petition dismissed.
