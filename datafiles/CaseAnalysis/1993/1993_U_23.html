Ratan Chandra Sammanta and Others v Union Of India And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

13 May 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

R.M. Sahai, A.M. Ahmadi

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1158; (1993) Supp4 SCC 67; (1994) SCC (L&S) 182; AIR 1993 SC 2276; 1994 (26) ATC 228; 1993 (2) AWC 1147; 1993 (1) CLR 1072; 1993 (67) FLR 70; JT 1993 (3) SC 418; 1993 LabIC 1672; 1993 (2) LLJ 676; 1993 (2) LLN 251; 1993(2) SCALE 974; [1993] 3 S.C.R. 751; 1993 (2) SLR 811; 1993 (2) UJ 168; 1993 (2) UPLBEC 1154

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 and 311 - Retrenchment of casual labour in railway - Approached court after a delay of 15 years without proof to show that they were covered by scheme for there re-employment - Held, delay disentitles them both of remedy and right - Petition dismissed.
