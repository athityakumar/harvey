Modi Industries Limited v State Of U.P.

Supreme Court of India

14 October 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

P.B. Sawant, A.S. Anand

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 714; (1994) 1 SCC 159; (1994) SCC (L&S) 286; AIR 1994 SC 536; 1993 (67) FLR 1062; JT 1993 (6) SC 103; 1994 (1) LLJ 383; 1994 (1) LLN 33; 1993(4) SCALE 155; 1993 (5) SLR 761

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Code of Civil Procedure, Grievance, Payment Of Wages Act, 1936, Uttar Pradesh Industrial Peace (Timely Payments of Wages) Act, 1978

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Uttar Pradesh Industrial Peace (Timely Payment of Wages) Act, 1978, s. 3 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 - Complaint - Non-payment of wages - Refusal/issue certificate by Labour Commr. - Reasons in support of order - Held, is necessary - When untenable frivolous pleas of employer in genuine dispute regarding entitlement of workmen to wages cannot be resolved without investigation - Appeal allowed.
