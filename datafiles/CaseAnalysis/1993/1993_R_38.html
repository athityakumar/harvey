Rajaram v State Of M.P.

Supreme Court of India

23 February 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jayachandra Reddy, G.N. Ray

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 368; (1994) Supp2 SCC 153; (1994) SCC (Cr) 889

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 34, 149, 302 and 326 - A riot took place on 26-03-1978 in Village Hasrai within the limits of Police Station Rahatgarh, as many as 47 accused were charge sheeted - Alleged that all of them armed with lethal weapons like lathies, ballams and axes attacked one Ratansingh and inflicted several injuries resulting in his death - Three appeals arise out of the same judgment of the Division Bench of High Court - Held, we set aside the conviction of Rajaram, Parma, Balloo, Dina and Gulab, and sentences of life imprisonment awarded against each of them for the said offences - We convict them u/s. 326 r/w s. 149 IPC, 1860, and sentence each of them to undergo 7 years' RI - We confirm conviction of Kiratsingh, u/s. 326 r/w s. 149 IPC, 1860 - Munna, the other accused not before us, his conviction u/s. 302 r/w. s. 149 IPC, 1860, is set aside and the sentence of life imprisonment awarded u/s. 326 r/w s. 149 IPC, 1860 - Other convictions and sentences awarded by HC against all the appellants and Munna are confirmed- Appeal partly allowed.
