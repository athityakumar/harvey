Narendra Kumar and Another v Dharam Dutt and Another

Supreme Court of India

17 March 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

N.M. Kasliwal, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 243; (1993) Supp3 SCC 205; (1993) SCC (L&S) 977; 1993 (25) ATC 376; 1993 (1) CLR 822; 1993 (66) FLR 1008; JT 1993 (2) SC 423; 1993 (2) LLJ 88; 1993(2) SCALE 53; [1993] 2 S.C.R. 413; 1993 (3) SLJ 139; 1993 (1) UJ 672

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution - Labour & Industrial Law - Constitution of India, 1950 - Report - Claim for benefits - Equal pay for equal work - Grant - Employees working in general wing of New Delhi Municipal Committee (NDMC), claimed benefit of report of Shiv Shankar Committee (SSC) in respect of grant of pay scales - Appellant filed W.P. u/art. 226 of Constitution in HC - Not received full relief - Employees of NDMC working in different departments filed W.P.s u/art. 32 of Constitution - R. D. Gupta and Others v Lt. Governor, Delhi Admn. and Others, 1987 Indlaw SC 28301, relied on - Whether all sections of ministerial staff should be given same pay scales as recommended by SSC - Held, allowed W.P. nos. 1, 3, 5, 13, and 292 of 1990 and granted them benefit of SSC pay scales in same manner as granted to other employees of ministerial staff as they belonged to same cadre - Respondent should take appropriate steps within three months of notice of order and make payment of entire arrears and grant future benefit in pay scales as recommended by SSC - W.P. nos. 1109 of 1990 and 409 of 1990 were dismissed as they did not belong to same cadre - Petition disposed of.
