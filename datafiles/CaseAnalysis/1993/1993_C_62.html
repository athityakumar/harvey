Collector of Central Excise v Fusebase Eltoto Limited

Supreme Court of India

20 July 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, P.B. Sawant

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 34; (1993) Supp3 SCC 385; AIR 1994 SC 1289; 1993 (48) ECR 2; 1993 (67) E.L.T. 30; JT 1993 (4) SC 280; [1993] Supp1 S.C.R. 315

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, s. 3, Notification No. 68/86 - Entitlement of exemption from excise duty through notification on Broadcasts Television receiver sets - Whether projection television sets are also included? - Determination of, by interpretation of Fiscal Statutes, when term not defined - Held, does not cover and same is not exempt - Appeal allowed.
