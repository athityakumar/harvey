Karuppaswamy and Others v C. Ramamurthy

Supreme Court of India

14 July 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Punchhi, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1218; (1993) 4 SCC 41; AIR 1993 SC 2324; 1993 (22) ALR 466; 1993 (2) CCC 616; JT 1993 (4) SC 192; 1993(3) SCALE 165; [1993] Supp1 S.C.R. 121; 1993 (2) UJ 394

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Limitation Act, 1963, s. 21(1), Proviso - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 1, r. 10, O. 22, r. 4 - Applicability of s. 21(1) proviso when suit filed against dead person and fact already come to knowledge of plaintiff from remark on returned summons - And he was seeking to bring LRs. on record promptly - Held, he is entitled to invoke proviso to s. 21(1) - Appeal dismissed.
