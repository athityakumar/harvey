Excise Commissioner, Karnataka And Anr. v V. Sreekanta

Supreme Court of India

05 March 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

G.N. Ray, P.B. Sawant

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1174; (1993) Supp3 SCC 53; (1993) SCC (L&S) 925; AIR 1993 SC 1564; 1993 (25) ATC 83; 1993 (1) CLR 878; JT 1993 (2) SC 384; 1993 LabIC 1319; 1993 (2) LLJ 717; 1993 (2) LLN 14; 1993(2) SCALE 1; [1993] 2 S.C.R. 267; 1993 (2) SLR 339; 1993 (2) UPLBEC 1177

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Constitution of India, Arts.309 and 311; Karnataka State Civil Services (Direct Recruit to Class III Posts) (Special) Rules, 1970, rr.3 and 6 - Seniority - Determination of - Inspector of Excise (Junior) - Ad hoc appointment - Regularisation of service after framing of Rules - Held, seniority counts form date of regularisation and not from date of ad hoc.
