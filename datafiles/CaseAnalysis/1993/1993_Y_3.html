Laxmishankar Harishankar Bhatt v Yashram Vasta (Dead) By Lrs

Supreme Court of India

26 February 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mohan, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 933; (1993) 3 SCC 49; AIR 1993 SC 1587; 1993 (2) ALT 9; 1993 (1) CCC 578; JT 1993 (Supp) SC 21; 1993 (2) RCR(Rent) 453; 1993 (1) RentLR 502; 1993(1) SCALE 726

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Rent Control

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 1, r. 9 - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958 - Suit for recovery of possession - Dismissed for non-joinder of necessary parties - Claim as to acquisition of entire ownership of impuned property - Tenant take defence of non-joinder of co-owners - No averments in Written Statement as to who are other co-owners and what rights they claim - Held, suit cannot be dismissed - And plea vague - Appeal allowed.
