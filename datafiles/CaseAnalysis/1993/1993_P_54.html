Prakash Chand Sachdeva v State

Supreme Court of India

14 December 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

R.M. Sahai, S. Ratnavel Pandian

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 141; (1994) SCC (Cr) 438; (1994) 1 SCC 471; AIR 1994 SC 1436; 1994 (1) CCR 60; 1994 CRLJ 2117; 1993 CRLR 758; 1993 CrLR(SC) 758; JT 1993 (6) SC 657; 1994 (3) RCR(Rent) 217; 1994 (1) UJ 158

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal Procedure Code, 1974, ss. 145 and 107 - Distinction between proceedings u/s. 145 and s. 107 was to be discussed - Held, proceedings under s. 145 neither be dropped on ground that proceedings under s. 107 are dropped nor on ground of pendency of civil suit for injunction when suit is not based on title but raises question only of dispossession of a co-owner by another co-owner.
