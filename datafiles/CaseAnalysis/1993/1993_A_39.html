General Labour Union (Red Flag), Bombay v Ahmedabad Manufacturing and Calico Printing Company Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

08 September 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

P.B. Sawant, Yogeshwar Dayal

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 536; (1995) Supp1 SCC 175; (1995) SCC (L&S) 372; 1995 (2) LLJ 765

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Maharashtra Recognition of Trade Unions and Prevention of Unfair Labour Practices Act, 1971 - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - Workmen employed by a contractor who was given a contract to run canteen - Complaint filed under Act, 1971 that they were not given service conditions as were available to other workmen of company - Held, it was not established that workmen in question were workmen of respondent and hence no complaint could lie under Act, 1971 - Appeals dismissed.
