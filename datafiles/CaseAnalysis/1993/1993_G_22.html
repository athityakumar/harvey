Surjet Kaur v Garja Singh (Mohan,J.)

Supreme Court of India

27 October 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mohan, S. Ratnavel Pandian

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 734; (1994) SCC (Cr) 259; (1994) 1 SCC 407; AIR 1994 SC 135; 1993 (3) CCC 623; 1993(4) SCALE 302; 1994 (1) UJ 223

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Family & Personal - Hindu Marriage Act, 1955, s. 17 - Customary marriage - Proof - Custom not pleaded - Nature of ceremonies performed not brought out - Living together as husband and wife - Would that confer status of husband and wife? - Held, would not by itself confer status - Appeal dismissed.
