Giass Miniature Bulb Industries v Additional Commissioner of Income Tax

Supreme Court of India

14 September 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, B.P. Jeevan Reddy

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1601; (1994) Supp3 SCC 44; 1994 (116) CTR 68; [1993] 204 ITR 352

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income tax Act, 1961, s. 256(1) - Whether sum of Rs. 60,000 paid to 'S' represented trading loss of assessment year 1964-65 ? - High Court pointed out, assessee's case before Appellate Tribunal (on basis of receipt dated 31-12-1961) was that 'S' had undertaken to obtain foreign exchange permit on or before 15-01-1962, and in event of delay or failure in doing so, he undertook to return amount - In circumstances HC held that trading loss claimed by appellant had occurred during accounting year ending on 30-04-1962, and cannot be allowed in assessment year 1964-65 - Held, no reason to take a different view of matter - Conclusion of HC is unexceptionable - Appeal dismissed.
