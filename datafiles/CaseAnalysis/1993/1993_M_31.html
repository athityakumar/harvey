Mahadevan and Others v D. C. Aggarwal

Supreme Court of India

17 August 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

R.M. Sahai, S. Ratnavel Pandian

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 561; (1993) Supp4 SCC 4; (1994) SCC (L&S) 8; AIR 1994 SC 961; 1994 (26) ATC 64; 1993 (2) CLR 614; 1993 (67) FLR 642; JT 1993 (4) SC 571; 1993 (2) LLJ 115; 1994 (1) MLJ(SC) 40; 1993 (5) SLR 53

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 311 - Promotion - Constitution of Committee comprising of officers against whom employee had filed contempt petition - Possibility of bias - Held, yet it is neither proper nor fair on their part to sit on board - Directions issued - Appeals decided by issuing directions.
