Bharat Petroleum Corporation Limited Ex-Employees Association and Others v Chairman and Managing Director Bharat Petroleum Corporation Limited, Bombay and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 August 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 567; (1993) Supp4 SCC 37; (1994) SCC (L&S) 20; AIR 1994 SC 1304; 1994 (26) ATC 83; 1994 (1) Bom.C.R. 589; 1993 (2) CLR 615; 1993 (67) FLR 588; JT 1993 (4) SC 566; 1993 (2) LLJ 689; 1994 (1) MLJ(SC) 37; 1993 (4) SLR 799

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Petroleum & Gas

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950 arts. 16 and 311 - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, sch. II, item 6 - Burmah Shell (Acquisition Of Undertakings In India) Act, 1976, s. 10 - Restoration of commuted portion of pension as per rule applicable to Central Govt. employees after 15 years of retirement from a Govt. Undertaking - Plea of additional burden - Held, not of consequence - Petition allowed.
