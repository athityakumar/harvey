Delhi Development Authority and Others v Surgical Cooperative Industrial Estate Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 August 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, N. Venkatachala, S. Mohan

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 556; (1993) Supp4 SCC 20; 1993 (2) CCC 189; 1993 (51) DLT 584; JT 1993 (5) SC 6; 1993(3) SCALE 451; 1993 (2) UJ 586

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Land & Property - Allotment of land to set up industrial units - On perpetual lease - Value - Determination of - Who were entitled to get plot in lesser rate? - Held, members who held industrial units earlier and who were to be re-allocated and re-settled at site in question were entitled to get plots at a lesser rate whereas remaining 33 who were to set up new units at allocated sites were rightly required to pay higher price - In all other cases to be determined by Advisory Committee of Delhi Development Authority - Defaulter entitled fresh offer - Appeal disposed of.
