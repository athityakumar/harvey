Sarifabibi Mohmed Ibrahim Smt. and Others v Commissioners of Income Tax, Gujarat

Supreme Court of India

14 September 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 616; (1993) Supp4 SCC 707; AIR 1993 SC 2585; 1993 (114) CTR 467; [1993] 204 ITR 631; JT 1993 (5) SC 257; 1993(3) SCALE 750; [1993] Supp2 S.C.R. 264; [1993] 70 TAXMAN 301; 1993 (2) TLR 1105

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 2(14), 47 and 54 B - Agricultural land - Land situated within municipal limits - Registered as agricultural land in revenue records - Assessee owners entering into agreement for sale with Housing Co-operative Society - Obtaining permission of Revenue authorities for transfer for non-agricultural purpose - Sale immediately thereafter - Land not cultivated for four years preceding sale - (A) Whether land was agricultural at time of sale - (B) Whether assessee, exempt from capital gain tax - Held, (A) Land was not agricultural - (B) Assessee not exempt - Appeal dismissed.
