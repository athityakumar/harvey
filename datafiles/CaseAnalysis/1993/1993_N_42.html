Collector of Central Excise Etc v Neoli Sugar Factory Etc

Supreme Court of India

30 March 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 442; (1993) Supp3 SCC 69; AIR 1993 SC 1921; 1993 (46) ECR 529; 1993 (65) E.L.T. 145; JT 1993 (2) SC 587; 1993 (2) MLJ 68; 1993 (2) MLJ(SC) 68; 1993(2) SCALE 295; [1993] 2 S.C.R. 758

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Indirect Tax - Excise - Central Excise Rules, 1944, r. 8(1) - (A) Notification issued by Central Govt. - Sugar factories - Rebate in excise duty - Notification giving different percentage of rebate for excess production in different periods of sugar year as compared to earlier sugar year - Whether rebate is available to a factory who had not produced any sugar in earlier sugar year - (B) Interpretation of statutes - Taxing statute - Exemption notification - Strict construction is the rule - Giving effect to notification - How -

Held, (A) Is available even to a factory who had not produced any sugar in corresponding period of earlier sugar year - (B) But purpose underlying such notification should also be kept in mind while giving effect to notification - Appeal allowed.
