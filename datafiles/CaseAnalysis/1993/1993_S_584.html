Ramadhar Pandey v State of Uttar Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

30 March 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mohan

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 41; (1993) Supp3 SCC 35; (1993) SCC (L&S) 918; 1993 (25) ATC 77; 1993 (2) CLR 365; 1997 (68) ECR 545; 1993 (66) E.L.T. 547; 1993 (67) FLR 322; JT 1993 (4) SC 72; 1993 (2) LLJ 817; 1993(2) SCALE 349; 1993 (3) SLJ 124; 1993 (4) SLR 349; 1993 (2) UJ 75

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Whether any public interest involved in transfer of Appellant as Joint Secretary? - Held, in absence of counter affidavit and relevant records - No public interest involved - Appellant due to retire in few months - No reason why he should be transferred as Joint Secretary in absence of any public interest - Judgment of High Court set aside - Appeal allowed.
