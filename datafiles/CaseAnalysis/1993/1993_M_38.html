Mahendra Singh v Jagbir Singh

Supreme Court of India

12 January 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

P.B. Sawant, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1334; (1993) 2 SCC 34; AIR 1994 SC 762; JT 1993 (1) SC 105

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Agriculture & Agri. Products

Keywords: Debt Laws

Summary: Banking & Finance - Uttar Pradesh Regulation Of Money-Lending Act, 1976, s. 26(4) - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 100 - Determination of creditor's status as money-lender by High Court on basis of isolated statement of debtor - Disentitled him from recovering loan - Validity - Held, not proper - Further held, that HC exceeded jurisdiction in recording for first time a finding of fact in second appeal - Case remanded back - Appeal allowed.
