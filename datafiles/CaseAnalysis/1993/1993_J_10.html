Food Corporation of India and Others v Jagannath Dutta and Others

Supreme Court of India

18 March 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, N.M. Kasliwal

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 404; (1993) Supp3 SCC 635; AIR 1993 SC 1494; AIR 1993 SCW 1425; JT 1993 (Supp) SC 85; 1993(2) SCALE 84; [1993] 2 S.C.R. 497

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Constitution of India, art.226 - Notice of termination - Contractual obligations - Food Corporation of India terminates agreement as regards private storage agency - Terms of contract permits for termination - Held, notice of termination cannot be set aside on ground of absence of policy decision - Question of contractual obligations cannot be gone into in writ jurisdiction.
