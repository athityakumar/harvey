Supreme Court of India

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, P.B. Sawant

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Civil Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 2(2) - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 44 - Fraud with Court - Litigant withholding of vital document - Obtaining preliminary decree for partition - Not mentioning release deed in favour of his employer before suit - Held, decree is vitiated by fraud - Appeal allowed.
