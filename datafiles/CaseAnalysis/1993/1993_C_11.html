Virkey Chacko v C.I.T.

Supreme Court of India

24 August 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, B.P. Jeevan Reddy

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1462; (1994) Supp1 SCC 264; 1993 (114) CTR 207; 1994 (114) CTR 207; [1993] 203 ITR 885; JT 1993 (5) SC 58; 1993(3) SCALE 537; [1993] Supp1 S.C.R. 800; [1993] 70 TAXMAN 152; 1993 (2) TLR 1076

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961,ss.256(2), 271(1)(C)(iii), 274(2) - Whether proceedings for imposition of penalty taken in case were governed by provisions of s. 274(2) as they stood prior to amendment or whether it was sub-section as amended that would apply and whether Income Tax Officer had no jurisdiction to levy penalty and that he should have referred case to Inspecting Assistant Commissioner for imposition of penalty? - Penalty for concealment of particulars of income or for furnishing inaccurate particulars of income can be imposed only when assessing authority is satisfied that there has been such concealment or furnishing of inaccurate particulars - Penalty proceeding, therefore, can be initiated only after an assessment order has been made which finds such concealment or furnishing of inaccurate particulars - Who at this point of time has authority to impose penalty is what is relevant - Whoever this authority may be, he is obliged to impose such penalty as was permissible under law in that behalf on date on which offence of concealment of income was committed, that is to say, on date of offending return - Two aspects must firmly be borne in mind, namely, who may impose penalty and in what measure - Held, in instant case, when ITO reached satisfaction that assessee had concealed income and made assessment order on 27-03- 1972, amended provisions of s. 274(2) were in operation and they entitled ITO to impose penalty in cases where amount of income in respect of which particulars had been concealed was, as here, less than Rs. 25,000 as rightly observed by HC - Appeal dismissed.
