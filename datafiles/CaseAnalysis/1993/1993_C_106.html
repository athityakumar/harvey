Emil Webber v Commissioner of Income Tax, V and M, Nagpur

Supreme Court of India

19 February 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 907; (1993) 2 SCC 453; AIR 1993 SC 1466; 1993 (1) CCR 90; 1993 (110) CTR 257; [1993] 200 ITR 483; JT 1993 (2) SC 555; 1993 (2) MLJ 112; 1993 (2) MLJ(SC) 112; 1993(1) SCALE 659; [1993] 2 S.C.R. 27; [1993] 67 TAXMAN 532; 1993 (2) TLR 475

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Criminal - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 2(24) and 56 - Whether income from other sources applies to assessee a foreign national receiving salary from Indian Company? - Company under legal obligation of paying income tax on salary - Held, amount paid by Company by way of tax is 'income' of assessee and can be taxed as income from other sources.
