Chettinad Corporation Private Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax

Supreme Court of India

06 January 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

M.N. Venkatachaliah, G.N. Ray

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1565; [1993] 200 ITR 320

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Companies (Profits) Surtax Act, 1964, s.8 - Income-tax Act, 1961, s.147- Appeal arises out of and was directed against the judgment of HC - Present case was referred to and approved for the reasons contained in and following pronouncement - Appeal dismissed.
