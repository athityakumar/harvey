H.H. Sir Rama Varma v C.I.T.

Supreme Court of India

02 November 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, B.P. Jeevan Reddy

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1572; (1994) Supp1 SCC 473; AIR 1994 SC 1904; 1994 (116) CTR 55; [1994] 205 ITR 433; JT 1993 (6) SC 183; 1993(4) SCALE 315; [1993] 71 TAXMAN 237; 1994 (2) TLR 498

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 80 T and 80 AB - Deductions from capital gains while calculating Income tax - When u/s. 80 T can be granted relief - Held, can be given only for amount of capital gains after capital loss is set off - Appeal dismissed.
