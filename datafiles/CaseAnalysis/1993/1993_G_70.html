Guru Nanak Dev University v Parminder Kr. Bansal and Others

Supreme Court of India

29 April 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

M.N. Venkatachaliah, S. Mohan, T.K. Thommen

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1112; (1993) 4 SCC 401; AIR 1993 SC 2412; AIR 1993 SCW 2695; JT 1993 (Supp) SC 449

Case Digest

Subject: Education

Summary: Education - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Admission - Whether it should be granted by means of interim directions without regard to eligibility of candidate? - Held, no - Interim Orders should not be passed without adjudication of basic issues - Appeal allowed.
