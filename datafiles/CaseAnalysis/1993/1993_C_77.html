Associated Cement Company Ltd. v Commissioner Of Income-Tax Bihar, Patna And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

23 March 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Venkatachala, B.P. Jeevan Reddy

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1581; (1993) 2 SCC 556; AIR 1993 SC 2281; 1993 (111) CTR 165; [1993] 201 ITR 435; JT 1993 (2) SC 411; 1993(2) SCALE 161; [1993] 2 S.C.R. 538; [1993] 67 TAXMAN 346; 1993 (2) TLR 633

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 194 C(1) - Scope of liability to deduct 2 per cent TDS while making payment to contractor in contract for carry out 'work' and not 'works-contract' - Held, it is not confined to income component of payment - Appeal dismissed.
