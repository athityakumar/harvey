Jamna v State Of U.P.

Supreme Court of India

21 September 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jayachandra Reddy, G.N. Ray

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1486; (1994) Supp1 SCC 185; (1994) SCC (Cr) 348; AIR 1994 SC 79; 1993 (3) AWC 1788; 1993 (4) CCR 379; 1993 (3) Crimes 546; 1993 CRLR 627; 1993 CrLR(SC) 627; JT 1993 (5) SC 334; 1993(3) SCALE 824

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss.300, 326, and 34 - Murder - Common object - Unlawful assembly - Some accused persons armed with ballamas while others with lathi - Only some of them causing grievous hurt by attacking deceased with ballamas - Numerous fatal injuries consequently - Held, common object not to commit murder, although - Accused persons armed with lathi convicted for causing grievous hurt - Conviction of rest under ss.302/34.
