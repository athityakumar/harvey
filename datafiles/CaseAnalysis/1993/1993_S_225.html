K.P. Tiwari v State Of M.P.

Supreme Court of India

29 October 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

P.B. Sawant, Yogeshwar Dayal

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 136; (1994) Supp1 SCC 540; (1994) SCC (Cr) 712; AIR 1994 SC 1031; 1994 CRLJ 1377; JT 1993 (6) SC 287

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 147, 148, 149, 506 and 302 - Petition by a judicial officer, who was Additional Sessions Judge at relevant time, for expunging remarks which were made against him by High Courts while reversing orders of bail passed by him - Held, Higher judiciary should not ignore judicial precautions and propriety even momentarily - Remarks of HC expunged from HC order - Petition allowed.
