Jattu Ram v Hakam Singh and Others

Supreme Court of India

15 September 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 619; (1993) 4 SCC 403; AIR 1994 SC 1653; JT 1993 (5) SC 423; 1993 (2) RentLR 441; 1993(3) SCALE 782; [1993] Supp2 S.C.R. 321; 1993 (2) UJ 746

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Defective Title

Summary: Land & Property - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 119 - Punjab Land Revenue Act, 1887, s. 31 - Record of rights - (A) Defect in title of land received in exchange as false entries made by patwari in contradiction to oral contract - Plea that party was lessee-at-will also proved to be false - Effect - (B) Nature of entries made by patwari in official record - Held, opposite party liable to return land to that extent - Are only for fiscal purpose, and no title is created by them - Appeal allowed.
