Triveni Rubber and Plastics, Tiruvalla, Kerala v Collector of Central Excise, Cochin

Supreme Court of India

30 March 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1021; (1994) Supp3 SCC 665; AIR 1994 SC 1341; 1994 (73) E.L.T. 7

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, s. 9 - Central Excise Rules, 1944, r. 173 E - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 136 - (A) Excise Duty - Determination of suppression of exceeding of exempted production - Show cause notice vis-a-vis levy of duty and penalty - (B) Constitution - Levy of duty and penalty - Concurrent finding of fact that assessee suppressing exceeding of exempted production - All relevant evidence and admissible material considered - Held, not sustainable as accounts were found fabricated and untrue and material relating to other factors were considered - Finding of facts cannot be disturbed u/art. 136 - Appeal dismissed.
