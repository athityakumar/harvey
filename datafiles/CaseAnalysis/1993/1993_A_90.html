Union of India and Another v Ashok Kacker

Supreme Court of India

27 September 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 669; (1995) Supp1 SCC 180; (1995) SCC (L&S) 374; 1995 (29) ATC 145

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Tribunal quashed charge sheet issued to respondent - Whether Tribunal could have quashed charge sheet? - Tribunal entertained respondent's application at a premature stage and quashed charge sheet issued during pendency of matter before Tribunal - Respondent has full opportunity to reply to charge sheet and to raise all points available to him - This was not stage at which Tribunal ought to have entertained such an application for quashing charge sheet - Impugned order of Tribunal quashing charge sheet set aside - Appeal allowed.
