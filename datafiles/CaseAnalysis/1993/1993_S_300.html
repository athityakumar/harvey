Anzar Ahmad v State of Bihar and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 October 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1366; (1994) 1 SCC 150; (1994) SCC (L&S) 278; AIR 1994 SC 141; AIR 1993 SCW 3777; 1994 ALJ 410; 1994 (26) ATC 504; 1994 (68) FLR 1; JT 1993 (6) SC 168; 1994 (1) LLJ 879; 1994 (2) LLN 1075; 1993(4) SCALE 268; 1994 (1) SLJ 187; 1993 (5) SLR 798; 1994 (1) UJ 284; 1994 (2) UPLBEC 1221

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution - Constitution, arts. 16 and 311 - Recruitment - Selection - Govt. in its letter to Public Service Commission stating that selection be made on basis of interview - Selection made as per past practice by allocating 50% marks for academic performance and 50% for interview - Procedure, if arbitrary - Held, is not arbitrary - Appeal dismissed.
