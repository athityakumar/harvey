Laxman Marotrao Navakhare v Keshavrao

Supreme Court of India

02 March 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

N.P. Singh, T.K. Thommen

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 878; (1993) 2 SCC 270; AIR 1993 SC 2596; AIR 1993 SCW 3286; 1994 (3) Bom.C.R. 510; JT 1993 (2) SC 187; 1993 (1) RCJ 461; 1993 (2) RCR(Rent) 7; 1993 (2) RentLR 437; 1993(1) SCALE 771; [1993] 2 S.C.R. 167; 1993 (1) UJ 682

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Constitution - Rent Control - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 96 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 136 - Central Province and Berar Letting of Houses and Rent Control Order, 1949, cl. 13A - Bar to passing of decree of eviction in any suit or proceeding filed and pending against tenant - (A) Whether applies to special leave petition pending before Supreme Court? - (B) While dismissing appeal filed u/art. 136 against an eviction decree passed by High Court, if Supreme Court passes an eviction decree - Held, (A) Does not apply to special leave petition - (B) Supreme Court does not pass eviction decree - Appeal dismissed.
