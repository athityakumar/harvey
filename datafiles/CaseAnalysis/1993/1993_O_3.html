State of Haryana and Another v O. P. Sharma and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 February 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, N.M. Kasliwal

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 893; (1993) Supp2 SCC 386; (1993) SCC (L&S) 554; AIR 1993 SC 1903; 1993 (24) ATC 380; 1993 (1) CCC 590; 1993 (1) CLR 735; 1993 (66) FLR 696; JT 1993 (2) SC 221; 1993 LabIC 1365; 1993 (2) LLJ 457; 1993(1) SCALE 731; 1993 (2) SLR 27; 1993 (1) UJ 517

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution - Constitution of India, 1950 art. 16 and 309 - Dearness allowance - Interim ad hoc relief - Excess payments made to employees - Recovery in phased manner after fixation of dearness allowance - If arbitrary, unreasonable, unfair or illegal - Held, Cannot be termed as arbitrary, unreasonable, unfair or illegal - Petition dismissed.
