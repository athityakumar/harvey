Prof.Sumer Chand v Union Of India

Supreme Court of India

07 September 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 500; (1994) 1 SCC 64; (1994) SCC (Cr) 99; AIR 1993 SC 2579; 1993 (3) CCC 71; 1993 (3) CCR 344; 1993 CRLJ 3531; JT 1993 (5) SC 189; 1993(3) SCALE 706; [1993] Supp2 S.C.R. 123; 1993 (2) UJ 599

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Prevention of Corruption Act, 1947 - Delhi Police Act 1978, s. 140 - Limitation Act, 1963, art. 74 and s. 29(2) - Suit for malicious prosecution against member of Delhi Police - Limitation of such suit was in question - Held, limitation was governed by s. 140 of Delhi Police Act and not art. 74 of Limitation Act.
