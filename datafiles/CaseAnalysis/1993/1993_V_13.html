Vasant Rao Roman v Union of India Through The Central Railway, Bombay and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 March 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

N.M. Kasliwal, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1060; (1993) Supp2 SCC 324; (1993) SCC (L&S) 590; JT 1993 (2) SC 451; 1993 (2) LLJ 6; 1993 (2) SLR 289

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Service - Special leave petition against judgment passed by Central Administrative Tribunal denying arrears of emolument - Held, order of Tribunal be set aside - Neither appellant had been put under suspension nor any disciplinary proceedings were pending against him - Appellant had been made to suffer on account of administrative reasons for which appellant was not responsible - Tribunal itself has allowed claim of appellant regarding seniority over his juniors, considering force in contention of appellant - Modified order of Tribunal to effect that appellant shall also be entitled to get all arrears of emoluments w.e.f 12-06-1961 as Shunter 'B' and w.e.f 17-12-1965 as Driver 'C' - Appeal allowed.
