Phool Chand Bajrang Lal and Another v Income Tax Officer and Another

Supreme Court of India

13 July 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1594; (1993) 4 SCC 77; AIR 1993 SC 2390; 1993 ALJ 1230; 1993 (113) CTR 436; [1993] 203 ITR 456; JT 1993 (4) SC 291; 1993(3) SCALE 180; [1993] Supp1 S.C.R. 28; [1993] 69 TAXMAN 627; 1993 (2) TLR 899

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 143, 147 and 148 - Permissibility reopening of assessment - Loan transaction found to be bogus on subsequent information - Disclosures at time of original assessment is not 'true' and 'full' facts in case - Held, reopening of assessment in such case - Permissible - Appeal dismissed.
