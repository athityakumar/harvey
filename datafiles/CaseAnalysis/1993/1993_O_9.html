Committee of Management of Pachaiyappa's Trust v Official Trustee of Madras and Another

Supreme Court of India

17 November 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, M.N. Venkatachaliah, A.S. Anand

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1383; (1994) 1 SCC 475; AIR 1994 SCW 1484; 1993 (3) CCC 784; 1994 (1) HLR 88; JT 1993 (6) SC 389; 1993(4) SCALE 465

Case Digest

Subject: Trusts & Associations

Keywords: Official Trustees Act, 1913, Andhra Pradesh Charitable and Hindu Religious Institutions and Endowments Act, 1966, Bombay Prohibition Act 1949

Summary: Trusts & Associations - Andhra Pradesh Charitable and Hindu Religious Institutions and Endowments Act, 1966 - Bombay Prohibition Act 1949 - Official Trustees Act, 1913, ss. 25 and 26 - Application filed by respondent 2 for grant of lease was treated and disposed of as an application u/s. 25 of the Official Trustees Act - Exercise of the supervisory jurisdiction by the HC u/s. 25 of the Official Trustees Act, 1913 - Court held, Act does not envisage an application being moved by any other person therefore respondent 2 were neither beneficially interested in the trust property nor trustees thereof, application not maintainable and High Court was in error in entertaining the application - Appeals allowed.
