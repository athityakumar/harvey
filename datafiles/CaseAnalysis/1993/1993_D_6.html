J. Chandrashekhar Reddy and another v D.Arora

Supreme Court of India

07 October 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, N. Venkatachala, P.B. Sawant

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1650; (1994) 1 SCC 228; (1994) SCC (L&S) 510; AIR 1994 SC 526; AIR 1993 SCW 3970; 1994 (26) ATC 583; 1994 (1) CLR 176; 1994 (68) FLR 100; JT 1993 (6) SC 485; 1994 (1) LLJ 1184; 1994 (1) LLN 6; 1993(4) SCALE 3; 1993 (8) SLR 534; 1993 (2) UJ 715

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 311 - Andhra Pradesh (Roads and Buildings) Engineering Service Rules, 1965, r. 3(1) - Recruitment to the category of Asst. Engineers - Fixed quota for direct recruits not followed resulting in under recruitment of direct recruits - Held, directions to work out quota rule at least from 1982.
