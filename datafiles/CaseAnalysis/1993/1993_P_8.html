P. V. T. Phillip v P. Narasimha Reddy and Others

Supreme Court of India

13 July 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

P.B. Sawant, A.S. Anand

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1208; (1993) Supp3 SCC 438; (1994) SCC (L&S) 50; AIR 1993 SC 2403; 1993 (25) ATC 629; 1993 (2) CLR 392; 1993 (67) FLR 383; JT 1993 (4) SC 155; 1993 LabIC 1987; 1993 (2) LLJ 702; 1993(3) SCALE 77; 1993 (4) SLR 367; [1993] Supp1 S.C.R. 100; 1993 (2) UJ 468

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution - Service - Constitution, 1950, arts. 309 and 313 - Andhra Pradesh State and Subordinate Services Rules, rr. 6(b), 9, 10, 10(a)(1)(i), 23(a) and 47 - Ex-Air Force Officer - Appointment as Jailor - Whether appellant is entitled to the benefit of his service as Jailor from June 26/27, 1969 to 30-09-1969 as a qualifying period of service for being appointed to the post of Deputy Superintendent of Jails on 30-07-1977? - Court held, respondents 1 to 3 came in the service as direct recruits for first time as Deputy Superintendents of Jails in 1979 when by that time the appellant had completed his service as the Deputy Superintendent of Jails for no less than two years - In the circumstances, orders passed by the Governor on 01-09-1976 and 23-10-1987 in exercise of his powers u/r. 47 have cured whatever defects might have been there earlier, in the matter of the regular appointment of the appellant as a Jailor - Further set aside the decision of the Tribunal and declare that the appellant shall rank as senior to respondents 1 to 3 both as Deputy Superintendent of Jails and as Superintendent of Jails - Appeal allowed.
