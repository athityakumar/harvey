Inacio Martins Deceased Through Lrs v Narayan Hari Naik and Others

Supreme Court of India

07 April 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, S. Mohan

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1346; (1993) 3 SCC 123; AIR 1993 SC 1756; 1993 (2) ALT 29; 1995 (1) Bom.C.R. 167; 1993 (2) CCC 119; JT 1993 (2) SC 723; 1993 (1) RentLR 741; [1993] 2 S.C.R. 1015

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Forcible Dispossession

Summary: Rent Control - Civil Procedure - Goa, Daman and Diu Agricultural Tenancy Act, 1964, ss. 7 and 58(2) - C PC, 1908, s. 11, O. 2, r. 2(3) - (A) Applicability - Earlier suit for injunction dismissed on technical ground - Subsequent suit for declaration of title and recovery of possession, whether barred by res judicata - Not barred u/O. 2, r. 2(3) - (B) Ouster of Civil Court's Jurisdiction - Suit for declaration that plaintiff is lessee and for recovery of possession of land filed in Civil Court - Amendment Act 17 of 1976 coming into force during pendency of civil suit - Act silent in such situation - How to decide - Held, (A) Neither barred by res judicata nor barred u/O. 2, r. 2(3) since causes of actions in both suits were distinct - (B) Question reffered to competent Revenue authority - Appeal allowed.
