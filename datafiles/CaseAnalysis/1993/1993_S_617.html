State Of Haryana v Sukhdev

Supreme Court of India

28 July 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, N.P. Singh

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1449; (1995) Supp1 SCC 34; AIR 1994 SC 1255; 1997 JIC 532

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 4(1) and 6 - Land Acquisition - (A) Notification u/s. 4 and declaration u/s. 6 published in Gazette within prescribed period - Validity of aquisition - (B) Validity of notification, when declaration as to lands not made within 3 years - Held, acquisition become final and valid - Further held, notification u/s. 4 lapses - Order accordingly.
