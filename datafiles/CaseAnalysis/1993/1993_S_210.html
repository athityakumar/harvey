K. Narayanan v State Of Karnataka

Supreme Court of India

02 September 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

R.M. Sahai, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1410; (1994) Supp1 SCC 44; (1994) SCC (L&S) 392; AIR 1994 SC 55; AIR 1993 SCW 3106; 1994 (26) ATC 724; JT 1993 (5) SC 102; 1993 LabIC 2259; 1993 (2) LLN 833; 1993(3) SCALE 614; 1993 (5) SLR 290; [1993] Supp2 S.C.R. 105

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution - Karnataka Public Works Department Service (Recruitment) Rules, 1960 - Constitution, arts. 14, 16 and 309 - Validity - Appointment of dimploma holder from cadre of Junior Engineer to Assistant Engineer from back date without test or selection - (A) If reasonable and fair - Retrospective operation of Rules - (B) If violative of the Constitution - Held, (A)Not reasonable and fair - (B) Violative of arts. 14 and 16 -

Appeal allowed.
