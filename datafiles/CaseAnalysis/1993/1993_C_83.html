Commissioner of Income Tax (Addl.) v Ganapathi Raju Jogi

Supreme Court of India

17 February 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1335; [1993] 200 ITR 612

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961 - Appeal.

Held, both the parties state that, following the decision of SC in CIT v. B. C. Srinivasa Setty, this appeal has to be dismissed. Appeal dismissed
