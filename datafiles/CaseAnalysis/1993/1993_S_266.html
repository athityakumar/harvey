Gauri Shankar v State Of U.P.

Supreme Court of India

12 August 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, R.M. Sahai

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1542; (1994) 1 SCC 92; AIR 1994 SC 169; 1993 (3) CCC 302; JT 1993 (Supp) SC 104; 1993(3) SCALE 371; [1993] Supp1 S.C.R. 667

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Calcutta Improvement Act, 1911, Bangalore Improvement Act, 1945, Colonial Courts Of Admiralty Act, 1890, City of Bangalore Improvement Act, 1945

Summary: Land & Property - Uttar Pradesh Awas Evam Vikas Parishad Adhiniyam, 1966, ss. 28, 32 and 55 - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 4 and 6 - Scope and applicability - Adaption of ss. 4(1) and 6(1) of 1894 Act in State Act - Effect of - Held, on limitation of three years according to first Proviso of s. 6 of 1894 Act - Equity will not relieve against public statute of general policy in cases admitted to fall within statute - Petition dismissed.
