Pushpa Rani and Others v Bhagwanti Devi and Another

Supreme Court of India

07 September 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, M.N. Venkatachaliah

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 508; (1994) Supp3 SCC 76; AIR 1994 SC 774; 1994 (2) RCR(Rent) 298; 1995 (1) RentLR 392

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958, s. 14 - Tenancy - Implied surrender of tenancy - Eviction petition - Non-residential tenancy - Death of tenant - One of legal heirs in occupation and carrying on business - Implied surrender by other heirs can be inferred - Subsequent decree of eviction against said heir in occupation - Execution - Challenged by other heirs - Held, even if non-residential tenancy is heritable, decree cannot be challenged - Appeal dismissed.
