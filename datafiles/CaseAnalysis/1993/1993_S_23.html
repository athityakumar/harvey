Union Of India And Ors. v S.L. Abbas

Supreme Court of India

27 April 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law; Sales Tax

Summary: Administrative - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 309 and 323 A - Fundamental Rules, rr. 11 and 15 - Administrative Tribunals Act, 1985, s. 14 - (A) Guidelines for transfer Govt. employee - Do not confer legally enforceable right - Order against guidelines, interference sought - (B) Jurisdiction of Central Administrative Tribunal - Held, cannot be interfered with by Court unless it is vitiated by mala fides or is made in violation of statutory provision - Is akin to jurisdiction of High Court u/art. 226, Constitution but it cannot substitute its own judgment - Appeal allowed.
