Southern Painters v Fertilizers And Chemicals Travancore Ltd.

Supreme Court of India

10 September 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

M.N. Venkatachaliah, A.S. Anand

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1508; (1994) Supp2 SCC 699; AIR 1994 SC 1277

Case Digest

Subject: Contract & Commercial

Summary: Contract and Commercial - Constitution, art. 14 - Natural justice - Effect of negation of prior notice and opportunity of being heard - PSU black listing contractor for 10 years on basis of Vigilance Report without prior notice or opportunity of being heard - Same essential as personal and professional reputation would be at stake - Held, contractor entitled to be included in list of qualified contractors - Appeal disposed of.
