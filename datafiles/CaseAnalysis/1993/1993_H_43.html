State of Maharashtra and Others v Husen S/O Jafar Sheikh and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 May 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, A.S. Anand

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 1309; (1994) Supp1 SCC 468; (1994) SCC (L&S) 483; 1994 (26) ATC 851; 1995 (1) LLJ 101; 1993 (8) SLR 444

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Rajasthan State Rules - Indian Limitation Act, 1908 - Whether requirement of compulsory minimum passing marks for a viva voce test is permissible in law? - Viva voce test is as important as a written test and prescription of minimum pass marks will not per se make a rule unconstitutional - Held, that prescription of minimum marks for passing viva voce test is not constitutionally unsustainable - Civil Appeal No. 3163 of 1989 arising out of SLP (C) No. 7054 of 1989 allowed.
