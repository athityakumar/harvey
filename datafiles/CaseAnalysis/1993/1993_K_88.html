Ishwar Singh Ajay Kumar and Others v Kuldip Singh and Others

Supreme Court of India

27 September 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, Yogeshwar Dayal

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 673; (1995) Supp1 SCC 179; (1995) SCC (L&S) 373; 1995 (29) ATC 144

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Selection and consequent appointments to posts of Industries/Managers Industrial Estate for Small-Scale Cottage and Village Industries challenged by two unsuccessful candidates - Allegation that interviews held for selection were a sham affair - Average 1 minute and 20 seconds was time taken for interview of each candidate - High Court quashed selection and appointments - Held, HC not justified in hearing writ petition in absence of selected candidates especially when they had already been appointed - Appeals allowed.
