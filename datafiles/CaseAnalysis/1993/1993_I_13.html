Shriyans Prasad Jain v Income Tax Officer and Others

Supreme Court of India

14 September 1993

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1993 Indlaw SC 617; (1993) Supp4 SCC 727; AIR 1993 SC 2612; 1993 (114) CTR 411; [1993] 204 ITR 616; JT 1993 (5) SC 292; 1993(3) SCALE 743; [1993] Supp2 S.C.R. 279; [1993] 70 TAXMAN 290; 1993 (2) TLR 161

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 7, 147, 245C, 2 and 61 - Reassessment - Settlement Commission gave reasons out of which one was unsatisfactory - Whether letter of employment prescribing term of employment and providing for payment of stated sum for each unexpired year of employment in event of premature termination of services - Letter disbelieved by commission - Held, once letter is disbelieved commission finding in that behalf was justified - Appeal dismissed.
