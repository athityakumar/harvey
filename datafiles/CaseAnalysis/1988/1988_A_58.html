United Offset Process Pvt. Limited v Asstt. Collector Of Customs, Bombay And Ors

Supreme Court of India

14 October 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, K. Jagannatha Shetty

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 251; (1989) Supp1 SCC 131; AIR 1989 SC 622; 1988 (4) Bom.C.R. 169; 1989 CRLR 109; 1989 CrLR(SC) 109; 1988 (19) ECR 571; 1988 (38) E.L.T. 568; JT 1988 (4) SC 198; 1988(2) SCALE 1022; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 531; 1989 (74) STC 81; [1988] Supp3 S.C.R. 531; 1989 (1) UJ 117

Case Digest

Subject: Customs

Keywords: Sea Custom Tariff Act 1975

Summary: Customs Tariff Act, 1975, Pre, s. 2, Sch. 1 - Classification of entries in Sch. on basis of trade parlance was in question - Expression was not defined and its meaning was to be considered - Held, its meaning should be understood in its popular sense i.e., how it is used in trade, industry or market.
