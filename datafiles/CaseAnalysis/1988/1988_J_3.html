State Of U.P. & Ors. v J.P. Chaurasia & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

27 September 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 933; (1989) SCC (L&S) 71; (1989) 1 SCC 121; AIR 1989 SC 19; 1988 (8) ATC 929; 1988 (2) CLR 597; 1989 (58) FLR 381; JT 1988 (4) SC 53; 1989 LabIC 1146; 1989 (1) LLJ 309; 1988 (2) LLN 978; 1988(2) SCALE 827; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 288; 1988 (5) SLR 638; [1988] Supp3 S.C.R. 288

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Advocates & Judges - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14, 16, 39(d), 226, 229(2) and 309 - Allahabad High Court Officers and Staff (Conditions of Services and Conduct) Rules, 1976, rr. 16 and 18 - Constitution - Service Rules - Bench Secretaries and Section officers of Allahabad HC - (A) Evaluation of posts - By expert bodies like Pay Commission - No interference by Courts normally - Equation of posts - Determination - (B) Equal pay for equal work - Two pay scales in same cadre of persons (Bench Secretaries) performing same or similar duties - Classification in Grade I and Grade II on merit-cum -Seniority basis - Constitutionality - Held, (A) Not equivalent posts and not entitled to similar pay scale - (B) Permissible and no violation of art. 14 of Constitution - Appeal allowed.
