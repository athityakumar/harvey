Messrs Commercial Aviation and Travel Company and v Vimal Pannalal

Supreme Court of India

13 July 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Dutt, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 508; (1988) 3 SCC 423; AIR 1988 SC 1636; 1988 BBCJ 57; 1988 (36) DLT 83; JT 1988 (3) SC 41; 1988 (2) PLR 288; 1988(2) SCALE 1; [1988] Supp1 S.C.R. 431; 1988 (2) UJ 354

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice and Procedure - Court-fees Act, 1870, s.7(iv)(f) - Punjab Courts Act, 1918 - Suits Valuation Act, 1887 - Suit for rendition of accounts in Union Territory of Delhi - Valuation of Court fee - Acceptance of plaintiff's valuation - Held, Court has to accept plaintiff's valuation tentatively - Appeal dismissed.
