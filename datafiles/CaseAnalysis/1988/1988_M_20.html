Budhu Mal Etc., v Mahablr Prasad And Ors., Etc.

Supreme Court of India

05 August 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

N.D. Ojha, R.S. Pathak, L.M. Sharma

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 781; (1988) 4 SCC 194; AIR 1988 SC 1772; 1988 (2) AWC 1057; 1988 BBCJ 123; JT 1988 (3) SC 281; 1988 (2) RCJ 354; 1988(2) SCALE 294; [1988] Supp2 S.C.R. 238; 1988 (2) UJ 514

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice and Procedure - Provincial Small Cause Courts Act, 1887, s.23 - Jurisdiction of Small Cause Court to entertain suit involving question of title - Return of plaint - Held, not obligatory on Small Cause Court - But discretion ought to be exercised in some cases, to do complete justice between the parties - Appeals allowed.
