Member-Secretary, Andhra Pradesh Stateboard For Prevention A v Andhra Pradesh Rayons Ltd. And Ors

Supreme Court of India

30 September 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, M.H. Kania, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 245; (1989) SCC (Tax) 30; (1989) 1 SCC 44; AIR 1989 SC 611; JT 1988 (4) SC 154B; 1988(2) SCALE 1181; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 380; [1988] Supp3 S.C.R. 380

Case Digest

Subject: Environment, Wildlife & Animal; Indirect Tax; Water

Keywords: Water Pollution, Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Cess Act, 1977

Summary: Water - Indirect Tax - Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution ) Cess Act, 1977, s. 2(c), 3, sch. 1 - For ascertaining liability as to cess - Company manufacturing Rayon Grade Pulp - A base material for manufacturing of synthetics or man made fabrics - Whether it is specified industry? - If so, whether liable to pay cess? - Held, is not specified industry - Not liable to pay cess - Petition dismissed.
