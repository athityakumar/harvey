Messrs Jetha Bai and Sons, Jew Town, Cochin and Others v Messrs Sunderdas Rathenai and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 February 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Natarajan, S. Ranganathan, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 426; (1988) 1 SCC 722; AIR 1988 SC 812; 1988 CRLJ 936; JT 1988 (1) SC 329; 1988 (1) KLT 386; 1988 (1) RCJ 439; 1988 (1) RCR(Rent) 379; 1988 (1) RentLR 430; 1988(1) SCALE 243; [1988] 2 S.C.R. 871; 1988 (1) UJ 507

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Code of Civil Procedure 1908

Summary: Rent Control - Constitution of India, 1950 - Karnataka Rent Control Act, 1961 - Kerala Buildings (Lease and Rent Control) Act , 1965 - (A) Revisional order of District Court u/s. 20 of Kerala Act - Second revision to HC - Maintainability and consideration of nature of decisions of SC in Aundal Ammal v. Sadasivan (1986 INDLAW SC 605) and Shyamaraju Hegde v. Venkatesha (1987 INDLAW SC 28419) - (B) Overruling of precedents - Decision good law for number of years, if could not be overruled - Not maintainable and decisions are not conflicting - Held, not a ground for not overruling it, when it is found to suffer from misconstruction of relevant provisions and weakness in reasoning - Appeal disposed of.
