Union Of India v All India Services Pensioners Association And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

14 January 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, B.C. Ray

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 812; (1988) 2 SCC 580; (1988) SCC (L&S) 651; AIR 1988 SC 501; 1988 (7) ATC 449; 1988 (56) FLR 377; JT 1988 (1) SC 96; 1988 LabIC 973; 1988 (2) LLJ 196; 1988 (2) LLN 175; 1988(1) SCALE 93; [1988] 2 S.C.R. 697; 1988 (3) SLJ 1; 1988 (1) SLR 353; 1988 (1) UJ 428

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 16, 309, 311 and 141 - All India Services (Death-cum-Retirement Benefits) Rules, 1958, r. 28(6) - Govt. Notification No. 33/12/73-AIS (ii) D/- 24-01-1975 - Service Rules - (A) Liberalised pensionary benefits - Gratuity - Entitlement of, to Members of All India Service who have retired prior to 1973 - Applicability of rates fixed in 1975 Notification and rule in D S Nakara's case ( 1982 Indlaw SC 158) - (B) Precedent - Order dismissing special leave petition - Reasons therefore given - Held, not entitled and case held, not applicable - Decision is binding as precedent u/art. 141 - Appeal allowed.
