Indian Cement And Ors. v State Of Andhra Pradesh And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

12 January 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, M.M. Dutt

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 687; (1988) SCC (Tax) 170; (1988) 1 SCC 743; AIR 1988 SC 567; JT 1988 (1) SC 86; 1988(1) SCALE 43; [1988] 2 S.C.R. 574; 1988 (69) STC 305; 1988 (1) UJ 416

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure; Sales Tax

Summary: Constitution - Municipalities & Local Governments - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 301 - Central Sales Tax Act, 1956, s. 8(5) - Andhra Pradesh General Sales Tax Act, 1957, s. 9(1) - Andhra Pradesh Notifications D/27-01-1987 and Karnataka Notification, D/28-02-81 A P Notifications D/1987 u/s. 8(5) and 9(1) and Karnataka Notification, D/81 u/s. 8(5) - Sustainability of - Held, not sustainable, as they are violative of provision of Part XIII of Constitution - Petition allowed.
