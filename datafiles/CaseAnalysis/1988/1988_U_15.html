Union Of India And Anr. v L.K. Ahuja And Co.

Supreme Court of India

05 April 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 470; (1988) 3 SCC 76; AIR 1988 SC 1172; 1988 (1) ARBLR 375; 1988 (1) AWC 698; JT 1988 (2) SC 82; 1988(1) SCALE 710; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 402; 1988 (1) UJ 702

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Arbitration Act, 1940 - Application for reference - Maintainability - Matters to be taken into account - Contractor executing construction works accepting payments and giving no claim declaration - Whether contractor claiming certain amount from given contract is maintainable? - Held, application was maintainable - Whether claim subsists or not is arbitrable - On completion of work, right to get payment would normally arise.
