Nat Steel Equipment Pvt. Limited v Collector Of Central Excise

Supreme Court of India

19 January 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 196; (1988) SCC (Tax) 128; (1988) 1 SCC 605; AIR 1988 SC 631; 1988 CRLR 198; 1988 CrLR(SC) 198; 1988 (36) DLT 97; 1988 (15) ECR 1; 1988 (34) E.L.T. 8; JT 1988 (1) SC 228; 1988(1) SCALE 214; [1988] 2 S.C.R. 732; 1988 (69) STC 58

Case Digest

Subject: Excise

Keywords: Tariff Item, Bombay Sales Act, 1953

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, ss. Sch. 1, Item No. 33 C, 68, 11 A - Excise Duty - (A) Manufacturer manufacturing equipments which were used in industrial canteens, five star hotels, big hospitals etc. - Equipments were similar appliances which are normally used in household - Item under which it is assessable - (B) Duty - Levy of - Absence of any proof of suppression of fact - Applicability of s. 11 A of Act - Said items would be taxable under Item 33 C and not Item No. 68 - Held, s. 11 A would not be applicable - Appeal rejected.
