Smt. Angoori Devi For Ram Ratan v Union Of India And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

06 December 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 227; (1989) SCC (Cr) 164; (1989) 1 SCC 385; 1989 (26) ACC 1; AIR 1989 SC 371; 1989 (26) AllCriC 1; 1989 (1) Crimes 145; 1989 CRLJ 950; 1989 CRLR 129; 1989 CrLR(SC) 129; JT 1988 (4) SC 587; 1988(2) SCALE 1497; [1988] Supp3 S.C.R. 1023

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal; Defence & Security Forces; Service

Keywords: Defence Regulations

Summary: National Security Act 1980, s. 3(2) - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 22 - Detention under solitary offence committed by Police constables with assistance of member of public - It had no connection to disturb public order - Held, their preventive detention was illegal.
