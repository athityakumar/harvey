Collector Of Central Excise, Madras v T.I. Millers Ltd. Madras And T.I. Diamond Chain, Madras

Supreme Court of India

28 March 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 614; (1988) Supp SCC 361; AIR 1988 SC 1154; 1988 CRLR 300; 1988 (17) ECC 56; 1988 (17) ECR 63; 1988 (35) E.L.T. 8; JT 1988 (2) SC 86; 1988(1) SCALE 716; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 355

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Criminal; Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, s.35L(b) - 'Related person' - Assessee company and buyer company - Buyer company executing major portion of sales - And, main distributer of holding company of the assessee company - Not holding company or subsidiary company or relative of the manufacturer - Held, not establishes the Related person concept.
