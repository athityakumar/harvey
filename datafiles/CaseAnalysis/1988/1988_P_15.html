Dharma Shamrao Agalawe v Pandurang Miragu Agalawe And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

22 February 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, K.N. Singh

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 375; (1988) 2 SCC 126; AIR 1988 SC 845; 1988 (3) Bom.C.R. 266; 1988 (2) DMC 275; JT 1988 (1) SC 376; 1988 MahLJ 273; 1989 (2) MLJ(SC) 9; 1988 MPLJ 164; 1988(1) SCALE 365; [1988] 2 S.C.R. 1077; 1988 (1) UJ 485

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal

Keywords: Coparcener, Death Of a Coparcener

Summary: Family and Personal - Hindu Adoptions and Maintenance Act, 1956 - Scope of Provisio (c) to s. 12 of the Act - Joint family - Only having the sole surviving coparcener and widow of deceased coparcener - Commencement of 1956 Act - Widow adopted the son - If they can claim partition - Held, can claim partition - Proviso (c) is not attracted - No question of divesting sole coparcener of his estate - Appeal dismissed.
