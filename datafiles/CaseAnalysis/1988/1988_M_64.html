Guru Govekar v Miss Filomena F. Lobo And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

06 May 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, N.D. Ojha

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 495; (1988) 3 SCC 1; (1988) SCC (Cr) 535; 1988 (2) ACC 625; 1988 (2) ACJ 585; AIR 1988 SC 1332; 1988 (2) AWC 1099; 1988 (3) Bom.C.R. 29; [1988] 64 Comp Cas 631; JT 1988 (2) SC 273; 1988(1) SCALE 834; [1988] Supp1 S.C.R. 170; 1988 (2) UJ 276

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Corporate; Practice & Procedure; Tort

Summary: Carriers and Transportation - Motor Vehicles Act 1939, ss.94, 95 - Liability of insurer regarding compensation for motor accident - When was in vehicle in custody of repairer and claimant, a third party suffered injuries in public place on account of negligence of mechanic engaged by repairer - Held, insurer liable to pay compensation found to be due to claimant - Appeal allowed.
