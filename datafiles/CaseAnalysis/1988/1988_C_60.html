Chuharmal S/O Takarmal Mohnani v Commissioner Of Income-Tax, M.P., Bhopal

Supreme Court of India

02 May 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 360; (1988) 3 SCC 588; AIR 1988 SC 1384; 1988 (70) CTR 88; [1988] 172 ITR 250; JT 1988 (2) SC 495; 1988(1) SCALE 1119; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 788; 1988 TAXLR 1205; [1988] 38 TAXMAN 190

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Finance Act, 1964, Finance Act, 1974

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Customs Act, 1962 - Evidence Act, 1872 - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss.143(2), 271(1)(c) - Confiscation of 565 wrist-watches of foreign make from the house and levy of penalty - Show cause notice u/s.143(2) of 1961 Act asking why there value should not be treated as his income from undisclosed sources - High Court upheld the imposition of penalty u/s. 271(1)(c) of Act as well as the addition of alleged concealed income in the income tax assessment of the petitioner - Validity - Held, it was proper - Appeal dismissed.
