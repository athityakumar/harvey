S. Appukutan v Thundiyil Janaki Amma And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

13 January 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Natarajan, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 310; (1988) 2 SCC 372; AIR 1988 SC 587; JT 1988 (1) SC 184; 1988 (1) KLT 512; 1988(1) SCALE 159; [1988] 2 S.C.R. 661; 1988 (1) UJ 524

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure; Agriculture & Agri. Products

Keywords: Tenancy and Land Laws Kerala Law reforms Act 1963

Summary: Land & Property - Municipalities & Local Governments - Kerala Land Reforms Act, 1964, ss. 2(25), Explanation IIA - Kundikidappukaran and Kudikidappu rights - As per amendment Act of 1972 - Entitlement - Person in occupation of homestead or hut belonging to another between 16-08-1968 to 01-01-1970 is entitled to such rights - Occupation is not referable to permission granted by owner of land - Petition dismissed.
