State of Gujarat and Another v Punjabhai Nathubhai and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 February 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

M.N. Venkatachaliah, M.H. Kania, S. Natarajan

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 696; (1988) 2 SCC 478; JT 1988 (1) SC 471; 1988(1) SCALE 335; 1988 (1) UJ 473

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Land & Property - Administrative - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 6 - Land Acquisition (Amendment and Validation) Ordinance, 1967 - Notification - Unreasonable delay - Legality - Appeal was filed by the State/appellant arose out of and was directed against the judgment of HC quashing, at the instance of land owners - Final notification, dt. 18-1-1969 issued u/s. 6 of the Act - Impugned notification was quashed by DB principally on the ground that there was an unreasonable delay in the publication of the final notification which was separated by 5 1/2 years from the preliminary notification u/s. 4(1) of the Act which was published on 26-7-1963 - Hence instant appeal - Whether HC was justified in passing the impugned order - Held, point raised in instant appeal was, therefore, fully covered by the pronouncement of SC in Gujarat State Transport Corporation v. Valji Mulji Soneji, 1979 Indlaw SC 234 - For the reasons set out in and following the said pronouncement - Impugned judgment of HC under appeal set aside and civil application before HC was dismissed - Appeal allowed.
