Hameedia Hardware Stores, Represented By Its Partner S. Peer Mohammed v B. Mohan Lal Sowcar

Supreme Court of India

29 March 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, N.D. Ojha

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 423; (1988) 2 SCC 513; AIR 1988 SC 1060; JT 1988 (1) SC 664; 1988 (2) RCR(Rent) 559; 1988 (1) RentLR 524; 1988(1) SCALE 615; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 384

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - T.N. Buildings (Lease and Rent Control) Act, 1960 - Tenancy - Whether there is a need for bona fide requirement for eviction of tenant from non-residential premises - When landlord already proving other ingredients u/s. 10(3)(a)(iii) of the Act - Held, landlord should establish that he bona fide requires premises, in addition to proving other ingredients - Appeal allowed.
