Ayya Alias Ayub v State Of U.P. And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

25 November 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

M.N. Venkatachaliah, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 42; (1989) SCC (Cr) 153; (1989) 1 SCC 374; AIR 1989 SC 364; 1989 (1) AWC 90; 1989 (1) Crimes 8; 1989 CRLJ 991; 1989 CrLR(SC) 668; JT 1988 (4) SC 489; 1988(2) SCALE 1489; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 967; [1988] Supp3 S.C.R. 967; 1989 (1) UJ 157

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: National Security Act 1980, s. 3(2) - Detention due to maintenance of public order was necessary - Detaining authority omitted to consider relevant material and assumed materiality - Held, detention order set aside on ground of non-application of mind.
