State Of Punjab & Ors. v Kailash Nath and another

Supreme Court of India

22 November 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

N.D. Ojha, B.C. Ray

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 41; (1989) SCC (Cr) 128; (1989) 1 SCC 321; AIR 1989 SC 558; 1989 (1) Crimes 126; 1989 CRLJ 813; 1989 CRLR 16; 1989 (58) FLR 32; JT 1988 (4) SC 502; 1989 (2) LLJ 209; 1989 (1) LLN 256; 1988(2) SCALE 1464; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 911; 1989 (1) SLR 12; [1988] Supp3 S.C.R. 911

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Service

Keywords: Punjab Police Rules 1934

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 309, 21 and 14 - Punjab Civil Services Rules 1959, column. 2, Chap. 2 , r. 2.2(b), proviso. 3 - Whether provision placing embargo on prosecution of Govt. servant comes under conditions of service - Prosecution of Govt. servant u/r. 2.2 was also in question - Held, provision does not fall within purview of conditions of service and r. 2.2 cannot be interpreted as placing embargo on prosecution of Govt. servant on expiry of period stipulated in rule.
