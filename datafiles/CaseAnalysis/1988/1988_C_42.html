Asian Paints India Limited v Collector Of Central Excise

Supreme Court of India

23 March 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 415; (1988) 2 SCC 470; AIR 1988 SC 1087; 1988 CRLR 259; 1988 CrLR(SC) 259; 1988 (16) ECC 172; 1988 (16) ECR 119; 1988 (35) E.L.T. 3; JT 1988 (2) SC 8; 1988(1) SCALE 628; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 339; 1988 (70) STC 38; 1988 (2) UJ 104

Case Digest

Subject: Excise

Keywords: Synthetic

Summary: Excise - Central Excises And Salt Act, 1944, s. 35L - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944 - Excise Duty - Tribunal - Finding that Decoplast is plastic emulsion paint, covered by item 14(i)(3)(iv) and not by item 14(i)(5) - On consideration of composition, characteristics, user and how article is known in the trade - Legality - Held, no misdirection in law - Supreme Court declined to interfere with finding of fact arrived at on relevant and valid materials - Appeal dismissed.
