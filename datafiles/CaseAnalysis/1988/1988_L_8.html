State Of Orissa & Ors. v Lall Brothers

Supreme Court of India

25 August 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 571; (1988) 4 SCC 153; AIR 1988 SC 2018; JT 1988 (3) SC 552; 1988(2) SCALE 542; [1988] Supp2 S.C.R. 579; 1988 (2) UJ 652

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Summary: Arbitration and ADR- Arbitration Act, 1940, ss.14, 30 and 35 - Arbitration Award - Challenged as unreasoned - And that lump sum amount was awarded, without specifying amount awarded on particular grounds - Whether liable to be set aside - Held, fact that a lump sum award has been given, is no ground to declare the award bad and it cannot be set aside as bad - Application dismissed.
