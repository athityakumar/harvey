Adltya Mills Limited v Union Of India

Supreme Court of India

29 August 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 891; (1988) 4 SCC 315; AIR 1988 SC 2237; 1988 (19) ECR 292; 1988 (37) E.L.T. 471; JT 1988 (4) SC 151; 1988(2) SCALE 1068; 1989 (73) STC 195; [1988] Supp2 S.C.R. 668

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax; Land & Property; Sales Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, sch.1 item 68 - Central Excise Rules, 1944 , r.173B - Excise Duty - Manufacturing process - Doubling together of two plies of Polyester Spun Yarn and one ply of Rayon Filament Yarn - A new yarn has come into being which is a separate and distinct item - Determination of taxability of resultant material - Held, resultant Yarn is taxable - Appeal dismissed.
