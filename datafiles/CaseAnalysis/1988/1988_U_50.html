M.L. Jain v Union Of India

Supreme Court of India

18 August 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, S. Natarajan

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 735; (1988) 4 SCC 121; (1988) SCC (L&S) 908; AIR 1989 SC 669; 1988 (36) DLT 118; 1988 (57) FLR 584; JT 1988 (3) SC 499; 1995 (3) LLJ 609; 1988 (2) LLN 956; 1988(2) SCALE 370; 1989 (1) SLJ 178; 1988 (4) SLR 495; [1988] Supp2 S.C.R. 496; 1988 (2) UJ 761

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Service

Keywords: High Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Act, 1954

Summary: Advocates and Judges - Constitution - High Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Act, 1954, s. 15(1)(b) - High Court Judges Rules, 1956, r. 2 - Constitution, arts. 14 and 221 - HC Judge - Fixation of revised pension - Liberty to State Govts. in appointing different dates for grant of revised pension - Constitutionality - Held, violative of art. 14 - Petition allowed.
