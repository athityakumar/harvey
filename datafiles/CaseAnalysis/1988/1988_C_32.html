Mahalakshmi Glass Works (P) Limited v Collector Of Central Excise, Bombay

Supreme Court of India

26 July 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 794; (1988) Supp SCC 601; AIR 1988 SC 1838; 1988 CRLR 519; 1988 CrLR(SC) 519; 1988 (18) ECC 159; 1988 (18) ECR 381; 1988 (36) E.L.T. 727; JT 1988 (3) SC 563; 1988(2) SCALE 718; 1989 (73) STC 17; [1988] Supp1 S.C.R. 587; 1988 (2) UJ 523

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Customs; Indirect Tax; Sales Tax

Keywords: Central Excise, Price List

Summary: Central Excises & Salt Act, 1944, s. 4(4)(d) - Packing material - Excise duty - Levy - 'Returnable by buyer to assessee' - Glass bottles manufacturing company - Packed in gunny bags and cartons for sale - Agreement not indicating whether packages were returnable - Held, levy of duty on such material is proper.
