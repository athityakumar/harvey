Collector Of Central Excise, Madras v Indian Oxygen Ltd.

Supreme Court of India

02 August 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, L.M. Sharma

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 364; (1988) 4 SCC 139; (1988) SCC (Tax) 491; AIR 1988 SC 1873; 1988 CRLR 526; 1988 CrLR(SC) 526; 1988 (18) ECC 172; 1988 (18) ECR 378; 1988 (36) E.L.T. 730; JT 1988 (3) SC 334; 1988(2) SCALE 599; 1989 (72) STC 118; [1988] Supp1 S.C.R. 761; 1988 (2) UJ 529

Case Digest

Subject: Excise

Summary: Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, s. 4(1)(a) - Value of excisable goods - Determination - Assessee manufacturing gases - Supply made in returnable cylinders - Rental charged and deposit taken for cylinders - Held, neither rent charged nor interest earned on deposit for safe return of cylinder can form part of assessable value.
