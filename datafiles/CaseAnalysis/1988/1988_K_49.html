Piara Lal v Kewal Krishan Chopra

Supreme Court of India

06 May 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Natarajan, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 922; (1988) 3 SCC 51; AIR 1988 SC 1432; JT 1988 (2) SC 502; 1988 (2) RCJ 115; 1988 (2) RCR(Rent) 32; 1988 (2) RentLR 181; 1988(1) SCALE 1012; [1988] Supp1 S.C.R. 202; 1988 (2) UJ 144

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - East Punjab Urban Rent Restriction Act, 1949, s.13(3)(a)(iii) - Tenancy - Validity of eviction on the ground that roof of one room in leased portion fell down - In absence of any finding that rest of building was damaged or had become weak - Held, improper - Appeal allowed.
