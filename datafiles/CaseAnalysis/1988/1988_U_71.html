Jayanti Kumar Sinha v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 September 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, M.N. Venkatachaliah

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 892; (1989) Supp1 SCC 1; (1989) SCC (L&S) 173; AIR 1989 SC 72; 1989 (9) ATC 280; 1989 LabIC 1130; 1989 (1) LLJ 566; 1989 (1) LLN 236; 1988(2) SCALE 800; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 5; 1988 (5) SLR 705; [1988] Supp3 S.C.R. 5

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: All India Services (Death-Cum-Retirement Benefits) Rules, 1958, Central Civil Services Refutations

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 311 and 309, Proviso - Civil Services Regulations, 1920, art. 459(h) - Civil Services - Deputy Chief Scientific Officer in Defence Electronic Research Laboratory - Compulsory retirement of - Order of premature retirement challenged on ground of malafide - Held, cannot be challenged - Further held, that unanimous conclusion and fair assessment of work by scientists under whom delinquent officer worked - Appeal dismissed.
