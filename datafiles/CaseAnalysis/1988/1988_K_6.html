K. Krishna Reddy And Ors. v Special Dy. Collector, Land Acquisition Unit Ii,Lmd Karimnag

Supreme Court of India

08 September 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 898; (1988) 4 SCC 163; AIR 1988 SC 2123; JT 1988 (3) SC 590; 1988(2) SCALE 577; [1988] Supp2 S.C.R. 853; 1989 (1) UJ 59

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property

Summary: Criminal - IPC, 1860, ss.96, 300, 304, Part II - Sentence - Dispute followed by exchange of abuse few day before the incident of murder - Accused alleged to have inflicted a stab wound on abdomen of deceased, resulting in his death - Defence that he inflicted the knife blow only after scuffle had started - But knowledge could be imputed to him that it may result in death - Conviction u/s.300 and sent to borstal school - Held, conviction of the appellant is altered to s. 304 Part II - Appeal allowed.
