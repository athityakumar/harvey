Braham Dass v State of Himachal Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

02 August 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

M.N. Venkatachaliah, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 863; (1988) 4 SCC 130; (1988) SCC (Cr) 908; AIR 1988 SC 1789; 1988 (14) ALR 710; 1988 (2) AWC 1026; 1988 BLJR 590; 1988 CRLJ 1816; 1988 CRLR 510; JT 1988 (3) SC 184; 1988(2) SCALE 308; 1988 (2) UJ 598

Case Digest

Subject: Socio-Economic

Keywords: Food Adulteration

Summary: Prevention of Food Adulteration Act, 1955, Secs. 2 (i-a), 2 (f), 2 (m) and 16 (1) (a) (i) read with Sec. 7-' Masur whole ' sample-No notification prescribing standard for Masur-Cl. (m) would not apply-Article was found insect infested and was otherwise unfit for human consumption-Cl. (f) will cover the situation-Conviction sustainable.
