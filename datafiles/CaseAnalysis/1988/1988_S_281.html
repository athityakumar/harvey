General Officer Commanding-In-Chief And Anr. v Subhash Chandra Yadav And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

25 February 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Dutt, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 386; (1988) 2 SCC 351; (1988) SCC (L&S) 542; AIR 1988 SC 876; 1988 (7) ATC 296; JT 1988 (1) SC 458; 1988 LabIC 1014; 1988 (2) LLJ 345; 1988 (2) LLN 937; 1988(1) SCALE 414; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 62; 1988 (3) SLJ 91; 1988 (1) SLR 759; 1988 (1) UJ 596

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Centralised Service

Summary: Service - Cantonments Act, 1924, s. 280 - Cantonment Board Servants Rules, 1937, r. 5 - Cantonment - Whether r. 5-C of Cantonment Board Service Rules is ultra vires provision of rule making power of Central Govt. under Cantonment Act? - Held, ultra vires - Appeal dismissed.
