Roop Chand Adlakha And Ors. v Delhi Development Authority And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

26 September 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

M.N. Venkatachaliah, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 234; (1989) Supp1 SCC 116; (1989) SCC (L&S) 235; AIR 1989 SC 307; 1989 (9) ATC 639; 1989 (58) FLR 549; 1989 LabIC 1268; 1989 (1) LLN 268; 1988(2) SCALE 897; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 253; [1988] Supp3 S.C.R. 253

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14 and 16 - Promotion - Engineers - From cadre of Junior Engineers to that of Asst. Engineers and from cadre of Asst. Engineers to that of Executive Engineers - Eligibility - Prescribing different conditions for diploma holders and graduates - Constitutionality - Held, not violative of arts. 14 and 16 - Appeal allowed.
