Spaco Carburettors (India) Limited v Collector Of Customs, Bombay

Supreme Court of India

24 February 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, M.M. Dutt

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 915; (1988) Supp SCC 156; 1988 (36) DLT 4; 1988 (16) ECC 1; 1988 (14) ECR 254; 1988 (34) E.L.T. 3; JT 1988 (1) SC 441; 1988(1) SCALE 429; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 37; 1988 (1) UJ 553

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Keywords: Custom

Summary: Indirect Tax - Customs Act, 1962, s. 130 E - Explanatory Note 84.45 (A) (5) of CCCN (Volume 3) - (A) Whether imported special purpose complex machine has to be charged to Customs duty under Item 89.59(1) as claimed by Revenue or under 84.45/48 of Tariff Schedule as maintained by appellant? - Machine is a machine tool working on metal and should legitimately find its way into entry 84.45/48 - Once it is so identified it does not get into residuary entry - Held, order of CEGAT vacated and finding of Collector restored - Appeal allowed.
