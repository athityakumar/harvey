Jugal Kishore v State Of Maharashtra And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

26 September 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 235; (1989) Supp1 SCC 589; AIR 1989 SC 159; 1988 (4) Bom.C.R. 274; JT 1988 (4) SC 109; 1988(2) SCALE 1076; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 270; [1988] Supp3 S.C.R. 270

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Maharashtra Agricultural Land (Celling on holdings) Act, 1961

Summary: Land & Property - Maharashtra Agricultural Lands (Ceiling on Holdings) Act, 1961, ss. 14 and 21 - Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Vidarbha Region) Act, 1958, s. 100 - Jurisdiction of ceiling authorities - Proceedings of ceiling - Landholder transfered land to tenants - Whether such transfers were bona fide or made to defeat provisions of ceiling law can be decided by ceiling authorities? - Held, ceiling authorities have jurisdiction to decide - Application dismissed.
