Dr. Baliram Waman Hiray v Justice B. Lentin And Others

Supreme Court of India

12 September 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, L.M. Sharma

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 704; (1988) 4 SCC 419; (1988) SCC (Cr) 941; AIR 1988 SC 2267; 1988 (90) BomLR 434; 1989 CRLJ 306; [1989] 176 ITR 1; 1989 (72) STC 384; [1988] Supp2 S.C.R. 942; [1989] 44 TAXMAN 111

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal

Keywords: Sick Industrial Companies (Special Provisions) Act, 1986, Coast Guard Act, 1978, Railway Act 1890

Summary: Family and Personal - Hindu Marriage Act, 1955, s.13(1)(iii) - Divorce - Suit for a decree of dissolution of his marriage - Whether mere branding of spouse as schizophrenic is sufficient for divorce on the ground of mental disorder - Held, mere branding will not suffice - Degree of mental disorder must be proved - Appeal dismissed.
