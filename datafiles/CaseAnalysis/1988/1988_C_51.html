Messrs Narne Tulaman Manufacturers Private Limited, Hyderabad v Collector of Central Excise, Hyderabad

Supreme Court of India

15 September 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, M.H. Kania, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 623; (1989) SCC (Tax) 64; (1989) 1 SCC 172; AIR 1989 SC 79; 1988 (18) ECC 165; 1989 (20) ECR 129; 1988 (38) E.L.T. 566; [1990] 183 ITR 577; JT 1988 (4) SC 1; 1989 (73) STC 81; [1988] Supp3 S.C.R. 1

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Indirect Tax; Sales Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excises And Salt Act, 1944, ss. 2(f) and 3, chap. 1, Tariff Item 45 - Manufacturer - Company - Assembling components of weigh bridge - And manufacturing one of its part - Whether liable to pay Excise Duty as manufacturer of weigh bridge? - Held, Company would become manufacturer of weigh bridge and as such liable to pay duty - Appeal dismissed.
