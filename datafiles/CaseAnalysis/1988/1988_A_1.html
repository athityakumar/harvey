A Secretary, Regional Transport Authority,Bangalore And Anot v D.P. Sharma And Others

Supreme Court of India

12 December 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Dutt, S. Natarajan

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 297; (1989) Supp1 SCC 407; AIR 1989 SC 509; 1989 (1) CCC 141; JT 1988 (4) SC 686; 1988(2) SCALE 1521; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 1038; [1988] Supp3 S.C.R. 1038; 1989 (1) UJ 183

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Civil Procedure; Constitution; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Constitutional Validity of a Provision, Grant Of Permit

Summary: Carriers & Transportation - Karnataka Contract Carriages (Acquisition) Act, 1976, ss. 3(g), 3(m), 14 and 20 - Motor Vehicles Act, 1939, ss. 62 and 63 - Grant of special permit - Public service vehicle, to which special permit not granted prior to enforcement of 1976 Act - Applicability of prohibition u/s. 20 of 1976 Act - Considering meaning of words 'has been' in definition of 'contract carriage' u/s. 3(g) of 1976 - Prohibition u/s. 20 of 1976 Act would be extended against issue of special permit - Appeal allowed.
