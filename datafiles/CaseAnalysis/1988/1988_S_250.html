Ranjeet Singh and Another v State of Rajasthan

Supreme Court of India

25 January 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, B.C. Ray, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 15; (1988) SCC (Cr) 229; (1988) 1 SCC 633; AIR 1988 SC 672; 1988 CRLJ 845; 1988 CRLR 173; JT 1988 (1) SC 473; 1988(1) SCALE 171

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 300 and 302 - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 133 - Petitioner, accused of cold blooded murder of his brother's sleeping family with lethal weapons, including innocent children - Recovery of weapons at instance of accused corroborated evidence of approver - Validity of death sentence - Held, imposition of sentence of death on accused was not inappropriate - Appeal dismissed.
