P.K.K. Shamsudeen v K.A.M. Mappillai Mohindeen And Ors

Supreme Court of India

24 November 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Natarajan, M.M. Dutt

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 288; (1989) 1 SCC 526; AIR 1989 SC 640; JT 1988 (4) SC 473; 1988(2) SCALE 1445; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 950; [1988] Supp3 S.C.R. 950; 1989 (1) UJ 103

Case Digest

Subject: Election; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Order For, Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1958

Summary: Election - Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1958, s. 178 - Panchayat President - Election petition u/s. 178 for setting aside election, recounting of votes - Tribunal passed order for recounting - Without averment or production of evidence - Legality - Held, order is illegal - Order cannot be justified on results of recount of votes Petition dismissed.
