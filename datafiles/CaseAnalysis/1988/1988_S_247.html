Mahesh Chand and Another v State of Rajasthan

Supreme Court of India

14 March 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

B.C. Ray, K. Jagannatha Shetty

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 18; (1990) Supp SCC 681; (1991) SCC (Cr) 159; AIR 1988 SC 2111; 1988 BLJR 468; 1989 CRLJ 121; 1989 CRLR 630; JT 1988 (1) SC 618

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal- - Penal Code, 1860 - Offence u/s.307 - Compounding of - Accused acquitted by trial Court but were convicted by HC - Offence not compoundable but parties praying before Supreme Court to treat it as special case - One of the accused was a lawyer practicing in the lower Court - Counter case from the same transaction was already compromised - Held, it would be proper that trial Court should permit the parties to compound offence - .
