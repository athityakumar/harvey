Indian Rayon Corpn. Limited v Raunaq And Company Pvt. Ltd.

Supreme Court of India

04 August 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, L.M. Sharma

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 778; (1988) 4 SCC 31; AIR 1988 SC 2054; 1988 (2) ARBLR 441; 1989 (1) CalLT(SC) 57; 1989 (1) CompLJ 101; JT 1988 (3) SC 482; 1988(2) SCALE 739; [1988] Supp2 S.C.R. 231; 1988 (2) UJ 627

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure; Corporate; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice and Procedure - Arbitration and ADR - Limitation Act, 1908 - Limitation Act, 1963 - Arbitration Act, 1940, s. 14(2) - Period of limitation as to application for setting aside award commences from date of service of notice - Whether limitation commences there from or from subsequent date on which notice u/s.14(2) was issued - When conduct of party revealing that it acknowledged filing of award - Held, limitation commences there from - Courts found that the notice was issued and served and the application made in this case was clearly barred by lapse of time - Appeal dismissed.
