Nagar Mahapalika Bareilly v State Of U.P. And Others

Supreme Court of India

02 February 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 353; (1988) 2 SCC 193; AIR 1988 SC 850; 1988 ALJ 527; 1988 (14) ALR 263; JT 1988 (1) SC 594; 1988(1) SCALE 307; [1988] 2 S.C.R. 865; 1988 (70) STC 97; 1988 (1) UJ 458

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Municipalities & Local Governments; Sales Tax; Service

Keywords: Uttar Pradesh Nagar Mahapalika, Adhiniyam, 1959

Summary: Municipalities & Local Governments - Uttar Pradesh Nagar Mahapalika, Adhiniyam, 1959, s. 172(2)(b) - Notification u/s. 172(2)(b) of Act, D/- 30-04-1986 - Determination of rate of octroi on country liquor (rectified spirit) which is not covered under item 'Foreign liquor or wines manufactured in India' - Held, imposable at 5 P per litre as on rectified spirit and not at Re 1/ - Appeal dismissed.
