All India Sainik Schools Employees Association v Defence Minister-Cum-Chairman Board of Governors

Supreme Court of India

04 October 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, M.N. Venkatachaliah

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 217; (1989) Supp1 SCC 205; (1989) SCC (L&S) 264; AIR 1989 SC 88; 1989 (9) ATC 827; 1988 (2) CCC 834; 1988 (57) FLR 813; JT 1988 (4) SC 22; 1989 LabIC 1125; 1989 (1) LLJ 263; 1989 (1) LLN 540; 1988(2) SCALE 868; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 398; 1988 (5) SLR 626; [1988] Supp3 S.C.R. 398; 1989 (1) UJ 288

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure; Trusts & Associations

Summary: Service - Constitution - Constitution of India, 1950, art.12, 14, 32, 39(d) and 226 - Sainik School employees - Service Conditions - Constitutionality - Held, pleas of discrimination and equal pay, equal work not available - However directions issued by Court - Order accordingly.
