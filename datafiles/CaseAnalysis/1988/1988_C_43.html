Elson Machines (P) Limited v Collector Of Central Excise

Supreme Court of India

15 November 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 276; (1989) Supp1 SCC 671; AIR 1989 SC 617; 1989 (19) ECC 80; 1988 (19) ECR 449; 1988 (38) E.L.T. 571; JT 1988 (4) SC 373; 1988(2) SCALE 1320; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 878; 1989 (1) ShimLC 339; [1988] Supp3 S.C.R. 878; 1989 (1) UJ 249

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Keywords: Tariff Item, Specified Goods

Summary: Excise - Central Excise Rules, 1944, r. 8 - Exemption Notification No. 80/80-C E, D/- 19-06-1980, Para 2, Expln. 5 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 133 - Excise duty - Exemption - To small scale manufacturers - If clearance value within specified limit - (A) Captive consumption of electric motors manufactured by asessee in manufacture of mono block pumps - Whether to be excluded while computing aggregate value of clearance as per Explaination. 5 - (B) Clearance of goods from factory - Captive consumption whether comes under purview of clearance - Held, (A) Not to be excluded - (B) Amounts to clearance - Appeal dismissed.
