Union Of India v Tata Yodogawa Limited

Supreme Court of India

21 September 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, M.H. Kania, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 937; 1988 (19) ECR 569; 1988 (38) E.L.T. 739; JT 1988 (4) SC 472

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Keywords: Counter-Affidavit, Anganbari Sevika

Summary: Excise - Practice & Procedure - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944 - Application for delay condonation - Delay of 51 days - Reason stated as delay due to 'inter-departmental correspondence and processing of matter to enable department to file instant petition' - Whether application for delay condonation can be allowed? - Held, there must be some way or attempt to explain cause for such delay - There is no sufficient cause to explain delay - Hence, application for condonation of delay is dismissed - Petition dismissed.
