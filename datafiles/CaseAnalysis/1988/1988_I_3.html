Tej Bhan Madan v Ii Additional District Judge And Others

Supreme Court of India

09 May 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

M.N. Venkatachaliah, R. S. Pathak, S. Natarajan

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 903; (1988) 3 SCC 137; AIR 1988 SC 1413; 1988 (14) ALR 457; 1988 (2) AWC 1017; JT 1988 (2) SC 574; 1988 (1) KarLJ 686; [1988] Supp1 S.C.R. 247

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Rent Control - Uttar Pradesh (Temporary) Control of Rent and Eviction Act, 1947, s.3(1)(f) - Evidence Act, s.115 - Tenancy - Appeal against rejection of challenge to decrees of ejectment on the ground that there was a denial of the title of the landlord u/s. 3(1)(f) of 1947 Act - Permissibility and effect of denial of title of purchaser by tenant - Held, precluded on the general principles of estoppel - Derivative title of subsequent purchaser could not have denied - Appeal dismissed.
