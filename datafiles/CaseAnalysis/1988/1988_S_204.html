Wazir Chand and another v State of Haryana and another

Supreme Court of India

01 December 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

M.H. Kania, B.C. Ray

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 203; (1989) SCC (Cr) 105; (1989) 1 SCC 244; AIR 1989 SC 378; 1989 (1) AWC 82; 1989 (1) Crimes 173; 1989 CRLJ 809; 1989 CRLR 124; JT 1988 (4) SC 558; 1988(2) SCALE 1477

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 107, 306 and 498 A - Dowry death - (A) Suicide by newly wedded wife - Abetment - Proof - Suicide not established with certainty by evidences - Conviction of husband and in-laws u/s. 306 - Validity - (B) Dowry - Harassment for - Conviction of husband and father in law - Repeated demands - Statements as to harassment of her parents - Dowry articles taken back by wife's family - Held, (A) Husband and in-laws could not be convicted u/s. 306 - (B) Liable to conviction u/s. 498 A - Appeal partly allowed.
