S.T. Muthusami v K. Natarajan And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

20 January 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 333; (1988) 1 SCC 572; AIR 1988 SC 616; JT 1988 (1) SC 159; 1988(1) SCALE 149; [1988] 2 S.C.R. 759; 1988 (1) UJ 531

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Election; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Election - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 226 and 227 - Effective alternative remedy - Commencement of election process - Interference in writ jurisdiction on ground of error in allotment of symbols - Validity - Held, not justified - Appeal allowed.
