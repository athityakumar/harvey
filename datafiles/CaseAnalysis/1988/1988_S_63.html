Ram Narain Gupta v Smt. Rameshwari Gupta

Supreme Court of India

12 September 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

M.N. Venkatachaliah, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 710; (1988) 4 SCC 247; AIR 1988 SC 2260; 1988 (14) ALR 722; 1988 (2) AWC 1231; 1988 (2) CCC 737; 1988 (2) DMC 364; 1988 (2) HLR 557; JT 1988 (3) SC 621; 1988(2) SCALE 670; [1988] Supp2 S.C.R. 913; 1989 (1) UJ 174

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Family & Personal; Women & Children

Summary: Family and Personal - Hindu Marriage Act, 1955, s.13(1)(iii) - Divorce - Suit for a decree of dissolution of his marriage - Whether mere branding of spouse as schizophrenic is sufficient for divorce on the ground of mental disorder - Held, mere branding will not suffice - Degree of mental disorder must be proved - Appeal dismissed.
