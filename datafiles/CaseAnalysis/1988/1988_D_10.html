Kashmeri Devi v Delhi Administration And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

25 April 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

K.N. Singh, M.H. Kania

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 22; (1988) Supp SCC 482; (1988) SCC (Cr) 864; AIR 1988 SC 1323; 1988 (2) AWC 1430; 1988 CRLJ 1800; 1988 CrLR(SC) 294; 1988 (36) DLT 247; JT 1988 (2) SC 293; 1988(1) SCALE 977; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 700

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, ss. 156 and 173(8) - Investigation - Transfer of - Trial Court - Power of - Act of Police - Partisan manner - Conversion of case - To favour real culprits - Held, transfer of investigation to Central Bureau of Investigation - Direction to Trial Court.
