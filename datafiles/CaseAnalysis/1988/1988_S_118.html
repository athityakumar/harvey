Haryana State Adhyapak Sangh And Ors. Etc. v State Of Haryana And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

28 July 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 535; (1988) 4 SCC 571; (1989) SCC (L&S) 21; AIR 1988 SC 1663; 1988 (57) FLR 243; JT 1988 (3) SC 172; 1989 LabIC 1314; 1995 (3) LLJ 710; 1988 (2) LLN 952; 1988(2) SCALE 101; 1989 (1) SLJ 123; 1988 (3) SLR 584; [1988] Supp1 S.C.R. 682; 1988 (2) UJ 401

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Parity

Summary: Constitution - Labour and Industrial Law - Constitution, arts.14, 16, 39, 41, 45, 136 and 226 - Education - Teachers of aided private schools - Pay scales and other emoluments of - Parity - With teachers of Govt. schools - Held, State Govt. directed to reimburse payment of Additional dearness allowance in 35 instalments - Appeals disposed of.
