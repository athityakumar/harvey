S. S. Rathore v State of Madhya Pradesh Through Collector, Betul

Supreme Court of India

02 February 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, N.D. Ojha

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 356; (1988) Supp SCC 522; (1988) SCC (L&S) 930; 1989 (3) CCC 86; 1989 (59) FLR 631; JT 1988 (1) SC 303; 1990 LabIC 398; [1989] 1 S.C.R. 43; 1990 (1) SLJ 98

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Service - Limitation Act, 1963, arts. 58 and 113 - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 309 and 311 - Administrative Tribunals Act, 1985, ss. 14 and 21 - (A) Applicability of art. 113 of Limitation Act - (B) Service disputes - Cause of action - Date of first accrual - Doctrine of merger - (C) Departmental enquiry - Appeal or Revision - Undue delay in redressal of grievances - Held, is general article and applies to suits to which no other article in Sch. applies - Applies only if remedy is statutory i.e., to decisions of departmental tribunals - Delay deprecated, service appeal should be disposed with in 6 months - Order accordingly.
