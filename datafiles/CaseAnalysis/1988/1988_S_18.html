N.M. Goel & Co. v Sales Tax Officer, Rajnandgaon & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

28 October 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, K. Jagannatha Shetty

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 263; (1989) SCC (Tax) 74; (1989) 1 SCC 335; AIR 1989 SC 285; 1988 (38) E.L.T. 733; 1988(2) SCALE 1313; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 657; 1989 (72) STC 368; [1988] Supp3 S.C.R. 657; 1989 (1) UJ 27

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Keywords: Packing Materials, Madhya Pradesh Sthaniya Kshetra Me Mal Ke Pravesh Par Kar Adhiniyam, 1976, Madhya Pradehs General Salestax Act, 1958

Summary: Sales Tax - Indirect Tax - Madhya Pradesh General Sales Tax Act, 1959, ss. 2(4) - Madhya Pradesh Sthaniya Kshetra Me Mal Ke Pravesh Par Kar Adhiniyam, 1976, ss. 3 and 13 - Sale - Contract for service of execution of work - When involves taxable sale of goods? - Construction work for public work Department - Requisite material supplied by PWD - Price of materials deducted from bill of assessee, contractor - Liable to pay entry tax - Held, contract involves sale of material to assessee - He is liable to pay entry tax - Appeal dismissed.
