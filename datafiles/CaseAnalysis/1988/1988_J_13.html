Mohan Lal v Jai Bhagwan

Supreme Court of India

23 March 1988

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1988 Indlaw SC 587; (1988) 2 SCC 474; AIR 1988 SC 1034; JT 1988 (2) SC 61; 1988 PLR 670; 1988 (1) RCJ 749; 1988 (1) RCR(Rent) 444; 1988 (1) RentLR 600; 1988(1) SCALE 675; [1988] 3 S.C.R. 345; 1988 (1) UJ 663

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Rent Control - Haryana Urban (Control of Rent and Eviction) Act, 1973, s.13 (2)(ii)(b) - Tenancy - Change of user for commercial purpose - Determination of entitlement to evict as tenant starting new business in absence of mischief or detriment to the premises - Held, tenant is not liable to be evicted u/s.13(2)(ii)(b) - Appeal allowed.
