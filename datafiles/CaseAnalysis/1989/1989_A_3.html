A.N. Parasuraman Etc. v State Of Tamil Nadu

Supreme Court of India

05 October 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

L.M. Sharma, T.K. Thommen

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 155; (1989) 4 SCC 683; AIR 1990 SC 40; JT 1989 (4) SC 69; 1989(2) SCALE 759; [1989] 1 S.C.R. 371; 1990 (1) SLR 834; [1989] supp1 S.C.R. 371

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Socio-Economic

Keywords: Essential commodities

Summary: Education - Tamil Nadu Private Educational Institutions (Regulation) Act, 1966, ss. 2(2)(c), 6, 7, 12 and 15 - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14 and 245 - Regulation of Pvt. educational institutions - Constitutionality - Held, Act confers unguided power on authorities, is ultravires and, therefore, whole Act held invalid - Appeal allowed.
