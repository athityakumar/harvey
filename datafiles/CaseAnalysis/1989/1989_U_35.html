Federation Of Hotel And Restaurant Associationof India, Etc. v Union Of India And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

02 May 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

M.N. Venkatachaliah, S. Mukharji, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 732; (1989) 3 SCC 634; AIR 1990 SC 1637; 1990 (3) CompLJ 238; 1989 (77) CTR 141; [1989] 178 ITR 97; JT 1989 (Supp) SC 168; [1989] 2 S.C.R. 918; 1989 (74) STC 102; [1989] 46 TAXMAN 47; 1990 (2) TLR 623

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Sales Tax

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Constitution - Expenditure Tax Act, 1987, s. 5(d) - Constitution of India, 1950 , arts. 14, 19(1)(g) and 246, sch. 7, List I, Entries 97, 19(1)(g) and 245 - Constitutionality of Expenditure Tax Act - Act is neither ultra vires nor violative of arts. 14 and 19(1)(g) - Not vague or arbitrary - Held, valid - Petition dismissed.
