Collector Of Central Excise, Hyderabad v Jayant Oil Mills Private Limited and another

Supreme Court of India

31 March 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, S. Ratnavel Pandian

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 556; (1989) 3 SCC 343; (1989) SCC (Tax) 423; AIR 1989 SC 1316; 1989 CRLR 518; 1989 (22) ECC 279; 1989 (22) ECR 161; 1989 (40) E.L.T. 287; JT 1989 (2) SC 8; 1989(1) SCALE 764; [1989] 2 S.C.R. 291

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Keywords: Tariff Item, Soap

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, sch. 1, Tariff Item 12, Tariff Item 68 - Hydrogenated rice bran oil - Whether lies under item 68 of item 12 of sch. 1 to Central Excises and Salt Act ? - Held, covered by item 12 and not under item 68 - Appeal dismissed.
