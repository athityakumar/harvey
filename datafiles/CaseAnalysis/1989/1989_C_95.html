Saheli, A Women's Resources Centre v Commissioner of Police Delhi Police Headquarters

Supreme Court of India

14 December 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

B.C. Ray, S. Ratnavel Pandian

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 343; (1990) SCC (Cr) 145; (1990) 1 SCC 422; 1990 (1) ACJ 345; AIR 1990 SC 513; 1990 CRLR 152; 1990 CrLR(SC) 152; 1990 (40) DLT 147; JT 1989 (4) SC 553; 1989(2) SCALE 1315; [1989] Supp2 S.C.R. 488; 1990 (1) UJ 434

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal; Tort

Summary: Woment & Children - Constitution - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 21, 32 and 300 - Police atrocities - Death of nine year old child because of beating and assault by police officer - Liability of State - Compensation - Held, State Govt. directed to pay compensation to mother of victim - Order accordingly.
