Council of Scientific and Industrial Research and Another v K. G. S. Bhatt and Another

Supreme Court of India

29 August 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, A.M. Ahmadi

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 211; (1989) 4 SCC 635; (1990) SCC (L&S) 45; AIR 1989 SC 1972; 1989 (11) ATC 880; 1989 (59) FLR 577; JT 1989 (3) SC 513; 1989 LabIC 2010; 1990 (1) LLJ 246; 1989(2) SCALE 395; 1989 (4) SLR 252; 1989 (2) UJ 566; 1990 (1) UPLBEC 5

Case Digest

Subject: Administrative; Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Research

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, sch. 2, item 6 - Administrative Tribunals Act, 1985, s. 19 - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 136 - Council of Scientific and Industrial Research Institute Bye-laws, Bye-law 71(b)(ii) - Promotion - Public or private organization - No promotion of Civil Engineer in 'administrative' category for 20 years - Administrative Tribunal granting relief wrongly holding that Bye-law 71(b)(ii) applied to case - Appeal by special leave to Supreme Court - Held, Supreme Court could not interfere in appeal by special leave as it would defeat justice - Appeal dismissed.
