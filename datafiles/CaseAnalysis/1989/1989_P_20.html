K. Saraswathy Alias K. Kalpana (Dead) By Lrs. v P.S.S. Somasundaram Chettiar

Supreme Court of India

01 May 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, Ranganath Misra, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 374; (1989) 4 SCC 527; AIR 1989 SC 1553; 1989 (2) BankCLR 226; 1990 (1) BC 243; 1989 (2) CCC 349; [1990] 67 Comp Cas 67; JT 1989 (2) SC 480; 1990 (1) MLJ(SC) 55; 1990 (1) RCR(Rent) 349; 1989(1) SCALE 1179; [1989] 2 S.C.R. 819; 1989 (2) UJ 185

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Civil Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - CPC, 1908, s. 47, O. 21, r. 1 - Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881 - Amount to be deposited in the Court - Cheque of the specified amount tendered on the last prescribed date - No money on the date of delivery of the cheque to support payment - The cheque subsequently realised in due course - Whether payment by cheque is valid depositon in Court - Payment through cheque held to be valid - Appeal allowed.
