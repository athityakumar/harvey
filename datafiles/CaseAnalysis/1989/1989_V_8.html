Anandi Mukta Sadguru Shree Mukta Jeevandasswami Suvarna Jaya v V.R. Rudani And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

21 April 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 589; (1989) 2 SCC 691; AIR 1989 SC 1607; 1989 (2) GLR 1357; JT 1989 (Supp) SC 128; 1989 LabIC 1550; 1989 (2) LLJ 324; 1989 (2) LLN 281; 1989(1) SCALE 1116; [1989] 2 S.C.R. 697; 1989 (2) UJ 130

Case Digest

Subject: Education

Keywords: Gujarat University Act, 1949, University Grants Commission

Summary: Education - Gujarat University Act, 1973, ss. 31 and 33 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - University - Affiliated Colleges - Closure of College - Payment of salary and other benefits - Closure compensation under Ordinance No. 120 E - Management is primarily liable - (A) Refusal to make full payment unless State compensates - (B) College having temporary affiliation before enactment of Ordinance, disown its liability - (C) Scope of writ of Mandamus whether extend to public trust - Held, refusal not tenable - Cannot disown - Issue of writ is not confined to statutory authorities and instrumentalities of State only but to any other person or authority performing public duty, not necessarily statutory one, so when University revising pay scale, public trust running college can be directed to pay it - Appeal dismissed.
