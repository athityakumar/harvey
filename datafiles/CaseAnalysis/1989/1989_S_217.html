Mahabir Kishore and Others v State of Madhya Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

31 July 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

K.N. Saikia, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 759; (1989) 4 SCC 1; AIR 1990 SC 313; [1990] 69 Comp Cas 16; 1989 (24) ECC 199; 1990 (26) ECR 494; 1989 (43) E.L.T. 205; [1990] 184 ITR 548; JT 1989 (3) SC 327; 1989(2) SCALE 276; [1989] 3 S.C.R. 596; 1990 (78) STC 404; 1989 (2) UJ 576

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure; Sales Tax; Contract & Commercial

Keywords: Collected

Summary: Contract & Commercial - Civil Procedure - Contract Act, 1872, s. 72 - Limitation Act, 1963, s. 17(1)(c) and art. 113 - Unjust enrichment - Money paid under mistake of law - Suit for refund filed - Determination of starting point of limitation period - Held, in a suit for refund of money paid by mistake of law, s. 72 is applicable, period of limitation is three years as prescribed by art. 113 - Provisions of s. 17(1)(c) would be applicable - Appeal allowed.
