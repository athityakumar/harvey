Custodian Of Branches Of Banco Nationalultramarino. v Nalini Bai Naique

Supreme Court of India

28 April 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

K.N. Singh, K.N. Saikia

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 607; (1989) Supp2 SCC 275; AIR 1989 SC 1589; JT 1989 (Supp) SC 159; 1989 (2) MLJ(SC) 25; 1989(1) SCALE 1410; [1989] 2 S.C.R. 810; 1989 (2) UJ 171

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 2(ii), O. 22, rr. 4 and 9 - 'LR' - Includes persons who are not legal heirs but represent estate of deceased - Widow acquiring 'Meeira' rights under Portuguese Law represents estate of her deceased husband - Widow brought on record within time - Scope of abatement of suit - Held, abatement liable to be set aside - Appeal allowed.
