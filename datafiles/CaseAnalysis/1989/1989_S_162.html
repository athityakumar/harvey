Sharad Kumar Tyagi v State Of Uttar Pradesh And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

18 January 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Natarajan, M.M. Dutt

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 404; (1989) SCC (Cr) 294; (1989) 1 SCC 736; AIR 1989 SC 764; 1990 (1) Crimes 473; 1989 CRLJ 830; 1989 CrLR(SC) 186; JT 1989 (2) SC 21; 1989(1) SCALE 138; [1989] 1 S.C.R. 257; 1989 (1) UJ 432

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: National Security Act 1980, s. 3(2) - Detention for maintenance of public order - Detenu threatening to shoot shopkeepers of locality if they failed to give him money - Terror stricken shopkeepers closing their shops - Held, activities of detenu affecting public order and therefore, detention was not illegal.
