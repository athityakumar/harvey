Jayantbhai Manubhai Patel And Others v Arun Subodhbhai Mehta And Others

Supreme Court of India

28 March 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

M.H. Kania, L.M. Sharma

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 531; (1989) 2 SCC 484; AIR 1989 SC 1289; 1989 (3) Bom.C.R. 126; 1990 (26) ECR 1; 1989 (1) GLH 591; 1989 (2) GLR 1085; JT 1989 (3) SC 156; 1989(1) SCALE 701; [1989] 2 S.C.R. 110; 1989 (2) UJ 315

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Bombay Provincial Municipal Corporation Act, 1949, Gujarat Municipalities Act, 1963, Bombay General Act, 1904

Summary: Municipalities & Local Govts. - Bombay Provincial Municipal Corporation Act, 1949, ss. 453, chap II, cl. 1(c) - Bombay General clause Act, 1904, s. 21 - Mayor - Power to convene meeting - Scope of and applicability of s. 21 of 1904 Act - Held, implies power to postpone or cancel it - Power has to be exercised bona fide and for proper purpose - Provision of s. 21 applies - Appeal allowed.
