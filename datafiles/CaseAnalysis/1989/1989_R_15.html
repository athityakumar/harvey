Gurdial Batra v Raj Kumar Jain

Supreme Court of India

18 July 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 650; (1989) 3 SCC 441; AIR 1989 SC 1841; JT 1989 (3) SC 96; 1989 (2) PLR 313; 1989 (2) RCR(Rent) 233; 1989(2) SCALE 17; [1989] 3 S.C.R. 423; 1989 (2) UJ 460

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: EP Urban Rent Restriction Act 1949

Summary: Rent Control - East Punjab Urban Rent Restriction Act, 1949, s. 13(2)(ii)(b) Tenancy - Change of user - Premises let out for running of cycle/ rickshaw repair shop - Sale of televisions also afterward carried on temporarily in premises along with repair business - Held, it is not change of user - Small change of user not actionable when interest of landlord not prejudiced - Appeal allowed.
