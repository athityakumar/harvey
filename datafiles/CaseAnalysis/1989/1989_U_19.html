D.P. Sharma And Ors. v Union Of India And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

21 February 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 480; (1989) Supp1 SCC 244; (1989) SCC (L&S) 353; AIR 1989 SC 1071; 1989 (10) ATC 372; 1989 (58) FLR 546; JT 1989 (1) SC 359; 1989 LabIC 1294; 1989 (2) LLJ 155; 1989 (1) LLN 550; 1989(1) SCALE 459; [1989] 1 S.C.R. 791; 1989 (2) SLJ 56; 1989 (1) SLR 780; 1989 (1) UJ 622

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Armed Forces Headquarters Civil service Rules 1968

Summary: Service - Constitution - Armed Forces Headquarters Clerical Service Rules, 1968, r. 1 - Constitution of India, art. 309 - Determination of inter se seniority - Length of service taken as basis prior to framing of 1968 rules - Basis changed to date of confirmation by rules - Held, seniority of those appointed prior to framing of rules cannot be disturbed - Appeal allowed.
