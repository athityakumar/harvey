Ashok Kumar Chatterjee v State of Madhya Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

02 May 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ratnavel Pandian, B.C. Ray

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 443; (1989) Supp1 SCC 560; (1989) SCC (Cr) 566; AIR 1989 SC 1890; 1989 (2) Crimes 423; 1989 CRLJ 2124; 1989 CRLR 462; JT 1989 (3) SC 451; 1989(1) SCALE 1313

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Tort

Summary: Criminal - Constitution - Appeal by special leave against conviction in murder matter - Whether findings of High Court suffer any illegality? - Held, findings of HC did not suffer from any illegality, or manifest and hence, not liable to be interfered with - Appeal dismissed.
