Carona Shoe Co. Limited And Anr. v K.C. Bhaskaran Nair

Supreme Court of India

09 March 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 689; (1989) 2 SCC 395; AIR 1989 SC 1110; 1989 (1) CCC 660; JT 1989 (1) SC 525; 1989 (2) KarLJ 89; 1989 (2) KLT 306; 1989 (1) RCJ 363; 1989 (1) RCR(Rent) 514; 1989 (1) RentLR 487; 1989(1) SCALE 588; [1989] 1 S.C.R. 974; 1989 (2) UJ 57

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property

Summary: Rent Control - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, ss. 76(a) and 111(c) - Kerala Buildings (Lease and Rent Control) Act, 1965, s. 11 - Eviction - Redemption of mortgage - Tenant in continued possession - Mortgagee not entitled to induct such tenants under mortgage deed - No approval of mortgagor to it - No landlord and tenant relationship between tenant and mortgagor - Induction, not prudent - Protection from being evicted, entitlement - Held, tenant not entitled to protection of Rent Act - Can be evicted in suit for recovery of possession - Appeal dismissed.
