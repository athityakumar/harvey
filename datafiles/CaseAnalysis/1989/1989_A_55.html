Gujarat Electricity Board And Anr. v Atmaram Sungomal Poshani

Supreme Court of India

31 March 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

K.N. Singh, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 63; (1989) SCC (L&S) 393; (1989) 2 SCC 602; AIR 1989 SC 1433; 1989 (10) ATC 396; 1989 (59) FLR 474; JT 1989 (3) SC 20; 1989 LabIC 1374; 1989 (2) LLJ 470; 1989 (2) LLN 299; 1989(1) SCALE 907; [1989] 2 S.C.R. 357; 1989 (3) SLJ 68; 1989 (3) SLR 684B; 1989 (2) UJ 222

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Civil Procedure - Electricity & Energy - Practice & procedure - Civil Procedure Code, 1908, s. 25 - Electricity (Supply) Act, 1948, s. 79 - Gujarat State Electricity Board Service Regulations, regn. 113 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 311 - General clauses Act, 1897, s. 27 - (A) Transfer of case from one bench to another - When can be claimed? - Oral hearing of case concluded - Bench expressing opinion in open Court for allowing appeal - Application for transfer of case making mischievous and frivolous objections - Maintainability - (B) Transfer - Representation against - Cannot avoid transfer on this ground - Sufficient warning for absence without leave - Disobedience of order by employee, proofs available - (C) Service of letter under registered cover - Presumption, as to - Rebuttal of - Endorsement that addressee refused to accept letter under registered cover - Held, (A) rejected - (B) Order of discharge from service of employee - (C) No material to show that endorsement was wrong so presumption not rebutted - Appeal allowed.
