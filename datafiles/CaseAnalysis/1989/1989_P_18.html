Majati Subbarao v P.K.K. Krishna Rao (Deceased) By Lrs.

Supreme Court of India

19 September 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

M.H. Kania, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 231; (1989) 4 SCC 732; AIR 1989 SC 2187; JT 1989 (3) SC 694; 1990 (1) RCJ 97; 1989 (2) RCR(Rent) 498; 1989 (2) RentLR 422; 1989(2) SCALE 602; [1989] 1 S.C.R. 153; [1989] supp1 S.C.R. 153

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Denial Of Title

Summary: Rent Control Land & Property - Andhra Pradesh Buildings (Lease, Rent and Eviction) Control Act, 1960, s. 10(2)(vi) - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 111(g) - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 136 - Eviction - Denial of landlord's title - Plea taken by tenant in proceedings for eviction itself - (A) Ground can be taken in proceeding in which title is denied - Whether bonafide? - (B) Objection as to omission to amend plaint not taken by tenant - Raised for first time before Supreme Court in appeal by special leave - Held, (A) Denial not bona fide and tenant can be evicted on that ground but separate proceeding not necessary - (B) Not maintainable - Appeal dismissed.
