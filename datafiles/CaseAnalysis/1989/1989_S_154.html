A.S. Mittal & Ors. v State Of U.P. & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

12 May 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

M.N. Venkatachaliah, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 716; (1989) 3 SCC 223; (1989) SCC (Cr) 539; AIR 1989 SC 1570; 1989 (2) CCC 426; 1989 CRLR 528; 1989 CrLR(SC) 528; JT 1989 (2) SC 419; 1989(1) SCALE 1535; [1989] 3 S.C.R. 241; 1989 (2) UJ 276

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Criminal; Tort

Keywords: Degree Of Care

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950 - Indian Penal Code, 1860 - Public health - Eye camps - Medical misadventure - Irreversible damage to Eyes of patients - Operation done in eye camp Public Interest Litigation - Held, Supreme Court on humanitarian considerations directed State Govt. to pay Rupees 12,500/- To each of victims in addition to interim relief already paid - Petitioner espousing cause of unfortunate persons - Costs granted - Public interest litigation - Cots to petitioner.
