 Elel Hotels And Investments Limitedand Anr. Etc. Etc. v Union Of India

Supreme Court of India

02 May 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

M.N. Venkatachaliah, R.S. Pathak, S. Natarajan, S. Ranganathan, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 445; (1989) 3 SCC 698; AIR 1990 SC 1664; 1990 (3) CompLJ 267; 1989 (77) CTR 168; [1989] 178 ITR 140; JT 1989 (Supp) SC 195; 1989(1) SCALE 1194; [1989] 2 S.C.R. 880; 1989 (74) STC 146; 1990 TAXLR 651; [1989] 44 TAXMAN 304; 1990 (2) TLR 651; 1989 (2) UJ 391

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Expenditure-Tax Act, 1987, Expenditure Tax Act, 1987, Hotel Receipts Tax Act, 1980

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Hotel Receipts Tax Act,1980, Pre. ss. 3, 5 and 6 - Constitution, arts. 14, 19(1)(g) and 246, sch. 7 list I entries 82 and 62 - 'Taxable receipts' - Comes within 'income' in wider constitutional meaning and sense of term - As also understood in entry 82 list I of Constitution - Liberal construction of statute - Whether the tax on gross receipts of certain category of hotels is valid - Held, tax on gross receipts of certain category of hotels - Held, not violative of arts. 14 and 19(1)(g) of Constitution - Petition dismissed.
