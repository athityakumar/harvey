Raj Steel And Ors. Etc. Etc. v State Of A.P. And Anr. Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

16 May 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, M.H. Kania

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 626; (1989) 3 SCC 262; (1989) SCC (Tax) 442; AIR 1989 SC 1696; JT 1989 (Supp) SC 226; 1989(1) SCALE 1573; [1989] 3 S.C.R. 305; 1989 (74) STC 379; 1989 (2) UJ 309

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Sales Tax

Keywords: Packing Material, Practice, Packing Materials

Summary: Sales Tax - Indirect Tax - Andhra Pradesh General Sales Tax Act, 1957, s. 5, ch. 1, items. 123, 157 and 6 C - (A) Packing material - Eligibility to tax - Transaction of sale of packing material whether independent transaction - (B) Provision for taxation of packing material by deeming sale thereof, at rate applicable to sale of goods themselves - Constitutionality - Held, (A) Must be determined by ascertaining facts of each case and determining actual ingredients of contract and intention of parties in each case - (B) Not discriminatory - Appeal allowed.
