Ashok Chand Singhvi v University Of Jodhpur And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

18 January 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Dutt, S. Natarajan

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 405; (1989) 1 SCC 399; AIR 1989 SC 823; 1989 (15) ALR 356; JT 1989 (1) SC 177; 1989(1) SCALE 166; [1989] 1 S.C.R. 230; 1989 (1) UJ 322

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Education - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 115 - Admission - To Engineering Degree Course - Application by Diploma holder working as instructor in Engineering College - After last date - In general seats - Objection by officer in charge, Admissions - That less than 60% marks in Diploma - Application accepted after considering - Dean 's order, putting admission in abeyance - Sustainability - Held, not sustainable - Appeal allowed.
