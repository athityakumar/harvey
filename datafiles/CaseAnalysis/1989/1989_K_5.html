Collector Of Customs, Bombay v K. Mohan And Co. Exports

Supreme Court of India

25 September 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ranganathan, M.H. Kania

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 241; (1989) Supp2 SCC 337; AIR 1989 SC 2250; 1989 (24) ECC 416; 1989 (25) ECR 1; 1989 (43) E.L.T. 811; JT 1989 (3) SC 740B; 1989(2) SCALE 712; [1989] 1 S.C.R. 231; [1989] supp1 S.C.R. 231; 1990 (1) UJ 110

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Customs Act, 1962, s. 25(1) - Customs Tariff Act, 1975, s. 3 - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, sch. 1 item 15 A(2) - Notification - Exempting articles made of plastics, all sorts - But excluding those articles given in table annexed to Notification - Whether 'metalised polyester films' is exempted - Held, 'metalised polyester films imported in shape of film rolls several meters long are 'films' and do not fall within any category mentioned in said table and therefore, they are exempt from duty - Appeal dismissed.
