R. Venugopala Naidu And Ors. v Venkatarayulu Naidu Charities And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

26 October 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, S. Natarajan

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 295; (1989) Supp2 SCC 356; AIR 1990 SC 444; 1989 (2) AWC 1479; 1989 (3) CCC 328; JT 1989 (4) SC 262; 1989(2) SCALE 902; [1989] 1 S.C.R. 760; 1990 (1) ShimLC 246; [1989] supp1 S.C.R. 760

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, ss. 11, 92, O. 1, r. 8 - Representative suit - Sale of Hindu religious trust properties with permission of Court - (A) Nature of representative suit - (B) Hindu Religious trust - Objects both of secular and religious nature - Non-Hindu whether can claim to be interested? - (C) Such sale by private negotiations - Permissibility - Held, (A) Binds all persons interested in trust as they have locus standi to raise objection, however, their names need not be in title of suit as parties - (B) Question not decided - (C) Should not be permitted by Court unless justified by special reasons, hence direction given to sell property by public auction after giving wide publicity - Appeal allowed.
