Star Paper Mills Ltd v Collector Of Central Excise, Meerut

Supreme Court of India

22 August 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

N.D. Ojha, S. Ranganathan, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 765; (1989) 4 SCC 724; AIR 1989 SC 2066; 1989 (2) CLR 504; 1989 CRLR 742; 1989 CrLR(SC) 742; 1989 (24) ECC 369; 1989 (24) ECR 625; 1989 (43) E.L.T. 178; [1990] 185 ITR 575; JT 1989 (3) SC 460; 1989(2) SCALE 337; [1989] 3 S.C.R. 892; 1990 (76) STC 312

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Indirect Tax; Sales Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, s. 2(f), sch. 1, item 68 - Notification No. 201/ 79, D/- 04-06-1979 (as amended by Notification No. 105/ 82, D/- 28-02-1982 - Manufacture of paper - Paper core would be constituent part of paper - Whether it falls within term 'Component parts' used in Notification in so far as manufacture of paper in rolls is concerned? - Held, falls under component terms - Petition dismissed.
