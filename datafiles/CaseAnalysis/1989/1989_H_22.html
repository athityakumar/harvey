Hindustan Lever Limited and Another v State of Maharashtra and Another

Supreme Court of India

19 October 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 771; [1991] 188 ITR 402

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Sales Tax - Indirect Taxes - Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973, ss. 9(1), 24(3) and 50 - Bombay Sales Tax Act, 1959, s. 13 AA - Penalty proceedings initiated u/s. 50 of 1973 Haryana Act - Scope, effect and validity of s. 13 AA of 1959 Bombay Act introduced by Maharashtra Act, 1982 -
