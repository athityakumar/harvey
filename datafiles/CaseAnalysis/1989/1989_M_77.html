Lakhanpal National Ltd v Monopolies and Restrictive Trade Practices

Supreme Court of India

02 May 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

L.M. Sharma, S. Ratnavel Pandian

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 776; (1989) 3 SCC 251; AIR 1989 SC 1692; [1989] 66 Comp Cas 519; 1989 (2) CompLJ 159; JT 1989 (2) SC 543; 1989(1) SCALE 1406; [1989] 2 S.C.R. 979; 1989 (2) UJ 358

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection; Corporate; MRTP

Keywords: Trade Practice

Summary: MRTP - MRTP Act, 1969, ss.55 , 36A(i) , 36A(v) - Unfair trade practice - The company producing electronic batteries in collaboration with a foreign company - Advertisement - Giving erroneous description of manufacturing company - Containing the names of its popular products instead of its name - Whether it would attract the provisions of s. 36A - Held, the erroneous description does not attract s. 36 - Appeal allowed.
