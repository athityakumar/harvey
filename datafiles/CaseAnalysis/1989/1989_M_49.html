Meenakshi Malik v University Of Delhi And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

01 May 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, M.M. Dutt, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 371; (1989) 3 SCC 112; AIR 1989 SC 1568; JT 1989 (Supp) SC 162; 1989(2) SCALE 1153; [1989] 2 S.C.R. 858; 1990 (5) SLR 159; 1989 (2) UJ 208

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Education - Constitution of India, 1953 ,art. 14 - Admission to Medical College in Delhi - Condition of schooling for last two years in school in Delhi - Held, unreasonable in case of candidate who had to leave for foreign country on transfer of parent - Appeal allowed.
