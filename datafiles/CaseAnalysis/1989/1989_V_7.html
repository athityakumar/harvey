Rama Dhondu Borade v V.K. Saraf, Commissioner Of Police And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

05 May 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ratnavel Pandian, B.C. Ray

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 618; (1989) 3 SCC 173; (1989) SCC (Cr) 520; AIR 1989 SC 1861; 1989 (2) Crimes 653; 1989 CRLJ 2119; 1989 CrLR(SC) 487; 1989 (24) ECR 7; JT 1989 (2) SC 579; 1989(1) SCALE 1581; [1989] 3 S.C.R. 191

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - National Security Act, 1980, s. 8 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 22(5) - Preventive detention - Representation by detenu - Authority must dispose it of as expeditiously as possible - Gap of 28 days between receipt and disposal - Delay attributed to delay of 14 days in receipt of further information and occurrence of certain holidays thereafter up to disposal - Held, delay unreasonable - Explanation unsatisfactory - Detention invalid - Appeal and petition allowed.
