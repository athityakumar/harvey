Union Of India And Ors v Filip Tiago De Gama Of Vedem Vasco De Gama

Supreme Court of India

30 November 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, A.M. Ahmadi

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 335; (1990) 1 SCC 277; AIR 1990 SC 981; 1990 (1) CCC 72; 1990 (41) DLT 72; 1990 (26) ECR 473; JT 1989 (4) SC 529; 1990 MahLJ 724; 1989(2) SCALE 1226; [1989] Supp2 S.C.R. 336; 1990 (1) UJ 292; 1990 (1) UPLBEC 192

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Customs; Indirect Tax; Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 23(1-A) and 23(2) - Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, 1984, ss. 30(1)(a)(b) and 30(2) - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 - (A) Higher solatium - Benefit of - Available also in cases where award is made after 24-09-1984 - Alternative construction of s. 30 of Act - Constitutionality - (B) Addl. compensation u/s. 23(1-A) - Availability - Notification issued in 1967 - Award passed in 1969 by collector - No proceeding pending before him on date prescribed u/s. 30(1)(a) of Amending Act - And s. 30(1)(b) also not applicable - Held, (A) Any other construction would be vulnerable to attack u/art. 14 - (B) Not entitled - Appeal allowed.
