Peoples' Union For Democratic Rights Through Its Secretary and Another v Police Commissioner, Delhi Police Headquarters and Another

Supreme Court of India

13 January 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

G.L. Oza, K.N. Saikia

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 386; (1989) 4 SCC 730; (1990) SCC (Cr) 75; 1990 (1) ACJ 192; 1990 CRLR 240; 1990 CrLR(SC) 240; JT 1989 (Supp) SC 1; 1989(1) SCALE 599

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal

Summary: Constitution - Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860 - Compensation - Police atrocity on labourers - Direction that amount paid as compensation or part thereof may be recovered from accused police personal out of their salaries after giving them opportunity to show cause - Writ petitions disposed of.
