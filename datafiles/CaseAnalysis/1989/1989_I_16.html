Rajnikant Jivanlal Patel And Another v Intelligence Officer, Narcotic Control Bureau, New Delhi

Supreme Court of India

26 June 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 46; (1989) 3 SCC 532; (1989) SCC (Cr) 612; AIR 1990 SC 71; 1989 (2) AWC 1031; 1989 BBCJ 109; 1990 CRLJ 62; 1989 CrLR(SC) 525; 1997 (68) ECR 572; 1992 (61) E.L.T. 330; JT 1989 (3) SC 67; 1989(1) SCALE 1586; [1989] 3 S.C.R. 377; 1989 (2) UJ 273

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Criminal Procedure Code, 1974, ss. 167(2), Proviso, (a),437(5) and 439(2) - Cancellation of bail was in question - Grant of bail under proviso (a) to s. 167(2) for non-filing of charge sheet within definite period - Investigation convincing commission of serious offence - Held, bail could be cancelled in such situations.
