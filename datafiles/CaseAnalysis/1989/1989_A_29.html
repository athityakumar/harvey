Shiv Chander Kapoor v Amar Bose

Supreme Court of India

28 November 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

J.S. Verma, M.N. Venkatachaliah, N. D. Ojha

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 332; (1990) 1 SCC 234; AIR 1990 SC 325; 1990 (40) DLT 7; JT 1989 (4) SC 471; 1990 (1) RCJ 108; 1990 (1) RCR(Rent) 143; 1990 (1) RentLR 202; 1989(2) SCALE 1168; [1989] Supp2 S.C.R. 299

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure; Rent Control

Keywords: Restoration Of Possession, Rent Control Tribunal, Code of Civil Procedure 1908

Summary: Rent Control - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958, ss. 21- Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 41, r. 27 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 136 - Land and property - Limited tenancy - Recovery of possession on expiry thereof - Objection, invalidity of earlier permission to creation of tenancy - (A) Scope of enquiry - (B) Notice to tenant, whether necessary - (C) Appeal - Additional evidence only at conclusion - Admission of - Held, restricted to determination of existence of jurisdictional facts u/s. 21 of Act - Not necessary - Application rejected - Appeal allowed.
