Chhagan Bagwan Kahar v N.L. Kalna And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

16 March 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ratnavel Pandian, B.C. Ray

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 521; (1989) 2 SCC 318; (1989) SCC (Cr) 367; AIR 1989 SC 1234; 1989 (1) Crimes 780; 1989 CRLJ 1145; 1989 CRLR 439; 1989 CrLR(SC) 439; JT 1989 (1) SC 572; 1989(1) SCALE 653; [1989] 2 S.C.R. 52

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Criminal - Detention - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 32 - Gujarat Prevention of Anti Social Activities Act, 1985, ss. 3 and 15 - Petitioner detained for preventing him from acting prejudical to maintainence of public order - Whether detention of petitioner proper and legal? - Held, detention order illegal and invalid as grounds of previous detention order taken into consideration while passing impugned detention order - Order accordingly.
