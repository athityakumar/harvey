State Of Maharashtra v Captain Buddhikota Subha Rao

Supreme Court of India

29 September 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, K.N. Saikia

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 803; (1989) Supp2 SCC 605; (1990) SCC (Cr) 126; AIR 1989 SC 2292; 1989 (3) Crimes 495; 1989 CRLJ 2317; 1990 CrLR(SC) 50; JT 1989 (4) SC 1; 1989(2) SCALE 707; [1989] 1 S.C.R. 315; [1989] supp1 S.C.R. 315; 1990 (1) UJ 192

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal; Electricity & Energy; Environment, Wildlife & Animal

Summary: Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, ss. 436 and 437 - Successive applications for bail were in consideration - Rejected by High Court in view of gravity of offence alleged to have been committed - No substantial changes, thereafter, necessitating release of accused on bail but bail was given to him by HC - Held, order granting bail thereafter by HC was illegal.
