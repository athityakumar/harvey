Bhavnagar Municipality v Union Of India And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

17 November 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, L.M. Sharma

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 318; (1989) Supp2 SCC 758; AIR 1990 SC 717; 1989 (1) CCC 631; JT 1989 (4) SC 516; 1989(2) SCALE 1218; [1989] Supp2 S.C.R. 219; 1990 (1) UJ 299

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Keywords: Authorisation, Indian Independence Act, 1947

Summary: Municipalities & Local Govts. - Practice & Procedure - Limitation Act, 1963, art. 65 - Bhavanagar Darbar Resolution No. 37, D/- 19-01-1948 - Suit for possession on basis of title - Defendant denying title - (A) Whether finding on title of plaintiff has to be given? - (B) Defendant in possession not pleading adverse possession - Effect - Resolution conferred only right or authorisation to sell State land as representing Govt. - No vesting of title in Municipality - Suit by municipal committee for possession of certain land on basis of title - Whether defendant is required to plead or prove adverse possession? - Held, (A) Finding has to be given - (B) Title not having been proved, defendant is not required to prove adverse possession - Appeal dismissed.
