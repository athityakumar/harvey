State of Uttar Pradesh and Others v Radhey Shyam Nigam and Others

Supreme Court of India

11 January 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 710; (1989) 1 SCC 591; AIR 1989 SC 682; 1989 (2) AWC 1078; 1989 BBCJ 44; JT 1989 (1) SC 58; 1989(1) SCALE 50; [1989] 1 S.C.R. 92; 1989 (1) UJ 327

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Media & Entertainment

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 4(1), 6(2) and 17(4) - Introduction of words 'after date of publication of notification u/s. 4 (1)' by 1984 amendment - Effect - On permissibility of Govt. to issue notification u/s. 4 and to make declaration as contemplated u/s. 6 simultaneously - Held, can't be issued simultaneously but on subsequent date - Appeal dismissed.
