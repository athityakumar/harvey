Mrs. Dhanalakshmi v R. Prasanna Kumar And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

15 November 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Fathima Beevi, E.S. Venkataramiah, K.N. Singh

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 301; (1990) Supp SCC 686; AIR 1990 SC 494; 1989 (2) AWC 1447; 1990 CRLJ 320; JT 1989 (4) SC 318; 1990 MahLJ 32; [1989] Supp2 S.C.R. 165

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 (as amended by Act No.25 of 2005 & Act No.2 of 2006)

Summary: Criminal - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, s. 482 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 494 and 496 - Quashing of proceeding by High Court - Under inherent powers - Complaint for offences u/s. 494 and 496 of IPC filed by wife - Magistrate took cognizance of - Specific allegations disclosing ingredients of offence - Legality of quashing - Held, illegal - Appeal allowed.
