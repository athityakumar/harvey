Vishwanath Sood v Union Of India And Anr

Supreme Court of India

24 January 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ranganathan, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 813; (1989) 1 SCC 657; AIR 1989 SC 952; 1989 (1) ARBLR 357; 1990 (3) CCC 361; JT 1989 (1) SC 585; 1989(1) SCALE 154; [1989] 1 S.C.R. 288

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure; Electricity & Energy; Environment, Wildlife & Animal

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration Act, 1940, ss. 2(a) and 30 - (A) Award - Non-speaking award - Conclusions reached by arbitrator after considering material - No prima facie error - Affirmation of award by High Court without interference - Validity - (B) Arbitration clause - Scope - Construction contract - Time made essence of - Stipulation that compensation to be paid by contractor for delay - Decision of Superintendent Engineer on amount of compensation made final under clause - Held, (A) was proper - (B) Matter as to amount of compensation could not be referred to arbitrator - Appeal allowed.
