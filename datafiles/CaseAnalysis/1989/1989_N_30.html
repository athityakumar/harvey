Noorali Babul Thanewala v K.M.M. Shetty And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

20 December 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

Veeraswami Ramaswami, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 35; (1990) 1 SCC 259; AIR 1990 SC 464; 1990 (2) Bom.C.R. 235; 1990 (1) CCC 146; 1990 CRLJ 316; JT 1989 (4) SC 573; 1990 (1) RCR(Rent) 82; 1990 (1) RentLR 456; 1989(2) SCALE 1426; [1989] Supp2 S.C.R. 561

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Contempt of Courts Act, 1971, s. 12 - Offence committed u/s. 12 of 1971 Act - Delivery of vacant possession of premises by tenant - Undertaking as to, given by him to Supreme Court - Breach of undertaking - Held, tenant, guilty of committing contempt of Court and therefore liable to be convicted - Order accordingly.
