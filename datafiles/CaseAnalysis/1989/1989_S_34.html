K.V. George v Secretary To Govt., Water And Powerdepartment, Trivandrum And

Supreme Court of India

05 October 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

B.C. Ray, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 153; (1989) 4 SCC 595; AIR 1990 SC 53; 1989 (15) ALR 807; 1990 (1) ARBLR 55; 1989 (2) AWC 1408; JT 1989 (4) SC 166; 1989(2) SCALE 822; [1989] 1 S.C.R. 398; 1990 (1) ShimLC 255; [1989] supp1 S.C.R. 398; 1990 (1) UJ 280

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure; Constitution

Summary: Arbitration Act, 1940 - Misconduct - Arbitrator making award by hearing only one side and neglecting other before making award - Issues not raised in first claim petition can not be raised in other such petition - Held, arbitrator committed misconduct by going through claim of on side and neglecting other side - The issues not taken in first claim can not be taken in second claim.
