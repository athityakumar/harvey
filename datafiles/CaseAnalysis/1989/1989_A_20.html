Bareilly Development Authority And Anr v Ajai Pal Singh And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

17 February 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ratnavel Pandian, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 475; (1989) 2 SCC 116; AIR 1989 SC 1076; 1989 (1) AWC 425; JT 1989 (1) SC 368; 1989(1) SCALE 439; [1989] 1 S.C.R. 743; 1989 (1) UJ 523

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Constitution - Constitution of India, 1953, arts. 12, 14, 226 and 299 - Construction of dwelling units by appellant - Appellant is state authority - Advertisement offering registration - Brochure issued containing general terms and estimated costs of dwelling houses, subject to change - Later price changed - Challenged by respondent - Held, since contract was non-statutory in nature, relations will be governed by legally valid contract which determines rights and obligations of parties inter-se - Appeal allowed.
