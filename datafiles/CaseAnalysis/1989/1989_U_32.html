Dr. Ms. O.Z. Hussain v Union Of India And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

15 November 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, K. Ramaswamy, P.B. Sawant

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 302; (1990) Supp SCC 688; (1991) SCC (L&S) 649; AIR 1990 SC 311; 1991 (16) ATC 521; 1990 (60) FLR 74; JT 1989 (4) SC 407; 1990 LabIC 322; 1990 (2) LLN 1185; 1989(2) SCALE 1134; 1990 (1) SLJ 188; 1990 (1) SLR 297; [1989] Supp2 S.C.R. 177; 1990 (1) UPLBEC 45

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 309 and 311 - Disparity in pay scales of medical and non medical wing of Directorate of Health Services - Group 'A' Scientists in non-medical wing - Whether entitled to benefit of promotion which are available to similarly placed scientists in other ministries? - Directions issued by Supreme Court - Allowance such as book allowance, risk allowance, conveyance allowance and higher degree allowance admissible to doctors in medical wing in Directorate of Health Services should also be admissible to non medical category 'A' Group Scientists - Non-medical category 'A' group Scientists are entitled to similar benefit of promotion - Supreme Court directed Ministry of Health and Family Welfare to frame rules providing suitable promotional avenues to said Group 'A' Scientists - Petition allowed.
