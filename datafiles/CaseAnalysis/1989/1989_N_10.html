Shridhar Son Of Ram Dular v Nagar Palika, Jaunpur And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

17 November 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

K.N. Singh, N.M. Kasliwal

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 315; (1990) Supp SCC 157; (1990) SCC (L&S) 600; AIR 1990 SC 307; 1991 (15) ATC 851; 1989 (2) AWC 1433; 1990 (60) FLR 386; JT 1989 (4) SC 327; 1990 LabIC 319; 1989 (2) LLN 970; 1989(2) SCALE 1115; 1990 (1) SLJ 84; 1990 (1) SLR 830; [1989] Supp2 S.C.R. 201; 1990 (1) UPLBEC 1

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Uttar Pradesh Municipalities Act, 1916

Summary: Civil Procedure - Municipalities & Local Govt. - Uttar Pradesh Municipalities Act, 1916, ss. 71 and 75 - Appointment of Municipality was set aside by Prescribed Authority without any opportunity of hearing to appointee - Held, said order void as it violated principles of natural justice - Appeal allowed.
