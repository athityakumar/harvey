Rashik Lal And Others v Shah Gokuldas And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

02 February 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

L.M. Sharma, S. Ratnavel Pandian

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 429; (1989) 1 SCC 542; AIR 1989 SC 920; 1989 (1) Bom.C.R. 610; JT 1989 (1) SC 207; 1989 (1) RCJ 247; 1989 (1) RCR(Rent) 194; 1989(1) SCALE 245; [1989] 1 S.C.R. 439; 1989 (1) UJ 549

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Rent Control - Constitution - Agreement between landlord and tenant - Notice demanding regular payment - Held, there was an implied agreement of payment and receipt of rent between landlord and tenant - Landlord not entitled to insist on rent to be paid every month would not be liable to be interfered with - Landlord under a duty to serve tenant with a notice demanding regular payment - Appeal allowed.
