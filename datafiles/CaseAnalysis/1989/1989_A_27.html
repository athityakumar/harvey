Mewa Ram Kanojia v All India Institute Of Medical Sciences And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

09 March 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

K.N. Singh, K. Jagannatha Shetty

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 690; (1989) 2 SCC 235; (1989) SCC (L&S) 329; AIR 1989 SC 1256; 1989 (10) ATC 51; 1989 (58) FLR 640; JT 1989 (1) SC 512; 1989 LabIC 1348; 1989 (2) LLJ 578; 1989 (1) LLN 566; 1989(1) SCALE 580; [1989] 1 S.C.R. 957; 1989 (2) SLR 37; 1989 (2) UJ 64

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Research, Rehabilitation, Parity

Summary: Constitution - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14, 16 and 39(d) - Equal pay for equal work - Classification - Different pay scales to Hearing Therapist and Audiologist in All India Institute of Medical Sciences - Belonging to same class - On basis of educational qualifications -Constitutionality - Held, Permissible classification - Justified in view of difference in educational qualifications for two posts - Petition dismissed.
