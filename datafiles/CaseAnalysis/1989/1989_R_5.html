R. Raghuram v P. Jayarama Naidu and Others

Supreme Court of India

15 December 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 345; (1990) Supp SCC 361; AIR 1990 SC 412; 1990 (1) CCC 97; JT 1989 (4) SC 538; 1989(2) SCALE 1320; 1990 (1) UJ 326

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Carriers & Commercial - Motor Vehicles Act, 1939, ss. 57(8), 63, 68B and 68C - (A) Notification of route - Existing operator exempted from operation of Scheme - Variation of permit to increase trip on overlapping portion - Whether amounts to granting of fresh permit? - (B) Scheme approved - Variation of permits of existing operators - Held, (A) Granting of fresh permit - (B) Question referred to Constitution Bench - Order accordingly.
