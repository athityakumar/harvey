Dharamdas Shamlal Agarwal v Police Commissioner And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

16 March 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ratnavel Pandian, B.C. Ray

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 520; (1989) 2 SCC 370; (1989) SCC (Cr) 378; AIR 1989 SC 1282; 1989 (2) Crimes 53; 1989 CRLJ 1130; 1989 CrLR(SC) 433; JT 1989 (1) SC 580; 1989(1) SCALE 658; [1989] 2 S.C.R. 43

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal; Defence & Security Forces

Keywords: Defence Regulations

Summary: Gujarat Prevention of Anti-Social Activities Act, 1985, s. 3 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 22(4) - Validity of preventive detention was in question - Subjective satisfaction of detaining authority - Material fact of acquittal of detenu in two of cases mentioned in table appended to grounds was not even placed before detaining authority - Held, since satisfaction is vitiated, detention is invalid.
