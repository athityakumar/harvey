Supreme Court of India

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, S. Natarajan

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 611; (1989) 3 SCC 77; AIR 1989 SC 1470; 1989 ALJ 692; 1989 (15) ALR 864; 1989 (1) AWC 639; JT 1989 (2) SC 261; 1989 (2) MLJ(SC) 24; 1989 (2) RCJ 283; 1989 (1) RCR(Rent) 493; 1989(1) SCALE 1147; [1989] 2 S.C.R. 769; 1989 (2) UJ 178

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 106 - Tenancy - Succession - Heirs of original tenant succeed to tenancy as joint tenants and not as tenants in common - Service of notice u/s. 106 on one of joint tenants who acted on behalf of others - Validity - Held, was sufficient - Appeal allowed.
