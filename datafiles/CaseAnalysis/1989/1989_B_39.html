Bharat Singh v State of Madhya Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 February 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, S. Natarajan

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 481; (1990) Supp SCC 62; (1990) SCC (Cr) 617

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 (as amended by Act No.25 of 2005 & Act No.2 of 2006)

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 324 and 326 - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973 - Grievous hurt - Conviction modified to one u/s. 324, IPC instead of u/s. 326, IPC - Compromise between appellant and PW1 - Compounding allowed - Accused acquitted - Appeal allowed.
