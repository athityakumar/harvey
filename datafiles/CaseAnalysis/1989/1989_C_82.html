Ahmedhussain Shaikhhussain (C) Ahmed Kalio v Commissioner Of Police, Ahmedabad And Anr

Supreme Court of India

19 September 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 800; (1989) 4 SCC 751; (1990) SCC (Cr) 86; AIR 1989 SC 2274; 1989 (3) Crimes 711; 1989 CRLJ 2312; 1990 CrLR(SC) 31; JT 1989 (3) SC 689; 1989(2) SCALE 545; [1989] 1 S.C.R. 177; [1989] supp1 S.C.R. 177; 1990 (1) UJ 151

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Externment, Gujarat Prevention of Anti Social Activities Act, 1985, Manipur University Act, 1980

Summary: Criminal - Gujarat Prevention of Anti-Social Activities Act, 1985, s. 3(2) - Petitioner was detained for illegal sale of liquor while already in jail in connection with a criminal offence and this detention order was challenged - Court held that there must be cogent and relevant material constituting fresh facts to necessitate making order of detention and mere possibility of detenu's release from jail is not a ground - Order quashed and petitioner set at liberty - Appeal allowed.
