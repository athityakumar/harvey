Rakapalli Raja Rama Gopala Rao v Naragani Govinda Sehararao And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

12 September 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, K. Jagannatha Shetty

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 337; (1989) 4 SCC 255; AIR 1989 SC 2185; JT 1989 (3) SC 629; 1989 (2) RCR(Rent) 445; 1990 (1) RentLR 676; 1989(2) SCALE 542; [1989] supp1 S.C.R. 115

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Land & Property - Rent Control - Andhra Pradesh Buildings (Lease, Rent And Eviction) Control Act, 1960, s. 10(2) Proviso - Default in payment - Tenant omitting to pay rent on bona fide belief that he had right to purchase property - In view of prior agreement to sell with predecessor of landlord - Earnest money paid by tenant to predecessor landlord - Held, fit case for Rent Controller to invoke proviso and give time to tenant for payment of rent - Appeal allowed.
