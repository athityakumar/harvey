Jawaharlal Nehru University v Dr. K.S. Jawatkar & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

12 May 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 719; (1989) SCC (L&S) 501; (1989) Supp1 SCC 679; AIR 1989 SC 1577; 1989 (11) ATC 278; 1989 (59) FLR 190; 1989 LabIC 1526; 1989 (2) LLJ 586; 1989(1) SCALE 1438B; [1989] 3 S.C.R. 273; 1989 (3) SLJ 48; 1989 (3) SLR 730; 1989 (2) UJ 463

Case Digest

Subject: Education; Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Education Norms

Summary: Service - Jawaharlal Nehru University Act, 1966, ss. 7(b) and 5(2) - Transfer of Asst. Professor from one university to other on transfer of center of PG studies established by university to another university - Effect of transfer of center on his transferability - Held, it would not result in transfer of employment of incumbent from one to other, except by his consent - Appeal dismissed.
