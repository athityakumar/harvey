Bihar State Electricity Board And Anr. v Dhanawat Rice And Oil Mills

Supreme Court of India

17 January 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

G.L. Oza, K.N. Saikia

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 392; (1989) 1 SCC 452; AIR 1989 SC 1030; JT 1989 (1) SC 425; 1989(1) SCALE 162; [1989] 1 S.C.R. 168; 1989 (1) UJ 379

Case Digest

Subject: Electricity & Energy

Summary: Electricity & Energy - Electricity (Supply) Act, 1948, s. 26 - Supply of electricity - Agreement - Electricity Board and consumers - Clause as to payment of annual minimum guarantee charges by consumers even if they consume less energy - Failure of Board to supply constant electricity due to tripping load shedding and power cut - Liability under clause, if still exits - Held, liability does not cease - Order accordingly.
