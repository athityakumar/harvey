Bajaj Auto Limited v Behari Lal Kohli

Supreme Court of India

08 August 1989

Case Analysis

Bench

L.M. Sharma, N.D. Ojha

Where Reported

1989 Indlaw SC 683; (1989) 4 SCC 39; AIR 1989 SC 1806; JT 1989 (3) SC 324; 1989 (2) RCJ 186; 1989 (2) RCR(Rent) 320; 1989 (2) RentLR 300; 1989(2) SCALE 285; [1989] 3 S.C.R. 730; 1989 (2) UJ 495

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958, s. 14(1) Proviso (b) - Registration Act, 1908, s. 49 - Tenancy - Sublease - Construction of deed - Unregistered lease deed - Clause therein permitting tenant to sublet only to its associate concern without consent of landlord - Sublet to another company - Whether liable to evicted ? - Held, liable to be evicted - Appeal dismissed.
