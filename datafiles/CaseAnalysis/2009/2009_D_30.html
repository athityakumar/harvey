Dalip Kaur and Others v Jagnar Singh and Another

Supreme Court of India

07 July 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, A.K. Ganguly

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 861; (2009) 14 SCC 696; AIR 2009 SC 3191; 2009 ALL MR (Cri) 3160; 2009 (3) JCC 2243; JT 2009 (9) SC 184; 2010 (1) MLJ(Crl) 133; 2009 (4) RLW 3488; 2009(9) SCALE 255; [2009] 10 S.C.R. 264

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - IPC, 1860, ss. 406, 420 - CrPC, 1973, s. 482 - Criminal breach of trust - Cheating - Appellant-owner received earnest money for agreement for sale - Cancellation of agreement - Non-return of earnest money - FIR registered against appellant - Quashing of FIR sought - HC refused to exercise the inherent jurisdiction u/s. 482 of Code - Appellant's petition dismissed - No application of mind by HC - Whether breach of contract of an agreement for sale would constitute an offence u/ss. 406 or 420 of IPC? - Matter remitted to HC.
