Izharul Haq Abdul Hamid Shaikh & Anr. v State Of Gujarat

Supreme Court of India

06 March 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

Altamas Kabir, Mukundakam Sharma

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 312; (2009) 5 SCC 283; (2009) 2 SCC (Cr) 653; 2009 (2) G.L.R. 1502; 2009 (3) JCC 1616; JT 2009 (3) SC 385; 2009 (2) RCR(Criminal) 390; 2009(3) SCALE 791; [2009] 4 S.C.R. 1

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: TADA Act, Terrorist Affected Areas (Special Courts) Act, 1984, Terrorists and Disruptive Activities Procedure (UP Amendment) Act, 1976

Summary: Criminal - Terrorists and Disruptive Activities (Prevention) Act, 1987 - Questions of law relating regarding grant of bail - Held, since the other labourers have been granted bail and there being no available material to presume that Jivan Raghu Varli had knowledge of the contents of the boxes, he may be granted bail on a parity with the other labourers - Appeals disposed of.
