Gujarat Agricultural University v All Gujarat Kamdar Karmachari Union

Supreme Court of India

31 July 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

R.M. Lodha, Tarun Chatterjee

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 941; (2009) 15 SCC 335; AIR 2010 SC 2507; 2009 (3) CLR 30; JT 2009 (10) SC 449; 2009 (4) LLJ 382; 2009(10) SCALE 555; [2009] 11 S.C.R. 875; 2010 (1) SLR 621; 2009 (4) UPLBEC 3182

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Daily Rate, Educational Institution, Festival, Period Of Operation, U.P. Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, Gujarat Agriculture University Act, 1969

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Change of conditions of service - Workmen entered into a settlement with Appellant-employer wherein 1 holiday in a week - State Govt declared holidays on 2nd and 4th Saturdays and 11 holidays on Diwali without wages - Appellant made applicable such holidays to workmen - Workmen contended that they forced to avail the holidays amounted to change of conditions of service settled in Settlement - Held, conditions of service applicable to workmen, although, they were daily rated employees - Provisions governing the holidays in Settlement was conditions of service - No new settlement entered into between the parties nor any award replaced the present settlement - Settlement continued to be in force - Provision of leave for more than 1 day in a week and 11days festival leave without following the prescribed procedure, is illegal - However, no work was taken from workmen during the holidays, hence, full wages cannot be given - Appellant brought changes in the holidays pattern as per the declared holidays of State - Appellant institution is a fully Govt aided institution - Action of the appellant was without any ulterior motive - However, 50% wages on the days of holidays to be ordered - Order accordingly.
