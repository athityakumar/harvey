Navanath and Others v State Of Maharashtra

Supreme Court of India

15 April 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Cyriac Joseph

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 493; (2009) 14 SCC 480; AIR 2009 SC (Supp) 1540; 2009 (3) AWC 2543; 2009 (4) Bom.C.R. 182; 2009 (2) RCR(Civil) 917; 2009 (108) RD 366; 2009(5) SCALE 575; [2009] 6 S.C.R. 632

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Land Acquisition Court, Orchard, Mango Trees

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894 - Classification of lands - Held, opinion of the High Court that saplings varying from 1 to 3 years of age are available for plantation from the Government as well as private horticulture nurseries is based on surmises and conjectures - Market value of the land cannot be determined both on the basis of sale instance as also on capitalization method keeping in view the fact that it had fruit bearing trees - Appeals allowed.
