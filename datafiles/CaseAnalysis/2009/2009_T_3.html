(1) Tika Ram and Others; (2) Saroj Agarwal; (3) v State Of Uttar Pradesh And Others

Supreme Court of India

09 September 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

V.S. Sirpurkar, Tarun Chatterjee

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 1166; (2009) 10 SCC 689; AIR 2010 SC (Supp) 805; JT 2009 (12) SC 1; 2009(12) SCALE 349; [2009] 14 S.C.R. 905

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property

Keywords: Retrospective Legislation, Special Courts, Constitutionality, Short Title, Standing Crops, Taxation on Income (Investigation Commission) Act, 1947, Land Acquisition (Uttar Pradesh Amendment and Validation) Act, 1991, Rajasthan Urban Improvement Act, 1987

Summary: Land & Property - Constitution - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 3(A), 3(B), 4, 5, 6, 7, 8 and 17(1), 17(1)(A), 17(3)(A) and 17(4) - Land Acquisition (Uttar Pradesh Amendment and Validation) Act, 1991(Validating Act), ss. 2, 3 and 4 - Land acquisitions by State for housing schemes - Notification u/s. 4(1) and declaration u/s. 6 of Land Acquisition Act made simultaneously by State which was challenged before High Court - HC upheld challenge, which was upheld by Supreme Court in 'Radhey Shyam' case - Validating Act passed to nullify effect of 'Radhey Shyam' case - HC and Supreme Court had on earlier occasions upheld Validating Act - Validity of Validating Act again sought to be challenged among other challenges before HC in instant case - HC vide its impugned judgment, relying upon decisions of Supreme Court, again affirmed validity of Validating Act and rejected argument that judgments of Supreme Court per incuriam and hence required re-consideration - Impugned judgment, whether proper? - Held, yes - Arguments raised by appellants stood fully answered by Court in 'Meerut Development Authority vs. Satbir Singh, 1996 (11) SCC 462' - Constitutionality of Validating Act, upheld - Impugned judgement of HC, sustained - Appeals dismissed.
