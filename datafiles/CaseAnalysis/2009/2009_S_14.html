(1) Som Datt Builders Limited; (2) State of Kerala v (1) State of Kerala; (2) Som Datt Builders Limited

Supreme Court of India

17 September 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

R.M. Lodha, Tarun Chatterjee

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 1146; (2009) 10 SCC 259; 2010 (78) ALR 204; 2010 (2) AWC 1390; JT 2009 (12) SC 555; 2010 (1) RCR(Civil) 5; 2009(12) SCALE 648; [2009] 14 S.C.R. 611

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Keywords: Appeals Disposed Of, Reasoned Award

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, s.31(3) - Award - Setting aside of - Award passed by arbitrator in favor of petitioner contractor - Application for setting aside award filed by respondent before District Court - Application dismissed by District Court - Appeal filed by respondent before HC - DB allowed the appeal in part and set aside the award relating to claim nos. 1 and 4B on the ground that the findings thereon do not have supporting reasons being violative of Act - Legality - Held, since case not covered u/s.31(3) of Act, arbitrator obligated to state reasons in support of award in respect of claim nos. 1 and 4B - However, an opportunity ought to have been given by HC to arbitrator to give reasons - Therefore, matter being remitted back to District Court for considering the objections in respect of claim nos. 1, 4B, 5 and 6 - Directions issued to District Court to first remit claims nos. 1 and 4B to arbitrator to give reasons and after receipt of reasons from arbitrator, proceed with hearing and disposal of objections - Appeals disposed of.
