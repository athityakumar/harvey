Dilip Kumar Garg and Another v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

03 March 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

Markandey Katju, R.V. Raveendran

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 231; (2009) 4 SCC 753; 2009 (121) FLR 396; JT 2009 (3) SC 202; 2009 (2) LLN 433; 2009 (3) RCR(Criminal) 41; 2009(3) SCALE 521; [2009] 3 S.C.R. 955; 2009 (3) SLR 313; 2009 (2) UPLBEC 1067

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Service

Keywords: Policy Decisions, Engineering Service Rules, U.P. Public works Department Group-B Civil Engineering Service Rules 2004, U.P. Service of Engineers (Building and Road Branch) (Class II) Rules, 1936

Summary: Service - U.P. Public works Department Group-B Civil Engineering Service Rules 2004 - Validity of r. 5(ii) - Dispute between Junior Engineers of the PWD department of U.P. Government who are degree holders and those who are only diploma holders - Held, it is entirely for the authorities to decide whether the degree holders and diploma holders should be treated at par or not for the purpose of promotion from the post of Junior Engineer to the post of Assistant Engineer - In the past they have been treated differently does not mean that they cannot be treated identically subsequently - Court should not ordinarily interfere in policy decisions unless there is clear violation of some constitutional provision or the statute - Appeal dismissed.
