Man Singh v Commissioner, Garhwal Mandal, Pauri & Ors

Supreme Court of India

03 March 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Mukundakam Sharma

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 273; (2009) 11 SCC 448; AIR 2009 SC 2316; 2009 (2) AWC 2037; JT 2009 (3) SC 289; 2009(4) SCALE 645; [2009] 3 S.C.R. 1102; 2009 (3) SLR 241; 2009 (2) UPLBEC 1071

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Service - Termination - Appeal against - Held, respondent had filled up the vacancies in terms of the rules - Appellant's name was not sponsored by the Employment Exchange - He might have got himself registered in the Employment Exchange but in absence of any proof that his name was sponsored by the Employment Exchange, the same could not have been considered - Life of a select list is one year - In absence of any notification extending the validity of such select list, no appointment can be directed to be made from such select list - Appeal dismissed.
