State of Himachal Pradesh v Sada Ram and Another

Supreme Court of India

18 March 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

Tarun Chatterjee, H.L. Dattu

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 673; (2009) 4 SCC 422; AIR 2009 SC (Supp) 302B; 2009 (75) ALR 170A; 2009 (2) CTC 655; JT 2009 (4) SC 165; 2009 (3) RCR(Civil) 718; 2009(3) SCALE 842

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Non-speaking order - HC, in its vide impugned order confirmed the order of Tribunal while disposing the writ petition of the appellant - Held, HC failed to give reasons while disposing appellant's writ petition and to pass a speaking and reasoned order - Therefore, impugned order is set aside without going into the merits and writ petition is restored in the file of HC - Appeal partly allowed.
