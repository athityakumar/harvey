C. R. Nagaraja Shetty v Special Land Acquisition Officer and Estate Officer and Another

Supreme Court of India

24 February 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

V.S. Sirpurkar, Tarun Chatterjee

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 216; (2009) 11 SCC 75; 2009 (4) ALD(SC) 17; 2009 (75) ALR 155; 2010 (2) AWC 2124; JT 2009 (3) SC 9; 2009 (4) RCR(Civil) 460; 2009 (106) RD 822; 2009(3) SCALE 359; [2009] 2 S.C.R. 1092

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Appeal partly allowed, Land Acquisition Officer, Highway

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act - Enhancement of compensation - Held, High Court recorded a finding that the rate of Rs.75/- per square feet would be a proper rate - Satisfied with this finding of the High Court, as the Counsel has not been able to show anything from the record to hold that the concerned land would deserve a higher price than the one awarded by the High Court - High Court has directed the deduction of Rs.25/- per square feet - High Court has not discussed the reason for this deduction of Rs.25/- per square feet nor has the High Court relied on any piece of evidence for that purpose - Set aside the judgment to that extent - Appeal partly allowed.
