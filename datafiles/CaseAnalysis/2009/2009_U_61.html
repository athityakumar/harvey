Avinash Mehrotra v Union Of India & Others

Supreme Court of India

13 April 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

Dalveer Bhandari, Lokeshwar Singh Panta

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 435; (2009) 6 SCC 398; 2009 (6) CTC 450; 2009 (2) ESC 337; 2009 (4) MLJ(SC) 298; 2009(5) SCALE 354; [2009] 5 S.C.R. 913; 2009 (5) SLR 514

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Education

Keywords: Fundamental Duties, Government Policy, Existing Laws, Directions issued

Summary: Constitution - Education - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 21A, 21, 45 and 51A(k) - National Building Code of India, 2005, Part IV Fire & Life Safety - Code of Practice of Fire Safety in Educational Institutions (IS 14435:1997) of the Bureau of Indian Standards - Fundamental rights of life and education - Of school going children - Public interest litigation for cause of safer schools - Incidence of fire in a private school in State of Tamil Nadu - Fire started in school's kitchen when cooks were preparing mid-day meal - Prayer in PIL to bring about safer school conditions - Affidavits filed by States and Union Territories on notice being issued by the Court - Deliberation upon the issue by the Court from the perspective of fundamental rights u/art. 21, 21A - Right to education incorporates the provision of safe schools - Directions issued - Affidavit sought within a period of one month as to compliance of directions issued.
