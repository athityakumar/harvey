M. Chandru v Member Secretary, Chennai Metropolitan

Supreme Court of India

17 February 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Mukundakam Sharma

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 188; (2009) 4 SCC 72; 2009 (1) CTC 835; JT 2009 (2) SC 606; 2009(2) SCALE 750; [2009] 2 S.C.R. 661

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Chennai Metropolitan Water Supply and Sewerage Act, 1978, Tamil Nadu Water Supply and Drainage Board Act, 1970, Chennai Metropolitan Water Supply and Sewerage Infrastructure Development Charges (Levy and Collection) Regulations, 1998

Summary: Land & Property - Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning Act, 1971 - Legality and/or validity of Infrastructure Development Charges - Matter providing for collection of IDC is a matter of procedure and not a substantive provision - It, in that view of the matter, is not a delegation of power - CMDA is not appropriating any fund collected on behalf of the Sewerage Board - Appeals allowed.
