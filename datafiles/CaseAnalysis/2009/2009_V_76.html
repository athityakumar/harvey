Rampur Fertiliser Limited v Vigyan Chemicals Industries

Supreme Court of India

18 February 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

Mukundakam Sharma, S.B. Sinha

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 196; (2009) 12 SCC 324; 2009 (3) ALD(SC) 99; 2009 (2) AWC 1569; 2009 (2) BC 171; JT 2009 (3) SC 544; 2009 (2) RCR(Civil) 118; 2009(3) SCALE 115; [2009] 2 S.C.R. 650; 2009 (1) WLN(SC) 158

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Interest on Delayed Payment To Small Scale and Ancillary Industrial Under-Taking Act, 1993

Summary: Civil Procedure - Suit for recovery of Rs. 15, 027.75 along with interest at the rate of 24% per annum and at 2% per month compounded with monthly rest from 23.9.1992 till actual date of recovery - Issue with regard to the nature of interest that the respondent is entitled to in respect of the amount for which he had laid claim and in respect of which a decree was passed in its favour - Held, quantum and rate of interest which the appellant is entitled to would be in accordance with the provisions of s. 34 of CPC - According to the provisions of s. 34 of the Code interest is to be awarded at a reasonable rate and on the principal amount - High court was not justified in granting interest at the rate of 18% per annum with monthly rests - Directed that pendente lite and future interest at the rate of 9% shall be paid - Appeal disposed of.
