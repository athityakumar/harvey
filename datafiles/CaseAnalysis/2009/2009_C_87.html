Radhey Shyam and Another v Chhabi Nath and Others

Supreme Court of India

15 April 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

A.K. Ganguly, Arijit Pasayat

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 487; (2009) 5 SCC 616; 2009 (3) RCR(Civil) 442; 2009 (108) RD 377; 2009(5) SCALE 630; [2009] 6 S.C.R. 236

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Keywords: Restoration Of Possession, Matter referred to Larger Bench

Summary: Civil Procedure - Property dispute - Whether the High Court in exercise of its extraordinary writ jurisdiction can interfere with a judicial order passed by a civil court of competent jurisdiction? - Held, unable to agree with the legal proposition laid down in Surya Dev Rai Vs. Ram Chander Rai and others - 2003 (6) SCC 675 that judicial orders passed by a Civil Court can be examined and then corrected/reversed by the writ Court under Article 226 in exercise of its power under a writ of certiorari - Matter referred to Larger Bench.
