Union Of India And Others v Dipak Mali

Supreme Court of India

15 December 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

Altamas Kabir, Markandey Katju

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 2039; (2010) 2 SCC 222; AIR 2010 SC 336; AIR 2010 SC (Supp) 196; 2010(1) ALL MR 989; 2010 (2) AWC 1730; 2010 (125) FLR 390; JT 2009 (15) SC 388; 2010 (5) MLJ(SC) 1132; 2009(14) SCALE 692; [2009] 16 S.C.R. 564; 2010 (1) SLR 293

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Central Civil Services (CCA) Rules, 1965

Summary: Service - Central Civil Services (CCA) Rules, 1965, r. 10 - Suspension - Respondent was suspended on 10-08-2002 - CCA Rules, 1965 amended by Notification dt. 23-12-2003 and sub-rules (6) and (7) were inserted which came into effect from 2-06-2004, but as a Review Committee was not constituted, respondent's suspension was not reviewed as required by amended Rules - Respondent filed petition submitting that suspension order dt. 10-08-2002, became invalid on expiry of 90 days from date on which sub-rules (6) and (7) of r. 10 came into force, since same had not been extended by Review Committee - Tribunal allowed application filed by respondent and quashed suspension order - HC dismissed appeal filed against said order - Hence, present special leave petition - Whether HC's order could be upheld? - Held, since review had not been conducted within 90 days from date of suspension, it became invalid after 90 days, since neither was there any review nor extension within said period of 90 days - Subsequent review and extension, could not revive order which had already become invalid after expiry of 90 days from date of suspension - Petition dismissed
