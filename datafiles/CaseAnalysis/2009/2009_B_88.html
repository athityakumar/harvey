State Of Assam v Barak Upatyaka D.U.Karmachari Sanstha

Supreme Court of India

17 March 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

R.V. Raveendran, Markandey Katju

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 331; (2009) 5 SCC 694; AIR 2009 SC 2249; 2009 (2) ESC 257; JT 2009 (4) SC 127; 2009(4) SCALE 355; [2009] 4 S.C.R. 467; 2009 (6) SLR 355

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Trusts & Associations

Keywords: Corporate Body, Assam Co-Operative Societies Act, 1949

Summary: Trusts & Associations - Assam Co-operative Societies Act, 1949 - Whether the High Court was justified in directing the state government to release grants to CAMUL, so as to enable CAMUL to pay the salary and other emoluments of its employees? - Held, there is no right created to demand the continuance of financial assistance to a co-operative society, on the ground that such assistance has been extended by the government, for several years - Appeal allowed.
