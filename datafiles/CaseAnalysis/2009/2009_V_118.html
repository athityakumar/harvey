Vodafone International Holdings B.V v Union of India

Supreme Court of India

23 January 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Mukundakam Sharma

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 1026; (2012) 6 SCC 757; 2009 (221) CTR 617; [2009] 179 TAXMAN 129

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 261 - Appeal - Held, question in regard to jurisdictional issue may be determined by authority concerned as a preliminary issue, as per SC decision in Management of Express Newspapers (P.) Ltd. v. Workers 1962 INDLAW SC 28 - Petitioner entitled to question decision of authority on preliminary issue before HC in event same is decided against it - Question of law to that extent shall remain open - Petition dismissed
