Vikram Greentech (I) Ltd. & Anr v New India Assurance Co. Ltd

Supreme Court of India

01 April 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

R.M. Lodha, D.K. Jain

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 467; (2009) 5 SCC 599; AIR 2009 SC 2493; 2009 (75) ALR 782; 2009 (2) AWC 1901; 2009 (3) CompLJ 385; 2009 (2) CPJ(Sc) 34; 2009 (4) MLJ(SC) 811; 2009 (2) RCR(Civil) 817; 2009(5) SCALE 183; [2009] 5 S.C.R. 437; 2009 (2) UC 907

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection; Insurance

Keywords: Irrigation System, Comprehensive, Re Insurance, Re Insurance Policy, Consumer Protection Act, 1996

Summary: Consumer Protection - Insurance - Consumer Protection Act, 1996 - Whether comprehensive floriculture insurance policy issued by the Insurance Company to the insured covered poly-houses 7, 8A and 8B? - Held, National Commission held that unable to accept the contention of the complainant that only houses 7, 8A or 8B were covered under the Insurance Policy in question especially when admittedly, they were non-existent on the date, the policy was taken and policy clearly mentioned the number of poly houses as 'Six' - Not find any error in the conclusion of the National Commission - Appeal dismissed.
