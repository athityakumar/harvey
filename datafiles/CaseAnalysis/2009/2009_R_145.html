State of Uttar Pradesh and Others v Ram Daras Yadav

Supreme Court of India

04 December 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

Dalveer Bhandari, A.K. Patnaik

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 1781; (2010) 2 SCC 236; 2010 (2) AWC 1722; 2010 (124) FLR 123; 2010 (1) JhrCR(SC) 189; JT 2009 (15) SC 107; 2010 (3) MPLJ 337; 2009(14) SCALE 367; 2009 (6) SLR 689

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Police Act, 1861-Section 7-Employment - Dismissal-Validity-Both respondent and G. K. as Constables in P.A.C.-Stated that in fact alleged incident had not taken place resulting in disciplinary proceedings-And they were coerced to file complaints at instance of superior officials - Difficult to examine veracity of this allegation-But punishment of dismissal-Clearly disproportionate-Order of dismissal set aside - Respondent's two increments be withheld - And respondent be reinstated in service forthwith with 50% back wages.
