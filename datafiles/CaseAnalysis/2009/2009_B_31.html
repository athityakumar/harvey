Babu Lal v Haryana State Agricultural Mkt. Board

Supreme Court of India

11 February 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

Tarun Chatterjee, V.S. Sirpurkar

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 111; (2009) 4 SCC 287; 2009 (2) ESC 208; 2009 (122) FLR 52; JT 2009 (2) SC 377; 2009 (2) LLN 92; 2009(2) SCALE 388; [2009] 3 S.C.R. 130

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Service - Suit for a decree for payment of salary from 2nd of November, 1995 to 30th of March, 2001- Held, plaintiff-appellant had worked as Executive Officer from 28th of March, 1997 to 31st of July, 1997, but this would not mean that he would be entitled to salary from 2nd of November, 1995 to 30th of March, 2001 - Office memorandum would make it clear that no arrears of pay will be made for the period for which he had actually not worked - Dismissed the suit of the plaintiff -appellant - Appeal dismissed.
