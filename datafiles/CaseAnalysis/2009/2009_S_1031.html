State of Rajasthan and Another v S. N. Tiwari and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 March 2009

Case Analysis

Bench

B. SUDARSHAN REDDY, S.B. Sinha, Mukundakam Sharma

Where Reported

2009 Indlaw SC 814; (2009) 4 SCC 700; AIR 2009 SC 2104; 2009(3) ALL MR 971; 2009 (2) AWC 2047; 2009 (2) ESC 283; JT 2009 (4) SC 96; 2009(4) SCALE 538; [2009] 4 S.C.R. 448; 2009 (3) SLR 591

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Loss Of Lien

Summary: Service - Rajasthan Service Rules, r. 18 - Lien - Respondent-employee was appointed on permanent basis in the Directorate of Economic and Statistics Department (parent department) - Thereafter he was sent to work in Medical and Health Department on deputation on urgent temporary basis as a Homeopathic Doctor under a Scheme for a period of 6 months or till the selection of the candidate whichever was earlier - Since no selection as such had taken place the respondent-employee continued in the said post until his attaining the age of superannuation - Whether respondent-employee entitled to claim promotion and other benefits in the parent department? - HC held that respondent-employee was temporarily appointed to work as Homeopathic Doctor in Medical and Health Services Department and always retained his lien in the parent department and therefore entitled to reliefs as claimed by him - Held, a government servants lien on a post stands terminated only on his acquiring a lien on a permanent post outside the cadre on which he is borne - Respondent-employee did not acquire a lien on a permanent post outside the cadre on which he is borne - No infirmity in the order passed by the HC - Appeal dismissed.
