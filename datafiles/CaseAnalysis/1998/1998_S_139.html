New India Assurance Company Limited v Sheela Rani Smt. and Others

Supreme Court of India

15 September 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Venkataswami, A.P. Misra

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1202; (1998) 6 SCC 599; (1999) SCC (Cr) 174; 1998 (2) ACC 408; AIR 1999 SC 56; 1999 (1) AWC 100B; 1999 (1) CalLT(SC) 16; [1998] 94 Comp Cas 339; JT 1998 (6) SC 388; 1998(5) SCALE 269; 1998 (7) SLT 524; [1998] Supp1 S.C.R. 591

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Corporate; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Motor Vehicles Act, 1939- Section 103A (Corresponding to Section 157 of 1988 Act)-Transfer of vehicle-Liability of insurer- Intimation of transfer to insurance company by transferee though not in prescribed form seeking transfer of insurance policy-No reply by appellant insurer-Certificate of insurance not to lapse-But deemed to have been transferred in favour of transferee as per Section 103A.
