State of Kerala and Others v Travancore Chemicals and Manufacturing Company and Another

Supreme Court of India

11 November 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, G.T. Nanavati, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1296; (1998) 8 SCC 188; AIR 1999 SC 230; JT 1998 (7) SC 558; 1999 (112) STC 191; [1998] Supp2 S.C.R. 651

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Indian Limitation Act, 1908 - Kerala General Sales Tax Act 1963, s. 59 A - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 - Power of Govt. to determine rate of tax was in dispute - S. 59 confer's absolute powers on state Govt., not amenable to appellate or revisional provisions provided by Act - Held, it is constitutionally invalid.
