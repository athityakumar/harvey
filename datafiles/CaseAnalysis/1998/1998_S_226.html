Jagtar Singh And Anr v State Of Punjab

Supreme Court of India

06 November 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

M.K. Mukherjee, A.P. Misra

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 752; (1999) SCC (Cr) 120; (1999) 2 SCC 174; AIR 1999 SC 75; 1998 (4) CCR 86; 1999 CRLJ 20; JT 1998 (7) SC 454; 1998(6) SCALE 45; 1998 (9) SLT 66

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - IPC, 1860, s. 300 - Whether the co-accused who assaulted deceased on his left ankle with the weapon he was carrying can be convicted of murder, when there is evidence that the deceased died due to head injury inflicted by the main accused - Held, the co-accused cannot be convicted of murder, he can be convicted u/ ss. 307and 326 - Appeal dismissed.
