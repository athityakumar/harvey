Srinivasiah v Sri Balaji Krishna Hardware Stores

Supreme Court of India

20 November 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Saghir Ahmad, M. Jagannadha Rao

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1771; (1998) 8 SCC 312; AIR 1999 SC 462; 1999 (1) CLT 6; JT 1998 (8) SC 72; 1998 (2) RCR(Rent) 658; 1998(6) SCALE 219; 1998 (9) SLT 399; [1998] Supp3 S.C.R. 83; 1998 (8) Supreme 546

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure Code 1908, O.47, r.4 - Powers of review - Eviction proceedings - Order by Supreme Court dismissing eviction suit filed by landlord on grounds of bona fide need of his son- Rent Controller and appellate authority found that the said accommodation was in nature of 'godown' and not 'shop' - Supreme Court did not notice as their judgments were not filed in paper book - Held, the assumption by Court that available accommodation was a shop, was erroneous, and done away with.
