Secy. - Cum- Chief Engineer, Chandigarh v Hari Om Sharma & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

29 April 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Saghir Ahmad, K. Venkataswami, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 714; (1998) 5 SCC 87; (1998) SCC (L&S) 1273; AIR 1998 SC 2909; 1998 (79) FLR 657; JT 1998 (3) SC 654; 1998 LabIC 2521; 1998 (3) LLN 571; 1998(3) SCALE 388; [1998] 3 S.C.R. 99; 1999 (1) SLJ 23; 1998 (2) SLR 735; 1998 (5) SLT 1; 1998 (4) Supreme 420

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution, art. 16 - Contract Act, 1872, s. 23 - Promotee in stop-gap arrangement continued on the post, is entitled to salary of promotional post and regular promotion - Enfoceability and validity of an agreement that he would not claim higher salary - Held, not valid and unenforceable u/s. 23 of Contract Act - Appeal dismissed.
