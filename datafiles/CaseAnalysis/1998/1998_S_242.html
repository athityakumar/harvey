Chandrakant Patil, Shyam K. Garikapatti, Subhash v State Through Cbi, Government of National Capital

Supreme Court of India

02 February 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, M.K. Mukherjee

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 464; (1998) 3 SCC 38; (1998) SCC (Cr) 720; AIR 1998 SC 1165; 1998 (1) CCR 237; 1998 (1) Crimes 151; 1998 CRLJ 1613; JT 1998 (1) SC 352; 1998 (1) RCR(Criminal) 840; 1998(1) SCALE 326; [1998] 1 S.C.R. 447; 1998 (2) SLT 396; 1998 (1) Supreme 428

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Socio-Economic

Summary: Terrorist And Disruptive Activities (Prevention) Act, 1987, ss.5 and 19; Constitution of India, art.142; Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, s.377 - Sentence - Enhancement of - Conviction of accused confirmed by Supreme under s.5 of TADA and issuing notices to accused on proposal to enhance Sentence - Held, accused has no right to plead for his acquittal at such stage by reviewing finding already made - Supreme Court has power to enhance sentence in absence of an appeal by Govt.
