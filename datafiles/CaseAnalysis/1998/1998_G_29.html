State of Punjab v Gian Kaur and Another

Supreme Court of India

05 March 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 2146; (1998) SCC (Cr) 942; AIR 1998 SC 2809; 1998 CRLJ 2061; JT 1998 (2) SC 563

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 300 - Indian Evidence Act, 1872, s. 32 - Whether dying declaration by deceased wife in dowry death case is credible? - Held, the instant dying declaration and other evidence of prosecution seem inconsistent in light of fact that thumb impression on dying declaration had clear ridges and curves while autopsy report stating deceased wife had sustained 100% burn injuries - Therefore, accused entitled to benefit of doubt.
