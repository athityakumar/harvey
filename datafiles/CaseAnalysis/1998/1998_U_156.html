Syed Zaheer Hussain v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 December 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Majmudar, M. Jagannadha Rao

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 2049; (1999) 9 SCC 86; (1999) SCC (L&S) 666; AIR 1999 SC 3367; 1999 (2) AWC 1183; 1999 (81) FLR 704; 1999 LabIC 2616; 1999 (2) UPLBEC 944

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 133 - Dismissal - Proportionality of punishment - Unauthorised absent from service - Placed under suspension when he tried to resume - Punishment of dismissal from service - Held, is harsh - Substituted by lesser punishment, that is, reinstatement with withdrawal of 50% of back - wages.
