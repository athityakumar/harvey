Boorgu Jagadeshwaraiah and Sons v Pushpa Trading Company

Supreme Court of India

16 April 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, K.T. Thomas, M.M. Punchhi

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1649; (1998) 5 SCC 572; AIR 1999 SC 1503; AIR 1998 SCW 3862; 1998 (3) MLJ(SC) 124; 1998 (2) RCR(Rent) 597; 1998(4) SCALE 282; 1998 (9) SLT 282

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Andhra Pradesh Buildings (Lease, Rent and Eviction) Control Act, 1960, s. 10(3)(a)(iii) - Eviction suit - Ground, additional requirement for purpose of business - Landlord owning a nonresidential building - Held, claim of landlord cannot be unlocked merely because he owns a non- relevant factors viz aspects of quality, size and suitability of building in possession of landlord are considerable factors - Remitted to High Court for considering relevant factors.
