Sheela Devi v Jaspal Singh

Supreme Court of India

13 July 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1029; (1999) 1 SCC 209; AIR 1999 SC 2859; 2001 (42) ALR 427; 1999 (5) SLT 70; 1998 (Supp) MLJ(SC) 25

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent control - Constitution - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Uttar Pradesh Urban Buildings (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972, s. 18 - Respondent straightway filed writ petition - Alternative remedy - No reasons given for not availing alternative remedy of revision u/s. 18 of Act - Whether impugned order of High Court re-examining facts liable to be set aside - Held, order of HC set aside - Liberty to avail remedy of revision given to respondent - Appeal disposed of.
