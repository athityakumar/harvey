Kalicharan Mahapatra v State of Orissa

Supreme Court of India

04 August 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 907; (1998) 6 SCC 411; (1998) SCC (Cr) 1455; AIR 1998 SC 2595; 1998 CRLJ 4003; JT 1998 (5) SC 269; [1998] 3 S.C.R. 961; 1999 (2) SLJ 123

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Prevention of Corruption Act, 1947, ss. 19 and 7 - Public servant - Prosecution of - Committing offence under Act as public servant - Held, can be prosecuted whether he continues in office or not at time of trial or during pendency of prosecution - With sanction contemplated in s. 19 of Act.
