Rajasthan Public Service Commission v Chanan Ram and Another

Supreme Court of India

26 February 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Majmudar, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1504; (1998) 4 SCC 202; (1998) SCC (L&S) 1075; AIR 1998 SC 2251; JT 1998 (2) SC 114; [1998] 1 S.C.R. 1099; 1998 (2) SLJ 226

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Uttar Pradesh Intermediate Education Act, 1921, Rajasthan State Agricultural Marketing Service Rules, 1986

Summary: Uttar Pradesh Intermediate Education Act, 1921 - Constitution of India,1950, art. 309 - Rajasthan State Agricultural Marketing Service Rules 1986, r .6 - Recruitment - Advertisement for posts of Asst. Director - Amendment of Rules leading to abolishing said posts and creating new posts of Marketing Officers - Fresh advertisement issued - Old vacancies were carried forward and got merged along with three more vacancies for newly created posts of Marketing Officers - Held, earlier advertisement became infructuous and candidate who made application under earlier advertisement cannot insist that recruitment process must be continued and must be brought to its logical end.
