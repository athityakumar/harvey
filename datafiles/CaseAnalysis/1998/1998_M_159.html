Sridhar M. A v Metalloy N. Steel Corporation

Supreme Court of India

27 January 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, G.N. Ray

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 2028; (2000) 1 SCC 397; AIR 1998 SCW 4112; 2000 (5) Bom.C.R. 320; 2000 (2) CCR 82; [1999] 98 Comp Cas 805; 2000 (125) PLR 670; 2000 (3) SLT 675

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Corporate; Criminal

Summary: Banking & Finance - Criminal - Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881, s. 138 - Deemed service - Appellant did not get notice - Whether Trial Court was justified in passing the order of acquittal? - High Court passed the order of conviction and sentence by proceeding on the basis of deemed service - Held, appellant is entitled to the benefit of doubt as to whether such service, in fact, had been effected on the appellant - Order of conviction and sentence passed by HC set aside - Appeal disposed of.
