T. Srinivasan v T. Varalakshmi (Mrs.)

Supreme Court of India

22 January 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, M.M. Punchhi, M. Srinivasan

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1416; (1998) 3 SCC 112; AIR 1999 SC 595; 1998 (33) ALR 593; 1998 (3) AWC 2024A; 1998 (3) Bom.C.R. 643; JT 1998 (4) SC 312; 1998 (2) MLJ(SC) 123

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Women & Children

Summary: Hindu Marriage Act, 1955, s. 23(1)(a) and 13(1A) - 'Misconduct' by husband - Decree for restitution of conjugal rights in favour of husband - Husband prohibiting wife to enter house on her demand - Even to relatives trying to rehabilitate wife - Held, conduct of husband positive wrong and amount to uncondonable misconduct - Husband not entitled to relief of divorce u/s. 13(1-A) on grounds of absence of restitution of conjugal rights.
