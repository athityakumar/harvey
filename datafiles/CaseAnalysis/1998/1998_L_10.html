Rena Drego (Mrs) v Lalchand Soni and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 March 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1685; (1998) 3 SCC 341; AIR 1998 SC 1990; 1998 (3) Bom.C.R. 320; JT 1998 (2) SC 369; 1998 (2) KLT 19; 1998 (1) RCJ 490; 1998 (1) RCR(Rent) 349; 1998(2) SCALE 256; [1998] 2 S.C.R. 197; 1998 (3) SLT 678; 1998 (2) Supreme 376

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 227 - Bombay Rents, Hotel and Lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947, s. 13(1)(g) - (A) Scope of supervisory jurisdiction u/art. 227 of Constitution - (B) Eviction of tenant on ground of bona fide required - Landlady living with her husband, grown up sons and grandchild in one bedroom apartment - Denial of eviction to her by High Court - Ground, her eldest son was having a flat of his own - Validity of - (C) Pleadings - Whether landlady is required to specify exact plinth area in her possession - Held, HC cannot disturb findings of facts recorded by lower court, by relying upon fresh materials - Improper when there was no evidence to show his possession - Further held, she need not specify it - Appeal allowed.
