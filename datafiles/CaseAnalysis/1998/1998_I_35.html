Coir Board Ernakulam Kerala State and Another v Indira Devai P. S. and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 November 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, M.K. Mukherjee, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1253; (2000) SCC (L&S) 120; (2000) 1 SCC 224; 1999 (1) LLJ 1109; 1998(6) SCALE 288; 2000 (4) SLT 785

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Reconsideration of judgment in Bangalore Water Supply & Sewerage Board v. A. Rajappa, (1978 Indlaw SC 260) - Held, judgment in Rajappa case does not require reconsideration - Appeals directed to be listed before the appropriate bench.
