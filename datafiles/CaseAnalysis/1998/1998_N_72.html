Collectors of Central Excise v Tobacco Company Etc

Supreme Court of India

09 January 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1986; (1998) 8 SCC 250; AIR 1998 SC 668; AIR 1998 SCW 319; 1998 (144) CTR 618; 1998 (74) ECR 478; 1998 (97) E.L.T. 388; 1998 (1) FAC 1; JT 1998 (1) SC 66; 1998 (2) MLJ(SC) 2; 1998 (24) RLT 457; 1998(1) SCALE 58; [1998] 1 S.C.R. 63; 1998 (2) SLT 152; 1998 (109) STC 376; 1998 (1) Supreme 189

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, s. 38 - Central Excise Rules, 1944, r. 8 - General clauses Act, 1897, s. 5 - Notification under Act - (A) When becomes effective - Publication of notification through Gazette - (B) Whether mere printing of it in Gazette is sufficient? - Held, it becomes effective from date it is made available to public - Mere printing of it in Gazette is not sufficient - Appeal partly allowed.
