Meghra Urkudaji Tempe v The State Of Maharastra

Supreme Court of India

13 May 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Jagannadha Rao, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 737; (1998) 5 SCC 221; (1998) SCC (L&S) 1315; AIR 1998 SC 2119; JT 1998 (4) SC 137; 1998 LabIC 2121; 1998 (3) LLN 54; 1998(3) SCALE 589; [1998] 3 S.C.R. 419; 1998 (4) SLR 384; 1998 (5) SLT 86; 1998 (5) Supreme 22

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution, arts. 16 and 309 - Maharashtra Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1982, r. 10(4)(a)(i) - Review of case of employee for compulsory retirement before he crosses age of 50 years not done - Whether case could be deemed to have been reviewed in employees favour - If second review could be made and criteria for it - Matter befre two judges Bench - Matter referred to a larger Bench.
