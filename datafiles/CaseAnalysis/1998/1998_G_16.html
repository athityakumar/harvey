Tax Recovery Officer II, Sadar, Nagpur v Gangadhar Vishwanath Ranade (Dead) Through Smt Shobha Ravindra Nemiwant

Supreme Court of India

10 September 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1246; (1998) 6 SCC 658; AIR 1999 SC 427; 1998 (4) CLT 6; 1998 (149) CTR 90; [1998] 234 ITR 188; JT 1998 (6) SC 277; 1998(5) SCALE 241; 1998 (7) SLT 466; 1998 (7) Supreme 268; [1998] 100 TAXMAN 236; 1999 (2) TLR 90

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income-tax Act, 1961, s. 281, chap. II, rr. 11(4) and 11(5) - Attachment of property - Objections - Assessee has conveyed property to wife and daughter during pendency of proceedings - Transfer declared void by Tax Recovery Officer without enquiry into question of possession - Held, is without jurisdiction.
