Sanchalakshri and Another v Vijayakumar Raghuvirprasad Mehta and Another

Supreme Court of India

18 November 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, B.N. Kirpal, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1303; (1998) 8 SCC 245; AIR 1999 SC 578; JT 1998 (8) SC 55; 1999 (1) LLJ 343; 1999 (2) MLJ(SC) 12; 1999 (2) SLJ 75; [1998] Supp3 S.C.R. 13; 1999 (1) UPLBEC 417

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Administrative Tribunals Act, 1985, s. 14 - Punishment imposed on teacher - Judicial review - Extent of - Teacher doing forged signature of District Education Officer - No financial benefit yet to him - Dismissal after inquiry - Substitution of sentence by court/tribunal - Held, punishment of dismissal not disproportionate - Amounts to grave misconduct - Order of court/tribunal not proper.
