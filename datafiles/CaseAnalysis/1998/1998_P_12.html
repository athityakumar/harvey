C.B.S.E. and Another v P. Sunil Kumar and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 May 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1330; (1998) 5 SCC 377; AIR 1998 SC 2235; AIR 1998 SCW 2128; 1998 (33) ALR 465; 1998 (4) ALT 12; 1998 (2) CLT 114; JT 1998 (4) SC 105; 1998 (1) KLT 929; 1998(3) SCALE 525; [1998] 3 S.C.R. 327; 1998 (4) SLR 378; 1998 (5) SLT 68; 1998 (4) Supreme 572

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Education - Admission to examination and issue of certificates - Direction by High Court to Secondary Board to admit students of unaffiliated institution to public examination conducted by Board - Whether further direction compelling Board to issue certificates in favour of those who have undertaken examination permissible? - Held, it would tantamount to subversion of law - Not permissible - Appeal allowed.
