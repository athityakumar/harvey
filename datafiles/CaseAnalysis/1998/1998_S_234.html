M/S. Upadhyay & Co v State Of U.P. & Others

Supreme Court of India

01 December 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Saghir Ahmad, K.T. Thomas

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 621; (1999) 1 SCC 81; AIR 1999 SC 509; 1999 ALJ 199; 1999 (1) CLT 63; JT 1998 (8) SC 257; 1999 (1) MLJ(SC) 100; 1998(6) SCALE 356; 1999 (1) SCJ 226; 1998 (10) SLT 1; [1998] Supp3 S.C.R. 234; 1998 (9) Supreme 129

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Keywords: Ground Of Res Judicata

Summary: Constitution of India, arts.136 and 226 ; Civil Procedure Code 1908, O.23, r.1(3) - The ban under O. 23, r.1(3) of CPC to file a fresh suit in case of withdrawal of suit without seeking permission to file a fresh suit is based on Rule of public policy - Rule of public policy is applicable to writ petitions filed under art.226 and Special Leave Petitions filed under art.136 - Held, cannot file another Special Leave Petition challenging the same order again.
