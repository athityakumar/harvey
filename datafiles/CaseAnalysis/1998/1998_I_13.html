Income Tax Officer and Another v K. L. Srihari (Huf) and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 March 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Srinivasan, S.C. Agrawal, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 2163; [2001] 250 ITR 193

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 147 - Scope and effect of reopening of an assessment - Assessment order makes a fresh assessment of the entire income of the respondent-assessee - On facts, High Court correct in holding that assessment order had been effaced by the subsequent order - SLP dismissed.
