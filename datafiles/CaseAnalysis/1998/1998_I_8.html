Municipal Board, Saharanpur v Imperial Tobacco of India Limited and Another

Supreme Court of India

24 November 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Majmudar, M. Jagannadha Rao

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1315; (1999) 1 SCC 566; AIR 1999 SC 264; 1999 ALJ 202; 1999 (1) AWC 561; 1999 (1) CRC 58; JT 1998 (8) SC 138; 1998(6) SCALE 252; [1998] Supp3 S.C.R. 87; 1998 (9) Supreme 1; 1999 (1) UPLBEC 89

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Uttar Pradesh Municipalities Act, 1916

Summary: U P Municipalities Act, 1916, ss. 2(2), 2(5) and 129, explanation(a) - Water tax - Imposition of - 'Common compound' - Building to be within radius fixed from stand pipe - Calculation of area restriction - In case of cluster of buildings situated in common compound - Part of common compound falling within fixed radius - Held, entire building complex is covered for tax purpose - Phrase 'common compound' wider than 'compound' - Concept of 'appurtenant land' cannot be applied.
