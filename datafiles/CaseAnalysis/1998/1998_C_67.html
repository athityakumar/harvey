Coca-Cola Export v Income Tax Officer and Another

Supreme Court of India

30 March 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, Ms. Justice Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 666; (1998) 4 SCC 166; AIR 1998 SC 1864; 1998 (146) CTR 250; [1998] 231 ITR 200; JT 1998 (2) SC 706; 1998(2) SCALE 507; [1998] 2 S.C.R. 528; 1998 (4) SLT 109; 1998 (3) Supreme 356; [1998] 97 TAXMAN 475

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Regular Assessment, Cash Basis, Exports

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s.147 - Limitation Act, 1908 - Non-resident company - Reassessment of income on the basis of information in possession of ITO - Letters issued by Govt. of India placing ceiling on Remitting prorated home office expenses and service charges in US Dollars under FERA - Notice -(A) If constitute information for initiating reassessment proceedings - (B) If notice is required to specify whether action is contemplated u/cl. (a) or cl. (b) of s. 147 of the Act - Held, cannot constitute - Need not specify - Appeal allowed.
