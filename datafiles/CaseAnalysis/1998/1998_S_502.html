Mohan Karan v State of Uttar Pradeh and another

Supreme Court of India

18 March 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Venkataswami, A.P. Misra

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 645; (1998) 4 SCC 30; (1998) SCC (L&S) 982; AIR 1998 SC 1747; JT 1998 (2) SC 590; 1998 LabIC 1577; 1999 (1) LLJ 633; [1998] 2 S.C.R. 404; 1998 (3) SLJ 124; 1998 (2) UPLBEC 1525

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Municipalities & Local Governments; Service

Summary: Constitution of India, arts.16 and 309 , U.P. Government Servants Seniority Rules 1991, rr.3 and 6 , U.P. Urban Planning and Development Act ,1973, s.55 , U.P. Development Authorities Centralised Services Rules 1985, r.7 -Whether the seniority of the member of the U. P. Development Authorities centralised service , is determined as per 1991 rules-Held, the seniority of the Government servants in U.P. has to be determined as per 1991 rules -Rule 3 of U. P. Govt.
