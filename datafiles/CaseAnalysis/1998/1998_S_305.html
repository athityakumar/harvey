Aggarwal Brothers v State of Haryana and Another

Supreme Court of India

01 September 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 2054; (1999) 9 SCC 182; AIR 1999 SC 2868; 1999 (113) STC 317

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Summary: Haryana General sales Tax Act, 1973, s. 2(1) - Sale deemed - Transfer of right to use goods for consideration - Transfer of shuttering for consideration to builders and building contractors for use in construction of building - Assessee owning shuttering - Held, is deemed sale - No need of legal transfer of goods nor transaction should be like a lease.
