Central Board of Secondary Education v Nikhil Gulati and Another

Supreme Court of India

13 February 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, M.M. Punchhi, M. Srinivasan

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1459; (1998) 3 SCC 5; AIR 1998 SC 1205; 1998 (2) AWC 1180; JT 1998 (1) SC 718; 1998(1) SCALE 634; [1998] 1 S.C.R. 897; 1998 (3) SLT 160; 1998 (2) Supreme 58

Case Digest

Subject: Education

Summary: Education - Constitution, arts. 136, 226 - Challenge to grant of permission to appear in examination granted by Court - Held, court should not in its discretion grant permission to ineligible students unless there are justifiable reasons - Court decline to interfere u/art. 136 - Petition dismissed.
