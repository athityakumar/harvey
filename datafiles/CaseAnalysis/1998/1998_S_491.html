Shanmuga Traders and Others v State of Tamil Nadu and Others

Supreme Court of India

22 April 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1910; (1998) 5 SCC 349; AIR 1999 SC 1516; 1998 (9) SLT 270; 1999 (114) STC 1

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Service

Summary: Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax Act 1959, s. 7A - Central Sales Tax Act 1956, ss. 14J and 4 - Declared goods - Exigible to single point tax - State Electricity Board exempted iron and steel sold by them, from tax vide notification - Notification has effect of shifting incidence of tax on iron and steel sold by Board to second or subsequent sale - Held, notification illegal.
