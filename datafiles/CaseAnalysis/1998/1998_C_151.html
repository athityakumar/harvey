Messrs Pappu Sweets and Biscuits v Commissioner of Trade Tax U.P. Lucknow

Supreme Court of India

06 October 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, S.P. Bharucha, M.K. Mukherjee

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 751; (1998) 7 SCC 228; AIR 1998 SC 3247; 2004 (178) E.L.T. 48; JT 1998 (7) SC 9; 1998 (111) STC 425; [1998] Supp2 S.C.R. 119

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Sales Tax - Indirect Tax - U. P. Sales Tax Act, 1948, s. 4 A - Factories Act, 1948 - Notification D/-27-07-91, annexure II, item 18 - Word ' sweetmeat ' (mithai) - If include toffees manufactured by industrial units - And if such industrial units are entitled to exemption from sales tax - Held, it does not include - Entitled - Appeal allowed.
