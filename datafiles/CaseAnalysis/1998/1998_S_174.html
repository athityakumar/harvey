Amrit Lal Sood and Another v Smt. Kaushalya Devi Thapar and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 March 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Srinivasan, K.T. Thomas

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 643; (1998) 3 SCC 744; (1999) SCC (Cr) 158; 1998 ACJ 531; AIR 1998 SC 1433; 1998 (2) AWC 1197; [1998] 92 Comp Cas 305; JT 1998 (2) SC 484; 1998 (3) MLJ(SC) 45; [1998] 2 S.C.R. 284

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Corporate; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Carriers & Transportation - Motor Vehicles Act, 1939, s. 95 - Whether in an accident the insurer, who agreed to indemnify insured against all sums which insured become liable to pay in respect of death or bodily injury to 'any person' travelling - Held, as the 'any person' include an occupant of the car who is gratuitously travelling in the car, insurer will be liable - Appeal allowed.
