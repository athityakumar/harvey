Real Value Appliances Limited Etc., Engineering v Canara Bank and Others Etc., Vardhman Spinning

Supreme Court of India

05 May 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Jagannadha Rao, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 726; (1998) 5 SCC 554; 1998 (5) ACC 554; AIR 1998 SC 2064; AIR 1998 SCW 1924; 1998 (4) Bom.C.R. 606; 1998 (3) CLT 1; [1998] 93 Comp Cas 26; 1998 (3) CompLJ 58; 1998 (79) FLR 675; JT 1998 (3) SC 715; 1998(3) SCALE 427; [1998] 3 S.C.R. 170; 1998 (5) SLT 220; 1998 (4) Supreme 478

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Sick Industrial Companies (Special Provisions) Act, 1986, ss.15, 22 and 16 - Reference to Board for Industrial and Financial Reconstruction - Relevancy of conduct of an applicant - Recovery and liquidation proceedings before High Court against company - Suppression of facts before High Court - Contrary plea before High Court and BIFR regarding application for reference - If vitiated thereby - Held, inquiry may commence - After completion of registration of reference after scrutiny - Inquiry includes investigation.
