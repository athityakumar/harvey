B. P. Oil Mills Limited v Sales Tax Tribunal and Others

Supreme Court of India

03 September 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

M.K. Mukherjee, S.P. Bharucha, G.T. Nanavati

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 85; (1998) 6 SCC 577; AIR 1998 SC 3055; 1998 (78) ECR 497; JT 1998 (6) SC 210; 1998(5) SCALE 135; 1998 (7) SLT 280; 1998 (111) STC 188; [1998] Supp1 S.C.R. 397; 1998 (7) Supreme 170

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Uttar Pradesh Sales Tax Act, 1948, s. 3(3)(b)(iii) - Liability to pay tax was in question - Processing of crude oil for purpose of making it refined oil which will be sold - Held, refined oil is within definition of 'goods manufactured' so dealer is liable to pay tax.
