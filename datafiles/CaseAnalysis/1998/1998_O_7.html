Food Corporation Of India Etc.Etc. v Om Prakash Sharma & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

14 August 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Srinivasan, S.C. Agrawal, A.P. Misra

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 822; (1998) 7 SCC 676; AIR 1998 SC 2682; 1998 (2) CLR 755; JT 1998 (5) SC 513; 1998 LabIC 2995; 1999 (1) LLJ 1215; 1999 (1) LLN 94; 1998(4) SCALE 545; [1998] 3 S.C.R. 1193; 1999 (1) SLJ 140; 1998 (4) SLR 788; 1998 (6) SLT 718; 1998 (6) Supreme 401

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Telephone, Food Corporation of India (Staff) Regulations, 1971, Food Corporation Of India Act, 1945

Summary: Labour and Industry - Constitution - Food Corporations Act, 1964, s. 45 - Food Corporation of India (Staff) Regulations, 1971Category 3 - Constitution, arts. 14 and 16 - Category 3 - Promotion - Classification on basis of educational qualifications - Posts of Asst. Grade I and Asst. Grade II in Catetory 3 - Graduates and matriculates in lower grades of the category eligible for promotion on completion of three years service - Amendment incorporating five years of service as eligibility criteria for non-graduates - No material produced by Corporation to show that promotional posts required higher efficiency which could be expected only of a graduate - Corporation even agreeing before High Court to withdrawal of amendments - Whether classification made by amendment is violative of equality clause - Whether retrospective operation could be given to them - Held, classification is violative of equality clause - Retrospective operation given to them is also violative of Arts. 14, 16 - Appeal dismissed.
