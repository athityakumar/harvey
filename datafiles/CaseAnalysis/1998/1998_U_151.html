Sangfroid Remedies Limited v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

11 September 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1289; (1999) 1 SCC 259; AIR 1999 SC 3468; 1999 (84) ECR 183; 1998 (103) E.L.T. 5; 1998 (28) RLT 703

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, s. 35P - Pre-deposit of duty during pendency of appeal - Rejection of claim of assessee that his product falls under Chap. heading 33.03 - Neither notice nor opportunity of being heard given to assessee - Ex parte order levying excise duty - Appeal against - Held, assessee cannot be directed to pay full excise duty amount for entertaining and hearing appeal.
