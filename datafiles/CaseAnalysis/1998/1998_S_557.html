Union Of India & Ors. v Subir Mukharji & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

29 April 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Kurdukar, G.T. Nanavati

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 713; (1998) 5 SCC 301; (1998) SCC (L&S) 1358; AIR 1998 SC 2247; 1998 (1) CLR 1112; 1998 (79) FLR 491; JT 1998 (3) SC 540; 1998 LabIC 2129; 1998 (2) LLJ 238; 1998 (3) LLN 938; 1998(3) SCALE 330; 1999 (1) SLJ 20; 1998 (2) SLR 718; 1998 (5) SLT 24; 1998 (4) Supreme 336

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India, art.16 - Regularisation of services - Labourers, members of Handling Porters Cooperative Society supplied by Railway Contractor for doing perennial nature of work for Railway - Working since past 10 years - CAT directions for regularisation as group D employees on discretion of Railways - Subject to their fitness - Held, is fair exercise of discretion - Question whether labourers are employees of society or Railways left open.
