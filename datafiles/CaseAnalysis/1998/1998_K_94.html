Krishna Kumar Singh and Another v State of Bihar

Supreme Court of India

08 May 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar, D.P. Wadhwa

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1113; (1998) 5 SCC 643; JT 1998 (4) SC 58; 1998 (3) MLJ(SC) 100; 1998(3) SCALE 482; [1998] 3 S.C.R. 206; 1998 (5) SLT 306; 1998 (4) Supreme 619

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Service

Keywords: Bihar State University Act, 1976, Orissa Municipalities Act, 1950, Bihar Non-Government Elementary Schools (Taking Over of Control) Act, 1976, Kameshwar Singh Darbhanga Vishwa Vidyalaya Act, 1960, Bihar Sanskrit Education Board Act, 1981

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950 - Bihar Non-Government Elementary Schools (Taking over of Control) Act, 1976 - General Clauses Act, 1897 - Indian Limitation Act, 1908 - Bihar State University Act, 1976 - Kameshwar Singh Darbhanga Vishwa Vidyalaya Act, 1960 - Bihar Sanskrit Education Board Act, 1981 - Orissa Municipalities Act, 1950 - Whether the 1st Ordinance is valid, or whether all are valid or whether all are unconstitutional? - Can a series of Ordinances be issued validly under art. 213 over a number of years without placing any of the Ordinances before the State Legislature? - Whether the Governor had power to repromulgate the same Ordinance successively without bringing it before the legislature.
