Indian Bank v K. Usha and Another

Supreme Court of India

28 January 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Majmudar, M. Jagannadha Rao

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1431; (1998) 2 SCC 663; AIR 1998 SC 866; 1998 (1) CLR 67; 1998 (1) CLT 101; 1998 (78) FLR 801; JT 1998 (1) SC 265; 1998 LabIC 633; 1998 (1) LLJ 643; 1998 (2) LLN 23; 1998(1) SCALE 226; [1998] 1 S.C.R. 358; 1998 (2) SLR 185; 1998 (2) SLT 401; 1998 (1) Supreme 392

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance

Summary: Banking Regulation Act, 1949, s. 45 - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 2(p) and 18 - Amalgamation of banks - Liability of transferee bank - Claim for compassionate appointments by heirs and LRs. of deceased employees of transferor bank on basis of earlier agreement - Refusal by transferee bank - Held, earlier agreement/settlement between recognised union of employees of transferor bank with its management - Is binding on transferee bank also - Legal.
