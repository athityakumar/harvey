Government Servant Cooperative House Building Society Limited and Others v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 August 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar, M. Srinivasan

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 978; (1998) 6 SCC 381; AIR 1998 SC 2636; JT 1998 (5) SC 280; 1998 (2) RCR(Rent) 189; 1998(4) SCALE 391; [1998] 3 S.C.R. 996; 1998 (7) SLT 267; 1998 (6) Supreme 264

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Calcutta Municipal Act, 1923, Punjab Urban Immoveable Property Tax Act, 1940

Summary: Delhi Municipal Corporation Act, 1957, s. 116 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 245 - Property tax - Annual rateable value - Rent Act not applicable - Held, Actual annual rent received can be taken as annual rateable value - Tax remains property tax and cannot be treated as a tax on income - Parliament can make law for all entries in all three lists.
