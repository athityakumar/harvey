Indian Bank v The Official Liquidator, Chemmeens Exports (P) Ltd. & Ors

Supreme Court of India

08 May 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

S.S.M. Quadri, B.N. Kirpal

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 806; (1998) 5 SCC 401; AIR 1998 SC 2111; 1998 (2) CLT 97; [1998] 93 Comp Cas 76; 1998 (3) CompLJ 211; 1998 (79) FLR 761; JT 1998 (4) SC 172; 1998(3) SCALE 531; [1998] 3 S.C.R. 255; 1998 (5) SLT 159; 1998 (Supp) MLJ(SC) 84; 1998 (4) Supreme 599

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Keywords: Unregistered, Companies (Amendment) Act, 1960

Summary: Companies Act, 1956, ss.446, s.125 - Void charges - Preliminary decree - Passed against respondents and official liquidator - Liberty to respondents to deposit amounts on or before certain date - No appeal by official liquidator - Default in making payment - Application by official liquidator before Company Court - For declaring preliminary decree as void - Jurisdiction of - Held, cannot declare a decree as void passed by a competent Court - Not maintainable.
