Kanwar Singh and Others v Union of India

Supreme Court of India

30 October 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

V.N. Khare, A.S. Anand

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1931; (1998) 8 SCC 136; AIR 1999 SC 317; 1999 (1) AWC 784; 1998 (4) CLT 220; JT 1998 (7) SC 397; 1998(6) SCALE 2; 1998 (8) SLT 515; [1998] Supp2 S.C.R. 505; 1998 (8) Supreme 253

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 23 - Assessment of compensation was in dispute - Compensation awarded to claimants of adjoining villages - Sale instances not leading to correct assessment of market value of acquired land - Situation and potentiality of lands in adjoining villages are different - Held, sale instances cannot be relied upon - Claimants not entitled to same rate of compensation as it was awarded to claimants of adjoining villages.
