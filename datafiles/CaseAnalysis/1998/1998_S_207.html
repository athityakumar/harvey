Ashirwad Ispat Udyog and Others v State Level Committee and Others

Supreme Court of India

03 November 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1991; (1998) 8 SCC 85; AIR 1999 SC 111; 1998 (79) ECR 748; JT 1998 (7) SC 588; 1998(6) SCALE 104; 1998 (9) SLT 170; 1999 (112) STC 207; [1998] Supp2 S.C.R. 542; 1998 (8) Supreme 338

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax; Land & Property

Summary: Sales Tax - M.P. General Sales Tax Act, 1959, s. 2(j) - Sales Tax - Interpretation of statutes - External aids - Other statutes - Scope of word 'Manufacture' as define u/s. 2(j) of the Act - Held, scope not restricted to production of new article - Process of cutting down scrap by mechanical process into pieces convenient for use in rolling mills and foundries - Amounts to manufacture - Appeal Allowed. .
