The Appropnate Authority And Another v Smt Sudha Patit And Another

Supreme Court of India

10 November 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1251; (1998) 8 SCC 237; AIR 1999 SC 181; 1998 (150) CTR 405; [1999] 235 ITR 118; JT 1998 (7) SC 571; 1998(6) SCALE 90; 1998 (9) SLT 162; [1998] Supp2 S.C.R. 619; 1998 (8) Supreme 366; [1998] 101 TAXMAN 286

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961 - Compulsory purchase of property - Determination of fair market value - Authority conclude understatement of consideration in agreement by amount more than 15% of fair market value - Presumption as to evasion of tax by transferor - Not rebutted by transferor - HC embarking upon enquiry as appellate authority and substituting its conclusions in place of those of tribunal - Held HC exceeded jurisdiction - Appeal Allowed.
