New India Assurance Company v C. M. Jaya and Others

Supreme Court of India

23 July 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1062; (1999) 2 SCC 47

Case Digest

Subject: Insurance

Summary: Insurance - Insurance Act, s. 95(2) - Conflict of decisions - Whether in a case of insurance policy not taking any higher liability by accepting a higher premium, in case of payment of compensation to a third party, the insurer would be liable to the extent limited u/s. 95(2)? - Whether the insurer would be liable to pay the entire amount and he may ultimately recover from the insured? - Conflict in the two three-Judge Bench decisions of Supreme Court - To resolve conflict between Shanti Bai case (1995 Indlaw SC 618) and Amrit Lal Sood's case (1998 Indlaw SC 643) matter referred to larger bench.
