C.K. Antony, P.T. Joseph, V.S. Thomas and Another v B. Muraleedharan and Ors., C. Balachandran Nair

Supreme Court of India

01 September 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Venkataswami, S. Saghir Ahmad, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 609; (1998) 6 SCC 630; (1998) SCC (L&S) 1624; AIR 1998 SC 3136; AIR 1998 SCW 3051; JT 1998 (6) SC 11; 1998 (2) KLT 755; 1998 LabIC 3519; 1999 (1) LLJ 572; 1998 (4) LLN 625; 1998(5) SCALE 32; 1999 (2) SLJ 106; 1998 (3) SLR 2; 1998 (7) SLT 204; [1998] Supp1 S.C.R. 280; 1998 (7) Supreme 75

Case Digest

Subject: Environment, Wildlife & Animal; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Constitution of India, arts.16 and 309; Special Rules for Kerala Forest Service, r.8; Kerala State and Subordinate Service Rules, 1958, rr.9 and 27 - Seniority - Direct recruits and promotees to post of Asst. Conservators of Forest - Promotees admittedly appointed prior to direct recruits - But only by way of stop gap arrangement - Held, not entitled to seniority over direct recruits - As, not appointed in accordance with the rules.
