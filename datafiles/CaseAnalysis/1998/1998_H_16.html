Harichand v Director of School Education

Supreme Court of India

14 January 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1369; (1998) 2 SCC 383; (1998) SCC (L&S) 560; AIR 1998 SC 788; 1998 CRLJ 1106; JT 1998 (1) SC 124; 1998 (2) LLJ 831; 1998 (2) LLN 36; [1998] 1 S.C.R. 143

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Defence & Security Forces; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 311 - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, sch. 2, item 3 - Probation of Offenders Act, 1958, ss. 12(1) and 4 - Removal of disqualification attaching to conviction - Govt. servant convicted for offence under IPC but released on probation u/s. 4 of Act - Consideration of conviction for removal from service, in light of meaning of words u/s. 12 of Act - Held, conviction can be taken into account for removing him from service - Appeal dismissed.
