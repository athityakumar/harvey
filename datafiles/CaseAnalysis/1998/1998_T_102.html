M/S Rickmers Verwaltung Gmb H v The Indian Oil Corporation Ltd.

Supreme Court of India

19 November 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1769; (1999) 1 SCC 1; AIR 1999 SC 504; 1999 (1) ARBLR 1; 1999 (1) CompLJ 13; JT 1998 (8) SC 85; 1999 (1) MLJ(SC) 62; [1998] Supp3 S.C.R. 42

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Arbitration and ADR - Arbitration Act, 1940, s. 33 - Reference of dispute to arbitrator - Draft charter party agreement executed among parties but not enforceable as terms of letter of credit and performance guarantee were not agreed - No concluded and binding contract - Legality of clause relating to arbitration - Held, clause had no existence in eye of law - Appeal dismissed.
