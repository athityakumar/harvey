Union of India and Another v Ravi Shankar and Another

Supreme Court of India

24 February 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, M. Srinivasan

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1945; (1998) 3 SCC 146; (1998) SCC (L&S) 959; AIR 1998 SC 2810; 1998 (1) CLR 885; JT 1998 (2) SC 170; [1998] 1 S.C.R. 1097; 1998 (2) SLJ 292

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 16 and 309 - Central Govt. Health Scheme Delhi (Group C) Ayurvedic, Recruitment Rules (1978), r. 1 -Eligibility for appointment as Pharmacist - Held, degree of Vaidya Visharad recognised by Hindi Sahitya Sammelan, Allahabad is not a recognised qualification under Rules and such degree holder cannot claim appointment as Pharmacist under same.
