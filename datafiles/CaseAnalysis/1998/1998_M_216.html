Muthaiah Sekhar v Nesamony Tpt. Corporation Limited and Another

Supreme Court of India

01 September 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Venkataswami, A.P. Misra

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 615; (1998) 7 SCC 39; (1999) SCC (Cr) 183; 1998 (2) ACC 324; AIR 1998 SC 3064; 1999 (1) AWC 11; JT 1998 (6) SC 44; 1998(5) SCALE 28; 1998 (7) SLT 214; [1998] Supp1 S.C.R. 225; 1998 (7) Supreme 70

Case Digest

Subject: Tort

Summary: Miscellaneous - Motor Vehicles Act, 1988, s.168 - Accident - Permanent disability - Compensation - Enhancement - Claimant, a practising lawyer aged about 25 years and was pursuing M.L. course at time of accident - Disability certificate genuine - Treatment in foreign country - No evidence showing that such medical facility was available in India - Compenstion of Rs. 1,76,000/- fixed by Tribunal - Compensation enhanced by Rs. 3 lakhs - Appeal allowed.
