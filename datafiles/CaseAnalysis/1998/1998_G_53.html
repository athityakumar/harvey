J. Jayalalitha v Government of Tamil Nadu and Others

Supreme Court of India

24 September 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, M.M. Punchhi

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1231; (1999) 1 SCC 53; AIR 1999 SC 2330; JT 1998 (9) SC 387

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 32 - Public interest litigation - Sports Stadium Ban for conducting activities other than sports and games - Ban lifted for honoring Chief Minister in view of his platinum jubilee celebrations - Held, permission not to be quoted as precedent so long as ban continues - Not justified.
