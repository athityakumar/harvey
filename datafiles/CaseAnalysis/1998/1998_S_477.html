Ajay Sharma v State of Rajasthan

Supreme Court of India

15 September 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

S.S.M. Quadri, M.K. Mukherjee

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1198; (1999) SCC (Cr) 74; (1999) 1 SCC 174; AIR 1998 SC 2798; 1998 (4) CCR 6; 1998 CRLJ 4590; JT 1998 (6) SC 378; 1998 (4) RCR(Criminal) 432; 1998(5) SCALE 276; 1998 (7) SLT 538

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 300, 34 and 324 - Whether accused share common intention with that of main accused of murder? - Held, averments in FIR and evidences of other witnesses only suggest that instigation was only to strike deceased and not to murder and therefore, it cannot be said that accused had shared common intention to kill deceased - Therefore, conviction of accused under s. 302/34 modified to one under s. 324 r/w s. 110.
