Devendra Nath Singh (Dead) Through Lrs. and Others v Civil Judge, Basti and Others

Supreme Court of India

23 September 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, M.M. Punchhi

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1227; (1999) 1 SCC 71; AIR 1999 SC 2264; 2000 (38) ALR 332; JT 1998 (9) SC 423; 2000 (91) RD 28

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Uttar Pradesh Imposition Of Ceiling On Land Holdings Act, 1960, ss. 13 A and 38 B - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 11 - Re-determination of surplus land - Whether prescribed authority in exercise of his powers under s. 13-A of U.P. Imposition of Ceiling on Land Holdings Act, can reopen a matter already decided - Held, prescribed authority has power to re - open matter within 2 years from date of notification - Power does not include power to entertain fresh evidence and re - examine question - No finding or decision given before commencement of section can be treated as bar against principle of res judicata, does not apply.
