Chandraprakash Madhavrao Dadwa and Others v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 September 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Jagannadha Rao, S.B. Majmudar

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1237; (1998) 8 SCC 154; (1999) SCC (L&S) 153; AIR 1999 SC 59; 1999 (2) Bom.C.R. 97; JT 1998 (6) SC 602; 1999 (3) SLJ 40

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Eligibility - Qualifications

Summary: National Sample Survey Organisation (Data Processing Division and Survey, Design and Research Division) (Data Processing Assistant) Rules, 1977, r. 1 - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 16 and 309 - Recruitment qualifications - Change with retrospective effect - Imposing changed qualifications on those already recruited - Transferring them from posts to which they were recruited to other one having lesser scale - Held, arbitrary and illegal.
