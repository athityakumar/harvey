Polychem Limited & Anr v The State Of Maharashtra & Ors

Supreme Court of India

04 August 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 534; (1998) 6 SCC 196; AIR 1998 SC 2546; 1998 (4) Bom.C.R. 699; JT 1998 (5) SC 311; [1998] 3 S.C.R. 972; 1999 (2) SLJ 128

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Keywords: Bombay Prohibition Act 1949

Summary: Bombay Prohibition Act 1949, s.58A - Supervision charges - Levy of - Retrospective effect to increase in wages and dearness allowance of staff deputed for supervision - Held, supervision charges towards cost of staff cannot be demanded retrospectively - Collection of supervision charges in advance cannot be revised as a consequence of revision of salaries to Government servants.
