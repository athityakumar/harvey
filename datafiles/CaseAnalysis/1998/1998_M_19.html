Gopal Krushna Rath v M. A. A. Baig (Dead) By Lrs. and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 September 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Srinivasan, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1204; (1999) SCC (L&S) 325; (1999) 1 SCC 544; AIR 1999 SC 2093; JT 1998 (9) SC 367; 1999 LabIC 2339; 1999 (3) LLN 415

Case Digest

Subject: Education; Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Education Norms

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16 - University Grants Commission - Selection process started - And last date of receiving application over - Subsequent change in qualification - Held, not affecting selection process - Requisition of fresh advertisement otherwise - Setting aside appointment on grounds that candidate did not possess changed qualification - Not proper.
