Andhra Pradesh Private Engineering College Management Association v Government of Andhra Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

28 August 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, D.P. Wadhwa

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 2286; (2000) 10 SCC 565

Case Digest

Subject: Education

Summary: Education - Administrative - Reservation for NRI - Claim of admission of non-NRIs - Petitioner was Association of private colleges - Petitioner prayed to grant of NRI/foreign quota for private engineering colleges in State at 5% with liberty to admit others within quota in case NRI/foreign students were not available - Hence instant petition.

Held, in counter-affidavit, it is stated by State that orders made in related case were modified by Court on 14-5-1993 when it was clarified that it shall be open to management of private colleges to admit NRI students to extent of 5% of total intake on a fee structure for NRIs to be fixed by a committee. Subsequently, on 9-8-1996, another order came to be made by Court, which confirmed directions earlier issued on 14-5-1993 and also provided that in event seats reserved for NRI candidates in 5% quota are not filled up, non-NRI candidates could be admitted against that quota. State Govt., subsequently, issued orders dt.24-12-1997, permitting management to fill up 5% seats under NRI quota and also to take non-NRI students in case that quota was not filled up by NRI students. These directions were, however, made subject to final orders to be made by Court in pending cases. Directions have been issued by Court on year-to-year basis regarding 5% seats of NRI quota. Govt. has also provided criteria for admission of non-NRIs against unfilled 5% seats of NRIs and fee structure regarding these seats. Court direct that State shall allow 5% NRI quota in private engineering colleges in State to be filled up in manner earlier directed by Court and to permit management of private engineering colleges to fill up unfilled NRI quota, at its own discretion, subject, however, to criteria of merit, qualification and fee structure as prescribed by Govt., till main matter is decided by this Court in pending cases. Petition dismissed.
