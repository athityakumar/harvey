U.P.S.E.B v Atma Steels and Others

Supreme Court of India

20 January 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, S.P. Kurdukar

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 443; (1998) 2 SCC 597; AIR 1998 SC 846; 1998 (1) CLT 126; JT 1998 (1) SC 313; 1998(1) SCALE 260; [1998] 1 S.C.R. 228; 1998 (2) SLT 477; 1998 (1) Supreme 383

Case Digest

Subject: Electricity & Energy; Land & Property

Keywords: Ex-Parte

Summary: Indian Electricity Act, 1910, s. 26(7) - Electricity meter - Demand raised on ground that electricity supplied not properly recorded due to faulty (PT) - Application against - Jurisdiction of - Held, Chief Electrical Inspector has jurisdiction to decide application - Appellate jurisdiction against order of electrical inspector lies to State Govt. - Court not ordinarily indicate who in Govt. may have jurisdiction.
