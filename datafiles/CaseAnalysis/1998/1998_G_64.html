State of Punjab v Gurdas Singh

Supreme Court of India

31 March 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, S.P. Kurdukar, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 2103; (1998) 4 SCC 92; (1998) SCC (L&S) 1004; AIR 1998 SC 1661; 1998 (1) CLR 1199; 1998 (79) FLR 317; JT 1998 (2) SC 693; 1998 LabIC 1401; 1998 (2) LLJ 324; 1998 (3) LLN 94; 1998(2) SCALE 570; 1998 (4) SLT 225; 1998 (3) Supreme 418

Case Digest

Subject: Administrative; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 309 and 311 - Punjab Civil Services (Premature Retirement) Rules, 1975, r. 3(1)(b) - Administrative Tribunals Act, 1985 - Service Rules - Authorities are required to consider whole record of service in case of premature retirement in public interest - Consideration of adverse entries there for - Held, adverse entry prior to earning of promotion and uncommunicated adverse entries can form basis for premature retirement - Appeal allowed.
