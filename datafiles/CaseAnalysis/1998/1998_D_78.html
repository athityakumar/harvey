Union of India and Others v Dr. Sudha Salhan Smt

Supreme Court of India

07 January 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Saghir Ahmad, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1716; (1998) 3 SCC 394; (1998) SCC (L&S) 884; AIR 1998 SC 1094; 1998 (2) CLR 554; JT 1998 (1) SC 622; 1998 LabIC 957; [1998] 1 S.C.R. 28; 1998 (2) SLJ 265

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16 - Promotion - Sealed cover procedure - Adoption of - Neither under suspension nor any departmental proceeding pending against respondent - Held, as on date of consideration of an employee for promotion no such pendency or suspension - Seal cover procedure is inapplicable.
