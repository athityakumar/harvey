Welfare Association of Absorbed Central Government Employees v Arvind Verma and Others

Supreme Court of India

01 May 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Venkataswami, S. Saghir Ahmad, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 607; (1999) 9 SCC 58; (1999) SCC (L&S) 632; AIR 1998 SC 2862; 1998 (33) ALR 497; JT 1998 (3) SC 644; 1998 (4) RCR(Criminal) 704; 1998(3) SCALE 391; 1999 (1) SLJ 26; 1998 (2) SLR 733; 1998 (4) Supreme 417

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16 - Contempt of Courts Act, 1971, s. 2 - Pension to Govt. servants absorbed in public sector undertakings - Restoration of one third portion of commuted pension directed in precedent case (Govt. failed to give effect to) - With attendant benefits as given to Central Govt. employees - Whether failure to give effect to judgment due to genuine doubt about its interpretation amounts to contempt of court? - Held, not contempt - Petition disposed of.
