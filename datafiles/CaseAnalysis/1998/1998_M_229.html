Punjab Urban Planning and Development Authority v Shiv Saraswati Iron and Steel Re-Rolling Mills

Supreme Court of India

24 March 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Venkataswami, A.P. Misra

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1571; (1998) 4 SCC 539; AIR 1998 SC 2352; 1998 (4) AWC 883; 1998 (2) CLT 29; JT 1998 (2) SC 587; 1998(2) SCALE 451; 1998 (3) SLT 696; 1998 (3) Supreme 269

Case Digest

Subject: Contract & Commercial

Summary: Contract & Commercial - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 136 - Appeal - Substantial question of law - Condition in agreement for re-rolling of M S bars out of rails and blooms, as to supply of steel weight to weight - Non-production of document as to transaction and non-explanation of relevant conditions including business language 'weight to weight' - Held, no question of law arise for consideration - Appeal dismissed.
