State of Bihar and Others v Suprabhat Steel Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 November 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, S.P. Bharucha, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1301; (1999) 1 SCC 31; AIR 1999 SC 303; JT 1998 (8) SC 2; 1998(6) SCALE 168; 1999 (1) SCJ 241; 1998 (9) SLT 304; 1999 (112) STC 258; [1998] Supp2 S.C.R. 699; 1998 (8) Supreme 564

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Summary: Bihar Finance Act, 1981, s. 7 - Industrial Incentive Policy of 1993, cl. 10.4(i)(b) - Issuance of notification - Power of state Govt. - Notification D/-04-04-1994 found to be repugnant to Industrial Policy declared in Govt. resolution - Striking down to extent by High Court - Held, not illegal - Old industrial units are entitled to facility of sales tax exemption even if their production started prior to 01-04-1993 but whose investments on plant and machinery do not exceed Rs. 15 crores on 01-04-1993 in terms of Industrial Incentive Policy.
