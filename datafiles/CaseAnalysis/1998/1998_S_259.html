Rashtriya Ispat Nigam Limited v State of Andhra Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

18 February 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Jagannadha Rao, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1471; (1998) 8 SCC 439; AIR 1999 SC 1487; JT 1998 (9) SC 435; 1999 (2) SLT 498; 1998 (109) STC 425

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Summary: Andhra Pradesh General Sales Tax Act, 1957, s. 2(n) - Whether 'Sale' or not? - Contract for construction of building - Contract between parties that custody and control of goods shall remain with public works department - Value of quantity of materials and stores so supplied was specified at a rate and got set off or deducted from any sum due or to become due thereafter to contractor - Held, it amounts to sale which is liable to tax.
