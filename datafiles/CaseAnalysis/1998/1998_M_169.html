Ammonia Supplies Corporation Private Limited v Modern Plastic Containers Private Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 September 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Misra, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 912; (1998) 7 SCC 105; AIR 1998 SC 3153; 1998 (4) CLT 46; [1998] 94 Comp Cas 310; 1998 (4) CompLJ 211; JT 1998 (6) SC 116; 1998(5) SCALE 147; 1998 (7) SLT 369; [1998] Supp1 S.C.R. 413; 1998 (7) Supreme 191

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Corporate; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Companies Act, 1956, ss. 155 and 446 - Rectification of Register of members - Jurisdiction of Company Court - Held, is summary in nature u/s. 155 - Civil Court's jurisdiction hence impliedly barred where it has been invested with exclusive jurisdiction of Company Court.
