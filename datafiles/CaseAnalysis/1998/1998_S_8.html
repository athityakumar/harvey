D. M. Deshpande and Others v Janardhan Kashinath Kadam (Dead) By Lrs. and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 November 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar, A.P. Misra

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1347; (1998) 8 SCC 315; AIR 1999 SC 1464; 1999 (2) Bom.C.R. 499; JT 1998 (8) SC 39; JT 1998 (8) SC 39B; 1998(6) SCALE 194; 1998 (9) SLT 355; [1998] Supp2 S.C.R. 669; 1998 (8) Supreme 473

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 115, O. 21, r. 36 - Revision - Powers of High Court - Execution proceedings - Objections - By respondent on plea of tenancy - Neither particulars thereof were given nor said plea was taken before - Rejection of objections by executing Court and appellate Court by concurrent findings - Held, Order of revisional court interfering with said findings without there being any factual basis - Not proper.
