N. Ammad v The Manager, Emjay High School & Ors

Supreme Court of India

07 September 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 764; (1998) 6 SCC 674; AIR 1999 SC 50; 1999 (1) CLR 322; JT 1998 (6) SC 221; 1999 (2) SLJ 296; 1998 (Supp) MLJ(SC) 43; [1998] Supp1 S.C.R. 453

Case Digest

Subject: Education

Summary: Kerala Education Act 1959, ss.2(5) and 36 ; Kerala Education Rules 1959, r.44(1) ; Constitution of India, art.30(1)- Minority school - Claim for protection and appointment of Headmaster was in dispute - Held, it cannot be said that the school can claim protection only after declaration of school as minority school by Govt. - Further, right of management to choose and appoint any qualified person is protected by art. 30 (1) of Constitution.
