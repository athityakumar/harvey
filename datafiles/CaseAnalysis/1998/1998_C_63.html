Union of India and Another v Chotelal and Others

Supreme Court of India

08 December 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 2092; (1999) SCC (L&S) 332; (1999) 1 SCC 554; AIR 1999 SC 376; 1999 (1) CLR 516; JT 1998 (8) SC 497; 1999 (1) LLN 685; 1999 (2) SLJ 15; 1998 (10) SLT 177; [1998] Supp3 S.C.R. 449

Case Digest

Subject: Administrative; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 311 - Administrative Tribunals Act, 1985, s. 14 - Civil post - Washermen appointed to wash clothes of cadets in National Defence Academy - Jurisdiction of Central Administrative Tribunal over them - Held, not a Civil Post - Payment to them neither from Consolidated Fund of India nor under control of Ministry of Defence - No jurisdiction stands.
