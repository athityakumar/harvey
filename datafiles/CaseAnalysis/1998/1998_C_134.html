B. A. Balasubramaniam and Bros. Company v Commissioner of Income Tax

Supreme Court of India

22 January 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, S.P. Kurdukar

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1897; (1999) 9 SCC 135; AIR 1999 SC 2869; 1999 (157) CTR 556; [1999] 236 ITR 977; [2001] 116 TAXMAN 842

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income-Tax Act, 1961, s. 271(c) - Penalty on concealed income - More then 20% difference between income assessed and income returned - Said explanation became applicable - Failure of assessee to discharge onus as contemplated in explanation - Held, imposition of penalty, justified.
