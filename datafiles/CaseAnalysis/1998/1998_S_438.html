Messrs Labha Ram and Sons and Others, Messrs v State of Punjab and Others

Supreme Court of India

30 April 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, M.M. Punchhi, D.P. Wadhwa

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 721; (1998) 5 SCC 207; AIR 1998 SC 2086; JT 1998 (3) SC 636; 1998(3) SCALE 367; [1998] 3 S.C.R. 112; 1998 (4) SLT 809; 1998 (4) Supreme 327

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Service; Agriculture & Agri. Products

Summary: Punjab Agricultural Produce Markets Act, 1961; Constitution of India, arts.19(1)(g) and 14 ; Punjab New Mandi Township (Development and Regulation) Act, 1960, ss.3 and 4 - Establishment of new market - Allotment of plots/shops - Whether preferential treatment should be given to existing traders - Held, the answer in the affirmative.
