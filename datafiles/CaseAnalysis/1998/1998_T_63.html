State of Tamil Nadu v T. N. Registration Department Ministerial Service Association

Supreme Court of India

29 July 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Saghir Ahmad, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 2285; (2001) 10 SCC 473

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Tamil Nadu Pension Rules, 1978

Summary: Service - Tamil Nadu Pension Rules, 1978,r.2 - Temporary service - Claim of pension - Eligibility - Post of section writer held by respondent was brought under graded pay from 1-10-1970 - Regarding Pensionary benefit of respondent, State opined that prior to 1-10-1970, respondent was paid fixed salary every month and period from 28-6-1950 to 2-12-1963 cannot be counted for pension as respondent was already paid month’s pay for every completed year of service - Tribunal passed order that respondent was entitled to count period from 28-6-1950 till 3-12-1962 as period for benefit of pension under Rules - Hence instant appeal.

Held, Tribunal found that r. 2 of Rules categorically provides that Rules were applied to all Govt. servants appointed to service and posts in connection with affairs of State which are borne by pensionable establishments, whether temporary or permanent. Even though respondent had rendered temporary service for period from 1950 to 1963, he would be entitled to count that period for purpose of pension. Tribunal also found that dt.1-10-1970 on which post of Section Writer was brought under graded pay has no nexus with benefit of pension payable to those Section writers who had, prior to that date, rendered temporary service. It was held that on basis of that date, employees could not be categorised into two classes. State contended that post of Section Writer was not a pensionable post and it became a pensionable post only from 1-10-1970 and, therefore, entire period of service rendered by respondent on this post prior to 1-10-1970 would have to be excluded. This was not contention raised before Tribunal nor has any rule to that effect been shown to Court that post of Section Writer was a non-pensionable post up to 1-10-1970. Appeal dismissed.

Ratio - Pensionary benefits of retired employees cannot be denied by Department merely on technical grounds so as to deny the benefit to employee.
