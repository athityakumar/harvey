Kala and Another v Madho Parshad Vaidya

Supreme Court of India

27 August 1998

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, A.S. Anand

Where Reported

1998 Indlaw SC 1172; (1998) 6 SCC 573; AIR 1998 SC 2773; JT 1998 (6) SC 187; 1998 (2) RCR(Rent) 279; 1998(5) SCALE 90; 1998 (7) SLT 244; [1998] Supp1 S.C.R. 149; 1998 (7) Supreme 113

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Himachal Pradesh Urban Rent Control Act, 1987, s. 14(2)(ii) - Suit for eviction on ground of subletting - Alleged sub-tenant found to be assisting tenant to manage her business, shop, her house and orchard after death of her husband - Nature of business change from selling of cigarettes etc to sanitary wares, if sufficient ground to infer sub-letting - And eviction if can be ordered - Held, no ground, onus not discharged by landlord - Eviction cannot be ordered - Appeal allowed.
