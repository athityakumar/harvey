Medical Council of India v Dr. M.G.R. Educational and Research Institute University and another

Supreme Court of India

11 February 2015

Case Analysis

Bench

Madan B. Lokur, Uday Umesh Lalit

Where Reported

2015 Indlaw SC 128; (2015) 4 SCC 580; JT 2015 (2) SC 331; 2015(2) SCALE 263; 2016 (1) UPLBEC 577

Case Digest

Subject: Education

Keywords: Medical College, University Grants Commission Act, 1956

Summary: Education - University Grants Commission Act, 1956,s.3 - Grant of renewal - Validity of admission - Institute was declared as Deemed to be University by notification issued by Ministry of Human Resource Development of Govt. of India-MHRD - Medical Council of India-MCI wrote to College for renewal of permission for admission of 2nd batch of students in academic year 2009-10 - MHRD passed order rejecting recommendations of University Grant Commission-UGC made to bring College in ambit of Institute - Single Judge quashed order passed by MHRD and direction was given to MHRD to issue notification u/s.3 of the UGC Act bringing College under ambit of Institute - DB set aside direction given by Single Judge and remanded entire matter for reconsideration by MHRD - MHRD passed order that College was constituent unit under ambit of Institute for two academic years that was 2008-09 and 2009-10 - Hence, instant Appeal - Whether admissions made in academic year 2009-10 by Institute, in 'College' for 2nd batch of MBBS course is valid.

Held, there is nothing to suggest that at relevant time College was within ambit of the Institute. In fact it is only when MHRD passed an order that recognition was given to Institute but limited only to conducting MBBS course commencing from 2008-09 and 2009-10 and thereafter in 2014-15. Though MCI and Ministry of Health & Family Welfare or -MH & FW accorded recognition and approval to admissions made by Institute in academic year 2008-09, no recognition or approval was accorded to admissions made by Institute to 2nd batch of students in the academic year 2009-10. However, MHRD went step ahead and accepted report of Expert Committee set up by it and brought College within ambit of Institute from 2009-10 onwards. Fact situation is somewhat different inasmuch as the MHRD has now brought the College within ambit of Institute not only for academic year 2008-09 (about which there is no dispute) but also for admissions made to 2nd batch in the academic year 2009-10. Admissions made were completely unauthorized at the relevant time but have now been granted approval and recognition as result of certain subsequent events which have been taken into consideration by MHRD. Thus, student admitted by Institution in academic year 2009-10 should be required to once again undergo final examination. Appeals disposed of.
