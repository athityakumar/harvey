Government Mohindra Instt. of Information Technology and another v All India Council for Technical Education and others

Supreme Court of India

10 August 2015

Case Analysis

Bench

Anil R. Dave, Kurian Joseph

Where Reported

2015 Indlaw SC 564; JT 2015 (7) SC 317; 2015(9) SCALE 14; 2016 (1) SLR 95

Case Digest

Subject: Education

Summary: Education - Initiation of new course - Deficiencies - Petitioner-college submitted application to respondent no.1 for setting up new technical institute for running MCA course, but the same was rejected by respondent no. 1 - On appeal, Standing Appellate Committee also rejected application of petitioner-college for initiation of new course - Hence, instant petition - Whether relief as prayed could be granted to petitioner-college.

Held, by not placing the letter of petitioner-college dt.24-4-2015 before Standing Appellate committee which had convened its meeting on 27-4-2015, the principles of natural justice had been violated and hence, the final order dt.30-4-2015 passed by Standing Appellate Committee of respondent no. 1 is liable to be quashed and set aside. Hence, letters of rejection dt.7-4-2015 and 30-4-2015 of respondent no. 1 are quashed and set aside. Further, in view of fact that deficiencies were removed, respondent no. 1 is directed to reconsider the case of petitioner-college at the earliest. Petition allowed.
