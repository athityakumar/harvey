Holostick India Limited v Commissioner of Central Excise, Noida

Supreme Court of India

30 March 2015

Case Analysis

Bench

R.F. Nariman, A.K. Sikri

Where Reported

2015 Indlaw SC 429; (2015) 7 SCC 401; 2015(5) SCALE 197; [2015] 4 S.C.R. 576

Case Digest

Subject: Excise

Summary: Excise - Appellant was engaged in manufacturing of security holograms and was issued with show cause notice wherein Department sought to classify security hologram under Tariff entry 39.19 of Central Excise Tariff 1999-2000 and on contrary, appellant contended that same ought to be classified under Tariff entry 49.01 - Commissioner agreed with contention of Department and appeal before Appellate Tribunal by appellant was also dismissed - Hence, instant appeal - Whether relevant tariff entry would be 39.19 or 49.01.

Held, in accordance with Entry 49 Note 2, security hologram part of the product in question is primary and the self adhesive part only incidental insofar as the user of the said goods is concerned. Hence, appeals are allowed and as appellant already paid duty pending appeals, the same needs to be refunded. Order accordingly.
