Pathubha Govindji Rathod and another v State of Gujarat

Supreme Court of India

21 January 2015

Case Analysis

Bench

Prafulla C. Pant, Dipak Misra

Where Reported

2015 Indlaw SC 45; (2015) 4 SCC 363; AIR 2015 SC 1118; 2015 CRLJ 1413; 2015 (2) JCC 848; 2015 (2) Law Herald (P&H) 1578; 2015 (1) MLJ(Crl) 489; 2015 (1) RCR(Criminal) 1014; 2015(1) SCALE 563; [2015] 1 S.C.R. 491

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 149, 300, 302, 304, 307, 326, 325, 324 - Murder - Plea of right of private defence - Sustainability - Appellants accused allegedly murdered the deceased - Trial Court convicted and sentenced appellants u/ss. 302, 307, 326, 325, 324 r/w s. 149 IPC - Appellants filed appeal before HC - HC partly allowed appeal - Hence, instant appeal.

Held, considering the number of persons involved in the incident it can be safely said that it is a case of free fight between two groups of people. The injured appellant no. 1 who was given deep knife blow on his back (scapular region) has retorted by using licensed firearm, and killed one of his rivals in the same incident. Accused/appellant has taken plea of private defence right from beginning of the trial. From the judgment of the Trial Court also, it is clear that the plea of private defence was taken by the appellant no.1. Considering the facts and circumstances of the present case and evidence on record, it is evident that accused/appellant no.1 who suffered knife injury in the incident has caused death of one of the deceased by firing several shots thereby exceeding right of private defence. Injuries suffered by both the sides are on record. It is a case of culpable homicide not amounting to murder covered under Exception 2 of s. 300 of IPC. Hence, conviction and sentence recorded against accused/appellant no.1 u/s. 302 r/w s. 149 IPC is set aside. Instead appellant no.1 is convicted and sentenced u/s. 304 Part-I IPC. However, conviction and sentence recorded against appellant no. 2 u/s. 304 Part I r/w s. 149 IPC, does not require any interference. Appeal disposed of.

Ratio - In the cases of free fights accused are to be fastened with individual liability taking into consideration the specific role assigned to each one of them, and normally right of private defence is not available in such cases unless circumstances in a given case warrant so.
