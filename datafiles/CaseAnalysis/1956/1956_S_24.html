S. Swamirathnam and Others v State of Madras

Supreme Court of India

14 September 1956

Case Analysis

Bench

Syed Jaffer Imam, Natwarlal Harilal Bhagwati, P. Govinda Menon

Where Reported

1956 Indlaw SC 125; AIR 1957 SC 340; 1957 CRLJ 422

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Procedure Code, 1898 - Indian Evidence Act, 1872 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 420 and 120-B - Conspiracy to cheat public - Appellants convicted by High Court on appeal by Govt. - Whether offence of conspiracy proved against appellants? - Contended misjoinder of charges as several conspiracies tried together at one trial - Held, circumstances clearly connects appellants with offences - Case of prosecution concerning existence of conspiracy as charged to cheat members of public, had been proved - No misjoinder of charges as instances of cheating were in pursuance of main conspiracy and hence, same transaction - No merit in appeal - Appeal dismissed.
