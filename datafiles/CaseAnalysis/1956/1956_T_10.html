Shambu Nath Mehra v State of Ajmer

Supreme Court of India

12 March 1956

Case Analysis

Bench

Vivian Bose, N. Chandrasekhara Aiyar

Where Reported

1956 Indlaw SC 81; AIR 1956 SC 404; 1956 CRLJ 794; 1956 (2) MLJ(SC) 1; 1956 SCJ 429; [1956] 1 S.C.R. 199

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Indirect Tax; Service; Socio-Economic

Summary: Criminal - Indian Evidence Act, 1872, s. 106 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 420 - Prevention of Corruption Act, 1947, s. 5 - Railways Act - Appellant did not pay fare for travelling - Acquitted by Sessions Judge and, on appeal by Govt. to Judicial Commr., case sent for retrial by Special Judge - Held, no conviction can be based on the material produced - Retrial order set-aside and acquittal order of Sessions Judge restored.
