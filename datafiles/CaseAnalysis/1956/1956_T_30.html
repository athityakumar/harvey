Haripada Dey v The State Of West Bengaland Another

Supreme Court of India

05 September 1956

Case Analysis

Bench

Natwarlal Harilal Bhagwati, Syed Jaffer Imam, P. Govinda Menon

Where Reported

1956 Indlaw SC 19; AIR 1956 SC 757; 1957 ALJ 70; 1956 (2) MLJ(SC) 65; 1956 SCJ 701; [1956] 1 S.C.R. 639

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860 - Constitution of India, 1950 art. 134 and 136 - Dishonestly received stolen property - Appellant convicted by Trial Court and conviction affirmed by High Court - HC granted leave to appeal on question of fact - Held, HC wrong in granting leave to appeal solely on point of fact - No justification in going behind concurrent findings of fact reached by courts below - No ground for interference - Appeal dismissed.
