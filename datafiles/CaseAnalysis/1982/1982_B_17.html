Kalipada Das and Others v Bimal Krishna Sen Gupta (Dead) By L.Rs

Supreme Court of India

25 October 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, Baharul Islam

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 276; (1983) 1 SCC 14; AIR 1983 SC 876; AIR 1996 SCW 625

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Advocates & Judges - Practice & Procedure - Whether appellant, tenants who failed to supply paper-books within prescribed time should be visited with such serious consequences of a penal nature as described by High Court itself? - Supplying paper book is a procedural step in aid of justice, and not substantive justice itself - Omission or lapse arising out at non-compliance with court's order was not of such a serious gravity as to close door of Court to appellants by dismissing appeal - Therefore, a procedural step which facilitates hearing of appeal cannot impede access to justice - Held, setting aside order of dismissal of Appeal No. 14/79 d/-19-06-1979 and remittal of case to HC and restoration of appeal to file of HC - Appeal allowed.
