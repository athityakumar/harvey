Commissioner of Income Tax, West Bengal Iii v Pigot Chapman and Company

Supreme Court of India

13 April 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

V.D. Tulzapurkar, R.S. Pathak, Amarendra Nath Sen

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 160; (1982) SCC (Tax) 97; (1982) 2 SCC 330; AIR 1982 SC 1085; 1982 (30) CTR 375; [1982] 135 ITR 620; 1982(1) SCALE 371; 1982 TAXLR 1118; [1982] 9 TAXMAN 7; 1982 UJ 359

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Reconstitution

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 25(4) - Partnership Act, 1932, ss. 40, 43 and 44 - Income Tax -Dissolution of old firm and constitution of new firm - Determination of - Held, to be determined on basis of documents executed by and between partners - Appeal Dismissed.
