Dhananjoy Das v District Magistrate and Another

Supreme Court of India

16 August 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

R.B. Misra, V.D. Tulzapurkar

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 39; (1982) SCC (Cr) 488; (1982) 2 SCC 521; AIR 1982 SC 1315; 1982 CRLJ 1779; [1983] 1 S.C.R. 122

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 22(5) - National Security Act, 1980, s. 3 - Appeal against detention u/s. 3 of Act - Contention that grounds of detention were vague and facts narrated in grounds related to law and order situation and not to public order - Held, detention is valid as it is upto court to determine whether a particular ground is vague going on facts and circumstances of each case - Appeal dismissed.
