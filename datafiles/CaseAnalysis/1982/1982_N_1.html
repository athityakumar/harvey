Roop Lal Sathi v Nachhattar Singh

Supreme Court of India

02 November 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, D.A. Desai

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 105; (1982) 3 SCC 487; AIR 1982 SC 1559; 1982(2) SCALE 976; [1983] 1 S.C.R. 702; 1983 UJ 101

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Election; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Allotment Of Symbols, Reserved Symbol, Election Symbols Order 1968

Summary: Representation of the People Act, 1951, ss. 123(7) and 100(1)(d)(iv) - CPC, 1908, O. 6, r. 16 - Election Symbols (Reservation and Allotment) Order, 1968, Paras 13 and 18 - (A) Breach of provisions of 1968 Order - Whether change of allotment of symbols is covered u/s.100(1)(d)(iv) - (B) Striking out of pleadings - Direction for deletion of certain paras of petition as non-disclosure of material facts sufficient to give rise to cause of action - Held, it falls within purview of s.100(1)(d)(iv) - Held, HC order cannot be sustained on the terms of O. 6, r. 16 of CPC, 1908 - Appeal Allowed.
