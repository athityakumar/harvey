I. J. Divakar and Others v Government of Andhra Pradesh and Another

Supreme Court of India

21 October 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 91; (1982) 3 SCC 341; (1983) SCC (L&S) 14; AIR 1982 SC 1555; 1982 (45) FLR 457; 1982 LabIC 1793; 1983 (1) LLJ 217; 1983 (1) LLN 327; 1982(2) SCALE 938; 1982 (3) SLR 475; 1982 UJ 835

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution, art.320(3), Proviso - A.P. State and Subordinate Service Rules, 1966, r. 10(a)(i)(1) - Service Rules - Applicant for Advertised post - Challenged G.O. withdrawing a the post from the purview of State Public Service Commission subsequent to its advertisement - Another G. O. regularising services of temporary servants claimed to be violative of Service Rules - Held, a candidate who has applied for a post gets no right to post applied for - No locus standi - Another G. O. regularising services held was also not violative - Candidate however given equitable relief - Appeal Allowed.
