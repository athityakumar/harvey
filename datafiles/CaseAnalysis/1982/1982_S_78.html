Harcharan v State of Haryana

Supreme Court of India

16 November 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 221; (1982) 3 SCC 408; AIR 1983 SC 43; 1983 (9) ALR 66; 1982(2) SCALE 1075; 1982 UJ 832

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - CPC, 1908, O. 6, r. 17, O.41, r. 3 and s. 151 - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 54 - Appeal regarding land acquisition - During the pendency of the appeal the appellant moved an application under O.6, r.17 r/w O.41 r.3 and s.51 of the Code for amendment of the memorandum of appeal seeking higher compensation on the allegation that the acquired land had the potentiality of a building site - Rejection of application - Determination of legality of rejection of application for amendment of memo of appeal merely on ground of delay - Held, illegal - Appeal allowed.
