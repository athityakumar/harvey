Ramji Lal and Another v State of Haryana

Supreme Court of India

06 December 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 216; (1983) SCC (Cr) 189; (1983) 1 SCC 368; 1983 CRLR 118; 1982(2) SCALE 1362C

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 34, 323 and 325 - Probation of offenders - Convicted u/s. 325 r/w. 34 - All offences for which appellants are convicted are compoundable and composition can be entered into with permission of court - Conviction set aside - Appeal allowed.
