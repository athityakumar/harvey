Rama Kant Misra v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 October 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 140; (1982) 3 SCC 346; (1983) SCC (L&S) 26; AIR 1982 SC 1552; 1982 ALJ 1341; 1982 (45) FLR 432; 1982 LabIC 1790; 1982 (2) LLJ 472; 1983 (1) LLN 1; 1982(2) SCALE 942; [1983] 1 S.C.R. 648; 1982 (2) SLJ 532; 1983 (1) SLR 135; 1982 UJ 862

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 11 A - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 136 - Industrial dispute - Jurisdiction u/s. 11A of Act - Exercise of, by Labour Court - Appeal u/art. 136 of Constitution - Supreme Court to examine correctness of exercise - Dismissal of employee for use of indiscreet language disclosing threatening posture - Award - Dismissal upheld and award liable to be set aside - Appeal allowed.
