Thrity Hoshie Dolikuka v Hoshiam Shavaksha Dolikuka B

Supreme Court of India

04 August 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

Amarendra Nath Sen, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 121; (1982) SCC (Cr) 505; (1982) 2 SCC 544; AIR 1982 SC 1276; 1982 (2) DMC 288; 1982(1) SCALE 608; [1983] 1 S.C.R. 49

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Women & Children

Keywords: Marriage & Divorce, Minority, Adoption & Guardianship

Summary: Guardians and Wards Act, 1890, ss. 7 and 17 - Parsi Marriage and Divorce Act, 1936, s. 49 - Custody of minor - (A) Relevant consideration in question as custody is welfare of child - Bitter squabbles among husband and wife - (B) Whether s. 49 of the 1936 Act confers some duty on court - Held, daughter aged 11 years directed to be kept in Boarding School under custody of mother - No duty cast on Court to interview minor for ascertaning the wishes of minor - Appeal Allowed.
