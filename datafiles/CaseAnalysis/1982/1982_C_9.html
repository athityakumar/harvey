Commissioner Of Income Tax, Delhi v Delhi Safe Deposit Co. Ltd

Supreme Court of India

12 January 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 149; (1982) 1 SCC 364; AIR 1982 SC 757; 1982 (26) CTR 411; [1982] 133 ITR 756; 1982(1) SCALE 6; [1982] 3 S.C.R. 1; 1982 UJ 139

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Agency

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 37 - Whether the assessee was entitled to any allowance on account of the share of loss made good by it to the managed company? - The assessee incurred the expenditure to avoid any adverse effect on its reputation, to protect the managing agency which was an income earning apparatus - The expenditure incurred exclusively for the purpose of the business is allowed as deduction - Held, expenditure in question allowed as deduction - Appeal dismissed.
