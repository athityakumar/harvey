Heramba Brahma and Another v State of Assam

Supreme Court of India

04 November 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 26; (1982) 3 SCC 351; (1983) SCC (Cr) 40; AIR 1982 SC 1595; 1983 BBCJ 6; 1983 (1) Crimes 150; 1983 CRLJ 149; 1982 CRLR 502; 1982(2) SCALE 992; 1983 UJ 10

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Evidence Act, 1872, s. 24 - Extra judicial confession - Credibility of - Confession in jail to an under trial - High Court treating as extra-judicial confession - Neither ascertainment of words nor credential of evidence examined - Validity - Held, unworthy of belief - Cannot be used to corroborate other evidence.
