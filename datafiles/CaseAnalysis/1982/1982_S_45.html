Prem Thakur v State Of Punjab

Supreme Court of India

17 November 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 144; (1982) 3 SCC 462; (1983) SCC (Cr) 88; AIR 1983 SC 61; 1983 (1) Crimes 141; 1983 CRLJ 155; 1982 CrLR(SC) 499; 1982(2) SCALE 1057; [1983] 1 S.C.R. 822; 1983 UJ 3

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Indian Penal Code, 1860 - Whether circumstances are of such a nature as to be capable of supporting the exclusive hypothesis that the accused is guilty of the crime- Murder and robbery - Held, fact that theft was the motive for the murder cannot connect the accused with the crime -Decision reversed.
