State of Uttar Pradesh v Mohd. Sharif (Dead) Through L. Rs

Supreme Court of India

13 January 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

V.D. Tulzapurkar, Amarendra Nath Sen

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 77; (1982) SCC (L&S) 253; (1982) 2 SCC 376; AIR 1982 SC 937; 1982 ALJ 452; 1982 (45) FLR 289; 1982 LabIC 1234; 1982 (2) LLJ 180; 1982 (2) LLN 408; 1982 (2) SLJ 259; 1982 (2) SLR 265

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution, arts. 226 and 311 - Police Act, 1861, s. 7 - Head Constable - Departmental inquiry - Absence of necessary particulars in the charge-sheet - Statements of witnesses recorded in preliminary inquiry not furnished - Legality of dismissal Order - Held, delinquent was denied reasonable opportunity to defend himself - Order illegal - Appeal Dismissed.
