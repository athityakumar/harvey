Om Prakash Gupta etc. v Dig Vijendrapal Gupta etc.

Supreme Court of India

05 March 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

R.B. Misra, A.D. Koshal, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 200; (1982) 2 SCC 61; AIR 1982 SC 1230B; 1982 ALJ 376; 1982 (8) ALR 242; 1982 (2) RCJ 93; 1982 (2) RCR(Rent) 383; 1982 (1) RentLR 355; 1982(1) SCALE 153; [1982] 3 S.C.R. 491

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Rent Control - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 115 - Uttar Pradesh Urban Buildings (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972, ss. 2(2) and 39 - Benefit u/s. 39 - Entitlement - Whether construction deemed completed on reporting of completion to authorities or on first date of assessment? - Held, the date of occupation would be taken to be the date of completion of the construction only when there is no report or record of the completion of the construction or no assessment, thereof - If there is an assessment, as in the present case it is, it will be the date of the first assessment which will be deemed to be the date of completion of the construction and in that view of the matter the building had not become more than ten years' old on the date when the revision came to be decided by the High Court, and therefore, there was no question of giving the benefit of s. 39 of the Act to the appellant - Appeal dismissed.
