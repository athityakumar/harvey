Mool Chand Yadav and Another v Raza Buland Sugar Company Limited, Rampur and Others

Supreme Court of India

15 October 1982

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1982 Indlaw SC 230; (1982) 3 SCC 484; 1983 (9) ALR 93; 1983 (9) ALR 403; 1982(2) SCALE 947; 1982 UJ 831

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - In a suit filed by respondents an injunction was granted restraining present appellants or their officers from occupying Hari Bhawan against which an appeal was filed by present appellants which is pending - Subsequently, an application was made alleging flouting of Court's order - Application resulted in order d/-06-08-1982, that defendant had to vacate disputed room within one month - An appeal from this order was preferred being F.A.F.O. No. Nil of 1982 - Whether Hari Bhawan is a property which has vested in Corporation and whether Corporation is entitled to occupy it and whether a part of it can be used by General Manager of Corporation? - Held, if orders are challenged and appeals are pending, one cannot permit a swinging pendulum continuously taking place during pendency of appeal - Judicial approaches requires that during pendency of appeal operation of an order having serious civil consequences must be suspended - Court accordingly, suspend operation of order d/-06-08-1982 till disposal of first appeal against that order pending in High Court of Allahabad - Appeal disposed of.
