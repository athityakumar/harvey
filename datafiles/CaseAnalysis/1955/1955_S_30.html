Eradu and Others v State of Hyderabad

Supreme Court of India

01 November 1955

Case Analysis

Bench

Natwarlal Harilal Bhagwati, Bhuvaneshwar Prasad Sinha

Where Reported

1955 Indlaw SC 108; AIR 1956 SC 316; 1956 CRLJ 559

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Procedure Code, 1898, - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 302 - Evidence Act, 1872 - Appellants convicted by trial court and sentenced to life - Sentence confirmed by High Court - Appeal - No direct evidence - Circumstantial evidence - Neither motive stated nor recoveries could be of any help to prosecution - Held, circumstances are not enough to connect appellants with crime and are entitled to be acquitted - Appeals allowed.
