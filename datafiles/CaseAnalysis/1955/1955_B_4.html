Bed Raj v State of Uttar Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

28 September 1955

Case Analysis

Bench

Vivian Bose, Bhuvaneshwar Prasad Sinha, B. Jagannadhadas

Where Reported

1955 Indlaw SC 26; AIR 1955 SC 778; 1955 CRLJ 1642; 1956 (1) MLJ(SC) 28; 1956 SCJ 38; [1955] 2 S.C.R. 583

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Criminal - Practice & Procedure - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 304 - Criminal Procedure Code, 1898, ss. 439 and 423 - Culpable Homicide - Question of a sentence - Appellant killed deceased with a knife while he was unarmed - High Court enhanced sentence without any adequate reason - Held, order of HC be set aside and that of Sessions Court restored, as an Appellate Court should not interfere to the detriment of an accused person except for very strong reasons - Appeal allowed.
