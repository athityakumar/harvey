J.N. Ganatra v Morvi Municipality, Morvi

Supreme Court of India

19 July 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 928; (1996) 9 SCC 495; AIR 1996 SC 2520; 1996 (2) CLR 603; 1996 (74) FLR 2228; JT 1996 (6) SC 661; 1996(5) SCALE 375; 1996 (3) SLJ 46; 1996 (4) SLR 598; [1996] Supp3 S.C.R. 742; 1996 (5) Supreme 544

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Bombay Provincial Municipal Corporation Act, 1949, Gujarat Municipalities Act, 1963

Summary: Service - Gujarat Municipalities Act, 1964, s. 253(1)(a) - Limitation Act, 1908 - Dismissal of employee without following mandatory procedure prescribed by Municipal Rules and not as Act - Whether suit to set aside dismissal is governed by limitation prescribed u/s. 253(1)(a) - Held, not governed - Appeal allowed.
