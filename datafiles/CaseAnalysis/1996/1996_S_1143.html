Jotindra Nath Roy v Surendra Hikram Singh Agarwal & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

09 April 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

Faizan Uddin, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 805; (1996) 4 SCC 403; (1996) SCC (L&S) 960; 1996 (3) AD(SC) 685; AIR 1996 SC 1736; 1996 (2) CLR 607; 1996 (2) CLT 221; 1996 (73) FLR 1394; JT 1996 (4) SC 296; 1996 LabIC 1545; 1996 (2) LLJ 246; 1996 (1) LLN 803; 1996(3) SCALE 499; 1996 (4) Supreme 250; 1996 (2) UJ 259; 1996 (4) UPLBEC 2909

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Payment of Gratuity Act of 1972, ss.1(3) and 2(e), notification dated 16-7-1972 - Employees' Provident Funds and Family Pension Fund Act (1952) - Constitution of India, art.16 - Whether a retired employee of religious & charitable trust is entitled to benefit of pension under scheme - Constitution of India, art.16 - Treating Trust estate as an Establishment.
