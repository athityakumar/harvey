Messrs Fair Air Engineers Private Limited and Another v N.K.Modi

Supreme Court of India

20 August 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2304; (1996) 6 SCC 385; AIR 1997 SC 533; 1996 (6) AllCJ 1238; 1996 (28) ALR 528; 1997 (1) AWC 120; [1997] 88 Comp Cas 663; 1996 (5) CompLJ 63; 1996 CPJ 1; 1996 (4) CTJ 749; JT 1996 (8) SC 253; 1996(6) SCALE 488; [1996] Supp4 S.C.R. 820

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection

Keywords: Code of Civil Procedure 1908, The Special Courts Act, 1979

Summary: Consumer Protection Act, 1986, ss. 3, 10, 16 and 20 - District forum, State Commission and National Commission are judicial authorities for purpose of s. 34 of Arbitration Act - Can said forums proceed with matters in accordance with act? - Held, by virtue of s. 3, said forums could proceed with matters related to provisions of Act rather than relegating parties to arbitration proceedings.
