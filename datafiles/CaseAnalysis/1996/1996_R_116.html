Ram Avtar and Others v Ram Dhani and Others

Supreme Court of India

26 September 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

Faizan Uddin, N.P. Singh

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2497; (1997) 2 SCC 263; 1996 (7) AD(SC) 651; AIR 1997 SC 107; 1996 (4) CLT 171; 1996(7) SCALE 393; 1998 (Supp) RD 581; [1996] Supp7 S.C.R. 45; 1996 (7) Supreme 381

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Family & Personal; Practice & Procedure; Women & Children

Keywords: Succession & Inheritance

Summary: Land & Property - Uttar Pradesh Zamindari Abolition and Land Reforms Abolition Act, 1950 - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 - Person who has been allotted property in lieu of compromise of maintenance is entitled to transfer property even if having limited interest - High Court rejected stand of appellants that as Smt. Phoola got lands by way of maintenance - And s. 11 shall be applicable where holder of sir or khudkasht lands allots lands in lieu of maintenance allowance - HC has rightly held that widow has the right to retain same as Bhumidhar thereof - Appeal dismissed.
