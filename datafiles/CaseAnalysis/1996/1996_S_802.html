Kamta Tiwari v State of Madhya Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

04 September 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

M.K. Mukherjee, S.P. Kurdukar

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2125; (1996) 6 SCC 250; (1996) SCC (Cr) 1298; 1996 (6) AD(SC) 562; AIR 1996 SC 2800; AIR 1996 SCW 3567; 1996 (3) CCR 141; 1996 (3) Crimes 241; 1996 CRLJ 4158; JT 1996 (8) SC 198; 1996(6) SCALE 337; [1996] Supp5 S.C.R. 507; 1996 (6) Supreme 482

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 (as amended by Act No.25 of 2005 & Act No.2 of 2006)

Summary: Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 302 and 376 - Rape and murder - Sentence - Victim aged of 7 years - Strangulated her to death - Throwing body in well and causing disappearance of evidence - Held, is 'rarest of rare' case - Death sentence - No interference.
