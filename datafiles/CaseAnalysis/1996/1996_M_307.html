Senjeevanagar Medical And Healthemployees Co-Operative Housing v Mohd. Abdul Wahab And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

08 February 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, S. Saghir Ahmad, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 789; (1996) 3 SCC 600; 1996 (1) AD(SC) 1098; AIR 1996 SC 3360; JT 1996 (2) SC 173B; 1996 (1) RentLR 326; 1996(2) SCALE 82; [1996] 2 S.C.R. 308; 1996 (2) Supreme 199

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Land and Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 4 - Limitation Act, 1908 - - Neither simultaneous nor immediate Local Publication is required notification under Acquisition Act - Delay of 40 days for same as provided retrospectively by A. P. Validation Act - If consistent with Central Amendment Act of 1984 - Can it be applied - Held, is inconsistent with Central Amendment Act and cannot be applied - Appeal allowednd and Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 4 - Indian Limitation Act, 1908 - Notification under - Neither simultaneous nor immediate Local Publication is required - Rigour of 40 days for same as provided retrospectively by A. P. Validation Act - If consistent with Central Amendment Act of 1984 - Can it be applied - Held, is inconsistent with Central Amendment Act and cannot be applied - Appeal allowed.
