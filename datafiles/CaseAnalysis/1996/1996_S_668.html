State of Haryana and Others v O. P. Gupta and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3382; (1996) 7 SCC 533; (1996) SCC (L&S) 633; 1996 (1) AD(SC) 978; AIR 1996 SC 2936; 1996 (33) ATC 324; 1996 (1) CLR 1004; 1996 (73) FLR 1290; JT 1996 (3) SC 141; 1996 LabIC 991; 1996(1) SCALE 602; [1996] 1 S.C.R. 468; 1996 (2) SLR 466; 1996 (1) Supreme 716; 1996 (1) UJ 339; 1996 (2) UPLBEC 1440

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 309 - Haryana Service of Engineers Class-II P W D Rules, 1970, r. 9 - Employees of public works department - Dispute regarding promotion to higher post - Supreme Court ordered to prepare seniority list - Entitlement to work in promotional posts only after seniority list prepared and promotions made - Not worked even on ad hoc basis - Whether entitled to arrears of salary? - Held, payment of arrears of salary does not arise, when they have not worked in those posts even on ad hoc basis.
