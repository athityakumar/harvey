Smt. Ambey Devi v State Of Bihar & Anr

Supreme Court of India

12 March 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3368; (1996) 9 SCC 84; 1996 (3) AD(SC) 392; AIR 1996 SC 1513; 1996 (2) CLT 84; JT 1996 (3) SC 674; 1997 (1) MLJ(SC) 81; 1996 (1) RentLR 423; 1996(3) SCALE 121; [1996] 3 S.C.R. 303; 1996 (3) Supreme 208; 1996 (2) UJ 146

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Land and Property - Civil Procedure - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 18, 23 and 30 - CPC, 1908, O. 1, r. 10 - (A) ss. 18 and 23 of 1894 Act - Compensation - Enhancement - One of the co-owners of acquired land filing reference in respect of his share only and getting his compensation enhanced - Failure of other co-owners to file reference - Whether they can seek enhancement of compensation of their share on basis of reference by other co-owners - (B) ss. 18 and 30 of 1894 Act - O. 1, r. 10 of CPC - Necessary or proper party - Impleadment, for complete adjudication of claim - procedure prescribed u/ss. 18 and 30 of Land Acquisition Act - Whether inconsistent with procedure prescribed u/O. 1, r. 10 of CPC - Held, (A) They cannot seek enhancement on that basis - (B) Is inconsistent - Appeal dismissed.
