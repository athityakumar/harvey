State Of Haryana And Others v Rajpal Sharma And Others

Supreme Court of India

25 July 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2909; (1996) 5 SCC 273; (1996) SCC (L&S) 1159; 1996 (6) AD(SC) 190; AIR 1997 SC 449; 1996 (2) CLR 933; 1996 (74) FLR 2088; JT 1996 (6) SC 710; 1996 (2) LLJ 804; 1996(5) SCALE 517; 1996 (4) SLR 728; [1996] Supp4 S.C.R. 23; 1996 (6) Supreme 76; 1996 (2) UJ 522; 1996 (4) UPLBEC 2905

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India, art.16, art.39(d); Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, sch.3, item 1 - Pay scale - Teacher - Private aided school in State of Haryana - Held, teachers are entitled to same salary and dearness allowance as is paid to teachers in Government schools
