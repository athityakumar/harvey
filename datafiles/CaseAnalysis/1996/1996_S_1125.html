U. P. State Sugar Corporation v Sumac International Limited

Supreme Court of India

04 December 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar, M.M. Punchhi

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3580; (1997) 1 SCC 568; AIR 1997 SC 1644; AIR 1997 SCW 694; 1997 ALJ 638; 1996 ARBLR 476; 1997 BC 286; 1996 (4) CCC 262; 1997 CLT 195; [1997] 89 Comp Cas 179; 1997 (4) CompLJ 38; JT 1996 (10) SC 709; 1996(8) SCALE 676; [1996] Supp9 S.C.R. 511; 1996 (8) Supreme 530

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure; Corporate

Keywords: Sick Industrial Companies (Special Provisions) Act, 1986

Summary: Arbitration Act, 1940, s. 41(b) - Sick Industrial Companies (Special Provisions) Act, 1986, s. 22 - Injunction - Termination of contract - Unconditional Bank guarantee - Invocation of - Dispute between parties regarding termination of contract - Held, cannot make invocation of bank guarantee fraudulent - Reference against contractor pending under Sick Industrial Companies(Special Provision) Act is not enough to bring case in ambit of 'irretrievable injustice' exception - Grant of injunction against invocation of bank guarantee - Liable to be vacated.
