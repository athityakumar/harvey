U. P. State Road Transport Corporation v Basudeo Chaudhary and Another

Supreme Court of India

27 February 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2633; (1997) 11 SCC 370; (1998) SCC (L&S) 155; AIR 1998 SC 57

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Service - Termination - On the ground of misconduct - Conductor in U.P. State Road Transport Corporation - Legality of - Held, this is a case where petitioner had tried to fabricate record regarding recovery of fare to show that passengers had travelled for a lesser distance from Khalilabad to Gorakhpur although they had actually travelled from Basti to Gorakhpur misconduct that was found established was thus serious in nature and Labour Court has rightly upheld punishment of removal from service that was imposed on petitioner - Appeal allowed.
