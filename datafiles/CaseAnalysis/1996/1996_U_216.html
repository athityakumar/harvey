M. C. Dhingra v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 February 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3296; (1996) 7 SCC 564; (1996) SCC (L&S) 646; 1996 (2) AD(SC) 235; AIR 1996 SC 2963; 1996 (33) ATC 338; 1996 (72) FLR 694; JT 1996 (2) SC 463; 1996 LabIC 1027; 1996(2) SCALE 321; 1996 (2) SCJ 313; [1996] 2 S.C.R. 132; 1996 (2) SLR 40; 1996 (2) Supreme 431; 1996 (1) UJ 716; 1996 (2) UPLBEC 745

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14 and 311 - Central Govt. circular, D/31-03-1982, cl. 4 - Grant - Pensionary benefits - Cut-off date - Validity - Govt. decision that persons rendering temporary service prior to their joining Govt. service be given benefit of fixation of pension payable by tagging their temporary service - Held, fixation of cut-off date and denial of benefits to employees retired earlier - Arbitrary and violative of art. 14.
