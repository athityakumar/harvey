Union Of India & Ors v K.V. Vijeesh

Supreme Court of India

27 February 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, M.K. Mukherjee, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 610; (1996) 3 SCC 139; (1996) SCC (L&S) 683; 1996 (3) AD(SC) 52; AIR 1996 SC 3031; JT 1996 (Supp) SC 537; 1996 LabIC 1057; 1996(2) SCALE 631; [1996] 2 S.C.R. 1077; 1996 (2) SLR 319; 1996 (2) Supreme 762

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution - Constitution, art. 16 and 311- Select list - Preparation of number of vacancies subsequently reduced - Names of 25 persons at bottom in select list withdrawn including name of respondent - Appointments made according to comparative merits of candidate - (A) Whether rejection of respondent's claim was arbitrary or discriminatory - (B) Whether order directing absorption of respondent in service solely on ground that his name was included in select list, can be sustained - Held, (A) Not arbitrary or discriminatory - (B) Cannot be sustained - Appeal allowed.
