State Of Tamil Nadu v Thiru K.V.Perumal And Others

Supreme Court of India

16 July 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, S.C. Sen

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3782; (1996) 5 SCC 474; (1996) SCC (L&S) 1280; AIR 1996 SC 2474; 1996 (2) CLR 519; 1996 (74) FLR 1999; JT 1996 (6) SC 604; 1996 LabIC 2069; 1996 (2) LLJ 799; 1996 (2) LLN 883; 1996(5) SCALE 379; 1996 (3) SLJ 43; 1996 (4) SLR 603; 1996 (5) Supreme 696

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Administrative Tribunals Act, 1985, s. 14 - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 133 and 311 - Jurisdiction of tribunal - Removal from service - Disciplinary proceedings - Order of enquiry officer against employee - Held, tribunal exceeded its jurisdiction as it is not appellate authority over enquiry officer.
