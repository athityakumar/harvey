Union of India and Another v Moti Lal and Others

Supreme Court of India

15 February 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3242; (1996) 7 SCC 481; (1996) SCC (L&S) 613; 1996 (2) AD(SC) 419; AIR 1996 SC 3306; 1996 (33) ATC 304; JT 1996 (Supp) SC 530; 1996 LabIC 1818; 1996(2) SCALE 406; [1996] 2 S.C.R. 727; 1996 (2) SLR 90; 1996 (2) Supreme 748

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Railway Establishment Code, r. 157 - Railway Establishment Manual II, para 2501 Appointment - Post of mate in class III - Direct post of mate in class III - Direct appointment - Held, not permissible - Being a promotional post from class IV posts of gangman and keyman - Petitioners appointed directly as casual mates cannot claim regularisation on their continued service for considerable period.
