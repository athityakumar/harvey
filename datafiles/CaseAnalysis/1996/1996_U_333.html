Uttam Das Chela Sunder Das v Shiromani Gurdwara Parbandhakcommittee, Amritsar

Supreme Court of India

20 May 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Punchhi, K.S. Paripoornan

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2868; (1996) 5 SCC 71; 1996 (5) AD(SC) 131; AIR 1996 SC 2133; JT 1996 (5) SC 285; 1996(4) SCALE 608; [1996] Supp3 S.C.R. 5; 1996 (5) Supreme 17

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal

Keywords: Sikhs

Summary: Family & Personal - Sikh Gurdwaras Act, 1925, ss. 8, 9 and 16 - Notification u/s. 7 of Act - Pleadings as to custom or practice by which hereditary office holder came to hold office - Objector placing line of succession from Guru to Chela - Held, pleadings as to succession to office were sufficient - Appeal allowed.
