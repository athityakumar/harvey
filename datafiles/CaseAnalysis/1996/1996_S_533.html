State Of Orissa And Others v Ram Chandra Das

Supreme Court of India

08 May 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

Faizan Uddin, G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3784; (1996) 5 SCC 331; (1996) SCC (L&S) 1169; 1996 (5) AD(SC) 195; AIR 1996 SC 2436; 1996 (2) CLR 930; JT 1996 (Supp) SC 613; 1996 LabIC 2062; 1997 (1) LLJ 238; 1996 (2) LLN 861; 1996(5) SCALE 14; 1996 (3) SLJ 65; 1996 (4) SLR 456; [1996] Supp2 S.C.R. 559; 1996 (5) Supreme 286

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 309, Proviso - Orissa Service Code, 1939, r. 71(a) - Compulsory retirement - Order of - Consideration of service record and pending departmental enquiry against employee - Held, is legal - Fact, promotion was given even after adverse entries - Is not sufficient to invalidate order.
