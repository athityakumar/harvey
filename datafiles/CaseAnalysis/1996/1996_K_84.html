Karnataka State Financial Corporation v Micro Cast Rubber And Allied Products (P) Ltd. And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

03 June 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, G.T. Nanavati

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2674; (1996) 5 SCC 65; 1996 (3) CCC 14; [1997] 88 Comp Cas 221; JT 1996 (6) SC 37; 1996(4) SCALE 675; [1996] Supp3 S.C.R. 40

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance

Summary: Banking & Finance - State Financial Corporation Act, 1951, s. 29 - Scope and applicability - In matter of a sale by State Financial Corporation in exercise of power conferred on it u/s. 29 of Act scope of judicial review is confined to two situations, namely, (1) where there is a statutory violation on part of State Financial Corporation, or (2) where State Financial Corporation acts unfairly, i.e., unreasonably - Held, High Court u/art. 226 no appellate authority to acts of Corporation - HC not justified in interfering with action of Corporation - Appeal allowed.
