Prabhat Sound Studios v Additional Collector of Central Excise

Supreme Court of India

07 November 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 65; (1997) 10 SCC 543; 1997 (68) ECR 764; 1996 (88) E.L.T. 635; JT 1997 (10) SC 392; 1997 (107) STC 70

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excise Tariff - Item 59 - Appellant on job work basis, recorded sound on magnetic cassette tapes or spool magnetic tapes - Such tapes supplied by customers - Whether Appellant was engaged in manufacture of goods which were excisable u/item 59? - Recorded sound could be erased and sound could be freshly recorded - Held, no process of manufacture involved - Thus it is held by court that it is unnecessary to go into alternative argument based on an exemption notification - Appeal allowed.
