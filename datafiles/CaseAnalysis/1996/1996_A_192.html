Inspector General of Registration, U. P, and Another v Avdesh Kumar and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 April 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3051; (1996) 9 SCC 217; (1996) SCC (L&S) 1222; 1996 (5) AD(SC) 29; 1996 (3) AWC 1480; JT 1996 (5) SC 365; 1996(4) SCALE 392; 1996 (4) SLR 32; [1996] Supp1 S.C.R. 293; 1996 (4) Supreme 698; 1996 (3) UPLBEC 1744

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Regularization Rules, rr. 4 and 4(1)(ii) - Whether direction to state to give appointment to respondents and pay them salary till regular selections were made, justified? - Issue raised covered by judgment in 1995 Indlaw SC 1795 - Held, persons appointed either prior or subsequent to 01-10-1986 to continue on ad hoc basis - Same directions to operate for payment of daily wages - Selection Comittee to relax age in case candidates have become overaged - Appeals allowed.
