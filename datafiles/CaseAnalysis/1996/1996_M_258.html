State of Madhya Pradesh and Others v Messrs M.V. Vyavsaya and Company

Supreme Court of India

28 November 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, S.C. Sen

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1991; (1997) 1 SCC 156; 1996 (9) AD(SC) 361; AIR 1997 SC 993; 1997 (1) AWC 2132; 1997 (1) CLT 175; JT 1996 (10) SC 868; 1996(8) SCALE 631; [1996] Supp9 S.C.R. 321; 1996 (8) Supreme 309

Case Digest

Subject: Contract & Commercial

Summary: Contract & Commercial - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Petition against excise contracts - Each party alleging that other party violated terms and condition of licence and rules - (A) Whether High Court should dismiss petition because it involved disputed questions of fact? - (B) Whether repeated interim orders by HC permitting supply of liquor to petitioner etc proper? - Held, (A) HC should dismiss petition as it involved disputed questions of fact - (B) passing of repeated interim order by HC was not proper - Petition dismissed.
