State of Tamil Nadu and Another v S. Subramaniam

Supreme Court of India

24 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3990; (1996) 7 SCC 509; (1996) SCC (L&S) 627; 1996 (1) AD(SC) 1024; AIR 1996 SC 1232; 1996 (33) ATC 317; 1996 (1) CLR 386; 1996 (72) FLR 767; JT 1996 (2) SC 114; 1996 LabIC 1046; 1996 (1) MLJ(SC) 97; 1996(1) SCALE 810; [1996] 1 S.C.R. 968; 1996 (1) SLR 816; 1996 (2) Supreme 113

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Administrative Tribunals Act, 1985, s. 19 - Appeal before tribunal but conclusion reached by authority based on evidence - Determination of powers of tribunal regarding re-appreciation of evidence - Held, tribunal is devoid of power to re-appreciate evidence and come to its own conclusion - Appeal allowed - I. A. dismissed.
