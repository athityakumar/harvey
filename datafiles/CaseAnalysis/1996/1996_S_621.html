Shree Krishna Gyanoday Sugar Limited and another v State of Bihar and Others

Supreme Court of India

14 August 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Majmudar, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 984; (1996) 10 SCC 11; 1996 (6) AD(SC) 457; AIR 1996 SC 2610; JT 1996 (7) SC 322; 1996(6) SCALE 17; [1996] Supp4 S.C.R. 680; 1996 (6) Supreme 718

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Medicinal and Toilet Preparations (Excise Duties) Act, 1955, Bombay Prohibition Act 1949, Orissa Excise Rules

Summary: Bihar and Orissa Excise Act, 1915, s.38; Bihar and Orissa Excise Rules, 1919, r.9 - Validity - Power of Commissioner to appoint officer and establishment to the charge of distillery - 'Or' in r.9 - Held, not ultra vires the provision of Act - Engrafted in the first part of Rule 9 - Spirits unfit for human consumption like denatured spirits
