Nagar Mahapalika, Meerut v Prem Nath Monga Bottlers Private Limited and Another

Supreme Court of India

18 March 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2724; (1996) 8 SCC 1; 1996 (3) AD(SC) 112; AIR 1996 SC 1302; 1996 (63) ECR 319; JT 1996 (3) SC 389; 1996(2) SCALE 816; [1996] 3 S.C.R. 515; 1996 (102) STC 97; 1996 (2) Supreme 765; 1996 (2) UJ 4; 1996 (2) UPLBEC 1393

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Customs; Indirect Tax; Sales Tax

Summary: Uttar Pradesh Municipalities Act, 1916, s. 128 - Notification D/- 4-01-1975, Entry 13 - Octroi - Exemption from - Articles containing 'mineral water bottles' - Held, means 'aerated water/cold drinks' - 'Mineral water' and 'aerated water/cold drinks' are different and distinct articles.
