Commissioner of Commercial Taxes, A.P., Hyderabad and Another v G. Sethumadhava Rao Others

Supreme Court of India

17 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, S.B. Majmudar, B.L. Hansaria

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3089; (1996) 7 SCC 512; (1996) SCC (L&S) 630; 1996 (1) AD(SC) 1032; AIR 1996 SC 1915; 1996 (33) ATC 320; JT 1996 (2) SC 44; 1996 LabIC 1616; 1996 (2) LLN 463; 1996(1) SCALE 721; [1996] 1 S.C.R. 693; 1996 (1) SLR 762; 1996 (2) Supreme 67

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution - Service - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 16(4) and 16(4 A) - Govt. service - Reservation - State, of opinion that scheduled castes and scheduled tribes are not adequately represented in promotional posts in certain class or classes or service under state - Whether state can apply rule of reservation in promotions also? - Held, it can apply rule of reservation also in promotions - Appeal allowed.
