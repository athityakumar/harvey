State of Uttar Pradesh v District Judge and Others

Supreme Court of India

11 October 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Majmudar, N.P. Singh

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1848; (1997) 1 SCC 496; 1996 (8) AD(SC) 68; AIR 1997 SC 53; 1997 (29) ALR 398; JT 1996 (9) SC 401; 1997 RD 83; 1996(7) SCALE 671; [1996] Supp7 S.C.R. 513; 1996 (8) Supreme 224

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property

Keywords: Defective Title

Summary: Uttar Pradesh Imposition of Ceiling on Land Holdings Act, 1961, s. 5(1) - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 53-A - Computation of ceiling area - Tenure-holder - Agreement to sell certain lands before appointed day - Possession handed over to proposed transferees - Held, land is still includible in tenure holder's holding for purpose of ceiling area - Section 53 - A of Transfer of Property Act does not apply.
