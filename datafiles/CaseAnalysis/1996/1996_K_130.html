Union of India v Kirloskar Pneumatic Company

Supreme Court of India

06 May 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, S.C. Sen

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 131; (1996) 4 SCC 453; AIR 1996 SC 3285; 1997 (1) Bom.C.R. 396; 1996 (64) ECR 509; 1996 (84) E.L.T. 401; JT 1996 (5) SC 26; 1996 (2) LLJ 401; 1996(4) SCALE 317; [1996] Supp2 S.C.R. 326

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Customs - Constitution - Customs Act, 1962, s. 27 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Refund of duty - Claim could be made only in accordance with s. 27 and within limitation prescribed therein - Whether court can direct authorities to not dismiss claim on ground of limitation? - Held, Court cannot give such direction - Appeal allowed.
