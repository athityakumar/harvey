Dr. S.K. Kacker v All India Institute of Medical Sciences and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 September 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

Faizan Uddin, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2126; (1996) 10 SCC 734; 1996 (7) AD(SC) 331; 2001 ARBLR 641; JT 1996 (12) SC 358; 1996 LabIC 2736; 2003 (4) RAJ 233; 1996(7) SCALE 1; 1996 (2) SCJ 343; 1997 (1) SLJ 35; 1996 (5) SLR 699; [1996] Supp5 S.C.R. 540; 1996 (7) Supreme 498; 1997 (1) UPLBEC 316

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Whether appellant on ceasing to be a Director on and from 15-10-1995 could revert to and continue in post of Professor of ENT Department till he attains superannuation? - Held, appellant is a permanent Govt. servant and cannot hold two substantive posts at same time - Appellant lost his lien in post of Professor and Head of ENT Department on his substantive appointment to post of Director - HC was right in holding that appellant cannot revert as Professor and Head of ENT Department, on his ceasing to be Director of AIIMS - Appeal dismissed.
