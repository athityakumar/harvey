State of Rajasthan v Messrs Kalyan Sundaram Cement Industries Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 February 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 855; (1996) 3 SCC 87; (1996) SCC (Cr) 466; 1996 (28) ALR 57; [1996] 86 Comp Cas 433; 1996 (3) Crimes 92; JT 1996 (3) SC 162; 1996 (2) MLJ 43; 1996 (2) MLJ(SC) 43; 1996(2) SCALE 403; [1996] 2 S.C.R. 463; 1996 (1) UJ 645

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Summary: Criminal - Negotiable Instrument Act, 1881, s. 138 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 420 - Cheque bouncing - Whether High Court correct in staying the proceedings of the civil suits pending disposal of the criminal cases? - Held, pendency of the criminal matters would not be an impediment to proceed with the civil suits - The Criminal Court would deal with offence punishable under the Act - HC proceeded on wrong premise that the accused would be expected to disclose their defence in the criminal case by asking them to proceed with the trial of the suit - HC order set aside - Appeal allowed.
