Union Of India And Anr. v M/S. Jesus Sales Corporation

Supreme Court of India

26 March 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

N.P. Singh, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 686; (1996) 4 SCC 69; 1996 (3) AD(SC) 497; AIR 1996 SC 1509; 1996 (63) DLT 398; 1996 (55) ECC 51; 1996 (64) ECR 169; 1996 (83) E.L.T. 486; JT 1996 (3) SC 597; 1996(3) SCALE 103; [1996] 3 S.C.R. 894; 1996 (3) Supreme 92; 1996 (2) UJ 409

Case Digest

Subject: Customs

Summary: Imports and Exports (Control) Act, 1947, s.4M(1), Proviso 3; Constitution of India, art.14 - Dispensing with requirement of pre-deposit - Appeal against order imposing penalty - Requirement of predeposit - Application for dispensing of pre-deposit - Held, hearing to applicant not necessary before deciding the application - The non-deposit of such amount itself is an exception.
