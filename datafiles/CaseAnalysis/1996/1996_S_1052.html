Prabhat Kumar Sharma and Others v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 July 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3015; (1996) 10 SCC 62; (1996) SCC (L&S) 1331; AIR 1996 SC 2638; 1996 ALJ 1519; 1997 ALJ 1548; 1996 (28) ALR 270; 1996 (2) ESC 257; JT 1996 (6) SC 579; 1996 LabIC 2268; 1996(5) SCALE 404; 1996 (3) SLJ 21; 1996 (6) SLR 371; [1996] Supp3 S.C.R. 424; 1996 (5) Supreme 733; 1996 (2) UJ 643; 1996 (3) UPLBEC 1959

Case Digest

Subject: Education; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Ad Hoc Appointment, Education Norms

Summary: Uttar Pradesh Secondary Education Services Commission and Selection Board (Removal of Difficulties) First Order, 1981, para 5 - Uttar Pradesh Secondary Education Services Commission and Selection Board Act, 1982, s. 18 - Teachers - Ad hoc appointments - Held, procedures under para 5 must be complied - Appointments made in contravention thereof - Void - First order of 1981 is permanent and not transient.
