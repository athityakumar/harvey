Central Welfare Board and Others v Anjali Bepari (Ms) and Others

Supreme Court of India

02 August 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2092; (1996) 10 SCC 133; (1996) SCC (L&S) 1358; 1996 (6) AD(SC) 788; 1997 (1) CalLT(SC) 53; 1996 (2) CLR 1017; 1996 (74) FLR 2336; JT 1996 (8) SC 1; 1997 (1) LLJ 576; 1997 (1) LLN 560; 1996(6) SCALE 302; 1996 (5) SLR 195; [1996] Supp4 S.C.R. 275; 1996 (6) Supreme 648; 1997 (1) UPLBEC 47

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Respondent appointed against a casual vacancy in Central Social Welfare Board Scheme - Whether she is entitled to regularisation of services? - Project was being wound up and services of employees dispensed accordingly - Held, respondent to be continued in any other temporary scheme - Further held, dispensing with services should be on last-come-first-go basis - Order accordingly.
