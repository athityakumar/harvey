Vikas Sales Corporation and another v Commissioner of Commercial Taxes and Another

Supreme Court of India

01 May 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, A.M. Ahmadi, S.C. Sen

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2581; (1996) 4 SCC 433; 1996 (5) AD(SC) 158; AIR 1996 SC 2082; 2017 (354) E.L.T. 6; JT 1996 (5) SC 482; 1996(4) SCALE 622; 1996 (102) STC 106; [1996] Supp2 S.C.R. 204; 1996 (5) Supreme 42

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Keywords: Bihar Sales Tax Act, 1959

Summary: Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax Act, 1959, s. 2(J) - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 366(29-A), Sch. 7, List 2, Entry 54, List 1, Entry 92 A - Central Sales Tax Act, 1956, s. 2(d) - Taxes on sale/purchase of goods - Expression 'Goods' - Held, to be given same meaning - Entries 54 and 92-A are not competing entries as regards meaning of word 'Goods' - Legislative entries in 7th sch. - To be construed liberally.
