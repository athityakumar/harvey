Balram Son Of Bhasa Ram v Tlam Singh And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

23 August 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

J.S. Verma, B.N. Kirpal

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1028; (1996) 5 SCC 705; 1996 (6) AD(SC) 540; AIR 1996 SC 2781; AIR 1996 SCW 3539; 1996 (3) CCC 326; 1996 (4) CLT 5; JT 1996 (7) SC 423; 1996(6) SCALE 133; [1996] Supp5 S.C.R. 104; 1996 (6) Supreme 505; 1996 (2) UJ 591

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure Code, 1908, O.21, rr.85 and 90 - Execution sale - Purchaser, decree holder - Failure to deposit full amount of sale price within time stipulated - Sale becomes nullity - Plea by Purchaser-decree holder that short deposit was due to mistake of Court in indicating amount to be set off - Held, cannot avert the effect of sale becoming nullity.
