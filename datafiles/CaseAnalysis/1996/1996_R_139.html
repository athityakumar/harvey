State of Gujarat and Others v Rama Rana and Others

Supreme Court of India

13 December 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2029; (1997) 2 SCC 693; AIR 1997 SC 1845; JT 1997 (1) SC 444; 1997(1) SCALE 159; [1996] Supp10 S.C.R. 102; 1997 (1) Supreme 308

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 31 - Compensation - Determination of value of land on basis of yield - Held, 50% of net value to be deducted from cultivation expenses, after deduction, balance would be net value of land - Compensation should be determined by applying multiplier of 10 - Appeal allowed.
