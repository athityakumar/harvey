H. U. D. A. v Kewal Krishan Goel

Supreme Court of India

09 May 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2963; (1996) 4 SCC 249; 1996 (4) AD(SC) 548; AIR 1996 SC 1981; 1996 (2) CCC 217; 1996 (2) CLT 377; JT 1996 (6) SC 62; 1996(4) SCALE 327; 1996 (2) SCJ 640; [1996] Supp2 S.C.R. 587; 1996 (4) Supreme 142; 1996 (2) UJ 287

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Land & Property - Haryana Urban Development Authority Act, 1977, s. 15 - Allotment of land under housing scheme - Allotee deposited some instalments - Authority has been informed about his incapacity to pay up balance installments - Refund of money paid was requested - Whether allotting authority entitled to forfeit earnest money or only 10% of total amount deposited by allottee till request is made? - Held, entitled to forfeit earnest money - Appeal allowed.
