Himachal Road Transport Corporation v Dinesh Kumar and another

Supreme Court of India

07 May 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Paripoornan, B.P. Jeevan Reddy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3920; (1996) 4 SCC 560; (1996) SCC (L&S) 1053; AIR 1996 SC 2226; AIR 1996 SCW 2727; 1996 (2) CLR 19; 1996 (73) FLR 1600; JT 1996 (5) SC 319; 1996 (2) LLJ 760; 1996 (1) LLN 801; 1996(4) SCALE 395; 1996 (2) SCJ 620; 1996 (4) SLR 246; [1996] Supp2 S.C.R. 456; 1996 (2) UJ 474; 1996 (3) UPLBEC 2181

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Administrative Tribunals Act, 1985, s. 14 - Jurisdiction of Tribunal - Appointment - Sought under 'Kith and Kin Policy' of employer - Absence of vacancy to particular post - Order of Tribunal directing concerned authorities to appoint applicant to post and also directing to create supernumerary post and then appoint such person to post - Whether legal and within jurisdiction? - Held, Illegal and without jurisdiction - Tribunal can only give direction to appropriate authority to consider case of applicant in light of relevant rules and subject to availability of post - Appeal allowed.
