The State Of Gujarat v Rajesh Kumar Chimanlal Barot And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

05 August 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, S.C. Sen

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2138; (1996) 5 SCC 477; 1996 (6) AD(SC) 289; AIR 1996 SC 2664; 1996 (3) CCC 237; [1996] 87 Comp Cas 919; 1996 (4) CTJ 653; JT 1996 (Supp) SC 380; 1996(6) SCALE 14; [1996] Supp4 S.C.R. 279; 1996 (6) Supreme 131

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Corporate

Summary: Civil Procedure - Consumer Protection Act, 1986, ss. 11, 17and 21 - CPC, 1908, s. 9 - (A) Consumer Redressal Forums - Jurisdiction of - Claim by Gram Panchayat for benefit of subsidised rate in respect of electricity consumed by it for supply of drinking water to residents - Whether said dispute falls within the purview of Consumer Redressal Forum - Can Forum assume jurisdiction on ground that party involved is Gram Panchayat and amount involved is small - (B) CPC, 1908, s. 9 - Jurisdiction - Court not having jurisdiction on a subject - Can it obtain jurisdiction on the ground that party involved is Gram Panchayat and amount involved is small - Held, (A) Does not 'legitimately fall within the purview of adjudication by Consumer Redressal Forum' - Forum cannot usurp jurisdiction on that ground - (B) Cannot usurp jurisdiction on this ground - Appeal dismissed.
