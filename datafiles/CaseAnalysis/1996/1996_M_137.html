Commissioner of Income Tax v Mafatlal Gangabhai and Company Private Limited

Supreme Court of India

12 March 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, M.K. Mukherjee

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3511; (1996) 7 SCC 569; 1996 (2) AD(SC) 573; AIR 1996 SC 3003; 1996 (1) BC 588; 1996 (132) CTR 248; [1996] 219 ITR 644; JT 1996 (3) SC 173; 1996(2) SCALE 676; [1996] 3 S.C.R. 227; 1996 (3) Supreme 397; [1996] 85 TAXMAN 381; 1996 (2) TLR 440

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 40(a)(v) and 40 A(5)(a)(ii) - Deduction - Payments not deductible - Cash payments by assessee to his/its employees - Whether fall within ambit of s. 40(a)(v) or s. 40A (5)(a)(ii) of Income Tax Act? - Held, do not fall within ambit of these sections - Appeal dismissed.
