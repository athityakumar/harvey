Shyam Sunder Agarwal And Co. v Union Of India.

Supreme Court of India

09 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, Faizan Uddin

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1174; (1996) 2 SCC 132; AIR 1996 SC 1321; AIR 1996 SCW 1464; 1996 (1) ARBLR 153; JT 1996 (1) SC 222B; 1996(1) SCALE 237B; 1996 (2) SCJ 40; [1996] 1 S.C.R. 245

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Scheduled Districts Act, 1874, s.6; Civil Procedure Code, 1908, s.115; Rules for Administration of Justice and Police in Khasi and Janitia Hills, 1937, r.36A - Revisional Powers of High Court - Appellate order by Deputy Commissioner - Clothed with powers of High Court - Revisional powers against, under r.36A - Held, must be exercised by High Court in conformity with power under s.115 of C.P.C.
