Sub-Divisional Inspector of Post, Vaikam and Others v Theyyam Joseph

Supreme Court of India

02 February 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 396; (1996) 8 SCC 489; (1996) SCC (L&S) 1012; 1996 (2) AD(SC) 278; AIR 1996 SC 1271; 1996 (2) AWC 721; 1996 (2) CLR 237; 1996 (72) FLR 690; JT 1996 (2) SC 457; 1996 LabIC 1059; 1996 (2) LLJ 230; 1996 (2) LLN 82; 1996 (2) MLJ(SC) 1; 1996(2) SCALE 386; 1996 (2) SCJ 370; [1996] 2 S.C.R. 93; 1996 (2) Supreme 487; 1996 (3) UPLBEC 1648

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 25F - EDA Conduct and Service Rules, r. 6 - Temporary employees - Meaning of - Whether temporary employees appointed as replacement are workmen under Industrial Disputes Act, 1947? - Held, method of recruitment, conditions of service, scale of pay and conduct rules regulating service conditions of ED Agents are governed by statutory regulation - It is now settled that these employees are civil servants regulated by the conduct rules - Therefore, they do not belong to category of workmen - Appeal dismissed.
