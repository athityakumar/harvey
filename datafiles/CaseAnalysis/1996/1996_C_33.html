Central Bureau Of Investigation, Spe,Siu (X), New Delhi v Duncans Agro Industries Ltd.,Calcutta

Supreme Court of India

09 July 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.N. Ray, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2982; (1996) 5 SCC 591; (1996) SCC (Cr) 1045; AIR 1996 SC 2452; [1996] 87 Comp Cas 849; 1996 (3) Crimes 60; 1996 CRLJ 3501; JT 1996 (6) SC 227; 1996(5) SCALE 99; [1996] Supp3 S.C.R. 360

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Corporate; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Floating Charge, Debt Laws

Summary: Code of Criminal Procedure, 1974, s. 482 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 405 and 420 - Criminal Appeals - Complaint of criminal breach of trust and cheating - Goods with floating charge in favour of banks - Disposed of by debtor-company - Civil Suit for recovery - Compromise decrees - Payments by debtor - Compounding of offence of cheating - Investigation not completed for long time - Whether Criminal complaint liable to be quashed? - Held, complaint for both offences was liable to be quashed.
