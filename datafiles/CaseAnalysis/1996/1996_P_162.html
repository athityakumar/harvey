Kanchan Devi Smt v Promod Kumar Mittal and Another

Supreme Court of India

03 April 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, Faizan Uddin

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2663; (1996) 8 SCC 90; 1996 (3) AD(SC) 579; AIR 1996 SC 3192; 1996 (2) Crimes 86; 1997 (1) DMC 257; 1997 (1) HLR 446; JT 1996 (5) SC 655; 1997 (6) KarLJ 75; 1996(3) SCALE 293; 1996 (3) Supreme 493

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 142 - Hindu Marriage Act 1955, s. 13 - Divorce - Successive application for maintenance by wife leading to appeal to Supreme Court for Divorce and maintenance - Husband and wife found to be living separately for more than a decade as their relations had broken down irretrievably and no possibility of any reconciliation was there - Held, Supreme Court in exercise of powers u/art. 142 granted divorce - Dispute regarding maintenance settled as per earlier settlement arrived at between parties.
