Namburi Basava Subrahmanyam v Alapati Hymavathi & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

02 April 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3462; (1996) 9 SCC 388; 1996 (4) AD(SC) 572; AIR 1996 SC 2220; 1996 (28) ALR 424; 1996 (2) CCC 452; 1996 (3) CLT 24; JT 1996 (5) SC 330; 1996(4) SCALE 278; [1996] 3 S.C.R. 1100; 1998 (Supp) RD 6; 1996 (4) Supreme 722

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Land and Property - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 8 - Succession Act, 1925, s. 2(h) - Interpretation of instrument - Settlement deed or Will - It creating right and interest 'in praesenti' in favour of settlee with life estate - Held, it is settlement deed and not will as settlor cannot subsequently bequeath same property to other - Appeal allowed.
