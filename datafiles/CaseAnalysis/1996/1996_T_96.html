Randhir Singh Rana v State (Delhi Administration)

Supreme Court of India

20 December 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

B.L. Hansaria, G.N. Ray

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1566; (1997) 1 SCC 361; AIR 1997 SC 639; 1997 (1) CCR 128; 1997 (1) Crimes 58; 1997 CRLJ 779; JT 1996 (11) SC 638; 1996(9) SCALE 447; 1997 (1) SCJ 35; [1996] Supp10 S.C.R. 880; 1997 (1) Supreme 278

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - CrPC, 1973, s. 173(8) - Investigation - Powers of Magistrate - Cognizance of offence taken by Magistrate on basis of police report - And ordered further investigation when accused appearing in pursuance of process - Validity - Held, Magistrate cannot of his own order further investigation in case - Appeal allowed.
