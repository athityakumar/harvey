Sita Devi and Others v State of Haryana and Others

Supreme Court of India

23 August 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2338; (1996) 10 SCC 1; (1996) SCC (L&S) 1321; AIR 1996 SC 2764; 1996 (2) CLR 1188; JT 1996 (7) SC 438; 1996 (2) LLJ 817; 1996 (2) LLN 476; [1996] Supp5 S.C.R. 173; 1997 (1) UPLBEC 567

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Service

Keywords: Equal Remuneration Act, 1976

Summary: Equal Remuneration Act, 1976 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16 - Pay Scale - Classification of employees on basis of educational qualifications - Non-matriculate instructors in Adult Literacy Programme - Refusal to extend pay scale which was extended to matriculate instructors by Govt. - Held, not illegal - Reasonable and permissible.
