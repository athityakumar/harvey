Director of Enforcement v M. C. T. M. Corporation Private Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

09 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, Faizan Uddin

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3350; (1996) SCC (Cr) 344; (1996) 2 SCC 471; AIR 1996 SC 1100; [1997] 88 Comp Cas 449; 1996 CRLJ 1623; 1996 (54) ECC 41; JT 1996 (1) SC 79; 1996(1) SCALE 133; 1996 (2) SCJ 81; [1996] 1 S.C.R. 215

Case Digest

Subject: Fema

Summary: Foreign Exchange Regulation Act, 1947, ss. 23(1)(a), 10 and 23F - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 20(2) - Contravention of s. 10 - Attract penalty u/s. 23(1)(a) - Held, mens rea is not an essential ingredient for s. 23(1)(a) of Foreign Exchange Regulation Act, 1947 - Even after penalty u/s. 23(1)(a), defaulter can be prosecuted u/s. 23-F - Art. 20(2) of Constitution not attracted.
