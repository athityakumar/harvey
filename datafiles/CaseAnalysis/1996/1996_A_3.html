Bharat Forge Company Limited v A. B. Zodge and Another

Supreme Court of India

20 February 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.N. Ray, B.L. Hansaria

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 408; (1996) 4 SCC 374; (1996) SCC (L&S) 945; 1996 (3) AD(SC) 58; AIR 1996 SC 1556; 1996 (2) CLR 345; 1996 (73) FLR 1754; JT 1996 (5) SC 628; 1996 (2) LLJ 643; 1996 (1) LLN 797; 1997 (1) MLJ(SC) 24; 1996(2) SCALE 731; [1996] 2 S.C.R. 912; 1996 (2) SLR 492; 1996 (2) Supreme 709; 1997 (1) UPLBEC 570

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 11 A - Uttar Pradesh Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - Validity of denial of opportunity by Industrial Tribunal to employer to lead evidence in support of order of dismissal before closure of proceedings - Held, not proper - Appeal disposed of.
