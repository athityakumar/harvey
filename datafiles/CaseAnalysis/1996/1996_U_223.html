Major Radha Krishan v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 March 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

M.K. Mukherjee, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3936; (1996) 3 SCC 507; (1996) SCC (L&S) 761; AIR 1996 SC 3091; 1996 (73) FLR 1430; JT 1996 (3) SC 650; 1996 (2) LLN 39; 1996(3) SCALE 241; [1996] 3 S.C.R. 836; 1996 (2) SLR 650; 1996 (1) UJ 817; 1996 (2) UPLBEC 1410

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Defence & Security - Army Act, 1950, ss. 19 and 122 - Army Rules, 1954, r. 14 - Termination of services - Trial by Court-martial for offences committed by delinquent barred by limitation - Can summary procedure for termination u/r. 14(2) be invoked, on ground that trial by court - martial was inexpedient or impracticable - Held, cannot be invoked - Appeal allowed.
