State Of Rajasthan v Mangilal Pindwal

Supreme Court of India

08 July 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, G.T. Nanavati

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3785; (1996) 5 SCC 60; AIR 1996 SC 2181; 1996 (2) CLR 926; JT 1996 (6) SC 162; 1996 LabIC 1846; 1997 (2) LLJ 756; 1996(5) SCALE 38B; 1996 (3) SLJ 67; 1996 (5) SLR 147; [1996] Supp3 S.C.R. 98; 1996 (5) Supreme 273; 1996 (2) UJ 571

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Service - Constitution, art. 309, Proviso - Validity of r. 244 (2) of the Rajasthan Service Rules, 1951 as amended in March 11, 1976 - Held, amendment not invalid on ground that it sought to amend provision which was not in existence - Appeal disposed of.
