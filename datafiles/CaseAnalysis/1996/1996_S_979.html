Pratap and Another v State of Rajasthanand Others

Supreme Court of India

27 February 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, N.P. Singh, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 607; (1996) 3 SCC 1; 1996 (2) AD(SC) 497; AIR 1996 SC 1296; JT 1996 (2) SC 759; 1996 (4) KarLJ 780; 1996 (1) RentLR 723; 1996(2) SCALE 543; [1996] 2 S.C.R. 1088; 1996 (2) Supreme 533; 1996 (1) UJ 822

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Land and Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 4, 11A, 17 and 17A - Rajasthan Urban Improvement Act, 1959, ss. 60A and 52 - Rajasthan Urban Improvement (Amendment And Validation) Act, 1990 - Acquisition of land which was vested in State Govt. - Whether subsequent extension of 1894 act to the State and amendment of 1959 Act, divest already completed acquisition - In the light of faces that failure to pass award and make payment within stipulated period - (B) No scheme prepared or finalised under Housing Board or Urban Improvement Act - If acquisition can be made for public purpose or for improvement - Held, will not divest as facts are immaterial - Acquisition can still be made - Appeal dismissed.
