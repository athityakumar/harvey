The State Of Maharashtra And Anr. v Shri Prabhakar Bhikaji Ingle

Supreme Court of India

11 March 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3313; (1996) 3 SCC 463; (1996) SCC (L&S) 749; 1996 (3) AD(SC) 296; AIR 1996 SC 3069; 1996 (2) CCC 43; 1996 (1) CLR 785; 1996 (73) FLR 1009; JT 1996 (3) SC 567; 1996 (2) LLJ 430; 1996 (2) LLN 51; 1996(3) SCALE 7; [1996] 3 S.C.R. 211; 1996 (2) SLR 521; 1996 (3) Supreme 245; 1996 (2) UJ 100; 1996 (4) UPLBEC 2903

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Administrative Tribunals Act 1985, s.22 ; Civil Procedure code, 1908, o.47, r.1; - Constitution of India, arts.136 and 311 - Order of dismissal of employees - Supreme Court dismissed the special leave petition - Final - Tribunal had knowledge of dismissal - Still Review - Whether such review is within the scope of Tribunal - Held, said order cannot be reviewed by Tribunal by setting aside dismissal ordered - When it has knowledge of dismissal of the special leave petition.
