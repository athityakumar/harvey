V. K. Ramamurthy v Union of India and Another

Supreme Court of India

13 August 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2279; (1996) 10 SCC 73; (1996) SCC (L&S) 1341; 1996 (6) AD(SC) 336; AIR 1996 SC 2658; 1996 (2) CLR 1054; 1996 (74) FLR 2218; JT 1996 (7) SC 296; 1996 LabIC 2275; 1997 (1) LLN 121; 1996(5) SCALE 829; 1997 (1) SLJ 16; 1996 (5) SLR 151; [1996] Supp4 S.C.R. 583; 1996 (6) Supreme 305; 1996 (2) UJ 536; 1997 (1) UPLBEC 439

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 311 - Switching over from Provident Fund Scheme to Pension Scheme - Permissibility - Retiree from railway given six opportunities to switch over to Pension Scheme before retirement - Option to switch over, however, not exercised - (A) Is he entitled to opt for Pension Scheme? - (B) Whether Pension and Provident Fund Schemes are different? - Held, (A) Not entitled to opt for Pension Scheme - (B) schemes are structurally different - Appeal dismissed.
