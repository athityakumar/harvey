Surjit Singh & Ors v Balbir Singh

Supreme Court of India

29 February 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, S. Saghir Ahmad, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3618; (1996) SCC (Cr) 521; (1996) 3 SCC 533; 1996 (33) ACC 343; 1996 (3) AD(SC) 217; AIR 1996 SC 1592; 1996 AllCriC 343; 1996 (4) Bom.C.R. 489; 1996 (2) CCR 122; 1996 CRLJ 2304; JT 1996 (3) SC 363; 1996(2) SCALE 865; [1996] 3 S.C.R. 70; 1996 (3) Supreme 250

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Criminal - CrPC, 1974, s.195(1)(b)(ii) - Forged document produced in evidence - Bar against congnizance of private complaint applies when original document is produced in Court - But cognizance already taken before filing of original document - Legality and effect - Held, Criminal Court not debarred from proceeding with trial - Appeal dismissed.
