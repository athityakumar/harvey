 Ujjagar Singh (Dead) By Lrs. and another v Collector, Bhatinda and Another

Supreme Court of India

01 August 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

N.P. Singh, Kuldip Singh, M.K. Mukherjee, S. Saghir Ahmad, M.M. Punchhi

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 871; (1996) 5 SCC 14; 1996 (6) AD(SC) 440; AIR 1996 SC 2623; JT 1996 (6) SC 713; 1996(5) SCALE 512; [1996] Supp4 S.C.R. 239; 1996 (6) Supreme 43

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land and Property - Pepsu Tenancy and Agricultural Lands Act, 1955, s. 32E(a) - Land declared surplus - However, its possession not taken over by State - Landowner remaining in possession throughout till commencement of Punjab Land Reforms Act - Whether surplus land vests in State since possession is not taken - Held, does not vest in State - Fresh determination has to be made under Punjab Land Reforms Act - Appeal allowed.
