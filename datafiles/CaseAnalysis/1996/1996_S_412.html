Basavva Smt. and Others v Spl. Land Acquisition Officer and Others

Supreme Court of India

15 March 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2697; (1996) 9 SCC 640; 1996 (4) AD(SC) 426; AIR 1996 SC 3168; 1996 (2) CCC 189; JT 1996 (5) SC 580; 1996 (1) RentLR 431; 1996(3) SCALE 299; [1996] 3 S.C.R. 500; 1996 (3) Supreme 766

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 23 - Determination of compensation - Deduction for development charges up to 53% is valid - Land acquired, capable of development but considering fact that development of land would have taken years, HC deducted 12% more i.e., 65% - Is order of HC rational and justified? - Held, is rational and justified - Appeal dismissed.
