Gayatrilaxmi Bapurao Nagpure v State Of Maharashtra And. Others

Supreme Court of India

15 March 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Venkataswami, M.M. Punchhi

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3245; (1996) 3 SCC 685; 1996 (3) AD(SC) 293; AIR 1996 SC 1338; 1996 (3) Bom.C.R. 687; 1996 (2) CCC 24; 1996 (2) CLT 56; JT 1996 (3) SC 405; 1996(3) SCALE 53; [1996] 3 S.C.R. 466; 1996 (2) SLR 868; 1996 (3) Supreme 126

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Socio-Economic - Constitution, arts.16, 226, 342 - Limitation Act, 1908 - Reservation - Committee denied to grant Caste Certificate without considering relevant materials or applying its mind to important document - And thereby depriving privileges conferred upon candidate Held, not proper - Greater care should be taken before granting/rejecting Caste Certificates - Appeal allowed.
