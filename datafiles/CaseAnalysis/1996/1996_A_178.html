Preetam Singh (Dead) By Lrs. & Ors. v Assistant Director Of Co

Supreme Court of India

09 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Punchhi, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1166; (1996) 2 SCC 270; 1996 (1) AD(SC) 877; AIR 1996 SC 2881; 1996 (1) CCC 155; JT 1996 (1) SC 471; 1996 (87) RD 192; 1996(1) SCALE 496; [1996] 1 S.C.R. 310; 1996 (1) Supreme 625; 1996 (1) UJ 539

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Keywords: Ex-Parte

Summary: Land and Property - UP Consolidation of Holdings Act, 1954, s. 48 - CPC,1908 - Consolidation proceedings - Revisional jurisdiction - Whether findings recorded by Settlement Officer (Consolidation) as to abandonment of land in dispute by previous tenants - If can be examined by Asstt. Director (Consolidation), if remand order had not been specifically put to challenge in separate and independent proceedings - Held, could be examined, in revisional jurisdiction - Appeal allowed.
