R. Balakrishna Warrier v Santha Varassiar & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

11 October 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, K.S. Paripoornan

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1855; (1996) 11 SCC 500; 1996 (8) AD(SC) 66; 1996 (4) CLT 220; 1997 (1) HLR 600; JT 1996 (9) SC 441; 1996(7) SCALE 583; 1996 (7) Supreme 794

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal

Keywords: Tarwad, Kerala Joint Hindu Family System Abolition Act, 1975

Summary: Family & Personal - Kerala Joint Hindu Family System Abolition Act, 1975 - Appellant filed suit for declaration of to do services in two temples as hereditary rights - Trial Court rejected appellant claim - On appeal High Court held that 1975 Act abolishes joint tenancy of tarwad and it can no more be said that marumakkathayam tarwad with joint tenancy as its feature exists any more and rejected appellants claim - Whether HC right to held that 1975 act had abolished joint family system? - Tarwad has become extinct - Held, no declaration can be given in favour of a non-existing entity - HC right to held that joint family system has been abolished by 1975 Act - Appeal dismissed.
