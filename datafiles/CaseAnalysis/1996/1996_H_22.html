Hanuman Prasad and Others v Union of India and Another

Supreme Court of India

06 September 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2160; (1996) 10 SCC 742; 1996 (7) AD(SC) 445; 1996 (74) FLR 2517; JT 1996 (8) SC 510; 1996(7) SCALE 270; 1996 (3) SLR 675; [1996] Supp5 S.C.R. 599; 1997 (1) UPLBEC 93

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Selection list - Cancellation of - Whether cancellation of select list on ground of malpractice committed in writing examinations, was justified? - Held, when selection committee makes recommendation for appointment of selected candidates they do not get any vested right or legitimate expectation until they are appointed - Competent authority cancelled selection so that regular and proper examination could be conducted giving opportunity to everyone in a fair manner - No prior opportunity need be given in case of mass copying - Appeal dismissed.
