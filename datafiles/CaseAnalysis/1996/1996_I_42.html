Dutta Associates Pvt.Ltd v Indo Merchatiles Pvt.Ltd and Others

Supreme Court of India

18 November 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, S.C. Sen

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1878; (1997) 1 SCC 53; 1996 (9) AD(SC) 30; 1997 (1) ARBLR 87; 1996 (4) CCC 185; 1997 (1) CLT 79; 1997 (1) CompLJ 65; JT 1996 (10) SC 419; 1996(8) SCALE 422; [1996] Supp8 S.C.R. 710; 1996 (8) Supreme 203

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure; Corporate

Summary: Contract & Commercial - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 - Rectified spirit - Commr. of Excise called for tenders for wholesale supply of rectified spirit (Grade 1) to Excise Warehouse - Seventeen tenders received quoting rate - Acceptance of appellant's tender - Challenged - Held, entire procedure followed by Commr. and Govt. of Assam in accepting tender of appellant is unfair and opposed to norms which Govt. should follow in such matters, viz, openness, transparency and fair dealing - Direction that fresh tenders should be floated - Appeal disposed of.
