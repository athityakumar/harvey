Messrs Kilpest Private Limited and Others v Shekhar Mehra

Supreme Court of India

08 October 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, S.B. Majmudar

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1247; [1996] 87 Comp Cas 615; 1999 (2) CompLJ 261; JT 1996 (9) SC 152

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Summary: Corporate - Companies Act, 1956, ss. 397, 398 and 402 - Allotment of additional shares - Alteration of articles - Without notice - Amounting to alteration of the basic structure of the company - Whether the respondents were to remain Joint Managing Directors for life, the principles applicable to an earlier partnership was relevant? - Held, on the perusal of the report filed by the Registrar of Companies which shows that no substance was, ultimately, found therein - Not a case for winding up the company - There is no breach of any order nor any contempt - Appeals dismissed.
