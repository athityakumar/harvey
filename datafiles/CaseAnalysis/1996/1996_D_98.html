Bharathi Knitting Company v Dhl Worldwide Express

Supreme Court of India

09 May 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, Faizan Uddin, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3599; (1996) 4 SCC 704; AIR 1996 SC 2508; 1996 (3) CCC 110; [1996] 87 Comp Cas 886; 1996 CPJ(Sc) 25; 1996 (4) CTJ 557; JT 1996 (6) SC 254; 1996(5) SCALE 142; [1996] Supp2 S.C.R. 653

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Consumer Protection; Corporate; Indirect Tax

Summary: Consumer Protection Act, 1986, s. 23 - Contract Act, 1872, s. 37 - Consignment delayed - Season over - Liability of consignee was excluded in contract for any damages or any indirect loss - Including loss of market or profits - Parties are bound by such terms - Still damages awarded - Whether such award valid? - Held, damages awarded for deficiency in service to extent of liability undertaken in contract between parties was legal.
