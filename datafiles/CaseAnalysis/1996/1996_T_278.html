Reserve Bank of India v Their Workmen

Supreme Court of India

28 February 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Paripoornan, A.M. Ahmadi, S.C. Sen

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 618; (1996) 3 SCC 267; (1996) SCC (L&S) 691; 1996 (3) AD(SC) 24; AIR 1996 SC 1241; 1996 (1) CLR 740; 1996 (73) FLR 965; JT 1996 (3) SC 226; 1996 LabIC 1048; 1996 (2) LLJ 42; 1996 (1) LLJ 1200; 1996 (1) LLN 465; 1996(2) SCALE 708; [1996] 2 S.C.R. 1176; 1996 (2) SLR 508; 1996 (3) Supreme 406

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Reserve Bank of India Act

Summary: Labour and Industrial Laws - RBI - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 2(S) - Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934, s. 58 - Canteen employees - If become workmen of establishment in which canteen is run - (A) When RBI is neither legally obliged to run canteen nor has effective control to supervise their work - (B) Precedents - Consideration of propositions laid down in previously decided case in regard to the matter - Held, not employees of RBI - Supreme Court declined to consider the Propositions - Appeal allowed.
