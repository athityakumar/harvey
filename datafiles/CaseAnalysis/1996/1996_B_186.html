State Of Kerala And Anr. v Bulders Association Of India And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

28 November 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, S.C. Sen

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1998; (1997) 2 SCC 183; 1996 (9) AD(SC) 437; AIR 1997 SC 3640; JT 1996 (10) SC 665; 1997 (1) KLT 88; 1996(8) SCALE 730; 1997 (104) STC 134; [1996] Supp9 S.C.R. 300; 1996 (8) Supreme 611

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure; Sales Tax

Keywords: Freedom Of Speech And Expression

Summary: Kerala General Sales Tax Act, 1963, s. 7 - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 246 and 366 - Works contract - Alternate method of taxation - Validity - Held, applicable only to contractor who opts for it - No compulsion - Provisions under r. 22 A are designed to ensure due realisation of tax due and r. 30 A provides procedure for exercising option under ss. 7 (7) and (7 A) of Act - Not invalid, unconstitutional or void.
