Vishnudas Trading As Vishnudas Kishendas v Vazir Sultan Tobacco Company Limited Hyderabad

Supreme Court of India

09 July 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.N. Ray, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2975; (1997) 4 SCC 201; AIR 1996 SC 2275; 1996 (2) ARBLR 222; 1996 (3) CCC 257; 1996 (3) CLT 251; JT 1996 (6) SC 366; 1996 (16) PTC 512; 1996(5) SCALE 267; [1996] 3 S.C.R. 329; [1996] Supp3 S.C.R. 329; 1996 (6) Supreme 208

Case Digest

Subject: Intellectual Property; MRTP

Keywords: Right To Use, Class Of Goods, Trade Marks Rules, 1959, Trade Marks Act, 1940

Summary: Trade and Merchandise Marks Act, 1958, s. 8 - Trade and Merchandise Marks Rules, 1959, sch. 4, cl. 34 - 'Manufactured tobacco' - Registration of trade mark by manufacturer manufacturing only cigarettes under said broad classification - Manufacturer having no bona fide intention to trade in or manufacture all goods or articles falling under said broad classification - Whether he can be permitted to enjoy monopoly in all article carrying under such classification? - Held, Cannot be permitted to enjoy monopoly - Appeal allowed.
