The Asstt. Collector Of Centralexcise & Ors. v Data India Ltd.

Supreme Court of India

07 May 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Sen, A.M. Ahmadi, B.L. Hansaria

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 239; (1996) 4 SCC 563; AIR 1996 SC 3380; 1996 (64) ECR 678; 1996 (84) E.L.T. 164; JT 1996 (5) SC 230; 1996 (14) RLT 613; 1996(4) SCALE 508; [1996] Supp2 S.C.R. 424

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, s. 4(1) - Central Excise Rules, 1944, r. 8(1) - Exemption of excise duty - Notification exempting footwear value which did not exceed Rs. 60/ - Per pair - Determination of value of footwear - Held, must be normal wholesale price - Appeal allowed.
