Lourdu Mari David v Louis Chinnaya Arogiaswamy and Others

Supreme Court of India

09 August 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2392; (1996) 5 SCC 589; 1996 (6) AD(SC) 708; AIR 1996 SC 2814; 1996 (3) CCC 316; JT 1996 (7) SC 499; 1996(6) SCALE 184; [1996] Supp4 S.C.R. 540; 1996 (6) Supreme 417

Case Digest

Subject: Contract & Commercial

Summary: Contract & Commercial - Specific Relief Act, 1963, s. 20 - If equitable relief of decree for specific performance of agreement to sell can be granted - When claimant not coming with clean hands - As property was purchased by bona fide purchaser for value without notice - Held, can be granted - Petition dismissed.
