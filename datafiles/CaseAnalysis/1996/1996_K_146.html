State of Tamil Nadu and Others v Kothari Sugars and Chemicals Limited

Supreme Court of India

08 February 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

J.S. Verma, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 785; (1996) 7 SCC 751; 1996 (1) AD(SC) 952; AIR 1996 SCW 842; 1996 (1) CLT 436; JT 1996 (2) SC 47; 1996(1) SCALE 756; 1996 (2) SCJ 360; [1996] 2 S.C.R. 275; 1996 (101) STC 197; 1996 (2) Supreme 1

Case Digest

Subject: Socio-Economic; Agriculture & Agri. Products

Keywords: Producer Of Sugar

Summary: Agriculture & Agri. Products - Essential Commodities Act, 1955 - Sugarcane (Control) Order, 1966, cls. 5-A - Whether for purchase of sugarcane from cane growers, a purchaser is liable to pay purchase tax under State Sales Tax Act on amount paid by purchaser to cane grower over and above price fixed u/cls. 3 and 5-A of Sugar cane (Control) Order, 1966? - Held, unless there be an agreement between grower and purchaser for purchase of sugarcane at a higher price, obligation of purchaser is to pay to grower only aggregate of amounts fixed u/cls. 3 and 5-A - In other words, under Statute there is no liability of purchaser to pay to grower any amount in excess of this aggregate amount - Further, there is no basis found in present case wherein excess amount forming part of advance was paid only under compulsion on direction contained in 'State advice' - Appeal dismissed.
