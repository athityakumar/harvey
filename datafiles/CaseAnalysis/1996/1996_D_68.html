Jag Mohan Chawla And Another v Dera Radha Swami Satsang And Others

Supreme Court of India

07 May 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3916; (1996) 4 SCC 699; AIR 1996 SC 2222; AIR 1996 SCW 2722; 1996 (2) CCC 423; JT 1996 (5) SC 428; 1996(4) SCALE 585; 1996 (2) SCJ 502; [1996] Supp2 S.C.R. 509; 1996 (2) UJ 167

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Civil Procedure Code, 1908, O. 8, r. 6 C - Whether in a suit for injunction, counter claim for injunction in respect of same or a different property is maintainable? - Cause of action from which counter claim arises need not necessarily arise from or have any nexus with cause of action - Held, maintainable - Appeal dismissed.
