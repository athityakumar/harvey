Calcutta State Transport Corporation v Commissioner of Income Tax, W.B

Supreme Court of India

29 February 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, K.S. Paripoornan, K. Ramaswamy, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2660; (1996) 8 SCC 758; 1996 (3) AD(SC) 415; AIR 1996 SC 1316; 1997 (2) CalLT(SC) 26; 1996 (132) CTR 283; [1996] 219 ITR 515; JT 1996 (5) SC 510; 1996(21) SCALE 855; [1996] 3 S.C.R. 50; 1996 (3) Supreme 376

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 10(20) - General clauses Act, 1897, s. 3(31) - Road Transport Corporations Act, 1950, ss. 18 and 19 - Local authority - Calcutta State Transport Corporation - Held, not a 'local authority' - Established under Road Transport Corporations Act - Hence, not entitled to claim exemption of its income by virtue of s. 10(20).
