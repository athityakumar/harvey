Raj Kishore Prasad v State of Bihar

Supreme Court of India

01 May 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Punchhi, K.T. Thomas

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3928; (1996) 4 SCC 495; (1996) SCC (Cr) 772; AIR 1996 SC 1931; AIR 1996 SCW 2273; 1996 AllCriR 703; 1996 (2) Crimes 142; 1996 CRLJ 2523; JT 1996 (5) SC 437; 1996(4) SCALE 87; 1996 (2) SCJ 413; [1996] Supp2 S.C.R. 125

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, ss. , 2(g), 209 and 319 - Commitment of case - Addition of another, person as accused in addition to one facing commitment - Whether Magistrate undertaking commitment u/s. 209 of a case triable by a Court of sessions can associate another person as accused, in exercise of power u/s. 319 - Held, Magistrate has no such power - Appeal allowed.
