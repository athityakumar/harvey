The Secretary To Government Oftamil Nadu v D. Subramanyan Rajadevan

Supreme Court of India

09 July 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2985; (1996) 5 SCC 334; (1996) SCC (L&S) 1172; 1996 (5) AD(SC) 496; AIR 1996 SC 2634; 1996 (2) CLR 526; JT 1996 (6) SC 456; 1996 LabIC 2264; 1996(5) SCALE 180; 1996 (3) SLJ 30; 1996 (4) SLR 498; [1996] Supp3 S.C.R. 385; 1996 (5) Supreme 646

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Tamil Nadu Civil Services (Disciplinary Proceedings Tribunal) Rules, 1955

Summary: Constitution of India, art.309; T.N. Civil Services (Classification, Control and Appeal) Rules, 1953, rr.17 and 2; T.N. Civil Services (Disciplinary Proceedings Tribunal) Rules, 1955, r.8 - Disciplinary proceedings - Procedure - Corruption - Against Government servant - Held, procedure under Special rule of Disciplinary proceedings Rules, r.8 has to be followed - No application of r.17 of Civil Service Rules.
