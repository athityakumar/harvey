Union of India and Another v Ranchi Municipal Corporation, Ranchi and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 February 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3255; (1996) 7 SCC 542; 1996 (2) AD(SC) 434; 1996 (27) ALR 699; JT 1996 (3) SC 171; 1996 (2) MLJ 48; 1996 (2) MLJ(SC) 48; 1996(2) SCALE 412; [1996] 2 S.C.R. 761; 1996 (2) Supreme 518

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Municipalities & Local Governments - Practice & Procedure - Railways Act, s. 135 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 285 - (A) Validity of demand of service charges - Relied on Purna Municipal Council case [1991 Indlaw SC 833] where respondent municipality was restrained from demanding any payment by way of service charges from Railways - Held, municipality has no right to demand service charges from UOI, demand made by municipality is clearly ultra vires its power - (B) Whether summary dismissal constitutes res judicata? - Further held, summary dismissal does not constitute res judicata for deciding controversy - Appeal allowed.
