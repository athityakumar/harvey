C. Subrahmanyam v K. Ramanjaneyullu and Others

Supreme Court of India

13 March 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Paripoornan, S.P. Bharucha, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2681; (1998) 8 SCC 703

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Election Constitution - Municipalities & Local Governments - Andhra Pradesh Panchayat Raj Act, 1994, s. 231 - Whether writ petition should have been entertained in view of remedy of election petition under Act? - High Court held that reference was made to a provision of Constitution in that order and, therefore, writ petition would lie and impugned order was quashed for violation of s. 231 - Held, main question for decision being non-compliance of a provision of Act which is a ground for an election petition in r. 12 framed under Act - Writ petition should not have been entertained for this purpose - Appeal allowed.
