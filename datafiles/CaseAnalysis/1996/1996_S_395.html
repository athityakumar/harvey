T. Venkata Narayana And Ors. v Smt.Venkata Subbamma (Dead) And Ors

Supreme Court of India

29 March 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 695; (1996) 4 SCC 457; 1996 (3) AD(SC) 764; AIR 1996 SC 1807; 1996 (28) ALR 70; 1996 (3) AWC 1264; 1996 (2) CCC 188; 1996 (2) CLT 256; 1996 (1) HLR 510; JT 1996 (4) SC 425; 1996 (2) MLJ(SC) 81; 1996(3) SCALE 689; [1996] 3 S.C.R. 1042; 1998 (Supp) RD 1; 1996 (3) Supreme 765

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Family & Personal; Women & Children

Keywords: Succession & Inheritance

Summary: Contract and Commercial - Specific Relief Act, 1963, s. 38 - Succession Act, 1925, s. 22 - Hindu Succession Act,1956 - Suit for perpetual injunction against alienation of immoveable property - Permissibility to adduce secondary evidence to prove execution of will on the basis of which possession is claimed - Held, not permissible as suit for injunction cannot be converted into suit for probation of will - Appeal allowed.
