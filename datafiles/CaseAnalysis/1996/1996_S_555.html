State Of U.P. & Ors. v Nand Kiskore Shukla & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

11 March 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3323; (1996) 3 SCC 750; (1996) SCC (L&S) 867; 1996 (3) AD(SC) 317; AIR 1996 SC 1561; 1996 ALJ 695; 1996 (1) CLR 786; JT 1996 (3) SC 551; 1996 (2) LLJ 672; 1996 (2) LLN 241; 1996(3) SCALE 69; [1996] 3 S.C.R. 219; 1996 (2) SLR 504; 1996 (3) Supreme 242; 1996 (2) UJ 471; 1996 (2) UPLBEC 1443

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution, art. 319 - Disciplinary authority to decide the nature of punishment to be imposed on delinquent employee under disciplinary proceedings - Order of removal when only one out of 5 charges was proved - Held, Court will not go into the question as appellate - Moreover when removal does not cast stigma on delinquent - Appeal allowed.
