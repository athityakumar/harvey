Collector of Central Excise, Bombay v Jayant Dalai Private Limited

Supreme Court of India

29 October 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, S.B. Majmudar

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3898; (1997) 10 SCC 402; 1997 (68) ECR 771; 1996 (88) E.L.T. 638; 1996 (17) RLT 1125

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excise Tariff, Items 15-A (2) and 68 - Whether Monomer castings produced from caprolactum are classifiable as articles of plastic under item 15-A (2)? - Held, having regard to the Tariff Advice which was issued by the Central Board of Excise and Customs, it is difficult to see how the Collector could have held otherwise - Appeal dismissed.
