State of Bihar and Others v Bihar Distillery Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

03 December 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, K.S. Paripoornan

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1604; (1997) 2 SCC 453; 1996 (9) AD(SC) 153; AIR 1997 SC 1511; AIR 1997 SCW 259; JT 1996 (10) SC 854; 1996(8) SCALE 768; [1996] Supp9 S.C.R. 479; 1997 (1) Supreme 121

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Excise - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 226 and 245 - Bihar and Orissa Excise Act, 1915 - Deduction of component of 70 paise per LPL as warehouse maintenance charges while fixation of cost price of country liquor - Constitutionality of Bihar Excise (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1995 - Held, unconstitutionality must be plainly and clearly established before enactment is declared void - Not illegal - Appeal allowed.
