Orissa Mining Corporation and Another v Ananda Chandra Prusty

Supreme Court of India

05 November 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1645; (1996) 11 SCC 600; 1996 (8) AD(SC) 731; AIR 1997 SC 2274; 1997 (34) AllCriC 8; 1997 (35) AllCriC 267; 1997 (1) CLR 188; 1997 CRLJ 353; 1997 (75) FLR 100; JT 1996 (10) SC 571; 1997 (1) LLN 130; 1996(8) SCALE 153; 1997 (1) SLJ 133; 1997 (1) SLR 286; [1996] Supp8 S.C.R. 433; 1996 (8) Supreme 190

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, sch. 2, item 3 - Evidence Act, 1872, ss. 101 and 104 - Industrial Disputes - Burden of proof - Whether on whom lies in disciplinary enquiry? - Held, does not always lie on department but depends upon nature of charges and explanation - Appeal dismissed.
