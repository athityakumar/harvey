Durga Das v The Collector & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

07 August 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 926; (1996) 5 SCC 618; 1996 (6) AD(SC) 513; AIR 1996 SC 2786; 1996 (3) CCC 394B; 1996 (3) CLT 283; JT 1996 (Supp) SC 462; 1996(6) SCALE 99; [1996] Supp4 S.C.R. 370

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Land and Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 23 - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 35 - Compensation for land acquisition - Claimant purchasing some lands from one of the co-owner brothers of family - No lease of other family lands - Whether he can claim compensation on basis of mutation entries on basis of earlier sale - Held, cannot claim - Appeal dismissed.
