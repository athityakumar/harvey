State of Uttar Pradesh and Another v Shri Krishna Pandey

Supreme Court of India

01 March 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3761; (1996) 9 SCC 395; (1996) SCC (L&S) 1250; 1996 (3) AD(SC) 290; AIR 1996 SC 1656; 1996 (1) CLR 915; [1996] 3 S.C.R. 183; 1996 (3) Supreme 354

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 309 - Central Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1972, r. 351A - Limitation - Departmental enquiry - Retired employee - Alleged embezzlement by delinquent employee taking place four years before to date of his retirement - Held, four years from date of misconduct proceedings must be instituted - Departmental proceedings vitiates.
