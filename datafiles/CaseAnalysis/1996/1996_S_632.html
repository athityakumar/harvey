Raja Ram Yadav and Others v State of Bihar

Supreme Court of India

11 April 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.N. Ray, B.L. Hansaria

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3029; (1996) 9 SCC 287; (1996) SCC (Cr) 1004; 1996 (3) AD(SC) 573; AIR 1996 SC 1613; AIR 1996 SCW 1882; 1996 (2) CCR 145; 1996 (2) Crimes 59; 1996 CRLJ 2307; JT 1996 (4) SC 140; 1997 (6) KarLJ 62; 1996(3) SCALE 430; [1996] Supp1 S.C.R. 174; 1996 (3) Supreme 482

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 302 - Death sentence - Rarest of rare cases - Murder, premeditated and calculated modus with extreme cruelty and brutality - Conviction based on evidence of child witness - Not telling names of four assailants to another witness - Held, still not fit to "Rarest of rare cases" - No capital punishment.
