Bani Singh & Ors. v State Of U.P.

Supreme Court of India

09 July 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, N.P. Singh, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3941; (1996) 4 SCC 720; (1996) SCC (Cr) 848; AIR 1996 SC 2439; 1996 ALJ 1399; 1996 (3) Crimes 54; 1996 CRLJ 3491; JT 1996 (6) SC 287; 1996(5) SCALE 126; [1996] Supp3 S.C.R. 247

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Land & Property

Keywords: Adjourn, Disposal on Merits

Summary: Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, s. 386 - Both appellant and his lawyer absent on appointed day for hearing - Court not compelled to adjourn case but should dispose of appeal on merits - Whether appeal should be dismissed? - Held, dismissal of appeal simply because of non - prosecution was not contemplated.
