Ajay Krishan Shinghal and Others v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

06 August 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2151; (1996) 10 SCC 721; 1996 (6) AD(SC) 644; AIR 1996 SC 2677; AIR 1996 SCW 3343; 1996 (3) CCC 383; JT 1996 (7) SC 301; 1996(6) SCALE 29; [1996] Supp4 S.C.R. 319; 1996 (6) Supreme 733; 1996 (2) UJ 636

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Delhi Development Act, 1957 - Land Acquisition Act 1894, ss. 4 and 3(f) - Cantonments Act, 1924, s. 5 - Acquisition of vast area of land for public purpose which is situated within Delhi Cantonment for planned development - Notification u/s. 4 not giving elaborate details of manner in which development is to be undertaken - Held, notification cannot be void on such account.
