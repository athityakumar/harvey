Municipal Commissioner, Thane and Anr v H and R Johnson (India) Limited and Anr

Supreme Court of India

06 August 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 176; (1996) 5 SCC 507; 1996 (6) AD(SC) 56; AIR 1996 SC 2703; 1997 (1) Bom.C.R. 123; 1996 (66) ECR 653; JT 1996 (7) SC 473; 1996(5) SCALE 590; [1996] Supp4 S.C.R. 301; 1996 (6) Supreme 493; 1996 (2) UJ 556

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Municipalities & Local Government - Indirect Tax - Maharashtra Municipalities Act, 1965, s. 105 - Maharashtra Municipalities (Octroi) Rules, 1968, sch. 1, class IV, entries 45 and 47 - Contract Act, 1872, s. 73 - (A) Octroi Rules, - Clay used for manufacturing ceramic tiles - Corporation though claiming application of higher chargeable entry, i.e. 45 not producing material in proof of their stand that clay was stone powder included in said entry - Whether clay imported by assessee is 'earth of any other kind' mentioned in entry 47 and taxable at lower rate? - (B) Contract Act, s. 73 - Refund of duty - Assessee moving for stay of collection of duty at higher rate - High Court refusing interim relief, observing that if assessee succeeded, corporation will have to refund excess duty - Parties are bound by that order - Can a plea be made by corporation for exemption from refund on ground of financial burden? - Held, (A) Is 'earth of any other kind' and taxable at lower rate - (B) No plea can be made by corporation - Appeal dismissed.
