Dhannalal v D. P. Vijayvargiya and Others

Supreme Court of India

07 May 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

N.P. Singh, Faizan Uddin

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3588; (1996) 4 SCC 652; (1996) SCC (Cr) 816; AIR 1996 SC 2155; 1996 (2) CCC 318; [1996] 87 Comp Cas 240; JT 1996 (5) SC 601; 1996 (2) KarLJ 328; 1996 (2) KLT 283; 1997 (1) MLJ(SC) 15; 1996(4) SCALE 458; 1996 (2) SCJ 466; [1996] Supp2 S.C.R. 417

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Civil Procedure; Corporate

Keywords: Deletion, Amending Act, Motor Vehicles (Amendment) Act, 1994

Summary: Carriers &Transportation - Motor Vehicles Act, 1988, s. 166(3) - Repeal of s. 166(3) of Act - Object of repeal - To save victims of accident or their heirs from grave injustice caused due to rejection of petition on ground of limitation - Whether claim petition pending on date on which s. 166(3) stood repealed, can be dismissed? - On ground that it was barred by limitation - Held, it could not be dismissed - Appeal allowed.
