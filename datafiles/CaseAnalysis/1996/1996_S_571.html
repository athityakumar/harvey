Lohara Steel Industries Limited and Another v State of Andhra Pradesh and Another

Supreme Court of India

20 December 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar, A.M. Ahmadi

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1567; (1997) 2 SCC 37; 1997 (1) AD(SC) 414; AIR 1997 SC 1694; JT 1997 (1) SC 326; 1997(1) SCALE 27; 1997 (1) SCJ 153; 1997 (105) STC 30; [1996] Supp10 S.C.R. 898; 1997 (1) Supreme 519

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 304(a) - Andhra Pradesh General Sales Tax Act, 1957, s. 9(1) - Notification granting exemption from sales tax to re-rolled finished products of steel re-rollers - Validity of notification on ground that exemption is granted only to products manufactured inside State - Held, discriminatory and violative of art. 304(a) of Constitution - So struck down to the extent it offends art. 304(a) - Appeal allowed.
