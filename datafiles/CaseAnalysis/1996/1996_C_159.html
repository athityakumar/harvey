Messrs Psi Data Systems Limited v Collector of Central Excise

Supreme Court of India

17 December 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, S.C. Sen

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2055; (1997) 2 SCC 78; 1997 (1) AD(SC) 176; AIR 1997 SC 785; 1997 (68) ECR 377; 1997 (89) E.L.T. 3; JT 1996 (11) SC 458; 1997 (18) RLT 1; 1996(9) SCALE 443; [1996] Supp10 S.C.R. 266

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Central Excise Tariff Act 1986, Item 85.24 - Assessable value for levy of duty on sale of computers was in dispute - Whether value of software sold along with computer be included in assessable value of computer for purposes of excise duty? - Held, answer in negative.
