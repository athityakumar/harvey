Common Cause, A Registered Society, through its Director v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 November 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, S.B. Majmudar

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1996; (1996) 6 SCC 775; (1997) SCC (Cr) 42; 1996 (8) AD(SC) 746; AIR 1997 SC 1539; AIR 1997 SCW 290; 1997 AllCriC 342; 1997 (1) Bom.C.R. 636; 1997 (1) CCR 248; 1996 (4) CLT 290; [1998] 94 Comp Cas 781; 1997 CRLJ 195; 1996 JCC 720; JT 1996 (Supp) SC 86; 1996(8) SCALE 557; [1996] Supp9 S.C.R. 296; 1996 (8) Supreme 302

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Constitution; Corporate; Criminal

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 21 - Whether direction by Supreme Court for release on bail, discharge or acquittal of undertrial is valid, where there is pendency of criminal proceedings for a very long time? - Held, direction of Supreme Court for discharge or acquittal or release on bail of undertrials is justified.
