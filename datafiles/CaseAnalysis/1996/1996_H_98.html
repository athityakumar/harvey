State of Madhya Pradesh v Hiralal and Others

Supreme Court of India

15 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1086; (1996) 7 SCC 523; 1996 (1) AD(SC) 874; AIR 1996 SC 1720; 1996 (27) ALR 313; 1996 (1) CCC 186A; JT 1996 (1) SC 669; 1996 (1) RentLR 236; 1996(1) SCALE 35; [1996] 1 S.C.R. 480; 1996 (1) Supreme 753

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property

Summary: Civil Procedure - Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894 - Notices deemed to have been served - Respondents are not entitled to the benefit of the provisions of Land Acquisition Act, 1894 as amended by Act 68 of 1984 - They are entitled to solatium at 15% and interest at 6% on the enhanced compensation from the date of taking possession till date of deposit - Appeal allowed.
