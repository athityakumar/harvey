Collector Of Central Excise,Hyderabad Etc.Etc. v M/S.Vazir Sultan Tobacco Companylimited, Hyderabad Etc.Etc.

Supreme Court of India

28 February 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, S.C. Sen, G.T. Nanavati

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 617; (1996) 3 SCC 434; 1996 (2) AD(SC) 580; AIR 1996 SC 3025; 1996 (63) ECR 359; 1996 (83) E.L.T. 3; JT 1996 (3) SC 112; 1996 (13) RLT 291; 1996(2) SCALE 603; [1996] 2 S.C.R. 1194; 1996 (3) Supreme 161

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Keywords: Central Excises And Customs Laws (Amendment) Act, 1991, Finance Act, 1978

Summary: Indirect Tax - Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, s. 3 - Finance Act, 1978, s. 37(1) - Levy of special duty of Excise - Levy came into effect on and from 01-03-1978 and was to remain in force till 31-03-1979 - Taxable event is manufacture or production of goods and not removal of goods - Goods manufactured prior to 01-03-1978 but removed after 01-03-1978 - Whether would attract levy of duty - Held, would not attract levy of duty - Appeal dismissed.
