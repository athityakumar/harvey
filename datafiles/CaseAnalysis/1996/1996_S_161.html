Uma Shanker (Dead) And Ors. v Sarabjeet (Dead) By Lrs. And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

23 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar, M.M. Punchhi

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1133; (1996) 2 SCC 371; 1996 (1) AD(SC) 806; AIR 1996 SC 1005; 1996 (1) CLT 387; JT 1996 (1) SC 476; 1996 (4) KarLJ 155; 1996(1) SCALE 479B; 1998 (Supp) RD 340B; 1996 (1) Supreme 506; 1996 (1) UJ 289

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Rent Control - Banaras State Tenancy Act, 1949, s.159 - CPC,1908 - Suit for possession by party in possession as per compromise decree and subsequently being dispossessed - Whether entitled to sue u/s.159 on basis of title under compromise decree - Held, would be entitled, since said suit not barred u/s. 47 of CPC, 1908 - Appeal allowed.
