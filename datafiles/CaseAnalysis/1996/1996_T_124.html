E. Ramakrishnan and Others v State of Kerala and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 September 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

Faizan Uddin, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2121; (1996) 10 SCC 565; 1996 (7) AD(SC) 330; JT 1996 (9) SC 286; 1997 (1) LLJ 1215; 1996(7) SCALE 76; 1996 (3) SLJ 311; 1997 (1) SLR 581; [1996] Supp5 S.C.R. 533; 1996 (7) Supreme 648

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Constitution - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 320 - Whether High Court was correct in directing respondents to appoint candidates selected through PSC? - Posts are to be filled up through selection by PSC recruitment norms - Govt. cannot take any decision contrary to Constitution to regularise services of candidates dehors recruitment rules and statutory process for selection through PSC - Held, HC has rightly given directions to Govt. to notify 30 vacancies or vacancies existing to fill up from amongst candidates selected by PSC - Appeal dismissed.
