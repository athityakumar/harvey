Abrol Watches Private Limited v Collector of Customs, Bombay

Supreme Court of India

17 December 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 59; (1997) 11 SCC 321; 1998 (77) ECR 246; 1997 (92) E.L.T. 311

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Customs - Customs Act, Sch. 1, Ch. 98 - Import of horological machinery - Classification and exemption granted under two notifications - Appellant uses classification of former and prefers exemption under second notification - Whether entitled to benefit of exemption under second notification? - Held, yes, appellant can claim exemption - Horological notification exempted horological machines, etc., without specifying heading under which they fell - They were entitled to benefit of exemption thereunder - Project imports notification clearly specified that exemption thereunder granted would not affect exemption granted in respect of customs duty under any other notification - Appeal allowed.
