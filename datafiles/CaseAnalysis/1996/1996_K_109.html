State of Punjab v Kesar Singh

Supreme Court of India

22 July 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, S.B. Majmudar

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 932; (1996) 5 SCC 495; (1996) SCC (Cr) 1034; 1996 (5) AD(SC) 741; AIR 1996 SC 2512; AIR 1996 SCW 3120; 1996 (4) CCR 235; 1996 CRLJ 3586; JT 1996 (Supp) SC 481; 1996(5) SCALE 444; [1996] Supp3 S.C.R. 787

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, s. 433 - Appeal against premature release of convict - Whether High Court has power to direct consideration of case of premature release to Govt.? - Held, HC can't direct any provision of u/s. 433 of CrPC as power to deal with provisions rests with appropriate Govt. - Appeal Allowed.
