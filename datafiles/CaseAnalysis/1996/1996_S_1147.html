State of Himachal Pradesh v Suresh Kumar Verma and Another

Supreme Court of India

24 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3143; (1996) 7 SCC 562; (1996) SCC (L&S) 645; 1996 (2) AD(SC) 239; AIR 1996 SC 1565; 1996 (33) ATC 336; 1996 (72) FLR 804; JT 1996 (2) SC 455; 1996 LabIC 1265; 1996 (1) LLN 299; 1996(2) SCALE 307; [1996] 1 S.C.R. 972; 1996 (2) SLR 321; 1996 (2) Supreme 592

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 16 and 309 - Appointment on daily wage basis is not appointment to post according to Rules - Termination of daily wagers on end of project - Whether directions can be issued by Court to re-engage or appoint them against existing vacancies? - Held, cannot be given - Appeal allowed.
