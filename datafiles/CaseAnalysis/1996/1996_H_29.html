Financial Commissioner (Taxation) Punjab and Others v Harbhajan Singh

Supreme Court of India

22 March 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2755; (1996) 9 SCC 281; 1996 (3) AD(SC) 669; AIR 1996 SC 3287; 1996 (3) CCC 40; 1996 (2) CLT 306; JT 1996 (4) SC 326; 1996 (1) RentLR 511; 1996(3) SCALE 561; [1996] 3 S.C.R. 812; 1996 (5) Supreme 136

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Land & Property

Keywords: Evacuee Property & Displaced Persons

Summary: Land & Property - Practice & Procedure - Displaced Persons (Compensation And Rehabilitation) Act, 1954, s. 33 - (A) Assignment - Assignee's brother obtained another assignment by fraud in collusion with revenue officials - Whether Commr. is empowered to correct same in revisional jurisdiction by treating it as suo motu review - (B) Grant of assignment - Can settlement officer granting assignment sit over his order as Appellate Authority as chief settlement Commr. - Held, (A) Commr. is so empowered -

(B) Cannot sit over his order as appellate authority - Appeal allowed.
