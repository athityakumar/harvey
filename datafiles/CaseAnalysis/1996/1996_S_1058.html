Ram Ganesh Tripathi and Others v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 December 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2052; (1997) SCC (L&S) 186; (1997) 1 SCC 621; 1997 (1) AD(SC) 304; AIR 1997 SC 1446; AIR 1997 SCW 126; 1997 (75) FLR 554; JT 1997 (1) SC 139; 1997 LabIC 301; 1996(9) SCALE 381; 1997 (1) SCJ 68; 1997 (1) SLJ 174; [1996] Supp10 S.C.R. 248; 1997 (4) Supreme 293; 1997 (4) Supreme 294; 1997 (1) UJ 558; 1997 (3) UPLBEC 1672

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Treating

Summary: Service - Uttar Pradesh Nagar Mahapalika Adhiniyam, 1959, s. 540 - Uttar Pradesh Palika (Centralised) Service Rules, 1966, rr. 9, 21 A and 40 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Jurisdiction of High Court - Writ Petition challenging selection grade given to some ad hoc appointees who were junior to petitioner - Meanwhile, seniority list published by Govt. showing petitioner as senior to ad hoc appointees - Writ petition dismissed on ground that list did not survive in view of seniority list - Whether dismissal of petition proper in view of grievance of petitioner that Govt. wrongly treated ad hoc appointees as regular appointees from certain date - Held, ad hoc employees cannot be treated as employees regularly appointed from date earlier to date of their regularisation nor Govt. can count their seniority from such earlier date in view of rr. 9 and 21-A - Decision of Govt. to treat them as permanently appointed promotees since two years after their date of appointment which was before their regularisation liable to be quashed - Appeal allowed.
