Supreme Court of India

27 September 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Prevention of Corruption Act 1987

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 311 - Departmental enquiry - Stay till conclusion of criminal trial - When may be ordered - Held, stay of disciplinary proceedings cannot be, and should not be, a matter of course - Regard to facts and circumstances of a given case.
