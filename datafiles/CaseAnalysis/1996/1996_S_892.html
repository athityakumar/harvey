State of Punjab and Others v Mohinder Singh Chawla Etc

Supreme Court of India

17 December 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2054; (1997) 2 SCC 83; (1997) SCC (L&S) 294; AIR 1997 SC 1225; JT 1997 (1) SC 416; 1997 (3) LLN 262; 1997(1) SCALE 135; 1997 (2) SLJ 25; 1997 (1) SLR 745; 1996 (Supp) AWC 745; [1996] Supp10 S.C.R. 279; 1997 (1) Supreme 46; 1997 (1) Supreme 546

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Reimbursement

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 21 - Right to life includes right to health - Medical reimbursement to State Govt. employee - Approval granted for specialized treatment in hospital outside State - Claim for reimbursement of room rent charges, rejected - Validity - Held, cannot be rejected - Appeal disposed of.
