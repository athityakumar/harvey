Pilla Sitaram Patrudu and Others v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 March 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2765; (1996) 8 SCC 637; (1996) SCC (L&S) 1086; 1996 (4) AD(SC) 450; AIR 1997 SC 250; 1997 (1) AWC 75; JT 1996 (4) SC 731; 1996(4) SCALE 192; [1996] 3 S.C.R. 870; 1996 (2) SLR 892; 1996 (4) Supreme 544

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16 - Promotion - Selection by direct recruitment - Delay in appointment for no fault of candidate - (A) Whether plea that he was not qualified for promotion since he had not completed required years of service tenable? - (B) Whether he is entitled to ranking given in select list and to be considered for promotion? - Held, (A) plea not tenable - (B) He is entitled to ranking given in select list and to be considered for promotion - Petition dismissed.
