Nityanand Sharma And Anr. v State Of Bihar And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

02 February 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, B.L. Hansaria, S.B. Majmudar

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3459; (1996) 3 SCC 576; 1996 (1) AD(SC) 986; AIR 1996 SC 2306; AIR 1996 SCW 782; 1996 (1) CLT 465; JT 1996 (2) SC 117; 1996 LabIC 975; 1996(1) SCALE 743; 1996 (2) SCJ 273; [1996] 2 S.C.R. 1; 1996 (1) SLR 765; 1996 (2) Supreme 167; 1996 (1) UJ 604

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Constitution of India, Arts.342, 366(25) and 348; Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes Orders (Amendment) Act, 1976, s.4, Sch.II, Part III; Evidence Act, 1872, s.57 - Scheduled Tribe - 'Lohars' - Held, not Scheduled tribe but other backward class - 'Lohar' wrongly included as ST in Hindi translated version - judicial notice - English version being an authoritative text of Act -Court cannot give declaration of social status as Scheduled Tribes in Act.
