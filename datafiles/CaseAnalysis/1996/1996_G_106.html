Sk.Sattar Sk. Mohd.Choudhari v Gundappa Amabadas Bukate

Supreme Court of India

11 October 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Saghir Ahmad, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1839; (1996) 6 SCC 373; 1996 (8) AD(SC) 145; AIR 1997 SC 998; 1997 (1) MLJ(SC) 128; 1997 (1) RCR(Rent) 98; 1996(7) SCALE 607; [1996] Supp7 S.C.R. 450; 1996 (8) Supreme 288

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure; Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Land & Property - Transfer of Property Act , 1882 , ss. 37, 109 and 111 - Hyderabad Houses (Rent Eviction and Lease) Control Act, 1954, s. 15 - Partition between co-lessors - (A) Whether suit for eviction filed by co-lessor to whose portion leased property fell on partition maintainable? - (B) Whether tenant can object to partition? - Held, (A) suit for eviction is maintainable - (B) Tenant cannot object to partition although he can show that partition was not bona fide - Appeal allowed .
