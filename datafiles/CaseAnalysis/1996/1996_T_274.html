The British Machinary Supplies Co. v The Union Of India & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

06 August 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 903; (1996) 9 SCC 663; 1996 (6) AD(SC) 52; AIR 1996 SC 2731; 1996 (4) CCR 96; 1996 (66) ECR 213; 1996 (86) E.L.T. 449; JT 1996 (7) SC 169; 1996(5) SCALE 645; [1996] Supp4 S.C.R. 294; 1996 (6) Supreme 284

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Indirect Tax - Customs - Customs Act, 1962, sch., entries 41, 41(1), 42(2) and 84 - Sewing machines - Domestic or industrial machine - (A) Determination circular issued by Central Board of excise and customs that machines requiring for operation 1/4 H. P. or more would fall outside scope of domestic machines - If relevant - Relating hooks complete with bobbin case requiring 1/3 H. P. and 1/2 H. P. for operation - (B) Whether be treated as spare parts of industrial sewing machines or of domestic sewing machines-Held, (A) Is relevant and binding on Deptt. - (B) Should be treated as spare parts of industrial sewing machines and not of domestic sewing machines - Appeal allowed.
