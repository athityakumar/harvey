Suresh Kumar Jain v Shanti Swarup Jain and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 November 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.N. Ray, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1934; (1997) 9 SCC 298; AIR 1997 SC 2291; 1997 ALJ 1209; 1998 (32) ALR 751; 1997 (1) RCR(Rent) 411; 1997 (1) RentLR 11; 1996(8) SCALE 536; [1996] Supp9 S.C.R. 28; 1997 (5) Supreme 280

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Uttar Pradesh Municipalities Act, 1916

Summary: Contempt Of Courts Act, 1971 - Uttar Pradesh Urban Buildings (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act 1972, ss. 2(2), explnation 1 - Determination of deemed date of construction of tenanted premises was in question - Tenant inducted in shop who is in exclusive possession of it after evicting of earlier tenant in certain year - Year in which tenant was inducted would be date of construction as tenant clearly indicated at that time unit was fit for occupation - Eviction suit filed much after expiry of ten years i.e. period of exemption from date of construction - Held, it could not be allowed on ground that premises were exempted from applicability of act.
