State Of Orissa & Ors. v Narain Prasad & Ors.,Etc.Etc.

Supreme Court of India

03 September 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, K.S. Paripoornan

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 891; (1996) 5 SCC 740; 1996 (7) AD(SC) 65; AIR 1997 SC 1493; JT 1996 (8) SC 50; 1996(6) SCALE 460; [1996] Supp5 S.C.R. 465; 1996 (8) Supreme 647

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Excise - Bihar and Orissa Excise Act, 1915, ss. 22 and 89 - Orissa (Excise Exclusive Privilege) Rules, 1970, rr. 6 and 6A - Excise Duty - Contract to lift particular quantity of liquor every month along with obligation to remit excise duty - Whether depends upon sale/purchase of liquor, lifting of minimum guaranteed quota - Held, is independent of sale/purchase and unrelated to lifting of minimum quota - Appeal allowed.
