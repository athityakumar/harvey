Vellore Citizens Welfare Forum v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 August 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, Faizan Uddin, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1075; (1996) 5 SCC 647; AIR 1996 SC 2715; AIR 1996 SCW 3399; 1996 (3) CLT 336; JT 1996 (7) SC 375; 1996(6) SCALE 194; [1996] Supp5 S.C.R. 241; 1996 (7) Supreme 307

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Environment, Wildlife & Animal; Municipalities & Local Governments; Water

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 21 and 32 - Environment Protection Act, 1986, s. 3(3) - Environmental protection - Water pollution - Tanneries - Untreated effluent finally discharged in river main source of water supply to residents of area - Held, comprehensive directions for maintaining stipulated standards by Pollution Control Board.
