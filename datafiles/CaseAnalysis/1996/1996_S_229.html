United Bank of India v Sh. Naresh Kumar and Others

Supreme Court of India

18 September 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2429; (1996) 6 SCC 660; 1996 (7) AD(SC) 208; AIR 1997 SC 3; 1996 (2) BC 550; 1996 (4) CCC 27; 1996 (4) CLT 51; [1997] 90 Comp Cas 329; 1997 (2) MLJ(SC) 1; 1996(6) SCALE 764; [1996] Supp6 S.C.R. 478; 1996 (7) Supreme 301

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Civil Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Civil Procedure Code, 1908, O. 6, r. 14, O. 29, r. 1 - Suit by bank for recovery of loan - Validity of dismissal on ground that plaint not signed and verified by competent person - Suit instituted or defended by Public Corporation like bank defeating public interest due to technical reason - Held, not proper - Appeal allowed.
