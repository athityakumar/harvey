Collector of Central Excise, Bombay v Kores (India) Limited, Thane

Supreme Court of India

10 December 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, S.C. Sen

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3703; (1997) 10 SCC 338; 1997 (68) ECR 760; 1997 (89) E.L.T. 441; 1997 (18) RLT 397

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Tariff advice - Tariff Item 68 - Stencil skin - Residuary tariff - Whether stencil skin was classifiable under residuary Tariff Item 68 for purposes of excise duty and not u/item 17(2) which covers paper and paper board, all sorts, including coated paper? Relied on CCE v Krishna Carbon Paper Co (1988 Indlaw SC 917) - Trade notices and Tariff advices were not relevant, as such in construing items in tariff Sch. - Held, a tariff advice or a trade notice issued by Board certainly does not bind tribunal or courts and an assessee may argue that it is erroneous, but it is not open to revenue to advance arguments that are contrary to terms thereof - Appeals dismissed.
