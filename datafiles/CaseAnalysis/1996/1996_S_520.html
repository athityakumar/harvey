Hashmattullah v State Of Madhya Pradesh And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

10 May 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, N.P. Singh

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3014; (1996) 4 SCC 391; 1996 (4) AD(SC) 657; AIR 1996 SC 2076; JT 1996 (5) SC 295; 1996(4) SCALE 556; 1996 (3) SCJ 113; [1996] Supp2 S.C.R. 755; 1996 (4) Supreme 362; 1996 (2) UJ 478

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Environment, Wildlife & Animal; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Competence Of The Legislature

Summary: Madhya Pradesh Agricultural Cattle Preservation Act, 1959, s. 4(1)(a) - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 19(1)(g), 48 and 226 - Validity - Total ban on slaughter of bulls and bullocks - Held, unreasonable restriction on fundamental rights of butchers - Sub-clause is held to be ultra vires.
