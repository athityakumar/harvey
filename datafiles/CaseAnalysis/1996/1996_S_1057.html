Raj Narain Prasad and Others v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

18 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3102; (1998) 8 SCC 473; (1998) SCC (L&S) 1697

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Service - Question regarding regularization of work charged employees and muster roll labour employed on different projects by State at different points of time - Held, liberal increase in workforce for first few years would satisfy large number of work charged employees who are working on different projects of each department for a number of years - Muster roll employees can only stand next to work charged employees and they could be absorbed as work charged employees to extent or at a certain percentage of posts failing vacant on latter being regularized under scheme - Petition disposed of.
