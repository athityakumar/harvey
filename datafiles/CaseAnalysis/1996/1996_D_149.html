Dr. Baljit Singh v State of Haryana

Supreme Court of India

09 December 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1731; (1997) SCC (L&S) 313; (1997) 1 SCC 754; AIR 1997 SC 2150; 1997 (2) ESC 1209; JT 1996 (11) SC 582; 1997 (2) LLJ 1070; 1997 (2) LLN 1002; 1997(1) SCALE 36; 1997 (1) SLR 743; [1996] Supp9 S.C.R. 695

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 311 - Indian Railway Establishment Code, 1951 - Voluntary retirement - Pendency of trial for serious offences in Criminal Court against employee - Three months notice of Voluntary retirement pending trial - Held, their jural relationship ceases on acceptance of request for voluntary retirement - Not merely on expiry of three month's notice period - Cannot be said to be voluntarily retired merely by handing over charge.
