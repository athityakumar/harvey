Swetambar Sthanakwasi Jainsamiti and Another v Alleged Committee of Managementsri R.J.I. Colleges Agra

Supreme Court of India

14 February 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3481; (1996) 3 SCC 11; 1996 (2) AD(SC) 184; AIR 1996 SC 1209; AIR 1996 SCW 1224; 1996 ALJ 598; 1996 (1) CLT 499; JT 1996 (3) SC 21; 1996 (2) MLJ(SC) 133; 1996(2) SCALE 195; 1996 (2) SCJ 336; [1996] 2 S.C.R. 623; 1996 (2) Supreme 137; 1996 (1) UJ 673

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution

Summary: Constitution - Constitution Of India, 1950, art.226 - Suit pending between parties - Interim and miscellaneous orders passed by Trial Court - Remedy of appeal or revision available to parties - Whether writ petition against such orders, maintainable - Held, not maintainable - Whether HC has the jurisdiction to interfere with interim/miscellaneous orders of Civil Court ? - Held, HC cannot convert itself into an appellate or revisional Court and interfere with interim/miscellaneous orders of Civil Court - Appeal allowed.
