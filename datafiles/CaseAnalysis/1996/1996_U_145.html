Union of India and Others v M. Bhaskar and Others

Supreme Court of India

06 May 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

B.L. Hansaria, K. Ramaswamy, S.B. Majmudar

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2872; (1996) 4 SCC 416; (1996) SCC (L&S) 967; 1996 (73) FLR 1676; JT 1996 (5) SC 500; 1996(4) SCALE 670; 1996 (2) SCJ 610; 1996 (2) SLJ 25; 1996 (4) SLR 450; [1996] Supp2 S.C.R. 358; 1996 (5) Supreme 68; 1996 (3) UPLBEC 2071

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Eligibility - Experience

Summary: Administrative - Indian Railway Establishment Manual - Indian Railway Establishment Code, 1951 - Cleavage of opinion among various Central Administrative Tribunals (CATs) of country with regard to meaning and contents of memorandum, it brought about some changes in recruitment of Traffic/Commercial Apprentices - One of changes being that on and from 15-5-1987 recruitment of these apprentices would be made in pay scale of Rs. 1600-2660 (this scale earlier was Rs. 1400-2300) and, instead of all posts being filled up by promotions, ratio of promotees was made 75%, and of remaining 25%, 10% were required to come through Railway Recruitment Boards and 15% on basis of Limited Departmental Competitive Examination - Pre-1987 apprentices laid their claim for higher scale of pay on basis of 1987 memorandum; and it is this claim which has come to be allowed by majority of CATs - Held, tribunals which allowed benefit of pay scale of Rs. 1600-2660 to all Traffic/Commercial Apprentices irrespective of grade of posts held by them, not only misunderstood memorandum of 1987, but misconceived provisions relating to recruitment and promotion of these apprentices as finding place in Establishment Manual,set aside judgments of tribunals which held that pre-1987 Traffic/Commercial Apprentices had become entitled to higher pay scale of Rs. 1600-2660 by force of memorandum of 1987 - Contrary view taken is affirmed, judgment of Ernakulam Bench which declared memorandum as invalid is set aside, recovery of amounts already paid not allowed - Appeals disposed of.
