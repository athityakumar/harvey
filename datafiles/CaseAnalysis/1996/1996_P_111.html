Pondicherry Telephones, Telecommunication Department, Pondicherry v Messrs Flexi Packs

Supreme Court of India

12 September 1996

Case Analysis

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 4001; 1997 (5) CTJ 226

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection; Indirect Tax

Summary: Consumer Protection - Respondent a telephone subscriber - Abnormal bill - Amount reduced - Assurance to adjust against future bills - Disconnection - Consumer complaint - Whether appellant is liable to refund excess amount collected? - Normal course that Department should have adopted was to pay back that amount - Department was no right whatsoever to keep any amount belonging to a subscriber except those which are authorised by law for purpose of security or otherwise - Held, explanation tendered on behalf of appellant that computer installed in office does not have adjustment facilities is utterly unacceptable - Scrupulous observations of this simple principle would have spared department and also subscribe many inconveniences - Liable to refund amount - Appeal dismissed.
