Paschim Banga Khet Mazdoorsamity Of Ors. v State Of West Bengal And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

06 May 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, G.T. Nanavati

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2871; (1996) 4 SCC 37; 1996 (4) AD(SC) 360; AIR 1996 SC 2426; 1996 (2) CCC 286; 1996 (2) CLT 343; JT 1996 (6) SC 43; 1996 LabIC 2054; 1997 (1) MLJ(SC) 7; 1996(4) SCALE 282; 1996 (3) SCJ 25; 1996 (6) SLR 346; [1996] Supp2 S.C.R. 331; 1996 (4) Supreme 260; 1996 (4) UPLBEC 2845

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure; Tort

Keywords: Risk

Summary: Constitution - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 21 and 32 - Fundamental rights - Govt. hospitals failed to provide timely emergency medical treatment - (A) Whether it amounts to breach of right guaranteed u/art. 21? - (B) Compensation for non fulfilment of constitutional obligation of State to provide adequate medical services to people and breach of art. 21 - Held, results in violation of his right to life - Compensation of Rs. 25,000/- granted to him - Furtherheld that State Govt. to take appropriate administrative action against officers - Petition disposed of.
