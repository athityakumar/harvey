State of Bihar and Another v Ranchi Zila Samta Party and Anr

Supreme Court of India

19 March 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, S.P. Bharucha, K.S. Paripoornan

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 651; (1996) 3 SCC 682; 1996 (3) AD(SC) 610; AIR 1996 SC 1515; 1996 (2) CLT 194; 1996 CRLJ 2168; JT 1996 (3) SC 751; 1997 (1) MLJ(SC) 31; 1996(3) SCALE 236; [1996] 3 S.C.R. 663; 1996 (2) UJ 138

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Defence & Security Forces; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Criminal Procedure Code, 1974, s.156; Constitution of India, Arts.226 and 136 - Investigation - Fodder scam, defalcation of public funds - Falsification of accounts in Animal Husbandry Department of State Govt. - State police investigating into - High Court transferring to CBI, an independent agency - Held, may command public confidence - No interference.
