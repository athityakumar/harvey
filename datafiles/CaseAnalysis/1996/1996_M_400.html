Mohd. Aslam v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

22 March 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Venkataswami, N.P. Singh, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2759; (1996) 2 SCC 749; 1996 (3) AD(SC) 409; AIR 1996 SC 1611; 1996 (2) CLT 62; JT 1996 (5) SC 566; [1996] 3 S.C.R. 782; 1996 (3) Supreme 51

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Election; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Representation of People Act, 1951, s. 123(3 A) - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 32 - Corrupt practice - Use of religion - Held, no confliction with concept of secularism held in S R Bommai case - Apprehension that it would enable misuse of religion for making an appeal for votes in election - Is misconceived.
