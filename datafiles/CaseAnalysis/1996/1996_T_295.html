Tribhuvandas G. Patel v Commissioner of Income Tax

Supreme Court of India

14 February 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, K.S. Paripoornan

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3518; (1998) 8 SCC 509; 1999 (157) CTR 519; [1999] 236 ITR 515

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 47(ii) - Whether amount actually received towards share of profit from firm or share of profit determined after assessment of income of firm, be included in total income of assessee? - Assessment of income of firm results in accruing of share of profit - Held, actual amount worked out in assessment is to be considered as income of assessee - Appeal dismissed.
