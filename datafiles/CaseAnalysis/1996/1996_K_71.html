Kanshi Ram v Om Prakash Jawal And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

15 April 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 710; (1996) 4 SCC 593; 1996 (4) AD(SC) 402; AIR 1996 SC 2150; AIR 1996 SCW 2606; 1996 (28) ALR 111; 1996 (2) CCC 366; 1996 (2) CLT 329; JT 1996 (4) SC 733; 1996 (2) MLJ(SC) 80; 1996(4) SCALE 194; 1996 (2) SCJ 594; [1996] Supp1 S.C.R. 330; 1996 (4) Supreme 285; 1996 (2) UJ 316

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Land and Property - Specific Relief Act, 1963, ss. 20 and 21 - Grant of decree of specific performance is not automatic - Rise in prices of property subsequent to agreement to sale - And vendor claimed alternative relief - Held, specific performance would be inequitable and unjust - Hence vendee directed to pay a particular sum to vendor - Appeal allowed.
