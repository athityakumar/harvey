State Of Haryana And Ors v Rani Devi And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

15 July 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

N.P. Singh, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2889; (1996) 5 SCC 308; (1996) SCC (L&S) 1162; AIR 1996 SC 2445; 1996 (74) FLR 2023; JT 1996 (6) SC 646; 1996 LabIC 2065; 1996 (2) LLN 309; 1996(5) SCALE 338; 1996 (3) SLJ 88; 1996 (4) SLR 505; [1996] Supp3 S.C.R. 560; 1996 (5) Supreme 451; 1996 (4) UPLBEC 2757

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Ex Gratia Payment

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 226, 309, 14 and 16 - Punjab Civil Services Rules, 1959, Chap. II, Para 2.6 - Compassionate appointment - Govt. Order extending benefit of appointment to one of dependents of 'deceased employee' - Claim for Compassionate appointment by widow of deceased working as Apprentice Canal Patwaris - Held, 'Employee' does not conceive casual or purely ad hoc employee or apprentice - Not tenable.
