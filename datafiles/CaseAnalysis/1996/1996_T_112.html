Rehmat v State of Haryana

Supreme Court of India

03 September 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Kurdukar, M.K. Mukherjee

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2106; (1996) 10 SCC 346; (1996) SCC (Cr) 1272; 1996 (6) AD(SC) 414; AIR 1997 SC 1526; 1996 (4) CCR 75; 1996 (3) Crimes 238; 1997 CRLJ 764; JT 1996 (7) SC 663; 1996(6) SCALE 330; 1996 (6) Supreme 475

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Defence & Security Forces

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 307 - Arms Act, 1959 - Terrorist and Disruptive Activities (Prevention) Act, 1987, s. 6 - Attempt to murder - Plea of self-defence by accused appears to be true - Medical papers evidence not mentioning name of assailant despite of it being a medico legal case - Conviction challenged - Held, conviction of accused liable to be set aside - Appeal allowed.
