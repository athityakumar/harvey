Gurbachan Singh and Anothe v Shivalak Rubber Industries and Others

Supreme Court of India

23 February 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

Faizan Uddin, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2615; (1996) 2 SCC 626; 1996 (2) AD(SC) 446; AIR 1996 SC 3057; JT 1996 (2) SC 615; 1996 (1) RCR(Rent) 398; 1996 (1) RentLR 340; 1996(2) SCALE 431; [1996] 2 S.C.R. 997

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - East Punjab Urban Rent Restriction Act, 1949, s. 13(2)(iii) - (A) Whether appeal is a mixed question of law and fact Alleged additions and alterations in premises by tenant? - (B) Eviction - Involving material impairment of premises - Affecting fitness of premises for use for desirable practical purposes and intrinsic worth from point of view of landlord - Whether tenant liable to be evicted? - Held, (A) is a mixed question of law and fact - (B) Liable to be evicted - Appeal allowed.
