Supreme Court Monitoring Committee v Mussoorie Dehradun Development Authority and Others

Supreme Court of India

29 November 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, K. Venkataswami, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2000; (1997) 11 SCC 605

Case Digest

Subject: Environment, Wildlife & Animal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Environment, Wildlife & Animal - Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980, s. 2(u) - Indian Forest Act, 1927 - Stay at construction - State of UP and UOI questioned as regards applicability of provisions of Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 and Rules made thereunder - Whether, area in question is a forest and stands covered under 1980 Act, to prevent building activity on open areas within forest? - Directions issued to State of UP as well as MDDA to make list of any forest land for non-forest purposes without seeking prior approval of Central Govt. - While examining question regarding grant of ex post facto approval, Central Govt. will also enquire into matter whether these permissions were granted on extraneous considerations or were only by way of a bona fide mistake - Interim orders.
