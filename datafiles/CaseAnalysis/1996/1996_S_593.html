Dr. Dilip Kumar Deka and Another v State of Assam and Another

Supreme Court of India

10 September 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, M.K. Mukherjee, S.P. Kurdukar

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 2526; (1996) 6 SCC 234; 1996 (7) AD(SC) 833; AIR 1997 SC 440; AIR 1996 SCW 4046; 1996 (4) CCR 219; 1996 (4) Crimes 14; 1997 (75) FLR 306; JT 1996 (8) SC 544; 1996(6) SCALE 723; 1996 (3) SCJ 239; 1996 (3) SLJ 112; 1996 (5) SLR 640; [1996] Supp5 S.C.R. 763; 1996 (7) Supreme 143

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16 - Recruitment - Departmental list, quashment of - Two lists prepared - Legality of preparation of first list and appointment thereunder not raised before Administrative Tribunal - Held, cannot be quashed - Gross illegality in preparation, yet, as vacancies were not available - Same is therefore liable to be quashed.
