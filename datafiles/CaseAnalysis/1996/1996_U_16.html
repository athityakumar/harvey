U.P. Jal Nigam & Ors. v Narinder Kumar Agarwal

Supreme Court of India

31 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Punchhi, K.S. Paripoornan

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3444; (1996) 2 SCC 363; (1996) SCC (L&S) 519; 1996 (1) AD(SC) 885; AIR 1996 SC 1661; 1996 (33) ATC 217; 1996 (1) CLR 385; 1996 (72) FLR 702; JT 1996 (1) SC 641; 1996 (1) LLN 428; 1996(1) SCALE 624; 1996 (2) SCJ 161; [1996] 1 S.C.R. 1118; 1996 (1) SLR 743; 1996 (1) Supreme 691; 1996 (1) UJ 636

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution, art. 311 - Procedure for down grading entries in ACR - Determination of sustainability of down grading entries when neither reason mentioned nor change communicated to employee in form of advice - Held, cannot be sustained - Petition dismissed.
