Major Sing v Rattan Singh (Dead) By L.Rs. and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 December 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1749; (1997) 3 SCC 546; AIR 1997 SC 1906; AIR 1997 SCW 1659; 1997 (1) CLT 335; 1997 (1) HLR 741; JT 1997 (1) SC 404; 1997(1) SCALE 216; 2000 (Supp) RD 123; [1996] Supp9 S.C.R. 828; 1997 (1) Supreme 514

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Civil Procedure Code, 1908, s. 100 - Appellant is a predecessor-in-title of his sister - High Court gone into substantial question of law and found that reasons given by courts below are flimsy - Validity of interference - Held, HC right in interfering in matter - Appeal dismissed.
