Gajraj Singh Etc v State Transport Appellate Tribunaland Others Etc

Supreme Court of India

12 September 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, B.L. Hansaria, S.B. Majmudar

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1468; (1997) 1 SCC 650; AIR 1997 SC 412; JT 1996 (8) SC 356; 1996(7) SCALE 31; 1996 (Supp) AWC 678; [1996] Supp6 S.C.R. 172

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation

Keywords: Administration of Evacuee Property Act, 1950, Renewal Of Permit

Summary: General Clauses Act, 1897, s.6; Motor Vehicles Act, 1988, 81 and 217 - Repeal of M.V. Act 1939 - Re-enactment - Renewal of permit - State carriage permit granting under old Act - Held, not saved by s.217 (2) (a) of new Act and s.6 of General Clauses Act - S.6 would apply unless repealing Act shows intention contrary to such application - Contrary intention to be ascertained from all relevant provisions of repealing Act - Renewal under new Act - Should be preceded by grant of permit under s.72 of new Act.
