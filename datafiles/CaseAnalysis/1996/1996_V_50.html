 Vijay Singh Etc v Vijayalakshmi Ammal

Supreme Court of India

10 October 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

N.P. Singh, Kuldip Singh, M.K. Mukherjee, M.M. Punchhi, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 1743; (1996) 6 SCC 475; 1996 (8) AD(SC) 269; AIR 1997 SC 47; AIR 1996 SCW 198; JT 1996 (9) SC 408; 1997 (1) MLJ(SC) 98; 1997 (1) RCR(Rent) 177; 1997 (1) RentLR 480; 1996(7) SCALE 676; [1996] Supp7 S.C.R. 385; 1996 (8) Supreme 776; 1997 (1) UJ 83

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Keywords: Demolition And Reconstruction

Summary: Rent Control - Tamil Nadu Buildings (Lease and Rent Control) Act, 1960, s. 14(1)(b) - Eviction of tenants sought on ground that landlord proposes to demolish building in question to erect new building - If permission can be granted - Held, cannot be granted on mere asking of landlord - Rent controller should record finding as to bona fide requirement for purpose - Appeal dismissed.
