S.B.International Limited Etc. v Asstt. Director Of General Of F.T. And Ors.Etc

Supreme Court of India

24 January 1996

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, B.N. Kirpal

Where Reported

1996 Indlaw SC 3416; (1996) 2 SCC 439; 1996 (1) AD(SC) 956; AIR 1996 SC 2921; 1996 (82) E.L.T. 164; JT 1996 (1) SC 588; 1996(1) SCALE 576; [1996] 1 S.C.R. 910; 1996 (2) Supreme 519

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax; Trade

Keywords: Export And Import Policy

Summary: Trade - Imports and Exports (Control) Act, 1947, s. 3 - Export and Import Policy , 01-04-1992 and 31-03-1997, cl. 66 - Duty Exemption Scheme - Applicability of value addition norm to duty free advance import licence - Applicant-exporter not entitled on date of application so likely to suffer some loss or prejudice - If invoke rule of promissory estoppel - Theory of vested right to import - Plea of delay taken - Held, no ground to invoke - Plea not sustainable - Issuance of licences is not a formality nor mere ministerial function - Appeal from SLP No.607 of 1995 dismissed and appeal from SLP (C) No.23900 of 1995 allowed.
