Mahesh Prasad v State of Uttar Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

29 October 1954

Case Analysis

Bench

B. Jagannadhadas, Bijan Kumar Mukherjea, Vivian Bose

Where Reported

1954 Indlaw SC 11; AIR 1955 SC 70; 1955 CRLJ 249; 1955 (1) MLJ(SC) 83; 1955 SCJ 153; [1955] 1 S.C.R. 965

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Illegal Gratification, Indian Railway Establishment Code, 1951

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 161 - Indian Railway Establishment Code, 1951 - Prevention of Corruption Act, 1947 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 311 - Public servant receiving illegal gratification - Conviction of appellant confirmed by Sessions Court and in revision by High Court - Appellant caught red-handed while accepting money - Held, s. 161 applies to every public servant, no one specific - No reason to reverse findings of Lower Courts that sanction is valid - Appeal dismissed.
