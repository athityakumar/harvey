State Of Punjab And Ors. v Dev Dutt Kaushal Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

28 August 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, G.N. Ray

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1160; (1995) Supp4 SCC 748; (1996) SCC (L&S) 237; AIR 1996 SC 85; AIR 1995 SCW 3743; 1996 (32) ATC 185; JT 1995 (6) SC 225; 1995 LabIC 2485; 1995(5) SCALE 67; 1996 (1) SLJ 103; 1995 (8) SLR 664; [1995] Supp3 S.C.R. 96; 1995 (3) UPLBEC 1655

Case Digest

Subject: Education; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Constitution of India, art.311 -Pension benefit - Entitlement of - Teachers of private college - Management of college taken over by Govt. from private hand - Terms providing teachers working in private college on appointment under Govt. shall be treated as 'new entrants' - Govt. rules providing entitlement of pension after 10 years of service - And retirement at age of 58 years - Teachers claiming for retirement age at 60 years like under earlier management - And entitlement of pension on basis of inclusion of service with private management - Held, is not tenable - No entitlement of extension of retrial age and pension without direct serving for 10 years to Govt.
