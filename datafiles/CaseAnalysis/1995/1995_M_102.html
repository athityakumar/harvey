Mansukhlal Dhanraj Jain and Others v Eknath Vithal Ogale

Supreme Court of India

08 February 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Majmudar, S. Mohan

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1712; (1995) 2 SCC 665; AIR 1995 SC 1102; 1995 (3) Bom.C.R. 240; JT 1995 (8) SC 293; 1995(1) SCALE 513; [1995] 1 S.C.R. 996; 1995 (1) UJ 559

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Keywords: Swadeshi Cotton Mills Company Limited (Acquisition and Transfer of Undertakings) Act, 1986

Summary: Presidency Small Cause Courts Act, 1882, s. 41(1)- Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 9 - Relating to recovery of possession - Held, covers suit for injunction for restraining licensor from effecting forcible recovery of possession from licensee - Small Causes Court has exclusive jurisdiction.
