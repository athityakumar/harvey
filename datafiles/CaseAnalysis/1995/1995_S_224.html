Mir Fazeelath Hussain And Ors. v Special Deputy Collector, Land Acquis Tion, Hyderabad

Supreme Court of India

23 March 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

B.L. Hansaria, Kuldip Singh, R.M. Sahai

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1621; (1995) 3 SCC 208; AIR 1995 SC 1424; 1995 (1) CCC 675; JT 1995 (3) SC 410; 1995(2) SCALE 424; 1995 (2) SCJ 149; [1995] 2 S.C.R. 985; 1995 (2) UJ 73

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 28 and 30(2) - Acquisition - Enhanced Interest - Interest awardable only when award by Collector or Court is made between 3004-1982 and date of commencement of Amending Act - Facts showing that award was passed before 30-1982 - (A) Whether claimants entitled grant of enhanced interest - (B) Interest visualised by s. 28 - If a pat of award - Held, (A) Not entitled to enhanced rate of interest, it cannot be claimed even from date of coming into force of Amendment Act dehors compliance of s. 30(2) and court cannot enlarge scope of s. 30(2), enhanced interest cannot also be ordered in equity - (B) Is not part of compensation and so not part of award - Point referred to this Bench, answered - Rest of appeal placed for final disposal before an appropriate Bench.
