Sultan Singh v State Of Haryana & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

12 December 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, B.L. Hansaria

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1898; (1996) 2 SCC 66; (1996) SCC (L&S) 751; AIR 1996 SC 1007; 1996 (32) ATC 847; 1996 (73) FLR 955; JT 1995 (9) SC 556; 1996 LabIC 915; 1996 (1) LLJ 879; 1996 (1) LLN 439; 1996 (1) SCJ 301; 1996 (1) SLR 598; [1995] Supp6 S.C.R. 629; 1996 (1) Supreme 211

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s.10 - Reference - Govt. rejecting - On ground of settled matter between parties - Subsequent second application by Minister - Making note directing Reference - Order of Labour Dept. communicated to employee that Govt. declined to reconsider its earlier decision as there no industrial dispute exists - Held, no reference was made to appropriate Tribunal/ Labour Court or Industrial Tribunal - Insurance of notice to employer or to hear him or to consider his objections by State Govt. - Not necessary before making a reference on second application.
