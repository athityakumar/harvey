Burn Standard Co. Limited And Ors. v Shri Dinabandhu Majumdar And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

21 April 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Venkatachala, A.M. Ahmadi

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1051; (1995) 4 SCC 172; (1995) SCC (L&S) 952; AIR 1995 SC 1499; AIR 1995 SCW 2282; 1995 (30) ATC 206; 1995 (2) CalLT(SC) 18; 1995 (71) FLR 282; JT 1995 (4) SC 23; 1995 LabIC 1825; 1996 (1) LLN 96; 1995(3) SCALE 37; 1995 (2) SCJ 441; [1995] 3 S.C.R. 712; 1995 (4) SLR 25

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Practice & Procedure - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 and 311- Evidence Act, 1872, s. 35 - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, sch. 2, item 6 - Employee of Government or its instrumentality - Writ petition by him for correction of date of birth - At fag end of service with object of continuing in service - Interim relief sought - Held, Ordinarily, High Court should not entertain it - Interim relief should not be granted - Appeal allowed.
