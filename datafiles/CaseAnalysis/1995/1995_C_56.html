Chinnadurai v State of Tamil Nadu

Supreme Court of India

11 January 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Punchhi

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1771; (1995) Supp3 SCC 686; (1995) SCC (Cr) 1147; AIR 1996 SC 546

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - IPC, 1860, ss. 307 and 398 - Robbery or dacoity - (A) Determination of participation of accused whose name was mentioned in FIR and statement of prosecution witness but not in dying declaration - False implication - (B) Whether scattering of articles in house constitute robbery or dacoity - (C) Reduction of sentence (7 years' R I) sought for offence of causing injuries on vital parts by formidable weapon forming attempt to murder - Held, no ground to doubt his complicity in crime - Does not constitute - Cannot be reduced - Appeal partly allowed.
