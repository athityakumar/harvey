Kalyanji Gangadhar Bhagat v Virji Bharmal and Another

Supreme Court of India

18 January 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Majmudar, S. Mohan

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1458; (1995) 3 SCC 725; 1995 (4) Bom.C.R. 273; 1996 (2) RCJ 235; 1995 (2) RCR(Rent) 455; 1996 (1) RentLR 374

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Civil Procedure - Bombay Rents, Hotel and Loading House Rates Control Act, 1947, s. 15 - Bombay Rents, Hotel and Loading House Rates Control (Amendment) Act, 1973 - M.P. Accommodation Control Act, 1961, ss. 2 and 14 - Delhi Rent Control Amendment Act (Act 18 of 1976), ss. 2 and 5 - Civil Procedure Code, 1908, O. 21, r. 102 - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 52 - Whether respondent/tenants would fall under the proviso to s. 15 of the Bombay Rents, Hotel and Loading House Rates Control Act, 1947? - Court held, it was a case of transfer of stock-in-trade and their goodwill thereof therefore, it had nothing to do with the contractual tenancy - Neither s. 52 of Transfer of Property Act, 1882 nor O. 21, r. 102 CPC, 1908 could be invoked by appellant - Appeals dismissed.
