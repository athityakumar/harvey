Birla Cement Works v G.M., Western Railways And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

02 January 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1516; (1995) 2 SCC 493; 1995 (2) ACC 467; 1995 ACJ 459; AIR 1995 SC 1111; 1995 (25) ALR 464; 1996 (2) CLT 238; JT 1995 (2) SC 59; 1995(1) SCALE 386; [1995] 1 S.C.R. 5; 1996 (Supp) AWC 87; 1995 (1) UJ 452

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Carrier & Transportation - Railways Act, 1890, s. 78 B - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 3 - Civil Procedure Code, 1908, s. 9 - Limitation Act, 1963, Preamble, s. 17(1)(c) - (A) Excess freight charges - Claim of refund - Limitation - Over charge falls within meaning of s. 75 B - Claim beyond 6 months from date of delivery - If barred - Held, provisions of s. 17(1)(c) of Limitation Act, has no application - Petition dismissed.
