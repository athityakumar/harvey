Chinnamma v Gopalan And Others

Supreme Court of India

13 October 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Paripoornan, A.M. Ahmadi, S.C. Sen

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1311; (1995) 6 SCC 491; AIR 1996 SC 363; JT 1995 (7) SC 276; [1995] Supp4 S.C.R. 319

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Rent Control

Summary: Civil Procedure - Land & Property - Rent Control - Transfer of Property Act, 1882 - Kerala Building (Lease and Rent Control) Act, 1965, ss. 11(2)(b) and 11(3) - What is amount that should be deposited by tenant u/s. 11(2)(c) of Act to set aside order passed u/s. 11(2)(b) of Act ? - Arrears of rent are arrears demanded by notice for payment of arrears of rent - Arrears due cannot be extended to rent which has fallen due after service of notice of demand - Held, judgment of learned Single Judge of High Court set aside - Order passed by District Judge restored that it is beyond power of Rent Controller in an application u/s. 11(2)(c) of Act to consider claims for arrears of rent that has become due after passing of an order u/s. 11(2)(b) of Act - Appeal allowed.
