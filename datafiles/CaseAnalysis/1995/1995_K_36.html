State of Maharashtra and Others v Kanchanmala Vijaysing Shirke and Others

Supreme Court of India

22 August 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

N.P. Singh, B.L. Hansaria

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1169; (1995) 5 SCC 659; (1995) SCC (Cr) 1002; 1995 ACJ 1021; AIR 1995 SC 2499; 1995 (26) ALR 422; 1995 (3) AWC 1829; 1996 (2) Bom.C.R. 153; 1995 (3) CCC 346; [1996] 85 Comp Cas 542; JT 1995 (6) SC 155; 1996 (1) MLJ(SC) 9; 1995 (3) PLR 375; 1995(5) SCALE 2; [1995] Supp3 S.C.R. 1

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Tort

Keywords: Death Or Permanent Disablement, Negligent, Fault

Summary: Motor Vehicles Act, 1939, s. 110 B - Torts - While returning to duty - Clerk in Govt. Department was driving official jeep with permission of intoxicated driver - Accident - Claim for Compensation - Whether State vicariously liabile for unauthorised and independent act - No nexus with business of State Govt. - Held, negligent act of driver and respondent was in course of employment - State liable.
