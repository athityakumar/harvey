Baldev Singh And Anr v State Of Punjab

Supreme Court of India

13 October 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Paripoornan, A.S. Anand

Where Reported

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Kirpan

Summary: Code of Criminal Procedure, ss. 154 and 357(3) - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 304, Part I - Culpable homicide or murder, sentence - No material discrepancy between First Information Report and testimony of eye-witness - Evidentiary value of - Land dispute on entitlement between parties - Victim and accused of same family - Death of son - Fatal injuries by whom unknown - Intention to cause death not proved - Held, is culpable homicide - First Information Report not substantive piece of evidence - Considering nature of crime and mitigating circumstances sentence of accused persons reduced to.
