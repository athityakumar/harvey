State Bank of Bikaner and Jaipur and Others v Prabhu Dayal Grover

Supreme Court of India

19 September 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

M.K. Mukherjee, B.P. Jeevan Reddy

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 223; (1995) 6 SCC 279; (1995) SCC (L&S) 1376; AIR 1996 SC 320; 1995 (31) ATC 492; 1995 (2) BC 485; 1995 (2) CLR 1158; 1996 (72) FLR 1; JT 1995 (7) SC 207; 1996 LabIC 210; 1996 (1) LLJ 288; 1995(5) SCALE 574; 1996 (1) SLJ 145; [1995] Supp3 S.C.R. 785; 1996 (1) UJ 30

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - State Bank of Bikaner and Jaipur Officers' Service Regulations 1979 - Regns. 68(2)(iii) and 70(2) - Whether appellant was removed from service in accordance with provisions of regn. 68(2)(iii)? - Held, rules of natural justice to be followed - Disciplinary Authority to direct fresh enquiry only if it disagrees with findings of Enquiry Officer - Order of punishment not liable to be struck down - Appeal allowed.
