Balkrishna Pandey v State Of Bihar & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

14 December 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, B.L. Hansaria

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1910; (1996) 2 SCC 282; (1996) SCC (L&S) 498; AIR 1996 SC 888; 1996 (33) ATC 205; JT 1995 (9) SC 566; 1997 LLJ 116; 1996(1) SCALE 7; 1996 (1) SLR 600; [1995] Supp6 S.C.R. 669; 1996 (1) Supreme 181

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Supervisor

Summary: Service - Constitution, art. 311 - Reversion - Employee appointed as Junior Satistical Supervisor - Subsequently appointed on deputation to temporary post of Senior Statistical Assistant (S.S.A.) in another department - Post becoming permanent - Reversion of employee to his parent department and promotion of permanent incumbent of that department on that post - Whether proper - Whether employee can claim lien on deputed post - Held, Proper - Employee cannot claim lien - Moreso when he was promoted as S.S.A. in his own right in his parent department - Appeal dismissed.
