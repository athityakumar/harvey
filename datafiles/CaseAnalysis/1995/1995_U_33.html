Yogendra Narayan Chowdhury & Ors v Union Of India & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

30 November 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, S.B. Majmudar

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1992; (1996) 7 SCC 1; (1996) SCC (L&S) 362; AIR 1996 SC 751; JT 1995 (9) SC 112; 1996 LabIC 759; 1996 (2) LLJ 831; 1996 (1) LLN 298; 1995(6) SCALE 759; 1996 (1) SLR 42; [1995] Supp6 S.C.R. 17

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution, art. 311 - Pay scale - Unskilled workers in military engineering - Classification of, for purpose of fitment - Unskilled workers on passing test wrongly classified into skilled category - Said mistake rectified by making their fitment into semi skilled category -(A) Whether proper - (B) Whether such fitmen is reversion - Held, (A) Is proper - (B) Cannot be said to be case of reversion but is one of proper fitment - Appeal dismissed.
