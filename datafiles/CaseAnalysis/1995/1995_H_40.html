G. Sridharamurti v Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Ltd. And Anr

Supreme Court of India

13 September 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

B.L. Hansaria, B.P. Jeevan Reddy, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1235; (1995) 6 SCC 605; AIR 1996 SC 264; 1995 (4) CCC 217; 1995 (2) RCJ 411; 1996 (1) RCR(Rent) 66; 1996 (1) RentLR 269; 1995(5) SCALE 612; [1995] Supp3 S.C.R. 634

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Rent Control - Karnataka Rent Control Act, 1961, s. 21(1)(f) - Eviction of tenant company on ground of sub-letting - Company merged into corporation, which become statutory tenant - Corporation continued to occupy premises - Whether continuance can be deemed to be due to sub-letting u/s. 21(1)(f) of 1961 Act - Held, cannot be deemed so - Appeal dismissed.
