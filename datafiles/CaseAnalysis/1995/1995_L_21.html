Larsen And Toubro Limited v Maharashtra Stateelectricity Board And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

13 September 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Paripoornan, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1232; (1995) 6 SCC 68; AIR 1996 SC 334; AIR 1995 SCW 4134; 1995 (2) ARBLR 482; 1996 BC 255; 1995 (3) CCC 397; [1996] 85 Comp Cas 214; JT 1995 (7) SC 18; 1995(5) SCALE 342; [1995] Supp3 S.C.R. 615

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure; Corporate

Summary: Contract Act, 1872, s. 126 - Bank guarantee - Encashment - Injunction - Bank guarantee given by contractor as security towards advance given by electricity board and for payment of liquidated damages to board - Board asked bank for extension of validity of guarantee for further 6 months or treat it as notice for encashment - Invocation of bank guarantee was in time - Dispute about money wrongly recovered, was pending before arbitrator - Court refused bank to order for injunction regarding encashing bank guarantee - Order affirmed
