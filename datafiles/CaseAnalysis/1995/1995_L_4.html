State Of Tamil Nadu & Ors.Etc. v L.Krishn N & Ors.Etc.

Supreme Court of India

01 November 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, K. Ramaswamy, B.L. Hansaria

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 679; (1996) 1 SCC 250; AIR 1996 SC 497; 1995 (4) CCC 61; JT 1995 (8) SC 1; 1996 (1) RentLR 82; 1995(6) SCALE 221; 1996 (2) SCJ 178; [1995] Supp4 S.C.R. 663

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Land and Property - Municipalities and Local Governments - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 4, 5A and 6 - T.N. Housing Board Act, 1961, ss. 35 and 70 - (A) Acquisition for purpose of housing Board - Existence of duly published final scheme prepared under Housing Board Act - Whether a pre-condition for issuance of notification u/s. 4 - (B) Notification under - Public purpose stated therein - Whether vague - Is question of fact - Acquistion of large area - Insistence to specify how each and every bit of land would be used - Whether proper - (C) Notification under - Challenge on ground of vagueness of public purpose - Not raised either in enquiry u/s. 5A or soon after declaration u/s. 6 of 1894 Act - Challenge raised for first time in writ petition filed 4 years after s.6 declaration - Whether to be dismissed on ground of laches - Held, (A) Not a pre-condition - (B) Not proper - (C) Dismissed on ground of laches - Appeal dismissed.
