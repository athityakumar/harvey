Vidyawati v Man Mohan And Others

Supreme Court of India

01 May 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, B.L. Hansaria

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 554; (1995) 5 SCC 431; AIR 1995 SC 1653; 1995 (2) CCC 357; 1996 (1) RCR(Rent) 7; 1996 (2) RentLR 214; 1995(3) SCALE 680; 1995 (3) SCJ 51; [1995] 3 S.C.R. 956

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - CPC, 1908, O. 22, r. 4 - On death of defendent his LR (Petitioner) impleaded as party defendant - All rights u/r. 4(2) of O.22, and defences now available to her - Determination of independent right, title, interest of petitioner LR - Held, she has to get impleaded herself in suit as party defendant and set up her own right title, interest - Petition dismissed.
