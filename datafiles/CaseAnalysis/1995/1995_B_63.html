Gurbachan Singh v Bhag Singh & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

24 November 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, B.L. Hansaria

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1966; (1996) 1 SCC 770; AIR 1996 SC 1087; 1995 (4) CCC 516; 1996 (1) CLT 141; JT 1995 (9) SC 208; 1995(7) SCALE 293; [1995] Supp5 S.C.R. 682

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Practice & Procedure - Indian Limitation Act, 1908 - CPC, 1908, O.8,r. 6 (A) (1) - Suit for injunction - Counter claim for possession by defendant - Whether can be entertained by virtue of O.8, r.6 (A) (1)? - Held, counter claim for possession by defendant can be entertained - Petition dismissed.
