Awadh Bihari Yadav and Others v State of Bihar and Others

Supreme Court of India

31 August 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Paripoornan, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1328; (1995) 6 SCC 31; AIR 1996 SC 122; JT 1995 (6) SC 248; 1995 (2) RentLR 577; 1995(5) SCALE 74; [1995] Supp3 S.C.R. 197

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 11 A, 17(1) and 48; Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Applicability - Govt. taking possession u/s. 17(1) - No award made within period prescribed by s. 11A - Signature - Illegible - Of District Land Acquisition Officer (Collector) on award - Held, proceedings would not lapse - Govt. cannot withdraw from acquisition u/s. 48 - Order should be treated as award, if substantial compliance - Same not in form is immaterial.
