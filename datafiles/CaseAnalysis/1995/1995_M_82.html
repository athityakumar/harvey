Major General Ipa Dewan v Union Of India

Supreme Court of India

07 March 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, K.S. Paripoornan

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1682; (1995) 3 SCC 383; (1995) SCC (L&S) 691; 1995 (29) ATC 579; 1995 (70) FLR 1070; JT 1995 (2) SC 654; 1995(2) SCALE 182; 1995 (3) SLJ 135; 1995 (2) SLR 130; 1995 (2) UJ 151

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Whether denial of promotion to appellant to rank of Lt. General valid in law? - Administrative orders affecting rights of citizens should contain reasons thereto - Memo containing adverse remarks in this case set out particulars in support of same - Held, there is no specific allegation of arbitrary conduct on part of Selection Board - No allegation of mala fides or arbitrariness has been leveled against Chief of Army staff who made remarks - Appeal dismissed.
