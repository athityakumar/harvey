State of Orissa and Another v Damodar Das

Supreme Court of India

15 December 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, B.N. Kirpal, Faizan Uddin

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 2207; (1996) 2 SCC 216; AIR 1996 SC 942; 1996 (1) ARBLR 221; 1997 (1) CalLT(SC) 1; 1996 (82) CutLT 110; JT 1995 (9) SC 419; 1996(1) SCALE 68; [1995] Supp6 S.C.R. 800

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration Act, 1940, ss. 2(a ) and 8 - Existence of arbitration agreement - Clause in contract indicating that Public Health Engineer would be empowered to decide all questions relating to claim, rights, quality of workmanship etc. but does not to any disputes - Clause not containing any express or implied reference to arbitrator - Held, in absence of clause, it is difficult to spell out existence of arbitration agreement - Order of appointment of arbitrator set aside - Appeal allowed.
