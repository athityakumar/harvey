Laxmikant Revchand Bhojwani And Anr. v Pratapsing Mohansingh Pardeshi Deceased Through

Supreme Court of India

18 September 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1247; (1995) 6 SCC 576; 1997 (3) Bom.C.R. 1; 1995 (3) CCC 425; JT 1995 (7) SC 400; 1995 (2) RCJ 451; 1995 (2) RCR(Rent) 638; 1996 (1) RentLR 120; 1995(5) SCALE 481; 1995 (3) SCJ 651

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Land & Property

Summary: Rent Control - Constitution - Bombay Rents, Hotel and Lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947, ss. 12(3)(a) and 12(3)(b) - Eviction from tenancy premises - Landlady served two notices in terms of s. 12(2) of Act - Notice received by appellants both times - Appellants send money order both time to landlady - Amount covered arrears up to 31-10-1972 - Landlady refused to accept both money orders - Held, High Court rightly come to conclusion that money order sent was within 'the period of one month after notice u/s. 12(2) of Act - Further held, when a money order or a demand draft is sent to landlord, during specified period, it cannot be said that tenant has 'neglected to make payment' - Appellants were not neglected to make payment within specified period after receipt of notice - HC judgment set aside, Appellate Court order restored - Appeal allowed.
