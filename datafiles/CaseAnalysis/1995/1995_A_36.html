Commissioner of Income Tax v Ajanta Electricals

Supreme Court of India

02 May 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, G.T. Nanavati

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 2017; (1995) 4 SCC 182; AIR 1995 SC 2172; 1995 (126) CTR 144; [1995] 215 ITR 114; JT 1995 (7) SC 429; 1995(3) SCALE 337; [1995] 3 S.C.R. 1083; [1995] 81 TAXMAN 166

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Application For Extension

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 139(2) - Income Tax Rules, 1962, r. 13 - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 148 - Procedure regarding filing return of income after stipulated time - Held, application for extension of time for filing return of income can be filed after expiry of period stipulated in notice and principle as regards procedural law u/s. 148 would be attracted - Appeal dismissed.
