Dr. Rashmi Srivastava v Vikram University And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

20 April 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Majmudar, P.B. Sawant

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 426; (1995) 3 SCC 653; (1995) SCC (L&S) 822; AIR 1995 SC 1694; 1995 (30) ATC 85; JT 1995 (4) SC 51; 1995 LabIC 2041; 1995 (2) LLN 121; 1995(2) SCALE 871; 1995 (3) SLJ 18; 1995 (3) SLR 21

Case Digest

Subject: Education; Service

Keywords: Fixation, Merit Promotion Scheme

Summary: Constitution of India, Arts.16 and 311; M.P. Vishwavidyalaya Adhiniyam, 1973, s.49; University Grants Commission Act, 1956, s.12 - Seniority - Promotion - Merit Promotion scheme - Direct recruit and promotee Readers and Professors - Distinct class from cadre employees - Held, nature of work they do is irrelevant - Ex-cadre posts cannot be treated equally for purposes of seniority and promotion - Illegal - University can create additional posts of Readers, Professors - But cannot reserve them for promotees.
