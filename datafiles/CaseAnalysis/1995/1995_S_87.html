Sawan Ram Malra v Union Of India And Ors

Supreme Court of India

13 September 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1230; (1995) Supp3 SCC 620; (1995) SCC (L&S) 1438; AIR 1996 SC 3348; 1995 (31) ATC 527; JT 1995 (7) SC 334; 1995(5) SCALE 348; 1995 (5) SLR 263; [1995] Supp3 S.C.R. 608; 1995 (3) UPLBEC 1832

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Service - Constitution - Postal Superintendent, Postmasters Group 'B ' Recruitment Rules, 1987, r. 1 - Constitution, art. 309 - Promotion to posts of Postal Superintendent / Postmasters Group 'B' under 6% quota reserved for General Line Officials belonging to Higher Grade I, Higher Selection Grade II and Lower Selection Grade by means of Departmental competitive examination - Whether such officials are eligible to be promoted to said 6% posts by means of Departmental promotion examination - Such officials with five years' regular service in either or all three cadres together are eligible - Appeal disposed of.
