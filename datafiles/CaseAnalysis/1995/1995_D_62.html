State Of Bihar v Dhirendra Kumar And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

27 April 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

B.L. Hansaria, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1070; (1995) 4 SCC 229; AIR 1995 SC 1955; 1995 (3) CCC 111; 1995 (2) MahLJ 340; 1995 (2) RentLR 250; 1995(3) SCALE 700; [1995] 3 S.C.R. 857; 1995 (2) UJ 389

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 9 - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 4, 6 and 11 Whether Civil Court has jurisdiction to decide validity of notification u/s. 4 and declaration u/s. 6 of Act of 1894 - Held, Civil Court has no jurisdiction to go into that matter only High Court do that in proceeding u/art. 226 - Appeal allowed.
