Income Tax Officer, Calcutta v Selected Dalurband Coal Company Private Limited

Supreme Court of India

29 March 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, G.T. Nanavati

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 972; (1997) 10 SCC 68; AIR 1995 SC 1934; 1996 (132) CTR 162; [1996] 217 ITR 597

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Inome Tax Act, 1961, ss. 147(a) and 148 - Validity of notice of reassessment - Notice to assessee colliery on basis of letter (material to form belief) received by ITO from Chief Mining Officer indicating under-reporting of coal raising and shortage of coal stock - Held - Notice valid - Appeal allowed.
