L.I.C. of India and Another v Consumer Education and Research Centre and Ors.Etc

Supreme Court of India

10 May 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, B.L. Hansaria

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 288; (1995) 5 SCC 482; AIR 1995 SC 1811; 1996 (4) ALT 887; [1995] 84 Comp Cas 168; JT 1995 (4) SC 366; 1995(3) SCALE 627; [1995] Supp1 S.C.R. 349; 1995 (2) UJ 442

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Insurance

Keywords: Amount Of Premium, Standard Form, Life Policy, Term Insurance

Summary: Constitution of India, art.14; Indian Contract Act 1872, s.126Insurance Act, 1938, s.2(11) - jurisdiction of Court - Public law and private law remedy - Term policy by Life Insurance Corporation - Terms and conditions - Writ Petition to test its validity - Held, maintainable - Actions bearing insignia of public law element or public character are amenable to judicial review - Term policy Confining only to salaried class from Government, Semi Government or reputed commercial firms - Violative of fundamental right.
