The Special Tahsildar (La), P.W.D.Schemes, Vijayawada v M.A. Jabbar

Supreme Court of India

11 January 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 2173; (1995) 2 SCC 142; AIR 1995 SC 762; 1995 AnWR 47; 1995 (1) CCC 94; JT 1995 (1) SC 383; 1995 (1) RentLR 526; 1995(1) SCALE 144; [1995] 1 S.C.R. 180

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property

Summary: Land and Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 23(1A) - Possession of land was taken in 1965 but notification u/s. 4 issued in 1980 - Entitlement to additional amount under award passed after amendment Act of 1984 came into force - Land-owner would be entitled to additional amount u/s. 23A only for the period from date of notification to date of award - Awarding additional amount from date of taking over possession would amount to giving retrospective operation to s. 23 (1A) - Appeal dismissed.
