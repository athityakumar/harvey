Oxford English School v The Government Of Tamil Nadu And Ors.Withcontempt Petition No.

Supreme Court of India

02 August 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar, A.M. Ahmadi

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 366; (1995) 5 SCC 206; AIR 1995 SC 2398; 1995 (4) CCC 164; JT 1995 (7) SC 481; 1996 (1) MLJ(SC) 80; 1995 (2) RentLR 217; 1995(4) SCALE 589; 1995 (3) SCJ 473; [1995] Supp2 S.C.R. 461; 1995 (2) UJ 596

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 4, 6 and 6(1), explanation 1 - Bombay Prohibition Act, 1949 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Land Acquisition proceedings - (A) Legality of setting aside proceedings after s. 4(1) notification and directions to hold fresh enquiry u/s. 5 A and declaration u/s. 6 - (B) Computation of period of declaration u/s. 6 by excluding period of stay under notification u/s. 4 as ordered Minister for local administration - Held, illegal - Order of stay should be passed by court and not by Minister for local administration - Appeal allowed.
