Khagesh Kumar And Ors v Inspector General Of Registration And Ors

Supreme Court of India

27 September 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, B.P. Jeevan Reddy

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1795; (1995) Supp4 SCC 182; (1996) SCC (L&S) 182; AIR 1996 SC 417; 1996 (32) ATC 116; 1996 (72) FLR 153; JT 1995 (7) SC 545; 1995(6) SCALE 102; 1995 (5) SLR 741; [1995] Supp4 S.C.R. 117; 1996 (1) UPLBEC 23

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Service

Keywords: Uttar Pradesh Regularisation Of Ad Hoc Appointments (On Posts Outside The Purview Of

Summary: Constitution of India, art.309; U.P. Registration Department (District Establishment) Ministerial Service Rules, 1978, r.15; U.P. Registration Manual, Para 97 - Registration clerks - Appointment on daily wage basis - Made with special sanction of Governor - Held, not in nature of regular appointment in view of relevant Rules - Regularisation only by continuous service of three years under Rules.
