State of Himachal Pradesh v Shri Pirthi Chand and Another

Supreme Court of India

30 November 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, S.B. Majmudar

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 2271; (1996) 2 SCC 37; (1996) SCC (Cr) 210; AIR 1996 SC 977; AIR 1996 SCW 422; 1997 (1) CalLT(SC) 12; 1996 CRLJ 1354; JT 1996 (9) SC 411; 1996(1) SCALE 48; [1995] Supp6 S.C.R. 29

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal Procedure Code, 1973 - Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act, 1985, ss. 20 and 50 - Unlawful possession of contraband was in question - Seizure panchnama would be admissible in evidence at stage of filing of chargesheet - Accused was discharged by Magistrate or Sessions Judge on ground of non compliance of s. 50 or other provisions - Held, such action was impermissible.
