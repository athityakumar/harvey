Krishan Prasad Gupta v Controller, Printing & Stationery

Supreme Court of India

18 October 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Saghir Ahmad, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 238; (1996) 1 SCC 69; (1996) SCC (L&S) 264; AIR 1996 SC 408; 1996 (32) ATC 211; 1995 (2) CLR 1134; JT 1995 (7) SC 522; 1996 (1) LLJ 296; 1995(6) SCALE 89; 1995 (5) SLR 763; [1995] Supp4 S.C.R. 436; 1996 (1) UJ 43; 1996 (1) UPLBEC 174

Case Digest

Subject: Administrative; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Payment Of Wages Act, 1936, ss. 15 and 17 - Administrative Tribunals Act, 1985, ss. 28 and 29 - Jurisdiction of authority to entertain claim cases u/s. 15 and appeal u/s. 17 - Whether affected by establishment of Administrative Tribunals? - Held, not affected - Appeal allowed.
