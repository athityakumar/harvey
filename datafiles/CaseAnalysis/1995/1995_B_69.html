Jethi Devi v Bhakra Beas Management Board And Anr

Supreme Court of India

24 August 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

B.L. Hansaria, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1175; (1995) 6 SCC 61; (1995) SCC (L&S) 1353; 1995 (31) ATC 470; 1996 (1) CLR 366; 1996 (1) LLJ 1118; 1996 (1) LLN 310; 1995(5) SCALE 171; [1995] Supp3 S.C.R. 53; 1995 (2) UJ 712

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Whether appellant made any false statement to gain benefit of rehabilitation scheme? - Held, in view of fact that no legally solemnised marriage exists, she is not incorrect in stating that she is widow of deceased - It is also revealed that since they are members of joint family, her statement about dependence is not false - If these two facts are excluded, there is no other ground to disentitle her to claim for benefit of appointment as per rehabilitation scheme - Appeal allowed.
