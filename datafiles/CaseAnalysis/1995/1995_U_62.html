M.R. Gupta v Union Of India And Ors

Supreme Court of India

21 August 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

J.S. Verma, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1163; (1995) 5 SCC 628; (1995) SCC (L&S) 1273; AIR 1996 SC 669; 1995 (31) ATC 186; 1995 (71) FLR 886; 1996 LabIC 599; 1995(5) SCALE 29; 1995 (5) SLR 221; [1995] Supp2 S.C.R. 852; 1995 (2) UJ 689

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16 - Fundamental Rules, r. 22C - Fixation of initial pay - Allegation, not according to Rules - Limitation of - Held, is continuing wrong - Cause of action arises afresh every month when employee's salary is paid with wrong calculation contrary to Rules - No question of limitation therefore - Claim to be paid in accordance with Rules, a right which subsists during entire tenure of service.
