Akshaya Restaurant v P. Anjanappa and Another

Supreme Court of India

27 March 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

B.L. Hansaria, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1641; (1995) Supp2 SCC 303; AIR 1995 SC 1498; AIR 1995 SCW 2277; 1995 (3) AWC 1872; 1996 (2) BC 209; 1996 (3) CLT 187; 1996 (1) HLR 85; JT 1995 (6) SC 269; 1996 (1) MLJ(SC) 43; 1995(2) SCALE 698; [1995] 2 S.C.R. 1154

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 6, r.17 - Amendment of pleadings - Averment in plaint taking definite stand that defendant had entered into agreement for sale of suit land with defendant - Application for amendment seeking to modify averment that defendant had entered into agreement with plaintiff for development of suit land - Order allowing application - Legality - Held, not illegal - Appeal dismissed.
