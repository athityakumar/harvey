Board Of Trustees For The Port Of Calcutta v Engineers-De-Space-Age

Supreme Court of India

07 December 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Sen, A.M. Ahmadi

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1; (1996) 1 SCC 516; 1996 (1) AD(SC) 89; AIR 1996 SC 2853; 1996 (1) ALT 29; 1996 (1) ALT(SC) 29; 1995 (24) ARBLR 733; 1996 (2) CalLT(SC) 17; 1996 (1) CLT 239; 1995(7) SCALE 274; 1996 (1) SCJ 54; [1995] Supp6 S.C.R. 327; 1996 (1) UJ 358

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Arbitration Act, 1940, ss. 29 and 13 - Arbitration award - Powers of arbitrator - Grant of interest - Pendente lite - Contract - Prohibition clause - Against claiming payment of interest of delayed payment to contractors - Held, discretion of arbitrator not stifles by terms of contract - Arbitrator has jurisdiction in awarding interest pendente lite.
