State Of M.P. And Others v Hyama Pardhi Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

16 November 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

B.L. Hansaria, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 839; (1996) 7 SCC 118; (1996) SCC (L&S) 466; 1996 (1) AD(SC) 253; AIR 1996 SC 2219; 1996 (32) ATC 789; JT 1995 (9) SC 578; 1995(7) SCALE 216; 1996 (1) SCJ 315; 1996 (1) SLR 66; [1995] Supp5 S.C.R. 448; 1996 (1) UJ 218

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Public Health and Family Welfare Department Non-ministerial (Related to Directorate Health Services) Class-III Services Recruitment Rules, 1989, r. 1 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 and 309 Proviso - Appointment of candidate not possessing requisite qualification - Legality of cancellation of candidate wrongly selected as it involves question of violation of principles of natural justice - Held, cancellation legal - And question does not arise - Appeal dismissed.
