Food Corporation of India v Thaneswar Kalita and Others

Supreme Court of India

06 March 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

B.L. Hansaria, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1665; (1995) 3 SCC 342; (1995) SCC (L&S) 685; AIR 1996 SC 644; 1995 (29) ATC 573; JT 1997 (10) SC 447; [1995] 2 S.C.R. 516; 1995 (2) SLR 425

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 311 - Whether employees, who were appointed on ad hoc basis and held post for long period and were promoted, can be considered for determination of seniority - Held, such period of service be considered fortuitous and cannot be counted for seniority - Appeal allowed.
