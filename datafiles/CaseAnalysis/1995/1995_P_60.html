Principal, Cambridge School and Another v Payal Gupta (Ms.) and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 August 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

Faizan Uddin, S.B. Majmudar, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1162; (1995) 5 SCC 512; AIR 1996 SC 118; 1995 (3) CTJ 777; 1996 (63) DLT 372; JT 1995 (6) SC 101; 1995(4) SCALE 811; 1995 (5) SLR 498; [1995] Supp2 S.C.R. 843; 1995 (2) UJ 722; 1995 (3) UPLBEC 1440

Case Digest

Subject: Education; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Educational Institution

Summary: Delhi School Education Act, 1973, ss. 16(3) and 28; Delhi School Education Rules, 1973, r. 145; Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Cut off level of marks - Head of an educational institution prescribes - Continuance of further studies in higher class in same school - Student passing public examination - Unaided recognised school - Held, not authorised.
