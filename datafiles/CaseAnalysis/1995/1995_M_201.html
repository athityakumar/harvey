Indian Oil Corporation Limited v Municipal Corporation And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

07 April 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1099; (1995) 4 SCC 96; AIR 1995 SC 1480; AIR 1995 SCW 2254; JT 1995 (3) SC 626; 1995 (2) MLJ 35; 1995 (2) MLJ(SC) 35; 1995(2) SCALE 744; [1995] 3 S.C.R. 246; 1995 (2) UJ 384

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Keywords: Gujarat Municipalities Act, 1963

Summary: Constitution of India,1950, art. 141; M P Municipal Corporation Act, 1956, s. 138(b) - Decision of Supreme Court - Binding - Annual value of building - Held, deems to be gross annual rent - For which at time of assessment building might be expected to be let from year to year - View that decision of Supreme Court does not bind to High Court or co-equal bench of Supreme Court, is not proper.
