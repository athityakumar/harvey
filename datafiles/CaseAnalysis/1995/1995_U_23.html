State of Maharashtra and Anr v Umashankar Rajabhau and Others

Supreme Court of India

09 November 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1357; (1996) 1 SCC 299; 1996 (1) CLT 162; JT 1995 (8) SC 508; 1996 (1) RentLR 108; 1995(6) SCALE 391; [1995] Supp5 S.C.R. 39

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 4(1) and 6 - Whether High Court was unjustified in quashing acquisition in respect of three plots of land of respondents 1 to 3 on the ground that notice was not served? - No - Held, since mutation had not been effected in the name of respondents 1 to 3 though purchased prior to the publication of notification u/s. 4(1), they could not be issued notices as required u/s. 9 - Appeal allowed.
