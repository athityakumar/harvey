Muni Lal v Oriental Fire and Generalinsurance Company

Supreme Court of India

09 November 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1377; (1996) 1 SCC 90; AIR 1996 SC 642; AIR 1995 SCW 4656; 1996 (27) ALR 91; 1996 (2) BC 166; 1995 (4) CCC 375; [1996] 86 Comp Cas 60; JT 1995 (8) SC 283; 1995(6) SCALE 501; [1995] Supp5 S.C.R. 42

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure Code, 1908, o.6, r.17; Specific Relief Act, 1963, s.34, proviso; Limitation Act, 1963, s.3 - Amendment of plaint - Sought to include unclaimed relief - In appeal - Relief time barred during pendency of proceedings - Permissibility - Held, at the stage cannot be permitted.
