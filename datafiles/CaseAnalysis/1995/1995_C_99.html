Porritts and Spencer (Asia) Limited v Collector of Central Excise, New Delhi

Supreme Court of India

21 March 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, S.P. Bharucha, K.S. Paripoornan

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 23; (1995) Supp3 SCC 219; AIR 1995 SC 2344; 1995 (58) ECR 621; 1999 (106) E.L.T. 18

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, ss. 2(f), ch. I, item 18 A and 18 E - Excise Duty on intermediate product i.e., multi fold yarn or doubled yarn - Prepared by twisting cotton yarn and nylon yarn - Has no separate identity nor marketability - Whether liable to duty - Held, not liable to duty - Appeal allowed.
