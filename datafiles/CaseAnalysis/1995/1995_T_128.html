Salauddin Abdulsamad Shaikh v The State Of Maharashtra

Supreme Court of India

11 December 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, S.C. Sen, K.S. Paripoornan

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1866; (1996) SCC (Cr) 198; (1996) 1 SCC 667; AIR 1996 SC 1042; 1996 (2) CalLT(SC) 12; 1996 (1) CCR 93; 1996 CRLJ 1368; JT 1995 (9) SC 165; 1995(7) SCALE 272; 1996 (1) SCJ 277; [1995] Supp6 S.C.R. 556

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - CrPC, 1973, s. 438 - Grant of ad interim bail application by Court and fixing outer date for continuance of bail - On it expiry Court directed accused to move regular Court for bail - Validity of direction - Held, proper - Petition disposed of.
