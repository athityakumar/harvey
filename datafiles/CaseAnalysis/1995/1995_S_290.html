S.S.Darshan v State Of Karnataka And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

14 November 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

J.S. Verma, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1879; (1996) 7 SCC 302; AIR 1996 SC 671; 1995 (4) CCC 249; JT 1995 (8) SC 229; 1996 (1) RentLR 101; 1995(6) SCALE 426; [1995] Supp5 S.C.R. 221

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property

Summary: Land and Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 4 and 17 - Karnataka Industrial Area Development Act, 1966, s. 27 - Constitution, art. 14 - Land acquisition notification - (A) Whethat validity of notification depends upon nature of use to land was put by landholder - (B) Whether acquisition is for public or private company - (C) Notification challenge on ground that purpose of acquisition is different than permitted land use under Master Plan - Not dependent - Held, acquisition was for public purpose and not for joint venture company and plea not tenable - Plea not tenable - Appeal dismissed.
