State Of Andhra Pradesh v Bodem Sundara Rao

Supreme Court of India

22 September 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, K.S. Paripoornan

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1760; (1995) 6 SCC 230; (1995) SCC (Cr) 1097; AIR 1996 SC 530; AIR 1996 SCW 4435; 1995 (4) CCR 108; 1995 (4) Crimes 153; JT 1995 (7) SC 90; 1995(5) SCALE 554; 1995 (3) SCJ 511; [1995] Supp4 S.C.R. 48

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - IPC, 1860, s. 376 - Sentence - Enhancement of - Heinous crime of committing rape on a helpless young girl of thirteen or fourteen years of age - No extenuating or mitigating circumstances available justifying imposition of sentence less than minimum prescribed u/s. 376 - Sentence - Held, sentence of 4 years' rigorous imprisonment enhanced to 7 years' R. I. - Appeal allowed.
