Food Corporation of India Staff Union v Food Corporation of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 February 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

B.L. Hansaria, Kuldip Singh, S.B. Majmudar

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1548; (1995) SCC (L&S) 656; (1995) Supp1 SCC 678; AIR 1995 SC 1344; 1995 (1) CLR 622; 1995 (58) DLT 664; 1995 (71) FLR 278; JT 1995 (9) SC 261; 1995 LabIC 1606; 1995 (2) LLJ 272; 1995 (1) LLN 783; 1995(1) SCALE 789; [1995] 2 S.C.R. 96; 1995 (3) SLJ 140; 1995 (2) SLR 77

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Trade Unions Act, 1926, s. 1 - Representative character of trade unions - Assessment by secret ballot - Employer and unions agreed to follow secret ballot system - Tailoring of method of Secret ballot - Supreme Court to lay down procedure for secret ballot - Held, procedure laid down.
