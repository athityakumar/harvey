Kartar Singh @ Naranjan Singh And Ors. v State Of Punjab

Supreme Court of India

21 March 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, N. Venkatachala, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1608; (1995) 4 SCC 101; AIR 1995 SC 1726; 1995 (2) CCC 11; JT 1995 (3) SC 422; 1995 (2) RentLR 116; 1995(2) SCALE 598; [1995] 2 S.C.R. 922

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Land & Property - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 144 - Restitution - Land acquired - Compensation awarded was enhanced on reference by owner of Land/decree-holder - On appeal, compensation was reduced - However, during pendency of appeal, decree holder recovered enhanced compensation with interest - Application for restitution by State in view of decree varied in appeal - Whether justified - Decree holder liable to restitute excess amount of compensation and interest - Direction to restitute amount with interest is within powers conferred on Court u/s. 144 - Appeal dismissed.
