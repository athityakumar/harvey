State Of Maharashtra & Ors. v Ishwar Piraji Kalpatri

Supreme Court of India

30 November 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, M.K. Mukherjee

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 2240; (1996) SCC (Cr) 150; (1996) 1 SCC 542; AIR 1996 SC 722; 1995 (4) Crimes 769; 1996 CRLJ 1127; JT 1995 (9) SC 345B; 1995(6) SCALE 674; 1996 (1) SCJ 240; [1995] Supp5 S.C.R. 812

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 227 - Withdrawal of writ petition was demanded - Appellant was losing party - Request was for permission of withdrawal of original proceeding to avoid adverse decision from Superior Court with a view to reagitate same contentions once again before subordinate Court - Not tenable - Held, it could not be allowed.
