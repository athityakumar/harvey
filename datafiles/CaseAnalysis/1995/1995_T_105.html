Khoday Distilleries Limited And Anr. v The Registrar General, Supreme Court Of India.

Supreme Court of India

05 December 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, Sujata V. Manohar, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 766; (1996) 3 SCC 114; 1996 (1) AD(SC) 144; JT 1995 (9) SC 109; 1995(6) SCALE 742; 1996 (1) SCJ 156; [1995] Supp6 S.C.R. 190

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 32 - Sequel of Khoday Distilleries Ltd v State of Karnataka 1994 Indlaw SC 1123 and connected matters - Review petitions rejected - Present petition u/art. 32 - Held, in a case like present, where in substance challenge is to correctness of a decision on merits after it has become final, there can be no question of invoking art. 32 of Constitution to claim reconsideration of decision on basis of its effect in accordance with law - Decision in Antulay 1988 Indlaw SC 467 discussed - Writ petition challenging validity of order and judgment passed by Supreme Court as nullity or otherwise incorrect cannot be entertained - Petition has no merit - Petition dismissed.
