Colgate Palmolive India Limited v Collector of Central Excise, Aurangabad

Supreme Court of India

09 August 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, K.S. Paripoornan, S.C. Sen

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1228; (1998) 9 SCC 420; 1998 (79) ECR 766; 1997 (96) E.L.T. 230; JT 1998 (8) SC 27

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excise Act, 1944 - Whether value of larger corrugated cartons used for packing purposes can be included for determining tax liability? - Test is, whether packing cost whereof is sought to be included is packing in which it is ordinarily sold in course of a wholesale trade to wholesale buyer - Held, a finding on this question of fact would determine whether cost of such packing has to be included or excluded - Matter remitted back to tribunal.
