Mukri Gopalan v Cheppilat Puthanpurayil Aboobacker

Supreme Court of India

12 July 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Majmudar, Faizan Uddin

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1785; (1995) 5 SCC 5; AIR 1995 SC 2272; JT 1995 (5) SC 296; 1996 (2) RCJ 77; 1995 (2) RCR(Rent) 323; 1995 (2) RentLR 260; 1995(4) SCALE 438; [1995] Supp2 S.C.R. 1

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Rent Control

Keywords: Delay In Filing Appeal, Uttar Pradesh Sugarcane (Regulation of Supply and Purchase) Act, 1953, Contempt Of Courts Act, 1952, Bihar and Orissa Co-operative Societies Act 1935

Summary: Rent Control - Practice & Procedure - Kerala Buildings (Lease and Rent Control) Act, 1965, s. 18 - Limitation Act, 1963, ss. 5 and 29 - Question relating to determination of period of limitation - Appellate Authority constituted u/s. 18 would functions as court and not as persona designata - Period of limitation prescribed u/s. 18 governing appeals by aggrieved parties will be computed keeping in view provisions of ss. 4-24 of Act, 1963 - Such proceedings would attract s. 29(2) and consequently s. 5 would also be applicable to such proceeding - Appeal allowed.
