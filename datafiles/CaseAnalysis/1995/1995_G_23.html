Kuldeep Singh v Ganpat Lal & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

24 November 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 861; (1996) 1 SCC 243; AIR 1996 SC 729; 1995 (4) CCC 371; 1996 (1) CLT 148; JT 1995 (9) SC 157; 1996 (1) RCR(Rent) 348; 1996 (2) RentLR 544; 1995(6) SCALE 683; [1995] Supp5 S.C.R. 655; 1996 (1) UJ 151

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Rent Control - Rajasthan Premises (Control of Rent and Eviction) Act, 1950, s. 19A(3)(c) - Rent - Arrears of - Tenant depositing it in court without satisfying conditions therein - (A) Whether it is a deposit according to cl. (c) and sub-s (3)? - (B) Whether tenant entitled to protection from eviction? - Held, (A) It is no deposit - (B) Not entitled to protection from eviction - Appeal dismissed.
