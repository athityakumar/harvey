K. Vasundara Devi v Revenue Divisional Officer (Lao)Withcivil Appeal Nos. 6811-1

Supreme Court of India

27 July 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, K.S. Paripoornan

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 477; (1995) 5 SCC 426; AIR 1995 SC 2481; 1995 (3) CCC 255; JT 1997 (10) SC 566; 1995 (2) RentLR 227; 1995(4) SCALE 631; 1995 (3) SCJ 447; [1995] Supp2 S.C.R. 376; 1995 (2) UJ 621

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property

Summary: Land and Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s.23 - While determining compensation for land acquisition, reliance was placed on genuine and reliable sale instances of comparatively smaller land - So 40% deductions (as per High Court) made to arrive at market value of large track of lands - Validity - Held, justified - Appeal dismissed.
