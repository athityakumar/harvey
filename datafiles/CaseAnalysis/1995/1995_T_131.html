S.R. Bhagwat And Ors v The State Of Mysore

Supreme Court of India

12 September 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Majmudar, Faizan Uddin, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1390; (1995) 6 SCC 16; (1995) SCC (L&S) 1334; AIR 1996 SC 188; 1995 (31) ATC 452; 1995 (2) CLR 797; 1995 (71) FLR 753; JT 1995 (6) SC 444; 1995 LabIC 2809; 1995 (2) LLN 1130; 1995(5) SCALE 270; 1995 (5) SLR 242; [1995] Supp3 S.C.R. 545

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Water

Keywords: Karnataka State Civil Services (Regulation of Promotion, Pay and Pension) Act, 1973, Arbitration (Orissa Second Amendment) Act, 1991, Karnataka Cauvery Basin Irrigation Protection Ordinance, 1991

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 246 - Karnataka State Civil Services (Regulation of Promotion, Pay and Pension ) Act, 1974, s. 11(2) - Constitutionality - S. 11(2) tries to do away with judgments, decrees and orders of any court which has become final against state - Held, such exercise of power is impermissible - Unconstitutional, illegal and void.
