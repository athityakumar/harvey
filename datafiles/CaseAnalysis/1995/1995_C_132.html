Commissioner Of Income Tax, Madrasand Anr. v M/S Dalmia Cement (Bharat) Ltd.

Supreme Court of India

16 August 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, S.C. Sen, G.T. Nanavati

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 501; (1995) 6 SCC 256; AIR 1995 SC 2453; 1995 (128) CTR 120; [1995] 216 ITR 79; JT 1995 (6) SC 60; 1995(4) SCALE 727; [1995] Supp2 S.C.R. 708; [1995] 82 TAXMAN 229

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss.143(3) and 157 - Carry forward and set-off of loss - (A) Intimation of refusal to take cognizance of Loss returns for AY 1952-53 to 1954-55 by ITO - No appeal against - Subsequently assessee for determination, carry forward and set-off of said loss against profits of subsequent year or years - (B) Powers of Tribunal - Held, claim not tenable - Tribunal can direct ITO to quantify the losses and allow set off against assessee's share income from firm for subsequent years - Appeal allowed.
