Mahavir and Another v Rural Institute, Amaravati and Another

Supreme Court of India

28 July 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, K.S. Paripoornan

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1123; (1995) 5 SCC 335; 1995 (4) CCC 398; JT 1997 (10) SC 580; 1995 (2) RentLR 391; 1995(4) SCALE 768; 1995 (3) SCJ 583; [1995] Supp2 S.C.R. 421; 1995 (2) UJ 619

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Land Acquisition Act, ss. 4(1), 9, 10 and 16 - Whether sales made after publication of notification u/s. 4(1) are void sales and State is bound by such a sale effected by owner? - Held, any encumbrance made by owner after notification u/s. 4 (1) was published, does not bind State - Possession was taken as a fact and handed over to third respondent-society - Society became absolute owner of acquired lands free from all encumbrances - Petitions dismissed.
