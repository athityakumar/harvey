Anil Kumar Gupta, Etc. v State Of Uttar Pradesh And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

28 July 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, S.C. Sen

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1126; (1995) 5 SCC 173; 1995 (3) AWC 1653; JT 1995 (5) SC 505; 1995(4) SCALE 573; 1995 (5) SLR 203; [1995] Supp2 S.C.R. 396; 1995 (3) UPLBEC 1447

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution - Education - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16(1), (4), 15(1) and (4) - Whether fifteen per cent reservation in favour of special categories is an overall reservation and not a compartmentalised reservation? - Held, it was wrong to direct fifteen per cent special reservation seats to be filled up first and then take up OC (merit) quota - Special reservations to be kept at minimum - Creation of additional seats is restricted to current admissions only and shall not be a permanent feature - Petitions disposed of.
