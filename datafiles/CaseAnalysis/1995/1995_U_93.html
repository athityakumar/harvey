Joint Action Council of Service Doctors' Organisations and Others v Union of India and Another

Supreme Court of India

14 December 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

B.L. Hansaria, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 883; (1996) 7 SCC 256; (1996) SCC (L&S) 568; 1996 (1) AD(SC) 239; 1996 (33) ATC 259; JT 1995 (9) SC 142; 1995(7) SCALE 224; 1996 (1) SLR 77; 1996 (1) Supreme 57; 1996 (1) UJ 214

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Whether the Tikoo Committee recommendations were binding on the respondent? - No vested right has been created by the force of the recommendations of the Committee on the petitioner-Association - Unless accepted cannot be said to be binding on the UOI - No further directions in regard to 1987 Package and 1989 Settlement - As to the unaccepted recommendations of the Tikoo Committee, the recommendations of the Vth Pay Commission may be awaited - Held, Petition Disposed Of.
