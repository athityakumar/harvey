Union Of India And Ors. v R.K.L.D. Azad

Supreme Court of India

09 August 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

M.K. Mukherjee, B.P. Jeevan Reddy

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 517; (1995) Supp3 SCC 426; (1995) SCC (L&S) 1283; AIR 1996 SC 845; 1995 (31) ATC 190; JT 1997 (10) SC 529B; [1995] Supp2 S.C.R. 602

Case Digest

Subject: Defence & Security Forces; Service

Keywords: Pension Regulations for the Army, 1961

Summary: Army Act, 1950, ss.123, 71 and 192 - Liability of offender - Junior Commissioned Officer - Retiring and hence not subject of Act - Dismissal - Entitlement for pension or gratuity on basis of his previous service - Held, can be dismissed from the service for committing offence under the Act - Not eligible for pension etc. on basis of his previous service.
