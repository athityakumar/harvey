The State Of Madhya Pradesh And Ors. v Dr. Yashwant Trimbak

Supreme Court of India

04 December 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1931; (1996) 2 SCC 305; (1996) SCC (L&S) 510; 1996 (1) AD(SC) 246; AIR 1996 SC 765; 1996 (33) ATC 208; JT 1995 (9) SC 430; 1996 LabIC 765; 1995(7) SCALE 131; 1996 (1) SCJ 118; 1996 (1) SLR 71; [1995] Supp6 S.C.R. 128; 1996 (1) UJ 436

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Service

Keywords: Civil Services Pension Rules

Summary: Constitution of India, Arts.309 and 166; M.P. Civil Services Pension Rules, 1976, r.9(2)(b)(i) - Departmental inquiry against retired employee - Initiation of - Order passed in the name of Governor and signed by Under Secretary - Held, duly authenticated cannot be questioned in any Court - Immunity from judicial enquiries under art.166(2) attracted - Authentication rules to sign the document signifies the consent of the Governor and acceptance of the advice of concerned Minister.
