Union of India and Others v Dinesh Kumar Saxena and Others

Supreme Court of India

24 February 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 754; (1995) 3 SCC 401; (1995) SCC (L&S) 696; AIR 1995 SC 1565; 1995 (29) ATC 585; JT 1996 (Supp) SC 647; 1996 (1) LLJ 14; 1996 (1) LLN 65; 1995(2) SCALE 14; [1995] 2 S.C.R. 341; 1995 (2) SLJ 104; 1995 (2) SLR 383; 1995 (3) UPLBEC 1756

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Whether termination of temporary posts created through census in 1981 when work was over, justified? - High Court ordered retrenchment of employees - Held, retrenched employees to be considered only if they fulfill all other norms laid down in connection with posts in question - Appeals partly allowed.
