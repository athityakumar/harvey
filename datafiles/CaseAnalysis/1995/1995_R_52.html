Morinda Co-Op. Sugar Mills Ltd v Ram Kishan And Others Etc

Supreme Court of India

25 August 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

B.L. Hansaria, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1180; (1995) 5 SCC 653; (1995) SCC (L&S) 1279; AIR 1996 SC 332; JT 1995 (6) SC 547; 1996 (1) MLJ(SC) 42; [1995] Supp3 S.C.R. 85; 1996 (1) UPLBEC 606

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 2(oo)(bb), 25 F - Retrenchment - Seasonal workers - Ceased to work subsequent to closure of season - Held, not amounts to retrenchment - Direction to employer to maintain register of workmen - And to publicise onset of new season and engage same workers in accordance with seniority and exigency of work.
