Kayamuddin Shamsuddin Khan v State Bank of India

Supreme Court of India

19 January 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

Faizan Uddin, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1470; (1998) 8 SCC 676

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Practice & Procedure - Civil Procedure Code, 1908, O. 41, r. 1(3) - Dismissal of appeal - On the ground of non-compliance of direction - Whether non-compliance of direction given for considering application for stay a ground to dismiss appeal? - Held, non-compliance with the direction given regarding deposit under sub-r. (3) of r. 1 of O. 41 would result in the Court refusing to stay the execution of the decree - In other words, the application for stay of the execution of the decree could be dismissed for such non-compliance but the Court could not give a direction for the dismissal of the appeal itself for such non-compliance - Appeal allowed.
