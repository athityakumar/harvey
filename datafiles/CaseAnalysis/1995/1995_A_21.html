Basant Industries Nunhai, Agra v Additional Collector of Customs, Bombay

Supreme Court of India

18 January 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, N.P. Singh

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1452; (1995) Supp3 SCC 320; 1996 (66) ECR 227; 1996 (81) E.L.T. 195

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Customs Act, 1962 - Whether Department was justified in proceeding against appellant on basis of comparison of two invoices? - It is not correct to proceed on premise of undervaluation merely on basis of comparison of two invoices - In business world considerations of relationship with customer are also a relevant factor - Held, department was wrong in revising price - Appeal allowed.
