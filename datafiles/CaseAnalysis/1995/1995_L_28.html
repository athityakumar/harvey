Shashi Gupta Smt v Life Insurance Corporation of India and Another

Supreme Court of India

24 February 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

B.L. Hansaria, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 2064; (1995) Supp1 SCC 754; AIR 1995 SC 1367; [1995] 84 Comp Cas 436; 1995 (2) CPJ 15; 1995 (2) CPR 74; 1996 (4) CTJ 6; 1995(2) SCALE 24; 1995 (1) UJ 779

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Indirect Tax

Summary: Life Insurance Corporation Act, 1956, s. 6 - Interpretation of statutes - Insurance policy lapsed for non-payment of third annual premium - Policy holder assassinated by terrorists - Widow was paid sum assured along with bonus by way of ex gratia payment under circular of corporation - Claim for double benefit by dependents - Two interpretations of circular possible - Held, interpretation beneficial to policy holder to be adopted.
