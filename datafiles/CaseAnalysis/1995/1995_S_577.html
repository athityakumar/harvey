 R. K. Sabharwal and Others v State of Punjab and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 February 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

B.L. Hansaria, Kuldip Singh, M.K. Mukherjee, S.B. Majmudar, S. Mohan

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 1756; (1995) SCC (L&S) 548; (1995) 2 SCC 745; AIR 1995 SC 1371; AIR 1995 SCW 1371; 1995 (29) ATC 481; 1995 (1) CLR 71; 1995 (70) FLR 985; JT 1995 (2) SC 351; 1995 LabIC 1618; 1995 (1) LLN 810; 1995(1) SCALE 685; [1995] 2 S.C.R. 35; 1995 (3) SLJ 227; 1995 (1) SLR 791

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 309 and 16(4) - Punjab Service of Engineers class I, P W D (Irrigation Branch) Rules, 1964, r. 9 - Promotion - Reservations for Scheduled Caste and Backward Classes - Percentage fixed for particular cadre - Held, cannot be varied or changed - Simply because some have already been appointed / promoted against general seats - 'Running account' is to operate only till quota provided and not thereafter.
