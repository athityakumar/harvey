P.S Ghalaut v State Of Haryana And Others

Supreme Court of India

03 August 1995

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, K.S. Paripoornan

Where Reported

1995 Indlaw SC 941; (1995) 5 SCC 625; (1995) SCC (L&S) 1270; AIR 1996 SC 351; 1995 (31) ATC 183; 1995 (71) FLR 874B; 1995(5) SCALE 56; 1996 (1) SLJ 92; [1995] Supp2 S.C.R. 506; 1995 (2) UJ 686

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Meritorious

Summary: Service - Constitution - Haryana Medical Education Service Rules, 1965, r. 13, 2nd Proviso - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16(4) - Inter se seniority - General candidate vis-a-vis reserved candidate - General candidate shown by P S Commission as more meritorious to reserved candidate - He shall be senior to reserved candidate - Appointment made by application of roster - Reserved candidate steals seniority over general class candidate - Whether arbitrary? - Held, not arbitrary - Appeal dismissed.
