Babul Mitra Alias Anil Mitra v State of West Bengal and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 September 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Dwivedi, Y.V. Chandrachud, J.M. Shelat

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 478; (1973) SCC (Cr) 353; (1973) 1 SCC 393; AIR 1973 SC 197; 1974 CRLJ 395; 1973 CRLR 319

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Maintenance of Internal Security Act, 1971, ss. 3(2),3(3)and 3(1)(a)(ii) - Detention - (A) One month's delay by State Govt. - In considering detenu's representation if constituted inordinate delay - (B) Distinction between 'law and order' and 'public order' - Held, did not constitute inordinate delay - True distinction is one of degree and extent of reach of act in question upon society - Petition dismissed.
