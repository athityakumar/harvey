V. V. Iyer of Bombay v Jasjit Singh, Collector of Customs and another

Supreme Court of India

22 September 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

A.K. Mukherjea, A.N. Grover, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 2; (1973) 1 SCC 148; AIR 1973 SC 194; 1985 ECR 1745; 1999 (110) E.L.T. 414

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Customs - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 226 - Writ Petition - Correctness of conclusions reached by customs authorities in adopting one reasonable view as against another while interpreting an entry under taxation law - Held, cannot be disturbed - Appeal dismissed.
