State of Orissa and Others v Harinarayan Jaiswal and Others

Supreme Court of India

14 March 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, P. Jaganmohan Reddy

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 122; (1972) 2 SCC 36; AIR 1972 SC 1816; 1972 CutLT 481; [1972] 3 S.C.R. 784; 1972 TAXLR 2298

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure; Service

Keywords: Excise Regulation

Summary: Excise - Bihar And Orissa Excise Act, 1915, ss. 22 and 29 - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14, 19(1)(g) and 226 - Sale of country liquor shops - Highest bid in auction sales - Rejected by Govt. - Order rejecting bid is not violative of arts. 14 and 19(1)(g) of Constitution nor is subject to judicial review - Sale of shops by private negotiation - Validity - Held, is not invalid - Petition dismissed.
