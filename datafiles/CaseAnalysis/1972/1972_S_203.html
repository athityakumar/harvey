Khatri Hemraj Amulakh v State of Gujarat

Supreme Court of India

07 February 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

Hans Raj Khanna, J.M. Shelat

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 635; (1972) 3 SCC 671; (1972) SCC (Cr) 715; AIR 1972 SC 922; 1972 CRLJ 626

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 302 - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 25 - (A) Conviction u/s 302 - Failure of prosecution to prove motive - And also to prove that accused was alone with deceased at time of occurrence - Accused furnishing plausible explanation as to his subsequent conduct in running with blood-stained clothes to police station - (B) Admissibility of confessional statement in First Information Report - Held,conviction was liable to be set aside - A confessional statement made by accused to Police which forms basis of first information report is inadmissible - Appeal allowed.
