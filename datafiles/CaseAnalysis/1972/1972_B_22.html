State Of Assam And Anr. Etc v Basanta Kumar Das Etc. Etc

Supreme Court of India

22 December 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

A. Alagiriswami, C.A. Vaidyialingam

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 238; (1973) SCC (L&S) 239; (1973) 1 SCC 461; AIR 1973 SC 1252; 1973 LabIC 920; 1973 (1) LLJ 119; [1973] 3 S.C.R. 158; 1973 SLJ 265; 1973 (1) SLR 921; 1973 UJ 523

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 133 and 309 - (A) Determination of nature of executive instruction, i.e. Memorandum D/-21-03-1963 issued by Assam Govt. - (B) Whether retention of employee in service beyond age of superannuation is discretitonary - Objection raised at late stage - Permissibility - Held, is merely executive instruction and not rule u/art. 309 - Is in discretion of Govt. - Appeal allowed.
