Firms Amar Nath Basheshar Dass v Tek Chand

Supreme Court of India

21 March 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

P. Jaganmohan Reddy, K.S. Hegde, G.K. Mitter

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 131; (1972) 1 SCC 893; AIR 1972 SC 1548; 1972 RCJ 845; 1972 RCR(Rent) 380; [1972] 3 S.C.R. 922

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - East Punjab Urban Rent Restriction Act, 1949, ss. 3 and 13 - (A) Interpretation of notification D/- 30-07-1965, u/s 3(b) - Exemption of decrees for eviction from operation of s. 13 - (B) Interpretation of Statutes - Duty of Court - Held, what is exempted by Notification is decree for ejectment and not suit itself - To ascertain meaning and intendment of Legislature - Appeal dismissed.
