Gulam Abbas v Haji Kayyum Ali and Others

Supreme Court of India

18 September 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Hameedullah Beg, A.N. Grover, A.K. Mukherjea

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 350; (1973) 1 SCC 1; AIR 1973 SC 554; [1973] 2 S.C.R. 300

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 115 - Family law - Relinquishment of future possible right of inheritance by Muslim heir - For a consideration - Effect - Held, may debar him from setting up his right when it actually comes into being - Appeal allowed.
