Kamini Kumar Das Choudhury v State Of West Bengal & Ors

Supreme Court of India

24 July 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Hameedullah Beg, A.N. Ray

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 161; (1972) 2 SCC 420; AIR 1972 SC 2060; 1972 LabIC 1270; [1973] 1 S.C.R. 718; 1972 SLR 746; 1973 UJ 214

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 226 - Limitation Act, 1908 - Condonation of delay - Standard for measuring reasonable delay - When analogy with statute of limitation is not available - Petition by dismissed public servant - Other grounds for refusal to issue writ - Held, acquiescence and presumed abandonment of right to complain inferred from inordinate delay, laches and public interest or public policy also are well established grounds for refusal to exercise discretion u/art. 226 - Appeal allowed.
