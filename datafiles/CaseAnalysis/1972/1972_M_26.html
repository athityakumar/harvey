Commissioner of Wealth Tax, Assam v Mahadeo Jalan and Mahabir Prasad Jalan and Others

Supreme Court of India

13 September 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

P. Jaganmohan Reddy, Hans Raj Khanna

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 267; (1973) SCC (Tax) 103; (1973) 3 SCC 157; AIR 1973 SC 1023; 1972 (1) CTR 395; 1973 (1) ITJ 320; [1972] 86 ITR 621; [1973] 2 S.C.R. 215; 1973 TAXLR 1757

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income and Direct Taxes - Wealth Tax Act, 1957, s. 7(1) - Constitution, art. 136(1) - Appeal by special leave - (A) Methods of valuation of shares in a limited company - (B) Scope of power of Supreme Court to re-frame the question as framed by the High Court which called for statement of the case u/s. 66 of the Income Tax Act - Held, method stated - The SC has the power to re-frame the question as long as new and different question is not raised in order to bring out the real issue between the parties - Appeals dismissed.
