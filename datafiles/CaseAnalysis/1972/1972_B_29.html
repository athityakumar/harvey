Bhagat Ram Patanga v State of Punjab

Supreme Court of India

07 April 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

C.A. Vaidyialingam, P. Jaganmohan Reddy, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 158; (1972) 2 SCC 170; AIR 1972 SC 1571; [1973] 1 S.C.R. 92

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Municipalities & Local Governments; Service

Keywords: Punjab Municipal Act, 1911

Summary: Municipalities & Local Governments - Punjab Municipal Act, 1911, s. 16(1)(e) - (A) Member of committee bringing in outside elements in order to create confusion and chaos at meeting - Effect - (B) Order removing a person from membership of committee passed by state Govt. - Duty of state Govt. - Held, member can be considered to have 'flagrantly abused his position as a member of committee' - Duty of state Govt. to give reasons and to consider explanation given by member - Appeal dismissed.
