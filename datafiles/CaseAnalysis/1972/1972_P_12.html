Pala Singh & Anr. v State Of Punjab

Supreme Court of India

23 August 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

I.D. Dua, Hans Raj Khanna, J.M. Shelat

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 177; (1972) 2 SCC 640; (1973) SCC (Cr) 55; AIR 1972 SC 2679; 1973 CRLJ 59; [1973] 1 S.C.R. 964; 1973 UJ 294

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal

Summary: CrPC, 1898, ss. 157 and 423 - Practice and Procedure - (A) Delay in receipt of occurrence report by Magistrate -Effect upon investigation - (B) Appeal against acquittal - Appellate Court's powers to review evidence - Held, by itself does not make the investigation tainted - Appellate Court has full powers to review evidence upon which order of acquittal is founded - Appeal Dismissed.
