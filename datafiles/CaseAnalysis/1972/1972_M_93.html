Munshi Singh & Others Etc. Etc v Union Of India Etc. Etc

Supreme Court of India

23 August 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, K.S. Hegde, D.G. Palekar

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 176; (1973) 2 SCC 337; AIR 1973 SC 1150; [1973] 1 S.C.R. 973

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 5A and 4(1) - Land acquisition - Mere words in notification u/s. 4(1) that land is needed for 'planned development of area' - Sufficiency as regard to legal requirements - Held, are not sufficient to satisfy requirements of law - Appeal allowed.
