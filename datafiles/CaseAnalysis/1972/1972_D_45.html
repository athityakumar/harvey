Laxmi and Company v Dr. Anant R. Deshpande and Another

Supreme Court of India

12 September 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, I.D. Dua

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 231; (1973) 1 SCC 37; AIR 1973 SC 171; [1973] 2 S.C.R. 172

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Declaratory Suit, Ex-Parte

Summary: Rent Control - Presidency Small Cause Courts Act, 1882, s. 42 A - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 136 - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 41, r. 33 - Tenancy - (A) When occupant can claim tenancy and protection against eviction? - (B) Practice and Procedure - Subsequent events - Taking notice of by court - Held, at time when he 'appears' at date of hearing - Court can take notice of subsequent events to shorten litigation, to preserve rights of both parties and to subserve ends of justice - Appeal allowed.
