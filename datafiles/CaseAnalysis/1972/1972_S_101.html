Sabharwal Brothers and Another v Smt. Guna Amrit Thandani of Bombay

Supreme Court of India

29 March 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

G.K. Mitter, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 137; (1973) 3 SCC 750; AIR 1972 SC 1893; 1972 RCJ 542; [1973] 1 S.C.R. 53

Case Digest

Subject: Trusts & Associations

Summary: Trusts & Associations - Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1961, s. 91(1)(b) - Whether dispute touching business of society? - Objects of society were to carry on trade of buying selling, hiring and letting land - Held, letting by a member of such society of flat purchased from society could not be said 'to touch business of society' - Appeal allowed.
