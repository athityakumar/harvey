State of Bihar v Deokaran Nenshi and Another

Supreme Court of India

24 August 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

J.M. Shelat, Hans Raj Khanna

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 504; (1972) 2 SCC 890; (1973) SCC (Cr) 114; AIR 1973 SC 908; 1973 CRLJ 347; 1973 (26) FLR 77; [1973] 1 S.C.R. 1004

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Mines & Minerals - Mines Act, 1952 - Shops and Establishments Act - Offence u/s 66 - Covered by substantive part of s. 79 and not by explanation thereto - A complaint for failure to furnish return by prescribed date filed - Held, complaint must, therefore, be filed within a period of six months - Appeal dismissed.
