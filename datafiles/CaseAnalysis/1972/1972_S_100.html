Ram Saran and Another v Smt. Ganga Devi

Supreme Court of India

17 April 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 578; (1973) 2 SCC 60; AIR 1972 SC 2685

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Specific Relief Act, 1877, s. 42 - Land and Property - Defendent having possession of some of the suit properties - Plaintiff not seek possession but claims a declaration that he is the owner of the suit properties - Maintainability of suit - Held, not maintainable - Appeal Dismissed.
