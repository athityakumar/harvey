Baikuntha Nath Paramanick (Dead) By His L. Rs v Sashi Bhusan Pramanik (Dead) By His L. Rs. and

Supreme Court of India

09 August 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover, D.G. Palekar

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 519; (1973) 2 SCC 334; AIR 1972 SC 2531; 1973 CRLR 406; 1973 UJ 207

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal

Summary: Evidence Act, 1872, s. 114 - Hindu Law - Joint family - Acquisitions - Presumption as to -Joint family is found to be in possession of nucleus sufficient to make the impugned acquisitions - Held, a presumption arises that the acquisitions standing in the names of the persons who were in the management of the family properties are family acquisitions - Appeal Dismissed.
