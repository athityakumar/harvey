The Management Of D.T.U. v Shri B. B. L. Hajelay & Anr

Supreme Court of India

06 September 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

D.G. Palekar, S.N. Dwivedi, J.M. Shelat

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 357; (1972) 2 SCC 744; (1973) SCC (L&S) 57; AIR 1972 SC 2452; 1973 (27) FLR 10; 1972 LabIC 1619; 1973 (1) LLJ 76; [1973] 2 S.C.R. 114; 1973 SLJ 19; 1972 SLR 787; 1973 UJ 386

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Municipalities & Local Governments; Service

Keywords: Delhi Road Transport Authority Act, 1950

Summary: Delhi Municipal Corporation Act, 1957, s. 95(1) and Proviso to s-s. 95(2) and 92(1)(b) - Delhi Road Transport Authority Act, 1950 - Service matter - Validity of dismissal of employee of DRTA absorbed in Delhi Corporation - Held, an employee of DRTA carrying a minimum monthly salary of Rs. 350 at the time of his absorption in the Delhi Corporation cannot be dismissed by an authority below the rank of General Manager (Transport) - Neither delegation of his functions nor a regulation of delegation can nullify this statutory protection u/s. 95(2) provisio - Appeal dismissed .
