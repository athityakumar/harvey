B. D. Gupta v State Of Haryana

Supreme Court of India

18 September 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

A.K. Mukherjea, A.N. Grover, M. Hameedullah Beg

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 197; (1973) SCC (L&S) 49; (1973) 3 SCC 149; AIR 1972 SC 2472; 1972 LabIC 1613; 1973 (1) LLJ 26; [1973] 2 S.C.R. 323; 1973 SLJ 25; 1972 SLR 845; 1973 UJ 418

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Punjab Civil Services Rules, 1953 - Fundamental Rules, r. 54 - Order u/r. 7.3 of 1953 Rules - Consideration of relevant facts - Opportunity to make out case to employee, when needed - Held, if order affects employee financially, it must be passed after objective consideration and assessment of all relevant facts and after giving him full opportunity to make out his case - Appeal Allowed.
