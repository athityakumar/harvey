 Lalit Mohan Deb and Others v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

08 February 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

D.G. Palekar, S.M. Sikri, A.N. Grover, A.N. Ray, M. Hameedullah Beg

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 632; (1973) SCC (L&S) 272; (1973) 3 SCC 862; AIR 1972 SC 995; 1972 LabIC 543; 1972 SLR 411

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art.309 - Tripura Employees' (Revision of Pay and Allowances) Rules, 1968 - Absence of statutory rules regulating promotion to slection grade posts - Competency of Govt. to issue administrative instructions - Held, can issue instructions as long as those instructions are not inconsistent with rules already framed - Appeal dismissed.
