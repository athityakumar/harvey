Kani Ram and Another v Smt. Kazani and Others

Supreme Court of India

19 April 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, K.S. Hegde, G.K. Mitter

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 146; (1972) 2 SCC 192; AIR 1972 SC 1427; 1972 RCJ 546; 1972 RCR(Rent) 528; [1973] 1 S.C.R. 254; 1972 UJ 921

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure; Rent Control

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 11 - Constructive res judicata - Rule applies to execution proceedings - Tenancy - Executive Court and First Appellate Court hold that Court which passed eviction decree was satisfied about existence of grounds mentioned in s. 13 of Delhi and Ajmer Rent Control Act - Whether it can be agitated in subsequent execution proceedings? - Decision held, was not one of law only but of a mixed question of law and fact and thus could not be agitated - Appeal allowed.
