 Bishan Sarup Gupta v Union Of India And Ors

Supreme Court of India

16 August 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

D.G. Palekar, S.M. Sikri, A.N. Ray, I.D. Dua, M. Hameedullah Beg

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 305; (1973) 3 SCC 1; AIR 1972 SC 2627; 1973 (1) SLR 115; [1975] Supp S.C.R. 491

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Land & Property

Keywords: Income Tax Officers (Class I, Grade II) service Recruitment Rules 1945

Summary: Service - Income Tax Officers (Class I, Grade II) service Recruitment Rules 1945 - Recruitment - Fresh seniority list - Directions - Instant appeals were filed challenging to the seniority list of Income-tax Officers prepared by the Central Board of Revenue in pursuance of the orders of HC - Whether the seniority list prepared on 15-7-1968 was correct and in accordance with the mandamus issued - Held, after fresh seniority list was made in accordance with the above directions it would be open to any direct recruit or promote to point out to the department that in the selections made to the post of Assistant Commissioner from 1962 onwards, he, being otherwise eligible, was entitled on account of the new seniority given to him to be considered for promotion to the post of Assistant Commissioner - Department might have to consider his case for promotion on his record as on the date when be ought to have been considered for selection but not so considered - If he was selected, his position would be adjusted in the cadre of the Assistant Commissioners without affecting the promote Assistant Commissioners who had been confirmed prior to 22-2-1967 - Seniority list was prepared by the Govt. in pursuance of the mandamus - Seniority list was not correct and would have to be prepared afresh in accordance with the directions and observations made - Demand made by the officers for implementation of mandamus was stilt unfulfilled and it could be achieved only after the Govt. file a proper list of seniority - Instant proceedings, therefore, would have to be kept pending till such, a, seniority list was prepared and filed in court - Respondents, were directed to prepare a fresh seniority list and file it in Court - Petition disposed of.
