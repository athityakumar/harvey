Union of India and Others v S. B. Kohli and Another

Supreme Court of India

20 December 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

A. Alagiriswami, I.D. Dua, C.A. Vaidyialingam

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 307; (1973) SCC (L&S) 136; (1973) 3 SCC 592; AIR 1973 SC 811; 1973 LabIC 423; [1973] 3 S.C.R. 117; 1973 SLJ 226; 1973 (1) SLR 1234; 1973 UJ 498

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Medical Council

Summary: Service - Health & Drug - Central Health Service Rules, 1963, sch. 2, annexure 1, items 2 and 3, sch. 2, annexure 2, item 7, r. 8 (3), Proviso - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14 and 16 - Service Rules - Health services - (A) F.R. C.S. u/sch. 2, Annexure 2, item 7, if can be deemed to be Post Graduate degree in Orthopedics - (B) Interpretation of annexures 1 and 2 of sch. 2 as - Necessary qualifications also for promotion to supertime grade 2 - Constitutionality of interpretation - Held, cannot be deemed so - Such interpretation does not violate arts. 14 and 16 of Constitution - Appeal dismissed
