State Of Tamil Nadu Etc v Cement Distributors Private Ltd. Etc. Etc

Supreme Court of India

22 November 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, P. Jaganmohan Reddy, Hans Raj Khanna

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 333; (1973) SCC (Tax) 220; (1973) 3 SCC 342; AIR 1973 SC 668; 1973 (2) CTR 100; [1973] 2 S.C.R. 1019; 1973 (31) STC 309; 1973 TAXLR 1933

Case Digest

Subject: Socio-Economic

Summary: T.N. General Sales Tax Act,1959, s. 2(n) - T.N. General Sales Tax Rules, 1959, r. 6(f) - Cement Control Order, 1958, cl. 6(4) - Supreme ourt Rules, 1966, O. 15, r. 5 - (A) Supplies of gunny bags ( in which the cement was supplied) by the producers as per directions given by the Central Govt. in pursuance of the 1958 - Liability as to tax -(B) Deduction from gross turnover - Meaning of expression 'Charges for packing' under - T.N. General Sales Tax Rules, 1959 - Meaning of - (C) New point - Point not raised in petition of appeal or by any special application - Permissibility in the light of Supreme Court Rules - (D) New point - Point not raised before High Court nor in the memo of appeal - If allowed to be raised for the first time in arguments - Held, did not amount to 'sales' within 1959 Act therefore producers are not liable to pay sales tax on the turnover relating to price of gunny bags as the price per bag of cement fixed by the Government from time to time was inclusive of the price of the gunny bags as well - Packing Charges include both the price of the packing material as well as the labour charges relating to packing - Cannot be permitted to be raised for the first time in arguments - Cannot be allowed to be raised for the first time in arguments - Appeals dismissed.
