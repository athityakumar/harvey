Dr. Govinddas and Another v Smt. Shantibai and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 January 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

S.M. Sikri, A.N. Ray, M. Hameedullah Beg

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 656; (1973) 3 SCC 418; AIR 1972 SC 1520; 1972 (8) DLT 66; 1972 (74) PLR 227; 1972 UJ 543

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Specific Relief Act, 1877, s. 12 - Certain person purchasing property with express notice of prior agreement between seller and another person - Suit by that other person for specific performance of agreement - Held, he was entitled to a decree - Appeal dismissed.
