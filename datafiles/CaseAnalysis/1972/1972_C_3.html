T. S. Krishna v C. I. T. Madras

Supreme Court of India

03 October 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

P. Jaganmohan Reddy, I.D. Dua, Hans Raj Khanna

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 119; (1973) 1 SCC 69; AIR 1972 SC 2674; 1973 (2) CTR 4; [1973] 87 ITR 429; [1973] 2 S.C.R. 533

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Debt Laws

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 40(a)(iia) and 57(iii) and 58(1A) - Income Tax (Amendment) Act , 1972, s. 5 - Deduction - Effect of amendment introduced in ss. 40 and 58 - Interpretation of s. 40(a)(ii) expln. - Wealth Tax paid on share stock holding during accounting year 1962-63 - If deductible - Held, is not an admissible deduction either u/s. 57(iii) or u/s. 58(1A) as amended in 1972 - Appeal Dismissed.
