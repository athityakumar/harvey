Abdul Aziz v Distt. Magistrate Burdwan and Others

Supreme Court of India

11 October 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, J.M. Shelat, I.D. Dua

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 218; (1973) 1 SCC 301; (1973) SCC (Cr) 321; AIR 1973 SC 770; 1973 CRLJ 590; [1973] 2 S.C.R. 646

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal; Defence & Security Forces

Keywords: Defence Regulations

Summary: Criminal - Maintenance of Internal Security Act, 1971, ss. 3 and 3 (1) (a) (ii) - Provision whether invalid for excessive delegation? - Held, expression 'internal security' is comprehensive enough to include concept of 'public order' - Not vitiated thereby - Petition dismissed.
