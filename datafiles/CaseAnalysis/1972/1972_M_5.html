Union Of India And Ors v M. Ravi Varma and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 January 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

Hans Raj Khanna, I.D. Dua, J.M. Shelat, G.K. Mitter

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 77; (1972) 1 SCC 379; AIR 1972 SC 670; 1972 LabIC 345; [1972] 2 S.C.R. 992; 1972 SLR 211

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law; Land & Property; Parliament & Legislature

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 309 - Conditions of service - Seniority - Office memorandum D/-22-02-1959 and provisions laid down in annexure thereto providing for determination of seniority - Of appointees to various central service by date of confirmation - Retrospectively - Held, are not retrospective and do not apply to persons appointed before date of that memorandum - Appeal dismissed.
