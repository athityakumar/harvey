Mewar Sugar Mills Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax, Rajasthan

Supreme Court of India

26 September 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

P. Jaganmohan Reddy, I.D. Dua, K.S. Hegde, Hans Raj Khanna

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 277; (1973) 3 SCC 143; AIR 1973 SC 2326; 1973 (2) CTR 75; [1973] 87 ITR 400; [1973] 2 S.C.R. 429

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 10(2)(xv) - Determination of nature of expenditure, capital or revenue - Acquisition of monopoly right to manufacture sugar - Payments in respect of monopoly right and towards royalty - Nature of - Held, to be capital expenditure - Appeal partly allowed.
