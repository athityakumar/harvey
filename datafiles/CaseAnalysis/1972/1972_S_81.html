Shri Kedar Nath Bahl v State of Punjab and Others

Supreme Court of India

02 February 1972

Case Analysis

Bench

D.G. Palekar, K.S. Hegde, P. Jaganmohan Reddy

Where Reported

1972 Indlaw SC 640; (1974) 3 SCC 21; AIR 1972 SC 873; 1973 (27) FLR 20; 1972 LabIC 433; 1972 SLR 320; 1972 UJ 681

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Service matter - (A) Master & Servant - Expiry of probation - Confirmation whether follows automatically - (B) Writ petition - Mala fide - Decision to abolish temporary post - Considerations - Held, intention and actions of Govt. and not of immediate superior to affected person are to be considered - Appeal dismissed.
