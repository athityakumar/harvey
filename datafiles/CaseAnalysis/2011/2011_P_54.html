New India Assurance Company Limited and another v Priya Blue Industries Private Limited and another

Supreme Court of India

09 March 2011

Case Analysis

Bench

B. SUDARSHAN REDDY, Surinder Singh Nijjar

Where Reported

2011 Indlaw SC 194; (2011) 4 SCC 231; 2011 (3) ALD(SC) 142; 2011 (86) ALR 2; 2011 (3) AWC 2364; [2011] 163 Comp Cas 42; 2011 (2) CPJ(Sc) 15; JT 2011 (3) SC 452; 2011 (2) RCR(Civil) 410; 2011(3) SCALE 502

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection; Insurance

Keywords: Proximate

Summary: Consumer Protection - Insurance - Consumer Protection Act, 1986 - Marine Insurance Act, 1963, ss. 57 and 60 - Repudiation of Insurance Claim - Non- disclosure of material facts - Complainant, engaged in the business of ship breaking and scrap dealing, purchased and imported a bulk ore and oil carrier for the purpose of scrapping under a Memorandum of Agreement - Complainant took a marine insurance policy for hull and machinery for the said vessel - Said vessel was damaged on the way because of extremely rough weather - Complainant filed insurance claim for the said vessel which was repudiated by the Insurance Company - Complainant filed complaint before National Commission - Insurance Company contended that complainant did not disclose at the time or before taking the insurance policy that one engine of the vessel was not working - National Commission rejected the contention of the Insurance Company and allowed the claim filed by the complainant - Hence, the instant appeal - Whether order of the National Commission suffers from any infirmity requiring our interference? - Held, complainant never made any representation that the vessel had two functional engines - On the other hand, memorandum of agreement speaks that starboard engine was not working - At no point of time the insurance Company took this plea to repudiate the contract - Said plea was raised for the first time in the written statement filed in the National Commission as an afterthought - Impugned order of the National Commission upheld - Appeals dismissed.
