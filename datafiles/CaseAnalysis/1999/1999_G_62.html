Veerabhadran R v Government of Andhra Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

11 August 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1584; (1999) 9 SCC 43; AIR 2000 SC 1918; 2000 (33) LabIC 1841; 2000 (2) LLJ 766

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 309 - Andhra Pradesh Revised Pension Rules, 1980, r. 52(1)(c) - Withholding of Gratuity - Interest on amount withheld - Rule expressly permits for - In view of pendency of judicial proceeding against employee - Criminal case pending - Petition by appellant before S A T against retirement during suspension - S A T also directed withholding of death-cum-gratuity till final order is passed thereon - Held, withholding of gratuity, therefore, is legal - Appellant acquitted - Was paid to full pension and gratuity - No delay in making payment - Appellant cannot claim interest on amount of gratuity so withheld - Appellant cannot claim deemed promotion from date on which his junior was promoted after his acquittal.
