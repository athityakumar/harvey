Ahmedabad Municipal Corporation and Another v Nilaybhai R. Thakore and Another

Supreme Court of India

13 October 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 770; (1999) 8 SCC 139; AIR 2000 SC 114; 2000 (1) G.L.R. 634; JT 1999 (8) SC 244; [1999] Supp3 S.C.R. 647

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 136 - Admission in medical college - Reservation of seats in medical college for local students argued - Whether High Court in dismissing r. 6(1) and 7 for admission to municipal medical college on the ground that such students are not within municipal limits and not be treated local students as violation of arts. 14 and 15 of Constitution is valid? - Relied on D. P. Joshi v. State of M.B. [1955 Indlaw SC 123], D. N. Chanchala v. State of Mysore, [1971 Indlaw SC 641], Seaford Court Estates Ltd. v. Asher 1949 Indlaw CA 100(CA)) and Pentiah v. Muddala Veeramallappa, 1960 Indlaw SC 433 - Held, validity of r. 6(1) and 7 upheld and 'local student' means a student passed HSC/new SSC examination from any high school or colleges situated within Ahmedabad Municipal Corporation limits and include permanent resident student of Ahmedabad municipality - Appeal allowed.
