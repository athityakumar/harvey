Surendra Nath Mohanty and Another v State of Orissa

Supreme Court of India

04 May 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

M.B. Shah, D.P. Mohapatra, K.T. Thomas

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1063; (1999) 5 SCC 238; (1999) SCC (Cr) 998; 1999 (38) ACC 942; AIR 1999 SC 2181; 1999 (1) AllCriC 942; 1999 (2) AllCriR 1306; 2000 (5) Bom.C.R. 99; 1999 CRLJ 3496; JT 1999 (3) SC 408; [1999] 2 S.C.R. 1005; 1999 (4) Supreme 421

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, s. 320(9) - Indian Penal Code,1860, s. 326 - Compounding of offence u/s. 326 of IPC - Fact under consideration that parties have settled their disputes outside Court - Incident occurred 10 years ago and accused have already undergone 3 months RI - Held, not compoundable - Sentence of imprisonment reduced to period already undergone - Appeal disposed of.
