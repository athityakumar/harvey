Ramesh Chand Bansal and Others v District Magistrate/COLLECTOR Ghaziabad and Others

Supreme Court of India

11 May 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Misra, N. Santosh Hegde

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 814; (1999) 5 SCC 62; AIR 1999 SC 2126; 1999 (4) ALD(SC) 51; 1999 ALJ 1547; 1999 (42) ALR 18; 1999 (36) ALR 742; 1999 (3) AWC 2431; JT 1999 (4) SC 469; [1999] 3 S.C.R. 462

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Land & Property - Stamp Act, 1899, s. 47 A - Uttar Pradesh Stamp Rules, 1942, r. 340 A(a) - Registration Act, 1908 - Stamp Duty - Sale deed - Circle rate - Supply of biennial statement recording circle rate by Collector under Rule - Not restricted - (A) If rate fixed by Collector is final - (B) Circular enhancing circle rate for subsequent year, if invalid - Held, is not final but prima facie rate of that area concerned and open to Registering Authority as well as person seeking registration to prove to contrary actual market value of property - Not invalid - Appeal dismissed.
