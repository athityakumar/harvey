T. Hamza v State of Kerala

Supreme Court of India

11 August 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Mohapatra, K.T. Thomas

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 119; (2000) SCC (Cr) 216; (2000) 1 SCC 300; AIR 1999 SC 2966; 2000 (5) Bom.C.R. 102; 1999 CRLJ 4059; 1999 (84) ECR 17; JT 1999 (5) SC 616; [1999] Supp1 S.C.R. 343

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act, 1985, s. 50 - Search and seizure - Contravention of provision - Illegal possession of contraband article - Omission of asking accused whether be searched before Gazetted Officer by police officer - No other evidence in support of charge - Held, such cases are entirely based on search of person of accused leading to recovery of article - Conviction of accused under circumstances liable to be set aside.
