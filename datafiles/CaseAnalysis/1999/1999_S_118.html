T. K. Lathika v Seth Karsandas Jamnadas

Supreme Court of India

31 August 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, A.P. Misra

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 685; (1999) 6 SCC 632; AIR 1999 SC 3335; JT 1999 (6) SC 400; 2000 (1) MLJ(SC) 41; [1999] Supp2 S.C.R. 116

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Kerala Buildings (Lease and Rent Control) Act, 1965, ss. 11(3), Proviso 3 - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 111(f) - Eviction petition - (A) Transferee landlord entering into fresh lease with erstwhile tenant - Applicability of moratorium of 1 year on transferee landlord - (B) Tenability in view of ban contained in s. 11(3), Proviso 3 of 1965 Act, is to be first considered by court - Entering into merits without deciding question of tenability - Held, would not curtail moratorium period - Would be improper - Appeal dismissed.
