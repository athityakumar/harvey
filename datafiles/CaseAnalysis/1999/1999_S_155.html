State of Jammu and Kashmir v Shiv Ram Sharma and Others

Supreme Court of India

30 March 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1304; (1999) 3 SCC 653; (1999) SCC (L&S) 801; AIR 1999 SC 2012; 1999 (1) CLR 1021; 1999 (2) ESC 1023; JT 1999 (2) SC 484; 1999 LabIC 2096; 1999 (1) LLJ 1080; 1999 (2) LLN 1005; [1999] 2 S.C.R. 251; 1999 (2) SLR 247

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Constitution of Jammu and Kashmir

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16 - Constitution of Jammu and Kashmir, s. 124 - Jammu and Kashmir Geology and Mining (Subordinate) Service Recruitment Rules, 1990, r. 1 - Service Rules - Rule prescribing qualification for promotion but not totally denied promotional avenue thereby - Qualification not favourable to certain employee - Whether this could be ground to direct modification of Rule by applying principle of avoidance of stagnation? - Held, cannot be ground to direct modification - Appeal allowed.
