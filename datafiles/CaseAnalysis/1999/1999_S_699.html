Executive Engineer, R.E.O v Suresh Chandra Panda (Dead) Through LRs

Supreme Court of India

21 January 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 5; (1999) 9 SCC 92; 1999 ARBLR 567; JT 1999 (10) SC 555; 2000 (1) RAJ 489

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration Act - Whether claims 2, 3 and 6 decided by arbitrator in arbitration u/cl. 23 of agreement between parties are covered by cl. 11 of agreement and are not arbitrable? - Held, u/cl. 23, except as otherwise provided in contract, all disputes are arbitrable as set out in that clause - Finality of rates u/cl. 11 is a provision to contrary in contract which is excluded from cl. 23 - Arbitration award insofar as it decides claims 2, 3 and 6 is set aside - Appeal allowed and impugned award and decree is modified.
