G. Govindan v New India Assurance Company Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

08 April 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Venkataswami, A.P. Misra

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1181; (1999) SCC (Cr) 483; (1999) 3 SCC 754; 1999 ACJ 781; AIR 1999 SC 1398; 1999 (2) AWC 1539; 1999 (3) CalLT(SC) 34; [1999] 97 Comp Cas 443; JT 1999 (2) SC 622; [1999] 2 S.C.R. 476

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Corporate; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Motor Vehicles Act, 1988, ss. 147 and 157 - Motor Vehicles Act 1939, ss. 95 and 103A - Insurance company - Liability of - Policy not transferred in name of transferee of vehicle - Compensation to victim or LRs. of victim by insurer - Denial of - Held, not permissible.
