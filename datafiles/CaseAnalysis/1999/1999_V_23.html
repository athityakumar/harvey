Kerala State Electricity Board and Another v Valsala K. and Another

Supreme Court of India

16 September 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, A.S. Anand, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 717; (1999) 8 SCC 254; AIR 1999 SC 3502; 1999 (2) CLR 868; 1999 (83) FLR 508; JT 1999 (7) SC 292; 1999 (3) KLT 348; 1999 (2) LLJ 1112; [1999] Supp2 S.C.R. 657; 1999 (3) UPLBEC 2450

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Workmen's Compensation Act, 1923, ss. 4 and 4 A (amendment w.e.f. 1995) - Compensation and interest - Determination of - Accident took place before amendment - Enhancement of compensatory amount by amendment - Award accordingly - Held, not entitled to enhanced amount on account of amendment 1995 - Award of enhanced amount not interfered in view of fact that amount involved was partly and long time has elapsed - Relevant date for determining is date of accident - And, not date of adjudication of claim.
