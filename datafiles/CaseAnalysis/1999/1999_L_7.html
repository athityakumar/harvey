Lachhman Das Arora v Ganeshi Lal and Others

Supreme Court of India

01 September 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, S. Rajendra Babu, R.C. Lahoti

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 462; (1999) 8 SCC 532; AIR 1999 SC 3101; JT 1999 (6) SC 455; [1999] Supp2 S.C.R. 174

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Election; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Election - Representation of People Act, 1951, s. 81(1) - General clauses Act, 1897, s. 10 - Limitation for presentation of election petition which presented on reopening day of High Court after summer vacations i.e., after prescribed period - But Punjab and Haryana HC issued notification by declared courts open for hearing election petitions during summer vacation - Held, benefit u/s. 10 not available to save period of limitation - Therefore petition held to be time - barred - Appeal dismissed.
