Rachamreddi Chenna Reddy and Others v State of Andhra Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

09 February 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 389; (1999) 3 SCC 97; (1999) SCC (Cr) 384; AIR 1999 SC 994; 1999 (1) CCR 86; 1999 (1) Crimes 65; 1999 CRLJ 1445; 1999 (1) JCC 137; JT 1999 (1) SC 412; 1999 (1) RCR(Criminal) 849; 1999(1) SCALE 385; 1999 (2) Supreme 1

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Indian Evidence Act, 1872, s. 3, Indian Penal Code, 1860, 149 - Eye-witnesses - Whether testimony of Eye - witnesses be discarded on ground that they are relations of deceased - Held, their testimony cannot be discarded merely on this ground that they are relatives of deceased and hence common object of accused persons proved and therefore conviction of accused by taking recourse to s. 149 is not illegal.
