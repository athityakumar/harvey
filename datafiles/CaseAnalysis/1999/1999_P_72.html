Regional Manger, Bank of Baroda v Presiding Officer, Central Government Industrial Tribunal and Another

Supreme Court of India

28 January 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Majmudar, S.N. Phukan

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1620; (1999) 2 SCC 247; (1999) SCC (L&S) 546; AIR 1999 SC 912; 1999 ALJ 673; 1999 (1) CLR 709; 1999 (1) ESC 556; JT 1999 (1) SC 241; 1999 LabIC 1102; 1999 (1) LLJ 656; 2000 (1) SLJ 113; 1999 (1) SLR 618; 1999 (1) SLR 818; 1999 (1) Supreme 265

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, sch. 2, item 3 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 32 - Termination against misconduct - Interference with - Making of false statement against criminal proceeding to secure job in Bank - Charge under s. 307 Indian Penal Code, 1860 - Management waiting for decision of proceedings while issuing show cause notice - Acquittal ultimately - Held, charge under s. 307, Indian Penal Code, 1860 has not direct impact on appointing Bank - Not grave misconduct - Termination order set aside, under circumstances and in interest of justice.
