Manoj Alias Bhau and Others v State of Maharashtra

Supreme Court of India

08 April 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, M.B. Shah

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1176; (1999) 4 SCC 268; (1999) SCC (Cr) 533; AIR 1999 SC 1620; 1999 (38) AllCriC 767; 1999 (4) Bom.C.R. 765; 1999 CRLJ 2284; JT 1999 (2) SC 580; [1999] 2 S.C.R. 487; 1999 (4) Supreme 179

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 300 and 149 - Murder - Allegations that accused persons having formed unlawful assembly surrounded and assaulted deceased resulting into his death - Witnesses not stating specific overt act to have been committed by co-accused persons - Whether guilt of co-accused established beyond reasonable doubt? - Conviction of co-accused set aside - Appeal partly allowed.
