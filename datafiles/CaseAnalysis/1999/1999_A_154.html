Commissioner of Income Tax v Associated Fibre and Rubber Industries Private

Supreme Court of India

03 February 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Srinivasan, U.C. Banerjee

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1188; (1999) 2 SCC 309; AIR 1999 SC 934; 1999 (152) CTR 21; [1999] 236 ITR 471; JT 1999 (1) SC 311; [1999] 1 S.C.R. 375; [1999] 102 TAXMAN 700

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 37 - Business expenditure - Interest on borrowed money - Taken for purchase of Machinery - Machinery, though not used in business at time when assessment was made same treated as business asset - Interest paid on amount borrowed for purchase of such machinery - Held, is allowable deduction.
