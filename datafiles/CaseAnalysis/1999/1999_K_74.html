Karnataka Chemical Industries v Union of India

Supreme Court of India

21 July 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 99; (2000) 10 SCC 13; 1999 (113) E.L.T. 17

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Customs Tariff Act - Question involved interpretation of provisions of statute - Previous decisions available where provisions have been interpreted - When there is no challenge to statutory provision, no reason as to why writ petition be filed bypassing alternative remedy provided under statute - Appeal dismissed.
