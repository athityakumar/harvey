Oberoi Hotel Private Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax

Supreme Court of India

10 March 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

M.B. Shah, S.P. Bharucha, N. Santosh Hegde

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1216; (1999) 3 SCC 127; AIR 1999 SC 1110; 1999 (152) CTR 474; [1999] 236 ITR 903; JT 1999 (2) SC 81; [1999] 1 S.C.R. 955; [1999] 103 TAXMAN 236; 1999 (2) TLR 354

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 28 - Capital receipt - Assessee - company operating hotel for fee - Agreement providing option or right or lien - To purchase or operate property if owner desired to transfer hotel or lease or part of hotel to any other person - Right to exercise option given up by supplementary agreement - Amount received by assessee - Held, is capital receipt and not revenue receipt.
