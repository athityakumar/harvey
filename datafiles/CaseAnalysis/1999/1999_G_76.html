Graphite India Limited and Another v Durgapur Projects Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

27 August 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, M.B. Shah

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 678; (1999) 7 SCC 645; AIR 1999 SC 3289; JT 1999 (6) SC 317; [1999] Supp2 S.C.R. 16

Case Digest

Subject: Electricity & Energy

Keywords: Supply Of Energy, Rating Committee, Clear Profit

Summary: Electricity (Supply) Act, 1948, s. 57, chap. 6 - Tariff - Revision by company supplying electricity, a sanction-holder - Ex post facto approval by State Govt. - Held, can relate back to date of increase in tariff - Conditions imposed by State Govt. to sanction - holder under s. 28 of Act - Are contractual and not statutory - Expression 'license' - Does not mean 'sanction' as well.
