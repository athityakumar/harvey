State of Tamil Nadu and Another v Board of Trustees of The Port of Madras

Supreme Court of India

20 March 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Jagannadha Rao, S.N. Phukan

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1322; (1999) 4 SCC 630; AIR 1999 SC 1647; JT 1999 (2) SC 410; 1999 (114) STC 520

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Keywords: Bengal Finance (Sales Tax) Act, 1941, Trader, Madras General Sales Tax Act, 1939, Rajasthan Sales Tax Act, 1954, C.P. and Berar Sales Tax Act, 1967, Madhya Pradesh General Sales Tax Act, 1959

Summary: Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax Act, 1959, ss. 2(g)(iii) and 2(d) - Sale of unclaimed and unserviceable goods by Port Trust - Exigibility to tax - Held, main activity of Port Trust not amounts to 'carrying on business' - Thus such sales would not be exigible to tax.
