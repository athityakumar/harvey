 Ajit Singh and Others v State of Punjab and Others

Supreme Court of India

08 December 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, G.B. Pattanaik, M. Jagannadha Rao, S.B. Majmudar, S.P. Kurdukar

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 627; (2000) 1 SCC 430; 2000 (1) AWC 574; JT 1999 (9) SC 542; 1999(7) SCALE 395; 2000 (5) SLR 753; 2000 (1) SLT 20; [1999] Supp5 S.C.R. 195; 1999 (10) Supreme 227; 2000 (1) UPLBEC 435

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India-Article 16 (4)-Employment-Reservation-Clause (4) of Article 16-Enabling provision-Not imposing any constitutional duty-Nor conferring any fundamental right for reservation-Merely because reservation for backward classes was created as reasonable classification-That does not detract from view that Article 16 (4) was only enabling provision.
