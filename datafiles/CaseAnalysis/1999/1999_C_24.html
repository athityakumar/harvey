Utkal Commercial Corporation v Central Coal Fields Limited

Supreme Court of India

20 January 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 918; (1999) 2 SCC 571; AIR 1999 SC 801; 1999 (1) ARBLR 642; 1999 (1) CLT 114; JT 1999 (1) SC 141; 1999 (1) RAJ 462; 1999 (90) RD 275; 1999(1) SCALE 133; 1999 (1) SCJ 339; [1999] 1 S.C.R. 166; 1999 (1) SLT 312; 1999 (1) Supreme 218

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Limitation Act, 1963 - Arbitration Act, 1940, s. 8 - Arbitrator - Appointment - Limitation - Application before High Court and Subordinate Judge found to be filed within three years from date of notice asking respondent to concur in appointment - Held, both these applications are within period prescribed under art. 137 - Application under s. 8 was not barred by limitation.
