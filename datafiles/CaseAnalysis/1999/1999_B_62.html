Babu Verghese and Others v Bar Council of Kerala and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 March 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Saghir Ahmad, D.P. Wadhwa

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 998; (1999) 3 SCC 422; AIR 1999 SC 1281; AIR 1999 SCW 968; 1999 (2) CTC 722; JT 1999 (2) SC 200; 1999(2) SCALE 65; 1999 (2) SCJ 222; [1999] 1 S.C.R. 1121; 2000 (3) SLR 444; 1999 (2) SLT 605; 1999 (3) Supreme 34

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Advocates Act, 1961, ss. 8, 8 A, 10 A and 15 - State Bar Council - Elected member - Term of office - Extension by Bar Council of India - Transaction of business by Bar Councils - Mode of transacting business by circulation - Extension of term of State Bar Council - Held, has to be granted before expiry of original term - Plea that Court should not interfere as election has already been held - Plea not tenable as jurisdictional issue has been raised - Extension granted is invalid.
