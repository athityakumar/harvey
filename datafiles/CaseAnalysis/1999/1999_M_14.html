Atlas Export Industries v Kotak and Company

Supreme Court of India

01 September 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1; (1999) 7 SCC 61; AIR 1999 SC 3286; 1999 (3) ARBLR 305; 2000 (3) Bom.C.R. 636; 2000 (1) CalLT(SC) 1; 2000 (1) CLC 175; JT 1999 (6) SC 451; [1999] Supp2 S.C.R. 192

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Corporate; Trade; Contract & Commercial

Keywords: Foreign Award

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration Act, 1940, s. 2(a) - Contract Act, 1872, s. 28, Exception 1 - Contract - Arbitration clause incorporated by reference - Not withstanding excepting terms, Standard Contract No. 15 of Grain and Food Trade Association Ltd., London (GAFTA) to apply - (A) Parties subsequently object that there was no agreement for reference to arbitrator accordingly - Plea not raised specifically - Rejection of objection - (B) Plea that said arbitration clause impliedly excluding remedy, raised for first time, before Supreme Court - Held, no interference - Cannot be permitted - Appeal dismissed.
