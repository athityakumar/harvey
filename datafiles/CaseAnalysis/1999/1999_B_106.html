Dwarika Prasad Satpathy v Bidyut Prava Dixit and Another

Supreme Court of India

14 October 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

M.B. Shah, K.T. Thomas

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 775; (1999) 7 SCC 675; (1999) SCC (Cr) 1345; AIR 1999 SC 3348; 1999 (37) ALR 733; 2000 (5) Bom.C.R. 731; 1999 (3) CalLT(SC) 61; 2000 CRLJ 1; JT 1999 (8) SC 329; [1999] Supp3 S.C.R. 684

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Women & Children

Summary: Hindu Marriage Act, 1955 - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1974, s. 125 - Maintenance proceedings - Performance of marriage - Proof - Whether performance of essential ceremonies need to be proved? - Held, strict proof is not required - It is sufficient if claimant prima facie satisfies Court that claimant and her husband lived as husband and wife - Performance of essential ceremonies need not also be proved.
