Calcutta Municipal Corporation v Pawan Kumar Saraf and Another

Supreme Court of India

13 January 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, K.T. Thomas, S.S.M. Quadri

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 912; (1999) SCC (Cr) 218; (1999) 2 SCC 400; AIR 1999 SC 738; 1999 (1) CCR 5; 1999 CRLJ 1125; 1999 (1) JCC 81; JT 1999 (1) SC 39; 1999 (1) RCR(Criminal) 699; 1999(1) SCALE 31; 1999 (1) SCJ 324; [1999] 1 S.C.R. 74; 1999 (1) SLT 167; 1999 (1) Supreme 30

Case Digest

Subject: Health & Drug

Summary: Health & Drugs - Prevention of Food Adulteration Act, 1954, s. 13 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 136 - Food Adulteration - Public Analyst Report - Supersession - Sample of Compounded Asafoetida - Public Analyst report showing adulteration on test conducted of full particulars of sample - Certificate of Director of CFL not containing test of some of elements - Offence u/ss. 7/16 of 1954 Act - (A) Quashing of proceedings against accused, if proper - Held, proper, such report of CFL would supersede report of Public Analyst - (B) Limitation Act, 1963, s. 5 - Special leave petition for condonation of delay without showing any sufficient cause shown - Petition barred by limitation - Appeal dismissed.
