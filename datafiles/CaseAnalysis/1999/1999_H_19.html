Hardwari Lal v State U. P. and Others

Supreme Court of India

27 October 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 795; (1999) 8 SCC 582; AIR 2000 SC 277; 2000 (1) AWC 96; 2000 (1) CLR 73; JT 1999 (8) SC 418

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Summary: Service - Whether principles of natural justice was observed in inquiry? - Held, non examination of two witnesses who had witnessed commission of cause of action and non consideration of knowledge possessed by them about offence was wrong on part of Tribunal and High Court - There was no proper enquiry held by authorities - Appeal allowed.
