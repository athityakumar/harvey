Vijayan Alias Vijayakumar v State Represented By Inspector of Police

Supreme Court of India

22 March 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, D.P. Mohapatra

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1021; (1999) 4 SCC 36; (1999) SCC (Cr) 505; AIR 1999 SC 1311; AIR 1999 SCW 1003; 1999 (38) AllCriC 634; 1999 CRLJ 2037; JT 1999 (2) SC 347; [1999] 2 S.C.R. 89; 1999 (3) Supreme 145

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Criminal - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 153 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 300 and 96 - Accused causing injury resulting to death of deceased - Different versions of prosecution witness - Plea of alibi taken on basis of different sale transactions - Held, sale receipt not unreliable merely because prosecution witness was noted as present in inquest report - As presence of prosecution witness doubtful, conviction set aside - Appeal allowed.
