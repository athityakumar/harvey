State Bank of Bikaner and Jaipur v Ballabh Das and Company and Others

Supreme Court of India

15 September 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, S.N. Phukan

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1336; (1999) 7 SCC 539; AIR 1999 SC 3408; 1999 (6) ALD(SC) 17; 1999 (37) ALR 516; 2001 (1) BC 47; 2000 (1) CLC 179; [1999] 98 Comp Cas 219; 2000 (2) CompLJ 30; 1999 (3) CTC 345; JT 1999 (7) SC 102; 1999(5) SCALE 534; 2000 (Supp) RD 808; [1999] Supp2 S.C.R. 465

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Trade

Summary: Recovery of Debts Due to Banks and Financial Institutions Act, 1993, ss. 2(g) and 31 - 'Debt' - Meaning of - Held, is liability 'alleged as due' from any person by bank - 'Alleged as due' does not suggest amounts claimed by bank must be determined or decided by competent forum.
