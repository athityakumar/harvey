State Of Karnataka v Management Of Ksrtc And Another

Supreme Court of India

18 February 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Majmudar, U.C. Banerjee

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1321; (1999) SCC (L&S) 566; (1999) 2 SCC 687; AIR 1999 SC 1059; AIR 1999 SCW 697; 1999 LabIC 1127; 1999 (1) LLJ 849; [1999] 1 S.C.R. 733; 2000 (1) SLJ 33; 1999 (2) SLR 294

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 19(2) - Settlement - Period of operation - In force till recognition of union continued or terminates on mutual consent - Unilateral termination by employer - Validity - Held, valid only when settlement expires on its own terms.
