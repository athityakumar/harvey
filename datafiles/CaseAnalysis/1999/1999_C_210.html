R. Dalmia and Another v Commissioner of Income Tax

Supreme Court of India

05 February 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, N. Santosh Hegde

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1137; (1999) 2 SCC 758; AIR 1999 SC 868; 1999 (152) CTR 383; [1999] 236 ITR 480; JT 1999 (1) SC 299; [1999] 1 S.C.R. 454; [1999] 102 TAXMAN 702; 1999 (2) TLR 315

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 144 B, 147 and 153 - Limitation Act, 1908 - Income of HUF escaping assessment - Limitation for assessment and reassessment made u/s. 147 of Act - But procedure u/s. 144-B to be followed - Availability of additional period - Held, additional period of 180 days u/s. 153 explanation 1(iv) is available - Appeal dismissed.
