Surana Steels Private Limited v Deputy Commissioner of Income Tax

Supreme Court of India

13 April 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1234; (1999) 4 SCC 306; AIR 1999 SC 1455; 1999 (153) CTR 193; [1999] 237 ITR 777; JT 1999 (5) SC 192; [1999] 2 S.C.R. 589; [1999] 104 TAXMAN 188; 1999 (2) TLR 469

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Interpretation of Statutes - Income Tax Act, s.115 J - Interpretation of term 'loss' u/s. 205(1), Proviso, cl. (b) of Companies Act- Held, it means amount arrived at after taking into account amount of depreciation - It has to be so read and understood in context of s.115 J of Income Tax Act - Appeal dismissed.
