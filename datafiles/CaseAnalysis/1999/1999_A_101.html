Union of India and Others v Anil Kumar and Others

Supreme Court of India

13 July 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

R.P. Sethi, K. Venkataswami

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 578; (1999) 5 SCC 743; (1999) SCC (L&S) 1020; AIR 1999 SC 2229; 1999 (3) ESC 1934; JT 1999 (4) SC 502; 1999 LabIC 2591; 1999 (3) LLN 855; [1999] 3 S.C.R. 835; 2000 (2) SLJ 129; 1999 (4) SLR 298; 1999 (3) UPLBEC 1799

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Eligibility - Qualifications

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 16 and 39(d) - Defence Research and Development Organisation Technical Cadre Recruitment Rules, 1995, r. 6(3)(a)(b) - Bifurcation into two grades - Validity - Cadre of Senior Scientific Assts. (SSA) in Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) - Different pay scales - Made on basis arbitration award in which Senior Scientific Assts. themselves have demanded two pay scales.
