Manju Ramesh Nahar v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

31 March 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Saghir Ahmad, R.P. Sethi

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1045; (1999) 4 SCC 116; (1999) SCC (Cr) 498; AIR 1999 SC 2622; 1999 CRLJ 2271; 1999 (82) ECR 215; JT 1999 (2) SC 559; [1999] 2 S.C.R. 342

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973 - Conservation of Foreign Exchange and Prevention of Smuggling Activities Act, s. 3 - Writ petition u/art. 226 of constitution - Execution of Order of detention after a year - No explanation for delay - Inference - Held, delay reflects upon 'satisfaction' of authority and vitiates detention - Petition dismissed as being infructuous.
