Steel Authority of India Limited v J. C. Budharaja, Government and Mining Contractor

Supreme Court of India

01 September 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

M.B. Shah, D.P. Wadhwa

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 3; (1999) 8 SCC 122; AIR 1999 SC 3275; 1999 (3) ARBLR 335; JT 1999 (6) SC 429; [1999] Supp2 S.C.R. 155

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Banking & Finance; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Arbitration Act, 1940, ss. 30, 20 and 37 - Jurisdiction - Contract prohibiting for making damages or compensation - Awarding damages - Validity - Contractor executing supplementary agreement for completion of work within stipulated time and at a higher rate - Held, does not waive right of contractor to take action within limit - Award illegal as beyond jurisdiction.
