Coastal Chemicals Limited v Commercial Tax Officer, A. P. and Others

Supreme Court of India

14 October 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, D.P. Mohapatra, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 776; (1999) 8 SCC 465; AIR 1999 SC 3855; 1999 (85) ECR 825; JT 1999 (8) SC 345; 1999(6) SCALE 597; 1999 (8) SLT 636; 2000 (117) STC 12; [1999] Supp3 S.C.R. 694; 1999 (9) Supreme 60

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax; Service

Keywords: Oil and Natural Gas Commission

Summary: Indirect Tax - Sales Tax - Oil and Natural Gas Commission - Andhra Pradesh General Sales Tax Act, 1957, s. 5 B(1) - Concessional rate of tax - For consumables - Appellant purchased natural gas and used it as fuel for manufacture of paper and paper products - (A) Whether natural gas used is a 'consumable' within meaning of s. 5 B(1)? - (B) Whether appellant, entitled to concessional rate of tax on purchase of natural gas from Oil and Natural Gas Commission? - Held, (A) natural gas used by appellant as fuel for manufacture of paper and paper products does 'not tend to making of end-product' - (B) It is not a 'consumable' - Appellant, therefore, is not entitled to concessional rate of tax - Appeal dismissed.
