United India Insurance v Ajmer Singh Cotton and General Mills and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 August 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

R.P. Sethi, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 868; (1999) 6 SCC 400; AIR 1999 SC 3027; 2000 (1) ALD(SC) 11; 1999 (37) ALR 121; 1999 (4) AWC 3311; 2000 (1) Bom.C.R. 44; [2000] 100 Comp Cas 741; 1999 (2) CPJ 10B; JT 1999 (6) SC 23; [1999] Supp1 S.C.R. 385

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection; Corporate

Summary: Consumer Protection Act, 1986, s. 14 - Whether insured is estopped from making any further claim from insurer after accepting insurance claim amount in full and final settlement of all claims? - Held, consumer on satisfying authority under Act that discharge voucher was obtained under suspicious circumstances would be justified in granting appropriate relief under circumstances of each case - Appeal allowed.
