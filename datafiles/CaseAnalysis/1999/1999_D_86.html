Shiv Sarup Gupta v Dr. Mahesh Chand Gupta

Supreme Court of India

30 July 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 615; (1999) 6 SCC 222; AIR 1999 SC 2507; 1999 (80) DLT 731; JT 1999 (5) SC 201; [1999] 3 S.C.R. 1260

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Rent Control

Summary: Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958, ss. 25 B(8), 14 and 14(1)(c) - Eviction - Grounds, bona fide need - Alternative accommodation - Held, convenience and safety of landlord and his family members are factors must be considered - An alternate accommodation must be reasonably suitable in comparison with suit accommodation - This entails denial to landlord - Re-appraisal of evidence - Can be entered into only for limited purpose to test whether order of Rent Controller is according to law or not.
