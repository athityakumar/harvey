Ayodhya Ram Alias Ayodhya Prasad Singh and Others v State of Bihar

Supreme Court of India

07 January 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, A.P. Misra

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1490; (1999) 9 SCC 139; (1999) SCC (Cr) 564; AIR 1999 SCW 4733

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 302, 307 and 149 - Murder - Right to private defence - Three eye-witnesses who were also examined as injured witnesses - Whether appellants had right to private defence in present case? - Held, right to private defence not reasonably exercised - Evidence of injured witness very cogent and convincing - Prosecution not bound to explain every injury on accused persons - No merit in appeal - Appeal dismissed.
