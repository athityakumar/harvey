Virender S. Hooda Others v State of Haryana and Another

Supreme Court of India

13 April 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, G.T. Nanavati

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1307; (1999) 3 SCC 696; (1999) SCC (L&S) 824; AIR 1999 SC 1701; 1999 (2) ESC 1637; 1999 LabIC 1838; 1999 (2) LLJ 800; 1999 (2) LLN 1001; 1999 (2) SLR 191; 1999 (4) Supreme 319

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 16 and 320 - Appointment - Civil Services - Vacancies filled up - Appointment of candidates on recommendation of Public Service Commission - Availability of further vacancies within six months of such recommendation - Govt. circulars - Providing to fill up vacancies out of waiting list maintained by commission - Held, commencement of selection process before arising of vacancies - Has no relevance - It cannot also be said that administrative instructions cannot be invoked by candidates as it was policy declared by Govt. as to manner of filling up post.
