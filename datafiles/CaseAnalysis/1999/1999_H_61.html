Hotel Seaking and Others v Kerala Financial Corporation

Supreme Court of India

20 January 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Srinivasan, R.P. Sethi

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 921; (1999) 6 SCC 51; [2000] 101 Comp Cas 1; JT 1999 (9) SC 440; 1999 (Supp) MLJ(SC) 28

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance

Summary: Banking & Finance - Civil Procedure Code, 1908 s. 34 - State Financial Corporation Act, 1951, s. 31 - Respondent filed petition u/s. 31 of 1951 Act, for realisation of amount due from appellant - District Court allowed petition - On appeal High Court held that s. 34, CPC, 1908 was not attracted to proceedings instituted u/s. 31 of 1951 Act and interest will be payable in accordance with terms of agreement between parties - Whether HC right to hold that interest will be paid in accordance with terms of agreement? - Held, proceedings u/s. 31 is akin to application for attachment of property in execution of decree at stage posterior to passing of decree - No question of passing any order u/s. 34 would arise - S. 34 cannot be invoked in proceedings instituted u/s. 31 and interest will be payable in accordance with terms of agreement - Judgment of HC is confirmed in toto - Appeal dismissed.
