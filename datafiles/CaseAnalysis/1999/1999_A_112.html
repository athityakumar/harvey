Union of India v Apar Private Limited

Supreme Court of India

22 July 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, M. Srinivasan, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 100; (1999) 6 SCC 117; AIR 1999 SC 2515; AIR 1999 SCW 2676; 1999 (84) ECR 63; 1999 (112) E.L.T. 3; JT 1999 (5) SC 160; 1999 (33) RLT 787; [1999] 3 S.C.R. 1056; 1999 (6) Supreme 348

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Keywords: Entry Inwards

Summary: Customs Act, 1962, ss. 15 and 25(1) - Taxability of imported goods - Relevant date to determine - Held, is date on which bill of entry made u/s. 46 - And, in case of warehoused goods, actual removal date of goods from warehouse - Goods exempt from duty when warehoused - Exemption withdrawn on date of their removal from warehouse - Goods liable to duty.
