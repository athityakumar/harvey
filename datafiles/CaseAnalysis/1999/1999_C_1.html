V. S. Palanichamy Chettiar Firm v C. Alagappan and Another

Supreme Court of India

03 February 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 990; (1999) 4 SCC 702; 1999 (2) ACJ 1521; AIR 1999 SC 918; 1999 (35) ALR 392; 1999 (2) AWC 1033; 1999 (1) CRC 393; JT 1999 (1) SC 216; 1999 (2) MLJ(SC) 33; [1999] 1 S.C.R. 349; 2000 (Supp) RD 566

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Specific Relief Act, 1963, s. 28 - Decree to enforce contract for sale - Time fixed by decree to deposit balance consideration - Extension of - Considerations - Contract in issue entered about 19 years ago - Extension of time to deposit sought 5 years after decree - No explanation put forth for non payment - Held, if now time is granted that would be going beyond period of limitation - Time could not be extended.
