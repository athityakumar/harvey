Ram Lakhan and Others v Presiding Officer and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 November 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Saghir Ahmad, D.P. Mohapatra, R.P. Sethi

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1602; (2000) 10 SCC 201; (2000) SCC (L&S) 422; AIR 2000 SC 1946; 2000 (3) ALD(SC) 53; JT 1999 (10) SC 466; 2000 (1) LLJ 1067; 2000 (2) LLN 958; 2000(2) SCALE 9; 2000 (2) SLR 177; [1999] Supp4 S.C.R. 463

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Corporate - Labour and Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 33(1) Application seeking permission to dismiss employee on completion of domestic enquiry - Employee can be suspended meanwhile - Determination of entitlement to subsistence allowance - Held, entitled - Appeal allowed.
