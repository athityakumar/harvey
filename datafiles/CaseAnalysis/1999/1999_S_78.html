N. K. Ogle v Sanwaldas Alias Sanwalmal Ahuja

Supreme Court of India

18 March 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, M.B. Shah

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1007; (1999) 3 SCC 284; (1999) SCC (Cr) 405; AIR 1999 SC 1437; 1999 (2) CCR 28; 1999 (2) Crimes 1; 1999 CRLJ 2105; 1999 (1) JCC 211; JT 1999 (2) SC 230; 1999 (2) RCR(Criminal) 207; 1999(2) SCALE 124; 1999 (2) SCJ 1; [1999] 1 S.C.R. 1233; 1999 (3) SLT 23; 1999 (3) Supreme 131

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Indian Limitation Act, 1908 - Madhya Pradesh Land Revenue Code, 1959 - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, s. 197 - Sanction for prosecution - Order by Collector for collecting lease money from respondent - Tehsildar serves Demand Letter on respondent accordingly - Respondent fails - Warrant of attachment against - Tehsildar seized and auctioned scooter of respondent - Respondent files a criminal complaint against Tehsildar - Held, act of Tehsildar was bona fide in discharge of official duty - No cognizance can be taken against Tehsildar by any Court without prior sanction of Competent Authority - Not maintainable without such sanction.
