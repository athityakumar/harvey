Central Bank of India Another v Saxons Farms and Others

Supreme Court of India

07 October 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Phukan, G.T. Nanavati

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 571; (1999) 8 SCC 221; (1999) SCC (Cr) 1411; AIR 1999 SC 3607; 2001 (1) BC 12; 2000 (5) Bom.C.R. 77; 2000 (1) Bom.C.R. 239; 2000 (1) CLC 85; [1999] 98 Comp Cas 712; 2000 (2) CompLJ 36; 1999 CRLJ 4571; JT 1999 (8) SC 58; [1999] Supp3 S.C.R. 534

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Corporate; Criminal; Trade

Keywords: Dishonour of cheques

Summary: Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881, s. 138, Proviso, cl. (b) - Dishonour of cheque - Notice - Expression in Notice that 'kindly arrange to make payment to avoid unpleasant action of my client' - Validity - Held, is clear demand under cl. (b) to s. 138 - Notice indicating intention of drawee to take action under provisions of IPC by informing police - Is valid.
