A. V. Reddy Trust and Others v Commissioner of Wealth Tax

Supreme Court of India

08 October 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

M.B. Shah, D.P. Wadhwa

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 622; (1999) 8 SCC 243; AIR 1999 SC 3352; 1999 (156) CTR 553; [1999] 240 ITR 409; JT 1999 (8) SC 155; [1999] Supp3 S.C.R. 580; [1999] 107 TAXMAN 84; 1999 (2) TLR 1060

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Wealth Tax Act, 1957, ss. 21(4) and 21(1) - Assessments of assets held by Trustee - Corpus of trust to be transferred to beneficiary, on completing stipulated age - How assessment is to be made? - Held, to be made on basis of beneficiaries interest as per s. 21(4)of Act and not on basis of corpus of trust fund - Appeal allowed.
