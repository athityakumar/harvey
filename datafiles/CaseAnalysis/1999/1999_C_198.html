Uco Bank v Commissioner of Income Tax, West Bengal

Supreme Court of India

13 May 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar, D.P. Mohapatra, R.C. Lahoti

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 107; (1999) 4 SCC 599; AIR 1999 SC 2082; 1999 (154) CTR 88; 1999 (111) E.L.T. 673; [1999] 237 ITR 889; JT 1999 (4) SC 40; [1999] 3 S.C.R. 635; 1999 TAXLR 587; [1999] 104 TAXMAN 547; 1999 (2) TLR 587

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 145 and 119 - Profits and gains of business - Interest on a loan whose recovery is doubtful and not brought to profit and loss account - Held, income of assessee cannot include such interest, if for three years such interest is not actually received - CBDT Circular dated 9th Oct 1984 clarifies mode in which these amounts are to be taxed - Not inconsistent with provisions of s. 145 of Act.
