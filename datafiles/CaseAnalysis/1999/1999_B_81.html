Bhagwan Singh and Another v State of Punjab and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 August 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1425; (1999) 9 SCC 573

Case Digest

Subject: Health & Drug

Summary: Health & Drug - Drugs and Cosmetics Act, 1940, ss. 8 and 16 - Drugs and Cosmetics Rules, 1945, r. 50 A - Qualifications of a Controlling Authority - Whether respondent 3 is qualified to be appointed as Controlling Authority? - Experience in related fields cannnot be counted as experience in manufacture or testing of drugs or enforcement of provisions of Act - Respondent 3's experience as a Chemical Examiner cannot be considered as relevant experience since his work did not directly deal with kind of testing which is contemplated under Act - Held, appointment of Respondent 3 as Controlling Authority is set aside as he does not posses requisite qualification - Appeal allowed.
