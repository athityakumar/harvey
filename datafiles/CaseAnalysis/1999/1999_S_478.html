Shivgonda Anna Patil and Others v State of Maharashtra and Others

Supreme Court of India

09 March 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, S.N. Phukan

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 396; (1999) 3 SCC 5; AIR 1999 SC 2281; 1999 (3) Bom.C.R. 78; JT 1999 (2) SC 132; 1999(2) SCALE 1; 1999 (2) SLT 509; 1999 (2) Supreme 510

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Urban Land (Ceiling and Regulation) Act, 1976, ss. 10 and 11 - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 31 A and 226 - Ceiling on land holding on vesting of excess land in State u/s. 10(3) of Act - Writ petition for reopening proceedings filed only after 10 years - Ground, non consideration of share of petitioner's sister in joint family property - (A) summary dismissal, if justified - (B) Absence of provision regarding payment of compensation at market rate for acquisition of agricultural land - Constitutionality - Held, justified, as sister though aware of proceeding did not file any objection before vesting - Act not violative of provisions of art. 31 A(1) as what is taken over by State is excess vacant land - Appeal dismissed.
