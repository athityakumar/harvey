Chief Executive Officer v Surendra Kumar Vakil and Others

Supreme Court of India

23 March 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

Sujata V. Manohar, R.C. Lahoti

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1024; (1999) 3 SCC 555; AIR 1999 SC 2294; AIR 1999 SCW 2360; JT 1999 (2) SC 315; 1999(2) SCALE 208; 1999 (2) SCJ 346; [1999] 2 S.C.R. 118; 1999 (3) SLT 151; 1999 (3) Supreme 369; 1999 (2) UPLBEC 1360

Case Digest

Subject: Defence & Security Forces; Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Cantonment Land Administration Rules, 1925

Summary: Land & Property - Cantonments Act, 1924, s. 280 - Cantonment Law Administration Rules, 1925, rr. 4, 5, 6, 7 and 8 - Resumption of land in cantonment area - Govt. retains right in respect of old grant tenure - Suit filed, claiming title - Except bare assertion, no supporting document produced - However cantonment authorities produce, General Land Register, showing old grant - Suit property whether an old grant tenure and if can be resumed - Held, suit property was held on old grant basis - Could be resumed by Govt. - Appeal allowed.
