Bhagat Ram(dead) v Teja Singh

Supreme Court of India

31 March 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

U.C. Banerjee, A.P. Misra

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1196; (1999) 4 SCC 86; AIR 1999 SC 1944; 1999 (37) ALR 6; 1999 (3) AWC 2406; 2002 (1) CTC 178; 1999 (157) CTR 50; [1999] 237 ITR 364; JT 1999 (4) SC 589; 1999 (3) MLJ(SC) 69; [1999] 2 S.C.R. 358; 2000 (Supp) RD 480

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Women & Children

Summary: Hindu Succession Act, 1956, ss. 15(1) and 15(2) - Whether female Hindu is entitled to inherit property from her mother? - Held, she is not entitled as on death of her mother property would devolve on her sister and not on heirs of her predeceased husband.
