Mohd. Hussain Farah v Union of India and Another

Supreme Court of India

24 August 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, S.N. Phukan

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 665; (2000) SCC (Cr) 191; (2000) 1 SCC 329; AIR 1999 SC 3343; 1999 (3) CCR 211; 1999 (4) Crimes 87; 1999 CRLJ 4303; 1999 (85) ECR 216; 1999 (113) E.L.T. 14; JT 1999 (6) SC 276; 1999 (4) RCR(Criminal) 62; 1999(5) SCALE 203; 1999 (7) SLT 313; 1999 (7) Supreme 329

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Criminal - Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act, 1985, s. 21 - Preventive Law - Possession of contraband - Search - Compliance of provisions of s. 42 not required - Evidence of those officers referred u/s. 42 - Conviction - Interference sought - Held, no interference, as there evidence was trustworthy and sufficient - Appeal dismissed.
