Collector Of Customs, Rajkot v Sarabhai International Ltd

Supreme Court of India

07 October 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, V.N. Khare, D.P. Mohapatra

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1973; (2000) 10 SCC 292; 2000 (91) ECR 776; 2000 (119) E.L.T. 6

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Customs Act, 1962, ss. 15 and 25 (1) - Customs Tariff Act, 1975 - What is date that is relevant for purposes of assessment of Customs duty payable on soda ash which is transferred from Free Trade Zone to domestic tariff area subsequent to import of Soda Ash into Free Trade Zone? - Obligation of respondents must be determined solely upon basis of notification D/- 17-04-1980 - It was clearly a condition of said notification that respondents would pay on surplus soda ash amount of duty, which had not been paid thereon when soda ash was cleared into Free Trade Zone - Held, respondents are liable to pay amount of duty that would have been leviable on surplus soda ash, had it not been cleared into Free Trade Zone under said notification - Appeal allowed.
