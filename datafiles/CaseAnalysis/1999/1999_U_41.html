Motiram Tolaram and Another v Union of India and Another

Supreme Court of India

05 August 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, R.P. Sethi

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 526; (1999) 6 SCC 375; AIR 1999 SC 3121; 1999 (84) ECR 48; 1999 (112) E.L.T. 749; JT 1999 (6) SC 48; 1999 (34) RLT 113; [1999] Supp1 S.C.R. 82

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Custom - Customs Tariff Act, 1975, s. 3 - Entitlement to benefits of notification no. 185 of 1983 - Polyvinyl alcohol imported by assessee - Applicability of notification to vinyl acetate monomer (raw material) was not subjected to duty under Indian law - Held, would not be applicable - Appeal dismissed.
