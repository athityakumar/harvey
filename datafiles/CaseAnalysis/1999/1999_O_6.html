Oil and Natural Gas Commission v M. C. Clelland Engineers S. A

Supreme Court of India

23 April 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, S.N. Phukan

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 1111; (1999) 4 SCC 327; AIR 1999 SC 1614; 1999 (2) ARBLR 509; 1999 (3) Bom.C.R. 537; JT 1999 (3) SC 202; [1999] 2 S.C.R. 830

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Indian Limitation Act, 1908 - Arbitration Act 1940, s. 29 - Interest on claim for interest - Arbitrator has power to grant - Claim made before arbitrator for interest on delayed payments which is nothing but claim for compensation/damages for delayed payment - Held, interest from date of award till date of realisation can be awarded on this claim for interest - S. 3 of Interest Act is not applicable to such situation.
