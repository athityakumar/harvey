Madras Petro-Chem. Limited v Collector of Central Excise, Madras

Supreme Court of India

23 March 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

R.P. Sethi

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 115; (2000) 10 SCC 34; AIR 1999 SC 3798; 1999 (108) E.L.T. 611

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, s. 11A - Whether demand notice can be issued within extended period of limitation in case where assessee fails to disclose required information? - Held, can be issued within extended period of five months - Appeal dismissed.
