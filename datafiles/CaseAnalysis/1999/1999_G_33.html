Union of Public Service Commission v Gaurav Dwivedi and Others

Supreme Court of India

13 May 1999

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

1999 Indlaw SC 538; (1999) 5 SCC 180; (1999) SCC (L&S) 982; AIR 1999 SC 2137; 1999 ALJ 1551; 1999 (3) AWC 2503; 1999 (4) Bom.C.R. 320; 1999 (3) ESC 1925; JT 1999 (5) SC 15; 1999 LabIC 2358; [1999] 3 S.C.R. 649; 1999 (6) Supreme 30; 1999 (2) UPLBEC 1633

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Service

Keywords: Civil Services Examination

Summary: Service - All India Services Act, 1951, s. 3 - Civil Services Examination Rules, 1998, r. 3 - Service Rules - No. of candidates to be called for viva voce for recruitment to Central Civil Service - Examination notification giving no of vacancies - That number could be and in fact reduced - No. of candidates to be called for viva voice proportionately reduced - Appeal against - Held, rules do not stipulate that entire process of selection should be completed on basis of original number of vacancies notified - Appeal allowed.
