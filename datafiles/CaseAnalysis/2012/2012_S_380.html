Kashinath Mondal v State of West Bengal

Supreme Court of India

31 July 2012

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranjana Prakash Desai, Surinder Singh Nijjar

Where Reported

2012 Indlaw SC 384; (2012) 7 SCC 699; AIR 2012 SC 3134; 2012 ALL MR (Cri) 3049; 2012 CRLJ 3974; 2013 (1) RLW 243

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 376, 302 - Rape - Murder - Conviction - Sustainability - Appellant accused raped and murdered complainants daughter - Accused was charged for offences punishable u/ss. 376, 302 IPC - Trial Court convicted accused - Appeal filed before HC which was dismissed - Hence instant appeal - Whether the orders of conviction was sustainable - Held, PW2 evidence and finding recorded in post mortem notes which have reproduced in preceding establish that it was proved - It was not indicate that victim was habituated to sexual intercourse - Therefore, rape was correct and in circumstances which was noted no one but accused could have been responsible for rape - Report of serologist did not help prosecution because serologist could not conduct analysis because of insufficiency of blood - PW2 stated that evidence of spermatozoon in vaginal swab indicated sexual intercourse but stated that spermatozoon might not be detected in vagina 10 hours after rape - Remissness and inefficiency of Investigating Agency should be no ground to acquit person if there was enough evidence on record to establish guilt beyond reasonable doubt - There was sufficient evidence to establish substratum of prosecution case then irregularities which occurred due to remissness of Investigating Agency which did not affect substratum of prosecution case should not weigh with Court - There was no hesitation in confirming concurrent findings recorded by Trial court and HC on minute examination of evidence that it was accused who was responsible for rape and murder of deceased - Hence, prosecution had established its case beyond reasonable doubt - There was no merit in instant appeal - Appeal dismissed.
