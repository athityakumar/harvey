M/S. Pushpa Sahakari Avas Samiti Ltd. v M/S. Gangotri Sahakari Avas S. Ltd. & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

30 March 2012

Case Analysis

Bench

Dipak Misra, Deepak Verma

Where Reported

2012 Indlaw SC 122; (2012) 4 SCC 751; AIR 2012 SC 1788; 2012 (4) ALD(SC) 22; 2012 (92) ALR 278; 2012 (4) ALT 37; 2012 (4) AWC 3628; 2012 (5) Bom.C.R. 162; 2012 (2) CLT(SC) 24; JT 2012 (3) SC 563; 2012 (5) MahLJ 69; 2012 (4) MLJ(SC) 341; 2012 (3) MPLJ 495; 2012 (2) RCR(Civil) 645; 2012 (116) RD 335; 2012(4) SCALE 93; [2012] 2 S.C.R. 648

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 47 - Execution of compromise decree - Maintainability - Appellant initiated civil action for permanent injunction against respondents - Parties compromised matter and on terms and conditions of compromise, decree was drawn up - Respondents violated terms of compromise, appellant filed execution petition before Executing Court - Respondents opposed petition stating that application was premature as such not maintainable - Executing Court rejected objection of respondents and directed for payment of entire balance amount as per compromise - Aggrieved respondent preferred Civil Revision, HC allowed revision - Aggrieved instant appeal - Whether HC was justified in deciding that Execution Petition filed by appellant was premature - Held, Executing Court did not commit any error by entertaining execution petition - HC, in civil revision had annulled order without any justification while doing so, Single Judge had not dealt with other objections raised by Judgment-debtor on ground that they were raised for first time - Impugned HC order was set aside and matter was remitted to HC to deal with objections on merits - Appeals allowed.
