Mulchand Khanumal Khatri v State Of Gujarat & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

27 March 2012

Case Analysis

Bench

R.M. Lodha, H.L. Gokhale

Where Reported

2012 Indlaw SC 120; (2012) 5 SCC 365; AIR 2012 SC 2525; 2012 (92) ALR 503; 2012 (4) AWC 3641; JT 2012 (3) SC 615; 2012 (4) MLJ(SC) 331; 2012 (2) RCR(Civil) 736; 2012 (116) RD 514; 2012(4) SCALE 159; [2012] 2 S.C.R. 586

Case Digest

Subject: Administrative; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Administrative - Land & Property - Practice & Procedure - Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, 1984, ss. 4,6, 11A - Limitation Act, 1963, s. 12 - Land Acquisition - Declaration - Time Lapsed - Validity - Appellants land was sought to be acquired for public purpose and revised notification u/s. 4 of Act was published on 22-9-1984 - Declaration u/s. 6 of Act was published on 5-1-1984 - Appellant filed application before HC challenging acquisition - , Interim relief was granted but same was dismissed by HC on 1-11-1996 - Dy. Collector passed award on 31-8-1998 - Appellant filed petition before HC contending that entire acquisition proceedings had lapsed by virtue of s. 11A of Act as award had been passed after 2 years from date of publication of declaration u/s. 6 of Act - HC dismissed the petition - Aggrieved instant appeal - Whether s. 11A of Act would permit exclusion of time that was taken in obtaining certified copy of judgment and order passed by HC and period from date certified copy was obtained and it was brought to notice of authority - Held, period prescribed in s. 11A of Act was mandatory, consequence of breach was provided in provision itself - Insofar as computation of period was concerned, period of two years commenced from date of publication of declaration - Where declaration had been published before Amendment Act, then period commenced from commencement of Amendment Act - Only period that was excludable was period during which action or proceedings to be taken pursuant to said declaration remains stayed under order of a court and no other - S. 11A of Act was a special provision for purposes of Act and legislative intent being clear from bare language of explanation appended thereto, no justification was found to read provisions of s. 12 of 1963 Act - HC was clearly in error in excluding period for obtaining certified copy of its order and that period should not be excluded under explanation appended to s. 11A of the Act - Impugned HC order was set aside - Appeal allowed.
