Akil @ Javed v State Of Nct Of Delhi

Supreme Court of India

06 December 2012

Case Analysis

Bench

Fakkir Mohamed Ibrahim Kalifulla, Swatanter Kumar

Where Reported

2012 Indlaw SC 523; (2013) 7 SCC 125; 2013 CRLJ 571; 2012 (195) DLT 321; JT 2012 (12) SC 200; 2013 (1) MLJ(Crl) 73; 2013 (1) RCR(Criminal) 530; 2012(11) SCALE 709; [2012] 13 S.C.R. 659

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Adjourn

Summary: Criminal - Practice & Procedure - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 302,392,34 - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, s. 309 - Robbery - Murder - Incriminating Circumstances - Recoveries - Conviction - Sustainability - Appellant- accused No. 1 and another were convicted for offence u/ss. 302,392,34 of IPC - Prosecution case was that appellant and another accused robed house of and attempted to molest owner of house and when deceased resisted appellant shot him - Trial Court appreciating evidence on record and recoveries made at instance of appellant convicted appellant and another accused for offence u/ss. 302,392,34 of IPC - Appeal before HC, SJ, confirmed conviction but DB, acquitted accused No. 2 for offence u/s. 302, and maintained conviction of appellant - Hence instant appeal - Whether Trial Court was justified in convicting appellant, which was concurred by HC - Held, recoveries from appellant along with co-accused having been proved in manner known to law, those were well established incriminating circumstances demonstrated before Courts below and there was no contra evidence for appellant and co-accused to get rid of offences alleged, having regard to said piece of evidence relating to recoveries prevailing on record presence of appellant along with co-accused at place of occurrence in manner described by witnesses, namely, PWs.17, 19, 20, 23 and 25 was clinching enough to rope in appellant along with co-accused in commission of crime as alleged in complaint and found proved against both of them - Appeal dismissed.
