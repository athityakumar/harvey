Manubhai Ratilal Patel Tr. Ushaben v State Of Gujarat & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

28 September 2012

Case Analysis

Bench

Dipak Misra, K.S. Panicker Radhakrishnan

Where Reported

2012 Indlaw SC 327; (2013) 1 SCC 314; AIR 2013 SC 313; 2013 CRLJ 160; 2013 (2) G.L.R. 1500; JT 2012 (9) SC 394; 2012 (4) RCR(Criminal) 655; 2012(9) SCALE 559; [2012] 8 S.C.R. 993

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Criminal - Practice & Procedure - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 467, 468, 471 and 114 - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, ss. 167(2), 439, 482 - Forgery - Bail - Denied - Challenged - Appellant (accused) allegedly committed offences u/ss. 467, 468, 471 and 114 of IPC and remand of appellant to police custody was granted by Judicial Magistrate First Class (JFCM) - HC by order dt.17-7-2012 stayed further proceedings in respect of investigation - Thereafter, appellant filed a bail application u/s. 4
