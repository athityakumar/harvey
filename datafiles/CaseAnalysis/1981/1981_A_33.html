Smt. Shanti Devi v Amal Kumar Banerjee

Supreme Court of India

09 January 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 596; (1981) 2 SCC 199; AIR 1981 SC 1550

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Transfer of Property Act, 1882, ss. 106, 111(a), 116 - W.B. Premises Tenancy Act, 1956, ss. 1(3), 13 - Tenancy -(A) Validity of notice u/s.106 - Applicability of section must be examined first - Lease for fixed period expired by efflux of time without allegation of holding over - (B) Extent and applicability of 1956 Act - Property situated in Notified area - Determination of contractual tenancy - Held, notice u/s. 106 not necessary - Tenant does not become statutory tenant - Appeal Allowed.
