State of Maharashtra v Vithal Rao Pritirao Chawan

Supreme Court of India

20 July 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 76; (1981) 4 SCC 129; (1981) SCC (Cr) 807; AIR 1982 SC 1215

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Whether prosecution has successfully established entrustment of Rs. 1453.18 to accused and that he received that amount in discharge of his official duty, so that a liability in law will arise for him to account for same? - Held, this is not a case where High Court should have refused leave to appeal without assigning reasons - Said point require fuller arguments - Matter remitted to High Court.
