Lalit Mohan Mondal and Others v Benoyendra Nath Chatterjee

Supreme Court of India

14 April 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 51; (1982) 3 SCC 219; (1982) SCC (Cr) 697; AIR 1982 SC 785; 1982 CRLJ 625; 1982 CRLR 570B; 1982 MLJ 329

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - CrPC,1973, ss. 341, 482, 397 and 397(2) - Order in appeal u/s. 341 - High Court held that it is not fit case of invoking the inherent power - Held, HC is entitled to examine the matter u/s. 482 of the CrPC which expressly overrules the bar contained in s. 341 of the Code thus remitting back the case - Order accordingly.
