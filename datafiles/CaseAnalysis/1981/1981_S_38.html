Shanti Kumari v Regional Deputy Director, Health Services, Patna

Supreme Court of India

08 January 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 159; (1981) 2 SCC 72; (1981) SCC (L&S) 285; AIR 1981 SC 1577; 1981 (43) FLR 263; 1981 LabIC 1102; 1981 (2) LLJ 312; 1981 (2) LLN 485; 1981 (2) SLJ 435; 1981 (3) SLR 215

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution, arts. 311 and 226 - Service - Transfer - Of an Auxiliary Nurse Mid - Wife - To a place other than home district - In contravention of State Government's directions - But failure to file a representation to the State Government - High Court declined to interfere - Decision of HC upheld by SC - Appeal Dismissed.
