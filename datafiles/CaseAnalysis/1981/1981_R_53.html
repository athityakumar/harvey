Rattan Singh Etc v State of Punjab and Others Etc

Supreme Court of India

22 October 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, A. Varadarajan, Amarendra Nath Sen

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 319; (1981) 4 SCC 481; (1981) SCC (Cr) 853; AIR 1982 SC 1; 1982 CRLJ 146; 1981(3) SCALE 1625; [1982] 1 S.C.R. 1010

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Criminal; Fema; Socio-Economic

Summary: Criminal - Conservation Of Foreign Exchange And Prevention Of Smuggling Activities Act, 1973, ss.3(1) and 11(1) - Detention - Petition u/art. 32 of Constitution challenging validity of an order u/s. 3(1) of Act - Representation by detenu not forwarded for consideration - Held, detention is illegal - Petition allowed.
