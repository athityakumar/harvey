State Of Maharashtra & Anr. v Chandrakant Anant Kulkarni & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

08 September 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, Baharul Islam, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 251; (1981) 4 SCC 130; (1981) SCC (L&S) 562; AIR 1981 SC 1990; 1981 LabIC 1568; 1981 (2) LLJ 433; 1982 (1) LLN 16; 1981(3) SCALE 1445; [1982] 1 S.C.R. 665; 1981 (2) SLJ 280; 1981 (3) SLR 326

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Right To Promotion, Eligibility - Qualifications

Summary: Service - States Reorganisation Act, 1956, s.115(5) - Constitution, art. 309, Proviso - Whether there was denial of 'fair and equitable treatment' within the meaning of sub-s. (5) of s. 115 of the 1956 Act - Court held satisfied that the State Govt. acted with the best of intentions - Appeal allowed.
