V. Vellaswamy v Inspector General of Police, Tamil Nadu, Madras

Supreme Court of India

03 August 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 494; (1981) 4 SCC 247; (1981) SCC (L&S) 598; AIR 1982 SC 82; 1982 (44) FLR 268; 1982 LabIC 355; 1982 (1) LLN 282; 1981 (3) SLR 39

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution, art. 226 - Tamil Nadu Police Subordinate Service (Discipline and Appeal) Rules - Alternative remedy - Service - Dismissal of writ petition against compulsory retirement of Police Officer - Ground, existence of alternative remedy i.e. review petition u/r. 15-A of Rules - Held, cannot be dismissed on the only ground - Appeal Allowed.
