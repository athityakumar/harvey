Commissioner Of Wealth Tax, Amritsar v Suresh Seth

Supreme Court of India

07 April 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 305; (1981) SCC (Tax) 168; (1981) 2 SCC 790; AIR 1981 SC 1106; 1981 (21) CTR 349; [1981] 129 ITR 328; 1981(1) SCALE 729; [1981] 3 S.C.R. 419; 1981 TAXLR 786; [1981] 6 TAXMAN 35; 1981 UJ 555

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Net Wealth, Mines Act, 1923, Finance Act, 1966, Finance Act, 1969

Summary: Wealth Tax Act, 1957, ss. 14, 15 and 18 - Constitution, 1950, art. 20 - General Clauses Act, 1897, s. 6 - Whether failure to file return u/s. 14(1) is a continuing offence - Held, omission to file return not a continuing offence - Amendments are not retrospective - Appeal Dismissed.
