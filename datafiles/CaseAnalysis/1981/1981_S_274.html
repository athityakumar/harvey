Ku. Sonia Bhatia v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 March 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, A. Varadarajan, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 604; (1981) 2 SCC 585; AIR 1981 SC 1274; 1981 ALJ 467; 1981 (7) ALR 244; [1981] 3 S.C.R. 239

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Contract & Commercial

Summary: Land & Property - Uttar Pradesh Imposition of Ceiling on Land Holdings Act, 1960, s. 5 - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 122 - Indian Contract Act, 1872, s. 2(d) - Authority acting under U.P. Imposition of Ceiling on Land Holdings Act, 1960, rejected claim of petitioners on basis of a gift which had been executed by her grand-father by a registered document D/- 28-01-1972 - Whether Deed of Gift would be valid in consideration of sub-s. (6) of s. 5 as amended with retrospective operation with effect from 08-06-1973? - Held, substantive provision which is contained in sub-s. (6) clearly provides that any transfer after 24-01-1971 would have to be ignored and not taken into account in determining surplus area - Cl. (b) of proviso, however, carves out an exception to general rule contained in sub-s. (6) providing an exception for those transfers which are done in good faith, to satisfaction of prescribed Authority, for adequate consideration, under an irrevocable instrument and which are not in nature of a benami transaction - Cl. (b) of proviso clearly and expressly exclude a transaction which is in nature of a gift and which is without consideration - Appeal dismissed.
