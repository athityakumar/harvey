Umrao Singh v State of Haryana

Supreme Court of India

10 April 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

V.D. Tulzapurkar, Amarendra Nath Sen

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 98; (1981) 3 SCC 91; (1981) SCC (Cr) 649; AIR 1981 SC 1723; 1981 CRLJ 1704; 1982 CRLR 11A

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Health & Drug - Prevention of Food Adulteration Act, 1954, s. 16 (1) (a) (i) - Selling adulterated milk - Appeal for reduction of sentence - Petitioner old as well as asthma patient - Whether sentence of accused is to be reduced to period already undergone? - Appellant/petitioner is aged about 70 and suffering from asthama illness and has a clean past record - Besides, percentage of deficiency that was noticed in milk sold by him was 0.4% in fat contents - Held, that sentence be reduced to already undergone - Order accordingly.
