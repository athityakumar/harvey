Ahmed Ibrahim Sahigra Dhoraji v Commissioner of Wealth Tax, Gujarat

Supreme Court of India

07 April 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 340; (1981) 3 SCC 77; AIR 1981 SC 1562; 1981 (21) CTR 356; [1981] 129 ITR 314; 1981(1) SCALE 694; [1981] 3 S.C.R. 402; 1981 TAXLR 1127; [1981] 6 TAXMAN 51

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Net Wealth, Finance Act, 1965

Summary: Wealth Tax Act, 1957, s. 2(m) - Debt owed - Computation of net wealth tax - Deduction of Income Tax payable on the amount disclosed by assessee pursuant to s. 68 of Finance Act, 1965 - Permissibility - Held, can be deducted while computing net wealth - Liability to pay tax on such amount arose not under Finance Act but u/s.3 of Income Tax Act, 1922 u/s. 4 of 1961 Act - Appeals Allowed.
