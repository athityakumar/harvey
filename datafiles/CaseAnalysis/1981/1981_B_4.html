S. Munishamappa and another v B. Venkatarayappa and Others

Supreme Court of India

30 March 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

Amarendra Nath Sen, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, A. Varadarajan

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 540; (1981) 3 SCC 260; AIR 1981 SC 1177; 1981(1) SCALE 655; 1981 UJ 272

Case Digest

Subject: Election

Summary: Representation of the People Act, 1951, s. 9A - Construction contract between candidate and the State Government - Subsistance of - On the date of filing nomination papers -Held, not subsisting on date of filing of nomination paper in view of facts and circumstances - Appeal Allowed.
