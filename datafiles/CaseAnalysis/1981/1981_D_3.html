Anupama Sen Gupta and Others v Deb Kumar Sen Sarma and Others

Supreme Court of India

22 September 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, R.S. Pathak, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 469; (1981) 4 SCC 544; AIR 1982 SC 25; 1982 (1) RCJ 654; 1981(3) SCALE 1472; 1981 UJ 779

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - West Bengal Premises Tenancy Act, 1956, ss. 29 B, 13(1), cl. (ff), cl. (6) - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958 - Petition u/s. 29B of 1956 Act, by Govt. employee - Home in name of his wife - Notice for eviction, in wife's name - Wife died - (A) Whether employee is entitled to maintain petition - (B) Retire Pendelite - Right of prosecuting petition, if lost thereby - Held, maintainable - Not lost - Appeal dismissed.
