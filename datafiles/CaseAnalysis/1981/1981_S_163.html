Rama Nand and Others v State of Himachal Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

06 January 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Sarkaria, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 129; (1981) SCC (Cr) 197; (1981) 1 SCC 511; AIR 1981 SC 738; 1981 CRLJ 298; 1981 CrLR(SC) 340; 1981 MLJ 241; 1981(1) SCALE 24; [1981] 2 S.C.R. 444; 1981 UJ 424

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Indian Evidence Act, 1872, s. 3 - Murder - Circumstantial evidence alone - Proof of homicidal death - Corpus of deceased not found - Effect and extent of - Held, must be definite and cogent circumstances pointing to guilt of accused only - Conviction possible.
