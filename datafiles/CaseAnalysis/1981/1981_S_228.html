Madi Ganga v State of Orissa

Supreme Court of India

19 February 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, Baharul Islam

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 24; (1981) 2 SCC 224; (1981) SCC (Cr) 411; AIR 1981 SC 1165; 1981 CRLJ 628; 1981 CRLR 337; 1981 UJ 503

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Evidence Act, 1872, ss. 24 and 80 - (A) Evidentary value of confession made to Magistrate - Examination of Magistrate - (B) Sufficiency of corroboration in a case where conviction is based upon confessional statement - Held, not necessary in all cases - General corroboration is sufficient to sustain conviction - Appeal dismissed.
