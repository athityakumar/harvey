State Of Gujarat v Sayed Mohd. Baquir El Edross

Supreme Court of India

01 September 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

A.D. Koshal, V. Balakrishna Eradi, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 365; (1981) 4 SCC 1; AIR 1981 SC 1921; 1981 (7) ALR 611; 1981 (22) GLR 1167; 1982 (1) RCR(Rent) 298; 1981(3) SCALE 1793; [1982] 1 S.C.R. 551; 1981 UJ 755

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: CPC, 1908, O. 22, r. 4 - Limitation Act, 1963, s. 5 - Application for bringing LRs on record - Time barred - Without showing reason for condonation of delay - Delay for setting aside abatement - Appeal dismissed in view of abatement - Held, strong case on merits cannot be a ground for condonation of delay - Appeal Dismissed.
