P.V. Shetty v B.S. Giridhar

Supreme Court of India

31 October 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 461; (1982) 3 SCC 403; AIR 1982 SC 83

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Constitution, art. 133 - Karnataka Rent Control Act, 1961, s. 14 - Tenancy - Fair rent - Application for fixation of, by tenant paying Rs. 640 per month - Allegation in eviction suit that protection of Act is not available - High Court rejected application for stay of suit - Appeal u/art. 133 of Constitution - Whether application of tenant be allowed - And whether fair rent fixed at Rs.500/- or less , entitles tenant to protection of Act - Disposal of application thus has impact on suit - Held, in the circumstances stay granted - Order Accordingly.
