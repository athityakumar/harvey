Bimal Chand Jain v Sri Gopal Agarwal

Supreme Court of India

27 July 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, Baharul Islam, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 399; (1981) 3 SCC 486; AIR 1981 SC 1657; 1981 ALJ 908; 1981 (7) ALR 556; 1981 (2) RCR(Rent) 314; 1981(3) SCALE 1099; [1982] 1 S.C.R. 124

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: CPC, 1908, O.15, r. 5(2) - Tenancy - Striking off defence in suit for ejectment - Reason, failure to deposit admitted rent and to make representation as per O. 15, r. 5 - Held, Court still has discretion not to strike off defence if on facts and circumstances already existing on record there is good reason for not doing so - Appeal Allowed.
