Ganapati Sitaram Balvalkar and Another v Waman Shripad Mage (Since Dead) Through Lrs

Supreme Court of India

10 August 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

A.D. Koshal, Amarendra Nath Sen

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 491; (1981) 4 SCC 143; AIR 1981 SC 1956

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Transfer of a tenancy after it ceases to be contractual is not permissible under the Bombay Rent Act - Conflict between Jai Singh case [1972 Indlaw SC 193] and Damadilal case [1976 Indlaw SC 386] - Jai Singh is a Four-Judge decision and is binding on vis-a-vis Damadilal case where judgment delivered by three Judges - Jai Singh case holds the field till today insofar as the Bombay Rent Act - SLP dismissed.
