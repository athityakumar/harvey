Rainbow Steels Limited and Another v Commissioner of Sales Tax, Uttar Pradesh and Another

Supreme Court of India

30 January 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

V.D. Tulzapurkar, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 204; (1981) SCC (Tax) 90; (1981) 2 SCC 141; AIR 1981 SC 2101; 1981 ALJ 737; 1981(1) SCALE 229; [1981] 2 S.C.R. 727; 1981 (47) STC 298; 1981 TAXLR 2985; 1981 UJ 227

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Keywords: Central Provinces And Berar Industrial Disputes Settlement Act, 1947

Summary: U.P. Sales Tax , 1948, s. 3A, sch. I, cl. (b) - Notification No. ST.II. 4949 / X-10(2)-74, D/- 30-05-1975, entry 15 - Sales Tax - Interpretation - Word 'old' in Entry 15 of the notification - Thermal Power Plant in perfect running condition - If 'old machinery' within meaning of Entry 15 and exigible to Sales Tax - Held, not exigible to sales tax - Principle 'noscitur a sociis' applicable - Appeal allowed.
