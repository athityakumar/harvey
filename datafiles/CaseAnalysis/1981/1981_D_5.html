Mohd. Zakir v Delhi Administration and Others

Supreme Court of India

02 April 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

A. Varadarajan, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 17; (1982) 3 SCC 216; (1982) SCC (Cr) 695; AIR 1982 SC 696; 1982 CRLJ 611

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 22(5) - Habeas Corpus - Petitioner detained without being supplied grounds of detention - Grounds supplied later on after a considerable delay - Delay violative of art. 22(5) - Held, continued order of detention of detenu is rendered void - Petition allowed.
