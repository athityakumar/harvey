Rafiq & Anr. v Munshilal & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

16 April 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, Baharul Islam

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 297; (1981) 2 SCC 788; AIR 1981 SC 1400; 1981 ALJ 704; [1981] 3 S.C.R. 509; 1981 UJ 505

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: CPC, 1908, O. 41, r. 17, O. 43, r. 1, s. 35 - Cost in an appeal dismissed for default of appellant's counsel - Party should not suffer for misdemeanour or inaction of his counsel - Dismissal set aside by Supreme Court - Costs directed to be recovered from appellant's counsel who absented himself - Appeal Allowed.
