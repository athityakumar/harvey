Natraj Studios Private Limited v Navrang Studios and Another

Supreme Court of India

07 January 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, Baharul Islam, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 376; (1981) 1 SCC 523; AIR 1981 SC 537; 1982 (2) CompLJ 551; 1981 (1) RCR(Rent) 350; 1981 (1) RentLR 448; 1981(1) SCALE 62; [1981] 2 S.C.R. 466

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Arbitration Act, Madhya Pradesh Accommodation Control Act, Uttar Pradesh (Temporary) Control of Rent and Eviction Act, Bombay Rent, Hotel and lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947, Maharashtra Co-operative Society Act, 1960, Cooperative Societies Act

Summary: Bombay Rents, Hotel and Lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947 - Whether building licensed to run business is premises? - Is licensee really a tenant? - Is Court of Small Causes exercising jurisdiction over any arbitration proceedings merely because agreement between parties contains an arbitration clause and Court is asked to stay the proceeding before itself - Held, building licensed to run business is premises - Licensee is not really a tenant.
