Vishundas Hundumal, Etc. v State Of Madhya Pradesh & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

13 March 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, A.D. Koshal, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 288; (1981) 2 SCC 410; AIR 1981 SC 1636; 1981(1) SCALE 589; [1981] 3 S.C.R. 234; 1981 UJ 306

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Constitution, arts. 14, and 136 - Motor Vehicles Act, 1939, s. 68C - Special leave petition - Under scheme of nationalisation of routes, certain existing operators allowed to operate on overlapping portions of notified routes but facility denied to petitioners - Discrimination - Interference sought - Supreme Court issued directions for garant of similar facilities for petitioners - Petition Allowed.
