Workmen of Metro Theatre Limited, Bombay v Metro Theatre Limited, Bombay

Supreme Court of India

31 July 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

V.D. Tulzapurkar, A. Varadarajan

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 157; (1981) SCC (L&S) 546; (1981) 3 SCC 596; AIR 1981 SC 1685; 1981 (43) FLR 369; 1981 LabIC 1230; 1981 (2) LLJ 348; 1981 (2) LLN 486; 1981(3) SCALE 1125; [1982] 1 S.C.R. 164; 1981 UJ 954

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Payment of Gratuity Act, 1972, s. 4(5) - (A) Industrial Award - Date of commencement - Held, impugned award wages and gratuity of workers were governed by earlier award on July 3, 1969 which was effective from January 1, 1967 while dearness allowance was governed by award effective from January 1, 1970 - Both these awards were duly terminated by notice and fresh demands for revision of wage scales, dearness allowance, etc - Effective from January 1, 1974 were submitted by union to management on April 15, 1974 - Reference to Tribunal was made on July 10, 1975 and by impugned award Tribunal granted - (ii) Dearness allowance - Determination of - Linkage to some rational factors - Held, on July 29, 1977, revision in wage scales and dearness allowance with effect from January 1, 1977 - Too much financial burden on company - Such additional burden could not have been more than Rs. 1, 00, 000 or Rs. 1, 20, 000 a year during three years 1974, 1975 and 1976 - Such profits for each of said three years being Rs. 6, 80, 912, Rs. 6, 51, 181 and Rs. 5, 70, 884 - Further held, it would be proper to give revision in wage scale and dearness allowance only from January 1, 1977 - Dearness allowance should be linked with cost of living index and Consumers' Price Index Number - (C) s. 4(5) of Payment of Gratuity Act, 1972 - Scheme of the Act - Further held, Act was exhaustive and was intended to ensure uniform payment of gratuity to employees throughout country - Tribunal accepted contention of management - That it could not go beyond scheme contemplated by Act - Appeal dismissed.
