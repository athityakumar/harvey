Vinod Kumar Shantilal Gosalia v Gangadhar Narsingdas Agarwal and others

Supreme Court of India

26 August 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, A.D. Koshal

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 259; (1981) 4 SCC 226; AIR 1981 SC 1946; 1981(3) SCALE 1459; [1982] 1 S.C.R. 392

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Defence & Security Forces; Practice & Procedure; Trade

Summary: Constitution - Goa, Daman and Diu (Laws) Regulation, 1962, s. 4 - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 1, 13 and 240 - Case of acquisition - Rights accrued under old laws - Recognisition of rights under new Govt. - Held, that rights under old laws cannot be enforced against new Govt. unless it chooses to recognise those rights - Appeal allowed.
