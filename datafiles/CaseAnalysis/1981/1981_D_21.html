Smt. Milavi Devi v Dina Nath

Supreme Court of India

20 January 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 592; (1982) 3 SCC 366

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Condonation of delay - Held, appellant had sufficient cause for not filing appeal before Tribunal - Order made by Tribunal as also order of HC set aside and remand appeal to Tribunal -Appeal allowed.
