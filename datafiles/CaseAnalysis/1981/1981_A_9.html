State of Gujarat v Adam Kasam Bhaya

Supreme Court of India

18 September 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

Baharul Islam, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 264; (1981) 4 SCC 216; (1981) SCC (Cr) 823; AIR 1981 SC 2005; 1981 CRLJ 1686; 1981 CRLR 541; 1981 (22) GLR 1278; 1981(3) SCALE 1563; [1982] 1 S.C.R. 740; 1981 UJ 853

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Detention - Jurisdiction - Conservation of Foreign Exchange and Prevention of Smuggling Activities Act, 1974, s. 3 - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 22 and 226 - State passed order of detention against respondents as they were found in possession of a trawler/vessel containing contraband goods - High Court quashed order of detention on ground that respondent was not aware of presence of contraband in vessel - Whether order of HC was justified? - Held, no - HC can only examine an order from point of incriminating material and evidence against accused and cannot reach finding of fact - HC has wrongly assumed jurisdiction of authority to inquire into order of detention - Appeal allowed.
