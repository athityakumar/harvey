Kundan Singh v State of Punjab

Supreme Court of India

20 April 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, A. Varadarajan

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 58; (1982) 3 SCC 213; AIR 1982 SC 62; 1982 CRLJ 626B

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 307 and 324 - Conviction under challenge - Held, conviction of appellant u/s. 307 was not justified and that conviction could be maintained only u/s. 324 since victims received simple injuries - Conviction of appellant altered to one u/s. 324 for causing simple injuries - Appeal allowed.
