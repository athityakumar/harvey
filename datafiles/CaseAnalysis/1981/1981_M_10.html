Madhya Pradesh Ration Vikreta Sanghsociety & Ors. Etc. Etc. v State Of Madhya Pradesh & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

22 September 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 266; (1981) 4 SCC 535; AIR 1981 SC 2001; 1981(3) SCALE 1420; [1982] 1 S.C.R. 750; 1982 UJ 42

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Consumer Protection; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Socio-Economic

Summary: Constitution - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14, 19 and 162 - Essential Commodities Act, 1955 - Madhya Pradesh Food Stuffs (Distribution) Control Order, 1960 - Whether Madhya Pradesh (Food-stuffs) Civil Supplies Public Distribution Scheme, 1981, formulated by State Govt. u/sub-cl. (d) of cl. 2 of Madhya Pradesh Foodstuffs (Distribution) Control order, 1960, is violative of arts. 14 and 19(1)(g) of Constitution, 1950? - Held, no - Scheme only envisages a rule of preference - Therefore, formulation of scheme does not exclude retail traders from making an application for appointment as agents and also scheme in no way infringes petitioners' right to carry on their trade in food grains - Therefore no violation of fundamental rights - Petitions dismissed.
