B.R. Ramabhadriah v Secretary, Food & Agriculture Department Andhra Pradesh &Ors

Supreme Court of India

30 July 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

V. Balakrishna Eradi, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 245; (1981) 3 SCC 528; (1981) SCC (L&S) 530; AIR 1981 SC 1653; 1981 (43) FLR 331; 1981 LabIC 1114; 1981 (2) LLJ 263; 1981 (2) LLN 494; 1981(3) SCALE 1103; [1982] 1 S.C.R. 159; 1981 (2) SLJ 263; 1981 (3) SLR 108; 1981 UJ 591

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution, arts. 226 and 311 Service - Fixation of seniority - Quashing of provisional gradation list sought - Change in circumstances pendelite - Lesser relief of quashing the list partly - Permissibility - Lesser relief of quashing the list partly cannot be denied to him on technical ground - Matter (Remanded) Remitted To High Court.
