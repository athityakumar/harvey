Needle Industries (India) Limited, and Others v Needle Industries Newey (India) Holding Limited

Supreme Court of India

07 May 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, P.N. Bhagwati, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 403; (1981) 3 SCC 333; AIR 1981 SC 1298; [1981] 51 Comp Cas 743; 1982 (1) CompLJ 1; 1981(2) SCALE 959; [1981] 3 S.C.R. 698

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Keywords: Fera, Companies Act, 1913, Issued Capital, Institutional, Companies (Amendment) Act, 1960, Renunciation

Summary: Corporate - Companies Act, 1956, ss. 3, 3A, 81, 94, 97, 260, 287, 290, 300 and 397 - FERA, 1973, ss. 19(1)(b) and 29 - Isuue as to sharehldings of company and its foreign holding company effecting interest of stakeholders - Court have to consider the following in the light of the Companies Act, 1956 : oppression, effect of isue of shares, whether question of mala fides in application can be decided on basis of affidavits, quorum, interested director, appointment of additional director and their powers - And some under FERA, 1973 like what costitute creation of interest in a security - Directions issued to ensure smooth functioning of the company - Technicalities cannot be permitted to defeat the provision of law - Director and Chairman of the Board, to continue functions - Appeals allowed.
