Haridas Aildas Thadani and Others v Godrej Rustom Kermani

Supreme Court of India

16 November 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 459; (1984) 1 SCC 668; AIR 1983 SC 319; 1982 (8) ALR 221; 1982 UJ 306

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice and Procedure - Subordinate Court allowing amendment of plaint, while exercising discretion - And High Court lightly interfere in revision - Legality - Held, High Court was clearly wrong in setting aside the order of the District Judge who had exercised a sound discretion in allowing the amendment of the plaint sought by the plaintiff in the circumstances of the case - Appeal allowed.
