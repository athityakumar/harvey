Brig. Sahib Singh Kalha and Others v Amritsar Improvement Trust and Others

Supreme Court of India

01 October 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, A. Varadarajan

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 465; (1982) 1 SCC 419; AIR 1982 SC 940; 1981(3) SCALE 1561; 1982 UJ 469

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 28 - Land - Acquisition - (A) Interest - Allowance - On on enhanced amount of compensation - (B) Matters to be considered while determining compenmsation for acquisition of land in undeveloped area - Award of Collector based on 'belting' principle - Held, interest @ 6 per cent per annum directed to be paid on enhanced compensation from date of taking possession of land till realisation - Held, cut of 33 per cent of market value in one case and 70 per cent in others was justified in determining amount of compensation - Petition Dismissed.
