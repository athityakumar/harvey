South India Viscose Ltd. v State Of Tamil Nadu

Supreme Court of India

22 July 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 236; (1981) 3 SCC 457; AIR 1981 SC 1604; 1981(3) SCALE 1049; [1982] 1 S.C.R. 44; 1981 (48) STC 232; 1981 TAXLR 3038; 1981 UJ 682

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax; Contract & Commercial

Keywords: Requirement

Summary: Central Sales Tax Act, 1956, s. 3(a) - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 114, Illus - Sales Tax - Whether inter State sale - Determination - Sale of yarn to allottees under Export Promotion Scheme - Movement of goods from Tamil Nadu to Bombay - Supply to allottees through agent at Bombay - Held, there was inter-State sale - Appeal Dismissed.
