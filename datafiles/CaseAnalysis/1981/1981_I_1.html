R.S. Makashi & Ors. v I.M. Menon & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

08 December 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

V. Balakrishna Eradi, A.D. Koshal, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 311; (1982) SCC (L&S) 77; (1982) 1 SCC 379; AIR 1982 SC 101; 1982 LabIC 38; 1982 (1) LLN 235; 1981(3) SCALE 1837; [1982] 2 S.C.R. 69; 1981 (3) SLR 280

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Bombay Rationing Organisation (Fixation of Seniority) Rules 1968

Summary: Service - Constitution, arts. 14, 16, 309 and 311 - Bombay Rationing Organization (Fixation of Seniority) Rules, 1968, rr. 4 and 7, Proviso - Rationing organisation - Creation - Merging employees of various departments - Seniority lists - Constitutionality of 1968 Rules and legality of allowing the petition challenging Rules, filed after long delay - Held, not violative of art. 14 and 16 - Petition, highly belated and was liable to be dismissed on the ground of laches and delay - Appeal allowed.
