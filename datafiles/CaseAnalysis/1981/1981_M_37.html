Assessing Authority-Cum-Excise and Taxation Officer, Gurgaon v Messrs East India Cotton Manufacturing Company Limited Faridabad

Supreme Court of India

23 July 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, A.P. Sen, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 401; (1981) 3 SCC 531; AIR 1981 SC 1610; 1981(3) SCALE 1067; [1982] 1 S.C.R. 55; 1981 (48) STC 239; 1981 TAXLR 3044; 1981 UJ 688

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Summary: Sales Tax - Central Sales Tax Act, 1956, s. 8(3)(b) - Companies Act, 1956 - Interpretation of Statutes - Scope and meaning of s. 8(3)(b) - Whether sale of manufactured goods must be by registered dealer manufacturing goods - Held, need not be registered dealer - Appeals allowed.
