Jayendra and Another v State of Uttar Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

24 July 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, Y.V. Chandrachud

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 78; (1981) 4 SCC 149; (1981) SCC (Cr) 809; AIR 1982 SC 685; 1982 CRLJ 1000; 1982 CRLR 20

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Uttar Pradesh Children Act, 1951 - As per report of Chief Medical Officer, appellant was about 16 years and 4 months old on date of commission of offence - Appellant about 23 years of age on date of report during appeal before Supreme Court - Held, appellant be released forth with in view of fact that he could neither be imprisoned in view of s. 2 of Uttar Pradesh Children Act, 1951 nor could he be sent to approved school, being more than 18 years of age - Petition disposed off.
