Kapurchand Shrimal v Commissioner of Income Tax, Andhra Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

28 August 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, P.N. Bhagwati

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 260; (1981) SCC (Tax) 312; (1981) 4 SCC 317; AIR 1981 SC 1965; 1981 (24) CTR 345; [1981] 131 ITR 451; 1981(3) SCALE 1330; [1982] 1 S.C.R. 505; 1981 TAXLR 1468; [1981] 7 TAXMAN 6; 1981 UJ 746

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1922, ss. 3, 5A , 23 and 25A - Income Tax - Assessment of - Claim of partition of HUF within reasonable time - Assessment without enquiry u/s. 25A - Validity -Held, liable to be cancelled - Appellate Tribunal is under duty to give further direction to pass fresh order of assessment - Order Accordingly.
