A.V. Nachane and Another v Union of India and Another

Supreme Court of India

28 December 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, A.C. Gupta, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 328; (1982) SCC (L&S) 53; (1982) 1 SCC 205; AIR 1982 SC 1126; 1982 LabIC 161; 1982 (1) LLJ 110; 1982 (1) LLN 65; 1981(4) SCALE 1958A; [1982] 2 S.C.R. 246

Case Digest

Subject: Insurance; Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Terms And Conditions Of Service, Life Insurance Corporation (Modification of Settlements) Act, 1976, Excessive Delegation, Employees Of The Corporation, Cotton Textiles (Control of Movement) Order, 1948, Life Insurance Corporation (Amendment) Act, 1981, Life Insurance Corporation of India Class III, class IV Employees (Bonus and Dearness Allowance) Rules 1981, Payment Of Bonus (Amendment) Act, 1976, Life Insurance Corporation Of India Class Iii And Iv Employees (Bonus)

Summary: Service - Bombay Industrial Disputes Act, 1938 - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14, 19 and 21 - Life Insurance Corporation (Amendment) Act, 1981 - Life Insurance Corporation (Modification of Settlements) Act, 1976 - Life Insurance Corporation Act, 1956, ss. 11, 40 and 49 - Life Insurance Corporation of India Class III And IV Employees (Bonus and Dearness Allowance) Rules, 1981 - Payment of Bonus Act, 1965 - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 48 - Whether Amendment Act and Life Insurance Corporation of India Class III and Class IV Employees (Bonus and Dearness Allowance) Rules, 1981 were violative of arts. 14, 19(1) (g) and 21 of Constitution, 1950? - Held, Life insurance Corporation (Amendment) Act, 1981 and Life Insurance Corporation of India Class III and Class IV Employees (Bonus and Dearness Allowance) Rules, 1981 are relevant legislation but in view of decision in Madan Mohan Pathak's case, these rules, in so far as they seek to abrogate terms of 1974 settlements relating to bonus, can operate only prospectively, that is, from 2-02-1981, date of publication of rules - Petitions allowed.
