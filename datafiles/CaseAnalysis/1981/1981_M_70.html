Man Mohan Tuli v Municipal Corporation Of Delhi & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

18 February 1981

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, A.D. Koshal, A. Varadarajan

Where Reported

1981 Indlaw SC 211; (1981) 2 SCC 467; AIR 1981 SC 991; 1981(1) SCALE 352; [1981] 2 S.C.R. 894

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Delhi Municipal Corporation Act, 1957, s.178 - Constitution, sch. 7, list 2, entries 52 and 56 - Delhi Terminal Tax Rules, 1958, r. 26 - Levy of terminal tax - Pre-requisites - Goods meant for destination other than Delhi and passing through Delhi - Whether such goods are exigible to Terminal Tax in the light of 1957, Act and 1958, Rules - Distinction between terminal tax and octroi considering sch. 7, list 2, entries 52 and 56 of Constitution of India - Held, not exigible to Terminal Tax - Distinction drawn - Appeal Allowed.
