Ishwar Singh and another v State of Uttar Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

04 August 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

A.C. Gupta, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 619; (1976) 4 SCC 355; (1976) SCC (Cr) 629; AIR 1976 SC 2423; 1976 CRLJ 1883

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, ss. 342 and 157 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 302, 326, 147, 148, 149, 323 and 324 - Murder - Eye-witness - Appellants convicted by Trial Court and upheld by High Court - Present appeal - Glaring defects in prosecution case - Some material witnesses not examined - Unexplained delay in dispatching FIR to magistrate - Medical evidence in contradiction with prosecution version - Nature of injuries not matches with injuries which could have caused by weapon as used as per prosecution - Held, appellants entitled to benefit of doubt - Convictions set aside - Appeals allowed.
