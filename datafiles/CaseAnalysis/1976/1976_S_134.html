Sri Vijayalakshmi Rice Mills, New Contractors Company and Others v State of Andhra Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

22 March 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

Jaswant Singh, A.N. Ray, M. Hameedullah Beg

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 402; (1976) 3 SCC 37; AIR 1976 SC 1471; [1976] 3 S.C.R. 775; 1976 UJ 367

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Socio-Economic; Agriculture & Agri. Products

Summary: A.P. Rice (Price Control) Order, 1964, cl. 2 - A.P. Rice Price Control (Third Amendment) Order, 1964, cl. 2 - Essential Commodities Act, 1955 - Retrospectivity of amendment Order - Mills supplied rice to State Govt. before Amendment Order - Price paid as per Control Order at that time - Whether millers are entitled to benefit of enhanced rate specified in Amendment Order and claim recovery of difference between the controlled prices specified in 1963 Order and the Amendment Order of 1964 - Held, not entitled - Amendment Order held not retrospective in operation - Appeals dismissed.
