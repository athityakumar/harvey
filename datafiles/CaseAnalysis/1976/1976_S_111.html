Rabindra Kumar Dey v State Of Orissa

Supreme Court of India

31 August 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, P.N. Bhagwati

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 485; (1976) 4 SCC 233; (1976) SCC (Cr) 566; AIR 1977 SC 170; 1977 CRLJ 173; 1976 CRLR 436; [1977] 1 S.C.R. 439

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Evidence Act, 1872, s. 154 - Prevention of Corruption Act, 1947 - (A) Discretion of Court in cross-examination of witness called by a party - Duty - (B) Conviction u/s. 5(2) r/ w s. 5(1)(c) of 1947 Act - Burden of proof - Held, must be exercised judicially - Held, it was for the prosecution to prove affirmatively in what manner the amount was misappropriated after it had been transferred from the custody of the accused - Appeal Allowed.
