State of Orissa and Others v Arun Kumar Patnaik and Others

Supreme Court of India

15 April 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, V.R. Krishna Iyer, N.L. Untwalia

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 391; (1976) SCC (L&S) 468; (1976) 3 SCC 579; AIR 1976 SC 1639; 1976 LabIC 1122; [1976] Supp S.C.R. 59; 1976 UJ 508

Case Digest

Subject: Defence & Security Forces; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Service - Orissa Service of Engineers Rules 1941, rr. 3(b) and 19(b) - Govt. of India Act, 1935 - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 226 - Service Rules - Appointment - Temporary and contract basis - Compliance of Rules - If appointment can be regularised retrospectively - Laches - Held, regularised by excercise of power vested to count period of temporary service as probation period - Held, that there was long and inexplicable delay and grievance was too stale to merit redress - Appeals allowed.
