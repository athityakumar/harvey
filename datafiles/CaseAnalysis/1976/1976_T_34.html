The Statesman Ltd. v Their Workmen

Supreme Court of India

22 January 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, A.C. Gupta, N.L. Untwalia

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 89; (1976) SCC (L&S) 218; (1976) 2 SCC 223; AIR 1976 SC 758; 1976 (32) FLR 202; 1976 LabIC 568; 1976 (1) LLJ 484; 1976 (1) LLN 251; [1976] 3 S.C.R. 228

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Constitution, art. 136 - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - Appeal by special leave against award of Industrial Tribunal - Supreme Court , if interfere - Held, SC will interfere only if extraordinary flaws or grave injustice or other recognised grounds are made out - Appeal Dismissed.
