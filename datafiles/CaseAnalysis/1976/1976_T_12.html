Kerala State Electricity Board, Trivandrum v T.P.K.K. Amsom And Besom, Kerala

Supreme Court of India

29 October 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, M. Hameedullah Beg, P.N. Shingal

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 268; (1976) 4 SCC 634; AIR 1977 SC 282; 1976 KLT 810; [1977] 1 S.C.R. 996; 1977 UJ 16

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Electricity Act, 1910

Summary: Limitation Act - Telegraph Act - Provision of art. 137 is applicable to any petition or application - Applicability of art. 137 of Limitation Act to Petition u/s.16(3) of Telegraph Act claiming enhanced compensation - Held, petition falls within scope of art. 137 - Appeal Allowed.
