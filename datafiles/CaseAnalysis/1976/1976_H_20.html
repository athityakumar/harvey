Baradakanta Mishra v High Court of Orissa and Another

Supreme Court of India

06 May 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, M. Hameedullah Beg, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 511; (1976) 3 SCC 327; (1976) SCC (L&S) 429; AIR 1976 SC 1899; 1976 LabIC 1202; 1976 SLJ 529; 1976 (2) SLR 186; [1976] Supp S.C.R. 561

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 311 and 235 - Control of High Court - High Court, if can reduce in rank or dismiss a District Judge - Distinction - 'dismiss, remove or reduce in rank' - Held, control u/art. 235 means Disciplinary Control, HC can only recommend but cannot order - Dismissal ordinarily disqualifies any future employment and removal ordinarily does not, losing some places in seniority list in same cadre does not amount to reduction in rank - Appeal dismissed.
