Dhaneshwarbuwa Guru Purshottambuwa Owner of Shri Vithalrukha v Charity Commissioner, State of Bombay

Supreme Court of India

01 March 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

P.K. Goswami, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 84; (1976) 2 SCC 417; AIR 1976 SC 871; [1976] 3 S.C.R. 518

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Trusts & Associations

Keywords: Ancestral Immovable Property

Summary: Trusts & Associations - Bombay Public Trust Act, 1950, ss. 2(13), 18, 18(1), 70, 72 and 72(4) - Whether Shri Vithal Sukhamai Sansthan at Amalner, (East Khandesh) was a private Devasthan or a public religious trust? - Definitions of ' public trust' and 'temple' in s. 2(13) and s. 2(17) - 'Sanad' was granted by then British Govt. in favour of 'Devasthan Shri Vithal Rukhamai' - Sanad refers to entry in village accounts - Sanad being an ancient Royal grant is a very important piece of evidence - It is clear from Sanad that Govt. treated temple as a public religious endowment - Held, it is not always possible to have all features of a public trust in a given case - When origin of an endowment is obscure and no direct oral evidence is available, Court will have to resolve controversy about character of trust on documentary evidence, if any, object and purpose for which trust was created, consistent manner in which property has been dealt with or managed by those in charge, manner in which property has long been used by public, contribution of public, to all intents and purposes, as a matter of right without least interference or restriction from temple authorities, to foster maintenance of worship accretion to trust property by way of grants from state of gifts from outsiders inconsistent with private nature of trust, nature of devolution of property, are all important elements in determination of question whether a property is a private or a public religious endowment - In this case all above tests are fulfilled - Oral and documentary evidence leave no room for doubt whatsoever that Sansthan and temple are public religious endowments - Shri Vithal Rukhamai Sansthan at Amalner is a public trust within meaning of s. 2(13) of Act- Appeal dismissed.
