Parashuram Pottery Works Company Limited v Income Tax Officer, Circle-1, Ward 'A', Rajkot

Supreme Court of India

16 November 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

Hans Raj Khanna, V.R. Krishna Iyer

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 350; (1977) 1 SCC 408; AIR 1977 SC 429; 1977 CTR 32; [1977] 106 ITR 1; [1977] 2 S.C.R. 92

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1961, ss.147 and 148 - Constitution, 1950 - Reopening of income tax assessment - Authority allowed excess depreciation by mistake - Liability of the assessee - Assessee cannot be held liable - Proceeding u/s. 147 cannot be taken - Appeal allowed.
