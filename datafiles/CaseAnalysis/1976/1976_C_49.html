Messrs Jaipur Mineral Development Syndicate v Commissioner of Income Tax, New Delhi

Supreme Court of India

16 December 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

Hans Raj Khanna, V.R. Krishna Iyer

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 421; (1977) 1 SCC 508; AIR 1977 SC 1348; [1977] 106 ITR 653; [1977] 2 S.C.R. 460; 1977 TAXLR 685

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: CPC, 1908, s. 151 - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 256 - Prohibition Act - Reference - High Court declining to answer - Ground, absence of party and non-filing of paper book - Application filed for rehearing - Sufficient cause - Inherent powers of HC - Held, HC has inherent power to recall its earlier order and dispose of the reference on merit - Appeal Allowed.
