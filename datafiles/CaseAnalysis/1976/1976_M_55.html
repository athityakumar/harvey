Commissioner of Sales Tax, Gujarat v Messrs Sabarmati Reti Udyog Sahakari Mandali

Supreme Court of India

26 April 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

P.K. Goswami, Hans Raj Khanna

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 389; (1976) 3 SCC 592; AIR 1977 SC 197; 1976 (5) CTR 244; 1976 (38) STC 203; [1976] Supp S.C.R. 158; 1977 TAXLR 1604; 1976 UJ 557

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Bombay Sales Tax Act, 1959, s. 2(28) - Ascertainment of nature of contract, whether contract of sale or works contract - Contract to manufacture and supply bricks made out of earth excavated from Government land - Held, contract of sale - Appeal allowed.
