Muni Lal and Others v Prescribed Authority and Others

Supreme Court of India

20 September 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, V.R. Krishna Iyer, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 470; (1977) 3 SCC 336; AIR 1978 SC 29

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Constitution - Rent Control - Nature and scope of power of High Court u/art. 226 - Finding on question of comparative hardship of landlord and tenant is a finding of fact - Interference with - Permissibility - Held, HC cannot disturb same in writ proceeding - Nor can it reappraise evidence and come to its own conclusion different from that of District Judge and Prescribed Authority - Appeal dismissed.
