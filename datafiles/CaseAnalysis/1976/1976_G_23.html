P. Radhakrishna Naidu and Others v Government of Andhra Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

09 December 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, M. Hameedullah Beg

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 343; (1977) SCC (L&S) 186; (1977) 1 SCC 561; AIR 1977 SC 854; 1977 LabIC 537; 1977 (1) LLN 412; [1977] 2 S.C.R. 365; 1977 SLJ 211; 1977 (1) SLR 258; 1977 UJ 84

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 14, 16, 32, 226, 311 and 359 - Constitution - Order - Compulsory Retirement - Constitutionality - Ancillary issues - (A) Petition alleging violation of art. 311,maintainability - (B) Provisions violative of art. 14 and 16 - (C) Ban on reappointment - (D) Several petitioners having separate causes of action combined - (E) Retirement in public interest - Order mentioning three different rules under which order is passed - Rules not applicable to petitioner not scored out - Held, constitutional - Not maintainable - Do not violate - Ban is not invalid - Held, disputed questions such as age of petitioner could not be gone into - Order not invalidated - Petitions dismissed.
