State of Orissa v Mr. Brahmananda Nanda

Supreme Court of India

31 August 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 484; (1976) 4 SCC 288; (1976) SCC (Cr) 596; AIR 1976 SC 2488; 1976 CRLJ 1985; 1976 CRLR 407; 1976 UJ 754

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Evidence Act, 1872, s. 3 - IPC, 1860, s. 302 - Murder - Appreciation of evidence - Credibility - When eye witness not disclosing name of assailant for a day and half - High Court rejecting it as untrustworthy and acquitting the accused - Held, such non-disclosure was a serious infirmity which destroyed the credibility of the evidence of the witness and that the HC was correct in rejecting it - Appeal Dismissed.
