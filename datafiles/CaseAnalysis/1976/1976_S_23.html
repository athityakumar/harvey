 State of Gujarat v Sardarabegum and Others

Supreme Court of India

19 April 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Sarkaria, A.N. Ray, M. Hameedullah Beg, P.N. Shingal, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 524; (1976) 3 SCC 416; AIR 1976 SC 1695; 1976 LabIC 1137; 1976 UJ 517

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution, art. 226 - Limitation Act, 1963, art. 124 - Writ Petition against discontinuance of political pension - Deletion of words 'in perpetuity' in prayer clause - At the instance of writ applicant - Legality of Order directing payment of pension to the heirs of deceased pensioner after his death - Held, illegal - Can be corrected in review application even after expiry of period of limitation - Appeal Partly Allowed.
