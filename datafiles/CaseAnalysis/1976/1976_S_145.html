N.K. Chauhan and Others v State of Gujarat and Others

Supreme Court of India

01 November 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, P.N. Bhagwati, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 204; (1977) SCC (L&S) 127; (1977) 1 SCC 308; AIR 1977 SC 251; 1977 LabIC 38; [1977] 1 S.C.R. 1037; 1977 SLJ 110; 1977 (1) SLR 205

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure; Trade

Summary: Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 14 and 16 - Promotion - Deputy Collector Cadre - Direct recruits and promotees - Govt. resolution - Quota, 50 percent each - 'as far as practicable' meaning of - Whether Govt. can vary ratio? - Seniority inter se, fixation - 'Quota' is interlocked with 'rota' - Govt. did what it could - Held, seniority will depend on length of continuous officiating service - Appeal allowed.
