Balchand Jain v State of Madhya Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

05 November 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, A.C. Gupta, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 351; (1976) 4 SCC 572; (1976) SCC (Cr) 689; AIR 1977 SC 366; 1977 CRLJ 225; 1976 CrLR(SC) 527; [1977] 2 S.C.R. 52

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Fetters, Defence And Internal Security Of India Act, 1971, Defence and Internal Security of India Rules, 1971

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Code Of Criminal Procedure, 1973, s. 438 - Defence and Internal Security of India Rules, 1971, r. 184 - Anticipatory bail - Conflict between s. 438 CrPC and Defence and Internal Security of India Rules, r. 1971 - Whether s. 438, CrPC, 1973 is repealed by r. 1984 of Defence and Internal Security of India Rules, 1971? - Held, that s. 438, CrPC, 1973 repealed or overruled by r. 184 of Rules of 1971 but two have to be read harmoniously without interfering with spheres contemplated by each of those provisions and hence remit case back to High Court to be decided on merits r. 184 of Rules of 1971 is only supplemental to s. 438 of CrPC, 1973 and contains guidelines which have to be followed by Court in passing orders for anticipatory bail - Appeal allowed.
