Loon Karan Sethia Etc. v Ivan E. John & Ors. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

20 October 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

Jaswant Singh, A.N. Ray, M. Hameedullah Beg

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 259; (1977) 1 SCC 379; AIR 1977 SC 336; [1977] 1 S.C.R. 853

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Contract & Commercial

Summary: Partnership Act, 1932, s. 69 - CPC, 1908, O. 20, r. 16 - (A) Applicability of bar u/s. 69 of - Applicability - (B) Deed - Construction - Effect of material alteration without consent of party liable under it - (C) Suit on basis of settled account - Findings that there was no settlement - Held, s.69 is mandatory in character - The effect of making such an alteration without the consent of the party bound is exactly the same as that of cancelling the deed - Held, it is not open to Court to go into accounts - Appeal No.C.A. 416/73 Dismissed; Appeal No.C.A. 572/74 Allowed.
