Bhupindra Nath Chatterjee v State of Bihar and Others

Supreme Court of India

14 December 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

Hans Raj Khanna, A.C. Gupta, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 422; (1977) SCC (L&S) 427; (1977) 3 SCC 491; AIR 1977 SC 746; 1977 LabIC 271; 1977 (1) SLR 590

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution, art. 311 - Claim of benefit of superannuation - Govt. of Bihar extended the age from 55 to 58 - Appellant's case was that when he entered service his date of birth was entered as Jan.1908 - HC in rejecting the claim of the appellant that he was born not on 01-01-1908 but on 16-01-1908 and that he was not entitled to the benefit of the notificationand - Record showed the age as 01-01-1908 - Supreme Court upheld the judgement of the - Appeal dismissed.
