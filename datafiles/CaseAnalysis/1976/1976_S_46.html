Shri Sarwan Singh and Another v Shri Kasturi Lal

Supreme Court of India

14 December 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, P.K. Goswami, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 424; (1977) 1 SCC 750; AIR 1977 SC 265; 1977 (1) RCJ 176; [1977] 2 S.C.R. 421

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958, ss. 14A, 25A, 25B, 25C and 54 - Slum Areas (Improvement and Clearance) Act, 1956 ss. 19 and 39 - Interpretation of Statutes - Non obstante clause in tenancy agreement - Notwithstanding s. 54, provisions of Delhi Rent Control Act, if prevail over that of 1956 Act - Former prevails over latter - Appeal dismissed.
