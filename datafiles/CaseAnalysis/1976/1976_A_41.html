Commissioner of Income Tax (Central), Calcutta v Ashoka Marketing Limited

Supreme Court of India

19 March 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

A.C. Gupta, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 303; (1976) 3 SCC 669; AIR 1976 SC 2081; 1976 CTR 238; [1976] 103 ITR 543; 1976 UJ 362

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1922, ss. 23 and 66(2) - Application for reference u/s. 66 (2) - (A) Dismissal without 'speaking order - (B) Question of concealment of income - Question of law or fact - Maintainability of application - Held, HC should have stated the reasons on which it dismissed the application - Order of the HC dismissing the application cannot be said to be wrong - Appeal Dismissed.
