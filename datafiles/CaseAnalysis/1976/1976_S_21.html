Gulab Chand Sharma v Saraswati Devi and Another

Supreme Court of India

14 December 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

A.C. Gupta, Y.V. Chandrachud

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 628; (1977) 2 SCC 71; AIR 1977 SC 242

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Transfer of Property Act, 1882 - Suit for redemption of mortagage - Injunction - Lease Deed in perpetuity - Notice of re-entry alleging that he had violated some of conditions of perpetual lease - Whether condition in cl. 9 is a clog on equity of redemption? - Held, condition in cl. 9 of mortgage which seeks to take away right of redemption even before period of four years within which mortgagor was entitled to pay off mortgage debt had run out, is obviously a clog on right of redemption - Appeal dismissed.
