Diwan Bros v Central Bank of India, Bombay and Others

Supreme Court of India

07 May 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, P.N. Bhagwati, A.C. Gupta

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 385; (1976) 3 SCC 800; AIR 1976 SC 1503; [1976] Supp S.C.R. 664

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Contract & Commercial

Summary: Court Fees Act, 1870 - Displaced Persons (Debts Adjustment) Act, 1951, ss. 1, 14, 15 and 40 - CPC, 1908, s. 2(2) - (A) Applicability of sch.II, art. 11 of 1870 Act - To Order of Tribunal under 1951 Act , which is not 'decree' - (B) Interpretation of Statutes - Fiscal Statutes - Held, Court fee payable u/art. 1 of sch.II art. II and not advalorem u/art. 1 of sch. I - Strict interpretation giving every benefit of doubt to subject necessary - Appeal allowed.
