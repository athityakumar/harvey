Special Land Acquisition Officer City Improvementtrust Board v P. Govindan

Supreme Court of India

10 September 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Hameedullah Beg, A.N. Ray, P.N. Shingal

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 198; (1976) 4 SCC 697; AIR 1976 SC 2517; [1977] 1 S.C.R. 549

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: City of Mysore Improvement Act, 1903, ss. 16, 18 and 23 - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss.4(1), 6 and 23 - Compensation for acquisition of land under 1903 Act - Determination of market value - Market value, held, should be determined with reference to date of notification u/s. 16 of 1903 Act - Matter Remitted To High Court.
