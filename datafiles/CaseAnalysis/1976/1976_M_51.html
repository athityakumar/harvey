State of Uttar Pradesh v Messrs Kores (India) Limited

Supreme Court of India

18 October 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

Jaswant Singh, Hans Raj Khanna

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 359; (1976) 4 SCC 477; (1977) SCC (Tax) 40; AIR 1977 SC 132; 1977 (6) CTR 7; 1990 (26) ECR 464; [1977] 1 S.C.R. 837; 1977 (39) STC 8; 1977 TAXLR 1601; 1976 UJ 876

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure; Sales Tax

Summary: Uttar Pradesh Sales Tax Act , 1948, s. 3A - (A) Whether expression 'paper other than hand paper' in sch. item 2 in Notification D/- 01-07-1966 - Covers Carbon paper - (B) Whether entry 'typewriters and parts thereof' in sch. to notification D/- 01-06-1963 covers 'Typewriter ribbons' - Held, carbon paper is not covered by expression - 'Typewriter ribbons' do not come within the expression and cannot be taxed thereunder - Appeals allowed.
