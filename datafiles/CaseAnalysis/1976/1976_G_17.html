Girdhari Lal (Dead) By Lrs v Hukam Singh and Others

Supreme Court of India

19 October 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Hameedullah Beg, A.N. Ray, P.N. Shingal

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 459; (1977) 3 SCC 347; AIR 1977 SC 129; 1977 (1) RCJ 509; 1976 UJ 885

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Rent Control - Land & Property - Civil Procedure Code, 1908, O. 20 r. 12 - Rajasthan Premises (Control of Rend and Eviction) Act, 1950, s. 6 - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 109 - (A)Whether decree for rent or mense profits during pendency of suit could not be granted under O. 20 r. 12, CPC, 1908? - Held, plaintiff had paid additional Court fee for a suit for ejectment therefore it was not open to defendant to object to award of mense profits on ground that there was no suit for ejectment before Court - (B)Whether under proviso to s. 109 of Transfer of Property Act, 1882 transferee is not entitled to arrears of rent due before transfer? - Further held, transferee is ordinarily not so entitled unless there is a contract to contrary - (C) Whether terms of compromise operated as a transfer which required a registered deed for its enforcement? - Further held, agreement between parties involving recognition of a transfer, could require registration unless terms of compromise decree necessarily involved execution of a deed of conveyance also - Appeals dismissed.
