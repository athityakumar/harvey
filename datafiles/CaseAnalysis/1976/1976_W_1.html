D.H. Maniar & Ors. v Waman Laxman Kudav

Supreme Court of India

24 August 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

N.L. Untwalia, P.N. Bhagwati, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 180; (1976) 4 SCC 118; AIR 1976 SC 2340; 1976 RCJ 822; 1976 RCR(Rent) 843; 1977 (1) RentLR 8; [1977] 1 S.C.R. 403

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Presidency Small Cause Courts Act, 1882, ss. 41 and 43 - Easements Act, 1882, s. 62(c) - Application u/s.41 - Against licensee - that too after expiry of term of licence - Rejection of defence that he was a tenant - Effect of introduction of s. 15A in Bombay Rent Act in 1973 during pendency of proceedings - Held, licencee not entitled to protection conferred on licensee by s. 15-A - Applicant held entitled to an order for possession u/s. 43 of the Act - Appeal Allowed.
