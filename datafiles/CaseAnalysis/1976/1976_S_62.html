Municipal Corporation, Indore and Others v Smt. Ratna Prabha and Others

Supreme Court of India

29 October 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Shingal, A.N. Ray, M. Hameedullah Beg

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 269; (1976) 4 SCC 622; AIR 1977 SC 308; 1977 (1) RCJ 443; 1977 (1) RCR(Rent) 431; 1977 (1) RentLR 313; [1977] 1 S.C.R. 1017; 1976 UJ 956

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Municipalities & Local Governments; Service

Summary: Land & Property - Madhya Pradesh Municipal Corporation Act, 1956, s. 138(b) - Madhya Pradesh Accommodation Control Act, 1961, s. 7 - Determination of standard rent - Fixation of rental value of premises - Used by owners and never let - Interpretation of non-obstante clause and relevancy of standard rent u/s. 7 of 1961 Act - Held, standard rent will be reasonable letting value, where standard rent has been fixed u/s. 7 of 1961 Act, and no prima facie fraud or collusion, otherwise if it never been let out etc, reasonable rent, can be fixed according to 1961 Act - Appeal allowed.
