P. Philip v The Director Of Enforcement, New Delhi &Another

Supreme Court of India

03 March 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Sarkaria, N.L. Untwalia

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 110; (1976) 2 SCC 174; (1976) SCC (Cr) 241; AIR 1976 SC 1185; 1976 CRLJ 920; 1976 CrLR(SC) 146; [1976] 3 S.C.R. 532; 1976 UJ 287

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Criminal

Summary: CrPC, 1973, ss. 482(2)(a) and 399(3) - Crpc, 1898 , s. 435 - FERA, 1947 - Saving clause - Meaning of term 'Application' in s. 482(2)(a) of 1973 Code - Revision application u/s. 435 of 1898 Code pending at the commencement of new Code - Disposal of , how - Held, it has to be disposed of according to old Code - Appeal Allowed.
