State of Haryana v Inder Prakash Anand H.C.S. and Others

Supreme Court of India

07 May 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, R.S. Sarkaria, P.N. Shingal, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 328; (1976) SCC (L&S) 372; (1976) 2 SCC 977; AIR 1976 SC 1841; 1976 LabIC 1190; 1976 SLJ 497; 1976 (2) SLR 223; [1976] Supp S.C.R. 603; 1976 UJ 641

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 311 and 235 - Punjab Civil Service Rules, r. 5.32(c) - Judicial Services - Nature and extent of compulsory retirement - Control of High Court over District Courts and Subordinate Courts - Recommendation of HC, effect - Held, it does not amount to dismissal or removal or reduction in rank u/art. 311 or under Service Rules rather it is in accordance with Terms and Conditions - Binding on State Govt. - Appeal dismissed.
