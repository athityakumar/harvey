Lal Chand (Dead) By L.Rs. & Ors. v Radha Kishan

Supreme Court of India

17 December 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, P.K. Goswami

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 291; (1977) 2 SCC 88; AIR 1977 SC 789; 1977 (2) RCR(Rent) 4; 1977 (1) RentLR 588; [1977] 2 S.C.R. 522

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Rent Control

Summary: CPC, 1908, s. 11, O. 41, r. 4 - Slum Areas (Improvement and Clearance) Act, 1956, ss. 19(1)(a), 37A - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958 - Delhi and Ajmer Rent Control Act 1952 - (A) Death of on of the several defendents of a Joint eviction decree - Maintainability of appeal by remaining defendants against whole decree - (B) Ejectment decree passed against tenants from building in slum area - Decree under 1952 Act - Judgment-debtors are tenants within the meaning of s. 19 (1) of 1956 Act - Fresh suit for possession by landlord against judgment debtors without obtaining permission of Competent Authority are not maintainable - Permission to execute decree granted in respect of only portion of rented premises - eviction suit for remaining portion - (C) Interpretation of Statutes - Use of same expression in same statute at different places - Held, maintainable - Suit barred by principle of res jndicate - The same meaning ought to be given to that expression, as far as possible - Appeal Allowed.
