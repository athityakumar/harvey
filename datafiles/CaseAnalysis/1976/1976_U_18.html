Siemens Engineering and Manufacturing Company of v Union of India and Another

Supreme Court of India

30 April 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, A.C. Gupta, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 519; (1976) SCC (L&S) 551; (1976) 2 SCC 981; AIR 1976 SC 1785; [1976] Supp S.C.R. 489

Case Digest

Subject: Customs

Summary: Tariff Act, 1934, s. 2 , sch. I, items 72(3) and 73(21) - Constitution, art. 226 - (A) Pot motors when imported separately from Rayon Spinning frames - Taxability - Provision - (B) Rules - Natural justice - Quasi-judicial Order must be supported by reasons - Held, fall within item 72 (3) and not under Item 73 (21) are chargeable as such to import duty - Rule is one of basic principle of natural justice - Mere pretence of compliance with it would not satisfy the requirement of law - Appeal Allowed.
