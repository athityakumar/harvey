Land Acquisition Officer, City Improvementtrust Board v H. Narayanaiah Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

16 August 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Hameedullah Beg, A.N. Ray, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 166; (1976) 4 SCC 9; AIR 1976 SC 2403; [1977] 1 S.C.R. 178

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Bangalore Improvement Act, 1945, Mysore Land Acquisition Act, 1894

Summary: City of Bangalore Improvement Act, 1945, ss. 14, 15, 16, 17, 18 and 27 - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 4, 6 and 23 - CPC, 1908, O. 41, r. 27 - (A) Land acquisition case - (1) Award of compensation - Market value - Date of - (2) Meaninf of words 'so far as they are applicable' - (B) Appeal to High Court - Questions relating to market value of particular pieces of land - Judgments not inter partes would be relevant if they relate to similarly situated properties and contain determinations of value on dates fairly proximate to the relevant date - HC admitting such evidence without recording reasons - (A) Date is to be regulated by s.23 of the Land Acquisition Act - Words do not exclude application of general provisions of Land Acquisition Act - (B) Judgment and order of HC set aside by Supreme Court and case remanded for deciding the case afresh - Appeal Allowed.
