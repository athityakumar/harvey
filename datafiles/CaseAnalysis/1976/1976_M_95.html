State Of Gujarat v Musamiyan Imam Haider Bux Razvi And Anr. Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

14 April 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

Jaswant Singh, A.N. Ray, M. Hameedullah Beg

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 141; (1976) 3 SCC 536; AIR 1977 SC 594; 1977 (18) GLR 405; [1976] Supp S.C.R. 28

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 4 and 6 - Public purpose - Govt. issuing notification for acquisation of land for Co-operative Housing Society - Whether acquisition is a public purpose and whether Govt. can cancel the notification issued by it u/s.6 of t he Act and issue a fresh notification dated u/s.4 of the Act - Held,Housing scheme for a limited number of persons is public purpose and Govt. can issue fresh notification in its place without a fresh notification u/s. 4 of the Act - Appeal allowed.
