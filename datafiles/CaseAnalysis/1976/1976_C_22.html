Shantilal Thakordas and Others v Chimanlal Maganlal Telwala

Supreme Court of India

23 August 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

N.L. Untwalia, A.N. Ray, P.N. Shingal

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 175; (1976) 4 SCC 417; AIR 1976 SC 2358; 1976 RCJ 811; 1976 RCR(Rent) 829; 1977 (1) RentLR 4; [1977] 1 S.C.R. 341; 1976 UJ 780

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Bombay Rents, Hotel and Lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947, s. 13(1)(g) - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958 - Tenancy - Landlord a partner in a firm - Bonafide requirement, for firm - Death of landlord - New firm including outsiders constituted - Effect on Ground of requirement - Held, it endures to his family members but not to new firm - Appeal dismissed.
