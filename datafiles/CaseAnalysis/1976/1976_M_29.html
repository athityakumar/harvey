Union Of India Etc. v Majji Jangamayya Etc.(With Connected Civil Appeals)

Supreme Court of India

05 November 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, M. Hameedullah Beg, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 219; (1977) SCC (L&S) 191; (1977) 1 SCC 606; AIR 1977 SC 757; 1977 LabIC 295; [1977] 2 S.C.R. 28; 1977 SLJ 90; 1977 (1) SLR 614

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Defence & Security Forces; Labour & Industrial Law; Land & Property

Keywords: Attorney-General

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950 - Government of India Act, 1935 - Promotion - Validity - Whether selection list for promotion of Income Tax Officers Class Service to post of Asst. Commr. of Income Tax is correct or not? - Held, seniority list which is basis for field of choice for promotion to post of Assistant Commr. was approved by Court on 16-04-1974 and promotions to post of Assistant Commr. is on basis of selection list prepared by Committee and are to be made prospectively and not retrospectively - Appeal allowed.
