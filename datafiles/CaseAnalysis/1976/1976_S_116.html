R.R. Dalavai v State Of Tamil Nadu

Supreme Court of India

07 May 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Hameedullah Beg, A.N. Ray, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 225; (1976) 3 SCC 748; AIR 1976 SC 1559; 1976 LabIC 1121; 1977 (1) MLJ(SC) 40; [1976] Supp S.C.R. 601; 1976 UJ 589

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution - Mandamus - Violation of art. 351 - Pension Scheme by Tamil Nadu Govt. - Granting pension to Anti Hindi agitators - Authorised by executive act rather than legislative sanction - Held, the scheme contained vice of disintegration and fomenting fissiparous tendencies and also was without legislative sanction and violative of art. 351 - Mandamus will go directing the State of Tamil Nadu to forbear from implementing the pension scheme - Appeal Allowed.
