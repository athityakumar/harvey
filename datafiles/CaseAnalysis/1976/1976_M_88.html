Farid Ahmed Abdul Samad and Another v Municipal Corporation of The City of Ahmedabad and Another

Supreme Court of India

29 July 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

P.K. Goswami, Y.V. Chandrachud, P.N. Shingal

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 233; (1976) 3 SCC 719; AIR 1976 SC 2095; 1977 (18) GLR 100; [1977] 1 S.C.R. 71

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments; Service

Summary: Land & Property - Bombay Provincial Municipal Corporation Act, 1949, ss. 284 J, 284 N, chap. B, chap. C, App I - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 5A - Provisions of Land Acquisition Act are part of Bombay Act - Effect of denial of personal hearing on compulsory acquisition - Order of acquisition is invalid from its inception - Appeal allowed.
