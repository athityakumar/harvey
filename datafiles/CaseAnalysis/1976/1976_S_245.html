V. Guruviah Naidu and Sons and Others v State of Tamil Nadu and Others

Supreme Court of India

02 November 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

Hans Raj Khanna, V.R. Krishna Iyer

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 598; (1977) 1 SCC 234; AIR 1977 SC 548; 1977 (6) CTR 14; 1978 (1) MLJ(SC) 1; [1977] 1 S.C.R. 1065; 1976 (38) STC 565; 1976 UJ 994

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: T.N. General Sales Tax Act, 1959 - Constitution, arts. 245, 246 and 304(a) - Constitutional validity of sch. 2, items 7(a) and 7(b) of 1959 Act - Held, item 7(a) does not cover inter-State sales and item 7(b) not violative of art. 304 (a) of Constitution - Appeal Dismissed.
