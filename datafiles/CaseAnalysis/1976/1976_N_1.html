Reserve Bank Of India v N. C. Paliwal & Others

Supreme Court of India

24 August 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, Hans Raj Khanna, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 179; (1976) 4 SCC 838; (1977) SCC (L&S) 82; AIR 1976 SC 2345; 1976 LabIC 1483; [1977] 1 S.C.R. 377; 1976 SLJ 569; 1976 (2) SLR 774

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Constitution, arts.14 and 16 - Introduction of Combined Seniority Scheme RBI - Constitutional validity of Circular, D/- 13-05-1972 issued for it - Held, it did not discriminate between transferee Grade II Clerks and original Grade II Clerks - And arts. 14 and 16 cannot stand in the way of the State (RBI) in integrating different Cadres into one Cadre - Appeal Allowed.
