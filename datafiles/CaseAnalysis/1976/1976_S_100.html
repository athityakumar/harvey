Travancore Tea Estates Co. Ltd. v State Of Kerala

Supreme Court of India

11 October 1976

Case Analysis

Bench

Hans Raj Khanna, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1976 Indlaw SC 270; (1976) 4 SCC 470; (1977) SCC (Tax) 33; AIR 1976 SC 2469; 1977 CTR 43; [1977] 1 S.C.R. 755; 1977 (39) STC 1; 1976 TAXLR 2083; 1976 UJ 860

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Keywords: Registration Certificate, Processing, Central Sales Tax (Registration and Turnover) Rules 1957

Summary: Central Sales Tax Act, 1956, s. 8(3)(b) - Central Sales Tax (Registration and Turnover) Rules, 1957, r. 13 - Company - Tea plantation and manufacturing - Fertilisers etc. for use in tea cultivation - If includible in registration certificate - Held, company is not entitled to get fertilisers and other goods mentioned above included in the registration certificate - Appeal Dismissed
