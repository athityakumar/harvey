Om Prakash Sharma and Others v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

11 April 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 488; (1985) Supp SCC 218; (1985) SCC (L&S) 854; AIR 1985 SC 1276; 1985 (2) CCC 716; 1985 LabIC 1498; 1985 (2) LLN 842; 1985(1) SCALE 1006; 1985 (3) SLR 569; 1986 (1) UJ 95

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution, arts. 14, 16 and 311 - Seniority - Repatriation of amalgamated units of deptt. under the Control of Divisional Electrical Engineer - Reamalgamation of deptts. - Question arise as to consideration of earlier seniority in service - Held, to be maintained - As respondents enjoyed accelerated promotion on amalgamation but when repatriated and re-amalgamated with original two offices and brought back on the common seniority list, they must find their original place qua the appellants - Mandamus issued directing the Railway Administration to assign seniority to the appellants - Appeal allowed.
