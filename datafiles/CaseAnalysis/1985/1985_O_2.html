Sant Raj and Others v O. P. Singla and Others

Supreme Court of India

09 April 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 317; (1985) 2 SCC 349; (1985) SCC (L&S) 435; AIR 1985 SC 617; 1985 (2) CompLJ 158; 1985 (46) CTR 166; 1985 (28) DLT 145; 1985 (50) FLR 452; [1987] 163 ITR 588; 1985 LabIC 810; 1985 (2) LLJ 19; 1985 (2) LLN 42; 1985(1) SCALE 668; [1985] 3 S.C.R. 623; 1985 (2) SLJ 75; 1985 UJ 1064

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 25 F - Service - Retrenchment found to be invalid - Reinstatement denied - Ground, bonafide termination and in accordance with service rules - Apparent error in it - Held, workman is entitled to reinstatement, but in his interest, compensation in lieu of reinstatement granted - Appeal partly allowed.
