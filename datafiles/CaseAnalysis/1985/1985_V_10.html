Commissioner Of Wealth Tax Orissa, Bhubaneshwar. v Vysyaraju Badreenarayana Moorthy Raju, Berhampur (Ganjam)-

Supreme Court of India

13 March 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, A.P. Sen, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 309; (1985) 2 SCC 303; AIR 1985 SC 1603; 1985 (45) CTR 217; [1985] 152 ITR 454; 1985(1) SCALE 451; [1985] 3 S.C.R. 306; 1985 TAXLR 1052; [1985] 21 TAXMAN 5; 1985 UJ 772

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Wealth Tax Act, 1957, ss.2(m), 2(q), 3 and 7 - References u/s.27(1) by Income-tax Appellate Tribunal - Whether, on the facts and in the circumstances of the case, the Wealth-tax Officer was in law justified in including in the net wealth of the assessee interest due on accrual basis (though not realised) on the outstandings of the money-lending business, the accounts of the assessee being maintained on cash basis - Held, question is answered in the affirmative; interest will nonetheless be included in net wealth of assessee - Answered in favour of Revenue
