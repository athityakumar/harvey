Case Analysis

Bench

Where Reported

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges

Summary: Advocates and Judges - Constitution, arts. 309 Proviso, 234 - Advocates Act, 1961 - Delhi Higher Judicial Service Rules, 1970, rr.13,14,15,16,17,18, App. cl.(6) - Petition challenged the validity of the proceedings relating to the competitive examination held by the High Court of Delhi for the purpose of recruiting candidates for filling the posts in the Delhi Judicial Service in the year 1984 and of the final list of selected candidates at that examination - Addition of names in qualifing list - Legality of exclusion of certain candidates from list prepared u/r. 18 - Held not legal - Petition allowed.
