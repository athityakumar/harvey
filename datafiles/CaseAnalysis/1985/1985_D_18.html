State Of Gujarat v Dhrangadhra Chemical Works Ltd.

Supreme Court of India

09 April 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

V. Balakrishna Eradi, D.A. Desai, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 255; (1985) Supp SCC 1; AIR 1985 SC 609; 1986 (1) ARBLR 99; 1985 (2) CCC 330; 1985 (2) CompLJ 254; 1985(1) SCALE 639; [1985] 3 S.C.R. 630; 1985 UJ 897

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure; Constitution; Corporate

Summary: Contract Act, 1872, s.10 - Contract - Liability to pay royalty on manufacture of salt - When manufacturer failed to manufacture the minimum specified quantity under the agreement - On construction of agreement held, royalty waspayable on stipulated minimum quantity - Appeal allowed.
