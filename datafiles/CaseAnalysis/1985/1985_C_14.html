Arkal Govind Raj Rao v Ciba Geigy Of India Ltd., Bombay

Supreme Court of India

06 May 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, V. Balakrishna Eradi, V. Khalid

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 100; (1985) 3 SCC 371; (1985) SCC (L&S) 808; AIR 1985 SC 985; 1985 (2) Bom.C.R. 346; 1986 (52) FLR 19; 1985 LabIC 1008; 1985 (2) LLJ 401; 1985 (2) LLN 270; 1985(1) SCALE 927; [1985] Supp1 S.C.R. 282; 1986 (1) UJ 386

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 2(s) - Uttar Pradesh Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - Reference of question of the validity and legality of the order of termination of service - Award of the Labour Court declaring the appellant not to be a workman - Test for determining who is 'Workman' - Held, primary and basic duties constitute the criterion and not incidental duties - Appeal allowed.
