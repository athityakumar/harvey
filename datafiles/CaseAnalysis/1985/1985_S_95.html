State Of Maharashtra v Narayan Rao Sham Rao Deshmukh And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

19 March 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, R.B. Misra, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 311; (1985) 2 SCC 321; AIR 1985 SC 716; 1986 (1) CCC 336; 1985 (46) CTR 349; [1987] 163 ITR 31; 1985(1) SCALE 601; [1985] 3 S.C.R. 358; [1985] 22 TAXMAN 38; 1985 UJ 856

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Family & Personal; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Women & Children

Summary: Land & Property - Maharashtra Agricultural Lands (Ceiling on Holdings) Act, 1961, ss. 2(11)(20), 3, 4 and 6 - Hindu Succession Act, 1956, s. 6 - Joint Hindu family consisting of Karta, son, wife and mother - Karta dies - Surviving members continuing to remain joint - Members - Ceiling limit - Held, entitled to as one ceiling unit only - Appeal allowed.
