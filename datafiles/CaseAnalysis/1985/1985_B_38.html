Somatya Organics (India) v Board of Revenue, U.P.

Supreme Court of India

29 November 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 370; (1986) 1 SCC 351; AIR 1986 SC 403; 1986 ALJ 257; 1986 (1) CCC 711; 1985(2) SCALE 1107; [1985] 3 S.C.R. 786; [1985] supp3 S.C.R. 786; 1986 (1) UJ 504

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Land & Property - Indian Stamp Act, 1899, s. 24, sch. 1B, art. 23 - Stamp duty - Company - Selling lands and buildings to another Co. - Subject to equitable mortgage executed by former Co. - For obtaining deferred payment guarantee from Bank - Chargeability - Held, amount of such contingent liability under equitable mortgage would also be chargeable for stamp duty - Appeals dismissed.
