D.K Mitra Ors. v Union Of India And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

01 July 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, Y.V. Chandrachud, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 170; (1985) Supp SCC 243; (1985) SCC (L&S) 879; AIR 1985 SC 1558; 1985 (51) FLR 329; 1985 LabIC 1137; 1986 (1) LLN 40; 1985(2) SCALE 336; 1985 (2) SLJ 598; 1985 (2) SLR 791; [1985] Supp1 S.C.R. 818

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Service - Constitution, arts. 309, 311 - Railway Medical Service (District Medical Officers) Recruitment Rules, 1965, r. 4 - Determination of inter se seniority for promotion on substantive and officiating basis - Promotees on officiating basis continuing for years on posts which were not temporary - Held, promotees on both basis should be treated equally - Petition allowed.
