Manick Chandra Nandy v Debdas Nandy and Others

Supreme Court of India

20 December 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Madon, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 455; (1986) 1 SCC 512; AIR 1986 SC 446; 1986 (1) CCC 698; 1985(2) SCALE 1478; 1986 (1) UJ 209

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, ss. 115 and 96, O. 9, r. 13 - Revisional jurisdiction - Finding based on evidence in proceedings under O. 9, r. 13 - Legality of interference in revisional jurisdiction on conjectures - Held, illegal - Cost imposed - Appeal allowed.
