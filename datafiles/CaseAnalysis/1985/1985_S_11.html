Ms. Jordan Diengdeh v S.S. Chopra

Supreme Court of India

10 May 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 278; (1985) 3 SCC 62; AIR 1985 SC 935; 1985 (2) CCC 509; 1985 (2) DMC 42; 1985 (2) HLR 199; 1985(1) SCALE 952; [1985] Supp1 S.C.R. 704; 1986 (1) UJ 247

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Family & Personal; Women & Children; Contract & Commercial

Keywords: Grounds For Divorce, Marriage & Divorce, Decree For Restitution, Bestiality

Summary: Indian Contract Act, 1872, Indian Divorce Act, 1869, Hindu Adoptions and Maintenance Act, 1956, Dissolution of Muslim Marriage Act, 1939, Hindu Marriage Act, 1955, Parsi Marriage and Divorce Act, 1936, Special Marriage Act, 1954 - Uniform Civil Code - Judicial separation, divorce and nullity of marriage - Held, reform of law of marriage -Introduction of mutual consent and irretrievable breakdown of marriage as grounds for divorce.
