Union of India and Others v Cibatul Limited

Supreme Court of India

27 September 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, P.N. Bhagwati, Amarendra Nath Sen

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 466; (1985) 4 SCC 535; (1986) SCC (Tax) 66; AIR 1986 SC 281; 1985 ECR 2005; 1985 (22) E.L.T. 302; 1985(2) SCALE 770; [1985] 3 S.C.R. 95; [1985] supp3 S.C.R. 95

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, ss. 4 and 2(f) - Excise Duty - Wholesale cash price - Seller manufacturing goods as per manufacturing programmer drawn up by him and buyer - Buyer's obligation to purchases such goods arises when they conform to buyer's standard - Held, seller manufactured goods on his own account and wholesale price of goods manufactured by seller was wholesale price at which it sold goods to buyer - Appeal dismissed.
