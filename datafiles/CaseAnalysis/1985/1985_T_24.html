K. Kamala Jammanniavaru (Dead) By Lrs. v The Special Land Acquisition Officer And Vice Versa.

Supreme Court of India

14 February 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 182; (1985) 1 SCC 582; AIR 1985 SC 576; 1985 (11) ALR 253; 1985 (1) ALT(SC) 383; 1985 (1) CCC 1143; 1985(1) SCALE 283; [1985] 2 S.C.R. 914; 1985 UJ 681

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property

Summary: Land and Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 23(2) - Land - Acquisition - Solatium increased to 30% - Retrospectivity of ammendment by Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, 1984 s. 30(2) - Held, limited to awards passed between 30-04-1982 to 24-09-1984 and appeals pending therefrom only - Appeal dismissed.
