V. J. Thomas And Ors. v Union Of India And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

23 April 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 157; (1985) Supp SCC 7; (1985) SCC (L&S) 516; AIR 1985 SC 1055; 1985 LabIC 424; 1986 (2) LLN 62; 1985(1) SCALE 1068; [1985] 3 S.C.R. 881; 1985 (2) SLJ 62; 1985 (2) SLR 152; 1985 UJ 1030

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Telegraph Engineering Service (Group B) Recruitment Rules 1981

Summary: Service - Telegraphic Engineering Service (Group B Post) Recruitment Rules, 1981 App.I sub-cl. (4) Note - Telegraphic Engineering Service (Class II) Recruitment Rules, 1966 - Constitution of India, 1950, art.14 - Promotion - Competitive examination - Failure of the attempt to pressurise junior engineers to boycott the examination - Appellant challenge the validity of cl. I(4) Note of 1981 Rules restricting competitive examination for batches prior to 1973 - Held, not discriminatory or unconstitutional - Classification is valid - Appeal disposed of.
