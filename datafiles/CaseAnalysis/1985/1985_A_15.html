Narcinva V. Kamat And Anr. Etc. v Alfred Antonio Doe Martins And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

25 April 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 210; (1985) SCC (Cr) 274; (1985) 2 SCC 574; 1985 (2) ACC 34; 1985 ACJ 397; AIR 1985 SC 1281; 1986 (1) Bom.C.R. 70; 1985 (2) CCC 9; [1985] 58 Comp Cas 383; 1985 CrLR(SC) 570; 1985(1) SCALE 947; [1985] 3 S.C.R. 951; 1985 UJ 1052

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation

Summary: Indian Evidence Act, 1872; Motor Vehicles Act 1939; Indian Partnership Act, 1932 - Insurance of motor vehicle - Partner driving Firm's vehicle - Construction of insurance policy - Plea by insurance company that there was breach of term of policy in that the driver of insured vehicle did not have valid licence - Onus of proof - Held, burden to prove is on company - No evidence produced by company to prove breach - Company's liability subsists.
