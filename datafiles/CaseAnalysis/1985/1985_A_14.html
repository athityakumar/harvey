Rasiklal Vaghajibhai Patel v Ahmedabad Municipal Corporation and Another

Supreme Court of India

14 January 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 445; (1985) 2 SCC 35; (1985) SCC (L&S) 392; AIR 1985 SC 504; 1985 (50) FLR 201; 1985 LabIC 729; 1985 (1) LLJ 527; 1985 (1) LLN 602; 1985(1) SCALE 101; [1985] 2 S.C.R. 556; 1985 (1) SLJ 180; 1985 (1) SLR 573; 1985 UJ 508

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 136 - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, Sch. 2, items 1, 3 and 6 - Service matter - Dismissal of employee for misconduct - (A) Shown to be guilty of suppression of material fact (in application for employment) - Would weigh with any employer in giving him employment - Petition u/art. 136 - Maintainability - (B) Alleged misconduct not falling within enumerated misconduct in service regulations or standing orders - Legality - Held, case did not merit consideration u/art. 136 - Order is illegal - Appeal dismissed.
