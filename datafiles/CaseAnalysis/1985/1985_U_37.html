Laxmi Kant Pandey v Union of India and Another

Supreme Court of India

27 September 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, R.S. Pathak, Amarendra Nath Sen

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 267; (1985) Supp SCC 701; AIR 1986 SC 272; 1986 (1) CCC 409; 1986 CRLR 52; 1986 CrLR(SC) 52; 1985(2) SCALE 849; [1985] 3 S.C.R. 71; [1985] supp3 S.C.R. 71

Case Digest

Subject: Women & Children

Summary: Women & Children - Guardians and Wards Act, 1890, ss. 7, 8, 9 and 11 - Children - Inter-country adoption - Supreme Court laid down normative and procedural safeguards laid down in certain previous Cases - Matter again come before Supreme Court - Held, normative and procedural safeguards therein, clarified and altered - Order accordingly.
