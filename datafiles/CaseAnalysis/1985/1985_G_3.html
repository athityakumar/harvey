Divisional Forest Officer and Another v G.V. Sudhakar Rao and Others

Supreme Court of India

31 October 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, D.P. Madon

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 283; (1985) 4 SCC 573; (1986) SCC (Cr) 34; AIR 1986 SC 328; 1986 (1) Crimes 313; 1986 CRLJ 357; 1986 CrLR(SC) 12; 1985(2) SCALE 897; [1985] 3 S.C.R. 680; [1985] supp3 S.C.R. 680

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Environment, Wildlife & Animal; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Forest, Confiscation proceeding

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973 - Inherent power of Court - Power to stay ( of High Court ) - Confiscation proceeding u/s. 44(2 A) of AP Forest Act, 1967 Act are different from trial before Court in light of ss. 44, 45 and 58 A of Act - Held, HC u/s. 482, CrPC, 1973 cannot stay confiscation proceedings - Appeal allowed.
