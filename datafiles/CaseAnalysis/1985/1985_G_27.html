Fomento Resorts And Hotels Ltd v Gustavo Ranato Da Cruz Pinto And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

20 February 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, E.S. Venkataramiah, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 494; (1985) 2 SCC 152; AIR 1985 SC 736; 1985 (2) Bom.C.R. 592; [1986] 59 Comp Cas 1; 1985(1) SCALE 394; [1985] 2 S.C.R. 937; 1985 UJ 729

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Land & Property

Summary: Land and Property - Market Value - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 4 - Land Acquisition (Companies) Rules, 1963, r. 4 - Land Acquisition - Whether compliance with r.4 of 1963 Rules is necessary before issuing notifications u/s. 4 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894 - High Court held that non-compliance with r.4 before the issuance of notification u/s.4(1) of the Act is bad - Held, not necessary - And it was not mandatory that before issuance of notification there should be an enquiry in compliance - Matter Remitted To High Court.
