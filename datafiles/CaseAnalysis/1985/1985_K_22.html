Jyasing Dnyanu Mhoprekar And Anr. v Krishna Babaji Patil And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

17 July 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 222; (1985) 4 SCC 162; AIR 1985 SC 1646; 1985 (2) CCC 627; 1986 (1) RCR(Rent) 14; 1985(2) SCALE 94; [1985] Supp2 S.C.R. 308; 1986 (1) UJ 402

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure; Trusts & Associations

Summary: Land and property - Bombay Paragana and Kulkarni Watans (Abolition) Act, 1950 - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 60 - Trusts Act, 1882, s. 90 - Extent of right of redemption of mortgage - Paragana Watan Inam lands - Whether by reason of the grant made in favour of the defendants the right to redeem the mortgage can be treated as having become extinguished - Held, the right end only in a manner known to law - Also when suit for redemption is filed unless he show that the right has come to an end or suit is liable to be dismissed - As 'Once a mortgage, always a mortgage' - Appeal dismissed.
