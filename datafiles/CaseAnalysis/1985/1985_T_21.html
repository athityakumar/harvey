The Dharangadhra Chemical Works v Dharangadhra Municipality Another.

Supreme Court of India

03 September 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

V.D. Tulzapurkar, V. Khalid

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 192; (1985) 4 SCC 92; AIR 1985 SC 1729; 1985(2) SCALE 669; [1985] Supp2 S.C.R. 757; 1986 (1) UJ 278

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Gujarat Municipalities Act, 1963, Bombay District Municipal Act, 1901, Bombay General Act, 1904

Summary: Indirect Taxes - Gujarat Municipalities Act, 1964, ss. 103 and 279(2 )(iv) - Saurashtra Terminal Tax and Octroi Ordinance, 1949, ss. 3 and 4 - Essential Supplies (Temporary Powers) Act, 1946 - Bombay General Clauses Act, 1904, s. 7 (b) - General Clauses Act, 1897, s. 6 - Dharangadhra Municipality Octroi Rules and Octroi Bye-laws, 1965, rr. 3, Byelaw 3 - (A) Interpretation of Statutes - Principle of implied repeal - (B) Rules framed by Govt. under Ordinance, 1949, position of r. 3 and Byelaw - (C) Validity of Rules and Bye Laws - Held, rules are impliedly repealed by Municipal Rules and Bye-laws - Rules and Bye-laws are saved u/s. 279 (2) (iv) of Act - Validity cannot be challenged on ground that corrigendum to Octroi Rules and Bye-laws issued by Gujarat Govt. virtually amounted to a modification without following procedure de novo - Appeal dismissed.
