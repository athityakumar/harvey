Samaresh Bose and Another v Amal Mitra and Another

Supreme Court of India

24 September 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

Amarendra Nath Sen, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 66; (1985) 4 SCC 289; (1985) SCC (Cr) 523; AIR 1986 SC 967; 1985 (2) Crimes 782; 1986 CRLJ 24; 1985 CrLR(SC) 524; 1985(2) SCALE 1225; [1985] 3 S.C.R. 17; [1985] supp3 S.C.R. 17

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 292 - Obscenity in books - Determination of - Responsibility to decide question of obscenity is essentially that of Court - Writer is liable for writing a vulgar literature- Novel with some exceptional slang language, emphasis on sex - Held, acquit appellants of charges against them - Novel is not obscene - Not offend s. 292 IPC -Fine, if paid refunded to them.
