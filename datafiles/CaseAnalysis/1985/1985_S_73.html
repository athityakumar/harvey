State Bank of India v Commissioner of Income Tax, Ernakulam

Supreme Court of India

31 October 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, V.D. Tulzapurkar

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 374; (1985) 4 SCC 585; AIR 1986 SC 680; 1985 (49) CTR 379; [1986] 157 ITR 67; 1985(2) SCALE 921; [1985] 3 S.C.R. 694; [1985] supp3 S.C.R. 694; 1986 (1) UJ 47

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 5 and 28 - Resident banking company take negotiable instruments in foreign currency - Appreciation in value due to devaluation of Indian rupee - Assessee unutilised amount (increase) in its business - Whether revenue receipt for pourpose of Income Tax ?- Held, appreciation is revenue receipt of assessee - Appeal dismissed.
