Hindustan Gum and Chemical Ltd. v State of Haryana and Others

Supreme Court of India

19 August 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 392; (1985) 4 SCC 124; AIR 1985 SC 1683; 1986 (1) CCC 774; 1985(2) SCALE 361; [1985] Supp2 S.C.R. 630

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Indirect Tax; Municipalities & Local Governments; Service

Keywords: Bombay District Municipal Act, 1901

Summary: Indirect Taxes - Punjab Municipal Act, 1911, s. 5(4) - Constitution, arts. 245 and 265 - Punjab Municipal (Haryana Amendment and Validation) Act, 1971, s. 4 - Levy of octroi in an area which was included in municipality in Haryana (Another State), subsequent to issue of notification - (A) Validity of imposition with retrospective effect from date of inclusion - (B) Legislation - Competency to overcome Court decision - Overcoming effect of decision setting aside imposition of tax with retrospective effect by amending relevant provisions - Permissibility - Held, valid - Permissible - Appeal dismissed.
