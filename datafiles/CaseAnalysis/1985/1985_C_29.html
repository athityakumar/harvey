Scientific Engineering House Private Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax, Andhra Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

01 November 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

V.D. Tulzapurkar, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 373; (1986) 1 SCC 11; AIR 1986 SC 338; 1985 (48) CTR 227; 1985 (49) CTR 386; [1986] 157 ITR 86; 1985(2) SCALE 908; [1985] 3 S.C.R. 701; [1985] supp3 S.C.R. 701; 1986 TAXLR 234; [1985] 23 TAXMAN 66; 1986 (2) TLR 234; 1986 (1) UJ 557

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Component Parts

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 32, 34 and 43(3) - Collaboration agreement for manufacture of scientific instruments whereby collaborator to supply technical know-how for payment - Assessee received various documents by way of documentation service under agreement - Entitlement as to depreciation thereon for income tax pourose - Held, documents would constitute book and are included under term 'plant' - Assessee held entitled to claim depreciation - Appeal allowed.
