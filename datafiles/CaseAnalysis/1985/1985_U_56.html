S. P. Sampath Kumar and Others v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

31 October 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 461; (1985) 4 SCC 458; (1985) SCC (L&S) 986; 1985(2) SCALE 1077

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Constitution - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 32 and 226 - How many additional benches are proposed to be set up by Govt. at places where seats of other High Courts are situated? - Directions for setting up additional benches at seat of HC where no bench of Tribunal is located by Govt. - Basic principle of administration of justice is that Courts should come closer to people and not run far away from them - Transfer Petitions allowed.
