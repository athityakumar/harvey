Commissioner of Sales Tax, U. P v Macneill and Barry Limited

Supreme Court of India

19 November 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 14; (1986) 1 SCC 23; AIR 1986 SC 386; 1986 ALJ 252; 1986 (1) CCC 254; 1986 (7) ECR 266; 1986 (23) E.L.T. 5; 1985(2) SCALE 1093; [1985] 3 S.C.R. 739; 1986 (61) STC 76; [1985] supp3 S.C.R. 739; 1986 TAXLR 2249; 1986 (1) UJ 142

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Sales Tax

Keywords: U.P. Sales Tax Act, 1948

Summary: Sales Tax - Uttar Pradesh Sales Tax Act, 1948, s. 3 - Notification No. ST 3124/X-1012(4)-1965 D/- 01-07-1966 - Taxability of ammonia paper and ferro paper which are not covered under entry 'Paper other than hand made paper' - Held, liable to be taxed as unclassified goods - Appeal dismissed.
