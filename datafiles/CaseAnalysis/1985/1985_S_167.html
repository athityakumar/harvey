State of Tamil Nadu v Ramalinga Samigal Madam and Others

Supreme Court of India

01 May 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

V.D. Tulzapurkar, V. Khalid

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 415; (1985) 4 SCC 10; AIR 1986 SC 794; 1985 (2) CCC 200; 1987 (1) MLJ(SC) 10; 1985(1) SCALE 1138; [1985] Supp1 S.C.R. 63; 1986 (1) UJ 489

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Land & Property

Keywords: Zamindar, Andhra Pradesh (Andhra Area) Estate (Abolition and Conversion into Ryotwari) Act, 1948

Summary: Land & Property - Tamil Nadu Revenue Recovery Act, 1864 - Andhra Pradesh (Andhra Area) Estate (Abolition and Conversion into Ryotwari) Act, 1948 - Tamil Nadu Estates (Abolition and Conversion into Ryotwari) Act 1948, ss. 11 and 64 C - Nature of land - Jurisdiction of Civil Court - Whether a Civil Court's jurisdiction to determine nature of land in respect whereof a Ryot has sought a Ryotwari Patta u/s. 11 of Act is ousted or barred u/s. 64-C of that Act? - Held, an applicant who has applied for a ryotwari patta u/s. 11 staking his claim on basis of his long and uninterrupted possession of ryoti land but Settlement Officer on materials before him is not satisfied that land in question is ryoti land, in that situation he will refuse patta to applicant - Settlement Officer cannot, for it lies within his power and jurisdiction merely to grant or refuse to grant patta on basis of materials placed before him - But such a person even after refusal of ryotwari patta would be entitled to protect his possessory title and long enjoyment of land and seek an injunction preventing Govt.'s interference otherwise than in due course of law and surely before granting such relief Civil Court may have to adjudicate upon real nature of character of land if same is put in issue - In other words since Settlement Officer has no power to do what Civil Court would normally do in a suit it is difficult to imply ouster of Civil Court's jurisdiction simply because finality has been accorded to Settlement Officer's order u/s. 64- C of Act - Confirm the HC view that Civil Court's jurisdiction to adjudicate on real nature of land is not ousted u/s. 64-C by reason of Settlement Officer's decision to grant or refuse to grant a patta u/s. 11 r/w proviso to s. 3(d) of Act - Appeal dismissed.
