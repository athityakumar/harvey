Calcutta Dock Labour Board And Anr. v Smt. Sandhya Mitra And Ors

Supreme Court of India

11 February 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, P.N. Bhagwati

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 284; (1985) 2 SCC 1; (1985) SCC (L&S) 383; AIR 1985 SC 996; 1985 (50) FLR 322; 1985 LabIC 714; 1993 (3) LLJ 412; 1985 (1) LLN 767; 1985(1) SCALE 213; [1985] 2 S.C.R. 826; 1985 (1) SLJ 189; 1985 (1) SLR 606; 1985 UJ 571

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Payment of Gratuity Act, 1972, ss. 4, 5, 13 and 14 - Gratuity payable - Whether gratuity payable to a workman employed under the Calcutta Dock Labour Board is attachable for satisfaction of a decree of the Court - Held, gratuity which was payable to him squarely came within the purview of the Act and, therefore, become entitled to immunity u/s. 13 thereof.not liable to attachment - Appeal allowed.
