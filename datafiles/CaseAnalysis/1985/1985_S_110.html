K. Nagaraj and Others Etc v State of Andhra Pradesh and Another Etc

Supreme Court of India

18 January 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, R.S. Pathak, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 444; (1985) SCC (L&S) 280; (1985) 1 SCC 523; AIR 1985 SC 551; 1985 (51) FLR 166; 1985 LabIC 746; 1985 (1) LLJ 444; 1985 (2) LLN 1; 1985(1) SCALE 31; [1985] 2 S.C.R. 579; 1985 (1) SLJ 277; 1985 (2) SLR 337

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Service - Andhra Pradesh Public Employment (Regulation of Conditions of Service) Ordinance, 1983, cl. 10 - Constitution, arts. 13, 14, 16, 311, 32, 213, 245, 300A and 309 Proviso - Fundamental Rules, r. 2 Proviso - Service Rules - Civil Services - (A) Retirement - Constitutional validity of - (1) Reducing retirement age from 58 to 55 - (2) Termination of employee on reaching age of superannuation - (3) Ordinance of 1983 , on ground of non-application of mind - (4) Rules of retirement, if take away right of livelihood - (B) Burden of proof in case of mala fides - Legislature cannot pass a law for an extraneous purpose - (C) Validity of deletion of Proviso to r. 2 of Fundamental Rules with retrospective effect from 23-2-1979 - (A) Held, neither arbitrary nor irrational - Does not amount to his removal from service within art.311(2) - Ordinance not invalid - Do not take away - (B) Transferred malice' is unknown in field of legislation - (C) Deletion is valid - Petitions dismissed.
