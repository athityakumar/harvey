Ganpat Roy And Others Etc. v The Additional District Magistrate And Others Etc.

Supreme Court of India

19 March 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Madon, V.D. Tulzapurkar, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 198; (1985) 2 SCC 307; AIR 1985 SC 1635; 1985 ALJ 900; 1985 (11) ALR 423; 1985 (2) CCC 825; 1985(1) SCALE 545; [1985] 3 S.C.R. 384

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Land and Property - Constitution of India, 1950, art.226 - U.P. Urban Buildings (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972, ss. 12(4), 16(1), Proviso and 18 - U.P. Urban Buildings (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Rules, 1972, r. 8 - Deemed vacancy - (A) District Magistrate immediately on receipt of report of the Rent Control Inspector directed the vacancy to be notified without posting the conclusion reached by the Inspector contained in his report on the Notice Board - Whether the act of the District Magistrate was proper - Held, action is violative of r. 8 and the correctness of correctness of Trilok Singh & Co.'s case (1976 INDLAW SC 134) is, therefore, open to doubt - (B) Determination of scope of revision u/s.18 - Held, is much narrower than in the case of an appeal - Appeal allowed.
