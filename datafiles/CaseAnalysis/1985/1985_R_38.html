Phool Chand Gupta v Regional Tansport Authority, Ujjain and Others

Supreme Court of India

23 August 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 253; (1985) 4 SCC 190; 1985 (2) ACC 540; AIR 1986 SC 119; 1985(2) SCALE 334; [1985] Supp2 S.C.R. 682; 1985 UJ 902

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Constitution; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Carriers & Transportation - Motor Vehicles Act, 1939, ss. 68C and 68D - Unreasonable delay in publication of approved scheme regarding motor vehicle - Effect - Held, draft scheme quashed - Further held, that order does not prevent the State Transport Undertaking from taking fresh steps for publishing a scheme u/s. 68C if it thinks that it is necessary to do so - Petition allowed.
