State Of Orissa v Sridhar Kumar Malik And Ors. Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

31 July 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, Amarendra Nath Sen

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 225; (1985) 3 SCC 697; AIR 1985 SC 1411; 1985(2) SCALE 317; [1985] Supp2 S.C.R. 349; 1985 UJ 830

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments; Service

Keywords: Orissa Municipal Rules 1953

Summary: Municipalities and Local Governments - Orissa Municipal Act, 1950 - Notification u/s. 417-A (1) for constituting certain area as notified area - Proclamation u/s. 417A (1a) - Inviting objections, vague - Validity of issued in English, rather than local language - Held, notification u/s. 417-A (1), not valid - Appeal dismissed.
