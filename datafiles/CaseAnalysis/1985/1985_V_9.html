State of Madhya Pradesh v Vyankatlal and Another

Supreme Court of India

28 March 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

R.B. Misra, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 428; (1985) SCC (Tax) 337; (1985) 2 SCC 544; AIR 1985 SC 901; 1985 (2) CCC 251; 1985 CRLR 224; 1985(1) SCALE 609; [1985] 3 S.C.R. 561; 1987 (64) STC 6

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Sales Tax

Keywords: Collected

Summary: Sales Tax - Madhya Bharat Essential Supplies (Temporary Powers) Act, 1948, s. 4 - Madhya Bharat Sugar Control Order, 1949, cl. 1 - Notification No. 7 CS 15/50 D/-14-01-1950 - Orissa Sales Tax Act, 1947 -Constitution of India, 1950, arts.14 and 265 - Imposition of levy styled sugar fund on sugar factories - Suit for refund of amount of Rs. 50000 paid towards difference between the supply price and the ex-factory price, which was to be credited of Madhya Bharat Government Sugar Fund - Issue arise as to: (A) Validity/ legality of levy - (B) Claim for refund by sugar factories - Permissibility - Held, it is illegal and discriminatory - Held, claim not permissible as it would amount to unjust enrichment - Appeal allowed
