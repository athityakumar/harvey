Ibrahim Bachu Bafan And Anr Etc v State Of Gujarat And Ors

Supreme Court of India

12 February 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, R.B. Misra, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, A. Varadarajan

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 293; (1985) 2 SCC 24; (1985) SCC (Cr) 149; AIR 1985 SC 697; 1985 (87) BomLR 264; 1985 (1) Crimes 602; 1985 CRLJ 533; 1985 CrLR(SC) 232; 1985(1) SCALE 257; [1985] 2 S.C.R. 891

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - COFEPOSA Act, ss. 11(1), 11(2) and 3 - Constitution, art. 226 - Preventive detention - Detention order quashed u/art. 226 - Fresh order u/s.11(2) - Can be passed only when previous order is revoked u/s. 11(1) - Legality of order - Held, fresh order u/s.11 (2) is illegal - Petition allowed.
