B.H.E.L. Workers' Association Hardwar and Ors v Union Of India And Ors., Etc. Etc

Supreme Court of India

18 January 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, V. Khalid

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 174; (1985) SCC (L&S) 371; (1985) 1 SCC 630; AIR 1985 SC 409; 1985 (50) FLR 205; 1986 LabIC 396; 1985 (1) LLJ 428; 1985 (1) LLN 596; 1985(1) SCALE 58; [1985] 2 S.C.R. 611; 1985 (1) SLJ 331; 1985 UJ 660

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Public Sector, Equal Remuneration Act, 1976, Hours Of Work, Contract Labour (Regulation And Abolition) Central Rules, 1971, Contract, Labour (Regulation And Abolition) Act, 1970, Contract Labour Central Rules 1971

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Contract Labour (Regulation and Abolition) Act, 1970, s.10 - Contract Labour (Regulation and Abolition) Central Rules, 1971, r.25(ii)(v)(a), Proviso - Abolition of contract labour system - Whether court is empowered to direct abolition of contract labour u/s. 10 of the 1970 Act or Rules of 1971 - Central Govt. rather than Court is the appropriate authority for it - Petition dismissed.
