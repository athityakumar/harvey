Bhag Singh And Ors. v Union Territory Of Chandigarh, Through The Land Acquisitionc

Supreme Court of India

14 August 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, Amarendra Nath Sen, D.P. Madon

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 246; (1985) 3 SCC 737; AIR 1985 SC 1576; 1985 (11) ALR 692; 1986 (1) CCC 91; 1985 (28) DLT 366; 1985(2) SCALE 246; [1985] Supp2 S.C.R. 949; 1985 UJ 910

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property

Summary: Land and Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 23, 31, 23(2), 28 and 30(2) - Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, 1984, s.30(2) - Compensation for land acquisition - (A) Legality of denial of enhancement of compensation to the appellants on account of non-payment of deficit court fee due - Held, not proper, claimants should have been allowed to pay up deficit court-fee - (B) Question of law relating to the interpretation of s.30(2) of the Amending Act - For determining applicability of amended ss. 23(2), 28 - Held, amended provisions of ss. 23 (2) and 28 are applicable to all proceedings relating to compensation pending at the date of commencement of amending Act or filed subsequent to that date whether before Collector or before Court or HC or Supreme Court - Appeal allowed.
