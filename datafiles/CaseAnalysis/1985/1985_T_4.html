Shenoy and Company v Commercial Tax Officer, Bangalore, and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 April 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

V. Khalid, D.A. Desai, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 337; (1985) 2 SCC 512; AIR 1985 SC 621; 1985 (2) CCC 219; 1985 (21) E.L.T. 14; [1985] 155 ITR 178; 1985(1) SCALE 779; [1985] 3 S.C.R. 659; 1985 (60) STC 70; 1986 (1) UJ 112

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax

Summary: Indirect Tax - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 141 and 226 - Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods into Local Areas for Consumption, Use or Sale therein Act, 1979 - Constitutional validity challenged - High Court holding Act as invalid - Appeal filed by Govt. in Supreme Court - Held, validity of Act upheld - Supreme Court's decision is binding on all persons though they were not parties to appeal - Appeals dismissed.
