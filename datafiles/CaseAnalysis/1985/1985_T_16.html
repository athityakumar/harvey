Messrs Sahney Steel and Pressworks Limited and v The Commercial Tax Officer And Others

Supreme Court of India

10 September 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, Amarendra Nath Sen

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 387; (1985) 4 SCC 173; AIR 1985 SC 1754; 1986 (1) CCC 782; 1985 (3) CompLJ 239; 1985(2) SCALE 789; 1985 (60) STC 301; [1985] Supp2 S.C.R. 780

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Corporate

Summary: Sales Tax - Central Sales Tax Act , 1956, ss. 3(a), 6 and 8 - Inter-State sales - Manufacturing company (assessee) based in Hyderabad - Goods sent to its branch offices outside State as per order procured by them - Whether the transaction is liable to CST as an interstate sale - Held, it was inter-State sales and as much liable to CST - Petition dismissed.
