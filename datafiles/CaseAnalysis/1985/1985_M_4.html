M. P. Irrigation Karamchari Sangh v The State Of M. P. And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

27 February 1985

Case Analysis

Bench

V. Khalid, D.A. Desai, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1985 Indlaw SC 188; (1985) 2 SCC 103; (1985) SCC (L&S) 409; AIR 1985 SC 860; 1985 (50) FLR 422; 1985 (1) LLJ 519; 1985 (1) LLN 781; 1985(1) SCALE 322; [1985] 2 S.C.R. 1019; 1985 (1) SLJ 666; 1985 (1) SLR 611; 1985 UJ 698

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Labour and Industrial Laws - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 10, 12(5) - Scope of State government's power of reference of industrial dispute to tribunal - State govt. refused to refer questions as to equal entitlement of Dearness Allowance and chambal allowance - As unable to bear additional burden of DA and Chambal allowance which was already included in consolidated pay given to employees - Deciding questions on their own - Held, exceeded jurisdiction by deciding question unilaterally - Appeal allowed
