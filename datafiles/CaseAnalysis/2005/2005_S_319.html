Sri Jayendra Saraswathy Swamigal,Tamil Nadu v State Of Tamil Nadu And Others

Supreme Court of India

26 October 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

G.P. Mathur, R.C. Lahoti

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 686; (2005) 8 SCC 771; 2005 CRLJ 4626; 2005 (124) DLT 645; JT 2005 (9) SC 69; 2005(8) SCALE 640; [2005] Supp4 S.C.R. 556

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Practice & Procedure - Criminal - CrPC, 1973, ss. 102, 164, 199(2), 277, 303, 321, 406 - Tamil Nadu Prevention of Dangerous Activities of Bootleggers, Drug Offenders, Forest Offenders, Goondas, Immoral Traffic Offenders, Slum Grabbers and Video Pirates Act, 1982 - IPC, 1860, ss. 109, 201, 213E, 451, 500, 506(2) - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 19, 21, 22(1), 26 - Transfer petition - Petition filed by Jayendra Saraswathy Swamigal, Sankaracharya of Kanchi Kamakoti Peetam seeking transfer of Case pending before Principal Sessions Court, Chenglepet, to any other State, out side Tamil Nadu - Held, a situation has arisen in case wherein lawyers engaged by petitioner and other co-accused cannot perform their professional duty in a proper and dignified manner on account of various hurdles created by State - Petitioner and other co-accused of case have a reasonable apprehension that they will not get justice in Tamil Nadu - Case transferred to Pondicherry - Petition allowed.
