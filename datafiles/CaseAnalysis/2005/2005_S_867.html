Suresh Seth v Commissioner, Indore Municipal Corporation and others

Supreme Court of India

06 October 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

G.P. Mathur, P.K. Balasubramanyan, R.C. Lahoti

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 618; (2005) 13 SCC 287; AIR 2006 SC 767; JT 2005 (9) SC 210; 2005 (4) RLW 2921; 2005(8) SCALE 514

Case Digest

Subject: Election; Parliament & Legislature; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Parliament & Legislature - Madhya Pradesh Municipal Corporation Act, 1956, s. 441-F - Election petition challenging election of Mayor on ground that he being a sitting member of Legislative Assembly, was disqualified for holding Office of Mayor of a corporation - Petition and revision dismissed - Appealed herein - Held, this is a matter of policy for elected representatives of people to decide and no direction in this regard can be issued by court - Court cannot issue any direction to Legislature to make any particular kind of enactment - Appeal dismissed.
