Balkar Singh v Jagdish Kumar and Others

Supreme Court of India

22 February 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, P.K. Balasubramanyan

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 117; (2005) 3 SCC 186; (2005) SCC (Cr) 689; AIR 2005 SC 1567; 2005 CRLJ 1712; JT 2005 (2) SC 556; 2005 (2) RCR(Criminal) 50; 2005(2) SCALE 257

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Criminal - CrPC, 1973, s. 482 - Quashing of FIR - Whether HC right in quashing the FIR and giving directions to police and Magistrate not to prosecute respective petitioners on basis of the said FIR? - Held, accepting a statement made by counsel for the State, HC could not have quashed petition solely on that ground - Impugned order set aside - Appeals allowed.
