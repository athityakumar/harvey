The Tata Iron and Steel Company Limited Bihar v The Collector of Central Excise, Patna

Supreme Court of India

02 March 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 172; (2005) 3 SCC 528; 2005 (181) E.L.T. 311; JT 2005 (3) SC 115; 2005(2) SCALE 513; [2005] 2 S.C.R. 457

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Indirect Tax; Trade

Summary: Excise - Central Excise Act, 1944 - Duty - Whether benefit of Notification No 281/86 is available to parts of loco wagon and rolling stock manufactured by assessee-appellant? - Held, crucial question is whether Department can proceed against consignee for not following procedure under Chapter-X if there was any violation of terms and conditions of Central Excise License No 6 issued in favour of Main Plant - This crucial issue has not been considered in proper perspective by CEGAT - Matter remanded to CEGAT for fresh adjudication keeping in view legal position - Appeals Disposed Of.
