Star India Private Limited v Commissioner of Central Excise, Mumbai and Goa

Supreme Court of India

30 March 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Ruma Pal, Arijit Pasayat, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 857; (2005) 7 SCC 203; 2006 (1) S.T.R. 73

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Service Tax

Summary: Service Tax - Companies Act, 1956 - Finance Act, 2001 - Finance Act, 1994 - Finance Act, 2002 - Whether appellant is liable to make payment of service tax? - Held, it is clear from language of validation clause,as that liability was extended not by way of clarification but by way of amendment to Finance Act with retrospective effect - It is well established that while it is permissible for legislature to retrospectively legislate, such retrospectivity is normally not permissible to create an offence retrospectively - Explanation to Validation Section which says that no act or acts on part of any person shall be punishable as an offence which would have been so punishable if Section had not come into force - Appeal allowed.
