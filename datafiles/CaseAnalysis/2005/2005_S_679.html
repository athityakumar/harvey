State of Rajasthan v Gurmail Singh

Supreme Court of India

23 February 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Singh, Arun Kumar

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 130; (2005) 3 SCC 59; (2005) SCC (Cr) 641; AIR 2005 SC 1578; 2005 CRLJ 1746; JT 2005 (2) SC 574; 2005 (2) RCR(Criminal) 58; 2005(2) SCALE 394

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Health & Drug

Summary: Criminal - NDPS Act, 1985, ss. 8 r/w 15 - Respondent was convicted by Special Judge but was acquitted by HC - Appeal against acquittal - Held, there is no evidence to prove satisfactorily that seals found were in fact the same seals as were put on sample bottles immediately after seizure of the contraband - Link evidence adduced by prosecution was not at all satisfactory - No error in acquittal -Appeal dismissed.
