Rekha Mukherjee v Ashis Kumar Das and Others

Supreme Court of India

03 March 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, N. Santosh Hegde

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 158; (2005) 3 SCC 427; AIR 2005 SC 1944; 2005 (59) ALR 284; 2005 (3) AWC 2920; 2005 (2) JhrCR(SC) 319; JT 2005 (3) SC 68; 2005 (2) MLJ(SC) 147; 2005(2) SCALE 523; [2005] 2 S.C.R. 473; 2005 (Supp) RD 123

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Land and Property - Practice and Procedure - CPC, O.47 r.7, O.12 r.6 - Suit for specific performance - Whether HC was justified in entertaining First Appeal filed by Respondents against original judgment and decree passed in Title Suit for specific performance of contract? - Respondents taking inconsistent and contradictory stands - Held, HC was not correct in holding that First Appeal filed by Respondents was maintainable - Respondents cannot take advantage of their own mistake - Appeals allowed.
