State of Haryana v Ram Pal and Others

Supreme Court of India

07 February 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 76; (2005) 3 SCC 347; (2005) SCC (Cr) 731; AIR 2005 SC 1552; 2005 CRLJ 1718; JT 2005 (2) SC 267; 2005 (1) KLT 870; 2005(2) SCALE 12; [2005] 1 S.C.R. 1112

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Criminal - CrPC, 1973, s. 378(3) - IPC, 1860, ss. 148, 149, 302, 323, 452, 506 - Application u/s. 378(3) - Dismissal by HC - Whether HC order sustainable? - Held, HC ought to have set forth its reasons, howsoever brief, in its order, indicative of an application of its mind; all the more when its order is amenable to further avenue of challenge - Absence of reasons has rendered HC order not sustainable - Appeal allowed.
