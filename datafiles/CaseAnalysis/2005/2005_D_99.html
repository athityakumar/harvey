District Collector, Ananthapur and Another v V. Laxmanna

Supreme Court of India

17 March 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, S.B. Sinha

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 202; (2005) 3 SCC 663; (2005) SCC (Cr) 882; AIR 2005 SC 2802; 2005 CRLJ 2150; JT 2005 (3) SC 309; 2005(3) SCALE 298

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Drug offenders

Summary: Criminal - Practice and Procedure - Andhra Pradesh Prevention of Dangerous Activities of Boot Leggers, Dacoits, Drug-offenders, Goondas, Immoral Traffic Offenders and Land Grabbers Act, 1986 - Detention order - Quashed by HC - Whether HC right in quashing detention order on ground that some of instances relied upon by detaining authority being stale entire detention order becomes invalid? - Held, if detaining authority is of opinion that it is necessary to detain a person to prevent him from indulging in sale of goods dangerous for human consumption same should be based on some material and copies of such material should be given to detenu - Appeal dismissed.
