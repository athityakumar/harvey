Rajani Kishor Pardeshi v Kishor Babulal Pardeshi

Supreme Court of India

18 February 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Variava, H.K. Sema

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 1577; (2005) 12 SCC 237

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Transfer of marriage petition - Financial incapacity - Petitioner-wife sought for transfer of Hindu marriage petition pending before Court of Civil Judge, Senior Division at Panvel, Mumbai to Family Court of proper jurisdiction at Satana on ground that she did not have financial capacity to contest litigation at Mumbai - Hence instant Petition.

Held, petitioner did not have financial capacity to contest litigation at Mumbai. Petitioner’s brother with whom she is staying is also unable to accompany her to contest petition in Mumbai as he is also financially weak. In this type of matter, convenience of wife is to be preferred over convenience of husband. Thus, Hindu Marriage Petition pending before Court of Civil Judge, Senior Division at Panvel, Mumbai Maharashtra is transferred to Family Court of proper jurisdiction at Satana, Madhya Pradesh. Petition allowed.

Ratio - In case of transfer of Hindu marriage petition, same can be transferred on convenience of wife is to be preferred
