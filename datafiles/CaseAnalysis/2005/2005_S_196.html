Chairman, Madhya Pradesh Electricity Board and v Shiv Narayan and Another

Supreme Court of India

24 August 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, H.K. Sema

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 487; (2005) 7 SCC 283; 2005 (61) ALR 791; 2005 (4) AWC 3639; 2006 (1) G.L.R. 387; JT 2005 (7) SC 551; 2005 (4) KLT 485; 2005 (4) RCR(Civil) 22; 2005 (4) RLW 2480; 2005(6) SCALE 723; [2005] Supp2 S.C.R. 958

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Keywords: National Labour Relations Act, North-Eastern Hill University Act, 1973

Summary: [A.] Electricity & Energy - Advocates & Judges - Electricity Consumption - Whether legal profession is a commercial activity or is it a trade or business - Held, unless user is commercial, rate applicable to be commercial user cannot be charged merely because it is not considered to be domestic user - Views expressed in New Delhi Municipal Council v Sohan Lal Sachdev (2000(2) SCC 494) does not appear to be correct - Matter referred to larger Bench.
