Controller of Defence Accounts (Pension) and v S. Balachandran Nair

Supreme Court of India

21 October 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, AR. Lakshmanan

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 898; (2005) 13 SCC 128; (2006) SCC (L&S) 734; AIR 2005 SC 4391; 2005 (6) AWC 5916; JT 2005 (12) SC 646; 2005 (4) KLT 703; 2005(8) SCALE 517; 2006 (1) SLR 51; [2005] Supp4 S.C.R. 431

Case Digest

Subject: Defence & Security Forces; Health & Drug; Practice & Procedure; Service

Keywords: Disability Pension

Summary: [A.] Service - Regulations for Medical Service of Armed Forces, 1983 - Disability pension - Held, in view of fact that Medical Board's opinion was clearly to effect that illness suffered by respondent was not attributable to military service, both Single Judge and Division Bench were not justified in granting disability pension - Appeal allowed.
