Union of India and Others v Narender Singh

Supreme Court of India

29 July 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, H.K. Sema

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 433; (2005) 6 SCC 106; (2005) SCC (L&S) 815; 2005 (6) AWC 5512; 2005 (122) DLT 364; 2005 (106) FLR 917; JT 2005 (6) SC 450; 2005(5) SCALE 684; [2005] Supp1 S.C.R. 899

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Customs; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: [A.] Service - Dismissal from service for accepting illegal gratification for getting two Afghan nationals cleared through Customs without paying Customs duty payable - Tribunal directed to reinstate respondent - Writ challenging order of reinstatement dismissed by HC as infructuous - Appealed herein - Held, merely because order of reinstatement had been implemented by appellant, that did not render WP infructuous - Matter is remitted to HC - Appeal allowed.
