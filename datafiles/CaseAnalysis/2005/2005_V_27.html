Director of Education, Uttaranchal and Others v Ved Prakash Joshi and Others

Supreme Court of India

15 July 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 424; (2005) 6 SCC 98; (2005) SCC (L&S) 812; (2005) SCC (Cr) 1357; AIR 2005 SC 3200; 2005 (3) AWC 2964; 2005 (6) Bom.C.R. 172; 2005 CRLJ 3731; 2005 (4) ESC 16; 2005 (106) FLR 817; 2006 (2) JhrCR(SC) 174; JT 2005 (6) SC 276; 2005 (3) MahLJ 930; 2005 (3) RCR(Criminal) 743; 2005(5) SCALE 529; [2005] Supp1 S.C.R. 620; 2005 (5) Supreme 116; 2005 (3) UPLBEC 2415

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: [A.] Advocates & Judges - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 215 - Contempt of Courts Act, 1971, ss. 12, 14, 15 - U.P. Regularization of Adhoc Appointments (on posts outside purview of U.P. Public Service Commission) Rules, 1979 - Challenging directions issued by Allahabad HC in a contempt petition - Held, while dealing with an application for contempt - Court cannot traverse beyond order, non-compliance of which is alleged - It cannot test correctness or otherwise of order or give additional directions or delete any direction - Order of HC set aside - Appeal allowed.
