Punjab Tractors Limited v Commissioner of Central Excise, Chandigarh

Supreme Court of India

10 February 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Ruma Pal, Arijit Pasayat, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 225; (2005) 11 SCC 210; 2005 (181) E.L.T. 380

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excise Rules - Whether appellant-assessee was entitled to pay duty on inputs which had been declared to be duty free and then claimed Modvat credit in respect of goods manufactured out of such inputs which themselves were also exempted from duty? - Held, appellant had in fact paid duty on procured components for junior tractors and that credit was not reversed ultimately when junior tractors were cleared - Violated provisions of rr.57 C, 57 F of Rules - Penalty for availing credit were valid but demand of duty set aside - Appeals disposed off.
