Management of Messrs Sonepat Cooperative Sugar v Ajit Singh

Supreme Court of India

14 February 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, N. Santosh Hegde

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 107; (2005) 3 SCC 232; (2005) SCC (L&S) 387; AIR 2005 SC 1050; 2005 (2) AWC 1130; 2005 (2) CLR 66; 2005 (3) ESC 305; 2005 (105) FLR 1; 2005 (2) JhrCR(SC) 311; JT 2005 (2) SC 370; 2005 (2) LLN 53; 2005 (140) PLR 645; 2005(2) SCALE 151; [2005] 2 S.C.R. 105; 2005 (3) SLR 74; 2005 (2) Supreme 26; 2005 (2) UPLBEC 1138

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Air Force Act, 1950, Navy Act, 1957

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 2(s) and 25F - Post of Legal Assistant - Whether respondent could be held to be a workman within meaning of s 2(s)? - Respondent not performing any stereotype job - His job involved creativity - He would also discharge a quasi-judicial functions as an Enquiry Officer in departmental enquiries against workmen - Such a job would not make him a workman - Appeals allowed.
