Management of Madurantakam Co-Operative Sugar v S. Viswanathan

Supreme Court of India

22 February 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, P.K. Balasubramanyan

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 129; (2005) 3 SCC 193; (2005) SCC (L&S) 372; AIR 2005 SC 1954; 2005 (1) CLR 1078; 2005 (104) FLR 1229; JT 2005 (2) SC 481; 2005 (2) LLN 38; 2005(2) SCALE 274; 2005 (2) SLR 700

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Misconduct - Dismissal - Discharge - Order of reinstatement by Labour Court - Whether HC right in reversing order of Labour Court? - Held, a consideration of impugned order of Single Judge shows that nowhere he has come to conclusion that finding of Labour Court is either perverse or based on no evidence or based on evidence which is not legally acceptable - Full back wages granted by Labour Court reduced to 50% of back wages - Order accordingly.
