CREF Finance Ltd. v Shree Shanthi Homes Private Limited and Another

Supreme Court of India

23 August 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Singh, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 523; (2005) 7 SCC 467; (2005) SCC (Cr) 1697; [2005] 127 Comp Cas 311; JT 2005 (8) SC 87; 2005(7) SCALE 53; [2005] Supp2 S.C.R. 873

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Corporate; Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Dishonour of cheques

Summary: [A.] Criminal - Banking & Finance - Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881, s. 138 - Code of Criminal Procedure 1973, ss. 156(3), 482 - Dishonour of cheques - Complaint - HC remitted matter to Magistrate - HC found process was issued against respondents without taking cognizance of offence and without application of mind - Appealed herein - Held, cognizance has been taken properly after application of mind - Magistrate issued process only after considering material placed before him - Judgment and order of HC is unsustainable and must be set aside - Appeal allowed.
