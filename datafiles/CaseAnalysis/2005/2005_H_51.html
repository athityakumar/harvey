Harwindra Kumar v Chief Engineer, Karmik and Others

Supreme Court of India

18 November 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Agrawal

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 736; (2005) 13 SCC 300; (2006) SCC (L&S) 1063; AIR 2006 SC 365; 2006 (1) AWC 524; JT 2005 (10) SC 32; 2005(9) SCALE 452; 2006 (1) SLR 27; [2005] Supp5 S.C.R. 317; 2006 (1) UPLBEC 20

Case Digest

Subject: Parliament & Legislature; Practice & Procedure; Service

Keywords: Government servant

Summary: [A.] Service - Uttar Pradesh Water Supply & Sewerage Act, 1975, ss. 15, 15(1), 3, 31, 31(1), 37, 46, 7(2), 89, 97, 97(2)(c) - Uttar Pradesh Fundamental Rules, r. 56(a) - Uttar Pradesh Jal Nigam Engineers (Public Health Branch) Service Regulations, 1978, regn. 31 - Whether amendment made in r. 56(a) by Notification enhancing age of superannuation of Govt. servants from 58 years to 60 years would be applicable to employees of Uttar Pradesh Jal Nigam? - Held, Regulations have been framed by Nigam specifically enumerating in regn. 31 thereof that Rules governing service conditions of Govt. servants shall equally apply to employees of Nigam - So long regn. 31 of Regulations is not amended, 60 years which is age of superannuation of Govt. servants employed under Uttar Pradesh shall be applicable to employees of Nigam - Appeals and writ petitions allowed.
