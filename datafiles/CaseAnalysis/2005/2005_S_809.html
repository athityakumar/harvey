Mahendra Baburao Mahadik and Others v Subhash Krishna Kanitkar and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 March 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, B.P. Singh

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 327; (2005) 4 SCC 99; AIR 2005 SC 1794; 2005 (3) Bom.C.R. 28; JT 2005 (3) SC 409; 2005(3) SCALE 235; [2005] 2 S.C.R. 903; 2005 (2) UPLBEC 1560

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Municipal Council, Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Act, 1976, Bombay Municipal Corporation Act, City of Nagpur Municpal Corporation Act, 1948

Summary: [A.] Municipalities & Local Governments - Maharashtra Regional and Town Planning Act, 1966, s. 124E(2), s. 143, s. 2(15), s. 2(19), s. 43, s. 44, s. 52, s. 53 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 119, s. 217, s. 34 - Maharashtra Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats and Industrial Townships Act, 1965, s. 189 - City of Nagpur Municpal Corporation Act, 1948 - Bombay Municipal Corporation Act - Illegal constructions - Order of demolition - Appeal against demolition - Resolution of Municipal Council whereof all unauthorized constructions within municipal area were sought to be regularized upon imposition of penalty and compounding of offences - Whether HC right in directing demolition of structure? - Held, Municipal Council did not have any jurisdiction to direct regularization of unauthorized constructions by reason of resolution or otherwise - Municipal Council is directed to carry out order of HC.
