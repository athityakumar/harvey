G. Mahalingappa v G. M. Savitha

Supreme Court of India

09 August 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Tarun Chatterjee, D.M. Dharmadhikari

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 476; (2005) 6 SCC 441; 2005 (4) AWC 3860C; 2005 (4) JhrCR(SC) 79; JT 2005 (7) SC 174; 2006 (2) MLJ(SC) 197; 2005 (4) RCR(Civil) 200; 2006 (101) RD 409; 2005(6) SCALE 384; [2005] Supp2 S.C.R. 487

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: [A.] Land & Property - Benami Transactions (Prohibition) Act, 1988, ss. 2(a), 3(1), 3(2), 4(2) - Benami transaction - Held, suit was filed long before coming into force of Act - Since Act cannot have any retrospective operation appellant was entitled to raise plea of benami in written statement and to show and prove that he was real owner of suit property and that respondent was only his benamidar - Appeal allowed.
