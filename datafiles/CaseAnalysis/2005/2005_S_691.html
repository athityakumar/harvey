Rajasthan Welfare Society v State of Rajasthan

Supreme Court of India

07 April 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.K. Sabharwal, Tarun Chatterjee

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 271; (2005) 5 SCC 275; (2005) SCC (L&S) 524; AIR 2005 SC 2066; 2005 (105) FLR 611; JT 2005 (4) SC 163; 2005 (2) LLJ 563; 2005 (3) LLN 51; 2005(3) SCALE 812; [2005] 3 S.C.R. 387; 2005 (4) SLR 707

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Payment of Bonus Act, 1965, Rajasthan Non-Government Educational Institution Act, 1989, Rajasthan Non-Government Educational Institutions (Recognition, Grant-in-aid and Service Conditions Etc.) Rules, 1993

Summary: [A.] Rajasthan Non-Government Educational Institution Act, 1989, s. 16, s. 2(a), s. 2(b), s. 2(q), s. 2(r), s. 29, s. 43, s. 7 - Rajasthan Non-Government Educational Institutions (Recognition, Grant-in-aid and Service Conditions Etc.) Rules, 1993, r. 10, r. 13, r. 14, r. 22, r. 82, r. 9 -Gratuity - Wealth Tax - Bonus - Whether amount of gratuity payable to employees of aided educational institutions has to be taken into consideration or not for determining amount of grantin-aid? - Held, gratuity cannot be included in approved expenditure as under Rule 9 State Govt. can section grants under for Heads provided therein and gratuity does not fall under any one of them - Communication to effect that Rules do not provide for grant-aid on amount of gratuity, same being not included in approved expenditures, cannot be held to be illegal - Appeals dismissed.
