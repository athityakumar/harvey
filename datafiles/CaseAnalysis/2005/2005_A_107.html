Andhra Pradesh Public Service Commission v Koneti Venkateswarulu and Others

Supreme Court of India

30 August 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Srikrishna, H.K. Sema

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 509; (2005) 7 SCC 177; (2005) SCC (L&S) 924; AIR 2005 SC 4292; 2005 (5) ALD(SC) 103; 2005 (6) AWC 5521; 2005 (107) FLR 157; 2005 (4) JhrCR(SC) 162; JT 2005 (8) SC 23; 2005(7) SCALE 23; 2005 (8) SLR 1; [2005] Supp2 S.C.R. 1050

Case Digest

Subject: Competition; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: [A.] Service - Suppression of facts in application form - Not filled column provided for details previous employment - Cancellation of candidature by A.P. Public Service Commission, the Appellant herein - Administrative Tribunal reversed finding and directed that First Respondent had to be accommodated in next available vacancy to post of Women and Child Welfare Officer reserved for ST candidates - Appeal ed herein - Held, a person who indulges in such suppresso veri and suggestio falsi and obtains employment by false pretence does not deserve any public employment - Appeal allowed.
