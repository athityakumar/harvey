Commissioner of Central Excise, Meerut v Maharshi Ayurveda Corporation Limited

Supreme Court of India

07 December 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Ashok Bhan, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 973; (2005) 13 SCC 584; AIR 2006 SC 557; 2006 (193) E.L.T. 10; JT 2005 (10) SC 360; 2005 (71) RLT 599; 2005(10) SCALE 33; 2006 (1) SCJ 372; [2005] Supp5 S.C.R. 537

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax; Trade

Keywords: Ad Valorem

Summary: [A.] Excise - Central Excise Act, 1944, s. 35L(B) - Central Excise Tariff Act, 1985 - Whether product 'Herbonic' tonic is classifiable under Central Excise Tariff Heading No 2001.90 or 2108.90? - Held, mixture prepared is of parts of plants, seeds and nuts which can be consumed as such - Tribunal is right in holding that product of respondents is covered by Chap. 20 and not Chap. 21 of Tariff Act - Appeals dismissed.
