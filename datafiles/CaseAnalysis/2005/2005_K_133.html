Kohinoor Elastics Private v Commissioner of Central Excise, Indore

Supreme Court of India

04 August 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Variava, Tarun Chatterjee

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 758; (2005) 7 SCC 528; 2005 (188) E.L.T. 3

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: [A.] Excise - Notification No 1/93-C E - Appellants manufacture elastics as per specific orders of customers, who are manufacturers of undergarments - As per orders of customers Appellants affix brand/trade names belonging to respective customers, on elastic manufactured for that customer - Whether or not Appellants are entitled to benefit of Notification? - Held, notification specifically states that exemption contained in Notification shall not apply to specific goods which bear a brand name or trade name (registered or not) of another person - Benefit cannot be given when it is clearly not available - Appeals disposed of.
