Savitaben Somabhai Bhatiya v State of Gujarat and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 March 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 185; (2005) 3 SCC 636; (2005) SCC (Cr) 787; AIR 2005 SC 1809; 2005 (2) CGLJ 360; 2005 CRLJ 2141; 2005 (2) G.L.R. 1378; JT 2005 (3) SC 164; 2005 (2) KLT 65; 2005 (2) RCR(Civil) 151B; 2005 (2) RCR(Criminal) 190; 2005(3) SCALE 80; [2005] 2 S.C.R. 638

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal; Family & Personal; Women & Children

Summary: Family and Personal - Women and Children - CrPC, 1973, s. 125 - Scope and ambit of s.125 - Whether there is any scope for giving an extended meaning to include a woman who is not legally married? - Held, evidence on record has been found sufficient by Courts below by recording findings of fact that earlier marriage of respondent was established - As position in law stands presently there is no escape from conclusion that expression 'wife' as per s.125 refers to only legally married wife - Application by wife claiming maintenance dismissed - Amount of maintenance to child can be enhanced to Rs. 850/ - Appeal disposed of.
