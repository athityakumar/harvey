Naresh Kumar Goyal v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 October 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Singh, Tarun Chatterjee, P.K. Balasubramanyan

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 620; (2005) 8 SCC 276; AIR 2005 SC 4421; JT 2005 (12) SC 428; 2005(8) SCALE 332; [2005] Supp4 S.C.R. 17

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: [A.] Criminal - Constitution - COFEPOSA - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 226, 32 - Conservation Of Foreign Exchange And Prevention Of Smuggling Activities Act, 1974, ss. 3(i), 3(ii), 3(iii), 7 - Detention order - HC dismissed writ and held that this was not an appropriate case in which HC could exercise its jurisdiction u/art.226 to quash an order of detention even before its execution - Appealed herein - Challenge on ground that order was passed for a wrong purpose - Held, case does not fall within any of exceptions enumerated in Govt. of India and others Vs Smt Alka Subhash Gadia and Anr.(1990 Indlaw SC 636) - HC justified in refusing to exercise jurisdiction u/art.226 to quash order of detention at pre-arrest stage - Appeal dismissed.
