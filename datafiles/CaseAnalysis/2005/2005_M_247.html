Commissioner of Trade Tax, Lucknow v Messrs Kanhai Ram Thekedar

Supreme Court of India

29 April 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

AR. Lakshmanan, S.N. Variava

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 335; (2005) 4 SCC 472; AIR 2005 SC 3033; 2005 (185) E.L.T. 3; JT 2005 (5) SC 13; 2005 (2) KLT 948; 2005(4) SCALE 495; [2005] 3 S.C.R. 984; 2005 (141) STC 1

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Summary: [A.] Sales Tax - Uttar Pradesh Trade Tax Act, 1948, s. 3-B, s. 4-B.(1-A), s. 7, s. 8(1), s. 8(1-A), s. 8(1-B), s. 8(2) - Uttar Pradesh Trade Tax Rules, 1948, r. 43 - Demand of interest - Whether no subsequent proceeding can be initiated against revisionist as subsequent proceeding to assessment is barred by limitation? - HC held that a notice of demand was necessary before passing order of interest - Held, HC was not justified for deleting interest levied by authorities on ground that no notice was served - On question of delay in demanding interest, demand has to be set aside - Appeal dismissed.
