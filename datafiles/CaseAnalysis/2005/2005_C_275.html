Commissioner of Public Instructions and Others v K.R. Vishwanath

Supreme Court of India

30 August 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, H.K. Sema

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 520; (2005) 7 SCC 206; (2005) SCC (L&S) 927; AIR 2005 SC 3275; 2005 (107) FLR 153; 2006 (2) JhrCR(SC) 59; JT 2005 (8) SC 33; 2005 (4) LLN 71; 2005(7) SCALE 12; [2005] Supp2 S.C.R. 1042

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Family & Personal; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: [A.] Service - Karnataka Civil Services (Appointment on Compassionate Grounds) Rules, 1990, r. 5 - Karnataka Civil Services (Appointment on Compassionate Grounds) (Amendment) Rules, 1998 - Appointment on compassionate grounds - Following Life Insurance Corporation of Inida vs. Asha Ramachandra Ambekar (Mrs.) and Anr. (1994 Indlaw SC 1383) - Held, Tribunal and HC were not justified in directing that respondent's case be considered for appointment in terms of Rules without taking note of limitation prescribed - Appeal allowed.
