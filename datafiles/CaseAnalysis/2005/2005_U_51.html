Center for Public Interest Litigation and Another v Union of India and Another

Supreme Court of India

06 October 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Ruma Pal, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 874; (2005) 8 SCC 202; AIR 2005 SC 4413; 2005 (6) AWC 5912; [2005] 128 Comp Cas 387; 2006 (1) G.L.R. 752; JT 2005 (12) SC 369; 2005(8) SCALE 389; 2005 (6) SLR 499; [2005] Supp4 S.C.R. 77

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Parliament & Legislature; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: [A.] Service - CrPC, 1973, s. 197 - All India Service (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1969, r. 8 - Whether appointment of respondent No 3 as Chief Secretary is proper? - Held, protection to responsible public servants against vexatious criminal proceedings for offences committed in discharge of official duty - Acts of corruption not protected by provision - Directed State Govt. to transfer respondent No 3 to some other post in cadre/grade to which she belonged - Question of her suitability to be included in cadre/grade, shall be examined in writ petition itself - Application disposed of.
