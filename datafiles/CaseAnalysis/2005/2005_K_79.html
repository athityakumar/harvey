Kankavali Shikshan Sanstha and others v M.R. Gavali and others

Supreme Court of India

16 December 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

AR. Lakshmanan, Ruma Pal

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 960; (2005) 13 SCC 638; (2006) SCC (L&S) 1256; AIR 2005 SC 3586; JT 2005 (10) SC 575; 2005(10) SCALE 253; 2006 (1) SCJ 315; 2006 (1) SLR 601; [2005] Supp5 S.C.R. 864

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: [A.] Service - Maharashtra Employees of Private Schools (Conditions of Service) Regulation Act, 1977, s. 9 - Maharashtra Employees of Private Schools Rules, 1981, r. 9(9)(a) - Candidate belongs to OBC and appointed in vacancy reserved for an ST candidate due to absence of ST candidate - Affidavits given by respondent No 1 himself stating that he was appointed on purely temporary basis and that he would not claim any right of permanency on said post - Termination of service - Order of reinstatement in service with continuity of service and back-wages - Appeal against - Held, vacancy not filled by a person belonging to SC/ST - HC taking into account of relevant facts of case arrived at an accurate and correct conclusion as regards rights of respondent No 1 - Respondent No 1 will be entitled for job with immediate effect - Appeal dismissed.
