Commissioner of Central Excise, Nagpur v Simplex Mills Company Limited

Supreme Court of India

23 February 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Mrs. Justice Ruma Pal, Arijit Pasayat, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 481; 120 ECR 126; 2005 (67) RLT 242; 2005 (140) STC 125

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax; Sales Tax; Trade

Summary: Excise - Central Excise Tariff Act, 1985, s. 2 - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, s. 37-B - Interpretation Rules, rr. 2 and 3(a) - Whether grey cotton canvas cloth, 100 per cent cotton/grey cotton, belting and duck classifiable under Tariff Headings (TH) 52.02, 54.08 or 59.09 of Schedule to Central Excise Tariff Act, 1985? - Larger bench decision in Jyoti Overseas Limited v. Commr. of Central Excise, Indore 2001 Indlaw 1027 held only 'made up' articles can be classified under chaps. 59.09 - Therefore, unprocessed textile fabrics do not fall within 59.09 (now 59.11) - Held, reasoning of Tribunal in Jyoti Overseas in unexceptionable - Products in question classified under chap. 52 and 54 - Appeals dismissed.
