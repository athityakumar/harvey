Hombe Gowda Edn. Trust & Anr. v State Of Karnataka & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

16 December 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, P.P. Naolekar

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 955; (2006) 1 SCC 430; 2006 (1) CLR 280; 2006 (2) JhrCR(SC) 24; JT 2005 (10) SC 598; 2005(10) SCALE 307; 2006 (1) SCJ 10A; 2006 (1) SLR 635; [2005] Supp5 S.C.R. 937

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: CCS (CCA) Rules, Karnataka Private Educational Institutions (Discipline and Control) Act, 1975

Summary: Karnataka Private Educational Institutions (Discipline and Control) Act, 1975 - Service - Misconduct - Assaulting a superior at a workplace - Dismissal from service - Tribunal as also High Court that the Respondents committed a misconduct but directed payment of 60% back wages after setting aside order of punishment of dismissal - Appeal against - Held, jurisdiction to interfere with quantum of punishment could be exercised only when, inter alia, it is found to be grossly disproportionate - Assaulting a superior at a workplace amounts to an act of gross indiscipline - Punishment of dismissal from services cannot be said to be wholly disproportionate - Appeal allowed
