Secretary to the Government and Another v M. Senthil Kumar

Supreme Court of India

28 February 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 137; (2005) SCC (L&S) 424; (2005) 3 SCC 451; AIR 2005 SC 1579; 2005 (105) FLR 70; 2005 (2) JhrCR(SC) 1; JT 2005 (2) SC 615; 2005 (2) LLN 64; 2005 (3) MLJ(SC) 143; 2005(2) SCALE 448; [2005] 2 S.C.R. 436; 2005 (2) SLR 795; 2005 (2) UPLBEC 1852

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Service - Posts of Police Constables - Tamil Nadu Service Recruitment Board - Policy of State Govt. in providing 10% special quota to children/wards of serving/retired/deceased personnel of police and like forces - Application against denial of selection - Whether HC right in declaring policy to be constitutionally invalid? - Held, there was no challenge to policy decision in petition filed before Tribunal or before HC - It was not open to HC to dismiss application on additional ground that policy decision was unconstitutional - Set aside that part of order of HC which invalidates policy decision - Appeal allowed.
