Commissioner of Central Excise, Mumbai v Johnson and Johnson Limited

Supreme Court of India

22 September 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Mrs. Justice Ruma Pal, P.P. Naolekar

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 753; 2005 (188) E.L.T. 467

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax; Trade

Summary: [A.] Excise - Central Excise Tariff Act, 1985 - Central Excise Act, 1944, s. 14 - Import of two particular medicines, namely, Eprex and Topamac - Whether these two medicaments were manufactured for purposes of attracting duty under Act? - Held, repacking would have to be from bulk packs to 'retail packs' so as to render product marketable directly to consumer - There is no evidence relied upon by appellants to effect that cardboard boxes in which units were placed or thermocole containers were 'retail packs' - Appeals dismissed.
