Amrit Foods v Commissioner of Central Excise, Uttar Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

26 October 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Ruma Pal, H.K. Sema

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 1196; (2005) 13 SCC 419; 2005 (190) E.L.T. 433

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax; Trade

Summary: Excise - Central Excise Act, 1944 - Central Excise Tariff Act, 1985 - Central Excise Rules, 1944 - Central Excise Rules, 2002 - Whether products manufactured by appellant, namely, 'Milk Shake Mix', 'Soft Serve Mix', 'Coffee Creamer' and 'Cream Packed' are classifiable under Tariff Heading 04.04 or Tariff Heading 1901.19 of Central Excise Tariff Act? - Tribunal has not addressed itself at all to various arguments raised by appellant - Held, matter remanded back to Tribunal for purpose of disposing of same on merits - Appeal disposed of.
