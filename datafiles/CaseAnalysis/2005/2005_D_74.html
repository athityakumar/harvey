Kailash Chand and another v Dharam Das

Supreme Court of India

04 May 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, Y.K. Sabharwal, G.P. Mathur

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 381; (2005) 5 SCC 375; AIR 2005 SC 2362; 2005 (59) ALR 764; JT 2005 (5) SC 139; 2005 (1) RCR(Rent) 551; 2005(4) SCALE 603; [2005] 3 S.C.R. 1182; 2006 (1) ShimLC 86

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Rent Control

Summary: [A.] Rent Control - Himachal Pradesh Urban Rent Control Act, 1987, s. 14(3)(a)(i) - Haryana Urban (Control of Rent) & Eviction Act, 1973 - Eviction - Rent Controller and Appellate Authority granted eviction - Whether HC right in setting aside eviction - Held, HC was not right in applying third proviso to facts of case and deny relief of eviction to appellants - First and second proviso also do not come in way of appellants - Their case of requirement within meaning of s.14(3)(a)(i) is fully made out - Appeal allowed.
