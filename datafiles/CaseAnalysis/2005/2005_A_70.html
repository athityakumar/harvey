Anderson Wright and Company v Amar Nath Roy and others

Supreme Court of India

19 April 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, G.P. Mathur

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 309; (2005) 6 SCC 489; AIR 2005 SC 2457; 2005 (59) ALR 606; JT 2005 (11) SC 300; 2005 (2) Law Herald (P&H) 657; 2005 (141) PLR 666; 2005 (2) RCR(Civil) 831; 2005 (1) RCR(Rent) 624; 2005(4) SCALE 207B

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure; Rent Control

Summary: [A.] Civil Procedure - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 20 r. 12 - Application seeking direction to appellants to pay or deposit, month by month, a fixed amount by way of mesne profits, as a condition precedent to order staying execution of decree for eviction of appellants - Held, taking an overall view of material made available by parties, appellants should, from date of decree of eviction, pay mesne profits/compensation for use and occupation @ Rs. 15/- Per sq ft subject to final determination of same by a competent forum - Application disposed of.
