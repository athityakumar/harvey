Management Committee of Montfort Senior Secondary v Vijay Kumar and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 September 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, H.K. Sema

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 548; (2005) 7 SCC 472; (2005) SCC (L&S) 966; AIR 2005 SC 3549; 2005 (123) DLT 188; 2005 (4) ESC 519; JT 2005 (8) SC 279; 2005(7) SCALE 254; 2005 (5) SLR 817; [2005] Supp3 S.C.R. 137; 2005 (6) Supreme 507

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Montfort School Staff Rules

Summary: [A.] Service - Delhi School Education Act, 1973, ss. 10, 11, 12, 15, 15(3), 15(3)(e), 8(3), 9, chap. 5 - Arbitration And Conciliation Act, 1996, s. 8(1) - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908 - Montfort School Staff Rules, r. 31 - HC found that Delhi School Tribunal was not required to refer appeal by a dismissed employee to an arbitrator on an application filed before it by management of school - Appealed herein - Held, very nature of employment has undergone a transformation and services of employees in minorities un-aided schools governed under Chapter V are no longer contractual in nature but they are statutory - Qualifications, leaves, salaries, age of retirement, pension, dismissal, removal, reduction in rank, suspension and other conditions of service are to be governed exclusively under statutory regime provided in Chapter IV - Tribunal constituted u/s. 11 is forum provided for enforcing some of these rights - Appeals dismissed.
