Ramesh and others v State of Tamil Nadu

Supreme Court of India

03 March 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

P. Venkatarama Reddi, A.K. Mathur

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 173; (2005) 3 SCC 507; (2005) SCC (Cr) 735; AIR 2005 SC 1989; 2005 CRLJ 1732; JT 2005 (3) SC 90; 2005 (2) RCR(Criminal) 67; 2005(2) SCALE 484; [2005] 2 S.C.R. 493

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Family & Personal; Practice & Procedure; Women & Children

Summary: Criminal - Family and Personal - Practice and Procedure - Women and Children - CrPC, s.482 - Offences under ss 498-A, 406 of IPC and ss 3, 4 of Dowry Prohibition Act - Petition to quash charge-sheet dismissed by HC - Appeal against this order - Prima facie, there is nothing in entire complaint which goes to show that any acts constituting alleged offences were at all committed at Trichy - Proceedings against appellant Gowri Ramaswamy quashed and her appeal stands allowed - Order accordingly.
