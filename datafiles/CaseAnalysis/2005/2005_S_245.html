Charanjit Lal Mehra and others v Smt. Kamal Saroj Mahajan and another

Supreme Court of India

11 March 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

A.K. Mathur, P. Venkatarama Reddi

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 177; (2005) 11 SCC 279; AIR 2005 SC 2765; 2005 (59) ALR 297; 2005 (2) AWC 1014; 2005 (118) DLT 396; 2005 (3) JhrCR(SC) 28; JT 2005 (3) SC 213; 2005 (2) Law Herald (P&H) 92; 2005 (3) RCR(Civil) 186; 2005(3) SCALE 70; [2005] 2 S.C.R. 661

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure; Rent Control

Summary: Land and Property - Rent Control - CPC, O.12 r.6 - Suit for eviction - Lease deed - Admission of claim by defendants - Single Judge accepted application under O 12 r.6 and passed a decree for eviction - SLP against this order - Clear admission on behalf of the defendants that there existed a relationship of landlord and tenants, the rent is more than Rs. 3500/- Held, tenancy in question was a joint / composite one and it is not an individual lease of demised premises which is a show room and defendants had to pay rent jointly -Where claim is admitted, the court has jurisdiction to enter a judgment for the plaintiff - No ground for interference made out - Petition dismissed.
