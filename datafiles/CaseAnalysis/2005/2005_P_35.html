Palanisamy Gounder and another v State Represented By Inspector of Police

Supreme Court of India

11 February 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.K. Sabharwal, P.P. Naolekar

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 1556; (2005) 12 SCC 327; 2006 (2) RCR(Criminal) 235

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Sustainability, Arraying as accused

Summary: Criminal - Practice & Procedure - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 302 - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, s. 319 - Arraying as accused - Sustainability - Appellants were added as accused nos. 4 and 5 in a trial case for alleged offence u/s. 302 of IPC - Appellants filed instant appeal challenging the judgment and order passed by HC confirming the order of Trial Court passed in exercise of power u/s. 319 of CrPC.

Held, power u/s. 319 of CrPC cannot be exercised so as to conduct a fishing inquiry. though the case against the appellants was not on solid foundation but Trial Court felt that to find out the real truth they deserved to be added as accused. Impugned judgment and order are to be set aside. Appeal allowed.
