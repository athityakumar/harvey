Supreme Court of India

11 August 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Ashok Bhan

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 465; (2005) 6 SCC 776; AIR 2006 SC 182; 2005 (4) AWC 3860M; 2005 (3) CPJ(Sc) 35; 2005 (4) JhrCR(SC) 149; JT 2005 (7) SC 258; 2006 (2) MLJ(SC) 178; 2005 (4) RCR(Civil) 155; 2005 (4) RLW 2223; 2005(6) SCALE 457; [2005] Supp2 S.C.R. 524

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Consumer Protection; Electricity & Energy

Summary: [A.] Constitution - Consumer Protection - Electricity & Energy - Electricity (Supply) Act, 1948, ss. 5, 79(j), 79-A - Indian Electricity Act, 1910, ss. 22, 24 - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 12, 14 - Consumer Protection Act, 1986 - Consumer dispute - Non-supply of electrical energy to consumers within a reasonable time despite taking deposits for same compliance of other formalities - Directions to release connections to all applicants and payment of interest at rate of 12% per annum on amo
