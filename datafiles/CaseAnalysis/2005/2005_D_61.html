M/S. S & S. Enterprise v Designated Authority & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

22 February 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Ruma Pal, Arijit Pasayat, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 223; (2005) 3 SCC 337; AIR 2005 SC 1527; 2005 (181) E.L.T. 375; JT 2005 (3) SC 457; 2005 (67) RLT 145; 2005(2) SCALE 237; [2005] 2 S.C.R. 255

Case Digest

Subject: Anti-Dumping; Customs; Indirect Tax; Trade

Summary: Customs Tariff (Anti Dumping Duty on Dumped Articles and for Determination of Injuries) Rules 1995 - Antidumping duty - Held, nobody has questioned preliminary finding of Designated Authority that quantity of imports from Bangladesh during period of investigation was less than 3% of total imports of 'like article' to India - Investigation therefore should have been promptly dropped against Bangladesh - Set aside antidumping duty.
