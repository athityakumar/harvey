Umesh Korga Bhandari v Mahanagar Telephone Nigam Limited and Another

Supreme Court of India

08 August 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, H.K. Sema

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 475; (2005) 6 SCC 451; (2005) SCC (L&S) 852; 2005 (6) AWC 5355; JT 2005 (7) SC 270; 2005(6) SCALE 345; 2006 (1) SLR 92; [2005] Supp2 S.C.R. 443; 2005 (3) UPLBEC 2921

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Telephone

Summary: Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - Service - Appellants working in Canteens maintained by Departmental Canteen Committee - Termination - High Court held that appellants were holding civil post, only forum to adjudicate their grievance was the Central Administrative Tribunal - Appeal against - Held, matter referred to a three-Judge Bench to decide the issue whether a person holding civil post can seek relief under the ID Act on the basis that he was a workman - Order accordingly.
