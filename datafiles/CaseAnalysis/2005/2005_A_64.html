State Bank of India v Allied Chemical Laboratories and Another

Supreme Court of India

05 September 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Singh, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 1063; (2006) 9 SCC 252; 2006 (1) BC 341; 2006 (1) DRTC 481; 2005(1) SCALE 277

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Constitution; Corporate

Keywords: Appeal allowed

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 136 - Recovery of Debts Due to Banks and Financial Institutions Act, 1993, s. 20 - Recovery of debts - Natural justice - Whether High Court in its decision that D.R.T. violated principle of natural justice in not permitting respondent to cross-examine the witness and not permitted to avail remedy u/s. 20 is valid? - Held, D.R.T. violated principle of natural justice by not permitting respondent to cross-examine the witness and liable to avail remedy u/s. 20 - Further held, HC judgment unjustified and set aside - Appeal allowed without any costs.
