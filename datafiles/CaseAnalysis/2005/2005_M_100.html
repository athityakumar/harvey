M/s. Vikram Cement v Commissioner of Central Excise Indore

Supreme Court of India

24 August 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Singh, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 517; (2005) 7 SCC 74; 2005 (187) E.L.T. 145; JT 2005 (7) SC 637; 2005(6) SCALE 744; 2006 (2) SCJ 262; [2005] Supp2 S.C.R. 930

Case Digest

Subject: Excise

Keywords: Modvat Scheme, Customs and Central Excise Duties Drawback Rules, 1995, Central Excise (No 2) Rules, 2001, Additional Duties of Excise (Textiles and Textile Articles) Act, 1978, Admissibility

Summary: [A.] Excise - Indirect Tax - CENVAT - MODVAT - Central Excise Tariff Act, 1985 - Customs Tariff Act, 1975, s. 3 - Income-Tax Act, 1961, s. 32 - Central Excise Act, 1944 - Central Excise Rules, 1944, r. 57AA(d), rr. 57J, 57T, ss. 3A, 57B - CENVAT Credit Rules, 2001, r. 2(f) - CENVAT Credit Rules, 2002, r. 2(g) - Customs and Central Excise Duties Drawback Rules, 1995 - Central Excise (No 2) Rules, 2001, r. 4(3) - Central Excise Rules, 2002, r. 4(3) - Admissibility of credit of duty paid on 'inputs' namely, explosives, lubricating oils and welding electrodes as also question of admissibility of credit on 'capital goods' namely, limestone crusher, mining equipment - Held, Commissioner of Central Excise, Jaipur v J K Udaipur Udyog Ltd (2004 Indlaw SC 741) needs reconsideration by a larger bench - Present case as well to be placed before larger bench for consideration - Interim Order.
