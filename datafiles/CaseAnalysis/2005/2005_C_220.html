Messrs Sharp Industries Limited v Commissioner of Central Excise, Mumbai-iii

Supreme Court of India

26 September 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Variava, Tarun Chatterjee

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 583; (2005) 7 SCC 676; 2006 (1) Bom.C.R. 262; 2005 (188) E.L.T. 146; JT 2005 (8) SC 533; 2005(7) SCALE 515; [2005] Supp3 S.C.R. 475

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: [A.] Excise - Central Excise Tariff Act, 1985 - Appeal against judgment of CEGAT - Product consists of Aluminum foil, whose thickness does not exceed 0.2 mm, which is then covered on one side with a polyester film and on other side with polyethylene and pouches out of same material - Whether products are classifiable under Tariff Heading 76.07 and 76.12, as claimed by Appellants, or under 39.20.38 and 39.23.90, as claimed by Respondent? - Held, in appellants own case, and for same period, it has already been held by Tribunal that product is classifiable under Tariff Heading 39.20 - Findings being based on facts and Tribunal being final authority on facts, this Court cannot interfere - Appeal dismissed.
