Babubhai Odhavji Patel, Etc v State of Gujarat

Supreme Court of India

27 October 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

K.G. Balakrishnan, B.N. Srikrishna

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 701; (2005) 8 SCC 725; AIR 2006 SC 102; 2006 (1) G.L.R. 691; 2006 (1) JCC (Narcotics) 35; 2006 (1) JhrCR(SC) 17; JT 2005 (9) SC 410; 2005 (4) KLT 779; 2005(8) SCALE 758; [2005] Supp4 S.C.R. 844

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Health & Drug

Summary: [A.] Criminal - Narcotic Drugs And Psychotropic Substances Act, 1985, ss. 17, 42, 50, 52, 55, 57 - Appeal against conviction - Held, it is proved satisfactorily that appellant who was owner of tanker lorry in question was found in possession of narcotic substance weighing more than 5 kg - It is proved that appellant was using this vehicle for illegally transporting narcotic substance - Appellant has rightly been found guilty by sessions court and his conviction was rightly upheld by HC - Appeal dismissed.
