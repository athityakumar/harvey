P.V. Mahadevan v Managing Director, Tamil Nadu Housing Board

Supreme Court of India

08 August 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Ruma Pal, AR. Lakshmanan

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 478; (2005) 6 SCC 636; (2005) SCC (L&S) 861; AIR 2006 SC 207; 2005 (6) AWC 5491; 2005 (4) ESC 559; 2005 (106) FLR 1003; JT 2005 (7) SC 417; 2005 (3) LLN 1028; 2005(6) SCALE 450; 2005 (5) SLR 384; [2005] Supp2 S.C.R. 474; 2005 (5) Supreme 611

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: [A.] Service - Tamil Nadu State Housing Board Act, 1961, ss. 118, 119 - Departmental Proceedings - Issuance of charge memo after 10 years of incident - No explanation - Held, protracted disciplinary enquiry against a Govt. employee should, therefore, be avoided not only in interests of Govt. employee but in public interest and also in interests of inspiring confidence in minds of Govt. employees - Quashed charge memo - Appeal allowed.
