Brij Pal Sharma v Ghaziabad Development Authority

Supreme Court of India

18 August 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

H.K. Sema, Arijit Pasayat

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 1702; (2005) 7 SCC 106; 2005 (4) AWC 3662; 2005 (3) CPJ(Sc) 43; 2005 (4) JhrCR(SC) 68; JT 2005 (12) SC 21; 2005 (4) RCR(Civil) 1; 2005(6) SCALE 613; [2005] Supp2 S.C.R. 768

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection; Land & Property

Summary: Consumer Protection - Land & Property - Respondent Authority (Ghaziabad Development Authority) floated a scheme called Karpuripuram Scheme for allotment of housing plots under the self-financing scheme - Pursuant thereto, the appellant applied for a plot of land and also deposited a sum as total and final payment - However, the land in question could not be allotted to the appellant on the ground that Karpuripuram Scheme had been cancelled and a new scheme had been floated - Aggrieved thereb
