Swamy Atmananda v Swami Bodhananda and Others

Supreme Court of India

13 April 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, B.P. Singh

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 772; (2005) 3 SCC 734; 2005 (59) ALR 609; 2005 (2) AWC 1355A; 2005 (2) JhrCR(SC) 248; JT 2005 (11) SC 379; 2005 (99) RD 232; 2005(4) SCALE 116; [2005] 3 S.C.R. 578

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Practice & Procedure - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908 - Pursuant to Civil Court decree, whether writ of mandamus maintainable for directing the respondents therein to give all assistance to the appellant in taking over management of the institutions - Held, no - Decree passed by the Civil Court must be passed in terms of the provisions contained in the Code of Civil Procedure,1908 - Respondent entitled to execute the decree in accordance with law - Appeal allowed.
