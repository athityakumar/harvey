Parsottambhai Maganbhai Patel and Others v State of Gujarat Through Deputy Collector Modasa

Supreme Court of India

06 September 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Singh, S.B. Sinha

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 542; (2005) 7 SCC 431; AIR 2005 SC 3464; 2005 (4) AWC 3457; 2006 (1) G.L.R. 142; 2006 (2) JhrCR(SC) 178; JT 2005 (8) SC 146; 2006 (2) MLJ(SC) 174; 2005 (4) RCR(Civil) 207; 2005(7) SCALE 143; 2005 (Supp) RD 571; [2005] Supp2 S.C.R. 1192

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Practice & Procedure - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 12(2), 18, 18(2)(a), 18(2)(b) - Application for making a reference u/s. 18 - HC held that application was barred by time - Appealed herein - Appellants knew of the date of declaration of award only when compensation was paid to them - Held, HC did not rejected plea of appellants that they came to know of award only when compensation was being paid to them in July, 1988 - Admittedly appellants had no notice u/s. 12(2) - Reference is within time - Appeal allowed.
