S. Pushpa and others v Sivachanmugavelu and others

Supreme Court of India

11 February 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

G.P. Mathur, R.C. Lahoti, K.G. Balakrishnan

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 91; (2005) 3 SCC 1; (2005) SCC (L&S) 327; AIR 2005 SC 1038; 2005 (5) AWC 3992; 2005 (3) CalLT(SC) 71; 2005 (2) ESC 173; 2005 (104) FLR 1128; JT 2005 (2) SC 137; 2005 (2) LLN 27; 2005 (3) MLJ(SC) 1; 2005(2) SCALE 49; [2005] 1 S.C.R. 1158; 2005 (2) Supreme 116; 2005 (1) UPLBEC 908

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure; Service

Keywords: Employment Under The State

Summary: [A.] Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 16(4), 239B, 240, 309, 331, 333, 341, 341(1) - Government of Union Territories Act, 1963 - Pondicherry (Administration) Act, 1962, s. 3 - Post of Selection Grade Teachers in Pondicherry administration - Selection made of migrant Scheduled Caste candidates against quota reserved for Scheduled Castes - Whether this selection is illegal and invalid? - Held, UT of Pondicherry having adopted a policy of Central Govt. whereunder all Scheduled Castes or Scheduled Tribes, irrespective of their State are eligible for posts which are reserved for SC/ST candidates, no legal infirmity can be ascribed to such a policy and same cannot be held to be contrary to any provision of law - There has been no violation of any constitutional or any other legal provision in making selection and appointment of migrant Scheduled Caste - Appeal allowed.
