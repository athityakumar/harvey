State of Nagaland v Lipok Ao and Others

Supreme Court of India

01 April 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 230; (2005) 3 SCC 752; (2005) SCC (Cr) 906; AIR 2005 SC 2191; 2005 (183) E.L.T. 337; JT 2005 (4) SC 10; 2005 (2) KLT 547; 2005 (2) Law Herald (P&H) 400; 2005 (2) RCR(Civil) 375; 2005 (2) RCR(Criminal) 414; 2005(3) SCALE 575; [2005] 3 S.C.R. 108

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Self Defence

Summary: Criminal - Practice and Procedure - Limitation Act, 1963, s.Delay of 57 dayin filing appe - Al - Ground that records were purportedly missing - Application for condonation of delay - Whether Single Judge right in dismissing condonation application and consequentially rejecting of application for grant of leave to appeal? - Held, delay of 57 days deserved condonation - Keeping in view long passage of time and points involved, proper to direct grant of leave to appeal - Appeal allowed - Appeal allowed.
