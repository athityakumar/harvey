Secretary, Andhra Pradesh Public Service v B. Swapna and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 March 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 246; (2005) 4 SCC 154; (2005) SCC (L&S) 452; 2005 (2) ESC 246; 2005 (105) FLR 464; JT 2005 (3) SC 468; 2005 (3) LLN 95; 2005(3) SCALE 338; [2005] 2 S.C.R. 991; 2005 (4) SLR 701; 2005 (2) Supreme 615; 2005 (2) UPLBEC 1545

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Service - Andhra Pradesh Service Commission (Procedure) Rules - Selection process - Whether HC in holding that though Rule was amended w e f 30.7.1997, it was applicable to present dispute and wait list was operative for period of one year and even during that period if any fall out vacancy has arisen and any new appointments are to be made for fresh vacancies, they should be filled up by candidates from wait list? - Held, Tribunal did not grant adequate time to Commission to produce relevant records - HC proceeded on erroneous premises that amended rules applied - Once a process of selection starts, the prescribed selection criteria cannot be changed - Rules regarding qualification for appointment if amended during continuance of the process of selection do not affect the same - HC judgment affirming Tribunal's judgment cannot be maintained - Appeal allowed.
