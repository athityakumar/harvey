Pahwa Chemicals Private Limited v Commissioner of Central Excise, Delhi

Supreme Court of India

15 September 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Variava, Tarun Chatterjee

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 799; (2009) 4 SCC 658; 2005 (189) E.L.T. 257

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Excise - Central Excise Act, 1944, s. 11A - Whether Respondents were entitled to invoke extended period of limitation u/s. 11 A? - Held, when all facts are before Department and a party in belief that affixing of a label makes no difference does not make a declaration, then there would be no willful mis-declaration or willful suppression - There is no justification for invoking extended period of limitation - Appeal allowed.
