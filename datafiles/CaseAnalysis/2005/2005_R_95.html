State of Uttar Pradesh and Others v Ram Bachan Tripathi

Supreme Court of India

02 August 2005

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, H.K. Sema

Where Reported

2005 Indlaw SC 446; (2005) 6 SCC 496; (2005) SCC (L&S) 858; AIR 2005 SC 3212; 2005 (6) AWC 5361; 2005 (106) FLR 1214; JT 2005 (10) SC 341; 2005(6) SCALE 19; 2006 (1) SLR 127; [2005] Supp1 S.C.R. 924

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: [A.] Service - Alleged misconduct - Termination - Appeal against order of reinstatement - Held, mere description of a background fact cannot be called as stigma - Mere statement that show-cause notices were issued and there was no response can not be treated as a stigma as observed by Tribunal and HC - Appeal allowed.
