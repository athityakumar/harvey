Employees State Insurance Corporation, Bhopal v Central Press and Another

Supreme Court of India

21 February 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Hameedullah Beg, A.C. Gupta, P.S. Kailasam

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 175; (1977) SCC (L&S) 310; (1977) 2 SCC 581; AIR 1977 SC 1351; 1977 (34) FLR 258; 1977 LabIC 884; 1977 (1) LLJ 479; 1977 (1) LLN 628; [1977] 3 S.C.R. 35; 1977 UJ 218

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: ESI Act , 1948, ss. 44, 45A, 75 and 99A - Scheme of Act as amended by 1966 Act - Employer failed to maintain registers and submit returns u/s. 44 - Duty of Corporation - Claim for recovery of contributions u/s. 75(1)(c) by Corporation - Held, duty of Insurance court to give direction to Corporation to perform its duty u/s.45A and to decide matter in dispute - Appeal Allowed.
