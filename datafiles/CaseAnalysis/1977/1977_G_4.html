Mistry Premjibhai Vithaldas v Ganeshbhai Keshavji

Supreme Court of India

14 April 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Hameedullah Beg, A.C. Gupta, P.S. Kailasam

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 70; (1977) 3 SCC 11; AIR 1977 SC 1707; 1977 (2) RCR(Rent) 341; 1977 (2) RentLR 9; [1977] 3 S.C.R. 569

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Bombay Rents, Hotel and Lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947, ss. 5(10), 12 and 12(3)(a) - Transfer Of Property Act, 1882 - Tenancy - (A) Application for fixation of standard rent - Effect of dismissal for non-prosecution - (B) Eviction - Meaning of readiness and willingness to pay - Held, reasonably inferred that it was not a bona fide application at all - Act does not cover the case of a person who is unable to pay owing to want of means but is otherwise 'ready and willing' - Appeal Allowed.
