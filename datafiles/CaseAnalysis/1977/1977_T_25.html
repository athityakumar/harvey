Dadu Yogendrenath Singh & Ors v The Collector, Seoni

Supreme Court of India

25 January 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Sarkaria, Hans Raj Khanna, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 154; (1977) 2 SCC 1; AIR 1977 SC 1128; 1977 (3) ALR 281; [1977] 2 S.C.R. 757; 1977 UJ 143

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Land Acquisition

Summary: Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 9 and 25(1) - Land - Acquisition - Compensation - (A) Assessment of - Determination of potential value as building site -Primarily question of fact - (B) Claim for, in pursuance of notice u/s.9 - Award of compensation in excess of claim - Held, circumstance as to voluntarily paying Rs. 500/- per acre as diversion charges , and within the Municipal limits, having buildings in immediate vicinity etc. , was sufficient to establish its potential value as building sites - Held, award is wrong -Appeal Allowed.
