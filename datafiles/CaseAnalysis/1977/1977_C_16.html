Narsinbhai Prajapati Etc. v Chhatrasinh Kanji and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 February 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, P.K. Goswami

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 363; (1977) 4 SCC 600A; AIR 1977 SC 1753; 1977 CRLJ 1144; 1977 CRLR 435; 1977 UJ 604

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 302 and 34 - Murder - Respondents convicted by Trial Court but High Court reversed conviction order and acquitted respondents - Present appeal by complainants - Lots of contradictions in statements of eye-witnesses - From evidence produced by prosecution, conviction cannot be sustained - Held, HC rightly reversed conviction - Appeal dismissed.
