State of Gujarat and another v Ramesh Chandra Mashruwala

Supreme Court of India

21 January 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, M. Hameedullah Beg, P.S. Kailasam

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 373; (1977) 2 SCC 12; (1977) SCC (L&S) 217; AIR 1977 SC 1619; 1977 LabIC 849; 1977 (1) LLN 421; [1977] 2 S.C.R. 710

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Constitution, arts. 235, 236 and 311 - CPC, 1908 - (A) Extent of disciplinary jurisdiction of High Court - (B) Disciplinary proceeding against Registrar - Failure to give copies of certain documents - Deprived of reasonable opportunity to defend - Effect - Held, Registrar, Small Causes Court is subject to such jurisdiction - Inquiry being contrary to art. 311 cases remitted back for this purpose - Matter Remitted To High Court.
