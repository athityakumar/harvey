Chander Bhan Harbhajan Lal v State of Punjab

Supreme Court of India

22 February 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

P.S. Kailasam, M. Hameedullah Beg

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 176; (1977) 2 SCC 715; AIR 1977 SC 1210; [1977] 3 S.C.R. 38; 1977 UJ 265

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Summary: Arbitration Act,1940 -Arbitration clause - Dispute to be referred to committee of three arbitrators nominated by Govt. - Whether appoinment of a fresh committee for arbitration is proper?- Held, there was no bar to Govt. appointing a fresh committee consisting of three officers.
