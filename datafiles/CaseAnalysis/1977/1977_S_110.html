Kurukshetra University and Another v State of Haryana and Another

Supreme Court of India

22 March 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, P.K. Goswami, P.N. Shingal

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 337; (1977) 4 SCC 451; (1977) SCC (Cr) 613; AIR 1977 SC 2229; 1977 BBCJ 145; 1977 CRLJ 1900

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: CrPC, 1973, ss. 154 and 482 - Exercise of inherent powers of High Court - When open - FIR, if can be quashed u/s.482 - Police had not even commenced the investigation and no proceeding at all is pending in any Court in pursuance of the FIR - Held, HC cannot quash a FIR - Appeal Allowed.
