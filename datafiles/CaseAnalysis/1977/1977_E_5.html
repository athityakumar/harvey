Everest Coal Company Private Limited v State of Bihar and Others

Supreme Court of India

29 September 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 108; (1978) 1 SCC 12; AIR 1977 SC 2304; [1978] 1 S.C.R. 571; 1977 UJ 659

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Practice & Procedure

Summary: CPC, 1908, O. 40, r. 1 - Contempt of Courts Act, 1971, s. 2 - Suit against Receiver - Leave of Court appointing Receiver must be obtained either prior to filing suit or during its pendency - Grant of leave is rule, refusal exception - Object - Held, rule is merely to prevent contempt - Appeal Allowed.
