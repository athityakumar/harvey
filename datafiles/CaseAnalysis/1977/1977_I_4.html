Commissioner of Income Tax, West Bengal I v Indian Aluminium Company Limited

Supreme Court of India

25 January 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

P.K. Goswami, Hans Raj Khanna, P.S. Kailasam

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 224; (1977) 4 SCC 598A; AIR 1977 SC 1141; [1977] 108 ITR 367

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Liability, Tax assessment

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Indian Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 15 C - Tax assessment - Liability - Whether Tribunal was justified in holding that s. 15 C of Act was applicable to new production units added to existing production units of assessee in respect of buildings, plants and machineries and directing exemption to be granted under aforesaid section accordingly - Held, Decision in case of Textile Machinery Corporation v Commissioner of Income-tax, 1977 Indlaw SC 225, relied on - Claim of assessee u/s. 15 C was allowed - Appeal dismissed.
