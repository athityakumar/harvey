Straw Board Manufacturing Company Limited v Workmen

Supreme Court of India

01 March 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 141; (1977) SCC (L&S) 243; (1977) 2 SCC 329; AIR 1977 SC 941; 1977 (34) FLR 269; 1977 LabIC 543; 1977 (1) LLJ 463; 1977 (1) LLN 399; [1977] 3 S.C.R. 91

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, sch.3, item 5 - Constitution, art. 136 - Gratuity - Principles to be followed in a scheme of gratuity for factory workers - Award framing scheme as to consolidated wage and not to basic wage - Scope of interference by Supreme Court in appeal u/art. 136 with award fixing 5 years as the period of eligibility - Award framing gratuity scheme held was proper and was not liable to be altered u/art.136 of Constitution - Order Accordingly.
