S. P. Deshmukh v Shah Nihal Chand Waghajibai Gujarati

Supreme Court of India

27 July 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, P.S. Kailasam

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 316; (1977) 3 SCC 515; AIR 1977 SC 1985; 1977 (2) RCJ 437; 1977 UJ 485

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Constitution, art. 227 - Tenancy - Payment of rent - Concurrent findings of Rent Controller and Collector - That tenant was not habitual defaulter - Interference with by High Court u/art. 227 - Validity - Held, HC exceeded limits of its narrow jurisdiction resulting in grave injustice in the circumstances of the case - Appeal Allowed.
