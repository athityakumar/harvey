Roshan Di Hatti v Commissioner of Income Tax, Delhi

Supreme Court of India

08 March 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, R.S. Sarkaria

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 206; (1977) 2 SCC 378; AIR 1977 SC 1605; 1977 CTR 200; [1977] 107 ITR 938; [1977] 3 S.C.R. 153

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 34(1) - Constitution, art. 136 - Escaped assessment - Burden of proof regarding income from undisclosed source - Finding of fact by Tribunal as to income from undisclosed source - Appeal against - Held, interference by Supreme Court - Appeal Allowed.
