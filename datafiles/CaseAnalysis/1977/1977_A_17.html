Govt. Of Andhra Pradesh & Anr. Etc v Anne Venkatesware Etc. Etc

Supreme Court of India

17 February 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

A.C. Gupta, P.N. Bhagwati

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 169; (1977) 3 SCC 298; (1977) SCC (Cr) 508; AIR 1977 SC 1096; 1977 CRLJ 935; [1977] 3 S.C.R. 7

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Defence & Security Forces

Keywords: Defence Regulations

Summary: Criminal - Imprisonment - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, s. 428 - Prisons Act, 1894, ss. 3 and 59 - Whether period of detention undergone by accused in before their conviction could be treated as part of period of imprisonment and be set off against imprisonment - Held, yes - S. 428 of CrPC, 1973 provides that remand detention shall be set off against term of imprisonment imposed on accused person on conviction - Liability of accused to undergo imprisonment on such conviction shall be restricted to remaining term of imprisonment - Only exception is period of preventive detention - Appeal allowed.
