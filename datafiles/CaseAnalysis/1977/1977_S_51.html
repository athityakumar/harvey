Brahmanand v Smt. Kaushalya Devi and Another

Supreme Court of India

11 April 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, R.S. Sarkaria, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 116; (1977) 3 SCC 1; AIR 1977 SC 1198; 1977 (2) RCJ 272; 1977 (2) RCR(Rent) 39; 1977 (2) RentLR 15; [1977] 3 S.C.R. 485

Case Digest

Subject: Air Law

Summary: U.P. (Temporary) Control of Rent and Eviction Act, 1947, s. 7C - Tenancy - Payment of rent - Whether Court deposit is equivalent to payment of rent to landlord - Held, if the landlord refuses under certain circumstances, then a court deposit will be the remedy - Appeal Allowed -
