P. Chitharanja Menon & Ors. v A. Balakrishnan & Ors

Supreme Court of India

25 April 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

P.S. Kailasam, M. Hameedullah Beg, A.C. Gupta

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 78; (1977) 3 SCC 255; (1977) SCC (L&S) 378; AIR 1977 SC 1720; 1977 LabIC 1215; [1977] 3 S.C.R. 687; 1977 SLJ 528; 1977 (2) SLR 289; 1977 UJ 420

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution, art. 226 - Delay and laches - Service matter - Promotions to posts of Panchayat Officers in Kerala - Integration of services of PEOs ( Madras Village Panchayat Act in Malabar area) and Panchayat Officers (Travancore Cochin Panchayats Act) - Appellants promoted to higher posts before respondents (PEOs ) were integrated in service on 01-01-1962 and confirmed by various order - Promotions and Gradation List challenged in 1974 - Held, respondents are not entitled to challenge the promotions - Appeal Allowed.
