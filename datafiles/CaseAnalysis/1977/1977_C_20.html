Bata Shoe Company Limited v City of Jabalpur Corporation

Supreme Court of India

11 March 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, P.N. Shingal

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 349; (1977) 2 SCC 472; AIR 1977 SC 955; [1977] 3 S.C.R. 182

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Prohibition Act, Board Of Revenue Act, 1913

Summary: CPC, 1908, s. 9 - Suits impliedly barred - C.P. and Berar Municipal Act, 1922, s. 84(3) - Whether jurisdiction of Civil Courts in suit challenging any valuation, assessment or levy of octroi is ousted by s. 84(3) of the 1922 Act - Held, it expressly prohibits a challenge to a valuation, assessment or levy 'in any other manner.....than is provided in this Act' - And the power of 'any other authority than is provided in this Act ' to entertain an objection - Appeal No C.A1923 of 1972 Dismissed; Appeal No.C. A. 1924 of 1972 Allowed.
