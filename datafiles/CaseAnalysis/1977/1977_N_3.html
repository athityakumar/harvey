State Of Orissa & Anr v N.N. Swamy & Ors. Etc

Supreme Court of India

27 January 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

P.K. Goswami, P.N. Shingal

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 155; (1977) SCC (L&S) 301; (1977) 2 SCC 508; AIR 1977 SC 1237; 1977 LabIC 558; 1977 (2) LLN 5; [1977] 2 S.C.R. 774; 1977 SLJ 337; 1977 (1) SLR 463

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution, arts. 16 and 311 - Service Matter - Promotion - Service in Private College before its taken over by Govt. cannot be obliterated - Requirement for promotion to the post of readers - Candidate must be drawing certain amount of salary on specified date - Constitutionality - Requirement, held, discriminatory - Appeal Dismissed.
