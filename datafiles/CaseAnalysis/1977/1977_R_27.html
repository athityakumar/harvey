Nai Bahu v Ramnarayan and Others

Supreme Court of India

14 October 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

P.K. Goswami, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 113; (1978) 1 SCC 58; AIR 1978 SC 22; 1978 (1) RCJ 102; 1978 (1) RCR(Rent) 211; 1978 (3) RentLR 53; [1978] 1 S.C.R. 723; 1977 UJ 712

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure; Rent Control; Tort

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil procedure , 1908, O. 23, r. 3 - Registration Act, 1908, s. 17 - Compromise decree in eviction suit - (A) Compromise in suit - Satisfaction of court - (B) Construction of compromise decree for eviction - Decree executable only after five years - Whether incorporated a lease for five years and is invalid in absence of registration? - Held, execution of compromise decree - Further held, High Court was wrong in holding that a lease was created by compromise and that decree was ineffective on account of non-registration - Appeal allowed.
