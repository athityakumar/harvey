Tula Ram and Others v Kishore Singh

Supreme Court of India

05 October 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, P.S. Kailasam

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 397; (1977) 4 SCC 459; (1977) SCC (Cr) 621; AIR 1977 SC 2401; 1978 CRLJ 8; 1977 CrLR(SC) 453; [1978] 1 S.C.R. 615

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: CrPC, 1973, ss. 156(3), 190(1)(a), 200, 202 and 204 - Complaint - Investigation Order u/s. 156(3) of the Code - Final report submitted by police - Issue of Process - Validity - Held, Magistrate can issue process u/s. 204 after recording statement of complainant and his witnesses - Appeal dismissed.
