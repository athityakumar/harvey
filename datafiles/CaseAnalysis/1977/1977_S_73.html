Prithvi Raj Taneja v State Of Madhya Pradesh And Others

Supreme Court of India

18 January 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

Hans Raj Khanna, R.S. Sarkaria, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 244; (1977) 1 SCC 684; AIR 1977 SC 1560; [1977] 2 S.C.R. 633

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Land and Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894 - Land Acquisition - Compensation - Determination of market value of land - Not satisfied with award of ADJ took matter HC - Whether assesssment made by HC was justified? - Held, there is an element of guess-work inherent in most cases involving determination of market value of acquired land, but essential thing is to keep in view relevant factors prescribed by Act and HC has taken into consideration relevant factors - Therefore assessment of market value of acquired land is correct - Appeal dismissed.
