Sunder Dass v Ram Prakash

Supreme Court of India

24 February 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 177; (1977) 2 SCC 662; AIR 1977 SC 1201; 1977 (1) RCJ 575; 1977 (2) RCR(Rent) 143; 1977 (1) RentLR 822; [1977] 3 S.C.R. 60

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property; Rent Control

Keywords: Evacuee Property & Displaced Persons

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 47 - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958, s. 50, Proviso 3 - Tenancy - Executability of decree for eviction - Duty of Executing Court - Addition of Proviso in s. 3 of 1958 Act with retrospective effect - Decree, if rendered null and void by virtue of s. 50 - Civil Court held, had no inherent jurisdiction to entertain suit and decree was a nullity - Appeal dismissed.
