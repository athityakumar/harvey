Minu B. Mehta and Another v Balkrishna Ramchandra Nayan and Another

Supreme Court of India

28 January 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

P.S. Kailasam, M. Hameedullah Beg, A.N. Ray

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 369; (1977) 2 SCC 441; 1977 ACJ 118; AIR 1977 SC 1248; 1977 (79) BomLR 380; [1977] 47 Comp Cas 736; [1977] 2 S.C.R. 886

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Corporate

Keywords: Quantum Of Damages, General And Special Damages

Summary: Motor Vehicles Act 1939 - Motor vehicle Accident - Claim for compensation - Accident cause due to mechanical defect in vehicle - As per the provision of ss. 95 and 110A - Liable if latent defect - Proof of negligence necessary for holding liable - Liability of owner or Insurance Company is to be determined - Owner held to be negligent - Appeal Dismissed
