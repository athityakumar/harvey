K. Karunakaran v T. V. Eachara Warrier

Supreme Court of India

16 November 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

P.K. Goswami, V.D. Tulzapurkar

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 121; (1978) 1 SCC 18; (1978) SCC (Cr) 32; AIR 1978 SC 290; 1978 CRLJ 339; 1977 CrLR(SC) 521; 1978 MLJ 237; [1978] 2 S.C.R. 209

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Tort

Keywords: Code of Criminal Procedure 1898, Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 (as amended by Act No.25 of 2005 & Act No.2 of 2006)

Summary: Criminal - Code of Criminal Procedure , 1973, s. 340 - CrPC, 1898, s. 476 B - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 136 - Appeal by special leave against High Court order of sanctioning a complaint for perjury an offence u/s. 193 IPC, 1860 - After enquiry u/s. 340(1) of CrPC, 1973 - For giving false affidavit by Home Minister in writ of Habeas Corpus - Held, interference by Supreme Court - Appeal dismissed.
