Trimbak Gangadhar Telang and Another v Ramchandra Ganesh Bhide and Others

Supreme Court of India

19 January 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

Jaswant Singh, M. Hameedullah Beg

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 376; (1977) 2 SCC 437; AIR 1977 SC 1222; 1977 UJ 167

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Constitution, arts. 133, 226 and 227 - Appellate jurisdiction of Supreme Court - When interference with exercise of discretionary power u/arts. 226 and 227 is possible - Held, only when an order of a Tribunal is violative of the fundamental basic principles of justice and fair play or where a patent or flagrant error in procedure or law has crept or where order results in manifest injustice - Appeal Dismissed.
