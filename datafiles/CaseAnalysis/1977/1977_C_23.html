Commissioner Of Income Tax, West Bengal v Tollygunge Club Ltd

Supreme Court of India

15 March 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 187; (1977) 2 SCC 790; AIR 1977 SC 1343; 1977 CTR 195; [1977] 107 ITR 776; [1977] 3 S.C.R. 225; 1977 UJ 312

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1922, ss. 3 and 4 - Income Tax - Taxable Income - Reasolution of General Body - Fee for admission to club enclosure - Charged in addition to levying from race goer surcharge, for local charities - Test for determie that includibility of assessee's receipts from surcharge, in its taxable income - Held, surcharge received by could not be regarded as income assessable to tax - True test is whether the amount sought to be deducted, in truth, never reached the assessee as his income - Appeal Dismissed.
