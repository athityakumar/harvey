Bhagabandas Agarwalla v Bhagwandas Kanu and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 February 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 179; (1977) 2 SCC 646; AIR 1977 SC 1120; 1977 (1) RCJ 572; 1977 (1) RCR(Rent) 754; 1977 (1) RentLR 770; [1977] 3 S.C.R. 75; 1977 UJ 194

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 106 - Monthly tenancy - Principle of interpretation to determine validity of notice to quit - Held, in the circumstances a valid notice of tenancy with effect from the mid-night of 31-10-1962 - A notice to quit must be construed not with a desire to find faults in it, which would render it defective, but it must be construed but resmagis valeat quam pereat - Appeal Allowed.
