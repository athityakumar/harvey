Namer Ali Choudhery and Others v Central Inland Water Transport Corporation Limited and Another

Supreme Court of India

16 November 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

N.L. Untwalia, P.S. Kailasam

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 27; (1977) 4 SCC 575; (1978) SCC (L&S) 19; AIR 1978 SC 275; 1978 (36) FLR 99; 1978 LabIC 166; 1978 (1) LLJ 167; 1978 (1) LLN 300; [1978] 2 S.C.R. 205; 1978 SLJ 205; 1977 UJ 744

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Laws - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 33 C(2) and 33 C(1) - Uttar Pradesh Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - Settlement - Claim of benefit under, by workmen - Maintainability of application u/s. 33 C(2) - Considering need of existence of dispute as to amount of money due - Held not necessary - Application could not be rejected on ground that there is no dispute about money due - And sub-s. (1) not attracted - Appeal allowed.
