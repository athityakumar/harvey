Krishna Kumar Mediratta v Phulchand Agarwala and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 January 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Hameedullah Beg, A.N. Ray

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 153; (1977) 2 SCC 5; AIR 1977 SC 984; 1977 (35) FLR 124; [1977] 2 S.C.R. 702

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Mines and Minerals (Regulation and Development) Act, 1957, s. 19 - Mineral Concession Rules, 1960, rr. 9(2) and 19 - Application for prospecting licence - Payment of fees - Payment of less fee due to over-sight - Application not void - Effect of subsequent acceptance of deficit fees - Held, it was bound to proceed on the assumption that there was a proper application before it valid from the date of filing it - It was precluded by its own dealings from denying, the validity of the application - Appeal Allowed.
