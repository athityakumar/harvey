Hem Chand v The Delhi Cloth & General Mills Co. Ltd.& Ors

Supreme Court of India

02 August 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

P.S. Kailasam, Y.V. Chandrachud

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 51; (1977) 3 SCC 483; AIR 1977 SC 1986; 1977 (2) RCJ 438; 1977 (2) RCR(Rent) 440; 1977 (2) RentLR 451; [1978] 1 S.C.R. 241; 1977 UJ 519

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958, ss. 15, 15(1), 14(1)(a) - Tenancy - Non-compliance of ss. 14 (1)(a), 15 (1) of Act - Failure of tenant to pay arrears of rent - Order to pay or deposit rent within one month from date of order - (A) Right to recover possession - (B) Discretion to extend time as u/s. 15 (1) of Act - Held, it cannot by itself be said that right to obtain an order for recovery of possession accrued to landlord - Rent Controllerhas no discretion to extend time - Matter remitted to the High Court.
