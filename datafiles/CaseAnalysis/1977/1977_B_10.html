Baikunthi Devi and Others v Mahendra Nath and Another

Supreme Court of India

18 March 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, R.S. Sarkaria, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 342; (1977) 2 SCC 496; AIR 1977 SC 1514; 1977 UJ 296

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Specific Relief Act, 1963, ss. 12(2) and 20 - U.P. Consolidation of Holdings Act, 1954, s. 30 - Contract of sale of agricultural land - While pendency of consolidation proceeding - Proceedings closed - Suit for specific performance thereafter - Held, since substantially the almost the same land ( less a tiny bit) was allotted in the new chak, no bar to a decree being granted - Appeal Dismissed.
