Gudikanti Narasimhulu And Ors v Public Prosecutor, High Court Of Andhra Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

06 December 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 86; (1978) SCC (Cr) 115; (1978) 1 SCC 240; AIR 1978 SC 429; 1978 CRLJ 502; 1978 CrLR(SC) 33; [1978] 2 S.C.R. 371

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal

Keywords: Code of Criminal Procedure 1898, Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 (as amended by Act No.25 of 2005 & Act No.2 of 2006)

Summary: Criminal - Practice & Procedure - Acquital - Conviction - Bail - Petitioners were acquitted by Trial court but HC set aside said acquittal in appeal and during pendency of appeal petitioners abused trust reposed by court allowing them to be at large - Hence, instant petition.

Held, petitioners have suffered imprisonment around a year and a reasonable prediction of time at the hearing of the appeal many take us to a few years ahead. Which means that incarceration during that period may possibly prove an irrevocable injury if the appeal ends in their favour And Judge's report about the conduct of the petitioners while in sub-jail is not uncomplimentary. Thus, cumulative result of these considerations persuades to direct the petitioners to be enlarged on bail, namely, on their own bond to appear to receive sentence in the event of an adverse verdct from Court. However they will be put on conditions that petitioners will keep out of the village except for one day in a week and they will be allowed to enter the village on that day only after reporting to the police at the police station. Further, they shall leave the village the next day and they will report to the police when they are departing from the village and this will help the police to have a vigilant eye on the petitioners and prevent them, from doing mischief inside the village and incidentally help the petitioners carry on their agricultural operations by once-a-week supervision. Hence, if the petitioners choose to report daily before any the therapeutic centre for psychic reformation, such as a transcendental meditation centre it is left to their option but may eventually prove to their good. Petition disposed of.
