State of Haryana and Another v Rattan Singh

Supreme Court of India

22 March 1977

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer

Where Reported

1977 Indlaw SC 2; (1977) SCC (L&S) 298; (1977) 2 SCC 491; AIR 1977 SC 1512; 1977 (34) FLR 264; 1977 LabIC 845; 1982 (1) LLJ 46; 1977 (2) LLN 50; 1977 SLJ 408; 1977 (1) SLR 750; 1977 UJ 298

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution, art. 311 - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 3 - Domestic enquiry - Extent to which Rules of evidence under Evidence Act are applicable - Held, in a domestic enquiry the strict and sophisticated rules of evidence under the Evidence Act may not apply - Appeal Allowed.
