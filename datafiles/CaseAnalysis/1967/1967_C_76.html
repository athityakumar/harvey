 S. Rm. Ct. Pl. Palaniappa Chettiar v Commissioner of Income Tax, Madras

Supreme Court of India

26 October 1967

Case Analysis

Bench

Vaidynathier Ramaswami, G.K. Mitter, Kailas Nath Wanchoo, K.S. Hegde, R.S. Bachawat

Where Reported

1967 Indlaw SC 352; AIR 1968 SC 678; 1968 CRLJ 657; [1968] 68 ITR 221; [1968] 2 S.C.R. 55

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Assessment Year

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Family & Personal - Income Tax Act, 1961 - Whether managing director's remuneration, commission and sitting fees received by the assessee (Karta) are assessable as the income of the Hindu Undivided Family? - No detriment to the joint family assets - No part of the joint family property was spent in earning the remuneration - Held, it could not be assessable as income of HUF - Appeal allowed.
