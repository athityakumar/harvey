Phoolchand and Another v Gopal Lal

Supreme Court of India

10 March 1967

Case Analysis

Bench

Kailas Nath Wanchoo, R.S. Bachawat, Vaidynathier Ramaswami

Where Reported

1967 Indlaw SC 457; AIR 1967 SC 1470; 1968 (2) SCJ 155; [1967] 3 S.C.R. 153

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Practice & Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 41, r.1 - Appellant filed suit for partition of joint family property in which preliminary decree was passed - Before passing final decree father and mother of appellant died - Appellant's brother claimed his share of father as same is bequeathed to him by way of will executed by father - Appellant claim his share in share of his mother as same was sold to him by mother - Shares re-distributed by trial court but no final decree prepared - High Court allowed appeal of appellant's brother against distribution and to stay proceedings related to preparation of final decree and shares re-distributed - Whether appellant entitled to share of his mother as well? - Held, that will executed by father in favour of appellant's brother genuine - Appellant not entitled to take advantage of share of mother and same must be equally distributed - Appeal dismissed.
