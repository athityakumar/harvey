Commissioner of Income Tax, Bombay City-1, Bombay v National Storage Private Limited, Bombay

Supreme Court of India

26 April 1967

Case Analysis

Bench

S.M. Sikri, Vaidynathier Ramaswami, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1967 Indlaw SC 263; AIR 1968 SC 70; 1968 CRLJ 806; [1967] 66 ITR 596; [1967] 3 S.C.R. 813

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Indian Income Tax Act, 1922, ss. 2 (4), 9, 10 and 12 - Cinematograph Film Rules, 1948 - Whether income earned from letting out vaults by respondent assessee is assessable u/s 10 or u/s 9? - Court ascertained that assessee was carrying on activities in nature of trade - Assessee was rendering special services to its licensees of vaults - Income was not earned merely being licensees of vaults - Held, income assessable u/s 10 - Appeal dismissed.
