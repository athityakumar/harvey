 Ram Bachan Lal v State of Bihar

Supreme Court of India

28 February 1967

Case Analysis

Bench

S.M. Sikri, K. Subba Rao, J.C. Shah, V. Ramaswami, C.A. Vaidyialingam

Where Reported

1967 Indlaw SC 461; AIR 1967 SC 1404; 1967 (1) ITJ 881; [1967] 3 S.C.R. 1

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Profession Tax, Bihar and Orissa Municipalities Act, 1922

Summary: Constitution - Municipalities & Local Government - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14, 19(1)(g), 31 and 32 - Bihar and Orissa Municipalities Act, 1922, ss. 4(1), 82(1)(ff) and 82(1), Proviso (iv) to ss. 82(1), 150A, 150B, 150C, 150D, 150E , 388 and 389 and Sch .IV - Bihar Municipal Act, 1953 - Indian Income Tax Act, 1922 - The Governor of Bihar applied the provisions of s. 82(1)(ff) and ss. 150A to 150E of the act of 1922 - The petitioner was not aware that these provisions had been applied to the area - The Governor of Bihar sanctioned the imposition by the Dehri-Dalmianagar Notified area Committee of the tax on trades, professions, callings and employments - This is alleged to infringe petitioner's Fundamental Right u/arts. 14, 19 and 31 - (A) Whether ss. 388 and 389 of the Income Tax Act confer arbitrary powers on the Govt.? - S. 4(1) of the Bihar and Orissa Municipal Act ,this gives sufficient guidance to the Govt. and no arbitrary power has been conferred on the Govt. by ss. 388 and 389 - Held ,Constitution of Municipalities and Notified Area Committees - ss. 388 and 389 do not confer arbitrary powers on the Govt. - Provisions do not violate art. 14 of the Constitution - (B) Whether the directions given u/s. 82 (1) of the Bihar and Orissa Municipalities Act is void on the basis of not giving any indication as to the circumstances under which the Govt. should direct the Commrs. to levy the tax u/s. 82(1)(ff)? - State Govt's powers to give directions to Commrs. of Notified Area Committee to levy tax - Such direction to be given only if the Commrs. do not carry out their duty properly or when necessary in the circumstances of the case - Held, Proviso (iv) to s. 82 (1) is not void merely because the circumstances under which the Govt. should direct the Commrs. to levy tax u/s. 82 (1)(ff) is not indicated - Petition dismissed.
