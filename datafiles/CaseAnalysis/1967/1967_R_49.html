State of Madras v Richardson and Cruddas Limited

Supreme Court of India

05 May 1967

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, S.M. Sikri, Vaidynathier Ramaswami

Where Reported

1967 Indlaw SC 75; 1968 (21) STC 245

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - Central Sales Tax Act, 1956 - Respondents carry on business as 'engineers and contractors' and as dealers in iron and steel goods and refrigerating and cooling units etc. - Assessing authority included in taxable turnover of respondents for assessment to Central sales tax items received by respondents for fabrication, supply and erection of steel structures for customers outside state - Fabrication and installation of 'bottle coolers' under orders from customers at different places - Whether Contract for fabrication and installation of 'bottle coolers' was a contract for sale or works contract? - Held, contract being one for supplying for an inclusive price a specifically designed fabricated unit to be assembled and installed by specially trained technicians in premises of customer, it was not a contract for sale of a unit or different parts of unit as specific goods, but a works contract - Appeal dismissed.
