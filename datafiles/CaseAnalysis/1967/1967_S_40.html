Government of Madras v Simpson and Company Limited

Supreme Court of India

04 May 1967

Case Analysis

Bench

S.M. Sikri, J.C. Shah, Vaidynathier Ramaswami

Where Reported

1967 Indlaw SC 68; 1968 (21) STC 21

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Keywords: Madras General Sales Tax Act, 1939

Summary: Sales Tax - Madras General Sales Tax Act, 1939 - Supply and fitment of diesel engines to chassis of customers - Whether transaction was one of sale or works contract? - Held, engine was contracted to be delivered as an engine and afterwards affixed to customer's Dodge bus - Appellate Tribunal was right in its conclusion that there was no works contract in this case - Appeal allowed.
