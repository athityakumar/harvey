Collector of Akola and Others v Ramchandra and Others

Supreme Court of India

30 August 1967

Case Analysis

Bench

J.M. Shelat, S.M. Sikri, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1967 Indlaw SC 204; AIR 1968 SC 244; 1968 (70) BomLR 128; 1968 (1) SCJ 525; [1968] 1 S.C.R. 401

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Indian Limitation Act, 1908 - Bombay Land Requisition Act, 1948, ss. 4, 5 and 15 - Whether order passed by appellant passed u/s. 15 for aqcuisition of respondent's land was right? - Words in s. 5 are sufficiently wide to include any public purpose whether temporary ot otherwise - Initiation of proceedings under Land Acquisition Act after requisitioning lands u/s. 5(1) of Act does not and cannot mean abuse of power under provisions of Act - Held, in case of urgency there in nothing act to prevent authority to initiate proceedings for permanent acquisition - Matter remitted to HC.
