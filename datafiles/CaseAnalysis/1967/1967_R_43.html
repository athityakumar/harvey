Hans Raj v Rattan Chand

Supreme Court of India

03 April 1967

Case Analysis

Bench

G.K. Mitter, Kailas Nath Wanchoo, Vishishtha Bhargava

Where Reported

1967 Indlaw SC 451; AIR 1967 SC 1780; 1968 MahLJ 164; [1967] 3 S.C.R. 365

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Execution Proceeding, Sale Of Property, Decreeholder

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 - O. 21, r. 58, O. 21, r. 63 - Provincial Insolvency Act, 1907, ss. 3, 4, 5, 22 and 68 - Whether application made after 21 days to set aside the wrongful attachment of property by the receiver of property in which the insolvent had no interest should be u/s. 68 of the Provincial Insolvency Act, or by s. 4 where in there is no period of limitation prescribed? - Held, the application being one u/s. 68 was incompetent on the ground of limitation after the lapse of 21 days - S. 4 is not the proper head for proceeding for relief - Appeal dismissed.
