Dr. S. Dutt v State of Uttar Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

18 August 1965

Case Analysis

Bench

Mohammad Hidayatullah, Kailas Nath Wanchoo, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1965 Indlaw SC 447; AIR 1966 SC 523; 1966 CRLJ 459; [1966] 1 S.C.R. 493

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 193, 195, 465, 471 -Scope of, 465, 471 IPC - 'Dutt examined as an expert witness by defence in a Sessions trial - Prosecution alleged that Dr. Dutt committed forgery of certain diploma produced in Court during course of his evidence and he has used these forged documents as genuine - Report lodged alleging that Dr. Dutt committed an offence u/s. 466/477 IPC while giving evidence in Sessions trial - Challenged - Broad distinction between offences - s. 465 deals with offence of forgery by making of a false document and s.471 with offence of using forged document dishonestly or fraudulently,s. 193 deals.with giving or fabricating of fals.e evidence, s. 196 with corruptly using evidence known to be false - Gist of offence in first group is making of a false document and gist of offences in second group is procuring of false circumstances or making of a document containing a false statement so that a judicial officer may form a wrong opinion in a judicial proceeding on faith of false evidence - Held, it is not case of prosecution here that Dr. Dutt diploma personally in any one of three ways mentioned in s. 464 but it is case that diploma was a forged and false document and he used it as genuine - Dr. Dutt did not intend to cause wrongful gain to one person or wrongful loss to another person when he brought diploma, whether forged or not, into court - He did not act dishonestly - His intention was not to cause any one to act to his disadvantage because he did not bring diploma voluntarily but under orders of court - He did not, therefore, have intent to cause voluntarily, a course of conduct in any person to that person's disadvantage Offence in present case did not fall withinss 465/471, IPC and prosecution launched cannot be allowed to go on - Appeal allowed.
