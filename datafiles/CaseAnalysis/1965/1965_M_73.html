Bishan Paul v Mothu Ram

Supreme Court of India

24 March 1965

Case Analysis

Bench

Mohammad Hidayatullah, Vaidynathier Ramaswami

Where Reported

1965 Indlaw SC 515; AIR 1965 SC 1994

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - East Punjab Urban Rent Restriction Act, s. 13(2)(i) - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 65 - Displaced Persons (Compensation and Rehabilitation) Rules, 1955 - Whether Appellate Authority was correct in confirming order of eviction passed against appellant by Rent Controller, Ludhiana, u/s. 13 of East Punjab Urban Rent Restriction Act, 1949? - Held, landlord's right to bring a suit for ejectment need not necessarily depend on issuance of certificate - U/s. 65 of Code, title is deemed to commence from date of auction and not when sale becomes absolute - Referred to Waryam Singh v Amaranth - Appeal dismissed.
