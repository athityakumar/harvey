United Bank of Indian Limited v Lekharam Sonaram and Company and Others

Supreme Court of India

01 February 1965

Case Analysis

Bench

Vaidynathier Ramaswami, R.S. Bachawat, Raghubar Dayal, J.R. Madholkar

Where Reported

1965 Indlaw SC 368; AIR 1965 SC 1591; [1965] 35 Comp Cas 471; 1965 (2) MLJ(SC) 5

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Land & Property - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, ss. 58(f) and 59 - Registration Act, 1908, s. 17 - Transfer of property - Essence of mortgage by deposit of title deeds - When parties choose to reduce contract to writing, registration if required - Held, actual handing over of tile deeds with intention of them to constitute a security is essence, but is it is in writing then deposit and document both form integral parts of transaction - Registration required - Appeal allowed.
