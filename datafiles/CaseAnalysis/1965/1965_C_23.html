Commissioner Of Income-Tax, Andhra Pradesh v The Cocanada Bank Ltd. Kakinada

Supreme Court of India

02 April 1965

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Subba Rao, S.M. Sikri, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1965 Indlaw SC 100; AIR 1966 SC 47; [1965] 35 Comp Cas 655; [1965] 57 ITR 306; 1965 (2) MLJ(SC) 36; [1965] 3 S.C.R. 619

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Corporate; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax Act , 1922, s. 24(2) - Finance Act, 1955 - Income Tax - Scope of s. 24(2)- 'Profits and gains of the assessee from the same business' - Interest Income from securities (part of assessee's trading assets) - Whether business loss of the earlier year could be set off against entire income including interest on securities - Held, could be set off - Appeal Dismissed.
