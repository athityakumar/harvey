 Bhaiyalal v Harikishan Singh and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 February 1965

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, Kailas Nath Wanchoo, S.M. Sikri, Mohammad Hidayatullah, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1965 Indlaw SC 385; AIR 1965 SC 1557; 1966 (2) SCJ 77; [1965] 2 S.C.R. 877

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Socio-Economic - Administrative - Constitution - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 341(1) - What is test to determine whether a schedule caste falls u/art. 341 of Constitution ? - Held, an elaborate enquiry before notification is conducted and it is as a result of this enquiry that social justice is sought to be done to castes, races or tribes as may appear to be necessary - Public notification issued by President in this regard serves as an evidence for determination of schedule castes u/art. 341 - Notification issued by President with reference to different areas is not, therefore, outside his authority u/art. 341(1) - Appeal dismissed.
