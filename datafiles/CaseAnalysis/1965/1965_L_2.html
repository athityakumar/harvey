Lakhmi Chand Khemani v Smt. Kauran Devi

Supreme Court of India

05 November 1965

Case Analysis

Bench

A.K. Sarkar, R.S. Bachawat, J.R. Madholkar

Where Reported

1965 Indlaw SC 414; AIR 1966 SC 1003; [1966] 2 S.C.R. 544

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958 - Ajmer Rent Control Act, 1952 - Slums Area Act, s. 19(1) - Whether High Court was right in its view that notwithstanding s. 19 of Slum Areas Act rendering decree, appellant ceased to be a tenant within meaning of Act of 1958 because of that decree? - Appellant has filed an appeal in HC from this judgment of learned Subordinate Judge and that appeal is pending - It will be for HC now to decide correctness of decree of learned Subordinate Judge of 12-08-1964 - No interference - Appeal dismissed.
