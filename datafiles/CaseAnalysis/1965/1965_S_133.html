 Mckenzies Limited v State of Maharashtra

Supreme Court of India

19 January 1965

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, Mohammad Hidayatullah, S.M. Sikri, R.S. Bachawat, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1965 Indlaw SC 484; 1965 (16) STC 518

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - Bombay Sales Tax Act, 1953, s.34 - Appellant engaged in business of body-building on motor chassis, entered into a contract for construction of 218 bodies with Govt. of India - Whether contract was for sale of goods or for work and labour? - Held, there is no clause in the agreement which militates against contract being a contract for sale of goods - Transaction held to be a contract for sale and not a contract for work and labour - Reading contract as a whole, it is held that it is a contract for sale of goods and not a contract for work and labour - Appeal dismissed.
