Biram Prakash and Others v Narendra Dass and Others

Supreme Court of India

06 October 1965

Case Analysis

Bench

Vaidynathier Ramaswami, A.K. Sarkar, Raghubar Dayal

Where Reported

1965 Indlaw SC 501; AIR 1966 SC 1011; 1966 (2) MLJ(SC) 30

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Family & Personal; Land & Property; Trusts & Associations

Summary: Civil Procedure - Trusts & Association - Land & Property - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 11, O. 1, r. 8, O. 21, r. 90 - Test of question of legal necessity - Question of legal necessity for impugned transaction - Regard must be paid to actual pressure on estate, immediate danger to be averted or benefit to be conferred upon trust estate - Held, applying the test in the present case, transaction of sale D/- 14-06-1945 in favour of respondents 3 and 4 was beneficial to the trust - Appeal dismissed.
