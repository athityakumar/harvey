Beharilal Shyamsunder v Sales Tax Officer, Cui Circle, Cuttack, and Another

Supreme Court of India

08 November 1965

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Subba Rao, S.M. Sikri, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1965 Indlaw SC 317; [1966] 60 ITR 260; 1966 (17) STC 508

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Constitution - Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Constitution of India, 1950, art.226 - Whether High Court right in dismissing Writ Petition seeking quashing of notification issued by Govt. of Orissa on ground that petitioner has not exhausted internal remedies under Oriss Sales Tax Act? - Sales Tax Tribunal could only decide disputes between assessee and Commr. in terms of provisions of Madras General Sales Tax Act, 1939, and the question of ultra vires was foreign to its jurisdiction - HC wrong in dismissing petition - Appeal allowed.
