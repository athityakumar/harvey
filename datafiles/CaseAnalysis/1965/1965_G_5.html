 State of Rajasthan and Others v Ghasilal

Supreme Court of India

21 January 1965

Case Analysis

Bench

S.M. Sikri, Kailas Nath Wanchoo, Mohammad Hidayatullah, J.C. Shah, J.R. Madholkar

Where Reported

1965 Indlaw SC 90; AIR 1965 SC 1454; [1965] 2 S.C.R. 805; 1965 (16) STC 318

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Keywords: Rajasthan Sales Tax Rules, Rajasthan Sales Tax Rules 1955

Summary: Indirect Tax - Sales Tax - Rajasthan Sales Tax Act, 1954, ss. 16(1)(b), 7, 7(2), 10, 3 and 5 - Rajasthan Sales Tax Rules, 1955, r. 31 - (A) What is condition to be fulfilled for imposition of penalty u/s. 16(1)(b) - Is it under charging sections, s. 3 r/w s. 5 or when tax is ascertained ? - Held, charging section creates a liability to pay tax but till tax payable is ascertained by assessing authority u/s. 10, or by assessee u/r. 7(2), no tax can be said to be due within s. 16(1)(b) - (B) Scope of r. 31 - Whether rules come into picture before assessment has been completed? - Further held, rules come into picture only when assessment has been completed - Appeals dismissed.
