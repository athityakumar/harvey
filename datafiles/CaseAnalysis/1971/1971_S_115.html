Sri Venkateswara Rice, Ginning And Groundnut Oil Mill Contractors Co. And Others v State Of Andhra Pradesh & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

23 August 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 234; (1971) 2 SCC 650; AIR 1972 SC 51; 1971 (2) ALT(SC) 201; 1972 (1) MLJ(SC) 61; [1972] 1 S.C.R. 346; 1971 (28) STC 599; 1972 TAXLR 1601

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Keywords: Andhra Pradesh General Sales Tax Act 1956

Summary: Sales Tax - AP General Sales Tax Act, 1957, ss. 5, 6 and 3, item - Central Sales Tax Act, 1956, ss. 14 and 15 - (A) Provision of s. 5 r/ w sch. 3, item 6 of 1957 Act - Determination of taxable event in regard to purchase tax - (B) Precedents - Whether one co-ordinate Bench of same High Court can take a view contrary to decision given earlier by another Bench of that Court - Held, very act of purchase by assessee (miller of declared goods) attracts liability to pay tax, irrespective of his subsequent dealings in those goods - Cannot take as that one is bound by such decision - Appeal dismissed.
