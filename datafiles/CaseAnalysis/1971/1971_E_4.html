Ellerman Lines Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax, West Bengal I

Supreme Court of India

22 October 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 447; (1972) 4 SCC 474; (1974) SCC (Tax) 304; AIR 1972 SC 524; 1972 CTR 7; [1971] 82 ITR 913; [1972] 2 S.C.R. 168

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 5(8), 10(2)(vi-b) - Income Tax Rules, 1922, r. 33 - Development rebate - Assessee non-resident British Shipping Company - ITO following second basis in r. 33 of 1922 Rules - Not taking into account investment allowance granted in UK assessments - Also instructions given by Central Board of Revenue not followed - Validity of assessment - Assessment is invalid - Appeal dismissed.
