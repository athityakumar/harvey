 Union of India and Another v B. N. Ananti Padmanabiah Etc

Supreme Court of India

22 April 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, S.M. Sikri, C.A. Vaidyialingam, K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 652; (1971) 3 SCC 278; (1971) SCC (Cr) 535; AIR 1971 SC 1836; 1971 CRLJ 1287; [1971] S.C.R. 460; [1971] Supp S.C.R. 460; 1971 UJ 601

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: CrPC, 1898, s. 429 - Prevention of Corruption Act, 1947, s. 5A - (A) CrPC, 1898, s. 429 - Reference to third Judge - A contention not raised before the third Judge - (B) Prevention of Corruption - Whether Magistrate of First-Class at Delhi can authorise the investigation of cases in the State of Assam - Held, the third Judge can deal with the whole case - Held, cannot authorise - Appeal Dismissed.
