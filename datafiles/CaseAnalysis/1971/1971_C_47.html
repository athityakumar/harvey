Commissioner Of Income Tax, West Bengal, v Balkrishan Malhotra

Supreme Court of India

28 July 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 216; (1971) 2 SCC 547; AIR 1971 SC 2219; [1971] 81 ITR 759; [1971] S.C.R. 951; [1971] Supp S.C.R. 951

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 34(3) - (A) Word 'assessment' - Interpretation of - (B) Interpretation of Statutes - Taxing statute - Held, the word 'assessment' in proviso to s. 34 (3) means not merely the computation of the income but also the determination of the tax Payable - Interpretation of a provision in a taxing statute rendered years back should not be easily departed from - Appeal Dismissed.
