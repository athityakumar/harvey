Har Sharan Verma v Tribhuvan Narain Singh, Chief Minister U.P. and

Supreme Court of India

16 March 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

S.M. Sikri, C.A. Vaidyialingam, J.M. Shelat

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 144; (1971) 1 SCC 616; AIR 1971 SC 1331; [1971] S.C.R. 1; [1971] Supp S.C.R. 1; 1971 UJ 482

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Constitution, arts. 164(4), 163, 164, 175, 177 - Appointment of Chief Minister - Challenged on the ground that he was not a member of either House of Legislature of the state at the time of his appointment - Maintainability of Challenge - Appointment held, cannot be challenged on the ground - Appeal Dismissed.
