Rohini Kumari v Narendra Singh

Supreme Court of India

02 December 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, K.S. Hegde

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 491; (1972) 1 SCC 1; AIR 1972 SC 459; [1972] 2 S.C.R. 657

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Women & Children

Keywords: Marriage & Divorce

Summary: Family & Personal - Hindu Marriage Act, 1955, s. 10(1)(a) - Hindu Adoptions And Maintenance Act, 1956 - Remarriage - Desertion - When proved - Held, there must be a determination to put an end to marital relation and cohabitation - Without animus deserendi there can be no desertion within meaning of s. 10(1)(a) of Act - Appeal dismissed.
