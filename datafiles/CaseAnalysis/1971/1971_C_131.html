Commissioner of Income Tax, West Bengal Ii v Dinesh Chandra H. Shah and Others

Supreme Court of India

27 August 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, K.S. Hegde

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 409; (1972) 3 SCC 231; AIR 1972 SC 29; [1971] 82 ITR 367

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 34(1)(b) - Chargeability of income on the part of reassessing officer - Change of opinion, regarding it , different from his own previous opinion or that of his predecessor in office - If action u/s. 34(1)(b) of the Act - Held, a mere change of opinion, does not justify taking action under the provision - Appeal dismissed.
