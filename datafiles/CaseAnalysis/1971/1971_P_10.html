Smt. Sushila Devi v Pandit Krishna Kumar Missir and Others

Supreme Court of India

08 February 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 723; (1971) 3 SCC 146; AIR 1971 SC 2236; 1971 UJ 298

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal

Summary: Succession Act, 1925, s. 74 - Land and Property - Non-bequest of property to children of testator - If invalidate the will - Non-bequest of property to children of testator does not make the will invalid if the execution of will is satisfactorily proved - Appeal Dismissed.
