Chajoo Ram v Radhey Shyam and Another

Supreme Court of India

23 March 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

I.D. Dua, P. Jaganmohan Reddy, S.M. Sikri

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 812; (1971) SCC (Cr) 331; (1971) 1 SCC 774; AIR 1971 SC 1367; 1971 (2) ALT(SC) 277; 1971 CRLJ 1096; [1971] S.C.R. 172; [1971] Supp S.C.R. 172

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Practice & Procedure - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1898, ss. 476 and 479 A - Sanction of prosecution for perjury - Whether person filing sworn affidavit is a witness? - Held, person filing sworn affidavit is not a witness - Court must be satisfied, there is prima facie case of deliberate falsehood before sanctioning prosecution for perjury - Appeal allowed.
