Managing Director (M.I.G.), Hindustan Aeronautics limited and another balanagar v Ajit Prasad Tarway

Supreme Court of India

05 November 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 27; (1972) 3 SCC 195; AIR 1973 SC 76; 1971 (23) FLR 355; 1973 LabIC 407; 1972 (1) LLJ 170; 1972 UJ 195

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 115 - Practice & Procedure - Interference by High Court - When lower Appellate Courts order within its jurisdiction? - Held, HC should not interfere even if order is right or wrong or in accordance with law or not, unless it has exercised its jurisdiction illegally or with material irregularity - Appeal No. 841 of 1971 dismissed, Appeal No. 840 of 1971 allowed.
