Kumari N. Vasundara v State Of Mysore & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

15 April 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

I.D. Dua, J.M. Shelat, Vishishtha Bhargava

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 164; (1971) 2 SCC 22; AIR 1971 SC 1439; [1971] S.C.R. 381; [1971] Supp S.C.R. 381

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Service

Keywords: Eligibility - Merit

Summary: Constitution, art. 14 - Education - Constitutionality of r. 3 of Mysore Rules - For selection of candidates for admission to preprofessional / B. Sc. Part I course leading to MBBS in Govt. Medical Colleges - Rule imposing condition of residence for minimum period of ten yearn in State of Mysore in addition to condition of being domiciled in that State - Held, does not violate art. 14 of Constitution - Petition Dismissed.
