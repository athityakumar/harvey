Food Inspector, Calicut Corporation v Cherukattil Gopalan and Another

Supreme Court of India

06 May 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

C.A. Vaidyialingam, A.N. Ray

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 799; (1971) 2 SCC 322; (1971) SCC (Cr) 522; AIR 1971 SC 1725; 1971 (2) ALT(SC) 282; 1971 CRLJ 1277; [1971] S.C.R. 721; [1971] Supp S.C.R. 721

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Health & Drugs - Prevention of Food Adulteration Act, 1954, ss. 2(xiii), 7, 16(1)(a)(i) - Sale - Whether proof of dealership necessary in order to be punitively proceeded against? - Held, sale of sample to Food Inspector is a 'sale' - Proof of dealership not necessary - Once person effects sale of adulterated article, person is punishable under Act - Appeal allowed.
