Kanda Padayachi alias Kandaswamy v State of Tamil Nadu

Supreme Court of India

27 August 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

J.M. Shelat, I.D. Dua, Subimal Chandra Roy

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 240; (1971) SCC (Cr) 623; (1971) 2 SCC 641; AIR 1972 SC 66; 1972 CRLJ 11; 1972 (1) MLJ(SC) 70; [1972] 1 S.C.R. 450

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Tort

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Evidence Act, 1872, ss. 24, 25 & 26 - Meaning of confession - An admission of a fact, however incriminating, but not by itself establishing guilt of maker of such admission - If confession - Held, does not amount to confession within meaning of ss. 24 & 25 of 1872 Act - Appeal dismissed.
