Commissioner of Income Tax, West Bengal v Brij Lal Lohia and Another

Supreme Court of India

29 July 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 382; (1972) 4 SCC 432; 1974 (3) CTR 167; [1972] 84 ITR 273; 1972 TAXLR 1112; 1972 UJ 426

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961 - Genuiness of gift - Held, Tribunal took into consideration all the relevant facts and confirmed that Donor was capable of making gifts of amount involved - No interference - Gift was genuine - Appeal dismissed.
