Lakshmiji Sugar Mills Company Private Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax, New Delhi

Supreme Court of India

27 August 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, K.S. Hegde

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 408; (1971) 3 SCC 526; AIR 1972 SC 159; [1971] 82 ITR 376; [1972] 1 S.C.R. 465

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 10(2)(xv) - U.P. Sugar Cane (Regulation of Supply & Purchase) Act 1953 - Capital expenditure or Revenue expenditures - Determination - Contribution made by the owner of sugar mills under 1953 Act - For development of roads ( a Govt. property ) between the cane producing centres and his mills - Apart from the element of compulsion - Held, a revenue expenditure and not capital expenditure - Appeal allowed.
