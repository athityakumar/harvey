Firm of Harbanslal Jagmohandas and Another v Prabhudas Shivlal

Supreme Court of India

16 April 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

C.A. Vaidyialingam, A.N. Ray

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 165; (1971) 2 SCC 155; AIR 1973 SC 2056; [1971] S.C.R. 397; [1971] Supp S.C.R. 397

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 136 - Appeal by special leave - Revocation of special leave - Misstatements and untrue averments in leave application - Effect, would entail revocation of leave granted - Grounds taken on legal points - Held, grounds cannot be considered to be false or untrue statements on material facts or matters of importance - Appeal dismissed.
