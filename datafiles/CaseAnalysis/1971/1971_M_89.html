Raja Shantrunji v Moazmat Azmat Azim Khan & Ors

Supreme Court of India

21 April 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, C.A. Vaidyialingam

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 171; (1971) 2 SCC 200; AIR 1971 SC 1474; [1971] S.C.R. 433; [1971] Supp S.C.R. 433

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure; Agriculture & Agri. Products

Keywords: Execution Of Decree, Debt Laws

Summary: CPC, 1908, O. 47, r. 1 - U.P. Zamindars' Debt Reduction Act, 1953, s. 4 - Debt Laws - (A) Requirements for claiming relief - The requirement that the mortgaged property must be one 'charged under the decree' for obtaining relief against a decree is dispensed with by the 1962 Amendment - (B) Review of order under the Act - Order rejecting relief under the Act on ground that conditions of s.4 are not satisfied - If can be reviewed after the retrospective amendment of the s. 4 in 1962 - Held, only requirement is that the mortgaged property must consist of estate acquired under 1953 Act - Held, can be reviewed - Appeal Dismissed.
