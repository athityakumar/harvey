Navinchandra Chhotelal v Central Board Of Excise And Customs & Ors

Supreme Court of India

13 January 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

C.A. Vaidyialingam, J.M. Shelat

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 108; (1971) 1 SCC 289; AIR 1971 SC 2280; 1981 (8) E.L.T. 679; [1971] 3 S.C.R. 357; 1971 TAXLR 1528

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Customs Act, 1962 - The s.129 (1) makes it obligatory on appellant to deposit duty or penalty pending the appeal or to comply with the order passed under the Proviso - Non compliance of s-s. (1) or order passed under the Proviso by the appellant - Appellate Authority can dismiss appeal - Appeal Dismissed.
