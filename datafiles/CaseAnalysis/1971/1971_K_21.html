Karnani Properties Ltd v Commissioner Of Income Tax, West Bengal

Supreme Court of India

27 August 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 507; (1971) 3 SCC 568; AIR 1972 SC 2315; [1971] 82 ITR 547; [1972] 1 S.C.R. 457

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 66(1) - Reference - Power of High Court in dealing with a reference u/s. 66 - Scope - Power is very limited - Consideration of facts in the statement - Held, it must take the fact as stated in the statement of the case unless the question whether the findings of Tribunal are vitiated for one or other of the reasons recognised by law is before it - Appeal allowed.
