Vishwesha Thirtha Swamiari & Ors v State Of Mysore And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

12 August 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

S.M. Sikri, A.N. Ray, D.G. Palekar

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 612; (1972) 3 SCC 246; AIR 1971 SC 2377; [1972] 1 S.C.R. 137; 1971 UJ 776

Case Digest

Subject: Administrative; Indirect Tax; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Constitution, sch. 7, List 2, entry 45, art.14 - Karnataka Land Revenue (Surcharge) Act, 1961, s. 5 - Land Revenue - (A) Entry 45 under List 2 of sch 7 of Constitution - Surcharge on the land revenue - Legislative competence to levy - (B) Imposition of additional land revenue - By surcharge as a temporary measure before the uniform land revenue law is enacted after reorganisation the State - Whether violate art.14 of Constitution - Held, surcharge on the land revenue being merely an enhancement of land revenue and not a tax on land revenue, it is within the legislative competence to levy surcharge on the exiting land revenue - Held, is not a hostile discrimination so as to be violative of the art.14 -

Appeal Dismissed
