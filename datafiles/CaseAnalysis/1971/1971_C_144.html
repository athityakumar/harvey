Purshottam N. Amersey and Another v Commissioner of Wealth Tax, Bombay City Ii

Supreme Court of India

16 September 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 423; (1972) 4 SCC 376; (1974) SCC (Tax) 66; AIR 1973 SC 2335; 1972 CTR 255; 1973 (1) ITJ 227; [1973] 88 ITR 417; 1972 TAXLR 1190; 1972 UJ 432

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Wealth Tax Act, 1957, ss. 2(e), 3 and 7 - Wealth Tax - Trust Fund for support, maintenance etc. of the settlor assessee - Valuation of interest of the assessee under the Trust - Held, to be valued on the basis of the principles enunciated in 1957 Act - Appeals dismissed.
