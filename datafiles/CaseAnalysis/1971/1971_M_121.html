 Commissioner of Income Tax, Mysore v Mysore Electrical Industries Limited

Supreme Court of India

27 April 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

G.K. Mitter, S.M. Sikri, C.A. Vaidyialingam, P. Jaganmohan Reddy, I.D. Dua

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 350; (1971) 2 SCC 243; AIR 1971 SC 1361; [1971] 41 Comp Cas 617; [1971] 80 ITR 566; [1971] S.C.R. 521; [1971] Supp S.C.R. 521

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Corporate - Companies (Profits) Sur-tax Act, 1964, sch 2, r. 1 - Company - Profit - Surplus - Treatment of reserves - Where allocations for reserves, being not possible on very first day of previous year, are made subsequently - Held, appropriations for reserves have to be treated as component of capital 'as on first day of previous year' in view of fact that division of undistributed profits becomes effective from first day of previous year - Appeal dismissed.
