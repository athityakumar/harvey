Makan Jivan and Others v State of Gujarat

Supreme Court of India

30 April 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 643; (1971) 3 SCC 297; (1971) SCC (Cr) 587; AIR 1971 SC 1797; 1971 CRLJ 1310; 1971 UJ 634

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: CrPC, 1898, ss. 342 and 537 - Effect of non-compliance with s. 342 of the Act - Held, errors or omissions in complying with the section, are curable irregularities and the question whether the trial is vitiated depends upon the degree of the error and upon whether prejudice has been caused to the accused - Appeal Dismissed.
