 Vivian Joseph Ferreira and Another v Municipal Corporation Of Greater Bombay & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

04 November 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

J.M. Shelat, I.D. Dua, Subimal Chandra Roy, S.M. Sikri, G.K. Mitter

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 498; (1972) 1 SCC 70; AIR 1972 SC 845; 1971 RCR(Rent) 991; [1972] 2 S.C.R. 257

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Land & Property; Trusts & Associations

Summary: Land & Property - Transfer of Property Act, 1882 - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 19(1)(f), 19(5), 246(3), 265, sch. 7, list 2, entry 49 - Bombay Buildings Repairs and Reconstruction Board Act, 1969, ss. 27, 28 - (A) Reasonable restriction - Levy of cess u/s. 27 of 1969 Act on residential buildings - Even though they are in sound and good condition - Do not require structural repairs for entire period of Act - (B) Equality before law - Imposition of tax u/s. 27 and exemption u/s. 28, 1969 Act - Held, does not amount to an unreasonable restriction and therefore does not violate art. 19(1)(f) - Further held, does not contravene art. 14 - Petitions dismissed.
