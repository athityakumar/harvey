State Of Maharashtra v Lazmen Abaji And Another

Supreme Court of India

20 April 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, C.A. Vaidyialingam

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 167; (1971) 2 SCC 146; AIR 1971 SC 1859; 1971 RCR(Rent) 466; [1971] S.C.R. 412; [1971] Supp S.C.R. 412

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Keywords: Hyderabad Abolition of Inams and Cash Grants Act 1955

Summary: Hyderabad Abolition of Inams and Cash Grants Act, 1955, ss. 3(2)(b), 6 and 33 - Hyderabad Abolition of Inams (Amendment) Act, 1956, s. 4 - Tenancy - (A) Determination of relationship of landlord and tenant - When inams were abolished, lands vested in the State and notification u/s.1(3)(b) (by Maharashtra Govt. ) made the entire Act applicable - (B) Crucial date for grant of occupancy rights - Held, relationship of landlord and tenant continued to subsist between 20-07-1955 when inams were abolished and lands vested in the State and 1-7-1960 when a notification u/s. 1 (3) (b) of the Act by Maharashtra Govt. made the entire Act applicable - Crucial date for grant of occupancy rights is 01-07-1960 when the entire Act came into effect and not the date of vesting of the land in the State on 20-07-1955 - Appeal Allowed.
