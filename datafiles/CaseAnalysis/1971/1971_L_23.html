Narinder Chand Hem Raj And Others v Lt. Governor, Administrator, Union Territory

Supreme Court of India

05 October 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 305; (1971) 2 SCC 747; AIR 1971 SC 2399; [1972] 1 S.C.R. 940; 1972 (29) STC 169; 1971 TAXLR 1734

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Sales Tax; Trade

Summary: Constitution, arts. 226 and 245 - Limitation Act, 1908 - Imposition of sales tax - Power to impose tax - Exercise of - Being a legislative power can be exercised either by the legislature directly or by its delegate - Held, but no court can issue a mandate to a legislature or a subordinate legislative body to enact or not to enact a law which it was competent to enact - Appeal Dismissed.
