State of Gujarat v Adam Fateh Mohmed Umatiya and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 March 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 694; (1971) 3 SCC 208; (1971) SCC (Cr) 381; 1971 UJ 466

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Code of Criminal Procedure,1973 - Indian Penal Code, 1860,ss. 302, 34, 147, 148, 149 and 404 - Arms Act, s. 25 - Murder - Accused persons acquitted by High Court- Whether acquittal order of High Court proper and justified? - Deceased and accused belonged to two different sects which had different beliefs leading to differences - Eye-witnesses - All witnesses interested and hence HC rightly disbelieved prosecution and acquitted the accused - Appeal dismissed.
