Collector of Lakhimpur v Bhuban Chandra Dutta

Supreme Court of India

29 January 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, K.S. Hegde

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 736; (1972) 4 SCC 236; AIR 1971 SC 2015; 1971 UJ 274

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 23(1) - Assam Land Requisition and Acquisition) Act, 1948, s. 7(1) - Land acquisition - Determination of compensation - Value fetched for small plot of land - Applicability to lands covering a very large extent - Held cannot be applied, the large area of land cannot possibly fetch a price at the same rate at which small plots are sold - Appeal Allowed.
