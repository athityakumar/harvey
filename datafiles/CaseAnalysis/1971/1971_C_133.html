Commissioner of Income Tax, West Bengal Ii v Electro House

Supreme Court of India

02 September 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 341; (1971) 2 SCC 647; [1971] 82 ITR 824; [1972] 1 S.C.R. 589; 1972 UJ 62

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Indian Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 33B - Validity of the notice issued u/s. 33B - Whether CIT validly exercised jurisdiction u/ s. 33B? - S. 33B merely requires that an opportunity of being heard should be given to the assessee and the stringent requirement of service of notice u/s. 34 cannot, therefore, be applied to a proceeding u/s. 33B - Held, notice issued did not contravene s. 33B and the Commr. validly exercised his jurisdiction u/s. 33B - Order accordingly.
