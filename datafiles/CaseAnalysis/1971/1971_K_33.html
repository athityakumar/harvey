Kishorilal Hans v Raja Ram Singh and Others

Supreme Court of India

30 November 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, K.S. Hegde

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 296; (1972) 3 SCC 1; AIR 1972 SC 598; [1972] 2 S.C.R. 632; 1972 UJ 369

Case Digest

Subject: Election

Keywords: Scheduled Caste

Summary: Election - Representation of People Act, 1951, s. 5(a) - Member of Legislative Assembly - Qualifications for membership of a Legislative Assembly for seat reserved for Scheduled Caste - Held, caste of candidate must have been recognised as a scheduled caste in a constituency in which his name is entered in electoral roll - Appeal dismissed.
