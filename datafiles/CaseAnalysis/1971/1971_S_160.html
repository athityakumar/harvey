Md. Usman and Others v State of Andhra Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

29 April 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 24; (1971) 2 SCC 188; AIR 1971 SC 1801; 1971 LabIC 1114; 1971 (1) LLJ 534; [1971] S.C.R. 549; 1971 (2) SLR 584; [1971] Supp S.C.R. 549

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution , art.14 - A.P. Registration Subordinate Service Special Rules, r.5 - Service Rules - (A) The r. 5, which provides for recruitment to Grade II Sub Registrars from among ministerial officials of Registration and Stamp Deptt. - Doctine of equality - If violation of - (B) Rules do not prescribe that recruitment from among ministerial officials should be made on basis of merit and merit alone - does not violate the doctrine of equality on ground that the Rule treats UDC and LDC as equal even though the position of UDC is superior to that of LDC - Recruitment on basis of seniority-cum-merit is resonable and in accordance with Rules - Appeal Allowed.
