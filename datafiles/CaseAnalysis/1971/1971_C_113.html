R. B. Jodha Mal Kuthiala v Commissioner of Income Tax, Punjab and Others

Supreme Court of India

09 September 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 417; (1971) 3 SCC 369; AIR 1972 SC 126; [1971] 82 ITR 570; [1972] 1 S.C.R. 639

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Annual Value, Bengal Agricultural Income Tax Act, 1944, Pakistan (Adiministration of Evacuee Property) Ordinance, 1949

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 9(1) - Owner of house property u/s. 9(1) - Evacuee from Pakistan - For s. 9, owner must be person, who can exercise rights of owner in his own right and not on behalf of owner - Held, an evacuee from Pakistan ceases to be owner of his house in Pakistan for purpose of this section after vesting of that property in Custodian of Pakistan - Appeals dismissed.
