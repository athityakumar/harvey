 U.N. R. Rao v Smt. Indira Gandhi

Supreme Court of India

17 March 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

S.M. Sikri, G.K. Mitter, K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover, P. Jaganmohan Reddy

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 148; (1971) 2 SCC 63; AIR 1971 SC 1002; [1971] S.C.R. 46; [1971] Supp S.C.R. 46

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Constitution, arts. 53(1), 74(1), 75(2) and 75(3) - Council of Ministers - Whether cease to hold office - After Dissolution of the House of the People - Held, the Council of Ministers does not cease to hold office even after dissolution of the House of the People - Appeal Dismissed.
