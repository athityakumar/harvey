Commissioner Of Income Tax, Gujarat v Distributors (Baroda) (P) Ltd.

Supreme Court of India

16 September 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 247; (1972) 4 SCC 353; AIR 1972 SC 288; 1972 (1) CTR 274; 1972 CTR 275; [1972] 83 ITR 377; [1972] S.C.R. 726

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Indian Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 261 - Indian Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 23A - Income Tax - Grant of certificate of HC for appeal to SC - Reasons not given - Efeect on appeal u/s. 261 of 1961 Act - Scope of applicability of s. 23A of 1922 Act - Held, appeal unsustainable - And s. 23A applies only to cases where the primary business activity of a company is in the dealing in or holding of investments - Appeal Dismissed.
