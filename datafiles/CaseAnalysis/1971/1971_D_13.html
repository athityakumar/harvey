 Dantuluri Ram Raju and Others v State of Andhra Pradesh and Another

Supreme Court of India

16 December 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

Hans Raj Khanna, I.D. Dua, S.M. Sikri, J.M. Shelat, G.K. Mitter

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 488; (1972) 1 SCC 421; AIR 1972 SC 828; 1973 (1) MLJ 7; 1973 (1) MLJ(SC) 7; [1972] 2 S.C.R. 900

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Keywords: Andhra Pradesh (Andhra Area) Estates Land Act, 1908, Travancore Cochin Land Tax Act, 1955, Andhra Pradesh (Krishna and Godavari Delta Area) Drainage Cess Act, 1968

Summary: Constitution - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 - Travancore Cochin Land Tax Act, 1955 - Constitutionality/ vires of Andhra Pradesh (Krishna and Godavari Delta Area) Drainage Cess Act, 1968, s. 3 has been challenged - Held, provisions of Act are not violative of art. 14 of Constitution - Appeal dismissed.
