Ram Kishan Singh v Harmit Kaur and Another

Supreme Court of India

09 December 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, D.G. Palekar

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 547; (1972) 3 SCC 280; (1972) SCC (Cr) 493; AIR 1972 SC 468; 1972 CRLJ 267; 1972 UJ 424

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Procedure Code, 1898, ss. 164, 439 - (A) Revision against acquittal - High Court's power - (B) Statement recorded by Magistrate - Evidentiary value of - Held, HC's power to direct retrial - A statement u/s. 164, CrPC, 1898 is not a substantive evidence - Appeal allowed.
