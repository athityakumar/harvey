P. K. Nedungadi v Malayalee Bank Limited (In Liquidation) and Others

Supreme Court of India

09 February 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, K.S. Hegde

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 464; (1971) 3 SCC 598; AIR 1971 SC 829; [1972] 42 Comp Cas 120

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Banking & Finance; Corporate

Summary: Corporate - Companies Act, 1913, s. 235 - Fraud - Banking Company - Under liquidation - A director enabling another director to perpetrate fraud - Result : loss to company - Liability of former director to be ascertain - Held, liable to repay and restore loss - Appeal dismissed.
