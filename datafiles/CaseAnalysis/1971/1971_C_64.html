Commissioner of Gift Tax, Kerala v P. Gheevarghese, Travancore Timbers and Products

Supreme Court of India

20 September 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, K.S. Hegde

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 428; (1972) 4 SCC 323; AIR 1972 SC 23; 1972 CTR 286; [1972] 83 ITR 403; [1972] S.C.R. 817

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Gift Tax Act, 1958, s. 5(1)(xiv) - Transfer of business assets to family members by inducting them as partner - Assessee converting his sole proprietary business into a partnership business by admitting daughters as partners (also contributed their share capital) - Transfer of all assets and property including goodwill of proprietary business to partnership - Taxability - No tax held payable on goodwill in view of facts - Gift of amount representing share capital of daughters held not exempted u/s. 5(1)(xiv) of Act - Order accordingly.
