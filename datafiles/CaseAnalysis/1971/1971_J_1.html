J. K. Synthetics Limited and Others v Central Board of Direct Taxes and Others

Supreme Court of India

24 September 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover, Hans Raj Khanna

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 438; (1972) 4 SCC 689; 1972 CTR 252; [1972] 83 ITR 335; 1972 UJ 220

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 119 - Writ petition is primarily directed against certain communications sent by the Central Board of Direct Taxes to the appellants - Decisions conveyed in those communications were not made in exercise of any of the powers conferred on the Board by any law they were merely replies to communications sent by the appellants and cannot be even considered as directions u/s. 119 - Petition dismissed.
