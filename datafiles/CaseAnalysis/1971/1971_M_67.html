 State of Madhya Pradesh and Others v Messrs Chhotabhai Jethabhai Patel and Company

Supreme Court of India

10 December 1971

Case Analysis

Bench

G.K. Mitter, I.D. Dua, Hans Raj Khanna, S.M. Sikri, J.M. Shelat

Where Reported

1971 Indlaw SC 283; (1972) 1 SCC 209; AIR 1972 SC 971; [1972] 2 S.C.R. 838; 1972 UJ 418

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Madhya Pradesh Patta (Vyapar Viniyaman) Adhiniyam Act 1964

Summary: Trade - Madhya Pradesh Tendu Patta (Vyapar Viniyaman) Adhiniyam, 1964, s. 5(2)(A)- Restriction on import of tendu leaves or their movement - (B) Interpretation of statutory provision capable of two constructions, one consistent with constitutionality and other offending constitution - Held, Act does not impose any express embargo on import of tendu leaves from outside State or restrict their movement once they are brought within State - Court will lean in favour if it is compatible with object of Act and mischief sought to be prevented - Appeal dismissed.
