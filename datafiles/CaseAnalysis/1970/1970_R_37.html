State of Uttar Pradesh and Others v Ram Naresh Lal

Supreme Court of India

13 March 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

S.M. Sikri, Vishishtha Bhargava, C.A. Vaidyialingam

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 537; (1970) 3 SCC 173; AIR 1970 SC 1263; 1970 LabIC 1063; 1970 SLR 819; 1970 UJ 470

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution, arts. 309 and 311(1) - Service matter - (A) Transfer of member of subordinate service - From one deptt. to another permanently - Need of reappointment to transferee deptt. - (B) Letter of Govt. No. 1195/ XXXV-B-587/56 D/- 21-05-1958 - Meaning of word 'Control' - (C) Powers of dismissal - By whom can be exercised - (D) Power of dismissal - Whether a person has a lien in one deptt. or in other deptt. , the Govt. is entitled, subject to the provisions of art. 311(1), to delegate power of dismissal to any officer - Fundamental rules , r. 14A, if modify it - Held, reappointment to service in transferee deptt. is not necessary - Includes disciplinary jurisdiction - Can be exercised by officer other than appointing authority, not subordinate in rank - Rule 14A of the Fundamental rules has not the effect of modifying this power - Appeal Allowed.
