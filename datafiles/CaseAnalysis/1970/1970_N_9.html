Nagar Rice & Flour Miills & Ors. v N. Teekappa Gowda & Bros. & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

27 February 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 119; (1970) 1 SCC 575; AIR 1971 SC 246; [1970] 3 S.C.R. 846

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Rice-Milling Industry (Regulation) Act, 1958, Rice Milling Industries (Regulation) Act, 1958

Summary: Constitution, arts. 19(1)(g), 226 - Rice Milling Industry (Regulation) Act, 1958, ss. 5(4) and 8(3)(c) - Change of location of rice mill - (A) Sanction for change obtained after shifting - Assuming previous sanction is required before moving of machinery - Competitor, if possess locus standi - (B) Applicability of s. 5 (4) - In granting permission u/s. 8(3)(c) - Held, competitor has no locus standi to challenge grant of permission - Held, s. 5(4) has no application in granting permission u/s. 8(3)(c) - Appeal Allowed.
