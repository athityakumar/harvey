 Kakinada Annadana Samajam Etc. v Commissioner Of Hindu Religious & Charitableendowments, Hyde

Supreme Court of India

02 December 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, G.K. Mitter, K.S. Hegde, A.N. Ray, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 126; (1970) 3 SCC 359; AIR 1971 SC 891; 1972 (1) MLJ 5; 1972 (1) MLJ(SC) 5; [1971] 2 S.C.R. 878

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution

Summary: Constitution, arts. 19(5), 19(1)(f) and 31 - A.P. Charitable and Hindu Religious Institutions and Endowments Act, 1966, ss. 2(15) and 15 - Religious Trust - (A) Wheher office of hereditary trustee is property - (B) Validity of restrictions imposed by 1966 Act on hereditary trustee - Held, is not 'property' within meaning of art. 19(1)(f) or art. 31 - Restrictions are reasonable - Appeal Dismissed.
