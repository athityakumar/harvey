Patel Premji Jiva By L. Rs v State of Gujarat and Others

Supreme Court of India

20 August 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, A.N. Grover, I.D. Dua

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 467; (1971) 3 SCC 815; 1970 UJ 813

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 4, 5A and 6 - General Clauses Act, 1897 - Gujarat Agricultural Procedure Markets Act, 1963, s. 10(2) - Bombay General Clauses Act, 1904, s. 3(26) - Notification u/s. 4 notified for acquisition an area of land in town of Gondal, for a public purpose - Enquiry u/s. 5A - Notification u/s. 6 of Act declaring that land was needed for a public purpose - Validity of notification - Ground that, Agricultural Procedure Markets Committee not being a 'local authority' within meaning of s. 6, Govt. of Gujarat was not competent to acquire land under Land Acquisition Act - Held, by virtue of s. 10(2) of Gujarat Agricultural Procedure Markets Act, 1963, market committee is a local authority within meaning of Bombay General Clauses Act, 1904 - A local authority being by virtue of s. 3(26) of Bombay General Clauses Act, 1904, a body which is entrusted by Govt. with control or management, inter alia, of a local fund, there is no scope for argument that market committee constituted under Gujarat Agricultural Markets Act, 1963, is not a local authority within meaning of s. 6 of Land Acquisition Act - Appeal dismissed.
