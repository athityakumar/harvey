Produce Exchange Corporation Ltd v Commissioner Of Income Tax

Supreme Court of India

27 April 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, A.N. Grover, K.S. Hegde

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 65; (1970) 2 SCC 92; AIR 1971 SC 2328; [1970] 77 ITR 739; [1971] 1 S.C.R. 382

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 24(2) - Set-off of loss against profits of several businesses is available only where all constitute one 'same business' - Tests for - Held, whether several separate lines of business are 'same business' within s. 24(2) is determined by inter-connection, interlacing, inter-dependence and other unity factors - Appeal Allowed.
