Siddanna Apparao Patil v State Of Maharashtra

Supreme Court of India

06 March 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, I.D. Dua

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 96; (1970) SCC (Cr) 224; (1970) 1 SCC 547; AIR 1970 SC 977; 1970 CRLJ 891; [1970] 3 S.C.R. 909

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance

Summary: CrPC, 1898, s. 421 - FERA, 1947 - (A) Criminal Procedure - Summary dismissal of appeal - Appeal u/s. 410 against sentence passed of Court of session - Discretion of High Court - Principles - (B) Appeal by special leave u/art. 136 - Powers of Supreme Court - When High Court dismissed appeal u/s. 410 in limine by single word - Even though it raised substantial and arguable points - Held, HC should give reasons for rejection - Dismissal is improper so Order set aside and appeal remanded for rehearing according to law - Appeal Allowed.
