Shri Radhey Shyam v Shyam Behari Singh

Supreme Court of India

12 August 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

J.M. Shelat, C.A. Vaidyialingam

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 479; (1970) 2 SCC 405; AIR 1971 SC 2337; [1971] 1 S.C.R. 783

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Letters Patent (All), cl. 10 - CPC, 1908, O. 21, r. 90 - Practice and Procedure - (A) Order of Single Judge of High Court in appeal arising out of proceedings u/r. 90 of O. 21 of CPC, 1908 - Appeal against u/cl. 10 - Maintainabiility - (B) Setting aside of sale - Essentials for - Held, is maintainable - In order to set aside an auction sale mere proof of a material irregularity such as the one u/r. 69 and inadequacy of price realised in such a sale, in other words injury, is not sufficient - Appeal Dismissed.
