Investment Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax, Calcutta

Supreme Court of India

21 April 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 215; (1970) 3 SCC 333; AIR 1970 SC 1815; [1970] 77 ITR 533; 1970 UJ 581

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 10 - Income Tax Act, 1961 - Capital loss or Revenue loss - Determination of - Relevancy of frequency of transaction - Capital Market - Company trading in secutities - Sale of securities - Loss therein - Held, securities held by the assessee as an investment and not as stock-in-trade and loss incurred thereon was a capital loss - Appeal Allowed.
