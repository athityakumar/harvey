Chetkar Jha v Viswanath Prasad Verma and Others

Supreme Court of India

07 May 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

J.M. Shelat, G.K. Mitter

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 138; (1970) 2 SCC 217; AIR 1970 SC 1832; [1971] 1 S.C.R. 586; 1970 SLR 805; 1970 UJ 681

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Education - Patna University Act, 1962, ss.9, 26, 30, 31, 57, 58 - Whether HC was right in annulling, order of Chancellor to be without jurisdiction as it was passed, on a wrong assumption of jurisdiction? - Held, Yes - Revised advertisement - Remission of matter to Commission - Recommendation of respondent by Commission and proceedings of Syndicate's meeting including revision of draft minutes were all in accordance with provisions of Act and University Statutes and therefore Chancellor had no jurisdiction u/s. 9(4) of Act to annul decision of Syndicate - Appeal dismissed.
