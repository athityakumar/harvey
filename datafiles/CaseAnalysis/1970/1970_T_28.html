Municipal Corporation of The City of Ahmedabad and Another v The New Shrock Spg. and Wvg. Company Limited and another

Supreme Court of India

17 April 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 498; (1970) 2 SCC 280; AIR 1970 SC 1292; [1971] 1 S.C.R. 288

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Competence Of The Legislature

Summary: Bombay Provincial Municipal Corporation Act, 1949, s. 152A, 152A(3) - Property Tax - (A) Tax illegally collected by municipality - Whether s.152A empower Corporation to retain if - (B) Provisions of s.152A (3) introduced by Gujarat Amending and Validating Ordinance 1969 - Constitutionality of - Legislature asking instrumentalities of State to disobey or disregard decisions of Court - Held, section does not empower ( scope of section indicated ) - is ultra vires the Constitution - Legislature cannot ask instrumentalities of State to disobey or disregard decisions of Court - Held, is ultra vires the Constitution and legislature cannot ask instrumentalities of State to disobey or disregard decisions of Court - Appeal Dismissed.
