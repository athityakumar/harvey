India Electric Works Ltd. v James Mantosh & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

15 September 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, K.S. Hegde, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 159; (1971) 1 SCC 24; AIR 1971 SC 2313; [1971] 2 S.C.R. 397

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Mesne Profits

Summary: Limitation Act, 1908, s. 14 - (A) Applicability of expression 'or other causes of a like nature' - Suit for recovery of damages for illegal occupation -Trial Court awarded future damages till recovery of possession - Set aside in appeal - Subsequent suit for damages for a period subsequent to that in earlier suit - Limitation - (B) Precedents - Requirement of public interest - Held, period of pendency of first suit could be excluded - Requirement of public interest should be considered in disturbing a question of law which has held field for long time - Appeal Dismissed.
