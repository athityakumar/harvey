Corporation Of Calcutta v Life Insurance Corporation Of India

Supreme Court of India

09 April 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, K.S. Hegde

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 98; (1970) 2 SCC 44; AIR 1970 SC 1417; [1971] 1 S.C.R. 248

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure; Service

Keywords: Calcutta Municipal Act, 1923, Municipal Corporation Act 1951

Summary: Calcutta Municipal Act, 1951, ss. 168(1) Proviso and 193 - West Bengal Premises Rent Control (Temporary Provisions ) Act, 1950, s. 9 - Municipalities - (A) Amount of consolidated rate, how to be fixed - Determination of annual value of premises - Maximum limit is annual standard rent - And when no order fixing standard rent u/s. 9 of 1950 Act passed - (B) Apportionment of consolidated rate - Provision of s. 193 - Determination of annual value for quantum of consolidation rate - Held, maximum limit of annual value is still annual standard rent - Section not relevant for determining annual value - Appeal Dismissed.
