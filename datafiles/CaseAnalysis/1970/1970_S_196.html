State of Orissa and Another v Kalinga Construction Company Private Limited

Supreme Court of India

11 September 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, K.S. Hegde, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 186; (1970) 2 SCC 861; AIR 1971 SC 1646; [1971] 2 S.C.R. 184

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration Act, 1940, ss. 39 and 30 - Setting aside award of arbitrator - Error of law or fact - Ambit of powers of Appellate Court - Held, Appellate Court cannot sit in appeal over conclusion of arbitrator by re-examining and re-appraising evidence considered by arbitrator to hold that conclusion reached by arbitrator is wrong - Finding of arbitrator not perverse - No error apparent in award - Court cannot set aside award - Appeal allowed.
