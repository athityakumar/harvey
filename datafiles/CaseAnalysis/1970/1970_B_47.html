S. N. Sharma v Bipen Kumar Tiwari and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 March 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

Vishishtha Bhargava, S.M. Sikri, C.A. Vaidyialingam

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 544; (1970) SCC (Cr) 258; (1970) 1 SCC 653; AIR 1970 SC 786; 1970 ALJ 1348; 1980 ALJ 1348; 1970 CRLJ 764; 1970 (2) MLJ(SC) 81; [1970] 3 S.C.R. 946

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: CrPC, 1898, ss. 156, 157 and 159 - Constitution, art. 226 - Interpretation of s. 159 - Investigation of cognizable offence by police - Magistrate has no power to stop it - And direct magisterial enquiry - What is the remedy against mala fide exercise of power of investigation by police - Held, remedy is to invoke writ jurisdiction - Appeal Dismissed.
