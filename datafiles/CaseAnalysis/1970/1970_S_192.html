Channabasappa Basappa Happali v State of Mysore

Supreme Court of India

16 October 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

Mohammad Hidayatullah, A.N. Ray

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 595; (1971) 1 SCC 1; AIR 1972 SC 32; 1972 LabIC 1; [1971] 2 S.C.R. 645

Case Digest

Subject: Defence & Security Forces; Service

Summary: Service - Departmental enquiry - Validity - Whether enquiry was in breach of any principles of natural justice? - At an enquiry facts have to be proved and person proceeded against must have an opportunity to cross-examine witnesses and to give his own version or explanation about evidence on which he is charged and to lead his defence - No distinction between admission of facts and admission of guilt - A person who admitted facts and did not wish to cross-examine any witness or lead evidence on his own behalf - Held, departmental enquiry was either one-sided or not fair - Appeal dismissed.
