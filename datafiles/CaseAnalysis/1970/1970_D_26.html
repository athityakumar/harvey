 District Collector Of Hyderabad & Ors. v M/S. Ibrahim & Co. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

05 February 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, Mohammad Hidayatullah, K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover, A.N. Ray, I.D. Dua

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 351; (1970) 1 SCC 386; AIR 1970 SC 1275; 1970 (2) MLJ(SC) 109; [1970] 3 S.C.R. 498

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Socio-Economic

Keywords: Defence of India Rules, 1962, Defence of India Ordinance, 1962

Summary: Constitution, arts. 14, 19, 302, 303, 304, 305, 358 and 359 - Essential Commodities Act, 1955 - Emergency - Suspension of fundamental rights - (A) Suspension of art. 19 - Executive action by State - Invalid of other grounds - If protected from challenge by art. 358 - (B) Suspension of art.14 - Executive order by Andhra State not passed under Defence of India Ordinance or under any rule or order made thereunder - If protected u/art. 359 - (C) Freedom of trade, commerce and intercourse - Can be restricted only by Legislative action within limits provided in arts. 302 - 305 but not by executive order - G.O.M. No. 2976 (Andhra) D/- 30-12-1964 putting restriction upon - Held, is not protected from challenge by art. 358 - Not protected from challenge by Presidential Order D/- 03-11-1962 issued u/art. 359 - Order held ultra vires - Appeal Dismissed.
