Commissioner Of Wealth Tax, Gujarat Atahmedabad v Mrs. Arundhati Balkrishna

Supreme Court of India

25 February 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 117; (1970) 1 SCC 561; AIR 1971 SC 915; [1970] 77 ITR 505; [1970] 3 S.C.R. 819

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Land & Property

Summary: Wealth Tax Act, 1957, ss. 2(e)(iv), 5 (1)(viii), 5 (1) (xv) - Succession Act, 1925, s. 173 - (A) Meaning of word 'Annuity' s. 2(e)(iv) of 1957 Act - Trust deeds - Trustees to pay annually to assessee a share of income (life interest ) from trusts funds after deducting expenses from it - Not fixed sums of money - Nature of payment - (B) Provison of s. 5(1) - Jewellery intended for personal use of assessee only - Held, payments are not annuities and assessee not entitled to exclude them u/s.2 (e)(iv) from computation of net wealth - Held, comes within s. 5 (1)(viii) and its value cannot be included in computation of net wealth of assessee while s. 5 (1) (xv) deals with jewellery in general - Appeal Dismissed.
