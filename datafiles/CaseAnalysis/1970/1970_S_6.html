S. Sethurathinam Pillai v Barbara Alias Dolly Sethurathinam

Supreme Court of India

25 March 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, A.N. Grover, A.N. Ray, I.D. Dua

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 517; (1971) 3 SCC 923; (1972) SCC (Cr) 171; 1970 UJ 505

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Procedure Code, 1898, s. 488 - Payment of maintenance - Influence of summary order -Summary order does not finally determine rights and obligations of parties - Decision to be made on basis of evidence available uninfluenced by summary order - Appeal dismissed.
