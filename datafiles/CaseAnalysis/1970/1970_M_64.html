Mohd. Ibrahim Etc. v Mohd. Ibrahim Etc.

Supreme Court of India

30 April 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, Mohammad Hidayatullah, I.D. Dua

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 328; (1970) 2 SCC 233; (1970) SCC (Cr) 416; AIR 1970 SC 1542; 1971 (1) MLJ(SC) 76; [1971] 1 S.C.R. 474

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation

Keywords: Additional Permit

Summary: Carriers & Transportation - Motor Vehicles Act, 1939, ss. 47(3), 57, 57(3), 48, 43, 63 - Interpretation of sections and provisions thereof - Stage carriage permits - In the instant case appeals were directed against the refusal to the appellants to grant the stage carriage permits - Whether, respondent had rightly refused the appellants applications for the stage carriage permits - Held, after detailed consideration of ss. 47(3), 57, 57(3), 48, 43, 63 of the Act and provisions thereof, instant court was of the view that the respondent erred while rejecting the appellants - After considering the each appeal in the view of interpretation of the said sections instant court had referred some appeals to HC and allowed some appeals - Order accordingly.
