State of Assam and Another v Daksha Prasad Deka and Others

Supreme Court of India

23 October 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 180; (1970) 3 SCC 624; AIR 1971 SC 173; 1971 LabIC 27; 1971 (1) LLJ 554; [1971] 2 S.C.R. 687; 1971 (2) SLR 14

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution, art. 311(2) - Service matter - Compulsory retirement - Date of, must be determined on basis of service record - Ple of deprivation of guarantee u/art. 311(2) - When open - Held, until the service record is first corrected consistently with appropriate procedure, employee cannot claim that he had been deprived of the guarantee - Application for rectification of service record as filed by Govt. servant concerned was itself not entertainable, guarantee held,not infringed - Appeal Allowed.
