Union Of India v Hindu Undivided Family Business Known As Ramlal

Supreme Court of India

21 August 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

Vishishtha Bhargava, K.S. Hegde

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 154; (1970) 2 SCC 472; AIR 1971 SC 2333; 1978 (2) E.L.T. 389; [1971] 1 S.C.R. 936

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, s.2(f) and sch. 1, item 25A(2) - Excise Duty - Rolling of billets of copper alloys in circles - Circles are uncut and not trimmed - Levy of excise duty - Held, excise duty under item 25A(2) is leviable, though circles are uncut and not trimmed - Appeal Allowed.
