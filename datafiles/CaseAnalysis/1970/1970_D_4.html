D. N. Roy And S. K. Bannerjee And Ors. v State Of Bihar And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

30 September 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, J.C. Shah, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 170; (1970) 3 SCC 119; AIR 1971 SC 1045; [1971] 2 S.C.R. 522

Case Digest

Subject: Mines & Minerals

Keywords: Mineral Concession Rules

Summary: Mines and Minerals (Regulation and Development) Act, 1957, s.30 - Mining Lease -Cancellation - Central Govt. in exercise of its suo motu power cancelling mining lease granted to appellant by State Govt. - Without informing him - Not even asked to show cause against exercise of such power - Held, failure of Central Govt. to do so had vitiated the order - Appeal Allowed.
