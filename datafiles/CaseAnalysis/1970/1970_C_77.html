Commissioner of Income Tax, U.P., and Others v Jawahar Lal Rastogi

Supreme Court of India

07 May 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, K.S. Hegde, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 242; (1970) 2 SCC 225; AIR 1970 SC 1651; [1970] 78 ITR 486; [1971] 1 S.C.R. 581; 1970 UJ 677

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Assessment Year

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 132 - Validity of search - High Court observed it is amounted to indiscriminate search and beyond the scope of s. 132 - The documents seized during the search were detained for 19 months by the authorised officer and did not comply with the requirements of the statute after expiry of the period of 180 days - Held, HC order sustained - Appeal dismissed.
