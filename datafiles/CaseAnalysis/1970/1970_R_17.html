Kulbhushan Kumar v Raj Kumari and Another

Supreme Court of India

20 October 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

G.K. Mitter, A.N. Ray

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 178; (1970) 3 SCC 129; AIR 1971 SC 234; 1971 ALJ 1047; [1971] 2 S.C.R. 672

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal

Summary: Hindu Adoptions and Maintenance Act, 1956 - Provisions of s. 23(2) and 23(2)(d) - (A) Claim for maintenance by wife and daughter - Test for determination of quantum - (B) Wife's claim for maintenance - Regularly received Rs. 2501- p.m. from her father during his lifetime - Not income but 'as only a bounty from her father which she might or might not get' - Date from which maintenance claimable - Held, sum so received by wife was not a fact to be taken into consideration u/s. 23(2)(d) - Appeal Dismissed.
