Smt. Jatan Golcha v Messrs Golcha Properties Private Limited

Supreme Court of India

16 December 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, K.S. Hegde, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 199; (1970) 3 SCC 573; AIR 1971 SC 374; [1971] 41 Comp Cas 230; [1971] 3 S.C.R. 247

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Summary: Corporate - Companies Act, 1956, ss. 483, 446 and 457 - Companies (Court) Rules, 1959, r. 139 - Company - Liquidation - Court ordered auction sale of lease-hold rights - Notice of either sale or of proceedings, not given to landlord either by official liquidator or by court - Need of notice - Ancillary issue that whether r. 139 of 1959 Rules, deprive landlord of right to appeal u/s. 483 of 1956 Act - Whether he have to file a separate suit u/s. 446 of Act - Held, both official liquidator and company judge are bound by rules of natural justice to issue notice to landlord - Right to file appeal not deprived - No necessity of filing separate suit - Appeal allowed.
