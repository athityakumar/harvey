Punithavalli Ammal v Ramalingam (Minor) And Anr

Supreme Court of India

04 March 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, J.C. Shah, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 92; (1970) 1 SCC 570; AIR 1970 SC 1730; 1970 (2) MLJ(SC) 70; [1970] 3 S.C.R. 894

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Women & Children

Summary: Hindu Succession Act, 1956, s. 14(1) - Female Hindu - Absolute owner of her property - Adoption by widow - Full ownership conferred on widow u/s.14(1) - Overriding effect of Act - Held, full ownership conferred s. 14 is not defeated by subsequent adoption - Appeal Allowed.
