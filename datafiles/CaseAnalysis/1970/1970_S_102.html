Gottipulla Venkata Siva Subrayanam and Others v State of Andhra Pradesh and Another

Supreme Court of India

19 January 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

I.D. Dua, Mohammad Hidayatullah, A.N. Ray

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 103; (1970) SCC (Cr) 114; (1970) 1 SCC 235; AIR 1970 SC 1079; 1970 CRLJ 1004; 1970 (2) MLJ(SC) 87; [1970] 3 S.C.R. 423

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Tort

Keywords: Danger

Summary: IPC, 1860, s. 97 - Right of private defence - Availability - When prosecution party was in unauthorised possession of Kunta - And accused party putting up bund on land - So prosecution party going in large number on land to remove bund and beating accused party mercilessly - Acused held had right of private defence - Appeal Allowed.
