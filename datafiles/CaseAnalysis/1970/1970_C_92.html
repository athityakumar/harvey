Chhitter Mal Narain Das v Commissioner of Sales Tax

Supreme Court of India

21 July 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, K.S. Hegde

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 321; (1970) 3 SCC 809; AIR 1970 SC 2000; [1971] 1 S.C.R. 671; 1970 (26) STC 344

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Keywords: Andhra Pradesh Sugarcane (Regulation Of Supply And Purchase) Act, 1961, Iron and Steel (Control of Production and Distribution) Order, 1941, Sugar and Sugar Products Control Order, 1946, Uttar Pradesh Wheat Procurement (Levy) Order, 1959

Summary: Sales Tax - UP Sales Tax Act, 1948, s. 2(h) - Essential Commodities Act, 1955 - Uttar Pradesh Wheat Procurement (Levy) Order, 1959 - Sale - Supply made to Regional Food Controller under 1959 order - Whether such supply is sale u/s. 2(h) of 1948 Act - Held, supply is not sale - Appeals allowed.
