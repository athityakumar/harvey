Messrs Karam Chand Ganga Prasad and Another, Etc v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 October 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

K.S. Hegde, J.C. Shah, A.N. Grover

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 429; (1970) 3 SCC 694; AIR 1971 SC 1244; 1971 CRLJ 1072; 1971 UJ 26

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Disposal on Merits

Summary: CrPC, 1898, s. 367 - Practice and Procedure - Decisions of Civil Courts are binding on Criminal Courts - Converse not true - Forfeiture of goods (maize) exported by dealers from one State to another - As export is banned by order issued under Essential Commodities Act - Criminal prosecution - Writ challenging forfeiture on ground that ban was lifted at time of export - High Court dismissing petition in view of pendency of criminal proceedings - Legality of - Dismissal held, is illegal - Appeal Allowed.
