Jaipur Hosiery Mills (P) Ltd., Jaipur v The State Of Rajasthan & Others

Supreme Court of India

27 April 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Grover, J.C. Shah, K.S. Hegde

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 66; (1970) 2 SCC 26; AIR 1971 SC 1330; [1971] 1 S.C.R. 396; 1970 (26) STC 341

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Sales Tax

Keywords: Rajasthan Sales Tax Act 1950

Summary: Constitution, art. 14 - Rajasthan Sales Tax Act, 1954, s. 4(2) - Notification excluding 'hosiery products' from exemption from sales tax -Discrimination, if any - When exemption was given to sale of garment the value of which did not exceed Rupees four in single piece - Held, is not discriminatory - Appeal Dismissed.
