Panchamal Narayan Shenoy v Basthi Venkatesha Shenoy

Supreme Court of India

20 February 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

C.A. Vaidyialingam, S.M. Sikri, Vishishtha Bhargava

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 346; (1970) 1 SCC 499; AIR 1971 SC 942; 1971 RCJ 41; [1970] 3 S.C.R. 734

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Karnataka Rent Control Act, 1961, s. 21(1), Proviso (j) - Tenancy - (A) Eviction sought on the ground of reasonable and bona fide requirement of landlord for immediate demolition and rebuilding - (B) Proceedings for eviction and recovery of possession - Claim for compensation - Landlord need not prove that condition of building is such that it immediately requires demolition - Claim for compensation for improvements cannot arise for consideration in such proceedings - Appeal Dismissed.
