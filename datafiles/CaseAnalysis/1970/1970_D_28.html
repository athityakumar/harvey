O. N. Mahindroo v Distt. Judge, Delhi and Another

Supreme Court of India

04 September 1970

Case Analysis

Bench

Mohammad Hidayatullah, G.K. Mitter, A.N. Ray

Where Reported

1970 Indlaw SC 130; (1971) 3 SCC 5; AIR 1971 SC 107; [1971] 2 S.C.R. 11

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Attorney-General

Summary: Advocates Act, 1961, ss. 37, 38 and 44 - Constitution, art. 137 - (A) Powers of Supreme Court - To review its previous order - When appeal lies both on facts and law - (B) Bar Council - Powers of - Supreme Court held, can review its previous order in appropriate cases - Powers of appeal and review stated - Application Allowed.
