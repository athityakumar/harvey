P. D. Goel v High Court of Himachal Pradesh, through its Registrar General

Supreme Court of India

08 August 2017

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Abdul Nazeer, J. Chelameswar

Where Reported

2017 Indlaw SC 623

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges

Keywords: Age, Himachal Pradesh Judicial Service Rules, 2004, Constitution of India, 1950

Summary: Advocates & Judges - Himachal Pradesh Judicial Service Rules, 2004 - Retrospective retirement of judges - Appellant filed WP before HC challenging notification dt.20-4-2005 issued by HC holding that appellant stands retired from service with effect from afternoon of 31-7-2005 - Single Judge held that retiring appellant at the age of 58 years vide order dt.20-4-2005 is contrary to law and had also struck down adverse entry made in Annual Confidential Report of appellant - Aggrieved, respondent filed appeal and DB of HC held that impugned notification has to be treated as recommendation of HC to Governor for removal of services of appellant and that Governor has to proceed and make necessary consequential order in accordance with recommendations of HC - Hence, instant appeal by appellant - Whether retiring appellant retrospectively on completing the age of 58 years is justified in law.

Held, instead of quashing impugned notification, DB treated it as recommendation of HC to Governor for removal of services of appellant and in terms of said order of DB, Governor passed order dt.31-1-2017 retrospectively retiring appellant with effect from 31-7-2005 which is not permissible in law since the Rules had not provided for retrospectively retiring judicial officers. Hence, order of HC retiring appellant at the age of 58 years could not take effect as it was without authority of law and appellant has to be treated to have been retired from service on completion of 60 years of age on 31-7-2017. Further, order of DB treating recommendation of HC to Governor for compulsorily retiring appellant could not be sustained and so, to that extent, order of DB is set aside and also order of Governor dt.31-1-2017 is set aside. Appellant is entitled to salary, allowances and all other consequential benefits till 31-7-2007. Appeal allowed.
