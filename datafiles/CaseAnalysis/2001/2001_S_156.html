O. P. Lather and Others Etc v Satish Kumar Kakkar and Others

Supreme Court of India

02 February 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

K.G. Balakrishnan, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 20511; (2001) 3 SCC 110; (2001) SCC (L&S) 528; AIR 2001 SC 821; 2001 (88) FLR 997; JT 2001 (2) SC 280; 2001 LabIC 744; 2001(1) SCALE 608; [2001] 1 S.C.R. 827; 2001 (1) SLT 955; 2001 (2) Supreme 22

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Electricity & Energy; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 136 - Indian Electricity Act, 1910 - Promotion - Service - Whether the clarification issued by the Govt. of Haryana by an executive order is proper and valid and whether it amounts to amendment of the rules made u/art. 309? - Relied on State of Haryana v. Shamsher Jang Bahadur 1972 Indlaw SC 37 and T. R. Kapur v. State of Haryana, 1986 Indlaw SC 242- Held, executive order valid and no need to amend rules u/art. 309 - Further held, executive order only clarificatory in nature and no illegality in promoting appellants to posts of ex-engineer - Appeal allowed without any costs.
