Executive Engineer C.P.W.D., Indore v Madhukar Purshottam Kolharkar and Anr

Supreme Court of India

27 March 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, Y.K. Sabharwal

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 14892; (2002) 9 SCC 622; (2002) SCC (L&S) 1087

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Maintainability of review - Review was filed against order of SC - Held, delay in filing review petition was condoned - On going through review petition and connected papers, it was clear that no merit was found in review petition - Review dismissed.
