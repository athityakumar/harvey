Dinesh Chandra Gahtori v Chief of Army Staff and Another

Supreme Court of India

19 January 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

Doraiswamy Raju, S.P. Bharucha, Y.K. Sabharwal

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 21088; (2001) 9 SCC 525; 2001 (2) UPLBEC 1275

Case Digest

Subject: Defence & Security Forces; Service

Keywords: Court Martial

Summary: Defence & Security Forces - Service - Appellant tried by a Summary Court Martial and had been found guilty of using criminal force against his superior officer - Awarded sentence of dismissal from service - High Court dismissed writ petition in limine contending that summary court-martial proceedings were conducted in State of Punjab and court had no territorial jurisdiction - Held, placing reliance only on cause of action was not justified - Appeal allowed.
