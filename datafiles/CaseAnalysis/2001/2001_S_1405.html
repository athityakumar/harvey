Union of India v Supreme Steels and General Mills

Supreme Court of India

15 October 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

Brijesh Kumar, S.P. Bharucha, Y.K. Sabharwal

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 431; (2001) 9 SCC 645; AIR 2001 SC 2987; 2001 (133) E.L.T. 513; JT 2001 (8) SC 518; 2001 (47) RLT 129; 2001(7) SCALE 235; 2001 (7) SLT 338; 2001 (7) Supreme 762

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Excise; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Indirect Tax

Keywords: Central Excise Rules

Summary: Excise - Central Excise Act, 1944, s. 3A - Finance Act, 1997 - Central Excise Rules, 1944, r. 96ZO - Whether r. 96ZO of rules is ultra vires Central Excise Act? - Two procedures namely one as provided under sub-s. (4) of s. 3A and other as provided under sub-rule 3 of r. 96ZO are alternative procedures and assessee has to opt for one - Once having done so he cannot claim benefit of other - Held, all cases of bunch stand finally disposed of accordingly.
