U.P. State Electricity Board v Searsole Chemicals Limited

Supreme Court of India

21 February 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, S.N. Phukan

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 19751; (2001) 3 SCC 397; AIR 2001 SC 1171; 2001 ALJ 696; 2001 (1) ARBLR 531; 2001 (2) AWC 1011; JT 2001 (3) SC 150; 2001 (1) RAJ 255; 2001 (2) RCR(Civil) 178; 2001(2) SCALE 148; 2001 (1) SCJ 502; [2001] 2 S.C.R. 13; 2001 (2) SLT 314; 2001 (2) Supreme 106; 2001 (1) UC 468

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Keywords: Uttar Pradesh Electricity (Regulation of Supply, Distribution, Consumption and Use) Order

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Electricity & Energy - Award - Challenge - Held, court not to re-appraise matter when arbitrator has applied his mind to pleadings, evidence adduced and terms of contract - If two views possible, arbitrator's view to prevail - Court not to consider materials, if not placed before arbitrator - No interference - Appeal dismissed.
