Commissioner of Customs, Visakhapatnam v Jaya Satya Marine Exports Private Limited

Supreme Court of India

01 May 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, S.P. Bharucha, Y.K. Sabharwal

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 324; (2001) 9 SCC 765; 2001 (131) E.L.T. 3; 2001 (5) SLT 95

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax; Agriculture & Agri. Products

Summary: Customs Act, 1962 - Agricultural Produce Cess Act, 1940, ss. 3 and 5A - Whether High Court erred in entertaining petition of respondent? - It is clear from s. 5A of Cess Act that alternate remedy by way of an appeal is available - Held, HC should not have entertained these writ petitions and should have relegated writ petitioners to alternate remedy - Appeal allowed.
