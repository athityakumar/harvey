Subhash v State of Maharashtra and Another

Supreme Court of India

04 September 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

Doraiswamy Raju, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 20597; AIR 2002 SC 2537; 2002 (1) AWC 375; 2002 (1) CLR 218; JT 2001 (10) SC 339; 2002 (35) LabIC 2473; 2002 (1) SLR 518; 2002 (1) SLT 746

Case Digest

Subject: Administrative; Civil Procedure; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Administrative Tribunals Act, 1985, s. 22(3)(f) - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 - Scope of review - Held, Tribunal could have interfered in matter if error pointed out was plain and apparent - Tribunal proceeded to re-examine matter as if it is an original application before it which is not scope of review, order on review made by tribunal set aside - Appeal allowed.
