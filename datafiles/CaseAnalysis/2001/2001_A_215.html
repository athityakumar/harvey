Real Optical Company v Appellate Collector of Customs

Supreme Court of India

01 February 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Phukan, S.S.M. Quadri

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 447; (2001) 9 SCC 391; AIR 2001 SC 1146; 2001 (2) ALD(SC) 127; 2001 (129) E.L.T. 7; JT 2001 (3) SC 125; 2001(2) SCALE 190; 2001 (1) SCJ 472; [2001] 2 S.C.R. 70; 2001 (2) SLT 429; 2001 (122) STC 555; 2001 (2) Supreme 389

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Keywords: Tariff Item, Uttar Pradesh Sales Tax Act

Summary: Excise - Uttar Pradesh Sales Tax Act - Whether Rough Ophthalmic Blanks falls under Tariff Item No. 23-A(4) or under Residuary Tariff Item No. 68 of Central Excise Tariff? - In interpreting items in Statutes like Excise Acts or Sales Tax Acts resorts should be had not to scientific or technical meaning of terms or expressions used but how is product identified by class or section of people dealing with or using product, however, if any term or expression has been defined in enactment then it must be understood in sense in which it is so defined - Held, rough optical blanks would not come under Tariff Item No. 23-A (4) and will come under Residuary Tariff Item No. 68 - Appeal allowed.
