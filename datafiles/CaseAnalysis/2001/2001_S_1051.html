Rajinder Singh Dr v State of Punjab and Others

Supreme Court of India

11 April 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

R.P. Sethi, K.T. Thomas

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 388; (2001) 5 SCC 482; (2001) SCC (L&S) 855; AIR 2001 SC 1769; 2001 (2) CLR 311; 2001 (89) FLR 738; JT 2001 (4) SC 538; 2001 LabIC 1368; 2001(3) SCALE 289; [2001] 2 S.C.R. 1108; 2001 (2) SLR 647; 2001 (3) SLT 620; 2001 (3) Supreme 322; 2001 (2) UPLBEC 1502

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 309 - Punjab Civil Medical (State Service Class I) Rules, 1972, rr. 9 and 9A - Service - Promotion - Whether High Court in upholding departmental recommendation committee that without amendment of rules class II service can be treated as class I service only by way of notification? - Held, violation of art. 309, recommendation of departmental recommendation committee set aside and post of deputy director left vacant and to appoint fresh recommendation committee - Appeal set aside.
