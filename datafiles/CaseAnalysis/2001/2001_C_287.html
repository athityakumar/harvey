Commissioner of Income Tax v Narendra Doshi

Supreme Court of India

26 July 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

Brijesh Kumar, S.P. Bharucha, Y.K. Sabharwal

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 20755; (2004) 2 SCC 801; 2002 (174) CTR 411; [2002] 254 ITR 606; [2002] 122 TAXMAN 717

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Interest on interest - Grant of simple interest - Tribunal and High Court followed D J Works case [1991 Indlaw GUJ 126] and Chimanlal S Patel judgment [1993 Indlaw GUJ 184] - Decisions hold that revenue is liable to pay interest on amount of interest which it should have paid to assessee but has unjustifiably failed to do - Revenue has not challenged correctness of D J Works case and Chimanlal S Patel - Held, revenue bound by principle laid down therein - Appeal dismissed.
