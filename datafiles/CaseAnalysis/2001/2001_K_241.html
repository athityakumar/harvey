Sumita Singh v Kumar Sanjay and Another

Supreme Court of India

26 February 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, Y.K. Sabharwal

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 21020; (2001) 10 SCC 41; AIR 2002 SC 396; 2001 (3) SLT 33; 2001 (5) Supreme 667

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Transfer of matrimonial proceedings

Summary: Civil Procedure - Practice & Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 24 - Transfer of matrimonial proceedings - Whether wife was educated woman who could travel - Held, husband's suit against wife - Wife's convenience must be looked at - Matrimonial Case pending was transferred - Petition allowed.
