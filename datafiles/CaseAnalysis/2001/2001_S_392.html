Ghulam Qadir v Special Tribunal and others

Supreme Court of India

03 October 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

R.P. Sethi, M.B. Shah

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 28; (2002) 1 SCC 33; AIR 2001 SCW 4022; JT 2001 (9) SC 231; 2001(6) SCALE 667; 2001 (7) SLT 50; [2001] Supp3 S.C.R. 504; 2001 (7) Supreme 518

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Rent Control

Keywords: Jammu and Kashmir Constitution Act, 1996, East Punjab Urban Rent Restrictions Act, 1949, Jammu Kashmir State Evacuees (Administration Of Property) Act

Summary: Allottees/lessees of the evacuee property are necessary parties to the proceedings initiated either under " Section 8 or Section 14 of the Jammu Kashmir State Evacuees (Administration of Property) Act. The scope of revisional power under the Act is wider than the powers exercisable in revision petitions.
