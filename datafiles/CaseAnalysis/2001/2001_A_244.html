S. B. I. and Others v Arvind K. Shukla

Supreme Court of India

21 February 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Agrawal, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 363; AIR 2001 SC 2398; 2001 (2) AWC 1441; 2001 (2) CLR 300; 2001 (89) FLR 849; JT 2001 (4) SC 415; 2001 (34) LabIC 2387; 2001 (1) LLJ 1419; 2001 (3) SLR 602; 2001 (3) SLT 579; 2001 (4) Supreme 496; 2001 (2) UPLBEC 1377

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - State Bank of India Service Rules, rr. 49(g) and 68(3)(iii) - Termination - Whether non-furnishing of reasons by disciplinary authority for disagreement with finding of enquiry officer vitiates order of termination? - Held, bank duty bound to give reasons for disagreement - Question concluded by decision in Managing Director, ECIL v. B Karunakar, Indlaw SC 430 - Appeal dismissed.
