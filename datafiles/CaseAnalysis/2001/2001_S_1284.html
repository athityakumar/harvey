Sri Kant Tripathi and Others v State of Uttar Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

07 September 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, Ruma Pal

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 12; (2001) 10 SCC 237; AIR 2001 SC 3757; 2001 (4) AWC 3010; 2001 (3) CLR 1004; 2002 (92) FLR 297; JT 2001 (7) SC 519; 2001 (34) LabIC 3337; 2001(6) SCALE 162; 2001 (3) SCJ 494; 2001 (4) SLR 251; 2001 (6) SLT 451; [2001] Supp2 S.C.R. 635; 2001 (6) Supreme 817; 2001 (3) UPLBEC 2401

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Uttar Pradesh Higher Judicial Service Rules, 1975, Delhi Sales Tax Act, 1978, Uttar Pradesh Higher Judicial Service Rules

Summary: Supreme Court : Appointments already made to the Higher Judicial Service, whether by direct recruitment or by promotion, need not be annulled and shall be continued subject to the Rules in this regard being implemented strictly for the purpose for determining seniority.
