Harsh Dhingra and Others v State of Haryana and others

Supreme Court of India

28 September 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, Doraiswamy Raju, K.G. Balakrishnan

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 98; (2001) 9 SCC 550; AIR 2001 SC 3795; JT 2001 (8) SC 296; 2002 (130) PLR 672; 2001(6) SCALE 500; 2001 (7) SLT 23; [2001] Supp3 S.C.R. 446; 2001 (7) Supreme 370

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property

Keywords: Haryana Urban Development Authority Act 1988

Summary: Land & Property - Civil Procedure - Haryana Urban Development Authority Act, 1988 - Clarification of order - Appellant grievance was that SC order dt. 7-5-1997 in Sanjay Jain vs. Anil Sabharwal & Ors, 1997 Indlaw SC 2051, had been misconstrued to mean that the legality of allotment of plots made under the discretionary quota even prior to 31-10-1989 had been directed by that order to be reopened and examined - It was submitted that such a misinterpretation results from a misconstruction of certain words in that order.

Held, it is clarified that by the order dt. 7-5-1997 SC upheld cancellation of the allotments out of the discretionary quota made after 31-10-1989 and it was further said that any remaining allotments of the same kind should be treated alike to complete the exercise. Order dt. 7-5-1997 contained the direction to treat all allotments out of the discretionary quota made after 31-10-1989 without any exception, in order to examine the accountability of the concerned authorities as also to avoid any discrimination between allottees subsequent to 31-10-1989. That order was, therefore, concerned entirely with the allotment made after 31-10-1989 and did not refer to any allotment prior to that date. Appeal disposed of.
