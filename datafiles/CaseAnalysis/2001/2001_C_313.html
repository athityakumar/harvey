Sakthi Trading Company v Commissioner of Income Tax

Supreme Court of India

02 August 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.K. Sabharwal, Brijesh Kumar

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 275; (2001) 6 SCC 455; AIR 2001 SC 3166; 2001 (169) CTR 297; [2001] 250 ITR 871; JT 2001 (6) SC 81; 2001(4) SCALE 663; 2001 (5) SLT 622B; [2001] Supp1 S.C.R. 214; 2001 (5) Supreme 724; [2001] 118 TAXMAN 301

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961 - Whether valuing closing stock on market value is justified where business is taken over by a partner without discontinuance on dissolution of firm? - Held, if business is also discontinued closing stock instead may be valued at cost or market value, whichever is less - Where business is not discontinued, question of revaluing stock cannot arise at all - Answered in favour of assessee - Appeal allowed.
