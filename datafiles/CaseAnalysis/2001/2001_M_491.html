 Bharat Petroleum Corporation Limited v Mumbai Shramik Sangha and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 April 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, S.P. Bharucha, Shivaraj V. Patil, V.N. Khare, Y.K. Sabharwal

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 233; (2001) 4 SCC 448; 2001 (3) Bom.C.R. 326; 2001 (89) FLR 1028A; [2001] 249 ITR 669; JT 2001 (5) SC 120; 2001 (2) LLJ 248; 2001 (2) LLN 832; 2001(3) SCALE 531; [2001] 3 S.C.R. 208; 2001 (2) SLR 609; 2001 (4) SLT 359A; 2001 (123) STC 106; 2001 (3) Supreme 553; [2001] 117 TAXMAN 377; 2001 (2) UC 50A; 2001 (2) UPLBEC 1764

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Labour & Industrial Law - Contract Labour (Regulation and Abolition) Act, 1970, s. 10 - Correctness of scope attributed to s. 10 in Gammon India Ltd [1974 Indlaw SC 62] - Held, decision of a Constitution Bench binds a Bench of Two Learned Judges - Matter directed to be heard by a Bench of Two Learned Judges.
