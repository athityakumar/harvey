Gurdial Singh v Union of India and others

Supreme Court of India

25 September 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

R.P. Sethi, M.B. Shah

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 212; (2001) 8 SCC 8; AIR 2001 SC 3883; JT 2001 (8) SC 165; 2001(6) SCALE 471; 2001 (3) SCJ 667; 2002 (1) SLJ 93; 2001 (4) SLR 536; 2001 (6) SLT 731; [2001] Supp3 S.C.R. 323; 2001 (7) Supreme 216; 2002 (1) UPLBEC 217

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Service

Keywords: Freedom Fighters Pension Scheme, 1972

Summary: As the object of the Freedom Fighters Pension Scheme, 1972 is to honour and to mitigate the sufferings of those who had given all for the country, a liberal and not a technical approach is required to be followed while determining the merits of the case of a person seeking pension under the scheme.
