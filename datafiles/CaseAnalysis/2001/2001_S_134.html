Santosh Yadav v Narender Singh

Supreme Court of India

30 October 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, P. Venkatarama Reddi

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 218; (2002) 1 SCC 160; AIR 2002 SC 241; 2001 (6) ALD(SC) 127; JT 2001 (9) SC 392; 2001(7) SCALE 538; 2001 (5) SCJ 377; 2001 (7) SLT 714; [2001] Supp4 S.C.R. 545; 2001 (8) Supreme 72

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Election

Keywords: Election Dispute, Grounds For Declaring Election To Be Void

Summary: An election petition seeking relief on a ground under section 100 (1) (d) of the Representation of the People Act, 1951 must precisely allege all material facts on which the petitioner relies in support of the plea that the result of the election has been materially affected.
