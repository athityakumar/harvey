Gorakhpur University and Others v Dr Shitla Prasad Nagendra and Others

Supreme Court of India

07 August 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

Doraiswamy Raju, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 20049; (2001) 6 SCC 591; (2001) SCC (L&S) 1032; AIR 2001 SC 2433; 2001 (3) AWC 2436; 2001 (3) CLR 262; 2001 (91) FLR 166; JT 2001 (6) SC 285; 2001 (34) LabIC 2821; 2001(5) SCALE 87; 2001 (3) SLR 735; 2001 (5) SLT 648; 2001 (5) Supreme 798; 2001 (3) UPLBEC 2479

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Recovery of penal rent from retirement benefits - Held, authorities accepted rent at normal rates till vacation of accommodation - No heed paid to request for allotment of said accommodation to respondents son who also in service of university - University showed lethargy in taking action against respondent - Disclosed facts also reveal that rates of penal rent increased one year after vacation of accommodation and thus same could not be applied against respondent - Appeal dismissed.
