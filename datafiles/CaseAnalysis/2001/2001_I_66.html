State of Punjab and Others v Ishar Singh and Others

Supreme Court of India

20 February 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Mohapatra, Doraiswamy Raju

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 20471; (2002) 10 SCC 674; AIR 2002 SC 2422; 2001 (3) AWC 2246; 2001 (2) CLR 919; 2001 (90) FLR 709; JT 2001 (5) SC 331; 2002 LabIC 2325; 2002 (2) SLR 289; 2001 (3) SLT 248

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Appointment on ad-hoc basis - Subsequently regularized - Whether period of ad hoc services can be counted for purpose of fixation of their pay in revised scales of pay and grant them senior and selection grade, proficiency step-up and seniority? - Held, ad hoc service rendered by employees is not to be included for purpose of calculating 8/18 years of service, only regular service is to be counted for purpose - Followed judgment in Haryana veterinary and AHTS association case - Appeal allowed.
