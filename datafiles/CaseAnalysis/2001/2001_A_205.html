Supreme Court of India

19 October 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

R.P. Sethi, S.N. Phukan

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 94; (2001) 8 SCC 540; AIR 2001 SC 2984; 2001 (4) AWC 3228; 2002 (1) CRC 137; JT 2001 (9) SC 84; 2001 (2) RCR(Rent) 514; 2001(7) SCALE 398; 2001 (7) SLT 500; 2001 (7) Supreme 794; 2002 (1) UC 223

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Keywords: Uttar Pradesh Urban Building (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972

Summary: If a period of more than three and a half years has elapsed since the date of purchase of the building, it is not mandatory for the landlord to give a notice of six months to the tenant, for eviction, under Section 21 of the Uttar Pradesh Urban Buildings (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972.
