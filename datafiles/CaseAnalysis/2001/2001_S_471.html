Shri Sant Sadguru Janardan Swami (Moingiri v State Of Maharashtra And Others

Supreme Court of India

25 September 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

V.N. Khare, B.N. Agrawal

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 225; (2001) 8 SCC 509; AIR 2001 SC 3982; 2002 AllCJ 341; 2002 (2) Bom.C.R. 149; JT 2001 (8) SC 287; 2001(6) SCALE 585; 2001 (3) SCJ 682; 2001 (7) SLT 1; [2001] Supp3 S.C.R. 333; 2001 (7) Supreme 362

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Election

Keywords: Grounds For Declaring Election To Be Void

Summary: The preparation of the electoral roll being an intermediate stage in the process of election of the managing committee of a specified society and the election process having been set in motion, the High Court cannot stay the continuation of the election process even though there may be some alleged illegality or breach of rules while preparing the electoral roll.
