S. N. Kapoor (Dead) By LRs v Basant Lal Khatri and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 November 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

Doraiswamy Raju, M.B. Shah

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 20390; (2002) 1 SCC 329; AIR 2002 SC 171; 2001 (6) ALD(SC) 123; 2002 (46) ALR 209; JT 2001 (9) SC 456; 2002 (1) RCR(Rent) 217; 2001(8) SCALE 16; 2001 (7) SLT 648; [2001] Supp5 S.C.R. 33; 2001 (8) Supreme 65

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958, ss. 14-B, 14-D, 14(1)(e) and 25-B - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 136 and 142 - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 7, r. 7 - High Court reject prayer for converting application filed initially for eviction u/s. 14(1)(e) of Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958 into one u/s. 14-D of Act - Whether orders of Rent Controller as well as of HC are contrary to law and that correct principles governing claim have not been properly applied? - Held, subsequent developments and altered circumstances were held to be relevant in adjudging nature and character of claim made, at all stages of proceedings therefore HC erred in refusing to allow application for modification of claim made u/s. 14(1)(e) into one u/s. 14-D, for being considered on its merits - Appeal allowed.
