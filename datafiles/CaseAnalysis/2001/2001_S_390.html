Agra Development Authority v Special Land Acquisition Officer and Others

Supreme Court of India

07 February 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Variava, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 20300; (2001) 2 SCC 646; AIR 2001 SC 992; 2001 ALJ 497; 2001 (1) AllCJ 526; 2001 (42) ALR 821; 2001 (1) AWC 845; JT 2001 (2) SC 489; 2001 (2) RCR(Civil) 118; 2001 RD 342; 2001(1) SCALE 679; 2001 (2) SLT 22; 2001 (1) Supreme 591; 2001 (2) UPLBEC 1127

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 4, 18 and 50 - Compensation - Notification u/s. 4 issued for acquiring land - Award made - Quantum of compensation challenged - As per provisions of s. 50 body for whom property is being acquired is given an opportunity to appear and adduce evidence for purposes of determining amount of compensation - In present case, no notice had been issued to them and they had not been given any opportunity to adduce evidence for purposes of determining amount of compensation - Matter requires to be sent back to Special Land Acquisition Officer for re fixing compensation payable - Appeal disposed of.
