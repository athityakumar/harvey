Ananga Uday Singh Deo v Ranga Nath Mishra and others

Supreme Court of India

12 October 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

Ashok Bhan, R.C. Lahoti

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 86; (2002) 1 SCC 499; AIR 2001 SC 2992; JT 2001 (8) SC 574; 2002 (1) MLJ(SC) 66; 2002 (1) RCR(Civil) 184; 2001(7) SCALE 172; 2001 (7) SLT 227; [2001] Supp4 S.C.R. 88; 2001 (7) Supreme 479

Case Digest

Subject: Election

Keywords: Parliament (Prevention of Disqualification) Act, 1959, Purity, Madhya Pradesh Municipalities Act, 1961, Rent Control and Eviction Act, Registration of Elections Rules, Prohibition of Simultaneous Membership Rules

Summary: Where the returning officer declares a candidate elected after following the procedure under Rule 79 of Conduct of Election Rules, 1961, then subsequent reference to the procedure provided under Rule 80 and 81 is unnecessary, as the winning candidate had polled the requisite quota of value votes.
