Income Tax Officer, New Delhi v Delhi Development Authority

Supreme Court of India

29 November 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

Brijesh Kumar, Y.K. Sabharwal

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 169; (2002) 1 SCC 695; AIR 2002 SC 264; 2001 (171) CTR 546; [2001] 252 ITR 772; JT 2001 (10) SC 127; 2001(8) SCALE 316; 2001 (8) SLT 227; [2001] Supp5 S.C.R. 383; 2001 (8) Supreme 488; [2002] 120 TAXMAN 120

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: The term 'assesee' includes actual assessees as well as deemed assessees under the provision of the Act and therefore it is not correct to contend that unless there are actual assessment proceedings pertaining to any person, he cannot be considered to be an assessee.
