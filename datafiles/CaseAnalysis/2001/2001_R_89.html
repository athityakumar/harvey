Commissioner of Income Tax v Rajaram Maize Products

Supreme Court of India

23 August 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

Ashok Bhan, S.P. Bharucha, Y.K. Sabharwal

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 20765; (2002) 9 SCC 713; AIR 2002 SC 490; 2001 (170) CTR 393; [2001] 251 ITR 427

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 4 and 28(iv) - Whether power subsidy received by assessee was a capital receipt not liable to be taxed within meaning of s. 28(iv) of IT Act, 1961? - Relied on Sahney Steel case [1997 Indlaw SC 1527] - Held, power subsidies are of revenue nature in as much as it went towards reduction of electricity bills - Appeals allowed.
