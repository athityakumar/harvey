All India Non-Sc/St Employees' Association (Railway) v V. K. Agarwal and Others

Supreme Court of India

31 January 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, Ruma Pal

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 21093; (2001) 10 SCC 165

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Service - Reclassification or readjustment - Whether reservation for SC and ST is applicable in the upgradation of existing posts? - Held, it is only if in addition to the total number of existing posts some additional posts are created that in respect of those additional posts the reservation will apply - Present case is restricted to all existing employees who were redistributed into different scales of pay as a result of the said upgradation - Interim orders
