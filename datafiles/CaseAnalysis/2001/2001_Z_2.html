Vinod Tanna and Another v Zaher Siddiqui and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 September 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, Ruma Pal

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 21006; (2002) 7 SCC 541; (2002) SCC (Cr) 1825; 2002 (1) AllCriR 350; 2002 (48) ALR 441; 2002 (1) AWC 376; 2005 (3) BC 379; 2002 (1) CCR 177; 2002 (4) CRJ 446; 2002 (1) JCC 86; JT 2001 (10) SC 345; 2003 (1) MPLJ 373; 2002 (130) PLR 783; 2002 (1) RCR(Criminal) 622; 2002(3) SCALE 320; 2002 (2) SLT 82

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Corporate; Criminal

Summary: Banking & Finance - Criminal - Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881, ss. 138 and 142 - Quashing of proceedings u/s. 138 and 142 - Whether cheque had not been honoured would not constitute offence u/s. 138 of Act? - Unless conditions precedent mentioned u/s. 138 are satisfied, it is not attracted - Held, in present case, HC erred in refusing to quash criminal proceedings - Appeal allowed.
