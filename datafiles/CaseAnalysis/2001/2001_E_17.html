Commissioner of Central Excise, Coimbatore v Elgi Equipments Limited

Supreme Court of India

08 January 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

S.S.M. Quadri, S.N. Phukan

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 450; (2001) 9 SCC 601; 2001 (74) ECC 284; 2001 (128) E.L.T. 52; 2001 (42) RLT 974

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excise Act, 1944, s. 11-AC - Penalty - High Court held that s. 11-AC is prospective in operation and illegality committed prior to insertion of s. 11-AC in Act, cannot be subject matter of penalty under said provision - Reliance on judgment of Mithilesh Kumari [1989 Indlaw SC 736] - Held, no justifiable ground to grant leave to appeal - SLP dismissed.
