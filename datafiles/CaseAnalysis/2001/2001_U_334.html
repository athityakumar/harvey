Sunder v Union of India

Supreme Court of India

19 September 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, R.C. Lahoti, N. Santosh Hegde, S.N. Variava

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 67; (2001) 7 SCC 211; AIR 2001 SC 3516; AIR 2001 SCW 3692; 2001 (5) ALD(SC) 136; 2002 (1) AllCJ 129; 2001 (45) ALR 397; 2001 (4) AWC 3084; 2001 (3) JhrCR(SC) 309; JT 2001 (8) SC 130; 2001 (3) KLT 489; 2001 (129) PLR 860; 2001 (4) RCR(Civil) 727; 2001(6) SCALE 405; 2001 (4) SCJ 254; 2001 (6) SLT 641; 2003 (Supp) Bom.C.R. 296; 2001 (Supp) MLJ(SC) 122; [2001] Supp3 S.C.R. 176; 2001 (7) Supreme 37; 2001 (7) Supreme 456; 2002 (1) UPLBEC 204

Case Digest

Subject: Administrative; Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Administrative - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 23 and 34 - Payment of interest on solatium - Whether State liable to pay interest on the amount envisaged u/s. 23(2) of the Act.

Held, the compensation awarded would include not only the total sum arrived at as per s. 23(1) of the Act but the remaining sub-sections thereof as well. It is thus clear from s. 34 of the Act that the expression 'awarded amount' would mean the amount of compensation worked out in accordance with the provisions contained in s. 23 of the Act, including all the sub-sections thereof. Hence the person entitled to the compensation awarded is also entitled to get interest on the aggregate amount including solatium. The reference is answered accordingly. Order accordingly.
