Sriniwas Ramnath Khatod v State of Maharashtra and others

Supreme Court of India

19 November 2001

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Variava, K.T. Thomas

Where Reported

2001 Indlaw SC 204; (2002) 1 SCC 689; AIR 2002 SC 187; 2002 (1) ALD(SC) 54; 2001 (45) ALR 828; 2002 (1) AWC 66; 2002 (1) Bom.C.R. 7; JT 2001 (9) SC 551; 2002 (1) KLT 140; 2002 (1) MLJ(SC) 84; 2002 (3) RCR(Civil) 775; 2001(8) SCALE 185; 2001 (8) SLT 77; [2001] Supp5 S.C.R. 255; 2001 (8) Supreme 194; 2002 (1) UC 121; 2002 (1) UPLBEC 401

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Under Section 4 of the Land Acquisition Act a 'declaration' must be made within one year from the date of last publication of the Notification, and thereafter the 'publication' under Section 6(2) can take place at a later date as it is merely a ministerial act.
