P. U. Iqbal v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

20 December 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ratnavel Pandian, M. Fathima Beevi

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 438; (1992) SCC (Cr) 184; (1992) 1 SCC 434; AIR 1992 SC 1900; 1992 (1) AllCriC 42; 1992 (1) CCR 661; 1992 (1) Crimes 166; 1992 CRLJ 2924; 1992 CrLR(SC) 72; JT 1991 (6) SC 496; 1991(2) SCALE 1413; [1991] Supp3 S.C.R. 515; 1992 (1) UJ 259

Case Digest

Subject: Fema

Summary: Criminal - Conservation of Foreign Exchange and Prevention of Smuggling Activities Act, 1973, s. 3 - Detention - Execution of - Delay of about one year in arresting detenu due to indifferent attitude of authorities and police officials - No explanation for delay - Held, detention invalid - Order accordingly.
