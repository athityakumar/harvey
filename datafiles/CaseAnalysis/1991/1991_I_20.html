State Of Punjab v Iqbal Singh And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

10 May 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, Veeraswami Ramaswami, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 244; (1991) 3 SCC 1; (1991) SCC (Cr) 513; AIR 1991 SC 1532; 1991 CRLJ 1897; 1991 CrLR(SC) 449; 1991 (44) DLT 317; 1991 (2) DMC 1; JT 1991 (2) SC 495; 1991(1) SCALE 923; [1991] 2 S.C.R. 790; 1991 (1) UJ 482

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Municipalities & Local Governments; Tort

Keywords: Danger

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 306 - Accused of abetting his wife to commit suicide - Relationship strained between spouses over dowry, deceased apprehended death owing to which she sought police protection - Pressure for dowry stepped up by husband on death of deceased's father and severe beating was given to deceased a day before she committed suicide - Held, circumstances for suicide was created by husband - Conviction awarded by lower court upheld - Convicted under s. 306 - Appeal allowed.
