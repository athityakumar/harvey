Ex. Capt. K. Balasubramanian and Others v State of Tamil Nadu and Anothers

Supreme Court of India

14 March 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, K. Jagannatha Shetty

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 993; (1991) 2 SCC 708; (1991) SCC (L&S) 792; 1991 (16) ATC 925; 1991 (2) CLR 381; 1991 (63) FLR 283; JT 1992 (2) SC 562; 1993 LabIC 425; 1991 (2) LLJ 277; 1991 (2) LLN 15; 1991(1) SCALE 481; [1991] 1 S.C.R. 845; 1992 (3) SLJ 75; 1992 (3) SLR 112; 1991 (1) UJ 667

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Defence & Security Forces; Environment, Wildlife & Animal; Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Treating, Tamil Nadu State of Subordinate services Rules 1955

Summary: Constitution - Defence & Security Forces - Environment, Wildlife & Animal - Labour & Industrial Law - Water - Constitution Of India, 1950 - Tamil Nadu State of Subordinate Services Rules 1955 - Water (Prevention And Control Of Pollution) Act, 1974 - Short Service Regular Commissioned Officers - The petitioners joined the Indian Army as Emergency Commissioned Officers (ECOs) in 1963 after the Chinese aggression - They were discharged from the Army - They joined the Commercial Tax Service of the State of Tamil Nadu on being selected by the Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission through a competitive examination.
