K. S. Paripoornan and Others v State of Kerala and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 December 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Punchhi, B.P. Jeevan Reddy

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 427; (1992) 1 SCC 684; AIR 1992 SC 1488; JT 1993 (6) SC 480; 1992 (2) MLJ 28; 1992 (2) MLJ(SC) 28; 1991(2) SCALE 1398; [1991] Supp3 S.C.R. 485; 1992 (1) UJ 474

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Labour & Industrial Law; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 1(2) and 23(1 A) - Land Acquisition Amendment Act, 1984, s. 30(1) - Land Acquisition Act - (A) Compensation - Benefit of additional sum u/s. 23(1 A) - Availability and ambit - (B) Extension of Act to part B States - Effect - Held, (A) Not available in each and every case decided by Civil Court after enforcement of Amendment Act, ambit and reach of retrospectively of s. 23(1 A) is defined by s. 30(1) of Amendment Act 68 of 1984 - (B) Act in its entirety becomes operative in part B States and it cannot be said that provisions of Central Act to which retrospective effect has been given will only become prospectively operational in Part B States - Order accordingly.
