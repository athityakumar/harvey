Maharashtra State Financial Corporation v Jaycee Drugs and Pharmaceuticals Private Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

19 February 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

N.D. Ojha, S.C. Agrawal, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 519; (1991) 2 SCC 637; AIR 1991 SCW 612; 1991 (2) BankCLR 123; 1991 (3) Bom.C.R. 481; 1991 (1) CCC 558; [1991] 71 Comp Cas 360; 1991 (1) CompLJ 315; JT 1991 (1) SC 524; 1991(1) SCALE 276; [1991] 1 S.C.R. 480; 1991 (1) UJ 505

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance

Keywords: State Financial Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1985, Bombay Court Fees Act, 1959, Bombay City Civil Court Act, 1948

Summary: Banking & Finance - State Financial Corporation Act, 1951, ss. 2(c), 29, 31(4), 32 and 46-B - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 - General Clauses Act, 1897 - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 69 - Court Fees Act, 1870, art. 7 - Delhi High Court Act, 1966 - Bombay Court Fees Act, 1959 - Bombay City Civil Court Act, 1948, s. 3 - Constitution of India, 1950 - State Financial Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1985 - Indian Contract Act, 1872 - Indian Companies Act, 1913 - (A) Whether a petition u/ss. 31 and 32 of State Financial Corporations Act, 1951 can be filed only in City Civil Court and HC, on its original side, has no jurisdiction to entertain it? - Held, no - City Civil Court has no jurisdiction to entertain true suits and proceedings of civil nature exceeding Rs. 50, 000 in value - U/s. 32, sub-s. (11) of Act value of claims exceeding more than Rs. 50, 000 proceeding can be instituted in HC - (B) Whether an order for payment of money can be passed u/ss. 31 and 32 of State Financial Corporation Act, 1951 against surety who has given only a personal guarantee? - Held, yes - Cl. (aa) inserted in sub-s. (1) of s. 31 of State Financial Corporation Act by Act 43 of 1985 uses words 'any surety'- 'Any surety' will include not only a surety who has given some security but also one who has given only a personal guarantee - Appeal allowed.
