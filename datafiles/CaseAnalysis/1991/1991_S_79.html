Spl. Tehsildar Land Acqn. Vishakapatnam v Smt. A. Mangala Gowri

Supreme Court of India

09 August 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, N.M. Kasliwal

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 803; (1991) 4 SCC 218; AIR 1992 SC 666; AIR 1992 SCW 319; JT 1991 (3) SC 444; 1992 (1) MLJ(SC) 6; 1991(2) SCALE 301; [1991] 3 S.C.R. 472; 1991 (2) UJ 446

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 23 - Determination of compensation - Bona fide transactions in respect of same land under acquisition - High Court determining compensation placing reliance on award of some other land - Whether justified? - Land acquisition for housing scheme - Deductions for development of lands - Held, not justified - 1/3 rd of market value should be deducted - Appeal allowed.
