N. Suresh Nathan And Anr v Union Of India And Ors

Supreme Court of India

22 November 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

J.S. Verma, L.M. Sharma, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 847; (1992) SCC (L&S) 451; (1992) Supp1 SCC 584; AIR 1992 SC 564; 1992 (19) ATC 928; 1992 (1) CLR 916; 1992 (64) FLR 326; JT 1991 (5) SC 354; 1992 LabIC 351; 1992 (2) LLN 850; 1991(2) SCALE 1106; 1992 (6) SLR 143; [1991] Supp2 S.C.R. 423; 1992 (1) UPLBEC 2

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 311 and 309, Proviso - Recruitment rules for post of Asst. Engineer in Public Works Department, r. 11 - Whether a Diploma holder Junior Engineer who obtains a Degree while in service becomes eligible for appointment as Asst. Engineer by promotion on completion of three years' service including therein period of service prior to obtaining Degree or three years' service as a Degree-holder for this purpose is to be reckoned from date he obtains Degree - Held, to be reckoned from date he obtains degree and not earlier - Appeal allowed.
