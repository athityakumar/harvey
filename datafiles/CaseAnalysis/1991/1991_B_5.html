Managing Director, Electronic Corporation of India v B. Karunakar

Supreme Court of India

05 August 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, Veeraswami Ramaswami, Yogeshwar Dayal

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 658; (1992) SCC (L&S) 361; (1992) 1 SCC 709; 1992 (19) ATC 652; 1992 (65) FLR 185; JT 1992 (3) SC 605; 1992 (2) LLN 385; 1992 (4) SLR 601A

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Whether question of furnishing a copy of report of enquiry in disciplinary proceedings held after Forty-second Amendment arises - Held, matter to be referred to a larger bench - Since any stay order prejudicially affects interest of respondent who has order of reinstatement, respondent be reinstated in service with payment of one-half back wage - Order accordingly.
