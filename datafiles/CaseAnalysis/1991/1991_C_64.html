A. Gasper v Commissioner of Income Tax

Supreme Court of India

21 August 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ranganathan, Veeraswami Ramaswami

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 933; (1993) Supp1 SCC 52; AIR 1992 SC 147; 1992 (102) CTR 161; [1991] 192 ITR 382; 1992 (2) TLR 194

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 256(1) and 261 - Reference of more than one question sought - Tribunal refusing reference of one of questions - Jurisdiction of High Court - Appeal before Supreme Court - Jurisdiction - If tribunal refuses reference thereof cannot be read into one of other questions referred - Held, such issues as arise properly on facts and questions referred to HC can alone be answered - Question involved is one of which reference was declined by Tribunal - Same would not be permitted to be raised in appeal before Supreme Court - Appeal dismissed.
