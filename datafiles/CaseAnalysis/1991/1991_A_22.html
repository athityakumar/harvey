G. M. Omar Khan v Additional Commissioner of Income Tax, A. P., Hyderabad

Supreme Court of India

28 August 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, M.M. Punchhi

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 781; (1992) Supp3 SCC 33; AIR 1992 SC 107; 1992 (106) CTR 288; [1992] 196 ITR 269; [1992] 63 TAXMAN 533; 1992 (2) TLR 40

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income tax Act, 1961, s. 2(14)(iii)(a) - Whether an agricultural land is a 'capital asset'? - Held that agricultural land falling within jurisdiction of municipality having population of more than 10 thousand is capital asset - Appeal dismissed.
