Bank Of India v Jagjit Singh Mehta

Supreme Court of India

22 November 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

J.S. Verma, L.M. Sharma, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 810; (1992) SCC (L&S) 268; (1992) 1 SCC 306; AIR 1992 SC 519; 1992 (19) ATC 528; 1992 (1) BankCLR 399; 1992 (1) BC 191; 1993 (66) FLR 959; JT 1991 (4) SC 460; 1992 LabIC 306; 1992 (1) LLJ 329; 1992 (1) LLN 1; 1991(2) SCALE 1108; 1992 (1) SLJ 81; 1992 (1) SLR 426; [1991] Supp2 S.C.R. 492; 1992 (1) UPLBEC 484

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Service - Socio - Economic - Bank of India (Officers') Service Regulations, 1979, Regn. 47 - Service Rules - Whether posting of husband and wife at same station can be claimed as a right - In light of undertaking as to transfer anywhere - Held, could claim as of right - Appeal allowed.
