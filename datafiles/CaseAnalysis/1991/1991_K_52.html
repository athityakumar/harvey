Commissioner Of Income-Tax, Haryana Etc. Etc. v Krishna Copper Steel Rolling Mills, Jagadhri and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 November 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ranganathan, Veeraswami Ramaswami

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 951; (1992) Supp1 SCC 732; AIR 1992 SC 422; 1991 (3) CompLJ 367; 1991 (100) CTR 114; [1992] 193 ITR 281; JT 1991 (4) SC 350; 1991(2) SCALE 1014; [1991] Supp2 S.C.R. 187; [1992] 60 TAXMAN 93; 1992 (2) TLR 200

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 33(1)(b),33(B)(i)(a) and 80 I - Income Tax - Higher rate of development rebate - Entitlement as to - Entry 'Iron and Steel (Metal), Ferro-alloys and special steels' - Covers mild steel rods, bars or rounds etc. - Held, Industries manufacturing said goods are entitled to higher rate of development rebate and relief u/s. 80 I, sch 5 Entries 1 and 2 - Appeals dismissed.
