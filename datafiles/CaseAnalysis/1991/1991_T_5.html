Appropriate Authority and Another v Tanvi Trading and Credits Private Limited

Supreme Court of India

23 April 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Fathima Beevi

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 1067; (1998) 8 SCC 414; 1991 (100) CTR 278; [1991] 191 ITR 307

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Issue of no-objection certificate - Immovable property - Two alternatives open under scheme of legislation, viz - (i) Union of India through appropriate authority could buy property, or (ii) in event of its decision not to buy, it has to issue a 'no-objection certificate' leaving it open to parties to deal with property - Petition dismissed.
