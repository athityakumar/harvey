Kalyankumar Ray v Commissioner of Income Tax

Supreme Court of India

06 August 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ranganathan, M. Fathima Beevi

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 926; (1992) Supp2 SCC 424; AIR 1992 SC 159; 1992 (102) CTR 188; [1991] 191 ITR 634

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 143, 144 and 156 - Assessment - Form of order - Form usually styled as 'assessment order' need not contain computation of tax as well - Held, to avoid controversies Supreme Court directed department to adopt practice of incorporating entire tax calculations in assessment order or in alternative to make demand note as annexure to it - Petitions dismissed.
