Dr. Uma Kant And Anr. v Dr. Bhika Lal Jain And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

04 October 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

N.M. Kasliwal, M.H. Kania, M. Fathima Beevi

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 261; (1992) SCC (L&S) 1; (1992) 1 SCC 105; AIR 1991 SC 2272; 1992 (19) ATC 35; 1991 (2) CLR 734; JT 1991 (4) SC 75; 1991 LabIC 2222; 1991 (2) LLN 826; 1991(2) SCALE 769; 1991 (5) SLR 624; [1991] Supp1 S.C.R. 415; 1992 (1) UJ 134

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Rajasthan University Teachers and Officers (Selection for Appointment) Act, 1974

Summary: Education - Service - Rajasthan University Teachers and Officers (Special Conditions of Service) Act, 1974, ss. 3, 4 and 5 - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 136 and 226 - University teachers and officers - Appointments - Reserve list - Purpose of - Selected person joining post - Reserve list does not become extinct - Vacancy occurring again during subsistence of reserve list - Filled by appointing candidate from reserve list - Such procedure in vogue for decade - Whether fresh selection necessary? - Whether Court will interfere? - Held, fresh selection not necessary - Court will not interfere - Appeal allowed.
