S.L. Chopra And Ors. Etc. Etc. v State Of Haryana And Ors. Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

05 April 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, K.N. Singh

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 999; (1992) Supp1 SCC 391; (1992) SCC (L&S) 975; AIR 1991 SC 1126; AIR 1991 SCW 1028; 1992 (19) ATC 493; 1991 (62) FLR 785; JT 1991 (2) SC 203; 1991 LabIC 1097; 1991 (1) LLN 1072; 1991(1) SCALE 612; [1991] 2 S.C.R. 221; 1992 (3) SLR 80; 1991 (2) UJ 438

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Eligibility - Qualifications

Summary: Constitution of India, arts. 309 and 311 - Punjab Service of Engineers, Class I, PWD (Public Health Branch) Rules, 1961, rr. 2(12)(a), 5(2), 8(11) and 11(4) - Seniority - Determination of - Promotee Class II officer holding cadre post within quota of direct recruit and direct recruit promoted subsequently - Held, direct recruit promoted subsequently is senior.
