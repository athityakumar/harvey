S.P. Biswas and Others v State Bank of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

27 August 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

J.S. Verma, L.M. Sharma

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 232; (1991) Supp2 SCC 354; AIR 1991 SC 2039; 1992 (19) ATC 321; 1991 (2) BankCLR 607; 1991 (63) FLR 795; JT 1991 (6) SC 100; 1991 LabIC 2057; 1992 (2) LLJ 108; 1991 (2) LLN 805; 1991(2) SCALE 408; [1991] 3 S.C.R. 769; 1991 (3) SLJ 190; 1992 (3) SLR 136; 1991 (2) UJ 567; 1991 (2) UPLBEC 1333

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law; Trade

Keywords: Meritorious

Summary: Service - State Bank of India Act, 1955, ss. 43 and 49 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 - Banking Regulation Act, 1949, s. 52 - Service matter - Promotion of bank employee - Vacancies in merit channel - Interview - Select list - Whether consideration of marks obtained in written test alone is arbitrary? - Held, not arbitrary, as marks obtained under other heads are based on service record - Appeal dismissed.
