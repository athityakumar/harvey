Ramaswamy Kalingaryar v Mathayan Padayachi

Supreme Court of India

30 August 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, M.M. Punchhi

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 785; (1992) Supp1 SCC 712; AIR 1992 SC 115; 1992 (1) MLJ 19; 1992 (1) MLJ(SC) 19

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Keywords: Code of Civil Procedure 1908

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, ss. 100 and 100 A - Second appeal - Is it concurrent finding of fact - Whether questionable in second appeal? - Finding in favour of certain party as to his being in sole possession of land - Held, it is finding of fact - Unquestionable in second appeal - Appeal allowed.
