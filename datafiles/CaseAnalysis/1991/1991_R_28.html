State of Rajasthan v Rajasthan Pensioner Samaj

Supreme Court of India

23 April 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jayachandra Reddy, S. Ratnavel Pandian

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 624; (1991) Supp2 SCC 141; (1991) SCC (L&S) 1176; AIR 1991 SC 1743; 1991 (17) ATC 342; 1991 LabIC 1651

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1953, arts. 309 and 311- Pension - Contributory provident fund retirees and employees opting for pension scheme form different classes - Whether contributory provident fund retirees can switch over to pension scheme - Held, it cannot be claimed as right to switch over to pension scheme and get benefit granted to pension scheme retirees - However, Supreme Court directed Govt. to consider case of such widows - Therefore, proposal of Govt. to grant ex-gratia payment of Rs. 150 per month to widows of contributory provident fund retirees who had not opted for pension scheme accepted - Appeal allowed.
