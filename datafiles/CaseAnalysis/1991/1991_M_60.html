Burn Standard Company Limited v Mcdermott International Inc. And Another

Supreme Court of India

03 April 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, M. Fathima Beevi, Veeraswami Ramaswami

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 547; (1991) 2 SCC 669; AIR 1991 SC 1191; AIR 1991 SCW 1105; 1991 (1) ARBLR 380; 1991 (2) BankCLR 199; 1992 (2) CalLT(SC) 18; 1991 (2) CCC 1; [1991] 72 Comp Cas 281; 1991 (2) CompLJ 142; 1991 CRLR 318; 1991 CrLR(SC) 318; JT 1991 (2) SC 95; 1991(1) SCALE 587; [1991] 2 S.C.R. 67; 1991 (1) UJ 608

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Exchange Control & FDI

Summary: Exchange Control & FDI - Arbitration & ADR - Foreign Exchange Regulation Act, 1973, s. 28 - Exchange Control Manual,1978, Para 25 A 2, Para 24 A 11 - Whether arbitration clause contained in Article XII of Technical Collaboration Agreement entered into at Dubai, between appellant and respondent is rendered void by virtue of agreement itself being ab initio void for want of general or special permission of RBI under s. 28 of FERA ? - Held, permission granted by RBI but proper procedure was not followed - Further held, permission was granted by RBI after applying its mind - Procedures are merely to aid RBI in processing applications - Appeal dismissed.
