Deepak Theatre, Dhuri v State of Punjab and Others

Supreme Court of India

06 December 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, M. Fathima Beevi, Yogeshwar Dayal

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 296; (1992) Supp1 SCC 684; AIR 1992 SC 1519; JT 1991 (6) SC 489; 1991(2) SCALE 1404; [1991] Supp3 S.C.R. 242; 1992 (2) UJ 434

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Media & Entertainment; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Cinematograph Films, Andhra Pradesh Cinemas (Regulation) Rules, Punjab Cinemas (Regulations) Act, 1952, Punjab Cinemas Rules

Summary: Media & Entertainment - Constitution - Punjab Cinemas (Regulation) Act, 1952, ss. 5 and 9 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 19(1)(g) - Cinema theatre - Rules framed under the Act, r. 4 - Classification of seats in theatre and fixation of rates - Constitutionality - Held, it is regulatory measure - Does not infringe art. 19(1)(g) - Appeal Dismissed.
