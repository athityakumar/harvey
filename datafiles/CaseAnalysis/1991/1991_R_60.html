Ratnakar Gajanan Godambe v Ratnaprabha Co-Operative Society Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

07 March 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

R.M. Sahai, T.K. Thommen

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 678; (1991) Supp2 SCC 672; AIR 1991 SC 1378; 1991 (4) Bom.C.R. 399

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Keywords: Maharashtra Co-operative Society Act, 1960

Summary: Land & Property - Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1961, s. 91 - Society - Eviction of non-member - Whether non-member was inducted by or on behalf of member or after induction was accepted as tenant - Matter remanded to High Court for fresh consideration - Order accordingly.
