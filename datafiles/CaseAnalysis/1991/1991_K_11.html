K. Satyanarayan Subudhi v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

20 March 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

B.C. Ray, M.N. Venkatachaliah, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 583; (1991) Supp2 SCC 153; (1991) SCC (Cr) 1013; AIR 1991 SC 1375; 1991 CRLJ 1536; 1991 CRLR 859

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Criminal; Fema; Socio-Economic

Summary: Criminal - Conservation of Foreign Exchange and Prevention of Smuggling Activities Act, 1973, s. 3 - Detention - Retraction of confessional statement not placed by detenu before detaining authority - Further non-consideration of, while making order - Detention held, invalid as detenu was under detention for over eight months and order of detention was for period of one year - Appeal allowed.
