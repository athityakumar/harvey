All India Reserve Bank Retired Officers Association and Others v Union of India and Another

Supreme Court of India

10 December 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, M.M. Punchhi

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 309; (1992) SCC (L&S) 517; (1992) SCC 644; (1992) Supp1 SCC 664; AIR 1992 SC 767; AIR 1992 SCW 460; 1992 (19) ATC 865; JT 1991 (6) SC 400; 1992 LabIC 633; 1992 (2) LLN 635; 1991(2) SCALE 1264; 1992 (3) SLR 35; [1991] Supp3 S.C.R. 256

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Service

Keywords: Reserve Bank of India Pension regulations 1990

Summary: Service - Constitution - Reserve Bank Of India Act, 1934, s. 58(2)(j) - Reserve Bank Of India Pension Regulations, 1990, regns. 3(3) and 31 - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 12, 14, 309 and 311 - Introduction of Pension Scheme in lieu of Contributory Provident Fund Scheme - Bank employees who retired prior to 01-01-86 not given benefit of Pension Scheme - (A) Whether cut off date arbitrary ? - (B) Whether pension a charity ? - Held, (A) Cut off date not arbitrary, artificial or whimsical - (B) Not charity, bounty or gratuitous payment - It is earned for rendering long service - Petition dismissed.
