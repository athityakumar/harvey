Union Of India And Anr. v Deoki Nandan Aggarwal

Supreme Court of India

04 September 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

Veeraswami Ramaswami, K. Jagannatha Shetty, Yogeshwar Dayal

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 185; (1992) SCC (L&S) 248; (1992) Supp1 SCC 323; AIR 1992 SC 96; AIR 1991 SCW 2754; 1992 ALJ 258; 1992 (19) ATC 219; JT 1991 (3) SC 608; 1992 (1) LLN 42; 1991(2) SCALE 481; [1991] 3 S.C.R. 873; 1991 (5) SLR 16; 1992 (1) UPLBEC 644

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Service

Keywords: High Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Act, 1954, High Court Judges (Conditions of service) Amendment Act 1986

Summary: Advocates & Judges - Service - High Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Act, 1954, sch 1, part 1, para 2 and 9 - (A) Applicability of sch 1, part 1, para 2 and 9 of 1954 Act - Provisions relating to pension as amended by Act 38 of 1986 - (B) Interpretation of Statutes - Court's power to legislate - It is not duty of Court either to enlarge scope of legislation or intention of legislature when language of provision is plain and unambiguous - Held, (A) Applicable to all Judges respective of their dates of retirement - (B) It has no power to legislate - Appeal allowed.
