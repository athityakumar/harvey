 Sub-Committee On Judicial Accountability v Union Of India And Ors

Supreme Court of India

08 May 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

L.M. Sharma, M.N. Venkatachaliah, S.C. Agrawal, B.C. Ray, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 731; (1991) 3 SCC 65; (1991) SCC (Cr) 734; (1991) SCC (L&S) 807; AIR 1991 SC 1598; 1991 (17) ATC 102; JT 1991 (2) SC 493; 1991(1) SCALE 902; [1991] 2 S.C.R. 741; 1991 (2) UJ 194

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Judges (Inquiry) Act, 1968

Summary: Advocates & Judges - Judges (Inquiry) Act, 1968, s. 3 - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 124(5) - Constitution of inquiry committee for judicial enquiry against allegations of misconduct against a sitting Judge of Supreme Court - Directions issued by Supreme Court for expeditious disposal of matter - Appeal allowed.
