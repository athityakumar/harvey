State Of Punjab v Babu Singh

Supreme Court of India

16 April 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

L.M. Sharma, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 262; (1991) 3 SCC 18; (1991) SCC (Cr) 562; 1991 (2) Crimes 179; 1991 CRLR 475; 1991 CrLR(SC) 475; JT 1991 (2) SC 347; [1991] 2 S.C.R. 421; 1991 (2) UJ 129

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 (as amended by Act No.25 of 2005 & Act No.2 of 2006.)

Summary: Criminal - Practice & Procedure - CrPC, 1973,ss. 433-A, 432, 433 - Respondent who was undergoing sentence of life imprisonment, made an application for pre-mature release - HC issued a direction to release respondent on bail - Whether direction of HC is in accordance with provisions of s. 433-A CrPC? - HC has not taken into consideration provisions of s. 433-A CrPC while passing order - Judicial proceeding dealing with conviction and sentence of accused had been earlier concluded, and order of his release on bail was passed while finally disposing of writ petition alleging delay in disposal of mercy petition - Thus no case is now pending before Court, and order of respondent's release on bail thus not been passed as an interim measure pending decision of a case before Court - Order for respondent's release on bail has not therefore, been passed as an interim measure pending decision of case before Court - Held, in such a situation provisions of s. 433-A are attracted - Matter remitted to HC for reconsideration - Appeal allowed.

Legislation Cited

Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973

Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 s 433A
