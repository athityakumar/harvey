Bhagawathulla Samanna And Ors. v Special Tahsildar And Land Acquisition Officervisakhapatnam

Supreme Court of India

18 September 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Fathima Beevi, N.M. Kasliwal

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 982; (1991) 4 SCC 506; AIR 1992 SC 2298; 1992 (19) ALR 116; 1991 (3) CCC 347; JT 1991 (4) SC 56; 1992 (1) MLJ(SC) 9; 1991(2) SCALE 613; 1991 (Supp) RD 178; [1991] Supp1 S.C.R. 172; 1992 (1) UJ 1

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property

Summary: Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 23 - Determination of Market value - Factors to be considered - Acquisition of large tract of land for housing purpose - Deduction in land value - Whether warranted when it is already developed and has roads, drainage, electricity, communications in vicinity? - Held, not warranted.
