Ghan Shyam Das Gupta And Anr. v Anant Kumar Sinha And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

17 September 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

L.M. Sharma, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 379; (1991) 4 SCC 379; AIR 1991 SC 2251; 1991 ALJ 958; 1991 (3) CCC 291; JT 1991 (4) SC 43; 1991 (2) RCJ 401; 1992 (1) RCR(Rent) 42; 1991 (2) RentLR 654; 1991(2) SCALE 611; [1991] Supp1 S.C.R. 119; 1991 (2) UJ 730

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 21, rr. 97 and 106 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Question regarding maintenance of petition questioning executability of decree - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 contains elaborate, exhaustive provisions for same - Writ petition not to be entertained and not maintainable - Appeal allowed.
