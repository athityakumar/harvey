Saraswati Sugar Mills v Haryana State Board And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

22 October 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

Veeraswami Ramaswami, A.M. Ahmadi

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 318; (1992) 1 SCC 418; AIR 1992 SC 224; 1991 (3) CCC 647; 1991 (3) CompLJ 377; 1993 (66) FLR 313; JT 1991 (4) SC 220; 1991(2) SCALE 913; [1991] Supp1 S.C.R. 523; 1992 (1) UJ 7

Case Digest

Subject: Water

Keywords: Marine Products Export Development Authority Act, 1972, Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Cess Act, 1977, Agricultural and Processed Food Products Export Development Authority Act, 1985

Summary: Water - Environment, Wildlife & Animal - Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Cess Act, 1977, sch. 1, Entry 15, 3 - Taxing statute - Vegetable products industry - Vegetable - Common parlance meaning to be given - Sugar cane - Not vegetable - Industries manufacturing sugar from sugar cane - And those manufacturing alcohol - Whether covered by Entry 15 of sch. 1 ? - Liability to pay Cess - Held, not covered by Entry 15 of sch. 1 and not liable to pay cess - Also not liable to Cess - Order accordingly.
