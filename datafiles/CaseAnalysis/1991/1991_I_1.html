Central Provinces Manganese Ore. Co. Ltd v I.T.O Nagpur

Supreme Court of India

20 August 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 759; (1991) 4 SCC 166; AIR 1992 SC 567; 1991 (98) CTR 161; [1991] 191 ITR 662; JT 1991 (3) SC 452; 1991(2) SCALE 362; [1991] 3 S.C.R. 627; [1991] 59 TAXMAN 17; 1992 (2) TLR 317

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 147(a) - Validity of reassessment notice by Income Tax Officer u/s. 147(a) on basis of finding as to under invoicing as arrived by custom authorities in proceedings against assessee - Held, justified - Appeal dismissed.
