Mani Nariman Daruwala Alias Bharucha (Deceased) v Phiroz N. Bhatena and Others

Supreme Court of India

30 April 1991

Case Analysis

Bench

B.C. Ray, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1991 Indlaw SC 644; (1991) 3 SCC 141; AIR 1991 SC 1494; JT 1991 (5) SC 357; 1991 (1) RCJ 675; 1991 (2) RCR(Rent) 354; 1991(1) SCALE 885; 1991 (2) UJ 277

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Finding of fact

Summary: Land & Property - Constitution - Rent Control - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 227 - Bombay Rents, Hotel And Lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947, s. 15A - (A) Validity of license - Appellant owner of house - License created by statutory tenant - Held, license created by statutory tenant is legal and valid - (B) Whether High Court was right in interfering into finding that license subsisted at time of enforcement of Rent Act? - Further held, as finding had been supported by material on record - HC interference was improper and not called for - Appeal dismissed.
