Abdul Rehman Antulay v Union Of India And Ors. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

17 April 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

Amarendra Nath Sen, D.A. Desai

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 138; (1988) 2 SCC 764; AIR 1984 SC 1358; 1984 CRLJ 1152; 1984 CrLR(SC) 236; 1984(1) SCALE 620; [1984] 3 S.C.R. 482

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal

Summary: Practice and Procedure - Constitution, art. 32 - Petition challenging valdity of order and judgment passed by Supreme Court as nullity or otherwise incorrect - Held, isn't any merit in partition and therefore such petition cannot be entertained - Petition dismissed.
