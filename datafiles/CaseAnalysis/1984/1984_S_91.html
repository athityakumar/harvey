C. Elumalai v State Of Tamil Nadu

Supreme Court of India

26 October 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 224; (1984) 4 SCC 539; (1985) SCC (Cr) 1; AIR 1985 SC 118; 1985 (1) Crimes 656; 1985 CRLJ 510; 1985 CrLR(SC) 33; 1984(2) SCALE 645; [1985] 1 S.C.R. 1057

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Borstal School, Tamil Nadu Borstal School Act 1925

Summary: Education - Criminal - Practice and Procedure - Tamil Nadu Borstal Schools Act, 1926, s. 10A - Whether adolescent offenders from borstal school convicted of a capital offence but sentenced to imprisonment of life after attaining the age of 23 years are to be released as per s. 10A of Act ? - Held, detenu entitle to release after he has attained 23 years of age in a Borstal School - The State Government of Tamil Nadu cannot keep any adolescent offender who is convicted of a capital offence but sentenced to imprisonment of life in respect of whom an order is made under s. 10-A of the Tamil Nadu Borstal Schools Act in a Borstal School or in any other kind of detention after he has attained 23 years of age - Petition allowed.
