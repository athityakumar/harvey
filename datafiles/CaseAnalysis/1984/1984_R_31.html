Ram Sumiran and Others v D. D. C. and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 December 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 352; (1985) 1 SCC 431; 1985 ACJ 569; AIR 1985 SC 606; 1985 (11) ALR 171; 1986 (12) ALR 503; 1985 (1) ALT 305; 1985 (1) ALT(SC) 305; 1985 UJ 551

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Constitution - Civil Procedure Code, 1908, O. 9 r.9 - Abatement of Writ Petition u/art. 226 - Due to failure to bring legal representatives of respondent on record within time - Petitioners not taking any steps to bring them on record for six years, despite of being aware of - Application for setting aside abatement - Held, as petitioners were persons from rural area- application allowed - Appeal allowed.
