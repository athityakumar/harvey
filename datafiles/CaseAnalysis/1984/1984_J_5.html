State of Uttar Pradesh and Others v Jagdeo Singh

Supreme Court of India

01 May 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, D.P. Madon, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 399; (1984) Supp SCC 413; (1985) SCC (L&S) 44; AIR 1984 SC 1115; 1984 CRLR 612; 1984 (49) FLR 217; 1984 LabIC 863; 1984 (2) LLN 238; 1984 UJ 701

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Police Act, 1861 Station House transferred as a Second Officer despite protested loss of the special emolument - Later transferred again as a Second Officer - Appeal against Judgment of High Court allowing his writ petition - Applicability of s.7 to transfer of police Officer - Provision apply only to transfer is by way of punishment - Transfer due to misconduct entry and involving loss of special emolument - Held, s.7 (d) attracted - Transfer order set aside - Appeal dismissed.
