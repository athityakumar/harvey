Hari Singh & Ors. v State Of U.P. And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

06 April 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 160; (1984) 2 SCC 624; AIR 1984 SC 1020; 1984 (10) ALR 652; 1984(1) SCALE 625; [1984] 3 S.C.R. 417; 1984 UJ 648

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property

Summary: Land and Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 4, 5A, 17 and 17(4) - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Land - Notification u/s. 4 and s.17(4) of the Act for acquisition of land for acquisition for a public purpose - Order exempting operation of s.5 - But no construction for 2 years - Petition filed after 2 1/2 years (A) Effect of laches on the writ petition challenging validity of notification - (B) Validity of order - Held, petition is liable to be dismissed - Order made u/s. 17(4) of the Act exempting the operation of s.5A of the Act is bad in law even though there appears to be some administrative delay in commencing the construction of the Market Yard - Appeal dismissed.
