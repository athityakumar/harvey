Yogendra Tiwari v District Judge, Gorakhpur and Others

Supreme Court of India

07 March 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 420; (1984) 2 SCC 728; AIR 1984 SC 1149; 1984 ALJ 498; 1984 (10) ALR 285; 1984(1) SCALE 608; 1984 UJ 549

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Keywords: Deemed Vacancy

Summary: Rent Control - U.P. Urban Buildings (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972, s. 16(1), Proviso, s. 12(4) - Tenancy - Appeal by special leave against order of restoration of allotment passed by the Rent Control and Eviction Officer u/s. 16(1)(a) of the 1972 Act - Order in respect of deemed vacancy after opportunity of hearing is mandatory - Legality of allotment without hearing landlord - Held, impugned order of allotment having been made without affording to the appellant an opportunity to have his say in the matter was clearly a nullity- Appeal allowed.
