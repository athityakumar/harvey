Keshab Narayan Banerjee and Another v State of Bihar

Supreme Court of India

11 July 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 30; AIR 1985 SC 1666; 1985 CRLJ 1857

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - CrPC, 1973, ss. 437 and 439 - Validity of condition for grant of bail - Condition as to furnishing security for Rs. one lakh in cash or fixed deposit in nationalised bank and two sureties residing in the State for like amount - Held, condition amounted to denial of bail itself - Ordered that bail bond for Rs. 25, 000/- with two sureties each for the like amount is required - Order accordingly.
