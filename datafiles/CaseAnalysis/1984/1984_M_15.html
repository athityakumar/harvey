Workmen of Messrs Hindustan Lever v Management of Messrs Hindusian Lever

Supreme Court of India

05 January 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, A. Varadarajan, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 346; (1984) SCC (L&S) 183; (1984) 1 SCC 728; AIR 1984 SC 516; 1984 (48) FLR 219; 1984 LabIC 276; 1984 (1) LLJ 388; 1984 (1) LLN 460; 1984(1) SCALE 15; [1984] 2 S.C.R. 307; 1984 UJ 281

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act,1947, ss. 2 and 10 - Industrial Disputes (Central) Rules, 1957, r. 10 B - Civil Procedure Code, 1908, s. 11 - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 115 - Applicability of principles of res judicata - Agreement to not to raise question of status of employee, before Tribunal - Tribunal if can decide jurisdictionnal facts - Held, principles analogous to res judicata apply, no estoppel against statute - Tribunal cannot decide - Appeal allowed.
