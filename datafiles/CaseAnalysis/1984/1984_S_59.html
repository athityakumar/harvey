Rajendra Singh & Ors. Etc. v Smt. Usha Rani & Ors. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

27 February 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, A. Varadarajan, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 114; (1984) 3 SCC 339; AIR 1984 SC 956; 1984 (10) ALR 241; 1984(1) SCALE 440; [1984] 3 S.C.R. 22; 1984 UJ 313

Case Digest

Subject: Election

Summary: Election - Practice and Procedure - Representation of the People Act, 1951, ss. 81(3), 86 - Filing of incorrect copies of election petition - Election petition challenged on the ground that the copy of the petition served was neither attested to be a true copy nor a correct copy of the original petition, as contemplated by the provisions contained in s . 81 (3) of the Act - Respondent also getting the same Non- compliance of s. 81(3) - Effect - Held, petition liable to be dismissed - Petition dismissed.
