Geep Flashlight Industries Limited v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 August 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 8; (2002) 9 SCC 545; 1986 (6) ECR 430; 1985 (22) E.L.T. 3; 1984(2) SCALE 380

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act - Tariff item No. 15 A(2) and 68 - Whether manufacture of plastic torch was governed by tariff item No. 68 or 15(A)2? - Articles made of plastic would mean article made wholly of commodity commercially known as plastics, and not articles made from plastics along with other materials - An expression used in a taxing statute for describing a commodity must be given meaning which is generally given to it by a person in trade or in market of commodities and should be interpreted in sense, person conversant with subject matter of statute and dealing with it would attribute to it - Held, decision of High Court that plastic torch manufactured by petitioner is covered by residuary tariff item No. 68 and not 15 A(2), is correct and unquestionable - Petition dismissed.
