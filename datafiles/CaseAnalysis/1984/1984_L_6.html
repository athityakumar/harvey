Lakshminarayan Guin And Ors. v Lakshminarayan Guin And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

03 December 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 231; (1985) 1 SCC 270; AIR 1985 SC 111; 1984 KLT 1060; 1985 (1) RCJ 152; 1985 (1) RCR(Rent) 27; 1985 (1) RentLR 395; 1984(2) SCALE 924; [1985] 2 S.C.R. 202

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Rent Control - WB Premises Tenancy Act, 1956, ss. 13(1) and 13(6) - Tenancy - Applicability of s.13 - Act extended to particular area after eviction decree but while pendency of appeal - Entitlement to claim protection - Held, s. 13 can be invoked - Tenant entitled to claim protection as appeal was continuation of suit - Change in law pending appeal to be considered - Appeal dismissed.
