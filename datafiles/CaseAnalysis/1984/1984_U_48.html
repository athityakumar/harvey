Satnam Verma v Union of India

Supreme Court of India

19 October 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 32; (1984) Supp SCC 712; (1985) SCC (L&S) 362; AIR 1985 SC 294; 1985 (50) FLR 6; 1985 LabIC 738; 1985 (1) LLJ 79; 1985 (2) LLN 55; 1984 (3) SLR 232

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 10 - Industrial Disputes (Punjab) Rules, 1958, rr.22 and 24 - Civil Procedure Code, 1908, O 9, r.13 - Industrial Dispute - Reference to Labour Court - Whether Labour Court committed any error in rejecting application of appellant for setting aside ex-parte award on sole ground that once an award is published in Official Gazette, even if it be an ex-parte one, Labour Court has no jurisdiction to entertain an application for setting aside such an award - Held, Labour Court has jurisdiction - Appeal allowed.
