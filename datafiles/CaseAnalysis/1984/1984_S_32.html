Shivram Anand Shiroor v Radhabai Shantram Kowshik and Another

Supreme Court of India

31 January 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, E.S. Venkataramiah, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 341; (1984) 1 SCC 588; AIR 1984 SC 786; 1984 (1) Bom.C.R. 415; 1984(1) SCALE 130; [1984] 2 S.C.R. 750; 1984 UJ 297

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Bombay Rents, Hotel and Lodging House Rates Control Act,1947, s.13A1 - (A) Tenancy - Remedy u/s.13A1 is available to a member or retired member of Armed Forces to recover possession of premises - Whether available only if he had himself let out the premises while a member of Armed Forces - Held, not restricted to such cases - (B) Interpretation of Statutes - Words 'regain possession' in the statement of objects and Reasons - Held, words 'regain possession' in the context, are merely meant to convey 'obtain possession' - Appeal allowed.
