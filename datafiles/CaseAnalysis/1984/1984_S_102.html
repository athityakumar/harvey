Bihari Chowdhary and Another v State of Bihar and Others

Supreme Court of India

26 March 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

V. Balakrishna Eradi, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 331; (1984) 2 SCC 627; AIR 1984 SC 1043; 1984 (10) ALR 315; 1984 BLJR 219; 1984 (1) RCR(Rent) 516; 1984(1) SCALE 536; [1984] 3 S.C.R. 309; 1984 UJ 619

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - CPC, 1908 - Notice u/s. 80 - Question that arises for consideration in this appeal by special leave concerns the true scope and application of s. 80 of CPC, 1908 - Without waiting for the statutory period of two months from notice to state u/s. 80 - Held, suit before expiry of two months was not maintainable - Appeal dismissed.
