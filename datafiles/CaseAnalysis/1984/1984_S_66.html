Surendra Kumar v State Of Bihar And Others

Supreme Court of India

09 November 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, A.P. Sen, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 235; (1984) 4 SCC 609; AIR 1985 SC 87; 1985 (11) ALR 119; 1984(2) SCALE 723; [1985] 2 S.C.R. 19; 1985 UJ 392

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution

Summary: Education - Constitution, arts. 32, 14 - Admission - Reservation - Medical colleges in State of Jammu and Kashmir - Seats reserved for candidates from State of Bihar - Nomination for - Basis of selection by Chief Minister not indicated - Held, blatant abuse of power by CM - List of names recommended quashed - Petition allowed.
