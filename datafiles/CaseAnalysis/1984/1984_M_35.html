Mithilesh Kumar Pandey v Baidyanath Yadav and Others

Supreme Court of India

02 January 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, E.S. Venkataramiah, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 101; (1984) 2 SCC 1; AIR 1984 SC 305; 1984(1) SCALE 1; [1984] 2 S.C.R. 278; 1984 UJ 800

Case Digest

Subject: Election

Summary: Election - Representation of People Act, 1951, ss. 81(3), 86 and 123 - Election Petition - Corrupt practices - Allegations of - Copy of petition supplied to elected candidate - Mistakes therein as to names of persons through whom such practices were alleged to have been committed - Omissions, giving wrong names etc - Effect - Held, such mistakes are fatal - Petition has to be dismissed in limine - Appeal allowed.
