J. D. Shrivastava v State Of M.P. And Others

Supreme Court of India

24 January 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, O. Chinnappa Reddy, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 56; (1984) 2 SCC 8; (1984) SCC (L&S) 206; AIR 1984 SC 630; 1984 LabIC 337; 1984 (3) LLJ 344; 1984 (1) LLN 490; 1984(1) SCALE 125; [1984] 2 S.C.R. 466; 1984 (1) SLJ 364; 1984 (3) SLR 342; 1984 UJ 980

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Constitution, art. 311 - Fundamental Rules, r. 56(3) - Constitutionality of decision of compulsory retirement on the basis of State Confidential Reports - Held, arbitrary - On perusal of these reports shows that there was nothing against the appellant which necessitated the action which was taken against him - Appeal allowed.
