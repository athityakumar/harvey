L.D. Jaikwal v State Of U.P.

Supreme Court of India

17 May 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

M.P. Thakkar, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 155; (1984) 3 SCC 405; (1984) SCC (Cr) 421; AIR 1984 SC 1374; 1984 CRLJ 993; 1984 CrLR(SC) 277; 1984(1) SCALE 862; [1984] 3 S.C.R. 833

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Contempt Of Courts Act, 1971- Guilty of criminal contempt - High Court imposing sentence on Advocate - Imputation a corrupt Judge - Held, Supreme Court declined to interfere, merely, because Advocate tendered his apology - Guilty of Criminal contempt.
