Harihar Polyfibres v Regional Director, ESI Corporation

Supreme Court of India

04 September 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, Amarendra Nath Sen

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 313; (1984) 4 SCC 324; (1984) SCC (L&S) 747; AIR 1984 SC 1680; 1985 (1) CompLJ 313; 1984 (2) Crimes 563; 1984 (49) FLR 371; 1984 LabIC 1570; 1984 (2) LLJ 475; 1984 (2) LLN 747; 1984(2) SCALE 295; [1985] 1 S.C.R. 712; 1985 UJ 209

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Employees' State Insurance Act, 1948 , s. 2(22) - Interpretation of Statutes - Social Welfare Legislature - Whether the expression 'wages', defined by s.2(22) of the ESI Act, includes 'House Rent Allowance', 'Night Shift Allowance, paid to those employees who are obliged to work in the night shift and the 'Heat, Gas and Dust Allowance' and 'Incentive Allowance' paid by an employer to his employees - Held, Wages includes - HRA , Night Shift Allowance, Heat, Gas and Dust Allowance and Incentive Allowance - Petition dismissed.
