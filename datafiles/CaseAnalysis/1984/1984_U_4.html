Shri Vallabh Glass Works Ltd. & Others v Union Of India & Others

Supreme Court of India

14 March 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 125; (1984) SCC (Tax) 186; (1984) 3 SCC 362; AIR 1984 SC 971; 1984 (2) CompLJ 59; 1984 (40) CTR 184; 1984 (2) ECC 208; 1984 ECR 841; 1984 (16) E.L.T. 171; [1985] 155 ITR 560; 1984(1) SCALE 480; [1984] 3 S.C.R. 180; 1984 TAXLR 631; 1984 (2) TLR 631; 1984 UJ 539

Case Digest

Subject: Excise

Keywords: Tariff Item, Medicinal and Toilet Preparations (Excise Duties) Act, 1955

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Evidence Act, 1972, s. 72 - Writ petition u/art. 226 of the Constitution for recovery of excess excise duty paid by mistake - On the basis of assessments made by department - Delay - Writ petition instead of suit - Whether the petition is maintainable and relief be granted - Held, relief should be granted not from date on which party approached High Court, but for three years prior to filing of writ petition - Appeal allowed.
