Kanaya Ram and Others v Rajender Kumar and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 December 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 355; (1985) 1 SCC 436; AIR 1985 SC 371; 1988 CRLJ 839; 1984(2) SCALE 1039

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Possession Of Land

Summary: Land & Property - Punjab Security of Land Tenures Act, 1953, s. 18(1) - Application for purchase of holding by tenant - Making land owner and transferees from landowner as opposite parties - Landowner or his heirs not impleaded - Meanwhile heirs declared small landholders - A favourable decree for possession and title also becoming final - Whether tenant had accrued right to purchase - Held, no accrued right to purchase on date of making application - Appeal dismissed.
