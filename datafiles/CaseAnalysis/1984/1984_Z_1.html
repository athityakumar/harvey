Zafar Khan and Others v Board of Revenue, U.P. and Others

Supreme Court of India

31 July 1984

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1984 Indlaw SC 177; (1984) Supp SCC 505; AIR 1985 SC 39; 1984(2) SCALE 135; [1985] 1 S.C.R. 287; 1984 UJ 1110

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Limitation Act, 1963, s. 14 - Uttar Pradesh Consolidation of Holdings Act, 1954, s.49 - (A) Time spent in prosecuting another civil proceeding - Exclusion of - Expression 'Cause of like nature' to be read ejusdem generis with expression 'defect of jurisdiction' - Instance of applicability of s. 14 - (B) Village under consolidation - Whether land settlement in such villages can be challenged by a suit before civil or revenue Court - Held, plaintiffs (Khudkasht holders) not entitled to benefit of s. 14 of 1954 Act - Further held, cannot be questioned by suit - Appeal Dismissed.
