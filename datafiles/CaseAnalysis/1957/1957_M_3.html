 Madhubhai Amathalal Gandhi v Union of India

Supreme Court of India

31 August 1957

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Subba Rao, Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, Bhuvaneshwar Prasad Sinha, J.L. Kapur, Kailas Nath Wanchoo

Where Reported

1957 Indlaw SC 77; AIR 1961 SC 21; [1960] 30 Comp Cas 667; 1961 (1) SCJ 442; [1961] 1 S.C.R. 191

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Corporate; Securities Exchange Board of India

Summary: Corporate - Securities Contracts (Regulation) Act,1956, s. 4 - Constitution of India, 1950, arts.13, 19(1)(g) and 19(6) - Notification D/- 31-08-1957, u/s. 4 of Act - Active member of Indian Stock Exchange for 12 months immediately preceding, condition for membership - Constitutionality - Held, law includes notification - Not bars to business in spot delivery contracts - Not arbitrary - Valid - Petition dismissed.
