Menakuru Dasaratharami Reddi v Duddukuru Subba Rao

Supreme Court of India

10 May 1957

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, Sudhi Ranjan Das, Syed Jaffer Imam, A.K. Sarkar

Where Reported

1957 Indlaw SC 147; AIR 1957 SC 797; 1957 (2) MLJ 175; 1957 SCJ 835; [1957] 1 S.C.R. 1122

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Trusts & Associations - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 92 - Whether the properties in suit are the subject matter of public charitable trust or are merely burdened or charged with the obligation in favour of the specified charities? - Held, the compromise decree had not created a public trust, it is unnecessary to consider any other point - The properties in the hands of the appellants are subject to the charge in favour of the said charities - Appeal allowed.
