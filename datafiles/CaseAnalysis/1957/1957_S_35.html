Prakash Chandra Pathak v State of Uttar Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

10 July 1957

Case Analysis

Bench

Bhuvaneshwar Prasad Sinha, Syed Jaffer Imam, J.L. Kapur

Where Reported

1957 Indlaw SC 172; AIR 1960 SC 195; 1960 CRLJ 283

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1898, s. 342 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 302 and 380 - Appellant sentenced to death for double murder - Present appeal - Circumstantial evidence - Accused in chronic need of money to meet his borrowings - After murdering his mother, appellant stole her jewellery - Found in unexplained possession of jewellery - Held, facts and circumstances proved guilt of appellant beyond all reasonable doubt - Appeal dismissed.
