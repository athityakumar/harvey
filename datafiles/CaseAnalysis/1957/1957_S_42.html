Surinder Kumar and Others v Gian Chand and Others

Supreme Court of India

20 September 1957

Case Analysis

Bench

J.L. Kapur

Where Reported

1957 Indlaw SC 90; AIR 1957 SC 875; 1958 (1) MLJ(SC) 74; 1958 SCJ 159; [1958] 1 S.C.R. 548

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 41 r. 27 - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 41 - Indian Succession Act, 1925, s. 63(C) - Supreme Court Rules, O. 45, r. 5 - Admission of additional evidence - R. 5 of O. 45 of Supreme Court Rules recognises inherent power of court to make such orders as may be necessary for ends of justice or to prevent an abuse of process of court - Held, Supreme Court has power to admit additional evidence in appeal - Matter remitted to High Court.
