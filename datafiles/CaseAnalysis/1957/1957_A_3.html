Aggarwal Chamber of Commerce, Limited v Messrs Ganpat Rai Hira Lal

Supreme Court of India

11 November 1957

Case Analysis

Bench

J.L. Kapur, Bhuvaneshwar Prasad Sinha

Where Reported

1957 Indlaw SC 134; AIR 1958 SC 269; [1958] 33 ITR 245; 1958 (1) MLJ(SC) 121; 1958 SCJ 397; [1958] S.C.R. 938

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Companies Act

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Trusts & Associations - Contract & Commercial - Partnership Act, 1932, s. 6 - Income-Tax Act, 1951, ss. 26 and 54(2) - What are elements to be taken into account in order to find real relationship between parties under s. 6 of Partnership Act, 1932 - Held, in determining whether a partnership exists or not, regard shall be had of all circumstances of case, especially conduct of parties, together with important terms of document - In circumstances, it cannot be said that it was intended to bring about relation of partnership - Appeal dismissed.
