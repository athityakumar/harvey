Shri Ambalal M. Shah and Another v Hathisingh Manufacturing Company, Limited

Supreme Court of India

21 August 1961

Case Analysis

Bench

K.C. Das Gupta, Raghubar Dayal, Kailas Nath Wanchoo, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1961 Indlaw SC 175; AIR 1962 SC 588; 1961 (3) FLR 436; 1961 (2) LLJ 678; 1962 (1) SCJ 718; [1962] 3 S.C.R. 171

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Keywords: Scheduled Industry

Summary: Corporate - Administrative - Industries (Development and Regulation) Act, 1951, ss. 18A(1)(b), 15(b) - (A) Whether Central Govt. can take action u/s. 18 A (1) (b), only where investigation made u/s. 15 was initiated on basis of opinion as mentioned in s. 15(b)? - Held, no, it is not so - Words 'in respect of' which an investigation has been made u/s. 15 in s. 18A(1)(b) are not cut down by restrictive phrase - Any investigation on basis of s. 15(a) would necessarily cover also quality of management of industrial undertaking - Appeal allowed.
