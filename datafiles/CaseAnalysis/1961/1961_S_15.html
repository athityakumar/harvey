Satinder Singh and Others v Amrao Singh and Others

Supreme Court of India

02 February 1961

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, Kailas Nath Wanchoo, K.C. Das Gupta

Where Reported

1961 Indlaw SC 503; AIR 1961 SC 908; 1961 (2) SCJ 372; [1961] 3 S.C.R. 676

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Attorney-General

Summary: Civil Procedure - Practice& Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 - Punjab Requisitioning of Immovable Property Act, 1951, s. 5 - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 23, 32 and 34 - For determining amount of compensation and its apportionment amongst claimants, provisions of relevant Act of 1948 are applicable - High Court rejects claim of claimant for payment of interest on compensation amount - Whether appointment of arbitrator invalid and so he had no power to conduct inquiry? - Held, that order of HC is modified by directing amount of compensation payable in respect of lands in Mataur, Saneta and Giddarpur may be divided half and half between appellant and respondent no. 1 - Interest should be paid on all items of compensation at 4% per annum - Appeal partly allowed.
