K. T. M. T. M. Abdul Kayoom and Another v Commissioner of Income Tax

Supreme Court of India

23 November 1961

Case Analysis

Bench

Mohammad Hidayatullah, S.K. Das, J.L. Kapur

Where Reported

1961 Indlaw SC 293; AIR 1962 SC 680; [1962] 44 ITR 689; [1962] 1 S.C.R. 518; [1962] Supp1 S.C.R. 518

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 10(2)(xv) - (A)Whether payment of yearly premium by assessee obtaining lease of exclusive right to take and carry away conch shells or chanks of specified type from sea for three years is an allowable deduction u/s. 10(2)(xv) of Income Tax Act, 1922? - Held, no - Payment of yearly premium is in nature of capital expenditure and not a revenue expenditure - Amounts could not be claimed as a deduction u/s. 10(2) (xv) of Act - Appeal allowed.
