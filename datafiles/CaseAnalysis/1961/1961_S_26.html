Hari Shankar Lal v Shambhunath Prasad and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 May 1961

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Subba Rao, Bhuvaneshwar Prasad Sinha, Raghubar Dayal, J.R. Madholkar

Where Reported

1961 Indlaw SC 142; AIR 1962 SC 78; 1961 ALJ 748; [1962] 2 S.C.R. 720

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Indian Limitation Act, 1908 - Arbitration Act, 1940 - Whether notice to act was given by appellant to arbitrators on 31-08-1950 and award made by them on 3-10-1950, was within time prescribed? - Held, notice was given long after expiry of four months from date when arbitrators entered on reference and therefore, they could no longer act pursuant to notice calling upon them to act - Proper course should have been to apply to court for extension of time u/s. 28 of Act - Appeal dismissed.
