Mst. Subhadra v Narsaji Chenaji Marwadi

Supreme Court of India

09 August 1961

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, Kailas Nath Wanchoo, Raghubar Dayal, K.C. Das Gupta

Where Reported

1961 Indlaw SC 446; AIR 1966 SC 806; 1963 (1) SCJ 433; [1962] 3 S.C.R. 98

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Bombay Rents, Hotel and Lodging Houses Rates, Control Act, 1947, ss. 5(8), 6 and 11 - Respondent by deed dated 25-04-1947, sublet plot to appellant - Appellant by application 27-10-1950, applied to Court of Small Causes, Ahmedabad for fixation of standard rent of plot u/s. 11 of Act - Whether said application u/s. 11 was maintainable? - Held, before appellant could maintain an application for fixation of standard rent u/s. 11, she had to establish that plot of land leased was 'premises' within meaning of s. 5(8) of Act and that it was let for residence, education, business trade or storage - Material date for ascertaining whether plot is 'premises' for purposes of s. 6 is date of letting and not date on which application for fixation of standard rent is made by tenant or landlord - Till 11-12-1949, plot was assessed for agricultural purposes under Bombay Land Revenue Code - Hence, plot in dispute could not be regarded as 'premises' inviting application of Part II of Act - Appeal dismissed.
