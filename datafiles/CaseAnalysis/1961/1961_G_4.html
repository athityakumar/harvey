Mahanth Ram Das v Ganga Das

Supreme Court of India

07 February 1961

Case Analysis

Bench

Mohammad Hidayatullah, J.L. Kapur, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1961 Indlaw SC 80; AIR 1961 SC 882; 1962 (1) ALT(SC) 412; 1961 BLJR 495; 1961 (1) KerLR 379; 1962 (1) MLJ 167; 1962 (1) MLJ(SC) 167; 1962 (1) SCJ 427; [1961] 3 S.C.R. 763

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Practice & Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, ss. 148, 149 and 151 - (A)Whether court has power u/ss 148, 149 and 151 of CPC, 1908, to extend time period to pay deficit court fee? - Held, s. 148 allows extension of time, even if original period fixed had expired - S. 149 is equally liberal - Provided, if sufficient cause is made for extension - Appeal allowed.
