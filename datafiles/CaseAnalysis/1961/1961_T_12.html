First National City Bank v Commissioner of Income Tax, Bombay City

Supreme Court of India

06 January 1961

Case Analysis

Bench

J.L. Kapur, Mohammad Hidayatullah, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1961 Indlaw SC 162; AIR 1961 SC 812; [1961] 42 ITR 17; 1962 (2) SCJ 45; [1961] 3 S.C.R. 371

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Business Profits-tax Act, 1947

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Business Profits Tax Act, 1947 - Whether in computing amount for purpose of abatement, 'Undivided Profits' can be treated as reserves and added to capital? - Held, amount designated as 'Undivided Profits' is a part of reserves and has to be taken into account when computing capital and reserves for purpose of abatement - Appeal allowed.
