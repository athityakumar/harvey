 Madan Lal Arora v Excise and Taxation Officer, Amritsar

Supreme Court of India

07 April 1961

Case Analysis

Bench

A.K. Sarkar, Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, Kailas Nath Wanchoo, K.C. Das Gupta, N. Rajagopala Ayyangar

Where Reported

1961 Indlaw SC 170; AIR 1961 SC 1565; 1961 (2) SCJ 580; [1962] 1 S.C.R. 823; 1961 (12) STC 387

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - Indirect Tax - Punjab General Sales Tax Act, 1948, s. 11 (4) - Duration within which best judgment assessment can be made by Sales Tax Authorities - Held, three years within which authority could proceed to make best judgment assessment have to be counted from end of each quarter in respect of which returns had been filed - As last of quarters in respect of which assessee had filed his returns ended on March 31, assessing authority could not proceed to make a best judgment assessment in respect of this quarter after March 31 - Petition allowed.
