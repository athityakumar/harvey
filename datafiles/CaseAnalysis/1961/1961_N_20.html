Nilkantha Shidramappa Ningashetti v Kashinath Somanna Ningashetti and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 April 1961

Case Analysis

Bench

Raghubar Dayal, Bhuvaneshwar Prasad Sinha, K. Subba Rao, J.R. Madholkar

Where Reported

1961 Indlaw SC 147; AIR 1962 SC 666; 1962 (64) BomLR 412; 1962 (1) MLJ 263; 1962 (1) MLJ(SC) 263; 1962 (1) SCJ 588; [1962] 2 S.C.R. 551

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Practice & Procedure - Arbitration Act, 1940, ss. 14 (2) - Civil Procedure Code, 1908, O. 3, r. 5 - Limitation Act, 1908, art. 158 - (A)Does term 'notice' u/s.158 of Limitation Act, 1908 include only 'formal intimation'?- Held, no ground to construe expression 'date of service of notice' to mean only a notice in writing served in a formal manner - Service of notice includes constructive or informal notice - Appeal dismissed.
