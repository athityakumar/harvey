Raje Anandrao v Shamrao and Others

Supreme Court of India

23 February 1961

Case Analysis

Bench

Kailas Nath Wanchoo, Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar

Where Reported

1961 Indlaw SC 497; AIR 1961 SC 1206; 1961 JabLJ 1065; 1962 (1) MLJ 234; 1962 (1) MLJ(SC) 234; 1962 (1) SCJ 534; [1961] 3 S.C.R. 930

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Trust & Association - Civil Procedure - Practice & Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, ss. 92 and 151 - Public Trust Act, s. 92 - Management of temple in question between Raja and Pujaris - Suit filed by Raja which was decreed but on appeal held that temple not private property of Raja - Raja was entitled as against pujaris to possession and control of institution - Scheme prepared for management of temple pursuant to subsequent decree in suit in which pujaris was not made parties - How far it is open to a court to amend a scheme once framed u/s. 92 of CPC, where a power to amend scheme is reserved in scheme itself? - Held, amendment of scheme subsequently does not constitute alteration of decree hence revised scheme does not take away pujaris right - Revised scheme accordingly approved with some modifications - Appeal allowed.
