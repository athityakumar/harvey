John Donald Mackenzie and Another v Chief Inspector of Factories, Bihar, Ranchi and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 April 1961

Case Analysis

Bench

J.R. Madholkar, K. Subba Rao, Raghubar Dayal

Where Reported

1961 Indlaw SC 524; AIR 1962 SC 1351; 1961 (3) FLR 342; 1961 (2) LLJ 412

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Legislations referred : Factories Act, 1948, ss. 100 and 2(n) - Whether the control of factory was transferred to Niackenzier? - Held, yes it was - The expression "occupier" in s. 2(h) is not to be equated with 'owner' - Ultimate control over a factory must necessarily be with an owner unless the owner has completely transferred that control to another person - No attempt was made to show that the ultimate control of the factory was transferred to Niackenzie either by execution of a document or by passing a resolution - Appeal dismissed.
