Subject: Indirect Tax; Service

Summary: Indirect Tax - Service - Orissa Municipal Act, 1950 - Whether ss. 4 and 5(1) of orissa Ordinance I of 1959 promulgated by the Governor of Orissa on 15-01-1959? - Held, appeals are allowed - Arts. 254(2) and 213(1) - Ss. 5(1) and 477A of the Indian Penal Code - Art. 213(1)
