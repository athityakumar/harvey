Harbans Singh and Another v State of Punjab

Supreme Court of India

16 October 1961

Case Analysis

Bench

K.C. Das Gupta, A.K. Sarkar, Kailas Nath Wanchoo, Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar

Where Reported

1961 Indlaw SC 361; AIR 1962 SC 439; 1962 (1) ALT(SC) 311; 1962 CRLJ 479; 1962 (2) SCJ 662; [1962] 1 S.C.R. 104; [1962] Supp1 S.C.R. 104

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Code of Criminal Procedure 1898, misa

Summary: Criminal - Code of Criminal Procedure 1898, s. 423 - Indian Evidence Act, 1872 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 302 - Probative value of dying declaration - Appreciation of evidence by High Court - Powers of HC in appeals against acquittals - Held, Appeal of appellant 2 allowed, conviction set aside but appeal of appellant 1 dismissed.
