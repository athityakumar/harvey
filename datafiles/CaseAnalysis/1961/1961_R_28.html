Ram Shankar Singh and Others v State of West Bengal

Supreme Court of India

19 October 1961

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1961 Indlaw SC 360; AIR 1962 SC 1239; 1962 CRLJ 296; 1962 (2) SCJ 337; [1962] 1 S.C.R. 49; [1962] Supp1 S.C.R. 49

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Misdirection, Code of Criminal Procedure 1898

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Procedure Code, 1898, ss. 342, 374, 376, 418 and 423 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 302, 148 and 149 - Direct evidence - Testimony of eye-witnesses - Scope of s. 418 of CrPC discussed - Held, ample evidence against appellants, High Court right in holding appellants guilty - Innocent persons mercilessly murdered with deliberation - Fit case for death sentence - Appeal dismissed.
