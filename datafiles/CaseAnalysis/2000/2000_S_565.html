Rainbow Colour Lab v State of Madhya Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

02 February 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 605; (2000) 2 SCC 385; AIR 2000 SC 808; 2000 (159) CTR 37; 2000 (91) ECR 771; 2001 (134) E.L.T. 332; JT 2000 (1) SC 498; 2000(1) SCALE 381; [2000] 1 S.C.R. 594; 2000 (118) STC 9

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 366(2A)(b) - Madhya Pradesh General Sales Tax Act, 1959, s. 2(n) - Power of States - To levy sales tax on photographer - Contract for sale of goods - And contract for supply of labour and service - Held, after amendment of art. 366 - Has power to levy sales on sale or purchase of goods, actual or deemed - Not on sale of goods involved in a works contract.
