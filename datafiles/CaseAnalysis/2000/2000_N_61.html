Sandeep Jain v National Capital Territory of Delhi Rep. By Secretary, Home Deptt

Supreme Court of India

18 January 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, M.B. Shah

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 588; (2000) 2 SCC 66; (2000) SCC (Cr) 316; AIR 2000 SC 714; 2000 (1) CCR 92; 2000 (1) Crimes 163; 2000 CRLJ 807; 2000 (2) JCC 639; JT 2000 (1) SC 166; 2001 (129) PLR 895; 2000 (1) RCR(Criminal) 517; 2000(1) SCALE 141; 2000 (1) SLT 368; 2000 (1) Supreme 140

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 402 and 406 - Bail order - Order was passed by Metropolitan Magistrate imposing condition that accused at FIR stage should pay a huge sum of Rs. 2 lakhs to be set at liberty - Held, Metropolitan Magistrate was not justified in imposing an onerous condition at FIR stage and thereby making bail order virtually useless for appellant - Bail order modified - Appeal allowed.
