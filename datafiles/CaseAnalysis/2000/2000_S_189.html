Commissioner of Income Tax v Shivsagar Estate

Supreme Court of India

27 July 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, R.C. Lahoti, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 3495; (2004) 9 SCC 420; 2002 (177) CTR 107; [2002] 257 ITR 59; [2002] 124 TAXMAN 606

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: No order as to costs.

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Held, having regard to fact that no appeal has been carried against orders of identical assessment for previous year, instant civil appeals and special leave petitions are dismissed - Appeal dismissed.
