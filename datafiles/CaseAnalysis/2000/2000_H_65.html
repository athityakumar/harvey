State of Haryana v Haryana Veterinary and A. H. T. S. Assn. and Another

Supreme Court of India

19 September 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, D.P. Mohapatra, Doraiswamy Raju

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 2979; (2000) 8 SCC 4; (2000) SCC (L&S) 1043; AIR 2000 SC 3020; 2000 (3) CLR 591; JT 2000 (10) SC 561; 2000 (33) LabIC 3127; 2000(6) SCALE 415; [2000] Supp3 S.C.R. 322; 2001 (1) UPLBEC 77

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Service - Regular service - Promotion - Entitlement - Respondent/petitioner joined post of Assistant Engineer on ad hoc basis on 4-1-1980 and later on applied to Commission for being considered for said post of Assistant Engineer on regular basis in accordance with Statutory Rules - Respondent was selected by Commission and he was appointed on regular basis w.e.f. 29-1-1982 and prayed for grant of selection grade on completion of total period of service for 12 years commencing from 4-1-1980 in accordance with circular of State Govt. dt. 2-6-1989 and was entitled to get selection grade scale w.e.f.January 1992 - State Govt. resisted claim of respondents - Aggrieved respondents filed writ petition before DB which was dismissed interpreting Circular letter dt.2-6-1989 to mean that under Circular it was only completion of 12 years regular service - Hence, instant appeal - Whether service rendered as result of ad hoc appointment could be counted for purpose of 12 years regular service in context of Circular No. 638/3 PR (FD) - 87 dt. 2-6- 89 of State Govt. - Held, majority judgment of HC committed serious error by equating expression 'regular service' with 'continuous service' and opined that 12 years period of respondent could be counted from year 1982 for being eligible to get Selection Grade under Govt. Circular dt.2-6- 1989 as well as clarification Circular dt. 16-5-1990 - Hence, set aside impugned judgment and directions passed by HC, allowed delay con donation - Appeals allowed.
