Om Parkash Sharma v Central Bureau of Investigation, Delhi

Supreme Court of India

24 April 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

Doraiswamy Raju, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 285; (2000) 5 SCC 679; (2000) SCC (Cr) 1014; AIR 2000 SC 2335; 2000 (2) CCR 132; 2000 (2) Crimes 276; 2000 CRLJ 3478; 2000 (2) JCC 598; JT 2000 (6) SC 554; 2000 (2) RCR(Criminal) 859; 2000(4) SCALE 264; [2000] 3 S.C.R. 188; 2000 (4) SLT 426; 2000 (3) Supreme 745

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, s. 91 - Summoning and production of documents - Powers of Court - Rejection of application by Trial Court and High Court - On consideration of nature of documents, their bearing and relevance - Held, Court by rejecting claim could not be held to be either condemnable or constitute any gross or improper failure to exercise their jurisdiction - No interference.
