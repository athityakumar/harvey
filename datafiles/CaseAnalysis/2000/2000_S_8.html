Rajappa Hanamantha Ranoji v Mahadev Channabasappa and Others

Supreme Court of India

11 May 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.K. Sabharwal, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 704; (2000) 6 SCC 120; AIR 2000 SC 2108; 2000 (40) ALR 325; 2001 (1) CGLJ 191; 2000 (2) CLT 251; JT 2000 (6) SC 597; 2000 (1) RCR(Rent) 539; 2000(4) SCALE 692; 2000 (4) SLT 701; 2000 (4) Supreme 591

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, ss. 100 and 33 - Adverse inference - In second appeal - Reappreciation of evidence - Non-examination of witness - No explanation given for - Held, adverse inference by High Court - And conclusion that decision of first appellate court was based on no evidence - Not amounts to reappreciation of evidence - Not liable to be set aside.
