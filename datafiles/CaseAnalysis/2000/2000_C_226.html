Hindustan Aeronautics Limited, Bangalore v Commissioner of Income Tax, Karnataka-I, Bangalore

Supreme Court of India

11 May 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, Y.K. Sabharwal

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 516; (2000) 5 SCC 365; AIR 2000 SC 2178; 2000 (160) CTR 524; 2000 (119) E.L.T. 513; [2000] 243 ITR 808; JT 2000 (7) SC 82; 2000 (1) MLJ(SC) 93; 2000(4) SCALE 690; [2000] Supp1 S.C.R. 230; [2000] 110 TAXMAN 311

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income tax Act, 1961, ss. 264 and 119 - Revision by Commr. - Scope - Order inconsistent with Circulars issued by Board which itself contrary to precedents of Supreme Court/High Court - Difference in relief claimed in Revision and claimed in Appeal before Tribunal - Maintainability - Held, is not maintainable.
