Meena Smt. W/O Balwant Hemke v State of Maharashtra

Supreme Court of India

17 April 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

Doraiswamy Raju, R.C. Lahoti

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 271; (2000) 5 SCC 21; (2000) SCC (Cr) 878; AIR 2000 SC 3377; 2000 (5) Bom.C.R. 865; 2000 (2) CCR 118; 2000 (2) Crimes 166; 2000 CRLJ 2273; 2001 (2) JCC 754; JT 2000 (4) SC 521; 2000(2) M.P.H.T. 333; 2000 (2) RCR(Criminal) 661; 2000(3) SCALE 305; [2000] 3 S.C.R. 12; 2000 (4) SLT 377; 2000 (3) Supreme 334

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Indirect Tax; Service; Socio-Economic

Summary: Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 161 - Prevention of Corruption Act, 1947, s. 5 - Bribery case - Recovery of - Chemically treated currency notes - On table of accused - Not from person or table drawer - Held, room for doubt exists - No inference of acceptance of bribe by accused.
