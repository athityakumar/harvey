State Bank of India and Others v State Bank of India Canteen Employees' Union (Bengal Circle) and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 April 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

M.B. Shah, A.P. Misra

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 270; (2000) 5 SCC 531; (2000) SCC (L&S) 714; AIR 2000 SC 1518; 2000 (2) CLR 241; JT 2000 (5) SC 63; 2000 LabIC 1481; 2000 (1) LLJ 1441; 2000 (2) LLN 906; 2000(3) SCALE 290; [2000] 3 S.C.R. 37; 2000 (3) SLJ 291; 2000 (3) SLR 1; 2000 (2) UPLBEC 1703

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Games

Summary: Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, sch. 3, item 1 - Canteen facilities by bank - Status of - Sastry award - Held, award does not cast any obligation on State Bank of India for providing canteen facilities to its staff - Canteen employees are not workmen of bank - As no statutory or contractual obligation to run canteen - Canteen run by Local Implementation Committees is non - statutory non - recognised canteens - Scheme only provides for grant of subsidy.
