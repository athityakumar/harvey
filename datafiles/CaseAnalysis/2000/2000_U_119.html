Hyderabad Industries Limited v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

18 January 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, S.P. Bharucha, R.C. Lahoti

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 586; (2000) 1 SCC 718; AIR 2000 SC 712; 2000 (88) ECR 747; 2000 (115) E.L.T. 593; JT 2000 (1) SC 179; 2000 (36) RLT 717; 2000(1) SCALE 143; 2000 (118) STC 293

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Customs Act, 1962 - Customs Valuation (Determination of Price) Rules, 1988, r. 9 - Whether service charges payable to Minerals and Metals Trading Corporation (MMTC) by appellant for importation of raw asbestos made by them, is includable in assessable value of import as provided in Rules, 1988 or not? - Purchase by MMTC from foreign seller and subsequent sale by it to Indian buyers are independent of each other - Therefore, MMTC when it includes service charges in its sale consideration, it does not include same as 'buying commission' - Held, service charges payable to MMTC by appellant for importation of raw asbestos made by them, is not includable in assessable value of import - Appeals dismissed.
