A. K. Raghumani Singh and Others v Gopal Chandra Nath and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 April 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

Ruma Pal, M. Jagannadha Rao

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 252; (2000) 4 SCC 30; (2000) SCC (L&S) 452; AIR 2000 SC 1580; 2000 (2) CLR 1; JT 2000 (4) SC 333; 2000 LabIC 1479; 2000 (2) LLN 925; 2000(3) SCALE 39; [2000] 2 S.C.R. 943; 2000 (3) SLJ 442; 2000 (2) SLR 633; 2000 (2) UPLBEC 1482

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 16 and 309 - Manipur PWD/Irrigation and Flood Control/Public Health Engineering (Superintending Engineer (Civil)/Superintendent Surveyor of Works) Recruitment Rules, 1977, r. 2 - Service Rules - Promotion - Whether Superintendent Engineer in Public Health Department eligible for promotion for degree or its equivalent with 6 years regular service in grade was eligibility criteria - Held, employee eligible for promotion - Appeal dismissed.
