Karnataka State Road Tpt. Corporation & Anr. v S. Manjunath

Supreme Court of India

24 April 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

Doraiswamy Raju, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 281; (2000) 5 SCC 250; (2000) SCC (L&S) 629; AIR 2000 SC 2070; JT 2000 (7) SC 66; 2000 LabIC 1986; 2002 (1) LLJ 910; 2000 (3) LLN 86; 2000(4) SCALE 249; [2000] 3 S.C.R. 234; 2000 (3) SLJ 270; 2000 (4) SLR 539

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16 - Karnataka State Road Transport Corporation Service Regulations, Regn. 11(8) - Probationer - Confirmation - Held, deemed confirmation on expiry of period of probation - Use of 'probation' and 'officiation' has different status and meaning - Substantial difference for purposes of confirmation between an appointee by promotion and otherwise than on promotion.
