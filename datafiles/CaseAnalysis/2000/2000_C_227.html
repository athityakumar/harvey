Eimco-Kcp Private Limited, Madras v Commissioner of Income Tax, Madras

Supreme Court of India

25 February 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S.S.M. Quadri, D.P. Wadhwa

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 177; (2000) 2 SCC 729; AIR 2000 SC 1112; 2000 (1) CLC 811; 2000 (159) CTR 137; [2000] 242 ITR 659; JT 2000 (2) SC 468; 2000(2) SCALE 106; [2000] 1 S.C.R. 1185; [2000] 109 TAXMAN 151

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure; Trade

Summary: Income-tax Act, 1961, s. 37 - Revenue expenditure - Allotment of shares by assessee company to American company - Assessee company promoted by two companies - One of which is foreign company - Contribution of technical know how by foreign company towards its equity share's contribution - Foreign company valued know-how etc. at certain sum and paid balance in cash as its contribution - Board of Directors of assessee company allotted equity shares being value of know - how to foreign - Held, allotment of shares not expenditure much less revenue expenditure.
