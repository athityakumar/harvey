Workmen of Hindustan Vegetable Oils Corporation Limited v Hindustan Vegatable Oils Corporation Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 April 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, Shivaraj V. Patil

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 2862; (2000) 9 SCC 534; JT 2000 (8) SC 501; 2001 (34) LabIC 2118; 2000 (2) LLJ 792; 2000 (3) LLN 1075A; 2000 (5) SLR 218; 2000 (5) SLR 218A; 2000 (3) UPLBEC 2425

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, s. 17-B - Sick company - Whether writ petition and application u/s. 17-B should be disposed of together? - Held, s. 17-B application should be disposed of with great promptitude and before disposal of the writ petition - Appeal allowed.
