Raj Singh v State of Haryana

Supreme Court of India

16 February 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, S.S.M. Quadri

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 143; (2000) 6 SCC 759; (2000) SCC (Cr) 1270

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Health & Drug; Human Rights; Women & Children

Summary: Criminal - Juvenile Justice Act, 1986 s. 2(h) - Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act, 1985, s. 20 - Whether appellant can be convicted as at time of offence he was a juvenile i.e less than 16 yrs of age? - Held, entire proceedings needs to be quashed as he was a juvenile at time of offence - Appellant to be dealt within accordance with provisions of Juvenile Justice Act - Appeal allowed.
