Kuldip Singh v Subhash Chander Jain and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 March 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, A.P. Misra

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 224; (2000) 4 SCC 50; AIR 2000 SC 1410; 2000 (39) ALR 380; JT 2000 (3) SC 526; 2000 (2) RCR(Civil) 595; 2000(2) SCALE 584; [2000] 2 S.C.R. 639; 2000 (3) SLT 233; 2000 (3) Supreme 19

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments; Tort

Summary: Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 39, r. 1 - Tort-Quia timet - Future nuisance - Whether actionable? - Held, preventive action maintainable if threat be so certain or imminent that an injury actionable in law will arise - Proof of absolute certainty is not required - Maintainable.
