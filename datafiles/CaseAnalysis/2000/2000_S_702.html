M. Ramalinga Thevar v State of Tamil Nadu and Others

Supreme Court of India

19 April 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, M.B. Shah

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 277; (2000) 4 SCC 322; 2000 (4) ALD(SC) 4; 2000 (39) ALR 630; 2000 (3) AWC 1814; JT 2000 (5) SC 27; 2000 (3) MLJ(SC) 72; 2000 (2) RCR(Civil) 726; 2000(3) SCALE 395; [2000] 3 S.C.R. 167; 2000 (4) SLT 513; 2000 (3) Supreme 395; 2000 (2) UPLBEC 1639

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 4, 6 and 11 - A - Land acquisition proceedings - Whether while calculating two years period for passing an award, period of operation of stay order granted by High Court be excluded? - If there is any stay regarding any of actions to be taken pursuant to declaration then consequence of lapse would not happen - Held, even when dispossession alone is stayed by Court, period during which such stay operates would stand excluded from time fixed for passing award, expiry of which would render acquisition proceedings lapsed - Appeal dismissed.
