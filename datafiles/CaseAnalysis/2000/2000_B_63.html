Vimal Chandra Grover v Bank of India

Supreme Court of India

26 April 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 2577; (2000) 5 SCC 122; AIR 2000 SC 2181; 2000 (40) ALR 588B; 2000 (3) AWC 1884; 2000 (4) Bom.C.R. 38; [2002] 110 Comp Cas 499; 2000 (2) CPJ(Sc) 11; JT 2000 (5) SC 287; 2000(4) SCALE 111; [2000] 3 S.C.R. 587

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance

Keywords: Overdraft Facility, Contract Act, 1972

Summary: Consumer Protection Act, ss. 2(1)(o), 2(1)(d) and 2(1)(g) - Service - Bank giving overdraft facility to its client, whether - Client requesting bank to sale some of pledged shares - Bank agreeing to sell - But unable to sell shares within reasonable time occasioning loss to client - Held, is deficiency in service.
