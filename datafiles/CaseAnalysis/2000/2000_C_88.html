Chimajirao Kanhojirao Shirke and Another v Oriental Fire and General Insurance Company Limited

Supreme Court of India

26 July 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

Ruma Pal

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 382; (2000) 6 SCC 622; AIR 2000 SC 2532; 2000 (5) ALD(SC) 55; [2000] 102 Comp Cas 9; JT 2000 (8) SC 354; 2000 (4) RCR(Civil) 78; [2000] Supp1 S.C.R. 696

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Corporate; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Motor Vehicles Act, 1939, s. 95 - Liability of insurance company - Death of insured - Covers unlimited personal injury in respect of his truck against additional premium - Claim towards death - Plea by insurer, mistakenly recorded in policy - Held, once such stand in fact is taken, plea that words would refer to third party only cannot be entertained.
