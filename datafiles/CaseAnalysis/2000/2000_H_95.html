Hiran Mayee Bhattacharyya v Secretary, S. M. School For Girls and Others

Supreme Court of India

13 November 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

K.G. Balakrishnan, M. Jagannadha Rao

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 3813; (2002) 10 SCC 293; (2003) SCC (L&S) 1033

Case Digest

Subject: Education

Keywords: Management for Recognised Non-Government Institutions (Aided and Unaided) Rules, 1969

Summary: Education - Administrative Law - Principels of Natural Justice - Management for Recognised Non-Govt. Institutions (Aided and Unaided) Rules, 1969, r. 28(8) - Whether disciplinary authority is to furnish a copy of enquiry report to appellant? - Rules requires principles of natural justice to be followed - Held, disciplinary authority to furnish a copy of enquiry report to appellant and then permit her to submit her representation/explanation to same and pass final orders thereafter - However, this will not lead to reinstatement or to back wages inasmuch as this Court - Appeal allowed.
