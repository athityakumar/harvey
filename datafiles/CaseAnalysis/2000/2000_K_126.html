Oriental Insurance Company Limited v Khajuni Devi and Others

Supreme Court of India

02 November 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

K.G. Balakrishnan, U.C. Banerjee

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 3790; (2002) 10 SCC 567; (2003) SCC (L&S) 802; 2006 (3) ACC 768; 2003 (3) LLJ 566; 2006 (4) MLJ(SC) 948

Case Digest

Subject: Insurance; Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Appeals allowed.

Summary: Insurance - Labour & Industrial Law - Workmen's Compensation Act, 1923 - Service of notice - No appearance on behalf of the respondents - Relied on KSEB v. Valsala K. [1999 Indlaw SC 717] - Instant judgment under appeal having been on specific reliance on Neelakandan judgment which judgment was overruled in the Vatsala case - Appeal allowed.
