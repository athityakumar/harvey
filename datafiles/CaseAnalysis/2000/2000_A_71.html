Additional District Magistrate (Rev.) Delhi Admn. Etc. v Siri Ram Etc.

Supreme Court of India

05 May 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Phukan, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 331; (2000) 5 SCC 451; AIR 2000 SC 2143; JT 2000 (6) SC 643; 2000(4) SCALE 483; [2000] 3 S.C.R. 1019; 2000 (4) SLT 592; 2000 (4) Supreme 615

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Delhi Land Revenue Act, 1954, ss. 20, 21, 65 A and 84 - Delhi Land Revenue Rules, 1962, rr. 49, 63, 65 A and 67 (Amendment 1989) - Validity - Excluding land in 'extended abadi' - From preparation of record of rights - Rights of tenure holder - Held, possession of land would not be reflected in record-of-rights and annual register - Adversely affects rights of tenure holder - Liable to be quashed.
