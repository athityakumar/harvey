Lakshmi Narayanan v S. S. Pandian

Supreme Court of India

04 September 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S.S.M. Quadri, Y.K. Sabharwal

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 263; (2000) 7 SCC 240; AIR 2000 SC 2757; 2000 (6) ALD(SC) 66; 2000 (41) ALR 103; 2000 (4) AWC 3034; JT 2000 (9) SC 620; 2000 (1) MLJ(SC) 118; 2000 (2) RCR(Rent) 334; 2000 (91) RD 684; 2000(6) SCALE 256; 2000 (6) SLT 565; [2000] Supp3 S.C.R. 72; 2000 (6) Supreme 125

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure Code, 1908, ss. 47, O. 21, r. 3 - Compromise - Creating fresh lease, entered pending execution of eviction decree - Compromise not recorded by executing Court - Held, order dismissing execution petition as withdrawn - Does not amount to recording of compromise - Landlord entitled to have decree executed and recover possession.
