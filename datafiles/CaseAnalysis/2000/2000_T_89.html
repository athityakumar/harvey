Hanuman Vitamin Foods Pvt. Ltd. & Ors v The State Of Maharashtra & Anr

Supreme Court of India

20 July 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Variava, M.B. Shah

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 367; (2000) 6 SCC 345; AIR 2000 SC 2571; 2000 (5) ALD(SC) 47; 2000 (4) Bom.C.R. 17; 2000 (1) CLC 1496; [2000] 102 Comp Cas 6; 2001 (2) G.L.R. 1065; JT 2000 (8) SC 65; 2000 (3) RCR(Civil) 547; 2000(5) SCALE 237; 2000 (5) SLT 587; [2000] Supp1 S.C.R. 623; 2000 (5) Supreme 272

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property

Keywords: Bombay Stamp Act, 1958.

Summary: Bombay Stamp Act, 1958, sch. I, art. 25 - Stamp-duty - Power of State - Transfer of share in Co-operative Society - Nature of - Right to occupy certain premises as incident of membership to member - transferor - Held, transfer is, therefore, a conveyance of property - duty under art. 25 and not under instrument of transfer of share - State competent to levy duty.
