Union of India v Sh. Rasila Ram and Others

Supreme Court of India

06 September 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, U.C. Banerjee

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 2989; (2001) 10 SCC 623; 2001 (4) ALD(SC) 57; 2001 (45) ALR 450; 2000 (4) AWC 3242; 2000 (3) CLR 588; JT 2000 (10) SC 503

Case Digest

Subject: Administrative; Labour & Industrial Law; Rent Control

Summary: Service- Public Premises (Eviction Of Unauthorised Occupants) Act, 1971 - Administrative Tribunals Act, 1985 - Whether an order passed by the competent authority under the Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) Act, 1971 for eviction would also some within the purview and jurisdiction of the Administrative Tribunal Act, 1985? - Tribunal held that the eviction of unauthorised occupants from the Government quarter, would tantamount to a service matter, and therefore, tribunal retains jurisdiction over the same, in view of the overriding effect given to the Act by virtue of s. 33 of the said Act.
