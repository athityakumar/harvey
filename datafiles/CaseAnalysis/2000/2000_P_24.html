P. Anand Gajapathi Raju and Others v P. V. G. Raju (Dead) and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 March 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, Ruma Pal

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 228; (2000) 4 SCC 539; AIR 2000 SC 1886; 2000 (4) ALD(SC) 46; 2000 (2) ARBLR 204; 2000 (2) CLT 162; JT 2000 (4) SC 590; 2000 (3) MLJ(SC) 69; 2000 (2) RAJ 213; 2000(3) SCALE 330; [2000] 2 S.C.R. 684; 2000 (4) SLT 123; 2000 (3) Supreme 464

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Extent Of Judicial Intervention

Summary: Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, s. 8 - Reference - During pendency of appeal - After submission of first statement - Validity - 'Which is subject of an arbitration agreement' - Meaning of - Held, also connotes an arbitration agreement being brought into existence while action is pending - Is valid, if other party does not objects.
