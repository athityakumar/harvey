Trustees of H. E. H. The Nizam's Supplemental Family Trust v Commissioner of Income Tax

Supreme Court of India

16 February 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, S.S.M. Quadri

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 146; (2000) 3 SCC 501; AIR 2000 SC 966; 2000 (159) CTR 114; [2000] 242 ITR 381; JT 2000 (2) SC 141; [2000] 1 S.C.R. 863; [2000] 109 TAXMAN 193

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 237 and 147 - Refund - Application for - Filed with return under s. 139 - Trust as assessee - Tax Officer Noting in file that refund was to be considered in hands of beneficiaries in relevant assessment year - Note not communicated to assessee - Held, no finality could be said to be given to refund thereby - Assessment cannot be made for relevant assessment year by virtue of ss. 143(3) and 147.
