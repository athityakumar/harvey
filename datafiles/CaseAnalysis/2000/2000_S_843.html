Tamil Nadu Electricity Board v Sumathi and Others

Supreme Court of India

27 April 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, S.S.M. Quadri

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 2605; (2000) 4 SCC 543; 2000 (2) ACC 1; AIR 2000 SC 1603; 2000 (2) ARBLR 460; 2001 (1) CGLJ 82; JT 2000 (5) SC 526; 2000 (3) MLJ(SC) 124; 2000 (2) RAJ 231; 2000 (3) RCR(Civil) 183; 2000(4) SCALE 297; [2000] 3 S.C.R. 708; 2000 (4) SLT 302; 2000 (3) Supreme 673; 2000 (2) UPLBEC 1611

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Constitution; Corporate

Summary: Constitution - Writ petition u/art. 226 seeking compensation for death due to tortuous act - Petition is not maintainable when disputed question of fact arises and there is clear denial of any tortuous liability - Still High Court entertained petition and referred matter to arbitrator - Validity - Held, not proper - Appeal allowed.
