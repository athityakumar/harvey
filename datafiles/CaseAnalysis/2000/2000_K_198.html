Madhvi Amma Bhawani Amma and Others v Kunjikutty Pillai Meenakshi Pillai and Others

Supreme Court of India

27 April 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Misra, M.B. Shah

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 311; (2000) 6 SCC 301; AIR 2000 SC 2301; 2000 (39) ALR 737; 2000 (3) ALT(SC) 35; 2000 (2) CLT 207; JT 2000 (5) SC 336; 2000 (2) KLT 518; 2000 (3) MLJ(SC) 78; 2000 (3) RCR(Civil) 187; 2000(4) SCALE 629; [2000] 3 S.C.R. 752; 2000 (4) SLT 188; 2000 (Supp) RD 13; 2000 (3) Supreme 666

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Civil Procedure; Family & Personal

Keywords: Court Of Limited Jurisdiction, Succession & Inheritance, Debt Laws

Summary: Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 11 - Res judicata - Scope - Decision in proceedings for grant of succession certificate - Issue not raised and decided - Findings incidentally recorded - Held, not adjudicates finally rights of parties - Not operates as res judicata.
