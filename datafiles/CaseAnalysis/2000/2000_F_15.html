Municipal Corporation of Delhi v Female Workers (Muster Roll) and Another

Supreme Court of India

08 March 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Saghir Ahmad, D.P. Wadhwa

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 396; (2000) 3 SCC 224; (2000) SCC (L&S) 331; AIR 2000 SC 1274; 2000 (3) ALD(SC) 36; 2000 (1) CLR 879; JT 2000 (3) SC 13; 2000 (33) LabIC 1033; 2000 (1) LLJ 846; 2000 (2) LLN 390; 2000 (125) PLR 371; 2000(2) SCALE 269; [2000] 2 S.C.R. 171; 2000 (3) SLJ 369; 2000 (2) SLR 2; 2000 (2) UPLBEC 963

Case Digest

Subject: Service; Women & Children

Keywords: Universal Declaration of Human Rights, Woman, Trade Union Act, 1926, Maternity Benefit Act, 1961

Summary: Maternity Benefit Act, 1961, s. 2 - Proviso - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 420 - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 2(j) - Maternity benefits - To working female employees in muster-roll - Municipal Corporation not within scope of - Not 'Industry' under Industrial Disputes Act - Held, in absence of any provisions - Directions to Industrial Tribunal and State Govt. for taking appropriate steps to give effect.
