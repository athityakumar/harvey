Collector of Central Excise and Others v Himalayan Cooperative Milk Product Union Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

07 November 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

Brijesh Kumar, U.C. Banerjee

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 2796; (2000) 8 SCC 642; AIR 2000 SC 3669; 2000 (122) E.L.T. 327; JT 2000 (Supp2) SC 643; 2000 (41) RLT 627; 2001 (123) STC 403; [2000] Supp4 S.C.R. 431

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Tariff Item

Summary: Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, Sch 1, Item 68 - Central Excise Rules, 1944, r. 8 - Benefit of exemption - Notification allowing exemption to industrial unit manufacturing 'said goods under clearance' - Having capital investment value not more than rupees ten lakhs - Plant manufacturing liquid nitrogen having less investment value - Liquid nitrogen itself finished product under item 68 - Value of other plants manufacturing butter etc. - Held, need not be added - Benefit of exemption available to liquid nitrogen plant.
