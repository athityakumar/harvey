Triveni Engineering and Industries Limited and Another v Commissioner of Central Excise and Another

Supreme Court of India

08 August 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S.S.M. Quadri, Shivaraj V. Patil

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 406; (2000) 7 SCC 29; AIR 2000 SC 2896; 2000 (120) E.L.T. 273; JT 2000 (9) SC 38; 2000 (40) RLT 1; 2000(5) SCALE 468; 2000 (6) SLT 94; [2000] Supp2 S.C.R. 199; 2000 (5) Supreme 459

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, s. 3, sch., Heading 85.02 - 'Excisable goods' - Manufacturing process - Process involving to form turbo alternator in a specified manner as a new commodity - Installing on platform constructed on land - Held, amounts to immovable property - Not within 'excisable goods' as under Heading 85 - Process with this new commodity is a manufacturing process.
