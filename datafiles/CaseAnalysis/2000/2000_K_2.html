Union of India v K. A. Kittu and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 November 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Phukan, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 2965; (2001) SCC (L&S) 8; (2001) 1 SCC 65; AIR 2000 SC 3727; 2001 (1) AWC 329; 2000 (4) ESC 2807; JT 2000 (Supp3) SC 17; 2001 LabIC 76; 2001 (1) LLJ 157; 2001 (1) LLN 20; 2000 (5) SLR 797; 2001 (1) UC 131; 2001 (1) UPLBEC 191

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Administrative Tribunals Act, 1985, s. 14 - Tribunal - Interference - With report of Inquiry Officer - No clear finding recorded by Inquiry Officer that sanction was illegal - Finding of Inquiry Officer mainly on ground that delinquent ought to have taken opinion of an expert - Evidence of all witnesses of delinquent not considered by Inquiry Officer - Held, Interference with finding of Inquiry Officer was proper.
