Divya Manufacturing Co.,Tirupati Wool Mills Shr Shangharsha v Union Bank Of India , The Official Liquidator & Others

Supreme Court of India

11 July 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

M.B. Shah, R.P. Sethi

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 523; (2000) 6 SCC 69; AIR 2000 SC 2346; 2006 (1) BC 428; [2000] 102 Comp Cas 66; 2000 (3) CompLJ 390; JT 2000 (7) SC 524; 2000 (1) MLJ(SC) 134; 2000(5) SCALE 138; 2000 (5) SLT 397; [2000] Supp1 S.C.R. 474; 2000 (5) Supreme 66

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Land & Property

Summary: Companies Act, 1956, s. 457 - Company sale - Reopening - On offers received subsequent to confirmation of sale - Held, permissible - On higher offer received before handing over possession or sale deed was executed - And application to set aside sale is filed within few days of confirmation.
