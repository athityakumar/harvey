Ulagappa and Others v Divn. Commr., Mysore, and Others

Supreme Court of India

26 July 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

V.N. Khare, S.N. Variava

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 3648; (2001) 10 SCC 639; AIR 2000 SC 3603B; JT 2000 (10) SC 206

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Keywords: Karnataka Municipalities Act (34 of 1964)

Summary: Muncipalities & Local Governments - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Karnataka Municipalities Act, 1964, s. 349 r/w s. 4 - Inclusion of certain area within the limits of the Tarikere Town Panchayat consequent - No notification issued - Writ petition filed by the petitioner was pre-mature and ought not to have been entertained by High Court - None of the rights of the petitioner were affected - Appeal disposed of.
