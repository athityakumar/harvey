Sanjay Kumar v State of Bihar and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 August 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

Doraiswamy Raju, M. Jagannadha Rao

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 427; (2000) 7 SCC 192; (2000) SCC (L&S) 895; AIR 2000 SC 2782; 2000 (4) AWC 3050; 2000 (87) FLR 132; JT 2000 (10) SC 156; 2000 (3) LLN 1072; 2001 (1) MLJ(SC) 44; 2000(6) SCALE 197; 2000 (5) SLR 265; 2000 (6) SLT 496; [2000] Supp2 S.C.R. 710; 2000 (6) Supreme 43; 2001 (1) UC 14

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16 - Compassionate appointment - Dependant/Claimant minor at time of death of employee - Argued vacancy cannot be reserved for him till he attains majority after number of years as such reservation would be against intent behind compassionate appointment, namely, to provide immediate relief to family of deceased employee - Held, argument was valid.
