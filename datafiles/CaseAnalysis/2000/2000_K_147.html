K. S. Bhoopathy and Others v Kokila and Others

Supreme Court of India

08 May 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Mohapatra, A.P. Misra

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 403; (2000) 5 SCC 458; AIR 2000 SC 2132; 2000 (4) ALD(SC) 24; 2000 (40) ALR 144; 2000 (2) CLT 289; JT 2000 (6) SC 272; 2000 (3) RCR(Civil) 195; 2000(4) SCALE 640; [2000] 3 S.C.R. 1168; 2000 (4) SLT 727; 2000 (4) Supreme 236

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 23, r. 1(3) - Withdrawal of suit with leave to file fresh suit - Grant of permission for same, at stage of second appeal was in question - Held, it is duty of Court to be satisfied about existence of proper grounds/reasons for granting permission - Merely stating that grant of permission would not prejudice defendants is not compliance with statutory mandate.
