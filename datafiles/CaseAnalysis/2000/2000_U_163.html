 Sunil Fulchand Shah v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 February 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

G.T. Nanavati, A.S. Anand, D.P. Wadhwa, K.T. Thomas, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 685; (2000) 3 SCC 409; (2000) SCC (Cr) 659; AIR 2000 SC 1023; AIR 2000 SCW 582; 2000 (1) CCR 235; 2000 (3) Crimes 44; 2000 CRLJ 1444; 2000 (2) G.L.R. 1532; 2000 (1) JCC 328; JT 2000 (2) SC 230; 2000 (2) RCR(Criminal) 176; 2000(1) SCALE 660; 2000 (1) SCJ 443; [2000] 1 S.C.R. 945; 2000 (2) SLT 246; 2000 (4) Supreme 682

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Conservation of Foreign Exchange and Prevention of Smuggling Activities Act, 1974 s. 10 - Parole - Period of detention - Computation of - Held, from date of actual detention and not from date of order of detention - Detenu has to approach to Govt. for securing release on parole - May be granted by way of temporary release as contemplated by ss. 12(1) or 12(1 A).
