Netai Bag and Others v State of W. B. and Others

Supreme Court of India

27 September 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

R.P. Sethi, K.T. Thomas

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 2656; (2000) 8 SCC 262; AIR 2000 SC 3313; AIR 2000 SCW 3590; 2001 (1) ARBLR 305; JT 2000 (Supp1) SC 181; 2000 (4) RCR(Civil) 622; 2000(6) SCALE 504; [2000] Supp3 S.C.R. 424

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 133 and 14 - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 17 - Appeal - Entertainment of issue - No basis mentioned in original proceedings - Challenge of arbitrariness in executive action - Onus - Lease of Govt. land made in favour of private company after negotiations - Distribution of largesse - Without auction or inviting tenders - Held, Not always arbitrary - Onus to proof violation of mandate of Constitution lies upon person approaching Court - Issue having no basis in original proceedings - Not permissible.
