Union of India v Satish Panalal Shah

Supreme Court of India

06 December 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, S.P. Bharucha, Y.K. Sabharwal

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 757; (2002) 1 SCC 605; 2001 (168) CTR 1; [2001] 249 ITR 221; 2001 (3) SLT 797; [2001] 117 TAXMAN 373

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961 - Orders under challenge by Revenue followed earlier judgment in Pradip Ramanlal Shah case, 1992 Indlaw GUJ 90 - Revenue did not accept correctness of judgment in case of Pradip Ramanlal Sheth case - Held, it is not open to Revenue to accept that judgment in case of assessee in that case and challenge its correctness in case of other assessees without just cause - Appeals dismissed.
