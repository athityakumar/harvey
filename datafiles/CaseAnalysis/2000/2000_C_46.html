Punjab Nat.Iona.L Bank, Dasuya v Chajju Ram & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

01 August 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, Ruma Pal

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 576; (2000) 6 SCC 655; AIR 2000 SC 2671; 2000 (40) ALR 648; [2000] 102 Comp Cas 41; JT 2000 (8) SC 456; 2000 (3) MLJ(SC) 181; 2001 (127) PLR 466; 2000 (4) RCR(Civil) 13; [2000] Supp2 S.C.R. 84; 2000 (3) UPLBEC 2441

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Corporate; Agriculture & Agri. Products

Keywords: Debt Laws

Summary: Banking and Finance - Recovery of Debts Due To Banks And Financial Institutions Act, 1993, ss. 31A, 31and 34 - CPC, 1908, O. 21, r. 10 - Banking - Debt - Transfer of Cases - Decree for recovery of money below Rs. 10 Lakhs passed before commencement of Amendment Act - Application for execution - Interest awarded - Application filed when amount became more than Rs.10 Lakhs - Jurisdiction - Held, tribunal only and not Civil Court would have jurisdiction of entertaining - Ground, original decree was for less than Rs.10 Lakhs, more so, as provisions of O. 21, r. 10 Civil Code do not apply - Appeal allowed.
