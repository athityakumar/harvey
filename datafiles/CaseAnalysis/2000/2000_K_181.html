Kshetriya Shri Gandhi Ashram v State of UP and Others

Supreme Court of India

08 November 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, S.N. Variava

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 893

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Uttar Pradesh Industrial Disputes Act, Ashram Rules

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Ashram Rules, r. 29 - Uttar Pradesh Industrial Disputes Act, s. 4 K - Transfer - Appellant held that workmen committed misconduct by refusing to obey order of transfer in terms of r. 29 of Ashram Rules - Services terminated - Industrial Tribunal set aside order of dismissal directing reinstatement with full back wages - High Court affirmed said order - Held, appropriate to direct appellant to pay within a period of three months a lump sum to each of contesting respondents in lieu of claim for reinstatement and payment of back wages - Appeal disposed of.
