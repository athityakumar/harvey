Hamzabi and Others v Syed Karimuddin and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 November 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

Ruma Pal, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 3670; (2001) 1 SCC 414; AIR 2000 SCW 4354; 2001 (1) ALD(SC) 44; 2001 AllCJ 735; 2001 (42) ALR 318; 2001 (1) AWC 337; 2001 (2) Bom.C.R. 778; JT 2000 (Supp3) SC 190; 2001 (1) MahLJ 678; 2001 (2) MLJ(SC) 27; 2001 (1) MPLJ 373; 2001 (1) RCR(Civil) 522; [2000] Supp5 S.C.R. 99; 2001 (1) UC 173

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Decree Of Court

Summary: Land & Property - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, ss. 53-A and 60 - Whether s. 53-A has any impact on right of redemption granted by s. 60? - Doctrine of part performance - Held, conditions necessary u/s. 53-A for making out defence of part performance to an action in ejectment by owner should be fulfilled - Language of section is mandatory - Equity of redemption in mortgagor/vendee is lost to extent that mortgagor cannot reclaim possession of mortgaged property - Fulfillment of conditions u/s. 53-A by petitioners predecessor in interest entitles respondent 1 to be debarred from claiming possession of mortgaged property - Appeal allowed.
