T. N. Administrative Service Officers Association and Another v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

19 April 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, M. Jagannadha Rao

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 279; (2000) 5 SCC 728; (2000) SCC (L&S) 748; AIR 2000 SC 1898; 2000 (3) AWC 1826; JT 2000 (5) SC 86; 2000 (3) LLN 474; 2000(3) SCALE 398; 2000 (2) SLR 659; 2000 (6) SLT 40; 2000 (3) Supreme 493; 2000 (2) UPLBEC 1683

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: IAS (Appointment by Promotion) Regulations, 1955, Ias (Fixation Of Cadre Strength) Regulations, 1955, Cadre Rules,, IAS (Recruitment) Rules, 1954

Summary: All India Services Act, 1951, s. 3 - IAS (Recruitment) Rules, 1954, r. 8 - Mandamus - Maintainability - Ex-cadre/temporary posts - State Govt. creating posts and filled up with IAS officer - Claim of ex - cadered petitioner as matter of right - For encadrement - Entitlement of - Held, posts are of permanent nature as continuous existence of these posts over decades suggests - Despite of confers no right on petitioner to claim - Court cannot issue mandamus to central Govt.
