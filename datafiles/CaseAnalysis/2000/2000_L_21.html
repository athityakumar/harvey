State of Maharashtra v Laljit Rajshi Shah and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 February 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, N. Santosh Hegde

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 182; (2000) SCC (Cr) 533; (2000) 2 SCC 699; AIR 2000 SC 937; 2000 (3) Bom.C.R. 240; 2000 (1) CCR 283; 2000 (2) Crimes 1; 2000 CRLJ 1494; 2000 (1) JCC 316; JT 2000 (2) SC 546; 2000 (2) RCR(Criminal) 71; 2000(2) SCALE 126; 2000 (2) SCJ 92; [2000] 1 S.C.R. 1239; 2000 (2) SLT 404; 2000 (2) Supreme 244

Case Digest

Subject: Trusts & Associations

Keywords: Maharashtra Municipalities Act, 1965, Maharashtra Co-operative Society Act, 1960

Summary: Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1961, ss. 2(20) and 161 - Indian Penal Code 1860, s. 21 - 'Public servant' - Chairman of Maharashtra Co - operative Societies Act - Held, Chairman is public servant under Societies Act - But not under IPC, 1860.
