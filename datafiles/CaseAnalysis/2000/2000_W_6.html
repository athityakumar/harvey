Supreme Court of India

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Jagannadha Rao

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 2668; (2000) 4 SCC 272; AIR 2000 SC 1379; 2000 (1) ARBLR 690; 2001 (4) CompLJ 249; JT 2000 (4) SC 135; 2000 (3) MLJ(SC) 1; 2000 (2) RCR(Civil) 760; 2000(3) SCALE 23

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Summary: Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, ss. 16, 11 and 7 - 'Existence' of arbitration clause' - Determination by - Arbitral Tribunal only or Court also - Held, s. 16 empowers Arbitral Tribunal - But not excludes jurisdiction of Chief Justice of India over issue.
