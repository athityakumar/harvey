Medchl Chemicals and Pharma Private Limited v Biological E. Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 February 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

U.C. Banerjee, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 666; (2000) 3 SCC 269; (2000) SCC (Cr) 615; AIR 2000 SC 1869; AIR 2000 SCW 682; 2000 (1) CCR 288; 2000 (2) Crimes 11; 2000 CRLJ 1487; 2000 (1) JCC 218; JT 2000 (2) SC 426; 2000 (2) RCR(Criminal) 122; 2000(2) SCALE 88; [2000] 1 S.C.R. 1169; 2000 (2) SLT 414; 2000 (2) Supreme 261

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, s. 482 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 24, 420, 415 and 418 - Quashing of complaint or chargesheet - Whether High Court correct in quashing complaint u/s. 482 CrPC? - Held, exercise of powers u/s. 482 CrPC is an exception and not a rule - Without expressing opinion on merits of case, appeal allowed - HC has to examine only prima facie case made out on face of complaint - Magistrate may proceed with case in accordance with law without expedition - Appeal allowed.
