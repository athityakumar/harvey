R. Rajgopal v S. S. Venkat

Supreme Court of India

11 February 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

K.T. Thomas, M.B. Shah

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 3082; (2001) 10 SCC 91; (2002) SCC (Cr) 1033; AIR 2001 SC 2432; 2000 (4) Crimes 63; 2001 CRLJ 4936; JT 2000 (5) SC 503; 2001 (3) RCR(Criminal) 347A; 2000 (6) Supreme 441

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Corporate; Criminal

Summary: Banking & Finance - Corporate - Criminal - Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881, s. 138 - High Court quashed complaint since partnership firm on whose behalf cheque was issued was not made an accused in complaint - Held, HC judgment cannot be sustained in view of decision in Anil Hada v India Acrylic Limited, 1999 Indlaw SC 853 - Appeal diposed of.
