Anandi D. Jadhav (Dead) By Lrs v Nirmala Ramchandra Kore and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 April 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S.S.M. Quadri, N. Santosh Hegde

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 320; (2000) 3 SCC 703; AIR 2000 SC 1386; 2000 (3) ALD(SC) 125; 2000 (3) Bom.C.R. 6; 2000 (3) G.L.R. 2754; JT 2000 (4) SC 96; 2000 (1) RCJ 604; 2000 (1) RCR(Rent) 442; 2000(3) SCALE 90; [2000] 2 S.C.R. 963; 2000 (3) SLT 521; 2000 (3) Supreme 159

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Rent Control

Keywords: Hindu Adoptions And Maintenance Act, 1956.

Summary: Bombay Rents, Hotel and Lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947, ss. 13(1)(I) and 5(11) - Hindu Adoptions and Maintenance Act, 1956, s. 20 - Eviction of tenant - Mother as tenant - Sons as member of family residing with - Alternative accommodation of mother in house of her son subsequently - 'Vacant possession' ground for eviction suit - Held, right of infirm Hindu parent to be maintained by sons - Not entitle parents to live along with son's families in their house - Vacant possession means possession of accommodation with legal right to reside - 'Tenant' not includes any residing member of family - No eviction.
