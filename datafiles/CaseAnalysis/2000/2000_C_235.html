Eskayef (Now Known As Smithkline Beecham Pharmaceuticals (India) Limited Etc.) v Commissioner of Income Tax

Supreme Court of India

20 July 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, Ruma Pal

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 768; (2000) 6 SCC 451; AIR 2000 SC 2567; 2000 (162) CTR 89; [2000] 245 ITR 116; JT 2000 (8) SC 218; [2000] Supp1 S.C.R. 634; [2000] 111 TAXMAN 561

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Surtax, Sales Promotion

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 40 and 37(3 A) - Advertisement expenditure - Physician's samples distributed by pharmaceutical companies to medical profession - Expenditure incurred on - Held, is advertisement expenditure - Falling within restrictive provisions of s. 37(3 A).
