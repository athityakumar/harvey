Union of India and Others v Dr. Vijayapurapu Subbayamma

Supreme Court of India

22 September 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

V.N. Khare, S.N. Phukan

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 473; (2000) 7 SCC 662; AIR 2000 SC 3113; 2000 (4) AWC 3320; 2000 (87) FLR 672; JT 2000 (Supp1) SC 41; 2000 LabIC 3298; 2000 (4) LLN 574; 2000(6) SCALE 453; 2001 (3) SLR 434; 2000 (6) SLT 823; [2000] Supp3 S.C.R. 372; 2000 (6) Supreme 353; 2001 (1) UPLBEC 525

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Public Sector

Summary: Central Civil Services (Temporary) Rules, 1965- Employment - Retirement- Pensionary benefits-Respondent retiring on 13.11.1980 when pension available only on having rendered service of 20 years-On recommendation of Pay Commission period of length of service for entitlement to pension reduced from 20 years to 10 years with effect from 1.1.1986-Whether respondent entitled to benefit of pension having put in more than 12 years of service in view of reduced period of 10 years of service?-Whether reduced period of 10 years of service applicable with retrospective effect?-Held “no“-Case law surveyed-Principles summarised.
