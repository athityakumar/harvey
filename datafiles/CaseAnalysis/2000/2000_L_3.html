Ajjam Linganna and Others v L.A.O.Revenue Div.Officer and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 November 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Jagannadha Rao, Doraiswamy Raju

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 1014; (2002) 9 SCC 426

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 18, 30 and 31 - Question relating to reference u/s. 18 - It was not open to appellants to have applied directly to reference court for impleadment and to seek enhancement u/s. 18 for compensation - Held, it was not open to reference court to implead appellants in reference court without their having approached LAO seeking reference earlier - Order accordingly.
