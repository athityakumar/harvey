E. Venkatakrishna v Indian Oil Corporation and Another

Supreme Court of India

17 August 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Variava, S.P. Bharucha, Y.K. Sabharwal

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 2601; (2000) 7 SCC 764; 2000 (3) ARBLR 316; JT 2000 (10) SC 558; 2000 (3) RAJ 63

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Corporate

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Corporate - Arbitration Act, 1940, s. 30 - Contract - Termination of dealership - Arbitration clause in agreement - Appointment of sole arbitrator - Validity of award challenged - Held, arbitrable clause is invoked only to extent of dispute or difference in relation to agreement - Question of restoration of distributorship would not arise under agreement - Arbitrator was in error and in fact had no jurisdiction to direct restoration of distributorship - If termination of distributorship was found unlawful only damages can be awarded not restoration - Relief given in award in respect of restoration stands deleted and other reliefs given stands - Appeals dismissed.
