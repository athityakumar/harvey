Mahavir Prashad Gupta and Another v State of National Capital Territory of Delhi and Others

Supreme Court of India

27 September 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Variava, K.T. Thomas, R.P. Sethi

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 2819; (2000) 8 SCC 115; (2000) SCC (Cr) 1453; AIR 2000 SC 3101; 2000 (4) AWC 3245; 2000 CRLJ 4665; JT 2000 (Supp1) SC 128; 2000 (4) RCR(Criminal) 480; 2000(6) SCALE 525; 2001 (1) UC 74

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Constitution; Corporate; Criminal

Keywords: Dishonour of cheques

Summary: Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, s. 482 - Quashing of FIR - Criminal breach of trust and forgery - Complaint merely pointing out that goods were entrusted to petitioner for transportation - And even though they were accepted and lorry receipt was issued, were not delivered and were withheld - Held, sufficient reason to register FIR and to make enquiry - Not interfered.
