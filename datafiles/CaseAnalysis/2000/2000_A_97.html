Ajit Jain v National Insurance Company Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

14 November 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Agrawal, G.B. Pattanaik

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 3791; (2002) 10 SCC 580; (2003) SCC (L&S) 877; 2003 (3) LLJ 558

Case Digest

Subject: Insurance

Summary: Insurance - Service - Dismissal from service - Appeal and Special appeal before DB dismissed, - Whether there was infirmity in departmental proceeding and order of infliction of punishment of dismissal was wrong? - Yes, court held, because of his physical condition of appellant which disentitles him to join the service, employer Insurance Co. pay compensation in terms of final settlement of all the dues of the appellant and therefore relationship of master and servant would cease on payment of such compensation - Appeal disposed of.
