Bharat Earth Movers v Commissioner of Income Tax

Supreme Court of India

09 August 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, N. Santosh Hegde, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 789; (2000) 6 SCC 645; AIR 2000 SC 2636; 2000 (162) CTR 325; [2000] 245 ITR 428; JT 2000 (8) SC 606; [2000] Supp2 S.C.R. 295; [2000] 112 TAXMAN 61

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act , 1961, ss. 37 and 258 - Income Tax - Business income - Deduction - (A) Assessee-company made provision for meeting leave encashment liability - If deductible - (B) Non filing of supplementary statement of case within stipulated time - Statement called from Tribunal by Supreme Court - Held, it is deductible, not a contingent liability - Inaction on part of Tribunal deprecated and cautioned to be more responsive and more sensitive to the directions of SC - Appeal allowed.
