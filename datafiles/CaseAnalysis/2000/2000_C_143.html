Himson Textiles Enginr. Indust. Ltd v Commissioner Of Customs, Mumbai

Supreme Court of India

01 February 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, N. Santosh Hegde, Ruma Pal

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 3486; (2000) 9 SCC 448; AIR 2000 SC 3390; 2001 (94) ECR 608; 2000 (117) E.L.T. 535; JT 2000 (3) SC 372

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Customs - Excise - Central Excise Act, 1944, s. 35L - CEGAT has applied the ratio of the decision in Essar Gujarat[1996 Indlaw SC 1896] and held Commr. (Appeals) was in error in following the decision of Mahindra and Mahindra Ltd. [ 1995 Indlaw SC 1720] - Held,judgment of Essar Gujarat has no application - Judgment set aside - Appeal allowed.
