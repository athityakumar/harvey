Union of India and Others v Arvind Shergill and Another

Supreme Court of India

13 September 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, D.P. Mohapatra

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 461; (2000) 7 SCC 601; (2000) SCC (Cr) 1422; AIR 2000 SC 2924; 2000 CRLJ 4055; 2000 (121) E.L.T. 3; 2001 (1) JCC 30; JT 2000 (10) SC 405; 2000 (4) RCR(Criminal) 251; 2000(6) SCALE 377; 2000 (6) SLT 706; [2000] Supp3 S.C.R. 244; 2000 (6) Supreme 332

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Customs; Indirect Tax

Keywords: COFEPOSA, Currency, Foreign Currency

Summary: Conservation of Foreign Exchange and Prevention of Smuggling Activities Act, 1974, s. 3 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Detention order - Delay in execution of - Nexus between object for which detenu is sought to be detained and circumstances in which he was ordered to be detained - Subjective satisfaction of detaining authority grounded on sufficiency of materials - Held, Court cannot substitute its judgment for satisfaction of detaining authority as if sitting in appeal on order passed by detaining authority.
