Bank of India v Indu Rajagopalan and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 April 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, R.C. Lahoti

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 2888; (2001) 9 SCC 318; (2002) SCC (L&S) 89; 2000 (87) FLR 288; JT 2000 (10) SC 334; 2001 LabIC 2120; 2000 (1) LLJ 1617; 2001 (3) LLN 730; 2000 (5) SLR 420; 2001 (8) SLT 95; 2001 (121) STC 1

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Scheme for voluntary retirement lapsed - New comprehensive pension scheme framed which is applicable uniformly to all bank employees - Applicability of new rules to those employees who have voluntarily retired prior to the framing of new rules - Number of employees who have retired very small - No interference called for - Appeals dismissed.
