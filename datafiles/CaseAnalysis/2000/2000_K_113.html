Rohini Prasad and Others v Kasturchand and Another

Supreme Court of India

03 March 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Wadhwa, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 194; (2000) 3 SCC 668; AIR 2000 SC 1283; JT 2000 (2) SC 573; 2000(4) M.P.H.T. 5; 2000(2) SCALE 189; 2000 (3) SCJ 363; [2000] 2 S.C.R. 88; 2000 (3) SLT 69; 2000 (2) Supreme 365

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Madhya Pradesh Land Revenue Code 1959, s. 259 - Jurisdiction of Civil Court was in question - High Court had abated findings of first Appellate Court on ground that appellant had acquired bhumiswami rights - Held, there was no evidence to come to conclusion that Rohini Prasad was in custody of land in maintenance or renewal of lease - Jurisdiction of Civil Court is not barred.
