State of Andhra Pradesh v Challa Ramkrishna Reddy and Others

Supreme Court of India

26 April 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Saghir Ahmad, D.P. Wadhwa

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 300; (2000) 5 SCC 712; 2000 (2) ACC 18; AIR 2000 SC 2083; 2000 (2) CLT 216; JT 2000 (6) SC 334; 2000(4) SCALE 471B; [2000] 3 S.C.R. 644; 2000 (4) SLT 413; 2000 (4) Supreme 741

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure; Tort

Keywords: Custodial Death, Court Martial, Tortious Liability, Joint Tort Feasor, Sovereign Powers

Summary: Limitation Act, 1963, arts. 72 and 113 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 21 - Applicability - Death of father of claimant due to bomb blast in jail - Failure of police to provide adequate security in spite of being asked - Compensation - Held, prisoner continue to have all fundamental rights including right to life - Art. 72 would not be applicable but art. 113 as available only in respect of bona fide acts.
