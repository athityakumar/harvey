Supdt., Narcotics Central Bureau, Chennai v R. Paulsamy

Supreme Court of India

30 March 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Mohapatra, K.T. Thomas

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 3147; (2000) 9 SCC 549; (2001) SCC (Cr) 648; AIR 2000 SC 3661; 2001 CRLJ 117; JT 2000 (9) SC 29

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Health & Drug

Summary: Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act, 1985, ss. 37, 52 and 57 - Bail - Recovery of 2 kg. heroin - From a room in possession of respondent - High Court order - To release him on bail on executing a bond - Challenged - Held, grant of bail on ground of prima facie violation of ss. 52 and 57 - Not proper - Compliance of ss. 52 and 57 is matter which could be established only at trial and not at stage of consideration for bail.
