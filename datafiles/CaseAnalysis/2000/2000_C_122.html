Pawan Biscuits Company Private Limited v Collector of Central Excise, Patna

Supreme Court of India

20 July 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, D.P. Mohapatra

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 366; (2000) 6 SCC 489; AIR 2000 SC 2565; 2000 (91) ECR 761; 2000 (120) E.L.T. 24; JT 2000 (8) SC 113; 2000 (39) RLT 891; 2000(5) SCALE 263; 2000 (5) SLT 590; [2000] Supp1 S.C.R. 628; 2000 (5) Supreme 239

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, ss. 4, Central Exci, (Valuation) Rules, 1975, r.6 - Assessable value - Determination - Assessee manufacturing biscuits for a Biscuit Company out of raw material supplied by a Biscuit Company - Held, for calculating assessable value of assessee's biscuits cost of raw material supplied by Biscuit Company will have to be included in addition to assessee's manufacturing costs and profit but not profit of Biscuit Company or expenses which are incurred after manufacture of biscuits by assessee.
