State of Tripura v Manoranjan Chakraborty and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 November 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Kirpal, Doraiswamy Raju, N. Santosh Hegde

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 3063; (2001) 10 SCC 740; 2001 (122) STC 594

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - Tripura Sales Tax Act, 1976 - Ss. 20 and 21 - Condition of pre-deposit of tax assessed or penalty imposed as a condition for entertainment of appeal or revision - Discretion given to authority for permitting payment of not less than 50% of tax assessed or penalty levied - Validity of provisions - Whether High Court was right in striking down ss. 20 and 21 of Act imposing condition of pre deposit of tax and penalty for entertaining appeal as invalid? - Held, in view of decisions of Supreme Court in case of Gujarat Agro Industries Co Ltd v Municipal Corporation of City of Ahmedabad 1999 Indlaw SC 1551 and Shyam Kishore v Municipal Corporation of Delhi 1992 Indlaw SC 416, it is held that impugned provisions are valid - Judgment of HC set aside - Appeals allowed.
