Hindustan Shipyard Limited v State of Andhra Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

20 July 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 696; (2000) 6 SCC 579; AIR 2000 SC 2411; 2000 (6) ALD(SC) 1; JT 2000 (8) SC 29; 2000 (119) STC 533; [2000] Supp1 S.C.R. 592

Case Digest

Subject: Contract & Commercial

Summary: Andhra Pradesh General Sales Tax Act, 1957, ss. 2(n) and 2(t) - Sale - Transactions involved in manufacture and supply of ships by Public Undertaking to its customer are 'sale' within meaning of s. 2(n) and not 'works contract' as defined in s. 2(t) - Held, transactions involved in manufacture and supply of ships are eligible to sales tax.
