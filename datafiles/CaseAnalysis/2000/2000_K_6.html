Union of India and Others v K. B. Rajoria

Supreme Court of India

28 March 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

Ruma Pal, M. Jagannadha Rao

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 225; (2000) 3 SCC 562; (2000) SCC (L&S) 665; AIR 2000 SC 1819; 2000 (85) FLR 714; JT 2000 (4) SC 213; 2000 LabIC 1470; 2000 (2) LLN 971; 2000(2) SCALE 627; 2000 (3) SCJ 16; [2000] 2 S.C.R. 613; 2000 (3) SLJ 276; 2000 (4) SLR 674; 2000 (4) SLT 83; 2000 (2) Supreme 649; 2000 (2) UPLBEC 1255

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Ad Hoc Appointment

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 16 - Central Public Works Department (Director General of Works) Recruitment Rules, 1986 (Amendment 1992), r. 1 - Promotion - Director General (Works) - Condition, having two years 'Regular service' as Addl. Director General (Works) - Candidate under notional promotion as Addl. Director General - As to compensate them - Eligibility - Held, not means actual physical service - Eligible after completing two years of service from date of notional promotion.
