Travancore Rubber and Tea Company Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax, Trivandrum

Supreme Court of India

14 March 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

Ruma Pal, D.P. Wadhwa

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 212; (2000) 3 SCC 715; AIR 2000 SC 1980; 2000 (160) CTR 1; [2000] 243 ITR 158; JT 2000 (3) SC 458; 2000(2) SCALE 409; [2000] 2 S.C.R. 290; 2000 (3) SLT 155; 2000 (2) Supreme 514; [2000] 109 TAXMAN 250

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure; Contract & Commercial

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 51 - Contract Act, 1872, ss. 74, 263 ,256(1) - Income Tax - Whether the earnest money amounts received by the appellant assessee in respect of an abortive sale transaction of rubber trees are capital or revenue receipts - Held, are capital receipts as phrase 'advance' and 'other money' u/s. 51of income Tax Act does not make any distinction between earnest money and advance - Appeal allowed.
