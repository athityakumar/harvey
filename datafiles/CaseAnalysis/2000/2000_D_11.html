Kamal Pushp Enterprises v D. R. Construction Company

Supreme Court of India

28 July 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

Doraiswamy Raju, M. Jagannadha Rao

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 387; (2000) 6 SCC 659; AIR 2000 SC 2676; 2000 (6) ALD(SC) 92; 2000 (5) ALT 39; 2000 (3) ARBLR 1; JT 2000 (8) SC 347; 2000 (1) MLJ(SC) 176; 2000 (4) RCR(Civil) 75; [2000] Supp2 S.C.R. 20

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure; Tort

Summary: Partnership Act, 1932, s. 69, Arbitration Act, 1940, s. 14(2) - Unregistered firm - Bar to enforce rights arising from contract - Proceedings for making award rule of court - Held, are not proceedings to enforce right arising under contract - Such proceedings are not barred by s. 69 - Does not apply to arbitration proceedings - More when unregistered firm was defendant.
