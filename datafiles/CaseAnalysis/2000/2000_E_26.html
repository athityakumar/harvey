Union of India v Era Educational Trust and Another

Supreme Court of India

05 April 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

M.B. Shah

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 317; (2000) 5 SCC 57; AIR 2000 SC 1573; 2000 ALJ 958; 2000 (2) CLT 306; JT 2000 (4) SC 241; 2000 (3) MLJ(SC) 32; 2000(3) SCALE 123; [2000] 2 S.C.R. 1001; 2000 (2) SLR 628; 2000 (3) SLT 604; 2000 (3) Supreme 195; 2000 (2) UPLBEC 1485

Case Digest

Subject: Education

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 39, r. 1 - Medical Council Act, 1956, s. 10 A - Grant for establishment of Medical College - Refusal by Central Govt. twice - High Court refusing to permit establishment of Medical College - Held, Permission to establish and run Medical College by way of interim mandatory order - Covers under HC's extraordinary jurisdiction under art. 226 - Order unsustainable.
