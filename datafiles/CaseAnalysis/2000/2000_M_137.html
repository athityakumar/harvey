Delhi Jal Board v Mahinder Singh

Supreme Court of India

01 September 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Jagannadha Rao, Ruma Pal

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 578; (2000) 7 SCC 210; (2000) SCC (L&S) 897; AIR 2000 SC 2767; 2000 (6) ALD(SC) 73; 2000 (4) AWC 3051; JT 2000 (10) SC 158; 2000 (2) LLJ 1604; 2000(6) SCALE 296; 2000 (5) SLR 274; [2000] Supp3 S.C.R. 69

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - onstitution, art. 16 - Service matter - Right to be considered by Departmental Promotion Committee for promotion is fundamental right of eligible person - Enquiry ended in employee's favour - Determination of entitlement of right - Held, entitled to benefit of assessment by first DPC. notwithstanding pendency of another inquiry - Petition dismissed. .
