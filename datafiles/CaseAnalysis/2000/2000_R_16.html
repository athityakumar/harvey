G. P. Srivastava v R. K. Raizada and Others

Supreme Court of India

03 March 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

R.P. Sethi, S. Saghir Ahmad

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 192; (2000) 3 SCC 54; AIR 2000 SC 1221; 2000 (4) ALD(SC) 54; 2000 ALJ 863; 2000 (39) ALR 110; 2000 (2) AWC 1294; 2001 (1) CGLJ 34; 2000 (2) CLT 116; JT 2000 (2) SC 569; 2000 (125) PLR 544; 2000 (2) RCR(Civil) 161; 2000 (91) RD 325; 2000(2) SCALE 198; 2000 (2) SCJ 30; [2000] 2 S.C.R. 97; 2000 (2) SLT 568; 2000 (2) Supreme 104

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 9, r. 13 - 'Sufficient cause' - For non-appearance on date of hearing - Production of medical certificate from private doctor - Relevancy - Held, is no ground to reject plea of sickness - Medical certificate from Govt. doctor is not necessary.
