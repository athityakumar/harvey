Rita Devi Smt. and Others v New India Assurance Company Limited and Another

Supreme Court of India

27 April 2000

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, D.P. Wadhwa

Where Reported

2000 Indlaw SC 307; (2000) 5 SCC 113; (2000) SCC (Cr) 912; 2000 (2) ACC 291; AIR 2000 SC 1930; AIR 2000 SCW 1579; 2000 (3) AWC 1860; 2000 (2) G.L.R. 1729; JT 2000 (5) SC 355; 2000 (2) KLT 526; 2000 (1) LLJ 1656; 2000 (3) MLJ(SC) 88; 2000 (125) PLR 768; 2000 (3) RCR(Civil) 200; 2000(4) SCALE 85; [2000] 3 S.C.R. 741

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Motor Vehicles Act, 1988, s. 163 A(1) - Murder or Motor accident - Death - Deceased authorickshaw driver employed to drive for carrying passengers on hire - Passenger murdering autorickshaw driver - Held, Word 'death' in Workmen's Compensation Act will be applicable - Both enactments operating in same field - Death is an 'accident' within meaning of Motor Vehicles Act.
