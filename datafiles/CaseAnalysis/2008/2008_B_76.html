Bal Krishna and Another v Bhagwan Das (Dead) and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 March 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

P.P. Naolekar, Lokeshwar Singh Panta

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 543; (2008) 12 SCC 145; AIR 2008 SC 1786; 2008 (3) ALD(SC) 27; 2008(3) ALL MR 809; 2008 (2) AWC 1760; 2008 (2) RCR(Civil) 732; 2008(4) SCALE 805; [2008] 5 S.C.R. 389; 2008 (2) UC 829

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure; Trusts & Associations; Contract & Commercial

Keywords: Unfair Advantage, Readiness To Perform

Summary: Specific Relief Act, 1963, s. 16 - Suit for specific performance of the contract to reconvey the suit property - Held, in the absence of pleadings or proof by the plaintiffs as to their willingness and readiness to perform their part of the contract and get the sale deed executed in their favour on payment of Rs.25, 000/-, no case is made out by the plaintiffs for specific performance of the contract of reconveyance - Appeal dismissed.
