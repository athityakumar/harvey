Vidyadhari & Ors. v Sukhrana Bai & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

22 January 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

V.S. Sirpurkar, S.B. Sinha

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 79; (2008) 2 SCC 238; AIR 2008 SC 1420; 2008 (1) AWC 1061; 2008 (105) CutLT 833; 2008 (3) G.L.R. 2671; 2008 (3) MahLJ 1; 2008 (3) MLJ(SC) 562; 2008 (2) MPLJ 351; 2008 (1) RCR(Civil) 900; 2008(1) SCALE 580; [2008] 1 S.C.R. 1030

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Land & Property

Summary: Indian Succession Act, s. 372 - Application for obtaining succession certificate with respect to the movable properties of deceased - Held, High Court completely ignored the admitted situation that this Succession Certificate was for the purposes of collecting the Provident Fund, Life Cover Scheme, Pension and amount of Life Insurance and amount of other dues in the nature of death benefits - That appellant was a nominee is not disputed by anyone and is, therefore proved - Appeal allowed.
