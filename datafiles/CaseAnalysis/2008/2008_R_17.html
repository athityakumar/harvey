Ramachandran v R. Udhayakumar and Others

Supreme Court of India

13 May 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, P. Sathasivam

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 816; (2008) 5 SCC 413; (2008) 2 SCC (Cr) 631; AIR 2008 SC 3102; 2008 CRLJ 4309; 2008 (2) JCC 1430; 2008 (4) JhrCR(SC) 136; JT 2008 (6) SC 582; 2008 (4) KLT 211; 2008 (3) RCR(Criminal) 47; 2008(7) SCALE 609; [2008] 8 S.C.R. 439

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: CrPC, 1973, s. 482 - IPC, 1860, ss. 148, 302, 307 and 324 r/w 149 - Petition seeking direction to re- investigate the case by the CBI in an alleged murder case - Fresh or re- investigation ordered - Appeal against - From s. 173 it is evident that even after completion of investigation u/s. 173(2) of CrPC, 1973, the police has right to further investigate u/sub-s. (8), but not fresh investigation or re-investigation - Directions of the High Court for re-investigation or fresh investigation are clearly indefensible - Appeal partly allowed.
