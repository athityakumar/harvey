Div. Manager, New India Assurance Company Limited v A. Sankaralingam

Supreme Court of India

03 October 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

H.S. Bedi, Tarun Chatterjee

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 2153; (2008) 10 SCC 698; AIR 2009 SC 309; 2009 (1) AWC 334; 2008 (5) CTC 365; 2008 (119) FLR 398; 2009 (1) JhrCR(SC) 86; JT 2008 (11) SC 128; 2008 (4) KLT 444; 2009 (1) LLJ 602; 2009 (1) MLJ(SC) 176; 2009 (153) PLR 348; 2009 (1) RLW 626; 2008(13) SCALE 292; [2008] 14 S.C.R. 139; 2008 (6) SLR 638

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Independent Contractor, Air Force Act, 1950, Navy Act, 1957

Summary: Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - Status of a part time employee and as to whether such an employee falls within the definition of "workman"? - Held, a workman employed on a part time basis but under the control and supervision of an employer is a workman in term of s. 2(s), and is entitled to claim the protection of s. 25F thereof, should the need so arise - The fact that the workman was working under the control and supervision of the appellant employer is admitted on all sides - Appeal dismissed.
