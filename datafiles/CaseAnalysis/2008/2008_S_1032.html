Rahimbux v State of Madhya Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

12 May 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

A.K. Mathur, Altamas Kabir

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 814; (2008) 12 SCC 270; AIR 2008 SC 3111; 2008 (2) JCC 1369; 2008 (4) JhrCR(SC) 141; 2008(7) SCALE 763; [2008] 8 S.C.R. 213

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Defence & Security Forces; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Indian Penal Code, ss. 302, 323; Arms Act, s. 25 - Appeal against conviction and sentence - Held, so far as the voluntarily causing injury to the deceased by the accused is concerned, there is no two opinion and the same has been fully substantiated by the relevant evidence - It is not a case in which the benefit of s. 304-I, I.P.C. or s. 304-II can be given - Appeal dismissed
