Bhanwar Singh And Others v Moti Singh (Dead), Through Lrs. And Others

Supreme Court of India

17 January 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Tarun Chatterjee, Dalveer Bhandari

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 2333

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Civil Procedure - Settlement of dispute - Dismissal of appeal - Dispute between parties regarding land in question were settled - Hence, instant Appeal.

Held, appellants do not wish to proceed with appeal as the land in dispute has been purchased by another person by registered deed of Sale. In view of said fact and dispute having been settled between the parties, the appeal shall not be proceeded with. Thus, appeal is dismissed for non-prosecution and interim orders, if there be any, stand vacated. Appeal disposed of.
