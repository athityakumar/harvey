U.P. State Electricity Board v Laxmi Kant Gupta

Supreme Court of India

26 September 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Markandey Katju, Altamas Kabir

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 1557; (2009) 16 SCC 562; AIR 2008 SC (Supp) 1112; 2008 (6) AWC 6438; JT 2008 (10) SC 534; 2009 (1) LLJ 593; 2008(13) SCALE 39; [2008] 13 S.C.R. 1051; 2009 (2) SLR 184; 2009 (1) UPLBEC 211

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: U.P. Industrial Disputes Act, 1947

Summary: U.P. Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - Termination - Order of reinstatement without backwages - Appeal against - Held, there is no hard and fast principle now that on the termination of service being found to be illegal, the normal rule is re-instatement with back wages - Compensation can be awarded instead, at the discretion of the Labour Court - Appeal allowed.
