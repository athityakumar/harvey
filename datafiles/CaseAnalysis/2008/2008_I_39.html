Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation Limited v Indian Railway Major and Minor Caterers Association and Others

Supreme Court of India

26 March 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

H.K. Sema, Markandey Katju

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 551; (2011) 12 SCC 792

Case Digest

Subject: Parliament & Legislature; Practice & Procedure; Service

Keywords: Policy Decisions

Summary: Service - High Court has interfered with the Catering Policy of 2005 in respect of reservations - Appeals against - Held, policy decisions of the Government should not be interfered in a routine manner unless the policy is contrary to the provisions of statutory rules or of the Constitution - Nothing has been brought to notice that the Policy is contrary to the provisions of the statutory rules or the Constitution - Appeals allowed.
