Union Of India v S. P. Singh

Supreme Court of India

07 May 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

H.S. Bedi, Tarun Chatterjee

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 1019; (2008) 5 SCC 438; AIR 2008 SC 2445; 2009(1) ALL MR 974; 2008 (3) AWC 2565; 2008 (5) ESC 784; 2008 (117) FLR 932; JT 2008 (6) SC 319; 2008 (3) LLN 633; 2008(7) SCALE 668; [2008] 7 S.C.R. 989; 2008 (4) SLR 577

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Central Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1972 - request for voluntary retirement rejected - Held, though the suspension order had been dispatched by facsimile before 1st September 2005 yet it had been dispatched to the wrong address and could not be deemed to have been communicated to the respondent - Appeal dismissed.
