Northn. Rly. Admn., Ministry of Railway v Patel Engineering Company Limited

Supreme Court of India

12 February 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

A.K. Mathur, Aftab Alam

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 2785; 2008(2) SCALE 424

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Held, earlier decision in the case of Ace Pipeline 2007 Indlaw SC 292 (supra) was not brought to the notice of the Bench, as a result of which a confusion may be created that which judgment is to be followed as both the judgments cover almost identical area of controversy. It is in the interest of justice that this case may be referred to a larger Bench so that there may not be a confusion in view of the two conflicting decisions of this Court. urgent consideration. Order accordingly.
