State of Maharashtra and Others v Bhaurao Punjabrao Gawande

Supreme Court of India

03 March 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

C.K. Thakker, Altamas Kabir

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 317; (2008) 2 SCC (Cr) 128; (2008) 3 SCC 613; AIR 2008 SC 1705; JT 2008 (3) SC 161; 2008 (2) RCR(Criminal) 175; 2008(3) SCALE 356; [2008] 3 S.C.R. 967

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Socio-Economic

Keywords: Past Conduct, Prevention of Blackmarketing and Maintenance of Supplies of Essential Commodities Act, 1980

Summary: Prevention of Black Marketing and Maintenance of Supplies of Essential Commodities Act, 1980 - High Court set aside order of detention passed by the Commissioner of Police - Appeal against - Held, an order of detention passed by a Detaining Authority under the relevant 'preventive detention' law cannot be set aside by a Writ Court at the pre-execution or pre-arrest stage unless the Court is satisfied that there are exceptional circumstances - This case does not fall within the category of exceptional cases and the High Court committed an error of law in setting aside the order of detention at the pre-execution and pre-arrest stage - Appeal allowed.
