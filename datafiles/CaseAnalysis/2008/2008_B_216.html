Ahmadasahab Abdul Mulla (D) By Proposed Lrs v Bibijan and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 April 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Tarun Chatterjee

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 676; (2008) 5 SCC 361; AIR 2008 SC (Supp) 1828; 2008 (71) ALR 530; 2008 (3) AWC 2434; JT 2008 (5) SC 566; 2008 (5) KarLJ 106; 2008 (105) RD 38; 2008(7) SCALE 206; [2008] 6 S.C.R. 716; 2008 (2) UC 1254

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Specific Relief Act, 1963, s. 20; Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 100; Limitation Act, 1908, s. 113 - Suit for specific performance of the contract on sale which was decreed by the trial court but was dismissed on the ground of limitation by the first Appellate Court - Second appellate court decreed the suit - Appeal against - Held, in view of the importance of the issues involved, it would be proper if this case is heard by a Bench of three Hon'ble Judges - Matter referred to larger bench.
