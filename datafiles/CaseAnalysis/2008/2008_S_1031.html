Pravin v State of Madhya Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

25 March 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

V.S. Sirpurkar, S.B. Sinha

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 545; (2008) 16 SCC 166; AIR 2008 SC 1846; 2008 CRLJ 2980; 2008 (2) JCC 1113; JT 2008 (4) SC 354; 2008 (2) RCR(Criminal) 575; 2008(4) SCALE 431; [2008] 5 S.C.R. 367

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal; Defence & Security Forces; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Graphic Description

Summary: Indian Penal Code, ss. 395 r/w 397, 450; Arms Act, ss. 25(1-B)(a), 27 - Appeal against conviction and sentence - Held, High Court as well as the Trial Court have gone into the intricacies of the evidence - Stolen property was recovered from the possession of the appellant - It would, therefore, be justified in using the presumption raised by s. 114 of the Evidence Act - Appeal dismissed.
