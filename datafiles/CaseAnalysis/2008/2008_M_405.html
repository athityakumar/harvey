Mohd. Yunus Khan v U.P. Power Corporation Limited And Others

Supreme Court of India

22 October 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Cyriac Joseph

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 2612; (2009) 1 SCC 80; AIR 2008 SC (Supp) 1163; JT 2008 (13) SC 33; 2008(13) SCALE 693; [2008] 14 S.C.R. 1114

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Service - Correction of date of birth in Service Book - Respondent/employer wrongly recorded another employee's date of birth in the service records of appellant/employee - Appellant filed a representation to respondents for correcting his service records with a copy of his school leaving certificate - Respondent did not take any action and stopped the payment of salary to appellant on the premises that he had superannuated on the basis of his date of birth in the service records - Appellant challenged respondents order before HC - Single Judge allowed appellant's writ petition, however, DB set aside the Single Judge order - Whether impugned order of DB is legally sustainable - Held, respondents failed to show that the mistake in the matter of recording of date of birth in the service record was known to appellant at any earlier point of time - Respondent did not receive appellant's representation with due and expeditious consideration nor gave the appellant a hearing - DB has committed a serious error in not considering the aspect of failure on the part of respondents to take any action, therefore, impugned judgments cannot be sustained and they are set aside - Appeal allowed.
