Tdm Infrastructure Private Limited v Ue Development India Private Limited

Supreme Court of India

14 May 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 853; (2008) 14 SCC 271; AIR 2008 SC (Supp) 1143; 2008 (3) AWC 3104; [2008] 144 Comp Cas 34; 2008 (3) CompLJ 451; 2008(8) SCALE 576; [2008] 8 S.C.R. 775

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Corporate

Keywords: Foreign Award, Domestic Award, Termination Of Proceedings, Malaysian Law and the Malaysian Arbitration Act, 2005, English Arbitration Act, 1975

Summary: Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, ss. 11(5) and 11(6) - Application for appointment of arbitrator - Directors and shareholders of petitioner-company were residents of Malaysia and Board of Directors of petitioner had its seat at Malaysia - Whether Supreme Court of India had jurisdiction to pass an order for appointing an arbitrator? - Whether agreement between the petitioner and respondent fell within the purview of s. 2 (1)(f) of the 1996 Act? - Held, if both the companies are incorporate
