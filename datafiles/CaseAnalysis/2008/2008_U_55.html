Pramod Kumar Saxena v Union Of India & Ors

Supreme Court of India

19 September 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

C.K. Thakker, D.K. Jain

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 1497; (2008) 9 SCC 685; (2008) 3 SCC (Cr) 866; 2009(2) ALL MR 413; 2008 (4) BC 505; 2008 CRLJ 4697; 2009 (1) JhrCR(SC) 79; JT 2008 (10) SC 195; 2009 (2) MLJ(Crl) 267; 2008 (4) RCR(Criminal) 351; 2008(12) SCALE 623; [2008] 13 S.C.R. 697; 2009 (1) WLN(SC) 27

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Criminal

Keywords: Jail Manual, Non-Bailable Warrants, Under-Trial, Personal Appearance, Prison Act, 1894

Summary: Code of Criminal Procedure (Amendment) Act, 2005, Section 436A- Bail - Article 32 Petition of an under-trial prisoner charged under ss. 406, 409 and 420 r/w S. 120B, IPC, 1860 and S. 138, NI Act, 1881.- Petitioner completed more than ten years in jail. Limited relief granted to the petitioner in accordance with the judgment in V.K. Sharma case. Rejected plea for consolidation of cases and trial of all the cases in one Court in view of judgment in Rajesh Syal case. Direction to appropriate court to release petitioner on bail on his executing a bond.
