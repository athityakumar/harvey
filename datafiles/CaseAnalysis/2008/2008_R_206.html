Prakash Kadam and etc. etc. v Ramprasad Vishwanath Gupta and another

Supreme Court of India

04 November 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Markandey Katju, Gyan Sudha Misra

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 1716; (2011) 6 SCC 189; AIR 2011 SC 1945; 2012 (1) CGLJ 322; JT 2011 (5) SC 629; 2011 (3) RCR(Criminal) 220; 2011(6) SCALE 236; [2011] 6 S.C.R. 800

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Health & Drug; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Manufacture For Sale, Prohibition Of Manufacture, Non- Cognizable

Summary: Drugs and Cosmetics Act, 1940, ss. 18 (c) r/w 27(b)(ii) - Appeal against order quashing the proceedings - Held, mere fact that the application for licence was filed, did not entitle the respondent to manufacture and/or to sell the concerned drugs - High Court, therefore, was not justified in quashing the proceedings - This is a not a case where threshold interference by exercising power under s. 482 of the Code was called for - Appeal allowed.
