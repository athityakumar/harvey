Rajkumar Gurawara (Dead) Thr. L.Rs v S.K. Sarwagi and Company Private Limited and Another

Supreme Court of India

14 May 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

P. Sathasivam, Arijit Pasayat

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 996; (2008) 14 SCC 364; AIR 2008 SC 2303; 2008 (3) AWC 3045; 2009 (1) CGLJ 240; 2008 (5) CTC 253; 2008 (8) MLJ(SC) 307; 2008 (4) RCR(Civil) 824; 2008(7) SCALE 471; [2008] 8 S.C.R. 700

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Payment Of Cost

Summary: C.P.C, O.6 r. 17 - Amendment application - Suit for declaration of his exclusive right to do mining operation in the suit property - Held, grant of application for amendment be subject to certain conditions, namely, (i) when the nature of it is changed by permitting amendment; (ii) when the amendment would result introducing new cause of action and intends to prejudice the other party; (iii) when allowing amendment application defeats the law of limitation - Plaintiff not only failed to satisfy the conditions prescribed in proviso to O. 6 r. 17 but even on merits his claim is liable to be rejected - Appeal dismissed.
