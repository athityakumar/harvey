Vaman Narain Ghiya v State of Rajasthan

Supreme Court of India

12 December 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Mukundakam Sharma

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 1940; (2009) 2 SCC 281; (2009) 1 SCC (Cr) 745; AIR 2009 SC 1362; 2009 CRLJ 1311; 2009 (234) E.L.T. 3; 2009 (1) JCC 434; JT 2008 (13) SC 137; 2009 (1) MLJ(Crl) 883; 2009 (1) RCR(Criminal) 473; 2008(16) SCALE 346; [2008] 17 S.C.R. 369

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - IPC, 1860, 413 - CrPC, 1973 - Question of grant of bail - Considerations for - Identical case the application of the applicant was rejected by High Court and the matter was carried to Superme Court and no interference was made - Order of discharge in respect of offence punishable u/s. 413 IPC was challenged by filing a revision before the High Court - Considering the aforesaid aspects the prayer for bail was rejected - Appeal - Held, while considering an application for bail, detailed discussion of the evidence and elaborate documentation of the merits is to be avoided so that no party should have the impression that his case has been pre-judged - Elaborate analysis or exhaustive exploration of the merits is not required - Appeal Dismissed.
