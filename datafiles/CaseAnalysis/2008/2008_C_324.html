Commnr. Of Customs, Central Excise,Noida v M/S Punjab Fibres Ltd., Noida

Supreme Court of India

14 February 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, P. Sathasivam

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 188; (2008) 3 SCC 73; 2008 (223) E.L.T. 337; JT 2008 (2) SC 458; 2008(2) SCALE 469; [2008] 2 S.C.R. 861

Case Digest

Subject: Excise

Keywords: Enactment

Summary: Central Excise Act, 1944 - Whether High Court has power to condone the delay in presentation of the reference under s. 35- H(1)? - Held, proviso to s. 35-G permits the Appellate Tribunal to allow the appeal even after the limitation prescribed in clause 1 is expired if the Tribunal is satisfied that there was sufficient cause for not filing the appeal within the prescribed time - No such provision for condonation of delay exist in s. 35-H - Parliament never intended that delay in filing the reference application under s. 35- H could be condoned - Appeals dismissed.
