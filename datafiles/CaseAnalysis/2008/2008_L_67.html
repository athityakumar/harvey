CIT v Lovely Exports Private Limited

Supreme Court of India

11 January 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

S.H. Kapadia, B. SUDARSHAN REDDY

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 2623

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 68 - Whether amount of share money be regarded as undisclosed income u/s. 68 of IT Act? - Held, if the share application money is received by the assessee company from alleged bogus shareholders, whose names are given to the Assessing Officer, then the Department is free to proceed to reopen their individual assessments in accordance with law - Hence, no infirmity with the impugned judgment - Special Leave Petition dismissed.
