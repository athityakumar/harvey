Daulatram S/O Sadram Teli v State Of Chhattisgarh

Supreme Court of India

19 September 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Mukundakam Sharma

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 1539; (2008) 17 SCC 112; AIR 2009 SC (Supp) 1113; 2008 CRLJ 4587; JT 2008 (10) SC 290; 2008 (4) RCR(Criminal) 587; 2008(12) SCALE 608; [2008] 13 S.C.R. 734

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: IPC, s. 302; Schedule Castes and Schedule Tribes (Atrocities) Act, 1989 - Appeal against conviction and sentence - Held, mere statement that being relatives of the deceased they are likely to falsely implicate the accused cannot be a ground to discard the evidence which is otherwise cogent and credible - Fact that co-accused has been acquitted, cannot be a ground for holding that appellants' conviction is infirm - Appeal dismissed.
