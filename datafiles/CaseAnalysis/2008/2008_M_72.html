M.M.T.C. Limited v Commissioner of Customs, New Delhi

Supreme Court of India

29 January 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Ashok Bhan, Dalveer Bhandari

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 943; 2008 (224) E.L.T. 516; 2008 (10) S.T.R. 225

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Service Tax

Summary: Customs Act, 1962, s. 130(A) - Whether liability for payment of customs duty can at all be on MMTC which is a canalizing agency when Customs notification no. 177/94 and 3/88, Chap. 8 of Exim Policy and Procedure, PEP Circular No. 22/88, and Customs Circular No. 10/88 and 2/92, require Gem and jewelery unit located in EOU/EPZ, to fulfil export obligation? - Reference made to High Court u/s. 130A of Act - Held, HC, instead of proceeding in terms of provisions of s. 130A of Act, confirmed order of tribunal as if it is deciding appeal/case finally on merits - Impugned order of HC set aside - Case remitted back to HC - Appeal allowed.
