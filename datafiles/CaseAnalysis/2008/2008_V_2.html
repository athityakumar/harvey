Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal

Keywords: ULC Act, Domestic Violence Act, British Matrimonial Homes Act, 1967

Summary: Hindu Adoptions and Maintenance Act, 1956 - Whether in the facts and circumstances of the case, the property of Appellant No.1 could have been sold in auction?; Whether in a case of this nature, the bail granted to the appellants should have been directed to be cancelled? - Held, Maintenance of a married wife, during subsistence of marriage, is on the husband - It is a personal obligation - Property in the name of the mother-in-law can neither be a subject matter of attachment nor during the life time of the husband, his personal liability to maintain his wife can be directed to be enforced against such property - Directions issued.
