Agricultural Produce Market Committee, Narela, Delhi v Commissioner of Income Tax and Another

Supreme Court of India

21 August 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

S.H. Kapadia, B. Sudarshan Reddy

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 1347; (2008) 9 SCC 434; AIR 2008 SC (Supp) 566; [2008] 305 ITR 1; JT 2008 (10) SC 17; 2008(11) SCALE 540; [2008] 12 S.C.R. 387; [2008] 173 TAXMAN 115

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Agriculture & Agri. Products

Keywords: Exemption From Payment, Annual Income, Delhi Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1998, Agricultural Produce Marketing, Market Fee, Agricultural Market Committee

Summary: [A.] Agriculture & Agri. Products - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 10(20), 260A - Delhi Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1998, ss. 2(1), 5 - Finance Act, 2002 - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 243(d), 243N, 243P(e), 243ZF - General Clauses Act, 1897, s. 3(31) - Cantonments Act, 1924, s. 3 - Delhi Development Act, 1957 - Constitution (Seventy-Fourth Amendment) Act, 1992 - Whether Agricultural Market Committee is a 'local authority' under Explanation to s 10(20)? - Held, 'local authority' to mean - A Municipal Committee, District Board, body of Port Commissioners or other authority - AMC(s) is neither a Municipal Committee nor a District Board under said Explanation to s.10(20) of 1961 Act - Appeals dismissed.
