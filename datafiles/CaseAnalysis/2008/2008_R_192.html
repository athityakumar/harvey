State of Haryana v Ramesh Kumar

Supreme Court of India

11 July 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, P. Sathasivam

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 1061; (2008) 11 SCC 435; AIR 2009 SC (Supp) 1335; 2008 (4) ALD(SC) 120; 2008 (6) AWC 6532; 2008 (2) CLR 1089; 2008 (118) FLR 694; 2008 (3) LLJ 609; 2008 (3) LLN 716; 2008(10) SCALE 57; [2008] 10 S.C.R. 862; 2009 (3) SLR 772

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - Termination - Labour Court took the view that respondent is presumed to have completed 240 days of service and provision of s. 25 was not complied with - High Court confirmed the order - Appeal against - Held, absence of reasons has rendered the High Court order not sustainable - Remitted the matter to High Court for fresh consideration in accordance with law - Appeal disposed of.
