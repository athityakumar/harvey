Sachida Nand Lal @ Sachida Nand Shah v State Of Bihar, (Now Jharkhand)

Supreme Court of India

07 November 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

C.K. Thakker, D.K. Jain

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 1729; (2008) 17 SCC 596; 2009 (1) AWC 723; 2009 (1) JhrCR(SC) 120; JT 2008 (12) SC 607; 2009 (4) MLJ(SC) 1128; 2008(15) SCALE 55; [2008] 15 S.C.R. 830

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Land Acquisition Act, 1894 - Appeal for enhancement of compensation - Held, since the appeal before the learned Single Judge was Appeal from Original Decree, i.e. First Appeal, the Division Bench ought to have considered the correctness or otherwise of the order passed by Single Judge by exercising same powers as exercised by the single Judge in the appeal from original decree - Letters Patent before the Division Bench was not in the nature of appeal from an appellate decree, i.e. Second Appeal, but it was continuation of appeal from original decree, i.e. First Appeal - Appeal allowed.
