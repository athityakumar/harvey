Deputy Collector, Land Acquisition, Gujarat and another v Madhubai Gobarbhai and another

Supreme Court of India

23 October 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Cyriac Joseph

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 2714; (2008) 17 SCC 443

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Civil Procedure - Adducing additional documents – Permission for - Reference Court refused to take on record some deeds of sale which were sought to be exhibited on behalf of State - Applicant sought for permission to adduce additional documents and to urge additional grounds – Hence, instant Application.

Held, reference Court while refusing record of document regarded decision of in P. Ram Reddy v. Land Acquisition Officer, HUDA 1995 Indlaw SC 1502. However, P. Ram Reddy case 1995 Indlaw SC 1502 has since been overruled by decisions of SC in Land Acquisition Officer and Mandal Revenue Officer v. U. Narasaiah 2001 Indlaw SC 20254 , Ranvir Singh v. Union of India 2005 Indlaw SC 547 and Cement Corpn. of India Ltd. v. Purya 2004 Indlaw SC 840. Therefore, applications for permission for urging additional grounds are allowed and documents annexed to said applications are directed to be taken on record as marked exhibits. Order accordingly.
