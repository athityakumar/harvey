Nagarjuna Construction Company Limited v Government of Andhra Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

20 October 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, P. Sathasivam

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 1661; (2008) 16 SCC 276; AIR 2009 SC (Supp) 4; 2008 (73) ALR 660; 2009 (6) CompLJ 172; 2009 (1) JhrCR(SC) 110; JT 2008 (12) SC 371; 2009 (3) MLJ(SC) 493; 2008(14) SCALE 476; [2008] 14 S.C.R. 859

Case Digest

Subject: Mines & Minerals; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Concession Rules, Mineral Concession, Minor Mineral Concession, Andhra Pradesh Minor Mineral Concession Rules, 1966

Summary: Andhra Pradesh Minor Mineral Concession Rules, 1966 - Seigniorage fee - Prayer for quashing the order passed by the State Government in Memorandum No.8817/M.II(1)/2001-6, dated 4.2.2002 and the consequent demand notice issued - Held, matter is remitted to the State Government to re-consider the matter after supplying to the appellants copies of reports/inspection notes on which the Department case rests - It shall also consider the effect of the concession made by the Department in the earlier rounds of proceedings before the High Court - Appeals allowed.
