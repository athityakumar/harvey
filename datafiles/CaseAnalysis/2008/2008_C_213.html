Asian Peroxides Limited v Commissioner of Central Excise, Guntur

Supreme Court of India

07 July 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, P. Sathasivam, Aftab Alam

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 1123; (2008) 12 SCC 789; AIR 2009 SC (Supp) 1303; 2008 (228) E.L.T. 5; 2008(9) SCALE 625; [2008] 10 S.C.R. 132

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Keywords: Manufacturing Process, Appeals Disposed Of

Summary: Central Excise Act, 1944 - Issue related to the eligibility of the appellant for the benefit of exemption under Notification No.8/97-CE dated 1.3.1997 for Hydrogen Peroxide manufactured and cleared by the appellant to the Domestic Tariff Area - Held, expression "raw material" is not a defined term - The meaning has to be given in the ordinary well accepted connotation in the common parlance of those who deal with the matter - CESTAT has not considered the materials on record in the correct perspective - Matter remitted to CESTAT.
