Poonam Kumari v Jai Prakash Pandey and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 April 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, P. Sathasivam

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 656; (2008) 5 SCC 325; 2008 (71) ALR 849; 2008 (3) AWC 2906; 2008(6) SCALE 694; [2008] 6 S.C.R. 740

Case Digest

Subject: Petroleum & Gas; Practice & Procedure; Contract & Commercial

Summary: Matter of allotment and appointment as a dealer of Indian Oil Corporation Ltd. - Appellant was placed at serial No.1 of the select list - Writ petition by respondent alleging that his father had given land to IOC on lease for about thirty years with an option of further renewal for thirty years and pleaded that preference ought to have been given to the writ-petitioner - Single judge that he found that there were certain procedural irregularities committed by the DSB and therefore a fresh consideration was warranted - Appeal against - Held, because of the procedural lapses noticed by Single Judge, directed that instead of DSB, which is no longer in existence, in the line of what has been directed by this Court in another case, directed that consideration shall be made by the Selection Committee nominated by the General Manager, IOC - Appeal disposed of.
