State of Andhra Pradesh and Another v T. Yadagiri Reddy and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 November 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

V.S. Sirpurkar, Lokeshwar Singh Panta

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 1901; (2008) 16 SCC 299; 2009 (2) ALD(SC) 46; JT 2009 (1) SC 104; 2008(15) SCALE 112; [2008] 16 S.C.R. 792

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Mortgage By Conditional Sale, Andhra Pradesh (Telangana Area) Tenancy and Agricultural Land Act, 1950

Summary: A.P. (Telangana Area) Tenancy & Agricultural Lands Act, 1950 - High Court recognized the rights of the respondents herein as the protected tenants and further held that they become absolute owners of the land by purchasing the land in respect of which they were protected tenants - Appeal against - Whether the concerned land can be included in the holding of the original land holders and be declared surplus to the detriment of the respondent Nos. 1-5? - Held, there are valid orders passed by the Tribunal, having the jurisdiction to pass the same, which would show that once the land was shown and concluded to be in the holding of the protected tenant, it could not have been included in the holding of the landlord, muchless, it could not have been declared to be surplus - Appeal dismissed.
