Hardip Singh v State Of Punjab

Supreme Court of India

20 August 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Mukundakam Sharma, Arijit Pasayat

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 1317; (2008) 8 SCC 557; (2008) 3 SCC (Cr) 580; AIR 2009 SC 432; JT 2008 (9) SC 328; 2008 (4) Law Herald (P&H) 2693; 2009 (153) PLR 136; 2008 (4) RCR(Criminal) 97; 2008(11) SCALE 513; [2008] 12 S.C.R. 311

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Health & Drug; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Cross Examination

Summary: Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substance Act, 1985, s. 18 - Appeal against conviction and sentence - Held, version of the defense is nothing more than a got up story of his own whims and caprices, thus in the facts and circumstances of the present case the question of bias does not arise - Appeal dismissed.
