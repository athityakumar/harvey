Raghu Raj Singh Rousha v Shivam Sundaram Promoters Private Limied and Another

Supreme Court of India

17 December 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Cyriac Joseph

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 2002; (2009) 2 SCC 363; (2009) 1 SCC (Cr) 801; AIR 2008 SC (Supp) 706; 2009 (1) JCC 405; JT 2009 (1) SC 61; 2009 (1) RCR(Criminal) 531; 2008(16) SCALE 411; [2008] 17 S.C.R. 833

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: List Of Witnesses

Summary: Cr.P.C - Whether the High Court in exercise of its jurisdiction under ss. 397 and 401 can pass an order in absence of the accused persons? - Held, question as to whether a process has to be issued or not lies within the exclusive domain of the Magistrate so as to enable him to arrive at a satisfaction that there is sufficient ground for proceeding but not with a view to see as to whether there is sufficient ground for conviction - Appeal allowed.
