Prabhu Dayal v Sadhan Sahkari Samiti Mujuri Vikas Khand Paniyara and Others

Supreme Court of India

27 February 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, C.K. Thakker, Lokeshwar Singh Panta

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 313; (2008) 4 SCC 34; AIR 2008 SC 1681; 2008 (2) AWC 1602; 2008 (1) CLR 886; JT 2008 (3) SC 323; 2008 (2) LLJ 499; 2008 (2) LLN 96; 2008 (3) MLJ(SC) 949; 2008(3) SCALE 309; [2008] 3 S.C.R. 617; 2008 (2) SLR 804; 2008 (2) UPLBEC 1838

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure; Service

Keywords: Cooperative

Summary: Payment of Wages Act, 1963; Uttar Pradesh Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - Challenge in this appeal to the recovery order issued by the labour authorities on the basis of a compromise award - Held, notification dated 30.6.1988 issued under s. 26(2) of Minimum Wages Act, 1948 makes the position clear that provisions of the aforesaid Act are not applicable to the service of workman employed under the societies which are registered with the Registrar of Cooperative Societies - Award was made on the basis of a settlement between the parties - That being so, High Court ought not to have set aside the award - Appeal allowed.
