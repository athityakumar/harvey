Seema v Ashwani Kumar

Supreme Court of India

09 July 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, P. Sathasivam

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 2290; (2008) 7 SCC 509; AIR 2009 SC (Supp) 1308; JT 2008 (7) SC 535; 2008(9) SCALE 813; [2008] 10 S.C.R. 566

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal

Summary: Family & Personal - Marriage - Compulsory registration - Non-compliance of - Directions by SC to States and Union Territories to frame necessary statutes regarding compulsory registration of marriages - Different States and Union Territories have placed on record details of the compliance made - Held, Let all the States and Union Territories who have - Order accordingly.
