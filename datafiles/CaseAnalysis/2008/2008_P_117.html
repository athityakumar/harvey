Pnb Finance Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax-I, New Delhi

Supreme Court of India

06 November 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

S.H. Kapadia, B. Sudershan Reddy

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 1864; (2008) 13 SCC 94; [2008] 307 ITR 75; JT 2008 (12) SC 493; 2008(14) SCALE 111; [2008] 15 S.C.R. 556; [2008] 175 TAXMAN 242

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Tenancy Rights, Balancing Charge, Punjab National Bank (PNB) by Banking Companies (Acquisition and Transfer of Undertakings) Act, 1970

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 256(1) - Whether transfer of Banking Undertaking on the facts and circumstances of this case gave rise to taxable capital gains under s. 45 and whether judgment in CIT v. Artex Manufacturing Co. reported in (1997) 227 ITR 260 is applicable? - Held, on the facts and circumstances of this case, which concerns assessment year 1970-71, it was not possible to compute capital gains and, therefore, the said amount of Rs. 10.20 cr. was not taxable under s. 45 - Appeal allowed.
