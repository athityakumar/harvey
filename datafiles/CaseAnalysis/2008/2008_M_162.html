Madan Mohan Sharma & Anr v State Of Rajasthan & Ors

Supreme Court of India

22 February 2008

Case Analysis

Bench

A.K. Mathur, Altamas Kabir

Where Reported

2008 Indlaw SC 228; (2008) 3 SCC 724; AIR 2008 SC 1657; 2008 (1) ESC 172; 2008 (119) FLR 1; JT 2008 (3) SC 304; 2008 (3) LLN 478; 2008(2) SCALE 740; [2008] 3 S.C.R. 232; 2008 (2) SLR 797

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Service

Keywords: Rajasthan Panchayat Samitis and Zila Parishad (Service) Rules, 1959

Summary: Rajasthan Panchayat Samitis and Zila Parishad (Service) Rules, 1959 - High Court directed State Government to forthwith constitute a Committee headed by the Chief Secretary to examine as to whether vacancies of Teacher Grade III pursuant to advertisement No, 1/96 still exist and whether after the judgment dated February 12, 2001 of learned Single Judge any appointment on the post of Teacher Grade III was given by giving relaxation under r. 296 - Appeal against - Held, question is once r. 296 conferring the power to the State to relax the qualification is struck down then the appointment of both these candidates cannot survive and this has been down by the Division Bench of the High Court and rightly so - Appeal dismissed.
