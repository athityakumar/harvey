Secretary, Minor Irrigation and Rural Engineering v Sahngoo Ram Arya and Another

Supreme Court of India

07 May 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, Shivaraj V. Patil

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 314; (2002) 5 SCC 521; (2002) SCC (L&S) 775; AIR 2002 SC 2225; 2002 (3) AWC 2509; 2002 CRLJ 2942; 2002 (2) JhrCR(SC) 167; JT 2002 (Supp1) SC 286; 2002 (3) RCR(Criminal) 413; 2002(4) SCALE 455; 2002 (4) SLT 49; 2002 (4) Supreme 91; 2002 (2) UPLBEC 1904

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Summary: While none can dispute the power of the High Court under Article 226 to direct an inquiry by the CBI, the said power can be exercised only in cases where there is sufficient material to come to a prima facie conclusion that there is a need for such inquiry.
