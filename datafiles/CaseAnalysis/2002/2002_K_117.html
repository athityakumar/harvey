Karnataka Steel and Wire Products and Others v Kohinoor Rolling Shutters and Engineering Works and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 November 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, S.B. Sinha

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 1595; (2003) 1 SCC 76; AIR 2003 SC 179; 3 CLC 1705; [2002] 112 Comp Cas 606; 2003 (1) CompLJ 219; 2003 (1) JhrCR(SC) 81; JT 2002 (9) SC 195; 2003 (1) KHCACJ 43; 2002(8) SCALE 344; 2002 (6) SLT 479; [2002] Supp4 S.C.R. 51; 2002 (7) Supreme 611

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Summary: Corporate - Practice & procedure - Limitation Act, 1963, art. 137 - Companies Act, 1956, ss. 441, 446, 446(2) and 458A - Whether s. 458A extends period of limitation for filing any suit or application and if so, whether a claim barred on date, application for winding up was filed, stands revived? - Held, s. 458A was enacted to enable a liquidator to file suits for company to prevent any loss to Co., its shareholders but it does not revive a time barred debt or claim - Appeals dismmissed.
