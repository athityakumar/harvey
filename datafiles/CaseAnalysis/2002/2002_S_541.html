Amarjit Singh v State of NCT of Delhi

Supreme Court of India

08 January 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, R.P. Sethi

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 2151; (2009) 13 SCC 769; 2002 (96) DLT 59; JT 2002 (1) SC 291; 2002 (2) RCR(Criminal) 543

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Criminal - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, s. 438 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 406 and 420 - Anticipatory bail - Condition of depositing amount of Rs. 15 lacs in form of FDR in Trial Court as a security for deposit amount, which was allegedly settled between them - Challenged - Held, having regard to facts and circumstances of present case, have no hesitation in coming to conclusion that imposition of condition to deposit sum of Rs. 15 lacs in form of FDR in Trial Court is an unreasonable condition and, therefore, it is set aside - Appeal allowed.
