V.K. Industries and Others v M.P. Electricity Board, Rampur, Jabalpur

Supreme Court of India

01 March 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

Shivaraj V. Patil, B.P. Singh

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 119; (2002) 3 SCC 159; AIR 2002 SC 1151; 2002 (1) AllCJ 390; 2002 (48) ALR 248B; 2002 (5) AWC 4120; 2002 (1) CRC 548; JT 2002 (2) SC 387; 2002 (2) MLJ(SC) 195; 2002 (131) PLR 502; 2002(2) SCALE 376; 2002 (2) SLT 269; 2002 (2) Supreme 142; 2002 (1) UC 634

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Code of Civil Procedure, 1908-Order IX, Rule 13-Setting aside of ex parte decree-Ex parte decree being for money-Court competent to impose conditions for setting aside-Conditions required to be reasonable and not harshly excessive-Order requiring to deposit Rs. 2,00,000 in cash and to furnish bank guarantee for rest of decretal amount being onerous-Order is set aside in the event of deposit of Rs. 1,00,000 only.
