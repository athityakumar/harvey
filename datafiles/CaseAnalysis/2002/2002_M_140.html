Land Acquisition Officer-cum-DSWO, A.P v Messrs B.V. Reddy and Sons

Supreme Court of India

14 February 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, S.N. Variava, S.N. Phukan

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 97; (2002) 3 SCC 463; AIR 2002 SC 1045; AIR 2002 SCW 773; 2002 (2) ALD(SC) 47; 2002 (47) ALR 247; 2002 (2) JhrCR(SC) 88; JT 2002 (2) SC 53; 2002(2) SCALE 162; 2002 (2) SCJ 60; [2002] 1 S.C.R. 1041; 2002 (2) SLT 44; 2002 (1) Supreme 660; 2002 (1) UC 628; 2002 (2) UPLBEC 1152

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Until the statutory rigor contained in Section 25(1) of the Land Acquisition Act stood obliterated by the amended provisions of Section 25 and until all restraints and embargoes placed for the Court stood totally liberated, the reference Court had no jurisdiction to award the amount in excess of the amount claimed by the claimant.
