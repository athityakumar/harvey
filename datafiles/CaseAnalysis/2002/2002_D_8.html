Ranbir Singh v Dalbir Singh and Others

Supreme Court of India

20 March 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Mohapatra, Brijesh Kumar

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 189; (2002) 3 SCC 700; AIR 2002 SC 1500; 2002 (2) CCR 26; 2002 (2) Crimes 24; 2002 (3) CRJ 651; 2002 CRLJ 2017; 2002 (2) JCC 732; 2002 (2) JhrCR(SC) 124; JT 2002 (3) SC 365; 2002 (2) RCR(Criminal) 275; 2002(3) SCALE 51; 2002 (2) SLT 647; 2002 (2) Supreme 484; 2002 (1) UC 612

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: The High Court, dealing with a proceeding under Section 145 Cr. P.C., is mainly concerned with possession of the property in dispute on the date of the preliminary order and dispossession, if any, within two months prior to that date and is not required to decide either title to the property or right of possession of the same.
