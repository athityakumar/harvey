A.V.G.P. Chettiar and Sons and Others v T. Palanisamy Gounder

Supreme Court of India

08 May 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

Ruma Pal, R.C. Lahoti

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 319; (2002) 5 SCC 337; AIR 2002 SC 2171; 2002 (4) ALD(SC) 53; JT 2002 (4) SC 572; 2002 (3) MLJ(SC) 24; 2002 (1) RCR(Rent) 574; 2002(4) SCALE 479; 2002 (3) SLT 709; 2002 (4) Supreme 134

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Trusts & Associations

Summary: Rent Control - Tamil Nadu Buildings (Lease and Control Act) Act, 1960 - Held, when, there was a bona fide dispute as to title raised by the appellants, then, the Rent Controller did not have jurisdiction to hear and finally adjudicate upon the application filed by the respondent before it.
