Kapil Kumar v Kudrat Ali and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 April 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

P. Venkatarama Reddi, D.P. Mohapatra

Where Reported

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation

Summary: Carriers & Transportation - Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 136 - Adequacy of compensation awarded by High Court to appellant, a minor who was injured in a bus accident challenged - Held, taking into consideration earning capacity on account of permanent partial disability and serious repercussions on studies, compensation of appellant increased by Rs. 20,000 to award made by HC - Appeal partly allowed.
