Karnataka State Road Transport Corporation v Ashrafulla Khan and Others

Supreme Court of India

14 January 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

V.N. Khare, Ashok Bhan

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 21; (2002) 2 SCC 560; AIR 2002 SC 629; AIR 2002 SCW 231; 2002 (46) ALR 492; 2002 (1) JhrCR(SC) 497; JT 2002 (1) SC 113; 2002 (1) RCR(Civil) 768; 2002(1) SCALE 138; 2002 (1) SCJ 272; [2002] 1 S.C.R. 194; 2002 (1) SLT 193; 2002 (1) Supreme 113; 2002 (1) UC 275

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Constitution

Summary: Under Chapter IVA of Motor Vehicles Act, 1939, since repealed, if the vehicle is to ply on the same line of travel on a notified route it is an 'overlapping' and if a non-notified route cuts across a notified route for its onward journey it is an 'intersection'.
