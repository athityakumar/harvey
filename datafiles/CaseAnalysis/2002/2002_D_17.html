Jayanti Roy v Dass Estates Private Limited

Supreme Court of India

30 April 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

D.M. Dharmadhikari, D.P. Mohapatra

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 276; (2002) 5 SCC 175; AIR 2002 SC 2394; 2002 (2) JhrCR(SC) 161; JT 2002 (4) SC 487; 2002 (3) RCR(Civil) 659; 2002 (1) RCR(Rent) 622; 2002(4) SCALE 275; 2002 (4) SLT 4; 2002 (4) Supreme 205

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: When, there was no material inconsistency between the original averments in the application and those proposed by amendment, then, the trial court as well as the high court should not have refused amendments to pleadings which were moved at the proper stage and were not unduly delayed.
