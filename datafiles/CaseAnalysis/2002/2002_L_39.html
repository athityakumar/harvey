Greater Cochin Development Authority v Leelamma Valson and Others

Supreme Court of India

06 February 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

Ruma Pal, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 72; (2002) 2 SCC 573; AIR 2002 SC 952; AIR 2002 SCW 650; 2002 (2) ALD(SC) 53; 2002 (47) ALR 682; 2002 (46) ARBLR 348; 2002 (5) AWC 4263; 2002 (1) CRC 501; JT 2002 (2) SC 5; 2002 (2) MLJ(SC) 113; 2002 (132) PLR 226; 2002 (1) RAJ 340; 2002(2) SCALE 32; 2002 (1) SLT 706; 2002 (1) Supreme 549; 2002 (1) UC 455

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Code of Civil Procedure, 1908-Section 11-Res-judicata-Decree in terms of arbitration award granted-Review application claiming 18% future interest dismissed-Appellate court having set aside interest pendente lite-Parties and courts having interpreted that the award was for interest up-to-date of decree only-Issue of future interest once raised and decided-Subsequently such question is barred by res-judicata.
