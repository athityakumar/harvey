Ratan Lal Jain and others v Uma Shankar Vyas and others

Supreme Court of India

30 January 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, Brijesh Kumar

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 66; (2002) 2 SCC 656; AIR 2002 SC 804; AIR 2002 SCW 461; 2002 (46) ALR 758; 2002 (1) AWC 692; 2002 (2) JhrCR(SC) 73; JT 2002 (1) SC 472; 2002 (130) PLR 752; 2002 (1) RCR(Rent) 168; 2002(1) SCALE 501; 2002 (1) SCJ 572; [2002] 1 S.C.R. 750; 2002 (1) SLT 585; 2002 (1) Supreme 413; 2002 (1) UC 513B

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Code of Civil Procedure, 1908-Section 47 and Order XXI, Rules 35 and 36-Transfer of Property Act, 1882-Section 108 (h)-Decree for delivery of possession-Shops constructed by lessee on land leased for 51 years-105 shops let out to different tenants-28 tenants sub-leasing shops in favour of others-Some sub-lessees further inducting their own tenants-Lessor committing default in payment of premium for more than six months-Suit for recovery of arrears and eviction of lessor and tenants-Impleadment of sub-tenants and tenants of sub-tenants rejected by trial court-Suit decreed for possession from defendants and symbolic possession from sub-tenants-Whether sub-tenants and tenants of sub-tenants can be ejected in execution of decree?-Held, “no”-Sub-tenants not bound by decree.
