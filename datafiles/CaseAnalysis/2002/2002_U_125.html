Magan Mehrotra and others v Union of India and others

Supreme Court of India

17 December 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

G.B. Pattanaik, K.G. Balakrishnan, S.B. Sinha

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 2388; (2003) 11 SCC 186

Case Digest

Subject: Education

Summary: Education - Practice & Procedure - Stipulation in Bulletin - Legality - Petitioner filed instant writ petition challenging stipulation in Bulletin of Information by Delhi University for year 2001-02 - Bulletin stipulated that candidates passed MBBS examination in any university other than Delhi University having been allotted the same under 15% quota by Director General, Health Services would also be eligible if he/she was a permanent resident of Delhi - Held, principles evolved and ratio given in Pradeep Jain case 1984 Indlaw SC 72 so far as it related to admission into postgraduate courses and question of institutional preference to be given to those who had studied their undergraduate courses in very institution as against 15% quota on all- India basis - Impugned Bulletin of Information issued by Delhi University in relation to postdoctoral (DM/MCh) postgraduate degree must be held to be contrary to direction of SC in Pradeep Jain case 1984 Indlaw SC 72 and same was accordingly quashed - This order should be effective from next academic session - SC directed States of Assam, Tamil Nadu, Goa and Karnataka to follow pattern of institutional preference as indicated by SC in Pradeep Jain case 1984 Indlaw SC 72 - Petitions disposed of.
