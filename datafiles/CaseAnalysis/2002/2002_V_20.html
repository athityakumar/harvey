Nagpur Improvement Trust and others v Vasantrao and others

Supreme Court of India

26 September 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Singh, Shivaraj V. Patil

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 1152; (2002) 7 SCC 657; AIR 2002 SC 3499; 2003 (1) AWC 161; 2003 (3) Bom.C.R. 167; JT 2002 (7) SC 447; 2003 (1) MLJ(SC) 22; 2002 (4) RCR(Civil) 551; 2002(7) SCALE 307; 2002 (4) SCJ 466; 2002 (5) SLT 487; [2002] Supp2 S.C.R. 636; 2002 (7) Supreme 15

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Land Acquisition (Amendment and validation) Act, 1967

Summary: Land Acquisition Act-S 6, 23(2) and 28 - stand legislatively incorporated in the State Acts, namely, The Punjab Town Improvement Act, 1922 -- the Nagpur Improvement Trust Act, 1936 and The Uttar Pradesh Avas Evam Vikas Parishad Adhiniyam, 1965. Further held, the claimants are entitled to the benefits conferred by Section 23(1-A), if applicable, and Section 23(2) and 28 of the Land Acquisition Act as amended by the 1984 Act for acquisition of land for the purposes of the State Acts under Sections 59 of both the Nagpur and the Punjab Acts.
