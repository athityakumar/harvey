State of Andhra Pradesh and Another v Rashtriya Ispat Nigam Limited

Supreme Court of India

06 March 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

Shivaraj V. Patil, B.P. Singh

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 1687; (2002) 3 SCC 314; AIR 2002 SC 1305; 2002 (2) ALD(SC) 124; JT 2002 (2) SC 493; 2002(2) SCALE 466; 2002 (2) SLT 376; 2002 (126) STC 114; 2013 (31) S.T.R. 513; 2002 (2) Supreme 221

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - Andhra Pradesh General Sales Tax Act, 1957, s. 5-E - Whether respondent was liable to sales tax u/s. 5-E of Act on ground that there was transfer of right to use machinery by respondent in favour of contractors? - On a careful reading and analysis of various clauses contained in agreement it becomes clear that transaction did not involve transfer of right to use machinery in favour of contractors - Transfer of right to use machinery, hire charges collected by respondent from contractors were not eligible to sales tax - Held, respondent not liable to sales tax - Appeal dismissed.
