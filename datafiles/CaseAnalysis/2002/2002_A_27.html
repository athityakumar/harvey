Abdul Rasak and Others v Kerala Water Authority and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 January 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, Brijesh Kumar

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 68; (2002) 3 SCC 228; AIR 2002 SC 817; 2002 (1) AWC 706; 2002 (1) CRC 378; JT 2002 (1) SC 652; 2002 (2) MLJ(SC) 48; 2002(1) SCALE 538; 2002 (1) SCJ 475; [2002] 1 S.C.R. 615; 2002 (1) SLT 631; 2002 (1) Supreme 442; 2002 (2) UPLBEC 1601

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Land Acquisition Act, 1894-Section 50-Compensation proceed-ings for land acquisition-Whether local authority/beneficiary of land acquired entitled to notice of pro-ceedings and to be impleaded as party in those proceedings?-Held, “yes”-Constitution Bench judgment in Gyan Devi, 1995 (2) SCC 326, so declaring-But law so declared by Constitution Bench not to have effect of reopening matters already con-cluded finally-Appeal in High Court against enhancement of compensa-tion by reference court pending on 20.10.1994, date of Constitution Bench judgment-Hence, matter not finally concluded-High Court justi-fied in holding that award given by reference court vitiated for want of notice to Kerala Water Authority.
