Collector Of Customs, Indore v Shree Synthetics

Supreme Court of India

20 March 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, N. Santosh Hegde, Arijit Pasayat

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 1334; (2002) 10 SCC 737; 2002 (142) E.L.T. 277; 2002 (6) Supreme 382

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Customs - Customs Act, 1962, s. 27 - Unjust enrichment - Captive consumption - Judgment in Solar Pesticides Pvt Ltd does not require reconsideration in the light of judgment in Bhadrachalam Paperboards Ltd v Govt. of A P [1999 (106)E L T 290 (S C )] - Latter judgment only comments upon the High Court drawing a presumption that the assessee must be deemed to have passed on the liability of the tax to the consumer - Whether the burden of duty was passed on by the assessee, not examined by the Tribunal? - Matter remanded to the Tribunal to consider the case in the light of the decision in Solar Pesticides Pvt Ltd [2000 Indlaw SC 249] - Order accordingly
