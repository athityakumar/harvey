Koluthara Exports Limited v State of Kerala and Others

Supreme Court of India

01 February 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

S.S.M. Quadri, U.C. Banerjee, S.N. Variava, Shivaraj V. Patil

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 84; (2002) SCC (L&S) 304; (2002) 2 SCC 459; AIR 2002 SC 973; 2002 (2) JhrCR(SC) 66; JT 2002 (1) SC 457; 2002 (1) KLT 658; 2002(1) SCALE 552; [2002] 1 S.C.R. 775; 2002 (1) SLT 643; 2002 (1) Supreme 478

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Social Security, Beedi and Cigar Workers (Conditions of Employment) Act, 1966, Kerala Fishermen's Welfare Fund Act, 1985, Kerala Fishermen Welfare Societies Act, 1980

Summary: The State cannot, in an Act under Entry 23 of List III, place the burden of an impost by way of contribution for giving effect to the Kerala Fishermen's Welfare Fund Act, 1985 and the scheme made thereunder for the social security and social welfare of a section of society, upon a person who is not a member of such section of society nor an employer of a person who is a member of such section of society.
