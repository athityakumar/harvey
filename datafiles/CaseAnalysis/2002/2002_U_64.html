E. Parmasivan and Others v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

14 February 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

Brijesh Kumar, D.P. Mohapatra

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 2105; (2003) 12 SCC 270; 2002 (3) CLR 188; 2002 (94) FLR 939; JT 2002 (5) SC 367; 2002 (4) LLN 1; 2002 (5) SLR 307

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Limitation - Claim of retired officers of military engineering services - Fixation of pay on promotion in terms of concordance table - Tribunal rejected contention raised on behalf of petitioners that grievance made by them in case is continuing and cause of action for application is a continuing cause of action - Anomaly in scale of pay of petitioners arose as early as on 12-01-1976 - Claim for pay fixation in terms of concordance table was not made till 1995 - Held, Tribunal right in holding that original application filed by petitioners was barred by limitation and hence no relief as claimed by them could be granted to them - SLP dismissed.
