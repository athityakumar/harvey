Sinkhai Synthetics & Chemicals Pvt. Ltd v Commissioner of Central Excise, Aurangabad

Supreme Court of India

16 April 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, N. Santosh Hegde, Shivaraj V. Patil

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 1350; (2002) 9 SCC 416; 2002 (83) ECC 5; 2002 (103) ECR 797; 2002 (143) E.L.T. 17; JT 2002 (Supp1) SC 443; 2002 (51) RLT 257

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excise Act, 1944, s. 11 B - Whether appellant entitled to refund in light of amendment in provisions of s. 11 B in 1991? - Ratio of Mafatlal case, 1996 Indlaw SC 1515 is applicable - Held, repayments shall be made within twelve weeks - Appeals allowed.
