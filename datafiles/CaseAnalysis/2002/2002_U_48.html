N. Parameswaran Pillai v Union of India and Another

Supreme Court of India

12 April 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

R.P. Sethi, D.M. Dharmadhikari

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 1972; (2002) 4 SCC 306; (2002) SCC (Cr) 796; 2002 (2) ACC 64; AIR 2002 SC 1834; 2002 (5) ALD(SC) 122; 2002 (47) ALR 545; 2002 (2) AWC 1305; 2002 (2) JhrCR(SC) 119; JT 2002 (4) SC 22; 2002(3) SCALE 518; 2002 (3) SLT 161; 2002 (3) Supreme 563

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation

Summary: Insurance - Railways Act, 1989 - Constitution of India, 1950 art. 136 - Compensation - Train accident - Occurred on 17-07-1997 - Claim for compensation - High Court refused to enhance compensation for death and denied benefit of amendment to Railways Accidents and Untoward Incidents (Compensation) Rules, 1990 as amended in 1997 relying upon judgment in UOI v. Thankaraj - Appeal - Held, claim of appellants is squarely covered by judgment of Supreme Court in Rathi Menon's case, 2001 Indlaw SC 20238, wherein it was appeal allowed.
