Babu Ram and Another v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

01 August 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, Brijesh Kumar

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 1634; (2002) 6 SCC 518; (2002) SCC (Cr) 1400; AIR 2002 SC 2815; 2002 (3) CCR 92; 2002 (3) Crimes 93; 2002 (6) CRJ 101; 2002 CRLJ 3745; 2002 (2) JCC 1342; JT 2002 (5) SC 542; 2002 (3) RCR(Criminal) 875; 2002(5) SCALE 421; 2002 (4) SLT 585; [2002] Supp1 S.C.R. 366; 2002 (5) Supreme 236

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Ballistics

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 302, 25,34, 147,148,149 and 428 - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, s. 428 - Murder - Conviction - Sustainability - Appellants/ accused persons allegedly murdered deceased by firing gun - Respondent/State approached Trial Court - Which convicted some accused u/ss. 148 and 302 r/w s.149 IPC ,some u/ss. 147 and 302 r/w s. 149 IPC - Aggrieved appellants/accused persons filed appeal before HC - HC had completely exonerated and acquitted 1 accused person and conviction of all other accused persons referable to ss. 147, 148 and 302/149 IPC had been set aside and convicted them u/s. 302 r/w s. 34 IPC - Hence, instant appeals - Whether lower Court's order was sustainable - Held, criminal act mentioned in s. 34 IPC was result of concerted action of more than one person, if said result was reached in furtherance of common intention, each person was liable for result as if he had done it himself - In one appeal filed by 2 appellants/accused was dismissed and upheld Trial Court's and HC order - In other appeal filed by appellants/accused persons conviction u/s. 302 r/w s. 34 IPC and sentence of imprisonment for life passed on each of them was set aside - Instead they were held guilty of offence punishable u/s. 325 r/w s. 34 IPC -Each one of accused was sentenced to undergo rigorous imprisonment for period of three years and also to pay fine of Rs. 1,000 and in default of payment of fine to undergo simple imprisonment for three months each - Therefore, 2 appellants were directed to be released on bail and should surrender to their bail - Appeal disposed of.
