Union of India and others v Pesticides Manufacturing and Formulators

Supreme Court of India

23 October 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

Ruma Pal, S.S.M. Quadri

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 1178; (2002) 8 SCC 410; AIR 2003 SC 1; 2002 (100) DLT 323; 2002 (84) ECC 242; 2002 (105) ECR 558; 2002 (146) E.L.T. 19; JT 2002 (8) SC 378; 2002 (53) RLT 7; 2002(7) SCALE 442; 2002 (3) SCJ 682; 2002 (6) SLT 175; [2002] Supp3 S.C.R. 231; 2002 (7) Supreme 374

Case Digest

Subject: Excise

Keywords: Finance Bill, 1996

Summary: Central Excise Tariff Act, 1985 - Chapter 28 and 29 - contention that TGP, Insecticides etc. are classifiable under Tariff Heading 38.08 and not under Chapter 28 and 29 - preparations with insecticidal, fungicidal properties are classifiable under Heading 38.08 - whether the 1996 and 1997 amendments to Chapter 38 serve to remove bulk TGP, insecticides etc. from that Chapter and place them in Chapter 28 or 29? - Held, the Chapter headings, the Notes and the Tariff Headings within Chapter 28 and 29 have not been changed or amended in their application to separate chemically defined compounds by the 1996 or 1997 amendments for the period in question. Therefore, generally speaking all separate chemically defined compounds are classifiable under Chapter 28 and 29 depending on their composition.
