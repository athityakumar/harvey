Commissioner of Income Tax v Hindustan Bulk Carriers

Supreme Court of India

08 October 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, B.P. Singh

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 1144; (2002) 7 SCC 705; AIR 2002 SC 3491; [2002] 258 ITR 399; 2002(7) SCALE 360; 2002 (6) SLT 26; 2002 (7) Supreme 104

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax Act - Determination of the starting point with regard to payment of interest arising from the order of the Settlement Commission. Held- Case involves the interpretation of the judgment of this Court in the case of CIT v. Express Newspaper Ltd. (1994 2 SCC 374) which is a judgment of a Bench of 3 Judges- matter to be heard with Mumbai etc. v. Damani Brothers etc. (2002 3 SCC 124)
