Wadi v Amilal and Others

Supreme Court of India

07 December 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

S.S.M. Quadri, S.N. Variava

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 2192; 2002 (49) ALR 54; 2002 (5) AWC 4133; 2002 (2) CRC 461; JT 2002 (6) SC 16; 2002 RD 869

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Family & Personal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Land & Property - Practice & Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 41, r. 27 - Hindu Succession Act, 1956 - Whether board of revenue was justified in declining to admit a certified copy of mutation No. 49, D/- 11-06-1961 filed as additional evidence u/O. 41, r. 27, CPC? - Document in question would throw light on germane issue and is, therefore, necessary for pronouncing judgment in case on question whether remand of case was justified - Held, board of revenue ought to have admitted additional evidence under O. 41, r. 27(1)(b) - Appeal allowed.
