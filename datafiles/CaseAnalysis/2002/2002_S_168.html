National Insurance Company Limited v Sky Gems

Supreme Court of India

09 January 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

K.G. Balakrishnan, D.P. Mohapatra

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 15; (2002) 2 SCC 273; AIR 2002 SC 545; 2002 (46) ALR 401; [2002] 110 Comp Cas 1; 2002 (1) CompLJ 246; 2002 (1) CPJ 1; 2002 (1) CPJ(Sc) 1; 2002 (1) CPR 42; 2002 (1) JhrCR(SC) 457; JT 2002 (1) SC 70; 2002 (1) MLJ(SC) 177; 2002 (130) PLR 562; 2002 (1) RCR(Civil) 731; 2002(1) SCALE 94; 2002 (1) SCJ 227; [2002] 1 S.C.R. 118; 2002 (1) SLT 127; 2002 (1) Supreme 33; 2002 (1) UC 314

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection

Summary: Consumer Protection - Claim policy amount - Respondent-Sky Gems exported two parcels of precious stones (Emerald) to London through the Foreign Post Office - However, the consignment did not reach the consignee and was believed to have been either stolen or lost in transit - Postal authorities admitted their liability and made payment - In respect of the two policies obtained from the appellant-insurance company, respondent preferred a claim and the appellant agreed to settle the same for Rs. 28, 30, 000 - Respondent claimed from the appellant an amount of Pounds Sterling 1, 07, 175.60 and insisted that the payments be made in Pounds - Respondent filed a petition before the National Commission - National Commission held that appellant was liable to pay to the respondent in Pounds Sterling - Hence, instant appeal.

Held, the right of the buyer to claim policy amount would arise when he obtained title to the property and he must produce the documents of transfer. Here, the buyer was not in possession of any such documents of title. The insurable interest over the goods continued to be with the respondent. Under such circumstances, the respondent is not entitled to receive the payment in Pounds Sterling. Further, the respondent has paid the insurance premium in India currency and continued to have title over the goods as it never passed to the consignee. Having regard to the facts and circumstances of the case, appellant is not liable to pay the insurance amount in Pounds Sterling. Appeals disposed of.

Ratio - Right of the buyer to claim policy amount would arise when he obtained title to the property and he must produce the documents of transfer
