Municipal Corporation of Greater Mumbai v K.V.Shramik Sangh & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

12 March 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

Shivaraj V. Patil, D.P. Mohapatra

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 239; (2002) 4 SCC 609; AIR 2002 SC 1815; AIR 2002 SCW 1788; 2003 (1) Bom.C.R. 206; 2002 (2) CLR 299; 2002 (93) FLR 838; JT 2002 (4) SC 115; 2002 LabIC 1672; 2002 (2) LLJ 544; 2002(3) SCALE 532; 2002 (3) SCJ 124; [2002] 2 S.C.R. 1122; 2002 (3) SLR 417; 2002 (3) SLT 469; 2002 (4) Supreme 169; 2002 (2) UPLBEC 1687

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Contarct Labour

Summary: As laid down in the Constitution Bench judgment in Steel Authority of India Ltd. & Anr. vs. National Union Waterfront Workers & Ors, absorption of contract labourers cannot be automatic and it is not for the court to give such direction under Section 10 of Contract Labour (Regulation & Abolition) Act, 1970.
