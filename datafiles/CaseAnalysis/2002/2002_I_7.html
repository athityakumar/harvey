Nutan Kumar and others v IInd Additional District Judge and others

Supreme Court of India

27 September 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Variava, Brijesh Kumar

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 1155; (2002) 8 SCC 31; AIR 2002 SC 3456; AIR 2002 SCW 4040; 2002 (2) AllCJ 1262; 2002 (49) ALR 251; 2003 (1) AWC 213; 2002 (2) CRC 693; JT 2002 (7) SC 481; 2002 LCD 1368; 2002 (2) RCR(Rent) 429; 2002(7) SCALE 81; 2002 (4) SCJ 496; 2002 (5) SLT 475; [2002] Supp2 S.C.R. 686; 2002 (7) Supreme 8; 2003 (1) UC 49

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Keywords: Uttar Pradesh Urban Building (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972, Central Provinces and Berar Letting of Houses and Rent Control Order, 1949, Bombay Hotel and Lodging Houses Rates Control Act, 1947, Companies Act, 1946

Summary: Uttar Pradesh Urban Buildings (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972 -S, 11, 12, 13, 17, 31- Issue - Whether agreement of lease between the landlord and the tenant for letting and occupation of a building in contravention of the provisions of the 1972 Act, 1972 void and enforceable. Held, Unless the statute specifically provides that a contract contrary to the provisions of the statute would be void the contract would remain binding between the parties and could be enforced between the parties themselves. Affirmed Nanakram vs. Kundairai, (1986) 3 SCC 83
