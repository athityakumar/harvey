Shambhu Murari Sinha v Project and Development India Limited and Another

Supreme Court of India

13 March 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Phukan, P. Venkatarama Reddi

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 160; (2002) 3 SCC 437; (2002) SCC (L&S) 444; AIR 2002 SC 1341; 2002 (2) AWC 1177; 2002 (1) CLR 1044; 2002 (94) FLR 229; 2002 (2) JhrCR(SC) 93; JT 2002 (3) SC 37; 2002 LabIC 1093; 2002 (2) LLJ 430; 2002(2) SCALE 587; 2002 (2) SCJ 409; [2002] 2 S.C.R. 396; 2002 (3) SLR 359; 2002 (2) SLT 491; 2002 (2) Supreme 391; 2002 (2) UPLBEC 1174

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Employment - Voluntary retirement-Appellant applying for voluntary retirement on 18.10.1995-Acceptance by management on 30.7.1997 with condition that 'release memo along with details/particulars will follow-Appellant withdrawing his option on 7.8.1997-Respondent company issuing memorandum on 25.9.1997 releasing appellant from service w.e.f. 26.9.1997 (afternoon)-Whether appellant could validly withdraw his proposal for voluntary retirement on 7.8.1997?-Held, “yes”-Because jural relationship of employee and employer between appellant and respondent did not come to end on date of acceptance of voluntary retirement, i.e., 30.7.1997-Respondent could not have refused to accept withdrawal of voluntary retirement of appellant-Appellant entitled to rejoin with all consequential benefits.
