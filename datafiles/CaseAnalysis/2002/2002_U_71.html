Metal Forgings and Another v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

22 November 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, B.P. Singh

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 1412; (2003) 2 SCC 36; AIR 2003 SC 291; 2003 (2) CalLT(SC) 56; 2002 (84) ECC 705; 2003 (108) ECR 490; 2002 (146) E.L.T. 241; JT 2002 (9) SC 582; 2002 (53) RLT 507; 2002(8) SCALE 531B; 2002 (7) SLT 59; 2002 (8) Supreme 316

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Central Excises & Salt Act, 1944 - First Schedule - Tariff Item 68 - (a) Do products manufactured by the appellants fall under Tariff Item 68? (b) Whether the demand of the revenue is barred by limitation? - held, in the absence of a show cause notice it is not open to the revenue to make a demand on the appellants even assuming that the contention of the revenue in regard to classification as held by the tribunal is correct - unnecessary to go into the first question as to the correctness of the classification made by the tribunal in the impugned order.
