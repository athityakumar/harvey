Om Prakash Gupta v Ranbir B. Goyal

Supreme Court of India

18 January 2002

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, Brijesh Kumar

Where Reported

2002 Indlaw SC 34; (2002) 2 SCC 256; AIR 2002 SC 665; 2002 (2) AllCJ 936; 2002 (47) ALR 203; 2002 (1) JhrCR(SC) 388; JT 2002 (1) SC 254; 2002 (1) LCD 612; 2002 (130) PLR 799; 2002 (1) RCR(Rent) 150; 2002(1) SCALE 227; 2002 (1) SCJ 395; [2002] 1 S.C.R. 359; 2002 (1) SLT 341; 2002 (1) Supreme 202

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property

Summary: The tenant cannot claim for relief for setting aside the decree for eviction if, he is placing reliance on an event happening after the institution of suit, i.e. a subsequent event, and cannot be taken cognizance of unless brought to the notice of the Court in accordance with established rules of procedure.
