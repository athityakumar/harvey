Anant Sakharam Raut And Ors. v State Of Maharashtra And Anr. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

14 November 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

V. Khalid, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 222; (1986) SCC (Cr) 535; (1986) 4 SCC 771; AIR 1987 SC 137; 1987 (1) Bom.C.R. 260; 1986 (88) BomLR 661; 1986 (3) Crimes 584; 1987 CRLJ 323; 1986 CrLR(SC) 549; JT 1986 SC 847; 1986(2) SCALE 796; [1987] 1 S.C.R. 221

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Defence & Security Forces; Service

Keywords: Defence Regulations

Summary: Criminal - National Security Act, 1980, s. 3(2) - Petitioner challenging against the Judgment of the Bombay High Court rejecting his plea to quash the order of detention - Petitioner was an undertrial prisioner and his detention was based on certain incidents in which the criminal cases were pending - Detaining authority not made aware of that detenu was enlarged on bail - Held, detention order void as there was total absence of application of mind on part of detaining authority - Appeal allowed.
