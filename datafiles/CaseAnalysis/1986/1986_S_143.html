State Of U.P. Etc. v Smt. Pista Devi And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

12 September 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, V. Khalid

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 236; (1986) 4 SCC 251; AIR 1986 SC 2025; 1986 (12) AWC 1027; 1986 (2) CCC 916; JT 1986 SC 420; 1986(2) SCALE 423; [1986] 3 S.C.R. 743

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Land and Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 4, 6, 5A, 17A, 17(1), 17(1A) and 23 - Delhi Development Act, 1957, s. 21(2) - UP Urban Planning and Development Act, 1973 - Land acquisition for planned development of urban area - Notification u/s. 4 - Invocation of urgency clause u/s.17(1) - (A) State Govt. dispensed with enquiry but no mala fides - Whether delay of one year between publication of notification and declaration u/s. 6 render decision of State Govt. taken u/s.17(1) and (4), improper or illegal - (B) Some buildings on such land - Non mention of s.17(1A) in notification, if fatal - (C) Acquisition by MDA - Providing of house or shop sites to persons from whom land was acquired - Applicability of principles of Delhi Development Act - Held, not by itself sufficient to render decision improper or illegal - Mere non mention of s.17(1A) in notification not fatal - Principles u/s. 21(2) of 1957 Act , though not in terms applicable should be followed - Appeal allowed.
