Aundal Ammal v Sadasivan Pillai

Supreme Court of India

09 December 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 605; (1987) 1 SCC 183; AIR 1987 SC 203; 1987 (1) CCC 216; JT 1986 SC 1028; 1987 (1) KLT 53; 1987 (1) RCJ 62; 1987 (1) RCR(Rent) 276; 1987 (1) RentLR 105; 1986(2) SCALE 1004; [1987] 1 S.C.R. 485; 1987 (1) UJ 79

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure; Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Kerala Buildings (Lease and Rent Control) Act, 1965, ss. 11,18 and 20 -CPC, 1908, s.115 - Constitution, arts.136 and 227 - Practice and procedure - Revision - Order of appellate authority u/s. 18(5) is final except as provided u/s. 20 of Kerala Act - Order of District Judge in revision u/s. 20 of the Act - Revision to HC u/s.115 of C.P.C. is barred but still entertained - Appeal to SC u/art.136 of the Constitution, against order of HC - Held, case cannot be remitted to HC as petition u/art. 227 - Since procedure under the two provisions are different , vesting jurisdiction in HC would be contrary to provisions of Act and public policy - Appeal allowed.
