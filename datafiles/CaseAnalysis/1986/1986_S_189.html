 Haji Usmanbhai Hasanbhai Qureshi and Others v State of Gujarat

Supreme Court of India

17 April 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

R.B. Misra, E.S. Venkataramiah, V. Balakrishna Eradi, V. Khalid, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 299; (1986) 3 SCC 12; AIR 1986 SC 1213; 1986 CRLR 370; 1986 CrLR(SC) 370; 1986(1) SCALE 537; [1986] 2 S.C.R. 719

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Environment, Wildlife & Animal

Keywords: Bombay Animal Preservation Act, 1954

Summary: Environment, Wildlife & Animal - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 19(1)(g) and 14 - Constitutionality of Bombay Animal Preservation Act, 1954 - Considering validity of provisions - (A) Ban of slaughter of bulls and bullocks below 16 years by cls.(c) and (d) of s. 5(1-A) - (B) No restriction of age in respect of buffaloes and other animals - If the provision discriminatory - Held, reasonable restriction and is not violative of art. 19(1)(g) - Provision is not discriminatory - Appeal dismissed.
