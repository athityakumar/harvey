G.K. Dudani and Others Etc v S.D. Sharma and Others

Supreme Court of India

06 April 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

D.P. Madon, R.S. Pathak, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 646; (1986) Supp SCC 239; (1986) SCC (L&S) 622; AIR 1986 SC 1455; 1986 (1) ATC 241; 1986 LabIC 1454; 1986 (2) LLN 766; 1986(1) SCALE 1374; [1986] 2 S.C.R. 250; 1986 (2) SLR 636

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Service

Keywords: All India Services, Bombay Civil Services Classification And Recruitment Rules, Bombay Civil Service Rules

Summary: Constitution - Service - Constitution, art. 311 - Fixation of seniority - Promotion of Mamlatdars to posts of Deputy Collectors in 1960-62 - Vacancies in permanent posts and certain number of temporary additional posts in the class of Deputy Collectors - Posts not fortuitous - Initially created for period of one year but were renewed from year to year from 1960 - Held, the appellants would be entitled to have their seniority fixed vis-à-vis direct recruits on the basis of principle of continuous officiation and would take precedence over direct recruits who have been appointed subsequently - Appeal No. 2359/80 partly allowed; Appeal No. 1816/80 allowed.
