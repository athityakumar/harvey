Commissioner Of Sales Tax, U.P. v Auriaya Chamber Of Commerce, Allahabad.

Supreme Court of India

10 April 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, K.N. Singh

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 144; (1986) 3 SCC 50; (1986) SCC (Tax) 449; AIR 1986 SC 1556; 1986 (2) CCC 706; 1986 (2) ECR 1; 1986 (25) E.L.T. 867; [1987] 167 ITR 458; 1986(1) SCALE 1068; [1986] 2 S.C.R. 430; 1986 (62) STC 327; 1986 TAXLR 2396

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Keywords: Sales Tax Authorities, Collected, Madras General Sales Tax Act, 1939, Uttar Pradesh Sales Tax (Amendment) Act (7 of 1959)

Summary: Sales Tax - Uttar Pradesh Sales Tax Act, 1948, s. 29 - Limitation Act, 1908, s. 5 and art. 96 - Whether application for refund of sales tax on forward contracts illegally recovered on mistake of law is barred by limitation - Held, it was not beyond time - Appeals dismissed.
