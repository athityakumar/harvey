Punjab State Electricity Board, Patiala and Another v Ravinder Kumar Sharma and Ors.

Supreme Court of India

27 October 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

B.C. Ray, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 212; (1986) 4 SCC 617; (1987) SCC (L&S) 13; AIR 1987 SC 367; 1986 (53) FLR 732; JT 1986 SC 743; 1986 LabIC 2076; 1987 (1) LLJ 115; 1987 (1) LLN 23; 1986(2) SCALE 690; [1987] 1 S.C.R. 72; 1987 (1) SLJ 128; 1986 (3) SLR 778; 1987 (1) UJ 273

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 - Promotion or post of line superintendent - Appellant, a lineman was non-diploma holder - Board fixed quota for both types of linemen, diploma as well as non-diploma holder - Diploma holders though junior were promoted - Appellant was left behind - Held, promotion was arbitrary - Petition dismissed.
