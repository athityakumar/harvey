Jarnail Singh And Ors. Etc. v State Of Punjab And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

07 May 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

B.C. Ray, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 316; (1986) 3 SCC 277; (1986) SCC (L&S) 524; AIR 1986 SC 1626; 1986 ATC 208; 1986 (53) FLR 265; 1986 LabIC 1086; 1986 (2) LLJ 268; 1986 (2) LLN 364; 1986(1) SCALE 1009; [1986] 2 S.C.R. 1022; 1986 (2) SLJ 157; 1986 (2) SLR 278; 1986 (2) UJ 235

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Constitution - Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 14, 16, 309 and 311 - Termination from service - Petitioners' appointment on ad- hoc basis - Govt. issued circular for regularisation of services - Termination of services of petitioners on expiry of term on basis of adverse remarks and allegations of embezzlement - Held, on facts termination is by way of punishment and orders are illegal for non-compliance of art. 311(2) - Appeal allowed.
