Punjab National Bank v Arjun Dev Arora and Others

Supreme Court of India

07 November 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, V. Khalid

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 446; (1986) 4 SCC 660; AIR 1987 SC 148; JT 1986 SC 784; 1986(2) SCALE 736

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Rent Control - Delhi Rent Control Act - Eviction - Tenant taking premises for residential purpose but using it for non-residential use - Landlord seeking eviction on ground of change of user of premises - Want of higher rent was real motive for asking eviction - Held, Supreme Court suggested fixation of higher monthly rental by taking into account location of property etc - Also directed controller to fix quantum of penalty for wrongful user of premises.
