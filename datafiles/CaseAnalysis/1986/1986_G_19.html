Vrindavan v Goverdhan Lal Pittie

Supreme Court of India

29 April 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

G.L. Oza, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 307; (1986) Supp SCC 308; AIR 1986 SC 1668; 1986 (2) CCC 528; 1986 (58) CTR 46; [1986] 160 ITR 318; 1986(1) SCALE 961; [1986] 2 S.C.R. 851; 1986 TAXLR 852; [1986] 26 TAXMAN 129; 1986 (2) TLR 852; 1986 (2) UJ 113

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Finance Act, 1969, Wealth Tax Act 1969

Summary: Constitution - Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Wealth Tax Act, 1957, s.18(1) - Delay in filing return - Imposition of penalty of 1/2% of total assessed wealth for each month's delay - Not confiscatory - Constitutionality - Held, arts. 19(1)(f) and 14 of the Constitution not contravened - Petition Dismissed.
