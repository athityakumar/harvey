Doburg Lager Breweries Pvt. Ltd v Dhariwal Bottle Trading Co. And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

14 March 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, M.P. Thakkar

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 278; (1986) 2 SCC 382; AIR 1986 SC 1547; 1986 (3) Bom.C.R. 392; [1986] 60 Comp Cas 1028; 1986 (2) CompLJ 119; 1986(1) SCALE 388; [1986] 1 S.C.R. 841; 1986 (1) UJ 566

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Banking and Finance - Corporate - Bombay Relief Undertakings (Special Provisions) Act, 1958, s. 3- Loan to relief undertaking - Industrial undertaking declared as Relief undertaking u/s 3. of 1958 Act - State Govt. granted loan it - Whether loan in question should have been advanced under the Act in order to attract the provisions of ss.3 and 4 of the Act - Held, loan need not be under the Act - Appeal allowed.
