Reserve Bank Of India And Ors. v C.N. Sahasranaman And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

30 April 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 309; (1986) Supp SCC 143; (1986) SCC (L&S) 547; AIR 1986 SC 1830; 1986 LabIC 1630; 1986 (2) LLJ 316; 1986 (2) LLN 778; 1986(1) SCALE 939; [1986] 2 S.C.R. 881

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Constitutionality, Reserve Bank of India (Staff) regulations 1948

Summary: Service - RBI - Constitution, arts.14 and 16 - RBI's Administrative circulars No. 8 and 9, D/- 13-05-1972 - Whether part of the scheme mentioned before introduced by the Reserve Bank of India is violative of guarantee of equality before law and of equal opportunity in public employment as enshrined in art.14 and 16 of the Constitution - Held, violative of arts. 14 and 16 on ground that seniority of employees on all India basis is not considered - Petition allowed.
