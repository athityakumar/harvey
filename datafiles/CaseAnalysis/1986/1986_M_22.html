Union Of India And Ors v M/S Modi Rubber Limited

Supreme Court of India

18 August 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, V. Khalid, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 186; (1986) 4 SCC 66; AIR 1986 SC 1992; 1986 (2) CCC 863; 1986 (30) DLT 445; 1986 (10) ECC 73; 1986 (2) ECR 723; 1986 (25) E.L.T. 849; JT 1986 SC 178; 1986(2) SCALE 269; [1986] 3 S.C.R. 587; 1986 TAXLR 2096; 1986 (2) UJ 646

Case Digest

Subject: Excise

Keywords: Auxiliary Duty, Finance Act, 1979, Finance Act, 1978, Central Excise Rules 1944 (contd), Central Excise Laws (Amendment and Validation) Act,1982

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, s. 3 - Finance Act, 1979, s.32 - Central Excise Rules, 1944, rr. 8(1) and 2(v) - Additional Duties of Excise (Goods of Special Importance) Act, 1957, s.3 - Question of the construction of the expression 'duty of excise' employed in two Notifications issued by the Government of India under sub-r. (1) of r. 8 of the Central Excise Rules 1944, one bearing No. 123/74-C.E. dated Ist August 1974 and the other bearing No. 27/81-C.E. dated Ist March 1981 - Availability of exemption - Held, covers only excise duty levied under the 1944 Act - And not special duty of excise under various Finance Bills and Acts or additional duty under 1957 Act - Exemption in notifications is in respect of excise duty - Appeal allowed.
