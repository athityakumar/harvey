Bharat Singh v Management of New Delhi Tuberculosis Centre

Supreme Court of India

04 April 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

V. Khalid, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 287; (1986) SCC (L&S) 335; (1986) 2 SCC 614; AIR 1986 SC 842; 1986 (52) FLR 621; 1986 LabIC 850; 1986 (2) LLJ 217; 1986 (2) LLN 4; 1986(1) SCALE 637; [1986] 2 S.C.R. 169; 1986 (3) SLJ 63; 1986 (2) SLR 58

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: National Tribunal, Industrial Disputes (Amendment) Act, 1982

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 17B - s. 17-B was inserted in I.D.A, 1947 via amendment in 1982 - Notified in Official Gazette on the 21st of August, 1984 - Whether Section 17-B applies to awards passed prior to 21st day of August, 1984 - Held, s. 17 B is applicable to even awards passed prior to 21-8-1984, a date appointed by the Central Govt. it they have not become final - Appeal Allowed.
