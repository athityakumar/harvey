Vice-Chancellor, Lalit Narain Mithila University v Dayanand Jha

Supreme Court of India

25 April 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, E.S. Venkataramiah, B.C. Ray

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 815; (1986) 3 SCC 7; (1986) SCC (L&S) 378; AIR 1986 SC 1200; 1986 BLJR 587; 1986 (2) SLJ 142; 1986 (2) SLR 657

Case Digest

Subject: Education; Service

Summary: Education - Service - Bihar State Universities Act, 1976, s. 10(14) - Whether Vice-Chancellor of University transfer any teacher of any department or college maintained by University from post of Principal of a constituent college to post of a Reader of another constituent college maintained by it? - Post of Reader could not be regarded as an equivalent post as that of Principal in legal sense - Held, when affairs of a college maintained by University are mismanaged, Vice-Chancellor may, for administrative reasons, transfer a Professor or Reader of any department or college maintained by it to post of Principal of such college, but converse may not be true - Post of Principal must be treated as equivalent to that of a Reader for purposes of s. 10(14) as amended - Appeal dismissed.
