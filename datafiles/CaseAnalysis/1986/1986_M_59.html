Provident Fund Inspector, Faridabad v Messrs Jaipur Textile, Faridabad and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 December 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 705; (1986) Supp SCC 678; (1987) SCC (L&S) 72; AIR 1987 SC 1738; 1987 LabIC 1597; 1987 (2) LLN 860; 1987(1) SCALE 61

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Employees Provident Funds and Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1952, s.14A - Constitution of India, 1950, art.141- Prosecution for non payment of arrears of provident fund - Supreme Court directed withdrawal of prosecution against employers who have paid all arrears or would pay within stipulated time - However, order would not serve as precedent - Petition disposed of.
