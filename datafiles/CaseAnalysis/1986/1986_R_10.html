K.R. Mudgal And Ors. v R.P. Singh And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

30 September 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 498; (1986) 4 SCC 531; (1987) SCC (L&S) 6; AIR 1986 SC 2086; JT 1986 SC 597; 1986 LabIC 1932; 1987 (1) LLJ 214; 1987 (1) LLN 19; 1986(2) SCALE 561; [1986] 3 S.C.R. 993; 1987 (1) SLJ 221; 1986 (3) SLR 752; 1987 (1) UJ 223

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 and 311 - Seniority between Assts. in Intelligence Bureau - Petition challenging inter se seniority filed 18 years after first seniority list was issued - Held, petition deserves to be dismissed on ground of laches alone - Order accordingly.
