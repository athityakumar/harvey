Madras Marine and Company v State of Madras

Supreme Court of India

16 July 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 4; (1986) 3 SCC 552; AIR 1986 SC 1760; 1986 (9) ECR 165; 1992 (61) E.L.T. 161; 1986(2) SCALE 126; [1986] 3 S.C.R. 236; 1986 (63) STC 169

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax; Sales Tax

Keywords: Warehouse, Stores, Transfer Of Documents Of Title

Summary: Customs Act, 1962, ss. 59 and 60 - Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax Act, 1959 - Territorial Waters, Continental Shelf, Exclusive Economic Zones Act, 1976, s. 5 - (A) Whether sales in question were within State of TN and as such subject to tax under Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax Act, 1959? - Sale is made in case of ascertained goods at time when contract of sale was made and in case of unascertained goods at time of their appropriation to contract by seller - Held, sale must be deemed to be within State of TN - Appeal dismissed.
