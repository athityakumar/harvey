Dudh Nath Pandey (Dead) By LRs v Suresh Chandra Bhattasali (Dead) By LRs

Supreme Court of India

24 April 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

R.B. Misra, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 754; (1986) 3 SCC 360; AIR 1986 SC 1509; 1986(1) SCALE 1259; 1987 (11) ShimLC 73; 1986 (2) UJ 527

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, ss. 100, O. 12, r. 1 - (A) Scope of second appeal - Finding on question of limitation recorded by first Appellate Court on appraisal of evidence after taking into consideration entire circumstances in case - Set aside by High Court - Legality - Held, it was a finding of fact which could not have been set aside by HC in exercise of power u/s. 100 of CPC - Further held, it cannot be interfered with - (B) Evidential value of admission by defendant in written statement - Permissibility to rely on part of admission - Further held, it is not permissible to rely on part of admission ignoring other - Appeal allowed.
