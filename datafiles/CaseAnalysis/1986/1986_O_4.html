Bal Kishan v Om Prakash And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

26 August 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, V. Khalid

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 189; (1986) 4 SCC 155; AIR 1986 SC 1952; JT 1986 SC 253; 1986 (2) RCR(Rent) 551; 1986 (2) RentLR 445; 1986(2) SCALE 347; [1986] 3 S.C.R. 622; 1986 (2) UJ 701

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Rent Control - Haryana Urban (Control of Rent and Eviction) Act, 1973, ss. 13(2)(ii)(a) and 2(h) - CPC 1908, O. 22, r. 2(2) - Eviction of tenant sought on groun of subletting without the written consent of landlord - LRs of tenants brought on record - Validity of order of eviction of LRs of tenant and sub tenant - Held, was proper - Appeal dismissed.
