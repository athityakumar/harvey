Hindustan Tea Company v K. Sashikant Company, and Another

Supreme Court of India

13 November 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Natarajan

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 801; (1986) Supp SCC 506; AIR 1987 SC 81; 1986 (12) ALR 692; JT 1986 SC 818; 1986(2) SCALE 756; 1987 (1) UJ 247

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Contract & Commercial

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration Act, 1940 - Whether award cannot be set aside on ground that arbitrator reached wrong conclusion or he failed to appreciate facts? - Under Law, arbitrator is made final arbiter of dispute between parties - Held, award cannot be set aside on ground that arbitrator reached wrong conclusion or he failed to appreciate facts.
