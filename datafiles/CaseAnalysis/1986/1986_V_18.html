J. P. Sharma v Vinod Kumar Jain and Others

Supreme Court of India

08 April 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 56; (1986) 3 SCC 67; (1986) SCC (Cr) 216; AIR 1986 SC 833; 1987 (1) CLR 1; 1986 (2) CompLJ 1; 1986 (2) Crimes 1; 1986 CRLJ 917; 1986 CrLR(SC) 244; 1986 (30) DLT 313; 1986(1) SCALE 859; [1986] 2 S.C.R. 388

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Corporate; Criminal

Keywords: Cognizable offence

Summary: Criminal - Crimianl Procedure Code, 1973, s. 482 - Complaint - Offences u/s. 120 B of IPC, 1860 and, 5 of Import, Export, (Control) Act, 1947 - Cognizance - Summons issued - Allegations if considered to be true making out prima facie case for trial - High Court quashing order u/s. 482 - Legality - Held, is illegal - Appeal allowed.
