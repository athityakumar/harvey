Messrs General Radio and Appliances Company v M.A. Khader (Dead) By Lrs.

Supreme Court of India

17 April 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

B.C. Ray, K.N. Singh, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 756; (1986) 2 SCC 656; AIR 1986 SC 1218; [1986] 60 Comp Cas 1013; 1986 (2) CompLJ 249; 1986 (2) RCJ 413; 1986 (2) RCR(Rent) 283; 1986 (2) RentLR 252; 1986(1) SCALE 595; [1986] 2 S.C.R. 607; 1986 (2) UJ 655

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Corporate; Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Corporate - Andhra Pradesh Buildings (Lease, Rent and Eviction) Control Act, 1960, s.10(ii)(a) - Subletting - Eviction of tenant - Whether the voluntary amalgamation of the companies amounts to a transfer of the first appellant's right under the lease within the meaning of s.10(ii)(a) of the Act - Consent of landlord for subletting as per rent agreement not obtained - Transfer held to be hit by the provision of s. 10(ii)(a) - Appeal dismissed.
