Om Parkash Agarwal Etc.Etc v Giri Raj Kishori And Ors. Etc.Etc.

Supreme Court of India

28 January 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 333; (1986) 1 SCC 722; AIR 1986 SC 726; [1987] 164 ITR 376; 1986(1) SCALE 110; [1986] 1 S.C.R. 149; 1986 (1) UJ 394

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Land & Property

Keywords: Legislative Powers

Summary: Constitution - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 265, sch. 7, List 2, entry 28 - Constitutional validity of Haryana Rural Development Fund Act, 1983 - Constitutional validity of 1983 Act challenged - Act levies cess u/s. 3, which is not fee as quid pro quo is absent and also cess cannot also be levied as tax - Held, s. 3 is liable to be quashed, other provisions being only machinery provisions, whole Act quashed - Appeals allowed.
