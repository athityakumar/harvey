Rattan Arya Etc. Etc. v State Of Tamil Nadu And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

16 April 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, B.C. Ray, K.N. Singh

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 417; (1986) 3 SCC 385; AIR 1986 SC 1444; 1986 ALJ 1168; 1986 (2) CCC 563; 1987 (1) MLJ(SC) 20; 1986 (2) RCR(Rent) 328; 1986 (2) RentLR 217; 1986(1) SCALE 993; [1986] 2 S.C.R. 596; 1986 (2) UJ 530

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Rent Control - Constitution, art. 14 - TN Buildings (Lease and Rent Control) Act, 1960, s. 30(ii) - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 57 - (A) Classification in s. 30(ii) of 1960 Act of tenants of residential buildings fetching rent of over Rs. 400/- per month - Constitutionality of depriving them of benefits of Act wthout any basis - (B) Evidence Act - Judicial notice of increase in rent - Held, s. 30(ii) is violative of art. 14 - Court can take judicial notice of enormous manifold increase of rents throughout the country, particularly in urban area - Appeal allowed.
