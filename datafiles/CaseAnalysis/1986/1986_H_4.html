H. Shiva Rao and Another v Cecilia Pereira and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 November 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, K.N. Singh

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 803; (1987) 1 SCC 258; AIR 1987 SC 248

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Rent Control

Summary: Land & Property - Rent Control - Karnataka Rent Control Act, 1961, s. 21(1) - Whether in view of s. 21(1) of Act decree was executable, because subsequent to decree for Possession Act has been made applicable to area in question? - Held, Courts must find out literal meaning of expression in task of construction - In doing so if expressions are ambiguous then construction that fulfills object of legislation must provide key to meaning - Sub-section does not prevent execution of order after coming into operation of Act, of any order or decree passed before coming into operation of Act - It only prevented passing of any order or decree for eviction after coming into operation of Act except on specified grounds mentioned in proviso to sub-s. (1) of s. 21 of Act - Judgment set aside - Appeal allowed.
