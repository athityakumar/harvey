Patnaik and Company Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax, Orissa

Supreme Court of India

16 July 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 17; (1986) 4 SCC 16; AIR 1986 SC 1483; 1986 (58) CTR 92; 1987 (10) ECR 425; 1986 (26) E.L.T. 673; [1986] 161 ITR 365; JT 1986 SC 202; 1986(2) SCALE 12; [1986] 3 S.C.R. 207; 1986 TAXLR 1043; [1986] 27 TAXMAN 287; 1986 (2) TLR 1043

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Indirect Tax

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 255 and 45 - (A) Whether High Court can interfere with findings of fact arrived at by Appellate Tribunal? - (B) Whether loss on sale of Govt. bonds purchased for increasing business with Govt. is capital loss or revenue loss? - Held, cannot be disturbed by High Court - It is revenue loss - Appeal allowed.
