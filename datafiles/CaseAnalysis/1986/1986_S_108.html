Ambica Quarry Works & Anr v State Of Gujarat & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

11 December 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, K.N. Singh

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 602; (1987) 3 SCC 213; AIR 1987 SC 1073; 1987 (2) CCC 394; JT 1986 SC 1036; 1986(2) SCALE 1037; [1987] 1 S.C.R. 562; 1987 (1) UJ 174

Case Digest

Subject: Environment, Wildlife & Animal

Summary: Environment, Wildlife and Animal - Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980, s.2 - (A) Whether rejection of application for renewal of mining lease after Forest (Conservation) Act come into force is valid - Held, it is in conformity with purpose of the Act of preventing deforestation and ecological imbalances resulting from deforestation - Appeal dismissed.
