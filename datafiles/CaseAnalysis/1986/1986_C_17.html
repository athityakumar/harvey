Centax (India) Limited v Vinmar Impex Inc. and Others

Supreme Court of India

19 August 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, B.C. Ray

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 389; (1986) 4 SCC 136; AIR 1986 SC 1924; [1987] 61 Comp Cas 697; 1999 (109) E.L.T. 28; JT 1986 SC 175; 1986(2) SCALE 254; 1986 (2) UJ 559

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Contract & Commercial - Contract Act, 1872, ss. 125 and 126 - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 39, r. 1 - Contract for sale of goods between Indian buyer and foreign firm - Enforcement of bank guarantee - Seller failed to forward original bills of lading - Delivery by Shipping Company without production of original documents - Basis, unconditional wording of letters of indemnity by purchaser's bankers and counter-signed by purchaser - Who selling goods but not paying anything for - Whether bank can be restrained from honouring guarantee? - Held, cannot be restrained - Appeal dismissed.
