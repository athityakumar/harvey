Ganpat Giri v Iind Additional District Judge,Balia And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

07 January 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, R.B. Misra

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 313; (1986) 1 SCC 615; AIR 1986 SC 589; 1986 ALJ 271; 1986 (12) ALR 165; 1986 (12) AWC 181; 1990 (2) MLJ(SC) 22; 1986(1) SCALE 21; [1986] 1 S.C.R. 15; 1986 (1) UJ 287

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Civil Procedure Code, 1908, ss. 97 and 21, rr. 72 and 72 A - Interpretation of Statutes - Repeal and Saving - Code amended - Addition of r. 72 A in O. 21 - Retaining sub-rr. (2) and (3) of r. 72 - Overriding effect of s. 97, CPC (Amendment) Act, 1976 - Fact that no amendment was made to r. 72 - Held, r. 72 does not remain in force in view of addition - Fact irrelevant - Appeal allowed.
