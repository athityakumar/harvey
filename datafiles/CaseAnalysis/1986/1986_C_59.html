Commissioner Of Income-Tax, Delhi v Mahalaxmi Sugar Mills Co. Ltd.

Supreme Court of India

15 July 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 164; (1986) 3 SCC 544; AIR 1986 SC 2111; 1986 (58) CTR 138; [1986] 160 ITR 920; JT 1986 SC 225; 1986(2) SCALE 166; [1986] 3 S.C.R. 150; [1986] 27 TAXMAN 267

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922, ss. 24, 49AA - Agreement for Avoidance of Double Taxation of Income chargeable in India and Pakistan, 1947, arts. IV to VII - (A) Whether dividend received from Pakistan Company is dedutable in determining total world loss u/s. 24(1) - (B) Assessee not claiming benefit of set off u/s. 24 while computation of total Income - Held, deductible - It is duty of ITO to apply s. 24 in appropriate cases - Appeal allowed.
