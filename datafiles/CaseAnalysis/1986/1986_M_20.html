Bajrang Gopilal Gajabi v M.N. Balkundri And Ors

Supreme Court of India

15 July 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

V. Balakrishna Eradi, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 169; (1986) 3 SCC 424; AIR 1986 SC 1752; 1986 CRLR 377; 1986 CrLR(SC) 377; 1986 (10) ECC 160; 1986 (8) ECR 690; 1986 (25) E.L.T. 609; JT 1986 SC 242; 1986(2) SCALE 72; [1986] 3 S.C.R. 181; 1986 TAXLR 2095

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal; Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, sch.I, item 19 - Levy of excise Duty - Sole proprietor manufacturing cloth - Fact that powerloom owners manufacturing cloth for and on behalf of proprietor - From yarn supplied on his behalf - Cloth could be said to be manufactured by Proprietor - Validity levy on him -Held, not invalid - Appeal dismissed
