S. Govindaraju v K.S.R.T.C. And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

15 April 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

K.N. Singh, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 291; (1986) 3 SCC 273; (1986) SCC (L&S) 520; AIR 1986 SC 1680; 1986 (53) FLR 59; 1986 LabIC 1191; 1986 (2) LLJ 351; 1986 (2) LLN 355; 1986(1) SCALE 794; [1986] 2 S.C.R. 509; 1986 (1) SLJ 470; 1986 (1) SLR 749; 1986 (2) UJ 542

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation

Keywords: Industrial Disputes (Amendment) Act, 1984, Karnataka Road Transport Corporation (Cadre and Recruitment) Regulations 1982

Summary: Carriers and Transportation - Road Transport Corporations Act, 1950, s. 45(2)(c) - Karnataka Road Transport Corporation (Cadre and Recruitment) Regulations, 1982, Regn.10(5) - Constitution, art. 226 - Service Rules - Select List for recruitment of labour (Temporary/Badli appointment ) - As per Regulations, right to be considered for appointment, as and when vacancy arises - If termination without oppertunity of being heard is justified - Held, unjustified - Appeal allowed.
