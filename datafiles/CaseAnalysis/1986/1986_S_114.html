T.R. Kapur And Ors. v State Of Haryana And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

17 December 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, S. Natarajan

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 242; (1986) Supp SCC 584; AIR 1987 SC 415; 1987 (2) ATC 595; 1987 (1) CCC 206; 1987 (55) FLR 306; JT 1986 SC 1092; 1987 LabIC 238; 1987 (2) LLJ 25; 1987 (1) LLN 437; 1986(2) SCALE 1051; [1987] 1 S.C.R. 584; 1986 (4) SLR 155

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Punjab Reorganisation Act, 1966, Punjab Re-organisation Act, 1966, Punjab Service of Engineers Class I, PWD (Irrigation Branch) Rules 1964 (as amended by State of Haryana by notification dated 22 June 1984)

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, arts.14 and 16(1) - Punjab Re-organisation Act, 1966 - Punjab Service of Engineers Class I, PWD (Irrigation Branch) Rules 1964 (as amended by State of Haryana by notification D/- 22-06-1984), r.6(b) - States Reorganisation Act, 1956 - Promotion - Post of Executive Engineer in Class I Service - Engineering degree made essential for promotion rendering members of Class II service which were Diploma holder as ineligible - Validity of a notification issued u/art. 309 by the State Govt. purporting to amend r.6(b) of 1964 rules with retrospective effect as violative of arts. 14 and 16(1) of the Constitution and also ultra vires the State Govt. by reason of the proviso to s.82(6) of the Punjab Reorganisation Act, 1966 - Held, notification declared to be ultra vires the State Govt. - Petition allowed.
