Harminder Singh Arora v Union Of India And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

09 May 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

R.B. Misra, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 745; (1986) 3 SCC 247; AIR 1986 SC 1527; 1986 (3) Bom.C.R. 193; 1986 (2) CCC 545; 1986(1) SCALE 1242; [1986] 3 S.C.R. 63; 1986 (2) UJ 1593

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Prohibition Act

Summary: Contract & Commercial - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 - Tenders invited for contract with Govt. for supply of fresh milk as per of tender notice - Tender of Govt. Milks Scheme for supply of pasteurised milk accepted, though appellant's tender was lowest - Held, illegal - Appeal allowed.
