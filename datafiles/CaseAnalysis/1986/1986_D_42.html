 Dr. D.C. Wadhwa And Ors. v State Of Bihar And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

20 December 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, Ranganath Misra, G.L. Oza, M.M. Dutt, K.N. Singh

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 247; (1987) 1 SCC 378; AIR 1987 SC 579; JT 1987 (3) SC 70; 1986(2) SCALE 1171; [1987] 1 S.C.R. 798

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice and Procedure - Constitution, arts. 32 and 213 - Locus standi - Ordinance - Re-promulgated time to time without getting them replaced by Acts - Challenged by petitioner - Held, practice is flagrant violation of constitutional provisions and petitioner being professor of political science who has done enormous research has locus standi to challenge the practice, as member of public - Petition allowed.
