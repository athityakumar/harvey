Atul Khullar And Ors. Etc. Etc. v State Of J And K And Ors. Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

15 April 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, R.B. Misra, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 294; (1986) Supp SCC 225; (1986) SCC (L&S) 608; AIR 1986 SC 1224; 1986 (2) CCC 721; 1986(1) SCALE 1329; [1986] 2 S.C.R. 560; 1986 (2) UJ 421

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution

Keywords: Jammu and Kashmir Government Medical Colleges (Selection of Candidates For Admission to the First year MBBS Course) Procedure Order 1983

Summary: Constitution, art.32 - (A) Admission to Medical Colleges - (1) Reservation of seats for candidates from backward areas - Validity of addition of villages to list of such areas - (2) Validity of certificates of tahsildar's produced by the candidates - (3) What are the minimum marks for candidates from general and reserved categories - (4) Selection of candidates - Validity of allocating 15 points for viva voce test - (B) Admission to Engineering Colleges - Selection of candidates - Viva voce test - Behaviour, physical bearing and oral expression of candidate are relevant - Need for records - Held, is valid - Enquiry into by Court - Prescription of minimum percentage of marks - Held, is not invalid - Selection Committee must maintain record and should not destroy immediately - Petition dismissed.
