Pakirbhai Fuiabhai Soinnki v Presiding Officer & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

08 May 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 594; (1986) 3 SCC 131; (1986) SCC (L&S) 411; AIR 1986 SC 1168; 1986 (2) CCC 72; 1986 (52) FLR 688; JT 1986 SC 394; 1986 LabIC 879; 1986 (2) LLJ 124; 1986 (2) LLN 74; 1986(1) SCALE 1191; [1986] 2 S.C.R. 1059; 1986 (2) SLJ 150; 1986 (2) UJ 101

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 33(3) - Issue regarding payment of subsistence allowance on dismissal of protected workman - Workman suspended while application by management for permission was before Tribunal and no allowance was paid - Permission granted after 6 years but opportunity to defend - Validity - Held, order of dismissal violates principles of natural justice - Appeal allowed.
