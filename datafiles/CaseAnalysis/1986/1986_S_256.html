Sita Ram Somani v State of Rajasthan and Others

Supreme Court of India

11 February 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, V. Khalid

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 58; (1986) 2 SCC 86; (1986) SCC (Cr) 104; AIR 1986 SC 1072; 1986 ALJ 916; 1986 CRLJ 860

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Criminal; Fema; Socio-Economic

Summary: Criminal -Conservation of Foreign Exchange and Prevention of Smuggling Activities Act, 1974, s. 3(1) -Preventive detention - Detaining authority passed detention order during pendency of criminal case under Act - Relevant materials not placed before detaining authority - (A)Whether detention was legal in view of relevant material not being placed before detaining authority ? - Held,since relevant material was not placed before detaining authority it was not considered by him - Thus, there was non-application of mind by him to relevant material and therefore detention was illegal - (B)Whether detaining authority has jurisdiction to make an order in pendency of criminal case against accused? - Held, detaining authority had jurisdiction to make order of detention despite retraction by accused of his earlier confessional statement and pendency of criminal case against him in which bail had been granted subject to conditions -No one can dispute right of detaining authority to make an order of detention if on a consideration of relevant material - Appeal allowed.
