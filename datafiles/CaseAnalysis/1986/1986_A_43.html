Anwar v Ist Additional District Judge,Bbulandshahr And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

11 August 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 215; (1986) 4 SCC 21; AIR 1986 SC 1785; 1986 (12) AWC 1021; 1986 (2) CCC 730; JT 1986 SC 111; 1986(2) SCALE 249; [1986] 3 S.C.R. 540; 1986 (2) UJ 718

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Civil Procedure

Summary: Carriers and Transportation - Motor Vehicles Act, 1939, ss.68C, 68D - CPC, 1908, s. 9 - Whether a suit is maintainable in a civil court for an injunction restraining the Hearing Authority appointed u/s. 68D of the 1939 Act from proceedings with the hearing of matters under that provision and from approving a scheme published u/s.68C of the Act either with or without any modification? - Held, not maintainable - Petition dismissed.
