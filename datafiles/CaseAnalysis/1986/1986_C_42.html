Atul Glass Industries (Pvt) Limited Etc. v Collector Of Central Excise, Etc

Supreme Court of India

10 July 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 206; (1986) 3 SCC 480; (1986) SCC (Tax) 620; AIR 1986 SC 1730; 1986 CRLR 363; 1986 (10) ECC 1; 1986 (8) ECR 513; 1986 (25) E.L.T. 473; JT 1986 SC 41; 1986(2) SCALE 15; [1986] 3 S.C.R. 126; 1986 (63) STC 322; 1986 TAXLR 2084

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Keywords: Finance Act, 1979

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, sch 1, Tariff Item No. 23 A(4), 68 - Orissa Sales Tax Act, 1947 - Excise Duty - (A) Whether glass mirrors fall under Tariff Item No. 23 A(4) or Tariff Item No. 68 of First Schedule to Central Excises and Salt Act? -Held, cannot be classified, they fall under residuary Tariff Item No. 68 - Whether glass screens fitted in motor vehicles as wind screens, rear screens and window screens fall under Tariff Item No. 23 A(4) or Tariff Item No. 34 A or Tariff Item No. 68 - Merits classification in residuary Tariff Item No. 68 - Appeal allowed.
