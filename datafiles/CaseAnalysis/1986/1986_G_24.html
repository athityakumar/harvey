Chenchu Rami Reddy and Another v Government of Andhra Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

01 April 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

M.P. Thakkar, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 414; (1986) 3 SCC 391; AIR 1986 SC 1158; 1986(1) SCALE 652; [1986] 1 S.C.R. 989

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Land & Property

Summary: Land and Property - AP Charitable and Hindu Religious Institutions and Endowments Act , 1966, s.74(1)(c) -Sale of land belonging to charitable endowment - By private negotiations - And not by public auction - Held, deprecated by Supreme Court and direction issued that property to be sold by public auction - Appeal allowed.
