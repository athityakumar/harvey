Chaganti Satyanarayan and Others v State of Andhra Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

08 May 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Natarajan, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 638; (1986) 3 SCC 141; (1986) SCC (Cr) 321; AIR 1986 SC 2130; 1986 (12) AWC 901; 1986 (2) Crimes 678; 1986 CRLR 256; 1986 CrLR(SC) 256; 1986(1) SCALE 1037; [1986] 2 S.C.R. 1128

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Criminal - CrPC, 1973, ss. 57, 167(2) Proviso (a), 309 - Commencement of period of 90/ 60 days u/s. 67(2) Proviso (a) - From date of remand or of arrest - Held, it run from date of order of remand and not from earlier date when accused was arrested - Appeal dismissed.
