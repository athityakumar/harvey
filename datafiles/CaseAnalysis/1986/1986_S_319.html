Om Prakash Rana Etc. Etc. v Swarup Singh Tomar & Ors. Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

09 May 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, P.N. Bhagwati

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 817; (1986) 3 SCC 118; (1986) SCC (L&S) 398; AIR 1986 SC 1672; 1986 (12) AWC 911; 1986 LabIC 1183; 1986 (2) LLN 464; [1986] 3 S.C.R. 1; 1986 (2) SLR 481

Case Digest

Subject: Education; Service

Keywords: Absorption Of Retrenched Employees

Summary: Service - Uttar Pradesh Secondary Education Services Commission and Selection Board Act, 1982 - Uttar Pradesh Intermediate Education Act, 1921, s. 16 G(2)(c) - Whether, in view of enactment of Uttar Pradesh Secondary Education Services Commission and Selection Boards Act, 1982 and Rules framed thereunder, provisions contained in s. 16-G(2)(c) of Uttar Pradesh Intermediate Education Act, 1921 and regns. 55 to 62 of Regulations framed under that Act in respect of transfer of a Principal from one Intermediate College to another continue to be operative and effective? - Held, constitution of a Commission under Services Commission Act is no longer possible for a vacancy in post of Principal, Headmaster or teacher of categories mentioned in Schedule to Services Commission Act to be filled by process of transfer u/s. 16-G(2)(c) of Education Act and its Regulations - As mandate imposed by s. 16(1)(a) of Services Commission Act that appointment of a Principal of an Intermediate College shall, on or after July 10, 1981 be made only on recommendation of Commission, and inasmuch as appointment by transfer of appellant as Principal of Veer Smarak Intermediate College took place after that date, appointment of appellant must be regarded as void - Appeal dismissed.
