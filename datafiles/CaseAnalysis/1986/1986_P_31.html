Dr. Ambesh Kumar Etc. Etc. v Principal, Llrm Medical Collegemeerut And Ors. Etc. Etc

Supreme Court of India

19 December 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

B.C. Ray, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 245; (1986) Supp SCC 543; AIR 1987 SC 400; JT 1986 SC 1124; 1986(2) SCALE 1211; [1987] 1 S.C.R. 661; 1987 (1) UJ 139

Case Digest

Subject: Education

Summary: Education - Constitution, 1950, art. 162, sch. 7, list 1, entry 66, list 3, entry 25 - Medical Council Act, 1956, s. 33 - State govt. laying down rules for admission to PG courses in medical colleges - The rules were in accordance with MCI - Contended that the rules were ultra vires to the constitution as thesubect lies under central list - Held, that the order of the State Govt. merely provides an additional eligibility qualification - Appeal dismissed.
