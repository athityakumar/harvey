B. Varadha Rao v State of Karnataka and Others

Supreme Court of India

26 August 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, B.C. Ray

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 585; (1986) 4 SCC 131; (1986) SCC (L&S) 750; AIR 1986 SC 1955; 1986 ATC 558; 1986 (53) FLR 505; JT 1986 SC 249; 1986 LabIC 1806; 1986 (2) LLJ 516; 1986 (2) LLN 753; 1986(2) SCALE 344; 1986 (3) SLR 60; 1986 (2) UJ 573

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Rent Contol - Karnataka Civil Services (Classification, Control and Appeal) Rules, 1957, rr. 19 and 18 - Service Rules - Transfer of Govt. servant - Transfer is an incident of service - No alteration of any condition of service to his disadvantage - Whether Order is appealable to State Govt. u/r. 19 - Held, not appealable - Peition dismissed.
