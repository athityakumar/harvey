Geep Industrial Syndicate Limited v Union of India

Supreme Court of India

02 April 1986

Case Analysis

Bench

V. Khalid, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1986 Indlaw SC 3; (2005) 3 SCC 483; 1997 (69) ECR 232; 1992 (61) E.L.T. 328; 1997 (18) RLT 671

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Whether cost of secondary packing in wooden boxes is liable to be added in determination of value of batteries and torches for purpose of excise duty? - Cardboard Cartons were placed in wooden boxes for purpose of protecting torches and cells against injury or damage during transport - Packing in wooden boxes not necessary for putting torches and batteries in condition in which they are generally sold in wholesale market at factory gate - Held, cost of wooden boxes is not liable to be included in value for levy of excise duty - Appeal allowed.
