Vijendrajit Ayodhya Prasad Goel v State of Bombay

Supreme Court of India

13 March 1953

Case Analysis

Bench

Mehr Chand Mahajan, Vivian Bose

Where Reported

1953 Indlaw SC 137; AIR 1953 SC 247; 1953 CRLJ 1097

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Procedure Code, 1898, s. 342 - Bombay Prohibition Act, 1949, s. 66(b) r/w s. 81 - Indian Evidence Act, 1872 - Constitution of India, 1950 - Whether appellant was found in possession of twenty gallons eight drums of rectified spirit on 08-06-1950? - Held, Magistrate was not justified in making use of statement of accused made u/s. 342 CrPC, 1898 for purpose of finding that fact - Further held, no force in contentions of appellant and no evidence to establish same - Appeal dismissed.
