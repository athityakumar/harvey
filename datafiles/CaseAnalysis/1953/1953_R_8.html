Ram Nath Madhoprasad and Others v State of Madhya Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

30 January 1953

Case Analysis

Bench

Mehr Chand Mahajan, Sudhi Ranjan Das, Ghulam Hasan

Where Reported

1953 Indlaw SC 140; AIR 1953 SC 420; 1953 CRLJ 1772

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 32 - Criminal Procedure Code, 1898, s. 107 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 302, 392, 120-B, 34, 109 and 114 - Appellant sentenced to death, two appellants sentenced to life imprisonment - On appeals, High Court sentenced all appellants to life imprisonment - Value of dying declaration? - Held, not safe to convict a person merely on basis of uncorroborated dying declaration as such statement not made on oath and not subject to cross-examination, HC in error in basing conviction on basis of uncorroborated dying declaration, Appellants entitled for benefit of doubt, Convictions set aside - Appeals allowed.
