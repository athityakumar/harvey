 Ram Narayan Singh v State of Delhi and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 March 1953

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Patanjali Sastri, Sudhi Ranjan Das, Ghulam Hasan, Bijan Kumar Mukherjea, Natwarlal Harilal Bhagwati

Where Reported

1953 Indlaw SC 40; AIR 1953 SC 277; 1953 CRLJ 1113; 1953 SCJ 326; [1953] 4 S.C.R. 652

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Code of Criminal Procedure 1898

Summary: Criminal - Code of Criminal Procedure 1898, s. 344 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 188 - Writ of Habeas Corpus - Detention sought to be justified on basis of two remand orders of Additional District Magistrate and trying Magistrate - Held, in habeas corpus proceedings, court is to have regard only to legality or otherwise of detention at time of return and not with reference to institution of proceedings - Petitioners entitled to be released - Petition allowed.
