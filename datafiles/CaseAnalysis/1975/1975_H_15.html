Ramesh Himmatlal Shah v Harsukh Jadhavji Joshi

Supreme Court of India

25 April 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

P.K. Goswami, A. Alagiriswami, P.N. Bhagwati

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 178; (1975) 2 SCC 105; AIR 1975 SC 1470; 1975 BomLR 549; [1975] Supp S.C.R. 270

Case Digest

Subject: Trusts & Associations

Keywords: Code of Civil Procedure 1908, Maharashtra Co-operative Society Act, 1960

Summary: Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1961, ss. 17(2), 29, 31 and 47 - Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Rules, 1961, r. 24 - CPC, 1908, s. 60 - Tenancy - Flat in a tenant Co- partnership Housing Society registered under the Act - Whether flat can be attached and sold in execution of a decree against the allottee - Flat held, can be attached and sold - Appeal Allowed.
