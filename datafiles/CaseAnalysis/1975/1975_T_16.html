State Of Andhra Pradesh & Anr v T. Gopalakrishna Murthi & Ors

Supreme Court of India

25 September 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

N.L. Untwalia, A. Alagiriswami, P.K. Goswami

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 252; (1976) SCC (L&S) 363; (1976) 2 SCC 883; AIR 1976 SC 123; 1976 LabIC 85; [1976] 1 S.C.R. 1008

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Keywords: Andhra Pradesh High Court Service Rules, 1959

Summary: Constitution, art. 229(2), Proviso - A.P. High Court Service Rules, 1959, r. 19 - Sercive Rules - Conditions of service of High Court staff - Powers of Chief Justice - Govt. refused recommendations of Chief Justice to equate pay scales of HC staff with those of equivalent posts in secretariat - HC issued mandamus for implementation of recommendations - Appeal Allowed.
