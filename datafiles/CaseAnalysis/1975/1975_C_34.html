Dollar Company, Madras v Collector Of Madras

Supreme Court of India

01 May 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, R.S. Sarkaria, A.C. Gupta

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 134; (1975) 2 SCC 730; AIR 1975 SC 1670; 1976 (1) MLJ(SC) 19; [1975] Supp S.C.R. 403; 1975 UJ 462

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 23 and 54 - Compensation - Determination of - Appeal to Supreme Court - Powers of appellate Court - Held, a court of appeal interferes not when the judgment under attack is not right but only when it is shown to be wrong - Appeal Dismissed.
