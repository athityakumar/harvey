State of Maharashtra v Sindhi Alias Raman

Supreme Court of India

19 February 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Sarkaria, V.R. Krishna Iyer

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 632; (1975) SCC (Cr) 283; (1975) 1 SCC 647; 1975 (12) ACC 179; AIR 1975 SC 1665; 1975 CRLJ 1475; 1975 CrLR(SC) 229; [1975] 3 S.C.R. 574

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: CrPC, 1898, ss. 374 and 465 - Practice and Procedure - Murder trial - Proceedings for confirmation of death sentence - Unsoundness of mind of accused - In incapacity to defend himself - Held, s. 465 applies and proceedings must be postponed under that section - Word 'trial' in s. 465 includes reference u/s. 374 - Appeal dismissed.
