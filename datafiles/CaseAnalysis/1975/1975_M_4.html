State of Tamil Nadu v M. K. Kandaswami and Others

Supreme Court of India

15 July 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Sarkaria, A.C. Gupta, V.R. Krishna Iyer

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 656; (1975) SCC (Tax) 402; (1975) 4 SCC 745; AIR 1975 SC 1871; 1975 (4) CTR 197; [1976] 1 S.C.R. 38; 1975 (36) STC 191; 1975 TAXLR 1935; 1975 UJ 521

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: T.N. General Sales Tax Act, 1959, s. 7A(1) - Scope of-Ingredients of s-s (1) - Whether goods were liable to purchase tax u/s.7-A - Purchase of arecanuts, turmeric, grams, gingelly and castor seeds, from agriculturists and butter from house-holders - Seeds crused into oil and butter converted into ghee - Transport of goods outside State for sale - Held, dealers would be liable to tax on the purchase turnover of these goods - Appeal Allowed.
