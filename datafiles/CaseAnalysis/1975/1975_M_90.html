Mangu Ram v Municipal Corporation Of Delhi

Supreme Court of India

10 October 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, R.S. Sarkaria

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 263; (1976) SCC (Cr) 10; (1976) 1 SCC 392; AIR 1976 SC 105; 1976 CRLJ 179; 1975 CrLR(SC) 653; [1976] 2 S.C.R. 260

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Code of Criminal Procedure 1898

Summary: Limitation Act, 1963, ss. 5 and 29 - CrPC, 1898, s. 417 - Applicability of s. 5 of 1908 Act to application for special leave u/s. 417(3) CrPC, 1898 - Held, mere provision of a period of limitation in s. 417(4) in howsoever peremptory or imperative language is not sufficient to displace the applicability of s. 5 - Petition Dismissed.
