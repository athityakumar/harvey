N. Lakshmana Rao & Ors. Etc. v State Of Karnataka & Ors. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

29 April 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, V.R. Krishna Iyer, A.C. Gupta

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 181; (1976) SCC (L&S) 328; (1976) 2 SCC 502; AIR 1975 SC 1646; 1975 (31) FLR 44; 1975 LabIC 1121; 1975 (2) LLJ 87; 1975 SLJ 560; 1975 (2) SLR 272; [1975] Supp S.C.R. 328

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Service - Karnataka State Civil Services (Age of compulsory Retirement) Rules, 1974, rr. 1 and 2 - Karnataka Compulsory Primary Education (Amendment And Miscellaneous Provisions) Act, 1969, s. 14(b) - Service Rules - Education - Constitutionality of rr. 1 and 2 of 1974, Rules - Retirement age of primary School teachers can be altered by Governor by rules made u/art. 309 of Constitution - Held, rules are constitutional and valid - Could not be altered - Appeal dismissed.
