Atic Industries Limited v H. H. Dave, Assistant Collector of Central Excise

Supreme Court of India

14 February 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, A.C. Gupta, N.L. Untwalia

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 670; (1975) SCC (Tax) 135; (1975) 1 SCC 499; AIR 1975 SC 960; 1975 (4) CTR 54; 1978 (2) E.L.T. 444; [1975] 3 S.C.R. 563; 1975 TAXLR 1515

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Central Excise

Summary: Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, s. 4(a) - Excise Duty - Wholesale cash price - Manufacturer selling goods in wholesale to a wholesale dealer - Value - Held, wholesale cash price charged by him less trade discount would represent value of goods for assessment of excise - Appeal Dismissed.
