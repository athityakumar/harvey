City Corner v Personal Asstt. To Collector & Addl. District, Magistrate,Ne

Supreme Court of India

29 September 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

A. Alagiriswami, P.K. Goswami, N.L. Untwalia

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 254; (1976) 1 SCC 124; AIR 1976 SC 143; [1976] 2 S.C.R. 38; 1975 UJ 835

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Places of Public Resort Act 1888

Summary: A.P. (Andhra Area) Places of Public Resort Act, 1888, s. 12 - Constitution, art. 226 -(A) Scope of revisional power u/s. 12 of the 1888 Act - (B) Order of cancellation of license - Necessity of complying with principles of natural justice - (C) Non-observance of principles of natural justice - In passing quasi-judicial order - Held, not a limited one but as wide as that of the original authority and not resticted to reason u/ss. 7 or 9 of the Act - Held, is a quasi-judicial order and the principles of natural justice to be followed before passing it - Appeal allowed though power of High Court u/art. 226 is limited to cases of error apparent on face of record - Appeal Allowed.
