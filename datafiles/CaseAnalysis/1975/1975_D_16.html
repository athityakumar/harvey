J. Fernandes and Company v Deputy Chief Controller of Imports and Exports

Supreme Court of India

07 March 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 413; (1975) 1 SCC 716; AIR 1975 SC 1208; [1975] 3 S.C.R. 867; 1975 UJ 310

Case Digest

Subject: Trade

Keywords: Import Control Policy

Summary: Trade - Constitution, arts.14, 19(1)(g), 32, 73(1)(a), 239(1), 240,46(4) - Goa, Daman and Diu (Administration) Act, 1962, ss. 4, 5 and 9 - Import Control Policy - Import Licence - Constitutionality - Ancillary questions of scope of validation, applicbility etc. - Held, found to be not unconstitutional - After liberation, recourse to the Portuguese laws for the validity of his licence can not be taken - Petition dismissed.
