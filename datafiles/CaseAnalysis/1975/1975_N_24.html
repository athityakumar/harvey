Newabganj Sugar Mills Co. Ltd. & Anr. v Union Of India And Etc

Supreme Court of India

16 September 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 245; (1976) 1 SCC 120; AIR 1976 SC 1152; [1976] 1 S.C.R. 803; 1975 UJ 824

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: CPC, 1908, s. 151 - Essential Commodities Act, 1955, s. 3(2)(c) - Indian Limitation Act, 1908 - Contract Act, 1872, s. 70 - Essential Commodities - Prices of levy sugar reduced by Govt. - Mill owners obtaining stay of Govt. order - Charging excessive price - Order of Govt. upheld by High Court - Validity - Held, consumers who have paid excess price must be repaid through court process - Order Accordingly.
