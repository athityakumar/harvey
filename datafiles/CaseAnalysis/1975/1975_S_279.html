Mohd. Dhana Ali Khan v State of West Bengal

Supreme Court of India

11 April 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, N.L. Untwalia

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 268; (1975) 2 SCC 586; (1975) SCC (Cr) 695; AIR 1976 SC 734; 1976 CRLJ 622; [1975] Supp S.C.R. 124

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Defence & Security Forces

Keywords: Defence Regulations

Summary: Criminal - Detention - Maintenance of Internal Security Act, 1971, s. 14 - Whether order of detention of appellant was justified? - Held, no - District Magistrate seems to be influenced by a history sheet of detenu - History sheet revealed that detenu was involved in four cases of theft - Magistrate considered materials which were not relevant as grounds of detention - Appeal allowed.
