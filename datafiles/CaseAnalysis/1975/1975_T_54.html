Madras Rubber Factory Ltd. v The Union Of India & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

03 December 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

N.L. Untwalia, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, P.K. Goswami

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 663; (1976) 2 SCC 255; AIR 1976 SC 638; 1976 (5) CTR 208; 1985 ECR 2298; 1983 (13) E.L.T. 1579; [1976] 2 S.C.R. 864; 1976 UJ 91

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Customs Act, 1962, s. 27(1) - Custom duty - Application for refund - Duty chargeable on V. P. Latex u/item 39 charged u/item 87 of the Tariff Act - Application beyond statutory period of six months - Dismissed not on merits but on ground of limitation -No running account under which duty was being paid - Alleged protest in paying duty proved to be no pertaining to duty in respect of which refund claimed - Application rejected - Validity - Held, application were rightly rejected - Appeal Dismissed.
