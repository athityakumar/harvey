Supreme General Films Exchange Limited v His Highness Maharaja Sir Brijnath Singhji Deo of Maihar and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 August 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Hameedullah Beg, A.C. Gupta

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 396; (1975) 2 SCC 530; AIR 1975 SC 1810; [1976] 1 S.C.R. 237; 1975 UJ 631

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Land & Property - Specific Relief Act, 1877, s. 42 - Transfer Of Property Act, 1882, s. 52 - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 64 - Theatre - Specific Relief u/s. 42 of 1877 Act - Discretion of Court as to declaratory relief - Lease pendente lite execution of attachment decree - Held, Court can grant declaration according to circumstances of case independently of requirements of s. 42 - Held, lease was struck by doctrine of lis pendens and by s. 64 CPC, 1908 - Appeal allowed.
