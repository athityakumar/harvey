Maloon Lawrence Cecil D' Souza v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

01 May 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

Hans Raj Khanna, A.N. Ray, M. Hameedullah Beg, Y.V. Chandrachud

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 132; (1976) SCC (L&S) 115; (1976) 1 SCC 599; AIR 1975 SC 1269; 1975 LabIC 816; 1975 (2) LLJ 98; 1975 SLJ 629; 1975 (2) SLR 255; [1975] Supp S.C.R. 409

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Constitution - Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 16, 32 and 309 - Maintainability - Seniority list published in 1956 - Petition filed after 15 years - Seniority list showing respondent as senior - Petitioner promoted prior to respondents - Held, that petition is not maintainable and promotion would not by itself make petitioner senior to respondents - Petition dismissed.
