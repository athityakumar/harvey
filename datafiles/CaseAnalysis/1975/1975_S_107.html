Dattatraya Narayan Patil v State Of Maharashtra

Supreme Court of India

16 April 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

N.L. Untwalia, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 169; (1975) SCC (Cr) 733; (1976) 1 SCC 11; AIR 1975 SC 1685; 1975 CRLJ 1490; 1975 CrLR(SC) 355; [1975] Supp S.C.R. 145; 1975 UJ 542

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Indirect Tax; Service; Socio-Economic

Summary: IPC, 1860, ss. 353 and 355 - CrPC, 1898, s. 251A - Public servant, If - Govt. Notification - Minister to preside over meetings of District Advisory Committee - Held, while presiding over a meeting Minister was executing or discharging his duty as a public servant - Appeal Dismissed.
