Zaffar Mohammad @ Z.M. Sarkar v The State Of West Bengal

Supreme Court of India

25 November 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, A.C. Gupta

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 284; (1976) SCC (Cr) 46; (1976) 1 SCC 428; 1976 ACJ 320; AIR 1976 SC 171; 1976 CRLJ 190; 1976 CrLR(SC) 44; [1976] 2 S.C.R. 782; 1976 UJ 21

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Health & Drug

Summary: Drugs and Magic Remedies (Objectionable Advertisements) Act, 1954, s. 2(b)(iii) - Meaning of 'Article' - Whether 'Machines of science' and 'electric treatment' advertised in newspaper to cure nervous diseases are drugs - Machines held are 'articles' within the meaning of s. 2(b)(iii) and therefore 'drug' for purpose of the Act - Appeal Dismissed.
