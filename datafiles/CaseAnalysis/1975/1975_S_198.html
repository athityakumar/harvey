Natabar Parida and Others v State of Orissa

Supreme Court of India

16 April 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

N.L. Untwalia, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 405; (1975) 2 SCC 220; (1975) SCC (Cr) 484; AIR 1975 SC 1465; 1975 CRLJ 1212; 1975 CrLR(SC) 360; [1975] Supp S.C.R. 137; 1975 UJ 553

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Practice & Procedure - Code Of Criminal Procedure, 1898, ss. 344 and 167 - CrPC, 1973, ss. 57 and 309 - Power of Magistrate to order remand - Comparison between old code and new one - Held, Court will have no power unless and until conferred by law - Assumption of High Court not correct without reference to s. 344 of old CrPC - Appeal dismissed.
