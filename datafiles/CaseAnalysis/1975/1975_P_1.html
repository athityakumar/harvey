Mahendra Mills Limited v P. B. Desai, Appellate Assistant Commissioner of

Supreme Court of India

04 March 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Sarkaria, A.C. Gupta, V.R. Krishna Iyer

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 318; (1975) 4 SCC 93; AIR 1975 SC 910; 1975 (4) CTR 82; [1975] 99 ITR 135; [1975] 3 S.C.R. 846; 1975 TAXLR 395

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 35 - Rectificatian of mistake - Closing stock of assessment year 1959-60 forms part of evidence relevant to assessment for A.Y. 1960-61 - Fact that finding of Tribunal in respect of the former year was recorded subsequent to the final appellate decision in respect of the latter year - (B) Jurisdiction u/s.35 - Duties of authorities and Tribunal while applying ratio of a decision - Held, fact was immaterial - Held, are supposed to apply the ratio of a decision, to the facts of particular cases with due care and discernment. bearing in mind the restricted score of their jurisdiction u/s. 35 and the object for which it is conferred - Appeal Dismissed.
