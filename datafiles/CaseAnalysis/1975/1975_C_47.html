J. K. Cotton Manufacturers Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax, Lucknow

Supreme Court of India

04 September 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, Hans Raj Khanna, A.C. Gupta, V.R. Krishna Iyer

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 322; (1975) 2 SCC 691; AIR 1975 SC 1945; 1975 CTR 243; [1975] 101 ITR 220; [1976] 1 S.C.R. 648; 1975 TAXLR 836

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Capital Expenditure

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 10(2)(xv) - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 37 - Termination of managing agency - Voluntary , in order to obtain enduring benefit - Claim for deduction under Conditions precedent - Disbursement of compensation - Nature of - Held, to be of capital nature and as such not deductible - Appeal Dismissed.
