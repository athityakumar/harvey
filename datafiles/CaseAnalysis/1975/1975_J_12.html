Jagir Singh and Others v State Of Bihar & Anr. Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

26 November 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, N.L. Untwalia

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 286; (1976) SCC (Tax) 204; (1976) 2 SCC 942; AIR 1976 SC 997; 1976 (5) CTR 200; [1976] 2 S.C.R. 809

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Carrier

Summary: Bihar Taxation on Passengers and Goods (Carried by Public Service Motor Vehicles) Act, 1961, s. 3 - Rajasthan Passengers And Goods Taxation Act, 1959 - Taxation - Motor vehicles - (A) Public service vehicles hired to booking or forwarding agencies by permit holders - Liability to pay tax - On whom lies - (B) Interpretation of Statutes - General rule of construction - Held, liability is of the permit holder and not hirer - Held, rule is not only to look at the words but to look at the context, the collocation and the object of such words relating to such matter and interpret the meaning according to what would appear to be the meaning intended to be conveyed by the use of the words under the circumstances - Petition Dismissed.
