Commissioner Of Sales Tax, U.P. v M/S. Sarin Textiles Mills

Supreme Court of India

21 April 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Sarkaria, A.C. Gupta

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 172; (1975) SCC (Tax) 300; (1975) 4 SCC 308; AIR 1975 SC 1262; 1975 (4) CTR 132; 1975 (35) STC 634; [1975] Supp S.C.R. 228; 1975 TAXLR 1617; 1975 UJ 401

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure; Sales Tax

Summary: U.P. Sales Tax Act, 1948 - Notifications issued u/s. 3-A of the Act - 'Woollen carpet kati' is neither 'yarn' nor 'woolen goods' - Taxability - Determination of - Held, it is unclassified item and taxable at rate of 2 per cent, u/s. 3 - Appeal Dismissed.
