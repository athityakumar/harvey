State Of Punjab & Ors. v Balbir Singh Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

06 October 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

N.L. Untwalia, A. Alagiriswami, P.K. Goswami

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 190; (1976) 3 SCC 242; (1976) SCC (L&S) 411; AIR 1977 SC 629; 1977 LabIC 281; 1976 (2) LLJ 4; [1976] 2 S.C.R. 115; 1976 SLJ 278; 1976 (1) SLR 36

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Master And Servant Relationship

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art.311 - Punjab Re-organisation Act, 1966 - Punjab Reorganisation Act, 1956 - Punjab Service of Engineer, Class II, PWD (Buildings and Roads Branch) rules, 1965 - Reversion - Validity - Whether orders of reversion were legally correct? - Held, Yes - Once an order is issued and it is sent out to concerned Govt. servant, it must be held to have been communicated to him, no matter when he actually received it - Appeal allowed.
