Tulshi Rabidas v State of West Bengal

Supreme Court of India

27 January 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, P.K. Goswami, R.S. Sarkaria

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 94; (1975) SCC (Cr) 264; (1975) 3 SCC 845; AIR 1975 SC 638; 1975 CRLJ 598; 1975 CrLR(SC) 195; [1975] 3 S.C.R. 401

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Detention - Indian Penal Code, 1860 - Ss. 224 and 225 - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 - s. 169 - Appellant appealed against his order of detention - Detention order was passed with a view to prohibit him acting in a manner prejudicial to essential supplies - Whether order of detention of appellant was liable to be set aside? - Held, no - It was established that petitioner was a person had a history about disrupting essential services - Order of detention justified - Rule discharged.
