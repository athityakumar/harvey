Bhabhi v Sheo Govind & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

21 April 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, N.L. Untwalia

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 173; (1976) 1 SCC 687; AIR 1975 SC 2117; [1975] Supp S.C.R. 202

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Representation of the People Act, 1951, ss. 92, 100(1)(d), 101 and 102 - Representation of the People Conduct of Elections Rules, 1961, r. 63 - Election - Circumstances under which order of sample inspection of ballot papers can be passed - Held, circumstances stated - Appeal Allowed.
