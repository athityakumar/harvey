State of Assam and Others v Akshaya Kumar Deb

Supreme Court of India

02 May 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Sarkaria

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 12; (1975) SCC (L&S) 295; (1975) 4 SCC 339; AIR 1976 SC 37; 1975 LabIC 1753; 1975 (2) LLJ 110; 1975 SLJ 592; 1975 (2) SLR 430; 1975 UJ 499

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Assam Fundamental and Subsidiary Rules, F.R.18 - Constitution, arts. 226 and 311(2) - Service Rules - Termination - Without opportunity to show cause - Termination under F.R. 18 if 'removal' within art. 311(2) of Constitution - Held, termination illegal - Per R.S. Sarkaria, J. termination under F.R. 18 not out of the category of 'removal' as envisaged by art. 311(2) but as per A.C. Gupta, J. it was doubtful - Appeal Dismissed.
