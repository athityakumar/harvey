Lila Ram and another v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

19 August 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

Hans Raj Khanna, V.R. Krishna Iyer, A.C. Gupta, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 395; (1975) 2 SCC 547; AIR 1975 SC 2112; [1976] 1 S.C.R. 341; 1975 UJ 697

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Delhi (Control of Building Operations) Act, 1955

Summary: Constitution, art. 133 - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 4 - Delhi (Control of Building operations) Act, 1955 - (A) New plea - Raised for the first time before Supreme Court, in an apppeal against dismissal of writ petition - (B) Public purpose - Notification for the acquisition of land for the execution of the Interim General Plan for the Greater Delhi - Held, SC cannot be allowed to agitage for the first time a question which was not raised before the HC - Not vague , notification is valid - Petition Dismissed.
