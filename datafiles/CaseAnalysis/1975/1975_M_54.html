Union of India v Messrs Chaturbhai M. Patel and Company

Supreme Court of India

09 December 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 240; (1976) SCC (Cr) 179; (1976) 1 SCC 747; AIR 1976 SC 712; 1976 (2) ALR 42; [1976] 2 S.C.R. 902; 1976 UJ 120

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution

Summary: Constitution - Carriers & Transportation - Criminal - Civil Procedure - Practice & Procedure - Railways Act, 1890, s. 73, cls. (f) - Proof with regard to fraud by consignor - Not proved beyond reasonable doubts - Held, charge of fraud, whether made in civil or criminal proceedings, must be established beyond reasonable doubt and suspicion alone can never take place of proof - Appeal dismissed.
