 High Court of Punjab and Haryana and Others v State of Haryana and Others

Supreme Court of India

24 January 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, Hans Raj Khanna, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, M. Hameedullah Beg, Y.V. Chandrachud

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 422; (1975) SCC (L&S) 229; (1975) 1 SCC 843; AIR 1975 SC 613; 1975 (1) ALR 136; 1975 LabIC 375; [1975] 3 S.C.R. 365; 1975 SLJ 189; 1975 (1) SLR 329

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Right To Promotion

Summary: Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 33 and 235 - Punjab Civil Services (Punishment and Appeal) Rules, 1952, rr. 9, 2 and 49 - Service Rules - Confirmation of appointment of District Judge - Reversion - (A) Power of reversion vests in High Court but Punjab Superior Judicial Service Rules, r. 10 confer power on Governor - (B) Reversion order passed by Governor based on enquiry without concurrence of HC - (C) Applicability of r. 9 to reversion of a probationer District Judge - Held, r. 10 is ultra vires Constitution - Order is not sustainable - R. 9, regarding show cause notice is attracted - Appeal partly allowed.
