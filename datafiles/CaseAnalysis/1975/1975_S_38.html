Sow Chandra Kanta And Another v Sheik Habib

Supreme Court of India

19 January 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, R.S. Sarkaria

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 410; AIR 1975 SC 1499

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: U.P. Consolidation of Holdings Act, 1954, ss. 4 and 5(2)(a) - Abatement of proceedings - Apeeal before Supreme Court - Meanwhile notification u/s. 4 - Review petition - Held, suit and appeal stand abated u/s .5(2)(a) of the Act - Parties may work out their rights before the appropriate consolidation authorities - Petition Dismissed.
