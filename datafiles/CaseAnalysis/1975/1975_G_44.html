Income Tax Officer, A-Ward, Indore v Gwalior Rayon Silk Manufacturing (Weaving)

Supreme Court of India

18 September 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, V.R. Krishna Iyer

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 335; (1975) 2 SCC 721; AIR 1976 SC 43; 1976 CTR 65; [1975] 101 ITR 457; [1976] 1 S.C.R. 855; 1976 TAXLR 43

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1961- Interpretation of Statutes - Interpretation of ss. 220(2) and 220(3) of the Act - Time granted at the request of assessee to pay arrears of tax by instalment - Extension of - Offer to pay 5 % interest on arrears instead of statutory rate of 4 % accepted - Rate enhanced to 6 % p.a.by amendment of s. 220(2) by Finance, Act, 1965 - Laibility of assesse - Assessee held was liable to pay higher rate of interest i.e.6 % on unpaid tax arrears from date of amendment - Appeals Allowed.
