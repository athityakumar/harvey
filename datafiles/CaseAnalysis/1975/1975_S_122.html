 State Of U.P. v Raj Narain & Ors

Supreme Court of India

24 January 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, A. Alagiriswami, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, R.S. Sarkaria, N.L. Untwalia

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 312; (1975) 4 SCC 428; AIR 1975 SC 865; [1975] 3 S.C.R. 333; 1975 (1) SLR 541

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure; Tort

Keywords: Freedom Of Speech And Expression

Summary: Evidence Act, 1872, ss. 123 and 162 - What constitutes unpublished official record - Claim of privilege - Duty of Court - Held, court will proprio motu exclude evidence the production of which is contrary to public interest - Where Court orders disclose an innocuous part, Court should seal up the other parts, which are said to be noxious because their disclosure would be undesirable - Appeal Allowed.
