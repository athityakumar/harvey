Jaila Singh & Anr. v State Of Rajasthan And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

02 May 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

A. Alagiriswami, P.N. Bhagwati, P.K. Goswami

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 138; (1976) 1 SCC 602; AIR 1975 SC 1436; [1975] Supp S.C.R. 428; 1975 UJ 475

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Keywords: Rajasthan Canal Project Areas Rules 1971

Summary: Constitution, art. 14 - Rajasthan Colonisation (Rajasthan Canal Project Pre1955 Temporary Tenants Government Land Allotment) Conditions, 1971, Condition 3 - Rajasthan Colonisation (Allotment of Government Land to Post 1955 Temporary Cultivation Lease Holders and Other Landless Persons in the Rajasthan Canal Project Area) Rules, 1971, r. 3(2) - Rajasthan Colonisation Act, 1954, ss.27, 28 - Scope (A) Constitutionality of Condition No. 3 and r. 3(2) of 1971 Conditions and Rules respectively - (B) Scope of ss. 27 and 28 of 1954 Act - Held, are ultra vires art.14 of Constitution - There is practically no guidance provided in the Act with regard to the principles to be applied in the matter of allotment of land - Appeal Allowed.
