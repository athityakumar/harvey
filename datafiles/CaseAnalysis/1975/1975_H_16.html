Municipal Committee, Amritsar v Hazara Singh

Supreme Court of India

12 March 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, A.C. Gupta, M. Hameedullah Beg, Y.V. Chandrachud

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 290; (1975) SCC (Cr) 354; (1975) 1 SCC 794; AIR 1975 SC 1087; 1975 CRLJ 928; [1975] 3 S.C.R. 914

Case Digest

Subject: Socio-Economic; Tort

Keywords: Food Adulteration

Summary: Constitution, arts. 136 and 141 - (A) Appeal against acquittal - Cases, insignificant and routine - Invocation of jurisdiction u/art. 136 of Constitution - (B) Value of obiter dictum of Supreme Court - Statements on matters other than law - Held, not proper - Statements on matters other than law (e.g. questions of fact) have no binding force - Appeal Dismissed.
