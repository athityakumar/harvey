Oil India Ltd. v The Superintendent Of Taxes & Others

Supreme Court of India

03 March 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, P.K. Goswami

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 117; (1975) SCC (Tax) 167; (1975) 1 SCC 733; AIR 1975 SC 887; 1975 CTR 62; [1975] 3 S.C.R. 797; 1975 (35) STC 445; 1975 TAXLR 1513; 1975 UJ 289

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Summary: Central Sales Tax Act, 1956, s. 3 - Inter-State sale, if - Crude oil transported through ipelines from oil fields in Assam and supplied to refinery in Bihar - Movement contemplated by parties - Held, it is a sale in the course of interstate trade - Petition Partly Allowed.
