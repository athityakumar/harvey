Rabindra Kumar Ghosel Alias Buli v State of West Bengal

Supreme Court of India

17 March 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, N.L. Untwalia

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 580; (1975) 4 SCC 111; (1975) SCC (Cr) 365; AIR 1975 SC 1408; 1975 CRLJ 1235; 1975 CRLR 288; 1975 UJ 451

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Maintenance of Internal Security Act, 1971, s. 3 - Detention - District Magistrate passing order of detention about three months after placing of the case of detention before him - No explanation for the delay - Detention is unsustainable - Petition Allowed.
