 Khemka and Company v State of Maharashtra

Supreme Court of India

27 February 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

A.N. Ray, Hans Raj Khanna, Kuttyil Kurien Mathew, M. Hameedullah Beg, Y.V. Chandrachud

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 416; (1975) 2 SCC 22; (1975) SCC (Tax) 227; AIR 1975 SC 1549; 1975 (4) CTR 106; [1975] 3 S.C.R. 753; 1975 (35) STC 571; 1975 TAXLR 1777

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure; Sales Tax

Keywords: Delegated Legislation

Summary: Bombay Sales Tax Act, 1953, s. 16(4) - Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957, s. 13 - Orissa Sales Tax Act, 1947 - Central Sales Tax Act - Default in payment of tax under the Central Act within the prescribed time - Imposition of penalty under State Sales Tax Act - Held, is not valid - Appeal No.C.A. 2118 of 1970 Dismissed; Appeal NoC.A. 2089 of 1969 Allowed.
