Hochtlef Gammon v State Of Orissa & Ors

Supreme Court of India

04 September 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

A. Alagiriswami, P.K. Goswami, N.L. Untwalia

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 387; (1975) SCC (L&S) 362; (1975) 2 SCC 649; AIR 1975 SC 2226; 1975 (31) FLR 27; 1975 LabIC 1608; 1975 (2) LLJ 418; [1976] 1 S.C.R. 667

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Labour & Industrial Laws - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 10 - Industrial Dispute - Orders of appropriate Govt. in matter of referring disputes for adjudication - Scope of court's scrutiny - Validity of rejection of application for modification of referring order - Held, Order of Govt. was unsustainable - Appeal allowed.
