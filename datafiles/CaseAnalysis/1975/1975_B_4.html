Bablu Das v State of West Bengal

Supreme Court of India

15 January 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, P.K. Goswami, R.S. Sarkaria

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 608; (1975) 4 SCC 108; (1975) SCC (Cr) 359; AIR 1975 SC 1513; 1975 CRLJ 1327; 1975 CRLR 155; 1975 UJ 234

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Constitution, art.22(5) - Maintenance of Internal Security Act, 1971, s. 8 - Detention - Grounds - Detenu was alleged to have taken part in wagon-breaking - But the history sheet before the Detaining Authority showed various other circumstances - Held, all the grounds on which the subjective satisfaction of detaining authority is based must be disclosed - Otherwise order of detention would be bad - Petition Allowed.
