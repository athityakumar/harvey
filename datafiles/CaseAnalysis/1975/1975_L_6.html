Executive Committee of Vaish Degree College v Lakshmi Narain and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 December 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, Hans Raj Khanna, P.N. Bhagwati

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 62; (1976) 2 SCC 58; (1976) SCC (L&S) 176; AIR 1976 SC 888; 1976 LabIC 576; 1976 (2) LLJ 163; 1976 (1) LLN 474; [1976] 2 S.C.R. 1006; 1976 (1) SLR 213

Case Digest

Subject: Insurance; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Agra University Hand-Book, 1965-66, Statute 14A - Road Transport Corporation Act, 1950 - Specific Relief Act, 1963, ss. 10, 14, 34 and 36 - Constitution, art.12 - (A) What constitute statutory body - Whether Executive Committee of a Degree College, registered under Registration of Co -operative Societies Act and affiliated to Universityis a statutory body - (B) Enforceability of contract of personal service - Grant of relief of deduction and injunction - Duty of Court while exercise of discretion - Held, not a statutory body merely because it is affiliated to the University or is regulated by the provisions of the University Act or the statutes made thereunder - Court must keep in mind the well settled principles of justice and fair play and should exercise the discretion only if the ends of justice require it, for justice is not an object which can be administered in vacuum - Appeal Allowed.
