Magan Gope v State of West Bengal

Supreme Court of India

12 February 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Sarkaria, V.R. Krishna Iyer

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 634; (1975) SCC (Cr) 191; (1975) 1 SCC 415; AIR 1975 SC 953; 1975 CRLJ 791; [1975] 3 S.C.R. 531

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Foreign Power

Summary: Maintenance of Internal Security Act, 1971, s. 3(1)(a)(ii) - Preventive detention - Grounds for - One of two grounds referring to smuggling activity of detenu having no nexus with public order - Order held, is invalid - Validity or otherwise of other ground immaterial - Petition allowed.
