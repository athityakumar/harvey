T. R. Sharma v Prithvi Singh & Anr. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

17 November 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

Hans Raj Khanna, P.N. Bhagwati, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 274; (1976) SCC (L&S) 1; (1976) 1 SCC 226; AIR 1976 SC 367; 1976 LabIC 294; [1976] 2 S.C.R. 716; 1976 SLJ 97; 1976 (1) SLR 55; 1976 UJ 11

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Service - Punjab Civil Services Rules, 1959, Vol. I, Part I, rr. 3.12, 3.14(a)(2) and 3.15 - Service Rules - Acquisition and suspension of lien of a Govt. servant - Scope of Rules - Held, as Governor had deconfirmed him from post of Block Development and Panchayat Officer, suspended lien on post of Agricultural Inspector would stand revived from specific date - Promotion by impugned order cannot be held to suffer from any legal infirmity - Appeal allowed.
