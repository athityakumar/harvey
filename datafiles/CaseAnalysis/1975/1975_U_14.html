Healthways Dairy Products Co v Union Of India

Supreme Court of India

06 October 1975

Case Analysis

Bench

N.L. Untwalia, A. Alagiriswami, P.K. Goswami

Where Reported

1975 Indlaw SC 673; (1976) 2 SCC 887; AIR 1976 SC 2221; 1976 CRLJ 1409; 1978 (2) E.L.T. 457; [1976] 2 S.C.R. 93

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Keywords: Excise Rule, Ad Valorem, Common Parlance, Central Excise Tariff, Writ Application, Excisability of goods, Preparation And Attempt, Central Board Of Excise And Customs

Summary: Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, ss. 3, 6 and sch. 1, item 1B - Central Excise Rules, 1944, r. 8(1) - Exemption of goods from Excise duty u/r. 8(1) - (A) Availability - Under CG Notification No. GSR 339, D/- 01-03-1970 - Condensed skimmed milk if excluded - (B) Exemption of goods from duty u/r. 8(1) - Need for licence to manufacture said goods - Held, excluded from exemption - Being a milk preparation within of the meaning of item 1B of the First Schedule a licence to manufacture such milk would be required - Appeal allowed.
