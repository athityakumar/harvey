Haryana Land Reclamation and Development Corporation Limited v Nirmal Kumar

Supreme Court of India

10 December 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, P. Sathasivam

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 1273; (2008) 2 SCC 366; AIR 2008 SC (Supp) 390; 2008 (1) Law Herald (P&H) 301; 2008 (1) LLJ 864; 2008 (2) MPLJ 348; 2007(4) SCALE 34; [2007] 13 S.C.R. 1; 2008 (1) SLR 792

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Daily Rate, Rajasthan Industrial Rules, 1958

Summary: Rajasthan Industrial Rules, 1958, r. 77; Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - Retrenchment - Labour Court held that services of respondent-workman was retrenched in violation of r. 77 - Order of reinstatement with 50% back wages - Appeal against - Adjudication was limited to quantum of back wages - Held, appellant was suffering from huge losses from 1990 onwards - Restricted the back wages to Rs.10, 000/- - Appeal disposed of.
