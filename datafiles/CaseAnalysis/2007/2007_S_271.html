Satya Narayan Agarwal v State Of Assam

Supreme Court of India

26 April 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, D.K. Jain

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 463; (2007) 3 SCC (Cr) 99; (2007) 9 SCC 156; AIR 2007 SC (Supp) 711; 2007 CRLJ 3733; 2007 (1) FAC 264; 2007 (3) JCC 1686; 2007(6) SCALE 166; [2007] 5 S.C.R. 669

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection; Criminal; Health & Drug; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Prevention of Food Adulteration Act, 1954, ss. 7, 16(1); Code of Criminal Procedure, s. 433 - Conviction under the Act -High Court did not accept the contention that it was a case of misbranding and hence did not deserve the minimum sentence - Found no scope for interference - Held, an application under s. 433 has been made and rejected - Neither case put forward by the appellant contains a direction to formalize the sentence - Appeal dismissed.
