P.S. Ranakrishna Reddy v M.K. Bhagyalakshmi and Another

Supreme Court of India

20 February 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Markandey Katju

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 647; (2007) 10 SCC 231; AIR 2007 SC 1256; 2007 (67) ALR 465; 2007 (2) AWC 1614; 2007 (2) CalLT(SC) 54; 2007 (2) CTC 357; JT 2007 (4) SC 223; 2007 (2) KarLJ 561; 2007 (4) MLJ(SC) 193; 2007 (2) RCR(Civil) 290; 2007(3) SCALE 409; [2007] 2 S.C.R. 876

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure; Trusts & Associations; Contract & Commercial

Summary: Suit for specific performance of an agreement for sale - Suit decreed - Appeal against - Held, contention of the appellant has been rejected both by the learned Trial Judge as also by the High Court upon assigning sufficient and cogent reasons - Findings of facts by both the Courts are concurrent ones and in our opinion no case has been made out to interfere therewith by this Court - Appeal dismissed.
