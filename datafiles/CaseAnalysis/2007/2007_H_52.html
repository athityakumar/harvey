Lal Dei and Others v Himachal Road Transport

Supreme Court of India

27 August 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

P.P. Naolekar, R.V. Raveendran

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 1496; (2007) 8 SCC 319; (2007) 3 SCC (Cr) 550; 2008(1) ALL MR 432; 2008 (1) MLJ(SC) 526; 2008 (1) MPLJ 480

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation

Keywords: Motor Accidents Act, 1988

Summary: Carriers & Transportation - Motor Accidents Act, 1988 - Family Pension - Motor Accidents Claims Tribunal has taken into consideration family pension while calculating amount of compensation - Award made by MACT with interest was upheld by High Court and cross-objection was dismissed - Present appeal - Held, while calculating dependency MACT and High Court committed an error in deducting family pension amount - Following, Helen C Rebello case [1998 Indlaw SC 1215] held that family pension is earned by an employee for benefit of his family in form of his contribution in service in terms of service conditions receivable by heirs after his death - Heirs receive family pension even otherwise than accidental death - No co-relation between two - Family pension amount paid to family cannot be deducted while calculating compensation awarded to claimants - Appeal allowed.
