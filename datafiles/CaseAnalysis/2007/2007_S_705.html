Mukarram Ali Khan v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

13 July 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Lokeshwar Singh Panta

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 743; (2007) 11 SCC 90; AIR 2007 SC (Supp) 985; 2007 (6) ALJ 655; 2007 (68) ALR 470; 2007 (4) AWC 3519; 2008 (1) MLJ(SC) 507; 2007 (4) RCR(Civil) 119; 2007(9) SCALE 218; 2007 (8) SCJ 585; [2007] 8 S.C.R. 340

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Urban Land (Ceiling and Regulation) Act, 1999

Summary: Urban Land Ceiling Regulation Act, 1976 - Point urged is that possession has not been taken pursuant to orders passed by the authorities under the Act - Held, in view of affidavit filed by the appellant to which no objection has been filed, undisputed position is that the State has not taken the possession over the surplus land. Therefore, the proceedings have to be treated to have abated under s. 4 of the Repeal Act - Appeal allowed.
