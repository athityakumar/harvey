Union of India v Pradeep Shivram Dhond and another

Supreme Court of India

20 April 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

C.K. Thakker, Tarun Chatterjee

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 1771; (2014) 13 SCC 17

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Criminal - Practice & Procedure - Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act, 1985, s. 80 - Effect of s. 80 - Consideration of - Petitioner sought to reconsider the effect of S. 80 of the Act with reference to SC decisions in Collector of Customs, New Delhi vs. Ahmadalieva Nodira, 2004 Indlaw SC 193; State of Uttaranchal vs. Rajesh Kuamr Gupta, 2006 Indlaw SC 1464.

Held, the effect of s. 80 requires to be considered. Leave is granted and directed the Registry to place the papers before a 3-Judge Bench. Order accordingly.
