Secretary, Andhra Pradesh Social Welfare-Residential Educational Institutions v Sri Pindiga Sridhar and Others

Supreme Court of India

19 March 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

H.K. Sema, D.K. Jain

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 219; (2007) 13 SCC 352; (2008) 2 SCC (L&S) 656; AIR 2007 SC 1527; 2007 (3) AWC 2295; 2007 (3) MLJ(SC) 870; 2007(4) SCALE 479; [2007] 4 S.C.R. 145; 2007 (3) SLR 893

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Appeal by special leave against the decision of Division Bench of High holding termination of the respondent suffered from the non-observance of the principles of natural justice - Appointment of respondent on compassionate ground later cancelled on ground of suppression of facts - Held, to sustain the complaint of the violation of principles of natural justice one must establish that he was prejudiced for non-observance of the principles of natural justice, facts clearly disclose that the appointment on compassionate ground was secured by playing fraud - Appeal allowed.
