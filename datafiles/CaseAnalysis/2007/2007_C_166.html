Pragati Silicons Private Limited v Commissioner of Central Excise, Delhi

Supreme Court of India

26 April 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

Ashok Bhan, Dalveer Bhandari

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 887; (2007) 9 SCC 470; AIR 2007 SC (Supp) 1258; 2007 (211) E.L.T. 534; 2007(6) SCALE 267; 2007 (5) SCJ 913; [2007] 5 S.C.R. 654; [2007] 8 VST 705

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax; Sales Tax; VAT

Keywords: Tariff Item

Summary: Excise - Central Excise Act, 1944, s. 35 L(b) - Classification - Whether 'plastic name plates' are to be classified under Headings Nos. 87.08 and 87.14 as 'parts and accessories' of motor vehicles? - Name plates convey to consumers distinct features it carries - Name plates add effectiveness and value to vehicle and are, at very least, accessories of vehicle - Held, since plastic name plates are 'parts and accessories' of motor vehicles and appropriate classification is under Headings Nos. 87.08 and 87.14 - Appeal allowed.
