Commissioner of Income Tax v Max India Limited

Supreme Court of India

01 November 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

S.H. Kapadia, B. SUDARSHAN REDDY

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 1387; (2007) 15 SCC 401; [2007] 295 ITR 282; [2008] 166 TAXMAN 188

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 80 HHC(3) - Validity of orders passed by Commissioner u/s. 263 - Dual meaning of 'profits' existed as term used in s. 80 HHC(3) - Held, amendment in 2005 having retrospective effect - Consideration to be give to position of law as it stood on date of order - Provision of s. 263 not attracted - Appeal dismissed.
