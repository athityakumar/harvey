Messrs Ibex Gallagher Private Limited and Another v Commissioner of Central Excise, Bangalore

Supreme Court of India

17 August 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, D.K. Jain

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 1031; (2007) 15 SCC 101; 2007 (215) E.L.T. 161; JT 2007 (10) SC 204; 2007(10) SCALE 161; [2007] 9 S.C.R. 70

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Keywords: Appeals disposed of.

Summary: Remand - Excisability - Plant and machinery assembled at site - Appellant contended that the adjudicating authority was not justified in holding that fabrication of the plants in question out of duty paid bought out items amounts to manufacture of a new marketable commodity and therefore dutiable - Held, as the basic factual aspects were not considered by the CEGAT, matter remitted for a fresh consideration in the light of the judgment in Commissioner of Central Excise, Indore v. Virdi Brothers 2007 (207) ELT 321 (SC) and Commissioner of Central Excise, Indore, v. Cethar Vessels Ltd. 2007 (212) ELT 454 (SC) and Circular No.58/1/2002-CX dated 15 January 2002.
