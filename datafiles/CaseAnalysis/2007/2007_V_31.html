Union Of India And Anr v Vicco Laboratories

Supreme Court of India

26 November 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, P. Sathasivam

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 1256; (2007) 13 SCC 270; AIR 2007 SC (Supp) 1225; 2008(3) ALL MR 453; 2008 (2) CTC 511; 2007 (218) E.L.T. 647; 2008 (4) MLJ(SC) 1272; 2007(13) SCALE 481; [2007] 12 S.C.R. 534

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Keywords: Medicament, Ayurvedic Medicines, Central Excises and Salt Act, 1994

Summary: Classification of products - Writ petition challenging show cause notice on the ground that the Commissioner was seeking to re-open and re-litigate the issues which have been finally concluded by the decision of the High Court and this Court in favour of the writ petitioner allowed - Appeal against - Held, as rightly observed by the High Court the impugned show cause notice was nothing but a repetition of the earlier show cause notices with slight variations which in no way was relatable to any different test - Appeal dismissed.
