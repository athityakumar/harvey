Arjun Singh Rathore and Others v B.N.Chaturvedi and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 October 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

H.S. Bedi, S.B. Sinha

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 1045; (2007) 11 SCC 605; AIR 2007 SC (Supp) 1673; 2008 (5) AWC 4843; 2007 (115) FLR 809; JT 2007 (13) SC 543; 2007(12) SCALE 342; 2007 (8) SCJ 107; [2007] 11 S.C.R. 320

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Practice & Procedure; Service; Contract & Commercial

Keywords: Mode Of Selection, Regional Rural Banks Act, 1976, Regional Rural Banks (Appointment and Promotion of Officers and others Employees) Rules, 1988

Summary: Regional Rural Banks (Appointment and Promotion of Officers and others Employees) Rules, 1988 - Promotion - Held, vacancies which had occurred prior to the enforcement of the Rules of 1998 had to be filled in under the Rules of 1988 and as per the procedure laid down - Judgment of Single Judge needs to be restored - Appeal allowed.
