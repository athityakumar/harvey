Shiv Gopal Sah @ Shiv Gopal Sahu v Sita Ram Saraugi and Others

Supreme Court of India

30 March 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

V.S. Sirpurkar, Ashok Bhan

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 667; (2007) 14 SCC 120; AIR 2007 SC 1478; 2007 (68) ALR 142; 2007 (3) AWC 2863; 2007 (2) Law Herald (P&H) 1399; 2007 (4) MLJ(SC) 378; 2007 (147) PLR 78; 2007 (2) RCR(Civil) 679; 2007 (103) RD 187; 2007(5) SCALE 198; 2007 (8) SCJ 67; [2007] 4 S.C.R. 568; 2007 (2) UC 1156

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure Code, s. 115, O. 6, r. 17 - Application for amendment - High Court approved amendments permitted by trial court - Contended that the application was liable to be dismissed on the ground that it permitted the plaintiffs to include a time barred claim - Held, clear that the original plaintiff as also the subsequent purchasers remained complacent and negligent all through for a period of more than 15 years and woke up for the first time to challenge the sale deed dated 4.10.1985 by seeking a declaration that it is bogus and did not create any title in favour of the original defendant - No explanation whatsoever for this delay - Appeal allowed.
