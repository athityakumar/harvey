Madhya Pradesh State Cooperative Bank Limited, Bhopal v Nanuram Yadav And Ors

Supreme Court of India

25 September 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

P. Sathasivam, Tarun Chatterjee

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 954; (2007) 8 SCC 264; (2007) 2 SCC (L&S) 883; AIR 2007 SC (Supp) 932; JT 2007 (11) SC 369; 2007 (4) LLN 117; 2007(11) SCALE 439; [2007] 10 S.C.R. 307; 2007 (6) SLR 115

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Madhya Pradesh Cooperative Societies Act, 1960, T.N. Industrial Establishments (Conferment of Permanent Status to Workmen) Act, 1981, Staff Service Rules, 1976, Madhya Pradesh Rajya Sahakari Bank Employees (Terms of Employment and Working Conditions) Rules, 1976, T.N. Cooperative Societies Rules, 1988

Summary: Service - How public appointments to be made, whether Lokayukt constituted under M.P. Lokayukt Evam Up-Lokayukt Adhiniyam, 1981 has jurisdiction to go into the appointment of employees of the M.P. State Cooperative Bank and whether 60 clerks-cum-typists appointed by the said Bank were in accordance with the service rules? - Held, it is not in dispute that elaborate procedures are to be followed before terminating the service of an employee under the provisions of the M.P. Cooperative Societies Act and the service rules made thereunder - In the absence of opportunity to the employees, the termination order which was sent at the instance of Commissioner, Cooperative Societies based on the report of Lokayukt cannot be sustained - Appeals disposed of.
