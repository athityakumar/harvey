Commissioner of Central Excise, Jamshedpur v Jamshedpur Beverages

Supreme Court of India

25 April 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

Ashok Bhan, Dalveer Bhandari

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 1355; 2007 (214) E.L.T. 321

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excise Act, 1944, s. 35-L - Whether assessee had wrongly availed the Modvat credit and was liable to pay the amount? - Excise duty paid and the Modvat credit availed were identical and therefore consequences of payment of excise duty after availing Modvat credit was revenue neutral - Question of law left open - Appeal dismissed
