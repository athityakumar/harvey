Union Of India v Messrs Bharat Battery Manufacturing Company

Supreme Court of India

13 August 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

H.K. Sema, Lokeshwar Singh Panta

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 807; (2007) 7 SCC 684; 2007 (68) ALR 924; 2007 (5) ALT 61; 2007 (4) AWC 3730; 2008 (4) CompLJ 32; JT 2007 (10) SC 129; 2007 (6) MLJ(SC) 719; 2007 (3) RCR(Civil) 858; 2007(10) SCALE 9; 2007 (6) SCJ 366; [2007] 8 S.C.R. 993

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Practice & Procedure; Contract & Commercial

Summary: Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, s. 11(6) - High Court appointed an arbitrator on a petition filed by the respondent - Appeal against - Held, appellants are stopped from making an appointment of the arbitrator in terms of Clause 24 of the agreement after s. 11(6) petition is filed by the respondent - Once s. 11(6) petition is filed before the Court, seeking appointment of an arbitrator, the power to appoint an arbitrator in terms of arbitration clause of the agreement ceases - Appeal dismissed.
