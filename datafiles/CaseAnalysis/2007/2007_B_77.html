ACE Pipeline Contracts Private Limited v Bharat Petroleum Corporation Limited

Supreme Court of India

04 April 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

A.K. Mathur, Tarun Chatterjee

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 292; (2007) 5 SCC 304; AIR 2007 SC 1764; 2007 (3) AWC 2667; 2008 (4) CompLJ 20; JT 2007 (5) SC 105; 2007 (6) MLJ(SC) 346; 2007 (4) MPLJ 1; 2007 (2) RCR(Civil) 407; 2007(5) SCALE 341; 2007 (7) SCJ 360; [2007] 4 S.C.R. 777

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Practice & Procedure; Contract & Commercial

Summary: Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, s. 11(5) - Single Judge dismissed the application for appointment of Arbitrator - Appeal against - Held, once a party has entered into an agreement with eyes wide open it cannot wriggle out of the situation that if any person of the respondent-BPCL is appointed as arbitrator he will not be impartial or objective - Appeal dismissed.
