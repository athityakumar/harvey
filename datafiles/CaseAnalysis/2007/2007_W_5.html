Vijay Kumar And Ors v Whirlpool Of India Ltd. And Ors

Supreme Court of India

22 November 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, P. Sathasivam

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 1152; (2008) 1 SCC 119; AIR 2007 SC (Supp) 1433; 2008 (5) AWC 4250; JT 2007 (13) SC 242; 2008 (1) Law Herald (P&H) 380; 2008 (1) LLJ 308; 2008 (1) MPLJ 475; 2007(13) SCALE 379; 2007 (8) SCJ 552; [2007] 12 S.C.R. 425; 2008 (4) SLR 644; 2008 (1) WLN(SC) 94

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - Voluntary retirement scheme - Settlement in terms of s. 12(3) - Issues relating to the age of retirement and to monetary benefits - Held, settlement covered only cases of existing employees. The question of any erstwhile workman attaining specified production target does not arise - Appeal dismissed.
