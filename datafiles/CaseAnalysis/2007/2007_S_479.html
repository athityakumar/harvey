Bhagubhai Dhanabhai Khalasi and Another v State of Gujarat and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 April 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Markandey Katju

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 300; (2007) 4 SCC 241; (2007) 2 SCC (Cr) 260; 2007(6) ALL MR 472; 2007 (3) AWC 2297; 2007 (3) G.L.R. 2414; 2007 (4) RCR(Civil) 519; 2007(5) SCALE 357; 2007 (8) SCJ 253; [2007] 4 S.C.R. 899

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: European Convention for the Protection of Human Rights and Fundamental Freedoms, 1950

Summary: Conservation of Foreign Exchange and Prevention of Smuggling Activities Act, 1974; Smugglers and Foreign Exchange Manipulators (Forfeiture of Property) Act, 1976 - Preventive detention - Detention cancelled and order for return of properties - Immovable properties were not returned - Held, Division Bench did not go into the correctness or otherwise of the allegations and counter allegations made by the parties before it - Whether appellant was guilty of any forgery or not was not determined - Part of the impugned order whereby and whereunder the Division Bench refused to grant leave to the appellant to ventilate his grievances before an appropriate forum cannot be sustained - Appeal partly allowed.
