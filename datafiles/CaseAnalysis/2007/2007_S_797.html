Commissioner of Central Excise and Customs v Suresh Synthetics

Supreme Court of India

21 August 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

S.H. Kapadia, B. SUDARSHAN REDDY

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 1179; 2007 (216) E.L.T. 662

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Central Excise Act, 1944 - Whether Tribunal erred in concluding that show cause notice was defective? - Duty in question is excise duty and not customs duty, even investigations were made under Central Excise Act - Appellate Tribunal cannot sustain case of Revenue against appellants on a ground not raised by Revenue either in show-cause notice or in order - Held, show cause notice was defective in law and since it was defective notice demand was not maintainable - Appeal dismissed.
