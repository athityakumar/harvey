Citibank, N.A v TLC Marketing PLC and Another

Supreme Court of India

05 October 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

Lokeshwar Singh Panta

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 991; (2008) 1 SCC 481; AIR 2008 SC 118; 2007 (4) AWC 4140; 2007 (5) CompLJ 177; JT 2007 (11) SC 445; 2007(11) SCALE 675; 2007 (7) SCJ 825; [2007] 10 S.C.R. 710

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Corporate; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Appointment Of Sole Arbitrator, Claim And Counter Claim, Appointment Of Arbitral Tribunal, Repudiation Of Contract

Summary: Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996 - Application for appointment of sole Arbitrator in an 'international commercial arbitration' - Held, in view of the instances of breaches of the terms and conditions of the relevant clauses of the agreement coupled with the breaches of specific obligations and responsibilities contained in the Appendix(s) and Enclosures attached and incorporated by reference as an integral part of the agreement and having regard to the words used in Clause 10 of the agreement and having regard to the fact that the parties have failed to determine an even number of arbitrators as per the provisions of s. 10(1), requirement of s. 10(2) is fully attracted in the present proceedings - Arbitrator is being appointed.
