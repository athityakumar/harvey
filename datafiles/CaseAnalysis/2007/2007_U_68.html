B. Ramakichenin @ Balagandhi v Union Of India And Others

Supreme Court of India

16 November 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

Markandey Katju, A.K. Mathur

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 1137; (2008) 1 SCC 362; AIR 2008 SC (Supp) 1234; 2008(1) ALL MR 480; 2008 (5) AWC 4510; JT 2007 (13) SC 91; 2007(13) SCALE 175; [2007] 12 S.C.R. 120; 2008 (1) SLR 651

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Service - Post of Deputy Director (Agriculture) - High Court set aside the appellant's appointment - Appeal against - Held, it was open to the UPSC to do short-listing by stating that it will call only those who have Ph.D. degree in Agriculture (although the essential degree was only M.Sc. degree in Agriculture) - A particular manner of short-listing has been prescribed in paragraph 3.1 of advertisement - It is not open to the UPSC to resort to any other method of short-listing even if such other method can be said to be fair and objective - Appellant has been working as Deputy Director (Agriculture) since 2001 in pursuance of the judgment of the Tribunal and the interim order of this Court - Upheld his appointment - Appeal allowed.
