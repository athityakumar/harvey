Bhagwan Das v Kartar Singh and Others

Supreme Court of India

14 May 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

Markandey Katju, S.B. Sinha

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 577; (2007) 11 SCC 205; AIR 2007 SC 2045; 2007 ALL MR (Cri) 2029; 2007 CRLJ 3420; 2007 (3) JCC 1768; JT 2007 (7) SC 36; 2007 (3) RCR(Criminal) 87; 2007(78) SCALE 167; [2007] 6 S.C.R. 474

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Indian Penal Code, ss. 306, 498, 34 - High Court held that no charge under s. 306/34 IPC was made out and it has set aside the charge - Appeal against - Held, mere harassment of wife by husband due to differences per se does not attract s. 306 r/w s. 107 IPC, if the wife commits suicide - If the suicide was due to demand of dowry soon before her death then s. 304B IPC may be attracted, whether it is a case of homicide or suicide - Appeal dismissed.
