S. C. Chandra And Others v State Of Jharkhand And Others

Supreme Court of India

21 August 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

A.K. Mathur, Markandey Katju

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 826; (2007) 8 SCC 279; (2007) 2 SCC (L&S) 897; AIR 2007 SC 302; 2007 (4) JhrCR(SC) 117; JT 2007 (10) SC 272; 2007(10) SCALE 209; [2007] 9 S.C.R. 130; 2007 (5) SLR 361

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Indian Copper Corporation (Acquisition of Undertaking) Act, 1972

Summary: Bihar Non-Government Secondary Schools (Taking over of Management and Control) Act, 1981 - Appellant seeking a writ of mandamus against respondent Nos.3 to 6 to release and pay D.A. with arrears along with interest and further a direction was sought to be issued to respondent Nos.3 to 6 not to close the school or in the alternative a direction was sought to be issued to respondent Nos.1 & 2 to take over the management and control of the school - Appeal against dismissal of writ petition - Whether a writ of mandamus could be issued against the management of HCL? - Held, so far as issuance of mandamus to the State Government for taking over of the proprietary school is concerned, that cannot be issued because the proprietary school as defined under s. 2(d) r/w s. 19 will have to make a request to the State of Jharkhand that they will bear all the financial responsibilities - Appellants are not the employees of BCCL, there is no question seeking any parity of the pay with that of the clerks of BCCL - Appeal dismissed.
