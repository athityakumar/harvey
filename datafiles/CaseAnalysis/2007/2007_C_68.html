Mohammad Akram Ansari and Others v Chief Election Officer and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 December 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

A.K. Mathur, Markandey Katju

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 1357; (2008) 2 SCC 95; AIR 2008 SC (Supp) 1247; 2008 (1) AWC 894; 2008 (1) RCR(Civil) 565; 2007(14) SCALE 30; [2007] 12 S.C.R. 901

Case Digest

Subject: Election; Family & Personal; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Qualification For Membership, WAKF (DELHI AMENDMENT) ACT, 2006, Corrupt Practice

Summary: Wakf Act, 1995; Wakf (Delhi Amendment) Act, 2006 - Whether the office of Chairperson or Members of the Walf Board is an office of profit so as to disqualify a person from being elected as a member of the Legislative Assembly of NCT of Delhi? - Held, even if the elected candidate was disqualified in the year 2003, he has to be deemed not to have been disqualified in view of Section 31-A which was inserted in the year 2006 - Appeals dismissed.
