Commissioner of Central Excise, Hyderabad v Novapan Industries Limited

Supreme Court of India

11 January 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

Ashok Bhan, Lokeshwar Singh Panta

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 1545; 2007 (209) E.L.T. 161

Case Digest

Subject: Excise

Summary: Excise - Central Excise Act, 1944, s. 11AB - Interest - Respondent filed price declaration in which it opted for provisional assessment and claimed interest on receivables as an abatement - Assistant Collector issued show cause notice demanding differential duty under Basic Excise Duty (BED) and under Special Excise Duty (SED) - Authority-in-Original affirmed the demand as per the show cause notices - On appeal, Commissioner set aside the Order-in-Original and remanded the case to the Authority-in-Original for a fresh decision in accordance with MRF case 2[(1986) Suppl. SCC 751 - On remand, Authority-in-Original reiterated its earlier findings and held that the judgment of SC in MRF case was distinguishable on facts and was not applicable to the present case - Commissioner set aside Order-in-Original - Tribunal dismissed appeal filed against impugned order - Hence, present appeal - Whether the amount of interest on receivables is deductible from the assessable value where such interest is inbuilt in the price and is not charged separately? - Held, interest being inbuilt in the price which had not been charged separately, was deductible from the assessable value - Point in issue being concluded by the decision of SC in MRF case 2[(1986) Suppl. SCC 751 and moreover, revenue did not file an appeal against the order of the Tribunal in ICI India case - No merit in these appeals - Revenue's appeals dismissed.
