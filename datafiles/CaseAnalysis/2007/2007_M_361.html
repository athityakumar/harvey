Mukesh Kumar v Union Of India And Others

Supreme Court of India

05 September 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, D.K. Jain

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 879; (2007) 8 SCC 398; (2007) 2 SCC (L&S) 626; AIR 2007 SC 3077; 2007 (7) AWC 7279; JT 2007 (10) SC 565; 2007 (4) Law Herald (P&H) 2789; 2007 (4) Law Herald (P&H) 2819; 2007(10) SCALE 543; 2007 (6) SCJ 716; [2007] 9 S.C.R. 711; 2007 (5) SLR 688

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Service - Compassionate appointment - Central Administrative Tribunal dismissed application on the ground that there was inordinate delay of 15 years in filing the application - Held, there is no indication as to on the basis of which materials the conclusion was arrived at - Matter is remitted to the Central Administrative Tribunal, Chandigarh Bench for fresh hearing - Appeal allowed.
