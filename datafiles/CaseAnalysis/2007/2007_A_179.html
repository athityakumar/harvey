Secretary, West Bengal Council of Higher v Ayan Das and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 September 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, P. Sathasivam

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 963; (2007) 8 SCC 242; (2007) 2 SCC (L&S) 871; AIR 2007 SC 3098; 2007 (69) ALR 494; 2008 (5) AWC 4854; 2008 (1) CalLT(SC) 1; 2007 (4) ESC 607; 2007 (4) JhrCR(SC) 217; JT 2007 (12) SC 130; 2007 (4) KLT 535; 2008 (1) MLJ(SC) 866; 2008 (1) MPLJ 229; 2007(11) SCALE 560; [2007] 10 S.C.R. 464; 2008 (1) SLR 394; 2008 (1) UPLBEC 441

Case Digest

Subject: Education; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Order passed by the Division Bench of the Calcutta High Court directing re- examination of answer script of respondent No.1 in English Paper 1 - Appeal against - Held, courts normally should not direct the production of answer scripts to be inspected by the writ petitioners unless a case is made out to show that either some question has not been evaluated or that the evaluation has been done contrary to the norms fixed by the examining body - Orders of Single Judge and Division Bench cannot be sustained and stand quashed - Appeal allowed.
