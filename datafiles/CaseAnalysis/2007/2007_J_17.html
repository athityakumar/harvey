Vice Chancellor, M.D. University, Rohtak v Jahan Singh

Supreme Court of India

08 March 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Markandey Katju

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 329; (2007) 5 SCC 77; (2007) 2 SCC (L&S) 118; AIR 2007 SC (Supp) 182; 2007 (6) AWC 6532; 2007 (113) FLR 428; 2007(4) SCALE 226; 2007 (6) SCJ 545; [2007] 3 S.C.R. 676; 2007 (3) SLR 381

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Service

Keywords: Maharishi Dayanand University Act, 1975

Summary: Service - Termination - Held, as respondent was not in the service of the University before he had made an application, a' fortiori the question of the respondent's application being sent by the employee through the University did not and could not arise - Division Bench of High Court was not correct in interfering with judgment of the Single Judge as the case of the respondent is not even covered by the regulations - Appeal allowed.
