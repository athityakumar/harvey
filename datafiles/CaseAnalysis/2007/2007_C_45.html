Kurmanchal Inst. of Degree and Diploma and Others v Chancellor, M.J.P. Rohilkhand University and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 May 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Markandey Katju

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 564; (2007) 6 SCC 35; AIR 2007 SC 2253; 2007 (4) ALJ 711; 2007 (6) AWC 5754; 2007 (3) ESC 480; JT 2008 (8) SC 24; 2007(8) SCALE 178; 2007 (7) SCJ 549; [2007] 7 S.C.R. 191; 2007 (4) SLR 601; 2007 (3) UPLBEC 2740

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Education; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Uttar Pradesh State Universities Act, 1973 - Chancellor disapproved the proposal for starting a new course in distance education - Held, study centres of the appellants being situated in Nainital, is beyond the territorial jurisdiction of the respondent university - No writ of or in the nature of mandamus as has been prayed for in the writ petition can be issued - Appeal dismissed.
