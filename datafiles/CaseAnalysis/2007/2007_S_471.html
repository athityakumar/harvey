Raymond Limited and Another v State of Chhattisgarh and Others

Supreme Court of India

20 February 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Markandey Katju

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 648; (2007) 3 SCC 79; AIR 2007 SC 2854; 2007 (2) CTC 456; JT 2007 (3) SC 582; 2007 (4) MLJ(SC) 198; 2007 (2) RCR(Civil) 426; 2007 (102) RD 505; 2007(3) SCALE 341; 2007 (5) SCJ 167; [2007] 2 S.C.R. 990

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Indian Stamp (Madhya Pradesh Amendment) Act, 1990

Summary: Indian Stamp Act, 1899 - Interpretation and application of ss. 31, 32 and 56 - Held, when determination made by a statutory authority is capable of being challenged by way of revision, it is axiomatic that only the revisional order shall be final and not the order of the original authority - Appeal dismissed.
