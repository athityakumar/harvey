Chairman, Uttar Pradesh Jal Nigam and Another v Radhey Shyam Gautam & Another

Supreme Court of India

30 March 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, R.V. Raveendran

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 282; (2007) 11 SCC 507; 2007 (6) AWC 5902; 2007 (4) ESC 497; 2007 (113) FLR 882; JT 2007 (5) SC 177; 2007(5) SCALE 216; 2007 (8) SCJ 245; [2007] 4 S.C.R. 583; 2007 (3) SLR 187; 2007 (2) UPLBEC 1500

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Transfer Of Employee, Uttar Pradesh Water Supply and Sewerage Act, 1975, Uttar Pradesh Jal Nigam Engineers (Public Health Branch) Service Regulations, 1978

Summary: Appeal filed to challenge the correctness of the Order passed by a Division Bench in an interim order passed by a learned Single Judge permitting the respondent to continue in service of the appellant till attaining the age of 60 years - Held, After the amendment made in Rule 56(a) of the Rules by the State Government enhancing the age of superannuation of government servants from 58 years to 60 years, the same would equally apply to the employees of the Nigam and in case the State Government as well as the Nigam intended that the same would not be applicable, the only option with it was to make suitable amendment in Regulation 31 of the Regulations after taking previous approval of the State Government - Appeal dismissed.
