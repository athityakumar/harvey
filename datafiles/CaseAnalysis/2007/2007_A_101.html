Principal Secretary To Government, Finance and Planning Department v Andhra Pradesh Pensioners Samaj and Others

Supreme Court of India

31 January 2007

Case Analysis

Bench

Markandey Katju, S.B. Sinha

Where Reported

2007 Indlaw SC 58; (2007) 13 SCC 137; (2008) 2 SCC (L&S) 592; 2007 (112) FLR 1065; JT 2007 (3) SC 232; 2007(2) SCALE 360; [2007] 2 S.C.R. 37; 2007 (3) SLR 114; 2007 (2) UPLBEC 1136

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Service

Keywords: Revision Of Pension, Matter referred to Larger Bench

Summary: Service - Pension - Respondent's seeking a direction to the State Government to extend the benefits of G.O.Ms. No.87, Finance & Planning (FW.Pen.1) Department, to all the pensioners irrespective of their date of retirement and also to compensate the loss sustained by the applicants - Tribunal held that though pensioners are entitled for re-fixation of their pension in terms of G.O. Ms.No.87 dated 25.5.1998 they were not eligible for any arrears that accrued prior to 25.5.1998 - High Court upheld the order - Appeal against - Held, in view of apparent conflict in the decisions, matter requires consideration by a larger Bench of this Court - Order accordingly.
