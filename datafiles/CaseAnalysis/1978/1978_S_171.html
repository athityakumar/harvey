Modan Singh v State of Rajasthan

Supreme Court of India

31 August 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

P.S. Kailasam, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 398; (1978) 4 SCC 435; (1979) SCC (Cr) 56; AIR 1978 SC 1511; 1978 CRLJ 1531; 1978 CRLR 631

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Supreme Court (Enlargement of Criminal Appellate Jurisdiction) Act, 1970

Summary: Evidence Act, 1872, s. 3 - CrPC, 1973, s. 52 - Criminal case - (A) Appreciation of evidence - Death caused by pistol - Not proved - (B) Seizure memo - Evidentiary value of evidence of seizure witnesses - Supreme Court set aside conviction and sentence as prosecution case not established - If the evidence of investigating officer who recovered the material objects is convincing, evidence as to recovery need not be rejected on the ground that seizure witnesses do not support the prosecution version - Appeal Allowed.
