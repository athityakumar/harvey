Gurupad Khandappa Magdum v Hirabai Khandappa Magdum and Others

Supreme Court of India

27 April 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, P.N. Shingal, V.D. Tulzapurkar

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 232; (1978) 3 SCC 383; AIR 1978 SC 1239; 1978 (4) ALR 522; 1978 HLR 729; [1981] 129 ITR 440; [1978] 3 S.C.R. 761; 1978 UJ 381

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Women & Children

Summary: Hindu Succession Act, 1956, s. 6, Proviso and expln. 1 - Mode of determination of widow's interest in coparcenary property - Held, must be ascertained by adding the share to which she is entitled at a notional partition during her husband's lifetime and the share which she would get in her husband's interest upon his death - Appeal Dismissed.
