Rajapalayam Mills Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax, Madras

Supreme Court of India

06 October 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, V.D. Tulzapurkar, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 218; (1978) 4 SCC 322; (1979) SCC (Tax) 8; AIR 1979 SC 117; 1978 (7) CTR 167; [1978] 115 ITR 777; 1979 (2) MLJ(SC) 9; [1979] 1 S.C.R. 1138; 1978 UJ 798

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922, ss. 15C, 10(2) - Income Tax Act, 1961, s. 84 - (A) Interpretation of Statutes - Pari materia - Provisions of s.15C of 1922 Act and s.84 of 1961 Act are in material respects in identical terms - (B) Exemption u/s.15C (1) of 1922 Act , when allowed - Applicability of Proviso (b) to cl. (vi) of s. 10(2) and cl.(vib) of s. 10(2) to mode of calculation of depreciation and development rebate - Held, interpretation placed on s. 15C of the former Act is bound to apply equally to s. 84 of the latter Act - Held on facs that assessee was entitled to benefit of s. 15C(1) - Appeals allowed.
