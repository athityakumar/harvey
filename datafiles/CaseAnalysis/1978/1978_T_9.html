Tamal Lahiri v Kumar P.N. Tagore

Supreme Court of India

13 September 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.V. Chandrachud, R.S. Sarkaria, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 77; (1979) 1 SCC 75; (1979) SCC (Cr) 143; AIR 1978 SC 1811; 1979 CRLJ 1; 1979 MLJ 213; [1979] 1 S.C.R. 739; 1978 UJ 758

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Bengal General Clauses Act, 1899, Bengal Municipal Act 1932 Ben Act 15 of 1932)

Summary: Municipalities & Local Govts - Practice & Procedure - Indian Limitation Act, 1908 - Bengal General Clauses Act, 1899, s. 3 (27) - Bengal Municipal Act 1932, ss. 240 (1)(b), 500(1)(b) and 533 - (A) Meaning of term 'Month' in General Clauses Act - It does not mean 180 days but six calendar months - (B) Time limit for removal of obstruction or encroachment - Commissioners can allow for removal of encroachment such time as they consider reasonable in circumstances of case - Impossible to except submission that offence must be deemed to have been committed when obstruction or encroachment was erected - Appeal allowed.
