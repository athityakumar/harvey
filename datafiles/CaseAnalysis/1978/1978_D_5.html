D. Nataraja Mudallar v State Transport Authority, Madras

Supreme Court of India

06 September 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, D.A. Desai

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 65; (1978) 4 SCC 290; AIR 1979 SC 114; 1979 (1) MLJ(SC) 35; [1979] 1 S.C.R. 552; 1978 UJ 708

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Carriers & Transportation - Motor Vehicles Act, 1939, ss. 50, 53 - Considerations for renewal of contract carriage permit - RTA acts quasi-judicially - Rejection of renewal application on ground of inadequateness of facilities provided by public sector undertakings - Validity - Rejection held was not proper - Appeal allowed.
