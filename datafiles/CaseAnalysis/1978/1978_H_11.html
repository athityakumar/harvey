Harsh Sawhney v Union Territory (Chandigarh Admn.)

Supreme Court of India

20 February 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, R.S. Pathak, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 325; (1978) SCC (Cr) 189; (1978) 2 SCC 365; AIR 1978 SC 1016; 1978 CRLJ 774; [1978] 3 S.C.R. 129; 1978 UJ 451

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, s. 437 - Grant of bail - In light of necessity of search of premises in the presence the accused and her presence for interrogation as grounds for taking accused into custody - On facts and circumstances of case, bail granted - Held, no grounds for taking into custody - Appeal allowed.
