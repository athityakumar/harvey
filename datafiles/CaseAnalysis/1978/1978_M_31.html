Maria Cristina De Souza Sodder and Others v Amria Zurana Pereira Pinto and Others

Supreme Court of India

30 August 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

V.D. Tulzapurkar, P.N. Bhagwati

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 257; (1979) 1 SCC 92; AIR 1979 SC 1352; 1978 UJ 718

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Repeal

Summary: Goa, Daman And Diu (Extension of CPC, and Arbitration Act), 1965 - Value of property in suit exceeding Rs. 10,000/- Under which court, suit of given value will be tried? - Held, under Indian Code of Civil Procedure r/w Goa Act since property involved in suit was of value exceeding Rs. 10,000 appeal clearly lay to Judicial Commissioner's Court, and not to trial Court under Portuguese Code.
