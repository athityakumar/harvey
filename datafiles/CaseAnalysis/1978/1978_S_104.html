Maharaja Book Depot v State of Gujarat

Supreme Court of India

24 October 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

V.D. Tulzapurkar, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 100; (1979) SCC (Cr) 275; (1979) 1 SCC 295; AIR 1979 SC 180; 1978 CRLJ 1859; 1978 CrLR(SC) 654; 1979 (20) GLR 99; 1979 MLJ 164; [1979] 2 S.C.R. 138; 1979 UJ 196

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection; Criminal; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Socio-Economic

Summary: Consumer Protection - Essential Commodities Act, 1955, s. 2(a)(vii) - Gujarat Essential Articles Dealers' Regulation Order, 1971, sch. 1, entry 13 - Scope of expression 'paper' to determine whether Exercise books can be confiscated for violaion of of 1971 Order - Held, excercise books are also covered by expression 'paper'- They can be confiscated for violation - Appeal dismissed.
