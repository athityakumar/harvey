Dr. S. L. Goswami v High Court of Madhya Pradesh At Jabalpur

Supreme Court of India

23 November 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

P.S. Kailasam, D.A. Desai, A.D. Koshal

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 364; (1979) SCC (Cr) 311; (1979) 1 SCC 373; AIR 1979 SC 437; 1979 CRLJ 193; 1979 CRLR 371; [1979] 2 S.C.R. 385

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1898, ss. 195(1)(c) and 196 A(2) - (A) Construction of meaning of expression 'of any offence described in s. 463' - Whether offence u/s. 466, Indian Penal Code comes u/s. 195 (1)(c) - (B) Meaning of expression 'in respect of a document produced or given in evidence in such proceeding' - Offence of criminal conspiracy committed during preparation of paper book for appeal to Supreme Court - Held, if offence falls u/s. 95 (1) then criminal conspiracy to commit such offence would also fall u/s. 195 (1) - Provision of s. 195 (4) held not attracted and complaint u/s. 196 A (2) necessary - Appeal dismissed.
