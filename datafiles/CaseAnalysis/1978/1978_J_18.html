Sher Singh (Dead) By Lrs v Joint Director of Consolidation and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 May 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

Jaswant Singh, N.L. Untwalia, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 301; (1978) 3 SCC 172; AIR 1978 SC 1341; 1978 ALJ 685; [1978] 3 S.C.R. 982

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: U.P. Consolidation of Holdings Act, 1954, s. 48 - U.P.Z.A. & L.R. Act, 1950 - Scope of Revisional Jurisdiction of Director of Consolidation - Erroneous decision on a question of fact or of law - When no relation to question of jurisdiction of Subordinate Officer - Held, cannot be corrected - Appeal allowed.
