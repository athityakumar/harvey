Tummalla Atchaiah v Venka Narasingarao

Supreme Court of India

24 January 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

N.L. Untwalia, P.S. Kailasam

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 452; (1979) 1 SCC 166; AIR 1978 SC 725

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 41, r. 33 - Power of Court of Appeal to interfere with decree of Trial Court - Validity of interference with decree without any specific ground in cross objection - Held, alteration of decree of trial is not proper - Appeal allowed.
