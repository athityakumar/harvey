Union of India v Prafulla Kumar Sanyal

Supreme Court of India

27 October 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

P.S. Kailasam, D.A. Desai

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 256; (1979) 3 SCC 631; AIR 1979 SC 1457; 1978 UJ 901

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Summary: Arbitration Act, 1940 - Whether court is not required to appoint arbitrator who has not actually been appointed but for whose appointment adequate provisions have been made? - Held, feasibility of appointing arbitrator according to terms of contract should be considered.
