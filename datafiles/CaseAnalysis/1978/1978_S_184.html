Basti Sugar Mills Company Limited v State of Uttar Pradesh and Another

Supreme Court of India

11 September 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, D.A. Desai

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 103; (1979) SCC (L&S) 61; (1979) 2 SCC 88; AIR 1979 SC 262; 1978 (37) FLR 265; 1979 LabIC 129; 1978 (2) LLJ 412; 1978 (2) LLN 468; [1979] 1 S.C.R. 590

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Trade Union Act, 1926, United Provinces Industrial Disputes Act 1947 (UP 28 of 1947) (ENG)

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Uttar Pradesh Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 3(b), 3(c) - Payment of Bonus Act, 1965, s. 34 - Govt. constitute a tripartite committee - Validity of agreement incorporated in Notification u/s. 3(b) - Held, agreement u/s. 3(1) of Bonus Act could be spelt out of notification u/s. 3(b) of 1947, Act - Appeal dismissed.
