Noor Mohd. Khan Ghouse Khan Soudagar and Another v Fakirappa Bharmappa Machenahalli and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 April 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

N.L. Untwalia, R.S. Sarkaria, P.S. Kailasam

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 309; (1978) 3 SCC 188; AIR 1978 SC 1217; [1978] 3 S.C.R. 789

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Evacuee Property & Displaced Persons

Summary: Karnataka Land Reforms Act, 1962, s.133 - CPC, 1908, s. 9 - Tenancy - Execution of partition decree - Jurisdiction of Civil Court to decide whether the respondent was tenant - Held, Civil Court's jurisdiction to decide question barred - Appeals dismissed
