Principal And Ors v Presiding Officer And Ors

Supreme Court of India

09 January 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

Jaswant Singh, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 145; (1978) SCC (L&S) 70; (1978) 1 SCC 498; AIR 1978 SC 344; 1978 (36) FLR 236; 1978 LabIC 184; [1978] 2 S.C.R. 507; 1978 (1) SLR 399; 1978 UJ 73

Case Digest

Subject: Education; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Reduction In Rank

Summary: Delhi School Education Act, 1973, ss.2(t), 2(e), 2(j), 8 and 11- Education - Unrecognised private school - Approval of Director of Education not necessary for termination of teacher - (A) Applicability of the provisions of the Act and Rules thereunder - (B) Appeal to Delhi School Tribunal - Validity - Held, not applicable, recognition essential - Manifestly incompetent - Appeal Allowed.
