Suleman Noormohamed Etc. Etc v Umarbhai Janubhai

Supreme Court of India

23 February 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

N.L. Untwalia, R.S. Sarkaria

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 154; (1978) 2 SCC 179; AIR 1978 SC 952; 1978 (19) GLR 566; 1978 (1) RCJ 711; 1978 (2) RCR(Rent) 12; 1978 (1) RentLR 765; [1978] 3 S.C.R. 387; 1978 UJ 253

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Keywords: Ex-Parte

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure,1908, O. 23, r. 3 - Bombay Rents, Hotel and Lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947, s. 12(3)(b) - Tenancy - Compromise - (A) Validity of of compromise in eviction suit - (B) 'By any lawful compromise' u/r. 3 of O. 23 - Whether it is necessary for Court to say in express terms in order that it was satisfied that compromise was a lawful one - Held, decree not in violation of Act, it is valid - Held that not necessary, it will be presumed to have done so, unless contrary is shown - Appeal allowed.
