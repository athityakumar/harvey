Gundaji Satwaji Shinde v Ramchandra Bhikaji Joshi

Supreme Court of India

05 December 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, P.N. Shingal

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 179; (1979) 2 SCC 495; AIR 1979 SC 653; 1979 MahLJ 283; [1979] 2 S.C.R. 586

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Rent Control - Bombay Tenancy And Agricultural Lands Act, 1948, ss. 70, 85, 85A and 63 - Tenancy - Scope and effect of ss. 70, 85 and 85-A of 1948 Act - Suit for specific performance of contract of sale of agricultural land - Whether plaintiff is agriculturist involved? - Exclusive jurisdiction of Mamlatdar to decide it - Jurisdiction of Civil Court is ousted - Held, procedure to be followed by Civil Court in dealing with suit indicated - Appeal allowed.
