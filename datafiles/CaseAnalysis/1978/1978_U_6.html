Union Of India & Anr v Swadeshi Cotton Mills & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

12 September 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, D.A. Desai, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 118; (1978) 4 SCC 295; AIR 1978 SC 1818; [1979] 49 Comp Cas 74; [1979] 1 S.C.R. 735; 1978 UJ 755

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Corporate

Summary: Constitution - Industries (Development And Regulation) Act, 1951 - Companies Act, 1956, s. 10E - Interlocutory order of stay - Appeal against u/arts. 136 and 226 of the Constitution - Scope for interference - Interim stay of operation of Company Law Board's order of induction of directors - Legality of the stay order is to be checked - Held , ad interim order of HC was liable to be set aside - Appeal Allowed.
