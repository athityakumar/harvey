Ramanbhai Nagjibhai Patel v Jashwant Singh Udesingh and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 February 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

N.L. Untwalia, V.D. Tulzapurkar

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 439; (1978) 3 SCC 142; AIR 1978 SC 1162; 1978 (19) GLR 833; 1978 UJ 340

Case Digest

Subject: Election

Summary: Representation of the People Act, 1951, ss.123(1)(A)(b) and 116A - Election - Appeal - Interference sought - When proof regarding corrupt practice of bribery found to be beyond reasonable doubt - And finding of High Court was erroneous - Supreme Court cannot lightly uphold the finding - Held, finding can be interfered with in appeal - Appeal Allowed.
