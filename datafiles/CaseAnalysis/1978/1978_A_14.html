Hussainbhai, Calicut v Alath Factory Thozhilali Union, Kozhikode and

Supreme Court of India

28 July 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, D.A. Desai, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 184; (1978) 4 SCC 257; (1978) SCC (L&S) 506; AIR 1978 SC 1410; 1978 (37) FLR 136; 1978 LabIC 1264; 1978 (2) LLJ 397; 1978 (2) LLN 276; [1978] 3 S.C.R. 1073; 1978 SLJ 675; 1978 UJ 565

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 2(s) and 2(g) - Test for determining who is workman and employer - Presence of intermediate contractors - Held, relationship ex-contractu is of no consequence - When, on lifting the veil or looking at the conspectus of factors governing employment, it is found, that the real employer is the Management, not the immediate contractor - Petition Dismissed.
