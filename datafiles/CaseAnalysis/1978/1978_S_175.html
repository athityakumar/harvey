Bhawarlal Ganeshmalji v State of Tamil Nadu and Another

Supreme Court of India

11 December 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, N.L. Untwalia

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 203; (1979) SCC (Cr) 318; (1979) 1 SCC 465; AIR 1979 SC 541; 1979 CRLJ 462; 1979 CrLR(SC) 380; [1979] 2 S.C.R. 633

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Fema

Summary: Conservation of Foreign Exchange And Prevention Of Smuggling Activities Act, 1974, ss. 3 and 4 - Smuggling Activities Act, 1974, ss. 3 and 4 - Habeas Corpus - Appellant detained under COFEPOSA - Plea, delay in arrest - Not proper representation in regard to furnishing essential particulars regarding grounds of detention - Held, delay was due to abscondance of detenu - Not asking for particulars is his fault - Detention proper.
