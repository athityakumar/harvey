Crompton Greaves Limited v Workmen

Supreme Court of India

03 April 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 32; (1978) 3 SCC 155; (1978) SCC (L&S) 447; AIR 1978 SC 1489; 1978 (36) FLR 329; 1978 LabIC 1379; 1978 (2) LLJ 80; 1978 (2) LLN 455; 1978 UJ 366

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 15, 24 and sch.2, item 5 - Constitution, art. 136 - Uttar Pradesh Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - Test for determining , whether strike is illegal or unjustified - Company failing to prove that the workmen resorted to force and violence during the period in question - Tribunal holding it legal and that workmen were entitled for wages for that period - Validity - Tribunal held, was justified in holding so - Supreme Court declined to interfere - Apeal Dismissed.
