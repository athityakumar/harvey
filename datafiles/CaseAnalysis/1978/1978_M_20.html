Lalla Ram v Management of D. C. M. Chemical Works Limited and another

Supreme Court of India

16 February 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

Jaswant Singh, Y.V. Chandrachud

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 26; (1978) 3 SCC 1; (1978) SCC (L&S) 396; AIR 1978 SC 1004; 1978 (36) FLR 258; 1978 LabIC 716; 1978 (1) LLJ 507; 1978 (2) LLN 1; [1978] 3 S.C.R. 82; 1978 UJ 185

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 33(2)(b) - Uttar Pradesh Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 - Industrial Dispute - Approval of action taken by management - Scope of enquiry by tribunal into dismissal of employee for misconduct - Held, jurisdiction of Industrial Tribunal is a limited one - Appeal dismissed.
