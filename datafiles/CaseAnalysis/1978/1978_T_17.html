Management Of Monghyr Factory Of Itc Ltd.,Monghyr,Bihar v The Presiding Officer, Labour Court Patna (Bihar)& 3 Ors

Supreme Court of India

24 July 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

N.L. Untwalia, R.S. Pathak, Jaswant Singh

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 298; (1978) SCC (L&S) 465; (1978) 3 SCC 504; AIR 1978 SC 1428; 1978 (37) FLR 98; 1978 LabIC 1256; 1978 (2) LLJ 354; 1978 (2) LLN 289; [1978] 3 S.C.R. 1044; 1978 UJ 574

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution - Constitution, 1950, arts.132 and 133 - Grant of certificate by High Court - Grounds - Held, High Court while granting the certificate should consider whether the case raises a substantial question of law and if a single such question of law is found to arise in the case, a certificate must be granted - Appeal allowed.
