Commissioner of Income Tax, West Bengal Iii v Rajendra Prasad Moody and others

Supreme Court of India

04 October 1978

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, V.D. Tulzapurkar, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1978 Indlaw SC 216; (1979) SCC (Tax) 43; (1979) 1 SCC 250; AIR 1979 SC 373; 1978 (7) CTR 141; [1978] 115 ITR 519; [1979] 1 S.C.R. 1047; 1979 TAXLR 123; 1978 UJ 825

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961 - Interpretation of s. 57(iii) - Deductions - Admissibility of interest on moneys borrowed for investment in shares which had not yielded any dividend - Held, is admissible u/s. 57 (iii) - Order accordingly.
