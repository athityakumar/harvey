Paragon Rubber Industries and another v Pragathi Rubber Mills and another

Supreme Court of India

29 November 2013

Case Analysis

Bench

Surinder Singh Nijjar, A.K. Sikri

Where Reported

2013 Indlaw SC 782; (2014) 14 SCC 762; 2014 (102) ALR 235; 2014 (1) AWC 805; 2014 (1) CLT(SC) 23; 2014 (3) Law Herald (P&H) 2383; 2013 (8) MLJ 761; 2014 (57) PTC 1; 2014 (2) RCR(Civil) 767; 2013(14) SCALE 273

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Intellectual Property

Summary: Copyright Act, 1957 (1957 Act)-Section 62 (2)-Trade and Merchandise Marks Act, 1958 (1958 Act)-Trade Marks Act, 1999 (1999 Act)-Sections 134 and 159 (4)-Composite suit claiming relief under 1957 Act and 1958 Act-Maintainability-From averments made in plaint, it is apparent that plaintiff filed composite suit-Such suit not maintainable unless court has jurisdiction to entertain suit in relation to entire cause of action and entire relief-High Court rightly held that composite suit claiming relief under 1957 Act and 1958 Act would not be maintainable-No error in impugned judgment of High Court.
