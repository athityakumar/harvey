Manish Trivedi v State of Rajasthan

Supreme Court of India

29 October 2013

Case Analysis

Bench

Chandramauli Kumar Prasad, Jagdish Singh Khehar

Where Reported

2013 Indlaw SC 711; (2014) 14 SCC 420; AIR 2014 SC 648; 2014 CRLJ 429; 2014 (1) JCC 83; 2013 (4) MLJ(Crl) 539; 2013 (4) RCR(Criminal) 1031; 2014 (2) RLW 1823; 2013(13) SCALE 328; [2013] 12 S.C.R. 205

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Illegal Gratification, Dropping of proceeding

Summary: Criminal - Practice & Procedure - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 21 - Prevention of Corruption Act, 1988, ss. 13(1)(d), 13(2), 2(c), 2(c)(viii), 7 - Rajasthan Municipalities Act, 1959, s. 87 - Illegal gratification - Dropping of proceeding - Denial - Justifiability - Accused/appellant was Councilor elected to Municipal Council - A case was registered against appellant u/ss. 13(1)(d), 13(2), 2(c), 2(c)(viii), 7 of the Act - Appellant filed an application before Trial Court for dropping proceeding against him as he does not come within definition of public servant and could not be put on trial - Trial Court rejected the application - Aggrieved appellant appealed before HC and same was dismissed - Hence instant appeal - Whether impugned order passed by Trial Court was justified - Held, municipal councilor or member of Board does not come within definition of public servant as defined u/s. 21 of IPC, but in view of legal fiction created by s. 87 of 1959 Act, they come within its definition - S. 87 of 1959 Act, made Councilor and member of Board a public servant within meaning of s. 21 of IPC - Meaning of expression public servant as defined u/s. 2(c) of the Act and, hence decisions rendered by SC while interpreting s. 21 of IPC, which in substance and content were substantially different than s. 2(c) of the Act, must have no bearing at all for decision in present case - As trial was pending since long, SC deemed it expedient that Trial Court must an endeavor to dispose of trial expeditiously and in no case later than six months from date of receipt of copy of this order - Appeal dismissed.
