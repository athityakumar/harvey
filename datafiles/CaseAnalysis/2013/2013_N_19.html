Union of India and another v Namit Sharma

Supreme Court of India

03 September 2013

Case Analysis

Bench

A.K. Patnaik, A.K. Sikri

Where Reported

2013 Indlaw SC 559; (2013) 10 SCC 359; AIR 2014 SC 122; 2013 (6) ALD(SC) 88; 2013 (6) ALT(SC) 6; 2013 (6) AWC 5974; 2013 (4) CLT(SC) 1; 2013 (4) CompLJ 447; JT 2013 (12) SC 276; 2013 (4) RCR(Civil) 223; 2013(11) SCALE 85; 2014 (2) SLR 383; 2013 (4) UPLBEC 3264

Case Digest

Subject: Administrative; Constitution

Keywords: Companies (Amendment) Act, 2002

Summary: Right to Information Act, 2005-Sections 12 (5), 12 (6), 15 (5) and 15 (6)-Constitutional validity of Sections 12 (5), 12 (6), 15 (5) and 15 (6) on the anvil of Article 14 of the Constitution-Held-Aforesaid sections not violative of Article 14 of the Constitution-Cannot be struck down as ultra vires of Article 14 of the Constitution-Judgment under review suffers from mistake of law-Directions and declarations in judgment under review-Recalled-Review petitions allowed-Writ petition disposed of with declarations and directions.
