K. N. Aswathnarayana Setty (Dead) and others v State of Karnataka and others

Supreme Court of India

02 December 2013

Case Analysis

Bench

Balbir Singh Chauhan, Sharad Arvind Bobde

Where Reported

2013 Indlaw SC 786; AIR 2014 SC 279; 2014(4) ALL MR 901; 2014 (102) ALR 281; 2014 (1) AWC 217; 2014 (1) CGLJ 113; 2014 (1) CLT(SC) 18; 2014 (2) CTC 86; 2014 (2) KarLJ 1; 2014 (1) RCR(Civil) 533; 2014 (122) RD 395; 2013(14) SCALE 565; [2013] 11 S.C.R. 1029

Case Digest

Subject: Administrative; Land & Property

Keywords: Legality, De-notification

Summary: Land Acquisition Act, 1894-Sections 48, 16 and 17-Acquisition of land-Release from acquisition-De-notifying suit land from acquisition-At behest of then owners of suit land Government de-notified land from acquisition issuing notification under Section 48-In case, where possession of land taken, application for release of land from acquisition-Not maintainable-Ample evidence on record to show that possession of suit land taken-In such fact-situation question of de-notifying acquisition of land could not arise-Order of Revenue Minister directing de-notifying acquisition of land-Not sustainable.
