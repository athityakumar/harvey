Digamber and others v State of Maharashtra and others

Supreme Court of India

01 August 2013

Case Analysis

Bench

V. Gopala Gowda, G.S. Singhvi

Where Reported

2013 Indlaw SC 499; (2013) 14 SCC 406; AIR 2013 SC 3532; 2013 (100) ALR 393; 2013 (6) AWC 6442; 2013 (6) Bom.C.R. 136; JT 2013 (11) SC 278; 2013 (121) RD 107; 2013(10) SCALE 77; [2013] 7 S.C.R. 1037

Case Digest

Subject: Administrative; Land & Property

Summary: Land Acquisition Act, 1894-Section 23-Compensation for land acquired-Determination of market value-Acquisition of land for purpose of formation of industrial estate and sale of plots for commercial purposes-Compensation awarded by Special Land Acquisition Officer at R 50,000 per hectare-Special Land Acquisition Officer while determining market value considered acquired land as agricultural land and awarded inadequate compensation-Special Land Acquisition Officer ignored potentiality of acquired land-Reference court rightly placed reliance upon sale instances for compensation with that of acquired land after satisfying fact that acquired land also has non-agricultural potentiality-Reference court noticed that there is trend of escalation of price of land situated in proximity of acquired land and found fault with Land Acquisition Officer in not determining market value of acquired land at rate of R 5 per sq. feet after deducting 40% area of acquired land which is used for purpose of development-Reasons recorded by reference court in determining market value of acquired land-Well founded and based on facts-Land Acquisition Officer in casual manner rejected claim of appellants and determined meagre sum of R 50,000 per hectare as market value of land-Which in unrealistic and contrary to legal evidence on record and law laid down by Apex Court-Findings of fact recorded by reference court on relevant issue-Erroneously set aside by High Court without assigning valid reasons-Just and proper to restore judgment and award of reference court-Appellants entitled for compensation as awarded by reference court.
