Minu Rout and another v Satya Pradyumna Mohapatra and others

Supreme Court of India

02 September 2013

Case Analysis

Bench

V. Gopala Gowda, G.S. Singhvi

Where Reported

2013 Indlaw SC 573; (2013) 10 SCC 695; 2013 ACJ 2544; AIR 2013 SC (Supp) 62; 2013 (6) ALD(SC) 115; 2013 (101) ALR 229; 2013 (6) AWC 5441; JT 2013 (12) SC 254; 2014 (1) Law Herald (P&H) 293; 2014 (2) MahLJ 534; 2014 (2) MPLJ 129; 2013 (2) OLR 811; 2013 (4) RCR(Civil) 871; 2013(11) SCALE 112; [2013] 10 S.C.R. 847

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Insurance

Summary: Motor Vehicles Act, 1988-Sections 166 and 168-Compensation-Death case-Enhancement-Head-on-collision between offending truck and car-Deceased at relevant time working as driver of car which is skilled job-Appellants dependents upon earnings of deceased-Tribunal awarded compensation of R 2,00,000 with interest @ 6% p.a.-High Court affirmed judgment and award of Tribunal-Whether impugned judgments and awards of Tribunal and High Court calls for any interference and case of enhancement made out?-Held, "yes"-Finding recorded by Tribunal that there was 50% contributory negligence of both drivers of offending truck and deceased-Erroneous-50% deduction out of total loss of dependency compensation determined by Tribunal-Not correct-Appellants entitled to full amount of R 9,98,400 towards loss of dependency-Tribunal erroneously awarded sum of R 5,000 for funeral expenses without taking into consideration actual amount required to be spent towards funeral expenses and obsequies ceremonies-Tribunal also inadequately awarded R 3,000 towards loss of love and affection for surviving child and wife-Applying Susamma Thomas case, (1994) 2 SCC 176, R 50,000 just and reasonable under conventional heads-Total compensation comes to tune of R 10,48,400 with interest @ 9% p.a. instead of 6% p.a.-Compensation amount enhanced by sum of R 8,48,400-Impugned judgments and awards of Tribunal and High Court-Set aside.
