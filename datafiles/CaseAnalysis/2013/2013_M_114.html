Daljit Kaur and another v Muktar Steels Private Limited and others

Supreme Court of India

19 November 2013

Case Analysis

Bench

Dipak Misra

Where Reported

2013 Indlaw SC 787; (2013) 16 SCC 607; 2014 (102) ALR 275; 2014 (2) ALT(SC) 1; 2014 (1) AWC 909; 2014 (2) CLT(SC) 100; 2014 (1) CTC 105; JT 2014 (1) SC 309; 2014 (2) Law Herald (P&H) 1172; 2013 (8) MLJ 755; 2014 (1) OLR 595; 2014 (1) RCR(Civil) 625; 2014 (122) RD 390; 2013(14) SCALE 527; 2014 (2) WLN(SC) 9

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Code of Civil Procedure, 1908-Section 96 (3) and Order XXIII, Rule 3, proviso-Consent decree-Appeal against consent decree-Maintainability-Trial court, after conducting due enquiry envisaged under proviso to Order XXIII, Rule 3 passed consent decree-Consent decree is not appealable being barred under Section 96 (3)-High Court in second appeal concurred with conclusion of first appellate court that decree in question being consent decree was not assailable in appeal-No fault found with impugned order of High Court.
