Dr. Poonam Tandon v Chancellor, Lucknow University and others

Supreme Court of India

28 November 2013

Case Analysis

Bench

Sharad Arvind Bobde, Balbir Singh Chauhan

Where Reported

2013 Indlaw SC 795; (2014) 13 SCC 795; 2014 (1) AWC 977; 2013(14) SCALE 499; 2014 (5) SLR 7

Case Digest

Subject: Education; Service

Summary: Employment - Appointment-Chancellor approved appointment of appellant by holding that appellant possessed prescribed and required qualification for post of Professor of Physics (Specialisation in Experimental Solid State Physics)-Chancellor passed order on reference made under Section 31 (8) (a) of U. P. State Universities Act, 1973-Order of Chancellor, unexceptionable, which must be upheld-No reason for High Court to interfere with order of Chancellor-Impugned judgment of High Court-Set aside-University authorities to act in accordance with decision of Chancellor and give effect to directions issued by Chancellor.
