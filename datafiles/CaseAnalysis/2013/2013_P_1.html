P. Dasaratharama Reddy Complex v Government of Karnataka and another

Supreme Court of India

25 October 2013

Case Analysis

Bench

G.S. Singhvi, V. Gopala Gowda, C. Nagappan

Where Reported

2013 Indlaw SC 701; (2014) 2 SCC 201; AIR 2014 SC 168; 2014(2) ALL MR 448; 2014 (2) AWC 1412; 2013(13) SCALE 179

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Contract & Commercial

Summary: Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996-Sections 11 (6) and (8)-Appointment of Arbitrator-Order passed by Designated Judge for appointment of Chief Engineer as Arbitrator and directed him to adjudicate matter in dispute-Relevant clauses in agreements neither postulate hearing of parties by Chief Engineer nor he can adjudicate dispute-Relevant clauses in agreements entered into between parties provide for settlement of disputes through court-Appellant at liberty to avail appropriate legal remedy for recovery of due amount, if any.
