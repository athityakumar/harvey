Pt. Ravishankar Shukla University and another v Gopal Mishra

Supreme Court of India

16 August 2013

Case Analysis

Bench

Madan B. Lokur, R.M. Lodha

Where Reported

2013 Indlaw SC 510; (2014) 14 SCC 680; 2013 (6) AWC 5670; 2013(10) SCALE 437; 2014 (3) SLR 380

Case Digest

Subject: Education

Keywords: Issue of mark sheet, Ordinance of University

Summary: Education-Examination-M.Com Examination of University-Issuance of mark-sheet to student appearing in subsequent examination held for improving division of M.Com Examination-Ordinance No. 24 of University does not oblige it to issue fresh mark-sheet to student who does not improve his division on taking subsequent examination held for improving division-Ordinance does not postulate giving student opportunity merely for improving marks without any improvement in division obtained-Factually, respondent improved upon marks earlier obtained by him-But he did not improve his division despite improvement in marks-Since respondent did not improve his division-Respondent not entitled to fresh degree in terms of Clause 9 (vii) of Ordinance-Ordinance does not postulate any obligation on University to issue fresh mark-sheet-Direction of High Court to University for issuing fresh mark-sheet-Not justified-No option but to set aside impugned judgment and order passed by High Court.
