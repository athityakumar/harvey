State of Uttar Pradesh and others v Ashok Kumar Srivastava and another

Supreme Court of India

21 August 2013

Case Analysis

Bench

Dipak Misra

Where Reported

2013 Indlaw SC 523; (2014) 14 SCC 720; AIR 2013 SC (Supp) 513; 2014 (1) AWC 140; JT 2013 (11) SC 451; 2013(10) SCALE 505; [2013] 11 S.C.R. 846; 2013 (5) SLR 485; 2013 (4) UPLBEC 2746

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: U. P. Ayurvedic Aur Unani Mahavidyalaya Adhyapakon Ki Seva Niyamawali, 1990-Rule 21-Employment - Seniority-Determination - Retrospective seniority-Claim of first respondent for conferment of retrospective seniority-Untenable-Seniority has to be decided on basis of rules in force on date of appointment and no retrospective promotion or seniority can be granted from date when employee not even born in cadre-Unless otherwise stipulated in letter of appointment-Seniority has to be computed from date of appointment to post-In instant case, nothing has been stipulated in letter of appointment-High Court fallen into error by granting retrospective seniority with consequential benefits-Impugned order of High Court-Set aside.
