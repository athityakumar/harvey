G. Sadanandan v State of Kerala and Another

Supreme Court of India

11 February 1966

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar

Where Reported

1966 Indlaw SC 363; AIR 1966 SC 1925; 1966 CRLJ 1533; [1966] 3 S.C.R. 590

Case Digest

Subject: Defence & Security Forces

Summary: Miscellaneous - Defence of India Rules, 1962, r. 30 - Travancore-Cochin Security Prisoners Order, 1950 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 32 - Whether detention u/r. 30(1)(b) of the Defence of India Rules, 1962 was legally correct? - No, Court held, appropriate authority should place before Court sufficient material to show that allegations made by the petitioner are not well-founded but failure of respondent No. 1 to place any such material, therefore plea made by petitioner that order of detention passed against him are totally invalid and unjustified, accepted - Petition allowed.
