Fakhruddin v State of Madhya Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

13 December 1966

Case Analysis

Bench

Mohammad Hidayatullah, Vishishtha Bhargava

Where Reported

1966 Indlaw SC 403; AIR 1967 SC 1326; 1967 CRLJ 1197; 1967 MPLJ 473

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Evidence Act, 1872, ss. 45, 47 and 73 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 465, 467, 417, 419, 471 and 120 B - Conspiracy, forgery, cheating, personation - Held, offence of forgery proved beyond reasonable doubt with help of handwriting expert - Conspiracy not proved because appellant is only one convicted - Sentence u/s 120 B quashed, other convictions will stand - Appeal dismissed.
