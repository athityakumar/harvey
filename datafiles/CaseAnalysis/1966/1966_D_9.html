Ghanshiam Das v Devi Prasad and Another

Supreme Court of India

11 March 1966

Case Analysis

Bench

Vaidynathier Ramaswami

Where Reported

1966 Indlaw SC 130; AIR 1966 SC 1998; 1966 ALJ 536; [1966] 3 S.C.R. 875

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Uttar Pradesh Zamidari Abolition and Land Reforms Act, 1951, ss. 6 and 9 - Respondents are owners of a brick kiln located on two plots, which they leased out to appellant under a registered lease deed D/- 29-12-1950 - Respondents filed a suit for recovery of arrears of rent from 01-10-1952 to 30-09-1953 - Appellant pleaded that after passing of Act, plots of land had vested in State of U.P. u/s. 6 of Act with effect from 01-07-1952 and respondents were not entitled to claim any rent from appellant - Whether kiln leased out to appellant is a 'building' within meaning of s. 9 of Act? - Held, brick kiln has no walls and no roof but it is a mere pit dug in ground with bricks by its side - There was no structure standing on Bhatta - Brick kiln leased out to appellant, in present case, is not a 'building' within meaning of s.9 of Act - Title to both plots along with brick kiln vested in State Govt. with effect from 01-10-1952 and respondents are not entitled to claim any rent from appellant - Appeal allowed.
