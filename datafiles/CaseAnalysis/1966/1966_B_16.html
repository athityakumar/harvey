Abdul Waheed Khan v Bhawani and Others

Supreme Court of India

21 February 1966

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Subba Rao, Vaidynathier Ramaswami

Where Reported

1966 Indlaw SC 360; AIR 1966 SC 1718; 1966 JabLJ 1022; 1966 MPLJ 954; [1966] 3 S.C.R. 617

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Civil Procedure Code, 1908, s. 9 - Whether a civil court had jurisdiction to entertain suit filed by respondents for recovery of possession of plaint-schedule land and mesne profits? - Held, u/s. 200(1) r/w sub-ss. 71, 89, 93, and 95 of Bhopal State Land Revenue Act, 1932 did not limit jurisdiction of Civil Court to entertain suit based on title - Appeal dismissed.
