 State of Bihar v Rambalak Singh and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 January 1966

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, S.M. Sikri, Vaidynathier Ramaswami, P. Satyanarayana Raju, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1966 Indlaw SC 386; AIR 1966 SC 1441; 1966 CRLJ 1076; [1966] 3 S.C.R. 344

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Keywords: Defence Of India Act, 1971

Summary: Constitution of India, 1950, art. 136 - Defence of India Rules, r. 30 - whether while entertaining a habeas corpus petition u/art. 226 of the Constitution filed on behalf of a detenu who has been detained u/r. 30 of the Defence of Indian Rules - The High Court has jurisdiction to release the detenu on bail pending the final disposal of the said habeas corpus petition? - Held, jurisdiction of HC to grant bail subject to proper and valid order passed - Appeal dismissed.

ICPL HEADNOTE

Respondent released on bail - Whether while entertaining a habeas corpus petition under art. 226 of the Constitution of India, 1950 filed on behalf of a detenu who has been detained u/r. 30 of the Defence of Indian Rules (hereinafter called the 'Rules'), the High Court has jurisdiction to release the detenu on bail pending the final disposal of the said habeas corpus petition?
