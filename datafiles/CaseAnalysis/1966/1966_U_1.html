Union Of India v The Metal Corporation Of India Ltd. & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

05 September 1966

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Subba Rao, J.M. Shelat

Where Reported

1966 Indlaw SC 63; AIR 1967 SC 637; [1967] 37 Comp Cas 1; 1967 (1) CompLJ 43; [1967] 1 S.C.R. 255

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Constitution; Corporate; Land & Property

Keywords: Debt Laws

Summary: Metal Corporation of India (Acquisition of Undertaking) Act, 1965, Pre. and sch. Para II, cl.(b) - Constitution, art. 31(2) -Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 32 and 43(6) - Rajasthan Land Acquisition Act, 1953, s. 23 - Validity of 1965 Act - Challenged on the ground that the Act does not provide for 'compensation' within meaning of art. 31 (2) of the constitution - Relation of Rule of depreciation allowed under Income Tax Act with the value of assets -

Act, held to be void - No relation to value of assets - Appeal Dismissed.
