Commissioner of Taxes Assam, Shillong v Prabhat Marketing Company Limited, Gauhati

Supreme Court of India

27 October 1966

Case Analysis

Bench

Vaidynathier Ramaswami, J.C. Shah, Vishishtha Bhargava

Where Reported

1966 Indlaw SC 273; AIR 1967 SC 602; [1967] 1 S.C.R. 961; 1967 (19) STC 84

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - Indirect Tax - Bombay Sales Act, 1953 - Assam Sales Tax Act, 1947, s. 32 - Whether value of containers of hydrogenated oil is assessable to sales-tax under Assam Sales Tax Act, 1947? - Mere fact that price of containers was not separately fixed would make no difference to assessment of sales-tax - Question as to whether there is an agreement to sell packing material is a pure question of fact depending, upon circumstances found in each case - Held, value of containers of hydrogenated oil is assessable to sales tax under Act if there is an express or implied agreement for sale of such containers - Appeal allowed.
