 Katra Educational Society v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 January 1966

Case Analysis

Bench

J.C. Shah, S.M. Sikri, Vaidynathier Ramaswami, P. Satyanarayana Raju, Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar

Where Reported

1966 Indlaw SC 388; AIR 1966 SC 1307; [1966] 3 S.C.R. 328

Case Digest

Subject: Education

Keywords: Scheme Of Administration

Summary: Education - Societies Registration Act, ss. 7, 8, 16(4) and (5) - Intermediate Education Act, s. 16 A to 161 - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 14, 19(1)(c), 19(1)(f), 31 and 226 - Whether Temporary deprivation of management to secure compliance with provisions of Education Act amounts to infringement art.19 and 31 of Constitution? - Held, temporary deprivation of management to secure compliance with provisions of Education Act does not amount to deprivation of property of educational institution which may attract protection guaranteed by art. 19 or 31 of Constitution - Appeal dismissed.
