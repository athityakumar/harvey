 Prabhu v Ramdev and Others

Supreme Court of India

28 February 1966

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, Kailas Nath Wanchoo, S.M. Sikri, Mohammad Hidayatullah, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1966 Indlaw SC 351; AIR 1966 SC 1721; [1966] 3 S.C.R. 676

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Rajasthan Tenancy Act, 1955, s. 15 - Whether respondents, who have been inducted into land as tenants by usufructuary mortgagee, can be ejected by appellant on ground that mortgage in question has been redeemed? - As soon as s. 15 came into operation on 15-10-1955, respondents' possession was substantially altered and they became Khatedars by virtue of s. 15 and cannot be ejected except under provisions of Act in view of s. 161 - Held, view taken by Board about status of respondents is right - Appeal dismissed.
