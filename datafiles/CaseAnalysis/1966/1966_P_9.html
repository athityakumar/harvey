Parekh Wadilal Jivanbhai v Commissioner of Income Tax, M. P. Nagpurand Bhandara, Nagpur

Supreme Court of India

28 October 1966

Case Analysis

Bench

Vaidynathier Ramaswami, Vishishtha Bhargava, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1966 Indlaw SC 124; AIR 1967 SC 448; [1967] 63 ITR 485; [1967] 1 S.C.R. 998

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Indian Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 26 A - Whether on basis of partnership deed under reference, can a firm be granted registration u/s. 26 A? - Partnership deed clearly specified shares of partners in profits and loss - Held, referred partnership deed was meeting requirements of s. 26 A and assessee firm was entitled to registration for relevant assessment year - Appeal allowed.
