Jiwanlal Achariya v Rameshwarlal Agarwalla

Supreme Court of India

26 August 1966

Case Analysis

Bench

Kailas Nath Wanchoo, J.C. Shah, R.S. Bachawat

Where Reported

1966 Indlaw SC 324; AIR 1967 SC 1118; [1966] 36 Comp Cas 866; [1967] 1 S.C.R. 190

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance

Summary: Banking & Finance - Maintainability of suit - Bihar Money-Lenders (Regulation of Transactions) Act, 1939 - Plaintiff-respondent was registered as money-lender in 1952 - Promissory note executed in 1954 - Suit was maintainable as it was based on loan advanced after registration as money-lender - Banking & Finance - Maintainability of suit - Indian Limitation Act, 1908, s. 20 - Date of payment of debt - When post-dated cheque delivered - Not the date mentioned on cheque or date when it was cashed - Suit barred by limitation - Appeal dismissed.
