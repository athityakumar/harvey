D.K. Soni v P.K. Mukerjee And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

27 October 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28449; (1988) 1 SCC 29; AIR 1988 SC 30; 1988 (1) AWC 421; JT 1987 (4) SC 225; 1988 (1) RCR(Rent) 466; 1987 (3) RentLR 648; 1987(2) SCALE 887; [1988] 1 S.C.R. 616

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Rent Control - Uttar Pradesh Urban Buildings (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972, ss. 43(2)(rr), 3(g) and 21 - Uttar Pradesh(Temporary) Control of Rent and Eviction Act, 1947, s. 3 - (A) Execution of eviction order passed under old Act which become final - Consideration of subsequent events - (B) Tenant - Heirs - Son and daughter of tenant not residing with him at time of his death - Whether heirs under provisions of 1972 Act? - Held, cannot be considered in view of s. 43(2)(rr) - Are not heirs as contemplated by s. 3(g) - Appeal dismissed.
