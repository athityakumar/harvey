State Of Karnataka v K. Gopalakrishna Shenoy And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

15 July 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Natarajan, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28314; (1987) SCC (Cr) 653; (1987) 3 SCC 655; AIR 1987 SC 1911; 1987 (2) Crimes 879; 1987 CRLR 480; 1987 CrLR(SC) 480; JT 1987 (3) SC 67; JT 1987 (3) SC 67B; 1987(2) SCALE 37; [1987] 3 S.C.R. 481

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Carriers & Transportation - Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1957, ss. 3(1), explanations 7 and 4 - Pronouncement of court sought on scope and effect of s. 3(1) of Mysore Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1957 - Held, s. 3(1) and its explanation have to be construed on its own force and not with reference to s. 38 of Motor Vehicle Act - Appeal allowed.
