Osmania University Teachers Association v State Of Andhra Pradesh And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

13 August 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, M.H. Kania, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28366; (1987) 4 SCC 671; AIR 1987 SC 2034; 1988 (1) BankCLR 124; JT 1987 (3) SC 424; 1987(2) SCALE 289; [1987] 3 S.C.R. 949; 1987 (4) SLR 581; 1987 (2) UJ 490

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Keywords: Andhra Pradesh Education Act, 1982, Andhra Pradesh Commissioner of Higher Education Act 1986

Summary: Constitution - University Grants Commission Act, 1956 - Constitution of India, 1950, sch. 7, list 1, entry 66, list 3, entry 25 - Validity and Constitutionality of Commissionerate Act of 1986 - Commissionerate Act is more or less similar to UGC Act - Convey same meaning - Held, Commissionerate Act is beyond legislative competence of State Legislature and is declared void and inoperative - Appeal allowed.
