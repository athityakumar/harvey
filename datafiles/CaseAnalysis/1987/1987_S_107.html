M.P.E.B. And Others v Smt. Basantibai

Supreme Court of India

10 November 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

B.C. Ray, K. Jagannatha Shetty

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28428; (1988) 1 SCC 23; AIR 1988 SC 71; 1988 BLJR 104; 1988 (2) CCC 93; 1988 CRLR 65; 1988 CrLR(SC) 65; JT 1987 (4) SC 294; 1987(2) SCALE 985; [1988] 1 S.C.R. 890; 1988 (1) UJ 44

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Criminal; Land & Property

Keywords: Land Acquisition

Summary: Electricity Act, 1910, s. 26(6) - Scope of act was in question in a dispute whether meter in question was correct one or a faulty one - Held, matter had to be decided by Electrical Inspector and during pendency of dispute, Electricity Board could not issue supplementary bill or threaten to disconnect supply of electricity.
