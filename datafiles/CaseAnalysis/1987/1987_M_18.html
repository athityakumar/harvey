Union Of India v M.A. Chowdhary

Supreme Court of India

07 May 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, M.M. Dutt

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28296; (1987) 4 SCC 112; (1987) SCC (L&S) 379; AIR 1987 SC 1526; 1987 (4) ATC 658; 1987 LabIC 1038; 1987 (2) LLN 684; 1987(1) SCALE 1254; [1987] 3 S.C.R. 424; 1988 (1) SLJ 43; 1987 (2) SLR 592; 1987 (2) UJ 322

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Constitution - Constitution of India , 1950, art. 311 - Held, appellants submits that art. 311 of the Constitution is applicable to Staff Artists of All India Radio. SC was in view that statement made on behalf of the Government represents true legal position because Staff Artists are holding civil posts under Government. Instant appeal filed against judgment of HC in appeal which has also taken the view that art. 311 of the Constitution is applicable to those Staff Artists has to be dismissed. Appeal dismissed.
