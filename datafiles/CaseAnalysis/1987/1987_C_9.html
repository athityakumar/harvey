Capt. (Now Major) Ashok Kshyap v Mrs. Sudha Vasisht And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

04 February 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, S. Natarajan

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28650; (1987) 1 SCC 717; AIR 1987 SC 841; 1987 (1) CCC 585; JT 1987 (1) SC 350; 1987(1) SCALE 265; [1987] 2 S.C.R. 151; 1987 (1) UJ 579

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Arbitration and ADR - Arbitration Act, 1940 - Registration Act, 1908 - (A) Whether the award could not be made a rule of the court because it effects the partition of immovable property and affects rights in immovable property - Held, document in question did not effect the partition if read properly - (B) Determination of need of registration of award - On an analysis of the award that it did not create any right in any immovable property and as such it was not compulsory to register it - Appeal allowed.
