Commlssloner Of Gift Tax, Gujarat v Executors And Trustees Of The Estate Oflate Sh. Ambalal Sarabh

Supreme Court of India

11 December 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

M.N. Venkatachaliah, S. Natarajan

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28125; (1988) Supp SCC 115; AIR 1988 SC 522; 1988 (67) CTR 247; [1988] 170 ITR 144; JT 1988 (1) SC 46; 1988(1) SCALE 6; [1988] 2 S.C.R. 341; [1988] 36 TAXMAN 162

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Gift Tax, Dividends, Gift Tax Act, 1958

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Gift Tax Act, 1958, s. 6 - Gift of shares - Valuation - Modes of - Held, parties can agree upon a principle permissible under and recognised by law - If two or more alternatives are equally valid and available, then any one of the modes can be agreed - Order accordingly.
