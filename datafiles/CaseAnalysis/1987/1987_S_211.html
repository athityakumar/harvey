Utkal Contractors and Joinery Private Limited and Others v State Of Orissa And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

07 May 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, V. Khalid

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28297; (1987) 3 SCC 279; AIR 1987 SC 1454; 1987 (1) BankCLR 1; 1987 (2) CCC 81; 1987 CRLJ 1860; JT 1987 (2) SC 466; 1987(1) SCALE 1162; [1987] 3 S.C.R. 317; 1987 (2) UJ 522

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Civil Procedure; Environment, Wildlife & Animal; Trade

Keywords: Orissa Forest Act, 1972

Summary: Environment, Wildlife & Animal - Orissa Forest Produce (Control Of Trade) Act, 1981, ss. 5(1)(a) and 5(31) - Interpretation of Statutes - Object of Act - Prevent smuggling of forest produce grown on Govt. land - While state enjoys monopoly in purchasing produce of private land - Grant of lease to a company for collecting sal seeds from Govt. forests - Lease agreement as to, if rescind by Act - Held, internal and external aids used for ascertaining reasons for enacting statute - On commencement of Act agreement granting lease does not stand rescinded - Appeal allowed.
