Subhash Bhandari and another v District Magistrate, Lucknow And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

03 November 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

B.C. Ray, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28080; (1987) 4 SCC 685; (1988) SCC (Cr) 36; AIR 1988 SC 74; 1988 AllCriC 48; 1988 (1) AWC 316; 1987 (3) Crimes 686; 1988 CRLJ 190; 1988 CrLR(SC) 61; JT 1987 (4) SC 277; 1987(2) SCALE 937; [1988] 1 S.C.R. 773

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - National Security Act, 1980, s. 3 - Ground for detention was in question - Whether maintenance of law and order was affected or not? - Criminal act emanated from business rivalry between detenu and complainant - Held, detention on this ground was illegal.
