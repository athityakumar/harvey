Nand Lal and Others v Sukh Dev and Another

Supreme Court of India

21 January 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Natarajan, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28541; (1987) Supp SCC 87

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Suit for redemption - Whether relationship of landlord and tenant must be treated to have come to an end when lessees accepted new relationship and became mortgagees of same property which they were holding as tenants? - Held, there is nothing to warrant an interference of relinquishment of rights of tenants by obtaining this mortgage deed - High Court was not right in coming to conclusion that on redemption of mortgage tenants right to possession ceased - Appeal allowed.
