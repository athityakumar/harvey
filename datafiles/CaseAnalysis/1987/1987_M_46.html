Bhag Singh and Others v Major Daljit Singh and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 February 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

B.C. Ray, S. Natarajan

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28855; (1987) Supp SCC 685; 1987 (11) ECR 510; 1987 (32) E.L.T. 258; JT 1987 (1) SC 701

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Civil Procedure Code, 1908, O. 22, r. 9 - Limitation Act, 1963, s. 5 - Delay in filing application for bringing legal representatives on record - Held, courts not to take too strict and pedantic stand which will cause injustice but to take a view which will advance cause of justice - Appellant has made out sufficient cause - Appeal allowed.
