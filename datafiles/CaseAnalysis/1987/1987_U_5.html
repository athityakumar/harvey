Shyamaraju Hegde v U. Venkatesha Bhat and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 September 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, M.M. Dutt

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28419; (1987) Supp SCC 321; AIR 1987 SC 2323; 1988 (2) CCC 257; JT 1987 (3) SC 663; 1987 (2) KLT 977; 1988 (2) RCJ 459; 1987 (2) RCR(Rent) 447; 1987 (3) RentLR 370; 1987(2) SCALE 646; [1988] 1 S.C.R. 340; 1988 (1) UJ 61

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure; Rent Control

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 115 - Karnataka Rent Control Act, 1961, s. 50(1)(2) - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 141- (A) Whether an Order passed by District Judge in revisional jurisdiction u/s. 50(2) of 1961 Act is reviseable - (B) Precedents - Statutory Provision - Full Bench took particular view on basis of Supreme Court decision - Later Full Bench disagreeing on basis that earlier Full Bench lost its binding value due to subsequent Supreme Court decisions rendered in different situations and setting - Permissibility - Held, is revisable by High Court u/s. 50(1) of 1961 Act r/w s. 115 of CPC - Not permissible - Appeal allowed.
