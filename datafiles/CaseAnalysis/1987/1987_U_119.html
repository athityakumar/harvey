State of Bihar and Another v Usha Martin Industries Limited

Supreme Court of India

25 February 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

K.N. Singh, R.S. Pathak, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28620; (1987) Supp SCC 710; 1987 (65) STC 430

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Summary: Civil Procedure - Held, appeal filed by appellant was concluded by law laid in Pournami Oil Mills v. State of Kerala where ratio laid down in M/s Motilal Padampat Sugar Mills Co. Ltd. v. State of U.P. was affirmed - It was not disputed by appellant - Appeal dismissed.
