Vimal Kumar Jain v Labour Court, Kanpur and Another

Supreme Court of India

20 January 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

V. Khalid, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28534; (1987) Supp SCC 40; (1987) SCC (L&S) 283; AIR 1988 SC 384

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Laws - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 2(s) - Question raised in this case is whether petitioner is a workman - When evidence is that he supervises work as Maintenance Engineer and he grants leave, initiates disciplinary proceedings etc. - He also has power to make temporary appointments - Held, not a workman - Petition dismissed.
