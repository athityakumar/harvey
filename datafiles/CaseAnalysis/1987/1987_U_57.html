Raja Ram Kumar Bhargava (Dead) By Lrs. v Union Of India

Supreme Court of India

11 December 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

M.N. Venkatachaliah, S. Natarajan

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28824; (1988) SCC (Tax) 132; (1988) 1 SCC 681; AIR 1988 SC 752; 1988 (68) CTR 174; [1988] 171 ITR 254; JT 1988 (1) SC 297B; 1988(1) SCALE 235; [1988] 2 S.C.R. 352; 1988 TAXLR 839; [1988] 36 TAXMAN 368

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Excess Profits Tax

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Excess Profits Tax Act, 1940, s. 21 - Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 67 - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 240, 244, 66(7) and 297(2)(i) - CPC,1908 - (A) 'Completed assessement' falling in s. 297(2)(i) of 1961 Act - Order of assessment passed by I.T.O. prior to commenccement of 1961 Act - Assessment Prior to Act - Reference reducing tax liability and order refund decided only after the commencement of 1961 Act - (B) CPC,1908 - Exclusion of Civil Courts's Jurisdiction - Inferrenced as to, when can be made - Held, matter falls u/cl. (i) of s.297 (2) and not u/cl. (a) and assessee not entitled to interest on refund of tax - Guiding considerations stated - Order accordingly.
