Asharfi Lal and Others v State of Uttar Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

30 March 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, V. Balakrishna Eradi

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28211; (1987) 3 SCC 224; (1987) SCC (Cr) 470; AIR 1987 SC 1721; 1987 CRLJ 1885; 1987 CRLR 331; 1987 CrLR(SC) 331; JT 1987 (2) SC 595; 1987(1) SCALE 766; [1987] 2 S.C.R. 722; 1987 (2) UJ 152

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 302 - Accused of murder of two small girls - Conviction awarded by High Court - Whether death sentence was proper? - Held, murder committed with extreme brutality to wreck their personal vengeance over dispute they had with regard to property with mother of victims - It is duty of court to impose a proper punishment depending upon degree of criminality and desirability to impose such punishment - Death sentence was proper - Appeal dismissed.
