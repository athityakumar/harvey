B. R. Mehta v Atma Devi and Others

Supreme Court of India

02 September 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28156; (1987) 4 SCC 183; AIR 1987 SC 2220; JT 1987 (3) SC 474; 1988 (1) RCJ 130; 1987 (2) RCR(Rent) 464; 1987 (2) RentLR 701; 1987(2) SCALE 436; [1987] 3 S.C.R. 1184; 1988 (1) UJ 23

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958, s. 14(1) (h) - Tenancy - Eviction of tenant sought on ground of alternative accommodation - Acquisition of official accomodation by wife of tenant - Entitlement of tenant to continue in tenancy - Held, cannot be disentitled to retain tenanted premises as husband, tenant have no right or domain or occupation over flat - Appeal allowed.
