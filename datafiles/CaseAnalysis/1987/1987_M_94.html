Messrs Shalimar Cinema v Bhasin Film Corporation and Another

Supreme Court of India

19 August 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, K. Jagannatha Shetty, M.H. Kania

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28380; (1987) 4 SCC 717; AIR 1987 SC 2081; JT 1987 (3) SC 362; 1987(2) SCALE 356; 1987 (2) UJ 508

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Keywords: Code of Civil Procedure 1908

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 - (A) Adjournment of Court auction, on spot - Bid list containing statement to that effect - Judgment-debtors questioning announcement on spot and putting it in issue in their objections to sale - Auction purchaser not examining auctioneer in support of case of announcement on spot - None of bidders in first auction participating in adjourned auction - Inference - (B) Requirement of r. 66 of O. 21 of Code - Held, inference is that no announcement as alleged was made - Court has a duty to see that they are complied with - Appeal allowed.
