Standard Fireworks Industries Sivakasi and Another v Collector of Central Excise, Madurai

Supreme Court of India

17 February 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28063; (1987) SCC (Tax) 138; (1987) 1 SCC 600; AIR 1987 SC 829; 1987 CRLR 200; 1987 (11) ECC 361; 1987 (11) ECR 358; 1987 (28) E.L.T. 56; JT 1987 (1) SC 460; 1987(1) SCALE 351; 1987 TAXLR 1893; 1987 (1) UJ 384

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - Andhra Pradesh General Sales Tax Act, 1957, s.2 (1) (s) (i) - Turnover - Whether insurance charges is a part of 'turnover' u/s.2(s) of the Act for the purposes of levy of sales tax - Finding of fact that insurance charges were claimed in the bills of sale - Held, includable in turnover for computing tax u/s. 2(1)(s)(i) - And there is no need to invoke cl. (iii)(c) - Appeal dismissed.
