Dr. D.C. Saxena v State Of Haryana And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

08 May 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

V. Khalid, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28352; (1987) SCC (L&S) 210; (1987) 3 SCC 251; AIR 1987 SC 1463; 1987 (3) ATC 814; 1987 (2) CCC 161; 1987 (55) FLR 145; JT 1987 (2) SC 425; 1987 LabIC 1018; 1987 (2) LLJ 360; 1987 (2) LLN 387; [1987] 3 S.C.R. 346; 1987 (2) SLJ 108; 1987 (2) UJ 292

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Education - Haryana Board of School Education Act, 1969, ss. 3(4), 4 A and 9 - Constitution of India, 1953 art. 226 - Termination of tenure of Chairman of education Board as per policy decision of State Govt. - (A) Principles of natural justice - Opportunity of tendering written explanation as envisaged by s. 9 not given to Chairman - Need of - (B) Interpretation of Statutes - Expression 'terms and conditions of service' u/s. 4 A of Act, if include tenure - Held, need not be given, respective field of application of ss. 4 A and 9 stated - Clearly includes tenure of service - Appeal dismissed.
