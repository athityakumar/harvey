Fazal Ghosi and others v State Of U.P. And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

29 June 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, V. Khalid

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28292; (1987) 3 SCC 502; (1987) SCC (Cr) 596; AIR 1987 SC 1877; 1987 (2) Crimes 892; 1987 CRLJ 1910; 1987 CrLR(SC) 470; JT 1987 (3) SC 22; 1987(2) SCALE 1; [1987] 3 S.C.R. 471

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Defence & Security Forces; Service

Keywords: Defence Regulations

Summary: National Security Act, 1980 - Whether detention under interest of national security valid or not ? - Detained person should be set at liberty - Held, order of detention in respect of each detenu quashed - Detenus are entitled to be set at liberty - Writ petitions and the appeals are allowed - Order of detention in respect of each detenu is quashed.
