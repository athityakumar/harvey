K.S. Vora And Others v State Of Gujarat And Others

Supreme Court of India

27 October 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28850; (1988) SCC (L&S) 256; (1988) 1 SCC 311; AIR 1987 SC 2348; 1987 (5) ATC 325; 1988 (1) CCC 836; 1988 (56) FLR 269; JT 1987 (4) SC 179; 1988 LabIC 43; 1988 (1) LLN 336; 1987(2) SCALE 835; [1988] 1 S.C.R. 611; 1989 (2) SLJ 190; 1987 (5) SLR 674; 1988 (1) UJ 199

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Gujarat Subordinate Secretariat Service (Seniority of Assistants) Rules 1977

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 309 and 311 - Gujarat Subordinate Secretariat Service (Seniority of Assistants) Rules, 1977, r. 4 - Service Rules - Civil Service - Seniority - Right of employer as to framing of rules - Prospects of promotion in future prejudiced by introduction of new rules - (A) Validity of r. 4 of 1977 Rules - (B) If Govt. can create common cadre - Held, r. 4 is valid - Govt. can create cadre - Appeal dismissed.
