Balram Gupta v Union Of India And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

01 September 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28862; (1987) Supp SCC 228; (1988) SCC (L&S) 126; AIR 1987 SC 2354; 1987 (5) ATC 246; 1987 (2) CCC 841; 1987 (55) FLR 855; JT 1987 (3) SC 480; 1988 LabIC 46; 1987 (2) LLJ 541; 1987 (2) LLN 855; 1987(2) SCALE 521; [1987] 3 S.C.R. 1173; 1988 (1) SLJ 79; 1989 (2) SLR 170; 1987 (2) UJ 746

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Service - Constitution, arts. 309 and 311 - Central Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1972, r. 48A(4) - Service Rules - Pension - Voluntary retirement - Withdrawal of three month notice given within time - Legality of Order allowing to retire prospectively on expiry of notice period without allowing withdrawal of notice - Order held, illegal on facts - No valid reason for withholding the permission of govt. - Appeal allowed.
