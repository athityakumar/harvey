Budh Ram v Ralla Ram Deceased Through LRs

Supreme Court of India

19 August 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

G.L. Oza, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28377; (1987) 4 SCC 75; AIR 1987 SC 2078; JT 1987 (3) SC 346; 1987 (2) RCR(Rent) 309; 1987 (2) RentLR 682; 1987(2) SCALE 349; 1987 (2) UJ 546

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Keywords: Indian Regulation Act 1908, Town Planning Act 1882, ELP Urban Rent Restriction Act 1949

Summary: Rent Control - East Punjab Urban Rent Restriction Act, 1949, s. 13 - Transfer of Property Act, 1882 - Registration Act, 1908 - Suit for eviction of tenant for arrears of rent in one year lease - When rent note stipulating for payment of rent in advance not registered and tenant proved to be in arrears of rent for 2 months - Landlord held entitled to decree for eviction - Appeal dismissed.
