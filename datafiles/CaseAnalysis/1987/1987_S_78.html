Baldev Krishna Sahi v Shipping Corporation Of India Limited And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

17 September 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, B.C. Ray

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28410; (1987) 4 SCC 361; (1987) SCC (Cr) 750; AIR 1987 SC 2245; 1987 (2) BankCLR 585; [1988] 63 Comp Cas 1; 1987 (3) CompLJ 57; 1987 (3) Crimes 449; 1987 CRLR 671; 1987 CrLR(SC) 671; 1989 (58) FLR 142; JT 1987 (3) SC 581; 1988 (2) LLJ 202; 1988 (2) LLN 590; 1987 (2) RCR(Rent) 424; 1987(2) SCALE 544; [1988] 1 S.C.R. 168

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Summary: Corporate - Companies Act, 1956 - Term 'Officer or employee' u/s. 630(1)(a)(b) - Scope - Held, applies not only to existing officers or employees but also to past officers or employees if such officer or employee either (a) wrongfully obtains possession of any property of company or (b) having obtained possession thereof during course of employment, withholds same after termination of his employment - Petition dismissed.
