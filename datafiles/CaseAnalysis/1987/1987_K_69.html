Virendra Kumar Avinash Kumar v Krishi Utpadan Mandi Samiti and Others

Supreme Court of India

23 September 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Dutt, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28823; (1987) 4 SCC 454; 1988 (1) AWC 65; 1987 (2) CCC 1077; [1988] 171 ITR 443; JT 1987 (3) SC 647; 1987(2) SCALE 664; [1988] 1 S.C.R. 308; 1988 (70) STC 360; 1988 (1) UJ 149

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Sales Tax

Summary: (A)-U. P. Krishi Utpadan Mandi Adhiniyam, 1964, Sec. 9 (1), Proviso-Applicability of-Producer of an agricultural produce within the Market Area is not exempted from taking out a licence-Exemption.

A producer of an agricultural produce within the Market Area is not exempted from taking out a licence merely because he is a producer of an agricultural produce. It is true that under the proviso, sub-section (1) of section 9 will not be applicable to a producer of agricultural produce. But such a producer must be a producer of agricultural produce processed, reared, caught or processed by him for his domestic consumption. If, however, the producer produces, rears, catches or processes the agricultural produce not for his domestic consumption, but for the sale thereof in the Market Area, such a producer will not come within the purview of the proviso and he will have to take out a licence under sub-section (1) of section 9 of the Act.

A producer who produces, rears, catches, or processes agricultural produce for his domestic consumption and also any person who purchases or stores any agricultural produce for his domestic consumption are exempt under the proviso from taking out any licence. In other words, sub-section (1) of section 9 will not apply to these two categories of persons as mentioned in the proviso.

(B)-U. P. Krishi Utpadan Mandi Adhiniyam, 1964, Secs. 9 (1) and 2 (y)-Applicability of Sec. 9 (1) to a producer of khandsari-Trader-Meaning of-A person producing khandsari sugar and selling it within the Market Area is a 'trader ' within the meaning of Sec. 2 (y) and also as contemplatad by Sec. 9 (1)- Producer-Trader explained.

(C)-U. P. Krishi Utpadan Mandi Adhiniyam, 1964, Sec. 17 (iii) (b) (1)-Market-fee-Liability to pay-Nothing to show that petitioners were selling their produce through a commission agent -They are liable to pay market-fee.
