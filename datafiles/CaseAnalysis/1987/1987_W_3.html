Union of India and Another v Wing Commander R.R. Hingorani (Retd.)

Supreme Court of India

30 January 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, S. Natarajan

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28889; (1987) 1 SCC 551; AIR 1987 SC 808; 1987 (2) ATC 939; JT 1987 (1) SC 290; 1987 LabIC 627; 1987 (2) LLJ 3; 1987 (1) RCR(Rent) 406; 1987(1) SCALE 203; [1987] 2 S.C.R. 94; 1987 SLJ 154; 1987 (1) SLR 479; 1987 (1) UJ 390

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Municipalities & Local Governments; Service

Keywords: Commutation Of Pension

Summary: Service - Pensions Act, 1871, s. 11 - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 115 - Fundamental Rules, r. 45 - Allotment of Govt. Residences (General Pool in Delhi) Rules, 1963, SR 317-B-11 and SR 317-22 - Recovery of damages equivalent to market rent for Govt. quarter under unauthorised occupation by employee - 'money due or to become due on account of pension' u/s. 11 of 1871 Act, include commuted portion of pension payable - (A) Prior notice, if necessary - (B) Whether rent due is deductible from amount of commuted pension - Held, relaxation by Govt. or estoppel against it could not be inferred in instant case - Cannot be deducted - Appeal allowed.
