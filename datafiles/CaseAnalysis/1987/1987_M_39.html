Mahant Dhangir And Another v Madan Mohan And Others

Supreme Court of India

28 October 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, B.C. Ray

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28788; (1987) Supp SCC 528; AIR 1988 SC 54; JT 1987 (4) SC 202; 1987(2) SCALE 874; [1988] 1 S.C.R. 679

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - CPC, 1908, O.41, rr. 33, 22 - Indian Limitation Act, 1908 - (A) Maintainability of cross objection - Sale of math property to former purchaser/seller declared invalid by Court but decree for possession refused - Appeal by subsequent purchasers - (C) Powers of appellate Court u/rr. 33 and 22 - Objection against decree - Held, math can file cross objection claiming possession - Constraints on power u/r. 33 stated - Appeal allowed.
