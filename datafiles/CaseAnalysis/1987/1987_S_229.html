Gupta Sugar Works v State Of U.P. And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

26 October 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, B.C. Ray

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28444; (1987) Supp SCC 476; AIR 1987 SC 2351; 1988 (1) CCC 272; JT 1987 (4) SC 154; 1987(2) SCALE 824B; [1988] 1 S.C.R. 577; 1988 (68) STC 407; 1988 (1) UJ 187

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection

Summary: Consumer Protection - Essential Commodities Act, 1955, s. 3 (3 C) - Uttar Pradesh Khandsari Sugar (Levy) Order, 1981 - Fixation of price of essential commodity - Matters to be considered by court while examining validity - Petitioner challenges validity of 1981 Order - Order held not invalid nor ultra vires and levy sugar was brought to public sale only to prevent deterioration when consumers refused to accept - Petition dismissed.
