Kalra Glue Factory v Sales Tax Tribunal and Others

Supreme Court of India

02 March 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

B.C. Ray, M.P. Thakkar

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28565; (1987) Supp SCC 465; 1987 (65) CTR 233; [1987] 167 ITR 498; JT 1987 (1) SC 713; 1987 (66) STC 292

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - Whether transaction was an inter-State sale? - Adverse inferences - Case remanded to tribunal for verification of bills and others documents - Tribunal will permit appellant to produce documents subject to being satisfied about genuineness thereof and to pronounce upon reliability of documents and impact of it on matter in issue - Appeal disposed of.
