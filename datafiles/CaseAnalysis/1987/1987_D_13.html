Damacherla Anjaneyulu and Another v Damcherla Venkata Seshaiah and Another

Supreme Court of India

19 February 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

B.C. Ray, M.P. Thakkar

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28594; (1987) Supp SCC 75; AIR 1987 SC 1641; JT 1987 (1) SC 748B

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Land & Property; Service; Trusts & Associations

Summary: Contract & Commercial - Specific Relief Act, 1963, ss. 10 and 20 - Suit for specific performance of contract for sale of land which is breached by vendor - Specific performance would result in special hardship as costly structures already built up on it - Held, directions issued to pay present value of disputed land instead of executing sale deed in vendee's favour - Appeal disposed of.
