Collector Of Central Excise Calcutta v Multiple Fabrics Pvt. Ltd. Etc."

Supreme Court of India

28 April 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28263; (1987) SCC (Tax) 228; (1987) 2 SCC 636; 1987 (1) BankCLR 214; 1987 CRLR 489; 1987 CrLR(SC) 489; 1987 (12) ECC 330; 1987 (12) ECR 1051; 1987 (29) E.L.T. 481; JT 1987 (2) SC 289; 1987(1) SCALE 1039; [1987] 2 S.C.R. 1226

Case Digest

Subject: Excise

Summary: Excise - Central Excises Tariff Act - Classification - Entry 22(3) - Whether PVC Conveyor Belting manufactured by respondents comes within purview of item 22(3) or would be governed by residuary entry 68 for purposes of excise duty under Central Excise Tariff? - Held, in view of higher percentage of PVC compound in commodity, it becomes difficult to treat ultimate goods as man-made fabrics, for holding that it is covered by item 22 - Tribunal was right in holding that goods were not covered by item 22 and, therefore, residuary item 68 applied - Appeal dismissed.
