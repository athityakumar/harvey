Jamana Flour And Oil Mill (P) Limited v State Of Bihar

Supreme Court of India

16 April 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28746; (1987) SCC (Tax) 248; (1987) 3 SCC 404; AIR 1987 SC 1207; 1987 (2) BankCLR 200; JT 1987 (2) SC 155; [1987] 2 S.C.R. 1047; 1987 (65) STC 462; 1987 TAXLR 2087; 1987 (2) UJ 230

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Sales Tax; Trade

Keywords: Bihar Sales Tax Act, 1959, Bihar Roller Mills Wheat Products (Price Control) Order 1964

Summary: Sales Tax - Bihar Roller Mills Wheat Products (Price Control) Order 1964,s.cl 3 - Bihar Sales Tax Act, 1959 - (A) Sale of gunny bags is implied in sale of wheat products - Assessment of sales tax at different rate on turnover of gunny bags - Whether direction of the Tribunal to ascertain the price of the containers (gunny bags) of wheat products sold for an all inclusive price under the provisions of 1964 Order, for taxing the same at a higher rate is legally valid? - Held, is proper - (B) Determination of admissibility of govt. communication along with affidavit in appeal before hon'ble Supreme Court - Held, liable to be rejected, being filed at belated stage - Appeal dismissed.
