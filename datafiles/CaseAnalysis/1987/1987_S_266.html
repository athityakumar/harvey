Messrs Fertilizer Corporation of India Limited v State of Bihar

Supreme Court of India

04 December 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ranganathan, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28227; (1988) Supp SCC 73; AIR 1988 SC 361; 1988 (14) ECR 209; JT 1987 (4) SC 549; [1988] 2 S.C.R. 148; 1988 (68) STC 158; 1988 (1) UJ 357

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Keywords: Bihar Sales Tax Act, 1959

Summary: Sales Tax - Bihar Sales Tax Act, 1959, ss. 14(1) and 15 - Income Tax Act, 1922 - Availability of rebate u/s. 15 of 1959 Act - Failure to file quarterly returns within prescribed period - Extension of period, not sought - However, returns accepted and acted upon by authorities - Rebate, if can be denied - Extension of period for filing returns can be inferred - Assessee cannot be denied rebate u/s. 15 - Appeal allowed.
