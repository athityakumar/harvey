D. Satyanarayana v P. Jagadish

Supreme Court of India

15 September 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

A.P. Sen, B.C. Ray

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28883; (1987) 4 SCC 424; AIR 1987 SC 2192; JT 1987 (3) SC 571; 1989 (1) MLJ(SC) 1; 1988 (1) RCJ 213; 1987(2) SCALE 577; [1988] 1 S.C.R. 145

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Rent Control - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 116 - Andhra Pradesh Buildings (Lease, Rent and Eviction) Control Act, 1960 - Estoppel - Tenant not permitted to deny title of landlord - Rule does not apply when tenant is under threat of eviction by person claiming paramount title - Order of eviction passed against sub-tenant u/s. 10(2)(i) and (vi) of 1960 Act - Held, on facts of case order was improper - Appeal allowed.
