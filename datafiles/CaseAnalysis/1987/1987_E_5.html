Smt. Arati Dutta v Eastern Tea Estate (P) Ltd.

Supreme Court of India

13 November 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28474; (1988) 1 SCC 523; AIR 1988 SC 325; [1988] 64 Comp Cas 313; 1988 (1) CompLJ 34; JT 1987 (4) SC 564; [1988] 1 S.C.R. 1070; 1988 (1) UJ 299

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Corporate; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Corporate - Bombay High Court Rules - Companies Act,1956 - Application filed u/ss. 397, 398 and 403 of the Companies Act, 1956 - Order passed by the single Judge of High Court - The provisions of s.483 attracted to such order - Whether the appeal against such order to Division Bench of High Court is maintainable - Held, appeal is maintainable irrespective of powers under Letters Patent - Appeal allowed.
