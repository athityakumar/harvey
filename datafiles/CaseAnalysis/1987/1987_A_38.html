State Of Gujarat v Akhilesh C. Bhargav And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

26 August 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Dutt, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28383; (1987) SCC (L&S) 460; (1987) 4 SCC 482; AIR 1987 SC 2135; 1987 (5) ATC 167; 1987 (2) CCC 850; 1987 LabIC 1899; 1987 (2) LLN 861; 1987(2) SCALE 42; [1987] 3 S.C.R. 1091; 1988 (2) SLJ 86; 1987 (5) SLR 270; 1987 (2) UJ 756

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Indian Police Service (Protection) Rules 1954

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 309 and 311 - Service Rules - Probationer IPS officer found to be confirmed, in view of combined effect of Rules and circular of Home Ministry D/- 16-03-1973, on relevant date - Order of discharge passed against him u/r. 12(bb) Indian Police Service (Probation) Rules, 1954 - Legality - Held, not legal - Appeal dismissed.
