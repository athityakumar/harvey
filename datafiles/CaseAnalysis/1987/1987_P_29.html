Parakunnan Veetill Joseph's Son Mathew v Nedumbara Kuruvila's Son and Others

Supreme Court of India

14 September 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28402; (1987) Supp SCC 340; AIR 1987 SC 2328; JT 1987 (3) SC 643; 1987(2) SCALE 588; 1987 (2) UJ 449

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Land & Property; Service; Trusts & Associations

Summary: Land & Property - Family & Personal - Specific Relief Act, 1963, s. 20 - Validity of a decree for specific performance granted by High Court Grant of specific performance of agreement to sell - Use of litigation as an instrument of oppression - Duty of Court - Held, duty is to see that litigation is not used as instrument of oppression to have unfair advantage to plaintiff - Appeal allowed.
