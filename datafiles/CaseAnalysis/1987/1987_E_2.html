S.K. Viswambaran v E. Koyakunju And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

03 March 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Natarajan, B.C. Ray, M.M. Dutt, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28354; (1987) 2 SCC 109; (1987) SCC (Cr) 289; AIR 1987 SC 1436; 1987 BBCJ 65; 1987 BLJR 774; 1987 (1) Crimes 769; 1987 CRLJ 1175; 1987 CrLR(SC) 334; JT 1987 (1) SC 680; 1987(1) SCALE 506; [1987] 2 S.C.R. 501

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 (as amended by Act No.25 of 2005 & Act No.2 of 2006)

Summary: Criminal Procedure Code, 1974, ss. 353, 354 and 482 - Gazetted Police Officer to seek expunction of certain adverse remarks passed against him by the High Court of Kerala in an order passed with reference to two Criminal Miscellaneous Petitions - No notice issued to him and he was not heard- Held, the adverse remarks against the appellant in the order of the High Court which have been extracted above will stand expunged.
