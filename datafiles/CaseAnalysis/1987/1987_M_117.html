Hanumant Kumar Telesara v Mohan Lal

Supreme Court of India

01 December 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

B.C. Ray, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28154; (1988) 1 SCC 377; AIR 1988 SC 299; JT 1987 (4) SC 545; 1988 (1) RCJ 160; 1988 (1) RCR(Rent) 57; 1988 (1) RentLR 238; 1987(2) SCALE 1470; [1988] 2 S.C.R. 99; 1988 (1) UJ 411

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Rent Control - Land & Property - Transfer of Property Act,1882, s. 76(a) - Rajasthan Premises (Control of Rent and Eviction) Act, 1950, s. 13 - Eviction - Mortgagor - Landlord filing suit for redemption of mortgage and recovery of possession against mortgagee and tenant from him - Finding of courts below, that letting was not prudent act in ordinary course of management, becoming final - Protection of Rent Act, availability - Held, landlord is entitled to get recovery of possession - Protection of Rent Act, not available - Appeal dismissed.
