National Engineering Industries Limited v Shri Shri Kishan Bhageria And Others

Supreme Court of India

11 November 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28899; (1988) Supp SCC 82; (1988) SCC (L&S) 428; AIR 1988 SC 329; 1988 (56) FLR 148; JT 1987 (4) SC 569; 1988 LabIC 384; 1988 (1) LLJ 363; 1988 (1) LLN 675; 1987(2) SCALE 1301; [1988] 1 S.C.R. 985; 1988 (2) SLJ 23

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 2(s) - Rajasthan Shops And Commercial Establishments Act, 1958, s. 28 A - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 136 and 254 - (A) Internal Auditor in company - Not doing supervisory work but only checking up on behalf of employer - Having no independent right or authority to take decision - Whether workman - (B) Validity of provisions of 1958 Act in light of 1947 - Held, employee, was 'workman' within meaning of s. 2(s) of 1947 Act - Both Acts tread on same field and are not inconsistent or repugnant to each other - Appeal dismissed.
