Gulab Mehra v State Of U.P. And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

15 September 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

B.C. Ray, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28406; (1987) 4 SCC 302; (1987) SCC (Cr) 721; AIR 1987 SC 2332; 1987 AllCriC 520; 1987 (3) Crimes 405; 1988 CRLJ 168; 1987 CrLR(SC) 637; JT 1987 (3) SC 559; 1987(2) SCALE 561; [1988] 1 S.C.R. 126

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 22(5) - National Security Act, 1980 - Detention u/s. 3 - Breach of public order - Detenu already in jail in respect of criminal proceeding in case - Satisfaction of detaining authority - Grounds vague - Counter affidavit filed by police officer and not by detaining authority - Legality - Held, on facts detention was illegal - Appeal allowed.
