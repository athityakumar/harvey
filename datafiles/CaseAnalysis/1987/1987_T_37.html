Khalil Ahmed Bashir Ahmed v Tufelhussein Samasbhai Sarangpurwala

Supreme Court of India

13 November 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, G.L. Oza

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28881; (1988) 1 SCC 155; AIR 1988 SC 184; JT 1987 (4) SC 342; 1988 (1) RCJ 180; 1988 (1) RCR(Rent) 70; 1988 (1) RentLR 351; 1987(2) SCALE 1034; [1988] 1 S.C.R. 1057; 1988 (1) UJ 133

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land and Property - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 105 - Constitution, art. 227 - Main question involved in this appeal is whether the appellant was a licensee or a tenant and also incidentally the question whether the Court of Small Causes, Bombay had jurisdiction to deal with the eviction petition in this case - Held, in view of the intention of the parties in the document and the facts and circumstances of this case, it was a licence and not a lease - Supreme court declined to interfere with High Court order uphelding the order of small cause Court - Appeal dismissed.
