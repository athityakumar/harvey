Kundan Lal Srikishan Mathura (U.P.). v Commissioner Of Sales Tax, U.P. And Another.

Supreme Court of India

03 February 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, M.M. Dutt

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28634; (1987) 1 SCC 684; AIR 1987 SC 793; 1987 (1) CCC 923; JT 1987 (1) SC 321; 1987(1) SCALE 234; [1987] 2 S.C.R. 140; 1987 (65) STC 62; 1987 TAXLR 2094; 1987 (1) UJ 600

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - Uttar Pradesh Sales Tax Act, 1948, ss.21 and 22 - Limitation Act, 1908 - Re-assessment - Determination of period of limitation within which application for rectification of an order of reassessment passed u/s.21 of the can be preferred u/s.22 of the 1948 Act - Held, it starts from date of order passed u/s.21 and not from date of original assessment order - Appeal allowed.
