Smt. Gulwant Kaur And Another v Mohinder Singh And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

20 July 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, K. Jagannatha Shetty

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28321; (1987) 3 SCC 674; AIR 1987 SC 2251; 1987 (2) CCC 561; 1987 (2) DMC 254; 1987 (2) HLR 416; JT 1987 (3) SC 121; 1987(2) SCALE 82; [1987] 3 S.C.R. 576; 1988 (1) ShimLC 48; 1987 (2) UJ 399

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Family & Personal; Practice & Procedure; Women & Children

Summary: Family & Personal - Hindu Succession Act, 1956 - Hindu Adoptions and Maintenance Act, 1956, s. 18 - Limited estate, if - Couple were estranged and were living apart - Husband gave to his wife lands, in lieu of maintenance - Wife obtained possession and enjoyed produce therefrom - Held, her right becomes absolute u/s. 14(1) of 1956 Act - Appeal allowed.
