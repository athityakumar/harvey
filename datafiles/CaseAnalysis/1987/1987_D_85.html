Dr. Smt. Kuntesh Gupta v Management Of Hindu Kanya Mahavidyalaya, Sitapur (U.P) Andors.

Supreme Court of India

25 September 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Dutt, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28876; 1988 (1) AWC 347; 1987 (32) E.L.T. 8

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Constitution of India, 1950 - Uttar Pradesh State Universities Act 1973, ss. 58 and 68 - (A) Whether Vice-Chancellor had power of review under Statutes of University or under U.P. State Universities Act, 1973? - Provisions of U.P. State Universities Act, 1973 or of Statutes of University do not confer any power of review on Vice-Chancellor - (B) Whether High Court was justified in dismissing writ petition of appellant on ground of availability of an alternative remedy? - It is well established that an alternative remedy is not an absolute bar to maintainability of a writ petition - Held, impugned order of Vice-Chancellor quashed - Appeal allowed.
