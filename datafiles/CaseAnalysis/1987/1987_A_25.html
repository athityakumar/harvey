Smt. Padmavati Jaikrishna v Additional Commissioner of Income Tax, Gujarat

Supreme Court of India

22 April 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, K.N. Singh, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28248; (1987) SCC (Tax) 252; (1987) 3 SCC 448; AIR 1987 SC 1723; 1987 (62) CTR 4; 1987 (62) CTR 14; [1987] 166 ITR 176; JT 1987 (2) SC 230; 1987(1) SCALE 958; [1987] 2 S.C.R. 1167; 1987 TAXLR 1006; [1987] 32 TAXMAN 32

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax and Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, s.57(iii) - Assessee, an individual derived income form 'other sources' in the shape of interest dividends etc. - Whether interest on loan is deductible u/s.57(iii) from income of assessee derived form interest, dividends etc. - When loan was raise to pay direct taxes and annuity deposit - Held, not deductible - Appeal dismissed.
