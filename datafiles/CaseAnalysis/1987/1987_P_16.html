M.K. Kunhimohammed v P.A. Ahmedkutty And Ors

Supreme Court of India

01 September 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, K.N. Singh

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28827; (1987) 4 SCC 284; (1987) SCC (Cr) 703; 1987 (2) ACJ 872; AIR 1987 SC 2158; 1987 (2) CCC 833; [1988] 64 Comp Cas 7; 1988 CRLR 46; JT 1987 (3) SC 465; 1987(2) SCALE 442; [1987] 3 S.C.R. 1149

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Corporate

Summary: Carriers and Transportation - Motor Vehicles Act, 1939, ss. 95(2)(b)(ii)(2) and 95(4) - Bus - Stage carriage - Accident - Provision of s.95(2)(b)(ii)(4) prescribes the maximum amount payable in respect of each passenger which insurer is liable to pay in case of death - Determination of insurer's liability when single passenger die - Held, insurer is liable to pay only 5,000/- as limited by the provisions of the Act - Petition dismissed.
