Durgacharan Misra v State of Orissa and Others

Supreme Court of India

27 August 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28385; (1987) 4 SCC 646; (1988) SCC (L&S) 36; AIR 1987 SC 2267; 1987 (5) ATC 148; JT 1987 (3) SC 459; 1987 LabIC 1914; 1988 (1) LLN 28; 1987(2) SCALE 417; [1987] 3 S.C.R. 1097; 1987 (3) SLJ 200; 1987 (5) SLR 276; 1987 (2) UJ 657

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Advocates & Judges - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 309 and 234 - Orissa Judicial Service Rules, 1964, rr. 16, 17 and 18 - Appointment of Munsifs - Written and Viva Voce tests prescribed - No minimum qualifying marks prescribed for viva voce test under rules - Public Service Commission prescribed minimum qualifying marks - Excluding candidate for not securing such marks - Legality - Held, illegal - Petition allowed.
