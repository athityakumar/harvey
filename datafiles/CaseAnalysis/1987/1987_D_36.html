Jadavji Purshottam v Dhami Navnitbhai Amaratlal & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

09 September 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Natarajan, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28393; (1987) 4 SCC 223; AIR 1987 SC 2146; JT 1987 (3) SC 523; 1987 (2) RCJ 466; 1987 (2) RCR(Rent) 403; 1987(2) SCALE 535; [1988] 1 S.C.R. 76

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Mortgagee In Possession, Anomalous, Bombay Rent, Hotel and lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947

Summary: Land & Property - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, ss. 76, 98 and 111 - Eviction of mortgaged property - Mortgagee in possession, was not given power to create lease binding on mortgagor after redemption of mortgagee - Eviction of tenant inducted by mortgagee - Held, liable to be evicted on redemption of mortgage - Appeal dismissed.
