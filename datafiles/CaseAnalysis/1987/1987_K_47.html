Kendriya Karamchari Sahkari Grih Nirman Samiti Limited Andanr v New Okhla Industrial Development Authority And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

28 October 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

B.C. Ray, K. Jagannatha Shetty

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28451; (1988) 1 SCC 63; AIR 1988 SC 1; JT 1987 (4) SC 194; 1987(2) SCALE 856; [1988] 1 S.C.R. 662

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Society

Summary: Land & Property - Uttar Pradesh Industrial Area Development Act, 1976, ss. 6 and 12 - Uttar Pradesh Urban Planning and Development Act (Presi Act ), 1973, s. 41 - Constitution of India, 1950 art. 14 - Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 4 - Land acquisition - Bar - If imposed by G. O. D/- 09-04-1980 - Held, does not create express bar on power of Govt. to acquire lands of Co-operative House Building Societies - Appeal and petition dismissed.
