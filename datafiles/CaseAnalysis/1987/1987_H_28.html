Smt. Shakuntala S. Tiwari v Hem Chand M. Singhania

Supreme Court of India

06 May 1987

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1987 Indlaw SC 28754; (1987) 3 SCC 211; AIR 1987 SC 1823; JT 1987 (2) SC 433; 1988 (1) RCR(Rent) 9; 1987 (3) RentLR 605; 1987(1) SCALE 1009; [1987] 3 S.C.R. 306; 1987 (2) UJ 439

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Rent Control - Legislation Bombay Rents, Hotel and Lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947 - Tenancy - Suit for recovery of possession by landlord - (A) what is the period of limitation for the recovery of possession of the demised premises - Applicability of art. 113 of Limitation Act - (B) Scope of ss. 12 and 13 of 1947 Act - The art. 67 and not art. 113 of Limitation Act would be applicable; period of limitation in this case would be 12 years - They co-exist and must be harmonised to effect purpose and intent of legislature for eviction - Appeal dismissed.
