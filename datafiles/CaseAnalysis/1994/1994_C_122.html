 J.K. Synthetics Ltd. v Commercial Taxes Officer

Supreme Court of India

09 May 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, M.N. Venkatachaliah, G.N. Ray, S.P. Bharucha, J.S. Verma

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 2185; (1994) 4 SCC 276; AIR 1994 SC 2393; 1994 (119) CTR 222; JT 1994 (3) SC 671; 1994(2) SCALE 1044; [1994] 3 S.C.R. 964; 1994 (94) STC 422

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Indirect Tax - Sales Tax - Central Sales Tax Act, 1956, ss. 9 and 10 - Rajasthan Sales Tax Act, 1954, s. 11 B - Rajasthan Sales Tax Rules, 1955, r. 25 - (A) Liability to pay interest when arises - Omission to include freight charged on commodity in return filed u/s. 7(2 A), bona fide - Subsequent filing of revised return including freight charges - Full amount of tax paid as per return - (B) Whether interest to be charged? - Held, (A) Does not arise from date of filing of original return u/s. 7 - (B) No interest charged - Appeal allowed.
