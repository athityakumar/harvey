Chandra Gupta, I.F.S. v Secy., Govt. Of India

Supreme Court of India

12 September 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mohan, M.N. Venkatachaliah, A.S. Anand

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 530; (1995) 1 SCC 23; (1995) SCC (L&S) 210; AIR 1995 SC 44; 1994 (28) ATC 722; 1995 (70) FLR 90; JT 1994 (6) SC 132; 1995 (1) LLN 867; 1994(4) SCALE 82; 1994 (3) SLJ 69; 1994 (5) SLR 1; [1994] Supp3 S.C.R. 370

Case Digest

Subject: Administrative; Service

Keywords: All India Services (Confidential Rolls) Rules, 1970, Indian Forest Service (Initial Recruitment) Regulations, 1966

Summary: All India Services Act, 1951, s.3; Indian Forest Service (Pay) Rules, 1968, r.3(3) - Adverse entry - Expunction of - Seniority and Promotion - Promotion - Post of Additional/ Chief Conservator of Forests - Selection on merits as per rules - Non selection of appellant due to adverse entries - Subsequent Expunction of adverse entries and consequent Promotion - May such Promotion relates back to date on which it was due - Held, essential is merit and not mere seniority - Can be decided only after comparative estimate of merits.
