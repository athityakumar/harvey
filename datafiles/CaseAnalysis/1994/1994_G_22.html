A.P. State Financial Corpn v Gar Re-Rolling Mills and Another

Supreme Court of India

10 February 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1633; (1994) 2 SCC 647; AIR 1994 SC 2151; AIR 1994 SCW 1953; [1994] 80 Comp Cas 140; JT 1994 (1) SC 586; [1994] 1 S.C.R. 857

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Land & Property; Contract & Commercial

Summary: State Financial Corporations Act, 1951, ss. 29 and 31 - Doctrine of election - Applicability - Default in payment - Order obtained by Corporation u/s. 31 - Recourse to remedy available under s. 29 - Held, can still be taken without executing order under s. 31 - Doctrine would not apply - Where ambit and scope of two remedies is essentially different.
