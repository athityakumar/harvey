Ram Pukar Singh v Heavy Engineering Corpn.

Supreme Court of India

31 August 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, P.B. Sawant, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 2123; (1994) 6 SCC 145; (1994) SCC (L&S) 1314; AIR 1995 SC 251; 1994 (2) CLR 1029; 1994 (69) FLR 801; JT 1994 (6) SC 26; 1995 (1) LLJ 214; 1995 (2) LLN 714; 1994(3) SCALE 916; 1994 (5) SLR 189; 1994 (2) UJ 517

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 18 - Employees availing benefit of arrears of salary and revised pay scale under earlier settlement - Whether new settlement binding on them or not - There being only one Union - Settlement was binding on all workers and therefore It was binding on them also despite of the fact whether they were members of Union or not - Appeal dismissed.
