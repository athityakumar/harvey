Dr. Arundhati Ajit Pargaonkar v State of Maharashtra and Others

Supreme Court of India

31 August 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

R.M. Sahai, N.P. Singh

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 990; (1994) Supp3 SCC 380; (1995) SCC (L&S) 31; AIR 1995 SC 962; 1994 (28) ATC 415; 1995 (2) CLR 821; 1994 (69) FLR 695; JT 1994 (5) SC 378; 1995 (1) LLJ 927; 1994(3) SCALE 887; 1994 (3) SLJ 87; 1994 (5) SLR 234; [1994] Supp2 S.C.R. 808; 1995 (1) UJ 89

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 309 and 311 - Temporary Govt. Servants Extension of Permanency Resolution, 1975 - Regularisation of service - Post for Lecturer in Dentistry - Temporary appointment against permanent post, however, not in accordance with recruitment Rules - Pre-condition for regularisation as per resolution, not satisfied - Post within purview of Public Service Commission - Whether claim for regularisation under 1975, Resolution maintainable - Held, appellant appointed in disregard to Recruitment Rules, therefore, she cannot claim to be regularised under 1975 Resolution - Claim not maintainable - Appeal dismissed.
