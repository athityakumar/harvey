Mohd.Quaramuddin v State Of A.P.

Supreme Court of India

10 May 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, G.N. Ray

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1646; (1994) 5 SCC 118; (1994) SCC (L&S) 1039; 1994 (27) ATC 814; 1994(3) SCALE 4; 1995 (7) SLR 330

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Summary: Service - Civil Procedure Code, 1908, s. 80 - Indian Limitation Act, 1908 - Limitation Act, 1963, art. 58 - Whether court below was right in taking view that suit filed by deceased Govt. servant was barred by limitation, having been filed three years after date of dismissal? - Held, where a statutory notice u/s. 80 CPC, 1908 is required to be given that period of 60 days would be added to period of limitation prescribed by Limitation Act, 1963 and if that period is added suit is clearly within period of limitation - Therefore Trial Court as well as Tribunal were in error in dismissing suit as barred by limitation - Relied on Rathore v. State of M P (1988 Indlaw SC 873) - Appeal allowed.
