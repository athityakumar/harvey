State of Andhra Pradesh v M. Ramakishtaiah and Company

Supreme Court of India

17 February 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, B.L. Hansaria

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 272; 1994 (93) STC 406

Case Digest

Subject: Sales Tax

Summary: Sales Tax - Indirect Tax - Andhra Pradesh General Sales Tax Act, 1957, ss. 20(2) and 22 - Service of order is after expiry of four years from date of order of assessment - Justification of delay - Held, no explanation from Deputy Commr. why it was so delayed - In absence of any explanation whatsoever, presumption that order was not made on date it purports to have been made - Appeal dismissed.
