Ramankutty Gupta v Avara

Supreme Court of India

03 February 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, B.L. Hansaria

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 309; (1994) 2 SCC 642; AIR 1994 SC 1699; AIR 1994 SCW 1533; 1995 (1) BC 7; 1994 (1) CCC 360; 2010 (6) CompLJ 422; 1994 (1) GLH 416; JT 1994 (1) SC 342; 1994 (2) MLJ 52; 1994 (2) MLJ(SC) 52; 1994 (1) RentLR 222; 1994(1) SCALE 390; [1994] 1 S.C.R. 542; 1994 (1) UJ 442

Case Digest

Subject: Contract & Commercial

Summary: Contract & Commercial - Specific Relief Act, 1963, s. 28 - Rescission of contract - (A) Maintainability of application for rescission/extension of time to make payment on default to pay consideration as per decree for specific performance - (B) Decree for specific performance sought to be executed in Court in which original suit was filed - Application for rescission before that Court - Held, is maintainable in Appellate Court since appeal is continuation of suit - Application ought to be treated on original side and not on execution side - Appeal dismissed.
