M. G. Shahani and Company (Delhi) Limited v Collector of Central Excise, New Delhi

Supreme Court of India

08 August 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mohan, M.N. Venkatachaliah

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 932; (1994) Supp3 SCC 269; AIR 1994 SC 2413; AIR 1994 SCW 3411; 1994 (73) E.L.T. 3; JT 1994 (5) SC 333; 1994(3) SCALE 696; 1994 (2) UJ 466

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Publicity, Advertisement and publicity

Summary: Excise - Central Excises & Salt Act, 1944, s. 35 B - Appeal before Tribunal - Procedure - Remand of case to Collector - Relevant materials before Tribunal - Held, Tribunal should have gone through evidence and rendered a finding - Matter remitted back to Tribunal.
