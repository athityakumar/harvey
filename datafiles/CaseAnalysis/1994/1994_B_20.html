State of Bihar and Others v Bageshwari Prasad and Another

Supreme Court of India

06 October 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 807; (1995) Supp1 SCC 432; (1995) SCC (L&S) 506; 1995 (29) ATC 349; 1995 (1) SLJ 178; 1996 (1) SLR 185; [1994] Supp4 S.C.R. 318

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 6(1) and 16(4) - Whether respondent was entitled to be promoted as he was a reserved candidate in 6th vacancy? - Held, it is not post that is material but it is vacancy which is material - In case of only one vacancy its second turn of same post would be filled by appointment from reserved castes - Appeal allowed.
