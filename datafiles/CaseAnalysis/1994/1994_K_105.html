Sardar Singh v Krishna Devi Smt. and Another

Supreme Court of India

26 April 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1506; AIR 1995 SC 491; AIR 1994 SCW 4729; 1994 (54) DLT 500; JT 1994 (3) SC 465; [1994] 3 S.C.R. 717; 1994 (Supp) RD 123

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Registration Act, 1908, s. 17(1)(b) - Specific Relief Act, 1963, ss. 20(1) and 12 - Documents compulsorily registerable - Award made by private arbitrator - Held, is non-testamentary instrument - Declares only pre-existing rights - Not compulsorily registrable.
