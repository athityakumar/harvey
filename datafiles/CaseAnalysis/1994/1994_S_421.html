Gabriel Saver Fernandes and Others v State of Karnataka and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 September 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 786; (1995) Supp1 SCC 149; (1995) SCC (L&S) 309; 1995 (29) ATC 105; 1994(4) SCALE 302; 1994 (5) SLR 625; [1994] Supp3 S.C.R. 105

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Distinction between Talathis and Village Accountants - Whether they constitute different categories and that, therefore they are entitled to separate scales of pay? - Held, Govt. to make a distinction between qualified and unqualified persons prescribed different scales of pay - No invidious discrimination made between two categories to club together and grant them same scale of pay - As appellants have already been paid scale of pay while in service and are retired now, it would not be appropriate for Govt. to recover from them salary - Appeals dismissed.
