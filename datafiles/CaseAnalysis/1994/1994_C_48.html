Chairman, Puri Gramya Bank v Ananda Chandra Das

Supreme Court of India

12 September 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1833; (1994) 6 SCC 301; (1994) SCC (L&S) 1384; 1994 (28) ATC 293; 1995 LabIC 342; 1994(4) SCALE 307; 1994 (5) SLR 615; [1994] Supp3 S.C.R. 489

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Direct recruitment as managers of Rural Bank - Whether the ranking among the direct recruits be determined from the date on which they joined duty or according to the ranking given by the Selection Board? - Held, if more than one are selected, the seniority is as per ranking of the direct recruits subject to the adjustment of the candidates selected on applying the rule of reservation and the roster - Mere fortuitous chance of reporting to duty earlier would not alter the ranking given by the Selection Board and the arranged one as per roster - Appeal disposed of.
