The Papnasam Labour Union v Madura Coats Ltd. And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

08 December 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

G.N. Ray, B.L. Hansaria

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 545; (1995) SCC (L&S) 339; (1995) 1 SCC 501; AIR 1995 SC 2200; AIR 1995 SCW 1593; 1995 (1) CLR 25; 1995 (70) FLR 319; JT 1995 (1) SC 71; 1995 LabIC 735; 1997 LLJ 938; 1995 (1) LLN 408; 1995 (1) MLJ 79; 1995 (1) MLJ(SC) 79; 1994(5) SCALE 153; 1995 (1) SLR 259; [1994] Supp6 S.C.R. 369

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Closure

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Practice and Procedure - Bombay Industrial Disputes Act, 1938 - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 25M - Constitution, art. 19(6) - Whether amended s. 25M requiring employer to obtain prior permission of concerned authority before effecting lay off ultra vires and void - Held, restriction imposed on employer's right is in greater public interest for maintaining industrial peace and to prevent unemployment - Restriction imposed reasonable - Amended section not ultra vires and void - Appeal allowed.
