Dr Umrao Singh Choudhary v State Of M.P.

Supreme Court of India

02 May 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1319; (1994) 4 SCC 328; (1994) SCC (L&S) 948; 1994 (27) ATC 580; 1994(2) SCALE 1008; [1994] 3 S.C.R. 842; 1994 (4) SLR 24; 1994 (2) UJ 337

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Madhya Pradesh Vishwavidyalaya Adhiniyam, 1973, ss. 52 and 52(1) - Whether action of Governor mala fide, illegal, arbitrary and violative of principle of natural justice? -Held, Governor acted with aid and advice of Council of Ministers in issuing notification u/s. 52 - For better administration of University - Petition dismissed.
