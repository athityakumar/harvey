Tarsem Singh v State Of Punjab

Supreme Court of India

12 July 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, Yogeshwar Dayal

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 222; (1994) 5 SCC 392; (1994) SCC (L&S) 1113; AIR 1995 SC 384; 1994 (27) ATC 886; 1994 (2) CLR 366; 1994 (69) FLR 519; JT 1994 (4) SC 303; 1995 (2) LLJ 104; 1994 (2) LLN 430; 1994(3) SCALE 145; 1995 (1) SLJ 187; 1994 (4) SLR 577; [1994] Supp1 S.C.R. 452; 1994 (2) UJ 575

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 309 - Punjab Labour Department (State Service Class III) Rules, 1969, r. 8 - Service Rules - Post of Labour Inspector ministerial employees who are in equal or lower pay-scale - Eligibility for promotion - Held, are eligible, and not a person holding higher pay scale and higher status - Appeal allowed.
