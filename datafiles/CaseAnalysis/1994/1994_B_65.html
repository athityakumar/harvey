Bhagwan Shukla S/O Sarabjit Shukla v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 August 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

A.S. Anand, Faizan Uddin

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 784; (1994) 6 SCC 154; (1994) SCC (L&S) 1320; AIR 1994 SC 2480; AIR 1994 SCW 3503; 1994 (28) ATC 258; 1994 (2) CLR 645; 1994 (69) FLR 570; JT 1994 (5) SC 253; 1994 LabIC 2493; 1995 (2) LLJ 726; 1994 (2) LLN 1074; 1994(3) SCALE 694; 1995 (2) SLJ 30; 1994 (4) SLR 614; [1994] Supp2 S.C.R. 419

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Wrong fixation of basic pay - Challenge to order reducing his basic pay with retrospective effect - Held, though appellant visited with civil consequences - He had not been granted an opportunity to show cause against reduction of his basic pay - No notice before his pay was reduced by department - No lawful procedure followed - Flagrant violation of principles of natural justice and appellant has been made to suffer huge financial loss without being heard - Central Administrative Tribunal fell in error in dismissing petition of appellant - Appeal allowed.
