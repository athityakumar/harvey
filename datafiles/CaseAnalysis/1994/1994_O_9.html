Oil and Natural Gas Commission v Collector of Central Excise

Supreme Court of India

07 January 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

M.N. Venkatachaliah, P.B. Sawant, S. Mohan

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 74; (2004) 6 SCC 437; 1994 (116) CTR 643; 1994 (70) E.L.T. 45; 1994(1) SCALE 324; 2011(2) SCALE 498

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Memo provided for Committee to be set up for resolving disputes between Central Govt. and its Public Sector Undertakings - Required further clarity - Held, no litigation to come to Court/ Tribunal without having been examined by Committee - If Committee unable to resolve matter, it shall grant clearance for litigation - Before filing for litigation, every endeavor to be made to have clearance of Committee - Application disposed of.
