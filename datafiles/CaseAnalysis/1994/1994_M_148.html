Mohd.Noor v Mohd. Ibrahim

Supreme Court of India

19 July 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

R.M. Sahai, B.L. Hansaria

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 899; (1994) 5 SCC 562; AIR 1995 SC 398; AIR 1994 SCW 4603; JT 1994 (5) SC 429; 1994(3) SCALE 350; [1994] Supp1 S.C.R. 790; 1994 (2) UJ 356

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land and Property - Transfer of Property Act, 1882 - Rajasthan Pre-emption Act, 1966s.2(viii) and 11 - Whether co-sharer of khatedari rights entitled to claim right of pre-emption - Held, khatedari not entitled to claim right of pre-emption as right not available in transfer of tenancy - Appeal dismissed.
