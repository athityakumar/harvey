 Attorney General For India v Amratlal Prajivandas

Supreme Court of India

12 May 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, S. Mohan, S.C. Agrawal, G.N. Ray, A.M. Ahmadi, P.B. Sawant, K. Ramaswamy, K. Jayachandra Reddy

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 400; (1994) 5 SCC 54; (1994) SCC (Cr) 1325; AIR 1994 SC 2179; [1995] 83 Comp Cas 804; 1995 CRLJ 426; JT 1994 (3) SC 583; [1994] Supp1 S.C.R. 1

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Single Act, Constitution (Thirty-Ninth Amendment) Act, 1975, Constitution (Thirty-Eighth Amendment) Act, 1975

Summary: Conservation of Foreign Exchange and Prevention of Smuggling Activities Act, 1974, Preamble; Constitution of India, Sch.7, List 3, Entry 3 - Scope - 'Security of a State' - Validity - Held, COFEPOSA is clearly relatable to Entry 3 of List-III - Related to security of the State.
