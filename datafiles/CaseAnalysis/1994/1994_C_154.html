Committee Of Management, K.D. College v Shambhu Saran Pandey And Others

Supreme Court of India

28 October 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1157; (1995) SCC (L&S) 324; (1995) 1 SCC 404; AIR 1995 SCW 43; 1995 ALJ 148; 1995 (29) ATC 123; 1995 (1) CLR 351; 1995 (70) FLR 352; 1995 (2) LLJ 625; 1994(4) SCALE 1082; 1995 (1) SCJ 203; 1995 (1) SLJ 156; 1995 (1) SLR 31; [1994] Supp5 S.C.R. 269

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Termination - Validity - Respondent was terminated from service without any opportunity of hearing and without being given a chance to examine evidence collected to corroborate his offence of misappropriation - In first instance he should be given opportunity for inspection and thereafter conduct enquiry and then hear delinquent at time of conclusion of his enquiry - Held, procedure was not adopted - Procedure in conducting enquiry adopted is clearly in violation of principles of natural justice - Appellant should conduct and complete a fresh enquiry within a period of six months from date of receipt of this order - Appeal disposed of.
