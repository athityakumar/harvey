Lic v Asha Ramchandra Ambekar

Supreme Court of India

28 February 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mohan, M.N. Venkatachaliah

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1383; (1994) 2 SCC 718; (1994) SCC (L&S) 737; AIR 1994 SC 2148; AIR 1994 SCW 1947; 1994 (23) ALR 447; 1994 (27) ATC 174; 1994 (1) CLR 638; 1994 (1) GLH 508; JT 1994 (2) SC 183; 1994 (2) LLJ 173; [1994] 2 S.C.R. 163

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Insurance

Keywords: Instructions

Summary: Life Insurance Corporation Act, 1956, s. 49 - Life Insurance Corporation of India (Staff) Regulations, 1960, Regn. 4 - Life Insurance Corporation Recruitment (of Class III and Class IV staff) Instructions, 1979, cl. 2(iii) - Appointment - Ex-gratia - Instructions, no appointment when one of the members of deceased's family is gainfully employed - Held, court cannot direct contrary to statutory Instructions.
