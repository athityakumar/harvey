Rainbow Industries Private Limited v Collector of Central Excise, Vadodara

Supreme Court of India

04 October 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

R.M. Sahai, M.K. Mukherjee

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 775; (1994) 6 SCC 563; AIR 1994 SC 2783; 1994 (55) ECR 1; 1994 (74) E.L.T. 3; JT 1994 (6) SC 393; 1994(4) SCALE 386; [1994] Supp4 S.C.R. 135

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Keywords: Price List

Summary: Excise - Central Excises & Salt Act, 1944, s. 11-A - Central Excise Rules, 1944, r. 173-C(2) - Whether classification and price list accepted by department and acted upon, found subsequently to be erroneous, is to be applied prospectively or retrospectively? - Once department accepted price list, acted upon it and goods were cleared with knowledge of department, then in absence of any amendment in law or judicial pronouncement re-classification should be effective from date department issued show-cause notice - Held, appellant is held liable to pay duty on dye-stuffs manufactured by it in manner calculated by department from 16-10-1976 - Appeal partly allowed.
