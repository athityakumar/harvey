Pt.Chet Ram Vashist v Municipal Corporation Of Delhi

Supreme Court of India

26 October 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

R.M. Sahai, N.P. Singh

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1147; (1995) 1 SCC 47; AIR 1995 SC 430; JT 1994 (7) SC 159; 1994(4) SCALE 695; [1994] Supp5 S.C.R. 180

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Municipalities & Local Governments; Service

Summary: Delhi Municipal Corporation Act, 1957, s. 313 - Municipalities - Sanction of lay out plan - Such conditions - Imposition of condition that area specified for part and school shall vest in Corporation - Held, violative of civil rights - Illegal - 'Such conditions' may be understood as to advance objective of provision.
