Meera Bhanja v Nirmala Kumari Choudhury

Supreme Court of India

16 November 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Majmudar, B.P. Jeevan Reddy

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1174; (1995) 1 SCC 170; AIR 1995 SC 455; AIR 1994 SCW 4680; 1995 (2) CCC 378; 1994 (2) HLR 697; JT 1994 (7) SC 536; 1995 (1) MahLJ 825; 1994(4) SCALE 985

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure Code, 1908, O. 47, r. 1 - Review - Jurisdiction - Limits - 'Error apparent on face of record' - Held, means an error which strikes once on mere looking at Record without any long drawn Reasoning process - Re-appreciating entire evidence and Reversing finding of appellate Court - Exceeding jurisdiction - Order liable to be set aside.
