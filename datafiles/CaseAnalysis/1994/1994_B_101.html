State of Orissa Through Its Principal Secretary v Bimal Kumar Mohanty

Supreme Court of India

21 February 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, B.L. Hansaria

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 240; (1994) 4 SCC 126; (1994) SCC (L&S) 875; AIR 1994 SC 2296; 1994 (27) ATC 530; 1994 (1) CLR 615; 1994 (77) CLT 867; 1994 (68) FLR 970; JT 1994 (2) SC 51; 1995 (1) LLJ 568; 1994 (1) LLN 889; 1994(1) SCALE 685; [1994] 2 S.C.R. 51; 1994 (2) SLJ 72; 1994 (2) SLR 384; 1994 (1) UJ 665

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Orissa Civil Services (Classification, Control and Appeal) Rules, 1962, r. 12 - Constitution, art. 309 - Suspension pending enquiry/ investigation - Audit report and vigilance report making serious allegations against delinquent - Suspension order, whether to be interfered with by Administrative Tribunal - Held, not liable to be interfered with - Appeal allowed.
