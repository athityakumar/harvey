K.S.Sunderaraju Chettiar v M.R. Ramachandra Naidu

Supreme Court of India

18 February 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

G.N. Ray, K. Jayachandra Reddy

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1356; (1994) 5 SCC 14; AIR 1994 SC 2129; JT 1994 (1) SC 679; 1994 (2) RCJ 1; 1994 (1) RCR(Rent) 584; 1994 (1) RentLR 340; 1994(1) SCALE 665; [1994] 2 S.C.R. 20; 1994 (2) UJ 208

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Tamil Nadu Buildings (Lease and Rent Control) Act, 1960, s. 10(3)(a)(iii) - Suit for eviction of tenant on ground not mentioned in notice for eviction - (A) Maintainability - (B) Propriety of second remand by High Court in revision - In light of finding of expansion of business run by landlord's family members by referring to Income Tax records - Held, maintainable - HC should have looked into records and considered justification of finding - Order accordingly.
