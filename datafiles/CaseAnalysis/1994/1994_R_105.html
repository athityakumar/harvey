Mafatlal Group Staff Association and Others v Regional Commissioner Provident Fund and Others

Supreme Court of India

29 March 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

B.P. Jeevan Reddy, Kuldip Singh, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 429; (1994) 4 SCC 58; (1994) SCC (L&S) 852; AIR 1994 SC 2271; 1994 (1) CLR 88; 1994 (68) FLR 1110; JT 1994 (3) SC 133; 1994 (2) LLJ 382; [1994] 3 S.C.R. 184; 1994 (2) UPLBEC 859

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Constitution - Employees' Provident Funds and Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1952, s. 6A - Employees Family Pension Scheme, 1971, r. 3, cl. 34D - Constitution, art. 14 - (A) Membership of Family Pension Fund - Option to become member thereof given to employees who were members of Provident Fund Scheme before 01-03-1971and not to employees becoming member of Provident Fnud thereafter - Scheme if discriminatory - (B) Benefits under Scheme- Amount of - If members can claim not only his contribution but also contributions of employer and Govt. - Held, (A) Not discriminatory on that count - (B) Should broadly approximate to and be commensurate with what he contributes -Member cannot claim that - Appeal dismissed.
