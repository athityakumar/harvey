A. Nagaraju Bros., Visakhapatanam v State of Andhra Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

19 July 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

B.L. Hansaria, B.P. Jeevan Reddy

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 875; (1994) Supp3 SCC 122; 1996 (62) ECR 270; 1994 (72) E.L.T. 801; JT 1994 (5) SC 371; 1994(3) SCALE 308; 1994 (95) STC 1; [1994] Supp1 S.C.R. 784

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax; Sales Tax

Keywords: Luggage

Summary: Customs - Sales Tax - Andhra Pradesh General Sates Tax Act, Entry 113 of Sch 1 - Whether 'VIP' suitcases are plastic articles within meaning of Entry 113 of Sch I ? - Main raw material which goes into manufacture of said suitcases is plastic - Even in common parlance these suitcases are understood to be plastic goods - It is true that certain other materials are also used in manufacturing said suitcases but applying common parlance test and test of usage in trade circles, these goods must be called 'plastic articles' - Held, order of assessing authority granting exemption with respect to turnover relating to said suitcases affirmed - Appeal allowed.
