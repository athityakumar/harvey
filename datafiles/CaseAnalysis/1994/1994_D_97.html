Dr. Ganga Prasad Verma and Others v State of Bihar and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 November 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1110; (1995) Supp1 SCC 192; (1995) SCC (L&S) 383; 1995 (29) ATC 154; 1995 (1) SLJ 152; [1994] Supp5 S.C.R. 455

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Education - Service - Indian Medical Council Act, 1956, s. 33 - Andhra Pradesh Medical and Health Services Special Rules, 1982, r. 5 - Appointment as professor - Whether qualification of M. Ch. in Neurosurgery is a must for post of Professor/Associate Professor? - Statute prescribed rules mandatory for promotion - Regulations made by Medical Council of India u/s. 33 bind State Govt. - Conditions prescribed therein namely, for promotion to post of Professor or Associate Professor, qualification in specialty namely M. Ch. concerned after M. S. /F. R. C. S. is a must - Held, acquisition of qualification of M.Ch. in Neurosurgery is a must for post of Professor/Associate Professor - Appeal dismissed.
