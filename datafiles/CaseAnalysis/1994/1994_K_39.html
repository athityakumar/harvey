P.K. Vijayan v Kamalakshi Amma And Others

Supreme Court of India

30 March 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, B.L. Hansaria

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1434; (1994) 4 SCC 53; AIR 1994 SC 2145; JT 1994 (3) SC 92; 1994 (1) KLT 942; 1995 (1) MLJ(SC) 16; 1994 (1) RCJ 588; 1994 (1) RCR(Rent) 744; 1994 (1) RentLR 383; 1994(2) SCALE 368; [1994] 3 S.C.R. 213

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Ground Of Res Judicata

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 11, Explanation IV - Kerala Buildings (Lease and Rent Control) Act, 1965, s. 11 Proviso - Kerala Land Reforms Act, 1964, ss. 125(3) and 106 - Res judicata - Bar of jurisdiction of Civil Court to entertain eviction petition due to his entitlement to fixity of tenancy u/s. 106 of Land Reforms Act - Failure to raise plea which might and ought to be raised in earlier rent control proceedings - Held, it is not open for tenant to plead that Land Tribunal and not Civil Court has jurisdiction to decide matter - Appeal dismissed.
