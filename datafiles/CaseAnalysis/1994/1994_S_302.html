Premium Granites v State Of T.N.

Supreme Court of India

04 February 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

G.N. Ray, M.N. Venkatachaliah

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1453; (1994) 2 SCC 691; AIR 1994 SC 2233; AIR 1994 SCW 2048; JT 1994 (1) SC 376; 1994 (2) MLJ 55; 1994 (2) MLJ(SC) 55; 1994(1) SCALE 393; [1994] 1 S.C.R. 579; 1994 (2) UJ 145

Case Digest

Subject: Mines & Minerals

Keywords: Granite, Tamil Nadu Minor Mineral Concession Rules, 1959, Interest Of Mineral Development, Madras Buildings (Lease and Rent Control) Act, 1949, Karnataka Minor Mineral Concession Rules, 1969, Civil Services Rules, Mysore Civil Services Rules, 1958, Bombay Prohibition Act 1949

Summary: Tamil Nadu Minor Mineral Concession Rules, 1959, r. 39 -Mines and Minerals (Regulation And Development) Act, 1957, s. 15 - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 14 - Validity - Rule - Power to State Govt. for grant of leases otherwise than in accordance with Rules in interest of mineral development and in Public interest - 'Public interest' and 'mineral developmental' - Held, harmonious construction - Not violative of art. 14.
