R.P.F. Commr. v K.T. Rolling Mills Pvt. Ltd

Supreme Court of India

22 November 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

B.L. Hansaria, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1199; (1995) 1 SCC 181; (1995) SCC (L&S) 272; AIR 1995 SC 943; 1995 (1) CLR 204; 1995 (70) FLR 286; JT 1995 (1) SC 138; 1995 LabIC 1069; 1995 (1) LLJ 882; 1996 (1) LLN 574; 1994(4) SCALE 1023; 1995 (1) SLJ 145; 1995 (1) SLR 19; [1994] Supp5 S.C.R. 643

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Employees' Provident Funds and Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1952, s. 14 B - Recovery of damages - Limitation - Period not prescribed - Should be exercised within reasonable period - Only one Regional Provident Fund Commissioner dealing with cases - Held, delay though appears to be of 12 years is of 1 1/2 years qua instant case - Not unreasonable - Damages levied, not liable to be quashed.
