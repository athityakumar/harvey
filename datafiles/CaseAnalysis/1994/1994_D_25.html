Directorate Of Enforcement v Deepak Mahajan

Supreme Court of India

31 January 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ratnavel Pandian, K. Jayachandra Reddy

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1308; (1994) 3 SCC 440; (1994) SCC (Cr) 785; AIR 1994 SC 1775; AIR 1994 SCW 1656; 1994 (2) ALT(SC) 173; 1994 (2) BLJR 912; [1995] 82 Comp Cas 103; 1994 CRLJ 2269; 1994 (70) E.L.T. 12; JT 1994 (1) SC 290; [1994] 1 S.C.R. 445

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Arrest - Foreign Exchange Regulation Act, 1973, s. 35 - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 20(3) and 22 - Code of Criminal procedure, 1973 - Customs Act, s. 104 - (1) Whether the Magistrate has jurisdiction to authorise detention of person arrested under section 35 of FERA, 1973 to order for detention of person under s. 167(2) of CrPC, 1973 - Held, yes - Provisions of s. 67 of CrPC, 1973 are applicable in case of person detained under s. 35 of FERA, 1973 - S. 35 of FERA, 1973 and section 104(2) of the Customs Act are in pari materia - It is not an indispensable pre-requisite that in all cases arrest should have been effected only by a police officer there must be records of entries of a case diary - Person arrested to be produced before Court - Disposed accordingly.
