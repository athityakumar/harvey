Sampuran Singh and Others v State of Haryana and Others

Supreme Court of India

19 January 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1261; (1994) Supp2 SCC 206; AIR 1994 SC 2126; JT 1994 (3) SC 361; 1994 (1) RentLR 559; 1994(1) SCALE 859; [1994] 1 S.C.R. 176; 1994 (1) UJ 687

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Haryana Ceiling on Land Holding Act, 1972, ss. 1 and 12(3) - Punjab Security of Land Tenures Act, 1953, s. 2(5) - Declaration as to surplus land under East Punjab Land Tenures Act becoming final - Previous land holders allowed to enjoy land - But surplus land vests in State on commencement of 1972 Act - (A) Whether previous Land Holders can claim fresh computation of land among him and his children who had become major - (B) Validity of Act - Held, cannot claim - Act not ultra vires on ground that it is given retrospective effect and it affects vested right of landholder - Appeal dismissed.
