Subhash, S/O Shriram Dhonde v State of Maharashtra and Another

Supreme Court of India

13 May 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

N.P. Singh, P.B. Sawant

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1539; (1995) Supp3 SCC 332; (1995) SCC (L&S) 1231; 1995 (31) ATC 146

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Motor Vehicles Department (Recruitment) Rules, 1991, rr. 3(c) and 3(e) - Working experience prior to acquisition of basic qualifications - Held, tribunal has committed an error in relying upon circular which cannot replace rules framed u/art. 309 of Constitution - As appellant satisfies qualifications required by rules - Tribunal order set aside - Appeal allowed.
