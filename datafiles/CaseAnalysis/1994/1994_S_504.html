S. Ramachandra Raju v State of Orissa

Supreme Court of India

31 August 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 989; (1994) Supp3 SCC 424; (1995) SCC (L&S) 74; AIR 1995 SC 111; 1994 (28) ATC 443; 1994 (69) FLR 832; JT 1994 (5) SC 459; 1995 (1) LLJ 1083; 1994 (2) LLN 1311; 1994(3) SCALE 927; 1994 (3) SLJ 95; 1994 (5) SLR 199; [1994] Supp2 S.C.R. 828; 1995 (2) UPLBEC 1008

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Constitution Of India,1950, art. 311 and 309, Proviso - Orissa Service Code, 1939, r. 71(a) - Compulsory retirement - Solitary adverse report of one year against employee - Basis for passing order - Earlier and subsequent report showing meritorious service records not considered - Held, order liable to be set aside - Entire service record, more particular, latest - Should form foundation for opinion.
