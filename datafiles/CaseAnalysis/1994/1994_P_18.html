Union Of India v P.N.Menon

Supreme Court of India

17 March 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

N.P. Singh, A.M. Ahmadi

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 2079; (1994) 4 SCC 68; (1994) SCC (L&S) 860; AIR 1994 SC 2221; 1994 (27) ATC 515; 1995 (1) CLR 590; 1994 (68) FLR 1212; JT 1994 (3) SC 26; 1995 (2) LLJ 307; 1994 (2) LLN 434; 1994(2) SCALE 276; [1994] 3 S.C.R. 44; 1994 (2) SLR 335; 1994 (2) UJ 25; 1994 (2) UPLBEC 944

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Computation Of Pension

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14 and 311 - Central Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1972, r. 33 Retirement benefits - Merger of portion of dearness allowance with pay for retirement benefits - Benefit of, extended to Govt. servants who retired on or after cut-off date - Held, is not arbitrary - Appeal allowed.
