Narender Kumar Chandla v Haryana

Supreme Court of India

04 February 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

B.L. Hansaria, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1454; (1994) 4 SCC 460; (1994) SCC (L&S) 882; AIR 1995 SC 519; 1994 (27) ATC 616; 1994 (1) CLR 627; 1994 (68) FLR 942; JT 1994 (2) SC 94; 1995 LabIC 309; 1994 (2) LLN 746; 1994(1) SCALE 763; [1994] 1 S.C.R. 657; 1994 (2) SLJ 133; 1994 (2) SLR 9; 1994 (1) UJ 675

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 21 and 311 - Life - Includes livelihood - Employee afflicted with unfortunate disease causing physical disablement thereby unable to perform duties - Transferred to lower pay scale - Held, not proper - Should be adjusted in suitable post - Pay to be protected - Appeal allowed.
