State Trading Corpn.Of India Ltd v Jainsons Clothing Corpn.

Supreme Court of India

14 September 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1676; (1994) 6 SCC 597; AIR 1994 SC 2778; AIR 1994 SCW 4331; 1995 (1) ARBLR 351; 1996 (1) BC 27; [1996] 85 Comp Cas 470; JT 1995 (5) SC 403; 1995 (2) MLJ(SC) 89; 1994(4) SCALE 332; [1994] Supp3 S.C.R. 527

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Contract & Commercial

Keywords: Contract Of Guarantee

Summary: Contract and Commercial - Contract Act, 1872, ss. 17 and 126 - STC entered into contract of supply of goods to a foreign buyer - And also with an indian supplier for supply of such goods to it - With a bank guarantee being a part of the latter agreement - Supplier failed to perform contract with STC - Cancellation of contract between Corporation and the foreign buyer - Suit filed - Injunction order against corporation invoking bank gaurantee - Appeal aginst order - Whether the court is justified in granting injunction on such grounds - Held, the Court would not be justified in granting injunction - Appeal allowed.
