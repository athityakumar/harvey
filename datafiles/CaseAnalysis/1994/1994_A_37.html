Additional Spl. Land Acquisition Officer, Bangalore v Thakoredas, Major and Others

Supreme Court of India

08 February 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1591; (1997) 11 SCC 412; AIR 1994 SC 2227

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 18(3)(b)(a) - Limitation Act, 1908, art. 137 - Failure of Deputy Commr. to make reference within 90 days - So applicant sought direction from Court to Deputy Commr. to make reference - Supreme Court held that application should be made within 3 years from date of expiry of 90 days as provided u/art. 137 of Limitation Act - Appeal allowed.
