Madura Coats Limited v Collector of Central Excise, Madurai

Supreme Court of India

10 January 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

J.S. Verma, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 76; 1994 (119) CTR 63; 1997 (57) ECC 148; 1995 (58) ECR 240; 1994 (71) E.L.T. 347

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Indirect Tax

Summary: Excise - Remedy of appeal to the Tribunal - Period for limitation prescribed for filing the appeal has elapsed - Direction that in case the appeal is filed by the petitioner within two weeks the appeal shall not be dismissed as time-barred - Pendency of proceedings be treated sufficient cause for condoning the delay - S.L.Ps. dismissed.
