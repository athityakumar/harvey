 K.S. Paripoornan v State Of Kerala

Supreme Court of India

12 September 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

P.B. Sawant, M.N. Venkatachaliah, R.M. Sahai, S.C. Agrawal, S.P. Bharucha

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1227; (1994) 5 SCC 593; AIR 1995 SC 1012; AIR 1994 SCW 4861; JT 1994 (6) SC 182; 1994 (2) KLT 763; 1994 (2) RentLR 373; 1994(4) SCALE 192; 1995 (1) SCJ 651; [1994] Supp3 S.C.R. 405

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Kerala Land Acquisition Act, 1961

Summary: Land & Property - Civil Procedure Code, 1908, ss. 2(2) and (9) - Land Acquisition Act, 1984, ss. 4(1), 9, 11, 15, 16, 17, 23, 28-A and 54 - Land Acquisition Act, 1961 - Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, 1984, s. 30(1) - Indian Limitation Act, 1908 - Whether benefit of s. 23(1-A) of Land Acquisition Act,1984 is to be granted only in proceedings for acquisition of land referred to in cls. (a) and (b) of s. 30(1) of Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, 1984 or it is to be granted in all proceedings pending before Courts on 24/09/1984? - Held, amending Act and in particular s. 23(1-A) was to give additional amount to deprived landowners in all proceedings which were pending before Collector on 30/04/1982 and before reference Court on 24/09/1984 ( date of commencement of Act) - Under cl. (a) of sub-s. (1) of s. 30, Collector has power to grant benefit of s. 23(1-A) of principal Act in every proceeding for acquisition which is pending before him on 30/04/1982 but in which he has made no award before that date and every proceeding for acquisition commenced after 30/04/1982 whether or not he has made his award in such proceeding before 24/09/1984 - If award has made in such proceeding before that date, he is empowered to reopen same and grant said benefit - Appeal disposed of.
