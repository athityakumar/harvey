Union Of India v Justice S.S. Sandhawalia

Supreme Court of India

11 January 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 387; (1994) 2 SCC 240; (1994) SCC (L&S) 530; AIR 1994 SC 1377; 1994 (26) ATC 922; 1994 (68) FLR 595; JT 1994 (1) SC 62; 1994 (2) LLJ 509; 1994 (1) LLN 866; 1994 (2) MLJ(SC) 32; 1994(1) SCALE 69; [1994] 1 S.C.R. 83; 1994 (3) SLJ 181; 1994 (1) SLR 343; 1994 (1) UJ 387; 1994 (1) UPLBEC 192

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: High Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Act, 1954, All India Services (Leave) Rules, 1955, All India Services (House Rent Allowance) Rules, 1977

Summary: Constitution of India, Arts.221 and 309; All India Services (Leave) Rules, 1955, r.20B; High Court Judges Rules, 1956, r.2; High Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Act, 1954, ss.2(c) and 22A - 'Actual service' - Leave Encashment - Computation - Held, city compensatory allowance and House Rent allowance is to be excluded.
