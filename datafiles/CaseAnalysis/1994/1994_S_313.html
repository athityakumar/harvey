State Of U.P. v Ramona Perhar

Supreme Court of India

02 September 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

M.N. Venkatachaliah, B.P. Jeevan Reddy

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 411; (1994) 6 SCC 1; AIR 1995 SC 241; AIR 1994 SCW 4390; 1994 (24) ALR 344; JT 1994 (5) SC 632; 1994(3) SCALE 955; 1994 (5) SLR 197; [1994] Supp3 S.C.R. 75; 1994 (2) UJ 677; 1994 (3) UPLBEC 1858

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Education - Constitution, art. 226 - Interim order - Granting admission to educational institution - Whether interim order granting admission to educational institution can be passed without full hearing - Held, interim order permitted transfer of student from outside State to State Medical College - Admissions to educational institutions should not be granted by passing interim orders without full hearing - Interim order should not be passed - Order set aside - Appeal allowed.
