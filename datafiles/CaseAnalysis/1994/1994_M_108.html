State Of U.P. v Mata Bhikha And Others

Supreme Court of India

09 March 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ratnavel Pandian, P.B. Sawant

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1622; (1994) 4 SCC 95; (1994) SCC (Cr) 831; AIR 1994 SCW 1935; 1994 (2) CCR 406; 1994 (1) Crimes 945; JT 1994 (2) SC 565; 1994(2) SCALE 235; [1994] 2 S.C.R. 368; 1994 (1) UJ 697

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Preliminary Order

Summary: Criminal - Practice & Procedure - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, ss. 145, 188 and 195(1)(a) - Interpretation of expression 'the public servant concerned' appearing in s. 195(1)(a) - Held, a successor in office of a public servant concerned will also fall within ambit of expression 'public servant concerned' - Appeal allowed.
