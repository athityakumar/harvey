Krishi Utpadan Mandi Samiti and Another v Makrand Singh and Others

Supreme Court of India

01 December 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 666; (1995) 2 SCC 497; JT 1995 (1) SC 487; 1995 (1) RentLR 528; 1995(1) SCALE 48; [1994] Supp6 S.C.R. 255

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894 - Land Acquisition (Amendment) Act, 1984, s. 6(1) - Whether High Court was right in concluding that declaration u/s. 6(1) was published after 3 years and last of publications shall be last date for purpose of computing three years' period envisaged in cl. (i) of proviso to s. 6(1)? or which date of publications in three steps i.e., publication in Gazette, two newspapers and local publication to be last date for purpose of computing three years' limitation prescribed in proviso to s. 6(1)? - Publication in Official Gazette already made under proviso to s. 6(1) is complete, as soon as declaration u/s. 6(1) was published in Official Gazette - That will be date for purpose of computation of three years' period from last of dates of publication of notification u/s. 4(1) - Held, HC, therefore, was not right in its conclusion that since declaration was published in the newspapers on 4-6-1987, after the expiry of three years, the declaration u/s. 6(1) and the notification u/s. 4(1) stood lapsed - It is clearly illegal - Appeals allowed.
