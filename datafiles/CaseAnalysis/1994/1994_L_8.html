Jugraj Singh And Another v Labh Singh And Others

Supreme Court of India

28 November 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 575; (1995) 2 SCC 31; AIR 1995 SC 945; AIR 1995 SCW 901; 1994 (3) CCC 768; JT 1996 (Supp) SC 642; 1995 (1) RentLR 368; 1994(5) SCALE 286; [1994] Supp6 S.C.R. 168; 1995 (1) UJ 221

Case Digest

Subject: Contract & Commercial

Summary: Contract & Commercial - Specific Relief Act, 1963, s. 16(3) - Suit - Specific performance of contract - Ready and willingness of plaintiff - Held, continuous readiness and willingness throughout from date of agreement till date of hearing of suit must be proved - Plea about ready and willingness of plaintiff is specifically available to vendor - Special leave petition dismissed.
