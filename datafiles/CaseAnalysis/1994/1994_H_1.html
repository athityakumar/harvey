H. P. Mineral and Industrial Development Corporation Employee's Union v State of Himachal Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

06 December 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Majmudar

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 2150; (1996) 7 SCC 139; (1996) SCC (L&S) 470

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Whether the provisions of s. 25-N were applicable to the present case and the workmen could be retrenched only by giving three months' notice which was not given? - S. 25-N which deals with retrenchment cannot apply to the present case where termination of the services of the workmen was brought about as a result of the closure of the undertaking - S. 25-N imposed restrictions in the matter of retrenchment of workmen employed in large undertakings and obligatory to the management - Of the responded - Company to give three months' notice as required by s. 25-N - Compensation should paid to the workmen - Appeal dismissed.
