U.P. State Industrial Development Corporation Limited v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

19 October 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, M.K. Mukherjee, S.C. Sen

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1124; (1995) 2 SCC 766; 1994 (3) CCC 641; 1994(4) SCALE 1137; [1994] Supp4 S.C.R. 581A

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property

Summary: Civil Procedure - Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 28 A - Whether Collector was right in determining compensation? - Claimants who received compensation under protest sought for and secured references u/s. 18 to Civil Court - Claimants who received compensation without protest made applications u/s. 28-A(1) - Following Babua Ram's case - Held, award of Collector made u/s. 28-A(2) quashed - Collector/Land Acquisition Officer is directed to keep application filed u/s. 28-A(1) of Act pending till disposal of appeal - On receipt of judgment from High Court or in an appeal by this Court Land Acquisition Officer is directed to determine compensation based on final judgment according to law - Appeal allowed.
