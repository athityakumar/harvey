Kavitha Trejam v Balsara Hygiene Products

Supreme Court of India

11 July 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

M.N. Venkatachaliah, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1667; (1994) 5 SCC 380; AIR 1995 SC 441; AIR 1994 SCW 661; 1994 (55) DLT 243; JT 1994 (4) SC 519; 1994(3) SCALE 168; [1994] Supp1 S.C.R. 340

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure; Tort

Keywords: Restitution, Common Law

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, ss. 144 and 151 - In cases not falling within ambit of s. 144 can restitution be made under inherent powers - Held, restitution could be made under inherent powers - Aggrieved party in such case can appeal to larger and general powers of restitution inherent in every court - Appeal allowed.
