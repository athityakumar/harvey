Billa Jagan Mohan Reddy v Billa Sanjeeva Reddy

Supreme Court of India

28 January 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

B.L. Hansaria, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 2072; (1994) 4 SCC 659; 1994 (23) ALR 631; JT 1994 (3) SC 339; 1994 (1) RentLR 400; 1994(2) SCALE 487; [1994] 1 S.C.R. 429; 1994 (Supp) RD 172

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Land & Property - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 13, r. 1, O. 41, r. 27 - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 30 - Limitation Act, 1963, s. 5 - Production of documents - Rebuttal - When documentary evidence to be produced? - Documents sought to be produced in Trial Court before arguments are completed - Documents not in custody of appellants - Delay in obtaining certified copies from authorities - Held, appellants entitled an opportunity and rebuttal if any and their relevance and effect may be considered in deciding issues that arose in controversy - Appeal allowed.
