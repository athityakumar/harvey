State of Uttar Pradesh and Another v Bihari Lal

Supreme Court of India

05 September 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, N. Venkatachala

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 788; (1994) Supp3 SCC 593; (1995) SCC (L&S) 177; AIR 1995 SC 1161; 1994 (28) ATC 586; 1995 LabIC 1597; 1995 (1) LLN 852; 1994(4) SCALE 308; 1995 (1) SLJ 110; 1994 (5) SLR 606; [1994] Supp3 S.C.R. 108

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 311 - Fundamental Rules, r. 56(j) - Interference with decision as to compulsory retirement - Held, decision taken on consideration of entire service record and bona fide and in public interest cannot be interfered with on ground that a different view is possible - Appeal dismissed.
