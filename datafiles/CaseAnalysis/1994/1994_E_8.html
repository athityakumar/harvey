Central Bank Of India v Elmot Engineering Co.

Supreme Court of India

27 April 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mohan, M.N. Venkatachaliah

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 624; (1994) 4 SCC 159; AIR 1994 SC 2358; [1994] 81 Comp Cas 13; 1994 (3) CompLJ 1; JT 1994 (7) SC 54; 1994(2) SCALE 739; [1994] 3 S.C.R. 766; 1994 (2) UJ 284

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Summary: Corporate - Companies Act, 1956, s. 446 - Winding up - Petition filed at Bombay High Court - Pendency - Equitable mortgages created, in respect of properties situated in 'R' District of Andhra Pradesh - Application for leave to prosecute said suit at Hyderabad - Rejection of - Order directing transfer of suits to Bombay HC on ground of convenience of official liquidator - If liable to be set aside - Held, order set aside - Appeal allowed.
