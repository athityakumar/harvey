Niaz Mohammad v Statee Of Harayana

Supreme Court of India

20 September 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

N.P. Singh, Kuldip Singh, M.N. Venkatachaliah

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 1054; (1994) 6 SCC 332; AIR 1995 SC 308; AIR 1994 SCW 4481; 1994 (24) ALR 563; 2001 CRLJ 4204; JT 1994 (6) SC 260; 1994(4) SCALE 292; 1994 (5) SLR 600; [1994] Supp3 S.C.R. 720; 1995 (1) UJ 124

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Civil Procedure; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Contempt of Courts Act, 1971, s. 2(b) - Willful disobedience - Claim for parity in pay scale by instructors under Adult and Non-Formal Education Scheme by treating them at par with squad teachers was allowed by Court - Direction issued that arrears should be paid with effect from their dates of appointments - Liability of payment of 28 crores on State - Said fact not known to court or parties to proceedings - Payment of only 20 crores made by State - Whether there is any willful disobedience on part of State in complying with direction of Court? - Held, no willful disobedience - Appeal dismissed.
