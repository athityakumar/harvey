University of Delhi v Raj Singh and Others

Supreme Court of India

08 September 1994

Case Analysis

Bench

S.P. Bharucha, A.M. Ahmadi

Where Reported

1994 Indlaw SC 945; (1994) Supp3 SCC 516; (1995) SCC (L&S) 118; AIR 1995 SC 336; AIR 1994 SCW 4520; 1994 (28) ATC 541; JT 1994 (6) SC 1; 1994(4) SCALE 10; 1994 (3) SLJ 116; 1994 (5) SLR 286; [1994] Supp3 S.C.R. 217; 1994 (2) UJ 753

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Education

Keywords: University Grants Commission (Qualifications Required Of a Person To Be Appointed

Summary: University Grants Commission Act, 1956, ss. 12 A(2) and 26(1)(c) - University Grants Commission (Qualifications required of a person to be appointed to teaching staff of a University and institutions affiliated to it) Regulations, 1991, Regns. 2 and 3 - Constitution of India, 1950, Sch. 7, List I, Entry 66 - Appointment of - Eligibility of teaching staff - Validity of - Held, regulations framed by U. G. C. - Has binding nature.
