Brihan Maharashtra Sugar Syndicate Limited v Janardan Ramchandra Kulkarni and Others

Supreme Court of India

22 February 1960

Case Analysis

Bench

A.K. Sarkar, Mohammad Hidayatullah, S.K. Das

Where Reported

1960 Indlaw SC 416; AIR 1960 SC 794; [1960] 30 Comp Cas 468; 1960 SCJ 760; [1960] 3 S.C.R. 85

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Corporate - Companies Act, 1956, s. 658 - Companies Act, 1913, s. 153C - General Clauses Act, 1897, s. 6 - Proceedings u/s. 153(C) repealed Act of 1913 Act - Applicability of s. 6, of 1897 Act - Effect on jurisdiction of District Judge - Held, s. 658 might have been enacted ex abundanti cautela and s. 6 remains applicable - Jurisdiction not affected - Appeal dismissed.
