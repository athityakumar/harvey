Lloyds Bank, Limited v Panna Lal Gupta and Others

Supreme Court of India

18 November 1960

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar

Where Reported

1960 Indlaw SC 27; AIR 1967 SC 428; 1961 (2) FLR 219; 1961 (1) LLJ 18

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes (Banking Companies) Decisions Act, 1955, s. 3 - Industrial Disputes Act, s. 2 - Whether tribunal was right in holding that three workmen could claim status of supervisors? - In deciding status of an employee their designation is not decisive factor -The nature and duties of function assigned to them should be considered - Held, having regard to nature of duties and function assigned to three employees by appellant, it is evident that they are not supervisors - Tribunals conclusion is erroneous and unsustainable - Appeal allowed.
