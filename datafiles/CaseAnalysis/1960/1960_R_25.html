Titaghur Paper Mills Company, Limited v Ram Naresh Kumar and Another

Supreme Court of India

03 March 1960

Case Analysis

Bench

Kailas Nath Wanchoo

Where Reported

1960 Indlaw SC 6; 1961 (2) FLR 528; 1961 (1) LLJ 511

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, s. 33 - Termination - Legality - Whether termination of service of respondent was justified and whether he was entitled to any relief? - Held, dereliction of duty was clearly established - Management had right of dismissal under relevant standing orders, proper enquiry was held and explanation of workman was unsatisfactory - Not a case of victimization - Order of tribunal set aside - Order of dismissal passed by appellant upheld - Appeal allowed.
