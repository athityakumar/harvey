State of Bombay v Bandhan Ram Bhandani and Others

Supreme Court of India

23 September 1960

Case Analysis

Bench

A.K. Sarkar, Syed Jaffer Imam, K.C. Das Gupta

Where Reported

1960 Indlaw SC 458; AIR 1961 SC 186; 1961 (63) BomLR 423; [1961] 31 Comp Cas 1; 1961 CRLJ 319; 1960 (2) KerLR 631; [1961] 1 S.C.R. 801

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Customs

Keywords: Wilfully

Summary: Corporate - Criminal - Companies Act, 1913 - ss. 32(3), 32(5), 76, 131 and 133(3) - Companies Act, 1956, ss. 161, 162, 166 and 210 -(A) Whether director of Co. is criminally liable u/ss. 32(5) and 133 ?- Held, as no meeting held and directors are liable for not organising meeting, a person charged with an offence could not rely on his own default as an answer to charge - S. 76 cannot be used as defence - Directors are liable - Appeals allowed.
