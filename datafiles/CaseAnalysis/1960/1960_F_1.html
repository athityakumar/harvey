Shri Birdhichand Sharma v First Civil Judge Nagpur and Others

Supreme Court of India

09 December 1960

Case Analysis

Bench

Kailas Nath Wanchoo, Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar

Where Reported

1960 Indlaw SC 232; AIR 1961 SC 644; 1961 (2) FLR 577; 1961 (2) LLJ 86; [1961] 3 S.C.R. 161

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Factories Act, 1948, s. 2 (1) - What are the tests to determine whether a particular person is a worker or an independent contractor? - Held, bidi workers not at liberty to work at their homes - Working within factory hours - Attendance noted in factory - Worker remaining absent for 8 days (without leave) could be removed - Payment on piece-rate basis - Particular person is a worker within meaning of s. 2 (1) of Factories Act, 1948 - Appeal dismissed.
