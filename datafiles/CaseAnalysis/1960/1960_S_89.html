 Diamond Sugar Mills Limited, and Another v State of Uttar Pradesh and Another

Supreme Court of India

13 December 1960

Case Analysis

Bench

K.C. Das Gupta, Raghubar Dayal, N. Rajagopala Ayyangar, Syed Jaffer Imam, J.L. Kapur

Where Reported

1960 Indlaw SC 444; AIR 1961 SC 652; 1961 (1) SCJ 652; [1961] 3 S.C.R. 242

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax

Summary: Constitution - Constitution of India, 1950, Entry 52, Sch. 7 - U P Sugarcane Cess Act, 1956, s. 3 - Whether s. 3 of U P Sugarcane Cess Act, 1956 falls within Entry 52 of State List in Sch. 7 of Constitution ? - Held, no - Meaning of words Local area in Entry 52 - An area administered by local bodies like municipality, district board, local board, union board, panchayat or like - Premises of a factory not a local area - S. 3 of U P Sugarcane Cess Act, 1956, empowering Governor to impose a cess on entry of sugarcane into premises of a factory did not fall within Entry 52 of State List - Appeal allowed.
