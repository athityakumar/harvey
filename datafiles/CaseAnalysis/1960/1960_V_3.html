State of Maharashtra v Vishnu Ramchandra

Supreme Court of India

18 October 1960

Case Analysis

Bench

Mohammad Hidayatullah, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1960 Indlaw SC 453; AIR 1961 SC 307; 1961 (63) BomLR 427; 1961 CRLJ 450; 1961 (1) SCJ 267; [1961] 2 S.C.R. 26

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Prohibition Act

Summary: Criminal - Bombay Police Act, 1951, s. 57 - Indian Penal Code, ss. 114 and 380 -(A)Whether externment order of offender u/s 57 of Bombay Police Act, 1951 can be based on past actions of offender committed before coming into force of Act? - Held, s. 57 is to protect public from activities of undesirable person who have been convicted of offences of a particular kind - Language of s. 57 does not bar an action based on past actions of offender before Act was passed - An externment order u/s 57 must be made bona fide - A conviction which is sufficiently proximate in time - Matter Remitted To High Court.
