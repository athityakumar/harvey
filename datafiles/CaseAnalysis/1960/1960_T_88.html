Ahmedabad Textile Industry's Research Association v State of Bombay and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 November 1960

Case Analysis

Bench

Kailas Nath Wanchoo, Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, A.K. Sarkar

Where Reported

1960 Indlaw SC 266; AIR 1961 SC 484; 1961 (2) FLR 33; 1960 (2) LLJ 720; 1962 (2) SCJ 645; [1961] 2 S.C.R. 480

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Research, Rationalisation

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act,1947, ss. 2(j), 2(k) and 10 - Whether activity of Ahmedabad Textile Industry's Research Association is an 'Industry' u/s. 2(j) of Industrial Disputes Act, 1947? - Held, tests to determine - Condition of the co-operation between the employer and employee necessary - Object to render material service to the community - It is an industry and not a purely educational institution - Appeal dismissed.
