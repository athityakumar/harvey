Commissioner of Income Tax, Bombay Circle Ii v National Syndicate, Bombay

Supreme Court of India

01 November 1960

Case Analysis

Bench

Mohammad Hidayatullah, S.K. Das, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1960 Indlaw SC 133; AIR 1961 SC 398; [1961] 41 ITR 225; 1962 (2) SCJ 640; [1961] 2 S.C.R. 229

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Motor Lorry

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Trade - Income Tax Act, 1922, s. 10(2)(vii) - Whether loss arising out of sale of machinery after closure of business during course of accounting year is a permissible deduction u/s. 10(2)(vii) of Income Tax Act, 1922 ? - Held, under scheme of s. 10 business must have been 'carried on by assessee' - Building, machinery or plant must have been used for purposes of business - Sale should have taken place during year of account - Loss to be brought into books of assessee and written off - Loss suffered being business loss is a permissible deduction u/s. 10(2)(vii) - Appeal dismissed.
