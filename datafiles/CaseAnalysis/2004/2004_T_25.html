Talcher Municipality v Talcher Regulated Mkt. Committee And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

28 July 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 506; (2004) 6 SCC 178; AIR 2004 SC 3954; JT 2004 (6) SC 65; 2004(6) SCALE 339; [2004] Supp3 S.C.R. 167; 2004 (3) UPLBEC 2852

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property; Trade

Keywords: Agricultural Produce Markets

Summary: [A.] Land & Property - Municipalities & Local Governments - Orissa Municipal Act, 1950, ss. 295, 296 - Orissa Agricultural Produce Markets Act, 1956, ss. 11, 2(vi), 2(vii), 4(1), 4(4), 4(5) - Constitution Of India, 1950 - Whether land and building of a daily market owned by a Municipality or a Gram Panchayat where notified agricultural produces are bought and sold is liable to be transferred to Market Committee, if requisition is made - Held, s.4(4) clearly mandates that even market of a Municipality or a Gram Panchayat falling within market area will have to be transferred if requisitioned therefore - Appeal dismissed.
