Collector Of Central Excise, Calcutta v Messrs Alnoori Tobacco Products and Another

Supreme Court of India

21 July 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, S.N. Variava

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 495; (2004) 6 SCC 186; 2004 (170) E.L.T. 135; JT 2004 (5) SC 593; 2004 (63) RLT 463; 2004(6) SCALE 232; [2004] Supp3 S.C.R. 109

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax; Trade

Summary: [A.] Excise - Central Excise Tariff Act, 1985 - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, s. 35(F) - Central Excise Rules, 1944 - Classification - Tobacco powder obtained by crushing of tobacco leaves, stems, stalks and butts whether classifiable under tariff sub-heading 2401.00 as un-manufactured tobacco or as manufactured tobacco under sub-heading 2404.90 - Held, CEGAT did not consider relevant aspects and proceeded to decide appeals on merits without examining propriety of dismissal of appeals by Collector (Appeals) for non compliance with requirements of Section 35(F) of Act - Remitted matter back to CEGAT for adjudication afresh.
