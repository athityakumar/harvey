N.H.P.C and Another v Nanak Chand and Another

Supreme Court of India

15 October 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 896; (2004) 12 SCC 487; 2005 (5) AWC 4165; 2004 (103) FLR 707; 2005 (1) JhrCR(SC) 111; JT 2004 (9) SC 191; 2005 (1) LLJ 240; 2005 (1) MLJ(SC) 47; 2004(8) SCALE 867; 2005 (1) SLR 1; [2004] Supp5 S.C.R. 596; 2005 (1) UPLBEC 60

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Service

Summary: Service - Appointment on compassionate ground - Division Bench of HC directing that respondent be given compassionate appointment by Union of India - Whether application for appointment on compassionate ground after 10 years of the death of his father sustainable? - Held, none of considerations can operate when application is made after a long period of time - Appeal allowed.
