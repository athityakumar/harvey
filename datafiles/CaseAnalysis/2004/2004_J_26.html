Narender Singh and Others v Jai Bhagwan and Others

Supreme Court of India

09 December 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

D.M. Dharmadhikari, H.K. Sema

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 1080; (2005) 9 SCC 157; AIR 2005 SC 582; 2005 (1) AWC 7; JT 2004 (10) SC 345; 2005 (2) LCD 1580; 2005 (2) RCR(Civil) 753; 2006 (100) RD 69; 2004(10) SCALE 335; [2004] Supp6 S.C.R. 828

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: [A.] Land & Property - Uttar Pradesh Consolidation of Holdings Act 1953, s. 49 - Uttar Pradesh Zamlndari Abolition and Land Reforms Act, 1950, s. 122-B - Suit for Specific Relief of agreement of sale - Granted decree for Specific Performance - Whether defendant's plea of joint ownership is barred in civil court by provisions of s.49 of UP Consolidation of Holdings Act 1953? - Held, proceedings for recording sons as joint owners having not been initiated HC was right in invoking bar against such plea in suit in accordance with s.49 - There is no equity in favour of defendant and his LR - Appeal dismissed.
