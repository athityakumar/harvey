Express Publications (Madurai) Limited v Union of India and Another

Supreme Court of India

11 March 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.K. Sabharwal, D.M. Dharmadhikari

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 642; (2004) 11 SCC 526; AIR 2004 SC 1950; 2004 (2) CLR 452; 2004 (4) FLR 198; JT 2004 (3) SC 510; 2004 (2) LLJ 356; 2004 (2) LLN 748; 2004(3) SCALE 187; [2004] 2 S.C.R. 1098; 2004 (3) SLR 315

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Land & Property

Keywords: Freedom Of Speech And Expression, Freedom Of Press, Constitutionality, Working Journalists And Other Newspaper Employees (Conditions Of Service) And Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1955, Working Journalists (Conditions of Service) and Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1955, Bombay Rent, Hotel and lodging House Rates Control Act, 1947

Summary: Constitution - Corporate - Labour & Industrial Law - Land & Property - Media & Entertainment - Rent Control - Service - Employees' Provident Funds Act, 1952, s. 5 - Working Journalists (Conditions Of Service) and Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1955, ss. 2 and 15 - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 14, 19(l)(a) and 32 - Andhra Pradesh Buildings (Lease, Rent and Eviction) Control Act, 1960, s. 32(b) - Whether employees of appellant/newspaper industry, for the purposes of provident fund scheme, fall in the category of excluded employees despite being paid as notified by Government of India from time to time?
