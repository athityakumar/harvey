Joginder Singh Sodhi v Amar Kaur

Supreme Court of India

08 October 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

C.K. Thakker, Arijit Pasayat

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 876; (2005) 1 SCC 31; 2005 (58) ALR 683; JT 2004 (9) SC 100; 2005 (139) PLR 364; 2004 (2) RCR(Rent) 493; 2004(8) SCALE 584; [2004] Supp5 S.C.R. 303

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure; Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - East Punjab Urban Rent Control Act, s. 13 - Eviction - Ground of sub-letting - Eviction granted by all courts - Held, it mandates that no tenant can sub-let tenanted property or part thereof without written consent of landlord - Order of eviction cannot be held illegal as doctrine of waiver cannot be applied - No interference order of eviction passed by Rent Controller and confirmed by Appellate Authority as well as by HC - Appeal dismissed.
