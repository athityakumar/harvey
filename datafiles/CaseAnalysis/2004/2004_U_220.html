Pratap Raj Tanwani and Another v Uttam Chand and Another

Supreme Court of India

08 September 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, P.P. Naolekar

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 778; (2004) 8 SCC 490; 2004 (57) ALR 444; JT 2004 (7) SC 366; 2004 (2) RCR(Rent) 436; 2004(7) SCALE 631; [2004] Supp4 S.C.R. 197

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure; Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Madhya Pradesh Accommodation Control Act, 1961, s. 12(1)(a)(b) (f) - Suit for eviction - Bona fide need - High Court considered subsequent events which appellants highlighted and tend to hold that bona fide need continues to subsist - HC's finding, whether justified? - Held, no - If there is deviation from purposes for which possession has been recovered respondents shall re-deliver possession to appellants-tenants - Appeal dismissed.
