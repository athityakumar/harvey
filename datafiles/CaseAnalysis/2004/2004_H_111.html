Yeshwant Sakhalkar and Another v Hirabat Kamat Mhamai and Another

Supreme Court of India

30 April 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 1123; (2004) 6 SCC 71; 2004(9) SCALE 38

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Civil Procedure Code, 1908, s. 115 - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 227 - Whether in terms of amendment made to s. 115 of CPC, High Court was justified in entertaining revisional application against appellate order, by exercising its jurisdiction u/art. 227 of Constitution? - Surya Dev Rai v Ram Chander Rai 2003 Indlaw SC 598 followed - Held, there cannot be any doubt that writ petition was maintainable, but its jurisdiction was limited - Appeal disposed of.
