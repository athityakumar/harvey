State of Uttar Pradesh v Section Officer Brotherhood and another

Supreme Court of India

27 September 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, N. Santosh Hegde

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 833; (2004) 8 SCC 286; (2004) SCC (L&S) 1115; AIR 2004 SC 4769; 2004 (4) AWC 3396; 2004 (103) FLR 570; JT 2004 (8) SC 179; 2004(8) SCALE 247; 2004 (6) SLR 129; [2004] Supp4 S.C.R. 744; 2005 (1) UPLBEC 3

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Service

Keywords: Eligibility - Seniority - Cadre

Summary: Service - Judiciary - Allahabad High Court Officers and Staff (Conditions of Service & Conduct) Rules, 1976 - Demand of increase in pay scale by Section Officers, Private Secretaries, Bench Secretaries and Assistant Registrars working in HC of Judicature at Allahabad on ground of scales of pay paid to their counterparts of Delhi High Court - Appropriate directions issued - Held, it will, be open to Chief Justice of Allahabad HC to frame appropriate rules as has been done in case of Private Secretaries or constitute an appropriate committee for said purpose - If such committee is constituted and any recommendation is made for enhancement of scale of pay for concerned officers by Chief Justice, same would be considered by State Govt.in its proper perspective - Appeals allowed.
