Union Of India v Sanjay Kumar Jain

Supreme Court of India

11 August 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 592; (2004) 6 SCC 708; (2004) SCC (L&S) 869; AIR 2004 SC 4139; 2004 (3) AWC 2722; 2004 (20) CLR 281; 2004 (113) DLT 61; 2004 (4) ESC 500; 2004 (102) FLR 1017; JT 2004 (6) SC 318; 2004 (3) LLJ 753; 2004(6) SCALE 564; 2004 (7) SLR 22; [2004] Supp3 S.C.R. 463; 2004 (3) UPLBEC 2588

Case Digest

Subject: Socio-Economic

Summary: Socio-Economic - Persons with Disabilities (Equal Opportunities, Protection of Rights and Full Participation) Act, 1995 - Indian Railway Establishment Manual - Visual handicap - Promotion from Group-C to Group-B - Respondent was considered unfit as visually handicapped - Tribunal and HC allowed promotion - Whether discrimination in matter of promotion merely on ground of physical disability is correct? - Held, no promotion shall be denied to a person merely on ground of his disability - Judgment suffers no infirmity - Appeal dismissed.
