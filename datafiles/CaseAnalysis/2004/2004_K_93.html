Kishori Lal v Rupa and Others

Supreme Court of India

23 September 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 753; (2004) 7 SCC 638; (2004) SCC (Cr) 2021; 2005 (1) JhrCR(SC) 164; JT 2004 (8) SC 317; 2004 (4) RCR(Criminal) 686; 2004(8) SCALE 126; [2004] Supp4 S.C.R. 628

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Criminal - CrPC, 1973, s. 389 - Grant of bail by High Court - Propriety - Held, mere fact that during trial, accused were granted bail and there was no allegation of misuse of liberty, is really not of much significance - What really necessary to be considered by HC is whether reasons existed to suspend execution of sentence and thereafter grant bail - Order directing suspension of sentence and grant of bail unsustainable and set aside - Appeal allowed.
