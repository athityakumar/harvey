National Textile Corpn. Ltd. & Ors. v M/S Haribox Swalram & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

05 April 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

G.P. Mathur, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 430; (2004) 9 SCC 786; AIR 2004 SC 1998; 2004 (5) ALD(SC) 120; 2004 (3) BC 494; JT 2004 (4) SC 508; 2004(4) SCALE 227

Case Digest

Subject: Contract & Commercial

Keywords: Rajasthan Urban Improvement Act, Textile Undertakings (Nationalisation) Act, 1995, Textile Undertakings (Taking Over of Management) Act, 1983

Summary: [A.] Contract & Commercial - Textile Undertakings (Taking Over of Management) Act, 1983, ss. 11, 3(7), 6 - Textile Undertakings (Nationalisation) Act, 1995 - Specific performance of a contract - Jurisdiction to entertain WP - Textile mills are situate in Bombay and supply of cloth was to be made by them ex-factory at Bombay - Money was paid to mills at Bombay - View taken by DB that Calcutta HC had jurisdiction is erroneous - Any liability incurred by a textile company in relation to textile undertaking before appointed day cannot be enforced against Central Govt. or Custodian - Appeal allowed.
