M/S. National Insurance Co. Ltd. v Baljit Kaur And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

06 January 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

V.N. Khare, S.B. Sinha, AR. Lakshmanan

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 57; (2004) 2 SCC 1; (2004) SCC (Cr) 370; 2004 (1) ACC 259; AIR 2004 SC 1340; 2004 (1) ALD(SC) 98; 2004 (54) ALR 549; 2004 (1) AWC 565; [2004] 118 Comp Cas 435; 2004 (2) G.L.R. 1071; 2004 (1) JCC 349; JT 2004 (1) SC 15; 2004 (1) KLT 938; 2004 (2) MahLJ 372; 2004 (1) MLJ(SC) 127; 2004 (2) MPLJ 4; 2004 (1) RCR(Civil) 722; 2004(1) SCALE 124; [2004] 1 S.C.R. 274; 2004 (1) SLT 269; 2004 (1) Supreme 3; 2004 (1) UC 652

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Insurance; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Carriers and Transportation - Motor Vehicles Act as amended in 1994 - s 147 - Interpretation of statutes - Mischief rule to be applied - gratuitous passenger on goods vehicle - insurer not liable to pay - in view of ruling in New India Assurance Co Ltd vs Asha Rani (2003) 2 SCC 223 - insurer to pay and recover the compensation from owner of the goods vehicle - Appeals partly allowed.
