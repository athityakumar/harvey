Daulat Ram and Others v Sodha and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 November 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Ashok Bhan, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 980; (2005) 1 SCC 40; AIR 2005 SC 233; 2005 (1) AWC 527; JT 2004 (10) SC 50; 2005 (2) MahLJ 170; 2005 (139) PLR 290; 2004 (4) RCR(Civil) 841; 2005 (Supp) Bom.C.R. 185

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Criminal; Family & Personal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Family and Personal - Indian Succession Act, 1925, s. 63 - Will - First and Second - First dated 11-01-1977 and second dated 08-05-1983 - First Will executed in favour of nephews of testator and no provision was made by testator for his wife or daughter from another wife - Daughter is respondent no. 1, herein - In second Will testator revoked/cancelled his ealier Will and bequeathed his property to his daughter/respondent no. 1 - Whether second Will dated 08-05 -1983 executed surrounded by suspicious circumstances and whether same was forged? - Held, burden to prove that Will dated 08-05-1993 executed in favour of his daughter was forged or was obtained by undue influence or by playing a fraud was on appellants which they have failed to discharge - No evidence was led by them on either of these points - Appeal dismissed.
