State of Himachal Pradesh v Pawan Kumar

Supreme Court of India

27 September 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.K. Sabharwal

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 770; (2004) 7 SCC 735; (2004) SCC (Cr) 2067; AIR 2004 SC 4743; 2004 CRLJ 4614; JT 2004 (8) SC 190; 2004 (3) KLT 660; 2004 (4) RCR(Criminal) 543; 2004(8) SCALE 238

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Health & Drug; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Criminal - NDPS Act, 1985, s. 18 and 50 - NDPS Rules, 1985, r. 2(c) - Convicted by Sessions Judge - HC set aside conviction - Appeal against acquittal - Non- Compliance of s 50 - Regarding the issue of non-compliance, difference of opinion between judges - Matter referred to a larger Bench.
