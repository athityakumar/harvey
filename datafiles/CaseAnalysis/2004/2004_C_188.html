Metlex (India) Private Limited v Commissioner of Central Excise, New Delhi

Supreme Court of India

12 February 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Variava, H.K. Sema

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 239; (2005) 1 SCC 271; AIR 2004 SC 4889; 2004 (165) E.L.T. 129; 2004 (61) RLT 259

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Excise; Indirect Tax; Trade

Summary: [A.] Excise - Central Excise Act, 1944 - Central Excise Tariff Act, 1985 - Customs Tariff Act, 1975, s. 3 - Central Excise Rules, 1944, r. 8(1) - Meaning of manufacture - Laminating/metallising of duty paid film does not amount to manufacture - No new product emerges - Erroneous filing of classification list - Not entitled to refund.
