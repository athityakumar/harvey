District Registrar and Collector, Hyderabad and v Canara Bank Etc

Supreme Court of India

01 November 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, Ashok Bhan

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 975; (2005) 1 SCC 496; AIR 2005 SC 186; [2005] 126 Comp Cas 356; JT 2004 (9) SC 379; 2004 (4) RCR(Civil) 724; [2004] Supp5 S.C.R. 833

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Regional Rural Banks Act, 1976, National Bank For Agriculture and Rural Development Act, 1981, Andhra Pradesh Act, 1986, Indian Stamp (Andhra Pradesh Amendment) Act, 1986, Right to Financial Privacy Act, 1978, Andhra Pradesh State Rules, 1986

Summary: Practice and Procedure - Indian Stamp Act, 1899, s. 73(As incorporated by Andhra Pradesh Act, 1986, by amending the Central Act in its application to the State) - Struck down by HC of Andhra Pradesh as ultra vires provisions of Indian Stamp Act as also of art 14 of Constitution - Whether order of HC right? - Held, under the garb of power conferred by s. 73 person authorized may go on rampage searching house after house i.e. residences of persons or the places used for the custody of documents - Deficiency pointed out by HC has not been removed even by rules - Held, provision in question ultra vires the Constitution - Appeals Dismissed.
