Registrar, High Court of Gujarat and Another v C.G. Sharma

Supreme Court of India

17 November 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

AR. Lakshmanan, K.G. Balakrishnan

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 976; (2005) SCC (L&S) 12; (2005) 1 SCC 132; AIR 2005 SC 344; 2005 (1) G.L.R. 649; JT 2004 (9) SC 602; 2005 (1) LLN 1; 2004(9) SCALE 478; 2005 (1) SLR 308; [2004] Supp6 S.C.R. 77; 2005 (1) UPLBEC 313

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Service - Judiciary - Gujarat Judicial Service Recruitment Rules, 1961, r. 5(4) - Interpretation of - Power ofGovt.to extend period of probation - Respondent's services terminated on account of unsuitability for post held by him under recommendations of HC - Whether expiry of period of two years of probation when respondent was allowed to continue, stood automatically confirmed?; whether termination of services without holding any enquiry possible? - Held, probationer remains probationer unless he has been confirmed on basis of work evaluation - There is no provision for automatic or deemed confirmation and/or deemed appointment on regular establishment or post - Appeals Disposed Of.
