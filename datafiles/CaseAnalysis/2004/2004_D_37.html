Supreme Court of India

12 March 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

G.P. Mathur, AR. Lakshmanan, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 1141; (2004) 4 SCC 79; AIR 2004 SC 1904; 2004 (4) ALD(SC) 25; 2004 (55) ALR 23; 2004 (2) AWC 1342; 2004 (4) Bom.C.R. 599; 2004 (2) CLR 158; 2004 (2) JhrCR(SC) 179; JT 2004 (3) SC 272; 2004 (137) PLR 794; 2004 (2) RCR(Civil) 278; 2004(3) SCALE 234; [2004] 2 S.C.R. 1156; 2004 (2) UPLBEC 1896

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1984, ss. 4, 5, 6, 9, 11, 16, 17, 23, 26, 28 and 34 - Civil Procedure Code, 1908, O. 41, r.22 - Whether claimant (owner of land) is entitled to interest for such anterior period in accordance with s. 34 of Act, when possession is taken before issuance of notification u/s. 4(1) of Land Acquisition Act? - Held, to save construction land acquisition proceedings were initiated again by issuing a notification u/s. 4(1) of Act and under award given by Collector claimant has been awarded Rs. 16,54 and 175 as market value of land and Rs. 4,96 and 252 as solatium, therefore, appellant has thus been more than adequately compensated and he is not entitled to any further amount - Appeal dismissed.
