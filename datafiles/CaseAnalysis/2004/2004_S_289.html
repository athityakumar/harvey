Vijay Kumar Prasad v State Of Bihar & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

07 April 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Doraiswamy Raju

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 273; (2004) 5 SCC 196; (2004) SCC (Cr) 1576; AIR 2004 SC 2123; 2004 (2) CGLJ 74; 2004 CRLJ 2047; 2004 (2) JCC 693; JT 2004 (4) SC 532; 2004 (2) KLT 435; 2004 (3) MahLJ 1101; 2004 (3) MPLJ 560; 2004 (2) RCR(Criminal) 470; 2004(4) SCALE 287; 2004 (2) SLT 1047; 2004 (3) Supreme 134

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Family & Personal

Summary: Women & Children - Criminal - Code of Criminal Procedure 1973, ss. 125, 125(1)(d), 126, 126(1) - Criminal Procedure Code, 1898 - Venue of proceedings for maintenance - Benefit given to wife and children to initiate proceeding at place where they reside is not given to parents - Application by father or mother claiming maintenance has to be filed where person from whom maintenance is claimed lives - Directed transfer of case to Sessions Division of Patna, with direction that learned Session Judge may pass appropriate orders so that matter can be placed before court of competent jurisdiction - Appeal allowed.
