Prakash Babu Raghuvanshi v State of Madhya Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

13 September 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 819; (2004) 7 SCC 490; (2004) SCC (Cr) 1966; 2004 CRLJ 4612; 2004 (3) KLT 761; 2004 (4) RCR(Criminal) 407; 2004(8) SCALE 95; [2004] Supp4 S.C.R. 390

Case Digest

Subject: Trade

Summary: Trade - Essential Commodities Act, 1955 s. 3 r/w 7(1)(a)(ii) - Whether s. 7 has application?; Whether there is violation of any order which is the primary requirement? - Held, it is essential for bringing in application of s. 7 to show that some order has been made under s. 3 and order has been contravened - It was necessary for prosecution to place on record 'order' which, according to it, was foundation for taking action against accused-appellant - Remitted the matter to HC to hear matter afresh - Appeal disposed of.
