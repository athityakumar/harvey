Friends Colony Development Committee v State Of Orissa & Ors

Supreme Court of India

01 November 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, Ashok Bhan

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 898; (2004) 8 SCC 733; 2005 (2) Bom.C.R. 691; JT 2004 (9) SC 418; 2004 (4) RCR(Civil) 787; 2004(9) SCALE 166; [2004] Supp5 S.C.R. 818; 2005 (1) UPLBEC 598

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure; Trusts & Associations

Keywords: Law Officer, Orissa Development Authorities Act, Cuttack Development Authority (Planning & Building Standard) Regulation, 2001

Summary: Orissa Development Authorities Act - Unauthorised constructions - Held, conduct of builder deserves to be noticed - He knew it fully well what was permissible construction as per sanctioned building plans and yet he not only constructed additional built up area on each floor but also added an additional fifth floor on building, and such a floor was totally unauthorized - Directed that two petitions shall proceed in High Court in such manner as the High Court may deem fit but keeping in view the directions given.
