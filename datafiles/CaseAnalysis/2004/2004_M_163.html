Punjab State Electricity Board and Others v Malkiat Singh

Supreme Court of India

11 October 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Shivaraj V. Patil, B.N. Srikrishna

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 846; (2005) 9 SCC 22; AIR 2004 SC 5061; 2004 (4) AWC 3696; 2004 (5) ESC 29; JT 2004 (8) SC 526; 2005 (1) LLN 33; 2004(8) SCALE 655; 2004 (6) SLR 775; [2004] Supp5 S.C.R. 329

Case Digest

Subject: Health & Drug; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Land and Property - Service - Land Acquisitions - Policy decision for providing employment on priority basis to one member of affected family whose land has been acquired - Post of Homeopathy Physician - Whether respondent entitled for the post?; Whether policy decision has retrospective application? - Held, scheme giving appointment on priority basis was only in nature of concession to eligible candidates which respondent could not claim as a matter of right having taken compensation amount for his land which was acquired, more so when he did not fulfill necessary requirements under revised scheme - HC's judgement set aside - Appeal allowed.
