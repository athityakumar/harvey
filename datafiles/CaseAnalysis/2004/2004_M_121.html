Vishwant Kumar v Madan Lal Sharma and Another

Supreme Court of India

18 March 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S.H. Kapadia, S.B. Sinha, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 1149; (2004) 4 SCC 1; AIR 2004 SC 1887; 2004 (110) DLT 404; JT 2004 (4) SC 435; 2004 (1) RCR(Rent) 517; 2004(3) SCALE 470

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Rent Control - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958, ss. 3, 4, 6 and 9 - Delhi Rent Control (Amending Act) Act, 1988, s. 3(c) - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 151 - Whether s. 3(c) as amended was applicable to standard rent application, which was pending before court on 01-12-1988 when Amending Act came into force? - Held, yes - Object of Amendment Act, 1988 is to strike a balance between claims of landlord who get meager rent, particularly in times of inflation and tenants who equally need protection from arbitrary eviction therefore u/s. 3(c) as amended, application for fixation of standard rent filed by tenant on 1-12-1988 has been correctly dismissed as infructuous - Appeal dismissed.
