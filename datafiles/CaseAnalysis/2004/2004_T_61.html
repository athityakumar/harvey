I.T.C. Limited v The Person Incharge Agricultural Market

Supreme Court of India

30 January 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

G.P. Mathur, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 31; (2004) 2 SCC 794; AIR 2004 SC 1796; 2004 (3) ALD(SC) 20; JT 2004 (2) SC 236; [2004] 2 S.C.R. 19; 2004 (135) STC 258

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Sales Tax

Keywords: Market Fee, Andhra Pradesh General Sales Tax Rules, 1957, Andhra Pradesh (Agricultural Produce and Livestock) Markets Act, 1966, Maritime Zones of India (Regulation of Fishing By Foreign Vessels) Act, 1981

Summary: [A.] Indirect Tax - Companies Act, 1956 - Andhra Pradesh (Agricultural Produce and Livestock) Markets Act, 1966, ss. 12, 12(1), 12(3), 12(a), 2, 2(v), 3, 4 - Maritime Zones Of India (Regulation Of Fishing By Foreign Vessels) Act, 1981, s. 2(b) - Andhra Pradesh General Sales Tax Rules, 1957, r. 5(2)(xxvi) - Whether dead and dried prawns purchased by appellant could be considered to be 'livestock' within meaning of Section of 1966 Act - Held, prawn is a livestock State Govt. is thus fully competent to issue a notification regarding prawns.
