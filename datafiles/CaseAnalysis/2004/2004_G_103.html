State of Uttar Pradesh and Others v Gulab Shankar Srivastava

Supreme Court of India

03 December 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S.H. Kapadia, Arijit Pasayat

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 1028; (2005) 9 SCC 682; (2005) SCC (L&S) 722; 2005 (1) AWC 44; 2005 (104) FLR 203; JT 2005 (11) SC 201; 2005 (1) LLJ 692; 2005 (2) LLN 61; 2004(10) SCALE 198; 2005 (1) SLR 375; 2005 (1) UPLBEC 925

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: [A.] Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 226 - Civil Services (Classification, Control & Appeal) Rules - Uttar Pradesh Civil Service Regulations, reg. 351-A - Financial irregularities - Whether HC erred in directing appellants to pay difference in salary for period of suspension with all benefits with 10% interest without deciding upon rights of appellants to continue departmental proceedings instituted prior to respondent's retirement under reg 351-A of U P Civil Service Regulations? - Held, questions indicated above ought to have been decided by HC particularly before ordering payment with interest @ 10% per annum - Applicability of reg 351-A requires compliance of pre-conditions including period of limitation for holding enquiry - HC ought to have examined scope and applicability of regulation in light of facts and circumstances - Matter remitted to HC.
