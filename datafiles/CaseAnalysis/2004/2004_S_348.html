Chinnamma v State Of Kerala

Supreme Court of India

24 February 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, B.P. Singh

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 169; (2004) 12 SCC 244; AIR 2004 SC 2816; 2004 CRLJ 1447; JT 2004 (3) SC 54; 2004 (3) RCR(Criminal) 563; 2004(2) SCALE 640; 2004 (1) UC 633

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Appeal allowed, Eye Witness, Chargesheet

Summary: IPC, 1860, s.302 - appellant accused of pouring kerosene on her sister-in-law and setting her ablaze - deceased dying 14 days after incident - two dying declarations recorded - after perusal the material on record - prosecution in this case has not established its case beyond all reasonable doubt to base a conviction on the appellant - conviction set aside, appeal allowed.
