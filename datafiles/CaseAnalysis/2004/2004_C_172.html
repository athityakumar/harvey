Messrs Tecumseh Products India Limited v Commissioner of Central Excise, Hyderabad

Supreme Court of India

05 May 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, G.P. Mathur

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 383; (2004) 6 SCC 30; AIR 2004 SC 4596; 2004 (167) E.L.T. 498; JT 2004 (Supp2) SC 399; 2004 (62) RLT 488; 2004(5) SCALE 786; [2004] Supp2 S.C.R. 202

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: [A.] Excise - Central Excise Act, 1944, s. 11A - Whether while repairing defective compressors any part such as stators replaced by appellant involves manufacturing activity attracting duty under Central Excise Act - Separate activities carried on by appellants which were identical to ones that was carried out in respect of new stator and, therefore, to extent of stator being made ready for purpose of using in repairing of compressor must be held to be an activity of manufacture - Application of extended period of limitation u/s. 11 A not applicable since it was not clear as to whether if any part is used for purpose of repairing a machinery would amount to manufacture.
