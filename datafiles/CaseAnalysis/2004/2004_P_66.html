State Of Punjab And Ors. v Phulan Rani And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

03 August 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 565; (2004) 7 SCC 555; AIR 2004 SC 4105; 2004 (6) ALD(SC) 28; 2004 (3) AWC 2586; 2004 (4) ESC 505; 2004 (102) FLR 1040; 2004 (4) JhrCR(SC) 60; JT 2004 (6) SC 214; 2004 (3) LLN 1245; 2004 (138) PLR 580; 2004 (3) RCR(Civil) 729; 2004(6) SCALE 425; [2004] Supp3 S.C.R. 303

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Service - Legal Services Authorities Act, 1987 - Entitlement of pension - Powers of disposal of cases by Lok Adalat - Whether matter can be disposed of by Lok Adalat - Held, Lok Adalat can dispose of a matter by way of a compromise or settlement between parties - Did not involve compromise or settlement and could not have been disposed of by Lok Adalat - If no compromise or settlement is or could be arrived at, no order can be passed by Lok Adalat - Writ Petition be restored to its original position - Appeal allowed.
