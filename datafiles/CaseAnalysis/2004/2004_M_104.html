Commissioner of Central Excise, Pune v M/s. Kripa Chemicals Private Limited

Supreme Court of India

04 November 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 1004; (2005) 1 SCC 12; AIR 2005 SC 232; 2004 (65) RLT 443; 2004(9) SCALE 448; [2004] Supp5 S.C.R. 970

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax

Summary: [A.] Excise - Cost of production - Whether excise duty paid on raw material, if modvatted, is to be included in determining cost of production of excisable products?- whether profit earned by job worker is to be included and to what extent? - Held, excise duty paid on raw material, if modvatted, is not to be included in determining cost of production of excisable products following Collector of Central Excise, Pune vs Daiichi Karkaria Limited - Value of job work done has to be taken into consideration following Ujagar Prints etc etc vs Union of India and Ors.
