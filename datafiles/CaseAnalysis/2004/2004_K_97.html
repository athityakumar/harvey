Union Of India & Ors. v Krishan Lal Arneja & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

28 April 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Shivaraj V. Patil, D.M. Dharmadhikari

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 341; (2004) 8 SCC 453; AIR 2004 SC 3582; 2004 (5) ALD(SC) 47; 2004 (56) ALR 401; JT 2004 (Supp2) SC 245; 2004 (3) RCR(Civil) 178; 2004 (97) RD 232; 2004(5) SCALE 207; [2004] Supp1 S.C.R. 801

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 17(1), 17(3A), 17(4), 4 - Requisitioning and Acquisition of Immovable Property Act, 1952 - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 14, 19, 226 - Questioning validity of notification, which invokes urgency cl - HC quashed impugned notification - Decision in Banwari Lal and Shakuntala Gupta of this Court in relation to same notification - Decisions are binding as precedents on question of validity of notification, which invokes urgency cl u/s. 17 of Act - Order of HC confirmed.
