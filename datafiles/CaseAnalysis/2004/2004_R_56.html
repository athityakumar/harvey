Rajasthan State Ganganagar S. Mills Limited v State of Rajasthan and Another

Supreme Court of India

13 September 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 731; (2004) 8 SCC 161; (2004) SCC (L&S) 1055; 2004 (4) AWC 2854; 2004 (3) LLJ 832; 2004(7) SCALE 624; 2004 (6) SLR 198; [2004] Supp4 S.C.R. 363

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 25F - Retrenchment - Division Bench holding that since retrenchment was found to be invalid on appreciation of evidence and for non production of relevant document - Held, Labour Court and HC failed to consider statutory requirements in their proper perspective - No definite finding whether engagement was seasonal in character - Remitted matter to Labour Court to consider the evidence and come to a definite conclusion as to whether workman had worked for 240 days during the period claimed - Appeal disposed of.
