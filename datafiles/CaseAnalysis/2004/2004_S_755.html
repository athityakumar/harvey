Krishna Kanwar @ Thakuraen v State of Rajasthan

Supreme Court of India

27 January 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Doraiswamy Raju

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 101; (2004) SCC (Cr) 607; (2004) 2 SCC 608; AIR 2004 SC 2735; 2004 (1) CCR 243; 2004 (1) Crimes 390; 2004 (91) ECC 545; 2004 (1) JCC 479; JT 2004 (1) SC 597; 2004(2) SCALE 22; [2004] 1 S.C.R. 1101; 2004 (2) SLT 60; 2004 (1) Supreme 815

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Health & Drug

Summary: [A.] Criminal - Indian Evidence Act, 1872, s. 27 - Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act, 1985, ss. 21, 41, 42, 43, 50, 57, 8 - Held on facts followed in case of two accused - Prosecution proves case beyond shadow of reasonable doubt - Conviction and sentence upheld - Acquittal of two other accused - Rightly acquitted by lower court for want of evidence - Appeals disposed of.
