Metro Marins and Another v Bonus Watch Company Private Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 September 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, S.B. Sinha, A.K. Mathur

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 783; (2004) 7 SCC 478; 2004 (57) ALR 449; 2004 (4) AWC 2851; JT 2004 (7) SC 394; 2004 (4) MLJ(SC) 115; 2005 (141) PLR 638; 2004 (2) RCR(Rent) 371; 2005 (98) RD 60; 2004(7) SCALE 581

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - CPC, 1908 - Suit for possession - Mandatory interim order of directing interim possession being handed over to plaintiffs - Challenges this order - Held, an interim mandatory injunction can be granted only in exceptional cases coming within exceptions noticed in Dorab Cawasji Warden vs Coomi Sorab Warden - Case of respondent not come under anyone of those exceptions and even on facts it is not such a case which calls for issuance of an interim mandatory injunction directing possession being handed over to respondent - Appeal allowed.
