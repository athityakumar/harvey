Usman Mian and Others v State of Bihar

Supreme Court of India

04 October 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 814; (2004) 10 SCC 786; JT 2004 (9) SC 235; 2004 (4) RCR(Criminal) 920; 2004(8) SCALE 456; [2004] Supp5 S.C.R. 21; 2005 (1) UC 96

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Criminal - Practice and Procedure - IPC, 1860, s. 302 r/w 34 - Conviction and sentence of imprisonment for life - Held, trial court elaborately dealt with medical evidence and found that doctor's opinion was not honest being inconsistent with objective finding as contained in post mortem report - Accused person absconded - There was an attempt to bury dead body hurriedly - Circumstances highlighted by trial court are sufficient to bring home accusation as has been rightly held by trial court and HC against appellants - Appeal dismissed.
