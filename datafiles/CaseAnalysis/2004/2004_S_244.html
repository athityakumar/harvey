Smt. Sudha Rani Garg v Sri Jagdish Kumar (Dead) and others

Supreme Court of India

08 September 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, P.P. Naolekar

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 777; (2004) 8 SCC 329; AIR 2004 SC 5120; 2004 (57) ALR 220; 2004 (4) AWC 2833; JT 2004 (7) SC 361; 2004 (2) RCR(Rent) 433; 2004(7) SCALE 603; [2004] Supp4 S.C.R. 206

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property; Practice & Procedure; Rent Control

Keywords: Uttar Pradesh Urban Building (Regulation of Letting, Rent and Eviction) Act, 1972, Completion Of Construction

Summary: Rent Control - Uttar Pradesh Urban Buildings (Regulation of Letting, Rent, and Eviction) Act, 1972, s. 2(2) - Transfer of Property Act, s. 106 - Suit for eviction u/s 106 of TP Act - Ground set out in the suit was that the tenancy was at will and provisions of the UP Act being not applicable, the tenant was liable for eviction - Applicability of UP Act - S. 2(2) provides for exemptions from the operation of UP Act, inter alia it provides that nothing in this Act shall apply to a building during a period of ten years from the date on which its construction is completed - Provision of s. 2(2) together with its explanation, perused - Explanation provides for four different dates for determining the date of completion of building. The dates are: (i) When the completion of the building is reported to the local authority; (ii) When the completion of the building is otherwise recorded by the local authority; (iii) When the first assessment of the building comes into effect; (iv) When it is actually occupied - Explanation I is a deeming provision - Undisputed fact of case is that the first assessment came into effect from 1.4.1983 and in the relevant column relating to enhancement or reduction of the tax "Q September 1982" is recorded - The quarter started from 1st July, 1982, it cannot mean that the construction of the building was completed by the date - Date of completion of construction can be any date falling between two terminals i.e. 1st July, 1982 to 30th September, 1982 - Impugned judgment of HC, upheld - Held, UP Act not applicable - Appeal dismissed.
