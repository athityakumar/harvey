State of Jharkhand and Another v Govind Singh

Supreme Court of India

03 December 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 1027; (2005) 10 SCC 437; (2005) SCC (Cr) 1570; AIR 2005 SC 294; 2005 (1) JhrCR(SC) 1; JT 2004 (10) SC 349; 2005 (1) KLT 34; 2005 (1) RCR(Criminal) 189; 2004(10) SCALE 174; [2004] Supp6 S.C.R. 651

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Constitution; Environment, Wildlife & Animal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Environment, Wildlife & Animal - Indian Forest Act, 1927, ss.52 (3), 68 - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 226 - Limitation Act, 1939, s. 22(2)(b) - Whether a vehicle seized for alleged involvement in commission of forest offence can be released on payment of fine in lieu of confiscation? - Held, vehicle as liable for confiscation may be released on payment of value of vehicle and not otherwise - Single Judge did not refer to power available u/s. 68 of Act and on contrary, introduced concept of reading into s 52 of Act, a power to levy fine in lieu of confiscation which is impermissible.
