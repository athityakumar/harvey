Union of India and Another v Chaya Ghoshal and Another

Supreme Court of India

13 December 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 1082; (2005) 10 SCC 97; AIR 2005 SC 428; JT 2005 (11) SC 518; 2005 (1) RCR(Criminal) 280; 2005 (66) RLT 111; 2004(10) SCALE 375; [2004] Supp6 S.C.R. 869

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Criminal - Conservation Of Foreign Exchange And Prevention Of Smuggling Activities Act, 1974, s. 3(1) - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 21, 22(5), 226 - Customs Act, 1962, s. 108 - General Clauses Act, 1897, s. 21 - Detention order - HC quashed detention order - Whether there was unusual delay in disposing of representation? - whether Central Govt. and not applied its mind to representation independently? - Held, factual position does not justify conclusion drawn by HC about unexplained delay in disposal of representation and/or non-application of mind by Central Govt. - Looked at from any angle order of HC is unsustainable and it is therefore set aside.
