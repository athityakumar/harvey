BPL Display Devices Limited v Commissioner of Central Excise, Ghaziabad

Supreme Court of India

08 October 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Ruma Pal, Arun Kumar

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 1131; (2005) 10 SCC 275; 2004 (174) E.L.T. 5; 2004 (65) RLT 664

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax

Summary: Customs - Whether appellant is entitled to benefit of Notification No 13/97-Cus as amended by Notification No 25/99-Cus ? - Held, object of grant of exemption was only to debar those importer/manufacturers from benefit of Notifications who had diverted products imported for other purposes and had no intention to use same for manufacture of specified items at any stage - No material distinction can be drawn between loss on account of leakage and loss on account of damage - Appeal allowed.
