Municipal Corporation, Amritsar v The Senior Superintendent of Post Offices

Supreme Court of India

21 January 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

H.K. Sema, S.N. Variava

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 48; (2004) 3 SCC 92; AIR 2004 SC 2912; 2004 (2) AWC 1717; 2004 (2) JhrCR(SC) 191; JT 2004 (1) SC 561; 2004 (3) MLJ(SC) 66; 2004 (137) PLR 787; 2004 (2) RCR(Civil) 143; 2004(1) SCALE 662; [2004] 1 S.C.R. 913; 2004 (2) SLT 545; 2004 (2) Supreme 577; 2004 (1) UPLBEC 775

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Constitution; Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Municipal Corporation Act

Summary: [A.] Municipalities & Local Governments - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 285 - Railways Act, 1989, s. 135 - Municipal Corporation Act Municipal Corporation demanding service charges from Department of Union Govt. - No provison in statute for service charge - Demand by way of tax - Not within competence of Municipal Corporation - Violates art. 285 - Appeals dismissed.
