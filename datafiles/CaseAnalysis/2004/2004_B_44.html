State of Punjab v Balbir Singh and others

Supreme Court of India

26 October 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 951; (2004) 8 SCC 702; 2005 CRLJ 97; 2004 (4) RCR(Criminal) 999; 2004(9) SCALE 23; [2004] Supp5 S.C.R. 643

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Health & Drug; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Criminal - NDPS Act, 1985, s. 15 - HC held, that in absence of compliance with requirements of s. 50 the conviction and sentence as recorded could not be sustained - Whether s. 50 can be extended to search of a vehicle or a container or a bag, or premises? - Held, s. 50 shows that it only applies in case of personal search of a person - It does not extend to search of a vehicle or a container or a bag, or premises - HC was not justified in directing acquittal of accused persons for purported non compliance with requirements of s. 50 - Appeals allowed.
