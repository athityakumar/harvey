Nandkishore Ganesh Joshi v Commissioner, Municipal Corporation of Kalyan and

Supreme Court of India

15 October 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, N. Santosh Hegde

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 950; (2004) 11 SCC 417; 2005 (1) BC 172; 2005 (2) Bom.C.R. 754; JT 2004 (9) SC 242; 2004(8) SCALE 850; [2004] Supp5 S.C.R. 584; 2005 (1) UPLBEC 144

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Municipalities and Local Govts - Mumbai Provincial Municipal Corporation Act, 1949, s. 73 - Prayer that whenever a proposal for grant of approval of contract in terms of s. 73 is placed before Standing Committee, same should contain tender forms and other documents submitted by contractor as well as other documents, if any, containing records of negotiations made by Commissioner of Corporation after tenders are opened or placed before it - Held, with a view to bona fide performing its statutory functions, if Standing Committee is unable to approve such proposal on basis of documents supplied to it, it is entitled to ask for relevant documents from Commissioner - Appeal allowed.
