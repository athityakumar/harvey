Midas Hygiene Industry and Another v Sudhir Bhatia and Others

Supreme Court of India

22 January 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S.N. Variava, H.K. Sema

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 27; (2004) 3 SCC 90; 2004 (73) DRJ 647; 2004 (28) PTC 121; 2004 (1) RAJ 586; 2004(2) SCALE 231; 2004 (2) SLT 627

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Intellectual Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Intellectual Property - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 39 r.1, O. 39 r.2 - Trademarks - LAXMAN REKHA and KAZY LINES - Infringement of trademark or copyright - Settled law that injunction restraining infringement should follow - Mere delay or latches in approaching court should not be ground for denial of injunction - Prima facie dishonest intention - Injunction granted by single judge to continue - Matter remanded.
