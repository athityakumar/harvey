Dolly Chhanda v Chairman, Joint Entrance Examination and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 October 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

G.P. Mathur, R.C. Lahoti, P.P. Naolekar

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 816; (2005) SCC (L&S) 734; (2005) 9 SCC 779; AIR 2004 SC 5043; 2005 (59) ALR 164; 2004 (4) AWC 3203; JT 2004 (8) SC 267; 2004 (4) MLJ(SC) 111; 2004(8) SCALE 422; 2004 (6) SLR 399; [2004] Supp5 S.C.R. 79

Case Digest

Subject: Education; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Education - Admission in MBBS course - Reservation under MI category - Reservation for children/widows of personnel of armed/paramilitary forces of Orissa, killed/disabled in action during war or peace time operation - Rejection of admission at time of counseling on ground that petitioner failed to produce certificate to show that she belongs to a reserved category - Held, there can be some relaxation in matter of submission of proof and it will not be proper to apply any rigid principle as it pertains in domain of procedure - Every infraction of rule relating to submission of proof need not necessarily result in rejection of candidature - Appeal allowed.
