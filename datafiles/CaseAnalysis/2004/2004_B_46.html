Ghaziabad Development Authority v Balbir Singh

Supreme Court of India

03 December 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

H.K. Sema, Tarun Chatterjee

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 1502; (2005) 9 SCC 573; AIR 2005 SC 1206; 2005 (1) AWC 32; 2004 (29) CCC 709; 2005 (2) CompLJ 75; JT 2005 (11) SC 171; 2005 (2) RCR(Civil) 754; 2004(10) SCALE 183; 2005 (Supp) Bom.C.R. 673

Case Digest

Subject: Consumer Protection; Intellectual Property

Summary: Consumer Protection - Refund of deposited or paid amount - Appeal against award passed by National Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission awarding interest 18% per annum - Appellant has deposited/paid entire amount of 18% interest, they are precluded from calling upon party to refund same - In that view of matter appeal is virtually rendered infructuous in view of order rendered by Supreme Court in Ghaziabad Development Authority vs Balbir Singh 2004 Indlaw SC 941 - Appeal disposed of.
