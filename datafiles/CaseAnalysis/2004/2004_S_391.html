Durgo Bai & Anr. v State Of Punjab

Supreme Court of India

10 August 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

P. Venkatarama Reddi, B.P. Singh

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 575; (2004) 7 SCC 144; (2004) SCC (Cr) 1865; AIR 2004 SC 4170; 2004 (2) CGLJ 196; JT 2004 (6) SC 351; 2004 (3) RCR(Criminal) 809; 2004(6) SCALE 530; 2004 (2) UC 1190B

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal; Defence & Security Forces; Health & Drug

Keywords: Wrong Section

Summary: Criminal - NDPS Act, 1985 - s. 22 - Violation of mandatory requirements of ss 42 & 50 and non-production of case property or custody of goods during intervening period or possibility of tampering seals of sample packets argued - Held, not gone into these factual aspects especially when there is no clear pointer one way or other from recorded evidence - Appeals Disposed Of.
