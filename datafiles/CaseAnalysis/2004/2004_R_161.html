Messrs Aero Traders Private Limited v Ravinder Kumar Suri

Supreme Court of India

27 October 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

G.P. Mathur, R.C. Lahoti, P.P. Naolekar

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 906; (2004) 8 SCC 307; 2004 (58) ALR 126; 2004 (114) DLT 753; JT 2004 (9) SC 283; 2004 (2) RCR(Rent) 491; 2004(9) SCALE 95

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property; Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958, s. 14(1)(a) - Petition for eviction - Ground, arrears of rent - Leave to defend - Pleadings therefor, whether made out or whether appellant made out any ground for exercising discretion in his favour of not striking out his defence? - Held, Apart from pleading that he had sent amount through cheques, no other fact which could be taken into consideration by Rent Controller for exercising discretion in his favour - Appeals Dismissed.
