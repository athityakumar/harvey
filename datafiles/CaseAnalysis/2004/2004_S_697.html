Messrs Medley Minerals India Limited v State of Orissa and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 September 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Srikrishna, Shivaraj V. Patil

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 747; (2004) 12 SCC 390; AIR 2004 SC 4851; 2005 (1) JhrCR(SC) 18; JT 2004 (8) SC 29; 2004 (4) RCR(Civil) 221B; 2004(8) SCALE 25; 2005 (Supp) RD 676

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Mines & Minerals; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Orissa Minor Mineral Concessions Rules, 1990

Summary: Mines and Minerals - Orissa Minor Mineral Concessions Rules, 1990, r. 12 - Quarry lease - HC quashed quarry lease renewed by State Govt.- Whether order of HC sustainable? - Held, State Govt was justified in treating appellant's application as continuation of application for renewal of lease earlier made - State Govt. had enough powers to relax provisions of Rules 'in interest of mineral development in deserving cases in such manner as they deem proper' - State Govt's order restored - Appeal allowed.
