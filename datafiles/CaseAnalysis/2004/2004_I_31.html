Y. Abraham Ajith & Ors v Inspector Of Police, Chennai & Anr

Supreme Court of India

17 August 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 625; (2004) 8 SCC 100; (2004) SCC (Cr) 2134; AIR 2004 SC 4286; 2004 CRLJ 4180; 2005 (1) JhrCR(SC) 15; JT 2004 (6) SC 497; 2004 (3) RCR(Criminal) 988; 2004(7) SCALE 26; [2004] Supp3 S.C.R. 604; 2004 (2) UC 1177

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Family & Personal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Criminal - CrPC, 1973, s 482 - Prayer for quashing proceedings - Single Judge of the Madras HC rejected prayer - Whether judgment of Single Judge sustainable? - Held, in factual scenario disclosed by complainant in complaint petition, inevitable conclusion is that no part of cause of action arose in Chennai and, therefore, concerned magistrate had no jurisdiction to deal with matter - Proceedings quashed thus justified - Appeal allowed.
