Oriental Insurance Company Limited v Nanjappan and others

Supreme Court of India

13 February 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Doraiswamy Raju

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 121; (2004) 13 SCC 224; (2005) SCC (Cr) 148; 2004 (1) ACC 524; AIR 2004 SC 1630; 2004 (54) ALR 704; 2004 (2) AWC 1839; 2004 (2) CLR 134; [2004] 119 Comp Cas 139; 2004 (2) JhrCR(SC) 84; JT 2004 (2) SC 452; 2004 (137) PLR 51; 2004(2) SCALE 451; [2004] 2 S.C.R. 365; 2004 (2) SLT 115; 2004 (1) Supreme 1061

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Insurance

Summary: [A.] Insurance - Motor Vehicles Act, 1988, s. 168 - Liability to pay compensation- Insurer had to pay compensation by way of indemnification - New India Assurance Co Ltd vs Asha Rani (2003 (4) SCC 223), Oriental Insurance Co Ltd vs Devireddy Konda Reddy (2003 Indlaw SC 46) Followed - Appeal disposed of.
