Polymat India Private Limited and Another v National Insurance Company Limited and Others

Supreme Court of India

01 December 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

A.K. Mathur, B.N. Agrawal

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 1021; (2005) 9 SCC 174; AIR 2005 SC 286; 2005 (58) ALR 3; 2005 (1) AWC 36; 2005 (1) CCC 1; [2005] 123 Comp Cas 663; 2004 (4) CPJ 49; 2004 (4) CPJ(Sc) 49; 2005 (2) JhrCR(SC) 96; JT 2004 (10) SC 292; 2004(10) SCALE 99; [2004] Supp6 S.C.R. 535

Case Digest

Subject: Insurance

Keywords: Employees Provident Funds Act, 1952

Summary: Insurance - Factories Act, 1948, s. 2(m) - Mines Act, 1952 - Employees Provident Funds Act, 1952 - Fire policies - Whether expression 'Factory-cum- Godown' includes all plant and machinery and all goods lying within boundary wall of factory, was covered under both Policies? - Held, intention between parties was to cover plant and machinery which were lying in factory, i e, in covered area and in shed and not goods which were lying outside covered area - What was sought to be covered by both Policies was only plant and machinery in shed and not goods which were lying outside plant and shed.
