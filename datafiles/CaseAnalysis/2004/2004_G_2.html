Shamsu Sumara Beevi v G. Alex And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

20 August 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Ashok Bhan, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 708; (2004) 8 SCC 569; 2005 (58) ALR 644; 2004 (4) AWC 3022; JT 2004 (8) SC 457; 2004 (3) KLT 754; 2004 (4) RCR(Civil) 252; 2004(7) SCALE 265; 2005 (Supp) RD 170; [2004] Supp3 S.C.R. 653; 2004 (2) UC 1313

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land and Property - Specific Relief Act, 1963, ss.21, 28 - Suit for specific performance - Compensation - Not claimed in plaint or amendment not sought at any stage to include compensation as an alternative pray to specific performance - HC granted compensation - Whether proper? - Held, No - HC has clearly erred in granting the compensation - Grant of such a relief in the teeth of express provisions of the statute to the contrary is not permissible - Court cannot ignore or overlook the provisions of the statute - Equity must yield to law - Appeal allowed.
