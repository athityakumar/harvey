State of Himachal Pradesh and others v M/s. Shivalik Agro Poly Products and Others

Supreme Court of India

14 September 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

G.P. Mathur, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 727; (2004) 8 SCC 556; AIR 2004 SC 4393; 2004 (4) AWC 3304; JT 2004 (7) SC 371; 2004 (4) RCR(Civil) 349; 2004(7) SCALE 658; [2004] Supp4 S.C.R. 393

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Constitution; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Registration Act, s. 17 - Transfer of Property Act, 1881 - Whether notification issued by StateGovt.on 14/4/1969 prescribing registration fee on a graduated form on basis of value of subject matter of instrument is in accordance with spirit of s 78 of Registration Act and is valid in law? - Held, HC and District Court did not at all examined whether there was any substantial discrepancy between the total amount of fee realized by the registration department and total amount of expenditure incurred by Govt. in maintenance and functioning of department - Notification issued by StateGovt.could not be struck down merely by taking into consideration registration fee paid by plaintiffs and quantification of value of services rendered to them - Appeal allowed.
