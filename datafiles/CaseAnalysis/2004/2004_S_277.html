Gubbala Venugopalaswamy And Ors v State Of Andhra Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

06 April 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, Doraiswamy Raju

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 402; (2004) 10 SCC 120; (2004) SCC (Cr) 1764; AIR 2004 SC 2477; 2004 CRLJ 2557; JT 2004 (4) SC 373; 2004(4) SCALE 256

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 120B, 148, 302, 326 - FIR not lodged at nearest Police Station is without substance - It cannot be said that when a portion of prosecution evidence is discarded as unworthy of credence, there cannot be any conviction - Evidence on record is sufficient to establish conviction - A-1 and A-4 are allowed to extent of reduction in sentence, and stands dismissed so far as A-2 and A-3 are concerned.
