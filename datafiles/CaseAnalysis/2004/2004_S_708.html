Saravanan and another v State of Pondicherry

Supreme Court of India

03 November 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

C.K. Thakker, Arijit Pasayat

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 942; (2004) 13 SCC 238; (2005) SCC (Cr) 161; AIR 2005 SC 57; 2005 CRLJ 117; 2004(9) SCALE 238; [2004] Supp5 S.C.R. 890

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Criminal - IPC, s. 304 part II r/w 34 - Conviction and sentence to undergo rigorous imprisonment for two years - Appeal against conviction - Whether courts below were right in invoking s. 34 IPC - Held, it was a clear case of doing of a criminal act in furtherance of common intention - Injury was sufficient in ordinary course of nature to cause death - Conviction and sentence recorded by trial court and confirmed by HC are legal and in accordance with law - Appeal dismissed.
