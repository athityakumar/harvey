Maulavi Hussein Haji Abraham Umari v State Of Gujarat And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

29 July 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, S.N. Variava

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 550; (2004) 6 SCC 672; (2004) SCC (Cr) 1815; AIR 2004 SC 3946; 2004 CRLJ 3860; 2005 (1) G.L.R. 585; JT 2004 (6) SC 227; 2004 (3) RCR(Criminal) 855; 2004(6) SCALE 320; [2004] Supp3 S.C.R. 202

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Indian Railways Act, 1989

Summary: Criminal - Prevention of Terrorist Act, 2002, s. 49(2) - Scope and ambit - Question as to during what period prayer for police custody can be made - Held, acceptance of application for police custody when an accused is in judicial custody is not a matter of course - s. 49(2)(b) provides inbuilt safeguards against its misuse by mandating filing of an affidavit by investigating officer to justify prayer and in an appropriate case reason for delayed motion - Appeal dismissed.
