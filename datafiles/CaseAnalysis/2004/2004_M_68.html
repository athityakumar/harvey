M/S Hanil Era Textiles Ltd. v M/S Puromatic Filters (P) Ltd.

Supreme Court of India

16 April 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

G.P. Mathur, S. Rajendra Babu

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 292; (2004) 4 SCC 671; AIR 2004 SC 2432; 2004 (4) ALD(SC) 22; 2004 (55) ALR 729; 2004 (3) AWC 2133; 2004 (111) DLT 39; JT 2004 (Supp1) SC 57; 2004 (138) PLR 308; 2004 (2) RCR(Civil) 808; 2004 (2) RD 8; 2004 (97) RD 80; 2004(4) SCALE 617; 2004 (3) SLT 118; [2004] Supp1 S.C.R. 333; 2004 (3) Supreme 110

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure; Contract & Commercial

Summary: [A.] Practice & Procedure - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 7 r. 10, ss. 151, 20 - Indian Contract Act, 1872, s. 28 - Dispute regarding territorial jurisdiction of Court at Delhi to try suit - Cl 17 - Any legal proceedings arising out of order shall be subject to jurisdiction of Courts in Mumbai, jurisdiction of all other Courts excluded - Offer to purchase goods was made by defendant at Bombay and was accepted by plaintiff's branch office at Bombay - Advance payment also made by defendant at Bombay - Thus, a part of cause of action accrued at Bombay - Court of Additional District Judge, Delhi had, therefore, no territorial jurisdiction to try suit.
