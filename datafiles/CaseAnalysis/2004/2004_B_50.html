Delhi Development Authority v Bali Ram Sharma and others

Supreme Court of India

03 August 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Shivaraj V. Patil, B.N. Srikrishna

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 573; (2004) 6 SCC 533; AIR 2004 SC 4114; 2004 (3) AWC 2579; 2004 (112) DLT 880; JT 2004 (6) SC 422; 2004 (4) RCR(Civil) 235; 2004(6) SCALE 452; 2004 (3) UPLBEC 2541

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Land and Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894 - Compensation - HC enhanced rate of compensation - Whether proper? - Karan Singh and others vs Union of India (1997) 8 SCC 186, covering lands acquired under very same Notification - Case almost identical - Held, it is not possible to take a different view as regards market value of lands covered by same Notification - Judgments modified by reducing amount of compensation from Rs 345/- Per sq yard (amounting to Rs 3,45,000/- Per bigha) to Rs 76,550/- Per bigha - Appeals allowed.
