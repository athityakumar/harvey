Associated Cement Companies Limited v State of Bihar and Others

Supreme Court of India

29 September 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 789; (2004) 7 SCC 642; 2005 (1) JhrCR(SC) 72; JT 2004 (8) SC 155; 2004(8) SCALE 335; 2004 (137) STC 389; [2004] Supp4 S.C.R. 868

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax

Keywords: Finance Act, 1981, Bihar Tax on Entry of Goods Into Local Areas For Consumption, Use Or Sale Therein Act, 1993, Bihar Finances Act, 1981, Sale Therein Act, 1993, Entry Tax Act, 1990, Land and Income Tax Act, 1954

Summary: Indirect Tax - Bihar Finances Act, 1981 - Bihar Tax on Entry of Goods into Local Areas for Consumption, use or Sale Therein Act, 1993 - Legality of notices issued proposing to levy tax for assessment years 1998-99, 1999-2000 and 1/4/2000 to 1/4/2000 - Whether appellant entitled to adjust entry tax paid under Entry Tax Act while computing tax payable? - Held, no dispute that assessee-appellant was liable to pay tax under s 3(3) of Entry Tax Act - Therefore, it was entitled to reduction to extent of tax paid under Entry Tax Act while working out tax payable by it under Bihar Finances Act - Appeal allowed.
