Akkayanaicker v A.A.A. Kotchadainaidu & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

23 September 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Ashok Bhan, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 763; (2004) 12 SCC 469; AIR 2004 SC 4822; 2004 (6) ALD(SC) 82; 2005 (1) DRTC 4; 2005 (1) JhrCR(SC) 33; JT 2004 (7) SC 593; 2004(8) SCALE 132; [2004] Supp4 S.C.R. 638; 2004 (2) UC 1419

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Banking and Finance - Tamil Nadu Indebted Agriculturists (Temporary Relief) Ordinance, 1975, ss. 3, 4 and 5 - Recovery suit - On promissory note - Decreed - While execution proceeding was pending Act passed - Execution petition filed after 12 years from date of passing of original decree because of legislative bar for execution - Whether execution petition is barred by Limitation? - Held, when there was a legislative bar for execution of a decree and later due to legislative intervention decree had to be scaled down and amended then enforceability of decree shall commence when bar ceases or from date decree is amended and scaled down - Petition not barred by Limitation - Order of the HC is set aside - Appeal allowed.
