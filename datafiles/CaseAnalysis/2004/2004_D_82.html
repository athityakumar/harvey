Dhananjoy Chatterjee Alias Dhana v State Of West Bengal & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

26 March 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

K.G. Balakrishnan, B.N. Srikrishna

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 235; (2004) 9 SCC 751; (2004) SCC (Cr) 1484; AIR 2004 SC 3454; 2004 CRLJ 2827; JT 2004 (4) SC 242; 2004 (2) RCR(Criminal) 483; 2004(4) SCALE 27

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal; Human Rights

Summary: [A.] Criminal - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 161, 72 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 302, 376, 380 - Code of Criminal Procedure 1973, s. 366 - Governor was deprived of opportunity to exercise his power in a fair and just manner - Material facts should have been placed before Governor - All material facts, including mitigating factors were not placed before Governor - Respondents directed to put up mercy petition to Governor again and bring all relevant facts to notice of Governor for an appropriate decision in case.
