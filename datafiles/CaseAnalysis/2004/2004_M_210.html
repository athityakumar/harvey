Supreme Court of India

01 November 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 899; (2005) 1 SCC 122; (2005) SCC (Cr) 283; AIR 2005 SC 9; 2005 CRLJ 92; JT 2004 (9) SC 486; 2004 (3) KLT 789; 2004 (4) RCR(Criminal) 937; 2004(9) SCALE 177; [2004] Supp5 S.C.R. 790

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Criminal - CrPC, 1973, ss. 482, 468 - Whether issuance of summons to appellants by Judicial Magistrate, 1st Class, Patna in complaint filed by respondent no 1 is proper? - Held, factual position shows that complaint was nothing but a sheer abuse of process of law and this is a case where power under s. 482 should have been exercised - HC erred in holding that complaint was not hit by limitation - Appeal allowed.
