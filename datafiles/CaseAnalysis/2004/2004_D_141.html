Durga Enterprises (Private) Limited and another v Principal Secretary, Government of Uttar Pradesh and others

Supreme Court of India

03 September 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

D.M. Dharmadhikari, B.N. Srikrishna

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 2052; (2004) 13 SCC 665

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition (Companies) Rules, 1963, r. 5 (2) (3) - Long pending dispute - Summarily rejection - Dispute between parties concerning respondent Authority's right to re-entry on disputed property u/r. 5 (2) and (3) of the Rules - Writ petition pending for a long period of thirteen years - HC dismissed writ petition summarily on the ground of availability of civil remedy - Hence, instant appeal - Held, Rules contained mechanism for adjudication of a dispute relating to alleged breach of terms of agreement - Rules also contained manner in which the dispute to be resolved - Pleadings were complete in writ proceedings before HC - HC ought to have decide case on merits instead of relegating parties to a civil suit - Hence, impugned order was set aside - Matter remitted to HC for taking a decision on merits - Status quo should be maintained - Appeal allowed.
