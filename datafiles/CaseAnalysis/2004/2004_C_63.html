Chandrika Prasad Yadav v State Of Bihar & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

05 April 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 428; (2004) 6 SCC 331; AIR 2004 SC 2036; 2004 (3) JhrCR(SC) 57; JT 2004 (4) SC 264; 2004 (2) RCR(Civil) 568; 2004(4) SCALE 201; 2004 (3) SLT 121

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Election; Practice & Procedure

Summary: [A.] Municipalities & Local Governments - Election - Bihar Panchayat Raj Act, 1993, s 140 - Bihar Panchayat Election Rules - Extent of jurisdiction of election tribunal to direct recounting of votes - Statutory remedies provided for shall be availed of - If such an opportunity is availed of by Election Petitioner; he has to state reasons therefore - If no sufficient explanation is furnished by Election Petitioner as to why such statutory remedy was not availed of, Election Tribunal may consider same as one of factors for accepting or rejecting prayer for recounting - Judgment of HC confirmed.
