Government of Andra Pradesh and Another v Syed Akbar

Supreme Court of India

19 November 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Shivaraj V. Patil, B.N. Srikrishna

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 983; (2005) 1 SCC 558; AIR 2005 SC 492; 2005 (58) ALR 366; JT 2004 (10) SC 569; 2004(9) SCALE 553; [2004] Supp6 S.C.R. 208

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Land and Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 18, 54A - Whether direction could be given to appellants to re-assign unused land to respondent, duly acquired by authorities and acquisition proceedings become final?; Whether Board's Standing Order No 90(32) and s. 54-A can be applied for reassignment of unused land in favour of respondent? - Held, when land is acquired under LA Act which is vested in State Govt. free from all encumbrances, question of reconveying land as claimed by respondent could not be accepted - Appeal allowed.
