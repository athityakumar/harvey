Messrs Pushpagiri Medical Society v State of Kerala and Others

Supreme Court of India

29 July 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Y.K. Sabharwal, D.M. Dharmadhikari, A.K. Mathur

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 544; (2004) 8 SCC 135; 2005 (2) Bom.C.R. 337; 2004(6) SCALE 481

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Education; Health & Drug

Summary: Kerala Self Financing Professional College (Prohibition of Capitation Fees and Procedure for Admission and Fixation of Fees) Act, 2004 - Constitutional validity - Challenges procedure that fifty per cent of total seats in each branch shall be Government quota and remaining fifty per cent shall be Management quota and fee structure - Held, as interim order students should provisionally pay fee as fixed by Justice Thomas which means that students should neither pay fee as claimed by petitioner Institution nor one postulated by Act - Refer to a larger Bench
