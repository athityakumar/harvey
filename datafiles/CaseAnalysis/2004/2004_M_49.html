Commissioner Of Central Excise,Lucknow v M/S Chhata Sugar Co. Ltd.

Supreme Court of India

27 February 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, V.N. Khare, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 243; (2004) 3 SCC 466; AIR 2004 SC 3005; 2004 (92) ECC 457; 2004 (113) ECR 448; 2004 (165) E.L.T. 369; JT 2004 (3) SC 167; 2004 (61) RLT 357; 2004(3) SCALE 6; [2004] 2 S.C.R. 790; 2004 (2) SLT 716

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax

Keywords: Municipal Corporation Act, Calcutta Municipal Act, Uttar Pradesh Sheera Niyantran Adhiniyam, 1964, Uttar Pradesh Sheera Niyantaran Niyamawali, 1974

Summary: Administrative charges collected by sugar factory for molasses sold from buyers/allottees on behalf of State Government in terms of U.P. Sheera Niyantaran Adhiniyam, 1964 - constituted a duty or impostion in the nature of a tax and consequently - not includible in assessable value - are distinct, separate and in addition to price of molasses which price is statutory price fixed.
