D.G.M, Oil and Natural Gas Corporation Limited v Ilias Abdulrehman

Supreme Court of India

17 December 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

N. Santosh Hegde, S.B. Sinha

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 1065; (2005) 2 SCC 183; (2005) SCC (L&S) 195; AIR 2005 SC 660; 2005 (5) AWC 4158; 2005 (1) CLR 488; 2005 (3) ESC 424; 2005 (104) FLR 300; 2005 (2) G.L.R. 1050; JT 2005 (1) SC 87; 2005 (1) LLJ 554; 2005 (1) LLN 659; 2005 (139) PLR 473; 2004(10) SCALE 518; 2005 (7) SLR 256; 2005 (1) Supreme 15

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: [A.] Service - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 10(1)(c), 25F - Termination of service - Whether there is non compliance of provisions of s.25 F?; whether number of days of work put in by respondent in broken periods, can be taken as a continuous employment? - Held, Industrial Tribunal justified in coming to conclusion that number of days of work put in by respondent in broken periods, cannot be taken as a continuous employment for purpose of s.25 F - Restored order of Industrial Tribunal.
