Messrs Sony India Limited v Commissioner of Central Excise, Delhi

Supreme Court of India

05 May 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, G.P. Mathur

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 371; (2004) 5 SCC 751; AIR 2004 SC 3404; 2004 (167) E.L.T. 385; JT 2004 (Supp1) SC 567; 2004 (62) RLT 602; 2004(5) SCALE 560; [2004] Supp2 S.C.R. 127

Case Digest

Subject: Excise; Indirect Tax; Sales Tax; Socio-Economic

Summary: [A.] Excise - Standards Of Weights And Measures Act, 1976 - Central Excise Act, 1944, ss. 11-AB, 11-AC, 4-A(2) - Central Sales Tax Act, 1956 - Whether appellant required to pay excise duty at ad valorem basis or at specific rates as provided in relevant notification; whether penalty imposed u/s. 11-AC and interest u/s. 11-AB was justified - Appellant cleared goods from factory without indicating price thereof but affixed price in their depots - Whole object of removing goods from their factory premises to their depots was with purpose of getting over payment of higher duty - Confirmed duty @ 18% ad valorem and penalty.
