Transmission Corporation Of A.P. v Ch. Prabhakar & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

26 May 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

G.P. Mathur

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 468; (2004) 5 SCC 551; (2004) SCC (Cr) 1681; AIR 2004 SC 3368; 2004 (3) JhrCR(SC) 238; JT 2004 (Supp2) SC 613; 2004 (3) RCR(Criminal) 692; 2004(6) SCALE 1; [2004] Supp2 S.C.R. 759

Case Digest

Subject: Electricity & Energy; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Andhra Pradesh Civil Courts Act, 1972, Government of India Act, Indian Electricity (Andhra Pradesh Amendment) Act, 2000, Immigration Act, 1971, U.S. Constitution (ENG), Amending Act, 1903

Summary: [A.] Practice & Procedure - Code of Criminal Procedure 1973 - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908 - Andhra Pradesh Civil Courts Act, 1972 - Constitution Of India, 1950 - Indian Electricity Act, 1910 - Indian Electricity (Andhra Pradesh Amendment) Act, 2000 - Whether Amending Act could have any retrospective operation, namely it could affect proceedings which had already commenced and pending before Courts - Interpretation of art.20 - Imposition of higher or more severe punishment; imposition of higher fine, direct payment of compensation, no right to file a criminal revision and appeal - Interpretation of art.20 as to its scope and ambit involved in these proceedings, refer question to a larger bench for consideration.
