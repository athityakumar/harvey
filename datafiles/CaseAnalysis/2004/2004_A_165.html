Punjab National Bank and others v Ashwini Kumar Taneja

Supreme Court of India

16 August 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arijit Pasayat, C.K. Thakker

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 614; (2004) 7 SCC 265; (2004) SCC (L&S) 938; AIR 2004 SC 4155; 2004 (4) AWC 2904; 2004 (20) CLR 293; 2004 (4) ESC 605; 2004 (102) FLR 1023; JT 2004 (6) SC 418; 2004 (3) LLJ 536; 2004 (3) LLN 1228; 2004(7) SCALE 16; 2004 (7) SLR 27; [2004] Supp3 S.C.R. 597; 2004 (6) Supreme 180; 2004 (3) UPLBEC 2944

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Service - Appointment on compassionate grounds - HC allowed appointment - Whether retiral benifits received by heirs of deceased employee cannot be made a ground for rejecting application for compassionate appointment? - Held, appointment on compassionate ground cannot be claimed as a matter of right - Retiral benefits have to be taken into consideration - HC judgment cannot be sustained and set aside - Appeal allowed.
