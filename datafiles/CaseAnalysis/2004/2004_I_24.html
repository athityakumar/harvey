Mahendra L. Jain and Others v Indore Development Authority and Others

Supreme Court of India

22 November 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, N. Santosh Hegde

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 981; (2005) SCC (L&S) 154; (2005) 1 SCC 639; AIR 2005 SC 1252; 2005 (1) CLR 9; 2005 (104) FLR 53; JT 2004 (10) SC 1; 2005 (1) LLJ 578; 2005 (1) LLN 40; 2004(9) SCALE 579; 2005 (1) SLR 39; [2004] Supp6 S.C.R. 242; 2005 (1) UPLBEC 513

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Fundamental and Supplementary Rules, Civil Services (Classification, Control and Appeal) Rules, Revised Leave Rules, Madhya Pradesh Industrial Employment (Standing Orders) Act, 1961, Madhya Pradesh Nagar Tatha Gram Nibesh Adhiniyam, 1973, Civil Services (Temporary Service) Rules, Madhya Pradesh Development Authority Services (Officers and Servants) Recruitment Rules, 1987

Summary: Labour and Industrial Law - Madhya Pradesh Industrial Employment (Standing Orders) Act, 1961, s. 6 - M P Industrial Employment (Standing Order) Rules, 1963 - Appellants employed on daily wages - Whether appellant could lay a valid claim for regularization of their services? - Held, it has been seen that even services of one of them had been requisitioned only for project work - HC right in arriving at conclusion that appellants were not entitled to be regularized in service - No post was sanctioned by StateGovt. - Appeal dismissed.
