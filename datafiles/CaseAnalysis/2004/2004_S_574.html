Pankaj Gupta and Others etc v State of Jammu and Kashmir and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 September 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

K.G. Balakrishnan, AR. Lakshmanan

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 729; (2004) 8 SCC 353; (2004) SCC (L&S) 1125; AIR 2004 SC 4715; 2005 (1) CLR 131; 2004 (103) FLR 341; JT 2004 (8) SC 531; 2004 (3) LLJ 1081; 2004(7) SCALE 682; 2004 (6) SLR 810; [2004] Supp4 S.C.R. 459

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: Service - Class IV employees of State of J and K - Appointments - Legality thereof - Whether appointees entitled to regularization? - There was no publication of a notification inviting applications for filling up the posts - Names of appellants recommended by Members of Legislative Council and Legislative Assembly for appointment - No evidence to show that any criteria approved by Govt.or any rules of recruitment were followed while making these appointments - Appointees have no right for regularisation in service because of erroneous procedure adopted by concerned authority in appointing such persons - Held, appointments not valid - Directions issued - Appeals Disposed Of.
