Zile Singh v State of Haryana and Others

Supreme Court of India

07 October 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

R.C. Lahoti, G.P. Mathur, P.K. Balasubramanyan

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 854; (2004) 8 SCC 1; AIR 2004 SC 5100; JT 2004 (8) SC 589; 2005 (141) PLR 85; 2005 (2) RCR(Civil) 744; 2004(8) SCALE 659; [2004] Supp5 S.C.R. 272; 2005 (1) UC 159; 2005 (1) UPLBEC 239

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Election; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Municipalities and Local Govts - Haryana Municipal Act, 1973(principal Act) - Haryana Municipal (Amendment) Act, 1994 - Insertion of s. 13A in chapter III of principal Act - Municipalities - Membership - Disqualification for holding office of member - Commencement of period of disqualification - In Sunil Kumar Rana Vs State of Haryana (2003) 2 SCC 628 this court held that intent to compute period of one year under proviso is from 'commencement of this Act' meaning thereby from date of coming into force of amendment Act - Whether this decision need re-consideration? - Held, Sunil Kumar Rana's case has been correctly decided - It does not call for any reconsideration - Appeal dismissed.
