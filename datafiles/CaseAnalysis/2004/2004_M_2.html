Supreme Court of India

02 April 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S.H. Kapadia, V.N. Khare

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 424; (2004) 7 SCC 505; AIR 2004 SC 2060; 2004 (55) ALR 484; JT 2004 (4) SC 139; 2005 (139) PLR 224; 2004 (2) RCR(Civil) 489; 2004 (1) RCR(Rent) 510; 2004(4) SCALE 163; 2004 (3) Supreme 34

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Land & Property; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: [A.] Land & Property - Punjab Public Premises and Land (Eviction and Rent Recovery) Act, 1973 - Indian Succession Act, 1925 - Indian Succession (Amendment) Act, 1962 - Whether Govt. hospital was owner or beneficiary in terms of cl 2 of will? - Cl 2 indicates that a complete bequest was made in favour of Hospital which was to operate for all times in future - Held, hospital was owner - Property vests in executors by virtue of will and not by virtue of probate - Executors never objected to vesting of said property in hospital - On expiry of lease, respondent No 1 was in wrongful and illegal use and occupation of property in nature of unauthorized occupation.
