Situ Sahu and Others v State of Jharkhand and Others

Supreme Court of India

10 September 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Srikrishna, Shivaraj V. Patil

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 891; (2004) 8 SCC 340; AIR 2004 SC 4918; 2004 (4) JhrCR(SC) 211; JT 2004 (7) SC 402; 2004(7) SCALE 484; [2004] Supp4 S.C.R. 258

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Land and Property - Chota Nagpur Tenancy Act, 1908, s.71A - Old tenancy - Surrender of lands made in the year 1938 - Special officer exercising powers under said s.and entertaining application for restoration in 1978 and restoring land - Unreasonably long period of time of 40 years - Cannot be sustained - Appeal allowed.
