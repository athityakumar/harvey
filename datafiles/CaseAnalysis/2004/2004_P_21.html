M/s. Dale and Carrington Invt. (P) Limited and v P.K. Prathapan And Others

Supreme Court of India

13 September 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Arun Kumar, Ruma Pal

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 732; (2005) 1 SCC 212; AIR 2005 SC 1624; 6 CLC 449; [2004] 122 Comp Cas 161; 2004 (4) CompLJ 1; JT 2004 (7) SC 434; 2004 (3) KLT 475; 2005 (2) MLJ(SC) 10; 2004(7) SCALE 584; [2004] Supp4 S.C.R. 334

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Keywords: Fera

Summary: Corporate - Companies Act, 1956, ss.397,398 - Company petition - Validity of allotment of equity shares - Scope of power of HC in an appeal under s. 10F of Companies Act - Held, neither a copy of a notice convening Board meeting nor log book meant to record signatures of Directors attending meeting of Board of Directors were produced - Alleged allotment of additional equity shares of company wholly unauthorized and invalid - If a finding of fact is perverse and is based on no evidence, it can be set aside in appeal even though appeal is permissible only on question of law - Perversity of finding itself becomes a question of law - Relief granted by HC was a proper relief in facts of case - Appeals Dismissed.
