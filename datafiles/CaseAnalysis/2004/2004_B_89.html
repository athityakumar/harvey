Hpa International, Bhagwandas Fatehchand Daswani And Others v Bhagwandas Fateh Chand Daswani And Others, Hpa International And Others

Supreme Court of India

13 July 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

D.M. Dharmadhikari, Shivaraj V. Patil

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 474; (2004) 6 SCC 537; AIR 2004 SC 3858; 2004 (5) ALD(SC) 91; 2004 (3) AWC 2294; 2004 (2) CLR 603; JT 2004 (5) SC 408; 2004 (3) RCR(Civil) 577; 2004(6) SCALE 188; [2004] Supp3 S.C.R. 31

Case Digest

Subject: Contract & Commercial

Keywords: Contingent Contract

Summary: [A.] Contract & Commercial - Specific Relief Act, 1963, ss. 12, 14, 15, 16, 17, 21, 26, 27 - Indian Trusts Act, 1882, ss. 90, 91, 92 - Indian Contract Act, 1872, ss. 31, 32, 56 - Transfer Of Property Act, 1882, ss. 43, 6(a) - Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 144 - Specific Performance of Contract of Sale and court sanction for sale - Contract entered into between parties was for conveying full interest in property namely life interest of vendor and chance of succession of reversioners - Failure to fulfil contract - Decree of Specific Performance of Contract of Sale of suit property granted by single judge set aside with certain directions to adjust equities between parties by DB - Question of fact to be determined who is guilty of act or omission to render contract void or unenforceable - Held, vendee tried his utmost to obtain court's sanction and cannot be blamed for transposing him as a co-plaintiff and prosecuting sanction suit, sanction could not be obtained for reasons beyond control of parties - Vendor can not be held guilty of breach as to entitle vendee to seek specific performance of life interest of vendor - Adjust equities with regard to construction cost and rental income derived from suit property - Judgment of DB confirmed.
