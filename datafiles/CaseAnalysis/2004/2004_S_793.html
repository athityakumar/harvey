Dr. Manju Varma v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

17 November 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

Ruma Pal, P. Venkatarama Reddi

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 978; (2005) 1 SCC 73; 2005 (1) AWC 520; 2005 (2) CompLJ 9; 2004 (178) E.L.T. 64; 2005 (2) JhrCR(SC) 159; JT 2004 (9) SC 569; 2005 (1) RCR(Civil) 769; 2004(9) SCALE 463; [2004] Supp6 S.C.R. 22; 2005 (1) UPLBEC 496; 2005 (1) UPLBEC 948

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure; Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Practice and Procedure - United Provinces High Courts (Amalgamation) Order, 1948, para. 14 - Transfer of writ petition - Transfer order by the Chief Justice by Allahabad HC to transfer petition from Lucknow Bench of HC to Allahabad for hearing - Whether appeal lie against this order under art 136? - Held, order of Chief Justice was, if not judicial, at least quasi judicial - State Govt.which issued impugned order and against which mandamus was prayed for by appellant is in Lucknow - Set aside order of Chief Justice directing transfer of appellant's writ petition and leave matter to Lucknow Bench which heard matter to proceed with it - Appeal allowed.
