Chattisgarh Rural Agriculture Extension Officers Asson v State Of M.P. And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

05 April 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, V.N. Khare, S.H. Kapadia

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 416; (2004) 4 SCC 646; (2004) SCC (L&S) 667; AIR 2004 SC 2020; 2004 (5) AWC 4232; 2004 (2) CLR 538; 2004 (101) FLR 691; 2004 (3) JhrCR(SC) 16; JT 2004 (4) SC 446; 2004 (2) KLT 265; 2004 (2) LLJ 1114; 2004 (2) LLN 729; 2004 (3) MLJ(SC) 139; 2004(4) SCALE 260; 2004 (3) SLR 305

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure; Service

Summary: [A.] Constitution - Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 14, 16, 309, 39(d) - Madhya Pradesh Revision of Pay Rules, 1983, r. 3 - Equal pay for equal work - Sanction of different pay scale to employees belonging to same cadre according to educational qualification - When recommendations are made by a Pay Commission, evaluation of job must be held to have been made but same by itself may not be a ground to enforce recommendations by issuing a writ of or in nature of mandamus although State did not accept same in toto and made rules to contrary by evolving a policy decision which cannot be said to arbitrary or discriminatory.
