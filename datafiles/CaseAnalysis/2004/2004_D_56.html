Dental Council Of India v S.R.M. Institute of Science and Technology and

Supreme Court of India

05 April 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Rajendra Babu, G.P. Mathur

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 426; (2004) 9 SCC 676; AIR 2004 SC 2057; 2004 (5) AWC 4217; 2004 (4) ESC 546; JT 2004 (4) SC 369; 2004(4) SCALE 184; 2004 (3) SLR 298; 2004 (3) Supreme 63; 2004 (3) UPLBEC 2419

Case Digest

Subject: Education; Health & Drug

Summary: [A.] Education - Dentists Act, 1948 - Starting a new or higher course in college - HC made an interim order to process application even in absence of permission or essentiality certificate - Application will not be complete without being accompanied by permission or essentiality certificate by State Govt. along with certain other documents - An incomplete application cannot be processed either by Central Govt. or Dental Council - Order made by HC is modified.
