General Manager (D and Pb) and Others v Kunti Tiwary and Another

Supreme Court of India

05 January 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

B.N. Agrawal, Mrs. Justice Ruma Pal

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 1481; (2004) 7 SCC 271; (2004) SCC (L&S) 943; 2004 (3) LLJ 1136; 2004(7) SCALE 155; 2004 (3) UPLBEC 2534

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Appointment - On compassionate ground - Whether appointment on compassionate ground depends upon financial condition of family? - Held, family of deceased not left in 'penury' or 'without any means of livelihood' - Relied on Umesh Kumar Nagpal v. State of Haryana, (1994 Indlaw SC 172) - Compassionate appointment exception where dependants of employee left without any means of livelihood and that unless some source of livelihood was provided a family would not be able to make both ends meet - Appeal allowed.
