State of West Bengal and Others v Surjit Kumar Rana

Supreme Court of India

20 January 2004

Case Analysis

Bench

S.B. Sinha, Doraiswamy Raju

Where Reported

2004 Indlaw SC 102; (2004) 4 SCC 129; (2004) SCC (Cr) 984; AIR 2004 SC 1851; 2004 (3) CalLT(SC) 55; 2004 (2) CRJ 514; 2004 (1) JCC 324; 2004 (4) JhrCR(SC) 137; JT 2004 (5) SC 157; 2004(1) SCALE 641; [2004] 1 S.C.R. 870; 2004 (2) SLT 129; 2004 (1) UC 589

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Karnatak Forest Act, 1963

Summary: [A.] Criminal - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 136, 226, 300, 300-A, 48, 48-A, 51-A(g) - Code of Criminal Procedure 1973, s. 482 - Indian Forest Act, 1927 - Criminal Procedure Code, 1898,s. 482 - Once a confiscation proceeding is initiated jurisdiction of criminal court being barred - HC also cannot exercise its jurisdiction u/s.482 of CrPC for interim release of property - Only judicial review possible - Appeal allowed.
