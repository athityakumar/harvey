Pratap Singh v Union Territory of Chandigarh

Supreme Court of India

03 September 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, P.S. Kailasam, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 369; (1979) 4 SCC 263; (1979) SCC (L&S) 369; AIR 1980 SC 57; 1979 LabIC 1359; 1980 (1) LLN 7; [1980] 1 S.C.R. 487; 1979 SLJ 590; 1979 (2) SLR 629; 1979 UJ 903

Case Digest

Subject: Defence & Security Forces; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Service - Punjab Police Rules, 1934, r. 128 - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 16, 309 and 311 - (A) Applicability of r. 128 of Rules to Asstt. Sub-Inspector appointed on temporary basis - (B) If probationer can be deemed to be automatically confirmed in absence of express order of confirmation on completion of probation - Held, not applicable - Cannot be so confirmed, presumed to be extended - Appeal dismissed.
