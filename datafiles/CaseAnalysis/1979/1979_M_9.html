B. Saha and Others v M. S. Kochar

Supreme Court of India

27 July 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Sarkaria, P.N. Shingal, O. Chinnappa Reddy

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 215; (1979) 4 SCC 177; (1979) SCC (Cr) 939; 1979 (16) ACC 318; AIR 1979 SC 1841; 1979 CRLJ 1367; 1979 CrLR(SC) 537; [1980] 1 S.C.R. 111

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Customs; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Criminal - CrPC, 1973, ss. 197 and 197(1) - Customs Act, 1962 - IPC, 1860, ss. 120B and 409 - (A) Stages at which question to sanction to prosecute u/s. 197 of 1973 Act can be raised - And factors to be considered while considering the question whether sanction is required - (B) Applicability of s. 197(1) - Whether sanction to prosecute for offence u/ss. 409 and 120B, IPC, 1860 is required - Held, can be raised and considered at any stage of the proceedings and court can take into account all the materials on the record - Sanction held, was not necessary - Appeal dismissed.
