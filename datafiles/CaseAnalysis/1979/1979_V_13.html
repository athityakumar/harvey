State Of Maharashtra v Veerappa R. Saboji And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

06 September 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

N.L. Untwalia, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 312; (1979) 4 SCC 466; (1980) SCC (L&S) 61; AIR 1980 SC 42; 1979 LabIC 1389; 1979 (2) LLJ 393; 1980 (1) LLN 17; [1980] 1 S.C.R. 551; 1979 SLJ 621; 1979 (2) SLR 527

Case Digest

Subject: Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Service - Constitution, arts. 309 and 311(2) - Bombay Judicial Service Recruitment Rules, 1956, r.4(2)(iv) - Service Rules - Judicial services - Probationer or temporary servant - (A) If continuation of Judicial Officer in service after period of probation can be deemed to be automatically confirmed - (B) Simple termination - If probationer or temporary servant can show by reference to official records that termination is by way of punishment - Officer held, cannot be deemed to be automatically confirmed - Allowed, if a sufficient case being made out , and the Court should not decline to peruse official records - Appeal allowed.
