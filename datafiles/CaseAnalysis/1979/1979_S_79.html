Smt. Santosh Anand v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

31 October 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

V.D. Tulzapurkar, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 348; (1981) 2 SCC 420; (1981) SCC (Cr) 456

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Comments

Summary: Criminal - Detention - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 22(5) - Conservation Of Foreign Exchange And Prevention of Smuggling Activities Act, ss. 3 and 11 - Whether detention of petitioner legal and justified? - Held, representation sent by detenu was not decided before sending it to advisory board and hence, art. 22(5) cannot be said to have been complied - And detenu deprived of his remedy to approach administrator as higher authority - Continued detention after detention order was illegal and unjustified - Petition allowed.
