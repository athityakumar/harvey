Smt. Sukhrani (Dead) By L. R. S. and Others v Hari Shanker and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 April 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, P.N. Shingal

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 408; (1979) 2 SCC 463; AIR 1979 SC 1436; [1979] 3 S.C.R. 671; 1979 UJ 482

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal

Summary: Family & Personal - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 11 - (A) Matter decided at earlier stage of suit by interlocutory order - No appeal taken therefrom - Effect on its consideration at some latter stage - (B) Right of erstwhile minor coparcener of partitioned Joint Hindu Family, to reopen partition on ground of inequality under Hindu Law - Held, Higher Court not precluded from considering matter again at later stage of same suit - Further held, a partition can be reopened at instance of a minor coparcener, despite of fact that his branch was represented by his father at partition, if partition was unfair or prejudicial to interest of minor - Appeal dismissed.
