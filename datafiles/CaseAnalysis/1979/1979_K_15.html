Navinchandra Ramanlal v Kalidas Bhudarbhai And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

21 February 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

D.A. Desai, P.N. Shingal

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 268; (1979) 4 SCC 75; AIR 1979 SC 1055; 1979 (20) GLR 715; [1979] 3 S.C.R. 329; 1979 UJ 374

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Bombay Tenancy Act, 1939, Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Amendment) Act, Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Amendment) Act, 1956

Summary: Rent Control - Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands Act, 1948, ss. 43C and 88(1)(b) - Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Amendment) Act, 1956 - Land and Property - Scope of ss. 43C and 88(1)(b) 1948 Act - Effect of bringing in land within area of Ahmedabad Municipal Corporation on 30-05-1959 - Held, would not enjoy benefit of exemption u/s. 88 (1) (b) - Appeal dismissed.
