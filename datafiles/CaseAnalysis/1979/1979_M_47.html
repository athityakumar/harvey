Meeenakshisundaram Chettiar v Venkatachalam Chettiar

Supreme Court of India

23 February 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

P.S. Kailasam, O. Chinnappa Reddy, R.S. Sarkaria

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 457; (1980) 1 SCC 616; AIR 1979 SC 989; 1979 (2) MLJ(SC) 19; [1979] 3 S.C.R. 385; 1979 UJ 449

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Court fees Act, 1870, s. 7(iv)(f) - Tamil Nadu Court fees and Suits Valuation Act, 1955, s. 35 - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O.7, r.11(b) - Determination of valuation of relief in suit for true and correct account of all transactions - Plaintiff is not obliged to state exact amount but valuation should be adequate and reasonable - Held, not satisfied that estimate of relief as given by plaintiff is inadequate or unreasonable or a deliberate underestimation - Appeal allowed.
