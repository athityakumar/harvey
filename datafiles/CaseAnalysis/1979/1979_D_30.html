Shyamcharan Sharma v Dharamdas

Supreme Court of India

04 December 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, V.R. Krishna Iyer, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 173; (1980) 2 SCC 151; AIR 1980 SC 587; 1980 (1) RCJ 333; 1980 (1) RentLR 78; [1980] 2 S.C.R. 334; 1980 UJ 117

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - MP Accommodation Control Act, 1961, ss. 12(1)(a), 12(3) and 13(6) - Tenancy - Striking out defence for non-payment of rent is a discretionary matter of court in eviction suits - Effect of deposit of monthly rent in Court - Held, extension of time granted and tenant not liable to eviction - Appeal allowed.
