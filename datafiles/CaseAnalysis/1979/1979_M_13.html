Union of India Etc v M.E. Reddy and Anr

Supreme Court of India

19 September 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 187; (1980) 2 SCC 15; (1980) SCC (L&S) 179; AIR 1980 SC 563; 1980 LabIC 221; 1980 (1) LLJ 7; 1980 (1) LLN 306; [1980] 1 S.C.R. 736; 1979 SLJ 738; 1979 (2) SLR 792

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Summary: Constitution - All India Services (Death-cum-Retirement Benefits) Rules, 1958, r. 16(3) - Violation of principles of natural justice - Respondent, a Police officer suspended and later Order of compulsory retirement was issued in public interest - Confidential reports not considered by appointing authority - Held, no violation of natural justice and the confidential reports to be considered by the appointing authority in passing the order of retirement u/r. 16(3) even if they are not communicated to the Officer concerned - Appeal allowed.
