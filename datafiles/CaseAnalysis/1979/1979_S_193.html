Rama Verma Bharathan Thampuran v State of Kerala and Others

Supreme Court of India

30 July 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, D.A. Desai, A.D. Koshal

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 211; (1979) 4 SCC 782; AIR 1979 SC 1918; [1980] 2 S.C.R. 136

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal

Keywords: Joint Hindu Family System, Kerala Joint Hindu Family System Abolition Act, 1975

Summary: Family & Personal - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 14 - Valiamma Thampuran Kovilakam Estate and Palace Fund (Partition) Act, 1961 - Kerala Joint Hindu Family System (Abolition) Act, 1976, ss. 3, 4 and 5 - Constitutional validity - Held, not invalid - Royal family estate is being partitioned on principles similar to those applicable to all other Kerala Hindu families and only difference is a Board instead of a court to allot shares by metes and bounds - Petition dismissed.
