Reliance Jute and Industries Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax, West Bengal

Supreme Court of India

10 October 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

R.S. Pathak, N.L. Untwalia

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 144; (1980) 1 SCC 139; AIR 1980 SC 251; 1979 (13) CTR 186; [1979] 120 ITR 921; [1980] 1 S.C.R. 906; 1980 TAXLR 8; [1979] 2 TAXMAN 417

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1922 - Applicability of 24(2)(iii) - Assessee, manufacturer of Jute - Whether its unabsorbed loss for assessment year 1950-51 can be set off against business income of assessment year 1960-61? - Held, cannot be set off - Appeal dismissed.
