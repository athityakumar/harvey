Financial Commissioner, Haryana and Others v Kela Devi and Another

Supreme Court of India

31 October 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Shingal, N.L. Untwalia, A.D. Koshal

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 87; (1980) 1 SCC 77; AIR 1980 SC 309; [1980] 1 S.C.R. 1120; 1980 UJ 237

Case Digest

Subject: Defence & Security Forces; Land & Property; Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Punjab Security of Land Tenures Act, 1953, ss. 10 A and 10 B - Punjab Security of Land Tenure Rules, 1956, rr. 20 A, 20 B, 20 C - When process of utilization of of 'surplus land' is complete - Effect of rr. 20 A, 20 B and 20 C and saving cl (b) in s. 10 A - Held, completes when possession thereof has been taken by allottee or allottees and other formalities have been completed - And s. 10 A (b) has effect of saving land, comprised in 'surplus area' if it has been acquired by an heir utilisation of deceased's 'surplus area' had not been completed by inheritance - Appeal dismissed.
