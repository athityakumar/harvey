State Of Kerala v Kumari T. P. Roshana & Anr.

Supreme Court of India

17 January 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 296; (1979) 1 SCC 572; AIR 1979 SC 765; [1979] 2 S.C.R. 974

Case Digest

Subject: Education

Summary: Constitution, arts.14, 15, 32, 136 and 226 - Admission to Medical Colleges - Whether linkage of division of seats with registered student-strength is discriminatory - Considering ancillary issues like writ jurisdiction, duty of court , principle of affirmative action - Held, discriminatory - Court cannot adopt a rigid attitude of negativity and sit back after striking down the scheme of Govt. - Principle with in jurisdiction of SC - Appeal and petition allowed.
