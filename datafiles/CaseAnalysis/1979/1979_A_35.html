Zohrabi v Arjuna and Others

Supreme Court of India

16 August 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

A.C. Gupta, P.N. Bhagwati

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 376; (1980) 2 SCC 203; AIR 1980 SC 101; 1980 MahLJ 58; 1979 UJ 725

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Hyderabad Tenancy and Agricultural Lands Act, 1950, s. 28(1), Proviso - Whether proviso to s. 28(1) of Act as amended in 1960 would apply - Application for possession was made long after amendment came into force; even right to institute a proceeding does not appear to have accrued before amendment because notice terminating tenancy was also issued after proviso was amended - Held, landlord not entitled to possession - Right to take advantage of provisions of an Act not an accrued right - Appeal dismissed.
