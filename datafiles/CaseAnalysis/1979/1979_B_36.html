Bhe Ram and another v State of Haryana

Supreme Court of India

29 March 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, A.D. Koshal

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 420; (1980) 1 SCC 201; (1980) SCC (Cr) 206; AIR 1980 SC 957; 1980 CRLJ 735; 1979 CRLR 741; 1980 MLJ 572

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Penal Code, 1860, s. 149 - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 3 - (A) Unlawful assembly - Need of attribution of specific overt act in murder (B) Appreciation of evidence - Applicability of principle of falsus in uno falsus in omnibus - Held, act need not be attributed to accused - Principle does not apply to criminal trials and duty of the Court ponted out - Appeal Dismissed.
