Union Of India v Mohd. Nizam

Supreme Court of India

18 October 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

A.C. Gupta, E.S. Venkataramiah

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 77; (1980) 1 SCC 284; AIR 1980 SC 431; [1980] 1 S.C.R. 968; 1979 UJ 907

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Contract & Commercial

Summary: Contract and Commercial - Post Office Act, 1898, ss. 2(f), 17, 23(3), 34 Provisio - Contract Act, 1872, ss.191, 182 - Agreement for the exchange of value payable Postal articles between India and Pakistan - Liability of Indian Govt. to pay the sender on default by Pakistani Govt. - (A) Whether the Post Office is an agent of sender of articles - (B) Whether Pakistan Govt. , sub-agent of Indian Govt. - Held, an agent - Pakistan Govt. not sub-agent of Indian Govt. - Appeal allowed.
