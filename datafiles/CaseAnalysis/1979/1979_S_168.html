Bhim Singh and Others v State of Haryana and Others

Supreme Court of India

24 July 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

V.R. Krishna Iyer, D.A. Desai, A.D. Koshal

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 385; (1981) SCC (L&S) 437; (1981) 2 SCC 673; AIR 1980 SC 768; 1979 UJ 829

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Service - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 115 - Doctrine of promissory estoppel - Govt. service - Inducement to employees to move into newly established department - Subsequent denial of rights and benefits which were promisd by State Govt. - Held employees having believed representations and having acted thereon could not be denied rights and benefits - Order accordingly.
