Marudanal Augusthi v State of Kerala

Supreme Court of India

29 March 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, A.D. Koshal

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 419; (1980) 4 SCC 425; (1980) SCC (Cr) 985; AIR 1980 SC 638; 1980 CRLJ 446; 1981 CRLR 85

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, s. 386 - Acquittal - High Court reversed judgment - Appellate Court must consider whether or not view taken by Lower Court could be reasonably possible - Held, on facts of case HC was not justified - Appeal allowed.
