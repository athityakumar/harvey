Mohd. Shabir v State Of Maharashtra

Supreme Court of India

17 January 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, A.D. Koshal

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 295; (1979) SCC (Cr) 356; (1979) 1 SCC 568; AIR 1979 SC 564; 1979 CRLJ 466; 1979 CrLR(SC) 118; 1979 MLJ 448; [1979] 2 S.C.R. 997; 1979 UJ 328

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Health & Drug

Summary: Health and Drug - Criminal - Drugs And Cosmetics Act, 1940, ss. 18, 27 - Accused a young man - Whether possession of cosmetics is punishable under s. 27 of the Act ?- Held, proof of manufacturing for sale, selling or stocks or exhibition for sale necessarily required - Possession not punishable - Accused young man coming from respectable family- Makes confession and pleads guilty - Deterrent sentence uncalled for - Appeal allowed.
