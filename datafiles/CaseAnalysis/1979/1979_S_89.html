Abdulla Mohammed Pagarkar and another v State (Union Territory of Goa, Daman and Diu)

Supreme Court of India

11 September 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

A.D. Koshal, S. Murtaza Fazal Ali

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 574; (1980) 3 SCC 110; (1980) SCC (Cr) 546; AIR 1980 SC 499; 1980 CRLJ 220; 1979 CrLR(SC) 653; [1980] 1 S.C.R. 604

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 420, 468 and 471 - Prevention of Corruption Act, 1947, s. 5(1)(d) - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973 - Burden to prove offences u/s. 5(1)(d) of 1947 Act lies on prosecution - Irregularities in execution of work - Failure to repel strong suspicion against accused - Conviction, if can be base upon - Held, not sufficient for their conviction - Appeals allowed.
