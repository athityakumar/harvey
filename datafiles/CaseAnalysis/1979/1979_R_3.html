S.Mohan Lal v R. Kondiah

Supreme Court of India

05 February 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, R.S. Sarkaria

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 280; (1979) 2 SCC 616; AIR 1979 SC 1132; 1979 (1) RCJ 487; [1979] 3 S.C.R. 12; 1979 UJ 338

Case Digest

Subject: Advocates & Judges

Summary: Rent Control - Andhra Pradesh Buildings (Lease, Rent and Eviction) Control Act, 1960, s. 10(3)(a)(iii) - Advocates Act, 1961- Interpretation of expression 'Business' u/s. 10(3)(a)(iii) of 1960 Act - Word to be interpreted in context of statute and not by reference to its use in another Act - Scheme of Act - Held, expression 'Business' is used in wide sense to include practice of profession of Advocate - Appeal dismissed.
