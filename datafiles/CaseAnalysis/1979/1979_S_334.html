Union of India v Surjit Singh Atwal

Supreme Court of India

18 January 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, R.S. Sarkaria

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 294; (1979) 1 SCC 520; AIR 1979 SC 1701; [1979] 2 S.C.R. 1002; 1979 UJ 209

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Defence & Security Forces; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, O. 6,r. 8, O. 8,r. 2 - Contract with Govt. - Plea of non- Compliance of s. 175(3) of Govt. of India Act - Raised years after filing of written statement by Govt. - Permissibility - Held, plea cannot be allowed - Appeal dismissed.
