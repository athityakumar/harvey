Commissioner Of Income Tax, Kerala v Ambat Echukutty Menon

Supreme Court of India

06 September 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

N.L. Untwalia, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 313; (1979) 4 SCC 298; AIR 1980 SC 71; 1979 (12) CTR 395; [1979] 120 ITR 70; [1980] 1 S.C.R. 539; [1979] 2 TAXMAN 293; 1980 UJ 229

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 4 and 5 - Income Tax - Revenue receipt or capital receipt - Determination of - Sale of trees of spontaneous growth whose trunks only are allowed to be cutted - Stumps and roots to be kept intact to regenerate income - But purpose proved to be protecting soil for cultivation - Nature of income from sale - Held, income from sale held not revenue receipt - Appeals Dismissed.
