Vimal Chand Jawantraj Jain v Pradhan and Others

Supreme Court of India

04 May 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

P.N. Bhagwati, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 555; (1979) 4 SCC 401; (1980) SCC (Cr) 4; AIR 1979 SC 1501; 1979 CRLJ 1131; 1979 CrLR(SC) 647; [1979] 3 S.C.R. 1007; 1979 UJ 814

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Conservation Of Foreign Exchange And Prevention Of Smuggling Activities, Act, 1974, s. 3 - Constitution Of India, 1953, art. 22(5) - Validity of detention order under Act - Held, Petitioner's representation not considered at time of passing of detention order - Order violated art. 22(5) - Subsequent consideration and rejection of representation could not cure invalidity of order of confirmation - Detention of petitioner must, therefore, be held to be illegal and void - Detention set aside - Order accordingly.
