Anand Narain Shukla v State of Madhya Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

02 August 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

N.L. Untwalia, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 56; (1980) SCC (L&S) 140; (1980) 1 SCC 252; AIR 1979 SC 1923; 1979 (39) FLR 325; 1979 LabIC 1214; [1980] 1 S.C.R. 196; 1979 (2) SLR 288

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 and 331 - Reversion - Writ petition against, filed by Govt. servant - Quashed on technical ground - Re-instatement - Permissibility to conduct second enquiry on merits - Held, permissible - Appeal dismissed.
