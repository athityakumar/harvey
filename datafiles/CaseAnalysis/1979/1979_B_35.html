Mathuralal v Bhanwarlal and Another

Supreme Court of India

13 September 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

O. Chinnappa Reddy, D.A. Desai

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 573; (1979) 4 SCC 665; (1980) SCC (Cr) 9; 1979 (16) ACC 325; AIR 1980 SC 242; 1980 CRLJ 1; 1979 CrLR(SC) 673; [1980] 1 S.C.R. 620

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, ss. 145 and 145 - Attachment on ground of emergency - It does not end jurisdiction of Magistrate to proceed with enquiry u/s 145 of Code - If required to wait for determination of rights of parties by competent court - Held, need not wait - Appeal dismissed.
