Pushpaben and Another v Narandas V. Badiani and Another

Supreme Court of India

29 March 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, A.D. Koshal

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 247; (1979) 2 SCC 394; (1979) SCC (Cr) 511; AIR 1979 SC 1536; 1979 CRLJ 960; 1979 CrLR(SC) 421; [1979] 3 S.C.R. 636

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Willful Disobedience

Summary: Criminal - Contempt of Courts Act, 1971, ss. 2(b) and 12(3) - Contempt - (A) Compounding of offence u/s. 420, IPC, 1860 - Acquittal on breach of undertakings - (B) Punishment for contempt of Court - Sentence - Legality - Held, breach of undertakings amounted to wilful disobedience of order of Court - Sentence of imprisonment is an exception - Appeal partly allowed.
