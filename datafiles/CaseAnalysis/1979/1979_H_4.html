State of Uttar Pradesh v Hakim Singh and Others

Supreme Court of India

12 October 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 602; (1980) 3 SCC 55; (1980) SCC (Cr) 534; AIR 1980 SC 184; 1980 ALJ 16; 1980 (4) ShimLC 290

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Constitution - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 302 and 34 - Indian Evidence Act, 1872 - Double Murder - Respondents convicted by Trial Court but acquitted by High Court - Whether acquittal of respondents proper? - Held, HC clearly at error in not appreciating testimonies of two eye-witnesses - HC wrongly concluded that these two eye-witnesses were interested - Trial Court order restored - Appeal allowed.
