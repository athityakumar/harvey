Babu Ram Gupta v Sudhir Bhasin and Another

Supreme Court of India

12 April 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Murtaza Fazal Ali, A.D. Koshal

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 164; (1980) 3 SCC 47; (1980) SCC (Cr) 527; AIR 1979 SC 1528; 1979 CRLJ 952; 1980 (17) DLT 23; [1979] 3 S.C.R. 685

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Contract & Commercial

Keywords: Contemner, misa

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration Act, 1940, s. 20 - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 - Contempt of Courts Act, 1971, ss. 19 and 2(b) - Whether disobedience of compromise decree amounts to contempt of court? - Held, Punishment for contempt of court - Disobedience of compromise decree or consent order - Does not amount to contempt - Punishment for contempt - Exercise of power - It must not be exercised lightly but should be exercised only to uphold majesty of law and dignity of courts - Appeal allowed.
