Commissioner of Sales Tax, Lucknow v D. S. Bist and Others

Supreme Court of India

11 September 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

N.L. Untwalia, R.S. Pathak

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 309; (1979) 4 SCC 741; (1980) SCC (Tax) 41; AIR 1980 SC 169; [1980] 1 S.C.R. 593; 1979 (44) STC 392; 1980 UJ 220

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure; Sales Tax

Keywords: Processing

Summary: Sales Tax - Uttar Pradesh Sales Tax Act, 1948, s. 2(i), Proviso - Sales Tax on sale proceeds of tea (agricultural produce) - Whether tea grown by agriculturist undergoing certain processes before it was sold ceases to be an agricultural produce? - Held, in its basic nature, it continued to be agricultural produce - Appeals dismissed.
