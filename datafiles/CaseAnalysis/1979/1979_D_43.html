Dora Phalauli v State of Punjab and Others

Supreme Court of India

24 July 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

N.L. Untwalia, A.P. Sen

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 217; (1979) 4 SCC 485; AIR 1979 SC 1594; [1980] 1 S.C.R. 93; 1979 UJ 599

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894 - Order u/s. 17 - Validity of mode of expression of satisfaction of Govt. - Order further directing Collector to take action on ground of urgency - Held, mere direction was not a legal and complete fulfillment of requirement of law - Appeal allowed.
