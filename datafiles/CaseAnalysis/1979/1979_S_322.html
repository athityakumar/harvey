Dadaji Alias Dina v Sukhdeobabu and Others

Supreme Court of India

07 November 1979

Case Analysis

Bench

E.S. Venkataramiah, V.D. Tulzapurkar

Where Reported

1979 Indlaw SC 61; (1980) 1 SCC 621; AIR 1980 SC 150; [1980] 1 S.C.R. 1135; 1980 UJ 26

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Socio-Economic

Keywords: Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes Orders (Amendment) Act, 1976

Summary: Constitution - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 332, 342 and 366 - Representation of People Act, 1951, ss. 5, 81 and 116-A - Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes Orders (Amendment) Act, 1976 - Status of Scheduled Tribe - Entitlement - Whether by reason of amendment made in year 1976, persons belonging to Mana community to which appellant belonged and which was not a Scheduled Tribe before such amendment can be considered as persons belonging to a Scheduled Tribe after such amendment? - Held, no - If Parliament intended to treat appellant's community also as a Scheduled Tribe, it would have shown 'Mana' community under a separate entry but no such entry is found in schedule - Appeal dismissed.
