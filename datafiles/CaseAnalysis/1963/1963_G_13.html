Sirsilk Limited and Others v Government of Andhra Pradesh and Another

Supreme Court of India

20 March 1963

Case Analysis

Bench

Kailas Nath Wanchoo, Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, K.C. Das Gupta

Where Reported

1963 Indlaw SC 120; AIR 1964 SC 160; 1965 (10) FLR 50; 1963 (2) LLJ 647; [1964] 2 S.C.R. 448

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Corporate; Labour & Industrial Law; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Code of Civil Procedure

Summary: Labor & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, ss. 17(1), 18(1), 18(3) and 17 A - Whether it is mandatory for Govt. to publish award by a tribunal in an industrial dispute? - Held, where in an industrial dispute, after proceedings before tribunal have come to an end - Tribunal has sent its award to Govt. - Before its publication, there is a settlement between parties to dispute which has become operative - It is open to Govt. in special circumstances not to publish award u/s. 17(1) - Appeal allowed.
