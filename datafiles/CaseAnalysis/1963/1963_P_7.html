 Patel Gordhandas Hargovindas v Municipal Commissioner, Ahmedabad

Supreme Court of India

28 March 1963

Case Analysis

Bench

Kailas Nath Wanchoo, Bhuvaneshwar Prasad Sinha, S.K. Das, A.K. Sarkar, K.C. Das Gupta

Where Reported

1963 Indlaw SC 123; AIR 1963 SC 1742; 1964 (66) BomLR 68; [1964] 2 S.C.R. 608

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments

Keywords: Madras District Municipalities Act, 1920

Summary: Municipalities & Local Govts - Bombay, Municipal Boroughs Act, 1925, ss. 73(1)(b) and 75 - Govt. of India Act, 1935, sch.7, list 1, item 55, sch 7, list 2, item 42 - Madras District Municipalities Act, 1920 - Interpretation of statues - Municipal Corporation of Ahmedabad - Rules framed for rating open lands - Permission of fixation of rate at percentage of capital value - Validity of r. 350-A r/w r. 243 in light of ss. 73 and 75 of Act challenged - Held, ultra vires sections - Appeal allowed.
