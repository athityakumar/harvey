 State of Orissa v Ram Chandra Dev and another

Supreme Court of India

25 November 1963

Case Analysis

Bench

Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, A.K. Sarkar, Kailas Nath Wanchoo, K.C. Das Gupta, N. Rajagopala Ayyangar

Where Reported

1963 Indlaw SC 415; AIR 1964 SC 685; 1964 (30) CutLT 213

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 226 - Whether High Court can issue a writ u/art. 226 of Constitution while issue of title to property is disputed question? - Held, HC was in error to issue a writ in favour of respondents - Because possession for a long period of time does not grant title against state as distinguished from private person - Writ proceedings not to be used for deciding issues of title which must be decided by suit before a proper court - Appeal allowed.
