Pulavarthi Venkata Subba Raoand Others v Valluri Jagannadha Rao and Others

Supreme Court of India

13 March 1963

Case Analysis

Bench

Mohammad Hidayatullah, Pralhad Balacharya Gajendragadkar, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1963 Indlaw SC 351; AIR 1967 SC 591; 1964 (2) ALT(SC) 359; 1964 (2) AnWR 112; 1964 (2) MLJ 112; 1964 (2) MLJ(SC) 112; 1964 (2) SCJ 518; [1964] 2 S.C.R. 310

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Civil Procedure - Practice & Procedure - Civil Procedure Code, 1908, s. 16 - Madras Agriculturists Relief Act, 1938 - Res judicata - Suit for recovery of loan - Respondent filed application for scaling down of amount - Suit ended in compromise decree - Whether respondent were agriculturists and were entitled to scaling down of decree? - All decrees executed before commencement of amending act were unaffected - Demand for scaling down valid - Amending act confers right upon agriculturists to save from operation loan taken at usurious rates of interest - Held, conduct of respondent in omitting to demand reduction to claim did not constitute resjudicata- Compromise decree was not decision of court so not res judicata- Compromise decree does not creates an estoppel against respondents - Appeal dismissed.
