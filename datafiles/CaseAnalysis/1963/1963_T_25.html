Ram Bilas Singh and Others v State Or Bihar

Supreme Court of India

29 January 1963

Case Analysis

Bench

J.R. Madholkar

Where Reported

1963 Indlaw SC 289; 1963 ALJ 609; 1964 CRLJ 573; [1964] 1 S.C.R. 775

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 147, 149, 302, 307 and 326 - Appeal against judgment altering conviction of appellants - Held, High Court has failed to determine questions which were essential for it to determine - Therefore, set aside that judgment and send back case to HC for deciding it afresh - Appeal allowed.
