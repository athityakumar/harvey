Nihal Singh and Others v State of Punjab

Supreme Court of India

10 May 1963

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Subba Rao, Raghubar Dayal, J.R. Madholkar

Where Reported

1963 Indlaw SC 187; AIR 1965 SC 26; 1965 CRLJ 105; [1964] 4 S.C.R. 5

Case Digest

Subject: Practice & Procedure

Summary: Criminal - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 148, 149 and 302 - High Court set aside order acquitting 5 appellants of charges u/ss. 148 and 302/149 and convicting them under said sections - Appeal - Held, HC has full power to review evidence when hearing appeal against acquittal u/s. 423, CrPC,1973 - Conclusion reached by HC after re-appreciation of evidences and facts correct - Appeal dismissed.
