Sri Gopal Jalan and Company v Calcutta Stock Exchange Association Limited

Supreme Court of India

09 May 1963

Case Analysis

Bench

A.K. Sarkar, Mohammad Hidayatullah, J.C. Shah

Where Reported

1963 Indlaw SC 375; AIR 1964 SC 250; [1963] 33 Comp Cas 862; 1963 CompLJ 198; 1963 (2) KerLR 261; [1964] 3 S.C.R. 698

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate

Summary: Corporate - Companies Act, 1956, s. 75(1) - Company - Allotment of shares - Re-issue of forfeited shares - Whether re-issue of forfeited shares is allotment and return is to be filed - Provisions of s. 75 (1) and (5) under judicial consideration - No Allotment and no question of filing any return - Provision of s. 75(5) is ex abundanti cautela and s. 75(1) to be interpreted without reference to it - Appeal dismissed.
