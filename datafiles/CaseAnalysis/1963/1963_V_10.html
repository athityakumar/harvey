Ittavira Mathai v Varkey Varkey and Another

Supreme Court of India

15 January 1963

Case Analysis

Bench

J.R. Madholkar, Syed Jaffer Imam, K. Subba Rao, Raghubar Dayal

Where Reported

1963 Indlaw SC 306; AIR 1964 SC 907; 1963 KerLJ 952; 1963 (2) KerLR 277; 1963 KLT 1133; [1964] 1 S.C.R. 495

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Attachment Of Property

Summary: Civil Procedure - Practice & Procedure - Civil Procedure Code, 1908 - Indian Limitation Act, 1908, art. 142 - Suit for declaration of title and recovery of possession of property - Contended that decree in suit on hypothecation bond executed by one party to another was a nulity as suit barred by time - Held,Respondent not established that they were in possession within 12 years so suit barred by time - Appellant has not raised sufficient plea of limitation by stating relevant facts - Appellant relied on documents which were sham and bogus - Appeal before High Court was heard by bench of three judges - High Court of Travancore was itself abolished as a result of merger and new HC came into being - No right of parties have been infringed and appeal was rightly heard by bench of two judges so decree of HC affirmed - Appeal dismissed.
