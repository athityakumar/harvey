Shantistar Builders v Narayan Khimalal Gotame & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

31 January 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, B.L. Hansaria, Sujata V. Manohar

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 683; (1990) 1 SCC 520; AIR 1990 SC 630; 1990 (16) ALR 173; 1990 (1) CCC 384; JT 1990 (1) SC 106; 1990 (1) RCR(Rent) 289; 1990(1) SCALE 86; 1990 (1) UJ 379

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Constitution; Land & Property

Keywords: Urban Land (Ceiling and Regulation) Act 1976

Summary: Land & Property - Constitution - Urban Land (Ceiling and Regulation) Act, 1976, ss. 20 and 21 - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 46 and 136 - Expression 'weaker sections of society' - Scope - Construction over exempted lands covered u/s. 20 - Guidelines as to, framed by State of Maharashtra - Modification - Held, Supreme Court directed Central Govt. to prescribe appropriate guidelines laying down true scope of expression - State Govt. directed to modify guidelines as indicated by Supreme Court - Petition disposed of.
