H. R. Siddique v Madhya Pradesh State Road Transport Corporation and Another

Supreme Court of India

23 October 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 494; (1991) Supp2 SCC 693; AIR 1991 SC 310; 1992 (19) ATC 92; 1990 (61) FLR 751; JT 1990 (4) SC 240; 1991 LabIC 301; 1991 MPLJ 230; 1990 (6) SLR 28

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Age Of Superannuation, Madhya Pradesh State Road Transport Employees Service Regulations 1964

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, sch 2, item 6 - Prescribed retirement age of employee of State Transport Corporation (STC) is fifty eight years when he entered service - Earlier retirement age was sixty of employees employed by erstwhile Transport Company - Claim of retirement age by employee of STC as sixty - Held, cannot claim that he should be retired at sixty on that ground - Petition dismissed.
