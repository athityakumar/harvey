Commissioner of Wealth Tax v Smt. Anjamli Khan

Supreme Court of India

06 November 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ranganathan, K.N. Singh

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 273; (1991) Supp2 SCC 681; AIR 1991 SC 2023; 1990 (90) CTR 22; [1991] 187 ITR 345; JT 1990 (4) SC 584; 1990(2) SCALE 934; [1990] 2 S.C.R. 563; [1990] Supp2 S.C.R. 563; [1991] 54 TAXMAN 198; 1991 (2) TLR 748

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Land & Property; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Land & Property - Indian Limitation Act, 1908 - Wealth tax Act, 1957, s. 2(c) - Net wealth - Asset - Assessee's right to receive compensation for land vesting in Govt. under West Bengal Estates Acquisition Act - Held, that it is asset - Value of - Includible in net wealth - However, value on relevant valuation dates and payable in future only can be included - Appeal disposed of.
