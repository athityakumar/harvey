Rohit Pulp And Paper Mills Limited v Collector Of Central Excise, Baroda

Supreme Court of India

26 April 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ranganathan, A.M. Ahmadi

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 818; (1990) 3 SCC 447; AIR 1991 SC 754; 1990 CRLR 341; 1990 CrLR(SC) 341; 1990 (28) ECC 187; 1990 (30) ECR 1; 1990 (47) E.L.T. 491; JT 1990 (3) SC 245; 1990 SCALE 55; [1990] 2 S.C.R. 797

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Customs; Indirect Tax; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Excise - Central Excises & Salt Act, 1944, sch. I, item 17(1) - Notification 24 of 1984, 25 of 1984 and 45 of 1985 - Limitation Act, 1908 - Excise Duty - (A) Coated paper - Used for industrial purpose - Not exempted - Art paper and Chromo paper manufactured from unconventional raw material - Also fall under description 'coated paper' in 2nd Proviso to Notification No. 25/84 - If exempted - (B) Interpretation of statutes - Maxims and precedents - Noscitur sociis - Applicability - Held, not covered by 2nd Proviso, entitled to concessional rate - Further held, maxims and precedents not to be applied mechanically however noscitur sociis apply here.
