State Of Haryana And Anr. v Ram Diya

Supreme Court of India

10 April 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

Mr.Justice S. Ratnavel Pandian, K. Jayachandra Reddy

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 862; (1990) SCC (Cr) 426; (1990) 2 SCC 701; AIR 1990 SC 1336; 1990 (3) Crimes 35; 1990 CRLJ 1327; 1990 CRLR 400; 1990 CrLR(SC) 400; JT 1990 (2) SC 434; 1990(1) SCALE 760; [1990] 2 S.C.R. 431

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Criminal

Keywords: Mercy Petition

Summary: Prisons Act 1894, s. 59 - Punjab Jail Manual, 1916, Para 516 B - Premature release was in question - Accused sentenced to life imprisonment during conviction recorded prior to introduction of s. 433 A, Code Of Criminal Procedure - Held, case of accused should be considered in accordance with rules or executive instructions existing and applicable at relevant time - Non consideration of premature release of accused on ground that they have not completed 14 years of substantive imprisonment was not proper.
