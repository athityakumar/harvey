Union Of India And Anr v Paras Laminates (P) Ltd

Supreme Court of India

17 August 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

T.K. Thommen, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 371; (1990) 4 SCC 453; (1991) SCC (L&S) 208; AIR 1991 SC 696; 1990 (14) ATC 798; [1991] 70 Comp Cas 214; 1990 (87) CTR 180; 1990 (30) ECC 38; 1990 (30) ECR 305; 1990 (49) E.L.T. 322; [1990] 186 ITR 722; JT 1990 (3) SC 510; 1990(2) SCALE 283; [1990] 3 S.C.R. 789; 1991 (80) STC 263; 1990 (2) UJ 557

Case Digest

Subject: Environment, Wildlife & Animal

Keywords: Sen Customs Act 1962

Summary: Environment, Wildlife and Animal - Orissa Forest Produce (Control of Trade) Act, 1981, Pre., ss. 1(3), 5(2)(b), 12 - Constitution, art. 245 - Orissa Forest Act, 1972, Pre. - Amended of 1981 Act by the Orissa Act , 1987 - (A) Constitutionality - (B) Determination of object of Orissa Forest Act, 1972 in the light of 1981 act - (C) Effect of amendment of Orissa 1981 Act on contracts to cut and remove bamboos - Amendments are procedural changes and valid exercise of legislative power to regulate and control trade in forest produce, Orissa Act , 1987 is valid - Object of 1972 Act is protection and management of forest while object of 1981 Act is to control and regulate trade in certain forest produce by creating State monopoly - All rights including right to profit a pendre stood rescinded retrospectively from date of commencement of Orissa Act, 1981- Appeals dismissed.
