Life Insurance Corporation Of India v India Automobiles And Co. And Ors

Supreme Court of India

01 August 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ranganathan, K.N. Saikia

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 200; (1990) 4 SCC 286; AIR 1991 SC 884; JT 1990 (3) SC 353; 1991 (1) MLJ(SC) 1; 1990 (2) RCJ 367; 1990 (2) RCR(Rent) 344; 1990 (2) RentLR 168; 1990(2) SCALE 180; [1990] 3 S.C.R. 545; 1990 (2) UJ 341

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control

Keywords: Denial Of Title

Summary: Rent Control - Tamil Nadu Buildings (Lease and Rent Control) Act, 1960, ss. 4 and 10 - Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 11 - Jurisdiction of rent controller over application for fixation of fair rent - His decision on question of title is not final and conclusive - If operate as res judicata - Held, no - Order accordingly.
