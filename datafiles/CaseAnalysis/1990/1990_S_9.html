S. B. Kishore v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

01 November 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, M.H. Kania

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 1029; AIR 1991 SC 90

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, s. 4(1) - Land acquisition proceedings - Whether appellant entitled to allotment of plot after 19 years since acquiring of land ? - Held, as respondent have entertained claims belatedly in some instances, they are directed to make allotment to appellant - Appeal disposed of.
