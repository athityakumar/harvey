Abdul Sattar Abdul Kadar Shaikh v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

24 January 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jayachandra Reddy, B.C. Ray

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 669; (1990) SCC (Cr) 242; (1990) 1 SCC 480; 1990 (1) Crimes 385; 1990 CRLR 322; JT 1990 (1) SC 75; 1990(1) SCALE 64

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Municipalities & Local Governments

Summary: Criminal - Detention - Gujarat Prevention of Anti Social Activities Act, 1985 - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 32 and 22 - Habeas Corpus - Petition of detenu dismissed previously also against same detention order - Petitioner alleged that he was not supplied copies of First Information Report with detention order documents - Held, all necessary documents supplied to petitioner with detention order - Detenu was afforded full and reasonable opportunity to make effective representation - No merits in petition - Petition dismissed.
