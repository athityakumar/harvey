Mallikarjuna Rao And Ors. Etc. Etc v State Of Andhra Pradesh And Ors. Etc. Etc

Supreme Court of India

10 April 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 164; (1990) SCC (L&S) 387; (1990) 2 SCC 707; AIR 1990 SC 1251; 1990 (13) ATC 724; JT 1990 (3) SC 34; 1991 (1) LLJ 212; [1990] 2 S.C.R. 418

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 226 and 309, Proviso - Andhra Pradesh Animal Husbandry Service Special Rules, 1977, r. 2 - Administrative Tribunals Act, 1985, s. 14 - (A) Veterinary Asst. Surgeon, Class V - Eligibility for promotion to Class IV - (B) Service rules - Framing of - Direction by High Court or Administrative Tribunals to executive for exercise of rule-making power - Necessity - Held, (A) Eligibility is restricted to their respective categories and seniority in Class V is irrelevant - (B) Not warranted - Order accordingly.
