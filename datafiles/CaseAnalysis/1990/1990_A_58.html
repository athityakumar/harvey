State of Punjab v Assessing Authority, Chandigarh

Supreme Court of India

09 November 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ranganathan, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 188; (1991) Supp1 SCC 153; AIR 1991 SC 1059; 1991 (80) STC 396

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Sales Tax

Summary: Indirect Tax - Sales Tax - Punjab General Sales Tax Act, 1948, ss. 22(2)(b) and 2(d) - (A) Act 46 of 1948 - Application to High Court - Calling for reference from tribunal - HC not calling for such reference and disposing of application on ground not considered by Sales Tax Authority and Tribunal - Whether justified? - (B) 'Dealer' - Hospitality organisation, Punjab a department of State Govt. running canteens without profit motive - Whether a dealer? - (C) Whether can be assessed to tax under Act? - Held, (A) Not justified - (B) Is not dealer - (C) Cannot be assessed to tax - Appeal allowed.
