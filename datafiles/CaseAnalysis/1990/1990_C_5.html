Munisami Naidu v C. Ranganathan

Supreme Court of India

30 October 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

M.M. Punchhi, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 519; (1991) 2 SCC 139; AIR 1991 SC 492; 1991 (1) GLH 378; 1991 (1) MLJ 42; 1991 (1) MLJ(SC) 42; 1991 (1) RCR(Rent) 676

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Land & Property

Keywords: Madras Cultivating Tenancy Protection Act 1955

Summary: Rent Control - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 111(g) - Eviction on ground of denial of title - Mere statement by tenant that he was not aware as to who was his landlord - (A) Whether amounts to denial of title? - (B) Whether decree of eviction can be passed on ground of such statement? - Held, (A) not denial of title - (B) Decree of eviction cannot be passed on that ground - Appeal allowed.
