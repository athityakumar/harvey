Vannarakkal Kallalathil Sreedharan v Chandramaath Balakrishnan And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

06 March 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, R.M. Sahai

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 829; (1990) 3 SCC 291; 1990 (1) AWC 569; 1990 BBCJ 72; 1990 (1) CCC 678; 1990 (41) DLT 314; JT 1990 (1) SC 390; 1990(1) SCALE 519; [1990] 1 S.C.R. 832

Case Digest

Subject: Banking & Finance; Civil Procedure; Land & Property; Trusts & Associations

Summary: Civil P.C., 1908, Or. 38, Rule 10, Sec. 64 - Property in dispute was agreed to be sold under an agreement - Before the sale deed was executed, a third party in execution of a decree got the said property attached - Sale deed was executed subsequent to attachment - Sale will prevail over the attachement - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, Secs. 40, 54 - Trusts Act, Sec. 91 - AIR 1971 Pb. & Haryana 381 Overruled.
