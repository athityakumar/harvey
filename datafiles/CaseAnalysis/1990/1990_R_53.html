State Of Madhya Pradesh And Ors. v Rameshwar Rathod

Supreme Court of India

10 July 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, K.N. Saikia

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 477; (1990) 4 SCC 21; (1990) 4 SCC 23; (1990) SCC (Cr) 522; AIR 1990 SC 1849; 1990 (2) CCC 702; 1990 CRLJ 1756; 1990 CrLR(SC) 577; JT 1990 (3) SC 298; 1990(2) SCALE 88; [1990] 3 S.C.R. 263

Case Digest

Subject: Socio-Economic

Summary: Criminal - Essential Commodities Act, 1955, ss. 6 A and 7 - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, ss. 456 and 457 - (A) Interpretation of statutes - Provision of s. 6 A of Act - Amendment in 1974, whether prospective or retrospective? - (B) Confiscation of vehicle - Application for return - Jurisdiction of Criminal Court - In light of amendment - Held, (A) Prospective only - (B) Not ousted by Amendment Act - Appeal dismissed.
