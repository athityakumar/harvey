G. C. Agarwal v Commissioner of Income Tax, Assam, Nagaland, Etc

Supreme Court of India

07 September 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

K.N. Saikia, K.N. Singh, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 904; (1992) Supp1 SCC 495; 1991 (95) CTR 257; [1990] 186 ITR 571

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 256(1) and 271(1)(c) - Penalties - Calculation of penalties - Difference between tax on income shown in first returns and tax on incomes assessed shall be taken as amount of tax that would have been avoided - High Court answered both questions against assessee - Approved, 1975 Indlaw GUW 14 - Held, no error of law warranting interference - Appeal dismissed.
