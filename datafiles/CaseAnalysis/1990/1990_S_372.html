Shambhu Dayal Agarwala v State of West Bengal and Another

Supreme Court of India

03 May 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 211; (1990) SCC (Cr) 489; (1990) 3 SCC 549; 1990 (2) AWC 829; 1991 (1) CalLT(SC) 1; 1990 (2) Crimes 665; 1990 CRLR 464; 1990 CrLR(SC) 464; JT 1990 (2) SC 314; 1990 SCALE 183; [1990] 2 S.C.R. 987

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Essential Commodities Act, 1955, Secs. 6A, 6E and 3-Collector to whom a report of seizure of any essential commodity is made u/Sec. 6A is not empowered by virtue of Sec. 6E to release the goods seized in pursuance of an order made u/Sec. 3 in relation thereto during the pendency of the proceedings before the Special Court-Word 'release' used in Sec. 6E-Meaning of-It is not used in the sense of 'return'.
