Smt. Pushpa Devi And Ors. v Milkhi Ram

Supreme Court of India

08 February 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, N.M. Kasliwal

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 846; (1990) 2 SCC 134; AIR 1990 SC 808; JT 1990 (1) SC 176; 1990 (1) RCJ 307; 1990 (1) RCR(Rent) 334; 1990 (1) RentLR 393; 1990(1) SCALE 136; [1990] 1 S.C.R. 278

Case Digest

Subject: Insurance; Land & Property

Summary: Rent Control - East Punjab Urban Rent Restriction Act, 1949, ss. 13(2)(i), proviso, 2(h), 2(i) - Property - Interpretation - Provisio to s. 13(2)(i) of Act, 'tenant' - For purpose of making deposit of arrears of rent etc on first day of hearing of eviction proceedings - Purpose is to relieve defaulting tenant from eviction - Construction of provisio - Liberally constuing 'person' includes 'Person claiming to be tenant' as admitted jural relationship of landlord and tenant need not exist - Appeal allowed.
