Krishna Kishore Firm v Govt. Of. A.P. And Others

Supreme Court of India

21 September 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

R.M. Sahai, K. Jagannatha Shetty

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 471; (1991) 1 SCC 184; AIR 1990 SC 2292; JT 1990 (4) SC 241; 1990 (2) RCR(Rent) 588; 1990(2) SCALE 709; [1990] 2 S.C.R. 8; [1990] Supp2 S.C.R. 8

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Keywords: Special Relief Act, 1963, Rules framed under Andhra Pradesh Cinemas (Regulation) Act 1955 (40 of 1955)

Summary: Media & Entertainment - Andhra Pradesh Cinemas (Regulation) Act, 1955, ss. 6 and 11 - Andhra Pradesh Cinemas (Regulation) Rules, 1970, r. 11 - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 111(d) - Cinema Licence - Application for renewal - Lessee in possession of premises - Interest of one of co-owners acquired by lessee before expiry of lease - Whether lessee is entitled to file application? - Held, possession of lessee is lawful and he is entitled to file application - Appeal allowed.
