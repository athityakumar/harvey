Yadlapati Venkateswarlu v State Of Andhra Pradesh And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

11 September 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

K.N. Saikia, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 542; (1992) Supp1 SCC 74; AIR 1991 SC 704; [1991] 190 ITR 375; JT 1990 (4) SC 19; 1990(2) SCALE 519; [1990] 1 S.C.R. 381; [1990] Supp1 S.C.R. 381

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Income Tax & Direct Taxes; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure

Summary: Municipalities & Local Govts - Andhra Pradesh Municipalities Act, 1965, s. 4(1) - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 246 - (A) Property tax - Validation of tax levied by Municipal Council after coming into force of new Act - Govt. directing levy from certain earlier date, under provisions of old (repealed) )Act - (B) Interpretation of Statutes - Construction of amending Act - (C) Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 246 - Scope of power to pass law - Levy so made stands validated retrospectively u/s. 4(1) - It should be read as if words of amendment has been written into Act - Includes power to validate sections retrospectively - Appeal dismissed.
