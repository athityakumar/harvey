Pukhraj Jain v Mrs. Padma Kashyap And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

20 March 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

Mr.Justice R.M. Sahai, K. Jagannatha Shetty

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 755; (1990) 2 SCC 431; AIR 1990 SC 1133; 1990 (41) DLT 319; JT 1990 (1) SC 486; 1990 (1) RCJ 547; 1990 (1) RCR(Rent) 466; 1990 (1) RentLR 669; 1990(1) SCALE 531; [1990] 2 S.C.R. 25; 1990 (2) UJ 213

Case Digest

Subject: Rent Control; Tort

Keywords: Tortfeasors

Summary: Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958, ss. 2(e), 14 and 21 - Landlord and tenant - Term 'landlord' -includes his Legal Representative - Induction of tenant for limited period u/s. 21 - After prior permission of Controller - Death of landlord before expiry of period of tenancy - Can his LR initiate and continue proceedings for recovery of possession - Held, LR can initiate and continue proceedings for recovery of possession - Reason for letting out did not die with death of landlord - Appeal dismissed.
