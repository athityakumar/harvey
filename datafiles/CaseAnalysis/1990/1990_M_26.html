S.P. Dubey v M.P.S.R.T. Corpn. And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

23 October 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

Kuldip Singh, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 497; (1991) Supp1 SCC 426; (1991) SCC (L&S) 1010; AIR 1991 SC 276; 1991 (16) ATC 939; 1991 CRLR 49; 1990 (61) FLR 755; JT 1990 (4) SC 236; 1991 LabIC 1373; 1991 (1) LLN 339; 1991 MPLJ 1; 1990(2) SCALE 819; [1990] 2 S.C.R. 328; 1990 (6) SLR 24; [1990] Supp2 S.C.R. 328

Case Digest

Subject: Carriers & Transportation; Service

Summary: Carriers & Transportation - Service - Road Transport Corporations Act, 1950, ss. 34 - Madhya Pradesh state Road Transport Corporation Employees service Regulations, 1964, Regn. 59 - Superannuation - Company in which appellant worked was overtaken by state Govt. - Earlier age of superannuation was 60 years but now 58 years - Held, that appellant won't be affected by new rules - Not to be made applicable to employee of taken over company when notification by which company was taken over by State Govt. specifically stated that existing staff of company would not be adversely affected with regard to their conditions of service - Appeal allowed.
