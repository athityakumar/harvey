Kishan Lal and another v State Of Rajasthan & Ors.

Supreme Court of India

23 March 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

R.M. Sahai, K. Jagannatha Shetty

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 773; (1990) Supp SCC 742; AIR 1990 SC 2269; [1990] 183 ITR 433; JT 1990 (1) SC 550; 1990(1) SCALE 555; [1990] 2 S.C.R. 142; 1990 (78) STC 338; 1990 (1) UJ 625

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Sales Tax

Keywords: Rajasthan Agricultural Produce Markets Act, 1961

Summary: Agriculture & Agri Products - Rajasthan Agricultural Produce Markets Act, 1961, Pre, s. 2(1)(i) - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 245 and 246 - (A) Inclusion of manufactured articles as agricultural produce in Schedule - Validity of s. 40 empowering State Govt. to do so - Constitutionality - (B) Whether sugar is 'Agricultural produce' within meaning of s. 2(1)(i) of Act - Held, (A) Contention as to amendment on ground of excessive delegation of legislative power, not tenable - (B) Sugar is 'agricultural produce' within meaning of Act - Petition dismissed.
