B. N. Saxena v New Delhi Municipal Committee and Others

Supreme Court of India

25 July 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, R.M. Sahai

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 352; (1990) 4 SCC 205; (1990) SCC (L&S) 588; AIR 1990 SC 2021; 1990 (14) ATC 881; 1990 (61) FLR 357; JT 1990 (3) SC 284; 1990 LabIC 1614; 1990 (2) UPLBEC 979

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Summary: Service - Constitution of India, 1950 art. 226 - Promotion - Post of Heal Draftsman of Municipal Corporation - Person having no diploma qualification but having experience of service for six year as Seniors and Junior Draftsman - Whether entitled to be considered for promotion to post of Head Draftsman? - Held, entitled to be considered for said promotion - Appeal allowed.
