Smt. Yamuna Malo0 v Anand Swarup

Supreme Court of India

28 February 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

Ranganath Misra, K. Ramaswamy, M.M. Punchhi

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 734; (1990) 3 SCC 30; AIR 1990 SC 1725; 1990 (40) DLT 449; JT 1990 (1) SC 497; 1990 (1) RCJ 451; 1990 (1) RCR(Rent) 548; 1990 (1) RentLR 462; 1990(1) SCALE 384; [1990] 1 S.C.R. 715; 1990 (2) UJ 83

Case Digest

Subject: Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure; Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958, ss .21 and 14 - Tenancy for limited period - Application for eviction - After expiry of lease by efflux of time - (A) Scope and object of s.14 and s.21 of Act - (B) Objection as to validity - Conditions to be fulfilled - Plea cannot be raised, might be raised by tenant prior to lapse of lease - Appeal allowed.
