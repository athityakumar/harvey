Vijay Kumar Dharna Alias Koka v Union of India and Others

Supreme Court of India

09 February 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, K. Jayachandra Reddy, S. Ratnavel Pandian

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 850; (1990) SCC (Cr) 247; (1990) 1 SCC 606; AIR 1990 SC 1184; 1990 (1) Crimes 619; 1990 CRLJ 1187; 1990 CRLR 200; 1990 (47) E.L.T. 201; JT 1990 (1) SC 183; 1990(1) SCALE 154; 1990 (1) UJ 623

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Conservation of Foreign Exchange and Prevention of Smuggling Activities Act , 1974, s. 3 - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 22(5) - Detention - Effective representation - Purpose of passing detention order as stated in order and grounds on which satisfaction on D A was reached at variance with each other - Effective representation against detention, if possible - Consequence - Held, impossible - Detention, liable to be quashed - Appeal allowed.
