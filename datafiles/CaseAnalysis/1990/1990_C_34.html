Prabartak Commercial Corporation Limited v Chief Administrator, Dandakaranya Project and Another

Supreme Court of India

14 December 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

T.K. Thommen, Mr.Justice R.M. Sahai

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 624; (1991) 1 SCC 498; AIR 1991 SC 957; 1991 ALR 242; 1991 (1) ARBLR 282; 1991 (2) CCC 219; JT 1991 (5) SC 105; 1990(2) SCALE 1260; 1991 (1) UJ 309

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Keywords: Arbitrator

Summary: Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration Act, 1940, s. 13 - Arbitrator - Jurisdiction - Appellant entered into contract with respondents for supply of 'hard granite chips' for the construction of NH/43 at four reaches - Dispute arose between the parties in respect of the rate of payment - Respondents contended that the matter in dispute was outside the scope of the arbitration clause - Objection was rejected by District Judge - Arbitrator was appointed - Preliminary decree was directed to be drawn up despite the objections filed by the respondents u/s. 33 of the Act - Appeal before HC - Appeal was dismissed - Hence instant appeal - Whether the order recorded by HC was sustainable.

Held, HC held that cl. 14 containing the arbitration agreement had no application to the dispute in question which fell u/cl. 13-A and, therefore, the arbitrator had no jurisdiction in the matter. HC held that the reference of the dispute to the arbitrator was invalid and the entire proceedings before the arbitrator including the awards made by him were null and void. Appeal dismissed.
