State Of Andhra Pradesh And Anr. Etc. v R.V. Rayanim Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

15 January 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Mukharji, K. Jayachandra Reddy, M.M. Punchhi

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 971; (1990) 1 SCC 433; AIR 1990 SC 626; 1990 (16) ALR 508; 1990 (1) ARBLR 1; 1990 (1) CCC 433; JT 1990 (1) SC 57; 1990(1) SCALE 47; [1990] 1 S.C.R. 54; 1990 (1) UJ 591

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR; Civil Procedure; Family & Personal

Summary: Practice & Procedure - Arbitration & ADR - Arbitration Act, 1940, s. 30 - Award - Setting aside of - While passing award arbitrator considering claim made on basis of escalation and damages but awarding total sum as he finds admissible in respect of claims which arbitrator had adjudged - Whether award can be set aside on ground of being passed in excess of jurisdiction, or by an error apparent on face of record ? - Held, award cannot be set aside on these ground - Petition dismissed.
