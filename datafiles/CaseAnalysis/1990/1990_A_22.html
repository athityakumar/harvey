Victoria Technical Institute v Additional Commissioner of Income Tax and Another

Supreme Court of India

16 November 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

Mr.Justice R.M. Sahai, K.N. Saikia

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 925; (1991) Supp2 SCC 389; AIR 1991 SC 997; 1991 (94) CTR 153; [1991] 188 ITR 57; JT 1990 (4) SC 546; 1991 (2) TLR 9

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Keywords: Activity For Profit

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 2(15) and 11 - Charitable purpose - Purpose must not involve carrying on of activity of profit - Fact that monies for accomplishment or implementation of purpose is obtained by carrying of an activity for profit, if material - Held, is immaterial - Appeals allowed.
