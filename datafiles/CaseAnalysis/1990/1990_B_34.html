Sheshrao Jangluji Bagde v Bhaiyya S/o Govindrao Karale and Others

Supreme Court of India

18 September 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

Veeraswami Ramaswami, M.M. Punchhi

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 962; (1991) Supp1 SCC 367; (1991) SCC (L&S) 1006; AIR 1991 SC 76; 1991 (16) ATC 938; 1990 (61) FLR 768; JT 1990 (4) SC 515; 1990 LabIC 2009; 1991 (2) LLJ 109; 1991 (1) LLN 36; 1990(2) SCALE 600; [1990] 1 S.C.R. 521; 1991 (1) SLR 172; [1990] supp1 S.C.R. 521

Case Digest

Subject: Family & Personal; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Maharashtra Ground Water Service Class (I) (Recruitments) Rules 1976 (as amended on Feb 16 1968 and Published in the gazette on 24 March 1988)

Summary: Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 311 - Maharashtra Ground Water Service, Class I (Recruitment) Rules, 1976, r. 3 - Promotion - Practical experience as eligibility criterion - Promotion of person having no such experience - Subsequent deletion of requirement for purpose of promotion; however, retained for direct appointment - Whether promotion was illegal ? - Held, promotion not illegal though employee was not eligible for direct appointment.
