M.M.R. Khan And Ors. Etc. v Union Of India And Ors. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

27 February 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

P.B. Sawant, K. Ramaswamy, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 729; (1990) Supp SCC 191; (1990) SCC (L&S) 632; AIR 1990 SC 937; 1991 (16) ATC 541; 1990 (61) FLR 271; JT 1990 (3) SC 1; 1995 (3) LLJ 166; 1990 (2) LLN 197; 1990(1) SCALE 324; [1990] 1 S.C.R. 687; 1990 (4) SLR 666; 1990 (1) UPLBEC 498

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Tamil Nadu Land Reforms (Fixation of Ceiling on Lands) Act, 1961

Summary: Carriers & Transportation - Railway Establishment Manual, Chap. XXVIII, Paras 2829, 2830 and 2832 - Railway Canteens - Statutory and Non-statutory Recognised - Employees of - Whether entitled to be treated as railway employees - Held, are entitled - But not employees in Non-statutory non-recognised canteens - Appeal and Petitions allowed.
