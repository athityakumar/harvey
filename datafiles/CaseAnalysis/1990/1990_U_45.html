Hind Builders Etc. v Union Of India And Vice-Versa

Supreme Court of India

24 April 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ranganathan, A.M. Ahmadi

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 805; (1990) 3 SCC 338; AIR 1990 SC 1340; AIR 1991 SCW 2349; 1990 (1) ARBLR 349; 1990 (2) CalLT(SC) 26; 1990 (2) CCC 182; JT 1990 (2) SC 186; 1990(1) SCALE 788; [1990] 2 S.C.R. 638; 1990 (2) UJ 272

Case Digest

Subject: Arbitration & ADR

Summary: Arbitration Act, 1940, s. 30 - Setting aside of Award - Error apparent on face of record - Arbitrators, experienced engineers - Certain clauses of contract on which two views were possible - Arbitrators acting upon particular interpretation - Interference by Court by adopting its own interpretation - Held, is not permissible.
