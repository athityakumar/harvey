Shrawan Kumar Jha and Others v State of Bihar and Others

Supreme Court of India

13 November 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 555; (1991) Supp1 SCC 330; (1991) SCC (L&S) 1078; AIR 1991 SC 309; 1991 (16) ATC 937; 1991 LabIC 1317; 1992 (6) SLR 718

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Constitution - Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 226 and 311 - Natural Justice - Appointments of teachers appointed by District Superintendent of Schools cancelled by Govt. on ground that District Superintendent had no authority - Need of opportunity of hearing - Validity of appointments - Held, prior opportunity of hearing should have been given - Direction issued that Secy., Education or his nominee should give opportunity and give finding whether appointments were validly made - Appeal dismissed.
