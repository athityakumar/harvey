Vijaya Bank v Commissioner of Income Tax (Additional)

Supreme Court of India

19 September 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

S.C. Agrawal, T.K. Thommen

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 915; (1991) Supp2 SCC 147; AIR 1991 SC 239; 1991 (94) CTR 216; [1991] 187 ITR 541; [1991] 57 TAXMAN 152

Case Digest

Subject: Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Income Tax Act, 1961, ss. 18, 19, 20 and 37 - Whether claim of deduction u/ss. 19, 20 and 37 on income earned as interest on securities by assessee bank was justified? - Held, income earned as interest on securities purchased by assessee bank is taxable u/s. 18 - Claim of deduction of expenditure was not justified as expenditure was not incurred for purpose of realising interest on securities - Claim in question not justified - Appeal dismissed.
