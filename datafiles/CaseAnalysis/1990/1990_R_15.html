Rajasthan State Electricity Board and Others v Laxman Lal and Others

Supreme Court of India

11 October 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

N.M. Kasliwal, P.B. Sawant

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 547; (1991) Supp2 SCC 531; 1992 (19) ATC 247; 1990(2) SCALE 778; [1990] 2 S.C.R. 277; [1990] Supp2 S.C.R. 277; 1991 (1) UJ 28

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Code of Civil Procedure 1908

Summary: Service - Electricity (Supply) Act, 1948, ss. 70 and 79 - Rajasthan State Electricity Board Employees (Emoluments) Regulations, 1978 - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 19(2) - Whether respondents who were appointed after 01-04-1974 entitled to pay scale No. 2 or pay scale No. 3? - Ambiguity of first settlement which simply made mention of Meter Reader/Meter Checker to whom pay scale No. 3 was given was clarified by an agreement between Board and Union representing employees as early as on 01-04-1974 itself to effect that Meter Reader/Meter Checker can be placed in two different grades and after this there was no restriction on Board to make appointment of Meter Reader/Meter Checker in grade II after 1-04-1974, also there was clear mention in appointment orders of respondents that they were appointed as Meter Reader/Meter Checker grade II in pay scale No. 2 and quashing of second settlement was at least a sufficient notice within meaning of s. 19(2) of Industrial Dispute Act that Board had terminated first settlement after 31-03-1974 - Held, Rajasthan Regulation made in exercise of powers conferred by s.79 sub-ss. (c) and (k) of Electricity (Supply) Act have statutory force and it has been clearly mentioned that they shall be deemed to have been made applicable from 1-04-1974, in Sch. II group 'B' at Item No. 7 Meter Reader II/Meter Checker II has been fixed in revised pay scale of Rs. 260-464 (original scale Rs. 80-194) and in group 'C' at Item No. 21 Meter Reader I/Meter Checker I in scale No. 3 revised pay scale Rs. 370-570 (original scale Rs. 126-250) - Appeals allowed.
