Gurmail Singh And Ors. Etc. Etc. v State Of Punjab And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

25 October 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

S. Ranganathan, N.M. Kasliwal, P.B. Sawant

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 503; (1991) SCC (L&S) 147; (1991) 1 SCC 189; AIR 1993 SC 1388; 1991 (62) FLR 458; JT 1991 (1) SC 351; 1993 LabIC 428; 1991 (2) LLJ 76; 1991 (1) LLN 587; 1990(2) SCALE 864; [1990] 2 S.C.R. 367; 1991 (2) SLJ 150; 1992 (7) SLR 744; [1990] Supp2 S.C.R. 367

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Labour Appellate Tribunal

Summary: Labour & Industrial Laws - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 25FF - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 14 - Transfer of undertaking by State to State owned Corporation - Re-employment - (A) Pension - Continuity of service - (B) Scope of s. 25FF - Judicial review by Court - Held, continuity of service upheld in instant case for sole purpose of pensionary benefits - Court can direct re-employment in appropriate cases - Order accordingly.
