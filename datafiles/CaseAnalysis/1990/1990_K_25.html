Union of India and Others v Kanunga Industries

Supreme Court of India

07 September 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

P.B. Sawant, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 289; (1992) Supp1 SCC 35; (1992) SCC (Cr) 76; AIR 1990 SC 2190; 1992 CRLR 220; 1990 (30) ECR 449; 2004 (178) E.L.T. 19; JT 1990 (3) SC 723; 1990(2) SCALE 447; 1990 (2) UJ 653

Case Digest

Subject: Trade

Keywords: Bombay Prohibition Act 1949, Sea Customs Act 1962

Summary: Customs Act, 1962, s. 112(a) - Interpretation - Taxation - Applicability of para 85 of Import Trade Control Hand Book, 1969 - By correction of entry 'Stainless steel strips' brought within prohibited import items - Licensee contracting with foreign party before correction - Effect - Import of strips, not unauthorised - Appeal dismissed.
