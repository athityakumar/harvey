Samrat International (P) Limited v Collector Of Central Excise Hyderabad

Supreme Court of India

21 September 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Fathima Beevi, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 469; (1992) Supp1 SCC 293; (1993) SCC (L&S) 580; AIR 1991 SC 369; 1993 (24) ATC 355; 1990 CRLR 667; 1990 CrLR(SC) 667; 1991 (31) ECC 207; 1991 (33) ECR 19; 1992 (58) E.L.T. 561; JT 1991 (1) SC 181; 1993 LabIC 38; 1990(2) SCALE 747; 1993 (1) SLJ 96; 1993 (1) SLR 118; [1990] Supp2 S.C.R. 1; 1993 (1) UJ 326

Case Digest

Subject: Excise

Keywords: Provisional Assessment

Summary: Excise - Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944, s. 11B, explanation para (B), cl. (e), para (B), cl. (f) - Central Excise Rules, 1944, rr. 173B and 173C - Limitation Act, 1908 - Excises Duty - Claim for refund - (A) Limitation - Relevant date - Assessee maintaining personal ledger account - Duty paid by debit entry - (B) Payment of duty on clearance of goods - 'Self-removal' procedure - Clearing goods by determining duty by assessee, himself - Amount debited in personal ledger maintained by him - Claim for refund of excess amount paid - Held, it is effective payment, cl. (f) does not apply - Provision of cl. (e) is attracted - Appeal allowed.
