H. C. Puttaswamy and Others v Hon'ble Chief Justice of Karnataka High Court and Others

Supreme Court of India

05 November 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 242; (1991) Supp2 SCC 421; (1992) SCC (L&S) 53; AIR 1991 SC 295; 1992 (19) ATC 292; 1991 (1) CLR 362; 1991 (62) FLR 8; JT 1990 (4) SC 474; 1991 LabIC 235; 1991 (1) LLN 352; 1990(2) SCALE 942; [1990] 2 S.C.R. 552; 1991 (1) SLR 166; [1990] Supp2 S.C.R. 552; 1991 (1) UJ 126

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Indirect Tax; Labour & Industrial Law; Service

Keywords: Code of Civil Procedure 1908, Karnataka Civil Services (General recruitment) Rules 1977, Karnataka Civil Services (Ministerial Posts) Recruitment Rules 1966, Karnataka Sub Ordinate Courts (Ministerial and Other Posts) recruitment Rules 1977

Summary: Constitution of India, arts. 309, 136 and 229 - Karnataka Civil Services (Ministerial Posts) Recruitment Rules, 1966, r. 1 - Appointment - Employees of Subordinate Court - Appointments made by Chief Justice of High Court - No consultation Public Service Commission - Held, not proper.
