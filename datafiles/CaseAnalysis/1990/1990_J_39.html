Mehra Brothers v Joint Commercial Officer, Madras

Supreme Court of India

14 November 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, S. Ranganathan

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 561; (1991) 1 SCC 514; AIR 1991 SC 1017; 1995 (61) ECR 204; 1991 (51) E.L.T. 173; JT 1990 (4) SC 750; 1990(2) SCALE 1063; 1991 (80) STC 233; [1990] Supp3 S.C.R. 61

Case Digest

Subject: Customs; Indirect Tax; Sales Tax

Keywords: Tamil Nadu Sales Tax Act, 1959

Summary: Sales Tax - Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax Act, 1959, s. 3(3), chap. 1, Item 3 - Motor Vehicles - Accessories - Whether car seat covers or upholstery are accessories and exigible to sales tax under entry 3 of sch 1 r/w s. 3(3) - Test has to be applied - Held, correct test would be whether article or articles in question would be adjunct or accompaniment or addition for convenient use of another part of vehicle or adds to beauty, elegance or comfort for use of motor vehicle - General adaptability may be relevant but may not by itself be conclusive - Car seat covers or upholstery are accessories for comfortable use of motor vehicles or for adding elegance to seat - They are therefore exigible to sales tax at 13 per cent under entry 3 of sch. 1 r/w s. 3(3) - Appeal dismissed.
