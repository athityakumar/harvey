Sohan Lal And Ors. v State Of Rajasthan

Supreme Court of India

21 August 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

K.N. Saikia, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 83; (1990) 4 SCC 580; (1990) SCC (Cr) 650; AIR 1990 SC 2158; 1990 (3) Crimes 120; 1990 (3) Crimes 121; 1990 CRLJ 2302; 1990 CrLR(SC) 509; JT 1990 (3) SC 599; 1990(2) SCALE 307; [1990] 3 S.C.R. 809

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Fresh Evidence, Charge - Sheet, Cognizance of an offence, Cognizable offence

Summary: Criminal - Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, ss. 216, 319 and 398 - (A) Accused already discharged of all charges - Application for addition or alteration of charge in relation to him - If maintainable - (B) Applicability of ss. 319, 398 of CrPC - Person found to have been accused in case - Held, (A) Not maintainable - (B) He cannot be proceeded against u/s. 319 - Order accordingly.
