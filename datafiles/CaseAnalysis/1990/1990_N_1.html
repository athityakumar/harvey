R.M. Narayana Chettiar And Another v N. Lakshmanan Chetfiar And Others

Supreme Court of India

11 October 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

M.H. Kania, L.M. Sharma

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 546; (1991) 1 SCC 48; AIR 1991 SC 221; 1990 (3) CCC 507; JT 1991 (5) SC 408; 1991 (1) KarLJ 219; 1991 (1) MLJ(SC) 36; 1990(2) SCALE 803; [1990] 2 S.C.R. 266; [1990] Supp2 S.C.R. 266; 1991 (1) UJ 22

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure

Keywords: Code of Civil Procedure 1908

Summary: Code Of Civil Procedure, 1908, s. 92 - Grant of leave in suit under s. 92 - Notice to defendants - Whether the court is bound ? - Held, appeal lies only against refusal of leave and not against grant of leave - Always open to defendant to file an application for revocation of leave - Under rule of caution it should normally give such notice - Court not bound to.
