H.S.S.K. Niyami And Ors v Union Of India And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

21 August 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, N.M. Kasliwal

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 384; (1990) 4 SCC 516; AIR 1990 SC 2128; JT 1990 (3) SC 579; 1990(2) SCALE 286; [1990] 3 S.C.R. 862

Case Digest

Subject: Socio-Economic

Keywords: Defence of India Rules, 1962

Summary: Socio-Economic - Essential Commodities Act, 1955, s. 3(3C) - Constitution of India, 1950, arts. 19(1)(g), 19(1)(f), 31 C and 226 - (A) Constitutionality of s. 3(3C) of 1955 Act - (B) Sugar factories - Zoning of, for fixation of price - Whether individual notice and hearing before placing them in particular zone be given? - Held, (A) Question not open as Act has been included in 9th Schedule to Constitution - (B) Need not be given, as it is a legislative act - Appeal dismissed.
