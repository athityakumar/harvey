T. N. Saxena and Others v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others

Supreme Court of India

22 August 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Ramaswamy, M.M. Punchhi, Ranganath Misra

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 388; (1991) Supp2 SCC 551; (1992) SCC (L&S) 82; AIR 1991 SC 235; 1992 (19) ATC 96; 1991 LabIC 214

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Service - Constitution - Constitution of India, 1950, art. 311 - Fixation of inter se seniority between direct recruits and promotees - Recruitment from two sources - Recruitment of cadre is subject to rules of 1964 - Determination of Seniority - Held, if rules are framed in this regard, it must ordinarily be followed strictly - Seniority is to be guided by quota rule - Order accordingly.
