 Krishena Kumar and Others v Union Of India And Ors.

Supreme Court of India

13 July 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

K.N. Saikia, B.C. Ray, M.H. Kania, S.C. Agrawal, S. Mukharji

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 316; (1990) 4 SCC 207; (1991) SCC (L&S) 112; AIR 1990 SC 1782; 1990 (14) ATC 846; JT 1990 (3) SC 173; 1990 LabIC 1490; 1991 (1) LLJ 191; 1990 (2) LLN 1063; 1990(2) SCALE 44; [1990] 3 S.C.R. 352; 1990 (4) SLR 716; 1990 (2) UPLBEC 1257

Case Digest

Subject: Service

Keywords: Computation Of Pension

Summary: Service - Constitution Of India, 1950, arts. 14 and 311 - Pension - Option - Cut off date - Railway employees covered by Provident Fund Scheme - Option given to them to switch over to pension scheme from specified cut - Whether discriminatory? - Whether clause giving option should be struck down? - Held, not discriminatory - Question of striking down of clause would not arise - Petition dismissed.
