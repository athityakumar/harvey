Barauni Refinery Pragatisheel Shramikparishad And Others v Indian Oil Corporation Limited And Others

Supreme Court of India

12 July 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, K. Ramaswamy

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 601; (1991) SCC (L&S) 1; (1991) 1 SCC 4; AIR 1990 SC 1801; 1990 (6) FLR 203; JT 1990 (3) SC 123; 1990 LabIC 1481; 1991 (1) LLJ 46; 1990 (2) LLN 288; 1990(2) SCALE 14; [1990] 3 S.C.R. 282; 1990 (4) SLR 702; 1990 (2) UJ 485

Case Digest

Subject: Labour & Industrial Law

Keywords: Age Of Superannuation, Industrial Employment (Standing Order ) Act, 1946

Summary: Labour & Industrial Law - Industrial Employment (Standing Orders) Act, 1946, s. 5 - Standing Orders of Indian Oil Corporation concerning Barauni Refinery, Standing Order No. 20 - Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, s. 18 - Settlement - Between employees' Union and Management - Binding nature of - Clause therein - Service conditions not changed by settlement - No specific mention about age of retirement - Whether employees can approach for modification of standing order for fixation of age of retirement? - Held, employees cannot approach authorities seeking modification of standing order for fixation of age of retirement - Appeal dismissed.
