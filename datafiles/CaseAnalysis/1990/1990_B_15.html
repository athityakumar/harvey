Union Of India And Ors. v Bakshi Ram

Supreme Court of India

01 March 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, R.M. Sahai

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 648; (1990) SCC (L&S) 288; (1990) 2 SCC 426; AIR 1990 SC 987; 1990 (12) ATC 914; 1990 (1) AWC 574; 1990 (1) CCC 533; 1990 CRLJ 1013; 1990 (60) FLR 571; JT 1990 (1) SC 339; 1990 LabIC 806; 1990 (1) LLJ 498; 1990 (2) LLN 228; 1990(1) SCALE 321; [1990] 1 S.C.R. 760; 1990 (2) SLR 65

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Criminal; Defence & Security Forces; Labour & Industrial Law

Summary: Probation of Offenders Act, 1958 - Central Reserve Police Force Act, 1949, s. 10 - Employee dismissed from service in view of conviction under s. 10 of CRPF Act - Subsequent release on probation under Probation of Offenders Act - Scope of s. 12 of Act - Whether entitled for reinstatement in service? - Held, Not entitled to reinstatement in service upon getting benefit of probation of good conduct - Department not precluded from taking action for misconduct leading to offence or conviction thereon under s. 12 of Probation of Offenders Act - Provision not intended to exonerate person from departmental punishment.
