Budhwa Alias Ramcharan And Ors v State Of Madhya Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

05 October 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

M. Fathima Beevi, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 58; (1991) Supp1 SCC 9; (1991) SCC (Cr) 237; AIR 1991 SC 4; 1990 CRLJ 2597; JT 1990 (4) SC 64; 1991 (2) ShimLC 26; [1990] Supp2 S.C.R. 101

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, s. 386 - Evidence Act, 1872, s. 3 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, ss. 147, 149 and 302 - Murder - Common Object - Group rivalry and enmity - Assault by number of persons - Confliction between medical evidence and evidence of witnesses - Participation of some accused only proved - Conviction of others - Held, conviction could be sustained only against accused persons whose offence proved beyond doubt - Others have benefit of doubt.
