Vijayee Singh And Ors. v State Of Uttar Pradesh

Supreme Court of India

20 April 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jayachandra Reddy, M. Fathima Beevi, S. Ratnavel Pandian

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 795; (1990) 3 SCC 190; (1990) SCC (Cr) 378; AIR 1990 SC 1459; 1990 ALJ 415; 1990 (2) AWC 1201; 1990 (2) Crimes 584; 1990 CRLJ 1510; 1990 CrLR(SC) 376; JT 1990 (2) SC 596; 1990(1) SCALE 163; [1990] 2 S.C.R. 573

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Keywords: Injuries On Accused

Summary: Criminal - Indian Evidence Act, 1872, ss. 3, 101, 102, 103, 104 and 105 - Indian Penal Code, 1860, s. 96 - General Exceptions - Plea of self-defence - Burden of proof - Scope - Held, lies on accused - Gets discharged if probability is proved or reasonable doubt about prosecution case is raised - Order accordingly.
