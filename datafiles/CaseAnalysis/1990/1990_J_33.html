Niranjan Singh Karam Singh Punjabi Andors Etc. Etc. v Jitendra Bhimaraj Bijje And Ors. Etc. Etc.

Supreme Court of India

07 August 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

A.M. Ahmadi, N.M. Kasliwal

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 81; (1990) 4 SCC 76; (1991) SCC (Cr) 47; AIR 1990 SC 1962; 1990 CRLJ 1869; JT 1990 (3) SC 408; [1990] 3 S.C.R. 633

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal

Summary: Criminal - Criminal Procedure Code, 1973, ss. 227 and 228 - Terrorist and Disruptive Activities (Prevention) Act, 1987, s. 3(1) - What is not Terrorist Act? - Persons belonging to gang committing offences like murder - Their prior statement that they would create terror and fear in minds of people and gain supremacy in under-world - Whether statement constitutes offence u/s. 3(1) - Held, statement revealed intention to kill and not to strike terror - Does not constitute offence - Appeal dismissed.
