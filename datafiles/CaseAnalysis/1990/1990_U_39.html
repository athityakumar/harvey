Abdu Salam @ Thiyyan S/O Thiyyan Mohammad,Detenu No. 962, Ge v Union Of India And Others

Supreme Court of India

17 April 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jayachandra Reddy, S. Ratnavel Pandian

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 994; (1990) 3 SCC 15; (1990) SCC (Cr) 451; AIR 1990 SC 1446; 1990 (3) Crimes 82; 1990 CRLJ 1502; 1990 CrLR(SC) 405; 1990 (48) E.L.T. 162; JT 1990 (3) SC 74; 1990(1) SCALE 31; [1990] 2 S.C.R. 517

Case Digest

Subject: Criminal; Fema

Summary: Criminal - Conservation Of Foreign Exchange And Prevention Of Smuggling Activities Act, 1974, s. 3 - Constitution Of India, 1950, art. 22(5) - Representation - Delay - (A) In consideration by Central Govt. - (B) In passing order - (C) In arresting detenu pursuant to order of detention - Explained - Constitutionality / validity - Held, art. 22(5) not violated - Delay reasonably explained - Delay by itself would not invalidate detention - Appeal dismissed.
