Rai Chand Jain v Miss Chandrakanta Khosla

Supreme Court of India

15 November 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

B.C. Ray, R.M. Sahai

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 569; (1991) 1 SCC 422; AIR 1991 SC 744; JT 1990 (4) SC 638; 1991 (1) RCJ 5; 1991 (1) RCR(Rent) 128; 1991 (1) RentLR 76; 1990(2) SCALE 1131; [1990] Supp3 S.C.R. 91

Case Digest

Subject: Civil Procedure; Municipalities & Local Governments; Practice & Procedure; Rent Control

Summary: Rent Control - East Punjab Urban Rent Restriction Act, 1949, ss. 13, 14 and 15(5) - Transfer of Property Act, 1882, s. 111 - Civil Procedure Code, 1908, s. 115 - Registration Act, 1908, s. 49 - Unregistered lease deed - Proof - Forfeiture - Findings of appellate authority not based on evidence on record - (A) Revisional jurisdiction of High Court u/s. 15(5) of 1949, Act as compared to that u/s. 115, CPC, 1908 - (B) Consideration of collateral for determining, pourpose of lease-residential or non-residential - (C) Proof - As per lease deed, in individual capacity and for residential purpose - But rent receipts on letter heads, tenant sole proprietor of business, voucher and landlady acknowledging same - (D) Building put upon lease land put to unauthorised use, whether lease can be forfeited in light of ss. 14(11) and 13 of Act? - Held, HC can reverse them as wider powers under Act - Can be considered - Inference that lease was for business purpose cannot be drawn therefrom - Not forfeited - Appeal dismissed.
