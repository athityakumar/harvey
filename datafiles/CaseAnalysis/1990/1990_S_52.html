Second Income Tax Officer v Stumpp Schuele And Somappa (P) Ltd.

Supreme Court of India

14 September 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

K. Jagannatha Shetty, K.N. Singh, Kuldip Singh

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 440; 1991 (94) CTR 160; [1991] 187 ITR 108

Case Digest

Subject: Corporate; Income Tax & Direct Taxes

Summary: Corporate - Income Tax & Direct Taxes - Companies (Profits) Surtax Act, 1964,ss. 8 and 16 - Whether the High Court was correct in quashing the notices issued u/ss. 8 and 16 of the Act? - Held, preponderance of judicial opinion in favour of the assessee - Appeal dismissed.
