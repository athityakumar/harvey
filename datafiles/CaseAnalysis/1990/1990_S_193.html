State Of U.P. And Ors. v Delhi Cloth Mills And Anr.

Supreme Court of India

09 October 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

K.N. Saikia, S.C. Agrawal

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 428; (1991) 1 SCC 454; AIR 1991 SC 735; JT 1990 (4) SC 131; 1990(2) SCALE 719; [1990] Supp2 S.C.R. 168

Case Digest

Subject: Constitution; Practice & Procedure

Keywords: Competence Of The Legislature

Summary: Excise - United Provinces Excise Act, 1910, s. 28 - United Provinces Excise Manual, rr. 636 and 814 - Excise Duty - Country liquor (spirit and military rum) - Export - Excess wastage of liquor occurring during transport - Validity of levy of Excise duty by exporting state on such excess wastage - Held, valid - Appeal allowed.
