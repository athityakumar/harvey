Hissar Improvement Trust v Rukmani Devi and Another

Supreme Court of India

18 July 1990

Case Analysis

Bench

T.K. Thommen, Veeraswami Ramaswami

Where Reported

1990 Indlaw SC 330; (1990) Supp SCC 806; AIR 1990 SC 2033

Case Digest

Subject: Land & Property

Summary: Land & Property - Land Acquisition Act, 1894, ss. 31 and 34 - Compensation - Amount not paid in time - Liability of Collector - Before taking possession of land, whether Collector has to pay or deposit amount awarded, as stated in s. 31, failing which he is liable to pay interest as provided in s. 34? - Held, landowner' right to be compensated is enforceable against State - It is liability of Collector to pay amount awarded together with interest - Order accordingly.
